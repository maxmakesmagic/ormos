Theros Beyond Death Promo Pack Updates
======================================



 Posted in **News**
 on January 15, 2020 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The Prerelease for *Theros Beyond Death* is just around the corner with events worldwide from January 17 to January 20. With every card finally revealed, we hope you're excited to journey back to the world of Theros this weekend.


But before you do, we have a small update to the promo packs you'll be receiving at your local game store. Starting with *Theros Beyond Death* and continuing with *Ikoria: Lair of Behemoths* and *Core Set 2021*, some promo packs will contain a card that may not be Standard-legal.


With 2020 being the year of Commander for *Magic*, we felt it was the perfect time to experiment with broadening the appeal of the promo packs such that it aligns with the different ways people like to enjoy the game. To do so, we're opening up the promo pack slot that is normally reserved to a curated list of Standard-legal rares to include some rares outside the format that are focused on Commander. This change only affects that one slot in the promo pack.


Of course, we wanted to keep the Theros feel of the promo packs intact, so there are only 20 non-standard cards you could see in the full list of 121 cards.
