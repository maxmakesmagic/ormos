By Adam Styborski




 Stybs has played Magic the world over, writing and drafting as part of the event coverage team and slinging Commander everywhere his decks will fit.

At Grand Prix Providence, these were the decks that earned an unblemished record on the first day, and set the stage for several to make a run at the Top 8.
