Tenth Edition Vampire Bats
==========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 8, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











During Selecting [*Tenth Edition*](/en/node/642111), you selected Chippy's sketch of [Vampire Bats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampire+Bats) for the card's *Tenth Edition* printing. The final art is done-and we thought you'd like to see it way, way in advance of *Tenth's* release in Summer 2007.

First, here was Chippy's original sketch:

  
*[Vampire Bats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampire+Bats) sketch by Chippy*

And here is the final piece in full color.

  
*[Vampire Bats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampire+Bats) art by Chippy*

*Tenth Edition* (expansion symbol: big Roman numeral X) hits shelves in Summer, 2007. Stay tuned to **magicthegathering.com** for more information on *Tenth Edition!*
