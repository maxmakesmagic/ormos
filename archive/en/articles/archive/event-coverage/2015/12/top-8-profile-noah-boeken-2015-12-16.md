Top 8 Profile - Noah Boeken
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPHEL00/166.jpg)**Name:** Noah Boeken  
**Age:** 19  
**Location:** Amsterdam, Netherlands  
**Occupation:** Pro Magic Player  
**Played Magic since:** Fallen Empires  
**Best accomplishments:** 2000 Euro Champion



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Helsinki?**  

A: The weather, no kidding, I came with my girlfriend to see Helsinki.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Top 16.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: Relaxed and delicious.



**Q: What do you think about the formats used in this tournament, and what was your strategy?**  

A: Good organisation, draft r/b/u.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Played well.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: PT Points.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: I win.
