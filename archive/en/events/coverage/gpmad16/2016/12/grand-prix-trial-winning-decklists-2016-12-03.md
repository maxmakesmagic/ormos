Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 3, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











The main event only started on Saturday, but the action had already begun on Friday. As always, Friday was the day when players had the final chance to earn two byes for the weekend's main event. Their last chance was our first. That is, our first opportunity to see which decks were doing great this weekend.


The Black-Green Delirium decks listed below and the high number of White-Blue [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) lists couldn't surprise anyone who was even vaguely familiar with the format, of course. The rise of Red-Green [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) probably wasn't news to people who followed Standard closely either. But things like Bant Humans proved that the format still had a few tricks up its sleeve!
