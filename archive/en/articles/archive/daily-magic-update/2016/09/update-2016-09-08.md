Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Adam Pierce's Pitch Dredge](http://www.eternalcentral.com/revenge-of-the-living-dead/)

Here's a little love for all of you Vintage fans out there! Adam turned in a second-place finish in the August *Magic Online* Power Nine challenge with this sweet number.


Now Dredge is basically the enemy of...well pretty much everyone. It's powerful, it's blisteringly fast, and it is incredibly hard to deal with if you don't devote a number of sideboard slots specifically devoted to the reanimating menace. Unfortunately for many Dredge players, that's exactly what happens in Vintage, and those sideboard slots are *veeeeery* good against it.


Still, despite this hate, the deck is capable of some really powerful stuff. If you seriously need more about it, go check out [Shuhei Nakamura playing Pitch Dredge in the finals of this most recent season of VSL](https://www.youtube.com/watch?list=PL3rP64NRtmbhGb6I6vTslqQKoLpfnrKry&v=QPQNpULQDzA).


For the uninitiated, Dredge takes advantage of cards with the dredge mechanic, such as [Golgari Grave-Troll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golgari+Grave-Troll), to fill the graveyard up with cards. It can then use cards like [Ichorid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ichorid), [Narcomoeba](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Narcomoeba), and [Prized Amalgam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam) to make an army out of nowhere. Prefer a more traditional necromantic route? Use [Bridge from Below](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bridge+from+Below) to make a Zombie army and reinforce it with a [Flame-Kin Zealot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flame-Kin+Zealot) that has been brought back by [Dread Return](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dread+Return). Or, if you just want to kill people in one shot (and do it around all of the graveyard hate), you can just kill them with Marit Lage thanks to [Dark Depths](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Depths) and [Thespian's Stage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thespian%27s+Stage).
