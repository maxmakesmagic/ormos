So Many Combos
==============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 11, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 







What better than one combo deck? Two combo decks! What's better than two combo decks? Three combo decks, of course! **Magic Online** user Bryang has created a truly innovative deck that combines many different combos into one similar deck. 

![Goblin Welder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Goblin+Welder) This blue-black-red deck starts with the classic [Counterbalance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterbalance) + [Sensei's Divining Top](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top) combo to lock your opponent out of a majority of spells. In addition, the [Painter's Servant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Painter%27s+Servant) + [Grindstone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grindstone) combo will mill your opponent's entire library. Why would you want to play both of these combos in the same deck? [Goblin Welder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Welder), of course! [Goblin Welder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Welder) can do nifty tricks with [Sensei's Divining Top](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top)â€”tap the Divining Top to draw a card, and use [Goblin Welder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Welder) to sacrifice it. You still get to draw a card while maintaining the full function of [Goblin Welder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Welder). By alternating two copies of [Sensei's Divining Top](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top) (or [Baleful Strix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baleful+Strix)) between your graveyard and the battlefield, [Goblin Welder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Welder) functions as an excellent card-drawing engine as well. 


[Imperial Recruiter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imperial+Recruiter) and [Trinket Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinket+Mage) provide the deck with some flexibility to play one copy of a card and easily access it. This flexibility is why the deck can put so many different combos and synergies into one deck.
