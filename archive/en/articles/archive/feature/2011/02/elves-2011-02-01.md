Looking for a new deck to try out in the brave new world of Standard with *Mirrodin Besieged*? Check out this list! Used by a Wizards of the Coast spellslinger, this take on Elves features the powerful new [Lead the Stampede](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lead+the+Stampede). With only creatures and lands in the deck (besides the Leads, of course), you're sure to be leading quite the stampede into the red zone!
