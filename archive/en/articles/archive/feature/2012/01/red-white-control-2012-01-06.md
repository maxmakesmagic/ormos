Red-White Control
=================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 6, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Even a year after its release, people are still finding new ways to use Scars of Mirrodin cards in Standard. **Magic Online** player MarthaJones recently piloted this unique Red-White Control deck to a 3–1 finish in a Daily Event. The unlikely companionship of [Glint Hawk Idol](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk+Idol) and [Day of Judgment](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Day+of+Judgment) helps the control deck keep an attacker even after the board has been cleared, and [Razor Hippogriff](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Razor+Hippogriff) helps to doctor up any artifact creatures that fall in the process. Check out the decklist, and consider it for Friday Night **Magic** tonight!
