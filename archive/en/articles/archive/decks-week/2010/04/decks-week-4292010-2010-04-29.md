Decks of the Week: 4/29/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on April 29, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











R*ise of the Eldrazi* is legal in Constructed play! This weekend features no fewer than 14 [Pro Tour–Amsterdam Standard qualifiers](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/amsterdam10-qualifiers) around the world plus three high-profile Standard tournaments in the United States: the [StarCityGames.com Open](http://forums.starcitygames.com/viewtopic.php?t=320989) in Atlanta, the [TCGplayer.com 5K](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?id=8826) in Boston, and the [Midwest Masters Series](http://www.magicmastersseries.com/) in Des Moines, Iowa. If you want to take a crack at the new state of Standard, cruise through the top decks from **Magic Online** and find the best spots to add *Rise of the Eldrazi* cards! *Rise of the Eldrazi* comes to **Magic Online** on May 10.
