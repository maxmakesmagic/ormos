Kamigawa Remix
==============



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on April 19, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_david_mcdarby.jpg)
By David McDarby











![Kamigawa Remix Logo](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/Q2sd8e3ns.png)


Welcome to *Kamigawa Remix*! This is a new Draft format coming only to *Magic Online* for a week starting April 20 that features cards from all four *Kamigawa* sets! So, if you've ever wanted to open a pack with both [Kabuto Moth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kabuto+Moth) and [Voltage Surge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltage+Surge), read on!


![Kamigawa Remix Booster](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/N237nsdyw.png)


In *Kamigawa Remix*, the timelines of original *Kamigawa* block and *Kamigawa Neon Dynasty* are colliding! In this format, you'll see an equal representation of cards from *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* and the original Kamigawa block of sets *Champions of Kamigawa*, *Betrayers of Kamigawa*, and *Saviors of Kamigawa* collectively. You also won't see every single card in all four of these sets but rather just the cream of the crop from each set for drafting:


* [![Sakura-Tribe Elder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Sakura-Tribe+Elder)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sakura-Tribe+Elder)
* [![Ninja of the Deep Hours](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Ninja+of+the+Deep+Hours)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ninja+of+the+Deep+Hours)
* [![Kiku, Night's Flower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Kiku%2C+Night%27s+Flower)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiku%2C+Night%27s+Flower)
* [![Kaito Shizuki](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Kaito+Shizuki)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kaito+Shizuki)

*Imagine seeing all these cards in the same pack!*


To accommodate all these timelines combining in one pack, packs are overcharged! What this means is that in each pack of *Kamigawa Remix* you will find:


* 1 Rare or mythic rare from *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty*
* 1 Rare from among *Champions of Kamigawa*, *Betrayers of Kamigawa*, and *Saviors of Kamigawa*
* 3 Uncommons from *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty*
* 3 Uncommons from among *Champions of Kamigawa*, *Betrayers of Kamigawa*, and *Saviors of Kamigawa*
* 4 Commons from *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty*
* 4 Commons from among *Champions of Kamigawa*, *Betrayers of Kamigawa*, and *Saviors of Kamigawa*
* 1 Land that has a 50/50 shot of being a basic land or a "gainland" that enters the battlefield tapped from the [Tranquil Cove](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tranquil+Cove) cycle

That's seventeen cards in each booster pack! As usual, mythic rares will replace a rare in the *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* slot at the normal rate you're accustomed to. But if you were around during the time of the original *Kamigawa* block, mythic rarity hadn't quite been invented yet. However, for *Kamigawa Remix*, a certain Equipment from *Betrayers of Kamigawa* has been given the honorary "mythic rare" treatment and will appear as if it was a mythic rare in the original *Kamigawa* block rare slot.


[![](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Umezawa%27s+Jitte)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Umezawa%27s+Jitte)
*Yep—still good.*


In addition, to really bring home the idea that timelines are merging, there's even a cycle of rares included on the *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* side from *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* Commander!


* [![Swift Reconfiguration](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Swift+Reconfiguration)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swift+Reconfiguration)
* [![Research Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Research+Thief)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Research+Thief)
* [![Ruthless Technomancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Ruthless+Technomancer)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ruthless+Technomancer)
* [![Myojin of Roaring Blades](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Myojin+of+Roaring+Blades)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myojin+of+Roaring+Blades)
* [![Go-Shintai of Life's Origin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Go-Shintai+of+Life%27s+Origin)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Go-Shintai+of+Life%27s+Origin)

As always, no matter if your feedback was from 1,000 years ago or made using the new technology of today, we here on *Magic Online* team read it, so keep at it! *Streets of New Capenna* releases on *Magic Online* immediately upon the conclusion of *Kamigawa Remix*, so get ready to choose your family and figure out where Halo comes from on *Magic Online*! You can always check out the *Magic Online* schedule on [our Calendar here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/schedule-magic-online-products-game-info).


Thank you again for joining us for this adventure through the past and present of Kamigawa, and we hope you enjoy playing this rather unique take on drafting on *Magic Online*. So, until next time, may your past be well remembered, your future be bright, and your present be exactly what you want it to be.
