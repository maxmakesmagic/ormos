Quarterfinals:
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 14, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall




 Brian David-Marshall is a New York–based game designer who has been involved with Magic since 1994, when he started organizing tournaments and ran a Manhattan game store. Since then, he has been a judge, a player, and one of the longest-tenured columnists on DailyMTG.com, as he enters his second decade writing for the site. He is also the Pro Tour Historian and one of the commentators for the Pro Tour. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpana03/qf1.jpg)This was a rematch of a Day One pairing between these very different players. Ben emerged victorious in that meeting and Gerard was looking to reverse his fortune by sitting in the opposite seat that he used yesterday. Ben arrived at the Feature Match area first and was sitting in the same seat as yesterday which was a great disappointment to Fabiano. "The last time I sat here I lost to you. I wanted to sit on the other side. Do you want to switch?" "No thanks."

"I would switch for you."

"Thanks, I'll remember that."

Gerard was all sunshine and smiles regardless and announced the reason he was so chipper, "This is my first Top 8."

"Ever? In anything?"

"I made the Top 4 of the team Pro Tour but that doesn't really count." Gerard quickly shifted gears, "How many girls do you think read the coverage?"

Ben shrugged, "My mom, Kibler's mom…"

Apparently Gerard was keen on one of the girls working the show, "I told the Bench Warmer girls to come over here after they were done-remember the Asian girl?" He was referring to a trading card of the girl that he and Rubin used to decide choice yesterday.

### Game 1

Gerard wiped his hands on his trusty towel and shook his opponent's hand, "Good luck man!"

Ben nodded, "Thanks"

Both players kept. Ben opened with an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) and a [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek). "Why now?" asked Gerard.

"I'm impatient I want to see." He saw two [Isochron Scepter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Isochron+Scepter)s, [Vampiric Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor), [Daze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze) and some lands.

Ben played out a [Seal of Cleansing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Cleansing) and passed. Gerard was sitting on a pair of Deltas. Ben played a Delta of his own and then when Gerard played [Bloodstained Mire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodstained+Mire) he whistled and commented that there were some pretty expensive cards in play.

Another [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek) showed [Daze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze), [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell), [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), two Scepters, Vampiric, and an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island). Ben left one mana open to cast a face down Angel. Gerard decided to break his lands and counter it. Gerard cast an [Isochron Scepter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Isochron+Scepter) and responded by sacrificing a fetch land. He eventually imprinted a [Daze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze) on it. Ben took a long time contemplating his next turn and ultimately played a fetch land and cast [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) with his fetch land available if needed. Gerard chose not to activate the Scepter, he [Vampiric Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor)ed at the end of the turn instead.

![Isochron Scepter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Isochron+Scepter)He passed the turn back without incident and Ben broke his land and took his turn. He played a [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) and waited for responses but Gerard had none and Ben named [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish). He also played a morph. Gerard cast the [Fact or Fiction](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) that he had searched for last turn. Gerard took a pile with Tog, Scepter and land over one with Tog and [Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire)/[Ice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ice).

Gerard played [Bloodstained Mire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodstained+Mire) and made a Scepter with [Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire)/[Ice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ice) on it. And he used it immediately to kill the morph-Ben still had the Seal. Ben flashed back a [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and then [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed. Ben then [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)ed and Gerard broke his Mire for a [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) and used it to [Vampiric Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor) in response. Ben saw Scepter, Tog and [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish). He took the Scepter. Ben used his Seal on the dangerous Scepter and passed the turn after swinging for two.

Gerard merely passed and when Ben used another [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) he cast FoF in response. He flipped over [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), [Counter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counter), [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox), Tog and [Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire)/[Ice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ice). Gerard took the first three and [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed in response. Gerard showed Ben a hand with Tog, Wish, Mox, and [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) and Ben took the [Counter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counter).

The Mage beat Gerard down to six and Gerard brainstormed EOT. He used a Mire to shuffle his deck and then cast FoF. Gerard revealed [Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire)/[Ice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ice), two [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)s, [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) and [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) and Gerard took the [Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire)/[Ice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ice) when it was put into a pile by itself.

Gerard cast a Tog and pried a, "That sucks." from the taciturn Rubin. He had the [Diabolic Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Edict) and he used it to kill the blocker and Gerard fell to three on the next attack. Ben promised to finish him off on the next attack when he played a [Shadowmage Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator). Gerard allowed it to resolve and passed his next turn with lethal damage staring him in the face.

When Ben attacked he killed the [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) and took one. Ben drew a card and passed the turn. Gerard cast [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) and pondered what to take. He had 26 cards in his yard and decided to get [Corpse Dance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corpse+Dance). 

Gerard's turn was indecisive. He could probably win if Ben did not have a [Diabolic Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Edict). Finally Gerard played the [Corpse Dance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corpse+Dance) and picked up a [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog). He went for the kill but Ben had the [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother). Gerard [Counter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counter)ed. The [Counter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counter) resolved but Ben had the Edict as well. Gerard wondered if he should have waited another turn to draw another counter.

**Game 2**

Gerard mulliganed to kick off Game 2, which came as a huge disappointment to the Gerard fan club that was engulfing the feature area. That is not to say that Ben was without his fans - Brian Hacker and Frank Gilson were looking on rapt until they were convinced to do a draft with Jeff Fung.

The second game was slow to develop and Ben was able to resolve a spell without fear thanks to an EOT [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek) at Gerard's hand that had no permission. He played a [Damping Matrix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Damping+Matrix) and he didn't have to worry about Gerard's Togs. A few turns later another [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek) allowed him to cast a face-up [Exalted Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel). 

He rode that all the way to victory even though Gerard gave him a scare in the end game playing as if he had an out. Ben used another [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek) to ascertain that Gerard was bluffing and the match was over shortly thereafter.

Final Result: Ben Rubin - 2 Gerard Fabiano - 0
