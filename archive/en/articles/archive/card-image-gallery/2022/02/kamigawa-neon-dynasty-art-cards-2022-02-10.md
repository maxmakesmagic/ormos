Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty Art Cards
================================



 Posted in **Card Image Gallery**
 on February 10, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Kendall Pepple











[*Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/kamigawa-neon-dynasty) Set Boosters include an art card showcasing one of 81 remarkable illustrations from the set. Even better, 1 in 20 art cards opened are gold stamped with an artist's signature or Planeswalker symbol.


Learn more about Set Boosters from the [*Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* Product Overview](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/kamigawa-neon-dynasty-product-overview-2022-01-27) and check out the art cards below (foil-stamped versions have the same art but with the artist's signature or Planeswalker symbol).
