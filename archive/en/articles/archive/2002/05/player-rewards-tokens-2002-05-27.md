Player Rewards tokens
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 27, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast

The last two -- the *Odyssey* Elephant and Wurm -- are not widely available yet, but will be sent out to players in the next few weeks!
