Balthor Commander
=================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 19, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorPic_GavinVerhey.jpg)
By Gavin Verhey




 When Gavin Verhey was eleven, he dreamt of a job making Magic cardsâ€”and now as a Magic designer, he's living his dream! Gavin has been writing about Magic since 2005. 






*Judgment* legend [Balthor the Defiled](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Balthor+the+Defiled) has risen yet again to lead another deck into the fray! This time, it's a Commander deck featured on the GatheringMagic.com video series CMDR Decks. Balthor is a reasonably popular black Commander, but the unique twist on this deck separates it from other iterations: [Relentless Rats](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Relentless+Rats)!


The deck is a black control deck at its core. Featuring plenty of staple mana acceleration, removal spells, and even some card drawing like [Phyrexian Arena](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Arena), it sets up like a traditional Commander deck might. However, the Rats mounting on this Commander deck's side of the board is a telltale sign something is amiss.


This deck is content to trade off and sweep away as many Rats as possible. Once the graveyard is well stocked, it's time for Balthor to make an appearance. His ability will reanimate all of your rats in one fell swoop at the end of someone's turn, allowing your rats to overrun someone immediately!


Of course, there are plenty of ways to help out your rats too. Cards like [Coat of Arms](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coat+of+Arms) and [Eldrazi Monument](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Monument) make your rats even more lethal, and [Ratcatcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ratcatcher) draws you one every turn. And, of course, no [Relentless Rats](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Relentless+Rats) deck would be complete without [Thrumming Stone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thrumming+Stone)!


If Relentless rats in Commander sounds fun to you, then you won't want to miss this decklist! Take a look at it below, and don't miss [CMDR Decks's video](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/balthor-the-defiled/) as they walk you through the cards!
