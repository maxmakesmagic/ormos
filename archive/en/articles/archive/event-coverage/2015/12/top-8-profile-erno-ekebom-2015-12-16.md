Top 8 Profile - Erno Ekebom
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPHEL00/162.jpg)**Name:** Erno Ekebom  
**Age:** 21  
**Location:** Helsinki, Finland  
**Occupation:** Helsinki University, Sociology  
**Played Magic since:** For 6 years  
**Best accomplishments:** 5th 200 Pro Tour Los Angeles, 11th 1998 Rome, 13th 1996 Worlds



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Helsinki?**  

A: I live in Helsinki, so there was no reason not to go.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Hoping for top 16 because of 3 byes. I like Invasion limited.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: 1st day - I went 6-0 first day before losing to Ryan Fuller. 2nd day - I drafted two mediocre decks and with luck swept the first table, then won one match and id'd twice in the second.



**Q: What do you think about the formats used in this tournament, and what was your strategy?**  

A: I like Invasion Rochester Draft but don't enjoy Sealed. I didn't have any particular strategy.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Playing Skill? :) (Good Sealed card pool)



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: Some money, nice to do well in any big tournament, especially in Finland.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: Arto Hiltunen will win and I'll finish 2nd.
