Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke










After 15 rounds of Standard, with a total of 2,144 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!





---

### Bill Chronopoulos


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplon15/t8_chronopoulos.jpg)


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Lawyer


**Hometown:** Athens, Greece


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Second at the 2014 World Magic Cup, captain of the Greek team at the 2015 World Magic Cup.


**What deck did you play and why?**


Jace-kai. My friend Tsorba told me so one day before the Grand Prix and I trusted him.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why?**


It's sceret. There's a World Magic Cup Qualifier coming next weekend for my friends!


**What's the most important card in your deck?**


[Jace, Vryn's Prodigy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+Vryn%27s+Prodigy).





---

### Erik Skinstad


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplon15/t8_skinstad.jpg)


**Age:** 34


**Occupation:** IT


**Hometown:** Stavanger, Norway


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


9-0 on Day 1 into 0-6 on Day 2 at Grand Prix Utrecht.


**What deck did you play and why?**


Green-White Aggro. The deck seemed well-positioned, and very strong.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why?**


Maybe fit a Wind Storm in the sideboard.


**What's the most important card in your deck?**


[Den Protector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Den+Protector) or [Dromoka's Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dromoka%27s+Command).





---

### Martin Jůza


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplon15/t8_juza.jpg)


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Tourist


**Hometown:** Plzen, Czech Republic


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Two Pro Tour Top 8s, 23 Grand Prix Top 8s.


**What deck did you play and why?**


Red-Green Dragons, because it's good in this metagame.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why?**


I would cut [Arbor Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arbor+Colossus) from the sideboard because that card is only good in the mirror.


**What's the most important card in your deck?**


[Stormbreath Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormbreath+Dragon)!





---

### Piotr Wald


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplon15/t8_wald.jpg)


**Age:** 21


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Warsaw, Poland


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Top 8 at Grand Prix Verona, won two PTQs.


**What deck did you play and why?**


Abzan Control. I feel very comfortable playing this style of deck.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why?**


Not sure if any are necessary.


**What's the most important card in your deck?**


[Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino).





---

### Fabrizio Anteri


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplon15/t8_anteri.jpg)


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** I may consider joing Martin Jůza as a professional tourist


**Hometown:** London, England (originally from Venezuela)


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Three 8s and two Top 64 finishes in the five English GPs I've played.


**What deck did you play and why?**


Abzan Aggro with [Hangarback Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hangarback+Walker). Ondřej Stráský told me he was 15-0 with the deck online, so I decided to try it went 14-0 before my first loss.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why?**


Depending on the metagame there could be an extra Sorin in the 75 or some [Wingmate Roc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingmate+Roc).


**What's the most important card in your deck?**


[Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino), of the top, three-of.





---

### Daniel Fior


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplon15/t8_fior.jpg)


**Age:** 29


**Occupation:** Lost


**Hometown:** Caracas, Venezuela


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Four Grand Prix Top 8s, made a lot of National teams.


**What deck did you play and why?**


Abzan, because Fabrizio told me to!


**What changes would you make to your deck and why?**


I did not like Ajani in the main deck, nor the [Hero's Downfall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero%27s+Downfall). Usure what to play instead, metagame dependent.


**What's the most important card in your deck?**


All of them.

### Marco Cammilluzzi


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplon15/t8_cammilluzzi.jpg)


**Age:** 29


**Occupation:** Bomberboss and Matteo Moure's baybsitter


**Hometown:** Rome, Italy


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


One Pro Tour Top 8, three Grand Prix Top 8s, defeated Riccardo Reale a lot of times.


**What deck did you play and why?**


Abzan Aggro … What else?


**What changes would you make to your deck and why?**


We will see …


**What's the most important card in your deck?**


[Hangarback Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hangarback+Walker) and [Wingmate Roc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingmate+Roc)!

### Matteo Moure


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplon15/t8_moure.jpg)


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Business man


**Hometown:** Rome, Italy


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


PTQ Top 8.


**What deck did you play and why?**


I wanted to play Blue-Red Thopters, but the night before the GP I chose to follow Cammilluzzi's suggestion to switch to Abzan. But I didn't have the cards, so I got up at six a.m. to go searching for them.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why?**


I would change one [Ultimate Price](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ultimate+Price) into one [Valorous Stance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valorous+Stance).


**What's the most important card in your deck?**


[Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino) and [Wingmate Roc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingmate+Roc).
