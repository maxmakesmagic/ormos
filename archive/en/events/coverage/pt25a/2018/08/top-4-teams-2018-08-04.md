Top 4 Teams of Pro Tour 25th Anniversary
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 5, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











Congratulations to the Top 4 Teams of Pro Tour 25th Anniversary!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/pt25a/Top4-HotSauceGames.jpg)


Wu – Hull - Orange
==================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/pt25a/Top4-Wu.jpg)


**Name:** Allen Wu


**Team:** Hot Sauce Games


**Format played:** Legacy


**For how many of *Magic*'s 25 years have you been playing?** 17


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


I mean, this. My second favorite is probably qualifying for my first Pro Tour on *Magic Online*. Third is trading my entire collection for my friend’s [Avatar of Woe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avatar+of+Woe).


**How awesome is your team?**


Pretty great. They let me play Legacy and trusted me to play my favorite deck.

Utter-Leyton – Stark – Jůza (ChannelFireball)
=============================================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/pt25a/Top4UtterLeyton.jpg)


**Name:** Josh Utter-Leyton


**Team:** ChannelFireball


**Format played:** Legacy


**For how many of *Magic*'s 25 years have you been playing?** 24


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


Making Top 8 of Worlds in San Francisco 2011 alongside three teammates. Two teammates in this one is almost as good!


**How awesome is your team?**


Well, Ben and Martin will probably be arguing about the answer to this question for the rest of the night.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/pt25a/Top4Stark.jpg)


**Name:** Ben Stark


**Team:** ChannelFireball


**Format played:** Modern


**For how many of *Magic*'s 25 years have you been playing?** 24


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


6-0ing the draft portion of the World Championship in 2013.


**How awesome is your team?**


Josh has six Pro Tour Top 8s and is the best deck builder ever. Martin is a very good technical player and Limited mastermind. Basically, I couldn't imagine better teammates.
