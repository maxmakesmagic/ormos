*Conflux* Intro Packs
=====================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 18, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Let's talk about the *Conflux* Intro Packs for a second. Just like in *Shards of Alara*, we're not having 60-card Theme Decks. Instead, we have Intro Packs. Each one is a 41-card preconstructed deck. Why not forty? Because that extra card is a foil rare, that's why!


Each deck also comes with a strategy insert, a learn-to-play insert, and a 15-card *Conflux* booster pack. And they look like this:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1742_intropack1_7ynto79vnb.jpg)Well, that's a little misleading. Only one of the five looks like that. Some of them actually look like this:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1742_intropack2_yx4dwsaf5q.jpg)Of course, there are also some that look like this:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1742_intropack3_xvrbcucy6i.jpg)That's not to ignore the valuable contributions of the Intro Packs that look like this:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1742_intropack4_o6senv0z70.jpg)Let's see ... that covers all of them. All of them, that is, except *this* one!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1742_intropack5_twi6vg9flt.jpg)Still here? Good! Because we have something special for you: a few pieces of *Conflux* card art to go along with their names.
