Shimmy on over to [the release article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/streets-new-capenna-magic-online-edition-2022-04-26) to learn all about *Streets of New Capenna*'s *Magic Online* release. Also be sure to check out [the updated Treasure Chest info page](https://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/treasure-chest-card-list-info) and the *Streets of New Capenna* redemption info below:

* **Redemption Reprint Guarantee Date**: September 7, 2022
* **Redemption Cutoff Date**: October 12, 2022

Alternative Play for *Streets of New Capenna* Season
----------------------------------------------------


Here's the schedule for the alternative play experiences you can expect during the *Streets of New Capenna* season*.* All events run for a week, except where noted:


* Until 10 a.m. PT tomorrow: ***Modern Horizons 2*** – We're bringing back *Modern Horizons 2* Draft for two weeks! This will be non-Phantom, so it's your chance to nab a ritzy [Ragavan, Nimble Pilferer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ragavan%2C+Nimble+Pilferer), [Urza's Saga](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Saga), or even a [Solitude](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solitude)!
* May 25: ***Proliferate Cube*** – Our very own Carmen Handy is back with another energizing update to *Proliferate Cube*! You can count on this being one infecting experience!
* June 1: ***Vintage Cube*** – We've bookended this calendar with Moxen! *Vintage Cube* is back, powered up with *Streets of New Capenna*, and will run for four awesome weeks of cubing action.

*Commander Legends: Battle for Baldur's Gate* on *Magic Online*
---------------------------------------------------------------


The tabletop Prerelease weekend for *Commander Legends: Battle for Baldur's Gate* starts on June 3, and many *Magic Online* players are wondering what to expect from the set. While a full *Magic Online* release to coincide with the tabletop release wasn't achievable for *Commander Legends: Battle for Baldur's Gate*, we will be adding a selection of cards from the set to *Magic Online* in treasure chests starting on June 15.


We know this news is disappointing to our Commander faithful hoping for the full set to be available. The team is always working hard to deliver as much great content to *Magic Online* as we can fit, and all of us—especially the passionate Commander players—wanted to deliver the full *Commander Legends: Battle for Baldur's Gate* experience at launch. Unfortunately, a resource crunch compounded by the complexities of transitioning *Magic Online* to Daybreak Games left us unable to meet that goal. *Double Masters 2022*, *Dominaria United*, and *The Brothers' War*, however, are all on track to ship all cards at launch as usual, and we are growing our team to better meet the needs of *Magic Online* set delivery.


We are keeping an eye on which *Commander Legends: Battle for Baldur's Gate* cards emerge as favorites in the Commander community, and as we develop a plan to release more of the cards from the set, we will share the details with you here. Thanks for your patience!

----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. [Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format!
