*Zendikar* Intro Packs
======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 19, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Do you have time to look at a few *Zendikar* intro packs? You do? Great!


Each intro pack contains a 41-card deck (including a rare foil) and a *Zendikar* booster, as well as a handy strategy guide. And they look ... like this!
