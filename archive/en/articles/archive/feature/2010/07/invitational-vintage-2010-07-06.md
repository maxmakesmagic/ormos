 The **Magic** Invitational was an invite-only tournament that featured the game's very best players playing wacky formats. Why? Because winning meant taking home the title of champion and earning the right to have your likeness festooned onto a real **Magic** card *you* designed! Jon Finkel used this deck when he won for the Vintage portion of the Invitational in 2000. The card he created? [Shadowmage Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator). Other cards from the event over the years include [Sylvan Safekeeper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Safekeeper), [Voidmage Prodigy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voidmage+Prodigy), [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage), and [Ranger of Eos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ranger+of+Eos).
