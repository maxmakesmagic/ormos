Invitations to the Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers for Pro Tour *Ravnica Allegiance* are awarded in the following manner:


* Winners of Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifiers tied to Regional Pro Qualifiers for Pro Tour *Ravnica Allegiance* (held July 21, 2018-September 23, 2018)
* Winners of Regional Last Chance Qualifiers that immediately precede each Regional Pro Tour Qualifier
* Players with a Pro Tour Players Club Bronze or Silver level as of the date of the Regional Pro Tour Qualifier
* Players that earned an invitation to this round of RPTQs via a top finish at Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers for Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*

If you believe that your finish at a Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifier is in error, please visit <http://www.wizards.com/Magic/PlaneswalkerPoints>. After logging in, click the "Appeals Form" link at the bottom of the page and fill out the form.


This list is updated every Wednesday to account for adjustments and player appeals.
