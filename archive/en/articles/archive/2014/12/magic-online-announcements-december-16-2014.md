**Magic Online Holiday Giveaway**


It's the season of giving on *Magic Online*! As a thank you to all of our players, anyone who logged in between Wednesday, November 26, and the downtime on Wednesday, December 17, will be granted 16 Phantom Points and a premium [Everflowing Chalice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Everflowing+Chalice) after the downtime. If you're really in the holiday spirit, those Phantom Points might go nicely with the upcoming [Holiday Cube](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online-holiday-festival-2014-11-12#swissholidaycube).


Thank you for playing, and happy holidays from *Magic Online*!

**Holiday Schedule Changes for *Magic Online*** 


Due to the upcoming Holiday Season, some *Magic Online* services and regularly scheduled maintenances will be delayed or rescheduled as follows. All times are Pacific (UTC -8).


* ***Magic Online* Holiday Downtime schedule**
* **Monday, December 22:** Specially scheduled *Magic Online* Downtime 8:00 a.m. – 12 p.m.
* **Wednesday, December 24:** No *Magic Online* Scheduled Downtime
* **Wednesday, December 31:** No *Magic Online* Scheduled Downtime
* **Monday, January 5, 2015:** Specially schedule *Magic Online* Downtime 8 a.m. - 12 p.m.
* **Wednesday, January 14:** Regularly scheduled *Magic Online* Wednesday downtimes resume.

Please note that redemption tokens will only be processed and removed during downtimes. Redemption orders processed during the 12/22 and 1/5 downtimes will be shipped after Thursday, January 8, due to holiday warehouse closures. Please plan accordingly! There will be no downtime on Wednesday, January 7. Downtimes will return to their regular Wednesday schedule on Wednesday, January 14.
