9-0 Decklists
=============



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX SHANGHAI 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpsha15)
 on May 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











With the field of 900 boiled down to just 118, Day 2 of Grand Prix Shanghai is underway!


Hall of Famer and World Champion Makihito Mihara leads the pack, along with Lin Yang and Kazuaki Fujimura. The trio managed to survive the entire day without a scratch and will kickstart their glorious Sunday morning with the perfect 27 points!



[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Courser+of+Kruphix)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courser+of+Kruphix)



All three players had 4 copies of [Courser of Kruphix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courser+of+Kruphix) in their 75, but in three very distinct archetypes! Quite a lot of people have been hounding us for decklists, especially Makihito Mihara's (and Yuuya Watanabe's) five-color Dragon Megamorph deck laden with eight Dragonlords!
