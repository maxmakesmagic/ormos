Pro Tour Atlanta Feature: Boat Rises Above All as Only 6-0 Team
===============================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 11, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_MikeTurian.jpg)
By Mike Turian




Before joining Magic Online as its digital product manager, Mike worked as a producer for Wizards's technology department, a product manager for organized play, and as an R&D lead developer. He has played Magic Online since it came out in 2002 and Magic since The Dark. 






Boat emerged at the top of the standings as the only team to go 6-0 through the Sealed deck portion of Pro Tour-Atlanta. Frederico Bastos, Tiago Chan and Rui Mariani, all from Portugal, defeated Paul Jordan LOL in the final round to clinch the 6-0 start. Les baltringues de Ludipia finished in second place at the end of the day with a 5-0-1 record. Here are the three decks that they played to an undefeated finish.
