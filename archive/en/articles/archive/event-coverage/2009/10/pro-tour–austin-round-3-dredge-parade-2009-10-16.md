Pro Tour–Austin Round 3: Dredge on Parade
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 16, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 







In the Arena, three Dredge decks tried to move to 3-0. How did Christophe Gregoir of Belgium, Robert van Medevoort of the Netherlands, and Pro Tour–Honolulu champion Kazuya Mitamura of Japan fare against their opposition? You'll find the answers here, plus quick interviews with Brian Kibler, Chris Lachmann, Paulo Vitor Damo Da Rosa, and Mr. Mark Rosewater.
