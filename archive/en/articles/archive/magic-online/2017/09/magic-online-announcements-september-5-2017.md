**Modern Cube begins tomorrow, September 6**


Modern Cube begins tomorrow on *Magic Online*! This event will be available until Monday, September 25 at 8 a.m. Pacific. 


For more details about Modern Cube, including the full cardlist, click [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-cube-cardlist).

***Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet* Sealed MOCS Open Saturday, September 9**


A Magic Online Championship Series (MOCS) *Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet* Sealed Open Event is coming up on September 9! MOCS Open events do not require an invitation or Qualifier Points to join. The winner qualifies for the Magic Online Championship, a Pro Tour, and the Pro Tour Challenge event at that Pro Tour.


Learn more about the 2017 Magic Online Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

**Modern PTQ for Pro Tour *Rivals of**Ixalan*Sunday, September 10**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, September 10 at 9 a.m. PT.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

***Magic Online* Format Challenges back this weekend**


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges introduced a new prize structure and recurring weekend events for Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper formats!


Events begin every Saturday at 8:00 a.m. PT, and run through Sunday. Check out [this page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-format-challenges) for further event details as well as the weekend schedule.

**Upcoming downtimes**


The next two downtimes will be September 20, which will be extended (3 a.m. PT/10:00 p.m. UTC), followed by a regular downtime on October 11.


Follow *Magic Online*'s [Twitter](https://twitter.com/MagicOnline) account for live updates during downtime.
