Summoning an Illusion
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 20, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











As you know, we like to print tokens that match up to the card that creates them. It just makes us feel all warm and cozy inside. For example, [Summoner's Bane](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoner%27s+Bane) and the Illusion Token from *Zendikar*:



|  |  |
| --- | --- |
|  |  |

You can see the cool face of the illusion on those two cards, right? Tell you what, just to make sure, we'll blow them up and get rid of those pesky card borders.
