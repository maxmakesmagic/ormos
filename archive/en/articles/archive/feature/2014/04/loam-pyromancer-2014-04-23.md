Loam Pyromancer
===============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 23, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson












Ever since Bronson Magnan won [Grand Prix Lincoln](/en/events/coverage/bronson-magnan-grand-prix-lincoln-champion) with [Life from the Loam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam)/[Seismic Assault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seismic+Assault), it has remained a fringe deck in Modern. With [Deathrite Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman) gone, graveyard strategies are a little safer, so it wouldn't surprise me if a Loam-based strategy got more popular. 

![Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Life+from+the+Loam) Whether or not to play creatures (and if yes, which ones) was always a hotly debated topic. In this case, you get to play [Smallpox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smallpox), which you didn't want to do if you had [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf)s in your deck. [Smallpox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smallpox) is a huge reason to play this deck, but you do want some way to win if your graveyard is being attacked. Planeswalkers are certainly an option, but [Young Pyromancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Young+Pyromancer) seems to fit the role better. With six retrace cards alongside a bevy of other cheap spells, you'll be making multiple tokens per turn. 


[Smallpox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smallpox) and [Raven's Crime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raven%27s+Crime) are your best cards to fight combo, but [Abrupt Decay](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay) helps against most combos, too. Against creatures, you have Elemental tokens to block for days, [Flame Jab](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flame+Jab) to clear out the weenies, and [Seismic Assault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seismic+Assault) to dominate the midgame. 

 There is always the issue of not drawing [Life from the Loam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam), so it's important to either have cards that find it or ways to function when you don't have it. [Faithless Looting](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faithless+Looting) is the best card available for bringing the entire package together, and I'm a big fan of the finished product.
