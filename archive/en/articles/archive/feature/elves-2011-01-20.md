The latest StarCityGames.com Open tournament concluded this past weekend in [San Jose](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/20916_Live_Coverage_of_StarCityGamescom_San_Jose.html), California. Noted ChannelFireball.com columnist Matt Nass managed to snag a 4th-place finish in the Standard portion with his take on the Elves! archetype. Featuring just about every Elf in Standard, Matt aggressively beats his opponents down with his green army, backed by [Ezuri, Renegade Leader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Ezuri%252C%2BRenegade%2BLeader), [Nissa Revane](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Nissa%2BRevane), and [Garruk Wildspeaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Garruk%2BWildspeaker)!
