Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 4, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptams10/top8_wafotapa.jpg)

**Guillaume Wafo-Tapa**


**Age:** 23  
**Hometown**: Nantes, France  
**Occupation**: **Magic** player  
**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Amsterdam?** Pro level  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s**: 6/3  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments**:  

Won Pro Tour-Yokohama


**What was your record by format?**  
**Extended:** 5-2-1  
**Booster Draft:** 5-1


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour-Amsterdam? Who did you prepare with?**  

I playtested draft on **Magic Online**. For the Extended portion, I played with Martial Moreau and Guillaume Matignon.


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**  

7


**What Extended deck are you playing and why?**  

5-Color Teachings. The best control deck I could come up with.


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic, or if you don't go to FNM anymore, where did you play?**  

I play in the care Le Theatre in Nantes.


**Pick a side: Mirran or Phyrexian?**  

Mirran

**Michael Jacob**


**Age:** 26  
**Hometown**: Farmington Hills, MI  
**Occupation**: **Magic Online** grinder  
**Number of times playing on the Pro Tour**: A lot, since *Mirrodin* Block  
**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Amsterdam?** Level 6  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s**: First PT Top 8, 3 GP Top 8.  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments**:  

National Champion (U.S.) and Team Worlds Champion


**What was your record by format?**  
**Extended:** 7-1-2  
**Booster Draft:** 5-1


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour-Amsterdam? Who did you prepare with?**  

Played some ***Magic** 2011* queues on **Magic Online** for draft. Tested with Kibler, Nelson, Rietzl, LSV, basically every U.S. pro. Most credit goes to Patrick Chapin for giving me the deck he created.


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**  

24 hours in a day – 8 hours of sleep = 16 X 7 = 104 hours in a week


**What Extended deck are you playing and why?**  

Grixis Control created by Patrick Chapin. I liked Cryptic Command and Punishing Fire. Preordain is the nuts!


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic, or if you don't go to FNM anymore, where did you play?**  

I judge FNM at RIW Hobbies in Livonia, Michigan.


**Pick a side: Mirran or Phyrexian?**  

Phyrexian






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptams10/top8_kibler.jpg)

**Age:** 29 (30 on Tuesday!)  
**Hometown**: Oceanside, CA  
**Occupation**: Game designer  
**Number of times playing on the Pro Tour**: lots

1st Grand Prix-Boston

Top 4 Pro Tour Chicago  

1997 New Hampshire State Champion (Junior division)


**What was your record by format?**  
**Extended:** 8-1-1  
**Booster Draft:** 4-2


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour-Amsterdam? Who did you prepare with?**  

Tested with Rubin, LSV, Brad Nelson, Michael Jacob, Tom Martell, Martin Juza, David Ochoa, Patrick Chapin, etc. etc. with a special shout out to Dan Burdick.


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**  

I may do one or two drafts a week online.


**What Extended deck are you playing and why?**  

Doran – very aggressive, highly disruptive deck expecting a wide open field with lots of combo.


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic, or if you don't go to FNM anymore, where did you play?**  

Sky High Comics in San Marcos, California.


**Pick a side: Mirran, or Phyrexian?**  

Whichever one has Treefolk or Dragons!
