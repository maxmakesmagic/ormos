Pro Tour–Berlin Round 11: Elves Battle the Pink Robots
======================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 1, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_DaveGuskin.jpg)
By Dave Guskin












Jan Doise vs. Shouta Yasooka
----------------------------


Jan Doise has made two Grand Prix Top 8s, one in Cologne and one in Genova, but hasn't yet made his breakout performance into a Pro Tour Top 8. This might be his weekend to do so, with his Elves! combo deck featuring both the [Mirror Entity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Entity) combo and going large with a team of Elves, which as it turns out is not too shabby either.


His opponent, Shouta Yasooka, is an accomplished mage. He has a stunning nine GP Top 8s along with the Pro Player of the Year title in 2006 and a trophy as one member of the winning trio at PT: Charleston. The quiet Japanese pro brought a Gifts Tron blue-black control deck to battle, finding his way to the top tables with a combination of powerful artifacts and insane card drawing.


Yasooka rolled 2d6, scoring only 5, which seemed unlikely to hit Doise's AC, but Doise had a critical failure on defense... with two 1s.


"Have fun," said Jan Doise, readying himself for the battle ahead.


### Game 1


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptber08/r11_doise.jpg)*Jan Doise manages a bevy of Elves.*
Yasooka led with a tapped [Watery Grave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Watery+Grave), and Doise took the opportunity to kick his Elf engine into gear by fetching a [Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) with [Wooded Foothills](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wooded+Foothills) and laying down one of the most important mana generators for his deck: [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid).


Yasooka showed his own form of mana generator: one piece of the lauded Urzatron, an [Urza's Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Mine), while Doise just beat down with the Druid and played another fetch into [Overgrown Tomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Tomb). Content to remain the beatdown, Doise took Yasooka to 18. An end step [Thirst for Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thirst+for+Knowledge) discarding [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) gave Yasooka the edge in cards, but would he have the time to use them? He entered phase two of Urzatron development with an [Urza's Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Tower) and played a [Dimir Signet](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dimir+Signet) to increase his colored mana count.


Doise's next turn was epic. He started with [Glimpse of Nature](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimpse+of+Nature), then played [Wirewood Symbiote](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Symbiote). Yasooka had him pause for a second to consider, but then let both [Wirewood Symbiote](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Symbiote) and the card draw resolve. A [Birchlore Rangers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birchlore+Rangers) and then a [Summoner's Pact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoner%27s+Pact) for [Nettle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel) kept the engine revving up to high speed. The [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid) provided enough ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220123123600im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/green.gif) for another Druid and a second [Nettle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel), kicking it into turbo. [Essence Warden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Essence+Warden) shot his life total up to 25 after a succession of one-mana elves and Symbiotes culminated in [Mirror Entity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Entity).


That's when the engine exploded.


[Wirewood Hivemaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Hivemaster) followed, and with an activation of [Wirewood Symbiote](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Symbiote) he renewed his [Nettle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel)s by replaying a [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid). After about six more Elves, Doise had 45 life, six insect tokens, 14 green mana, and an army of the little green people. He activated [Mirror Entity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Entity) for 1 to make the Insects into Elves, and then tapped them for mana and used Entity with X = 20 to attack with an army of one gigantic 20/20 [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid).


Yasooka was cold as ice as he [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother)ed it.


Doise seemed only a little shaken by this turn of events. After combat, Doise used a [Chord of Calling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling) with X = 2 to fetch a second [Wirewood Hivemaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Hivemaster) and passed the turn.


Yasooka, an unstoppable, unflappable pillar of calm, played [Damnation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Damnation). Doise thought for a second, returned [Nettle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel), [Wirewood Hivemaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Hivemaster), and [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid) to his hand using the three [Wirewood Symbiote](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Symbiote)s, and binned his massive army. Yasooka then completed the Tron and played an [Engineered Explosives](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Explosives) with X = 1.


Doise paid for his previous megaturn's Pact, then laid a land and his [Nettle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel). After beating down for 2, taking Yasooka to 13, he restarted his previously stalled engine with another [Glimpse of Nature](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimpse+of+Nature), putting on the gas with Hivemaster into Visionary into [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid) into another Visionary. Yasooka responded to that by Exploding [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid) and [Nettle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel). Doise, unfazed, played another [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid) and then Chorded for a second Hivemaster. A Pact later and he had his fourth and final Symbiote, allowing him to kick back into overdrive despite his lack of [Nettle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel). Unlike last time, however, Doise merely went into cruise mode, with one more Elf for a few more tokens, ending the turn at 45-13 with eleven Insect tokens and seven Elves ready to untap and smash.


Yasooka played another Signet and ended his turn, missing the second [Damnation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Damnation) to stop the extreme Elf beats. When Doise remembered his Pact payment and came in with the full team, Yasooka packed it in.


Doise 1, Yasooka 0


### Game 2


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptber08/r11_yasooka.jpg)*Shouta Yasooka does not seem to be on the right side of this board state.*
Yasooka decided he wouldn't be left out of the mana game this time, spending turn one playing [Urza's Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Tower), [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) removing [Deathmark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathmark), Signet. On turn two, Yasooka went for the jugular with [Deathmark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathmark) of Doise's [Nettle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel) and [Chalice of the Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chalice+of+the+Void) at X = 1. Doise played around it with a [Wirewood Hivemaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Hivemaster). Yasooka completed his triumvirate of terror with [Persecute](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Persecute) naming green on turn three, losing 3 life in the process to a [Polluted Delta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta) fetching untapped [Watery Grave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Watery+Grave). It was a small price to pay, however, as his opponent discarded a hand of three [Birchlore Rangers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birchlore+Rangers), [Chord of Calling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling), and another Hivemaster.


Doise played a mystery morph, sliding slightly around the nearly impenetrable wall of the Chalice, and bashed in on the next turn, making the life totals 19-14 in his favor. Yasooka just replied with an end-step [Gifts Ungiven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven) for the remaining Tron pieces along with their partner in crime [Tolaria West](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tolaria+West). [Damnation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Damnation) followed by the great and powerful [Grim Poppet](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Poppet) convinced Doise to concede.


Doise 1, Yasooka 1


**Game 3**


Doise immediately went to 17 to fetch a dual for his [Nettle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel). Yasooka replied with an [Urza's Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Tower), earning him 2 points of damage from the Sentinel. On turn two, Doise provided the gas with two [Birchlore Rangers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birchlore+Rangers) and an [Elvish Visionary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Visionary). Yasooka kept the Tron flowing with an [Urza's Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Mine), but appeared not to have much in the way of answers to the impending Elf horde. Doise continued to pummel himself by fetching duals, going down to 13 and playing a [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid). Rather than attacking, he passed the turn.


Yasooka played an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) rather than complete the Tron, and played a [Chalice of the Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chalice+of+the+Void) with X = 1. Doise nodded and revealed why the Elves decided not to attack, calmly tapping all of his Elves on Yasooka's end step for a [Chord of Calling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling) with X = 3. It turned out they were just waiting for the shiniest Elf, [Mirror Entity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Entity), who pumped the team to 4/4s and brought in 24 damage and the match.


Doise 2, Yasooka 1
