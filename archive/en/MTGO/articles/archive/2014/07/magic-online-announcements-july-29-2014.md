Wizards of the Coast is Looking for New Team Members
Ever wanted to work at Wizards of the Coast? Now's a good time to apply! The *Magic* Digital Team is currently seeking to fill two positions.


[***Duels of The Planeswalkers*, Technology Project Manager**](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/technology-project-manager-duels-planeswalkers-job-renton-wa-us)
If you love *Duels of the Planeswalkers* and have a passion for delivering games on-time, on-budget, and of the highest quality – then this might be the career for you!


[***Magic Online* – Card-Set Project Manager**](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/technology-project-manager-temporary-job-renton-wa-us)
Fundamental to everything we do – the Project Manager for *Magic Online* card-sets will have a passion working with multiple teams to deliver amazing experiences to our players – on-time, on-budget, and of the highest quality!





July MOCS and MOPR Seasons End July 30, 2014
As a reminder, the July seasons for MOCS and MOPR both end tomorrow, July 30. After the downtime tomorrow, the new August MOCS season begins! August's promo is [Show and Tell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell)! The July MOCS promo, [Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft), as well as the July MOPR promos, [Dissolve](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dissolve) and [Magma Spray](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magma+Spray), will be granted during the downtime.
