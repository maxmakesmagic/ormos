Gaudenis Vidugiris took down the Top 8 Draft at Grand Prix Denver this past weekend with this unusual red-black deck. The deck uses its impressive removal suite and a somewhat ragtag band of creatures to hold the ground until it can take control of the game using bombs like [Carnifex Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carnifex+Demon), [Argentum Armor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Argentum+Armor), and [Prototype Portal](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prototype+Portal). Vidugiris's deck took down more traditional draft decks—red-green "dinosaurs," red-blue with [Furnace Celebration](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Furnace+Celebration), and white-blue fliers—to win the GP Denver title. For more about Grand Prix Denver and *Scars* block Limited, check out the [event coverage](/en/events/coverage/vidugiris-victorious-denver).
