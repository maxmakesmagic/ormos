Magic Online Cube: Holiday 2012
===============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 27, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












The [Power Nine](/en/articles/archive/power-nine-2003-10-15-0) is coming to **Magic Online** on December 19, and you can play with them in the new **Magic Online** Cube: Holiday 2012! Completely redesigned, this 540-card Cube includes the nine most powerful **Magic** cards ever printed, along with other over-powered accomplices. If you've never had a chance to cast a [Black Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Lotus) or the Moxen for some over-the-top first turn play, here is your chance.


![Magic Online Cube Booster](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/MTGPOW_booster_sty_001.png)As always, Cube is about the cards, and with the Holiday Cube Max MaCall and the R&D team didn't pull any punches. All of the heavy-hitters are present, including many with new art! [Read Max's article](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/feature/223b&date=11/27/2012) for design insight into the Holiday Cube and check out the full card list below.


The Holiday Cube drafts begin after the downtime on Wednesday, December 19, and run until the downtime on Wednesday, January 2. Two queues—a single-elimination queue and a Swiss queue—will be available during this time and the full event details can be found below. Like last time, joining the Holiday Cube drafts requires either Event Tickets or a combination of Event Tickets and Cube Tickets. If you have Cube Tickets left over from last time, you can use those to join the new Holiday Cube.


We are very excited about the Holiday Cube and hope you have fun slinging the most powerful **Magic** cards ever printed. Good drafting and good luck!


**Note:** There will be no **Magic Online** Championship Series Qualifier Point (QP) prizes for events after the downtime on December 19 until 10:00 AM on December 26. 2013 **Magic Online** Championship Series QPs will only be available as prizes for events that start after 10:00 AM on December 26.


### **The Magic Online Cube: Holiday 2012 Draft Queues**


*START TIMES:*  On demand beginning after the downtime on Wednesday, December 19, 2012, until the downtime on Wednesday, January 2, 2013.  
*LOCATION:*  Limited Queues room.  
*ENTRY OPTION(S):*  8 Event Tickets **OR** 2 Event Tickets and 10 Cube Tickets.  
*PRODUCT:*  **Magic Online** will provide 3 Phantom **Magic Online** Cube: Holiday 2012 booster packs for this event. Cards that players draft and play with in a Phantom event are NOT added to those players' collections.  
*SIZE:*  8 Players.  
*DURATION:*  Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, single elimination **OR** 3 rounds, Swiss pairings depending on event entered.  
*PRIZES:*  Prizes for single-elimination events are based on placement at the end of the tournament. Prizes for Swiss pairing events are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points.  
**Note:** Booster pack prizes for Holiday Cube draft events from December 19 until 10:00 AM on December 26 will be from *Urza* Block. Booster pack prizes for Cube draft events after 10:00 AM on December 26 until the downtime on January 2 will be from *Masques* Block.
