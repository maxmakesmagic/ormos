![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpmos14/2-sergiy-sushalskyy.jpg)


### Sergiy Sushalskyy


**Age:** 31  
**Hometown:** Kiev, Ukraine   
**Occupation:** Sales Manager and a IESE Student  
  

**Previous **Magic** Accomplishments:** 
  
Top 8 at Grand Prix Warsaw in 2014, some Top 32 in the Grand Prix I attended in the 2013 / 2014 season.
 



**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**
  
Jund Monsters, because it beats most of the field.
 



**What changes would you apply to your deck and why?**
  
I wouldn't apply any changes. I'm quite happy with the way it worked out.
 



**What are the most important cards in your deck?**
  
[Rakdos's Return](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rakdos%27s+Return)




  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpmos14/3-dmitriy-butakov.jpg)

![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpmos14/6-igor-gorbunov.jpg)


### Igor Gorbunov


**Age:** 32  
**Hometown:** Moscow, Russia   
**Occupation:** Owner of a restaurant chain  
  

**Previous **Magic** Accomplishments:** 
  
I advanced to the finals of my National Championship. I have participated in the **Magic** World Championship in both Kyoto and San Francisco and I made it to the Pro Tour Valencia and attended 2 Grand Prix prior to this one.
 



**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**
  
Boros Burn - I'm a huge fan of red decks. Whenever I can make a burn deck work, I will play it in the respective tournament.
 



**What changes would you apply to your deck and why?**
  
I would swap out [Eidolon of the Great Revel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eidolon+of+the+Great+Revel) for [Young Pyromancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Young+Pyromancer) in my main deck, because I think it would have worked better in the current environment.
 



**What are the most important cards in your deck?**
  
[Chandra's Phoenix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%27s+Phoenix)!




  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpmos14/7-lee-shi-tian.jpg)


### Lee Shi Tian


**Age:** 26  
**Hometown:** Hong Kong   
**Occupation:** Accountant  
  

**Previous **Magic** Accomplishments:** 
  
Top 4 Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica*, Top 8 Pro Tour *Born of the Gods*, Grand Prix Birmingham 2008 Champion, Hong Kong National Champion 2008, APAC Representative for the Player's Championship 2013
 



**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**
  
Mono-Black Devotion with a splash of Blue. Nam Sung Wook's combined record in 4 consecutive events was an impressive 37 - 3, so I decided to follow the master!
 



**What changes would you apply to your deck and why?**
  
I would cut [Notion Thief](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Notion+Thief) for an additional copy of [Dark Betrayal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Betrayal), but it's Nam's signature card and I don't dare to make the change!
 



**What are the most important cards in your deck?**
  
[Mutavault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutavault). They have been following me since 2008 where I won the Grand Prix!
