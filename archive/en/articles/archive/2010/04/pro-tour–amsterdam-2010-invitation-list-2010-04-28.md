Pro Tour–Amsterdam 2010 Invitation List
=======================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 28, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Invitations to Pro Tour-Amsterdam are awarded in the following manner:


 


* The 2009 World Champion.
* The 2009 Pro Tour Player of the Year.
* The Top 50 players from Pro Tour–San Juan
* Players with 2010 Pro Tour Players Club level 6 or higher.
* Players with 2010 Pro Tour Players Club level 4\* & 5\* (invitation for level 4 & 5 players are not awarded until September 3, 2010).
* Players with 2010 Pro Tour Players Club level 3\* are invited to 1 Pro Tour or World Championship of their choice in the 2010 season (invitation for level 3 players are not awarded until September 3, 2010).
* The Top 16 finishers of each Grand Prix tournament that feeds Pro Tour-Amsterdam.
* Players who earn invitations through Pro Tour–Amsterdam Qualifier tournaments (including **Magic Online**  Qualifier Tournaments).
* The Top 100 DCI-ranked players in the DCI Total format on July 21, 2010.

\* Players with Pro Tour Players Club levels 3, 4 and 5 are allowed to play in Pro Tour qualifiers if their *only* invitation to the Pro Tour is due to their level in the Players Club (see below)


If you believe that your rating or finish at a Pro Tour Qualifier or Grand Prix is in error, please Visit <https://webapp.wizards.com/login.aspx?target=personal>. After logging in, click the "Appeals" link and fill out the form.
