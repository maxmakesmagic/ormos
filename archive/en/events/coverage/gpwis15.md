Lévy's Cards Converge for Victory!
==================================




Pro Tour Hall of Fame player Raphaël Lévy took a more colorful road to victory at Grand Prix Madison, defeating 2014 *Magic Online* Champion Magnus Lantto in the finals.

TOP 8 COVERAGE



Raphaël Lévy picked up his fifth lifetime Grand Prix victory in Madison, taking down a Top 8 filled with competitors like fellow Pro Tour Hall of Fame member and fourth-ranked Paul Rietzl, two-time Grand Prix winner Samuel Pardee and, in the finals, the 2014 Magic Online Champion Magnus Lantto.


While devoid and other colorless cards become the breakout strategy in the two drafts to determine Top 8, Lévy's winning approach eschewed that for more colorful threats and answers. Throughout Sealed and Draft, three colors wasn't an unusual option to appear in action despite the clear preference for two-colors when it was possible. Lévy's last deck didn't feature any way to fix his mana but it didn't stop the power of his converge cards from clinching the win.


With the first hurdle of the Wisconsin the run complete all eyes looked forward to more action at Pro Tour *Battle for Zendikar*. With a fresh Grand Prix win under his belt, Lévy was just one of the many players eager to aim even higher the following weekend. Congratulations again to now five-time Grand Prix winner Raphaël Lévy, champion of Grand Prix Madison!
