*Shards of Alara* Block Constructed has already shaped up to be an exciting format, and now with *Conflux* soon to be added to the mix, things look to be even more exciting! So what decks rule the format? Jan Franen used the above list to make the Top 8 of a recent premier event. A standard control list, Jan looked to control the early game with things like [Tidehollow Strix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tidehollow+Strix), [Scourglass](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scourglass), and [Courier's Capsule](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courier%27s+Capsule), then gain a huge advantage late in the game with [Sharuum, the Hegemon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sharuum%2C+the+Hegemon) returning the previous efforts. Jan also had enough room to squeeze in *three* different planeswalkers in the Tezzeret, Ajani, and Elspeth form.
