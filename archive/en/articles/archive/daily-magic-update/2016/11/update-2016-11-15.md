Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Temur Energy](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2016-11-14)

The aptly named Simic\_Observer went 5-0 with this Temur Energy deck that goes a bit above and beyond what most other energy decks are trying to do.


They're not trying to [Electrostatic Pummeler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrostatic+Pummeler) anyone, and there are precisely zero pump spells, slowing the deck down significantly. But, in return, the deck gets a ton of resilience and more late game mojo. [Confiscation Coup](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Confiscation+Coup) and [Jace, Unraveler of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+Unraveler+of+Secrets) see to the long game, and [Whirler Virtuoso](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whirler+Virtuoso) makes sure you have something to do with all of that energy that doesn't require attacking to matter.


The deck also grabs a bunch of sideboard cards that occasionally came with the deck in the past as well—since splashing was so easy in an [Aether Hub](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Hub)/[Attune with Aether](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Attune+with+Aether)/[Servant of the Conduit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Servant+of+the+Conduit) deck—including [Negate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Negate), [Spell Shrivel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Shrivel), and [Ceremonious Rejection](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ceremonious+Rejection).


It's a neat evolution of the Red-Green Energy Deck, and one worth looking at for the upcoming Standard Showdown.
