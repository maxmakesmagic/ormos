**Magic Online** 9th Anniversary Celebration
============================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 7, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











M**agic Online** turns nine years old on June 20 and we can't think of a better way to kick off an Anniversary Celebration than with the 2011 Community Cup. Starting June 15 we will offer several ways for you to celebrate nine great years of MTGO. The Anniversary Celebration will last until June 29.

The **Magic Online** 9th Anniversary Celebration will consist of the following: 

* *Masters Edition IV* Farewell
* Increased Prizes for Constructed Gold 2-Player Queues, 8-Player Queues, and Premier Events
* Employee Gunslinging
* Double *Magic Online* Player Rewards Points
* **Magic Online** Player Rewards Lifetime Points Rewards

  
***MASTERS EDITION IV* FAREWELL**

*Masters Edition IV* will be leaving the store on June 29, but to say goodbye we are introducing *Masters Edition* 4-Booster Sealed Queues and Daily Events. These events will use one of each booster back released for *Masters Edition*: I, II, III, and IV! Prizes will also be a combination of *Masters Edition* booster packs. 

***MASTERS EDITION IV* 4-BOOSTER SEALED QUEUES**  
*START TIME:* On demand beginning after the scheduled downtime on Wednesday, June 15, until the beginning of the scheduled downtime on Wednesday, June 29.   
*LOCATION:* Sealed Swiss Queues room.   
*ENTRY OPTION(S):* 16 Event Tickets OR 1 *Masters Edition I* booster pack, 1 *Masters Edition II* booster pack, 1 *Masters Edition III* booster pack, and 1 *Masters Edition IV* booster pack.  
*PRODUCT:*
 **Magic Online** will provide 1 *Masters Edition I* booster pack, 1 *Masters Edition II* booster pack, 1 *Masters Edition III* booster pack, and 1 *Masters Edition IV* booster pack for this event.  
*SIZE:* 8 Players.   
*DURATION:* Deck building time plus 3 Rounds, [Swiss pairings](http://wizards.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/465).   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points.

**WIZARDS OF THE COAST EMPLOYEE GUNSLINGING**

Throughout the 9th Anniversary Celebration, various Wizards of the Coast employees will go to the casual room and announce they are gunslinging. Join their game to challenge them! If you defeat one of our gunslingers, you will receive an alternate-art [Hero of Bladehold](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero+of+Bladehold) promo card within 72 hours!

**DOUBLE MAGIC ONLINE PLAYER REWARDS POINTS FOR ALL EVENTS**

Finally, during the entire Anniversary Celebration we will double all MOPR points you earn for playing in events. That's right; each event you play in between the downtimes on June 15 and June 29 will give you twice as many points! So get out there and get playing! 

**MOPR LIFETIME REWARD**

In addition to increased Rewards Points, the July 6 Player Rewards grant will include bonus cards based on your lifetime points. Watch the [**Magic Online** Group](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/pro-tour-gatecrash-qualifier-season-top-8-standard-decklists-2015-50) for more news soon!
