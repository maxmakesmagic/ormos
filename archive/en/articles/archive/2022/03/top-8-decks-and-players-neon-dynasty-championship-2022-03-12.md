The Top 8 Decks and Players of the Neon Dynasty Championship
============================================================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on March 13, 2022 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Corbin Hosler and Adam Styborski











From 229 players came the final eight returning for the Sunday showdown. These are the Top 8 competitors of the [Neon Dynasty Championship](https://magic.gg/events/neon-dynasty-championship).


![](https://images.ctfassets.net/ryplwhabvmmk/2kg2GPPsz4oC07MA2lQguX/324e82102805d9c92e80871cbfda5d0f/1920__1080-Neon-Dynasty-Championship-Top-8-Social.jpg)



![](https://images.ctfassets.net/ryplwhabvmmk/5yjyou2DcPHoxbZeUoe0FO/29d322cf38228f276e6e8be29565f5b8/281x225-Jim-Davis-Player-Card-Front.png)  
*Jim Davis*


What a way to break through for Jim Davis, a longtime high-level competitor who battled to the top of the SCG Tour and becoming a *Magic* content creator before making his first career Top Finish in the most dominant way possible. He turned in a 12-0 run across Alchemy and Historic matches to lock up his Top 8 berth early—much to his own surprise—and entered Sunday as the top overall seed.

*Jean-Emmanuel Depraz*


From winning Grand Prix Warsaw in 2017 to captain of the winning team from France at the 2019 World *Magic* Cup to Top Finish after Top Finish across 2019 and 2020 with a *Magic* World Championship XXVII finalist appearance in 2021: the accolades for and persistent, consistent success of Jean-Emmanuel Depraz continue to pile up. Attacking metagames with tuned, thoughtful decks, his surprise Historic list (Azorius Affinity) was a deck no one expected but was certainly successful as Depraz, again, clinched a Top Finish at the Neon Dynasty Championship.

*Jonny Guttman*


Jonny "ginky" Guttman is one of the group of fast-rising young talents in the game, and he's quietly been behind some of the best Standard decks of the past few years. His team was at it again for the Neon Dynasty Championship, and in this case that meant playing streamlined versions of staple decks: Mono-White Aggro in Alchemy and Golgari Food in Historic.


It paid off for Guttman, whose star only keeps growing brighter with the addition of his first career Top Finish.

*Eli Kassis*


Kassis continues to build on an outstanding career with his second Top Finish, and the *Magic* Pro League member will now set his sights on improving on his quarterfinals exit in his last premier Top 8 appearance.


He'll have a good chance at that thanks to the Orzhov Venture deck his team pioneered in Alchemy. The brew was one of the top-performing decks throughout the Neon Dynasty Championship, and Kassis took it to a perfect 7-0 finish in the Alchemy rounds. With a diverse Top 8 field to work his way through next, Kassis hopes he can continue the run with his dungeon deck.
