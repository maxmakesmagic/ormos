### When


All Tournaments will be held on the weekend of April 22nd, 2006


### Entry Fee & Product Mix


* Check with your local tournament organizer for specific entry fee information.
* Cost of entry will provide you, one (1) *Ravnica: City of Guilds* tournament pack and three (3) *Dissension* boosters.
* In addition, some locations may offer 3-person Team & 2-person Two Headed Giant formats.
	+ Each 3-person team will receive two (2) *Ravnica: City of Guilds* tournament packs & six (6) *Dissension* boosters to build 3 decks.
	+ Each 2-person Two Headed Giant team will receive one (1) *Ravnica: City of Guilds* tournament pack & five (5) *Dissension* boosters to build two decks. For further information about Two Headed Giant format, please go to the [Two Headed Giant FAQ](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=dci/announce/dci20050805a).

### Tournament Structure


* The structure of the main *Dissension* Prerelease tournament is modified Swiss-style.
* *Dissension* Prerelease side tournaments will be offered throughout the day and will consist of single-elimination rounds.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2006/dis_pre.jpg)*Yours for playing, while supplies last.*
### Tournament Formats


The format for each Prerelease tournament is:


Please consult your local tournament organizer for regional availability and start times.


* DCI sanctioned, *Dissension*, Sealed Deck.
* Select locations, DCI sanctioned, *Dissension*, Team Sealed Deck.
* Select locations, DCI sanctioned, *Dissension*, Two Headed Giant Sealed Deck.
* Select locations, DCI sanctioned, *Ravnica-Guildpact-Dissension* 8-person Booster Draft.

### Prizes


* Select locations, check with organizer, will offer Door Prizes of 3 *Dissension* Booster Packs to be given out at random each hour.
* Special commemorative *Dissension* prerelease foil card for each tournament participant, ***while supplies last***.
* Additional packs of *Dissension* cards to top players, (as determined by your local prerelease Tournament Organizer).
* Other items for winners of the main tournament and side tournaments may be awarded, (as determined by your local prerelease Tournament Organizer).
