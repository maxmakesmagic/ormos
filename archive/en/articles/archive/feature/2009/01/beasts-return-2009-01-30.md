Looking for the latest in Extended innovation? James Wise managed to make it all the way to Day 2 at Grand Prix-Los Angeles with this build of Beasts. The deck was created with Kyle Sanchez, and featured interactions between old cards and new. Take [Woolly Thoctar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woolly+Thoctar) and [Contested Cliffs](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+Cliffs) for example. What's better than a 5/4 for three mana? How about one that can blow up all your opponent's creatures for two mana? For all the official coverage of Grand Prix-Los Angeles (including [this](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/grand-prix%E2%80%93los-angeles-day-2-blog-2009-01-18) special feature on rogue decks), [visit here](/en/events/coverage/luis-scott-vargas-conquers-la). To find out how to get to an Extended Pro Tour Qualifier for Honolulu near you, visit the [official PTQ page](/en/node/640716).
