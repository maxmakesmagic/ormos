Erik Landriz is a player who puts the mean in green. He managed to Top 8 his Mobile, Alabama PTQ with Monogreen Goodness. Featuring such green-heavy creatures as [Deity of Scars](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deity+of+Scars), [Creakwood Liege](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Creakwood+Liege), [Oversoul of Dusk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oversoul+of+Dusk), and [Wilt-Leaf Cavaliers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wilt-Leaf+Cavaliers) Erik made sure his [Primalcrux](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primalcrux)es would be as large as possible. As an added bonus, since [Primalcrux](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primalcrux) counts the green mana symbols in *permanents* you control, Erik gets an extra bonus from his [Garruk Wildspeaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Garruk%2BWildspeaker)s and [Fertile Ground](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fertile+Ground)s! Find out which cards from the exciting new set *Shards of Alara* you could add to Erik's deck with the [*Shards of Alara* Visual Spoiler](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/shardsofalara/spoiler).
