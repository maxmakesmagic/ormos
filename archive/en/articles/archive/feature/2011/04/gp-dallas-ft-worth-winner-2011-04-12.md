Grand Prix Dallas/Fort Worth was the largest tournament ever held in Texas! With just under 1,200 players, the Standard event was truly something to behold. The winner was David Shiels, who managed to beat out all the other competitors with this take on Caw-Blade. Check out the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/shiels-stands-alone-dallas) for more.
