*Zendikar* Game Day Promos
==========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 26, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Game Day is coming! It's [on October 31](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/gameday-facts), which should make it extra spooooooky.

Here's the Participation Card. It's full-frame, but it's not foil.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/306_NissasChosen_qr7p7vj2rt.jpg)  
And here's the card for winners. It's both full-frame *and* foil!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/306_EmeriaAngel_ezvzls0kxb.jpg)  
Once again, that's [*Zendikar* Game Day](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/gameday-facts), taking place on Saturday, October 31 at a store near you.
