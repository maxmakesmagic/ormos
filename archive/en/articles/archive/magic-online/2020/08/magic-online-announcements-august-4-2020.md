MOCS Showcase Last Chance Events
--------------------------------


If you haven't earned an invite for the Format Showcase Qualifier of your choice, your final attempts to make it in will take place Sunday through Wednesday in the MOCS Showcase Last Chance Events. Players who finish with 5 wins will earn an Entry Token for the Showcase Qualifier in that format. See the schedule grid above for times and formats, and visit the [Premier Play info page](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#slce) for more details.

Showcase Challenge Weekend
--------------------------


Players looking to prove their mettle against the best players in *Magic Online* will want to use their QPs to enter *Magic Online* Champions Showcase (MOCS) events such as the returning Showcase Challenges this weekend. These MOCS events are a set of linked events designed to highlight the best players on *Magic Online* each season, culminating in the Champions Showcase and its prize pool of $70,000 and a Players Tour Finals invitation.


First up on Saturday, August 8, we have the Standard (6 a.m. PT), Modern (8 a.m. PT), and Vintage (10 a.m. PT) Showcase Challenges. Then on Sunday, August 9, we finish off with the Pioneer (6 a.m. PT), Legacy (8 a.m. PT), and *Double Masters* Sealed (9 a.m. PT) Showcase Challenges. Each of these events requires 40 QPs to enter. The Top 8 finishers from each event will earn a qualification token to enter the appropriate Showcase Qualifier in August.


Learn more about Showcase Challenges events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#schallenges).
