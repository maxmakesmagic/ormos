*Magic 2014* Event Deck Packaging
=================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 2, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







Surprising no one, there is indeed an Event Deck for ***Magic** 2014—Core Set*. Now if that didn't surprise, you, maybe this will. The packaging? It has Chandra on it.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1267_kjhahf_box.jpg)Still unsurprised? Good for you. Because, there's... nothing surprising about this!


Event Decks are meant to be something you can snag at your local game store when you forget your deck at home for Friday Night **Magic**, or as a slightly more competitive deck base for new players looking for something a step above the Intro Packs.


Check back in a few weeks and I'll be able to show you what is inside!
