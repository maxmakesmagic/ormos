2015-16 Mid-Season Master Standings
-----------------------------------


#### Updated February 7, 2016


Congratulations to Owen Turtenwald, the 2015-16 Mid-Season Master, on his 2016 *Magic: The Gathering* World Championship invitation!


The player with the most Pro Points up through Pro Tour *Oath of the Gatewatch* is identified as the 2015-16 Mid-Season Master. The Mid-Season Master standings track a player’s Pro Points earned at Pro Tours, the 2015 World *Magic* Cup, and the best six finishes from Grand Prix during the 2015-16 season.


The 2015-16 Mid-Season Master is awarded and invitation to the 2016 *Magic: The Gathering* World Championship.
