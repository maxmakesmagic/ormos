DAY 1 COVERAGE



Welcome to sunny Seville, Spain, where this weekend hundreds of players from all over Europe have gathered to compete in a Standard Grand Prix. And it's a very special Standard Grand Prix, as for the first time players will be able to harness the power of *Fate Reforged* in the format!


This will surely leave us with a lot of news about the new cards and potential new decks, as players dash head first into the set, ferociously delve deep into the format, bolster existing archetypes, and prove their prowess at *Magic*.


Stay tuned while we bring you all the action and all the excitement straight from the battlefield of Grand Prix Seville 2015!
