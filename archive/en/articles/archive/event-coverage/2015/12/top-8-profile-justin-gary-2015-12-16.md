Top 8 Profile - Justin Gary
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPMAN00/Images/T8Gary.jpg)**Name:** Justin Gary  
**Age:** 20  
**Location:**  Miami, USA  
**Occupation:** Student - Dartmouth University  
**Played Magic since:** 1995, The Dark  
**Best accomplishments:** 1997 U.S. National Champion, Top 8 Pro Tour Rome  


**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Manchester?**  

A: Term abroad in Europe. Short trip.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: I was hoping for top 8 to ensure a Masters invite for Chicago.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: Tight-as-hell.



**Q: What do you think about the tournament location-/organisation-wise?**  

A: Great site but everything is closed Sunday evening.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: I think that I just had a very good intuitive grasp of the environment. Since Invasion draft is so new this is a big help.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: This is my first top 8 this year though I have been very close several times. It feels good to break through again.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: Myself and my teammate Darwin Kastle will play in the finals.
