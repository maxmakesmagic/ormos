Announcing Premium Deck Series: Graveborn
=========================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 21, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











![Premium Deck Series: Graveborn](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/737_3trbvae5c7.jpg)  
### Call Forth the Dead.

Legions of corpses await your sinister commands. This 60-card, all-foil deck entombs, exhumes, and reanimates some of the most powerful **Magic** creatures from all five colors of mana. Harvest your graveyard for an assortment of these face-beaters as you walk over a field of bones to claim your victory.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/737_y53ofs5m2t.jpg)  
**Contents:**


* 60-card premium foil deck, including 8 rare cards
* Exclusive Spindown life counter
* Foil deck box
* Strategy insert
* **Magic** "learn to play" guide

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/737_fh3hxxt4v3.jpg)Art by [Raymond Swanland](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Raymond+Swanland%22%5D)

**Deck Design and Development**: David Humpherys   
**Release Date**: November 18, 2011   
**MSRP**: $34.99
