Mana Maze: The Puzzling
=======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 29, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












Yesterday, we looked at [Mana Maze Solitaire](/en/articles/archive/mana-maze-solitaire-2012-08-28), a solitaire variant of **Magic** developed by Mark Rosewater in 1995. Today, we have a puzzle based on those rules!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1047_puzzle.jpg)Remember, the rules of Mana Maze Solitaire are based on the rules of **Magic** in 1995, which means that mana burn is a thing that will kill you. Here we go! Take it away, 1995 Mark Rosewater!


*Your opponent is...you. Having some time on your hands, you've decided to play a game of Mana Maze, a **Magic** solitaire variant. You've chosen to use a sixty-four card deck with an 8x8 open-hand layout. You start the game with one life point and you've made it your goal to destroy the [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan). Good luck!*

### Solution


by Mark Rosewater


The object of this puzzle is to destroy the [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan), that 10/10 menace in the top row. So where do we start? Unlike the traditional **Magic** puzzles, Mana Maze Solitaire puzzles are a little easier to jump into, because there are a smaller number of first moves. First, we check out what mana we have available. Only [Birds of Paradise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) (for one mana of any color) is in play, but a significant amount of mana is available in the second row ([City of Brass](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Brass), [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain), [Sisters of the Flame](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sisters+of+the+Flame), [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island), [Badlands](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Badlands), and [Princess Lucrezia](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Princess+Lucrezia)).


Next, we see what we can quickly clear out of the puzzle to get to the mana. We could use the Birds to either [Red Elemental Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Red+Elemental+Blast) or [Unsummon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsummon) something, but the [Prodigal Sorcerer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prodigal+Sorcerer) lets us eliminate two cards at once (itself and the Merfolk) without using mana. So:


**1) Tap the [Prodigal Sorcerer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prodigal+Sorcerer) to destroy the Merfolk.** This uncovers the [Sisters of the Flame](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sisters+of+the+Flame), which we can turn into a red mana. The only red spell in the first two rows is a [Red Elemental Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Red+Elemental+Blast), so we might as well use it. The [Air Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Air+Elemental) seems the obvious recipient, because we will want to use the [Unsummon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsummon), and the [Lifetap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lifetap) can be taken care of by the [Tranquility](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tranquility) we just exposed.


**2) Tap [Sisters of the Flame](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sisters+of+the+Flame) to add one red mana to your mana pool. Use the red mana to cast [Red Elemental Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Red+Elemental+Blast) and destroy the [Air Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Air+Elemental).** Once we expose the [Badlands](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Badlands), we need to tap it for a red mana. We do this now for three reasons. First, we need it for the [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) in the third row. Second, if we wait until [Conversion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conversion) is in play, we won't be able to get the red out of it we need. And third, if we wait too long, we'll suffer damage from the [Dingus Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dingus+Egg).


**3) Tap [Badlands](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Badlands) to add one red mana to the pool.** We know we want to use the [Tranquility](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tranquility). Unfortunately, the only green mana available is the [City of Brass](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Brass), and tapping it will deal us 1 damage. As we start with only 1 life, this means we will have to gain more life before we can use the City. To do that we need to cast the [Healing Salve](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Healing+Salve).


**4) Tap [Birds of Paradise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) to add one blue mana to the mana pool. Use the blue mana to cast [Unsummon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsummon) to return [Frost Giant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frost+Giant) to your hand.**


**5) Tap the [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) to add one white mana to your pool (due to [Conversion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conversion)). Use the white mana to cast [Healing Salve](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Healing+Salve). Life Total is now 4.** Thanks to the exposed [Stone Calendar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Calendar), we only need tap one other land ([Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)) to cast [Tranquility](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tranquility).


**6) Tap [City of Brass](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Brass) and the [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) to add one green mana and one blue mana to your mana pool. Tapping [City of Brass](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Brass) inflicts 1 point of damage. Life Total is now 3. Use the green and blue mana to cast [Tranquility](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tranquility), destroying [Conversion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conversion) and [Lifetap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lifetap).** Now we tap the Princess. The next move is pretty obvious because we only have two mana, two spells we can cast, and one appropriate target. The only important thing to remember is that we have to tap [Princess Lucrezia](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Princess+Lucrezia) before we [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) the [Bronze Horse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bronze+Horse), as the damage is reduced to zero if she's still in play.


**7) Tap [Princess Lucrezia](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Princess+Lucrezia) to add one blue mana to the pool. Use the red mana from step 3 to cast [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) at the [Bronze Horse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bronze+Horse). Use the blue mana to cast [Psychic Purge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychic+Purge) on the Horse, destroying it.** After we finish off the Horse, we free up only one source of mana, the [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves). Tapping it, we then [Crumble](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crumble) the artifact in play of our choise. The [Dingus Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dingus+Egg) gets elected simply because we still need the [Stone Calendar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Calendar), [Mana Matrix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Matrix), and [Celestial Prism](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Celestial+Prism). (And we even get 4 life for our trouble)


**8) Tap [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) to add one green mana to the pool. Use the green mana to cast [Crumble](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crumble) on the [Dingus Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dingus+Egg), destroying it. Gain 4 life. Life total is now 7.** Using the same reasoning, we then use the Mox and [Twiddle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Twiddle) to rid us of the [Su-Chi](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Su-Chi).


**9) Tap the [Mox Sapphire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Sapphire) to add one blue mana to the pool. Use the blue mana to cast [Twiddle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Twiddle) on [Su-Chi](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Su-Chi), tapping (and destroying) it. [Su-Chi](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Su-Chi) adds four colorless mana to the pool when it goes to the graveyard.** We then sacrifice the [Coal Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coal+Golem) to turn three colorless mana to red, which we use right away to [Fissure](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fissure) the [Grizzly Bears](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grizzly+Bears) and [Shatter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shatter) the [Stone Calendar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Calendar) (at a reduced casting costâ€”the reason we kept the Calendar and Matrix around).


**10) Use three colorless mana and sacrifice the [Coal Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coal+Golem) to add three red mana to the pool. Use two red mana to cast [Fissure](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fissure) on the [Grizzly Bears](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grizzly+Bears), destroying it. Use the remaining red mana to cast [Shatter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shatter) on the [Stone Calendar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Calendar), destroying it. One colorless mana remains in the mana pool.** Now we must get at some mana sources, and also move up thw row toward our target, the [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan). We set our sights on [Xenic Poltergeist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenic+Poltergeist), [Mana Matrix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Matrix), and [Paralyze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Paralyze).


**11) Tap [Sol Ring](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sol+Ring) to add two colorless mana to the pool. Use two colorless mana to activate and tap [Celestial Prism](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Celestial+Prism) to add one black mana to the pool. Tap [Xenic Poltergeist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenic+Poltergeist) to animate [Mana Matrix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Matrix). Use the black mana to cast [Paralyze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Paralyze) on animated [Mana Matrix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Matrix), tapping (and destroying) it.** Now we want to get the [Mana Flare](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Flare) into play, but getting the [Craw Giant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Craw+Giant) and [Serra Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serra+Angel) out of the way will cost a lot of mana and (because we do it using [Ashes to Ashes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashes+to+Ashes)) a lot of life.


**12) Sacrifice [Gaea's Touch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gaea%27s+Touch) to add two green mana to the pool. Use one green mana and tap [Fire Sprites](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire+Sprites) to add one red mana to the pool. The pool now has one green, one red, and one colorless mana in it.**


**13) Tap [Elves of Deep Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elves+of+Deep+Shadow) to add one black mana to the pool. Take 1 damage for tapping the Elves. Life total is now 6. Use the black mana to cast [Dark Ritual](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Ritual) and add three black mana to the pool. Use two black and one colorless mana to cast [Ashes to Ashes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashes+to+Ashes) and destroy the [Craw Giant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Craw+Giant) and [Serra Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serra+Angel) (bringing [Mana Flare](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Flare) into play). Suffer 5 damage for casting [Ashes to Ashes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashes+to+Ashes). Life Total is now 1. One black, one green, and one red mana remain in the pool.** We get more mana by tapping the [Desert](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desert), [Plains](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains), and [Taiga](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Taiga), which we use to rid ourselves of [Raging River](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+River) and [Al-abara's Carpet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Al-abara%27s+Carpet). The first is done to get the [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan) into play (so that we can destroy it), the second to get us much-needed life.


**14) Tap [Taiga](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Taiga), [Plains](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains), and [Desert](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desert) to add two green, two white, and two colorless mana to the pool (increased because of [Mana Flare](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Flare)). Use three green, two colorless, and one red mana to cast [Desert Twister](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desert+Twister) and destroy [Raging River](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+River). Use two white mana to cast [Divine Offering](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Divine+Offering) on [Al-Abara's Carpet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Al-Abara%27s+Carpet), destroying it. Gain 5 life to raise life total to 6. One black mana remains in the pool.** Given that the spells we have to take down the [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan), [Psionic Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psionic+Blast) and [Banshee](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banshee), will damage us in the process, we will need even more life.


**15) Sacrifice [Bayou](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bayou) to [Dark Heart of the Wood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Heart+of+the+Wood). Gain 3 life, raising total to 9.** No one card that we have can do 10 damage by itself, so we have to defeat the [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan) in stages. First, we get the mana available from [Tundra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tundra), [Apprentice Wizard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Apprentice+Wizard), [Mana Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Vault), and [Black Lotus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Lotus). (We don't tap the [Volcanic Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Island) because we don't need the mana and don't want the mana burn at the end of the game).


**16) Tap [Tundra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tundra) to add two blue mana to the pool. Use one blue mana and tap [Apprentice Wizard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Apprentice+Wizard) to add three colorless mana to the pool. Use one blue and two colorless mana to cast [Psionic Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psionic+Blast) on [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan), doing 4 damage. Suffer 2 damage from [Psionic Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psionic+Blast). Life Total is now 7. One black and one colorless mana remain in the pool.**


**17) Tap [Black Lotus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Lotus) and [Mana Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Vault) to add three blue mana and three colorless mana to the pool. Use all eight mana in the pool (one black, three blue, four colorless) and tap [Banshee](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banshee) to do 4 damage to [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan). Suffer 4 damage from [Banshee](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banshee) as well. Life total is now 3.** Although we could do more than 4 damage with the [Banshee](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banshee), four is all we need. Now, the coup de grace.


**18) Tap [Psionic Entity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psionic+Entity) to do 2 points of damage to [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan). This brings the damage on the [Leviathan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leviathan) to 10, destroying it.**


**19) Clear mana pool. No mana is in the pool and so there is no mana burn. Win with a final life total of 3.**
