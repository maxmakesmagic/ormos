Pauper is a popular **Magic Online** format that allows players to use only commons when building their decks. The result? SlashRus recently used Rats, of all things, to succeed in the format. Both [Chittering Rats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chittering+Rats) and [Crypt Rats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Rats) saw play front and center alongside black's most powerful common removal like [Tendrils of Corruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tendrils+of+Corruption). Add some card drawing in the form of [Mulldrifter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter) and you've got a recipe for success!
