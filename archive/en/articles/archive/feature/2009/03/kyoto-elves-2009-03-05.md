Elves has been one of the most popular archetypes of the past two years, but it was all but unheard of in Standard over the past few months. Gael Bailly-Maitre changed that with a solid Day 2 performance at Pro Tour-Kyoto. His Black-Green build contains some very familiar cards, as well as a few new additions, like [Wall of Reverence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Reverence), that are just gaining popularity. Make sure to [check out all the coverage from the event](/en/events/coverage/nassif-greater-power).
