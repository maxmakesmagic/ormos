Grand Prix Lille 2018
---------------------


Busson Burns Bright in Lille
============================




In an epic final battle between an aggressive red and an aggressive blue deck, red mage Etienne Busson defeated Hall of Famer Gabriel Nassif to become champion of Grand Prix Lille 2018!






Oct 27-28, 2018


Lille Grand Palais



Etienne Busson
