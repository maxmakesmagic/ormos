Like Demons on the plane of Grixis, Thor Nordwick managed to rule the Kamigawa Block PTQ circuit. It didn't hurt that he turned to the dark side for some assistance, playing Demons himself in the form of the mighty [Yukora, the Prisoner](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yukora%2C+the+Prisoner) and [Kagemaro, First to Suffer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kagemaro%2C+First+to+Suffer). You can learn more about Grixis, Demons, and the world of Alara at the *Shards of Alara* [product page](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/shardsofalara).
