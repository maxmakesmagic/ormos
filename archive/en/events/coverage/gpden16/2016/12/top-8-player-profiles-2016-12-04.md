Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 4, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/halliesanto_icon.jpg)
By Hallie Santo











The Top 8 competitors at Grand Prix Denver ranged from first-time Pro Tour qualifiers to seasoned veterans of the game, but each of them had a lot to play for on Sunday night. We sat down with each of the players to get to know them a little better.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpden16/t8-severa.jpg)**


**Name:** Matt Severa  
**Age:** 37  
**Occupation:** software developer  
**Hometown:** Madison, WI  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Grand Prix Top 8 competitor


**What deck did you play, and why?**  

Mardu Vehicles. I didn't have time to learn to how play W/U Flash or B/G Delirium well.


**What was the best card in your deck?**  
[Toolcraft Exemplar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Toolcraft+Exemplar)


**Would you play the deck again after this weekend? What would you change?**  

Yes. I'm not sure about the [Fragmentize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fragmentize) in the board.


**What does making Top 8 mean to you?**  

More pro points so I can get Gold for next year.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpden16/t8-hull.jpg)**


**Name:** Ben Hull  
**Age:** 34  
**Occupation:** research engineer  
**Hometown:** Vancouver, BC  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8, Pro Tour *Kaladesh*


**What deck did you play, and why?**  

Reid Duke's R/W list. I didn't have time to test and I played R/W at the Pro Tour.


**What was the best card in your deck?**  

I have no idea.


**Would you play the deck again after this weekend? What would you change?**  

Maybe. No clue.


**What does making Top 8 mean to you?**  

A few pro points closer to Gold.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpden16/t8-snyder.jpg)**


**Name:** Michael Snyder  
**Age:** 30  
**Occupation:** sales  
**Hometown:** Sacramento, CA  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** None.


**What deck did you play, and why?**  

R/G Aetherworks. It felt great against the best decks, and tested well. Shout out to twitch.tv/jaberwocki for teaching me a ton.


**What was the best card in your deck?**  

Ulamog. It was great in the mirror.


**Would you play the deck again after this weekend? What would you change?**  

Not if [Panharmonicon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Panharmonicon) takes off.


**What does making Top 8 mean to you?**  

It's my first Pro Tour invite and the result of a lot of effort.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpden16/t8-rubin.jpg)**


**Name:** Steve Rubin  
**Age:** 24  
**Occupation:** *Magic* player, ultimate frisbee coach  
**Hometown:** Pittsburgh, PA  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: 6 Grand Prix Top 8's. Pro Tour *Shadows Over Innistrad* Champion.


**What deck did you play, and why?**  

Temur Aetherworks. The R/G version is solid and adding blue helps improve your worst match-ups.


**What was the best card in your deck?**  
[Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel).


**Would you play the deck again after this weekend? What would you change?**  

Sure. At this moment, nothing.


**What does making Top 8 mean to you?**  

I'm always pleased to be playing for a title.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpden16/t8-wolbers.jpg)**


**Name:** Andrew Wolbers  
**Age:** 27  
**Occupation:** field service technician  
**Hometown:** Eden Prairie, MN  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 25th, Grand Prix Dallas-Fort Worth


**What deck did you play, and why?**  

U/R Zombie-Emerge. It felt strong against B/G and other top decks.


**What was the best card in your deck?**  
[Fevered Visions](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fevered+Visions). It gives you free wins against a lot of decks.


**Would you play the deck again after this weekend? What would you change?**  

Yes. Not sure what I'd change yet.


**What does making Top 8 mean to you?**  

It's my first big *Magic* accomplishment and first Pro Tour qualification.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpden16/t8-pisano.jpg)**


**Name:** Rob Pisano  
**Age:** 32  
**Occupation:** software developer  
**Hometown:** Las Vegas, NV  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 32, Pro Tour *Shadows Over Innistrad* Top 32, Pro Tour *Kaladesh* 2nd, Grand Prix Indianapolis 2016


**What deck did you play, and why?**  

W/U Flash, because it's the best.


**What was the best card in your deck?**  
[Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter).


**Would you play the deck again after this weekend? What would you change?**  

Yes, and I'd change nothing. It's perfect.


**What does making Top 8 mean to you?**  

It means I'm slightly closer to my goal of achieving Platinum status this season.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpden16/t8-manfield.jpg)**


**Name:** Seth Manfield  
**Age:** 26  
**Occupation:** content provider for TCGPlayer.com  
**Hometown:** Laurel, MD  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 2015 World Champion, 5 Grand Prix wins, 2 Pro Tour Top 8's


**What deck did you play, and why?**  
[Panharmonicon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Panharmonicon). It's fun!


**What was the best card in your deck?**  
[Panharmonicon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Panharmonicon)


**Would you play the deck again after this weekend? What would you change?**  

I would play the same 75 next weekend.


**What does making Top 8 mean to you?**  

I had a great start, and getting this far is more of a relief than anything.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpden16/t8-nagro.jpg)**


**Name:** Jacob Nagro  
**Age:** 21  
**Occupation:** student  
**Hometown:** Albuquerque, NM  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 50, Pro Tour *Kaladesh*


**What deck did you play, and why?**  

W/U Flash. I like casting [Reflector Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reflector+Mage).


**What was the best card in your deck?**  
[Archangel Avacyn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+Avacyn).


**Would you play the deck again after this weekend? What would you change?**  

Yes. I would play [Westvale Abbey](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Westvale+Abbey) in the main deck.


**What does making Top 8 mean to you?**  

I'm glad to qualify for the Pro Tour again and now I'm getting close to Silver.
