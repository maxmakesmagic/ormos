Shrines of Karn
===============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 17, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












The [Shrine cycle in *New Phyrexia*](http://gatherer.wizards.com/pages/search/default.aspx?action=advanced&name=+%5B%22shrine%22%5D&set=%7C%5B%22new%20phyrexia%22%5D) contains five depictions of Karn. They're not Karn himself; they're statues the Phyrexians made of their adopted "Father of Machines." But each Shrine is different. Let's take a look!
