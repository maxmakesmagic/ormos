The Tokens of Eternal Masters
=============================



 Posted in **Card Preview**
 on May 27, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






There have been some weird tokens created over the years, and with a set like *Eternal Masters*, you're going to catch a lot of strange ones.


Many of the cards that produce these tokens have been previewed during this week. For the rest, check out the [Card Image Gallery](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/eternal-masters), which has been fully updated as of today!


You can collect all of these when *Eternal Masters* releases June 10!
