Fantasy Pro Tour Update
=======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*by Monty Ashley*


The Fantasy Pro Tour for Pro Tour Los Angeles has 1,242 participants, each picking eight-player teams (although 633 slots were left blank).



The most popular player, appearing on 293 teams, is Truc Bui, a bargain at zero Pro Points. Bui is followed by PT Champions Casey McCarrel (285 teams), and Steven O'Mahoney-Schwartz (232 teams) before the zero-point bracket is represented by Dustin Stern, who appears on 196 teams.



The top ten are:



1. Truc Bui (293 teams)
2. Casey McCarrel (285)
3. Steven O'Mahoney-Schwartz (232)
4. Dustin Stern (196)
5. Yoshikazu Ishii (175)
6. Tony Tsai (161)
7. Mike Long (160)
8. Dan Silberman (159)
9. Chad Ellis (146)
10. David Price (135)

The most expensive player, Bob Maher, Jr., cost 83 points. He still looked worth the points to 18 particpants, including himself. Jon Finkel was second at 71 points, and appeared on 100 teams.
