Solar Flare was a popular control/reanimator strategy during the days of *Ravnica* Standard. Now, thanks to [Sphinx of Lost Truths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx+of+Lost+Truths) and [Rise from the Grave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rise+from+the+Grave), the Esper-colored deck is making a return! SorryMsJackson recently took this list to the top of the standings in a Standard event on **Magic Online**!
