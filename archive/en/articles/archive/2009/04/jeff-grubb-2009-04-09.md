Jeff Grubb
==========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 9, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Freelance author and game designer Jeff Grubb has been at the heart of a number of major settings over the years: he is one of the original co-conspirators of **Dragonlance** and the co-founder of the **Forgotten Realms** with Ed Greenwood. Together with his wife Kate Novak, he is the author of *Masquerades, Finder's Bane,* and *Tymora's Luck.* With Ed Greenwood, he wrote *Cormyr: A Novel.* *The Brother's War* was his first foray into the multiverse of **Magic: The Gathering.**
