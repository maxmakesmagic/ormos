Quarterfinals: Kyle Goodman vs. Josh Tabak
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 29, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_509.png)
By Mary Van Tyne











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a721.jpg)Josh is no stranger to the Feature Match tables, but neither player has seen Feature Match tables like this. There are lights and cameras everywhere, and both players comment on the theme music that played before the match. But if they're nervous, it sure isn't showing as the players shuffle their cards.

### Game 1

Kyle led with a [Tranquil Thicket](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tranquil+Thicket), and Josh went down to 19 from his [Karplusan Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karplusan+Forest) for a first-turn [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer). Kyle's second land was a plains, and he allowed Josh to play a [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) as well. He threw down an [Astral Slide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) on his next turn and passes to Josh.

Josh attacked with the [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) and the Lavamancer, discarding a [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) and a [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption) to deal 10 points of damage to Kyle almost immediately. Josh used the madness mechanic in an incredibly skillful manner – he's almost completely unfazed by the lights and the cameras. 

Kyle's only play on his turn is a [Nantuko Monastery](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Monastery). Josh left a green floating after tapping his [Mossfire Valley](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mossfire+Valley) to [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) Kyle, then attacked – but Kyle cycled away [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith) to remove the attacking [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) from the game. Kyle played another [Astral Slide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) on his turn.

Josh attacked again with the Rootwalla and the [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel), but Kyle used the [Astral Slide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) to remove both from the game. Josh said, "I'll [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) you," casually, bringing Kyle to 5, then used his [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer)'s ability to deal more damage to Kyle.

"After the end-of turn trigger goes on the stack, I'll cycle [Slice and Dice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slice+and+Dice)," Kyle said, "and kill your [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer)." Josh replied with a flashed-back [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) on his turn. When Josh attacked again, Kyle cycled a [Secluded Steppe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Secluded+Steppe) to remove the [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) from the game. During the rest of his turn, Josh played a [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) and a Llanowar Elf. In response, Kyle cast a [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith). Josh just shrugged and played another [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel), leaving one card in his hand.

Kyle played a face-down morph, which promptly was forced to block an attacking [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). Josh pumped the [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) with [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd), and Kyle cycled away a [Forgotten Cave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forgotten+Cave) to remove Josh's [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) and his own morph, which turned out to be an [Exalted Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel). Josh flashed back the [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) and passed the turn with one card left in his hand.

Kyle attacked with his [Exalted Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel), then cast a [Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartographer) to return a [Forgotten Cave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forgotten+Cave) to his hand. He then cycled away the Cave, removing [Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartographer) and [Exalted Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel) from the game. And, when the [Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartographer) came back, Kyle returned a [Secluded Steppe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Secluded+Steppe) to his hand. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a723.jpg)Josh attacked with all his creatures – 2 [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel)s, Llanowar Elf, and an Elephant token from [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd). Kyle blocked the Elephant with the Angel and the [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) with the [Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartographer), then cycled away the Steppe and removed the Angel and the [Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartographer) from the game. Kyle's life total fell to 7.

Kyle attacked with the [Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartographer) and the Angel, taking Josh to a perilous 8 life. Kyle then played a [Teroh's Faithful](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teroh%27s+Faithful) to go back up to 15 life, and played and sacrificed a [Wooded Foothills](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wooded+Foothills). With one more mountain on the table, he must have felt much more confident. He cycled away the [Secluded Steppe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Secluded+Steppe) again to remove his creatures, then returned [Forgotten Cave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forgotten+Cave) to his hand at the end of the turn.

Josh drew his card, surveyed his options, and conceded as he realized that things looked pretty hopeless.

Josh Tabak 0 – Kyle Goodman 1

### Game 2

"How come we don't get a camera?" Kyle quipped as he and Josh shuffled for their second game. He was, no doubt, referring to the camera hanging over the other Feature Match area, where Sam Stein and Richard Oleksak were waiting to begin their second game as well.

Josh led this game with a [Mountain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) and a [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer). He attacked on his second turn, but did not follow it up with a second-turn play (such as a [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel)). Kyle, meanwhile, was simply playing more lands – he ended his second turn with a plains, a mountain, and a [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift) in play. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a708.jpg)Josh attacked with the Lavamancer again and then cast a [Volcanic Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer) at Kyle, who went down to 14. Kyle then cycled away a [Slice and Dice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slice+and+Dice) to slay the Lavamancer. Josh kept the offense going, though, with a [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption) at Kyle's head, taking him to 10 life.

"You don't have anything?" Kyle asked as he cycled away [Krosan Tusker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Tusker), dealt two points of damage, got a forest, and drew a card. Somehow, that felt like cheating, even though all plays were perfectly legal. Josh shook his head and kept throwing burn at Kyle's face, following up his previous [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption) with another. 

Kyle tossed off a second [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift) and an [Exalted Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel), but Josh's next play was a [Static Orb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Static+Orb). Kyle's Angel went on the offense, as Josh shook his head again as his poor luck. After playing a considerable amount of land, Kyle eventually cycled away a [Forgotten Cave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forgotten+Cave) and a [Secluded Steppe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Secluded+Steppe) to deal just enough damage to finish Josh off.

**Final Result:** Josh Tabak 0 – Kyle Goodman 2
