Modern Masters Packaging Concerns
=================================



 Posted in **News**
 on May 21, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











We have heard concerns about *Modern Masters 2015 Edition* booster packaging. You can be assured that the new *Modern Masters 2015* packaging is subject to rigorous security, safety, and quality testing. As always, we recommend purchasing your *Magic* products from a trusted source, such as a WPN location.
