Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 29, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











Learn more about Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation* Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they’ve done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!


 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/pthou/t8_sasabe.jpg)Name:** Yusuke Sasabe


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** Tokyo


**Team:** Dora-Kichi (which means “Draft Junky”)


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** GP Shizuoka 2017 Top 8


**Limited Record:** 5-1


**Standard Record:** 7-3


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


1. Black-Green 3-0
2. White-Black 2-1

**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Mono-Black Zombies with Kalitas in the main deck. It's pretty good against Mono Red [Ramunap Red].


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?**


[Kalitas, Traitor of Ghet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kalitas%2C+Traitor+of+Ghet)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


My Dream.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/pthou/t8_kurata.jpg)Name:** Shintaro Kurata


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** London/Tokyo


**Team:** Waseda MTG


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Grand Prix Kobe 11th, BMI 8th


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Standard Record:** 8-1-1


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


1st Green-white-red ([Overcome](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overcome) deck)


2nd Green-White


I liked the second one.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Mono Red (and Black), because of [Collective Brutality](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collective+Brutality) and [Ammit Eternal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ammit+Eternal). They are strong against mono red. Other decks I made couldn’t beat mono red.


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?**


[Ammit Eternal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ammit+Eternal)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


FUN. Let’s go to the PT with me, friend!




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/pthou/t8_yam.jpg)Name:** Yam Wing Chun


**Age:** 25


**Hometown:** Hong Kong


**Team:** No team (but testing with MTG Mint Card and MTG Bent Card Squad)


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** PT Fate Reforged Top 16, 2 GP Top 8


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Standard Record:** 9-1


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


1. White-Black Zombies
2. Blue-Black Aggro

**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Ramunap Red. Jason Chung was winning with it a lot on *Magic Online*. After going to the arcade with him the night before the Pro Tour, I was convinced to play his 75.


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?**


[Hazoret the Fervent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hazoret+the+Fervent). It won me so many matches.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


A dream come true. Also, I would get to play in the World Championship with the squad (Lee Shi Tian, Eduardo Sagjalik, and Kelvin Chew).

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/pthou/t8_manfield.jpg)Name:** Seth Manfield


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** Columbia, Maryland


**Team:** Genesis


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


World Champion 2015, two other Pro Tour Top 8s, five Grand Prix wins


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Standard Record:** 8-2

Red-Green and White-Black. I love Red-Green monsters and removal. I also went 3-0 with that deck (no rares).


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Mono Red [Ramunap Red] – It is the best deck and I like the Genesis list.


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?**


[Hazoret the Fervent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hazoret+the+Fervent) – card is bonkers.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


I play *Magic* every day waiting for an opportunity like this. It would be amazing!

A fulfillment of a decades-long dream.
