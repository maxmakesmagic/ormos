The Pro Tour returns to sun-splashed Valencia for the first time since 2007. Pro Tour *Born of the Gods* features the debut of the new *Born of the Gods* set on the premier-play stage. **Attending Pro Tour *Born of the Gods* as a Spectator**


The tournament hall is open to the public starting Friday morning. Spectators are invited to watch the Pro Tour on projection screens showing the live webcast and matches from along the rail in designated viewing areas. The play area has been set up to allow for nearly every table to be visible to spectators.


**Tournament Hall Hours for Spectators**  

Friday: 8 a.m. - 8 p.m.  

Saturday: 8 a.m. - 8 p.m.  

Sunday: 8:30 a.m. - 7 p.m.


Single-card vendors [Card Haus](http://www.cardhaus.com/) and [Gaming Etc.](http://www.gamingetc.com/) will be on-site all weekend to help you find that special card or collectible.


Sunday features a full slate of festivities that begin at 9 a.m. Key activities are scheduled around the Top 8 playoff schedule, making it so you won't have to miss a minute of the action.


* **Ask Ramp;D Session**: **Magic**  developer Tom LaPille answers your questions and talk about what awaits in the **Magic** Multiverse. To be held Sunday between the last quarterfinal match and the first semifinal match. Due to travel complications, Aaron Forsythe will not be at the Pro Tour as previously announced.
* **Artist Signings**: Get exclusive foil art prints signed by artists [Peter Mohrbacher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoileramp;method=visualamp;action=advancedamp;artist=+%5b%22Peter+Mohrbacher%22%5d) and [Tyler Jacobson](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advancedamp;output=spoileramp;method=visualamp;artist=+%5B%22Tyler%20Jacobson%22%5D). Unfortunately, Ryan Barger will not be in attendance as previously announced.
* **Tokenize Yourself:** Get your face on a token creature from the world of Theros.
* **Meet Top Pros**: Get autographs from World Champion Shahar Shenhar and other top Pros.

**Tournament Length**


* Day One will consist of 3 rounds of Booster Draft followed by 5 rounds of Modern
* Day Two will consist of 3 rounds of Booster Draft followed by 5 rounds of Modern
* 16 Swiss Rounds total, 55 minutes per round
* When time is called, the active player will finish the turn and five additional turns will be taken
* Stalling and slow play rules will be strictly enforced during the tournament.

 


**Advancement**


* All players may participate in all 8 Swiss rounds of Friday's competition.
* Players with 12 or more match points after round 8 will advance to Saturday's competition.
* The Top 8 players after round 16 will advance to Sunday’s single-elimination playoff rounds.

Players must have a signed waiver and consent form to compete at Pro Tour *Born of the Gods*. Forms can be downloaded at [www.wizards.com/protour](/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/protour) and will be available onsite.


**Minors must have a parent or guardian’s signature on these forms. Minors will not be able to register or play unless we receive a waiver signed by a parent or guardian. No exceptions!**


Bring these forms with you to registration. We will make forms available for competitors at registration, if necessary.


To register, all participants must present one of the following forms of identification:


* a driver’s license, or
* a passport, or
* a copy of a birth certificate and photo IDs (school IDs are acceptable).

* Playoff rounds will be Modern format
* Playoff quarterfinal and semifinal matches will be best 2 of 3. The finals match will be best 3 of 5.
* Standings after the Swiss rounds will be used to determine final order in the standings for losing players in the quarterfinal and semifinal playoff rounds.
* While there are no time limits in the playoff rounds, players are expected to play at a reasonable pace and finish their matches within 60 minutes (for quarterfinal and semifinal matches) and 90 minutes (for the finals match).
* For the first game of each match in the playoff rounds, the player that finished higher in the Swiss rounds chooses either to play first or to play second. For subsequent games in each playoff match, the usual Play/Draw rule applies (loser of the previous game decides whether to play first in the next game.)

 


**Notes**


* Planeswalker Points Multiplier 12x (Lifetime Total only), REL: Professional
* Players are required to use the same Modern deck for Day One, Day Two, and the Top 8 playoff
* Players are responsible for knowing all DCI rules and [high-level tournament rules](http://archive.wizards.com/protour/images/HighlevelRules.pdf).
* Players are responsible for bringing their own appropriate counters or tokens.
