A Brief History of Masticores
=============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 1, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











The noble masticore goes back to [Urza's Destiny](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Destiny), when Paolo Parente provided this art for the Famous Original [Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore):

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/571_masticore_ud.jpg)[Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore) | Art by [Paolo Parente](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Paolo+Parente%22%5D)

After a brief stop in the art for [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), the [Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore) got a revamp in *From the Vault: Relics*, when Steven Belledin gave us a leaner version.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/571_masticore_ftv.jpg)[Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore) | Art by [Steven Belledin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Steven+Belledin%22%5D)

The next masticore was *Fifth Dawn*'s [Razormane Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Razormane+Masticore), from the plane of Mirrodin. Jim Murray provided a more sharp-edged example of the species.

![Razormane Masticore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Razormane+Masticore)  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/571_masticore_5d.jpg)[Razormane Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Razormane+Masticore) | Art by [Jim Murray](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Jim+Murray%22%5D)

And that brings us up to *Scars of Mirrodin*, and the ominous [Molten-Tail Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten-Tail+Masticore). Whit Brachna illustrated this mythic monster!

![Molten-Tail Masticore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Molten-Tail+Masticore)  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/571_masticore_som.jpg)[Molten-Tail Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten-Tail+Masticore) | Art by [Whit Brachna](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Whit+Brachna%22%5D)
