Grand Prix Memphis to Proceed as Scheduled
==========================================



 Posted in [ARTICLES](/en/articles)
 on February 20, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










Pastimes, the tournament organizer for Grand Prix Memphis, has confirmed that Grand Prix Memphis will go forward as scheduled.



Currently a winter storm warning for Shelby County, Tennessee, is in effect until 9 p.m. local time (CT) tonight. Weather is expected to improve Saturday and Sunday.



Wizards of the Coast encourages players to monitor the [Grand Prix Memphis](http://www.mtggp.com/updates) website as well [weather forecast updates](http://wreg.com/weather/) for any changes, and to exercise caution and discretion while traveling.



The Grand Prix Memphis main event is Standard Constructed. Registration can be done onsite and [online](http://www.mtggp.com/eventregistration/) until 9 p.m. local time (CT). The main event begins at 9 a.m. tomorrow.
