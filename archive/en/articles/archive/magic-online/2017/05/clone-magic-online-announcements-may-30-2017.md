**Standard MOCS Monthly on Friday, June 2**


A *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) Standard Monthly Event is coming up on June 2! Players can spend 35 Qualifier Points (QPs) to enter. The series will contain 24 Monthly Events, each of which qualify top players for one of the four Playoff Events. These Playoff Events qualify top players for the *Magic Online* Championship, a Pro Tour, and the Pro Tour Challenge event at that Pro Tour.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points for the year. Top players on the Leaderboard at the end of the 2017 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.


We look forward to seeing you on June 2 for the latest Monthly Event! Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online* Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

**Standard PTQ for *Pro Tour Ixalan*on Saturday, June 3**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, June 3!


This will be the last Constructed Finals event to have Preliminaries feeding it – please read tomorrow's article for changes to Constructed PTQ events. The last PTQ Preliminaries will be held on Friday, June 2.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#HOU_schedule).


[Top](#top)

***Amonkhet* Sealed PTQ for *Pro Tour Ixalan*on Sunday, June 4**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! The weekly suite of Preliminaries on Thursday through Tuesday, June 1 through June 6, will take place at their normal times. We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, June 4!


Remember, you are no longer required to enter into the PTQ Finals immediately following the Preliminary that you qualified through, thanks to the redesigned PTQ system that *Magic Online*Events Manager Lee Sharpe announced [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/pro-tour-qualifier-changes-2015-11-16).


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#HOU_schedule).
