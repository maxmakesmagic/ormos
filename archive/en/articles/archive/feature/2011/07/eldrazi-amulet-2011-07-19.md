Players around the world have been curious how ***Magic** 2012* would change Standard as we know it.Now, thanks to Japan Nationals, we have our first taste from a large premier event. One card that caught the eye of deckbuilder Toshiyuki Kadooka? [Quicksilver Amulet](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quicksilver+Amulet). The reprinted artifact served Kadooka well, allowing him to skip paying for his giant creatures and avoid getting them countered at the same time! To read more about Japan Nationals, visit the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/ishida-man-steel-japan-nationals-2011).
