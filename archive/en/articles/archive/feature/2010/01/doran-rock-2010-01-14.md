[Doran, the Siege Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower) was a popular addition to Extended Rock decks when it first saw print. Fast forward to this season's Pro Tour–San Juan qualifiers and it looks like Doran Rock is back! User Calcano took the deck to a first place finish in the opening Pro Tour Qualifier on **Magic Online**. The deck controls the ground with hand kill and removal spells like [Putrefy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Putrefy), then takes over with its powerful namesake or creatures like [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf).
