Bazaar of Moxen Modern Winner
=============================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on May 16, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 





The [Bazaar of Moxen](http://www.bazaar-of-moxen.com/en/bazaar-of-moxen-coverage-bom9,24.html) tournament series, in Annecy, France, recently added Modern to its suite of Eternal offerings, with the main event drawing 307 players. At the end of the day, Serafin Wellinger was the last player standing, piloting a unique green-white deck with a variety of disruptive elements.



![Leonin Arbiter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?size=small&type=card&name=Leonin%20Arbiter&options=)The core of this deck uses [Leonin Arbiter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Arbiter) and [Thalia, Guardian of Thrabe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thalia%2C+Guardian+of+Thrabe) to restrict the mana of opposing decks. [Leonin Arbiter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Arbiter) increases the power of [Path to Exile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) or [Ghost Quarter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghost+Quarter), preventing the opponent from searching up a basic land. However, the deck is more than just mana denial. [Restoration Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Restoration+Angel) and [Flickerwisp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flickerwisp) provide a quick clock, especially when combined with [Blade Splicer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blade+Splicer). To top things off, Serafin's sideboard is filled with some of the most hateful cards in Modern, with nearly something for everyone. Of particular note is [Akroma, Angel of Fury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroma%2C+Angel+of+Fury), who can only be cast in this deck via morph. [Flickerwisp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flickerwisp) and [Restoration Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Restoration+Angel) are needed to unlock the [Angel of Fury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Fury).
