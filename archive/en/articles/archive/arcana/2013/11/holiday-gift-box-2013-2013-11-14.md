Holiday Gift Box 2013
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 14, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







Tomorrow, the new [*Theros* Holiday Gift Box](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/tcg/productarticle.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/holiday2013giftbox/productinfo) finally arrives on shelves! Pick it up and you can get four *Theros* booster packs and a special premium [Karametra's Acolyte](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karametra%27s+Acolyte).


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1364_card.jpg)  
This box is great for holding collections and storing miscellaneous cards from your collection!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1364_box.jpg)  
Note! You do not get the loose cards pictured above. Here is what is inside the box:


* Four *Theros* booster packs
* Beautiful storage box that can hold more than 2,000 **Magic** cards
* Twenty *Theros* basic lands
* Six illustrated plastic dividers
* Sticker sheet for customizing the dividers
* An alternate-art [Karametra's Acolyte](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karametra%27s+Acolyte) from the *Theros* set
