Video Deck Tech: Grand Heart-chitect with Alan Comer
====================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 17, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 






Magic Pro Tour Hall of Famer and noted deck designer Alan Comer shows off his fun creation for the Standard portion that hinges on [Heartless Summoning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heartless+Summoning+)  powering out [Myr Superion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Superion)s. A decklist will be posted at the end of the tournament.
