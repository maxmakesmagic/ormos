Duplicate Limited Master List of Cards
======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*by Sideboard Staff*


*For this year's Duplicate Limited, the players will be using cards that have all had their casting costs altered. Some cost more than they used to; some cost less.*


The Abyss  

1BB  

Enchant World  

At the beginning of each player's upkeep, that player destroys target nonartifact creature he or she controls. It can't be regenerated.  


Aladdin  

2R  

Creature - Aladdin  

1/1  

1RR, T: Gain control of target artifact as long as you control Aladdin.  


Animate Dead  

3B  

Enchantment  

When Animate Dead comes into play, put target creature card from a graveyard into play under your control. Animate Dead becomes an enchant creature enchanting that creature.  

Enchanted creature gets -1/-0.  

When Animate Dead leaves play, destroy enchanted creature. It can't be regenerated.  


Arctic Wolves  

4GG  

Creature - Wolf  

4/5  

Cumulative upkeep 2  

When Arctic Wolves comes into play, draw a card.

1/1  

WW, T: Return target artifact card from your graveyard to your hand.

4R

2/2  

Haste #(This creature may attack and T the turn it comes under your control.)#  

Echo #(At the beginning of your next upkeep after this permanent comes under your control, sacrifice it unless you pay its mana cost.)#  

When Avalanche Riders comes into play, destroy target land.  


Bazaar of Wonders  

U  

Enchant World  

When Bazaar of Wonders comes into play, remove all cards in all graveyards from the game.  

Whenever a spell is played, counter it if a card with the same name is in play or in a graveyard.
