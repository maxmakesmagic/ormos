9-0 Decklists
=============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 5, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











There were three players out of the field of 757 who finished Day 1 without a blemish to their record. To make things even more interesting, none of the trio were playing Aetherworks Marvel!  
  

To summarize, Ryochi Tamada did it with White-Blue Flash, while local heroes Mark Anthony Biala and Matias Monico Palu-Ay achieved perfection with Mono Black Zombies and Black-Green Aggro respectively!
