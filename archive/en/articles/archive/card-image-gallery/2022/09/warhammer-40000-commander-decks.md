Warhammer 40,000 Commander Decks Card Image Gallery
===================================================



 Posted in **Card Image Gallery**
 on September 16, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Check out the latest cards revealed from [*Warhammer 40,000* Commander decks](https://magic.wizards.com/products/warhammer-40000-commander) below. Note that the Collector's Edition versions of *Warhammer 40,000* Commander decks include surge foil versions of cards shown here.


*Warhammer 40,000* Commander decks release October 7 and will be available at [your local game store](https://locator.wizards.com/), [online through Amazon](https://gtly.to/PPb9Kv0es), and everywhere else *Magic* is sold.
