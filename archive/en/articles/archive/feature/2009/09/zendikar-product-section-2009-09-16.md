*Zendikar* Product Section
==========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 16, 2009 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By magicthegathering.com Staff











T*Zendikar* Prerelease on September 26-27 is only a week and a half away, which means that the [Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/zendikar) has updated! There's a new puzzle, a new wallpaper, and a new setting preview: *The Secrets of Zendikar*! You like secrets, don't you? And the Visual Spoiler, an archive of all preview cards that have been officially released. Watch the Visual Spoiler and the Product Section intently, because it's going to be updated frequently as we count down to the *Zendikar* Prerelease. Which is on September 26-27.


[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/features/feature54a_ss.jpg)](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/zendikar)
  
That's pretty much all we can say about it, so why don't you just [go to the *Zendikar* Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/zendikar) now?
