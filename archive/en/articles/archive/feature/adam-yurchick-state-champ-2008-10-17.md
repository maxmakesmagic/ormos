Adam Yurchick has seen Top 8 action at the Grand Prix level and nearly at Pro Tour–Hollywood where he got 9th. Before that rise to glory, however, he cut his teeth as a state champion, earning the title in 2007 in Ohio. His deck of choice was this powerful mono-blue Pickles deck, so named because of the "lock" combo of [Brine Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brine+Elemental) and [Vesuvan Shapeshifter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vesuvan+Shapeshifter). One of his most important sideboard cards? [Jace Beleren](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace+Beleren). The planeswalker quickly became a go-to sideboard addition in control mirrors, and Adam was one of the earliest adopters of the powerful card.
