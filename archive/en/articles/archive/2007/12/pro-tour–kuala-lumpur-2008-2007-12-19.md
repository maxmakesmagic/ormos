Pro Tour–Kuala Lumpur 2008
==========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 19, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












*Updated February 6, 2008*


Invitations to Pro Tour-Kuala Lumpur are awarded in the following manner:


 


* The 2007 World Champion.
* The 2007 Pro Player of the Year.
* The Top 50 players from Pro Tour-Valencia.
* The Top 50 players from the 2007 World Championships.
* The Top 4 Teams from the 2007 World Championships.
* Players with 2008 Pro Tour Players Club level 6 or higher.
* Players with 2008 Pro Tour Players Club level 4 & 5\* (invitation for level 4 & 5 players are not awarded until February 14, 2008).
* Players with 2008 Pro Tour Players Club level 3\* are invited to 1 Pro Tour of their choice in the 2008 season (invitation for level 4 & 5 players are not awarded until February 14, 2008).
* The Top 16 finishers of each Grand Prix tournament that feeds Pro Tour-Kuala Lumpur.
* Players who earn invitations through Pro Tour Qualifier tournaments that feed Pro Tour-Kuala Lumpur.
* The Top 100 DCI-ranked players in the Limited format on January 2, 2008.

\* Players with Pro Tour Players Club levels 3, 4 and 5 are allowed to play in Pro Tour qualifiers if their *only* invitation to the Pro Tour is due to their level in the Players Club (see below)


If you believe that your rating or finish at a Pro Tour Qualifier or Grand Prix is in error, please Visit [webapp.wizards.com/login.aspx?target=personal](https://webapp.wizards.com/login.aspx?target=personal). After logging in, click the "Appeals" link and fill out the form.
