Warhammer 40,000 Commander Decklists
====================================



 Posted in **News**
 on September 13, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











[*Warhammer 40,000* Commander decks](https://magic.wizards.com/products/warhammer-40000-commander), an exciting new *Universes Beyond* collaboration for fans of *Magic* and *Warhammer* alike, hit stores worldwide on October 7, and we have four new Commander decks to show off, filled with powerful legends and all-new cards.

You can check out the cards from the *Warhammer 40,000* Commander decks in the [Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/warhammer-40000-commander-decks). Note that the Collector's Edition versions of *Warhammer 40,000* Commander decks are English-only and include surge foil versions of cards and tokens.


![Surge foil Abbadon the Despoiler](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/7YwyDbwDrr.jpg)


Look for the *Warhammer 40,000* Commander decks at [your local game store](https://locator.wizards.com/), [online through Amazon](https://gtly.to/PPb9Kv0es), and everywhere else you buy your *Magic: The Gathering* products.


***(Editor's Note: The decklists below pull the newest printing of each card automatically from*** [***Gatherer***](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Default.aspx)***, including printings which are not in this product. Decklists are not card-for-card product displays but rather interactive lists of the cards included in each deck. Additionally, card images for new* Warhammer 40,000 *Commander deck cards will appear in decklists at a later time.)***
