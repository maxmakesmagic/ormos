Top 8 Players
=============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 27, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_meghanwolff_0.jpg)
By Meghan Wolff




 Meghan is one half of the Good Luck High Five podcast and an adjunct professor at Tolarian Community College. She loves Limited, likes Modern, and dips her toes into each Standard season. She's decidedly blue and is the #1 hater of Siege Rhino in the Multiverse. 






After sixteen rounds, eight players remain in the running for the glory, fortune, and prestige of winning Mythic Championship II! Learn more about them below.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/2019MC2/Top8-Hayne.jpg)


**Name:** Alexander Hayne


**Age:** 30


**Hometown:** Montreal, Quebec, Canada


**Occupation:** MPL Pro


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation:** Can you say Dimir if you are Dimir? Izzet.


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* Champion


4 Grand Prix wins


**Limited Record:** 5-1


**Modern Record:** 7-2-1


**Why did you choose your Modern deck for this weekend?**


I didn't play a game of Modern in the last year and Tron seemed easy to play.


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?**


I survived until now.


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


Hoisting up Steve Rubin when he won Pro Tour *Shadows Over Innistrad*.


**If you were a Planeswalker on Ravnica battling Nicol Bolas, what ability would he steal from you?** 


+2: Reveal the top 2 cards of your library. Place those cards face down in any order. You opponent guesses which one is which. If they guess wrong, put them into your hand. Otherwise put them into your graveyard and you lose two life.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/2019MC2/Top8-Nguyen.jpg)


**Name:** Thien Nguyen


**Age:** 28


**Hometown:** Boston, Massachusetts


**Occupation:** QA Engineer at CrunchTime!


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation:** **GRUUL**


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


2 Grand Prix Top 8s. 4-0 FNM with [Curse of Exhaustion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Curse+of+Exhaustion) + [Knowledge Pool](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knowledge+Pool) combo


**Limited Record:** 6-0


**Modern Record:** 8-1-1


**Why did you choose your Modern deck for this weekend?**


I’ve played it for 5 years and I know all the matchups by heart.


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?**


Deciding to get therapy. I am blessed to have a professional assist me in processing the things in my life.


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


Top decking [Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan) against Willy Edel in Round 2 of Grand Prix Pittsburgh 2015 when he had lethal on board.


**If you were a Planeswalker on Ravnica battling Nicol Bolas, what ability would he steal from you?** 


Magic Ability - playing a land for 2 mana (+1 loyalty)


Real Life Ability - The ability to never quit and try even when it’s hard or impossible.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/2019MC2/Top8-Zhu.jpg)


**Name:** Adrian Zhu


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** Vancouver, Canada


**Occupation:** Software Engineer


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation:** Selesnya


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


I once 3-0’d an FNM.


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Modern Record:** 8-1-1


**Why did you choose your Modern deck for this weekend?**


Honestly, I’m not familiar with the format and wanted something that wouldn’t fry my brain over two full days of Magic.


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?**


My career and, as cheesy as it sounds, the friends I’ve made.


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


This! It still feels surreal.


**If you were a Planeswalker on Ravnica battling Nicol Bolas, what ability would he steal from you?** 


+7: You win the next die roll.
