Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 29, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











Learn more about Grand Prix Phoenix's Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they've done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!


 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppho17/t8_ruan.jpg)Name:** Kai Ruan


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** Phoenix, Arizona


**Occupation:** Software Consultant


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


I made [Resounding Scream](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Resounding+Scream) a staple in Standard Singleton.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?**


Red-[Greed](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Greed), 8-1.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck and why?**


Otpec Huntmaster; it enabled races in otherwise unwinnable games.


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?**


Black-Green stuff, 2-1.


White-Black better stuff, 3-0.


**What was the best card in either of your Draft decks and why?**


Fathom Fleet Caption; won a game almost singlehandedly.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


This GP was my Prerelease. Donald Duck carried me the whole event.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


Time to learn a little Spanish.

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppho17/t8_dean.jpg)Name:** Paul Dean


**Age:** 28


**Hometown:** Toronto, Ontario


**Occupation:** Accountant


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Got some nice Collector’s Edition Platinum tokens with my face on them. They’re out of print now though. Mythic Rare.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?**


Red-White Dinosaurs, 8-1.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck and why?**


[Territorial Hammerskull](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Territorial+Hammerskull) + Dino [Stampede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stampede) combo.


Hammerskull makes it hard for them to stabilize, then [Stampede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stampede) makes sure you win the race.


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?**


Red-Green Dinos, 3-0.


Blue-Black Pirates, 3-0.


**What was the best card in either of your Draft decks and why?**


RG deck had 3 [Otepec Huntmaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Otepec+Huntmaster) and 2 [Drover of the Mighty](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drover+of+the+Mighty). Bonnie + Clyde.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


My life is dope + I do dope things.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


I’ve always been pretty bad at Limited, but recently joined Team Genesis + Revelation and tried very hard to learn and improve. So it is gratifying to see that effort materialize.

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppho17/t8_nagro.jpg)Name:**Jacob Nagro


**Age:** 22


**Hometown:** Albuquerque, New Mexico


**Occupation:** Student


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Top 8 Grand Prix Denver 2016, Silver Pro 2016–17 Season


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?**


I played Black-Green splashing both red and white, 9-0.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck and why?**


[Vona, Butcher of Magan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vona%2C+Butcher+of+Magan). While I never got to take over a game with Vona, it required my opponents to 3 for 1 themselves on two occasions.


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?**


I drafted White-Black Vampires in both drafts and went 3-0, and 1-1-1.


**What was the best card in either of your Draft decks and why?**


[Adanto Vanguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Adanto+Vanguard) in my second draft deck. If you assert yourself as the beatdown it’s very hard to beat.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


I really love *Magic* and I’m glad I’m continuing to prove myself as a player who deserves to play PTs.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


I currently wasn’t qualified for PT RIX and my goal is to play every Pro Tour, so now I’m off to a good start!
