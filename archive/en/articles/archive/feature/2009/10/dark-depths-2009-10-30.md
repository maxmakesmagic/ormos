Upon the release of *Zendikar*, players were abuzz with the possibilities of combining [Dark Depths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Depths) with [Vampire Hexmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampire+Hexmage). For just two mana and a land drop, you could make a 20/20, flying, indestructible Marit Lage token. Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa demonstrated the deck is for real by riding it to the Top 8 at Pro Tour–Austin. Read more from Pro Tour–Austin in the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/kibler-completes-comeback-austin-victory).
