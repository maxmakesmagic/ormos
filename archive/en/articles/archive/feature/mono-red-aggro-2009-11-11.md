While the red-white Boros deck has received the most attention as new aggro deck *du jour*, Almerick Li of Australia proved on **Magic** Game Day 2009 that good ole' [Mountain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) can still get the job done. A bevy of burn spells like [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) and [Burst Lightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning) were backed up by aggressive creatures in the form of [Goblin Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) and [Hellspark Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hellspark+Elemental). The strategy just goes to show while new sets come and go, sometimes you just can't beat a good burn spell.
