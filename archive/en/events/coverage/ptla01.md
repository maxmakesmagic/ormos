2001 Pro Tour Los Angeles
-------------------------


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pt_lg.gif)![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/PTLA00/images/QueenMary.jpg)Mike Pustilnik has won Pro Tour Los Angeles, defeating Kamiel Cornelissen, 3-2 in the finals.
