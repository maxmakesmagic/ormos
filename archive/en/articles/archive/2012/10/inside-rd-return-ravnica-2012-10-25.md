Inside R&D: *Return to Ravnica*
===============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 25, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












If you haven't been watching the Pro Tour coverage, you might have been missing out on a pretty cool feature. The **Inside R&D** videos let members of **Magic**'s R&D department share behind-the-scenes stories in a convenient-to-watch video format.


There are seven videos so far, but we'll start with the most recent one, about the creation of *Return to Ravnica*. Then, once you're hooked, you can browse through the rest of the playlist. Enjoy!
