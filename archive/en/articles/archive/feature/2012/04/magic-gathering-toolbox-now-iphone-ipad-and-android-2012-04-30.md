The **Magic: The Gathering** Toolbox Now on iPhone, iPad, and Android
=====================================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 30, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











With the *Avacyn Restored* release happening at your local game store in a few short days, now is the perfect time to download the **Magic: The Gathering** Toolbox! A no-cost download that is now available for iPhone, iPad, and Android, the **Magic: The Gathering** Toolbox includes a life counter, a deck builder, an event finder, and more. Any players who download the app for the first time will have access to the *Avacyn Restored* set included with their download. 

### iPad Screenshot

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/features/feature192b_ipad2.jpg)  
Don't worry, if you have the original iPhone app, just update it and *Avacyn Restored* will be added to the deck builder and card database at no cost! You can find more information and screenshots [here](http://archive.wizards.com/%20http://staging.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/feature/192b&date=4/30/2012), or check out the  [original announcement](/en/articles/archive/magic-gathering-toolbox-2012-01-05) for even more screenshots. 

Now go download the app and head to your local game store for the *Avacyn Restored* release!
