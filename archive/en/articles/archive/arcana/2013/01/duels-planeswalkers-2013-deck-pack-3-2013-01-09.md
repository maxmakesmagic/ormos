Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013 Deck Pack 3
===========================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 9, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











There is another Deck Pack for [**Duels of the Planeswalkers** 2013](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/digital/duelsoftheplaneswalkers.aspx)! Pack One had [Boros and Simic](/en/articles/archive/duels-2013s-deck-pack-2012-11-07), and Pack Two had [Orzhov and Gruul](/en/articles/archive/duels-planeswalkers-2013-deck-pack-2-2012-12-05). The new Deck Pack has Dimir and a five-color deck. And the five-color deck has [Progenitus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Progenitus)!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1143_deckpack3_yk4yxy9hzu.jpg)  
### Rogues' Gallery

Manipulate and infiltrate with the blue and black "Rogues’ Gallery" deck. Disrupt your enemies’ plans with spells, then summon stealthy mages and shadowy undead to deliver the killing blows.
