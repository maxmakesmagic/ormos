Looking for a cheap introduction to the world of [Magic Online](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=Magic/Digital/MagicOnline.aspx)'s Classic format? Try Eddie112's take on burn. It was good enough for a 3-1 record in a recent event, and it's easy to assemble and simple to play! The deck proves that time-honored adage: "Good old burn. Nothing beats burn."
