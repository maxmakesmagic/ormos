Luis Scott-Vargas is one of the game's very best players, and he recently earned a spot in the Finals of the StarCityGames.com Standard Open with a green-blue Turboland deck that has begun seeing increased success in Standard. How does the deck work? You play out lots of extra lands with [Oracle of Mul Daya](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle+of+Mul+Daya), [Explore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Explore), and [Rampant Growth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth). Those lands let you abuse [Avenger of Zendikar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avenger+of+Zendikar), and [Time Warp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp) lets you take extra turns to do that! Fun.
