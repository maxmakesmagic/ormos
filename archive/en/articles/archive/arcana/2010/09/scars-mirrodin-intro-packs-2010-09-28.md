### Myr of Mirrodin

![Myr of Mirrodin](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/546_1.jpg)The "Myr of Mirrodin" deck combines Myr artifact creatures with powerful white cards. Your goal? Overpowering your opponent with an [Inspired Charge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inspired+Charge) to give each of your creatures +2/+1. You'll have lots of help maximizing the number of creatures you have on the battlefield. With [Myrsmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myrsmith) on the battlefield, you'll get a 1/1 Myr artifact creature every time you cast an artifact!

[Myr Battlesphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Battlesphere) creates an army of creatures in one card, and plays right into your plan with [Inspired Charge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inspired+Charge). Of course, at seven mana, it's a bit pricey to cast, but [Palladium Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Palladium+Myr) and [Gold Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gold+Myr) can provide some of that mana. Be careful when casting [Sunblast Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunblast+Angel), thoughâ€”you don't want to destroy too many of your own creatures! But if you have a [Razor Hippogriff](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Razor+Hippogriff) in hand, don't be afraid to destroy your own artifact creature, then bring it back and gain some life.

The *Scars of Mirrodin* set contains even more resources for this Myr deck. Take to the skies with [Auriok Sunchaser](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Auriok+Sunchaser) and some help from your artifacts. Return Myr from your graveyard with [Myr Reservoir](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Reservoir), which also helps you cast them and activate their abilities. [Tempered Steel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tempered+Steel) turbocharges your artifact creatures with a boost in both power and toughness. And when you protect your team with the powerful [Indomitable Archangel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Indomitable+Archangel), your opponents will be powerless to stop you!

### Metalcraft

![Metalcraft](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/546_2.jpg)With the "Metalcraft" deck, you'll be taking advantage of a powerful new ability from the *Scars of Mirrodin* set. When you accumulate three artifacts on the battlefield, your creatures and spells with metalcraft will become even more powerful. [Galvanic Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Galvanic+Blast) doubles in damage from 2 to 4, all for a single red mana! Need help fi nding artifacts? [Trinket Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinket+Mage) does the work for you, searching for cards like [Memnite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite) when it enters the battlefield.

Once you have metalcraft, use [Lumengrid Drake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lumengrid+Drake) to bounce an artifact creature back to your hand, then recast it to charge up [Golem Foundry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golem+Foundry) and gain a life from [Golem's Heart](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golem%27s+Heart). Or bounce your [Trinket Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinket+Mage) to search up another artifact! Don't forget to save an artifact or two to cast after [Embersmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embersmith) and [Riddlesmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Riddlesmith) hit the battlefield. They'll deal damage and let you draw more cards, probably more artifacts to keep the fun going.

Even more power awaits your deck in *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs. Call on [Trigon of Thought](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trigon+of+Thought) to draw extra cards each turn. Turn to the [Grand Architect](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grand+Architect) to pump all of your blue creatures, and to give your artifact creatures the same boost by making them blue. [Kuldotha Phoenix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kuldotha+Phoenix) is a 4/4 flier that just won't stay dead if you have metalcraft!

### Deadspread

![Deadspread](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/546_3.jpg)With the "Deadspread" deck, you'll reap a lot of rewards for a little bit of work. Your aim is to control your opponent's creatures by giving them -1/-1 counters. Then you'll *proliferate*, a new ability that allows you to add counters to permanents and players that have them. A single -1/-1 counter will soon become a real nightmare for careless opponents as you use [Fume Spitter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fume+Spitter) and proliferate to spread death. Or use the 6/6 [Carnifex Demon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carnifex+Demon) to spread counters while flying in for some massive damage.

Proliferate affects other types of counters, too. Use it to refill [Necrogen Censer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrogen+Censer), which can remove charge counters to make a player lose 2 life. Proliferate with a [Culling Dais](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Culling+Dais), after a creature has been sacrifi ced to it, to draw multiple cards. Have an artifact creature that's going to die from blocking or an opponent's removal spell? Sacrifi ce it to [Throne of Geth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Throne+of+Geth) to proliferate and get one last use out of your cards!

A host of powerful *Scars of Mirrodin* spells are waiting to take you to even greater heights. [Inexorable Tide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inexorable+Tide) allows you to proliferate every time you cast a spell. [Grasp of Darkness](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grasp+of+Darkness) will help you take out an opponent's largest threat at instant speed. Or call on the dark powers of [Geth, Lord of the Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geth%2C+Lord+of+the+Vault) to return the most powerful of your opponent's artifacts and creatures after you destroy them!

### Relic Breaker

![Relic Breaker](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/546_4.jpg)With the "Relic Breaker" deck, you'll punish your opponents who rely on the many artifact goodies in the *Scars of Mirrodin* set. The name of the game is beating your opponents down while blowing up virtually every card they play. Trouble from a creature that's picked up a pesky Equipment? [Turn to Slag](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turn+to+Slag) will deal 5 damage to the creature and blow up the Equipment all at once. [Hoard- Smelter Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hoard-+Smelter+Dragon) will eat your opponent's artifacts and attack much stronger for doing it.

To maximize your advantage, hold your artifact removal until you've cast [Viridian Revel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Revel). You'll destroy your opponent's most prized possessions and draw a card each time you do so. Use [Iron Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iron+Myr) and [Copper Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr) to cast [Barrage Ogre](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barrage+Ogre) ahead of schedule, then sacrifice the Myr to throw them at a player or creature for 2 damage. And if you wait until you have [Molder Beast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molder+Beast) on the battlefield, you'll give the trampler +2/+0 each time!

More powerful cards are in store for you in *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs. Looking for a powerful monster to add to your team of attackers? [Alpha Tyrranax](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Alpha+Tyrranax) tips the scales at a whopping 6/5. [Slice in Twain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slice+in+Twain) destroys an artifact and lets you draw a card all by itself. [Spikeshot Elder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spikeshot+Elder)'s damage is limited only by the amount of mana you have. [Liege of the Tangle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liege+of+the+Tangle)'s massive hulk tramples through your opponent's tough defenses and transform your lands into 8/8 attackers themselves.

### Phyrexian Poison

![Phyrexian Poison](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/546_5.jpg)The "Phyrexian Poison" deck returns to an age-old victory path for defeating your opponents: poison! Infect your opponent with ten poison counters and you win the game. [Putrefax](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Putrefax) can reach half that goal with one swing, while your [Ichor Rats](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ichor+Rats) and [Necropede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necropede)s can make steady progress in the early game. Use [Corpse Cur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corpse+Cur) to return your fallen creatures from the graveyard (like another [Corpse Cur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corpse+Cur)) to ensure you always have a creature on the battlefield. Worried about what to do if your opponent manages to stop you from attacking? Equip your creatures with [Heavy Arbalest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heavy+Arbalest) to poison your opponent directly, avoiding the combat phase altogether. Your reinforcements are immediately deadly with [Hand of the Praetors](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hand+of+the+Praetors), which gives your opponent a poison counter whenever you cast a creature with infect!

Find more cards to improve your deck in *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs.
