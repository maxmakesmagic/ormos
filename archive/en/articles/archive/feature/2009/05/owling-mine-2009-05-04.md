Last week on Daily Deck's we presented a Howling Mine-based [Sanity Grindin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sanity+Grindin)g that paid homage to a number of [Howling Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) decks to have come before it. One such deck? The famous Owling Mine from Pro Tour-Honolulu! This version, played by Tiago Chan, made it all the way to the Top 8 before succumbing to the deck's worst matchup: beatdown. [Kami of the Crescent Moon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kami+of+the+Crescent+Moon), [Howling Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine), and [Mikokoro, Center of the Sea](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Mikokoro%252C%2BCenter%2Bof%2Bthe%2BSea) made sure both players' hands were stocked with cards, while [Ebony Owl Netsuke](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ebony+Owl+Netsuke) went to town on the opponent's life total. The Pro Tour will return to Honolulu at the beginning of June with the second stop on the 2009 season. Stay tuned to **magicthegathering.com** for all the details!
