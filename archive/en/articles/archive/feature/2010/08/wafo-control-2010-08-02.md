Hall of Fame eligible Guillaume Wafo-Tapa made the Top 8 of the France National Championship with a new take on White-Blue Control starring [Sun Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sun+Titan). The deck put *three* total copies into the Top 8 and even won the tournament! For more on France's National Championship, read the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/parez-prevails-france-nationals) (much of which is in French).
