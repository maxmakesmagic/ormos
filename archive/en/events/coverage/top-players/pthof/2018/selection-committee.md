Selection Committee
-------------------


Listed below are the members of the 2018 Hall of Fame Selection Committee. Each was chosen for their place in the history of the game. For the 2018 committee, players with 150 or more lifetime Pro Points as of the conclusion of Pro Tour *Dominaria*are assigned to that category unless they are also a current Hall of Fame member.
