The God of Storms
=================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 10, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 







Purphoros demands devotion to red. As it turns out, so do many of the other gods on Theros. [Keranos, God of Storms](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Keranos%2C+God+of+Storms) also takes advantage of devotion to red, giving a red devotion deck a heavy incentive to find as many red mana symbols on permanents as possible. 

![Keranos, God of Storms](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Keranos%2C+God+of+Storms) This deck accommodates the need for red mana symbols with many different color-intense cards. [Ash Zealot](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ash+Zealot), [Burning-Tree Emissary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning-Tree+Emissary), [Frostburn Weird](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frostburn+Weird), and [Hammer of Purphoros](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hammer+of+Purphoros) provide a good base of permanents from which to draw devotion from. If this sounds similar to the red devotion decks that have been around since [Pro Tour *Theros*](/en/events/coverage/dezani-proclaims-allez-les-bleus-pro-tour), that because it is! 

 The new twist on the deck comes from the blue splash, primarily enabled by [Keranos, God of Storms](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Keranos%2C+God+of+Storms). The other blue cards come in the form of support spells such as [Turn & Burn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turn+%26+Burn) and [Cyclonic Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cyclonic+Rift), which function as the creature removal in this deck. One truly exciting addition is [Ral Zarek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ral+Zarek), who conveniently has the ability to untap [Nykthos, Shrine to Nyx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nykthos%2C+Shrine+to+Nyx). As [Voyaging Satyr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyaging+Satyr) can tell you, that is quite a bit of mana!
