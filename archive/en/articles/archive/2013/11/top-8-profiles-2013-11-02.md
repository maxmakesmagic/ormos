Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in [ARTICLES](/en/articles)
 on November 2, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author_pic_nate_price.jpg)
By Nate Price




 A longtime member of the Pro Tour and Grand Prix coverage staff, Nate Price now works making beautiful words for all of you lovely people as the community manager for organized play. When not covering events, he lords over the @MagicProTour Twitter account, ruling with an iron fist. 






  


  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/eternal13/t8lax.jpg)
### Ari Lax


**Age:** 23  
**Hometown:** Boston


**Profession:**  

Electrical Engineeer


**Previous **Magic** Accomplishments:**   

2 PT Top 16s, 6 GP Top 8s, demonstrated ability to count to ten.


**What deck are you playing in the Legacy Championships and why?**  

Death and Taxes. It seemed reasonable, so I had to test it for Grand Prix DC.


**How did you prepare for this event?**  

I borrowed a deck and played nine rounds with it today.


**What is your favorite thing about Legacy?**  

Cheap spells = intricate sequencing decisions.


**What Legacy card would you most like to own a full-size, alternate art version of?**  
[Lion's Eye Diamond](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lion%27s+Eye+Diamond)


**What card has been the most impressive for you this weekend?**  
[Thalia, Guardian of Thraben](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thalia%2C+Guardian+of+Thraben). It's good against creatures and noncreatures.




  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/eternal13/t8lebedowicz%20.jpg)

**Age:** 33  
**Hometown:** New Brunswick, NJ


**Profession:**  

Marketing Research


**Previous **Magic** Accomplishments:**   

Last place 2004 **Magic** Invitational


**What deck are you playing in the Legacy Championships and why?**  

UR [Delver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=+226749). Chris Pikula suggested a RUG shell that could support [True-Name Nemesis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=True-Name+Nemesis).


**How did you prepare for this event?**  

Listened to Chris Pikula and was insulted via email by Eric Berger.


**What is your favorite thing about Legacy?**  

There's a lot of decisions to make and little room for error.


**What Legacy card would you most like to own a full-size, alternate art version of?**  
[Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide)


**What card has been the most impressive for you this weekend?**  
[True-Name Nemesis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=True-Name+Nemesis)!




  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/eternal13/t8grenbaum.jpg)
### Micah Greenbaum


**Age:** 33  
**Hometown:** Duxbury, MA


**Profession:**  

Cleaning Solution Magnate


**Previous **Magic** Accomplishments:**   

Top 8 SCH Open, SCG Baltimore Legacy Champ, Day 2'd a Grand Prix


**What deck are you playing in the Legacy Championships and why?**  

Tiny Pickles aka Death and Taxes. It has a good [Show and Tell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell) matchup, I'm comfortable with it, and it has no [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm).


**How did you prepare for this event?**  

Played SCG Indy Open last weekend.


**What is your favorite thing about Legacy?**  

Almost any deck is good, and I've had success.


**What Legacy card would you most like to own a full-size, alternate art version of?**  
[Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam) or [Mother of Runes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mother+of+Runes).


**What card has been the most impressive for you this weekend?**  
[Thalia, Guardian of Thraben](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thalia%2C+Guardian+of+Thraben). She's done tons of work in conjunction with [Wasteland](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland) and [Rishadan Port](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishadan+Port).

### Paul Lynch


**Age:** 30  
**Hometown:** Baltimore, MD


**Profession:**  

Drafting Manager


**Previous **Magic** Accomplishments:**   

SCG Baltimore Open champ, 18th GP Providence 2011, multiple SCG Open Top 8s


**What deck are you playing in the Legacy Championships and why?**  

UWR Miracles. I have lots of experience and I think it's well positioned right now.


**How did you prepare for this event?**  

Testing at Brewpoint Games with the Baltimore Legacy crew.


**What is your favorite thing about Legacy?**  

The diversity of the format and the complexity of the decision-making skills needed.


**What Legacy card would you most like to own a full-size, alternate art version of?**  
[Meddling Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage). It's my fav!


**What card has been the most impressive for you this weekend?**  
[Vedalken Shackles](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vedalken+Shackles) in the maindeck, and [Pyroclasm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm) in the board.




  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/eternal13/t8jarman.jpg)
### Brad Jarman


**Age:** 30  
**Hometown:** Newark, DE


**Profession:**  

Credit Analyst


**Previous **Magic** Accomplishments:**   

Top 32 GP Atlantic City, several PTQ Top 8s, undisputed pauper EDH World Champion


**What deck are you playing in the Legacy Championships and why?**  

RUG [Delver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=+226749). All I want to do in Legacy is counter broken spells and apply constant pressure. RUG [Delver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=+226749) lets me do both.


**How did you prepare for this event?**  

Playing in local events and talking theory with my teammates Tommy Evaristo and Andrew Flemming.


**What is your favorite thing about Legacy?**  

There's a large variety of decks and you can play with old cards.


**What Legacy card would you most like to own a full-size, alternate art version of?**  
[Stangg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stangg)


**What card has been the most impressive for you this weekend?**  
[Rough & Tumble](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rough+%2F%2F+Tumble) from the board
