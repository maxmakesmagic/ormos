The Doctor is In
================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 21, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson












As some of you may know, I'm a pretty big fan of [Nivmagus Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nivmagus+Elemental). While there is no storm equivalent in Standard (thankfully), there is the cipher mechanic, which allows you to grow your [Nivmagus Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nivmagus+Elemental) over time. Additionally, the cipher spells that target work pretty well with the heroic mechanic as well! 


![Nivmagus Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Nivmagus+Elemental)
 All the one-drop heroes and [Nivmagus Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nivmagus+Elemental) are auto-inclusions in a deck, but we need some more creatures. [Judge's Familiar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Judge%27s+Familiar), while not super powerful, is a great candidate to encode onto. [Akroan Skyguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Skyguard) is in a similar position, but actually has heroic! 


 The rest of the deck is cheap spells like [Gods Willing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gods+Willing) to protect your threats and [Retraction Helix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Retraction+Helix) to clear the way. [Springleaf Drum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Springleaf+Drum) allows you to function with such a low land count, especially with [Akroan Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Crusader) providing some help. 


 All in all, this deck probably isn't going to break any formats, but it's capable of ridiculous draws with [Hidden Strings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hidden+Strings) and/or [Bioshift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bioshift), so it shouldn't be underestimated.
