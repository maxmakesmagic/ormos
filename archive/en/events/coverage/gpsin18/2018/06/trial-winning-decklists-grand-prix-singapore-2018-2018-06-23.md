Trial Winning Decklists of Grand Prix Singapore 2018
====================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 23, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











Want to earn two byes as these 15 players did?


Next time a Grand Prix rolls around, head down on Friday and participate in the Last Chance Trials! If you win all your matches, you'll earn the Sleep-In Special as well as advanced standings in the main event!


While there were concerns that [Goblin Chainwhirler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Chainwhirler) decks and [Teferi, Hero of Dominaria](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Hero+of+Dominaria) decks might dominate, it seemed like players have adapted to survive!


Without further ado, let's take a look at all 15 winning decklists, featuring a wide range of archetypes, including Mardu Vehicles, White-Green Midrange, Blue-Black Midrange, White-Black Knights, Mono-Red Aggro, Black-Red Midrange, White-Blue Approach, and more!
