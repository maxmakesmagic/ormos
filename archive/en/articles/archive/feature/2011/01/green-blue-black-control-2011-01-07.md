Looking for something fun to try in the world of Standard? Why not give Green-Blue-Black Control a chance? Lennyo did during a Standard queue on [**Magic Online**](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/MagicOnline.aspx) and managed to go 4-0 as a result. The deck solves the debate over which is better, [Frost Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Frost%2BTitan) or [Grave Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grave+Titan), by simply playing both! [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) and [Oracle of Mul Daya](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle+of+Mul+Daya) ensure a steady stream of card advantage while [Doom Blade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doom+Blade) and [Spreading Seas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spreading+Seas) help make sure the opponent is kept off balance.
