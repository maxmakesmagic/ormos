Buenos Aires Grinders
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 27, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/080214_1291.jpg)
By Jacob Van Lunen




Jacob Van Lunen began playing Magic in 1995. He has participated in organized play at every level of competition and was a member of the winning team at Pro Tour San Diego in 2007, thanks to an innovative draft strategy. As a writer, Van Lunen has had more than three hundred Magic strategy pieces published 






Yesterday, hundreds of Magic players came to the Grand Prix site to play side events and mingle with their people. Some of those traveling to Grand Prix Buenos Aires still needed to earn valuable byes that could greatly improve their chances in the main event. There were sixteen Grinder events that each awarded two byes; Of those, two were Sealed Deck events (Won by Juliano G Souza and Loris Ziggiotto) while fourteen were Standard Constructed. Here are the undefeated lists from this weekend's Standard grinder events!
