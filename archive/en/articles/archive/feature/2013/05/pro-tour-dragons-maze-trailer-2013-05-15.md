Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze* Trailer
================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 15, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze* is this weekend in San Diego, California. Hundreds of the world's greatest **Magic** players will come together to battle for prizes and to hoist the champion trophy on Sunday.


Nate Holt and Shawn Kornhauser of [Walking the Planes](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL3rP64NRtmbg0g-A_fwwpfaTpkmmwcwYl) put together this short trailer to get you excited for what's coming up. And if you missed it, read [Monday's preview](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/feature/247) to this weekend's tournament by Rich Hagon.
