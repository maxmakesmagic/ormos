[Progenitus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Progenitus). Every tournament player's worse nightmare, nigh unkillable. Emale has taken the 10/10 to a whole new level with this **Magic Online** Classic deck. [Proteus Staff](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Proteus+Staff) and [Polymorph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polymorph) serve to sneak the gigantic monster into play. [Swords to Plowshares](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swords+to+Plowshares), [Daze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze), [Counterbalance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterbalance), and an assortment of removal and counters keep the board clear long enough to pull the feat off!
