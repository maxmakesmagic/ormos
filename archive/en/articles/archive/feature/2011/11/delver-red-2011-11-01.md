Delver Red
==========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 1, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











At the StarCityGames.com Open in Kansas City this past weekend, **Magic** writer Caleb Durward unveiled his new deck for Standard: Delver Red! Taking a cue from Counter-Burn decks from the past, Caleb used countermagic and bounce to slow his opponents down while relying on *Innistrad* cards like [Delver of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets) and [Brimstone Volley](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brimstone+Volley) to quickly deal 20 to the opponent. Check out his list!
