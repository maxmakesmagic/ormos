A staple of Standard during the days of *Lorwyn* and *Shadowmoor*, Tokens is back! Featuring lots of 1/1 creatures powered by pump effects, Douglas Bishop took this version to a Top 8 finish at the 2010 [State Championships](http://www.the2009s.com) tournament in the Yukon Territory in Canada. Check out new *Scars of Mirrodin* cards [Asceticism](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Asceticism) and [Elspeth Tirel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth+Tirel) pulling no punches in Douglas's main deck!
