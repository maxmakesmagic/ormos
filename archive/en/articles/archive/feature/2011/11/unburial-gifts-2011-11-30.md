Unburial Gifts
==============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 30, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











ChannelFireball.com writer Alexander Shearer went to Worlds to play in side events and unveiled a new Modern deck with some powerful interactions. Alexander is no stranger to [Gifts Ungiven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven) decks, and his familiarity let him exploit a loophole in the way it works. You can choose to find only two cards with [Gifts Ungiven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven), and then your opponent must choose to put those two cards into your graveyard. The two cards Alex was choosing? [Unburial Rites](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unburial+Rites) and [Iona, Shield of Emeria](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iona%2C+Shield+of+Emeria)! Could this interaction become a staple of the Modern format? Read about it in Alexander's article on [ChannelFireball.com](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/alexander-shearer/in-development-the-gifts-that-keep-on-giving/), then try it for yourself and find out!
