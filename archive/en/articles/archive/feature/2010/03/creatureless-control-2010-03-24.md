Tired of all those pesky Elves and Thopter tokens in Extended? Zack Shaffner came up with an elegant solution: [Night of Souls' Betrayal](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Night+of+Souls%27+Betrayal). The enchantment shuts down any and all 1/1s automatically. Zack packed the rest of his deck with counters and spot removal. To win? Pick your choice of two of the biggest and/or baddest planeswalkers who've ever roamed the planes: [Nicol Bolas, Planeswalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+Planeswalker) or [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor). The creative concoction was good enough to earn Zack a Top 8 at a recent PTQ!
