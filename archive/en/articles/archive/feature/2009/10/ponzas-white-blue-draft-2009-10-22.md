The second **Magic Online** Pro Tour Qualifier has come and gone, and Ponza was the champion! Not the deck from days of yore—the **Magic Online** player. It was an aggressive blue-white deck that took the title, utilizing evasive fliers to get through in the air while white ground pounders kept Ponza alive. And for additional opponent disruption? Check out the whopping *four* copies of [Excommunicate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Excommunicate) main deck!
