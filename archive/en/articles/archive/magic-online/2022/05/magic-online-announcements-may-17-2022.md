Alternative Play for *Streets of New Capenna* Season
----------------------------------------------------


Here's the schedule for the alternative play experiences you can expect during the *Streets of New Capenna* season*.* All events run for a week, except where noted:


* May 11: ***Modern Horizons 2*** – We're bringing back *Modern Horizons 2* Draft for two weeks! This will be non-Phantom, so it's your chance to nab a ritzy [Ragavan, Nimble Pilferer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ragavan%2C+Nimble+Pilferer), [Urza's Saga](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Saga), or even a [Solitude](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solitude)!
* May 25: ***Proliferate Cube*** – Our very own Carmen Handy is back with another energizing update to *Proliferate Cube*! You can count on this being one infecting experience!
* June 1: ***Vintage Cube*** – We've bookended this calendar with Moxen! *Vintage Cube* is back, powered up with *Streets of New Capenna*, and will run for four awesome weeks of cubing action.



---

*Modern Horizons 2* Draft May 11–25
-----------------------------------


*Modern Horizons 2* Draft returns for a two-week run from May 11–25. This event is non-Phantom, so cards you open will be added to your collection! Entry options include 25 Event Tickets, 250 Play Points, or 4 Event Tickets plus 3 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters.

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. [Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place.


*NOTES:* 


* *Vintage and Standard Challenges are never pre-empted.*
* *Saturday Legacy Challenges require 32 players and give prizes to Top 32.*
* *Sunday Legacy Challenges require 64 players and give prizes to Top 64.*
