Sketches: Green Kamahl
======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 22, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











This week's "Sketches" focuses on the green "face of the set" for *Onslaught*, the new [Kamahl](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kamahl) by Matt Wilson.

### 1. Art Description

These are the instructions given to the artist:

"Location: Krosan Forest. Action: Kamahl in action pose. Mood: Bad-ass. Smug (not comically). Powerful. Notes: Story card. Remember, this is Kamahl as a Green druid."
