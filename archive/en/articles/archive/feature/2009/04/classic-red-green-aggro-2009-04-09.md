Think the older formats are just about who can afford the most expensive cards? Think again! Eddie112 managed a Top 8 in a recent **Magic Online** Premier Event featuring the Classic format, which includes all cards available online. The deck of choice? Red-Green Aggro, featuring some of the most aggressive early creatures backed by some of the game's most powerful burn. It just goes to show, when all else fails, "Three to the head" is still a valuable game plan!
