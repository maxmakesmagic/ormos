### Grand Prix Sapporo
 Pro Tour Los Angeles Qualifier 
 September 23 - 24, 2000


**The Grand Prix tournament series is an international 
 circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple 
 invitations to Pro Tour events.** 

**Tournament Format & Schedule**


* The format for Grand Prix - Sapporo will be DCITM Sealed-Deck with Rochester-Draft finals.
* **Saturday, September 23**: Grand Prix registration, 8:00 - 9:00 A.M.
* **Saturday, September 23**: Grand Prix tournament begins at 9:00 A.M. Top 64 players advance to Sunday.
* **Sunday, September 24**: Finals begin at 9:00 A.M.

**Tournament**


* The main tournament at Grand Prix - Sapporo is open to **Magic**Â® players of all ages.  
*In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher in that Grand Prix. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*
* **Tournament Format**  

 The format for Grand Prix - Sapporo will be Sealed Deck with Rochester-Draft finals. All DCI Sealed-Deck rules and **Magic** Floor Rules will be announced on September 1, 2000, along with all subsequent updates, will be enforced.
* **Tournament Structure**  

 The structure for Grand Prix - Sapporo will be modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds, number of rounds determined by number of participants), with the Top 64 players advancing to the second day of competition for prizes and invitations.
* **Tournament Byes** (Byes are not cumulative throughout the season or year.)  

 To be announced.


**Prize Payout**  

Grand Prix - Sapporo will feature a total purse of USD $25,000, including the rating-flag payouts.
