Decks of the Week: 10/11/2013
=============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on October 11, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Relative newcomer Justin Robb overcame a field of more than 450 Modern players to win [Grand Prix Brisbane](/en/events/coverage/justin-robb-triumphant-brisbane). Across the Pacific, Pierre-Christophe Mondon won the first Grand Prix using *Theros* Limited in [Oklahoma City](/en/events/coverage/mondon-devoted-victory-okc). Congratulations, Justin and Pierre-Christophe! And speaking of *Theros* Limited, that is not only the format for this season's PTQs, but it's also on display this weekend at [Pro Tour *Theros*](/en/events/coverage/dezani-proclaims-allez-les-bleus-pro-tour)Pro Tour *Theros*. It's too late to get into that Pro Tour, but you can still qualify for the next one. Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#bog)! Good luck; have fun!
