DRAFTING WITH XU SU
===================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX SHANGHAI 2014 - COVERAGE - EVENTS](/en/events/coverage/gpsha14)
 on October 5, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim












 Xu Su is one of the most experienced players in all of China. Ever since placing 2nd place at Chinese Nationals back in 2003, he never stopped garnering the respect of the local community. Today, he gets a shot at breaking into his very first Grand Prix Top 8.



Entering Pod 1 for the second draft with a 11-1 record, he would probably need only a win and a draw to secure his berth for the final draft of the day.




*Xu Su* 




  
Booster One
===========



 Xu opened an unspectacular booster and went with [War-Name Aspirant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=War-Name+Aspirant), quickly jumping into Mardu by picking [Mardu Roughrider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mardu+Roughrider). He collected three of his five copies of [Disowned Ancestor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disowned+Ancestor) and was happy to receive [Ponyback Brigade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ponyback+Brigade) seventh. Now, if only he could pick up some support spells to go with the aggressive assortment of creatures.




[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Mardu%20Roughrider)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mardu%20Roughrider)

*Pink rhinos are a good reason to ally with the Mardu Horde.*

Booster Two
===========



 A weak booster prompted Xu to select [Ride Down](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ride+Down) from his second booster and he quickly snapped up [Rush of Battle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rush+of+Battle) in anticipation of all the warriors that would follow. At this point, the player seated to his left happened to be in Mardu as well. His picks dried up quickly and wound up with only a handful of average playables.

Booster Three
=============



[Bellowing Saddlebrute](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bellowing+Saddlebrute) is one of the meanest creatures at its cost, so Xu had no qualms taking that. [Retribution of the Ancients](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Retribution+of+the+Ancients) is a decent combo with the bunch of [Disowned Ancestor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disowned+Ancestor)s he gathered. [Mardu Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mardu+Charm) and [Rush of Battle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rush+of+Battle) were the key takeaways from this booster and he ended the booster drafts by picking some average morph creatures.




[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Retribution%20of%20the%20Ancients)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Retribution%20of%20the%20Ancients)

*A decent combo with the Outlast creatures Xu’s got.*
