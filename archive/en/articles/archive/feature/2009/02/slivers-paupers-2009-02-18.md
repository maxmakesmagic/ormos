You may be familiar with **Magic Online** as a great place to catch a Draft, Standard queue, or play your familiar tournament formats around the clock. But did you know **Magic Online** also offers plenty of "non-traditional" ways of playing the game? Like the Pauper format, for instance, which generally prohibits players from using rares and/or uncommon. A recent Premier Event with the format saw petkos use this Slivers deck to make the Top 8!
