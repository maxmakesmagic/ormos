QUICK QUESTIONS #4
==================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on January 4, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/080214_1291.jpg)
By Jacob Van Lunen




Jacob Van Lunen began playing Magic in 1995. He has participated in organized play at every level of competition and was a member of the winning team at Pro Tour San Diego in 2007, thanks to an innovative draft strategy. As a writer, Van Lunen has had more than three hundred Magic strategy pieces published 





Fate Reforged preview season is in full swing. A lot of people are excited about cards like Ugin, the Spirit Dragon, but I wanted to get a deeper feel of what types of interactions better deck builders were starting to recognize. 


I found a few familiar faces and asked them, "Based on what you've seen thus far from Fate Reforged, what card currently in Standard are you most excited about playing alongside the new cards?"





![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpden15/seck.JPG)

***The Ben Seck:** "[Eidolon of Blossoms](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eidolon+of+Blossoms), Kruphix, and [Prophet of Kruphix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prophet+of+Kruphix) all seem very fun with the Frontier Siege."* 




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpden15/kibler.JPG)

***Brian Kibler:** "[Savage Knuckleblade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savage+Knuckleblade) and [Boon Satyr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boon+Satyr) seem like great partners for Flamewake Phoenix."*
