From the Vault: Realms Contents
===============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 17, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












Okay, enough dancing around. You want to know about *From the Vault: Realms*? Here's what you need to know. *From the Vault: Realms* is a collection of fifteen premium foil lands from the history of **Magic**. It's got an extremely limited print run and features new art, cards that have never been in foil before and cards that have never been in the modern card frame.



> 
> **Release Date**: August 31, 2012  
> **MSRP**: $34.99  
> **Twitter Hashtag**: [#FTVRealms](http://twitter.com/#!/search/%23ftvrealms)
> 
> 
>
