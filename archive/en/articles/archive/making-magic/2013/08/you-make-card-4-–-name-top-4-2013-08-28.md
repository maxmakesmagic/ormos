You Make the Card 4 – Name Top 4
================================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on August 28, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_ethanfleischer.jpg)
By Ethan Fleischer




Ethan Fleischer works for Magic R&D as a designer. He can sing, but not dance, and is an indifferent fencer. He lives near Seattle with his wife, three sons, and mother-in-law. 







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/features/feat262a_ymtc_card.jpg)


 


You Make the Card 4 is in its final stretch! We've designed a card, and now we're working on its creative treatment. Two days ago we voted on the Top 8 names submitted by members of the audience. (Well, seven of them were submitted by the audience, one name was created by a member of the creative team.) Half the names remain.


**YMTC Vote #4: Name Bracket**  Thanks all of you who voted! The polls are closed and we are tabulating the final results. Come back Friday, August 30, for the final vote in You Make The Card 4!
