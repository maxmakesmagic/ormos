Magic Dossier: Emi
==================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 10, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/142_dossier.jpg)**Name:** Emi

**Job title and duties:** Graphic Designer, make things look good.

**Age:** Expired Christmas Cake

**Education:** BFA in Graphic Design, Art Institute of Seattle

**Began working at Wizards:** April, 2007

**Previous job:** Graphic Designer for toy distributor. 

**How you got your job here:** Through great networking and my most nervous interviews ever.

**Magic accomplishments prior to working here:** I did have **Magic** cards in elementary school. I've *never* won a game. Ever. But I had them! 

**Expansions or projects you've worked on:**
*Shadowmoor*, *Shards of Alara*, *Duel Decks: Divine vs Demonic*, various! They've all been enjoyable.

**Favorite part of your job:** Working with the lovely variety of people. Everyone is talented, amazing, and inspiring.

**Least favorite part of your job:** When the air conditioner gets really cold in the winter. And when I forget to bring my lunch and don't have $.

**Thing you've worked on that you're most proud of:** MapleStory.

**Other games you currently enjoy playing:** A weekly all-ladies **D&D** campaign! And any sort of charades-type of game with fun company.

**In your spare time:** Being outside on sunny days. Sewing and doodling cute things.

**Favorite Magic card(s):** The Homunculus artifact token card (Lil' Baby Buttons). Illustrated by Howard Lyon.
