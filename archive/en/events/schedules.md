SCHEDULE
--------

Major Magic events are held around the world throughout the year.


Find an event near you and see how you stack up against other players! Pro Tour Qualifiers, Grand Prix Trials, and Grand Prix are open to the public. Pro Tours, World Magic Cup Qualifiers, the World Magic Cup, and the Magic World Championship are invitation-only events.
