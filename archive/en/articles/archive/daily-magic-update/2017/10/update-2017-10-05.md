The October 5, 2017 Update
==========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on October 5, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_KatieAllison.jpg)
By Katie Allison




 Katie is a copy editor and occasional writer for DailyMTG, as well as a longtime Magic player with a fondness for giant green creatures. She especially loves Commander, despite having the patience of a fruit fly, and might glare at you if your turn takes too long. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on October 5, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by [Jace Is Ace](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/DLTzvLeX0AATC0D.jpg). Thanks, [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/magicTCG/comments/74ax9o/jace_is_ace_was_your_slogan_from_todays_magic/)!


Today's Must
============


1. [*Ixalan* Brew Blitz](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/35946_Ixalan-Brew-Blitz.html) | StarCityGames.com | Chris Lansdell

Chris has whipped up some inventive new Standard lists revolving around cards from *Ixalan*, and they're all worth a look. Not only are they brand-new strategies with actual, viable paths to victory, they also have some truly excellent names, including "Vampire Horses Look in the Mirror," which sounds like a Gothic indie rock album title from the late '90s. Win-win.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [New *Ixalan* Standard](http://www.hareruyamtg.com/article/en/category/detail/193?src=wizards) | Hareruya English | Petr Sochůrek

Petr looks at the earliest stages of *Ixalan* Standard, including a Dinosaurs list (woo! Team Dinosaur!) that recently went 5-0 in a competitive *Magic Online* League. Unsurprisingly, the field is still packed with familiar faces like Four-Color Energy, but who knows what crazy new brew will come out on top after the World Championship this weekend.


1. [Worlds 2017: Power Rankings and Predictions](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/benfriedman-10052017-worlds-2017-power-rankings-and-predictions/) | Gathering Magic | Ben Friedman

With Worlds so close you can taste it (I'm getting hints of salted caramel?), Ben Friedman offers a comprehensive ranking of the players, with detailed breakdowns of his reasoning. Will Yuuya Watanabe steamroll his way to yet another victory? Will Martin Müller leapfrog over his higher-ranked competitors to steal the title? Nobody knows! That's what makes it exciting! Watch, or else I'll use even more exclamation points!


1. [One Goblin's Ballot](https://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2017/10/one-goblins-ballot-ib-halfheart-commander/) | Hipsters of the Coast | Ryan Sainio

Apparently there's some serious political strife brewing in Goblin land. On behalf of [Ib Halfheart, Goblin Tactician](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ib+Halfheart%2C+Goblin+Tactician) (slogan: "Notorious. Cowardly. Tactical."), Ryan makes an impassioned plea for Goblin loyalists to turn away from [Krenko, Mob Boss](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krenko%2C+Mob+Boss), whom he paints as a deceptive usurper, and rejoin the Ib Nation. I think we can all agree this is a very important issue.[​](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/against-the-odds-esper-jace-tokens-standard?src=wizards)


1. [5 Reasons I'm Excited for Worlds](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/5-reasons-im-excited-for-worlds/) | ChannelFireball | Mike Sigrist

There are lots of reasons to be excited about the World Championship, but in case you needed a few more, Mike Sigrist has you covered. In particular, there are some truly outstanding teams to keep an eye on, and with the format so fresh, pretty much anything could happen!

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [\_Phoenix\_'s Abzan Tokens](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-10-04)

*Magic Online* user \_Phoenix\_ recently took this list 5-0 in a Competitive Standard Constructed League. The deck looks to go as wide as possible, then eat the board with [Fumigate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fumigate) to gain life off all the tokens, *then* go wide again before the opponent can rebuild. This strategy wouldn't likely be possible without [Anointed Procession](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anointed+Procession) doubling the efficiency of all your token makers, [Legion's Landing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Legion%27s+Landing) transforming into almost [Kjeldoran Outpost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kjeldoran+Outpost), and [Hidden Stockpile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hidden+Stockpile) filtering your draws while helping build out the board.


Green is barely a splash in this deck, but [Vraska, Relic Seeker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vraska%2C+Relic+Seeker) is a welcome inclusion as yet another token maker and all-around value machine. She can also act as removal for problematic permanents of most kinds, while rewarding you with more Treasure—and she can even put your opponent straight to 1 life if she goes unchecked!
