Kelly Explodes as Eternal Weekend's Vintage Master!
===================================================




Brian Kelly's blend of Oath of Druids and an explosive surprise took him to the top of the Vintage world.
















2015 Vintage Championship
-------------------------




* [COVERAGE](#tabs-0)


COVERAGE



Five rounds into the 460 player Vintage Championship and Brian Kelly's friends had called it: His mix of Oath of Druids with the Bomberman combination of Black Lotus, Auriok Salvagers, and Pyrite Spellbomb was just the surprise he needed to take down one of the largest Vintage tournaments in history. Defeating his finals opponent, Robert Greene playing a Grixis Thieves deck, with both potent angles ended a long weekend of Eternal *Magic*.


With two Eternal champions crowned, and another year for the Eternal Weekend to clock in even larger numbers, it's clear that as along as there are players interested fascinating formats like Vintage will have a strong home.


After all, Kelly's story is just beginning. Congregations again to the 2015 Vintage Champion of Eternal Weekend, Brian Kelly!
