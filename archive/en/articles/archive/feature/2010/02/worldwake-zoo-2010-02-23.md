Zoo has been one of the most popular decks during the Extended PTQ season for San Juan. Patrick Cox took his version to the Top 8 of Grand Prix–Oakland. [Loam Lion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Loam+Lion), known as "Kird Kitty" while being tested in R&D, was one of the new inclusions to the deck from the new *Worldwake* set. Perhaps most intriguing about Patrick's deck was the use of [Damping Matrix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Damping+Matrix) in his sideboard. Read the full coverage from the event [here](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=judge/article/20090817a).
