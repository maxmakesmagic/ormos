Duels Gold Bundle
=================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 27, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











![Duels of the Planeswalkers 2012 Logo](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/937_logo.jpg)If you've never played [**Duels of the Planeswalkers 2012**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/digital/duelsoftheplaneswalkers.aspx), now would be a great time to start. That's because Steam and PS3 now have the Gold Bundle. For $19.99, that includes:


* **Duels of the Planeswalkers 2012**
* The [**Duels of the Planeswalkers 2012** Expansion](/en/articles/archive/duels-planeswalkers-2012-expansion-1-2011-09-13)
* All three Deck Packs, giving you access to [every playable deck in the game](/en/node/631066)

Did you purchase the game when it first came out? We still have something to offer you! The Gold Deck Bundle costs $9.99 and it gives you all nineteen [playable decks](/en/node/631066) and the foil conversion for each one. Play in style!
