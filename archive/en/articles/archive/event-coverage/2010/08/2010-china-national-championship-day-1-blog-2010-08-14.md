2010 China National Championship Day 1 Blog
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 14, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












经过七轮的激烈斗争，麦俊伟（图左）是唯一持有不败纪录的玩家，在最后一轮击败了孟凡譞（图右）。拥有十八分的玩家一共有六位，明天仍有挺高的机杀进八强。紧跟在后头的还包括2009世界赛国家冠军队队员张之洋和杜桐，各有十五分。


由于明早还有两轮的轮抽竞赛，今日不能透露有关Pod2的任何资料。明天将带给您更多精彩的战情！2010年国家冠军将在明天出炉，就让我们拭目以待吧！


After 7 rounds of intense competition, Mai Jun Wei (pictured left) remains the only undefeated player with the much coveted untarnished 7-0 record, edging out Meng Fan Xuan (pictured right) in the last round of the day. Six players, each with eighteen points, tail closely behind, whilst reigning Worlds Team Champion 2009 members Zhang Zhiyang and Wu Tong remain in contention at fifteen points each.


Because two more rounds of booster draft would take place tomorrow morning (followed by the last three constructed rounds), reports involving Pod 2 will not be posted until then. Stay tuned for more updates as we creep closer to the crowning of the 2010 Chinese National Champion!

Saturday, 8:00 a.m. – LCQ Winning Decklists
-------------------------------------------


*by Event Coverage Staff*

The Chinese National Championships will commence tomorrow and we are about to witness the genesis of a brand new National Team. Currently, approximately a hundred players are registered for the event so we are expecting some fierce competition tomorrow. As for the others who aren't qualified, they swarmed down to the luxurious tournament venue in a bid to get an envelope to compete tomorrow.


A total of 8 [Last Chance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Last+Chance) Qualifiers were held today, allowing 16 additional players to be added to the roster tomorrow. After half a day of intense competition, there is no doubt that RG Ramp would be a force to be reckoned with, since it helped a third of these players clinch an invite for tomorrow's prestigious tournament. In addition, Blue White Control, Fauna Naya, Jund and Turboland also qualified 4, 3, 2 and 1 players respectively. Here are the winning decklists for your viewing pleasure!


**LCQ 赢家套牌**


中国国家冠赛明天将掀开序幕，举国上下都在等待新的国家队出炉。目前，大约100名玩家已经取得参赛资格，明天的比赛想必会相当激烈。其他的牌手们纷纷莅临豪华的比赛会场，争夺明天的参赛资格。


今天一共举行了8场LCQ（本地俗称PK赛），让16位玩家在明天"有机可乘"。经过半天的纷争后，我们见证了加速泰坦的实力。这副以太古泰坦命名的套牌摘三分之一的坐席，一共有6件代表作。除此之外，从PK赛获得国冠资格的套牌有4套蓝白控、3套祭师纳雅、2套勇得和1套蓝绿加速。现在就和大家分享这些套牌吧！

### 第一局


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/fm%20r5%20-%20JiaBin.jpg)*Jia Bin (贾斌)*
M11轮抓看似简单，其实并不容易。除了要了解各个颜色的搭配，还得精准地读出上家的讯号，以免狗咬狗两败俱伤。基本上，任何颜色混合都可能成为3-0的套牌。贾斌因少登记了一张牌不幸被叛一盘输，还没开打就败了第一局。他的红白快攻套牌能否帮他打败徐斌的黑蓝套牌？


双方玩家选择不调度，贾斌在第一回就叫出了资深步兵[[Infantry Veteran](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Infantry+Veteran)]。在快攻的战略中，他可说是个举足轻重的角色，可为对手造成阻挡的问题。徐斌恰巧拥有快攻的克星之一，第二回合叫唤了沿海守卫[[Maritime Guard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Maritime+Guard)]来助阵。攻打不过烦人的1/3，贾斌只好施出放荡烈焰术士[[Prodigal Pyromancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prodigal+Pyromancer)],不过立刻就被对方的沼地病症[[Quag Sickness](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quag+Sickness)]感染，奄奄一息。没有第四张地牌的贾斌只好将沿海守卫[[Maritime Guard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Maritime+Guard)]逐出教会[[Excommunicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Excommunicate)]。


徐斌的注定[[Preordain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Preordain)]为他拿到以血立契[[Sign in Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sign+in+Blood)],并将眠梦咒[[Sleep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sleep)]放置在牌库顶。贾斌把暴锋飞马[[Stormfront Pegasus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormfront+Pegasus)]加入行列，便开始三点，三点的攻击。徐斌试着使用莉莲娜仆幽灵[[Liliana's Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%27s+Specter)]来阻挡，可是却被闪电击[[Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)]劈了下来。


剩下7点生命的徐斌只好使用眠梦咒[[Sleep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sleep)]来拖延点时间，不过当贾斌的撒拉天使[[Serra Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serra+Angel)]和云际十字军[[Cloud Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloud+Crusader)]来临时，已经病入膏肓，无药可救了。


第一局贾斌获胜，目前打平。

贾斌在再次拥有资深步兵[[Infantry Veteran](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Infantry+Veteran)]和暴锋飞马[[Stormfront Pegasus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormfront+Pegasus)]的劲爆开头，不过徐斌毫不迟疑的使用送终刀锋[[Doom Blade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doom+Blade)]将它送至坟场。接着又陆续召了莉莲娜仆幽灵[[Liliana's Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%27s+Specter)]和庄园吸血鬼[[Barony Vampire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barony+Vampire)]。除了利用闪电击[[Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)]将那飞行生物解决，徐斌没有任何举动。吸血鬼的命运也好不到哪儿去，先是被逐出教会[[Excommunicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Excommunicate)]，后又被茜卓的暴怒[[Chandra's Outrage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%27s+Outrage)]烤焦了。

Saturday, 7.20 p.m.: Round Seven: Mai Jun Wei vs Meng Fan Xuan
--------------------------------------------------------------


*Chapman "randomchap" Sim*

Day One is about to draw its curtains and we are left with only two undefeated players. The winner of this match would only need a single win tomorrow to practically lock up a spot in the Top 8. The loser here should not be disheartened either, since 6-1 is still a relatively great score to end the day with.


**星期六, 7.20 p.m.: 第7轮: 麦俊伟 对战 孟凡譞**


中国国冠赛第一天即将到了尾声，此刻只剩下两位全胜的玩家。这轮的优胜者在明天的竞赛中只需再多一胜就几乎锁定在八强以内，今晚可高枕无忧。输家也不用气馁，毕竟6-1也是个相当漂亮的成绩。


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Sat%201920%20-%20Mai%20Jun%20Wei.jpg)*Mai Jun Wei ( 麦俊伟)*
**Game 1**  
**第一局**


Both players had little action and did nothing but drop lands on the first few turns. Mai Jun Wei was the first to act, summoning [Barony Vampire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barony+Vampire) to commence the beatdown. It was quickly [Excommunicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Excommunicate)d by Meng Fan Xuan, who followed up his third turn "bounce" spell with a [Prized Unicorn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Unicorn). [Quag Sickness](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quag+Sickness) was sufficient to remove the oversized Taunting Elf.


Unfortunately for Meng Fan Xuan, he had started to landflood and was rumored to have draw eight of them consecutively. Mai Jun Wei summoned [Earth Servant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earth+Servant), [Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion) and [Ember Hauler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Hauler) over the next few turns and threatened to end the game within the next two turns. [Naturalize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize) on Trisky reduced the clock considerably, but Meng Fan Xuan had no real way to deal with the gigantic [Earth Servant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earth+Servant) without [Pacifism](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pacifism). He could only be content with slapping [Armored Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Armored+Ascension) onto his lone [Knight Exemplar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+Exemplar). All Mai Jun Wei had to do was attack with his creature and [Fling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fling) the fattest one at Meng Fan Xuan to clinch Game 1.


两位玩家在前几回合都没有大动干戈，分别下地并结束回合。一直到第三回，麦俊伟才叫出了庄园吸血鬼 [[Barony Vampire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barony+Vampire)]，但迅速被逐出教会[[Excommunicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Excommunicate)]。孟凡譞的第一只生物，珍奇独角兽[[Prized Unicorn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Unicorn)]立即感染了沼地病症[[Quag Sickness](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quag+Sickness)]。不幸的是，孟凡譞开始"爆"地，据说连抓了八张地牌。之后，麦俊伟陆陆续续放置大地仆役[[Earth Servant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earth+Servant)]，三臂铁人[[Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion)]和烬火搬运工[[Ember Hauler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Hauler)]进场。孟凡譞利用回归自然[[Naturalize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize)]对付那神器生物，还给骑士模范[[Knight Exemplar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+Exemplar)]贴上了武装飞腾[[Armored Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Armored+Ascension)]。不过，只操控一只生物的他，始终不够抵御来临的蹂躏。一张投掷[[Fling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fling)]足以说服他投降。


**Meng Fan Xuan wins Game 1**  
**麦俊伟第一局获胜。**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Sat%201920%20-%20Meng%20Dan%20Xuan.jpg)*Meng Fan Xuan (孟凡譞)*
**Game 2**  
**第二局**


A second turn [Stormfront Pegasus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormfront+Pegasus) from Meng Fan Xuan was extremely pivotal in this game, since Mai Jun Wei kept a relatively slow opener of seven. His first play was a [Warlord's Axe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warlord%27s+Axe) on turn three, which would go well on the [Prized Unicorn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Unicorn) he summoned the next turn. It was only until the sixth when he finally had a solution ([Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion) makes an appearance again) to the 2/1 flyer who had taken half of his life away. [Naturalize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize) took it down once again, but he had already lost his Pegasus.


Having no other creatures to cast, Mai Jun Wei was forced to plop [Howling Banshee](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Banshee) into play, further reducing his own life total to a mere 7 life. Meng Fan Xuan tried to punish him by trying [Excommunicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Excommunicate) on the banshee, but Mai Jun Wei clearly did not want to draw that card again and Flung [[Fling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fling)] it at his opponent.


Ironically, he drew his second copy of the [Banshee](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banshee) few turns player, taking himself down to 4 life. Meng Fan Xuan pacified it without any delay, and also dropping [Llanowar Elves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) thereafter. Just as he was about to die from some Elf beatdown, he had managed to draw 2 [Gravedigger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravedigger)s to recur his [Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion) with. Not even [Obstinate Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obstinate+Baloth) could save his opponent and Meng Fan Xuan eventually succumbed.


孟凡譞第二回合施出暴锋飞马[[Stormfront Pegasus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormfront+Pegasus)]，开始无情的攻打。麦俊伟的速度相当慢，第三和第四回合只有军头斧[[Warlord's Axe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warlord%27s+Axe)]和珍奇独角兽[[Prized Unicorn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Unicorn)]，一直到了第六回合才使用三臂铁人[[Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion)]的异能摆平了那只飞马。不过它还算有功劳，足足造成了10点伤害。回归自然[[Naturalize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize)]再次出现，使三臂铁人[[Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion)]离场。


在没有其他生物的情况下，麦俊伟只好把手中的哭号女妖[[Howling Banshee](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Banshee)]放置进场（仅剩7生命），不幸却被迫逐出教会[[Excommunicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Excommunicate)]。女妖不愿意再次抓到女妖，于是将它投掷[[Fling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fling)]，向孟凡譞造成三点伤害。


不过，天总爱捉弄人。麦俊伟两三回合后又抓到他第二张女妖，泄气的将它拍在桌上，如今只剩下4条生命。不幸，它立即成了和平主义[[Pacifism](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pacifism)]者。眼看就要被罗堰妖精[[Llanowar Elves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves)]打死的时候，突然出现一道曙光。他居然抓到了掘墓怪[[Gravedigger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravedigger)]，把三臂铁人[[Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion)]移会手中。孟凡譞的顽强巴洛西[[Obstinate Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obstinate+Baloth)]虽然进场，但却抵挡不住第二张掘墓怪[[Gravedigger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravedigger)]的出现。


Mai Jun Wei wins Round Seven and becomes the very last undefeated player!  
**麦俊伟第七局获胜，成为唯一7-0玩家！**
