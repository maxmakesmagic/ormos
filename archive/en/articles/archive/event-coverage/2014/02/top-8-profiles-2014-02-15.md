Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 15, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/n2KoE-g2_bigger.jpeg)
By Mike Rosenberg




 Mike Rosenberg is a writer and gamer and has been part of the Magic text coverage team since 2011. He joined Wizards as organized play’s content specialist in June 2014. 








  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpmex14/flores_top8.jpg)
### Mario Flores


**Age:** 21  
**Hometown:** México D.F.  
**Occupation:** Student


 

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  

7th Worlds 2010 (Team finish)2/2 Top 8 Grand Prix


**What was your record and best card from the Sealed Deck:**  

7-0-1, [Prognostic Sphinx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prognostic+Sphinx)


**What was your record and best card from the first draft pod:**  

3-0, [Phalanx Leader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phalanx+Leader)


**What was your record and best card from the second draft pod:**  

1-0-2, [Scholar of Athreos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scholar+of+Athreos)


**What is your favorite card from *Born of the Gods* for Limited and why?**  
[Fall of the Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fall+of+the+Hammer). It’s good removal.


**What card from *Theros* has changed in value the most (better/worse) with the addition of *Born of the Gods*?**   
[Phalanx Leader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phalanx+Leader)

### Derek Woloshyn


**Age:** 30  
**Hometown:** Chicago, IL  
**Occupation:** Category Analyst


 

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  
*Gatecrash* Prerelease Top 8… so none.


**What was your record and best card from the Sealed Deck:**  

6-2, [Agent of the Fates](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Agent+of+the+Fates). The card just wins games.


**What was your record and best card from the first draft pod:**  

3-0. [Spiteful Returned](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spiteful+Returned). Bestow on a creature with intimidate is awesome.


**What was your record and best card from the second draft pod:**  

2-0-1. [Wingsteed Rider](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingsteed+Rider). Gets out of control very quickly.


**What is your favorite card from *Born of the Gods* for Limited and why?**  
[Hunter’s Prowess](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hunter%E2%80%99s+Prowess). Drawing cards in green feels really good.


**What card from *Theros* has changed in value the most (better/worse) with the addition of *Born of the Gods*?**  
[Voyage’s End](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyage%E2%80%99s+End) has gotten more important because there is less bounce with *Born of the Gods*.
