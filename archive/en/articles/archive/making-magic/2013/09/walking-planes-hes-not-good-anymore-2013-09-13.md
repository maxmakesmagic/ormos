Walking the Planes: He's Not That Good Anymore
==============================================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on September 13, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Nathan Holt and Shawn Kornhauser












After a crushing loss at the hands of Wizards of the Coast in last year's Community Cup, the community is out for revenge. No matter how strange the format, whether it's Wacky Draft or Ironroot Chef, the Community is unafraid of these titans who make **Magic**. Why? Apparently, they're not that good anymore. 

![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220216050541im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/img/interface/video-place-holder.png)[.](http://www.youtube.com/embed/TglIDt6R2RE)  
  
In case you missed the live coverage of the Community Cup, take a look at the three judges' complete review of each teams' offerings in the Ironroot Chef format.
