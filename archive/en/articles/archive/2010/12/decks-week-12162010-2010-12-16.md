Decks of the Week: 12/16/2010
=============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on December 16, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












If you're a fan of deck lists (and we're guessing you are, since you clicked on the link), get ready for a holiday feast of metagame information. The [2010 Magic World Championships](/en/events/coverage/theres-no-jace-guillaume) were held last weekend in Chiba, Japan, featuring multiple tournaments across formats. From the top Standard and Extended decks in the main event to a all-foil *Scars of Mirrodin* Rotisserie Draft, there's sure to be something to catch your eye. Congratulations to Magic's newest world champions Guillaume Matignon, Carlos Rom√£o, and Team Slovak Republic (Robert Jurkovic, Ivan Floch, and Patrik Surab)!
