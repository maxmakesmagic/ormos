2008 Worlds Video Deck Tech: Luis Scott-Vargas
==============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 12, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_9.png)
By By Event Coverage Staff











There may not be a hotter player on the Pro Tour right now than Luis Scott-Vargas. After posting a win at Pro Tour–Berlin in Extended, he followed that up with a Sealed/Booster Draft win at Grand Prix–Atlanta. Take a look as he shares his draft strategy and key decisions during his first *Shards of Alara* draft here at Worlds.
