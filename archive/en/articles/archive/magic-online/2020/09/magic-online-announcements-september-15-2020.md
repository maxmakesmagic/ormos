*Zendikar Rising*: *Magic Online* Edition
-----------------------------------------


*Zendikar Rising* is arriving September 17 after this week's update with system downtime on Wednesday, September 16. As normal, this is planned to start around 9 a.m. Pacific time (UTC-7) and last until noon Pacific time.


You can read more about *Zendikar Rising* on MTGO in [Alli's release article here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/zendikar-rising-magic-online-edition-2020-09-11), and you can check out the updated Treasure Chest contents [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/treasure-chest-card-list-info).

*Modern Cube*
-------------


*Modern Cube* returns this Wednesday, September 16! Updated with cards from our most recent releases, this cube features the most powerful cards you can play in Modern wrapped up in that awesome Cube wrapper. The Draft league and single-elimination Draft queue will be available until October 7, and you can read more about this latest *Modern Cube* update [right here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-cube-update-2020-09-14) and view the full card list [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-cube-cardlist).

*Magic* at PAX Online
---------------------


PAX Online continues through this weekend! Join us from September 12–20 and check out all the things in store for *Magic: The Gathering* at [pastimesevents.com](https://pastimesevents.com/).

Limited Split Championship Qualifier Saturday
---------------------------------------------


On Saturday, September 19, there will be a *Zendikar Rising* Sealed Split Championship Qualifier at 7 a.m. PT! The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points. The winner qualifies for the *Zendikar Rising* Split Championship on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Split Championship Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#ptq).




---

Pauper Split Championship Qualifier Sunday
------------------------------------------


On Sunday, September 20, there will be a Pauper Split Championship Qualifier at 7 a.m. PT! The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points. The winner qualifies for the *Zendikar Rising* Split Championship on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Split Championship Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#ptq).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats. To learn more about Premier Play qualification, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020).


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges now run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place. *NOTE: The Pauper Challenge is never pre-empted**.*

Build Notes
-----------


* *Zendikar Rising* has been added to the client. Events will begin on Thursday.
* [Simic Manipulator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Simic+Manipulator)'s activated ability will now properly take control of a creature if enough counters were removed to pay the cost of activating the ability.
* The appearance of card context menus has been updated.
* [Crown-Hunter Hireling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crown-Hunter+Hireling) is now tradeable and playable in all appropriate formats.
* Muting *Magic Online* in the Windows Volume mixer will now properly prevent sound from being played.
