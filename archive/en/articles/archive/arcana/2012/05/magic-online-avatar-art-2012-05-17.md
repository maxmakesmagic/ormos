Magic Online Avatar Art
=======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 17, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Hhere's the art for the [Avacyn, Angel of Hope](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avacyn%2C+Angel+of+Hope) avatar in **Magic Online**:

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/974_avacyn_thin.jpg)  
That's pretty! But it's not the full illustration. Here's the full-width version.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/974_avacyn.jpg)[Avacyn, Angel of Hope](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avacyn%2C+Angel+of+Hope)**Magic Online** Avatar | *Avacyn Restored* Participation Award
