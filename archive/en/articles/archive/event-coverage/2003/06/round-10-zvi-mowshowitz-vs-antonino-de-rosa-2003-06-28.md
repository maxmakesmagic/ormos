Round 10: Zvi Mowshowitz vs. Antonino De Rosa
=============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 28, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











### Game 1

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a793.jpg)De Rosa started with [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves), and Mowshowitz had [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress). He saw a hand of [Firebolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt), [Volcanic Hammer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer), [Elephant Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide), [Basking Rootwalla](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) and a land- [Elephant Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide) went to the graveyard. The Elf attacked for one, and De Rosa played the Rootwalla followed by [Grim Lavamancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer). [Chainer's Edict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainer%27s+Edict) caused De Rosa to lose his Lavamancer, and the resulting attack dealt four damage to Mowshowitz as De Rosa missed his third land drop.

He continued to simply attack with his Rootwalla and Elf, until Mowshowitz cast [Mutilate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate) to kill both creatures. De Rosa came back by summoning another Lavamancer, and sent [Firebolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) to the dome. [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) forced [Volcanic Hammer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer) to the graveyard, and De Rosa was holding [Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) and [Violent Eruption](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption). [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood) dealt with the Lavamancer, and [Visara](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara) came out on turn six.

De Rosa- "That's not fair."  
 Mowshowitz- "I play lands, sure it's fair!"

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a792.jpg)Mowshowitz- 1 De Rosa- 0

De Rosa- "I think my sideboard is only good against your deck."

De Rosa- "Zvi, I'm like 0-6 against you."  
 Mowshowitz- "You know why? You always play these ghetto decks with lots of guys, and I play decks that kill guys."

### Game 2

De Rosa had a Rootwalla, and once again Mowshowitz had a first turn [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), which took out [Compost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost). A second turn [Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) continued the curve, and [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood) killed the Rootwalla. [Call of the Herd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) made an elephant token, and the next attack was for five damage, bringing Zvi to twelve.

Mowshowitz played his fourth land but opted to not cast the [Mutilate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate) in his hand, instead using [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) to kill the token. De Rosa flashed back the [Call](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call), Mowshowitz untapped, and cast [Mutilate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate). He had missed his fifth land drop, and De Rosa came right back with [Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) and [Grim Lavamancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer). [Chainer's Edict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainer%27s+Edict) killed the Lavamancer, and a Centaur attack brought Mowshowitz to five. Mowshowitz still couldn't find a fifth land and had no answer.

Mowshowitz- 1 De Rosa- 1

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a794.jpg)A second turn [Compost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost) spelled bad news for Mowshowitz. [Call of the Herd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) made an elephant, which died to [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) on end step as De Rosa drew a card. [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) was followed by a [Volcanic Hammer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer) to the dome, and Mowshowitz cycled [Undead Gladiator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Undead+Gladiator), letting De Rosa draw another card. [Chainer's Edict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainer%27s+Edict) would have killed the [Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel), but De Rosa discarded a Rootwalla into play to be the sacrificial lamb.

De Rosa kept getting ahead with every 1-for-1 trade, and flashed back the [Call](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call). Mowshowitz cast [Diabolic Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Tutor), and De Rosa attacked with the [Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) and elephant token. Two madness [Violent Eruption](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption)s pumped the [Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) twice and dealt an extra ten damage to Mowshowitz, giving De Rosa the match.

**Final Result:** Mowshowitz- 1 De Rosa- 2
