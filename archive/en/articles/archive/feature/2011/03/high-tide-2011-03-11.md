The StarCityGames.com Legacy Open in Edison, New Jersey saw the largest field ever for a Legacy Open. Alix Hatfield, a renowned Legacy player, managed to take the top slot using a powerful [High Tide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Tide) build of his own creation. The deck is newly playable after the unbanning of [Time Spiral](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Spiral) and is very similar to the original [High Tide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Tide) decks of the late 1990s. It uses [High Tide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Tide) to juice up the amount of mana its lands make, then uses untap effects like [Time Spiral](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Spiral) and [Candelabra of Tawnos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Candelabra+of+Tawnos) to generate tons of mana with said lands. The last spell of the game is usually a lethal "Draw X" that forces the opponent to draw more cards than are in his or her library.
