Learn to Play!
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 15, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast













We have some news! Our new [Learn to Play](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/NewtoMagic.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/newtomagic/learntoplay) application is now online! So if you've got someone who wants to know how to play **Magic**, send 'em [here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/NewtoMagic.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/newtomagic/learntoplay).


And in addition to the interactive application, we've also got the "Planeswalker Primer" videos to help the process along. And here they are!
