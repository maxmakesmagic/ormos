In 2007 Michael Jacob joined the U.S. Nationals team as a member at the World Championships. In 2008, Jacob will lead the team after repeating his Top 8 performance and finishing the tournament with the championship trophy. His Mono-red deck utilized many cards pundits considered bad. Where most players had [Ghitu Encampment](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghitu+Encampment), Mike had [Keldon Megaliths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Keldon+Megaliths). Where other players had [Keldon Marauders](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Keldon+Marauders), Mike opted to play [Blood Knight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Knight). Ultimately his ingenuity paid off as Michael Jacob became the 2008 U.S. National Champion. For coverage of the 2008 U.S. Nationals Championship, including all the Standard decklists, go [here](/en/events/coverage/jacob-re-ignites-great-chicago-fire).
