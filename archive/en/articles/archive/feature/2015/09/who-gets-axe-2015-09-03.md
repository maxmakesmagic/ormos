Who Gets the Axe?
=================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 3, 2015 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff











How would you like a chance to win Crovax's mighty axe? Beginning February 14, Wizards of the Coast and *TopDeck*TM magazine give you the opportunity to do just that. Two characters will bite the dust in the *Nemesis*TM novel, which is based on the *Magic: The Gathering®-Nemesis* expansion. All you need to do is correctly identify the two victims and you could win Crovax's axe, a one-of-a-kind reproduction cast in metal and wood-not to mention loads of *Nemesis* cards. Who could ask for more?


Just log onto the Wizard of the Coast® website at [www.wizards.com/topdeck](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/pro-tour-avacyn-restored-top-block-constructed-decks-2012-05-13) for entry information and contest rules.
