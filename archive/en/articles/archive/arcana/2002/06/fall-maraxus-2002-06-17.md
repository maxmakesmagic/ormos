Fall of Maraxus
===============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 17, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The set *Weatherlight*, fittingly, is where the *Weatherlight* Saga began in earnest. And, like *Tempest* after it, *Weatherlight* had sequences of card art that told little pieces of the story.

The most notable of these is on the cards [Desperate Gambit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desperate+Gambit) and [Debt of Loyalty](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Debt+of+Loyalty). In the former, [Gerrard Capashen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Gerrard%2BCapashen) is battling [Maraxus of Keld](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Maraxus+of+Keld), and looks to be losing. But [Starke of Rath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Starke%2Bof%2BRath) is running up in the background.
