Grand Prix-Nagoya 2000 Attendance Statistics
============================================



 Posted in **Feature**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












### Competitors


217 total teams.


651 total players.


5 countries represented.


### Country Distribution


Japan: 635  

USA: 8 (3 of which from Yokota Airbase in Japan)  

Taiwan: 6  

Singapore: 1  

Korea: 1


### Byes


6 teams have two-round byes.


7 teams have one-round byes.


### Notes


This is the highest attendence for a Team Grand Prix ever (Frankfurt is the next highest).


This is higher than the average attendence for a Japanese Grand Prix. Japan is one of the top countries for interest in team-format events.
