Borderland Cudgel
=================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on July 20, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











This is [Cudgel Troll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cudgel+Troll), illustrated by Jesper Ejsing.

![Cudgel Troll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Cudgel+Troll)  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/236_Cudgel_Troll.jpg)  
  
This is [Borderland Ranger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Borderland+Ranger), also illustrated by Jesper Ejsing.

![Borderland Ranger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Borderland+Ranger)  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/236_Borderland_Ranger.jpg)  
  
  
And this is what happens if you put the two pieces of art next to each other.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/236_TrollRanger.jpg)  
It's no surprise that the two of them are in the same forest, I guess. But who knew they were so close to each other? That ranger had better look where he's going, or he'll run into a nasty surprise!
