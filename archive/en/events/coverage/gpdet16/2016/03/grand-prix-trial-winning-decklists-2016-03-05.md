Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 5, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











Not only does the war between the Eldrazi and the world happen during the tournaments, but it also spills into the night before! The Grand Prix Trials are a time-honored way to spend your Friday night before a tournament Saturday. If you do well enough, you can earn two byes for the next morning.


Pro Tour Historian Brian David-Marshall talked with many of the winners of these trials, and showed the results on stream. Below are the complete decklists of all the Modern trials from Grand Prix Detroit.


It's a perfect mix of Eldrazi and non-Eldrazi, and the non-Eldrazi are pretty fun lists—Elves, Lanterns, Lieges, and Zombies.
