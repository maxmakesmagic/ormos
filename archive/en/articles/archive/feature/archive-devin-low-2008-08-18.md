Archive: Devin Low
==================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 18, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Devin Low became the Head Developer of Magic: The Gathering in 2007. He cracked his first booster packs of Alpha in 1993, opened Mox Emerald and Gaea's Liege, and has loved the game ever since. Devin earned Bachelor's and Master's Degrees in Computer Science from Harvard University. He started competing on the Magic Pro Tour in 2000 before joining Wizards of the Coast in 2003. He enjoys instants, sorceries, and attacking for 2.
