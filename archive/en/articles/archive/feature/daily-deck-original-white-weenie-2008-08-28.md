The *Lorwyn* and *Shadowmoor* blocks' Kithkin tribe has brought a resurgence of a popular **Magic** archetype: White Weenie. But long before the Kithkin hordes came 1996 World Champion Tom Chanpheng. He used his White Weenie deck to cut through a field of [Necropotence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necropotence) players to win the championship title. And he did it after a deck registration error forced him to play with a [Sleight of Mind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sleight+of+Mind) in his sideboard he couldn't actually use!
