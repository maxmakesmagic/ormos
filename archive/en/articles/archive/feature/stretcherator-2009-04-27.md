Coverage veteran Brian David-Marshall was on hand for all of the Extended festivities at Grand Prix-Kobe this past weekend, and boy did he pick up a doozy! Hisaya Tanaka managed to take a deck he dubbed "Stretcherator" to some last second byes to start the weekend off undefeated. Featuring [Time Stretch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Stretch), [Tezzeret, the Seeker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret%2C+the+Seeker), and more, the deck builds up its mana before taking a long string of turns either through its namesake [Time Stretch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Stretch), or by taking all of its opponent's turns with [Mindslaver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslaver). For more on Grand Prix-Kobe, read the official event coverage [here](/en/events/coverage/saito%E2%80%99s-back-back) .
