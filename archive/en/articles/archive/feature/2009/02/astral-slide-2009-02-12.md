Former U.S. Nationals finalist Gabe Walls narrowly missed the Top 8 at Grand Prix-Los Angeles with a version of the classic Astral Slide deck. His efforts have caught on with players all over the world. FFfreaksgrandmother used this build to Top 8 a Premier Event on **Magic Online**. The Slide deck seeks to gain a wealth of card advantage by using cyclers in conjunction with [Astral Slide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) itself. [Life from the Loam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam) helps by returning all the lands cycled to its owner's hand, allowing the Slide player to keep going late into the game.
