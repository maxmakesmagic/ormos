Last weekend was a National Championship blowout weekend! The United States, Germany, and Great Britain all held tournaments to decide who would represent them at the 2010 World Championships in Chiba, Japan. For the United States, Josh Utter-Leyton, known by his **Magic Online** name "Wrapter," will captain the Americans. His Mythic Bant strategy took him to the top of the standings. Read full coverage of the event [here](/en/events/coverage/utter-leyton-crowned-us-champ).
