Round 15: Zvi Mowshowitz vs. Steve O'Mahoney-Schwartz
=====================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 15, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a747.jpg)Both of these players are long time veterans from New York. They both worked their way up at the same time playing at Neutral Ground and learning through the Junior Pro Tour before moving up to the big leagues. All that work paid off, as Steve and Zvi both have a Pro Tour victory to their names, and countless other high finishes. Zvi currently has the advantage over Steve this week, going 0-1-1 against him. Both players are playing green/blue decks, but Steve's version is based around [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation) while Zvi's version is much more tempo oriented.

### Game 1

Zvi cast a second turn [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear), while Steve played [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation), fetching two [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) and [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Zvi cast [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) and declared "Ancestral Recall" as he played [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill). Once Steve got to four mana he flashed back a Roar, breaking the [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill). Zvi had [Aether Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Burst) to handle that threat, played [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) to get threshold, and busted out more creatures with another [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) and [Nimble Mongoose](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nimble+Mongoose). Steve flashed back the other Roar, but Zvi was able to attack with all his creatures and force through enough damage to win the game.

Mowshowitz- 1 O'Mahoney-Schwartz- 0

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a748.jpg)Steve mulliganed his opening hand. Zvi's [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) put [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) right into the graveyard, and Steve's [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) was matched by Zvi's Mongrel. Steve cast his own [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) to get [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) into the graveyard, and then cast [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation). [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) gave Zvi a significant clock to work with, but Steve's [Aether Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Burst) bounced back the Centaur and Mongrel, while Steve added a second [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) to the board. The Centaur came back out and Steve had another Burst, and attacked for eight. He followed up with [Catalyst Stone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Catalyst+Stone), and Zvi played another [Aether Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Burst) to bounce Steve's team. Zvi's Centaur and Mongrel came back out, and Steve got to work building a Wurm army thanks to the Stone. Zvi had, you guessed it- another [Aether Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Burst), and Steve's Wurm died while his two [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear)s came back. Zvi attacked with the Centaur and Mongrel, and cast another Mongrel and a [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) to put a ton of pressure on the board. Steve flashed back two more Roars, which kept Zvi from attacking. 

Steve untapped, thought for a bit, and went all out, attacking with all his creatures. Zvi blocked and was left with only a Centaur to Steve's two Wurms. Zvi drew Mongrel off the top, and Steve cast another [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation).

Zvi- "Do I lose?"  
 Steve- "No."  
 Zvi- "Why not?"  
 Steve- "Because I suck. I never play this format."

It got [Grizzly Fate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grizzly+Fate) and two [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace), and the [Grizzly Fate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grizzly+Fate) was flashed back right away thanks to the two [Catalyst Stone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Catalyst+Stone)s. That was enough.

Mowshowitz- 1 O'Mahoney-Schwartz- 1

Steve- "My deck's pretty good. I learn some new things every round."

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a749.jpg)Zvi's first turn Study put [Krosan Reclamation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Reclamation) in the graveyard, and [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) into play. Steve's Study was less impressive, putting two lands in the graveyard. Zvi filled up his graveyard like crazy on turn two, playing [Mental Note](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mental+Note) followed by [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study), discarding [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) and a land. Steve cast [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear), while Zvi attacked for three with his Rootwalla. Steve summoned [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder), while Zvi built up his side a little more by playing [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). [Grizzly Fate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grizzly+Fate) improved Steve's board a bit, and [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) chump blocked on Zvi's attack. [Centaur Garden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Centaur+Garden) helped Zvi squeeze through some more damage on the following turn, bringing Steve to five life points. Steve attacked with two bears and Zvi flashed back Reclamation to put the [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder)s back into Steve's deck. Zvi untapped and attacked with his creatures, and Steve had a Reclamation of his own before blockers. It put [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) back into Zvi's library, and [Aether Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Burst) bounced back the Rootwalla after it became huge thanks to [Centaur Garden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Centaur+Garden). This left Zvi defenseless, but he was still at a relatively healthy ten life points while Steve was at five. 

Both bears attacked to bring Zvi to six, and Steve played [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation), getting two [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) and [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm). Zvi untapped and played another Reclamation to shuffle back Steve's Reclamation and Roar. Steve flashed back [Grizzly Fate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grizzly+Fate), but Zvi had [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) to take the match.

**Final Result:** Mowshowitz- 2 O'Mahoney-Schwartz- 1
