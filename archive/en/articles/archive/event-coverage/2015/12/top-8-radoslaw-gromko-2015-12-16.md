Top 8: Radoslaw Gromko
======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPPORTO00/Images/204.jpg)**Name:**  Radoslaw Gromko  
**Age:**  21  
**Location:** Bialystok, Poland  
**Occupation:** Student, Warzawa, Computer Science  
**Played Magic since:** 1995, Ice Age  
**Best accomplishments:** -



Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Porto?  

A: One, to check what I've learned at Master Workshop '00. Two, I'm on vacation across Europe. Three, I like to play in Master Events.



Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?  

A: Top 32 because I wanted to make the second day and get some money to pay for the travel.



Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.  

A: First day I got a good w/b swarm deck. The worst matchup was g/u, but I went 2-1 with them. First draft I got g/r beatdown, second draft b/r with only 14 big creatures.



Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?  

A: I rarely make mistakes when playing, my drafting and constructing skills got better after Master Workshop '00.



Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?  

A: Attending PT and going to the next GPs.



Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?  

A: As everyone else - winning.
