Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on March 14, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










These are the Top 8 Players of Grand Prix Auckland 2015.




---


Yifan Wei
=========


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/GPNZ_Top8_Yifan_Wei.jpg)


**Hometown:** Tianjin, China.  



**What were your colours in draft 1?** Green-Red, splash Blue.


**What were your colours in draft 2?** White-Black, splash Green.


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** 36th Pro Tour Journey into Nyx.


**What would you rather eat, a Trumpet, a Jaffa, or a Perky Nana? (these are New Zealand foods, I promise you.)** “I have no idea what they are...”




---


Fabian Dickmann
===============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/GPNZ_Top8_Fabian_Dickmann.jpg)


**Hometown:** Bochum, Germany.  



**What were your colours in draft 1?** Red-White.


**What were your colours in draft 2?** Blue-Red, splash two White cards.


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** GP Finalist in Bochum.


**What would you rather eat, a Trumpet, a Jaffa, or a Perky Nana? (these are New Zealand foods, I promise you.)** “Easy, Perky Nana! I don’t know what that is, but the name is cool.”




---


Jason Chung
===========


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/GPNZ_Top8_Jason_Chung.jpg)


**Hometown:** Where Juzza lives.  
**Age:** 22  
**Occupation:** PJax’s (Paul Jackson) stylist. (Specialist: hats.)  
**What colours did you play on Day 1?** Blue-Green, splash Red.


**What were your colours in draft 1?** Collateral Damage. Mono Red, splash Bloodsoaked Champion.


**What were your colours in draft 2?** Blue-Green, splash Red.


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** 11th Pro Tour Fate Reforged.


**What would you rather eat, a Trumpet, a Jaffa, or a Perky Nana? (these are New Zealand foods, I promise you.)** “I don’t eat instruments, I’m not a cannibal. However, thinking of bananas does make me perky.”

Dylan Goldsmith
===============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/GPNZ_Top8_Dylan_Goldmsith.jpg)


**Hometown:** Christchurch, New Zealand.  
**Age:** 17  
**Occupation:** High School Student  
**What colours did you play on Day 1?** Black-Green “big butts” deck. Sideboarded 3 Plains, Abzan Guide, and [Dromoka, the Eternal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dromoka%2C+the+Eternal) every match.


**What were your colours in draft 1?** White-Black control, splashing Blue for Glacial Stalker.


**What were your colours in draft 2?** Sultai Control.


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** Here and now.


**What would you rather eat, a Trumpet, a Jaffa, or a Perky Nana? (these are New Zealand foods, I promise you.)** A jetplane.
