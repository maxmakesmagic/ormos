Burning on the outer fringes of Standard are constant reminders of the powers of combo decks. For Daniel Villamizar, the strategy was good enough to take him to the Top 8 of a Pro Tour Qualifier for Amsterdam in Boston. His combo centers around taking tons of extra turns with [Time Sieve](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Sieve), [Time Warp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp), and [Open the Vaults](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Open+the+Vaults). Cards like [Howling Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) and [Prophetic Prism](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prophetic+Prism) help fuel the combo, while [Angelsong](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelsong) and [Pilgrim's Eye](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pilgrim%27s+Eye) provide some stalling along the way. Find a Pro Tour Qualifier near you on the [Amsterdam Qualifiers page](/en/node/639906).
