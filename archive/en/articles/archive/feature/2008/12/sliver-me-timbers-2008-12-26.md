Blake Patrow is the 2008 Legacy Champion, earning the title at this year's Gen Con and doing it on the back of one of the game's most cherished tribes: Slivers! The famous hivelings, first debuting in *Tempest*, become more powerful with each additional copy put into play. If you played every single one of the troublesome critters from Blake's deck you'd have at least a 9/9 flyer with shroud and "pay 2 life: return this creature to its owner's hand." That's enough to send a "sliver" down anyone's spine! Find out which cards from the exciting new set *Shards of Alara* you could add to Blake's deck with the [*Shards of Alara* Visual Spoiler](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/shardsofalara/spoiler).
