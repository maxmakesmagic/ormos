The October 13, 2016 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on October 13, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Nicholas_Wolfram.jpg)
By Nicholas Wolfram




 Nicholas Wolfram fell promptly in love with tabletop and video games when he was only two years old, and he has been writing about them since he figured out how words worked. Now he puts together words about Magic, which he started playing way back in 2000. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on October 13, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by the irrepressible excitement for Pro Tour *Kaladesh*!


Today's Must
============


1. [The Top 8 Most Impactful and Underrated *Kaladesh* Cards](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?ID=13559&writer=Adam+Yurchick&articledate=10-13-2016) | TCG Player | Adam Yurchick

With Pro Tour *Kaladesh* starting tomorrow, Adam offers some last-minute insight on eight ubiquitous *Kaladesh* cards that have already reshaped the Standard metagame, along with eight underutilized cards we may see reshaping the metagame after this weekend.


What to Watch This Weekend
==========================


1. [Pro Tour *Kaladesh*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptkld)

The 2016–17 premier play season heats up this weekend with Pro Tour *Kaladesh* in sunny Honolulu! Coverage on [twitch.tv/magic](http://twitch.tv/magic) starts Friday, October 14 (that's tomorrow) at 9 a.m. local time/12 p.m. PT/3 p.m. ET/7 p.m. UTC. Don't miss it!


After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [Hawaii 3-0](http://www.magictheamateuring.com/mta-episode-194-hawaii-3-0/) | Magic the Amateuring | Meghan Wolf and Maria Bartholdi

Maria leaves Meghan in the cold of Minnesota as she broadcasts live from Honolulu for Pro Tour *Kaladesh*. The pair discusses the many virtues of drafting *Kaladesh* and looks back on [Grand Prix Atlanta](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpatl16) while Maria drinks out of a pineapple. Finally, they bring out some exciting special guests who are getting ready for the Pro Tour!


1. [The Two Decks I Recommend This Weekend](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/the-two-decks-i-recommend-this-weekend/) | ChannelFireball | Josh Silvestri

Josh offers some deck counsel for those who don't want to wait until the Pro Tour to put together their weekend decks.


1. [The 5 Best *Kaladesh* Pauper Cards](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8VK-vO_HuSE) | Tolarian Community College | The Professor

New cards from *Kaladesh* are encouraging innovation and atypical thinking across the Pauper format. Tired of seeing [Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter) everywhere, The Professor brings his top five favorite *Kaladesh* commons for the format to the table, even though that five turns out to be seven (because *Kaladesh* just has too much to talk about).


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/8VK-vO_HuSE>


1. [Against the Odds: Five-Color Panharmonicon](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/against-the-odds-five-color-panharmonicon) | MTG Goldfish | SaffronOlive

SaffronOlive brings some ambitious and against-the-grain deck tech with this five-color list. Based entirely around using [Panharmonicon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Panharmonicon) to amplify and duplicate enters-the-battlefield (ETB) abilities, the deck ended up being the most successful deck in the history of Against the Odd's 50-plus episodes. Intrigued yet?


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/fnTkhx_6gKI?list=PLtLlcD-b2JRGx0F79AhkUZgbXx2g0xPhC>


1. [Lessons from Grand Prix Atlanta](http://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2016/10/lessons-grand-prix-atlanta/) | Hipsters of the Coast | Brendan McNamara

Brendan reflects on his experiences at Grand Prix Atlanta last weekend and how he (and hopefully you) can learn from the misfortunes and missteps of his weekend.


1. [*Kaladesh* Cube Update](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/usmanjamil-10132016-kaladesh-cube-update/) | Gathering Magic | Usman Jamil

Usman analyzes *Kaladesh* scrupulously and gives a comprehensive analysis of the set for Cube on a card-by-card basis. If you're a fan of Cube, don't miss this!


1. [High Energy](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/33841_High-Energy.html) | StarCityGames.com | Matt Higgs

Matt looks at energy, one of the favorite new *Kaladesh* mechanics. He goes over some of the best ways to build up massive stores of energy, before contemplating the best ways to use those stores to crush your opponents.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Gabor Kocsis's Red-White *Kaladesh* Draft Deck](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gplon16/top-8-decks-2016-10-09)

Kocsis drafted a Red-White Aggro deck at [GP London](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gplon16), a popular archetype so far in *Kaladesh*. With a cadre of fast creatures backed by some speedy burn and removal spells, Kocsis was able to take the number-two spot in London without using a single rare.
