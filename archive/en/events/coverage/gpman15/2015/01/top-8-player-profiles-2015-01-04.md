TOP 8 PLAYER PROFILES
=====================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on January 4, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff










After sixteen rounds of Modern, only eight players remained in the running for the title of Grand Prix champion. About time to get to know them a little better, isn't it?


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpman15/T8-Kuo-Tzu-Ching.jpg)Name:**  Tzu-Ching Kuo  





---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpman15/T8-Lee-Shi-Tian.jpg)Name:** Lee Shi Tian  
**City:** Hong Kong  
**Age:** 27  
**Occupation:** Accountant  
**Score for Day One:** 8-1 (3 byes)  
**Score for Day Two:** (4-0-2)  
**Pro Tour and Grand Prix Top 8s:** 3 Pro Tour Top 8s, 5 GP Top 8s (including this one!)  
**Other Achievements:** Last place in Worlds Championship 2014. Hong Kong Captain for 2009, 2013 and 2014.  
**What deck are you playing today and why?**  

Umbrella Revolution, with only a couple of cards different from my PT Honolulu Top 8 list. Sometimes we just need to persist until the day we succeed! (PS. Cheep up Yui!!)  
**What is your MVP this weekend?**  
[Jeskai Ascendancy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jeskai+Ascendancy). Yet I was a fool for not playing maximum [Torment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torment)ing Voice before.
