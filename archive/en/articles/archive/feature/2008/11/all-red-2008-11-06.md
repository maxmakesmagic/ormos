Rashad Miller is a prominent judge, player, and deckbuilder from the American Midwest. While he’s probably best known for his services as a person in stripes (aka as a judge), he does know how to play. He used his version of All-in Red to become the highest finishing player playing the deck at Pro Tour–Berlin. The creation looks to do something “broken” on the first or second turn via ritual effects. Over the course of the weekend that ranged from [Deus of Calamity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deus+of+Calamity) to double [Demigod of Revenge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demigod+of+Revenge) (after discarding one!), to powerful [Blood Moon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon) effects that could lock the non-basic land heavy decks of the format completely out of the game.

For more on Pro Tour–Berlin including blogs, feature matches, podcasts, and video coverage, check out [the official Pro Tour–Berlin event coverage](/en/node/502416)!
