David Price Top 8 Profile
=========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**by Sideboard Staff**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPAMS01/871.jpg)**Name:**David Price   
**Age:** 25   
**Location:**   
**Occupation:** Writer/Magic Player   
**Played Magic since:** 1994   
**Best accomplishments:**Winner of PT LA '98 



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Amsterdam?**  

A: the same thing that makes me decide to travel to Grand Prix Porto, The Magic Invitational in Kuala Lumpur Malaysia or the World Championships in Tokyo: I love the game.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: I was hoping to win. As far as expectations go, I had none. I left my crystal ball at home.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: I've been playing well and drafting well.




**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: I kept winning matches.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: It keeps me on the gravy train and it reaffirms my belief that I'm good at this game.
