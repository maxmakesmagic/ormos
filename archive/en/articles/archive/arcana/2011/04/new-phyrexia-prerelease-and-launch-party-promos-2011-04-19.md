*New Phyrexia* Prerelease and Launch Party Promos
=================================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 19, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











You're excited about *New Phyrexia*. Obviously. It shows in your eyes. Your excited, excited eyes. So you're probably already planning on going to the Prerelease already. Maybe you'll even take in a Launch Party. But you're on the fence. What could convince you? Well, what if your eyes got a look at the promo foils you could get for playing (while supplies last)? Let's find out!

First, for the *New Phyrexia* Prerelease (May 7-8), here's Sheoldred, Whispering One.

![Sheoldred, Whispering One](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/692_2zgxmuwsas.jpg)  
Nice! And for the Launch Party (May 13-16), here's Phyrexian Metamorph!

![Phyrexian Metamorph](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/692_zveytfd3yo.jpg)
