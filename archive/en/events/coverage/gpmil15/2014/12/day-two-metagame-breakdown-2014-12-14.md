DAY TWO METAGAME BREAKDOWN
==========================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX MILAN 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpmil15)
 on December 14, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/olle_rade_author.jpg)
By Olle Rade










The numbers are in. We have dissected all the 210 decklists from the players on Day 2, and the result is exciting to say the least. Delver is the most popular archetype, with an impressive 16,6 per cent of the field. Among those opting to go with Delver, Temur and straight Blue/Red are the most popular with 12 players each, and 8 opting for the Jeskai version.  





| Deck | Players | % of field |
| Delver | 35\* | 16,6 |
| Birthing Pod | 31 | 14,7 |
| Scapeshift | 28 | 13,3 |
| Abzan midrange | 24 | 11,4 |
| Affinity | 16 | 7,6 |
| Burn | 11 | 5,2 |
| Red/Green Scapeshift | 8 | 3,8 |
| Slippery Boggles | 7 | 3,3 |
| Jeskai Ascendancy | 7 | 3,3 |
| Splinter Twin | 6 | 2,8 |
| Zoo | 5 | 2,3 |
| Red/Green Tron | 5 | 2,3 |
| Martyrs | 4 | 1,9 |

\* 12 Temur Delver, 12 Blue/Red Delver and 8 Jeskai Delver.
