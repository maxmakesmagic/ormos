Something for Legacy Veterans
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 14, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson











Any casual observer might not recognize today's deck as a Legacy staple, but it's one of the many decks that exist in Legacy without ever breaking through to the big time. This deck is known as "Nic Fit," but I'd prefer to call it BUG Midrange so players unfamiliar with Legacy have an idea of what the deck is and how the deck works.

![Veteran Explorer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Veteran+Explorer) The deck revolves around finding [Veteran Explorer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Veteran+Explorer) and killing it to get some extra lands, which fuel your late game. There are few Legacy decks that run many basic lands, so you're generally getting the better end of the bargain. [Green Sun's Zenith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Green+Sun%27s+Zenith) provides a toolbox, allowing you to find more lands with [Veteran Explorer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Veteran+Explorer) or [Coiling Oracle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coiling+Oracle), a [Regrowth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Regrowth) via [Eternal Witness](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Witness), or some life with [Thragtusk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thragtusk). 

 If you need a threat, [Thrun, the Last Troll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thrun%2C+the+Last+Troll) is one of the best aroundâ€”I recommend sacrificing [Veteran Explorer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Veteran+Explorer) to your opponent's [Liliana of the Veil](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil). In the late game, Damia, Sage of Stone is a very exciting way to keep the gas flowing. 

 Previous versions of this deck found it difficult to fit [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm); [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor); and [Force of Will](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will), but Fizzeler makes it happen! While it used to suffer due to a poor combo matchup, this version doesn't seem to have those issues.
