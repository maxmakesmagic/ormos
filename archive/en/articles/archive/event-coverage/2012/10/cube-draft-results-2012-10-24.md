Cube Draft Results
==================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)

Here's how the scoring works: There will be three rounds of Swiss pitting a member of one team against a player from the other team. Your first match win in a format is worth 4 points, your second win 6, and third win 7, meaning that a single player can contribute up to 17 points to his or her team's score in a format.
