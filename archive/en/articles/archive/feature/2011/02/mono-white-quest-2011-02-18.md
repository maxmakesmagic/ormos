Hall of Famer Frank Karsten has established himself as the type of player you should listen to when looking for a new deck in Standard. At Pro Tour Paris, he brought a Mono-White Quest deck. His build could quickly abuse [Quest for the Holy Relic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quest+for+the+Holy+Relic), allowing him to transform casting creatures into attacking with something equipped with [Argentum Armor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Argentum+Armor). Read more about Pro Tour Paris, part of **Magic** Weekend Paris, [here](/en/events/coverage/stark-flies-high-planeswalkers-paris).
