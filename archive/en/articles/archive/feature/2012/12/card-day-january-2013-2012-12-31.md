Card of the Day - January, 2013
===============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 31, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley














![Obzedat, Ghost Council](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Obzedat%2C+Ghost+Council)**[Obzedat, Ghost Council](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obzedat%2C+Ghost+Council)** – *Gatecrash* mythic rare. From the [*Gatecrash* FAQ](/en/node/634111):
 When Obzedat is returned to the battlefield by its last ability, it gains haste indefinitely. This may be relevant if a player gains control of it later using a spell or ability that doesn't give it haste. 





![High Priest of Penance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=High+Priest+of+Penance)**[High Priest of Penance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Priest+of+Penance)** – *Gatecrash* rare. From the [*Gatecrash* FAQ](/en/node/634111):
 High Priest of Penance's ability triggers once for each instance of damage dealt to it, not once for each 1 damage. The ability will trigger even if High Priest of Penance is dealt lethal damage. 





![Enter the Infinite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Enter+the+Infinite)**[Enter the Infinite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enter+the+Infinite)** – *Gatecrash* mythic rare. From the [*Gatecrash* FAQ](/en/node/634111):
 If you are playing with the top card of your library revealed (due to [Garruk's Horde](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Garruk%27s+Horde), for example), you'll reveal each card before you draw it. 





![Call of the Nightwing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Call+of+the+Nightwing)**[Call of the Nightwing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Nightwing)** – *Gatecrash* uncommon. From the [*Gatecrash* FAQ](/en/node/634111):
 You can exile [Call of the Nightwing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Nightwing) encoded on the Horror creature token it just created.
