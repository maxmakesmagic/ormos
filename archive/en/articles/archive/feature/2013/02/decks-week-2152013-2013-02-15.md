Decks of the Week: 2/15/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 15, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations to Timothee Simonot, who drafted his way to victory at Grand Prix London! And speaking of *Gatecrash* draft, today marks the beginning of  [Pro Tour *Gatecrash*](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/gatecrash13)! Do you dream of making it to the Pro Tour and playing against the best in the game? Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#dgm) and check out recent PTQ results to see what you might face! Good luck; have fun!
