Announcing *Shadowmoor*
=======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 4, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**Magic**'s May 2008 large set, code-named "Jelly" and the first set of the "Jelly / Doughnut" block, is called *Shadowmoor*.

![Shadowmoor Logo](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1433_ShadowmoorLogo.jpg)

| --- | --- |

| **Design Team** | Mark Rosewater (lead) Sean Fletcher Mark Gottlieb Devin Low Ken Troop |
| **Development Team** | Aaron Forsythe (lead) Devin Low Alexis Janson Matt Place Jake Theis Steve Warner Doug Beyer |
