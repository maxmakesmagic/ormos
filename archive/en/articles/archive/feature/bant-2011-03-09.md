Bant continues to play a big role in the world of Extended, sending many a player on a free ride to Nagoya, Japan during this Pro Tour Qualifier season. Magic Online user reiderrabbit is the latest such beneficiary, taking the top slot in a recent online event using the blue, white, and green strategy. This list is similar to one we ran last week, but the differences are important. [Hero of Bladehold](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero+of+Bladehold), [Finest Hour](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Finest+Hour), and the singleton [Baneslayer Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel) are out, the powerful combo of [Stoneforge Mystic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic) and [Sword of Feast and Famine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+Feast+and+Famine) is in, and the Extended metagame continues to evolve....
