Looking for a deck that puts some serious hurt on players using black or red? Joel Allen's qualifying list for U.S. Nationals is right up your alley! It plays all of white's best spells, like [Day of Judgment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Day+of+Judgment), [Oblivion Ring](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Ring), and [Path to Exile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile), as well as a creature engine powered by [Emeria, the Sky Ruin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emeria%2C+the+Sky+Ruin). Build up to seven [Plains](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains) with [Pilgrim's Eye](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pilgrim%27s+Eye) and [Kor Cartographer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Cartographer), then start recurring [Wall of Omens](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens) to draw extra cards or [Lone Missionary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lone+Missionary) to gain life. Opponent cast [Spreading Seas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spreading+Seas) on your [Emeria, the Sky Ruin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Emeria%252C%2Bthe%2BSky%2BRuin)? Bounce it with [Kor Skyfisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Skyfisher)! See more National Qualifier deck lists [here](/en/events/coverage/2010-national-qualifiers-top-8-standard-deck-lists).
