The latest member of **Magic** R&D is Dave Humpherys, and he definitely earned his spot! The Pro Tour Hall of Famer belonged to arguably the game's most famous team, Your Move Games, and has numerous top finishes on his resume. At Pro Tourâ€“Rome in 1999, home to the infamous [Tolarian Academy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tolarian+Academy) and [High Tide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Tide) decks, Dave took another path and finished 9th with "Free Whaley." The deck centered around a ruling at the time that allowed [Recurring Nightmare](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Recurring+Nightmare) and [Great Whale](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Great+Whale) in the graveyard to generate infinite mana. Once you could do that, you could switch Nightmare targets to [Merfolk Traders](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Traders) to draw your deck, [Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion) to kill your opponent, or simply cast [Stroke of Genius](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stroke+of+Genius) to deck an unassuming competitor.
