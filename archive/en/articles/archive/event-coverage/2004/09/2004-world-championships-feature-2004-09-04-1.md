### Sebastian Zink


**Draft position? (A, B, or C?)** A


**Age:** 19


**Hometown:** Bochum


**Occupation:** Starting Civil Service on September 16th


**Number of times playing on the Pro Tour:** Never before.


**How did you do in the individual event?** 14th


**Number of GP, PT Top 8's:** 0


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** Beat up a lot on my brother [former World Champion Daniel Zink] while playtesting. 12 PTQ top 8s without ever winning one.


**What was your personal record on**   
**Day 1:** 4-2  
**Day 2:** 4-1-1  
**Day 3:** 4-2  
**Day 4:** 4-0


**What do you think of your chances on Sunday?**  

 We already beat the guys from Belgium, so I think we're all right.


**Do you have any interesting stories from the team draft day?**  

 I had [Salvaging Station](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Salvaging+Station) in play with [Conjurer's Bauble](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conjurer%27s+Bauble) in the graveyard and played [Flamebreak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flamebreak), decimating my opponent's board while saving my own fleshgrafter and drawing four extra cards.
