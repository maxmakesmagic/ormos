One of the surprise hits in Standard these days are decks centered around [Sanity Grinding](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sanity+Grinding). Bill Stark took that idea one step further by adding [Howling Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) to his build. While opponents are forced to discard all the extra cards they're drawing, [Reliquary Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reliquary+Tower) ensures Bill will have as big a hand as he wants! [Boomerang](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boomerang), [Evacuation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Evacuation), [Cryptic Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command), and [Plumeveil](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plumeveil) keep creatures contained and for the grand finale? [Sanity Grinding](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sanity+Grinding) copied by [Twincast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Twincast)!
