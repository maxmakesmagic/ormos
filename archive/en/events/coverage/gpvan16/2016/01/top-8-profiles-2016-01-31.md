 TOP 8 PROFILES
===============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 31, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Melissa_DeTora.jpg)
By Melissa DeTora




 A former Magic Pro player and strategy writer, Melissa's career Top 8s include PT Gatecrash and 2 GPs. She's currently a development intern at WotC. 






Learn more about Grand Prix Vancouver's Top 8 competitors by taking a look through their profiles!  

 




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpvan16/t8_barnes.jpg)Name:** Gabe Carleton-Barnes


**Age:** 36


**Occupation:** Software Developer


**Hometown:** Portland, Oregon


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**  

12 PT Day 2s. Many PTQ wins. Once beat a [Pack Rat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat) in Limited.


**What color combination was your Sealed Deck, and what was your record?**  

W/R/b/g- 6-1


**What was your best card in Sealed and why?**  

Goblin Dark Dwellers because I had two [Devour in Flames](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devour+in+Flames).


**What color combinations were your draft decks, and what was your record?**  

B/<>/r: 3-0 W/B/r: 2-1


**What was your best card in each draft and why?**  
[Transgress the Mind](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Transgress+the+Mind) because it hit bombs and triggered the three [Sky Scourer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sky+Scourer)s, and [Resolute Blademaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Resolute+Blademaster) with fifteen allies.


**What card surprised you the most this weekend?**  
[Visions of Brutality](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visions+of+Brutality). It was surprisingly effective.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpvan16/t8_sun.jpg)  

Name:** Allen Sun


**Age:** 37


**Occupation:** Software Engineer


**Hometown:** Alexandria, Virginia


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**  

Attended Pro Tour Vancouver and was one match win away from Top 8 at GP Salt Lake City.


**What color combination was your Sealed Deck, and what was your record?**  

U/B splash W- 9-0


**What was your best card in Sealed and why?**  

Seer’s Lantern. It won me many games when the game went long.


**What color combinations were your draft decks, and what was your record?**  

W/R 2-1, U/G 2-0-1


**What was your best card in each draft and why?**  

In first draft it was [Fall of the Titans](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fall+of+the+Titans) because it just kills your opponent.  

In second draft it was Seer’s Lantern because with it I was always drawing gas.


**What card surprised you the most this weekend?**  

Seer’s Lantern for the better.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpvan16/t8_slind.jpg)**


**Name:** Nick Slind


**Age:** 35


**Occupation:** Software Engineer


**Hometown:** Spokane, Washington


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Top 64 Grand Prix Tacoma


**What color combination was your Sealed deck, and what was your record?**  

R/B/U, 9-0


**What was your best card in Sealed, and why?**  
[Chandra, Flamecaller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Flamecaller) because she dominates at any stage in the game.  
  
**What color combinations were your draft decks, and what was your record?**  

W/B/G, 2-1  

R/B, 2-0-1


**What was your best card in EACH draft, and why?**  
[Felidar Sovereign](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Felidar+Sovereign), he can stabilize against a quick aggro draw;  
[Rolling Thunder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rolling+Thunder), its versatility in finishing the game or pulling ahead.


**What card surprised you the most this weekend?**  
[Relief Captain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Relief+Captain), thought it was OK; it’s a bomb.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpvan16/t8_hewitt.jpg)  

Name:** Chris Hewitt


**Age:** 34


**Occupation:** City of Calgary


**Hometown:** Calgary, Alberta


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

A few Grand Prix Day 2s. Provincial/State Champion. Not much else.


**What color combination was your Sealed deck, and what was your record?**  

BUwg + colorless, greedy. 9-0


**What was your best card in Sealed, and why?**  
[Blinding Drone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blinding+Drone), it lets me get to all my bombs.


**What color combinations were your draft decks, and what was your record?**  

Draft 1: White with a little bit blue and Nissa’s Judgment, 2-1.  

Draft 2: White-Black Ally Fliers, 2-1.


**What was your best card in EACH draft, and why?**  

Draft 1: [Hero of Goma Fada](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero+of+Goma+Fada) won every game I cast him in.  

Draft 2: Roil’s [Retribution](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Retribution) was cast twice and killed about seven things.


**What card surprised you the most this weekend?**  
[Vampire Envoy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampire+Envoy) was even better than I thought.
