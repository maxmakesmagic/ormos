Sidisi-Whip at Sunday Super Series
==================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on February 11, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/shuhei_authorpic.jpg)
By Shuhei Nakamura




Shuhei Nakamura is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, former Player of the Year, and is one of just six players with more than 400 lifetime pro points. 






I know I talked a lot about [Tasigur, the Golden Fang](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tasigur%2C+the+Golden+Fang) last week. But he isn't only good in Modern. A 4/5 that can be played on turn three is a huge threat in Modern, but even more so in Standard. Of course, it does need a little help to do so.



Sidisi Whip is the perfect home for Tasigur with the combination of fetch lands and discard spells, similar to that of a Modern deck. He can also utilize unnecessary cards in the graveyard from [Satyr Wayfinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Satyr+Wayfinder) and support the deck by putting cards into the graveyard with his own ability.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Tasigur%2C+the+Golden+Fang)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tasigur%2C+the+Golden+Fang)

Another fascinating card is the single [Torrent Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrent+Elemental).



I love delving it to cast Tasigur after milling it from an early Wayfinder, and then later summoning it from the exile zone or returning it from [Whip of Erebos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whip+of+Erebos).



This deck can really abuse this rare ability.



It's also a good size and can open a path for an attacking [Sidisi, Brood Tyrant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sidisi%2C+Brood+Tyrant) with its tapping ability.
