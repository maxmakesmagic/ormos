[Ajani Vengeant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani+Vengeant), [Ranger of Eos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ranger+of+Eos), [Elspeth, Knight-Errant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant), and [Knight-Captain of Eos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight-Captain+of+Eos) are just a few of the new cards **Magic Online** fan MrCriticalError used to earn a Top 8 finish in a recent premier event. The recent State Championships have created a host of innovative Standard decklists for players to enjoy, with the big question on the minds of many being "Which deck(s) will conquer the upcoming World Championships?" Whether it looks like the list posted here, we'll have to wait and see but you catch everything live as it happens from **magicthegathering.com** all weekend long. Worlds kicks off Thursday, December 11 and [the live webcast](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/webcast/home) is set to kick off from Memphis at 9:45 a.m. on December 14th.
