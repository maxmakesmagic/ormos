### DAILY DECK


Focus on Locus
==============



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)

Adam Prosak

From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer.


July 15, 2014
 







Legacy is home to many different "tutor" effects, or effects that can search your library for some class of card. Most of these are fairly narrow, and only get a small subset of cards. Today's deck focuses on searching for lands. [Expedition Map](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Expedition+Map), [Crop Rotation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crop+Rotation), and [Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan) are all cards that can let you find any type of land. Together, they help assemble a powerful mana base with a wide variety of abilities.


![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Cloudpost)
The trio of [Cloudpost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudpost), [Glimmerpost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimmerpost), and [Vesuva](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vesuva) (copying one of the former) will be your main targets, helping you to assemble a large amount of mana, enough to cast an [Eldrazi Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&type=+[%22Legendary%22]&subtype=+[%22Eldrazi%22]) of your choice. If you have your mana but not the Eldrazi, then [Eye of Ugin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eye+of+Ugin) can do the trick. [Cavern of Souls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cavern+of+Souls) can help force them through counterspells, while [Karakas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karakas) can allow you to recur the triggers on the Eldrazi. Few things in life are better than bouncing your own Emrakul each turn just to recast it and take all of the turns.


But the fun doesn't stop there! [Dark Depths](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Depths) and [Thespian's Stage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thespian%27s+Stage) can combine for a quick 20/20, [Bojuka Bog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bojuka+Bog) can protect you from opposing graveyard shenanigans, and [Glacial Chasm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glacial+Chasm) or [The Tabernacle at Pendrell Vale](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Tabernacle+at+Pendrell+Vale) from the sideboard can put a halt to opposing creature swarms. This deck takes advantage of its lands like few other decks!
