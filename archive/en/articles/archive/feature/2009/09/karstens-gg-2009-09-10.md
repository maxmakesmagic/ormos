[Greater Good](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Greater+Good) was an almost-ran rare when it was reprinted in time for the 2005 World Championships. Newly selected Hall of Fame member Frank Karsten didn't let that stop him from using it to great effect, making it all the way to the Finals at that event before losing to Katsuhiro Mori. The finish was just one of many great performances in his stellar career that included building powerful strategies around equally inane cards like [Greater Good](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Greater+Good).
