Pro Tour Gauntlet: Shadows over Innistrad
=========================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on April 26, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Pro Tour *Shadows over Innistrad*'s Top 8 featured some great players, each with a different deck. In keeping with the theme of *Shadows over Innistrad*, the Pro Tour Gauntlet will feature 13 decks, including all top 8 decks plus 5 additional decks that performed well. Sign up for the Pro Tour Gauntlet and test drive a random one of these decks today!


Pro Tour Gauntlet events for Pro Tour: *Shadows over Innistrad* run from the April 27 downtime until May 4. Event details and available decklists can be found below!


* [Pro Tour Gauntlet Event Details](#PTGauntletSOIEvents)
* [Pro Tour Gauntlet Decklists](#Decks)



---


**Pro Tour Gauntlet**


**Start Times:** Wednesday, April 27, after downtime until Wednesday, May 4, downtime.  
**Location:** Play Lobby -> Constructed Tournaments -> Queues  
**Entry Option:**


* 6 Event Tickets
* 60 Play Points

**Product:** One randomly-selected phantom deck from the Pro Tour Gauntlet decklists. Phantom product will not be added to a player's collection at the conclusion of the event.  
**Size:** 8 players  
**Play Style:** Swiss  
**Format:** Standard  
**Duration:** 3 minutes deck review. Three rounds, each round up to 50 minutes.  
**Prizes:**




| Place | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 3 Wins | 90 Play Points | 1 |
| 2 Wins | 60 Play Points | 0 |
| 1 Win | 20 Play Points | 0 |

**NOTE:** Wizards of the Coast employees and other persons affiliated with it are eligible to play in this event. For more information, please see [this article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/wizards-announces-wizards-play-policy-2015-01-30) and [this FAQ](https://wizards.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/2312) about this policy.
