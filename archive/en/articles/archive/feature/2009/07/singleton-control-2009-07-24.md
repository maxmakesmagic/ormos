A format for fun, 100 Card Singleton has a simple rule: each deck has to play 100 cards, and you may only play one copy of each card that's not a basic land. ChrisKool took that concept to a whole new level with a control deck that featured a whopping two creatures. Still, the creatures that were played were significant threats in the form of [Simic Sky Swallower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Simic+Sky+Swallower) and [Empyrial Archangel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Empyrial+Archangel). When your attackers are that big, you don't need a lot of them to win!
