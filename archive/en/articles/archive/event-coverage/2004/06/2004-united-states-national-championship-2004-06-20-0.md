2004 United States National Championship
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 20, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












After two days of competition and sixteen marathon rounds of swiss, Jeff Garza is the 2004 JSS Champion! Garza, who piloted a unique mono-green Tooth and Nail deck sporting the [Urzatron](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urzatron) and zero copies of [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), takes home some impressive bragging rights as well as a $20,000 scholarship. Joining him in the top 8 are the following players:


![Jeff Garza](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/usnat04/jsschamp.jpg)Top 8 Final Standing
--------------------




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |

| 2. **Kyle Goodman** | $10,000 |
| 3. **Kristian Browne** | $6,000 |
| 4. **Marshall Putnam** | $6,000 |
| 5. **Jonathan Watson** | $3,500 |
| 6. **Tim McMath** | $3,500 |
