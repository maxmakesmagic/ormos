### DAILY DECK


Prowess in Legacy
=================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/shuhei_authorpic.jpg)

Shuhei Nakamura

Shuhei Nakamura is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, former Player of the Year, and is one of just six players with more than 400 lifetime pro points.


October 14, 2014
 










While it is to be expected that new cards grab the spotlight at the Pro Tour, the shockwave generated by the arrival of *Khans of Tarkir* is reaching beyond just Standard.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Monastery Swiftspear)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Monastery Swiftspear)
"Prowess." The Jeskai keyword ability gives a small temporary bonus to creatures whenever you cast a noncreature spell. This ability truly comes into its own in an environment where there is a rich variety of cheap draw spells.


An environment like, oh, Legacy.


An environment with what is arguably the supreme draw spell, [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm). An environment with [Preordain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Preordain) and [Ponder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ponder), spells so powerful they were banned in Modern. Oh, and let's not forget the zero-mana [Gitaxian Probe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gitaxian+Probe), or [Thought Scour](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought+Scour), which let you pad your graveyard as well.


With a veritable constellation to choose from, it's easy to set up a cascade of draw spells. This is what leads us to [Bob Huang's UR combo deck here](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/cruising-to-victory-in-new-jersey/), which looks to win with a single, decisive stroke.


In the past, this deck would have looked to green for an extra punch, but with the expansion of the one-mana stratum through the addition of [Delver of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets) and [Monastery Swiftspear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Monastery+Swiftspear), it can streamline down to two colors and improve its reliability.
