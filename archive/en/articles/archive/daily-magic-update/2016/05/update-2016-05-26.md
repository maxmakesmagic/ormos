Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Buuuuuuurrrrrrrrnnnnnnn](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/displaydeck.php?DeckID=102918)

With previews for both [Chain Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+Lightning) and [Price of Progress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Price+of+Progress) coming over the past few days, there's been a lot of talk about Legacy Burn serving as an entry point for a lot of players, now that a number of the cards in the deck are far more accessible.


In fact, a lot of people are seeing the upcoming availability of [Chain Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+Lightning) and [Price of Progress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Price+of+Progress) and noticing that Legacy Burn has had a lot of its cards printed recently. Let's take a look at Eric Miller's 13th-place deck from the last StarCityGames.com Legacy Classic as an example.


Of the creatures, [Grim Lavamancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer), [Monastery Swiftspear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Monastery+Swiftspear), and [Eidolon of the Great Revel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eidolon+of+the+Great+Revel) have all been printed in the past few years. [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) is an outlier, and can be pretty difficult to find these days.


[Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) is readily available, as are [Lava Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lava+Spike) and [Rift Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rift+Bolt), all three having been printed in various *Modern Masters* sets. [Fireblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireblast) hasn't been in a regular booster printing in a while, but it was in *Duel Decks: Jace vs. Chandra*. [Exquisite Firecraft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exquisite+Firecraft), meanwhile, is currently in Standard. The sideboard is even pretty locked up, with [Pyroblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroblast) seeing print in *Eternal Masters*, [Smash to Smithereens](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smash+to+Smithereens) also currently in Standard, and [Searing Blaze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Searing+Blaze) available in *Duel Decks: Venser vs. Koth*.


[Sulfuric Vortex](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sulfuric+Vortex) has had a few printings as well—in *Duel Decks: Sorin vs. Tibalt* and the first *Conspiracy* release—but more copies are always nice.


So let's do that.
