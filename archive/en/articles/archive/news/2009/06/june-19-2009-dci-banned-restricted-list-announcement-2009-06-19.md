June 19 2009 DCI Banned & Restricted List Announcement
======================================================



 Posted in **News**
 on June 19, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**Announcement Date:** June 19, 2009  
**Effective Date:** July 1, 2009

**Vintage**  
[Thirst for Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thirst+for+Knowledge) is restricted

[Crop Rotation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crop+Rotation) is no longer restricted  
[Enlightened Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enlightened+Tutor) is no longer restricted  
[Entomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Entomb) is no longer restricted  
[Grim Monolith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Monolith) is no longer restricted

**Standard, Extended, Legacy, *Shards of Alara* Block Constructed**
