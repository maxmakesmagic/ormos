Tribes in *Mirrodin*
====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 11, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












The artifact-heavy impact of *Mirrodin* has begun tearing up metagames, but the tribal mechanics of the *Onslaught* block still hold sway in Standard and casual play alike. *Mirrodin* is the artifact block, but it still meshes with today's tribal-icious environment nicely (even if you don't count the [Replica cycle](/en/articles/archive/mirrodin-playtest-cards-ii-2003-11-03)).


Here's a master list of all the *Mirrodin* creatures belonging to the eight major *Onslaught* block tribes, ranked by number of creatures in each tribe. Enjoy integrating *Mirrodin* into your tribal decks.
