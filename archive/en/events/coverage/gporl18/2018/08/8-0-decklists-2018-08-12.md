The 8-0 Decks of Grand Prix Orlando 2018
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 12, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Craig-Jones-Author-Photo-150x150_0.jpg)
By Craig Jones











997 players turned up to play Standard at Grand Prix Orlando. After 8 rounds of play, 7 of them ended the day with perfect 8-0 records. They were Jacob Tilk, Luke Feeney, Patrick Tilsen, Andrew Calderon, Vincent Daniels, and Kyle Cooper. Mountains (and [Goblin Chainwhirler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Chainwhirler)s) continued to be well represented, but we also had some variety in Mono-Blue Storm and Esper Control to round out the undefeated bracket.
