Pro Tour Kobe 2004 starred the original *Mirrodin* Block Constructed format. The big deck at the event? Affinity. The powerful combination of [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), [Disciple of the Vault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+the+Vault), and [Arcbound Ravager](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcbound+Ravager) was very tough to beat. Ben Stark played this version of the archetype into the Top 8 long before the Phyrexians invaded. Read more about the event from the coverage archives [here](/en/events/coverage/live-coverage-2004-pro-tour-kobe).
