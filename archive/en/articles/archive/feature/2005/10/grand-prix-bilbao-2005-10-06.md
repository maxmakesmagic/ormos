Grand Prix - Bilbao
===================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 6, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The **Magic: The Gathering** Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro Tour events.

**Win a trip to Grand Prix Lille**  
 ...and many other **Magic** prizes!  
 At Grand Prix Bilbao fill out the voucher\* that you'll find at the registration desk and deposit it in the designated box Saturday, November 19 before 5 pm. A draw will be held on Saturday, November 19 - at 6pm.

* 1st prize: Trip to Grand Prix Lille  

	+ Plane ticket to and from the GP
	+ 3 hotel nights (breakfast included)
	+ 1 free side event per day (2 in total)
* 2nd - 10th prize: A Grand Prix Sweater.
* 11nd - 20th prize: Free side event voucher (to be used at any European GP in 2005)

\* Only 1 entry per player.

### Pre-Registration

There is no pre-registration necessary for this event because it is open for everyone to enter.  


### Tournament Format And Schedule

The format for Grand Prix–Bilbao is *Extended*. All DCI Constructed-format rules, **Magic** Floor Rules, and all subsequent updates will be enforced.

**Friday, November 18**: Registration from 4:00 – 8:00 PM.  
**Saturday, November 19**: Registration from 8:00 - 9:00 A.M.  
 Tournament begins at 10:00 A.M. The Top 64 (or 128) players advance to competition on Sunday   
**Sunday, November 20**: The Top 64 (or 128) players begin play at 8:00 A.M.

### Tournament

The main tournament at Grand Prix–Bilbao is open to **Magic** players of all ages.  


* **Tournament Structure**  
 The structure for Grand Prix–Bilbao will be modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds), with the Top 64 players (or 128 if 800 or more players registered for the main event) advancing to the second day of competition for prizes and invitations. The top 16 Players will receive invitations to the Pro Tour in Honolulu
* **Tournament Divisions and Prizes**  
 Grand Prix–Bilbao will feature the standard “main-tournament” prize schedule as well as a special “Amateur" prize schedule. The Amateur prizes will be paid to the highest-finishing amateur players *in addition to any winnings those players receive from the main tournament prize schedule.* All prizes are in U.S. Dollars.

* **Amateur Status**  
 A player is considered to have amateur status if he or she has 0 lifetime pro points. If a player has ever been awarded ANY pro points from a Grand Prix, Pro Tour, or World Championship event, that player no longer has amateur status, may not participate in the Amateur Championship or Junior Super Series tournaments, and is not eligible for amateur awards at Grand Prix or other tournaments.
* **Tournament Byes** (*Byes are not cumulative throughout the season or year*)  
 Byes will be given to players in the Grand Prix based on the following criteria:  
***Third-Round Byes***  

	+ Players with a Players Club level of 3 or higher
	+ DCI players with a Constructed rating of 2025 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed ratings published on September 7, 2005.***Second-Round Byes***  

	+ Players with a Players Club level of 2
	+ DCI players with a Constructed rating of 1925 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed ratings published on September 7, 2005.***First-Round Byes***  

	+ Players with a Players Club level of 1
	+ DCI players with a Constructed rating of 1850 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed ratings published on September 7, 2005.
