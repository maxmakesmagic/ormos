Round Fourteen Feature Match:
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 25, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpoka04/r14fm1en1.jpg)For most players, a successful tournament will lead to one key match towards the end. For Hirabayashi and Lee, that match is now. The winner will most likely make Top 8, and the loser will probably have to be content with a money finish. Both players shuffled up and prepared to play, but were picked for a deck check. Hirabayashi got up and walked around, while Lee sat at the feature match table, obviously a bit nervous and taking a moment to focus. When Hirabayashi and the decks came back, the tone at the table was nothing short of completely serious. ### Game 1

Hirabayashi accelerated with [Mox Diamond](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Diamond) on his first turn, using it to play a [Burning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish) that searched up [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive). Lee summoned [Skirk Prospector](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Prospector), and Hirabayashi used [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive) to put [Sutured Ghoul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sutured+Ghoul) and two [Krosan Cloudscraper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Cloudscraper)s into his graveyard. With this kind of explosive draw, time was not on Lee's side. He attacked with his Prospector and had no other plays. Hirabayashi cast [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), and used [Burning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish) a turn later to get [Exhume](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exhume), putting a 26/26 Ghoul into play. Lee seemed to have a great out in [Living Death](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+Death), but Hirabayashi responded with [Fling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fling) to take the first game.

Hirabayashi- 1 Lee- 0

### Game 2

Hirabayashi mulliganed down to six. Lee had [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) to take away [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive), leaving only [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) and lands in Hirabayashi's hand. Lee played a Prospector, and used a Matron on the following turn to get [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander). Hirabayashi had played [Bloodstained Mire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodstained+Mire), and used it with the [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) to improve the card quality of his hand. He then cast [Burning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish), searching up [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive). Lee attacked for two with his Goblins, and sacrificed the Matron to the Prospector to get the Commander out. Hirabayashi played [Damping Matrix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Damping+Matrix), which would slow down Lee's assault a bit by nullifying the Commander's special ability. With the help of [Mox Diamond](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Diamond) and [City of Traitors](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Traitors), he was able to cast [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive) in the same turn. This left him with one card in hand, and one turn away from a win. Lee attacked, and cast [Haunting Echoes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haunting+Echoes), which got a shock out of Hirabayashias he scooped.

Hirabayashi- 1 Lee- 1

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpoka04/r14fm1en2.jpg)Once again, Hirabayashi mulliganed his opening hand. He played a second turn [Burning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish) to get [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive), and Lee played a Wish of his own to get [Decompose](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decompose). This set Hirabayashi back quite a bit, as he would need [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) or enough mana to play [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive) and a reanimation spell in the same turn. A few turns later, Lee cast [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) to get [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive) out of Hirabayashi's hand, leaving him with [Polluted Delta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta), [Fling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fling), [Sutured Ghoul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sutured+Ghoul) and [Mystical Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor). He attacked with his lone creature, a [Goblin Piledriver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Piledriver), and cast a second. The Piledrivers attacked, and a Matron fetched [Goblin Warchief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Warchief). The Matron was now sacrificed to flash back [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), and Hirabayashi responded with [Mystical Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor) for [Burning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish). The Therapy now resolved, getting [Fling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fling) out of Hirabayashi's hand and leaving him with [Sutured Ghoul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sutured+Ghoul) and a land. He drew and played the Wish, getting [Pyroclasm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm) to kill the Piledrivers. Lee simply cast [Living Death](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+Death) and brought everyone back, including the Matron to gain even more card advantage. This was enough for Hirabayashi to concede, sending Lee to the Top 8.

Hirabayashi- 1 Lee- 2
