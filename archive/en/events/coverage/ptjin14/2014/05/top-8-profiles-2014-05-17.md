Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 17, 2014

By Event Coverage Staff











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptjou14/Top8.jpg)
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptjou14/top8_chapin.jpg)

### Patrick Chapin


**Age:** 33  
**Hometown:** Denver, CO


**Limited Record:**  

5-1


**Constructed Record:**  

8-1-1


**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**  

4 PT Top 8s, 3 GP Top 8s, **Magic** Pro Tour Hall of Fame


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**  

B/G and B/W. Both were good but not great. Very tough table on day 2, so happy to escape 5-1.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**  

Junk Rares. It has the most bombs, Green for fixing, Black for removal, and White for powerful threats.


**What, if any, Constructed decks surprised you this weekend?**  

None.


**What has been your best memory of this event so far?**  

Seeing my CFB Pantheon teammates Reid Duke and Jamie Parke top 8.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**  

An invitation to compete in the World Championship. My dream in **Magic** is to be the World Champion and Three wins tomorrow qualifies me for a shot.


**Who did you prepare with?**  

ChannelFireball Pantheon – Reid Duke, Jamie Parke, Tom Martell, Jon Finkel, Kai Budde, Sam Black, Owen Turtenwald, Andrew Cuneo, William Jensen, Jelger Wiegersma, Gabriel Nassif, Matt Sperling, Gaudenis Vidugiris, Zvi Mowshowitz





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptjou14/top8_cifka.jpg)

### Stanislav Cifka


**Age:** 26  
**Hometown:** Český Krumlov


**Limited Record:**  

5-1


**Constructed Record:**  

7-2-1


**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**  

Pro Tour Return to Ravnica Champion

2-1 BG; 3-0 UG – both decks were solid but without any bombs.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**  

RW Heroic, I wanted to play a pro-active deck.


**What, if any, Constructed decks surprised you this weekend?**  

None


**What has been your best memory of this event so far?**  

When I realized that [Fall of the Hammer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fall+of+the+Hammer) for 4 doesn’t kill Polukranos.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**  

I don’t know yet, but it would be super sweet winning another one.


**Who did you prepare with?**  

Czech guys + Ivan Floch. They were great teammates.





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptjou14/top8_duke.jpg)

### Reid Duke


**Age:** 24  
**Hometown:** Sugar Loaf, NY


**Limited Record:**  

6-0


**Constructed Record:**  

6-3-1


**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**  

10 GP Top 8s2011 **Magic Online** Champion


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**  

BG both times. Both very solid, but not insane.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**  

BUG Control. It featured many of the best cards in the format and is the best deck for [Prognostic Sphinx](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prognostic+Sphinx) .


**What, if any, Constructed decks surprised you this weekend?**  

Stanislav Cifka and Ivan Floch playing WR Heroic.


**What has been your best memory of this event so far?**  

Making my first Top 8 alongside my friends, teammates and role models: Patrick Chapin and Jamie Parke.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**  

Everything. The achievement of a lifetime.


**Who did you prepare with?**  

CFB: Pantheon

### Andrea Mengucci


**Age:** 20  
**Hometown:** Senigallia Italy


**Limited Record:**  

6-0


**Constructed Record:**  

6-3-1


**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**  

Top 16 GP Verona, Top 12 GP Strasbourg, 4 Pro Tour Day 2 Appearances


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**  

Both were UG, very similar decks with tempo cards, flash creatures and good late game.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**  

Naya, it beat the aggro deck, like Mono-Red, WU, Mono-Black, but I’ve never played against aggro.


**What, if any, Constructed decks surprised you this weekend?**  

UB Inspired, Levy is a master in new brew.


**What has been your best memory of this event so far?**  

When I beat Reid Duke in Round 12, going 11-1, he’s my favorite player and I was so happy to beat him.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**  

A dream come true, and so much money that I definitely don’t know how to spend.


**Who did you prepare with?**  

It was split between Italians and Americans. With Giusti and the other Italians in Warsaw and the team Through the Gate, with captain Mike Sigrist here in Atlanta





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptjou14/top8_nam.jpg)

### Nam Sung Wook


**Age:** 32  
**Hometown:** South Korea


**Limited Record:**  

5-1-0


**Constructed Record:**  

7-2-0


**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**  

GP Melbourne 2014 Champion, GP Beijing 2014 Top 8


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**  

Draft 1: Black Red. Much removal and good creatures. Draft 2: Red Green. Fatties and ramp


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**  

Junk Enchantress, because we predicted the wrong metagame.


**What, if any, Constructed decks surprised you this weekend?**  

BUG Control. In our playtest, it loses to Junk Enchantress and Mono Black.


**What has been your best memory of this event so far?**  

Round 14, chaining [Eidolon of Blossoms](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eidolon+of+Blossoms) into [Courser of Kruphix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courser+of+Kruphix) into [Herald of Torment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Herald+of+Torment) to kill the opponent’s Elspeth.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**  

It is my second Pro Tour. I did not make Day 2 at the first one. This would prove my play skill.


**Who did you prepare with?**  

Team MTG Mint Card (Kuo Tzu Ching, Chapman Sim, Lee Shi Tian, Park Jun Young, Yam Wing Chun, Kevin Chew, Ryan Young, Goa Tam, Han Bing). Good friends and good team. Thank you to my team!

### Josh Utter-Leyton


**Age:** 28  
**Hometown:** Rohnert Park, CA


**Limited Record:**  

4-2


**Constructed Record:**  

8-1-1


**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**  

Reigning Player of the Year, 4 Pro Tour Top 8s, US National Champion


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**  

UB. The deck was good, but not great, but it went 3-0.RG. The deck was atrocious and I went 1-2.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**  

BUG Control. It’s playing as many of the format’s best cards as possible, and they form a coherent and consistent strategy.


**What, if any, Constructed decks surprised you this weekend?**  

The biggest surprise was the Thassa’s Ire infinite combo getting played. It was something we had joked about but never considered it as something you could realistically do.


**What has been your best memory of this event so far?**  

Hearing my name called for the Top 8.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**  

It’s what all of the weeks of preparation and travel and years and years of learning are for. I’m here to join the storied list of Pro Tour champions.


**Who did you prepare with?**  

ChannelFireball - Luis Scott-Vargas, David Ochoa, Brian Kibler, Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa, Eric Froehlich, Pat Cox, Frank Karsten, Martin Juza, Shuhei Nakamura, Bob Maher, Ben Stark, Paul Rietzl, and Shahar Shenhar.
