*Innistrad* Flashback Draft
---------------------------


We're kicking off a triple *Innistrad* Flashback Draft to celebrate Halloween! This draft will actually start *on* Halloween, making it all the more spooky. Triple *Innistrad* Draft runs through November 7, so you'll have plenty of time to enjoy the creepiest of creatures.

PTQ Finals
----------


On Saturday and Sunday at 7 a.m. PT, we're having PTQ Finals events featuring *Guilds of Ravnica* Sealed. Enter this event with a Chandra or Jace Limited PTQ Finals Entry Token.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

Downtimes October 24 and December 5
-----------------------------------


The next two downtimes will be October 24, from 3 a.m. to noon PT (UTC -7) and December 5, 3 a.m. to noon PT. Please note that there are no more "extended" or "normal" downtimes; in the new world with fewer downtimes, they're all the same length of time.

Build Notes
-----------


* General – Various issues with split cards are now fixed and they can correctly be placed into libraries.
* Standard, Limited – [Vance's Blasting Cannons](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vance%27s+Blasting+Cannons) and [Piston-Fist Cyclops](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Piston-Fist+Cyclops) now correctly see when you cast cards owned by your opponent.
* Standard, Limited – [Gruesome Menagerie](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gruesome+Menagerie) now always returns the correct converted mana cost worth of creatures to the battlefield.
* Standard – [Skirk Prospector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Prospector) no longer allows you to double dip a Goblin for mana while casting a spell with convoke.
* Standard, Limited – [Etrata, the Silencer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Etrata%2C+the+Silencer) correctly does not end the game when the third exiled creature is a token.
* Modern, Legacy – [Ricochet Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ricochet+Trap) and [Refraction Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Refraction+Trap) can now be correctly cast with their alternative costs.
* Legacy, Vintage – [Metalworker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker) can no longer prevent the game from continuing.
* Commander – [Opal Palace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opal+Palace) now functions correctly.
