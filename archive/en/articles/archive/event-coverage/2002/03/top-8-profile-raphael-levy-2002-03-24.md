Top 8 Profile: Raphael Levy
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 24, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbar02/a911.jpg)**Name:** Raphaël Lévy

**Age:** 20 

**Occupation:** Student

Where do you live? Toulouse

**Since when do you play Magic?** 8 Years (!)

**Best accomplishments:**  

Top 4 Pro Tour Chicago '99   

Top 4 Worlds '98  

Grand Prix Lyon winner

**What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Barcelona; what did you expect to find?**  

Close to home and to play Magic of course. I expected Spanish players.

**What do you think is the main reason for you to make Top 8 in this tournament and why?**  

Hm.... I guess one Top 8 a year is enough for me J

**What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

Absolutely no idea.
