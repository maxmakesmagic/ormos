The October 11, 2016 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on October 11, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Nicholas_Wolfram.jpg)
By Nicholas Wolfram




 Nicholas Wolfram fell promptly in love with tabletop and video games when he was only two years old, and he has been writing about them since he figured out how words worked. Now he puts together words about Magic, which he started playing way back in 2000. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on October 11, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by The Android's Dungeon, the most trusted local game store in all of Springfield!


Today's Must
============


1. [The Road to #SCGRegionals Glory!](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/33827_The-Road-To-SCGRegionals-Glory.html) | StarCityGames.com | Chris VanMeter

Chris VanMeter, recent winner of SCG Open Indianapolis, discusses the deck that led him to victory along with the other decks he expects to see winning Standard tournaments over the next few months. Always adapting, Chris reveals some tweaks to his own deck as well as tweaks to other archetypes succeeding in the competitive environment.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [*Car and Driver* Magazine: *Kaladesh* Edition](http://www.mtgoacademy.com/car-and-driver-magazine-kaladesh-edition/) | MTGO Academy | Two Jesses

Jesse K and Jesse T take a cruise through *Kaladesh* and give the shiny new Vehicles the *Top Gear* treatment. What walls can be smashed? Who shouldn't be driving what? Why does [Fleetwheel Cruiser](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleetwheel+Cruiser) look so awesome? Some of these questions may be answered!


1. [7 Keys to Sideboarding Like a Pro](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/7-keys-to-sideboarding-like-a-pro/) | ChannelFireball | Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa

Sideboarding is easy to ignore but often essential to winning a full match. Paulo wants you to sideboard more, and he is here to help see that happen.


1. [Top 5 Standard Decks in the *Kaladesh* Gauntlet](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/huang-hao-shan/top-5-standard-decks-in-the-kaladesh-gauntlet) | MTGMintCard | Huang Hao Shan

Huang weighs in on the top five deck archetypes that have been dominating Standard since the premiere of *Kaladesh*.


1. [LSV Streams a *Kaladesh* Draft](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z3CCNaWj6t4)

Luis Scott-Vargas joined a *Magic Online* draft yesterday and, being the generous soul that he is, streamed the whole thing for us to enjoy. Will his [Angel of Invention](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Invention) and [Wispweaver Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wispweaver+Angel) destroy the competition? Probably.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/Z3CCNaWj6t4>


1. [Drafting Green +1/+1 in *Kaladesh*](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/drafting-green-11-in-kaladesh/) | ChannelFireball | Brian DeMars

This is one of my favorite decks to draft in *Kaladesh*. I've always loved giving my creatures permanent boosts, and *Kaladesh* makes it delicious through many commons and uncommons. Find out Brian's take on some of the best ways to make this work.


1. [Dear Azami: The Blind Eternities](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/33828_Dear-Azami-The-Blind-Eternities.html) | StarCityGames.com | Sean McKeown

In this week's Dear Azami, Sean goes over the many virtues of [Rashmi, Eternities Crafter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rashmi%2C+Eternities+Crafter) as a commander.


What People Are Talking About
=============================


1. [*Magic* has made its debut in *The Simpsons*' universe](https://www.reddit.com/r/magicTCG/comments/56wedb/a_magic_the_gathering_gathering_on_the_latest/)

That's right: *Magic* is invading all your favorite adult-ish cartoons. It only took us 27 years to make it into *The Simpsons* and 17 years to get into *South Park*. Stay tuned for our big debut in *Bob's Burgers* in 2035!
