Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 19, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Walkinshaw-Profile.jpg)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw











Do you want to know more about our Top 8 players here at Grand Prix Brisbane? Well lucky for you, so did I, so I made them answer the following questions and pose awkwardly to have their photos taken.




---


### Oliver Oks


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpbri17/GPBrisbane_T8P_Oks.jpg)


**Hometown:** Adelaide, South Australia.  
**Age:** 37  
**Occupation:** Solicitor  
**What deck did you play?** Lantern Control  
**Day 1 Record:** 8-1  
**Day 2 Record:** 5-0-1  
**What's your best *Magic* event finish and where/when?**  3rd place, GP Melbourne 2012.  
**Which deck do you least want to face in this Top 8?** Black-Green Tron (Sean Hume).




---


### Zen Takahashi


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpbri17/GPBrisbane_T8P_Takahashi.jpg)


**Hometown:** Auckland, New Zealand  
**Age:** 19  
**Occupation:** Full-time student, part-time business intelligence analyst.  
**What deck did you play?** Dredge. Lee Shi Tian broke it so I didn't have to play burn.  
**Day 1 Record:** 8-1  
**Day 2 Record:** 4-1-1  
**What's your best *Magic* event finish and where/when?**  This. My third GP Top 8 in the last 4 Australian GP's. It gives me the confidence that I can play constructed.  
**Which deck do you least want to face in this Top 8?** White-Red Nahiri (Tetsu Kawaguchi), it's my Quarters matchup!




---


### Lee Shi Tian


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpbri17/GPBrisbane_T8P_Lee.jpg)


**Hometown:** Hong Kong  
**Age:** 29  
**Occupation:** Accountant  
**What deck did you play?** Dredge, because [Golgari Grave-Troll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golgari+Grave-Troll) was banned.  
**Day 1 Record:** 9-0  
**Day 2 Record:** 4-0-2  
**What's your best *Magic* event finish and where/when?**  GP Birmingham 2008 winner, Hong Kong National Champ, 5 Pro Tour Top 8s  
**Which deck do you least want to face in this Top 8?** White-Red Nahiri (Tetsu Kawaguchi) and Lantern Control (Oliver Oks)




---


### Timothy Cheng


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpbri17/GPBrisbane_T8P_Cheng.jpg)


**Hometown:** Sydney, New South Wales.  
**Age:** 33  
**Occupation:** Full time scrubber.  
**What deck did you play?** Living End.  
**Day 1 Record:** 7-2  
**Day 2 Record:** 3-1-2  
**What's your best *Magic* event finish and where/when?**  Probably this one.  
**Which deck do you least want to face in this Top 8?** Lantern Control (Oliver Oks) or Dredge (Lee Shi Tian or Zen Takahashi).
