=============================================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles?source=MX_Nav2020)
 on October 2, 2019

By MTG Arena Development Team 











![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/images/magic/arena/launch/event_details/mtga_pad_wec.jpg)


**WIN EVERY CARD CHALLENGE** **– STANDARD**
===========================================


**Event Details**: We'll provide access to playsets (four copies) of every card in Standard, which you can use to build and play any deck in Standard. You can build as many decks as you like, and you can change them between matches.  
**Event Date**: October 5 at 8 p.m. PT (3 a.m. UTC) to October 7 at 8 a.m. PT (3 p.m. UTC)  
**Entry Fee**: None, ***but you can only play once!***  
**Format**: Standard Best-of-One  
**Event Record**: 12 wins or 2 losses (whichever comes first)  
**Event Rewards**: You will earn rewards based on your total number of wins, as outlined below. Please note that rewards do not stack (i.e. each bullet point lists the total rewards you will receive):


* **12 Wins:** 1 Copy of *every* card in Standard + 1 Sealed Event Token + [Golden Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Egg) Sleeve
* **11 Wins:** 1 Copy of *every* card in *Throne of Eldraine* + 1 Sealed Event Token + [Golden Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Egg) Sleeve
* **9–10 Wins:** 1 Sealed Event Token + 5 *Throne of Eldraine* Rare ICRs + [Golden Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Egg) Sleeve
* **8 Wins:** 5*Throne of Eldraine* rare ICRs + [Golden Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Egg) Sleeve
* **6–7 Wins:** 4*Throne of Eldraine* rare ICRs + [Golden Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Egg) Sleeve
* **4–5 Wins:** 3*Throne of Eldraine* rare ICRs + [Golden Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Egg) Sleeve
* **2–3 Wins:** 2*Throne of Eldraine* rare ICRs + [Golden Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Egg) Sleeve
* **0–1 Wins:** 1*Throne of Eldraine* rare Individual Card Reward (ICR) + [Golden Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Egg) Sleeve

TIPS AND TRICKS
===============


* Remember! **You can only play in this event once!**
* You use any card that is considered Standard-legal on *MTG Arena*—this includes cards from the *Throne of Eldraine* Planeswalker and Brawl decks as well as buy-a-box promos!
* You can create as many decks as you like, and you can make changes—or swap them completely—in-between matches. (Psst! We also increased the maximum number of decks you can have at any given time to 75!)
* It's best to create your decks through the in-game event page so it'll properly show you having access to playsets of every card in Standard. You can still build (and save) decks through the game's Decks page, but you'll be warned if you use cards that aren't in your collection. Don't worry, you'll still be able to submit them as decks to play in the event!
* If a card allows you choose a card you from outside the game (e.g. [Fae Of Wishes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fae+Of+Wishes), [Vivien, Arkbow Ranger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivien%2C+Arkbow+Ranger), [Karn, the Great Creator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karn%2C+the+Great+Creator)) you can only chose cards that are in your sideboard.
* Want to learn more about the newest cardset, *Throne of Eldraine*? You can take in the sights by heading over to [our card image gallary](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/throne-of-eldraine/cards), allow us to regal you with [tales of the new mechanics](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/throne-eldraine-mechanics-2019-09-04), or do a little bit of [tome raiding](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=473030) through the full [*Throne of Eldraine* set release notes](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/throne-eldraine-release-notes-2019-09-20).

**How many times can I participate in this event?**


This event is open to all players, and has no entry fee ***but you can only play in this event once***. Once you have claimed your prize you will not be able to participate in this particular event again.


**What happens if I win every card?**


Congrats! If you earn 12 wins, click the "Claim Prize". . . claim your prize! Your rewards will pop-up on screen: A Set Card to represent a single copy from every card in the five Standard sets (*Guilds of Ravnica*, *Ravnica Allegiance*, *War of the Spark*, *Core Set 2020*, and *Throne of Eldraine*), your Sealed Event Token, and your [Golden Egg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Egg) Sleeve.


If you already have playsets of any of the cards in these sets you will receive Vault progress from the additional commons/uncommons, and you will receive Gems for the additional rares/mythic rares.


**How do Sealed Event Tokens work?**


If you receive a Sealed Event Token as part of your rewards, you will be able to join a single Sealed event without having to pay the entry fee. If there is currently no Sealed event available, you will see the token by your Wildcard, Gold, and Gem trackers. When joining a Sealed event, if you you have a Token you'll be able to choose whether or not to use the token (Ticket Entry) or pay the fee.


**How do the *Throne of Eldraine* rare ICRs work?** 


Each card reward will redeem for a rare or higher card from *Throne of Eldraine*. Each rare ICR has a 1:8 upgrade rate to a mythic rare, and if you would receive a rare or mythic rare of a card you already have a playset of you will receive Gems instead—20 Gems per rare, 40 Gems per mythic rare.


**What should I do if I experience an issue while participating in this event?**


If you experience any issue while playing Magic: The Gathering Arena, please [contact support](https://mtgarena-support.wizards.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=360000317092).
