Round 16: Raphael Levy vs. John Larkin
======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 16, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_aaronforsythe.jpg)
By Aaron Forsythe











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a735.jpg)Ireland has fallen to second place in the team standings behind the surging Argentinean team, and John Larkin blames Raphael Levy, who fell last round to the top Argentinean, Diego Ostrovich.

Levy cast a [Mental Note](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mental+Note), and then Larkin cast a [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend), taking a moment to decide to steal Levy's [Aether Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Burst). Levy played a [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear), and then Larkin one-upped him with a [Shadowmage Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator). Levy tried putting out some small creatures like [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) and [Nimble Mongoose](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nimble+Mongoose), but Larkin had Edict's at the ready and the Infiltrator was drawing more.

At one point Levy cast [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) and discarded a [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) and a [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder), but when he flashed back the Roar, Larkin first insulted him for his "pathetic" token (a gold button), and then dispatched the token with a [Faceless Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher). Larkin had played a [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade), and it, along with the Finkel, were wailing away n Levy.

At one point, Levy emptied his hand a cast a [Breakthrough](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Breakthrough) for three, and [Aether Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Burst)ed a few of Larkin's creatures, but in the end he had no way to block the Infiltrator.

Larkin 1 - Levy 0

Levy needed one of his [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel)s to show up to stop the Infiltrator, and he had no such luck in game 2.

He played out a [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) on turn 2, which was Edicted, another Werbear on turn three, and then a [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) and a [Nimble Mongoose](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nimble+Mongoose) on turn four. Larkin had put out a Finkel, and then played a [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend). Levy showed his hand of an island and another [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder).

Levy: "This was a bad draw."  
 Larkin: "I thought it would be better than *this*."

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a739.jpg)With an empty hand and six mana and a Mongoose in play, Levy topdecked an [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval). He cast it, replayed a [Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) and dropped the Mongoose. He wasn't even close to threshold. Larkin passed the turn, and Levy played a [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear). Larkin recast his [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) and was saddened to see that Levy had drawn a [Breakthrough](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Breakthrough) which would be capable of giving him threshold at a moment's notice, so he took that card.

Unfortunately Levy drew another [Breakthrough](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Breakthrough) on his next turn and he cast it for two, discarding three [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder)s and getting threshold. He attacked for seven with the Bear and the 'Goose.

Larkin had a [Coffin Purge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coffin+Purge), good enough for two of the [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder)s, and an Edict for the [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear). But the third [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) and the Mongoose were enough to kill Larkin over then next few turns.

Larkin 1 – Levy 1

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a737.jpg)Both players started with 2/1's in the deciding game—Larkin played a [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade) and Levy a [Seton's Scout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Scout). Levy's was killed with an Edict, and he replaced it with a [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear), fully thresholded thanks to a pair of Careful Studies.

[Faceless Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) took care of that Bear, and then Larkin dropped the hammer in the form of [Rancid Earth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth) on Levy's only forest. Levy was stuck with uncastable spells, and Larkin started playing out guys.

When Levy finally drew another forest, Larkin had another [Rancid Earth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth) at the ready. With no access to green mana, Levy couldn't play any of his creatures and Larkin took the match.

**Final Result:** Larkin 2 – Levy 1

Larkin had sided out the [Rancid Earth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth)s for the second game, but after losing to [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval) he put them back in.
