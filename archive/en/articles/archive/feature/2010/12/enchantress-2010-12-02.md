Enchantress is an archetype that has been around for a long time. Using "[Enchantresses](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&text=+%5Benchantment%5D+%5Bdraw%5D+%5Ba%5D+%5Bcard%5D+%5Bcast%5D&name=+%5BEnchantress%5D&type=+%5B%22Creature%22%5D)," cards like [Verduran Enchantress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verduran+Enchantress) and [Argothian Enchantress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Argothian+Enchantress), the deck seeks to draw extra cards by casting as many enchantments as possible. For pryite199, the deck was good enough to earn a 3-1 performance in a recent Legacy event on **Magic Online**. [Enchantress's Presence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enchantress%27s+Presence) and [Argothian Enchantress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Argothian+Enchantress) provide the fuel for [Solitary Confinement](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solitary+Confinement), while cards like [Moat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moat) and [Aura of Silence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aura+of+Silence) keep the opponent all tied up.
