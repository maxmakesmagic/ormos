**Holiday Cube Update**


Randy Buehler is a longtime *Magic* player, a friend of the Vintage format, co-host of the [Standard Super League](http://standardsuperleague.com/), and a lover of *Magic Online* Cubes everywhere.


Randy and *Magic* R&D have set their sites on updating the *Magic Online* Holiday Cube! Read up on the redesign in the [full article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/holiday-cube-cardlist-2015-06-22) then check out Lee Sharpe's [latest article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/june-2015-magic-online-event-updates-2015-06-08#singleelemholiday) for details about the upcoming Holiday Cube Events from July 8 through July 22.


Read about what went into the redesign in the full [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/holiday-cube-updates-2015-06-22).


Check out the full, newly-updated Holiday Cube list [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/holiday-cube-cardlist-2015-06-22).

**Modern Festival Starting Starting Soon**


Now's your chance to win one copy of every Modern-legal set in the *Magic Online* [Modern Festival Finals](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11#ModernFinals)!


Since the start of [*Modern Masters 2015 Edition* Release Events](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04), the Modern format has taken center stage on *Magic Online*—and the celebration doesn't stop there. Whether you're looking to get into Modern, already love the format, or are just looking for some sweet promos, the upcoming [Modern Festival](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11) has something for you. Events kick off after the Wednesday, June 24, downtime!


Get the full [event details](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11).

***Mirrodin* Block Phantom Draft Events Coming Soon**


Revisit *Mirrodin*, *Darksteel*, and *Fifth Dawn* after the June 24 downtime in the [Mirrodin Block Three-Round Phantom Draft](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11#Mirrodin) event. As part of the [Modern Festival](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11), you can get a great Modern promo—a full-art [Lightning Helix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Helix)—just for participating.


Get the full [event details](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11#Mirrodin).

**Extending Some *Modern Masters 2015 Edition* Events**


*Modern Masters 2015 Edition* is available on *Magic Online* now!


In the spirit of the [Modern Festival](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11)l, we're extending the duration of *Modern Masters 2015 Edition* events! All events listed below will remain active through Wednesday, July 8, instead of their previous end date of the June 17 downtime. Not only will events keep running, but *Modern Masters 2015* will also remain on sale in the *Magic Online* Store until Wednesday, July 8!


*Modern Masters 2015 Edition* Events that are being extended through Wednesday, July 8:



* [Modern 3-2-1-1 Single Elimination Queue](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04#prizes)
* [Modern, Legacy, and Vintage Constructed 2-Player Queue Prize Changes](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04#queueprizes)
* [Modern, Legacy, and Vintage Daily Event Prize Changes](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04#dailyprizes)
* [*Modern Masters 2015 Edition* Phantom Draft](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04#MM2draft)
* [*Modern Masters 2015 Edition* Phantom Sealed](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04#MM2sealed)
* [*Modern Masters 2015 Edition* 6-2-2-2 Draft](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04#MM26222)
* [*Modern Masters 2015 Edition* 8-4 Draft](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04#MM284)


Check out the updated event schedule [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04) for the full details on Release Events.

**Standard Super League Semifinals Tonight**


Stop by tonight for the Standard Super League semifinals! Over the course of the past season, a commentary team of two *Magic* Hall of Fame members, Randy Buehler and Luis Scott-Vargas, has hosted eight-player single-elimination tournaments every Tuesday night to see which competitor emerges as the first Standard Super League Champion. Don't miss the finals, next Tuesday night at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7) on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic)!


For a recap of what happened in last week's episode of the Standard Super League, head over to Randy Buehler's latest [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/standard-super-league-episode-8-recap-2015-06-23).


Remember, we'll be posting each player's deck lists for the upcoming episode of the Standard Super League to [MTGO.com](http://magic.wizards.com/en/game-info/products/magic-online) at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7) so that viewers can follow along at home! Stop by tonight to get the semifinals deck lists.

**Double Pro Tour Qualifier Weekend**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls, there's a double Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) weekend coming up! Standard Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries and the *Dragons of Tarkir-Fate Reforged* Sealed Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries begin tomorrow, June 24, and run through the day preceding each Finals Event.


Learn more about PTQs on *Magic Online* and get the full schedule [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/content/pro-tour-qualifiers-battle-for-zendikar).

**Upcoming Wizards Account System Maintenance for Monday, June 22**


The Wizards Account System will be undergoing maintenance on Monday, June 22, from 7:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7) through Tuesday, June 23 7:00 a.m. Pacific. Customers will be able to file for reimbursement and submit bug reports during this time, but responses will be delayed.

**Extended Downtime for June 24, 2015**


* All tournaments will begin to close Tuesday, June 23, at 11:00 p.m. Pacific (6:00 UTC)
* Store and trade activity will be suspended at approximately 2:45 a.m. Pacific (9:45 UTC)
* The system will be down from 3:00 a.m. Pacific (10:00 UTC) until 12:00 p.m. Pacific (19:00 UTC)
