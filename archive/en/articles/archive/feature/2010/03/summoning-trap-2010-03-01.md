The Pro Tour is a gigantic, one-of-a-kind **Magic** experience open to all. Danny Ecker, who was competing in the Pro Tour itself, played a fun deck that could still put up solid numbers. Who doesn't want to blow out the competition by plopping Iona onto the table out of nowhere with [Summoning Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoning+Trap)? For the full story of Pro Tour–San Diego, visit the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/g%C3%B6rtzen-goes-all-way-san-diego).
