Grand Prix–Brussels:Top 8 Player Profiles
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 28, 2010 











### Top 8 Profiles


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpbru10/T8_Group.jpg)

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpbru10/T8_Giusti.jpg)*Emanuele Giusti*


**Name:** Emanuele Giusti


**Age:** 19


**Occupation:** Student


**Where do you live?** Arezzo, Italy


**Where do you play FNM?** Fumetto di Angelo Rossi in Arezzo


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?** Jund, because it is the strongest deck


**What is your best matchup?** Blue white allies!


**What is your worst matchup?** The mirror match


**What was your record in the swiss?** 8-1 on day 1, 6-1 on day 2


**What did you lose to in the swiss?** Mono red burn and Boros


**How do you beat Jund?** By playing Jund and having better draws


**What’s the best thing about Belgium?** The beer!





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpbru10/T8_Nagy.jpg)*Tamas Nagy*


**Name:** Tamas Nagy


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** I.T. Manager


**Where do you live?** Hungary


**Where do you play FNM?** Online in my clan at New Times


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?** Mythic. I like to attack, and I had all the cards to test online.


**What is your best matchup?** White weenie


**What is your worst matchup?** Allies


**What was your record in the swiss?** 13 wins, 1 loss, 2 draws


**What did you lose to in the swiss?** Allies


**How do you beat Jund?** By killing my opponent before he can kill all my creatures


**What’s the best thing about Belgium?** I had my finest hours here (cue audience groan)





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpbru10/T8_Szoke.jpg)*Zoltan Szoke*


**Name:** Zoltan Szoke


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Office assistant


**Where do you live?** Budapest, Hungary


**Where do you play FNM?** [www.szellemlovas.hu](http://www.szellemlovas.hu)


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?** Mythic, because the blue/red Runflare Trap deck is bad


**What is your best matchup?** Everything!


**What is your worst matchup?** Mythic


**What was your record in the swiss?** 13-1-2


**What did you lose to in the swiss?** Jund.


**How do you beat Jund?** I’m faster than Jund


**What’s the best thing about Belgium?** This tournament!





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpbru10/T8_Bernstein.jpg)*Steve Bernstein*


**Name:** Steve Bernstein


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Finance


**Where do you live?** London, England


**Where do you play FNM?** Tom Baker’s house in Pimlico


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?** [Foil](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Foil) Jund. Lots of foils Jund.


**What is your best matchup?** Everything. It’s the best deck.


**What is your worst matchup?** Naya allies?!? [Kabira Evangel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kabira+Evangel)???


**What was your record in the swiss?** 13-2-1


**What did you lose to in the swiss?** Jund and Allies


**How do you beat Jund?** Join them.





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpbru10/T8_Lambach.jpg)*Nicolas Lambach*


**Name:** Nicolas Lambach


**Age:** 20


**Occupation:** Student


**Where do you live?** Colman, France


**Where do you play FNM?** Baka Neko in Colman


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?** Naya, because I didn’t want to play Jund


**What is your best matchup?** Jund


**What is your worst matchup?** Jund


**What was your record in the swiss?** 13-2-1


**What did you lose to in the swiss?** Two Jund decks (I beat the other nine though!)


**How do you beat Jund?** Skills


**What’s the best thing about Belgium?** The beer





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpbru10/T8_Gregoir.jpg)*Tamas Nagy*


**Name:** Christophe Gregoir


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** IT consultant


**Where do you live?** Belgium! Gent in Belgium


**Where do you play FNM?** Outpost Gent


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?** Jund, because it is the best deck in the format for sure


**What is your best matchup?** Anything that isn’t Jund


**What is your worst matchup?** By process of elimination, Jund I guess


**What was your record in the swiss?** 13-2-1


**What did you lose to in the swiss?** Jund and Mythic


**How do you beat Jund?** Play more [Bituminous Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bituminous+Blast)s than my opponent


**What’s the best thing about Belgium?** Beer! And Erika Van Tielen (a Belgian radio/TV personality)





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpbru10/T8_Cipolleschi.jpg)*Francesco Cipolleschi*


**Name:** Francesco Cipolleschi


**Age:** 19


**Occupation:** Student


**Where do you live?** Arezzo, Italy


**Where do you play FNM?** Fumettopuli di Angelo Rossi in Arezzo


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?** Jund. I tested Naya a lot, but in the end I could not get all the cards. Many thanks to Samuele Estratti for the list!


**What is your best matchup?** [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) naming ‘[Mind Funeral](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Funeral)’. It happened to me in the second round after I had only played a tapped [Savage Lands](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savage+Lands) (!)


**What is your worst matchup?** Jund


**What was your record in the swiss?** 13-2-1


**What did you lose to in the swiss?** Jund (twice)


**How do you beat Jund?** By playing Jund


**What’s the best thing about Belgium?** The beer.
