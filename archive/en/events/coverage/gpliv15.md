TOP 8 COVERAGE




 The age of the khans is fading as though it never happened and dragons are looming on the horizon of Tarkir. In one of the last opportunities to play *Fate Reforged/Khans of Tarkir* Limited before the dragons take over, Martin Dang from Denmark emerged victorious with a decidedly-non-khan-colored blue-black-red deck, defeating Nikolas Labahn's Abzan deck in the final in two extremely one-sided games. On his route to the final, Dang defeated Daniel Oppliger's blue-red deck in the quarters and Lucas Blohon's white-green deck in the semi-finals. Blohon at least had an additional invite to Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir* as a consolation prize as his quarter-final win bumped him up to Silver level. This was also the ninth Grand Prix Top 8 for Portuguese player Marcio Carvalho.



In total 1,772 players descended on the city of Liverpool to see which of Tarkir's clans would bless them with powerful cards for their sealed decks. Of those, 199 players made it through to Sunday's booster draft. Eight survived to the final draft and when the dust settled only one player was left. Congratulations to Martin Dang, champion of Grand Prix Liverpool.

DAY 2 COVERAGE



Welcome back for Day 2 of Grand Prix Liverpool 2015! What had started with 1,772 players Saturday morning was whittled down to just 199 through the first day's nine rounds of *Fate Reforged*/*Khans of Tarkir* Sealed Deck. Now it's time for *Fate Reforged*/*Khans of Tarkir* Booster Draft, six more rounds, followed by the Top 8.


Leading the field at the start of the day were five players with pristine records of 9-0: Andreas Jonsson, Benjamin Jean, Maciej Kawonczyk, Benjamin Van Koekenbeek, and Ed Ross. Fierce competition wasn't far behind, though, with Eliott Boussaud, Raphaël Lévy, Nicolai Herzog, Javier Dominguez, Alexandre Darras, Lukas Blohon, Chapman Sim, Ondrej Strasky, Andrea Mengucci (to name but a few) all on 24 points.


Meanwhile, we find ourselves in the midst of preview season. With the Prereleases for *Dragons of Tarkir* a mere two weeks away, the previewed cards were all the talk within the community, and we were sure to pick up some of that as well.


So say tuned for all the latest, all the news and stories, all the action and excitement, straight from the battlefield of Grand Prix Liverpool 2015!

Day one of GP Liverpool is in the books. 1772 players traveled to the ACC Liverpool to battle it out with Fate Reforged/Khans of Tarkir Limited.
