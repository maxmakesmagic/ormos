Red Deck Wins. It is an old adage, but it remains a true one, as Gene Richtsmeier proved recently. He made the Top 16 of the StarCityGames.com Legacy Open in St. Louis, Missouri using a new take on an old archetype for Legacy. Powerful burn spells backed by cheap and efficient creatures; it doesn't get much more red than that! You can read StarCity's coverage of the event [here](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/18414_Live_Coverage_of_the_StarCityGamescom_10K_Weekend_in_St_Louis.html).
