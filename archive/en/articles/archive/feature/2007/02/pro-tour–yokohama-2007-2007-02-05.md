Yokohama, Japan is the second stop in the 2007 Pro Tour season. But not only the Pros will be enjoying themselves: the Pro Tour is above all a great weekend of **Magic**  that brings you fantastic opportunities. Try out the new Two-Headed Giant team format with your friends, win great prizes, and much more!


Pro Tour–Yokohama is a *Time Spiral*  Block Constructed event that runs April 20-22, 2007. The Last-Chance Qualifier will be run on Thursday, April 19 at the tournament site (registration begins at 11 a.m.).

### Pro Tour Registration


**Date:** April 19, 2007  
**Time:** 6 p.m. - 9 p.m.  
**Location:**  [Pacific Convention Plaza Yokohama](http://www.pacifico.co.jp/english/)  

 1-1-1, Minato Mirai, Nishi-ku  

 Yokohama, Japan


Players must have a signed waiver and consent form to compete at Pro Tour-Geneva. Forms can be downloaded at [www.wizards.com/protour](/en/articles/archive/pro-tour%E2%80%93amsterdam-artists-2010-08-26) and will be available onsite. Minors must have a parent or guardian’s signature on these forms. Minors will not be able to register or play unless we receive a waiver signed by a parent or guardian. No exceptions!


Bring these forms with you to registration. We will make forms available for competitors at registration, if necessary.


To register, all participants must present one of the following forms of identification:


* a driver’s license, or
* a passport, or
* a copy of a birth certificate and photo IDs (school IDs are acceptable).

We encourage players to attend mandatory registration on Thursday. However we understand that delays might cause players to miss this and we have a registration on Friday morning between 7:30 a.m. and 8:30 a.m. Players at late registration do not always receive the same gifts as those at mandatory registration.


Players not registered by 8:30 a.m. on April 20 may be denied entry to the Pro Tour.


If you anticipate any difficulty registering during the official registration hours, please contact please contact Scott Larabee at (206) 793-3534 or [scott.larabee@wizards.com](mailto:scott.larabee@wizards.com).


### Tournament Format and Schedule


Pro Tour–Yokohama is a three-day Block Constructed format event with a prize purse of $240,245. For more information about the format, schedule and prize payout, [click here](/en/node/641746).


### Site Attractions


**SIDE EVENTS**  

Play **Magic** all weekend long, even if you're not qualified for the Pro Tour! Information on all the events being held at Pro Tour-Yokohama can be found in the [Side Event Schedule](/en/node/641801)


**ARTISTS ON SITE**  

Pro Tour–Yokohama 2007 will feature appearances by **Magic** artists **Adam Rex**, **Dan Frazier**, **Ittoku**, and **Hideaki Takamura**! [Click here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/yokohama07-artists) to read more about these artists.


**WATCH THE TOP 8**  

All of Sunday's Top 8 action can be viewed on a projection screen in the hall, with Randy Buehler and Brian David-Marshall providing the play-by-play and color commentary. Sunday's action begins at 10:45 a.m. local time, which is 9:45 p.m. ET Saturday, and is also available as a [webcast](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgevent/webcast/home) from the comfort of your own home.
