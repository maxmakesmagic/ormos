Christopher Schiber was all too excited to play a brand new *Conflux* card at his recent PTQ for Honolulu. The card? [Nyxathid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nyxathid). The 7/7 is perfect for a discard deck, and between [Augur of Skulls](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Augur+of+Skulls), [Smallpox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smallpox), [Stupor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stupor), and [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), you can bet the rare was a veritable under-costed fattie all weekend long for Christopher.
