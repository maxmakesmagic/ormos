The 9-0 Players' Decklists
==========================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX WARSAW 2016](/en/events/coverage/gpwar16)
 on October 30, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











1,113 players had been in the running, but after the first day's nine grueling rounds of Standard play, only four of them could still boast a perfect record of 9-0. Leading the field overnight were Sam Rolph, Bart van Etten, Pro Tour *Theros* champion Jérémy Dezani, and Oleksii Riabokon (pictured below, left to right).


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpwar16/WAR_9-0decks.jpg)


Two of them played Mardu decks, two of them chose black and green. But the pair of decks each differed quite a bit: One of the Mardu decks had more Vehicles, the other featured a little splash of blue for counter spells out of the sideboard. Meanwhile, one of the black-green decks was the usual high-curve delirium deck, whereas the other was a proper aggro deck featuring [Gnarlwood Dryad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gnarlwood+Dryad), [Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter), and [Verdurous Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verdurous+Gearhulk).


Check out the full lists below!
