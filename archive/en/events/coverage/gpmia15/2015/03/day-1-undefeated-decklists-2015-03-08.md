Day 1 Undefeated Decklists
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 8, 2015 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Peter Rawlings










Of the more than 1,300 players that set sail to Miami this weekend, only four made it through Saturday without once running aground. Forests and Plains proved popular vessels as three of the four Day 1 undefeated players ran an assortment of green and white spells in their decks, with Andrew Boswell piloting Abzan Aggro and Royce Walter slinging Abzan Midrange, while Adam Yurchick brought a Green-White Devotion deck featuring Mastery of the Unseen. The other undefeated player, Ryan Grodzinski, struck strictly to Mountains as his Mono-Red Aggro build put him into Day 2 at a pristine 9-0.
