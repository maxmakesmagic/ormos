Ixalan Comes to Magic Online on September 25!
=============================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on September 20, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/lee_author_image_0.jpeg)
By Lee Sharpe











Pirates! Dinosaurs! Vampires! Merfolk! Explore *Ixalan* with events beginning at 10am Pacific Time on *Magic Online* Monday, September 25, the day after prerelease events in your [local game store](http://locator.wizards.com/). If you want to see the set in action already, I joined several other fine humans in the LoadingReadyRun Pre PreRelease where we gave *Ixalan* its first public look:


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/cf1ESNK5ckI>


No matter if you prefer friendly, intermediate, or competitive structures—or whether you want to play Constructed, Draft, or Sealed—*Magic Online* has a variety of ways for you to enjoy the new set! Our structures will follow the same pattern as other recent sets, but you can see the bottom of this article for full details on all structures.


Now let's look at what else is happening.


Standard Rotation
=================


On *Magic Online*, Standard officially rotates with today’s downtime. This means that cards in *Ixalan* will be legal and cards from the *Battle for Zendikar*, *Oath of the Gatewatch*, *Shadows over Innistrad*, and *Eldritch Moon* sets will no longer be legal. As you start getting *Ixalan* cards on Monday, you can put them in your Standard decks right away and begin exploring the new format. You can also Opt to put some new cards in your Modern deck, if that's your thing. (Even before Monday, you can sneak *Ixalan*’s sweet reprints into your decks if you’re so inclined.)


2018 *Magic Online* Championship Series Starts Soon
===================================================


The 2018 *Magic Online* Championship Series is just around the corner! The current structure of the *Magic Online* Championship Series was well received by players, so the overall structure, including prizes and leaderboard points, will not change from last year’s version.


Earn Qualifier Points through your regular *Magic Online* play and then use them to enter in MOCS events that feed the *Magic Online* Championship. The 2018 Championship includes a 200,000 USD prize pool and will happen in early 2019. You can also play in one of the eight MOCS Opens and try to earn a spot in the 2018 Championship directly.


If the 2018 season is starting soon, it means the 2017 season is coming to a close. The last chance to use Qualifier Points for the 2017 series is the MOCS Monthly event on October 21. During the downtime on October 25, all QPs will be removed from players accounts, with the exception of any QPs earned after October 18 at 8 a.m. Pacific, which can be used for the 2018 *Magic Online* Championship Series events!


You can read the full rules and details [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2018-magic-online-championship-series).

This is a lot of fun stuff we’ve got coming up, and I hope you’re looking forward to it! Stay tuned for specific details on these events as they get closer.


New Redemption Schedule
=======================


As mentioned in Chris Kiritz’s Moving Forward [article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/news/magic-online-moving-forward-2017-09-07), we’re trying to have as few downtimes as possible. A consequence of this has been an inconsistent redemption processing schedule. With our deployment today, we’ve added our ability to process redemptions without bringing the *Magic Online* system down! This lets us get back to a regular redemption cadence.


Starting tomorrow, *Magic Online* will process redemptions every Thursday morning between 2 a.m. and 3 a.m. Pacific (this will be in addition to any redemptions processed during today’s downtime). If you want to redeem a set, make sure to purchase the appropriate redemption object from the *Magic Online* store before this time. Also, to ensure your redemption order is processed, make sure each card in any sets you are trying to redeem is in your account, isn’t being used in league or tournament, and isn’t involved in an active trade when processing starts, or that entire redemption order will fail to process.


For more information about redemption, please review our redemption policy [page](https://magic.wizards.com/en/gameinfo/products/magiconline/redemption).


Prestige Avatars
================


During *Ixalan* season (starting September 20 for Constructed and September 25 for Limited), you'll be able to earn these prestige avatars by earning enough trophies in *Magic Online* Leagues. You can earn the Silver, Gold, and Mythic avatars as follows:


Silver Prestige Avatar—Jace, Ingenious Mind-Mage


* 5 trophies in the Friendly *Ixalan* Draft League
* 5 trophies in the Friendly *Ixalan* Sealed League
* 5 trophies in the Intermediate *Ixalan* Draft League
* 2 trophies in any single Friendly Constructed League

Gold Prestige Avatar—Huatli, Warrior Poet


* 4 trophies in the Competitive *Ixalan* Draft League
* 4 trophies in the Competitive *Ixalan* Sealed League
* 4 trophies in any single Competitive Constructed League

Mythic Prestige Avatar—Vraska, Relic Seeker


* 12 trophies in the Competitive *Ixalan* Draft League
* 6 trophies in the Competitive *Ixalan* Sealed League
* 6 trophies in any single Competitive Constructed League

Finishing with six or more wins in any single MOCS Monthly event *Ixalan* season will also qualify you for the Mythic Prestige Avatar.

*Ixalan* Event Structures
=========================


**FRIENDLY DRAFT LEAGUE**


* Product: 3 *Ixalan* Boosters
* Structure: Draft, then single-game matches at your convenience until either 7 wins or 4 losses, whichever comes first
* Entry Options:
	+ 12 Event Tickets
	+ 3 *Ixalan* Boosters + 2 Event Tickets
	+ 120 Play Points
* Prizes:
	+ 7 wins: 120 Play Points
	+ 6 wins: 100 Play Points
	+ 5 wins: 80 Play Points
	+ 4 wins: 40 Play Points
	+ 3 wins: 20 Play Points
	+ 2 wins: 10 Play Points

**INTERMEDIATE DRAFT LEAGUE**


* Product: 3 *Ixalan* Boosters
* Structure: Draft, then up to 3 matches at your convenience
* Entry Options:
	+ 12 Event Tickets
	+ 3 Boosters + 2 Event Tickets
	+ 120 Play Points
* Prizes:
	+ 3 wins: 6 *Ixalan* Boosters + 1 Qualifier Point
	+ 2 wins: 2 *Ixalan* Boosters

**COMPETITIVE DRAFT LEAGUE**


* Product: 3 *Ixalan* Boosters
* Structure: Draft, then play matches at your convenience until either 3 wins or 1 loss
* Entry Options:
	+ 15 Event Tickets
