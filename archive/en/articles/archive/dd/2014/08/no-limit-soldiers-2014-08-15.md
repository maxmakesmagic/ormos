No Limit Soldiers
=================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on August 15, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 





[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Obelisk of Urd)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obelisk of Urd)
One of the most exciting decks to come out of  [Pro Tour *Magic* *2015*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptm15)  was played by Alex Sittner. Alex's 8â€“2 record in Standard was fueled by as many Soldiers as he could get his hands on. The key to this deck is [Obelisk of Urd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obelisk+of+Urd), naming Soldier. Alex's deck has a whopping 23 Soldiers that cost a single mana, in addition to 4 [Raise the Alarm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raise+the+Alarm) that provide two Soldiers for two mana. This means that if you draw a hand with at least three lands, three creatures, and an [Obelisk of Urd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obelisk+of+Urd), then you can cast the artifact on the third turn. From there, attacking with a large army of Soldiers is likely to finish the game quickly.


In addition to [Obelisk of Urd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obelisk+of+Urd), there are some synergies that give the deck a little extra power to help win games. [Akroan Crusader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Crusader) and [Favored Hoplite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Favored+Hoplite) give the deck a small heroic theme, fueled by [Launch the Fleet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Launch+the+Fleet) and [Dynacharge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dynacharge). [Dynacharge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dynacharge) is exceptionally effective with so many creatures that cost a single mana, closing games without the [Obelisk of Urd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obelisk+of+Urd).
