The November 3, 2017 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on November 3, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on November 3, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by Pro Tour *Ixalan*. Obviously.


What to Watch Today
===================


1. [Pro Tour *Ixalan*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/events/premierplay/protour/ptxln) | [Twitch.tv/magic](https://go.twitch.tv/magic) | 8 a.m. PT/11 a.m. ET/3 p.m. UTC

It's here! Like, right now here. Tune in to [Twitch.tv/magic](https://go.twitch.tv/magic) discreetly during work to see the best in the world try to crack Standard right in half. Or they’re just playing Ramunap Red or Temur Energy. One of the two.

1. [My Two Control Decks For Pro Tour *Ixalan*](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/36111_My-Two-Control-Decks-For-Pro-Tour-Ixalan.html) | StarCityGames | Shaheen Soorani

All week I’ve been struggling to put together a deck for FNM this week. I’ve gone through about five different decks (all [Search for Azcanta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Search+for+Azcanta) decks, of course) and keep coming back to two decks—Grixis and [Approach of the Second Sun](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Approach+of+the+Second+Sun). All I’m trying to say is that Shaheen Soorani is my Patronus and I wish him all the luck with either deck at the Pro Tour.


1. [Before The Night Is Through](http://shoutengine.com/MagicwithZuby/episode-089-before-the-night-is-through-45970) | Magic with Zuby | Matthew Zubritsky

No content creator is more passionate about small *Magic* content creators than Zuby. If you haven’t listened to his podcast, this isn’t a bad one to start with, as Zuby talks Commander and brings on a special guest star (hint: it’s his wife) to talk about women in *Magic*.


1. [This Week in Legacy: SCG Open Washington DC](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/this-week-in-legacy-scg-open-washington-dc?src=wizards) | MTGGoldfish | Sean Brown

If you’re a Legacy fan, this is a must-read, super-informative look at interesting decks coming out of the latest SCG Open.


Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Standard Ramunap Red](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/standard-ptq-2017-10-29)

Despite being a known entity basically forever, Ramunap Red keeps churning out victories. This particular decklist comes from SandyDogMTG (you might know him as Brandon Burton, MTGO ringer), and it won him a *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifier. This deck will definitely be a player this weekend at Pro Tour *Ixalan*—the question is just how much of a player it will turn out to be.
