Magic Online Championship Series - 2009 Season 4
================================================



 Posted in [Magic Online](/en/en/gameinfo/products/magiconline/resources)
 on July 30, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Players earned QPs during 2009 Season 4 as follows:
---------------------------------------------------

* 3 QPs were earned for finishing in the Top 8 of any Premiere Event or Daily Event.
* 1 QP was earned for being the winner of any queue with 8 or more players (any format).
* The Season 3 LCQs and Championship did not award any QPs for Season 4.
* Magic Online Live Series events awarded 1 QP to the winner.
* Three weeks in this season had 8-player constructed queues given format for that week (Standard, Extended, and Classic) award 3 QPs to the winner and 1 QP to second place.
