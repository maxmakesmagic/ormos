Today's Musts
=============


1. [Magical](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WLccZ5Cu3w8) | The Art of Christopher Moeller | Christopher Moeller

A fond farewell to [Christopher Moeller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5b%22Christopher+Moeller%22%5d), who is retiring from *Magic* art after nineteen fantastic, beautiful years. He's off to create even more art elsewhere, and we wish him the best in the world. We'll remember you fondly with [Umezawa's Jitte](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=81979)–wielding [Voidmage Prodigies](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=108811) and [Sower of Temptation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sower+of+Temptation)s stealing [Siege-Gang Commanders](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=193751).


Watch his video below or for a deeper dive, check out the [interview with Moeller from Mike Linnemann at Gathering Magic](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/mikelinnemann-news-04182017-magic-artist-chris-moeller-retires/).


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/WLccZ5Cu3w8>

The LSV set reviews have become as much a part of preview season as the actual previews, so we're doubling up on "musts" today. Get your fill of ratings, knowledge, and, yes, Cat puns.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Hellbent Jund Combo](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/34928_The-Beginning-Of-The-Beautiful-Amonkhet-Brew-Season.html) | StarCityGames.com | Chris Lansdell

There has been a lot of talk about the interaction between [Shadow of the Grave](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadow+of+the+Grave), [Noose Constrictor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noose+Constrictor), and [Flameblade Adept](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flameblade+Adept), but Landsdell went further than talk and put together an aggressive Jund deck that has a ton of ways to get your hand empty, and a bunch of ways to take advantage of that.


For one, [Lupine Prototype](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lupine+Prototype) has been waiting for a deck like this since it was first assembled by Ludevic. Then [Bloodrage Brawler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodrage+Brawler), [Hazoret the Fervent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hazoret+the+Fervent), and [Flameblade Adept](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flameblade+Adept) came along and things started coming together for the Wolf Construct.


Lansdell included two primary ways to refill your hand once you've gone down the discard hole—[Ghirapur Orrery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghirapur+Orrery), which should almost always be active in this deck, and [Shadow of the Grave](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadow+of+the+Grave), which combos nicely with all the cards that require you to discard cards.
