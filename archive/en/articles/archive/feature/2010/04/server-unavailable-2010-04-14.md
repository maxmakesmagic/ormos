Server is Unavailable
=====================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 14, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











We are sorry for the inconvenience, but the new account creation process for **Magic: The Gathering Online** is currently unavailable. Please try again later. If this problem persists, please contact [**Wizards of the Coast** Game Support](http://wizards.custhelp.com/cgi-bin/wizards.cfg/php/enduser/std_adp.php?p_faqid=489). Thank you.
