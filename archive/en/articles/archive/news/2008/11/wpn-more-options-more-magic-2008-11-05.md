WPN: More Options, More Magic
=============================



 Posted in **News**
 on November 5, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The Wizards Play Network (WPN) expands your options for playing **Magic: The Gathering** and puts a global directory of play locations at your fingertips. WPN locations are convenient places to take your friends to play **Magic**, meet new people, and socialize. With locations offering a robust selection of activities, formats, and ways to excel, getting into **Magic** has never been easier. Beginning in November 2008, play how, when, and where you want with the WPN.

The WPN offers many exciting new ways for you to play **Magic**, hang out, have fun, and earn cool promo cards, including:

* Getting involved with League Play, a fun, flexible way to earn a promo card in a social, relaxed environment that is all about friendly competition.
* Earning a promo card by completing Planeswalker Achievements, such as participating in a sanctioned standard tournament and donating old Basic Land cards.

These are just some of the potential options that will be available to you through the WPN. Activities offered will vary and are at the discretion of the participating WPN locations. Please contact your local WPN location to find out what activities they will be offering and other pertinent information. 

Find a convenient WPN location [here](http://ww2.wizards.com/Magic/EventLocator.aspx?searchtype=Stores) and start exploring the Multiverse of **Magic: The Gathering**!
