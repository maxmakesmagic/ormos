The June 19, 2017 Update
========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on June 19, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on June 19, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by Supreme Will's artist, Alayna Danner, whose first *Magic* card she's done art for was revealed over the weekend. I'll take twelve, please.


Today's Previews
================


1. [EXCLUSIVE!!! Hour of Devastation Preview Card: Pride Sovereign](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kkzgOIljoCw) | Magic the Amateuring | Meghan Wolf and Maria Bartholdi

After a weekend full of previews, there are just a handful today, including Mark Rosewater's on DailyMTG. Meghan and Maria have a card of their own and it's purrrrrrfect. 


1. Others to watch for later today

Before going to sleep (depending on where you live), you may see one card coming out of China and one or two others out of Europe from DreamHack Sweden and [local DailyMTG pages](http://magic.wizards.com/de/articles) in European languages, as they live in the future.


Oh, and to celebrate my upcoming wedding, I've given myself ([@blakepr](https://twitter.com/blakepr)) a preview card today as well that, I believe, will become a Commander staple.

After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [Grand Prix Las Vegas Wrap-Up](http://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2017/06/grand-prix-las-vegas-wrap/) | Hipsters of the Coast | Rich Stein

Apparently today is recap day. Stein recaps Las Vegas and looks ahead to what it means for future *Magic* events.


Hopefully it means another massive Grand Prix in Las Vegas two years from now.


1. [Dismantling Adages Throughout *Magic*](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/35305_Dismantling-Adages-Throughout-Magic.html) | Emma Handy | StarCityGames.com

If this were a clickbait article, it would read "Emma Handy totally DESTROYS everything you know about *Magic: The Gathering*!" Since it's not, we can just tell you that it's about getting past some common phrases players take as gospel in *Magic*, like "Always [Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) the [Bird](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise)," and "Never concede until you're dead."


1. [Standard Power Rankings: A New Format](https://www.channelfireball.com/power-rankings/) | Channel Fireball

[Oketra's Monument](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oketra%27s+Monument) on the list of best cards in Standard? This is a format I'm interested in. If it's something that interests you, [we're also starting to see results come in from *Magic Online*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-06-19).
