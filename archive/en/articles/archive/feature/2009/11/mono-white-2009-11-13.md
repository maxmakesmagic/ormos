If you're a fan of white cards, you'll love the deck Eulaers Wannes took to **Magic** Game Day in Antwerp, Belgium. Using powerful creatures like [Captain of the Watch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Captain+of+the+Watch) and [Baneslayer Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel), Eulaers was able to stall the ground against aggressive opponents. [Emeria, the Sky Ruin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emeria%2C+the+Sky+Ruin) helped ensure a solid long-game plan against control players, and making sure it all came together was none other than [Kor Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Cartographer). The 2/2 could make sure the land drops were hit for the expensive creatures while also guaranteeing Emeria would be active and minimizing the amount of land draws Wannes experienced.
