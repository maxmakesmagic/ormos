![Broodmate Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=BROODMATE+DRAGON)Kai Budde was a near-unanimous decision for induction into the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, and he returned to the game for the 2009 World Championships. His deck of choice for the weekend? Jund, which made up over 1/3rd of the field. His version featured standards like [Garruk Wildspeaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Garruk+Wildspeaker), [Broodmate Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodmate+Dragon), and [Sprouting Thrinax](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sprouting+Thrinax) with powerful sideboard options like [Goblin Ruinblaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ruinblaster). For more on the 2009 World Championships, [visit the official event coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12).
