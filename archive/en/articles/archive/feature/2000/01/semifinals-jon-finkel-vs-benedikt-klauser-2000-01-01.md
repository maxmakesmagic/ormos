### Game One:


**Turn one** :  

F: Play Island  

F: Play Voltaic Key


K: Play Forest  

K: Play Birds of Paradise


**Turn two** :  

F: Play Island  

F: Play Grim Monolith  

F: Play Metalworker


K: Play Forest  

K: Play Rofellos, Llanowar Emissary


**Turn three** :  

F: Tap Metalworker, show Phyrexian Processor and Masticore  

F: Use Key to untap Metalworker, tap Metalworker, show Processor and Masticore  

F: Play Masticore  

F: Shoot the Rofellos, shoot the Birds of Paradise


K: Play Forest  

K: Play Llanowar Elf  

K: Play another Rofellos, Llanowar Emissary


**Turn four** :  

F: Discard Island to Masticore  

F: Tap Metalworker, show Metalworker and Phyrexian Processor  

F: Shoot the Rofellos and the Birds  

F: Attack with his Masticore  

F: Untap Metalworker with the Key  

F: Tap Metalworker, show Processor and Metalworker  

F: Play Phyrexian Processor, pay eight life


K: Play forest


**Turn five** :  

F: Untap, upkeep tap Metalworker, show Metalworker during upkeep to float mana, discard Metalworker to Masticore  

F: Untap Monolith  

F: Make an 8/8 Minion token


K: Play Forest  

K: Play Deranged Hermit


**Turn six** :  

F: Untap, discard Tinker to Masticore  

F: Tap Metalworker, show Mishra's Helix  

F: Shoot Deranged Hermit  

F: Untap the Grim Monolith  

F: Play Tinker, sacrifice Monolith to get a Thran Dynamo  

F: Shoot two Squirrel tokens  

F: Attack Masticore and Minion token


K: Block token with a Squirrel, let Masticore through


K: Play Priest of Titania


**Turn seven** :  

F: Untap, upkeep tap Metalworker, show Mishra's Helix, shoot the Priest of Titania and the last squirrel token  

F: Attacks with Masticore and Minion token


Finkel 1 - Klauser 0


### Game Two:


Klauser sideboards in Splinters and Uktabi Orangutans. Finkel doesn't sideboard. Klauser mulligans once.


**Turn one** :  

K: Play Forest  

K: Play Birds of Paradise


F: Play Island  

F: Play Voltaic Key


**Turn two** :  

K: Play Treetop Village


F: Play Rishadan Port


**Turn three** :  

F: Upkeep Port Treetop Village


K: Play Forest


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/448.jpg) F: Play Island  

F: Play Grim Monolith  

F: Play Thran Dynamo  

F: Play Voltaic Key  

F: Play Tangle Wire  

F: Play Metalworker


**Turn four** :  

Klauser does nothing during his turn except from tapping his lands to the Tangle Wire.  

F: Upkeep, taps Island, Tangle Wire, Voltaic Key to the Tangle Wire  

F: Attack Metalworker


**Turn five** :  

Klauser taps permanents to Tangle Wire.


F: Upkeep, Port a land


K: Play Rishadan Port


F: Untap Grim Monolith during Klauser's end of turn step


F: Upkeep, tap Tangle Wire and island to Tangle Wire  

F: Tap Metalworker, show Phyrexian Processor and ?  

F: Use Key to untap Metalworker, show Processor and ?  

F: Play Phyrexian Processor, pay ten  

F: Make a token, untap Processor, make a token


**Turn six** :  

K: Tap forest and Rishadan Port to Tangle Wire


F: Port a land


Klauser thinks for a while, then concedes.


Finkel 2 - Klauser 0


### Game Three:


**Turn one** :  

K: Play Treetop Village


F: Play Saprazzan Skerry


**Turn two** :  

K: Play Forest  

K: Play Rofellos, Llanowar Emissary


F: Play Grim Monolith  

F: Play Island  

F: Play Thran Dynamo  

F: Play Metalworker


**Turn three** :  

K: Play Forest  

K: Play Priest of Titania  

K: Play Tangle Wire


F: Upkeep, add four mana to his pool from the lands he taps for Tangle Wire  

F: Untap Monolith  

F: Play Crystal Vein  

F: Play Thran Dynamo


**Turn four** :  

K: Taps Tangle Wire, Forest and Forest to Tangle Wire  

K: Play Gaea's Cradle  

K: Activate Treetop Village  

K: Attack Treetop Village, Priest of Titania, Rofellos


F: Tap three lands for the Tangle Wire  

F: Play Island  

F: Play Phyrexian Colossus


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/445.jpg)**Turn five** :  

K: Tap Tangle Wire and Forest to Tangle Wire  

F: Play Uktabi Orangutan, kill the Colossus  

K: Play Skyshroud Poacher  

F: Tap two islands to Tangle Wire  

F: Play Saprazzan Skerry  

F: Play Metalworker


**Turn six** :  

K: Untap, tap the Tangle Wire to the Tangle Wire  

K: Activate Treetop Village  

K: Attack Treetop Village and Uktabi Orangutan


F: Doubleblocks the Orangutan with two Metalworkers


Finkel scoops.


Finkel 2 - Klauser 1


At this time, the cameras switched away from the match. Finkel went on to win 3-1.
