Top 8 Profile - Erik Leander
============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPHEL00/168.jpg)**Name:** Erik Leander  
**Age:** 34  
**Location:** Tranemo, Sweden  
**Occupation:** Teacher  
**Played Magic since:** 1996  
**Best accomplishments:** This tournament



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Helsinki?**  

A: Haven't really decided yet.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: To have fun and meet friends.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: Quite a few mistakes, but lots of topdecking both days.



**Q: What do you think about the formats used in this tournament, and what was your strategy?**  

A: It was fun, but I'd be lying if I claimed to have a strategy. Last night I tried Rochester Draft with Invasion for the first time.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Lots of luck.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: It's certainly my best result ever, and it means I'm going to LA.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: I think I'll be very nervous.
