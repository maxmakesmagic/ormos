**Release date:** September 14, 2011\*


\* Availability may vary by platform and region


Features:


* Take on 3 opponents with your scheme deck: Now you get to play as the Archenemy in single-player and online multiplayer formats.
* Three new playable decks: Take on your opponent's with Liliana Vess's "Grave Whispers" deck, Ajani Goldmane's "Auramancer" deck, and Ral Zarek's "Cloudburst" deck.
* Cards from the latest expansion: The expansion includes six exclusive sneak-preview cards from the upcoming Innistrad expansion.
* New Campaign levels: The expansion adds on six new single-player campaign levels and eleven Archenemy single-player campaign levels.
* New cards for your favorite decks: The expansion provides four additional card unlocks for all ten original *Duels of the Planeswalkers 2012* decks.
* Plus even more: Such as four new challenges now where you are the Archenemy, new achievements on XBox, Steam®, and PlayStation®, and mew loading screens featuring art and text exclusive to expansion.
