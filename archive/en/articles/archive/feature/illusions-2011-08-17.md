With the release of ***Magic** 2012*, many a player has been looking for the best way to use the Illusions tribe, now backed by a lord for the first time in the form of [Lord of the Unreal](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lord+of+the+Unreal). Griffin Corrigan may have hit upon the correct strategy: the above list is the one Griffin used at the TCGPlayer.com Championship to earn a Top 16 finish. Read [full coverage](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/TCGplayer_Championship_Series/75k/) of the event at TCGPlayer.com.
