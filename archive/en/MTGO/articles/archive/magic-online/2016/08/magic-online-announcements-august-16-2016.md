**Prize Change for the Upcoming August 21 MOCS Monthly Event**


Due to the attendance in limited MOCS Monthly events exceeding expectations, we are issuing a one-time increase in prizes for the last limited MOCS Monthly of the current year. You can see the new prize structure below. Please note that these prize changes apply **only** to the August 21 MOCS Monthly event. This event is the last opportunity to use you Qualifier Points in a limited MOCS Monthly. Also, this event is capped at **1,024** players. Please register early if you intend to play, as we expect this event to fill to capacity.


**Start Times:** Sunday, August 21, 7:00 a.m. All times are Pacific Standard Time (for UTC, add 7 hours).  
**Location:** Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Scheduled  
**Entry Option:** 35 Qualifier Points  
**Product:** *Magic Online* will provide product for Limited Events.  
**Size:** 8–1,024 players  
**Play Style:** Swiss  
**Format:** *Shadows over Innistrad*-*Eldritch Moon* Sealed, follwed by a Top 8 Draft

**Build Notes for Deployment, Beginning 8:00 a.m./3:00 p.m. UTC (Occurs During Downtime)**


**Gameplay**


* Cardset | Cards such as Oath of Nissa, Ancient Stirrings, and Peer Through Depths, that have a player reveal one of a small number of cards from the top of their library, once again put the revealed card into the Revealed Cards area.
* Cardset | Bloodhall Priest's ability will correctly trigger if it enters the battlefield during an opponent's turn.
* Cardset | Mirrorwing Dragon no longer occasionally creates spell copies controlled by an incorrect player.
* Cardset | The delirium counter in a player’s graveyard will now correctly persist for the duration of the game once it appears, even if a player no longer has any delirium cards on the battlefield, in the graveyard, or in exile.
* Cardset | Slayer’s Cleaver no longer forces non-Eldrazi creatures to block the equipped creature.
* Cardset | Flames of the Blood Hand now deals the correct amount of damage.
* Cardset | Text displayed during the resolution of a spell or ability that requires its controller to reveal a card from the top of their library, such as Vessel of Nascency, has been fixed.
* Cardset | An issue causing green highlighting to persist after resolving a spell or ability that involves selecting a card in the graveyard, such as Corpse Churn, has been resolved.
* Cardset | Double-faced and meld cards are now showing related cards correctly in zoom view.
* Cardset | Gisa and Geralf no longer prevents the use of Karador, Ghost Chieftain's ability.
* Cardset | Meld cards can no longer be incorrectly turned face down.
* Cardset | Spells that exile a permanent until end of turn now correctly return both cards of a melded permanent.
* Cardset | Copies of planeswalkers no longer display a copy icon overlapping their loyalty counters.
* Cardset | Eldrazi Scion tokens now display the proper art, mana symbol, and card frame.
* Cardset | Rite of Flame once again adds the correct amount of mana to the controller's mana pool.
* Duel Scene | Planeswalkers with four abilities now correctly display all four while in a player’s hand.

**Client**


* Duel Scene | A rare issue where players would sometimes be paired incorrectly in tournaments has been fixed.
* General | Sort by Rarity once again works as intended in draft, deckbuilding, sideboarding, and the Collection Scene.


**Build Notes for Hotfix, Occuring at Approximately 2:00 p.m. PT/9:00 p.m. UTC (No Player-Facing Downtime)**


Following tomorrow’s downtime, at 2:00 p.m. PT/9:00 p.m. UTC, there will be a new client build deployed to address the below issues. In order to get the new version, players will need to restart their client. This hotfix deployment will not impact server connectivity.


* Duel Scene | Fixed an issue where players could get two Duel Scene windows when re-joining a League match in progress
* Duel Scene | Fixed an issue where players could get stuck in the sideboard when re-joining a League match in progress
