When a group of European players introduced the five color "Quick 'n Toast" deck at [Pro Tour–Hollywood](/en/events/coverage/gindy-puts-name-lights), the Standard format was irrevocably changed. The impact of that deck can still be felt today, and this list used by Chris Poff to win the Texas State Championship is a great example. Chris added platinum hits from *Shards of Alara* like [Violent Ultimatum](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Violent%2BUltimatum) and [Cruel Ultimatum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Ultimatum), [Bant Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bant+Charm), [Esper Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Esper+Charm), and [Broodmate Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodmate+Dragon) to beat opponents into submission.
