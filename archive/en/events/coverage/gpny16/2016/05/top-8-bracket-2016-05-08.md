TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 8, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/080214_1291.jpg)
By Jacob Van Lunen




Jacob Van Lunen began playing Magic in 1995. He has participated in organized play at every level of competition and was a member of the winning team at Pro Tour San Diego in 2007, thanks to an innovative draft strategy. As a writer, Van Lunen has had more than three hundred Magic strategy pieces published 






After many rounds of Swiss and some great games of *Magic*, the Top 8 for Grand Prix New York is set! Learn more about this event’s Top 8 competitors, their history at the top tables, and more by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles.  

 




---


### Brandon Ayers


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Banking


**Hometown:** Omaha, NE


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Playing trios with John Rolf and Pako. Also, a couple of Pro Tours.


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Green-White Tokens. My Roommate Jake audibled decks so all the cards were ready for battle!  
  
**What is the most important card in your 75 cards?**  
[Declaration in Stone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Declaration+in+Stone). Slops to only two [Oath of Nissa](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oath+of+Nissa).


**What was the coolest deck you played against, or what was the coolest thing that happened in a game this weekend?**  

Grixis opponents not casting spells.


**Any Mom shout-outs for Mother’s Day?**  

Thanks for everything Mom!




---


### Louis Samuel-Deltour


**Age:** 24


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Paris, France


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

3 Grand Prix Top 8s, Top 4 French Nationals


**What deck did you play and why?**  

4C Cryptolith. Very good.


**What is the most important card in your 75 cards?**  
[Collected Company](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collected+Company)


**What was the coolest deck you played against, or what was the coolest thing that happened in a game this weekend?**  

I sacrificed an Ormendahl to [Westvale Abbey](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Westvale+Abbey) to prevent lifelink and won on the swingback!


**Any Mom shout-outs for Mother’s Day?**  

Mother’s Day is only a couple weeks from now in France, but I of course love my mom! First person I called after the Top 8 announcement!

### Michael Majors


**Age:** 24


**Occupation:** Magic


**Hometown:** Augusta, GA


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

1st Grand Prix San Diego


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Green-White Tokens. Gerry [Thompson] showed me a picture of his board.


**What is the most important card in your 75 cards?**  
[Archangel Avacyn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+Avacyn). A [Serra Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serra+Angel) that plays offense, defense, and is a sweeper!


**What was the coolest deck you played against, or what was the coolest thing that happened in a game this weekend?**  

Daniel Musser exiled three creatures permanently with an [Eldrazi Displacer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Displacer) and [Hallowed Moonlight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hallowed+Moonlight)!


**Any Mom shout-outs for Mother’s Day?**  

I am unbelievably blessed to have Nancy Majors as a mother! I love you!

**Age:** 33

**Hometown:** Foxboro, MA


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**

[Archangel Avacyn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+Avacyn). It’s incredibly hard to play around.

She told me she wanted a trophy for Mother’s Day and I don’t want to let Mom down!
