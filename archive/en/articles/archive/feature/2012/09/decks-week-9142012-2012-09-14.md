Decks of the Week: 9/14/2012
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 14, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The qualifying season for Pro Tour *Gatecrash* is under way! Find out about the Limited formats used in the current season of Pro Tour Qualifiers, as well as a schedule of PTQs, [here](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/gatecrash13-qualifiers). And to give you an idea of what kinds of archetypes players are drafting these days, check out the Top 8 decklists from recent PTQs, including the one held last weekend on **Magic Online**. You might also be interested in looking at the [*Return to Ravnica* Card Image Gallery](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/returntoravnica/cig), which shows off some of the cards that will undoubtedly be played at the Pro Tour in October! Enjoy!
