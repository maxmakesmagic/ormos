*Dark Ascension* Tokens
=======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 18, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











There aren't many new tokens in *Dark Ascension* booster packs, but the ones that exist do have a lot of "new" to show off.

![Human](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/888_9o5nz8vmds_1.jpg)  
There are well over a thousand Humans in **Magic**. Isn't it about time there was a Human token? As Mark Rosewater pointed out during *Innistrad* previews, there's no point having vampires and werewolves and monsters in your horror story if there aren't humans to be menaced.

![Human](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/888_9o5nz8vmds_2.jpg)  
Oh, and speaking of Vampires, there are so many of them on Innistrad (and in *Innistrad* and *Dark Ascension*) that it only makes sense to be generating some tokens. But that's not the big news. This is:

![Human](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/888_9o5nz8vmds_3.jpg)  
This is the first physical emblem token to show up in regular booster packs. It ties in to [Sorin, Lord of Innistrad](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/stf/177), who makes these emblems at a cost of just two loyalty. So you might need a few!
