Feature Match Round 3 - Laura Karem vs. Andy Stokinger
======================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 11, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_133.png)
By Eli Kaplan











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpmil02/983.jpg)Andy Stokinger, who is coming off a top 64 finish at Pro Tour: Nice and is noted for maintaining the notorious Team Academy website, faced off against Laura Karem. Karem hails from Kentucky and recently finished 27th at Grand Prix Atlanta. Both players came to the table cool, calm, and collected.

The matchup pitted Karem's blue, red, and white control deck versus Stokinger's madness-fueled green-blue deck with a splash of red. Victory would be determined by seeing if Stokinger's quick beats of [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) and [Arrogant Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm) would make it past Karem's counters and bounce tactics.

Stokinger won the roll, and chose to play first. He started the party with a second turn [Merfolk Looter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter). He tried to follow that up with a [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel), but Karem would have none of that and Counterspelled the dog. But when it rains, it pours, and on his next turn his next Mongrel made it to the table assisted by a madness-paid [Circular Logic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpmil02/984.jpg)Karem's stock appeared to sink low as she could only meet Stokinger's third Mongrel with a [Memory Lapse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse). Stokinger's [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) kept nipping at her heels, bringing her life total to 14. She threw a [Prophetic Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prophetic+Bolt) at Stokinger's [Merfolk Looter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter), but it was met a [Circular Logic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic). She then was able to Fire away the Looter, and [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction)ed to [Prophetic Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prophetic+Bolt) a newly cast Mongrel. But Stokinger topdecked a fourth Mongrel, and Karem needed amazing draws to stay in the game.

At 6 life, Karem rolled the dice and threw down a [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) which was split into 2 [Fire/Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire%2FIce)s and a pile with [Repulse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Repulse) and 2 land. Karem took the two [Fire/Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire%2FIce)s. Stokinger couldn't commit more beef to the table while Karem was Icing his [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel), his [Arrogant Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm) meeting [Memory Lapse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse). Karem continued to grab for board control, turning over an amazing five cards with [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction). Stokinger stared at [Earthquake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake), [Goblin Trenches](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Trenches), [Syncopate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Syncopate), [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), and [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell).

Stokinger split the piles into Trenches/[Syncopate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Syncopate) and [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell)/[Earthquake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake)/[Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), and Karem reached for the smaller pile. She then played [Goblin Trenches](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Trenches) with 8 lands on the table. 

Stokinger kept attacking with his sole creature, [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). But the well-trained Goblin Soldiers kept cropping up out of the [Goblin Trenches](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Trenches). His [Arrogant Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm) was repeatedly [Repulse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Repulse)d, and finally countered. On the other side of the table, Karem kept dropping land after land. Stokinger sighed. "It would be extremely unsportsmanlike to call my opponent evil." Karem started to send her armies of Goblin Soldiers at Stokinger, whose life fell from 14, to 11, to 7, and finally 0. Karem's perseverance had paid off. "Sometimes this deck wins when it really shouldn't."

Laura Karem - Andy Stokinger 1-0

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpmil02/985.jpg)Game 2 was a far smoother affair, with Stokinger bringing the beats with a turn 2 Mongrel. Karem failed to make a land drop on her third turn. She drew a mountain off the top on her fourth turn and played an [Earthquake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake) for two. Stokinger discarded an irrelevant [Epicenter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Epicenter) and kept the beats up, adding a [Merfolk Looter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter) to his side. Karem had a [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God) in hand, but few counters and needed to draw a second source of white mana to clear the board. She couldn't find it, and Stokinger put his own life on the line to soak up a [Flaming Gambit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flaming+Gambit) and defeat Karem.

Laura Karem - Andy Stokinger 1-1

The third game saw Stokinger draw into a hand with 3 land, [Epicenter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Epicenter), [Merfolk Looter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter), [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), and [Jilt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jilt), but no offense. He mulliganed and drew into a virtually identical hand (minus the [Jilt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jilt)). An Ice kept Stokinger from playing his [Merfolk Looter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter) until turn 3, and Karem added insult to injury over his next two turns by Repulsing the Looter not once, but twice. On the third attempt, the Looter stayed on the table. But after countering a Karem [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), Stokinger was dismayed when the Looter was sacked without notice and Fired. 

Karem locked down the board by dropping a [Goblin Trenches](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Trenches). Her cigarette Goblin Soldier tokens would prove to be cancer to Stokinger, who attempted to stop the bleeding with [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). 

*Facing battalions  
 Even swift hounds resemble  
 Dust in tornados*

Despite using [Circular Logic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic) to keep a [Flametongue Kavu](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flametongue+Kavu) from eradicating his faithful Mongrel, Stokinger couldn't handle the onslaught of Goblin Soldier Tokens. A Jilt with Kicker could only slay two of the red and white emblazoned army, and the masses of Trench-spawn marched over to bring Kasem the win.

Laura Karem- Andy Stokinger 2-1
