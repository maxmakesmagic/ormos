Top 8 Profile: Stan van der Velden
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 24, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbar02/a916.jpg)**Name:** Stan van der Velden

**Age:** 18

**Occupation:** Student

**Where do you live?** Heerlen, the Netherlands

**Since when do you play Magic?** Since Mirage

**Best accomplishments:**   

25th Pro Tour New Orleans  

7th Grand Prix Frankfurt  

I beat Jelger (Wiegersma) once...

**What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Barcelona; what did you expect to find?**  

Qualification for Pro Tour Nice: I expected to qualify and I did...

**What do you think is the main reason for you to make Top 8 in this tournament and why?**  

I played Magic Online a lot.   

That helps to develop your drafting-with-suboptimal-players skill lots.

**What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

I will not win. I slept for one hour in the last two nights L 

**Anything else you'd like to share with the Sideboard Online readers?**  

ROAR!
