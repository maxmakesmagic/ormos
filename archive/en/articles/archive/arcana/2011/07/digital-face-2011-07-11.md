What's the Digital Face-Off, you ask?


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/751_GarrukLiliana.jpg)We'll give the basics here, but there's [complete information](http://community.wizards.com/magicthegathering/blog/2011/07/07/digital_face_off%20) available. Basically, eight teams from big digital gaming companies will battle each other in **Duels of the Planeswalkers** 2012. And you (yes, *you*!) get to play along by predicting who will win.
