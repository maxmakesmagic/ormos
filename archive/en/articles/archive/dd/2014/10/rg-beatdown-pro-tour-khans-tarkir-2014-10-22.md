RG Beatdown at Pro Tour Khans of Tarkir
=======================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on October 22, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_shuheinakamura.jpg)
By Shuhei Nakamura










I am sympathetic to people who feel that an ordinary beatdown deck is too simplistic but also don't want to go with Green Devotion-style decks because they're too top-heavy. I too have felt that way for a long time. If you're of a similar mind, I recommend for you Benjamin Weitz's RG deck.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Crater's Claws)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crater's Claws)
While the basic structure of using mana creatures to accelerate into [Polukranos, World Eater](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polukranos%2C+World+Eater) is the same as a Green Devotion deck, in terms of speed the deck is a completely different animal with the addition of what is probably the best three-mana aggro creature, [Goblin Rabblemaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Rabblemaster), and the walking burn spell known as [Sarkhan, the Dragonspeaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sarkhan%2C+the+Dragonspeaker). There has been some debate as to whether [Stormbreath Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormbreath+Dragon) or Sarkhan is better; this deck chooses not to take sides by putting as many copies of both in.


Normally, a build with seven copies of cards in the five-mana range would be seen as way too slow, but with a total of *eleven* mana creatures it's not a problem for this deck. The hasty creatures will do an efficient job of whittling down the opponent's life, and that will put him or her into range of the four [Crater's Claws](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crater%27s+Claws) included as burn spells.


It feels really good to go Dragon, Dragon, [Crater's Claws](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crater%27s+Claws).
