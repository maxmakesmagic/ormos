Team Round 3: United States vs. Scotland
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 17, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a617.jpg)If history has taught us one thing, it is to never count out the United States team. Last year, the accomplished Trevor Blackwell came to Worlds with two unknown commodities in Eugene Harvey and Brian Hegstad. On day one they had a combined record of 6-12, headed by Blackwell's 0-6 record. After spectators and participants alike had counted out the Americans, they went on a 16-2 tear in draft on their way to Sunday and a surprising victory. 

This year was the exact same scenario, except this time Harvey was the accomplished player, and Eric Franz and Andrew Ranks both had zero Pro Tour points. Not many people expected the United States to win this year, especially with The Netherlands and Germany having two powerhouse teams. After an unimpressive start this week that seemed to prove those predictions right, the United States does still have a chance to make it to Sunday, but will need to win the next two matches and have some assistance with tiebreakers.

Dave Price- "If you guys can't beat a bunch of Scotsmen with hangovers...we SAW them last night!"

Ranks stopped a [Barbarian Lunatic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbarian+Lunatic) cold with [Phantom Tiger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Tiger), and [Otarian Juggernaut](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Otarian+Juggernaut) gave him another threat. Campbell increased his bear population with [Mad Dog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mad+Dog), and Ranks dropped the powerful [Treacherous Vampire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treacherous+Vampire). [Mad Dog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mad+Dog) simply charged into the Juggernaut, which was finished off with [Flame Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flame+Burst). Not much was left, as Campbell only drew four spells the whole game. 

Franz started off with an impressive board of [Militant Monk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Militant+Monk) and [Ember Beast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Beast), but Ross had the ever-annoying [Shieldmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shieldmage+Advocate) hanging around. It was joined by [Possessed Nomad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Possessed+Nomad), then [Battlewise Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlewise+Aven) and [Benevolent Bodyguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Benevolent+Bodyguard) to make the concept of coming through for damage very foreign to Franz. [Balshan Griffin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Balshan+Griffin) added more offense in the sky, and a second Advocate made things even worse. With a draw of eleven land and five spells, Franz was ill-equipped to deal with Ross' draw and lost. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a618.jpg)Penman's [Frightcrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler) and [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) were doing a bad job of holding off Harvey's [Possessed Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Possessed+Centaur) and [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) with [Ghostly Wings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghostly+Wings). [Execute](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Execute) did deal with Harvey's [Hallowed Healer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hallowed+Healer), and [Acorn Harvest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acorn+Harvest) provided a little more to work with. Harvey [Spelljack](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spelljack)ed a Boneyard Slasher, and eventually cast it when [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) got him to threshold. That flying team was good enough to give Harvey the first game. 

The second game was a bit rougher for Ranks, due to Campbell using [Faceless Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) to take out [Phantom Tiger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Tiger). However, he did have [Treacherous Vampire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treacherous+Vampire) again, Campbell's [Frenetic Ogre](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frenetic+Ogre) attacked, and Ranks cast [Waste Away](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Waste+Away) before blockers on the Butcher to get back his Tiger. It blocked and killed the Ogre, and suddenly the American had [Treacherous Vampire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treacherous+Vampire) and a 2/1 Tiger to the Scotsman's [Barbarian Outcast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbarian+Outcast). The Tiger traded with the Outcast, and [Morgue Theft](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morgue+Theft) brought it right back as a 3/2, while Campbell had no creatures in play. He did have a [Goretusk Firebeast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goretusk+Firebeast) and a [Mad Dog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mad+Dog), but it wasn't nearly enough. Ranks played [Sickening Dreams](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sickening+Dreams) and used [Organ Grinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Organ+Grinder) in response, and attacked for the win to put the United States up one match.

The second game between Penman and Harvey had a deadlocked board, with Penman's [Springing Tiger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Springing+Tiger), [Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartographer) and [Anurid Swarmsnapper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anurid+Swarmsnapper) staring down Harvey's [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba), [Nantuko Tracer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Tracer) and Swarmsnapper. Harvey got a bit of an advantage with [Psionic Gift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psionic+Gift), but Penman had a great threat of his own in [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). 

The second game between Ross and Franz was completely different, as Ross got stuck at two lands and was run over to bring their match to a third and deciding game.

Harvey summoned [Cephalid Looter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Looter), and now had a real threat that would eventually give him the answers to break through for damage. Penman seemed to have the answer in [Narcissism](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Narcissism), but Harvey [Spelljack](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spelljack)ed it. Stealing the powerful enchantment proved to be enough to take the game once an unblocked [Possessed Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Possessed+Centaur) became bigger with [Sudden Strength](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sudden+Strength). This gave the United States the win, and a win next round will put them in contention for Top 2. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a619.jpg)The last game between Ross and Franz saw Ross build up an impressive board with [Aven Windreader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Windreader), [Phantom Nomad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nomad), [Possessed Nomad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Possessed+Nomad) and [Border Patrol](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Border+Patrol). Franz only had [Phantom Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Flock), and a [Vigilant Sentry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigilant+Sentry) that had been enchanted with [Stupefying Touch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stupefying+Touch). [Benevolent Bodyguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Benevolent+Bodyguard) gave Ross the insurance he needed, and he attacked with everyone but a 1/1 Nomad. [Anarchist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anarchist) and [Vigilant Sentry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigilant+Sentry) blocked [Possessed Nomad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Possessed+Nomad), and [Phantom Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Flock) blocked [Aven Windreader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Windreader) and became bigger thanks to [Guided Strike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Guided+Strike). The Bodyguard saved the Windreader before first strike damage. Franz played [Book Burning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Book+Burning) for the second time in the game, and Ross responded by activating the Windreader, which revealed...[Shower of Coals](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shower+of+Coals).

Ross- "[Book Burning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Book+Burning) can resolve!"

[Strength of Isolation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strength+of+Isolation) enchanted the [Phantom Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Flock), giving Franz a creature that could block the Windreader all day long. Still, the Advocate made getting through damage very difficult. A second one made it practically impossible. [Ambassador Laquatus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ambassador+Laquatus) finished it up, but the United States still won the match 2-1.
