Magic: The Gathering Collector Coins
====================================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on January 10, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Today we are announcing our partnership with the New Zealand Mint to produce a series of **Magic: The Gathering** collector coins! Each coin is a one ounce 0.999 silver coin featuring iconic Magic art. Our first coin will feature the artwork of none other than [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) by Jason Chan.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1385_jacecoin.jpg)*Front*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1385_coinback.jpg)*Back*
Each coin will be packaged in a display case and will come with an individually numbered certificate of authenticity.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1385_displaybox.jpg)Since this item will make use of real silver in each coin, the pricing will be finalized once we get closer to launch. If you are a retailer interested in finding out more about purchasing these collector items, visit [this page on the New Zealand Mint's website](http://www.nzmint.com/coins/Mtg) and complete their form to sign up. If you're an individual interested in purchasing one of these coins, visit [this page on NZMint.com](http://www.nzmint.com/coins/Magic_Register).


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1385_nzmint.png)
