The first results from the Modern format on **Magic Online** are in! User superchibi put in a 4-0 performance with a Modern take on the [Living End](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End) deck. Cycle and sacrifice until your graveyard is full of creatures, then cascade into [Living End](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End), simultaneously getting rid of your opponent's creatures and flopping most of your graveyard onto the battlefield. For more Modern deck lists, check out [the **Magic Online** page](/en/articles/archive/2009-06-29)!
