Janos Csomos is the 2009 Slovakian National Champion! He used this take on the popular Cascade Swans deck to take the top slot. Interesting changes? Three [Primal Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Command)s, which allow the deck to recycle all its discarded lands and, in effect, "go infinite" by continuously recycling the [Primal Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Command)s as well. He'll head to Rome later this year to defend his nation's pride at the World Championships.
