Top 8: Michael Pustilnik
========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPPORTO00/Images/203.jpg)**Name:** Michael Pustilnik  
**Age:** 35 :(  
**Location:** Brooklyn, USA  
**Occupation:** Biochemist (formerly)  
**Played Magic since:** 1994, Fallen Empires  
**Best accomplishments:** Top 8 PTNY '97, 1st GP Memphis '99, 13th PTNY '99.  


Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Porto?  

A: Pro Tour points ---> Masters invitations



Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?  

A: Top 32. I expected to be jet-lagged out.



Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.  

A: I alternated between well and poorly played matches.



Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?  

A: My insanely broken sealed deck.



Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?  

A: After a poor year, it feels great to have a good result.



Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?  

A: Dave Williams wins.
