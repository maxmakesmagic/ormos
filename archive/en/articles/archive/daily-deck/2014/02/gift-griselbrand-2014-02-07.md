The Gift of Griselbrand
=======================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on February 11, 2014 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Gerry Thompson











 When I saw this deck, I knew I had to feature it. Both [Griselbrand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Griselbrand) and [Gifts Ungiven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven) are among my favorite cards and this deck is different than most of the things I've seen people doing in Modern. 

 Decks based on [Gifts Ungiven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven) typically feature an [Unburial Rites](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unburial+Rites) and a fatty, as you can search for only two cards with Gifts and your opponent is forced to send them both to the graveyard. What this deck does differently is feature Gifts in a mostly aggressive deck. 

 I love that Smaland uses [Griselbrand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Griselbrand) as the only fatty, as it's more aggressive than something like [Iona, Shield of Emeria](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iona%2C+Shield+of+Emeria) or [Elesh Norn, Grand Cenobite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elesh+Norn%2C+Grand+Cenobite), cards that are both focused on locking someone out of the game. [Griselbrand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Griselbrand) combos well with [Vapor Snag](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vapor+Snag) and [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt), spells that are incredibly cheap ways to affect the board. 

If you're looking for a super fun deck to try in Modern, I can't recommend this one highly enough.
