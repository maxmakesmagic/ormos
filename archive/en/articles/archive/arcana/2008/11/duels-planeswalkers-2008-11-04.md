Duels of the Planeswalkers
==========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 4, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Pro Tour–Berlin featured the public debut of a much-anticipated product: the *Duels of the Planeswalkers* game for XBOX and PC!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1713_playing.jpg)*Players at Pro Tour–Berlin try out the new game*  
It's going to be available on XBOX Live in Spring 2009, and it will feature a Campaign Mode (single-player or co-op) as well as online dueling and 2HG. The PC game will be single-player Campaign only. There will be challenges and puzzles galore. And now, with more details and a look at the game itself, here are coverage reporters Brian David-Marshall and Randy Buehler with the scoop:
