Decks of the Week: 4/12/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 12, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Last weekend, seventy-one countries across the globe ran their first of three [World **Magic** Cup Qualifiers](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/event/worldmagiccup/qualifiers). If you missed last weekend's event in your country, don't worry! Two more qualifiers will run, on the weekends of April 20–21 and June 1–2. We're going to take a look at some of the Top 8 decks from those WMCQs, which is relevant for those of you hoping to win a Pro Tour *Theros* Qualifier… because just like the PTQ season, every WMCQ uses Standard! Find the nearest Standard PTQ to you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros)! Good luck; have fun!
