White Weenie is an archetype as old as the game itself, and its representatives have graced this page more than once. But Patrizio Golia did something on **Magic Online** that few players would have thought possible at the beginning of the Extended season: Top 8ed with the archetype at the Pro Tour Qualifier level. The deck features powerful two-drop creatures like [Samurai of the Pale Curtain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Samurai+of+the+Pale+Curtain) and [Ethersworn Canonist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ethersworn+Canonist), which can significantly impact some of the more popular archetypes in the format. Patrizio also had [Mana Tithe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Tithe) for the occasional blowout. The counter could frequently catch an unassuming opponent off guard, punishing them for tapping low against a white deck and assuming the coast was clear.
