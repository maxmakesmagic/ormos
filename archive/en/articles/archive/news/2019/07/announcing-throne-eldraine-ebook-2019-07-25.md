Announcing the Throne of Eldraine Ebook
=======================================



 Posted in **News**
 on July 25, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The *Throne of Eldraine* story will be available as an ebook very soon. In fact, you'll be able to read the entire story before previews have even begun!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/images/daily/Fnsj3jush9.jpg)


*Throne of Eldraine: The Wildered Quest* is written by Kate Elliott, author of the popular Nicol Bolas origin story that debuted with *Core Set 2019*. Kate has been writing science fiction and fantasy for over 25 years; she's best known for her Crown of Stars epic fantasy series. We intend to keep working with premier authors, and we're very excited for Kate to write her second *Magic* story.


The full story will be available in English for $3.99 everywhere [Penguin Random House](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/634136/throne-of-eldraine-the-wildered-quest-by-kate-elliott/?pdivflag) publishes ebooks, including [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07VP1PQ81), [Barnes & Noble](https://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/throne-of-eldraine-kate-elliott/1132704843;jsessionid=6D8D6C23ACA8B2E5EB482C6388A30B3A.prodny_store02-atgap18?ean=9780786967087), and the [Apple Store](https://itunes.apple.com/us/book/isbn9780786967087). You can preorder the book beginning July 27 for delivery when the book is released on September 4.


This will be the only way to go deeper than the cards and read the full *Throne of Eldraine* story, and we can't wait for you to get your hands on it!
