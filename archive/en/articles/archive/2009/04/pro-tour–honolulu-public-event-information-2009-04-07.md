Pro Tour–Honolulu Public Event Information
==========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 7, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












### General Information


**Pro Tour Honolulu – "King of the Hill"**




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Enter a Scheduled Public Event | 1 Point |
| Finish Top 4 of a Scheduled Public Event | 2 Points |
| Enter an 8-player Event | 1 Point |
| Win an 8-player Event | 2 Points |

Play in a public event and earn points. Be a top finisher in a public event and earn more points! At the end of the weekend, the 5 players that have earned the most points will get to choose a reward!


The 5 players that earn the most points at the end of the weekend will get to choose (in order) from these rewards:


 


* 1 set of *Alara Reborn* block uncut, foil sheets (3 sheets total)
* 1 set of *Shards of Alara* block cards
* 1 display box of *Urza's Legacy* boosters
* 1 display box of *Urza's Destiny* boosters
* 1 *From The Vault: Dragons* limited edition box set

**A Planeswalker's Journey through the Pro Tour**


"Do you rule?"


Join us for this fun activity at Pro Tour Honolulu! In order to participate, pick up your badge at the Wizards Information Booth or the Public Events area. The badge has 15 achievements for you to try and accomplish during your weekend at the Pro Tour.


When you fulfill one of the items, your badge will be stamped to show your achievement. Get as many stamps as you can and lord it over your friends!


**2009 Magic Online Live Championship**


The 2009 Magic Online Live series provides new and old Magic Online players the chance to compete for cash prizes and an opportunity to talk with some of the developers and managers of Magic Online face to face.


At Pro Tour Honolulu, there will be 4 Challenges run throughout the weekend. Each qualifies 2 players to the Championship on Sunday. There is a $5000 purse for the Championship, with the winner receiving $2000.


* [Complete details](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/040209a)

**Open Dueling**


Players bring their own Standard-format deck. Players are then matched up against others for game play. After 5 games, players receive an *Alara Reborn* Booster. Play whenever you have time! Play the whole weekend! Cost: FREE!


**Prizes**


Booster pack prizes will be awarded based on attendance. Unless otherwise noted, top finishing players will receive current set boosters. Special events with a posted prize (i.e. iPod, Power 9, Xbox, etc) the winner of the special prize will receive only that prize.

### Public Events (Thursday – Sunday)


The following applies to all Public Events:


Unless otherwise indicated, all events are run Swiss +1 with no single elimination finals. Prizes are based on standings.


Registration opens 1 hour before the scheduled start time and closes 15 minutes before the scheduled start time. Players should be seated at least 5 minutes before the scheduled start time.

**\* Travel:**  

The players eligible to receive a travel award will receive instructions from Wizards of the Coast on whom they must contact and the deadline by which they must arrange their travel awards. Wizards of the Coast will not refund travel made outside this service. Wizards of the Coast reserves the right to award a cash equivalent award in lieu of any travel award in its sole discretion.


All travel itineraries must be from the international airport nearest the player's home and to the host city of the applicable Pro Tour or World Championship or event, and must include all the dates of that event. No award will be substituted if member is unable to participate. All other expenses are the responsibility of the member and may include, without limitation, accommodations, ground transportation, taxes, itinerary change fees, passport and visa fees.


This award must be redeemed through member's arrangement of travel itinerary no later than (30) days before departure. Failure to complete travel arrangements as directed by Wizards of the Coast prior to this deadline will result in forfeiture of the award. Every member receiving a travel award must provide to Wizards of the Coast an executed liability release, the appropriate US taxation form (W9 or W8) (or have one on file), current contact information (<http://webapp.wizards.com/dcimember/>), proof of ID and passport (when required by international destination). In all cases, all such documentation must be provided to Wizards of the Coast, or previously on file, at least thirty (30) days before departure. Failure to timely provide such documentation will result in forfeiture of the award.


**All awards:**


Players must attend, compete (though not necessarily on the same team), and be listed as finishing in the applicable tournament for which he or she received the award. Failure to comply with these requirements will result in forfeiture of the award.


All taxes are the responsibility of the members, and members acknowledge Wizards' recommendation that they consult a tax advisor in their jurisdictions for questions regarding their tax liabilities.


Awards are subject to change without notice.
