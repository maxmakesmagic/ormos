U.S. Nationals 
 1999 Reports
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 18, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/USNAT99/images/AwardsTeam.jpg)**Welcome 
 to Sideboard Online's ongoing coverage of the 1999 U.S. Nationals. We 
 will be posting round-by-round pairings, standings and match reports. 
 Keep checking back for updates. 


**Congratulation to Kyle Rose - U.S. National Champion! Congratulations 
 also to John Hunka and Christopher Tobler and Zvi Mowshowitz who have 
 won spots on the U.S. National Team.** 


 *Last update: Sunday, July 4, 1999 - 4:00 P.M. PST*


### Finals
