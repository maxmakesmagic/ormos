### NEWS


Magic 2015—Duels of the Planeswalkers Now on iPad
=================================================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)

Blake Rasmussen

Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever.


July 9, 2014
 







Psst. Come here. Closer. No, closer than that. We have a special announcement just for you, iPad users. A little present just for you being you.


Starting today, July 9, *Magic 2015—Duels of the Planeswalkers* is available on iPad—one week before other platforms begin releasing July 16.



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/news20140701_dotp15.jpg)

That means that iPad users get an exclusive first-play experience with Duels, allowing them to, ahem, [Hunt Bigger Game](http://www.huntbiggergame.com). Players play as a Planeswalker attempting to stop Garruk Wildspeaker from hunting new quarry—you.



<http://youtube.com/embed/5bhAXuDg7MA>
Don't have an iPad? Don't worry. *Magic 2015—Duels of the Planeswalkers* will later launch on PC via Steam®, Xbox 360, Xbox One, Amazon Kindle, and Android on Google Play beginning July 16.


And remember, purchasing *Magic 2015—Duels of the Planeswalkers* gives you access to a cool, six-card promotional booster featuring one of these two cards.





![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/arcana/afa7666ddf_1.png)


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/arcana/afa7666ddf_2.png)









Purchasing *Magic 2015—Duels of the Planeswalkers* on iPad, PC via Steam®, Android tablet via the Amazon App Store, and Google Play will give you access to Soul of Zendikar. Xbox 360 and Xbox One entitle you access to Soul of Ravnica.


So what are you waiting for? Jump on your iPad and start hunting bigger game—today!
