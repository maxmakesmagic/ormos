Decks of the Week: 9/1/2011
===========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 1, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The Modern format has arrived! **Magic Online** has been cranking out Daily Events for a week now (which you can find below), and on Friday the pros get their hands on the format at Pro Tour Philadelphia. The format stretches all the way back to *Eighth Edition* and the first *Mirrodin* block, providing more than 6,000 cards with which to craft an entirely new metagame. The [live Pro Tour Top 8 webcast](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/webcast/home) begins Sunday at 10:45 a.m. ET. One name we'll be watching for is Yuuya Watanabe, who just won back-to-back Grand Prix with his victory in Pittsburgh!
