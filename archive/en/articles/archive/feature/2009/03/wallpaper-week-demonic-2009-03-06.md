Wallpaper of the Week: Demonic
==============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 6, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












This week's desktop wallpaper is *two* wallpapers! As a special preview of *Duel Decks: Divine vs. Demonic*, we're going to show you two of **Magic**'s most demonic cards: [Lord of the Pit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lord+of+the+Pit) (by Chippy) and [Demonic Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demonic+Tutor) (by Scott Chou)! Remember, you can get your hands on the actual cards with *Duel Decks: Divine vs. Demonic* hits stores on April 10!
