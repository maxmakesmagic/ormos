1998 World ChampionshipsSaturday Coverage
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Day 4 of Worlds competition: the Team Competition. Each four-person team received four Starter Decks of *Fifth Edition* and four Booster Packs of *Fifth Edition*, from which they constructed four decks. The top 2 teams after six rounds played in single-elimination finals, in which two members of each team played Standard and two will play Rath Cycle constructed. They used the same decks they used through the rest of the tournament.
