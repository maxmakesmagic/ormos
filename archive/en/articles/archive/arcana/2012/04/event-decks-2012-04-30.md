 Event Decks
============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 30, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











The *Avacyn Restored* Event Decks won't be out for a few weeks, but that doesn't mean you can't look at them now. You can look at them... and you can *dream*. We can't take that away from you!

### Death's Encroach

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/961_pack1_2vb0dppmm3.jpg)  
Death’s Encroach Time to devour the living! Master a frighteningly quick force of flesh-hungry undead while you trip up your foe with a barrage of disruptive spells. Tonight is the last night the humans will draw breath.

### Humanity’s Vengeance

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/961_pack2_2vb0dppmm3.jpg)  
The humans of Innistrad fight to take back their home! Your crafty mortals will combine their talents to outmaneuver and outthink the hollow-skulled hordes. Slay enough ghouls and it’ll be safe once more to have a pulse.
