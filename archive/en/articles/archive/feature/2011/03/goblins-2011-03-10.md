Everyone knows red decks can win, but did you know that in today's Standard, they can win on a theme? Just ask Douglas Scheinberg! He made the Top 16 of the StarCityGames.com Standard Open in Edison, New Jersey playing this modern take on a Goblins deck. [Ember Hauler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Hauler), [Goblin Chieftain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Chieftain), [Goblin Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide), and [Spikeshot Elder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spikeshot+Elder) are just some of the members of the red tribe that allowed him to damage the opponent enough for his smattering of burn spells to finish the deed.
