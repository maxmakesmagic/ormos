Wallpaper of the Week: Shadowmoor
=================================



 Posted in **Wallpaper**
 on March 28, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












This week's desktop wallpaper combines the five pieces of art that adorn *Shadowmoor* boosters, featuring Murderous Redcap by Dave Allsop, Reaper King by Jim Murray, Grief Tyrant by Izzy, Ashenmoor Gouger by Matt Cavotta, and Faerie Macabre by rk post. Remember, *Shadowmoor* previews start here on **magicthegathering.com** on Monday, March 31.
