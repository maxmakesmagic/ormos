At one time a popular Extended archetype, Aggro Loam has made a comeback in Legacy. Using the powerful combination of [Life from the Loam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam) and cycling lands from *Onslaught*, the deck is able to put the hurt on both control and aggro strategies. Just don't ask them about [Extirpate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Extirpate)!
