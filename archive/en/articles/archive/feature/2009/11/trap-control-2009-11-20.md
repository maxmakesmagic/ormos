![Grazing Gladehart](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Grazing+Gladehart)Looking for a new format to give a chance? **Magic Online** brings you *Zendikar* Block Constructed tournaments around the clock! What 60-card deck can you build utilizing only the cards found in the *Zendikar* set? Rainin6 waded into a field filled with Vampire decks and held strong on the back of [Grazing Gladehart](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grazing+Gladehart), Iona, and [Rampaging Baloths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampaging+Baloths). With access to tournaments 24 hours a day, what strategy will you use to make your mark on **Magic Online**?
