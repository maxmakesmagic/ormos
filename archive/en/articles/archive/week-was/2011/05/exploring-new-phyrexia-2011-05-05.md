Exploring New Phyrexia
======================



 Posted in [The Week That Was](/en/articles/columns/the-week-that-was-archive)
 on May 5, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Brian David-Marshall










More than any set in recent memory, the impending release of *New Phyrexia* has the deckbuilding part of my brain firing on all synapses. I wrote [last week](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/twtw/140) about [Jin-Gitaxias, Core Augur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jin-Gitaxias%2C+Core+Augur) and its potential home in my Commander deck, but I am also champing at the bit to see exactly which of these cards will shine in the new Standard format.

![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/twtw/twtw141_jin.jpg)
