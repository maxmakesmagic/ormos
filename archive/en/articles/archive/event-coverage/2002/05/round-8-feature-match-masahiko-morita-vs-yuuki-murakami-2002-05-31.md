Round 8 Feature Match: Masahiko Morita vs. Yuuki Murakami
=========================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 31, 2002 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff











Both Murakami and Morita are undefeated, having fought their way to the top of the Swiss standings yesterday with [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) decks. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/jpnat02/946.jpg)The match began with both players taking a mulligan. That didn't help Murakami much, as his second hand still only had one land. However, it was enough, allowing him to make a [Diligent Farmhand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diligent+Farmhand), followed the next turn by a [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla). On the other hand, Morita's draw was suboptimal, with only 2 lands and a bunch of 4CC spells. Fortunately, he was able to draw into land, and established control over the game with a [Battlewise Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlewise+Aven) and [Phantom Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Flock). By the time Murakami was able to draw another land, Morita had already taken over the game, Murakami conceded when Morita showed him the two [Morbid Hunger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger)s in his hand.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/jpnat02/945.jpg)The second game started with Murakami playing a Rootwalla on the first turn, which quickly began whittling away at Morita's life total. Morita tried to stabilize with a [Gravegouger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravegouger) and a [Battlewise Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlewise+Aven), but they were quickly removed with a [Swelter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swelter). The game was over in three minutes, with Morita conceding when Murakami played an [Anurid Scavenger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anurid+Scavenger).

The deciding game started poorly for Morita, who was forced to take a double mulligan. Murakami showed no mercy, playing a first turn Rootwalla yet again, followed by a [Nullmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nullmage+Advocate) and a [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). Unable to produce any kind of defense, Morita swiftly went under the assault of Murakami's green army. This left Murakami as the only undefeated player, just one win away from a guaranteed slot in the Top 8. 

**Results: Murakami wins 2-1**
