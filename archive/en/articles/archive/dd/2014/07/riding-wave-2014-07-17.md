### DAILY DECK


Riding the Wave
===============



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)

Adam Prosak

From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer.


July 17, 2014
 







While devotion is primarily a Standard mechanic, the vast card pool of Modern has many more cards that would enable devotion cards.



![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Master of Waves)

If you wanted to build a [Master of Waves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Waves) deck for Modern, naturally you would look for cards that have mostly blue mana symbols in their cost. Looking through the Modern card pool shows a theme—Merfolk! [Lord of Atlantis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lord+of+Atlantis) and [Master of the Pearl Trident](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+the+Pearl+Trident) are both efficient "lords" that boost all other Merfolk. [Master of Waves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Waves) is even a Merfolk!



Once you have this core, you want to fill your deck with the right balance of additional Merfolk and efficient interaction. Dismember, [Relic of Progenitus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Relic+of+Progenitus), and [Spreading Seas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spreading+Seas) are capable of keeping an opponent off balance enough for your Merfolk to swim through the red zone. [Spreading Seas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spreading+Seas) serves double duty, disabling opposing mana bases and providing an Island to make your Islandwalking Merfolk unblockable.



You have many options to build a Merfolk deck, even in Mono-Blue.
