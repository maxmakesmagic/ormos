![Nissa Revane](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Nissa+Revane)Chiba, Japan, which will host the 2010 **Magic** World Championships, prepared by participating in the [2009 *Zendikar* Game Day](/en/events/coverage/zendikar-game-day). Takagi Yui took an innovative take on green-blue to a solid finish. [Nissa's Chosen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa%27s+Chosen) (powered by [Nissa Revane](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa+Revane) herself) helped hold the ground, allowing Yui plenty of time to set up powerful endgame spells like [Sphinx of Lost Truths](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx+of+Lost+Truths) and [Rite of Replication](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rite+of+Replication).
