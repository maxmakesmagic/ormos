Pro Tour Gauntlet: Kaladesh
===========================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on October 18, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Even if you don't have the cards in your collection, you can test drive some of the best and most interesting decks from the latest Pro Tour in Pro Tour Gauntlet events on *Magic Online*! Pro Tour Gauntlet events for Pro Tour: *Kaladesh* run from the October 19 downtime until October 26. Event details and available decklists can be found below!


In the past, we have offered Pro Tour Gauntlet as a way to allow players to give constructed formats a try before obtaining a deck. Having run several Pro Tour Gauntlets now and had an opportunity to examine player behavior, this doesnâ€™t seem to be happening to the extent that we hoped. Rather, the vast majority are treating it as an alternative play experience, similar cube drafts. With that in mind, we are happy to offer it in this way, but the entry and prize details for it are changing to be similar to those events. Have fun playing the top decks from Pro Tour *Kaladesh*!


* [Pro Tour Gauntlet Event Details](#PTGauntletEvents)
* [Pro Tour Gauntlet Decklists](#Decks)

**Pro Tour Gauntlet**


**Start Time**: Wednesday, October 19, after downtime  
**End Time**:Wednesday, October 26, at 12:00 a.m. PT  
**Location**:Play Lobby -> Constructed Tournaments -> Queues  
**Entry Option**:


* 10 Event Tickets
* 100 Play Points

**Product**: One randomly-selected phantom deck from the [Pro Tour Gauntlet decklists](#Decks). Phantom product will not be added to a player's collection at the conclusion of the event.  
**Format**: Standard  
**Duration**: 3 minutes of deck review, then three rounds each up to 50 minutes  
**Prizes**:




| Place | Prizes |
| --- | --- |
| 3 Wins | 150 Play Points |
| 2 Wins | 100 Play Points |
| 1 Win | 40 Play Points |
| 0 Wins | 10 Play Points |

**NOTE**: Wizards of the Coast employees and other persons affiliated with it are eligible to play in this event. For more information, please see [this article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/wizards-announces-wizards-play-policy-2015-01-30) and [this FAQ](https://wizards.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/2312) about this policy.
