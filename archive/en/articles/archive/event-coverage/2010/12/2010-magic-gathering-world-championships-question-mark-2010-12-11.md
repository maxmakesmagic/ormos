2010 Magic: The Gathering World Championships Question Mark
===========================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 11, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Mark Rosewater has been running his Question Mark trivia game show at **Magic** World Championships for at least 75 years (EDITOR: please fact-check this). This year, it was a cross between a trivia game show and a panel discussion, as each person who answered a question correctly got to ask Mark a question in return. And we have Mark's questions right here! You won't get to ask Mark your question, but you'll get to feel clever for knowing so much about **Magic**.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/events/worlds2010/questionmark_1.jpg)  
**1) How many cards in *Scars of Mirrodin* have proliferate?** See the answer

Six. [Contagion Clasp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contagion+Clasp), [Contagion Engine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contagion+Engine), [Inexorable Tide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inexorable+Tide), [Steady Progress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steady+Progress), [Throne of Geth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Throne+of+Geth), and [Thrummingbird](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thrummingbird)

**2) What was the first set to mention the Phyrexians?** See the answer

*Antiquities*

**3) Soul Foundry was originally designed in *Urza's Saga* but got changed last minute. The card ended up being a tournament worthy card that Jon Finkel used to win the 2000 World Championships. What card was it?** See the answer

[Phyrexian Processor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Processor)

**4) Name the four new named mechanics introduced in *Mirrodin*.** See the answer

Affinity, entwine, equipment, and imprint

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/events/worlds2010/questionmark_2.jpg)  
**5) What was the first set to have poison counters?** See the answer

*Legends*

**6) Mirrodin is one of a few different planes to have been visited in different blocks. Can you name the two others?** See the answer

Dominaria and Rath

**7) Affinity was considered to return in *Scars of Mirrodin*. How many different things have cards had affinity for?** See the answer

Six. Artifacts, Plains, Islands, Swamps, Mountains, and Forests

**8) How many myr appear in common in *Scars of Mirrodin*?** See the answer

Seven. [Copper Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr), [Gold Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gold+Myr), [Ichorclaw Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ichorclaw+Myr), [Iron Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iron+Myr), [Leaden Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leaden+Myr), [Perilous Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Perilous+Myr), and [Silver Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silver+Myr)

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/events/worlds2010/questionmark_3.jpg)  
**9) *Scars of Mirrodin* uses charge counters. What was the first set to use charge counters?** See the answer

*Fourth Edition*, on the [Mana Batteries](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?text=+%5Bmana%5D+%5Bbattery%5D).

**10) Fifth Dawn was the dawn of which color sun on Mirrodin?** See the answer

Green

**11) What percentage of the cards in [Scars of Mirrodin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scars+of+Mirrodin) have a Phyrexian watermark?** See the answer

20%

**12) In the previous incarnation of the Phyrexians, who was their leader?** See the answer

Yawgmoth

**13) Who brought the Phyrexian oil to Mirrodin?** See the answer

Karn

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/events/worlds2010/questionmark_4.jpg)  
**14) Which planeswalker has appeared both as a planeswalker and as a legendary creature?** See the answer

Venser and Nicol Bolas. ([Venser, Shaper Savant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Venser%2C+Shaper+Savant) and [Venser, the Sojourner](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Venser%2C+the+Sojourner); [Nicol Bolas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas) and [Nicol Bolas, Planeswalker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+Planeswalker))

**15) What plane does Elspeth call home?** See the answer

Bant (or Alara). She adopted that as her home after her original home was overrun by Phyrexia.

**16) Name four proper nouns to appear in name of more than one set.** See the answer

Alara, Kamigawa, Mirrodin, and Urza's

**17) Name the only mythic rare skeleton.** See the answer

[Skithiryx, the Blight Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skithiryx%2C+the+Blight+Dragon)

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/events/worlds2010/questionmark_5.jpg)  
**18) Name a creature in *Scars of Mirrodin* that has a power higher than its converted mana cost.** See the answer

[Glint Hawk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk), [Memnite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite), and [Scoria Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scoria+Elemental).

**19) Not counting evergreen keywords, keywords that appear in every set, how many keywords appear on cards with the Mirran watermark?** See the answer

Zero. Imprint and metalcraft are both ability words.

**20) In all of **Magic**, there are exactly the same number of artifacts with imprint as there are with indestructible. How many are there?** See the answer

Eighteen.

**21) What is the creature type of all five "smiths" in *Scars of Mirrodin*?** See the answer

Human Artificer

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/events/worlds2010/questionmark_6.jpg)  
**22) There are two repeats in *Scars of Mirrodin* that are not from *Mirrodin* block. What are they?** See the answer

[Bloodshot Trainee](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodshot+Trainee) and [Disperse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disperse)

**23) There is a card subtype that appeared for the first time in *Mirrodin* block and appears for the second time in *Scars of Mirrodin* block. What is it?** See the answer

Locus

**24) What *Scars of Mirrodin* card was called Robot Factory in design?** See the answer

[Golem Foundry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golem+Foundry)

**25) How many legendary cards are in Scars of Mirrodin?** See the answer

Six. [Ezuri, Renegade Leader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ezuri%2C+Renegade+Leader); [Geth, Lord of the Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geth%2C+Lord+of+the+Vault); [Kemba, Kha Regent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kemba%2C+Kha+Regent); [Mindslaver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslaver); [Mox Opal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Opal); [Skithiryx, the Blight Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skithiryx%2C+the+Blight+Dragon)

**26) How many of *Scars of Mirrodin*'s fifteen mythic rare cards are artifacts?** See the answer

Seven. [Lux Cannon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lux+Cannon), [Mindslaver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslaver), [Molten-Tail Masticore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten-Tail+Masticore), [Mox Opal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Opal), [Platinum Emperion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Platinum+Emperion), [Sword of Body and Mind](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+Body+and+Mind), and [Wurmcoil Engine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wurmcoil+Engine).

**27) Of Elspeth, Koth & Venser, how many have a planeswalker card that creates an emblem?** See the answer

Three. They all do. [Elspeth, Knight-Errant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant) was given errata to grant an emblem.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/events/worlds2010/questionmark_7.jpg)  
**28) Arrest is reprinted in *Scars of Mirrodin*. What was the first set it appeared in?** See the answer

*Mercadian Masques*

**29) There is a card in *Scars of Mirrodin* that is the fifth card in a cycle. The first four cards of the cycle appeared in the original *Mirrodin*. What is the card?** See the answer

[Tower of Calamities](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tower+of+Calamities)

**30) How many times does the number 6 appear on Argentum Armor?** See the answer

Four. In the mana cost, twice in the +6/+6, and in the equip cost.

(Some answers were corrected after publication.)
