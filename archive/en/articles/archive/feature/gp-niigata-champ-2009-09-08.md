Tsuyoshi Ikeda is the 2009 [Grand Prix–Niigata](/en/events/coverage/ikeda-sacks-niigata) champion! His aggressive blue-white draft strategy served him very well in the Top 8. He dispatched the only non-Japanese player, Gaudenis Vidugiris, in the Quarterfinals, then held on against two fellow Japanese countrymen to take the title. His powerful fliers backed by a smattering of removal allowed him to take the trophy!
