Top 4 Teams of Grand Prix Indianapolis 2018
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 21, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











These teams have shown us their prowess on the battlefield all weekend. Let's learn some more about the final four.




---


### Xinyu Fei / Chih-Cheng (Peter) Yeh / Zirui Zhou


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpind18/gpINDY2018_T8_3_Landscape.jpg)


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpind18/gpINDY2018_T8_3A_Fei.jpg)


**Seat:** A  
  
**Name:** Xinyu Fei  
  
**Age:** 22  
  
**Hometown:** China  
  
**Occupation:** Cleveland, Ohio  
  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

65th at Grand Prix Indianapolis 2017  

Won PTQ Beijing  
  
**What is your favorite tribe in *Rivals of Ixalan*, and why?**  

Dinosaurs, I hate 2 drops and like 5-8 drops.  
  
**Which *Rivals of Ixalan* card impressed you the most?**  

Green 12/12 Trample Elder Dinosaur [[Ghalta, Primal Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghalta%2C+Primal+Hunger)]  
  
**How did your teammates help you this weekend?**  

They are always positive, and they are good players and good guys.  
  
**What was your most memorable moment of this weekend?**  

On the draw, I kept a one-land hand with [Commune with Dinosaurs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Commune+with+Dinosaurs). I completely missed on Commune and was stuck on one land for a turn. I still won that game. That was our win-and-in.

### Eric Severson / Benjamin Weitz / Neal Oliver


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpind18/gpINDY2018_T8_2_Landscape.jpg)


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpind18/gpINDY2018_T8_2A_Severson.jpg)


**Seat:** A  
  
**Name:** Eric Severson  
  
**Age:** 25  
  
**Hometown:** Davis, California  
  
**Occupation:** Applied Mathematician  
  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

Gold Pro with Team Massdrop East  

This is my 6th Grand Prix Top “8”  
  
**What is your favorite tribe in *Rivals of Ixalan*, and why?**  

Dinosaurs. A little too slow for Limited, but *Magic* has long been missing this as a creature type.  
  
**Which *Rivals of Ixalan* card impressed you the most?**  
[Forerunner of the Heralds](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forerunner+of+the+Heralds).


In any good Merfolk deck, your turns after playing him will be insane.  
  
**How did your teammates help you this weekend?**  

Carried me! I did not win a match that mattered for the first 10 rounds!  
  
**What was your most memorable moment of this weekend?**  

Two very close losses where I could have played differently to win. Good thing Ben & Neal are great at *Magic*!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpind18/gpINDY2018_T8_2B_Weitz.jpg)


**Seat:** B  
  
**Name:** Benjamin Weitz  
  
**Age:** 27  
  
**Hometown:** St. Paul, Minnesota

**Which *Rivals of Ixalan* card impressed you the most?**

**How did your teammates help you this weekend?**  

Pointing out the wrong lines.  
  
**What was your most memorable moment of this weekend?**  

Playing a really close game in Round 8 where we screwed up, but barely managed to win anyway.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpind18/gpINDY2018_T8_2C_Oliver.jpg)


**Seat:** C  
  
**Name:** Neal Oliver  
  
**Age:** 29  
  
**Hometown:** Sacramento, California  
  
**Occupation:** Special Education Technician  
  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

GP Vegas 2013 — 1st  

GP Oakland 2013 — 2nd  

GP Ottawa 2014 — 2nd


Member of U.S. World *Magic* Cup team — twice


Podcast co-host of [Allied Strategies](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Allied+Strategies)  
  
**What is your favorite tribe in *Rivals of Ixalan*, and why?**  

Chupacabras. I had three in my Day 2 deck and that tribe is REAL good.  
  
**Which *Rivals of Ixalan* card impressed you the most?**  
[Arch of Orazca](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arch+of+Orazca). The card lets you win a grindy game and Ascend is sweet.  
  
**How did your teammates help you this weekend?**  

Ben won almost all his matches. Eric, not so much. But he won a ew key times. Also, we’re all great friends.  
  
**What was your most memorable moment of this weekend?**  

Top 4ing with one of my podcast members but not the other. Now I can poke fun at Tristan Killeen on the podcast, one of my favorite pastimes.

**What was your most memorable moment of this weekend?**  

Coming back from an 0-4 hole on Day 1 vs. the Madison Crew [(18) Matt Severa, (15) Sam Black, Justin Cohen]. I honestly wasn’t aware of the score at the time, but watched the coverage later and was in awe.
