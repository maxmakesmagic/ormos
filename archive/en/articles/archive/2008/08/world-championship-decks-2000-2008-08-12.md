### Collector's Highlights


Each of the 4 decks contains a 60 card constructed deck, a 15 card sideboard, 12 blank "proxy" cards, and 3 information cards about the deck.




### Game Features


The 2000 World Championships featured constructed decks including cards from *Classic Sixth Edition*, The *Urza's Saga Block* and the *Mercadian Masques* Block.

The world's best professional **Magic: The Gathering** players competed for a share of more than $250,000 in cash prizes at the 2000 World Championships, held August 2-6 in Brussels, Belgium.


The fierce competition featured both individual matches and an international team tournaments that showcased teams from more than 40 countries.


The World Championship Decks for 2000 showcase four of the top decks from the event, allowing you to play with decks designed by four of the top players in **Magic**.


Whether you play with Jon Finkel's "artifact-heavy" deck or Tom van de Logt's "creature-less" deck, you'll find a wealth of strategy from the minds of some of the best players in the game.
