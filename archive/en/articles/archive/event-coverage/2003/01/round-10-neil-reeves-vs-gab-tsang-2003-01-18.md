Round 10: Neil Reeves vs. Gab Tsang
===================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 18, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a797.jpg)Gab Tsang is a dying breed within Magic. He made his first Top 8 in 1997, and is still going strong. Neil Reeves is one of the new young players on the scene, and is still looking for his first Top 8. It's going to be a hard accomplishment for either player at this point, as Reeves came to the table with a 5-3-1 record, and Tsang was 6-3. 

Reeves- "You never put me in Feature Matches!"  
 Rosewater- "We featured Courtney's Boys a lot."  
 Reeves- "Oh sure, Bob Maher, Gary Wise and that other guy."

### Game 1

Tsang used [Wirewood Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Elf) to accelerate into a third turn [Snarling Undorak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snarling+Undorak), and Reeves brought out [Battlefield Medic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlefield+Medic) followed by [Gustcloak Harrier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gustcloak+Harrier). Tsang missed a land drop, but played another Undorak. Reeves attacked for two with a Harrier, and summoned another. "My guys are good, your guys are better." Indeed, the Undoraks were swinging for six a turn while Reeves' Harriers were dealing four.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a798.jpg)Tsang played another land, and brought out a morph creature. Reeves played an Ironfist Crusader, and was one mana short of knocking it down due to the Medic. However, a pump and a [Vitality Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vitality+Charm) got the job done. [Sandskin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sandskin) slowed down one of the Undoraks, and Tsang's morph flipped to reveal [Charging Slateback](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Charging+Slateback), putting more pressure on the ground. Reeves attacked with just one Harrier, and brought out [Dive Bomber](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dive+Bomber). Reeves chump blocked to stay alive for a turn, and conceded, noting that he drew too many lands.

Reeves- 0 Tsang- 1 

### Game 2

Reeves had a second turn Medic, and Tsang brought out [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith). A Harrier joined Reeves' side, as he joked "This might be quick." Tsang played a morph, and used [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) on end step and then main phase to kill the Medic. The morph traded with Reeves' [Dive Bomber](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dive+Bomber), while Tsang accelerated into [Treespring Lorian](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treespring+Lorian) with [Wirewood Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Elf). Reeves played [Tribal Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tribal+Golem), which was 4/4 first strike thanks to the Harrier. Tsang sent the Lorian right in, and Reeves took five, not willing to take the risk.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a799.jpg)This let him attack back with his whole team, but Tsang used [Pinpoint Avalanche](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pinpoint+Avalanche) to take out the Golem. The Elf blocked Reeves' morph and [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) tried to finish the job, but it flipped over as Ironfist Crusader. The 5/4 and [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) attacked for six, and Tsang played a morph creature.

The race was on, and Reeves attacked for four, and then played a morph and his own [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith). Tsang attacked with his morph, which was blocked by Reeves' own. Tsang's morph turned out to be [Towering Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Towering+Baloth). The Harrier brought Tsang down to three, and Reeves continued to chump block. [Krosan Tusker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Tusker) gave Tsang more on the ground, and Reeves was one life point short of winning before he was overwhelmed.

**Final Result:** Reeves- 0 Tsang- 2
