Deck of the Day
===============


1. Modern Dredge

Do we finally have enough (unbanned) goodies in Modern to make Dredge a thing again? *Magic Online* player Aiteth thinks so, having gone 5-0 in a Competitive Modern League with a Jund-flavored version of the deck (that happens to have a few incidentally blue cards in it).


Thanks to two new additions from *Shadows over Innistrad*—[Insolent Neonate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Insolent+Neonate) and [Prized Amalgam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam)—Aiteth might just be on to something. Neonate can help get the dredge chain started by first discarding a dredger and then immediately drawing it back. [Faithless Looting](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faithless+Looting), [Burning Inquiry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Inquiry), and [Tormenting Voice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tormenting+Voice) give the deck more chances to dredge things back, and [Narcomeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=370359), [Bloodghast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodghast), and [Prized Amalgam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam) are all ready to jump out of the graveyard for free.


The real twist is [Conflagrate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conflagrate), a new way to discard all of your dredgers at once that also happens to lessen your opponent's life total, getting you that much closer to hasty [Bloodghast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodghast)s and, you know, winning the game.
