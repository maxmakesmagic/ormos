Innistrad: Double Feature
=========================



 Posted in **Card Image Gallery**
 on November 15, 2021 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Available at [your local WPN game store](https://locator.wizards.com/), [*Innistrad: Double Feature*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/innistrad-double-feature-product-overview-2021-11-15) combines cards across both [*Innistrad: Midnight Hunt*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/midnight-hunt) and [*Innistrad: Crimson Vow*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/innistrad-crimson-vow) into a beautiful draft-ready booster to play and collect! To read more about *Innistrad: Double Feature*, check out [the product overview article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/innistrad-double-feature-product-overview-2021-11-15).
