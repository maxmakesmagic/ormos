*Lorwyn* Preview Video
======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 29, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Lorwyn is an idyllic world where nature thrives in its most pristine state. The sun never drops below the horizon, and midsummer never ends. The creatures of fable who dwell there know nothing of gloom or malice—


—but they are consumed with *rivalry*.
