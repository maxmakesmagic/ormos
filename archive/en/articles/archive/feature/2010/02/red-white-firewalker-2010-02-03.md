*From* Worldwake *lead designer Kenneth Nagle:*

This is a [Kor Firewalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Firewalker) deck that I used to play in the FFL. The goal is to play a creature with protection from red on turn two, then an [Earthquake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake) or [Chain Reaction](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+Reaction) on turn three or four. The [Pilgrim's Eye](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pilgrim%27s+Eye) helps fix colors and curve into turn-four plays, while the big Angels, Dragons, and Contructs bat cleanup. An alternative to any of these cards, if you're looking for one, would be [Wall of Reverence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Reverence).
