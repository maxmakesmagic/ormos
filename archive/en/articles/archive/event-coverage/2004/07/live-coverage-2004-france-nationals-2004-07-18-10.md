Live Coverage of 2004 France Nationals
======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 18, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_92.png)
By Mark Wraith











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/frnat04/fm11_1.jpg)Leading the field at 9-1 after defeating Alexandre Peset last round, Damien needs only a draw to make sure of top eight. Steven was two points back at 8-1-1, needing a win in either of his last two rounds. Damien is playing Big Red, and Steven is playing Affinity ### Game 1


Steven took a mulligan but was happy with his six card hand. Damien played a [Guardian Idol](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Guardian+Idol) on his second turn and [Molten Rain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Rain)ed a [Seat of the Synod](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seat+of+the+Synod) on his third. Steven's draw was pretty good, he made a Worker on turn 1, a Ravager on turn 2, and a Plating on turn 3 although he couldn't equip due to the land destruction.


Reabourg dropped an Arc Slogger but it wasn't enough. Steven untapped, played a Disciple and an [Ornithopter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ornithopter), equipped the plating to his [Arcbound Worker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcbound+Worker) and attacked with it and the Ravager. Damien was on 16 and Steven had exactly 16 points of damage in play if he sacrificed everything to his Ravager. He spent a good five minutes counting and recounting the damage. He had a 2/2 Ravager, 6 other artifacts of which one was a Worker. This would make the Ravager 9/9. Seven artifacts in total sacced to the Disciple would make 16. Eventually Steven came to the first conclusion, and Damien scooped in reply to the first sacrifice.


Steven Gouin 1 - Damien Reaubourg 0


### Game 2


This time Steven had a first-turn Disciple and second turn Ravager and [Ornithopter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ornithopter). Damien again had a [Guardian Idol](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Guardian+Idol). He missed a third land drop which meant that he could only cast a [Molten Rain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Rain), rather than an [Electrostatic Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrostatic+Bolt) as well. Steven didnt have another land either so he just attacked with the Disciple and the Ravager.


Damien drew a [Chrome Mox](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox), and said go. Gouin drew another non-land and attacked with his Disciple and Ravager into an active [Guardian Idol](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Guardian+Idol) ! The Idol blocked and killed the Ravager, Reaubourg Bolted the Disciple, untapped and [Demolish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demolish)ed Steven's remaining land.


Gouin missed another land drop and Damien played an Arc Slogger. On to game 3.


Steven Gouin 1 - Damien Reaubourg 1


### Game 3


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/frnat04/fm11_2.jpg)Steven had to mulligan an opening hand with five lands,a Worker and a [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak). His second hand was just as bad and he sent that back too. He eventually kept a hand with one [Great Furnace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Great+Furnace) as it's only mana source. He drew a second on his third turn. Damien [Magma Jet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magma+Jet)ted it and Bolted a second.


Reaubourg dropped a Simulacrum, and then [Pyroclasm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm)ed when Steven played a third Ravager and an [Ornithopter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ornithopter). Steven used the Ravager to pump up his [Ornithopter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ornithopter), but that was [Detonate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detonate)d. Reaubourg played a [Rorix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rorix) and attacked. Steven took a hit and then [Shrapnel Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shrapnel+Blast)ed it away. This left him with two lands and a Disciple as his only permanents. Damien played an Arc Slogger, untapped and used it, a [Magma Jet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magma+Jet), and a [Pulse of the Forge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulse+of+the+Forge) to burn Steven out.


Damien Reaubourg 2 - Steven Gouin 1
