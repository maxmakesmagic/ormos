Hall of Famer Zvi Mowshowitz would finally manage a Pro Tour win in Tokyo, but by the time he did he was as famous for his writing as he was for his decks. One of the articles, or rather series of them, was entitled "My Fires" and walked players through the path he had taken to come up with his Pro Tour–Chicago Top 8 deck. For more on that event, read the [official event coverage archive](http://www.wizards.com/sideboard/article.asp?x=PTCHI00%5C840t8decks).
