QUICK QUESTIONS #5
==================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on October 5, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











 What are the best cards in both your Booster Drafts today?







![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Gao-Tan.jpg)

***Gao Tan:** Pod 1: [Sagu Mauler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sagu+Mauler)*




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Han-Bing2.jpg)

***Han Bing:** Pod 1: [Crater's Claws](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crater%27s+Claws)*










![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Charles-Lai.jpg)

***Charles Lai:** Pod 1: [Winterflame](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Winterflame). It won me a crucial game.*




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Yuuki-Ichikawa.jpg)

***Yuuki Ichikawa:** Pod 1: [Empty the Pits](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Empty+the+Pits). That card is crazy.*










![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Zhang-Mengqiu.jpg)

***Zhang Mengqiu:** Pod 1: [Butcher of the Horde](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher+of+the+Horde)   
 Pod 2: My deck is terrible, I think the best card is [Warden of the Eye](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warden+of+the+Eye).* 




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Albertus-Law.jpg)

***Albertus Law:** Pod 1: [Ashcloud Phoenix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashcloud+Phoenix)   
 Pod 2: [Mantis Rider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mantis+Rider)*








![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Yuuya-Watanabe.jpg)

***Yuuya Watanabe:** Pod 1: [Incremental Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Incremental+Growth). I had two copies!   
 Pod 2: [Necropolis Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necropolis+Fiend)*
