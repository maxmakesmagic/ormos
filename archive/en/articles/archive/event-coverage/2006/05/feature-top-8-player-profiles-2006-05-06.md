4-color Green, with plenty of bounce lands.

**Joseph Crosby**


**Age:** 23


**Hometown:** Atlanta, GA


**Occupation:** Semi-professional poker player, CCG business owner


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** D.I. over 2


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour Prague?** Won a PTQ in Atlanta


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** Zip.


**Other Previous Magic Accomplishments:** 10th at PT Chicago (individual Rochester), 2 top 16s at Team PTs, several GP top 16s


**What was your record by draft pod:**


1: 2-1  

 2: 3-0  

 3: 3-0  

 4: 1-1-1  

 5: 2-0-1


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?** 


One monitor for poker, one for **Magic Online**. I also prepared at Player One Games in Atlanta. Don Smith was my main playtest partner; I wouldn't be here without him.


**What was your draft strategy? Did you stick with it all weekend, or did you have to change it?**


Trust that I have what it takes to win, and play it by feel. Specifically, an open-ended draft strategy that emphasizes consistency.


**What is your favorite draft colour combination, and what are the key cards to make it work?**


U/G/R, with Peel From Reality from Ravnica, setting up to get good Izzet in pack two, followed by graft and early drops from Dissension.
