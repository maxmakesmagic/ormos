Isolation Cell
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 28, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Let's take a look at [Isolation Cell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Isolation+Cell) from *New Phyrexia*.

![Isolation Cell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Isolation+Cell)  
Now, would you like to see something neat? Here's the art, somewhat larger.

![Isolation Cell](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/729_isolationcell.jpg)  
Those green pods are the titular isolation cells. And, in fact, if you look carefully at the pod in the upper right, you can see a Mirran imprisoned in one. Neat!
