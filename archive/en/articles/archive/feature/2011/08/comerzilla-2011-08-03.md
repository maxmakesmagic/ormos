Reanimator has long been a popular archetype in formats around the **Magic** multiverse. One of the most popular early forms of the deck was played by the famous Alan Comer, and aptly nicknamed "Comerzilla." The deck's game plan was to plop a fatty like [Verdant Force](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verdant+Force) into the graveyard via [Hidden Horror](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hidden+Horror) or [Firestorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firestorm), then hop it back into play on the cheap with a card like [Animate Dead](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Animate+Dead) or [Necromancy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necromancy). Comer earned a reputation as a deckbuilder over the course of his professional career, and was even enshrined into the Pro Tour Hall of Fame. It's now voting season for the 2011 class; find out who is up for election on the [Hall of Fame page](/en/node/574031).
