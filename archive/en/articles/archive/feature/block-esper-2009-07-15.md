The recent [Pro Tour–Honolulu](/en/events/coverage/mitamura-finds-gold-paradise) saw the *Shards of Alara* Block Constructed format stealing the limelight. So what has been the impact on the **Magic Online** metagame, where the format is still popular? BadBeatBret provides the answer, heading to the Top 8 a Premier Event in the digital realm using a version of the Top 8 deck Paul Rietzl and Brian Kibler rode to great success in Hawaii.
