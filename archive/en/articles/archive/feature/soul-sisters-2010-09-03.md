We ran an early story on the Soul Sisters life-gain deck for Standard [last week](/en/articles/archive/get-ahead-life-2010-08-27). The brainchild of Kyle Sanchez, it stormed U.S. Nationals in the hands of Conley Woods, who finished in the Top 16 of that event. But its first major Top 8 was in the hands of Jake Lehmkuhl at the StarCityGames.com Standard Open in Denver. He managed to use his take on the deck to make the Top 8 of that event. The combo of [Soul Warden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Warden) and [Soul's Attendant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul%27s+Attendant) means Jake can make his [Serra Ascendant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serra+Ascendant) and [Ajani's Pridemate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani%27s+Pridemate) very large, very fast. That leads to opponents being very dead, very fast!
