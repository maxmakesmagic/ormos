Top 8 Players of Grand Prix Turin 2018
======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 29, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After fifteen rounds, with 2 to the power of 10 (1,024) others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gptur18/t8_kohl.jpg)


**Name:** Reinhold Kohl


**Age:** 38


**Occupation:** Credit controller


**Hometown:** Originally Cologne, Germany, now living in the Netherlands


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Two GP Top 8s, ninth in the Rookie of the Year Race 2009.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Banefire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banefire), red-green, 7-2.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Abnormal Endurance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abnormal+Endurance), black-red, 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


See above.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gptur18/t8_lemke.jpg)


**Name:** Gero Lemke


**Age:** 20


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Berlin, Germany


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Like two Top 8s at local PPTQs.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Mentor of the Meek](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mentor+of+the+Meek)/[Lena, Selfless Champion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lena%2C+Selfless+Champion), pretty good combo apparently. I played white-blue with a very underwhelming pool otherwise. 7-2 ... after starting 2-2 with two byes.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Heroic Reinforcements](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heroic+Reinforcements). Boros Aggro seems to be quite good in this format: 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Demon of [autocard]Catastrophe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demon+of+%5Bautocard%5DCatastrophe)s[/autocard]. Although [Reassembling Skeleton](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reassembling+Skeleton) worked overtime too. Mono-black. 3-0.


My first draft in this format was a turbo draft on Friday ... so yeah.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gptur18/t8_brancewicz.jpg)


**Name:** Michał Brancewicz


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** No, just visiting. (Financial analyst)


**Hometown:** Łódź, Poland


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


GP Barcelona 2017 Top 32 and a couple of PTQ Top 8s under the old qualification system.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Sigiled Sword of Valeron](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sigiled+Sword+of+Valeron), black-green splashing white and blue.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Green-white, 3-0, [Rustwing Falcon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rustwing+Falcon).


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-green, [Bristling Boar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bristling+Boar), a win followed by two intentional draws.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gptur18/t8_gauthier.jpg)


**Name:** Guillaume Gauthier


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Urban planner


**Hometown:** Paris, France


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


I won a Kamigawa draft last year. But now I feel like Kylian Mbappé in the final of the World Cup.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Talons of Wildwood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Talons+of+Wildwood). I had two in my Green-White Aura Sealed Deck. Pretty good at breaking stalls.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Red-white, and I had three copies of [Volley Veteran](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volley+Veteran).


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Blue-red splashing black for ... [Nicol Bolas, the Ravager](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+the+Ravager). Terrific Dragon, I hope he's going to win in the next set.

**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Herald of Faith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Herald+of+Faith), white-black, 2-0-1.
