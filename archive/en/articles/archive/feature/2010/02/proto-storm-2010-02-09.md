In Toronto, Charles Trottier managed to find himself in the Top 8 of his local Pro Tour Qualifier for San Juan with this take on a proto-Storm combo deck. Instead of [Pyromancer's Swath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer%27s+Swath), Trottier opted for [Pyromancer Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer+Ascension), allowing him to create numerous copies of his spells for free. Is this an Extended one-hit wonder, or will Charles's deck begin tearing up the PTQ scene?
