Notice anything out of the ordinary about this deck? Astute viewers will pick up on the fact the deck has but a single land: a lonely [Taiga](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Taiga). That's because it's based on a similar deck from the world of Vintage that centers around landless mana like [Simian Spirit Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Simian%2BSpirit%2BGuide) and [Elvish Spirit Guides](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Spirit+Guides), [Tinder Wall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinder+Wall), [Land Grant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Land+Grant), and others to drop a fast [Empty the Warrens](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Empty+the+Warrens) or [Goblin Charbelcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Charbelcher). With just a single land in the deck, and that land being a [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain), the 'Belcher is practically guaranteed to be lethal every time it activates. Rukcus tuned this version to take a top spot in a recent Premier Event on **Magic Online**.
