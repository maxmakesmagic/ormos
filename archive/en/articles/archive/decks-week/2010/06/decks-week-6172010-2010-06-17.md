Decks of the Week: 6/17/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on June 17, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The early-summer Premier Event schedule came to a close last weekend at [Grand Prix–Manila](/en/events/coverage/nakada-emerges-unscathed-manila), which was won by Naoki Nakada of Japan with an [Eldrazi Conscription](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Conscription+)  Bant deck that defeated Taufik Indrakesuma's [Polymorph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polymorph+)  deck despite Taufik landing [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Aeons+Torn) in play in all three games. Check out the Top 32 Standard lists below, along with lists from nine Pro Tour–Amsterdam qualifiers, six National Qualifiers from Japan, and the usual horde of **Magic Online** decks. There are plenty of Standard options to choose from as you continue to prepare for National Qualifiers and National Championships over the next three months.
