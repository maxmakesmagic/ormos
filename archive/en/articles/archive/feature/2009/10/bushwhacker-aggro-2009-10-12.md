Looking for a fun, new aggressive strategy in the new Standard? Kelly Reid of QuietSpeculation.com recently posted this list, starring [Goblin Bushwhacker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Bushwhacker), to his blog. It features the most aggressive threats red-white has to offer (check out [Cerodon Yearling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cerodon+Yearling) finally seeing some play!) and attempts to burn an opponent to dark and crispy in a New York minute.
