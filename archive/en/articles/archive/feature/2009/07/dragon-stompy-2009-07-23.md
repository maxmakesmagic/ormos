Dragon Stompy is a tried and true strategy from the world of Legacy, but Suczi is taking it to the next level using this build to dominate a recent Classic Premier Event. The premise of the deck is simple: use accelerated mana in the form of [Ancient Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb), [City of Traitors](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Traitors), and temporary mana effects like [Seething Song](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seething+Song) to speed out a super-fatty or super-disruptive spell as early as the first turn. Many a mage's blood has turned cold at the sight of a first turn [Magus of the Moon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magus+of+the+Moon) shutting down their deck's manabase.
