Next, the things that make the decks most different: the "one-ofs" that are used for specific purposes, like killing artifacts or opponents. This is where we differentiated among the various Elves! decks; the decks with [Predator Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Predator+Dragon) are "Dragon Elves," the decks with [Mirror Entity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Entity) are "Elvish Entities," and the decks with [Grapeshot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grapeshot) are "Grapeshot Elves."

The land appears to be largely up to individual taste, with Saito keeping well out of [Blood Moon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon) range with 16 of his 17 land being basic [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest)s. Everyone is playing 17 land, except for Scott-Vargas, who is living life to its fullest and only playing 16 land.

The sideboards are a mixture of overlapping cards (everyone has extra [Viridian Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Shaman)s) and individual choices (Saito is the only one with [Blasting Station](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blasting+Station) or [Gleeful Sabotage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gleeful+Sabotage), Juza is the only one with [Naturalize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize) or [Voidstone Gargoyle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voidstone+Gargoyle), Doise has [Seal of Primordium](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Primordium) and an extra [Chord of Calling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling), Thaler has [Choke](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Choke) and [Sundering Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sundering+Titan), and Scott-Vargas has [Mycoloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mycoloth), [Nullmage Shepherd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nullmage+Shepherd), and a spare [Pendelhaven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pendelhaven)).
