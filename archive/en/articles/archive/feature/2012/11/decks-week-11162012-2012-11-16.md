Decks of the Week: 11/16/2012
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 16, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Last weekend, Jacob Wilson won [Grand Prix Chicago](/en/events/coverage/jacob-wilson-first-second-city) in a Modern Jund mirror match. Tracking what happens in recent Grand Prix is a lot of fun, but if you want to prepare for Pro Tour *Gatecrash* you will need to practice both Standard and *Return to Ravnica* Booster Draft. Fortunately for you, the current PTQ season is all about *Return to Ravnica* Limited, so you have plenty of opportunities to practice! Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#gtc). In the meantime, see what other people are putting together in this season's Limited environments with the decklists here. Enjoy!
