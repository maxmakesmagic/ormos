If you haven't heard by now, the [Game in the Gulf Cruise](/en/events/coverage/magic-cruise-qualifying-season-top-8-decklists) from Legion Events is a fantastic opportunity to enjoy your favorite game (**Magic**) while also enjoying a fantastic cruise! Plus, there are [tournaments all over the country offering a free cruise to winners](http://www.legionevents.com/cruise/CruiseQualifier.htm)! One such event was recently held in Roanoke, Virginia. Joshua Owens finished in a respectable Top 16 with his blue-white-green Bant deck. It featured such *Shards of Alara* hits as [Elspeth, Knight-Errant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant) and [Stoic Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoic+Angel). No doubt we'll be hearing more from Bant at the upcoming State and Provincial championships. For more information on those tournaments, visit [www.mtgchamps.com](http://mtgchamps.com/).
