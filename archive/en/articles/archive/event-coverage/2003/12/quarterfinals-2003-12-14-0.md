Quarterfinals:
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 14, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall




 Brian David-Marshall is a New York–based game designer who has been involved with Magic since 1994, when he started organizing tournaments and ran a Manhattan game store. Since then, he has been a judge, a player, and one of the longest-tenured columnists on DailyMTG.com, as he enters his second decade writing for the site. He is also the Pro Tour Historian and one of the commentators for the Pro Tour. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpana03/qf2_1.jpg)This Quarterfinal match featured two solid competitors that both wanted to get their hands on that trophy. Paul Rietzl from Your Move Games brought a [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) deck, which will be completely unaffected by the January first bannings, to the table. Peter, on the other hand, brought a deck that will cease to exist come January first. ### Game 1

Peter won the die roll and elected to play first. He kept his opening hand while Paul Parised to six.

The first two turns of the game consisted of "land go" from both sides. On Peter's third turn he played an [Ancient Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb) and dropped [Metalworker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker). Paul came back with a [Polluted Delta](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta) and passed the turn back. Peter was then able to trap Paul under a [Tangle Wire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire), leaving his only play to be [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) for [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction).

Paul then cast [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) during his upkeep, played a land and passed the turn. Over the next couple turns, while Paul was under the [Tangle Wire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire), Peter was able to [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) out a [Mindslaver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslaver).

Paul revealed his hand to be [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition), two [Force Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+Spike)s, [Upheaval](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval), [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak). He drew a counterspell for his turn, then Peter made him cast [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) and [Force Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+Spike) it.

PTR's next turn was simply [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker)ing out [Myr Incubator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Incubator) and activating it.

"Well, at least that wasn't really close at all" - Paul Rietzl

PTR 1 - Paul 0

### Game 2

Paul's first play was a turn two [Powder Keg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Powder+Keg) which blew up Peter's [Seat of the Synod](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seat+of+the+Synod) to cut off his mana. Two turns later PTR tried for a [Tangle Wire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire), which was [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak)ed.

PTR's next turn was a repeat of the last, having a [Tangle Wire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire)[Force Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+Spike)d after not drawing a land. Peter played out a [Metalworker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker) while Paul cast [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) for [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge)s. 

Paul [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)ed Peter taking [Myr Incubator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Incubator) seeing [Stroke of Genius](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stroke+of+Genius), [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker), and [Gilded Lotus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gilded+Lotus), then dropped [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog). Peter's next two turns consisted of having a Pair of [Thran Dynamo](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thran+Dynamo)'s countered. After PTR's last effort [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) gets countered, he scooped.

Paul Rietzl 1 - PTR 1

### Game 3

Paul boarded out 2 more cards after G2, PTR shuffled quietly while watching the other feature match.   
 "First" - PTR "Second" - Paul  
 PTR mulliganed his hand and kept his six. Paul kept his opening seven.

The first few turns saw little action outside of "land… go" until Paul cast an end of turn [Cunning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning) wish for [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), which PTR responded to with a [Stroke of Genius](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stroke+of+Genius) for four which was [Daze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze)d. 

Paul replayed his [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) and passed the turn back to Peter. Peter had a more lively turn with [Tangle Wire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire) and [Metalworker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker). Paul intuitioned for AKs during PTR's endstep.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpana03/qf2_2.jpg)Paul got tapped down on his upkeep, and Peter tried to take advantage by playing [Mindslaver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslaver) which got [Annul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Annul)led. Paul plays through the [Tangle Wire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire) for a few turns until he casts an end of turn [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) revealing [Force Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+Spike), [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak), two [Polluted Delta](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta)s and a [Daze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze). Peter split them as "land or no". Paul plays a [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) on his turn and passes back the turn with five land open. [Mindslaver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslaver) got countered, but a [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) resolved to get [Myr Incubator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Incubator). Paul didn't draw an answer and the game was over.

Peter Szigeti defeats Paul Rietzl 2-1
