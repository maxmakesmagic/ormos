Top 8 Decklists
===============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 10, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











After six rounds of Standard Constructed and six rounds of Amonkhet/Hour of Devastation Booster Draft, the Top 8 was born! Displaying prowess in both formats, these eight players need to advance to the finals in order to secure a spot on the Japan National Team at the upcoming World Magic Cup. Let’s take a look at their Standard decklists which they will be relying upon in the single-elimination playoffs!
