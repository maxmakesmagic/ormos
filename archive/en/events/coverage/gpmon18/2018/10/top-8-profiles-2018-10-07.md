Top 8 Profiles of Grand Prix Montreal 2018
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 7, 2018 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Adam Styborski & Corbin Hosler











These eight players stood a head above the rest here in Montreal, as they proved their mastery with *Guilds of Ravnica* in its Grand Prix debut. Congrats to all the members of the Top 8!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmon18/gp-montreal-2018-Top8-Isaak-Krut.jpg)


**Name:** Isaak Krut  
**Hometown:** Kazan, Russia  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Made Day 2 of the Pro Tour twice.


**What did you play in Sealed? What were your most critical cards?**  

Abzan (green-white-black). [Find // Finality](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Find+%2F%2F+Finality) and [Izoni, Thousand-Eyed](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Izoni%2C+Thousand-Eyed) plus [Underrealm Lich](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underrealm+Lich) plus good removal. I went 7-2.


**What did you draft? What were your records, and which cards were key to winning?**   

Golgari. Almost the same as above, and I went 3-0. Then I had Boros and went 2-0-1 with [Dawn of Hope](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dawn+of+Hope) of the best card.


**What would winning Grand Prix Montreal mean to you?** The greatest achievement of in life.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmon18/gp-montreal-2018-Top8-Christopher-Leonard-Huu-Nguyen.jpg)


**Name:** Christopher Leonard Huu Nguyen  
**Hometown:** Montreal  
**Occupation:** Inventory technician in a warehouse.  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** This is my first Day 2 and my first Top 8.


**What did you play in Sealed? What were your most critical cards?**  

Izzet. My best card was [Gravitic Punch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravitic+Punch) and [Invert // Invent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Invert+%2F%2F+Invent) and [Erratic Cyclops](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Erratic+Cyclops). It steals games.


**What did you draft? What were your records, and which cards were key to winning?**   

3-0 with Dimir and 2-1 with Golgari.


**What would winning Grand Prix Montreal mean to you?**  

A lot!!!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmon18/gp-montreal-2018-Top8-Andrew-Reesor.jpg)


**Name:** Andrew Reesor  
**Hometown:** Boston, MA  
**Occupation:** Actuarial consultant  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 4 a RPTQ.


**What did you play in Sealed? What were your most critical cards?**  

Boros. Tajic and [Legion Warboss](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Legion+Warboss).


**What did you draft? What were your records, and which cards were key to winning?**   

Dimir splashing green, with a lot of surveil cards with two [Vigorspore Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigorspore+Wurm) and [Lotleth Giant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotleth+Giant) as a top-end, going 3-0. Then 2-01 with Boros with [Experimental Frenzy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Experimental+Frenzy) and [Skyknight Legionnaire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyknight+Legionnaire).


**What would winning Grand Prix Montreal mean to you?**  

It would be my most meaningful accomplishment in Magic and I have been playing off and on since *Mirrodin*, so it would be nice to finally hold a trophy.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmon18/gp-montreal-2018-Top8-Chris-Ha.jpg)


**Name:** Chris Ha  
**Hometown:** Toronto  
**Occupation:** Clinical Research Assistant  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Driving to GPs and having someone in the car win or Top 8 - it's happened four times.


**What did you play in Sealed? What were your most critical cards?**  

Sultai (blue-black-green) with [Artful Takedown](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Artful+Takedown) and [Nightveil Predator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nightveil+Predator).


**What did you draft? What were your records, and which cards were key to winning?**   

Grixis with four Mountains and two [Artful Takedown](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Artful+Takedown)s (2-1), then Grixis with five gates, two [Artful Takedown](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Artful+Takedown) and [Etrata, the Silencer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Etrata%2C+the+Silencer).


**What would winning Grand Prix Montreal mean to you?**  

It would be a crazy finish to this cinderella story. Also the money would let me buy more avocados.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmon18/gp-montreal-2018-Top8-Alex-Watt.jpg)


**Name:** Alex Watt  
**Hometown:** Brantford, Ontario  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Won a PTQ on *Magic Online.* 


**What did you play in Sealed? What were your most critical cards?**  

Boros. My most critical cards were [Inescapable Blaze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inescapable+Blaze) and [Boros Challenger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Challenger).


**What did you draft? What were your records, and which cards were key to winning?**   

3-0 with Izzet where [Murmuring Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Murmuring+Mystic) was my best card, along with Ral and [League Guildmage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=League+Guildmage). Then I went 0-2-1 with Boros.


**What would winning Grand Prix Montreal mean to you?**  

I could finally justify the amount of hours spent on *Magic Online.* 




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmon18/gp-montreal-2018-Top8-Martin-Eric%20Gauthier.jpg)


**Name:** Martin Eric Gauthier  
**Hometown:** Longueuil, Quebec  
**Occupation:** Pastry chef  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8 at GP Montreal in 2015.


**What did you play in Sealed? What were your most critical cards?**  

Izzet. [Niv-Mizzet, Parun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Niv-Mizzet%2C+Parun) and that awesome [Quasiduplicate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quasiduplicate) card.


**What did you draft? What were your records, and which cards were key to winning?**   

I went 5-0-1, drafting Dimir twice with good removal and big flyers.


**What would winning Grand Prix Montreal mean to you?**  

Bringing the title home to Montreal.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmon18/gp-montreal-2018-Top8-Michael-Van-Vaals.jpg)


**Name:** Michael Von Vaals  
**Hometown:** Oshawa, Ontarioa  
**Occupation:** Software developer  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Several GP Top 16s and Top 32s.


**What did you play in Sealed? What were your most critical cards?**  

Sultai with two [Status // Statue](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Status+%2F%2F+Statue).


**What did you draft? What were your records, and which cards were key to winning?**   

3-0 with Dimir with two [Disinformation Campaign](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disinformation+Campaign)s, and then Dimir splashing two [Status // Statue](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Status+%2F%2F+Statue) and two [Underrealm Lich](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underrealm+Lich).


**What would winning Grand Prix Montreal mean to you?**  

Helps me get to my first Pro Tour and hopefully get to Silver. It also helps to pay off some of my student loans.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmon18/gp-montreal-2018-Top8-Andrew-Abela.jpg)


**Name:** Andrew Abela  
**Hometown:** Toronto, Ontario  
**Occupation:** Neuroscientist  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 16 Nationals in 2017.


**What did you play in Sealed? What were your most critical cards?**  

Selesnya splashing Aurelia and Tajic.


**What did you draft? What were your records, and which cards were key to winning?**   

Izzet splashing white. In one game I cast [Expansion // Explosion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Expansion+%2F%2F+Explosion) to deck my opponent. Then I drafted Golgari with two Findbroker, a [Find // Finality](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Find+%2F%2F+Finality) and Izoni.


**What would winning Grand Prix Montreal mean to you?**  

Deep down, I’m still the 12-year-old kid drafting *Apocalypse at* Dollar’s in Scarborough. I’m here to have a good time.
