Looking for a way to keep warm this winter? Maverick Kimman combined the best burn red has to offer with the most powerful white cards to succeed at the 2009 **Magic** Game Day. Using a control deck featuring nearly zero creatures, the power of [Ajani Vengeant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani+Vengeant); [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle); and [Chandra Nalaar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra+Nalaar) helped propel the Minnesota player to a successful Game Day weekend.
