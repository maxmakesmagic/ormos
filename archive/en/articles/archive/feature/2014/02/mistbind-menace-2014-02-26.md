The Mistbind Menace
===================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 26, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson












It was inevitable.

  
![Mistbind Clique](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Mistbind+Clique) Ever since the unbanning of [Bitterblossom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bitterblossom) in Modern, people have been wondering what the best Faeries decklist would look like and whether or not it would be a player, especially in the face of [Wild Nacatl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Nacatl). Well, it didn't take long until Faeries started showing up in the **Magic Online** tournaments. Osmanozguney's list is pretty much what you'd expect, but he took it all the way to 1st place in a recent Premier Event. 

 Two of the biggest reasons why Faeries will succeed in Modern is because of [Spell Snare](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Snare) and [Dismember](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember). While Osmanozguney didn't even play [Dismember](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember), the fact that Faeries has a one-mana counterspell and one-mana removal spell will do wonders for the deck that used to be comprised mainly of two-drops and four-drops. Faeries used to struggle in the early game, as its turn ones and turn threes were generally tame, but now the deck can play multiple spells per turn if necessary. 

 One of Faeries's biggest weaknesses was maintaining board parity until its powerful four-drops, [Mistbind Clique](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistbind+Clique) and [Cryptic Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command), could come online, but now that may no longer be an issue. Everyone thinks the real villain is [Bitterblossom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bitterblossom), but cut [Mistbind Clique](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistbind+Clique) from the deck and see how many matches you win! [Bitterblossom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bitterblossom) is powerful, but the Clique is what you really need to watch out for.
