Buy a Box Promotion
===================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on September 18, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/tcg/products/ths/EN_THS_BuyABox_Booster_Display.jpg) Here's an offer that is sure to appeal to every player: The first 20 (for Core locations) or 30 (for Advanced locations) people that preorder or purchase a **Magic: The Gathering***Theros* booster box in your retail store, will get an Alternative Art Premium Sylvan Caryatid promo card! But hurry, this offer is limited to only the first 20 or 30 people per store who either preorder or purchase a full booster box before September 27! 

  
![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/tcg/products/ths/EN_THS_BuyABox_Promo_Card.jpg)* [List of participating stores (North amp; Latin America)](/Magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/daily/events/thsbuyaboxpromolocations)

 If you have questions or concerns, please contact Wizards at [Wizards.com/CustomerService](http://wizards.custhelp.com/), or (800) 324-6496. For all other countries, please find the global contact information at [Wizards.com/GlobalCS](http://company.wizards.com/contactus)
