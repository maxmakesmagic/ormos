Elfball is an age-old strategy for **Magic**, and it saw a resurgence in Extended this past PTQ season. Now it’s seeing new life in the world of **Magic Online**  Classic, finishing second in a recent Premier Event. An added twist unique to the format? The [Earthcraft](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthcraft)/[Squirrel Nest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squirrel+Nest+)  combo. Simply play [Earthcraft](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthcraft), then play [Squirrel Nest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squirrel+Nest). Each Squirrel you create with your land will be able to untap a land. Untap the land you used to make the first Squirrel, and make another! Rinse, wash, and repeat to Squirrel your opponents to death.
