Pauper Fiend
============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 18, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Pauper is **Magic Online**'s format that uses only commons, but that doesn't mean it isn't a format capable of quick wins. \_Shaper\_ finished 2nd in a Pauper Premier Event with a Pauper version of a deck that current **Magic** R&D intern [Gerry Thompson played at Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica*](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/video-deck-tech-elemental-combo-gerry-thompson-2012-10-20). This deck's plan is to get a [Kiln Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiln+Fiend) or [Nivix Cyclops](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nivix+Cyclops) on the battlefield and then play a lot of cheap instants and sorceries to quickly make its power huge.
