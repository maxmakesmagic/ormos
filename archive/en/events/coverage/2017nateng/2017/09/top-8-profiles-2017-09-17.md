Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 17, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Craig-Jones-Author-Photo-150x150_0.jpg)
By Craig Jones











After twelve Swiss rounds, the field of 390 has been reduced down to just 8! Who are the Top 8 competitors competing for two World *Magic* Cup slots and the prestigious title of England National Champion?




---


### Peter Ward


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/2017nateng/T8Profile_PeterWard.jpg)


**Age:** 20


**Hometown and Local Game Store:**


Bath/Cardiff. Firestorm Games.


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 


85th PT Aether Revolt.


1st English WMCQ.


2nd at 2 English WMCQ.


**What did you play in Standard and how did you do in the Constructed portion?**


Zombies.


**What decks did you draft and how did you do in the Limited portion?**


U/G Hoppoe – 2-1.


G/B Midrange – 2-1.


**What does winning Nationals and representing England at the World *Magic* Cup mean to you?** 


Just everything.




---


### Quentin Martin


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/2017nateng/T8Profile_Martin.jpg)


**Age:** 33


**Hometown and Local Game Store:**


Isle of Man.


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 


PT Prague 2006 Top 8.


4 GP Top 8s.


3rd Canadian Nationals.


1st UK Pro Points.


**What did you play in Standard and how did you do in the Constructed portion?**


Dwarves!!! 4-0-2.


**What decks did you draft and how did you do in the Limited portion?**


U/W Vizier (3x) embalm, Naya removal/bombs/2/5s.


**What does winning Nationals and representing England at the World *Magic* Cup mean to you?** 


Everything. I've been wanting to do this since 1999! 2nd only to winning a PT.
