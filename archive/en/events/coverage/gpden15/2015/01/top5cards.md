### 
 5. [Pearl Lake Ancient](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pearl+Lake+Ancient)



 Every game that Andrew Brown won in the finals was closed out by [Pearl Lake Ancient](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pearl+Lake+Ancient). Things got blurry when watching Brown's Blue Black Control deck go through the motions when it had already established control, but [Pearl Lake Ancient](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pearl+Lake+Ancient) would come down like a ray from the heavens, signaling the end was near.
