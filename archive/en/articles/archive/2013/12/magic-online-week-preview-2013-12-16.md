Magic Online Week Preview
=========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 16, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/claytonkroh_0.jpg)
By Clayton Kroh




 Clay is a content strategist, writer, and editor. He's a content editor and writer for DailyMTG, and has contributed creative work for *Magic: The Gathering* sets. On the side, he writes science fiction, fantasy, and horror. 








![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/logo-mtgo-trim.jpg)
[Download **Magic Online**](https://accounts.onlinegaming.wizards.com/)
 
It’s [**Magic Online**  Week on DailyMTG.com](http://www.dailymtg.com/)! Every day this week, you’ll find new articles from key members of the **Magic Online**  business and R&D teams that discuss all aspects of the game. You’re also going to find daily deck lists, downloadable art, and more.
Here’s a preview of just a few items you’ll find this week:



Magic Online Week Preview
**Monday, December 16**


* Get to know **Magic Online**'s Executive Producer Worth Wollpert as he kicks off the week with a [**Magic Online**  Executive Summary](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/magic-online%E2%80%94-executive-summary-2013-12-16)  detailing goals and plans. You definitely don't want to miss this!
* New to **Magic Online**? [“**Magic Online**  101”](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/magic-online-101-2013-12-16) gathers tutorial videos that will help introduce you to the game, the client user interface, finding matches, tournament events, and more.

**Tuesday, December 17**


* Digital Product Manager Mike Turian, a **Magic**  veteran with years of experience throughout Wizards of the Coast, stops by on Tuesday to talk about [**Magic Online**'s many event offerings](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/balancing-act-2013-12-17), what each entails, and the importance of balance in each.
* ORCs—the Online Response Crew—are live, in-game support reps available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. You don’t find that kind of support very often in online games nowadays. [Find out more about the team](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/highlight-game-support-2013-12-17), their passion for **Magic Online**, and their dedication to solving problems fast.

**Wednesday, December 18**


* The **Magic Online**  update baton is passed to Chris Kiritz on Wednesday as he goes in-depth into [the plans for the future](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/seeing-future-2013-12-18).
* The **Magic Online**  Holiday Cube kicks off on Wednesday, and R&D steps up to the plate with Max McCall, who [explores the Holiday Cube](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/merry-wubrg-and-happy-new-cube-2013-12-18). Whether you’re a Cube enthusiast or you have yet to try the format, you’ll find great information about this extremely popular yearly event.

**Thursday, December 19**


* Jon Loucks, a regular contributor on [MTGO.com](http://www.mtgo.com/)  from the R&D team, stops by on Thursday to talk about [the importance of card text style](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/text-me-maybe-2013-12-19)  in **Magic Online**. What does blue text mean? What does it mean when text is in black italics? Jon explores these and a lot more on this simple but important aspect of the game.
* [James Sooy takes up the user interface topic](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/feature/278h&date=12/19/2013) to discuss in more detail the design strategy of the **Magic Online**  client.

**Friday, December 20**


* Earlier in the week, Max McCall whetted our appetites for the Holiday Cube. On Friday, [Ryan Spain visits to share strategies](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/unfairest-them-all-2013-12-20) on how to master this format.
* Lauren Beauregard provides an overview of the [upcoming MOCS Championship event](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/2013-magic-online-championship-2013-12-20) to be held in San Francisco with a prize pool of $116,000!
* Clayton Kroh wraps up **Magic Online**  week with a timely article on the [Holiday Cube and Commander](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/holiday-cube-and-commander-2013-edition-2013-12-20)—on the day ***Magic: The Gathering**—Commander* (2013 Edition) is released in **Magic Online**!

That's just a taste—there’s a lot more to come this week, so be sure to visit [DailyMTG.com](http://www.dailymtg.com/) each day!
