### ETERNAL WEEKEND 2014


QUICK QUESTIONS #3
==================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/080214_1291.jpg)

Jacob Van Lunen

Jacob Van Lunen began playing Magic in 1995. He has participated in organized play at every level of competition and was a member of the winning team at Pro Tour San Diego in 2007, thanks to an innovative draft strategy. As a writer, Van Lunen has had more than three hundred Magic strategy pieces published


October 26, 2014
 











**"Tell me about the first piece(s) of Power 9 you acquired."**






![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/eternalweekend14/QQMaher.jpg)

***Hall of Famer, Bob Maher** - "I went to my first *Magic* tournament. There were no formats then, just *Magic* tournaments. I realized pretty quickly that I couldn't compete without the restricted cards. The first singles I ever bought were a full set of power and four copies of [Chaos Orb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chaos+Orb)."* 




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/eternalweekend14/QQRynkiewicz.jpg)

***Grand Prix Providence Champion, James Rynkiewicz** - "When I was thirteen years old, I needed a [Mox Ruby](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Ruby) for my [Burn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burn) deck."*
