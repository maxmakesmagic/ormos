Pro Tour Dragons of Tarkir Investigations Update
================================================



 Posted in **News**
 on April 29, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










The Investigation Committee has reviewed the investigations that were opened as a result of incidents that took place at Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir*. After offering their recommendations, all of these incidents have now been closed.



Felipe Valdivia has been suspended from playing in sanctioned *Magic* tournaments for six months following his investigation.



Stephen Speck has been suspended from playing in sanctioned *Magic* tournaments for 36 months following his investigation.



Wizards of the Coast expects all players, especially at the Pro Tour, to be professional and responsible, and will take action to ensure the integrity of its tournaments is preserved.Â
