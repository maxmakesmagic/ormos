The Extended season is winding down, but Grand Prix–Houston recently crowned a champion. One of the most innovative decks in the Top 8 was Charles Lancaster's white-black [Pox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pox) deck. Using [Flagstones of Trokair](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flagstones+of+Trokair) as well as creatures like [Bloodghast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodghast) and [Kitchen Finks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks) to minimize the effects of the card on himself, Charles fought his way to a star-studded Top 8. Read more about the event in the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/yurchick-first-last).
