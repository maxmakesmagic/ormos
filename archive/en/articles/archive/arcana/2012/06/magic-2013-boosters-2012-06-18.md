Magic 2013 Boosters
===================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 18, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











When you go to the ***Magic** 2013* Prerelease on July 7-8, you will find that the cards are enclosed in booster packs. And they'll look like this!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/996_booster1.jpg)  
The first one clearly shows Liliana. Hi, Liliana! Let's move on.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/996_booster2.jpg)  
Who's this? Well, it appears to be someone with fins on his face. Draw all the conclusions you like.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/996_booster3.jpg)  
This booster features some sort of large monster. Scary!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/996_booster4.jpg)  
Ah! A humanoid lizard of some sort. That's clearly a Viashino, right?


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/996_booster5.jpg)  
And we end with a large feline warrior of some sort. Specifically, it's the "Ajani" sort. Hi, Ajani!
