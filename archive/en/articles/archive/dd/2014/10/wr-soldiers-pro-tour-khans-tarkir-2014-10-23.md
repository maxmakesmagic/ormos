WR Soldiers at Pro Tour Khans of Tarkir
=======================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on October 23, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_shuheinakamura.jpg)
By Shuhei Nakamura










Previously, we looked at Stanislav Cifka's  [WR heroic beatdown deck](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/dd/rw-heroic-pro-tour-journey-nyx-2014-10-02) during Block Constructed theme week; Yoshihiko Ikawa has made an impressive Standard version of this deck.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Obelisk of Urd)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obelisk of Urd)
The two obviously bear a lot of similarities to one another, but they act in completely different ways. Ikawa's deck is hoping to get a first-turn [Akroan Crusader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Crusader), followed up by a second-turn [Raise the Alarm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raise+the+Alarm), and then drop [Obelisk of Urd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obelisk+of+Urd), naturally naming "Soldier." The next turn, playing [Launch the Fleet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Launch+the+Fleet) leads to 18 points of damage (the Obelisk effect plus the heroic triggers plus the [Raise the Alarm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raise+the+Alarm) tokens plus the Fleet triggers). On the fourth turn, drop a land, target the Crusader to make yet another token and deal 21 damage. Ikawa's version is closer to being a combo deck than a beatdown deck.


If it can get the Obelisk down, even mass removal only slows this deck down. With heroic triggers, [Akroan Crusader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Crusader) and [Vanguard of Brimaz](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vanguard+of+Brimaz) allow you to quickly beef up your forces and continue the fight.
