Standard has seen [Pyromancer Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer+Ascension) decks and it has seen [Howling Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) decks, but Naoki Sakaguchi, the Japanese National Team's alternate, combined both for his Worlds Standard deck. With [Howling Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) making sure he always drew plenty of fuel, his [Pyromancer Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer+Ascension) was always maxed out on effect. [Time Warp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp), [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt), [Worldly Counsel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Worldly+Counsel), whatever spell he wanted to cast—he got a free copy to add insult to injury!
