Grand Prix-Nashville Day 1 Undefeated Decklists
===============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 20, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











There were many hard-fought battles back and forth in the long slog that was Day 1. However, a few battlers managed to stave off the "back" and only provide "forth." Here are the undefeated decklists after Round 10 here in Nashville. Oh, and also Conley Woods' and a few other 9-1 lists. Because though these fighters dropped Round 10, the official Day 2 cut-off was after Round 9 so they were technically undefeated going into Day 2, and, well, because I like them.
