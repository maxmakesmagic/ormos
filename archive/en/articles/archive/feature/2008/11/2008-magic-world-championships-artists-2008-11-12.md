2008 Magic World Championships Artists
======================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 12, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Looking for something to do in between games at the World Championships? Take some time to meet actual **Magic**  artists, have them sign your cards, and check out cool prints and other artwork! 


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2008/worlds_tedin1.jpg)### Mark Tedin


Mark Tedin has worked in the game industry for fifteen years on everything from **Magic: The Gathering** to roleplaying games. He’s illustrated such famous cards as [Necropotence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necropotence), [Ponder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ponder), and [Forgotten Ancient](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forgotten+Ancient). 


* [Complete list of Mark Tedin's Magic card artwork](http://beta.gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Spoiler.aspx?method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+tedin)

  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2008/worlds_pollack1.jpg)### Alan Pollack


Alan Pollack hails from New Jersey and got his start with *Dungeon*  magazine. After deciding to make fantasy illustration his life’s work, he has gone on to illustrate numerous TCGs, RPGs, book covers, and more. His famous **Magic**  cards include [Story Circle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Story+Circle), [Plague Wind](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plague+Wind), and [Recoil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Recoil).


* [Complete list of Alan Pollack's **Magic** card artwork](http://beta.gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Spoiler.aspx?method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+pollack)

  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2008/worlds_cavotta1.jpg)### Matt Cavotta


Matt Cavotta is best known to players as the man behind [Anavolver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anavolver), [Questing Phelddagrif](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Questing+Phelddagrif), [Noble Panther](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noble+Panther), and [Iridescent Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iridescent+Angel). As you can tell from most of his work, Matt has a unique style that strays a bit from conventional fantasy art.


* [Complete list of Matt Cavotta's **Magic** card artwork](http://beta.gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Spoiler.aspx?method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+cavotta)
