WHAT IS ORGANIZED PLAY?
=======================





















Organized Play is your door to the wider world of Magic. It brings together players of all skill levels to provide a fun, inclusive experience that creates communities, provides new challenges, and, lets you play Magic in an organized way. No matter what type of experience you’re looking for—from a casual atmosphere to intense competition—Organized Play provides it.


[LEARN ABOUT THE PATH TO THE PRO TOUR](/content/kitchen-pro-tour)









GATHERING TOGETHER
------------------


Above all, Organized Play fosters a fun, enjoyable experience, whether you hop into a store in San Juan, a Grand Prix in Grand Rapids, or a draft in Denmark. It’s about having a place for players to come together, meet new people, and play the game they all love. 


[FIND A STORE NEAR YOU](http://locator.wizards.com/)

 











### GETTING STARTED AT FNM




The best place to get into Organized Play is with a local Friday Night Magic. Happening in stores all over the world every Friday Night, these events are a great place to meet local players, hone your skills, and discover a world of Magic that holds more possibilities than you ever imagined. So find your nearest store and get started on your journey through Organized Play today!
