Faeries is definitely one of the breakout archetypes to come from the *Lorwyn* block, and saw plenty of Top 8 action throughout the Nationals season. One of the most intriguing Top 8 finishes came out of the Italy National Championships. What was so interesting? Not just the [Murderous Redcap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Murderous+Redcap)s in the maindeck or the [Bottle Gnomes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bottle+Gnomes) in the sideboard, but the player piloting the list: Filippo Kratter. Fans of X-sports will recognize Filippoâ€™s name from the world of professional snowboarding. In fact, you can check him out in action on the slopes [here](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6zt6bbfGtcw) and slinging spells at Nationals [here](/en/events/coverage/2008-italy-national-championship).
