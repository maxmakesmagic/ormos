![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/616_symbol_ef0ellebp2.jpg)Standard rotates once each year, with the release of the October large set. ***Magic** 2012* will enter Standard on its release without knocking anything out, then ***Magic** 2011* and *Zendikar* block will leave Standard with the release of "Shake." The Standard format between ***Magic** 2012* and "Shake" will consist of *Zendikar* block, *Scars of Mirrodin* block, ***Magic** 2011*, and ***Magic** 2012*. After the release of "Shake," it will consist of *Scars of Mirrodin* Block, ***Magic** 2012*, and "Shake."
