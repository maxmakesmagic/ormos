The Unspeakable Combo
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 20, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











C*hampions of Kamigawa* contains within it a three-part ritual to summon a powerful legendary kami. Though you dare not speak its name, you could instead utter these three spells:

  
First this...  
![Peer Through Depths](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Peer+Through+Depths)Then this...  
![Reach Through Mists](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Reach+Through+Mists)
Then this...  
![Sift Through Sands](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Sift+Through+Sands)
And you'll summon *this!*  
[The Unspeakable](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=The%2BUnspeakable)  
*The Unspeakable - Art by Khang Le*
