David Vo made the Top 8 of the recent StarCityGames.com Legacy Open in Atlanta. The deck that got him there there? A combo build, but this "combo" deck plays a LOT of creatures. That's because it's Elf combo! By casting a ton of the cheap threats after a [Glimpse of Nature](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimpse+of+Nature), he gets to draw a card repeatedly. Each Elf combines with [Heritage Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid) to generate mana, and from there you're just a hop, a skip, and a jump to casting [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Aeons+Torn) to take an extra turn and annihilate your opponent (literally!). You can read [coverage of the event](http://www.starcitygames.com/php/news/expandnews.php?Article=21537) at StarCityGames.com.
