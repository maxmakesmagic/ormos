It was good enough to win Pro Tour-Berlin, and just a few months later, Lino Burgold did it again! He took Extended Elves all the way to the top slot at Grand Prix-Hanover. His deck used the innovative kill of making an army of arbitrarily large Elves via [Mirror Entity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Entity). To read more about Lino and Grand Prix-Hanover, check out [the official Grand Prix-Hanover event coverage](/en/events/coverage/hometown-hero-takes-gold).
