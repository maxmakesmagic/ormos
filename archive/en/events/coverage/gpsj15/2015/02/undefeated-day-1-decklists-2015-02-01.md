Undefeated Day 1 Decklists
==========================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on February 1, 2015 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Adam Styborski and Marc Calderaro










While there’s no guarantee that going undefeated on Day 1 of a Grand Prix ensures Day 2 success – namely, making Top 8 or Top 4 for Team Limited – it is one of the best ways to get a head start on that. Two teams are Grand Prix San Jose managed the feat: At an unblemished 9-0 are No. 12-ranked Eric Froehlich, Pro Tour Hall of Fame player Luis Scott-Vargas, and Paul Cheon, and at lossless 8-0-1 are Will Sampson, Andy Lee, and Perry Steever.


These are the decks they used to do it.
