Rivals of Ixalan Planeswalker Deck Lists
========================================



 Posted in **News**
 on January 11, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






It's a time-honored tradition (if by time-honored you mean the last few years) the week of the Prerelease to show off the Planeswalker Deck lists that you'll be able to play with this very weekend (and after this very weekend—they're not going anywhere).


**Prerelease**


Want to get a first look at *Rivals of Ixalan*? [Find a Prerelease near you now](https://magic.wizards.com/en/event-types/prerelease)!


You'll have two choices for battling Planeswalkers this time around—a white-black deck led by Vraska, Scheming Gorgon and a black-red deck led by everyone's favorite angry pirate dad, Angrath, Minotaur Pirate. Check out the lists below and see which one you want to use to battle your friends this weekend.
