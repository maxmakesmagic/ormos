White Weenie managed to put two players into the Top 8 of Pro Tour–Amsterdam, and now that the Pro Tour Qualifier season for Nagoya is underway, it's rearing its head again! This time it was **Magic Online** player Avantasian using the deck to make the Top 8 of a PTQ held in the digital realm. Good old-fashioned beaters backed by pump and a small modicum of protection are still enough to lead to a successful performance in this day and age.
