Draft Viewer: 7/7/2010
======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 7, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Welcome back to the Draft Viewer! This week we've got a triple *Rise of the Eldrazi* draft from the Top 8 of a Judge Open Sealed Deck event. This event, along with [another like it](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/MagicOnlineTourn.aspx?x=mtg/digital/magiconline/tourn/1351196), was free to certified judges to thanks them for all their hard work. The winners received sponsorships to judge a Pro Tour in 2011.


Enjoy!
