December 18 2009 DCI Banned & Restricted List Announcement
==========================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on December 18, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**Announcement Date:** December 18, 2009  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2010


***Zendikar* Block Constructed**, **Standard, Extended, Legacy, Vintage**  
 No changes


Changes to **Magic Online**–only formats are now announced monthly on the [**Magic Online** group page in Wizards Community](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/pro-tour-gatecrash-qualifier-season-top-8-standard-decklists-2015-50).


For the complete list of all banned and restricted cards by format, click [here](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=judge/resources/banned).
