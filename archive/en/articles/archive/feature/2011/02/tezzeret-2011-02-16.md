Guillaume Matignon of France narrowly missed the 2010 Player of the Year title at Pro Tour Paris, part of **Magic** Weekend Paris. The Standard deck he opted to bring to battle is a [Tezzeret, Agent of Bolas](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret%2C+Agent+of+Bolas) very similar to the list that took American Patrick Chapin to the Top 8 of the event. Though it wasn't good enough to fend off Brad Nelson, who dispatched Matignon to lock up the title, it is a fascinating new addition to the world of Standard. Read full coverage of the event [here](/en/events/coverage/stark-flies-high-planeswalkers-paris).
