Got Flash?
==========



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 30, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![Mystical Teachings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Mystical+Teachings)So you're a time-bending chronomancer, consulting with a being from some dimension outside of time, and this being is about to let you in on one of its chronomantic secrets. In other words, you have [Mystical Teachings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) on the stack—what kinds of cards can you tutor up?
 Since *Time Spiral*, the flash keyword has shortened the Oracle text of several cards. Which ones now say “flash” on them matters a lot for [Mystical Teachings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings), so let's take a trip through the pre-*Time Spiral* cards that were affected by the new flash keyword.
