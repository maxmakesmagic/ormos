Mistform Ultimus Watch: *Magic 2013* Edition
============================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 15, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana300/mistformultimuswatch.jpg)It is time once again to check in on everyone's favorite Kobold Pincher, [Mistform Ultimus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Ultimus). It, along with the [Changelings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?name=+%5Bchangeling%5D), has every creature type in **Magic**. And that means it's always changing as new creature types get added. One day it's a perfectly normal Caribou Assassin and then the next day it discovers it's a Germ Eldrazi Ally. Let's take a look at the entire list!
