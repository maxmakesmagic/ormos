Grand Prix - Singapore
======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 2, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












The **Magic: The Gathering** Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro Tour events.


**TOURNAMENT FORMAT AND SCHEDULE**  

The format for Grand Prix–Singapore is Extended. All DCI Constructed-format rules, **Magic** Floor Rules, and all subsequent updates will be enforced.


**Friday, March 18**: Onsite registration is from 4:00 – 9:00 P.M. at Meeting Room 306.  
**Saturday, March 19**: Late registration from 8:30 – 9:30 A.M. Tournament begins at 10:00 A.M. The Top 64 players advance to competition on Sunday.  
**Sunday, March 20**: The Top 64 players begin play at 9:00 A.M.

### TOURNAMENT


**Divisions**  

The main tournament at Grand Prix–Singapore is open to **Magic** players of all ages.


* **Head Judge**  

 The Head Judge for Grand Prix–Singapore will be Wearn Ee Chong, Level 3 from Malaysia.
* **Tournament Structure**  

 The structure for Grand Prix–Singapore will be modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds), with the Top 64 players advancing to the second day of competition for prizes and invitations.
* **Amateur Status**  

 A player is considered to have amateur status if he or she has 0 lifetime pro points. If a player has ever been awarded any points from a Grand Prix, Pro Tour, or World Championship event, that player no longer has amateur status, and is not eligible for amateur awards at Grand Prix or other tournaments.
* **Tournament Divisions and Prizes**  

 Grand Prix–Singapore will feature the standard main tournament prize schedule, as well as a special Amateur division prize schedule. Players who have 0 lifetime Pro Points will be eligible for the Amateur prizes. The Amateur prizes will be paid to the highest-finishing amateur players *in addition to any winnings those players receive from the main tournament prize schedule.* All prizes are in U.S. Dollars.

* **Pro Tour Invitations**  

 The Top 8 previously uninvited players will each win an invitation to Pro Tour–Philadelphia. *In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher in that Grand Prix. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*
* **Tournament Byes** (*Byes are not cumulative throughout the season or year*)  

 Byes will be given to players in the Grand Prix based on the following criteria:  
***Third-Round Byes***
	+ Players with 28 or more professional points in the Columbus 2004 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Constructed rating of 1950 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed ratings published on December 15, 2004.***Second-Round Byes***
	+ Players with 14 or more professional points in the Columbus 2004 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Constructed rating of 1850 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed ratings published on December 15, 2004.***First-Round Byes***
	+ Players with 7 or more professional points in the Columbus 2004 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Constructed rating of 1750 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed ratings published on December 15, 2004.

### SIDE EVENTS


* **Grand Prix-Singapore Trials** ([Full List of Trials](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=grandprix/singapore05/trials))  
**Friday, March 18:** Trials start at 12:00 P.M., 2:00 P.M., 4:00 P.M., and 6:00 P.M. Registration closes 15 minutes prior to each Trial. Maximum 32 players per Trial, single-elimination. The top finisher receives a third-round bye at Grand Prix–Singapore and one display of *Betrayers of Kamigawa* boosters. The format is *Extended*. Cost: S$15
* **Pro Tour– Philadelphia Qualifier**  
**Sunday, March 20:** Registration closes at 9:15 A.M.; play begins at 9:30 A.M. The top finisher receives US$375 and an invitation to Pro Tour– Philadelphia. The format is *Extended*. Cost: S$20
* **On Demand Tournaments**  

 Eight-person single-elimination, on-demand tournaments will be offered throughout the weekend. Costs: Constructed S$10, Draft S$15, Sealed S$30
