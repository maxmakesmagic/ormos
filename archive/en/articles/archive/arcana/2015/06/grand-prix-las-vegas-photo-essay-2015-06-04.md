Grand Prix Las Vegas Photo Essay
================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 4, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamstyborski.jpg)
By Adam Styborski




 Stybs has played Magic the world over, writing and drafting as part of the event coverage team and slinging Commander everywhere his decks will fit. 






It’s impossible to capture what it was like to be a part of Modern Masters Weekend in Las Vegas. There were two Grand Prix, hundreds of staff, thousands of players, and hundreds of thousands of cards present over the long four days here, and it was an extraordinary experience that was shared the world over.



These photos are just a sampling of what we saw in Las Vegas, and like the stories of the event to come in the years that follow these will only remain a sliver of the enormity of *Magic*’s historic weekend.
