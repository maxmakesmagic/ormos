A number of players took a Naya Zoo deck powered by [Stoneforge Mystic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic) and created by Tom "The Boss" Ross to a dominating performance at [Pro Tour–San Diego](/en/events/coverage/g%C3%B6rtzen-goes-all-way-san-diego). Following the event, the deck has been a big factor in tournaments held on **Magic Online**. The above list was used by Brad Nelson, one of the players who played it in California, to go undefeated in a digital event. It makes a great choice for playing at your local [Friday Night **Magic**](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm)!
