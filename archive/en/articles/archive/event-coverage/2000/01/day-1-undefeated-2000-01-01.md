Day 1: The Undefeated
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_501.png)
By Jun-Wei Hew and Josh Bennett











Waltzing into Day 2 without having dropped a match is a pretty good feeling. The six competitors assembled below are waist deep in that feeling and loving it. They also prove that the field is as wide-open as has been suspected, each piloting a different deck to a pristine finish. All but one are aggressive decks, punishing the format's dependance on sometimes-wonky mana bases. Three of them play the tempo game, with a fourth running on pure aggression. The odd man out is a classic blue-black-red control deck. Make of them what you will.
