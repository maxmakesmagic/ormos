Luis Scott-Vargas is on a tear! After winning Pro Tour-Berlin, then Grand Prix-Atlanta, Luis Scott-Vargas has won back-to-back North American Grand Prixs with a title in Los Angeles! The deck that took him there? An innovative Blue-Red Storm concoction that had just enough black to swap [Grapeshot](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grapeshot) for [Tendrils of Agony](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tendrils+of+Agony). You can read all about Grand Prix-Los Angeles in [the official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/luis-scott-vargas-conquers-la). To find out how to get to an Extended Pro Tour Qualifier for Honolulu near you, visit the [official PTQ page](/en/node/640716).
