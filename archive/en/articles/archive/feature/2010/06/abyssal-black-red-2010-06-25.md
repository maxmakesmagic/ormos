Aggressive red decks have always been short one thing: fatties. But Scott Alshansi didn't let that stop him from using [Abyssal Persecutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abyssal+Persecutor) to make up for that fact on his way to the Top 8 of the recent Pro Tour Qualifier held in Madison, Wisconsin. The 6/6 could be blown up with [Gatekeeper of Malakir](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gatekeeper+of+Malakir), [Terminate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terminate), and [Sarkhan the Mad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sarkhan+the+Mad) giving Scott the time he needed to beat down, then giving him plenty of outs to toss the giant monster!
