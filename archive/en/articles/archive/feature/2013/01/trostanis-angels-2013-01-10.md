Trostani's Angels
=================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 10, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







As Friday approaches, it becomes time to scour **Magic Online** results for sweet decks to play at [Friday Night **Magic**](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm). Toward the end of a season, it can be hard to find something new to surprise the competition with, but when you're out of ideas, there's almost always someone doing something cool on **Magic Online**. 


![Trostani, Selesnya's Voice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Trostani%2C+Selesnya%27s+Voice)
 Today I look at a moderately aggressive midrange Bant deck that's heavy on permanents. It's looking to overwhelm the opponent with awesome creatures and Planeswalkers, with several ways to make tokens for [Trostani, Selesnya's Voice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trostani%2C+Selesnya%27s+Voice) to populate. The best of those is [Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft), which can let Trostani start making 4/4 fliers (and 4 life, in addition to the 4 life you'll gain just for attacking with [Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft)). 


 The most surprising card in the deck is [Thalia, Guardian of Thraben](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thalia%2C+Guardian+of+Thraben) alongside seventeen noncreature spells, but sometimes symmetrical cards that hurt you will hurt your opponent more enough to be worth it. 


 If you're looking to make changes, I would suggest [Rootborn Defenses](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootborn+Defenses) to further the populate strategy while having excellent synergy with [Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft). It's also the perfect card against [Supreme Verdict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Supreme+Verdict).
