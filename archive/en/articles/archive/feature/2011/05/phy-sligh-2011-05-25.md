[Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) has been waiting for its moment to shine, and with the new Phyrexian mana spells, now is its day! Anthony Eason took this deck, nicknamed "Phy Sligh," to the Standard Open in Louisville held by StarCityGames.com this past weekend. The way the deck works is simple: pay tons of life to get mana reductions on Phyrexian cards, turning your life total into a resource for mana, then cast a gigantic [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) because your life is low from casting all your spells! Read more in the [coverage](http://www.starcitygames.com/php/news/expandnews.php?Article=21896) from StarCityGames.com.
