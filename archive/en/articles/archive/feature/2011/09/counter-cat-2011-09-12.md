Zoo has been a popular archetype for many years, and a brand-new format has demonstrated it can be just as effective now as it has been historically. Take this version by Josh Utter-Leyton, part of the Team ChannelFireball crew that took it to Pro Tour Philadelphia. He made it to the finals, proving that burn, efficient creatures, and now a tiny bit of counter magic can still compete with the best in the world. For more on Pro Tour Philadelphia and Modern, visit the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/estratti-starts-modern-revolution-philly).
