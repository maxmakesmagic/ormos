Antoine Ruel Top 8 Profile
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**by Sideboard Staff**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPAMS01/858.jpg)**Name:**Antoine Ruel   
**Age:** 21   
**Location:** Aubervilliers France   
**Occupation:** Student   
**Played Magic since:** 5 years   
**Best accomplishments:**1st GP Cannes, 1st GP Porto, 6th PT New York (team), 1st team Invitational. 



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Amsterdam?**  

A: It's near Paris and I wanted to win PT points and have a good trip with friends.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Top 8 because I wanted to confirm after GP Porto.



**Q: What do you think about the format?**  

A: In Invasion, the only interesting format is Rochester Draft.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Because I took it very seriously and I really wanted to succeed here.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: PT Points, money and respect.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: I'm gonna crush them.
