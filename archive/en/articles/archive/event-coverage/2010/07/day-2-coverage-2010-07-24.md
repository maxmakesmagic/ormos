---------------------------------------------------------


*by Josh Bennett*

Both players were still shaking off the cobwebs as they shuffled up for the round. The excitement was helping. Thibeault was hoping to extend his undefeated streak.


He got off on the right foot in the first game. He opened with a curve of [Black Knight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Knight), Liliana's Spectre, [Howling Banshee](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Banshee) while Bower played land and passed. Finally Bower got out an [Assault Griffin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Assault+Griffin) and traded it for the [Banshee](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banshee), falling to seven life.


Thibeault had more in reserve, replacing it with [Air Servant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Air+Servant). Bower gamely played [Rise from the Grave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rise+from+the+Grave) to get back his Griffin, but Thibeault didn't forget that [Air Servant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Air+Servant) had an ability, and they were on to game two.


Thibeault 1 - Bower 0


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/canat10/fm8Bower.jpg)*Devin Bower*
Bower flipped the script for game two, curving out with [War Priest of Thune](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=War+Priest+of+Thune) and [Wild Griffin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Griffin). Thibeault put [Liliana's Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%27s+Specter) in the way (catching [Sign in Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sign+in+Blood)) and Bower refused to risk his Griffin. His War Priest hit for two. Bower also summoned [Squadron Hawk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squadron+Hawk), fetching its twin, but he was stuck on three land.


Thibeault bolstered his defenses with [Gargoyle Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gargoyle+Sentinel). Bower swung in with all his creatures, and after the 2/2s were blocked a [Mighty Leap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mighty+Leap) killed the [Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sentinel), saving the War Priest in the process. Bower summoned an [Elite Vanguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elite+Vanguard). A [Black Knight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Knight) from Thibeault put an end to his ground assault.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/canat10/fm8Thibeault.jpg)*Vincent Thibeault*
Bower swung in the air for one and played his second Hawk. Thibeault stocked up on cards with two [Sign in Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sign+in+Blood). He cast [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) on one of the incoming Hawks, taking one. Bower brought out [Assault Griffin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Assault+Griffin).


Thibeault dug for more answers with [Foresee](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Foresee), and kept all four on top. One of them was [Combust](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Combust) for the Griffin. Bower played a Strongbox with five land in play and one card in hand. Thibeault used his seven mana to play [Gravedigger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravedigger) to get back his Specter and snatch it: [Grave Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grave+Titan).


Bower sprung his Strongbox on the first try and played [Brittle Effigy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brittle+Effigy). Still, Thibeault was up a ton of cards and his position was only getting stronger. Another [Black Knight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Knight) and a [Rotting Legion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotting+Legion), and now he was sending in the attacks. Unable to recover his footing, Bower scooped.


Vincent Thibeault defeats Devin Bower 2-0
