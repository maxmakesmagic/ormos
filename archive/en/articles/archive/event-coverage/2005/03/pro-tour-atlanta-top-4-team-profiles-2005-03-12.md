Pro Tour Atlanta: The Top 4 Team Profiles
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 12, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff













![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptatl05/top4_lupidia.jpg)*Benjamin Caumes, Nicolas Bornarel, Camille Fenet*

**Les baltringues de Ludipia**


**Members:** Benjamin Caumes, Nicolas Bornarel, Camille Fenet


**What is the story behind your Team Name?**   

It is the name of the store we own in Ludipia, and a joke based on a not-real name between us.


**Ages:** Caumes: 28; Bornarel: 26; Fenet: 26


**Hometown:** Caumes: Clermont, France; Bornarel: Clermont; Fenet: Clermont


**Occupations:**   

Caumes: Seller in a game store  

Bornarel: Seller in my game store  

Fenet: Informatician


**Number of times playing on the Pro Tour:**   

Caumes: A little less than 20 (18, I think)  

Bornarel: about 10  

Fenet: 1


**How did you qualify for PT Atlanta?**   

Winning a PTQ in France


**Number of GP, PT Top 8s?**   

Caumes: 3  

Bornarel: 1 (this one)  

Fenet: 1


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**   

Caumes: Top 8 in PT Yokohama 2003, Finalist in GP-Reins, Finalist of French Team championships  

Bornarel: Second in French Team championships in 2004


**What do you think of your chances on Sunday?**   

Caumes: We will see.  

Bornarel: We'll see. I didn't think I would play Sunday since I never played on Saturday before.  

Fenet: 50/50


**What card or cards earned your team the most wins on Saturday and why?**  

Caumes: First Volley (we won two matches thanks to it) and Meloku (you might know why)  

Fenet: First Volley


**Tell us one interesting or surprising thing from Friday or Saturday.**   

Caumes: One surprising thing from Friday is the qualification for Day 2 of Nicolas Bornarel and also two Jitte and Meloku in the same sealed deck.  

Bornarel: Two Jitte and Meloku in the same Sealed! And 2 Iwamori, 1 Kumano, Keiga in another.
