Modern Owling Mine
==================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on December 16, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











 This is an old-school deck from Kamigawa times that has been rearing its head in Modern on **Magic Online**. What's interesting is just how similar it is to the old-school version from that timeframe. **Magic** Invitational winner and [Snapcaster Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snapcaster+Mage) himself, Tiago Chan, piloted [a version of this deck](https://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/pthon06/t8decks) to the Top 8 of Pro Tour Honolulu in 2006.
