Back from the Dead
==================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on February 13, 2014 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Gerry Thompson











 While not a true Reanimator deck, this deck from Patrick Chapin has a big graveyard theme, including [Whip of Erebos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whip+of+Erebos). Most Whip decks based on *Theros* tended to focus too heavily on reanimation with [Abhorrent Overlord](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abhorrent+Overlord) and [Rescue from the Underworld](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rescue+from+the+Underworld), but they ended up being too clunky. Instead, Chapin streamlined the deck into a [Nighthowler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nighthowler)/[Shadowborn Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowborn+Demon) deck that looks much more appealing. 

[Commune with the Gods](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Commune+with+the+Gods), [Grisly Salvage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grisly+Salvage), and [Satyr Wayfinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Satyr+Wayfinder) do a good job of filling your graveyard. [Nemesis of Mortals](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nemesis+of+Mortals) and [Nighthowler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nighthowler) are great early threats while [Shadowborn Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowborn+Demon) cleans up in the late game. Some [Abrupt Decay](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay)s round out a very solid midrange deck. 

 I would expect Mono-Blue Devotion to be a difficult matchup, but Patrick wisely chose to play six protection from blue creatures in his sideboard. Matchups like Mono-Black Devotion and WU Control are both weak to [Whip of Erebos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whip+of+Erebos), so I'd look at including some more of those in the sideboard. 

This deck likely has some issues but the card quality is clearly there.
