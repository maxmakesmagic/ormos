Headed into 2007's Grand Prix–Columbus the world was ready for Flash Hulk. The potent combo deck, which sought to use [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) to put [Protean Hulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Protean+Hulk) into play in order to set up a convoluted combo that saw the opponent dead as early as turn one (on a quite fortunate draw), was both prevalent and prevalently hated. That didn't stop Steve Sadin from using this particular build, created by Billy Moreno, to take the top slot. You can read more about Steve's weekend in the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/hulk-flash-smashes-columbus)and keep an eye out for the upcoming Grand Prix–Chicago, which will again feature the Legacy format. Rumor has it [Natural Order](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Natural+Order) to play an early [Progenitus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Progenitus) is all the rage in the format, but will it face the same fate as Flash Hulk, hated out due to its own heavy weight? You'll have to check back to find out!
