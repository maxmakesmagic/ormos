Feature Match - Round 10:
=========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 1, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![Feature Match: Masashi Oiso vs. Jin Okamoto](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptno03/896.jpg)It wouldn't be much of a stretch to say that Masashi Oiso and Jin Okamoto are the two best **Magic** players in Japan right now. Oiso made the finals of Pro Tour Yokohama, which helped carry him to a Rookie of the Year title. "The Last Emperor" Jin Okamoto finally made a Pro Tour Top 8 after many near misses when he reached the finals of Worlds in Berlin a few months ago. Japan produced four Pro Tour Top 8's last season, but the country is still looking for its first Pro Tour Championship. Without a doubt, Oiso and Okamoto represent the best hope for accomplishing that, and are in prime position to do so this weekend with 7-2 records. Oiso is playing [Tinker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker), and Okamoto is playing a Gob-Vantage deck similar to the one he helped put on the map at Worlds, but with added kick from [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox), [Seething Song](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seething+Song) and [Goblin Charbelcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Charbelcher).

### Game 1

![OISO!!!!!!!!](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptno03/897.jpg)Oiso double mulliganed to start. He played [Voltaic Key](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key), and Okamoto summoned [Mogg Fanatic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogg+Fanatic). [Seething Song](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seething+Song) on turn two (thanks to [Ancient Tomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb)) brought out a pair of [Goblin Warchief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Warchief)s, putting a significant amount of pressure on the board. Meanwhile, Oiso drew and missed his third land drop, and seemingly had a draw that was ill-prepared to deal with Okamoto's quick Goblin assault. When [Goblin Matron](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Matron) came out on turn three, he quickly scooped up his cards.

Oiso 0 - Okamoto 1

### Game 2

![OKAMOTO!!!!!](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptno03/898.jpg)Once again, Oiso mulliganed down to five with a cringe. Okamoto summoned a Prospector, and Oiso was stuck with just a [Rishadan Port](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishadan+Port) for land. The Prospector helped bring out a second turn [Goblin Warchief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Warchief), a Matron searched up a Piledriver, and the match was soon over as Oiso was never able to put up a fight.

Oiso 0 - Okamoto 2
