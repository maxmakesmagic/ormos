Modern Rakdos
=============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 13, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorPic_GavinVerhey.jpg)
By Gavin Verhey




 When Gavin Verhey was eleven, he dreamt of a job making Magic cards—and now as a Magic designer, he's living his dream! Gavin has been writing about Magic since 2005. 






The exciting Modern format was showcased on **Magic Online** this past weekend, as a premier event at half the cost brought people out to play. Anything and everything seems to be viable, from midrange to combo to control... and, of course, beatdown! \_Megafone\_ brought a hyper-aggressive Rakdos burn deck to the table. Sporting only nineteen lands and the best burn spells printed in the past nine years, this deck is tuned to kill you in short order!


This deck contains some of the culprits you might expect. [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) leads the pack, and both [Lava Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lava+Spike) and [Bump in the Night](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bump+in+the+Night) quickly send your opponent's life total spiraling toward 0. Even [Shard Volley](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shard+Volley) joins the fray as an additional way to deal 3! With a total of twenty-six ways to deal 3 or more damage for one mana, your opponent is put on a lightning-quick clock.


[Faithless Looting](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faithless+Looting) is one of the key cards here, digging this deck closer to its burn. [Thunderous Wrath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thunderous+Wrath) is phenomenal when paid for its miracle cost, but nigh-uncastable if stuck in your opening hand. [Faithless Looting](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faithless+Looting) allows you to toss them—and any excess lands—away in favor of more effective cards.


If you like attacking on a budget, this is a great choice for Modern that will result in some fast and furious games! Take a look at the decklist below.
