Wizards of the Coast R&D member Mons Johnson is known for making some wacky decks, and *Rise of the Eldrazi* has proven no different! This time around, Mons has concocted a "combo" deck that utilizes [Sovereigns of Lost Alara](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sovereigns+of+Lost+Alara) for a powerful punch. How? Simply attack with a lone creature after casting the 4/5, and whammo! Your opponent is facing down an attacker made large with [Eldrazi Conscription](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Conscription). That's right, +10/+10 *and* trample! And even if it's too late for annihilator to trigger (this turn), you even get an exalted trigger from the Sovereigns. You can see a different take on this deck from Jacob Van Lunen in [today's Building on a Budget](/en/articles/archive/building-budget/sovereigns-found-conscription-2010-04-21).
