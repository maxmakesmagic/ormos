Australian National Championship
--------------------------------


Nicastri Conquers Australia Nats
================================




July 21 - 23, 2006












![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/aunat08/team_pagetop.jpg) Aaron Nicastri came, he saw and he conquered. Armed with the "Aussie Assault" [Swans of Bryn Argoll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swans+of+Bryn+Argoll) deck his team gave him on Friday night, he remained undefeated all weekend until he started conceding to let friends into the Top 8. Once there, he bested two Faeries decks before taking down Brandon Lau's Black/Red Tokens deck in the finals. All credit to Brandon though, he made Aaron fight for it, taking the match to the fifth game and forcing him to actually attack with the Swans for the win. Joining Aaron and Brandon representing Australia at the World Championships later this year will be Justin Cheung and Marcus Rokman. Congratulations all 'round, lads!

Sure enough, Cheung was able to power up some [Mutavault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutavault)s to join the fight and finally dispatch Neeman.
