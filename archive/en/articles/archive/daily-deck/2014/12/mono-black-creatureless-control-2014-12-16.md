When you ask most players what they think the best deck in Standard is, Mono-Black will likely be the most popular answer. However, these players are generally referring to a devotion deck featuring [Desecration Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desecration+Demon) and [Gray Merchant of Asphodel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gray+Merchant+of+Asphodel). Today's deck, while still mono-black, is strategically much different than the Black Devotion decks that have had so much success in Standard for the past year.


This deck has no creatures! No [Pack Rat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat). No [Lifebane Zombie](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lifebane+Zombie). No [Desecration Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desecration+Demon). These staples of Mono-Black Devotion are nowhere to be found. Instead, this deck is fueled by the card drawing provided by both [Sign in Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sign+in+Blood) and [Underworld Connections](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underworld+Connections). However, these cards cost life points, as does [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize). This makes the lifegain provided by [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt) vitally important, and is the reason you see [Elixir of Immortality](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elixir+of+Immortality) and [Pharika's Cure](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pharika%27s+Cure) in the main deck. [Liliana Vess](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+Vess) is a very important card in this deck, if only because she finds these important [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt)s for you.
