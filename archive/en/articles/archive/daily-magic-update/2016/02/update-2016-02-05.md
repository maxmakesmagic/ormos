It's Here!
==========


1. [Pro Tour *Oath of the Gatewatch*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptogw)

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Ascending to New Heights](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/modern-constructed-league-2016-02-04)

If, as many pros are predicting, [Abrupt Decay](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay) will be on the decline in Modern, now seems like a pretty good time to start breaking out the three-mana permanents, right? And since [Jeskai Ascendancy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jeskai+Ascendancy) is one of the most powerful three-mana permanents around, it might be time for a re-emergence. Parjona certainly thought so, and turned in a 5-0 performance in a Modern League with this masterwork.


How does it work? With an Ascendancy in play, untapping either [Fatestitcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatestitcher) or an active [Faerie Conclave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faerie+Conclave) with every spell nets you mana and cards every time, while growing that creature. That finds you more [Fatestitcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatestitcher)s, which nets more mana and more attackers. Eventually, you swing with a large enough creature and your opponent is dead in a flurry of cantrips.
