Day 2 Archetype Exemplars
=========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 30, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke










The Top 8 featured five different deck archetypes (Hangarback Abzan, Jeskai, Black-Red Dragons, Blue-Black Control, and Esper Dragons), looking at the full Top 16 added another three (Abzan Control, Red-Green Dragons, and Atarka Red). But all in all, 23 different archetypes made it to the second day at Grand Prix Prague.


Check out lists of representatives for the remaining fifteen here!
