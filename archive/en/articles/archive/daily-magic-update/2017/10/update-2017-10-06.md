The October 6, 2017 Update
==========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on October 6, 2017 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20171007152350im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_KatieAllison.jpg?itok=Wj4seNJ3)
By Katie Allison




 Katie is a copy editor and occasional writer for DailyMTG, as well as a longtime Magic player with a fondness for giant green creatures. She especially loves Commander, despite having the patience of a fruit fly, and might glare at you if your turn takes too long. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on October 6, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by the 2017 *Magic* World Championship!


Today's Must
============


1. [The 2017 World Championship Starts Today](https://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/2017wc)

Iiiiiit's time! 24 of the world's top *Magic* players have gathered in Boston to compete for their share of a $300,000 prize pool, and the thrilling, nail-biting, card-tapping action is already under way! A new set and a still-fresh Standard format make this World Championship even more exciting than usual. Tune in at [twitch.tv/magic](https://go.twitch.tv/magic) now through Sunday to catch it all live.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [The Deadliest Uses for Hostage Taker](http://www.toplevelpodcast.com/hostage-taker/) | Top Level Podcast | Mike Flores and Patrick Chapin

[Hostage Taker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hostage+Taker) has quickly become the most talked-about Pirate on the high seas, and the Top Level guys take a look at its power in the current Standard format (where it conveniently shares colors with [The Scarab God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God)). They focus particularly on a Sultai brew that brings in [Blossoming Defense](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blossoming+Defense) to protect the [Hostage Taker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hostage+Taker), all but guaranteeing that you'll get to untap with it in play, for the bargain-basement price of one green mana.


1. [Odds of Victory for All 24 *Magic* World Championship Competitors](https://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2017/10/odds-for-magic-world-championship/) | Hipsters of the Coast | Rich Stein

Rich Stein weighs in with his take on each individual Worlds competitor's odds of victory. He's done his research, and the calculations are based on each player's specific strengths and history—but the great thing about *Magic* is that *literally anything can happen*. (Hey, you can't be totally sure a player won't turn into an actual dragon, okay?)


1. [An Introduction to *Magic: The Gathering*](https://www.obilisk.co/magic-gathering-introduction/) | Obilisk | Heather Tinsley

Have you ever wished it were easier to get friends into *Magic*? Not quite sure where to start selling them on such a big game even though you love it? Well, you might want to try sending them this article. Heather is a fairly new *Magic* player, and she breaks down the game from the perspective of someone who hasn't been playing since "Mono Artifact" was a card type. Her enthusiasm is infectious, and your friends might just get a taste of how much fun they could be having!


1. [Missed-alan](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/35953_Missed-alan.html) | StarCityGames.com | Matt Higgs

While *Ixalan* cards haven't drastically altered the landscape of Standard yet, Matt thinks there are some cards and decks that are poised to break into the format's highest levels. [Vona, Butcher of Magan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vona%2C+Butcher+of+Magan) is one such card, and as he points out, although you do have to pay a hefty 7 life for it, its ability to "tap and kill planeswalkers" is worth paying serious attention to.


1. [Week 1 of *Ixalan* Standard](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/week-1-of-ixalan-standard/) | ChannelFireball | Josh Silvestri

With a week of the new Standard format behind us and the World Championship kicking into gear, it's a great time to survey the metagame and speculate wildly (or in Josh's case, educatedly) about what decks will come out on top. One takeaway: between the continued pressure of Ramunap Red and the barrage of two-drops from Sultai Energy, the format looks fast. Control decks need to be ready with answers to the early threats, or they risk being run over.


1. [Under the Sea](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/carlosgutierrez-10062017-under-the-sea/) | Gathering Magic | Carlos Gutierrez

The song is already stuck in your head, isn't it? Anyway, Carlos examines the likelihood of a Merfolk tribal deck being a viable threat in Standard, and he's got me convinced that it's possible. Good tempo, a curve of evasive creatures, and a top end of [Skysovereign, Consul Flagship](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skysovereign%2C+Consul+Flagship) or [Tishana, Voice of Thunder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tishana%2C+Voice+of+Thunder) sounds like a pretty solid game plan!

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [JoseCabezas's Esper Reanimator](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-10-02)

JoseCabezas's 5-0 list is particularly remarkable for its lack of a single copy of [The Scarab God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God) in the main deck. Also interesting is the inclusion of [Vona, Butcher of Magan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vona%2C+Butcher+of+Magan)—apparently Vona's lifelink plus [Angel of Invention](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Invention) and [Gate to the Afterlife](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gate+to+the+Afterlife) equates to enough life gain to make the cost payable. Plus, bringing back [Angel of Invention](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Invention) as a 4/4 with [God-Pharaoh's Gift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=God-Pharaoh%27s+Gift) feels like a pretty sweet deal.
