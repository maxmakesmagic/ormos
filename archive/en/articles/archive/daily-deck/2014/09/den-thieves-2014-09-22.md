Den of Thieves
==============



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on September 22, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 






[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Dack%20Fayden)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dack%20Fayden)
[Dack Fayden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dack+Fayden) is the greatest thief in the Multiverse, but Vintage enthusiast Rich Shay wanted some additional thievery for his latest Vintage concoction. Enter [Notion Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Notion+Thief). With [Dack Fayden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dack+Fayden) and [Notion Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Notion+Thief) in play, you can use Dack's +1 ability to force the opponent to draw two cards and discard two cards. Unfortunately for the opponent, his or her draws will be stolen by [Notion Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Notion+Thief) and will still be forced to discard two cards. That type of card advantage is tough to come back from!


[Dack Fayden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dack+Fayden) works in Vintage primarily because of how many powerful artifacts there are. [Time Vault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Vault) and [Voltaic Key](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key) are in many decks, while a [Mishra's Workshop](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Workshop) deck is almost 100% artifacts. Finally, [Tinker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) is around to bring out some heavy-hitting artifacts such as [Blightsteel Colossus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blightsteel+Colossus). The rest of the deck is full of interactive Vintage staples such as [Force of Will](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will) and [Mental Misstep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mental+Misstep), as well as some of the restricted cards that help define Vintage: [Ancestral Recall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancestral+Recall), [Yawgmoth's Will](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yawgmoth%27s+Will), and [Tinker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker).
