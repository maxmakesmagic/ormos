Looking for a use for [Nyxathid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nyxathid)? Look no further! Alongside [Rotting Rats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotting+Rats) and [Blightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blightning) the 7/7 shines in this *Shards of Alara* Block Constructed deck from Yliajo. It recently managed a Top 8 in a Premier Event on **Magic Online**, beating down opponents by keeping their hand size reduced and pounding through the ground with the aforementioned [Nyxathid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nyxathid). What does *Alara Reborn* add to the deck? Find out by picking up the latest set from your favorite TCG!
