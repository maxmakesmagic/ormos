Video Feature: Announcing Modern Masters
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 20, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_65.png)
By Rich Hagon and Aaron Forsythe











Senior Director of Magic R&D Aaron Forsythe announces Modern Masters, a card set coming out on June 7, 2013 featuring reprinted cards from *Eighth Edition*  up to *Alara Reborn*. More information will be available Monday, Oct. 22 on DailyMTG.com.
