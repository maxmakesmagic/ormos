2018 World Magic Cup
--------------------


Deux fois c'est bien
====================




Team France—Jean-Emmanuel Depraz, Arnaud Hocquemiller, and Timothée Jammot—have won the 2018 World *Magic* Cup to win France its second title after five years!

 



Dec 14-16, 2018


Fira Barcelona - Montjuic



Jean-Emmanuel Depraz, Arnaud Hocquemiller, and Timothée Jammot
