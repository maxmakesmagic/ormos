Deck of the Day
===============


1. Scion of the Ur-Dragon

Today is Dragon Day, so why not a Dragon Deck? Created by a Tappedout user [going by the name Kevinpetsca](http://tappedout.net/users/Kevinpetsca/), this [Scion of the Ur-Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scion+of+the+Ur-Dragon) deck looks to use its commander as a toolbox to Dragon all over people. It's also capable of some pretty quick kills, often using [Moltensteel Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moltensteel+Dragon) to pump it sky-high, and then [Skithiryx, the Blight Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skithiryx%2C+the+Blight+Dragon), or it can play controlling with any number of dangerous dragons. If Dragons get you going, this is a good place to start getting ideas for upgrading your Dragon *Commander 2017* deck.
