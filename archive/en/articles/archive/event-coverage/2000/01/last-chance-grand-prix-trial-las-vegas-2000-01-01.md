Last Chance Grand Prix Trial for Las Vegas
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_508.png)
By Jon Chabot











An astonishing number of players turned up to grab the all-important 3 byes offered for GP LV; 137, making it the 2nd largest twilight gathering in North American history. Starting at 7 PM, the 8 round Trial's main rounds carried through to the early morning hours, before beginning the Top 8 at nearly 5 AM. 


The bleary eyed competitors carried a wide range of archetypes into the Top 8. While Trix carried 3 players to the Top 8, the discard packing Kix and Dark Force, plus the Walamie's style control deck from PT New Orleans fame, carried their players to 3 Byes. Trix, the unsinkable monster, carried Brian Faires to the 4th set of Byes, and seemed to again put up the best overall numbers on the day.
