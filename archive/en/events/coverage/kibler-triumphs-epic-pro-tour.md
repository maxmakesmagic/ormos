Move over, Dragonmaster…Brian Kibler may need to be known as the Huntmaster now. The Pro Tour Hall of Famer put [Huntmaster of the Fells](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells+)  to exceptional use in his Wolf Run Ramp Standard deck at Pro Tour *Dark Ascension* , defeating Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa in a five-game final. Kibler got to the final with an astounding semifinal victory over the legendary Jon Finkel in what will go down as one of the greatest matches in **Magic** history. Not to be lost amid this memorable Sunday is the accomplishment of Raphael Levy, who tied Kai Budde for the all-time lead in career Pro Points at 500.


In a comeback to the game that started with a Top 8 at Pro Tour Honolulu in 2009, Kibler joins an elite group of two-time Pro Tour champions. Congratulations to the new Huntmaster, Brian Kibler.
