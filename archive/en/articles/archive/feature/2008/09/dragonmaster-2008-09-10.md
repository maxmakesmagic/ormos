In celebration of the impending release of *Shards of Alara,* Daily Decks looks at past creations that combined the colors of **Magic** into those of the shards found in Alara. Representing Naya is the world famous Dragonmaster, Brian Kibler himself. At Pro Tourâ€“Chicago in 2000, Brian used his red-green-white deck, including double copies of [Rith, the Awakener](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rith%2C+the+Awakener), to make it all the way to the semifinals. The iconic Dragon forever tied Brian to the Dragon tribe. The Top 8 in Chicago was also one of the toughest the Pro Tour has ever seen featuring Jon Finkel, Kamiel Cornelissen, Kai Budde, Robert Dougherty, Michael Pustilnik, Zvi Mowshowitz, Jay Elarar, and of course Kibler himself.
