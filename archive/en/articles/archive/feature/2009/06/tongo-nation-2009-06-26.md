A surprise hit at Pro Tour-Chicago in 1997, “Tongo Nation” was a mid-range control deck featuring the best spells from blue, white, and red. The deck put two players into the Top 8 and put David Mills into the finals of the Pro Tour. He controlled his opponent’s game plans with cards such as [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt+)  and [Swords to Plowshares](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swords+to+Plowshares+)  before finishing with a huge [Fireball](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball+)  or [Earthquake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake+)  or repeated attacks from the hard-to-kill [Frenetic Efreet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frenetic+Efreet).
