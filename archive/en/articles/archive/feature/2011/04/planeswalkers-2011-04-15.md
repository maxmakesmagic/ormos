Yesterday we highlighted an Extended take on blue-black-red control. Today it's time for the Standard version. Alex Muhich loves planeswalkers and wanted to play as many as he could at Grand Prix Dallas-Fort Worth. His control strategy enabled him to do exactly that, starring [Koth of the Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Koth+of+the+Hammer); [Tezzeret, Agent of Bolas](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret%2C+Agent+of+Bolas); and [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor). The build was good enough to make the second day of competition! For more on Grand Prix Dallas-Fort Worth, check out the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/shiels-stands-alone-dallas).
