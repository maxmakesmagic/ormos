**Age:** 24  
**Hometown:** Porto Alegre  
**Occupation:** Student/player


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:**  

30


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour *Dark Ascension*:**  

Pro points


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:**  

11(?) / 9


**Other previous Magic accomplishments:**  

I have the most lifetime pro points out of anyone not in the Hall of Fame.


**What was your record by format?**


**Standard:**  

9-1


**Booster Draft:**  

4-2


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour *Dark Ascension*?  

Who did you prepare with?**  

We rented a house for the entire ChannelFireball team (same as Kibler).


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**  

Zero


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

Wolf Run Ramp. We were expecting Humans and random decks and this is good against those.


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic, or if you don't go to FNM anymore, where did you play?**  

I used to play at our local store Jombõ.


**Did you spend time in Hawaii prior to the Pro Tour?  

If so, what did you do?**  

I got here two weeks ago, but we've mostly been testing. I'm staying the week after to go scuba diving, and to visit the volcano on the island.
