Modern Decklist Blowout
=======================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on June 1, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_davidwhitfield.jpg)
By David Whitfield




 David works on the Magic community team as a content specialist. He spends his days writing about Magic Online and trying to play too many colors at once in Limited. 







Modern Masters Weekend came to an end on Sunday, May 31, [*Modern Masters 2015 Edition* events](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04#events) are going on now on *Magic Online*, and [Preliminary Qualifiers](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11#ModernPrelims) for the [Modern Festival](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11) are coming up on June 24. With all of the excitement around Modern, and a grand prize of one copy of every [Modern-legal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&format=+%5B%22Modern%22%5D) set for triumphing in the [Modern Festival Finals](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11#ModernFinals), it's a great time to learn the Modern ropes if you haven't already.


But how does one begin to play Modern? How do you help Sally Standard and Larry Limited get into this exciting format? Let's ask the experts!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mtgo/6-1-2015_img2.png)


[Conclave Phalanx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conclave+Phalanx) | Art by [Wayne Reynolds](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Wayne%20Reynolds%22%5D)


Follow the links below to see expertly crafted Modern decks by some of the best *Magic* players out there. Decklists include the Modern decks used by the Top 4 2014 *Magic Online* Championship competitors and the winning deck from Standard Super League's special [Modern week](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/standard-super-league-episode-4-recap-2015-05-26). We also have decklists from the top five players over the past several weeks in eight-player [Modern 5-3-2-2](#modern5322) single-elimination queues. Remember, you can download all of these decklists by clicking this icon:


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mtgo/6-1-2015_img1.png)


Then [import](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/importing-and-creating-decks) them directly into *Magic Online*!

Put a few of these through their paces in the special [Modern Constructed](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-masters-2015-edition-event-schedule-2015-05-04#prizes) events that begin with the Wednesday, June 3, downtime. Get your practice in—you'll need it for the [Modern Festival](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-modern-festival-2015-05-11).


These decks are a great place to start with Modern and, once you get a feel for the format, there are 9,081 [Modern-legal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&format=+%5B%22Modern%22%5D) cards for you to experiment with, so get to playing!
