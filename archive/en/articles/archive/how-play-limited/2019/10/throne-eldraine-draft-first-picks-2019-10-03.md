Throne of Eldraine Draft First Picks
====================================



 Posted in **How to Play Limited**
 on October 3, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_2016_LSV.jpg)
By Luis Scott-Vargas 




Luis Scott-Vargas plays, writes, and makes videos about Magic. He has played on the Pro Tour for almost a decade, and between that and producing content for ChannelFireball, often has his hands full (of cards). 






Welcome back to Pack 1, Pick 1: *Throne of Eldraine* edition! Today, we are going to look at the cards you want to see staring back at you when you crack your first pack. As with any pick-order list, after the first couple picks, the cards you're looking for change, so this will be most useful as a guide to kick you off. Let's get to it!

At this point I'm grumbling to myself a little, but I'll still take one of these cards and likely add it to my deck. These are among the better commons, but not quite the best, and are below-average first picks. Still, you'll have to take cards from this list plenty of times, so don't be too bummed out when it happens.


This format is awesome, and I've been having a blast so far. There is a lot of cool stuff going on, and underneath all of it is some incredible flavor. I'm actually a big flavor fan, both in my food and my cards, so it's really neat to see a set tying both of those together. Enjoy *Throne of Eldraine*, and I hope you open cards from the top of the article rather than the bottom!
