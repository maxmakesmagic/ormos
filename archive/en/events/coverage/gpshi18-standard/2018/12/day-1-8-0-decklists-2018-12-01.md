Day 1 8-0 Decklists
===================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 2, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











A total of 1960 players arrived in Shizuoka to battle it out in Standard. However, only 13 players managed the coveted 8-0 score!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpshi18/gpShizuoka2018_S_Day1_UndefeatedBIG.jpg)


They were Kosuke Nakamoto, Toshiki Okai, Liu Xuan, Kazuhiro Noine, Masayasu Tanahashi, Ryosuke Urase, Uta Suzuki, Nobuaki Azuma, Taiga Tsujikawa, Syuusuke Yamada, Hiroaki Nuruki, Shimazu Yoshiko, and Takuya Natsume!


From the looks of it, Selesnya Tokens performed the best so far, allowing five players to steamroll through Day 1's competition. Boros Aggro, Golgari Midrange, Izzet Drakes, and Jeskai Control were also represented! Let's take a look at all 13 decklists!
