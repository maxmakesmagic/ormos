Grand Prix Naples Top 8 Profiles
================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 7, 2002 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff











### Raphaël Levy

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpnap02/a893.jpg)**Age:** 20

**Occupation:** Student

**Where do you live?** Toulouse

**Since when do you play Magic?** Since 8 years

**Best accomplishments:** Beating Antoine Ruel three times in a Pro tour this year J

**What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Naples; why do you think the players that stayed at home were wrong?**  

Grand Prix events are still fun! Come on, this is the last one in the season!

**What do you think is the main reason for you to make Top 8 in this tournament and why?**  

Kai shuffled my deck during the deciding match.

**What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

... A 7th win for Kai would be insane... but what can we do?

**Pasta or pizza?**  

Pizza... definitely!

**Anything else you'd like to share with the Sideboard Online readers?**  

Oh, you really want me to share this one pizza?

### Olivier Ruel

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpnap02/a890.jpg)**Age:** 20

**Occupation:** Student

**Where do you live?** Aubervilliers, France

**Since when do you play Magic?** 4th Edition

**Best accomplishments:** I beat Ryan Fuller once

**What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Naples; why do you think the players that stayed at home were wrong?**  

Pro Points and money. Because if you don't go, you can miss the Masters again, Farid.

**What do you think is the main reason for you to make Top 8 in this tournament and why?**  

I'd say, triple Mongrel, triple Rootwalla on my last draft. Because my neighbours passed them to me.

**What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A German or a French will win. Or maybe a Dutch...

**Pasta or pizza?**  

It depends, pizza salmon or pineapple would be great.

**Anything else you'd like to share with the Sideboard Online readers?**  

My luck, in the last few months, I've had enough for me and all the people who will read this.
