The June 28, 2017 Update
========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on June 28, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Nicholas_Wolfram.jpg)
By Nicholas Wolfram




 Nicholas Wolfram fell promptly in love with tabletop and video games when he was only two years old, and he has been writing about them since he figured out how words worked. Now he puts together words about Magic, which he started playing way back in 2000. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on June 28, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by Blake Rasmussen, who will be filling in for me on the Daily *Magic* Update starting tomorrow. Thanks, Blake!

After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [Green-White Cats with *Hour of Devastation*](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/gw-cats-with-hour-of-devastation/) | Channel Fireball | Frank Karsten

Kitties! Leave it to an Egyptian-themed set to enable Cat tribal for Standard.


1. [Rhystic Study: Unesh, Criosphinx Sovereign](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/prophet-of-kruphix/rhystic-study-unesh-criosphinx-sovereign) | MTG Mint Card | Prophet of Kruphix

The *Magic* world finally has a Sphinx lord, and Commander brewers are brewing. Other than jamming the deck full of Sphinxes, what should be happening in an Unesh deck? The Prophet has some thoughts.


1. [Nicky B's Hour of D](http://www.magictheamateuring.com/mta-episode-231-nicky-bs-hour-of-d/) | Magic the Amateuring | Meghan Wolff and Maria Bartholdi

Maria and Meghan have the DL on *Hour of Devastation*, including thoughts on the story and the cards we've previewed so far.


1. [With Marvel Out of the Picture, Tons of New Decks Are Cropping Up in Standard](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/with-marvel-out-of-the-picture-tons-of-new-decks-are-cropping-up-in-standard/) | Channel Fireball | Pascal Maynard

Even without *Hour of Devastation*, Standard is seeing some serious shakeups. I look forward to seeing the craziness that hits the format once the new set drops!
