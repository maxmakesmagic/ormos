Decks of the Week: 5/20/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on May 20, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











If you're looking for a top Standard deck, Decks of the Week is the place for you. National Qualifiers took place in North America last weekend and more than 40 locations have submitted their Top 8 decks. You still have time to find the right Standard deck for you to take to [*Rise of the Eldrazi* Game Day](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/gameday-facts) or [Grand Prix–Washington D.C.](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/grandprix/washingtondc10) this weekend, so get clicking!
