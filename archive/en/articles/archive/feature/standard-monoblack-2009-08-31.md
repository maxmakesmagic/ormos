The 2009 **Magic** Game Day has come and gone, and deck lists from all over the world have poured in as the event marked one of the most successful such efforts in history. From Saint-Nazaire, France we have a mono-black deck played by a player who listed his name only as "Kevin." [Black Knight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Knight) made its debut in a Top 8 after being reintroduced to the world of Standard by way of ***Magic** 2010*. On top of that, there's a mini-Vampire theme, with [Child of Night](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Child+of+Night) and [Vampire Aristocrat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampire+Aristocrat) being lorded over by [Vampire Nocturnus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampire+Nocturnus). If you're a fan of the skull, Kevin's deck is perfect for your next Standard event!
