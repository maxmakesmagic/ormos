Know Your Flavor: *Magic 2011*
==============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 25, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Tom Jenkot












How well have you been paying attention?


Some reprinted cards in ***Magic** 2011* received new flavor text. Some cards lost their flavor text. Some cards are completely new.


***Magic** 2011* has been out for a few months, so you've had plenty of time to memorize all of the flavor text, right? Perfect, because we have a quiz.












How did you do?
