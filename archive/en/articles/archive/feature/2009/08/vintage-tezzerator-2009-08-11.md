Vintage is that unique format that allows players to play with nearly every card that's ever been fit to print in the Multiverse. David Ochoa, a **Magic** writer and pro, took the opportunity to try his hand at the format during a recent Public Event held at the 2009 U.S. National Championships. His deck of choice? A control deck based on [Tezzeret the Seeker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret+the+Seeker) that powered him all the way to the finals.
