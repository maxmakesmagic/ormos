![Quenchable Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Quenchable+Fire)Burn decks have been around since the beginning of **Magic**, and at the [2009 World Championships](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12) they reared their heads yet again. Championing the fiery red spells was David Howard, who managed to go 5-1 with his take on the archetype. One of the biggest innovations in his deck? Using [Quenchable Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quenchable+Fire). With few players playing lands that could produce blue mana, David got free burn from the *Conflux* card almost every time!
