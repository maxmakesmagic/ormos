Undefeated Decklists
====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX HONG KONG 2015](/en/events/coverage/gphk15)
 on August 8, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim










851 players have been reduced down to 110, leaving only three players at the perfect 9-0 record.


Yam Wing Chun, Chye Yian Hsiang and Victoriano Lim have all done it with the help of Mountains, an indication that red might be the mightiest color of them all.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Mountain)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain)
Grand Prix Washington D.C. Top 8 competitor, Yam Wing Chun achieved the home run with the help of [Ghirapur Gearcrafter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghirapur+Gearcrafter)s and [Thopter Engineer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thopter+Engineer), in combination with haymakers such as [Blazing Hellhound](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blazing+Hellhound) and [Liliana, Heretical Healer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%2C+Heretical+Healer).


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Blazing+Hellhound)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blazing+Hellhound)[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Liliana%2C+Heretical+Healer)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%2C+Heretical+Healer)[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Liliana%2C+Defiant+Necromancer)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%2C+Defiant+Necromancer)Transform
Chye Yian Hsiang and Victoriano Lim both opted to support their powerful red cards with white, playing with "bread and butter" such as [Akroan Jailer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Jailer), [Grasp of the Hieromancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grasp+of+the+Hieromancer) and [Suppression](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Suppression) Bonds. Both players also interestingly had the same rares, [Pia and Kiran Nalaar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pia+and+Kiran+Nalaar) as well as [Kytheon's Irregulars](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kytheon%27s+Irregulars)!


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Pia+and+Kiran+Nalaar)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pia+and+Kiran+Nalaar)[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Kytheon%27s+Irregulars)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kytheon%27s+Irregulars)
Good job guys!
