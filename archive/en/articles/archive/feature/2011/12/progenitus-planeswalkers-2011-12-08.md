Progenitus Planeswalkers
========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 8, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











It's no secret that R&D member Ken Nagle loves Commander. He has a deck for almost everything—even planeswalkers! If you've ever wanted to cast [Creeping Renaissance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Creeping+Renaissance) naming planeswalker and return seven of them to your hand, this planeswalker-themed deck (using [Progenitus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Progenitus) as the Commander) is the deck for you! [Doubling Season](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doubling+Season) doubles the counters planeswalkers get on the way in, allowing most of them to use their "ultimate" immediately, and [Rings of Brighthearth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rings+of+Brighthearth) works overtime letting you get two activations of out of each planeswalker each turn. Ken's deck contains a whopping twenty-three planeswalkers. Can you figure out which ones are missing?
