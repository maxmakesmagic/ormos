### DAILY DECK


Australian WMCQ Winning Deck
============================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)

Blake Rasmussen

Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever.


August 19, 2014
 







World *Magic* Cup Qualifiers happened all over the world this past weekend. Justin Robb took one of the breakout decks of Pro Tour *Magic 2015*, Jund Planeswalkers, and won the Australian WMCQ with his take on the archetype. Congratulations to Justin! See the full schedule of World *Magic* Cup Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/wmcq/schedule).
