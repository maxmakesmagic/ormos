The 2011 StarCityGames.com Standard Open season has begun! The first stop was in Kansas City, and Pat McGregor managed to win the Saturday event. He is a repeat champion, having also won the Legacy Open on the debut of the 2010 season in St. Louis. His Boros deck represents the best of the Standard aggro world, and he dispatched Pro Tour Top 8 competitor Conley Woods in the Finals.
