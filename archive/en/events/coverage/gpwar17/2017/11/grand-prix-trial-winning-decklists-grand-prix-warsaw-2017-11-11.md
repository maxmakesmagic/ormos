Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists of Grand Prix Warsaw 2017
============================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 11, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











The main event only started on Saturday morning, but the action already began on Friday. Naturally, we were most interested in the Last-Chance Trials run that day, as those actually influenced the Grand Prix itself, by awarding byes to all 5-0 players. Plus, their decklists could provide a first idea of what to expect over the course of the weekend. Which was: a lot of energy decks, a lot of Ramunap Red, but also some surprising strategies succeeded, like Black-Red Aggro or a creature deck based in blue and black!
