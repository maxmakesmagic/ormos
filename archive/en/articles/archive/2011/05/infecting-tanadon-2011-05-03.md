Infecting the Tanadon
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 3, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












The *New Phyrexia* card [Thundering Tanadon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thundering+Tanadon) shows a Mirran beast that has been fully Phyrexian-ized:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/702_thunderingtanadon.jpg)[Thundering Tanadon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thundering+Tanadon) | Art by [Dan Scott](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Dan+Scott%22%5D)

Click "Previous" and "Next" to step through the process. It all coheres!
