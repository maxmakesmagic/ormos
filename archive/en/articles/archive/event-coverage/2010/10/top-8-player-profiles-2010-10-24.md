Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 24, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff












![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gptor10/top8-pat%20cox.jpg)*Pat Cox*



**Name**: Pat Cox


**Age**: 26


**Occupation**: Mechanical Engineer


**Hometown**: Melbourne, Florida


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Top 8 at GP Oakland.


**Byes This Weekend**: 2


**How did you prepare for this event**: Did a bunch of drafts at the Hollands' when the set came out.


**What were your Sealed Deck all-stars**: [Contagion Engine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contagion+Engine)


**What card performed better than expected in Sealed**: [Wall of Tanglecord](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Tanglecord)


**What was your record Day 1**: 8-1-1


**What were your results for your two drafts**: 2-1, 3-0


**What was your plan for draft**: Anything but poison, because I don't know how to draft it.


**What was your most difficult draft pick this weekend**: [Engulfing Slagwurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engulfing+Slagwurm) vs [Volition Reins](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volition+Reins), and [Skithiryx, the Blight Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skithiryx%2C+the+Blight+Dragon) vs [Volition Reins](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volition+Reins). Both in this Top 8. Rough life.





---

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gptor10/top8-stephen%20zhang.jpg)*Stephen Zhang*



**Name**: Stephen Zhang


**Age**: 23


**Occupation**: Corporate Finance


**Hometown**: Wilbraham, Massachusettes


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Day 2 at the Two-Headed-Giant GP. A few PTQ Top 8's.


**Byes This Weekend**: 0


**How did you prepare for this event**: A few practice sealeds with friends.


**What were your Sealed Deck all-stars**: [Sunblast Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunblast+Angel), [Koth of the Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Koth+of+the+Hammer).


**What card performed better than expected in Sealed**: [Tumble Magnet](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tumble+Magnet)


**What was your record Day 1**: 8-2


**What were your results for your two drafts**: 3-0, 2-0-1


**What was your plan for draft**: I have almost no experience with the draft format, just a few team drafts with friends and an 8-man. I took the best cards for my colors and moved on.


**What was your most difficult draft pick this weekend**: [Shatter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shatter) vs [Spikeshot Elder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spikeshot+Elder)

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gptor10/top8-ben%20stark.jpg)*Ben Stark*



**Name**: Ben Stark


**Age**: 27


**Occupation**: Poker Player


**Hometown**: Fort Lauderdale, Florida


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: GP Boston 2007 Finalist. 4 GP Top 8s, 2 PT Top 8s.


**Byes This Weekend**: 3


**How did you prepare for this event**: Drafted a little in Florida and talked about the format with The [Channel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Channel)[Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) guys.


**What were your Sealed Deck all-stars**: [Myr Battlesphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Battlesphere)


**What card performed better than expected in Sealed**: [Moriok Replica](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moriok+Replica)


**What was your record Day 1**: 9-1


**What were your results for your two drafts**: 2-1, 2-1


**What was your plan for draft**: Either poison or Metalcraft


**What was your most difficult draft pick this weekend**: [Chrome Steed](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Steed) or [Plague Stinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plague+Stinger)

Twice I took [Scrap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrap)melter, and twice I think I was wrong.

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gptor10/top8-david%20howard.jpg)*David Howard*



**Name**: David Howard


**Age**: 25


**Occupation**: Student


**Hometown**: Jacksonville, Florida


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: 11th GP Boston, 7th GP DC teams, 9th or 11th GP Pittsburgh Teams. 2000 limited on *Magic* Online three times.


**Byes This Weekend**: 2


**How did you prepare for this event**: Played the sealed 5K in Boston.


**What were your Sealed Deck all-stars**: [Indomitable Archangel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Indomitable+Archangel), [Ezuri's Brigade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ezuri%27s+Brigade), [Engulfing Slagwurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engulfing+Slagwurm), [True Conviction](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=True+Conviction), [Contagion Engine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contagion+Engine).


**What card performed better than expected in Sealed**: [Soul Parry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Parry)


**What was your record Day 1**: 8-2


**What were your results for your two drafts**: 3-0, 2-0-1


**What was your plan for draft**: I prefer Red/x but draft what comes.


**What was your most difficult draft pick this weekend**: Pick one, Pack one, Kemba vs [Contagion Clasp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contagion+Clasp). I took Kemba.

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gptor10/top8-dustin%20faeder.jpg)*Dustin Faeder*



**Name**: Dustin Faeder


**Age**: 27


**Occupation**: Law Student / Philosophy Teacher


**Hometown**: Knoxville, Tennessee


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: None


**Byes This Weekend**: 1


**How did you prepare for this event**: 2 drafts at my local store, Prerelease and Release weekends, 2 *Magic* Online Release Sealeds, and three grinders.


**What were your Sealed Deck all-stars**: [Elspeth Tirel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth+Tirel), [Razor Hippogriff](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Razor+Hippogriff), [Spikeshot Elder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spikeshot+Elder), [Strata Scythe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strata+Scythe), [Precursor Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Precursor+Golem)... and [Sunspear Shikari](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunspear+Shikari)!


**What card performed better than expected in Sealed**: [Sylvok Lifestaff](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvok+Lifestaff)


**What was your record Day 1**: 9-1


**What were your results for your two drafts**: 3-0, 1-1-1


**What was your plan for draft**: Find the stream.


**What was your most difficult draft pick this weekend**: Took [Accorder's Shield](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accorder%27s+Shield) over Ezuri. Also [Painsmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Painsmith) vs. mana Myr.

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gptor10/top8-eric%20froehlich.jpg)*Eric Froehlich*



**Name**: Eric Froehlich


**Age**: 26


**Occupation**: Poker Player


**Hometown**: Las Vegas, Nevada


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: PT San Diego Top 8, US Nationals 2010 Top 8. This is my 6th GP Top 8.


**Byes This Weekend**: 3


**How did you prepare for this event**: Panorama drafts featuring David Williams, Brock Parker and Scott Seirer.


**What were your Sealed Deck all-stars**: [Hoard-Smelter Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hoard-Smelter+Dragon) and [Arrest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrest)


**What card performed better than expected in Sealed**: Everything was expected.


**What was your record Day 1**: 9-1


**What were your results for your two drafts**: 3-0, 1-0-2


**What was your plan for draft**: Go infect if it was there.


**What was your most difficult draft pick this weekend**: The packs were so bad for me that most of my picks were easy. The third pick of each draft when choosing to stay metalcraft or to go infect is the toughest.

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gptor10/top8-jon%20smithers.jpg)*Jon Smithers*



**Name**: Jon Smithers


**Age**: 23


**Occupation**: Student


**Hometown**: Ottawa, Ontario


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Meh.


**Byes This Weekend**: 1


**How did you prepare for this event**: Too many Limited PTQ Top 4 exits (3) in the past few months.


**What were your Sealed Deck all-stars**: [Molten-Tail Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten-Tail+Masticore), [Tempered Steel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tempered+Steel), [Steel Hellkite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steel+Hellkite), [Kuldotha Phoenix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kuldotha+Phoenix)... just to name a few.


**What card performed better than expected in Sealed**: [Ferrovore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ferrovore) and [Origin Spellbomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Origin+Spellbomb).


**What was your record Day 1**: 10-0


**What were your results for your two drafts**: 2-1 with Black-White, 1-0-2 with Red-Blue.


**What was your plan for draft**: Stay open, always. Do not force Infect.


**What was your most difficult draft pick this weekend**: Not sure. It was hard to read the signals when trying to decide on a second color.
