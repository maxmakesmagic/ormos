Invited Players
---------------


#### Last Updated: September 19, 2018


[Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica* Event Information](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/premierplay/protour/ptgrn)


Invitations to Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica* are awarded in the following manner:


* The top finishing players from each Regional Pro Tour Qualifier that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*.
* The top finishing players from each *Magic Online* Regional Pro Tour Qualifier that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*.
* The winner of each *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifier Final that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*.
* The Top 2 finishers of each *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) Quarterly Playoff tournament that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*.
* The winner of each *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) Open tournament that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*.
* Players that finished with 30 or more match points at Pro Tour 25th Anniversary.
* The Top eight (8) finishing players plus any other players that finished with 39 or more match points from each individual-format Grand Prix that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*.
* Team members from the top four (4) finishing teams plus any team members from a team that finished with 36 or more match points from each team-format Grand Prix that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*.
* The winner of each Grand Prix Sunday side event Pro Tour Qualifier that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*.
* The team members from the top eight (8) finishing teams in the 2017-2018 Pro Tour Team Series Leaderboard.
* Players with a Pro Tour Players Club Silver, Gold or Platinum level as of the start date of Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica*.
* Players that are members of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame.

Note: The Pro Tour invitation criteria listed above do not generate a pass down of invitations to other players.


If you believe that your finish at a Regional Pro Tour Qualifier or Grand Prix is in error, please visit <http://www.wizards.com/Magic/PlaneswalkerPoints>. After logging in, click the "Appeals Form" link at the bottom of the page and fill out the form.
