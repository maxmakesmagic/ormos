Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 31, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











Learn more about Grand Prix Nagoya's Top 8 competitors by taking a look through their profiles!




---


### Shawn McNeace


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpnag16/TOP8_ShawnMcneace.jpg)


**Hometown:** Okinawa, Japan  
**Age:** 29  
**Occupation:** Information Technology


**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Day 1:** 8-1 with White-Red Aggro  
**Day 2 Draft 1:** 3-0 with Blue-Green Landfall featuring [Nissa, Voice of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa%2C+Voice+of+Zendikar)  
**Day 2 Draft 2:** 2-1 with Red-Green Aggro and many bad cards


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 10th at GP Kobe 2015


**What is your favorite card from *Oath of the Gatewatch*?**   
[Eldrazi Displacer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Displacer)! It does EVERYTHING!


**What was your happiest moment this weekend, and why?**  

My girlfriend cried in joy when she heard that I made the Top 8. She's my biggest supporter!




---


### Sean Li


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpnag16/TOP8_SeanLi.jpg)


**Hometown:** Cambridge, UK  
**Age:** 23  
**Occupation:** English Instructor


**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Day 1:** 8-1 with Jeskai, but sideboarding into Bant almost everytime  
**Day 2 Draft 1:** Blue-Green splash Red with 7 rares  
**Day 2 Draft 2:** d-Green splash Black and lucky topdecks!



**What is your favorite card from *Oath of the Gatewatch*?**   
[Baloth Null](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baloth+Null)(drifter). Too much value in one card.


**What was your happiest moment this weekend, and why?**  

Getting to play in the same tournament with many great and supportive friends! Dinner after Day 1 was also pretty super.




---


### Ryota Takeuchi


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpnag16/TOP8_RyotaTakeuchi.jpg)


**Hometown:** Shizuoka, Japan  
**Age:** 24  
**Occupation:** System Engineer


**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Day 1:** -0 with Blue-White Fliers including [Drowner of Hope](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drowner+of+Hope) and [Strip Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strip+Mine)!  
**Day 2 Draft 1:** 2-1 with White-Green “Support” Aggro  
**Day 2 Draft 2:** 2-1 with Blue-Black splash White. [Eldrazi Displacer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Displacer), [Guardian of Tazeem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Guardian+of+Tazeem), and [Kalitas, Traitor of Ghet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kalitas%2C+Traitor+of+Ghet) are pretty good


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 2nd place at the last Junior Japan Nationals


**What is your favorite card from *Oath of the Gatewatch*?**   
[Goblin Dark-Dwellers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Dark-Dwellers)


**What was your happiest moment this weekend, and why?**  

Using [Strip Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strip+Mine) to kill my opponent's colored mana source. I won the match and then Nico Nico asked me to go on camera!
