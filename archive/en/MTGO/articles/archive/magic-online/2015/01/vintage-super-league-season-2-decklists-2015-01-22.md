Vintage Super League Season 2 Decklists
=======================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on January 22, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Below are the first set of decklists for members of the Vintage Super League. Throughout the season, when players change up their decks, we'll post updated decks to [another page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-season-2-decklists) the Wednesday following the episode in which the new decks are featured. Click on player names below to bring you right to their decklists.


Watch the Vintage Super League live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic) Tuesday nights at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7).


View the current set of Vintage Super League decklists [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-season-2-decklists).


View the second set of Vintage Super League decklists [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/vintage-super-league-season-2-decklists-2015-02-25).
