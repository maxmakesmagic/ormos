Magic Online 10th Anniversary Celebration!
==========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 12, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












On June 20, 2002, Wizards of the Coast released **Magic: The Gathering Online**, creating a place where players could gather any time, day or night, and find a game of **Magic**. Ten years, 60 sets, and nearly 14,000 cards later **Magic Online** is more popular than ever. Kick off your summer and celebrate 10 years of killer drafts, awesome sealed deck pools, and epic topdecks by joining us for the **Magic Online** 10th Anniversary Celebration!


The MTGO 10th Anniversary Celebration starts after the downtime on June 20 and runs until 10:00 AM on July 5. The 10th Anniversary Celebration features the following:


* MTGO 10th Anniversary *Invasion* Block Booster Drafts
* MTGO 10th Anniversary 8-Player Constructed Queues
* 2-player Gold Constructed Queues
* Spotlight Tournaments: Pauper, Standard, Phantom *Avacyn Restored* Sealed Deck, and Legacy
* Double Magic Online Player Rewards
* Lifetime Magic Online Player Rewards

**MTGO 10TH ANNIVERSARY *INVASION* BLOCK BOOSTER DRAFTS**  

Relive the **Magic Online** launch by playing in some *Invasion* Block Booster Drafts!


*START TIMES:* On demand starting after the downtime on Wednesday, June 20, and ending on Thursday, July 5, at 10:00 AM.  
*LOCATION:*  Limited Queues room.  
*ENTRY OPTION(S):* 15 Event Tickets OR 3 Event Tickets plus product.  
*PRODUCT:* 1 *Invasion* booster pack, 1 *Planeshift* booster pack, and 1 *Apocalypse* booster pack.  
*SIZE:* 8 players.  
*DURATION:* Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, single elimination.  
*PRIZES:*




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Place** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 1st | 2 *Invasion* booster packs, 1 *Planeshift* booster pack, and 1 *Apocalypse* booster pack | 1 |
| 2nd | 1 *Invasion* booster pack, 1 *Planeshift* booster pack, and 1 *Apocalypse* booster pack | 0 |
| 3rd - 4th | 1 *Planeshift* booster pack and 1 *Apocalypse* booster pack | 0 |


 

**MTGO 10TH ANNIVERSARY 8-PLAYER CONSTRUCTED QUEUES**  

We've added an extra booster to the prizes of some of your favorite 8-player constructed queues. Look for the following 10th Anniversary queues: *Innistrad* Block, Standard, Modern, Legacy, Classic, and Pauper.


*START TIMES:* On demand starting after the downtime on Wednesday, June 20, and ending on Thursday, July 5, at 10:00 AM.  
*LOCATION:*  Constructed Queues room.  
*ENTRY OPTION(S):* 6 Event Tickets.  
*PRODUCT:* A format legal deck for the chosen queue.  
*SIZE:* 8 players.  
*DURATION:* 3 rounds, single elimination.  
*PRIZES (ALL FORMATS):*




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Place** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 1st | 6 *Avacyn Restored* booster packs | 1 |
| 2nd | 3 *Avacyn Restored* booster packs | 0 |
| 3rd - 4th | 2 *Avacyn Restored* booster packs | 0 |


 

**2-PLAYER CONSTRUCTED QUEUES**   

Want to play in a tournament, but don't have a lot of time? The 2-player queues are perfect for you, and with the Gold queues you can win five booster packs per match!


*START TIMES:* On demand starting after the downtime on Wednesday, June 20, and ending on Thursday, July 5, at 10:00 AM.  
*LOCATION:*  Constructed Queues room.  
*ENTRY OPTION(S):* 10 Event Tickets.  
*PRODUCT:* A format legal deck for the chosen queue.  
*SIZE:* 2 players.  
*DURATION:* 1 round, single elimination.  
*PRIZES (ALL FORMATS):*




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Place** | **Prizes** |
| 1st | 5 *Avacyn Restored* booster packs |


 

**SPOTLIGHT TOURNAMENTS: PAUPER, STANDARD, PHANTOM *AVACYN RESTORED* SEALED DECK, AND LEGACY**  

Play in one of several spotlight tournaments that only require 5 Event Tickets to enter!


*START TIMES:*


* Pauper: Saturday, June 23, at 10:00 AM.
* Standard: Sunday, June 24, at 2:00 PM.
* Phantom *Avacyn Restored* Sealed Deck: Saturday, June 30, at 9:00 AM.
* Legacy: Sunday, July 1, at 11:00 AM.

*LOCATION:* Premier Events room.  
*ENTRY OPTION(S):* 5 Event Tickets.  
*PRODUCT:* Format legal deck for constructed events. 6 *Avacyn Restored* boosters for the Phantom Sealed Deck event.  
*SIZE:* 33-512 players.  
*DURATION:* Number of [Swiss pairing rounds determined by attendance](http://wizards.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/465/). The "Top 8" will be 3 rounds, single elimination.  
*PRIZES:*

| 1st | 33 *Avacyn Restored* booster packs | 6 |

| 5th - 8th | 6 *Avacyn Restored* booster packs | 3 |
| 9th - 16th | 3 *Avacyn Restored* booster packs | 0 |


 

**DOUBLE MAGIC ONLINE PLAYER REWARDS**  

Starting after the downtime on June 20, players will receive double the **Magic Online** Player Rewards points for each qualifying event they play in. The double point period ends at 10:00 AM on Thursday, July 5, so all 10th Anniversary events that start before that deadline earn you double rewards points!


**LIFETIME MAGIC ONLINE PLAYER REWARDS**  

As part of the **Magic Online** 10th Anniversary, we will be awarding promo cards to players who have reached certain lifetime MOPR points totals. Cards will be distributed during the downtime on Wednesday, July 11, and players will receive cards for each level they have qualified for. Check out the chart below to see if you qualify:




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Lifetime MOPR Points** | **Reward** |
| 0-599 | Not qualified |
| 600-2,999 | 1 random *Unhinged* basic land card |
| 3,000-5,999 | 1 alternate art Hymn to Tourach |
| 6,000-9,999 | 1 random premium foil *Unhinged* basic land card |
| 10,000-24,999 | 1 premium foil alternate art Hymn to Tourach |
| 25,000+ | 1 random *Unhinged* basic land card, 1 random premium foil *Unhinged* basic land, 1 alternate art Hymn to Tourach, and 1 premium foil alternate art Hymn to Tourach |
