Deckbuilders of all shapes and sizes are trying to get a feel for the world of Standard now that *Scars of Mirrodin* is available for sale and legal in the format! One of the game's top deck builders, Patrick Chapin, recently presented this take on a [Koth of the Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Koth+of+the+Hammer) deck in his regular column on StarCityGames.com. The goal? Use proliferate to turbo-charge Koth and earn yourself an emblem. The deck is powerful, capable of accomplishing that very play as early as the third turn! Awesome!
