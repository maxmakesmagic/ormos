Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 20, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











Learn more about Grand Prix Denver's Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they've done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpden17/T8_rolf.jpg)**Age:** 28


**Hometown:** Omaha, Nebraska


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Top 4 Grand Prix Cleveland 2017


Top 16 U.S. Nationals


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**


Ramunap Red because of its consistency and ability to go big. Nathan Smith brewed the list and assured me it was broken.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**


[Chandra, Torch of Defiance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Torch+of+Defiance); it’s the new Jace.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**


The two extra Chandras because value.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


Long-time *Magic* *Online* grinder


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


A lot! Shoutout to my brother David, Brandon Ayers, and Nathan Smith for always supporting me!




---

  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpden17/t8_pardee.jpg)**Name:** Sam Pardee


**Age:** 28


Hometown: Denver, Colorado


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


2 Pro Tour Top 8s


12 Grand Prix Top 8s


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**


Mono-Red [Ramunap Red]. If you can’t beat ‘em, join ‘em.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**


[Hazoret the Fervent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hazoret+the+Fervent)! It kills them dead.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**


[Chandra, Torch of Defiance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Torch+of+Defiance). Good once the games slow down post-board, and good to kill [Kalitas, Traitor of Ghet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kalitas%2C+Traitor+of+Ghet) with.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


I’m a game designer here in Denver. I taught myself to read as a kid to play *Magic* with my father.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


Hopefully not another second place.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpden17/t8_kiihne.jpg)Name:** Zachary Kiihne


**Age:** 20


**Hometown:** Concord, Massachusetts


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


5th Grand Prix [Providence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Providence) 2016


5th Grand Prix Cleveland 2017 with Lucas and Jack Kiefer


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**


Mono-Red [Ramunap Red]. It was the deck I had the most experience with.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**


[Chandra, Torch of Defiance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Torch+of+Defiance). It wins games on its own.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**


[Glorybringer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glorybringer). It comes in in most matchups and provides the extra reach needed.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


I’m just trying to bring glory to Team Cardhoarder


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


Getting back on the Pro Tour and the first step towards Silver.




---

  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpden17/t8_bbd.jpg)**Name:** Brian Braun-Duin


**Age:** 31


**Hometown:** Roanoke, Virginia


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


2016 World Champion


2x Grand Prix Champion. Brad Nelson’s better half. Corey Baumeister’s worse half.


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**


Temur Energy.


Did well for me last weekend. Good versus most top decks.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**


The long tusk of the law. [[Longtusk Cub](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Longtusk+Cub)]


Love me a cub that gets out of control.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**


[Confiscation Coup](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Confiscation+Coup).


I like stealing things.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


Well, it all started on a cold and blustery night in Michigan in 1986 ... I was birthed into this world holding a Swamp, a [Mutavault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutavault), and an [Obzedat, Ghost Council](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obzedat%2C+Ghost+Council) in my hands. The rest, as they say, is history.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


Means I am out of my rut!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpden17/t8_michel.jpg)**Name:** Gregory Michel


**Age:** 24


**Hometown:** Minneapolis, Minnesota


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


A couple of Grand Prix Top 32s.


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**


White-Blue Monument. It’s the deck I know the best.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**


Obviously Oketra’s Monument.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**


[Angel of Sanctions](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Sanctions) — it comes in for almost every matchup.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


I’ve been playing GPs for about two years and I’m thrilled to finally have a Top 8.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


A ton! Both my teammates just qualified for the Pro Tour and I’m thrilled to be able to go to one too.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpden17/t8_baumeister.jpg)**Name:** Corey Baumeister


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** Fargo, North Dakota


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


4th Grand Prix Top 8; 1 win in New Jersey


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**


Temur Energy. It crushed everything and BBD is GASSSSSSS.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**


The Longest of the Cubs, puts people on the back foot. [[Longtusk Cub](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Longtusk+Cub)]


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**


[Negate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Negate) by a mile! An aggressive deck with counter back-up.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


That me and Brad are the first brother to Top 8 back-to-back.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


Everything. History in the making. Time fore the Kiefer bros to step up to the plate!
