Round Seven Feature Match:
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 7, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall




 Brian David-Marshall is a New York–based game designer who has been involved with Magic since 1994, when he started organizing tournaments and ran a Manhattan game store. Since then, he has been a judge, a player, and one of the longest-tenured columnists on DailyMTG.com, as he enters his second decade writing for the site. He is also the Pro Tour Historian and one of the commentators for the Pro Tour. 






![He really doesn't have any Islands. Or DOES he?!](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpoak04/r7fm1_1.jpg)Nate Heiss and his teammate Jon Sonne were both sitting on a single loss. Another loss would drop the recipient perilously close to elimination. Nate was not the picture of optimism as he asked, "Do you want help with Feature matches tomorrow?" Josh Ravitz came over to watch the match and asked Nate about his deck. "Its okay…it has a lot of colors."

Nate won the toss and announced, "I guess I am going to draw."

Josh shook his head in disgust at the notion and cheered Sonne on, "Don't lose to Nate Heiss again!" Nate beat Jon on Day One of Grand Prix Kansas City en route to his finals appearance at that event.

### Game 1

Jon mulliganed but threatened to recoup the card disadvantage with [Liar's Pendulum](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liar%27s+Pendulum). He followed up with [Copper Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr). Nate also had the [Copper Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr) in play on his turn two.

Jon activated his Pendulum and named [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island). Nate guessed yes and Jon showed him that he did not have one. "You lied to me. Before the match he said he would tell the truth."

Nate played a [Hematite Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hematite+Golem) despite the knowledge that Jon was holding [Domineer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domineer). Jon activated his Pendulum and named [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) and Nate guessed he did not draw it. Jon did have it and drew another card. 

Nate played a [Lightning Greaves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Greaves) and attempted to equip the Golem but Jon returned it to his hand with an [Aether Spellbomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Spellbomb). Jon activated his Pendulum and named [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) again. Nate correctly guessed that he was lying. Jon [Dismantle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismantle)d the [Lightning Greaves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Greaves) and passed the turn back to Nate who cast [Thirst for Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thirst+for+Knowledge) on his own turn. 

Jon removed the [Pewter Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pewter+Golem) that Nate discarded with a [Mirror Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Golem). Josh Ravitz was watching the match and laughed that Jon was trying to disrupt Nate's reanimation strategy. He was kidding but Nate rolled his eyes and played a [Woebearer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woebearer) on his next turn. "Reanimation is a real strategy." He was also holding Betrayal of the Flesh.

Jon attacked for three and played a Vulshok Battelgear. He then [Domineer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domineer)ed the [Copper Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr). Nate got in for two with the [Woebearer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woebearer), Electrostaic Bolted the Golem, and played a [Fireshrieker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireshrieker) despite being stuck on four mana. 

Jon played a [Myr Enforcer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Enforcer) and equipped his Myr with the Battlegear. "I'm going to need more Electrostaic Bolts."

Nate fell to three on the next attack from Jon's team and [Essence Drain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Essence+Drain) finished him off, "Put me out of my misery."

"Who gets me for two cards with the Pendulum? I am so good with that card but Jon is so deliberate and methodical I can't tell anything."

Sonne - 1 Heiss - 0

![Liar's Pendulum](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Liar%27s+Pendulum)### Game 2

"So you are going to go second again?"

"Yeah."

"Great."

"You are not going to draw two cards on me with the Pendulum again. I'm not going to let you."

Jon opened with [Aether Spellbomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Spellbomb) and followed with [Copper Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr). Nate played a [Lightning Greaves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Greaves). Jon stumbled on his land drops and cycled his Spellbomb but did not find land number three. Nate played a [Spikeshot Goblin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spikeshot+Goblin) and attacked for one when he slapped the Greaves on it. 

Jon continued to miss lands and tapped out to play a Battlegear. Nate shot the Myr on his next turn. He played a [Pewter Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pewter+Golem) and when he attempted to move the equipment, Jon killed the artifact with [Echoing Decay](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Echoing+Decay).

Jon found his third land and played a [Neurok Spy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Neurok+Spy). Nate returned the Battlegear to the top of Jon's library with a [Looming Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Looming+Hoverguard). "I have some good cards."

Josh Ravitz: "They're not helping you any."

"Not helping me any he says as I play my [Fireshrieker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireshrieker)."

Jon replayed the equipment and attacked for two. He got in for five on the next turn but never found his fourth mana. Nate [Terror](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terror)ed the Spy and Jon conceded.

Sonne - 1 Heiss - 1

Game 3  
 Jon Sonne: "I guess I'll draw."

Nate laughed, "You think my four-color deck can't win on the play?" Nate played a turn two Greaves and a third turn Elf replica that attacked past Jon's [Copper Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr). "I am too quick for you!"

Jon played a [Silver Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silver+Myr) and passed the turn. Nate attacked with his Elf and a hasty [Hematite Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hematite+Golem) but Jon was able to kill the Replica with [Echoing Decay](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Echoing+Decay) and only took one. Jon played a [Myr Enforcer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Enforcer) and attacked for one with his leftover mana Myr.

Nate played a [Vedalken Engineer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vedalken+Engineer) and gave it haste to play out a [Darksteel Ingot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Ingot) before passing the equipment back to the Golem. Jon played his Battlegear and made a 4/4 Myr and attacked for eight. "This seems very familiar."

Nate was not concerned though and promised, "This is going to be a very good turn." He killed the Battlegear with a Rustpore Ram and Bolted the [Myr Enforcer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Enforcer). He had enough mana left over to pump his Golem.

Jon killed the Ram with an entwined [Barbed Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbed+Lightning) and attacked for two with his Myr. Nate fell to six. Jon fell to seven on the counter attack but Nate had to tap out to do it. Jon thought over his next turn and gave Nate hope, "This means I am not dead." All Jon did was [Dismantle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismantle) the [Lightning Greaves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Greaves).

Nate sent the Golem into battle to eat a Myr on the next turn. Nate equipped his Engineer with a [Leonin Bola](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Bola). Nate had no response and allowed the Myr to attack for one. Nate fell to five. Jon [Domineer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domineer)ed the [Hematite Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hematite+Golem), "I was thinking about that card."

![He knows what lurks in the hearts of men.](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpoak04/r7fm1_2.jpg)Nate dug for cards with [Thirst for Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thirst+for+Knowledge) and discarded a Stinger. He [Arrest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrest)ed the stolen Golem and this time when Jon announced attackers he tapped the [Silver Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silver+Myr). Jon played his own [Hematite Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hematite+Golem) during the second main phase and Nate put it on top of the library with a [Looming Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Looming+Hoverguard). 

Jon replayed the Golem with one unknown card left in hand. Nate used his Engineer in order to have enough mana to play and equip his [Looming Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Looming+Hoverguard) with [Fireshrieker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireshrieker). He had enough mana left over to finish him off with [Barbed Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbed+Lightning).

Sonne - 1 Heiss - 2
