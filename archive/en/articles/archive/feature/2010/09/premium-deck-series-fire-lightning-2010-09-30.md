### Collector's Highlights


Contents include:


* 60-card premium foil deck, including 6 rare cards
* Exclusive Spindown life counter
* Foil deck box
* Strategy insert
* **Magic** "learn to play" guide




**Blaze Your Path**


Baked or fried? You choose how to burn down your opponents with this powerful, 60-card, all-foil deck. It contains famous flames, bolts, and beats from across **Magic**  history, including several never before released in foil! Blaze your path to victory and serve your foes well done.

### Game Features


*Premium Deck Series: Fire & Lightning* will be available world-wide in English only, and will have a limited print run.


Each card will be a black bordered foil and tournament legal. This means that these cards are legal for use in any tournaments where the original printings are still legal.


**Deck Design and Development Team:** Aaron Forsythe and Zac Hill   
**Release date:** November 19, 2010   
**MSRP:** $34.99
