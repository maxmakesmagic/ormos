The little white tribe that can is back! Kithkin were last seen during their heyday of *Lorwyn / Shadowmoor* Block Constructed play, but they returned in the hands of Daniel Vigil at last weekend's [Pro Tour Qualifier for Nagoya](/en/node/641001) in Denver, Colorado. The archetype saw two versions in the Top 8. The other copy was a more traditional mono-white build, but Daniel squeezed in just enough blue in to run 4 copies of [Negate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Negate) in his sideboard.
