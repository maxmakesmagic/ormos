Modern Cube Ends December 6
---------------------------


The Modern Cube is a great way to try out all the iconic cards and synergies that shape one of *Magic*'s most dynamic formats. Modern Cube can be found in the Limited Leagues section of the Play Lobby and will run through December 6, with the start of *Ultimate Masters* events.


For event details, and to see the complete card list and the latest changes to it, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-cube-cardlist). Happy Cubing!

Saturday Standard PTQ
---------------------


On Saturday, December 1, there will be a Standard PTQ Finals at 9 a.m. PT. It will cost 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).




---

Sunday MOCS Monthly
-------------------


On Sunday, December 2, at 7 a.m. PT, we're having a MOCS Monthly event for the December 22 MOCS Playoff. The event will feature *Guilds of Ravnica* Phantom Sealed and costs 35 MOCS Qualifier Points to enter. Remember, this is your last chance to use those hard-earned 2018 MOCS Qualifier Points.


To find out more information, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2018-magic-online-championship-series).

Downtime December 5
-------------------


The next downtime will be December 5, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-8 from UTC. Please note that there are no more 'extended' or 'normal' downtimes; in the new world with fewer downtimes, they're all the same length of time.
