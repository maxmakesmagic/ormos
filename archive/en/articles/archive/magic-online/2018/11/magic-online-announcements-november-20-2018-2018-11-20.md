Last Day of *Time Spiral* Block Flashback Draft
-----------------------------------------------


Tomorrow, November 21, is your last chance to get timeshifted! Players will be able to play a full *Time Spiral* block Flashback Draft event until then.




---

Modern Cube Starts November 20
------------------------------


The Modern Cube is a great way to try out all the iconic cards and synergies that shape one of *Magic*'s most dynamic formats. Modern Cube can be found in the Limited Leagues section of the Play Lobby and will run through December 6, with the start of *Ultimate Masters* events.


For event details, and to see the complete card list and the latest changes to it, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-cube-cardlist). Happy Cubing!




---

*Commander 2018* in Treasure Chests
-----------------------------------


When we come up from downtime on December 5, players will find a new Treasure Chest collation waiting for them. This new collation will include *every new card from Commander 2018*. Ready to build your [Aminatou, the Fateshifter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aminatou%2C+the+Fateshifter) deck? Or do you just want to waken [Arixmethes, Slumbering Isle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arixmethes%2C+Slumbering+Isle)? It'll all be possible starting in just over two weeks. Full collation details will come later.

*Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifier Updates
-----------------------------------------


The [Pro Tour Qualifier page](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers) has been updated with the schedule for all PTQs feeding PT London.


Please note that your last chance to use a Blue Limited PTQ Finals Entry Token before it expires is the Finals held this Sunday, November 25. Any Red PTQ Tokens you've earned since August will continue to be valid through March 2, the end of the London qualifier round.




---

Saturday MOCS Monthly
---------------------


On Saturday, November 24, at 7 a.m. PT, we're having a MOCS Monthly event for the December 22 MOCS Playoff. The event will feature Standard and costs 35 MOCS Qualifier Points to enter.


We hope to see you there!




---

PTQ Sunday
----------


On Sunday, there will be a *Guilds of Ravnica* Sealed PTQ Finals at 7 a.m. PT. This event costs 1 Limited PTQ Finals Token.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

Downtime December 5
-------------------


The next downtime will be December 5, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC. Please note that there are no more 'extended' or 'normal' downtimes; in the new world with fewer downtimes, they're all the same length of time.
