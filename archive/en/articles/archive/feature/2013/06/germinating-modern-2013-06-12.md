Germinating Modern
==================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 12, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







I played Melira Pod in [Grand Prix Portland](/en/events/coverage/its-pardee-time), and I was extremely impressed with [Voice of Resurgence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence) and [Kitchen Finks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), but not so thrilled with the card [Birthing Pod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod). I've been wondering about other things a deck like that could do, and I've begun to wonder about [Golgari Germination](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golgari+Germination). 

![Golgari Germination](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Golgari+Germination)  

[Golgari Germination](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golgari+Germination) is excellent with creatures with persist or undying because it has the potential to make two creatures for each of them, and it's great with [Voice of Resurgence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence) because it keeps the tokens huge. It's also outstanding with [Blood Artist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Artist), which becomes something of the focus of this deck. 


[Golgari Germination](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golgari+Germination) also provides tokens to tap to convoke [Chord of Calling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling), which allows us to keep some of the tutoring power from [Birthing Pod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod). Melira still gains infinite life with [Kitchen Finks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), this deck has plenty of sac outlets to let that happen, and if you need to actually kill your opponent, a [Golgari Germination](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golgari+Germination) will generate infinite tokens and a [Blood Artist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Artist) will make the opponent lose any amount of life. 


[Chord of Calling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling) should generally either be setting up a Melira combo or finding [Blood Artist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Artist), or, better yet, [Reveillark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reveillark) to get [Blood Artist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Artist)(s). Another option is to find Varolz or [Death's Shadow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow), to Scavenge for +13/+13 if you can get a creature through. [Death's Shadow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) is an excellent bullet to find with [Ranger of Eos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ranger+of+Eos) or [Chord of Calling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling) if you're low on life, and Varolz is a good sac outlet, so having access to the combination of the two comes at a low cost. 

 There's a good chance that [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) should be in the starting sixty, but I really wanted to push the creatures. I included [Birthing Pod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod) in the sideboard to help grind out midrange opponents, but another option would be to have access to [Zealous Persecution](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zealous+Persecution) instead, as it can be extremely powerful with so many tokens.
