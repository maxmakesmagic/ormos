![Maelstrom Pulse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Maelstrom+Pulse)Matej (mah-tie) Zatlkaj (zaht-uhl-kye) is one of the nicest players on the Pro Tour, and with a Top 8 at Pro Tour-Berlin last year, proved he's one of the better players as well. At the 2009 World Championships, his deck of choice was a Junk aggro deck. It featured many of the game's most powerful green and white cards ([Elspeth, Knight-Errant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant), [Baneslayer Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel), [Lotus Cobra](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Cobra)) but also included a touch of black for [Maelstrom Pulse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Maelstrom+Pulse). For more on the 2009 World Championships, [visit the official event coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12).
