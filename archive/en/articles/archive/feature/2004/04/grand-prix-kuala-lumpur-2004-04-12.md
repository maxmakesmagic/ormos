Grand Prix - Kuala Lumpur
=========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 12, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












The **Magic: The Gathering** Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro Tour events.


**TOURNAMENT FORMAT AND SCHEDULE**  

The format for Grand Prix–Kuala Lumpur is Standard Constructed. All DCI Universal Tournament Rules, Magic Floor Rules, and all subsequent updates will be enforced.


**Friday, July 23**: Onsite registration is from 3:00 p.m. – 8:00 p.m.  
**Saturday, July 24**: Late registration from 8:30 a.m. – 9:30 a.m. Tournament begins at 10:00 a.m. The Top 64 players advance to competition on Sunday.  
**Sunday, July 25**: The Top 64 players begin play at 9:00 a.m.

**TOURNAMENT**  

The main tournament at Grand Prix–Kuala Lumpur is open to **Magic** players of all ages.


* **Head Judge**  

 The Head Judge for Grand Prix–Kuala Lumpur will be Wearn Chong, Level 3 from Malaysia.  

 If you wish to volunteer as a judge at Grand Prix–Kuala Lumpur, please contact us at the above player contact.
* **Tournament Structure**  

 The structure for Grand Prix–Kuala Lumpur will be modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds), with the Top 64 players advancing to the second day of competition for prizes and invitations.
* **Pro Tour Invitations**  

 The Top 8 previously uninvited players will each win an invitation to Pro Tour–Columbus. *In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher in that Grand Prix. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*
* **Tournament Divisions and Prizes**  

 Grand Prix–Kuala Lumpur will feature the standard main tournament prize schedule, as well as a special Amateur division prize schedule. Players who have 0 lifetime Pro Points will be eligible for the Amateur prizes. The Amateur prizes will be awarded to the highest-finishing amateur players *in addition to any winnings those players receive from the main tournament prize schedule.* All prizes are in U.S. dollars.

* **Amateur Status**  

 A player is considered to have amateur status if he or she has 0 lifetime pro points. If a player has ever been awarded any points from a Grand Prix, Pro Tour, or World Championship event, that player no longer has amateur status, and is not eligible for amateur awards at Grand Prix or other tournaments.

**SIDE EVENTS**



* **Grand Prix–Kuala Lumpur Trials**  
**Friday, July 23**: Registration for Trials will begin from 1:00 P.M. and will start as soon as there are 32 players. Last Trial for the day will begin at 5:00 P.M. The top finisher of each Trial receives a third-round bye at Grand Prix–Kuala Lumpur and one display of *Fifth Dawn* boosters. Standard Constructed format; Single-elimination; Cost: MYR 30
* **Pro Tour–Columbus Qualifier**  
**Sunday, July 25**: Registration closes at 9:45 A.M.; play begins at 10:00 A.M. The top finisher receives USD375 and an invitation to Pro Tour–Columbus. *Mirrodin* Block Constructed format; Swiss with single-elimination finals; Cost: MYR 40
* **On Demand Tournaments**  

 Eight-person single-elimination, on-demand tournaments will be offered throughout the weekend. Costs: Constructed MYR 30, Draft MYR 40, Sealed MYR 70
