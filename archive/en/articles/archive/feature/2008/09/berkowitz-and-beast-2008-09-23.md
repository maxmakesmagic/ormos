On the shard of Naya, monsters of mythic proportions rule the land. These gargantuans are so revered they're treated almost as gods; Philadelphian Jordan Berkowitz is also no stranger to respecting Beasts. He used a plethora of the over-sized creatures to earn himself a Top 8 at Pro Tour-Venice in 2003. The deck was nearly entirely lands and Beasts save for four copies of [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock). To learn more about the Beastly inhabitants of Naya, check out the *Shards of Alara* [product page](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/shardsofalara).
