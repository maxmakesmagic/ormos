*Guilds of Ravnica* Events Live!
--------------------------------



*Guilds of Ravnica* is here, and the fun continues! Jump in on anything from Friendly Sealed Draft to Competitive Constructed League. There is something for everyone in Guilds of Ravnica. Check out [this article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/guilds-ravnica-magic-online-edition-2018-09-19) for details. Note that Guilded League will be ending October 8, so make sure you get your matches in before then.




---

PTQ Saturday
------------


There is a Standard PTQ Finals on Saturday, that starts at 9 a.m. PT. The entry options are 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).




---

PTQ Sunday
----------



The Limited PTQ Finals are on Sunday. The event starts at 7 a.m. PT, and costs one Chandra or Jace Limited Token to enter.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).




---


*Dominaria* Redemption
----------------------



Friendly reminder that this is your last week to redeem *Dominaria* cards. Please do that as soon as possible!




---


M19 Redemption
--------------


M19 Regular redemption sets are currently out of stock and we will be starting the reprint process, which will take several weeks. We will announce the restock here the week before they are available. Thanks for your patience.

Downtimes October 24 and December 5
-----------------------------------



The next two downtimes will be October 24, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC and December 5, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC. Please note that there are no more 'extended' or 'normal' downtimes; in the new world with fewer downtimes, they're all the same length of time.
