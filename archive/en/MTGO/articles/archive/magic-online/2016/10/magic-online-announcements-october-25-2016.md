**The Year of Modern Flashbacks Continues with *Innistrad* Starting Today**


It's 2016: The Year of Modern Flashbacks! If you want to build up a collection for Modern, these Flashback Drafts add everything you draft to you collection once the event is over. Even if Modern isn't your thing, you can enjoy a leisurely Draft through the planes of the past with rotating formats each week!


Triple *Innistrad* Flashback Drafts start tomorrow and run until Wednesday, November 2 and are followed up by *Dark Ascension-Innistrad-Innistrad* Drafts. Get the full schedule [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/content/magic-online-limited-events#YearOfModernFlashbacks) and mark your calendar for the formats you're most excited about!

**Upcoming *Kaladesh* Phantom Sealed MOCS Monthly Event on October 29**


A *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) *Kaladesh* Phantom Sealed Monthly Event is coming up this Saturday, October 29! Players can spend 35 Qualifier Points (QPs) to enter the event. The series will contain 24 Monthly Events, each of which qualify top players for one of the four Playoff Events. These Playoff Events qualify top players for the *Magic Online* Championship, a Pro Tour, and the Pro Tour Challenge event at that Pro Tour.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points. Top players on the Leaderboard at the end of the 2017 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.


We look forward to seeing you on October 29 for the latest Monthly Event! Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online* Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

**Upcoming Extended Downtime on Wednesday, October 26**


There will be an extended downtime on Wednesday, October 26, for *Magic Online*. Events close at 12:00 a.m. PT/7 a.m. UTC, and store activity and trades end at 2:45 a.m. leading up to the system coming down at 3:00 a.m. The system is estimated to return from downtime at 12:00 p.m.

**Build Notes**


* General | Magic Online player rewards for September have been granted. Those cards are listed in [this article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-player-rewards).
* General | The new product window now remembers its location correctly and will reset to default setting if displayed off screen.
* Collection | Increased maximum binder size to 60,000 items.
* Limited | Lathnu Hellion's end of turn trigger can now be paid if it triggered an enter the battlefield effect when cast.
* Pauper | Fixed Might Beyond Reason and Hunger of the Howlpack so that they now put counters on the targeted creature.
* Standard | Casting Remorseless Punishment no longer prevent the match from proceeding.
* Collection | Players can correctly add cards not in their collection to decks.
* Account Settings | Canceling a change to account settings no longer saves the changes.
* Trade | The scroll bar correctly appears when using the Search Tool during a trade.
* Events | The first player to submit during sideboarding will stay on the sideboarding screen until their opponent submits. This player can make and submit additional changes, but as soon as the opponent submits the match will start and any unsubmitted changes will be lost.
* Chat | Joining a channel in chat no longer causes a rare crash.
* Limited Scene | Fixed a number of rare circumstances that caused cards to become duplicated and illegal in deckbuilding and sideboarding.
* Store | Updated the VAT rate for Greece in the Magic Online store.
* Trade | The active trade binder now saves upon initiating a trade, ensuring that your trade partners always see the most up-to-date version of the binder.
* Collection | Fixed a crash while viewing cards in the Collection Scene.
* Collection | The deck builder now displays an error message when you exceed the 1,400 card deck size limit.
* Limited | Corrected the blue text that displays on Midnight Oil when it has no counters.
* Limited | Aetherborn Marauder's trigger no longer move counters if the card is in the graveyard on resolution, nor will it allow your opponent to determine how those counters move if cast on their turn.
* Limited | Corrected the activated ability prompt text of Animation Module.
* Modern | Dispatch now exiles creatures when it's metalcraft condition is met.
* Legacy | Corrected the set symbol rarity on the Marit Lage token.
