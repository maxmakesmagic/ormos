States and Champs Return November 8
===================================



 Posted in **News**
 on September 5, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











We are excited to announce the return of a proud tradition: **Magic: The Gathering - States**! Get ready to take your Magic game to the next level, pit yourself against the fiercest competition your state has to offer and claim the title of State Champion. Step up and see if you have the chops to be the best in your state!

We are also pleased to announce the return of **Magic: The Gathering - Champs** to Canada! Think you have the wits to dominate the competition and become a Provincial Champion? Get in the game and let's find out!

**Magic: The Gathering - States** and **Magic: The Gathering - Champs** are presented by Tournament Organizers in cooperation with Wizards of the Coast, and will be held in all 50 states and 10 Canadian provinces. Tournament locations in the U.S. and Canada will utilize the Standard format.

Be on the lookout for more information. 

**Magic: The Gathering - States** and **Magic: The Gathering - Champs** return November 8, 2008!
