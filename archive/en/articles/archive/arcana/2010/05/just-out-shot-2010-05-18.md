Just Out of Shot
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 18, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Let's consider [Hand of Emrakul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hand+of+Emrakul).

![Hand of Emrakul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Hand+of+Emrakul)As you can see, this monstrosity is digging into the side of a mountain. It's flinging huge chunks of rock out of its way. But there's something you don't know, because it's behind the name of the card. Let's blow this up!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/452_handofemrakul.jpg)[Hand of Emrakul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hand+of+Emrakul) | Art by [Chippy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Chippy%22%5D)

See that bit up there next to the rock that's flying through the air?

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/452_inset.jpg)  
Oh no! It looks like some poor mountaineer has gotten in the way! According to the art description, it's probably a goblin. So now you know!
