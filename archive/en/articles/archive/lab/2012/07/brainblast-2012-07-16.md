Brainblast
==========



 Posted in **From the Lab**
 on July 16, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Noel deCordova












Hello and welcome back to the Lab. It's a Lab on wheels this time around; I'm typing this nearly halfway through a personally charged road trip across the US. Accordingly, I'm currently flush with adventure. There's something truly liberating about traveling.


I've met some friendly folks so far on my literally coast-to-coast journey. I'll throw a quick shout-out to [Games +1](http://gamesplus1.com/) of Davenport, Iowa, where I participated in a ***Magic** 2013* Prerelease event, which was a blast. It reminded me of how fun Prereleases can be (it was my first proper one in quite a while). The Limited experience (opening packs, building a forty-card deck, playing it against similar from-scratch decks, making the tiniest modifications throughout) remains a thrilling mind-whirl. I went with a white-blue deck filled with little beaters and topped off with a [Captain of the Watch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Captain+of+the+Watch), which turned out to play extremely well with [Griffin Protector](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Griffin+Protector) and [Healer of the Pride](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Healer+of+the+Pride) (the Healer was best buds with my [Rhox Faithmender](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhox+Faithmender)). [Sleep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sleep) handed me a few tight games also.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/ftl/ftl204_omni.jpg)[Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) | Art by [Jason Chan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5B%22Jason%20Chan%22%5D)


As a Johnny observing the battlefields of M13 Limited, I appreciated the combinations possible in the format. One of my opponents had insane shenanigans with [Roaring Primadox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roaring+Primadox). [Bond Beetle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bond+Beetle)s and [Mwonvuli Beast Tracker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mwonvuli+Beast+Tracker)s caused all sorts of trouble for me, and in one very close game, [Bloodhunter Bat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodhunter+Bat) drained me for 2 no less than ten times. I was also delighted to be on the receiving end of a [Spelltwine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spelltwine), which grafted [Divination](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Divination) and [Talrand's Invocation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Talrand%27s+Invocation) together. That's probably enough chatter about the Prerelease, especially since, by now, that weekend's fading into memory obscurity. But from my current typing perspective, it occurred yesterday!


Wanna know something else random? Today, July 16, is my birthday! And thanks to my recent shift to Mondays, my column landed on it! So wish me a fun one, or else you'll face no repercussions whatsoever.


Alright, onto some crazy decks and other variants of zany. [Last week](/en/articles/archive/lab/old-new-and-new-new-2012-07-09), I requested your deck ideas concerning ***Magic** 2013*, but primarily centered on [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience). You guys came through with some fun ideas! So let's proceed to the spillage of my inbox.


### Know It All


When I first read and absorbed [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience), miniature blue fireworks began to explode all around me. I knew it was too staggering of a Johnny card to be ignored here, so I sounded the reader conch shell in hopes of presenting a wide spectrum of deck ideas that involved it.


Russell Lee sent in a powerful deck that, based on my scant view on tournament matters, might actually compete in Legacy. He wrote:



> 
> The best part of [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) is that it's a hyperactive [Dream Halls](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dream+Halls), so my first instinct was to just replace [Dream Hall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dream+Hall)s with it. However, it turned out to be quite a bit more. It's very Legacy, more broken than fun, though easy enough to disrupt if [Show and Tell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell) gets circumvented.
> 
> 
>

A quick [Show and Tell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell) can enable early [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience), and from there things speed up dramatically. A free [Conflux](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conflux) can find a bizarre spread of [Prismatic Omen](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Omen); [Progenitus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Progenitus); and [Coalition Victory](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coalition+Victory), which wins the game. That seems good.

A reader known as Highwayman sent me a list, which makes use of one of my all-time favorite cards: [Jhoira of the Ghitu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jhoira+of+the+Ghitu). And [Worldfire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Worldfire). Okay, *three* of them. Highwayman wrote:



> 
> I figured I'd try to get the M13 "twin Timmy giants" of [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) and [Worldfire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Worldfire) into the same deck. The win conditions are [Worldfire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Worldfire) with either [Keldon Marauders](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Keldon+Marauders) in play, [Rift Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rift+Bolt) suspended, or another creature further up the stack (courtesy of Jhoira or [Quicken](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quicken)).
> 
> 
> To cast the win condition, you are going to want to land mana accelerate into it, use Jhoira, or have [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) out. [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) can itself be mana accelerated into or suspended in the same way.
> 
> 
>

> 
> But what if you don't draw the combo with [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) out? No matter, that's where [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) has another trick: [Wheel of Fate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wheel+of+Fate). [Wheel of Fate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wheel+of+Fate) can be hard-cast with [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) out, and you are hoping either to draw the combo with the next seven cards or another [Wheel of Fate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wheel+of+Fate). The [Lotus Bloom](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Bloom) can be helpful here, allowing you to suspend [Rift Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rift+Bolt) when you are otherwise tapped out but using your [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) to cast spells.
> 
> 
> [Vedalken Æthermage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vedalken+%C3%86thermage) is Jhoira copies five and six, and an extra creature for casting creature + [Quicken](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quicken) + [Worldfire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Worldfire), all courtesy of [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience).
> 
> 
>
