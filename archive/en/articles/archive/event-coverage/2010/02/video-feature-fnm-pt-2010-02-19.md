Video Feature: From FNM to the PT
=================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 19, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 






Looking for the heart and soul of **Magic**? Look no further than your local [Friday Night **Magic**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm), the stomping grounds for longtime Pros such as Brian Kibler and Patrick Sullivan as well as PT first-timers Jared and Corey Mann, and find out why Friday night is the best night of the week.
