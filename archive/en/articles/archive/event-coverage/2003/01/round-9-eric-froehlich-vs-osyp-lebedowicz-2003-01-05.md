Round 9: Eric Froehlich vs. Osyp Lebedowicz
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 5, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a956.jpg)Osyp "Joe Black" Lebedowicz, frontman of the audacious Team TOGIT, is a man on the move. He concedes a small portion of his busy schedule to pursue his first love: Magic. The CMU-TOGIT connection is still going strong, and as a result, Lebedowicz is with [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), also sported by Eugene Harvey. This round he squares off against Limited dynamo Eric Froehlich. Froehlich is with blue-green madness, probably because he's been putting the final polish on his draft game for Chicago.

### Game 1

Lebedowicz won the die roll, and kicked off with island. Froehlich immediately outclassed him with [Careful Study](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) discarding [Rootwalla](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootwalla) and [Wonder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder). An [Aquamoeba](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) was countered, but a second resolved as Lebedowicz [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition)ed for [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge)s. He untapped, and [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother)ed the [Aquamoeba](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba). Froehlich made a [Rootwalla](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootwalla) in response. Lebedowicz passed it back with two blue untapped.

Now Froehlich started hitting hard. His pair of [Rootwalla](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootwalla)s brought Lebedowicz to eleven. Lebedowicz made a [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) and ended his turn. Froehlich swung back. Lebedowicz let it through, and Froehlich pumped just once to bring him to seven. His three mana fronted [Circular Logic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic). Nevertheless, Lebedowicz tried his AK. Froehlich had the [Logic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Logic).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a954.jpg)Lebedowicz drew [Thwart](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thwart), a turn too late, hit for one and passed it back. Froehlich tried a Bouncer. Lebedowicz countered. Froehlich dropped a sixth land and attacked Lebedowicz to one. Lebedowicz simply untapped, played [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), and attacked with a lethal [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog).

Lebedowicz 1 - Froehlich 0

### Game 2

"Good ol' die roll." - Eric Froehlich

Froehlich's opener of turn one [Rootwalla](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootwalla), turn two [Rootwalla](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootwalla) was trumped by Lebedowicz's [Powder Keg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Powder+Keg) on turn two. Froehlich had [Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ice) to push through six more damage, but Lebedowicz was still at nine. He Kegged away Froehlich's team, but did not have a fourth land. He [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak)ed Froehlich's [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition), and when he still failed to draw a fourth land, he played a mainphase [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) for two. It served up a swamp.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a955.jpg)Froehlich had another [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition). This one resolved. It got three Deep Analyses, for some serious long-term card advantage. He flashed one back, and in response Lebedowicz played the third Knowledge. That was the death knell. Froehlich played on, but Lebedowicz resolved a [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), and after Froehlich tapped out Lebedowicz was free to [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) up [Wonder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) for the kill.

As they did a post-mortem on the match, the two discovered that they had wildly different ideas about who was favored in the match. Lebedowicz claimed an even match, while Froehlich felt himself the heavy favorite. A big part of Lebedowicz's case was four maindeck [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother)s.

**Final Result:** Osyp Lebedowicz defeats Eric Froehlich 2-0
