The last of the Regionals are happening all over the world, and from Chiba prefecture in Japan we have this take on Black-Green Elves featuring the popular-of-late [Putrid Leech](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Putrid+Leech). Yuya Yanagida took the deck all the way through to the Top 4 in a field dominated by Black-Green Elves in general. Next on the horizon? The stepping stone up from Regionals: Nationals tournaments!
