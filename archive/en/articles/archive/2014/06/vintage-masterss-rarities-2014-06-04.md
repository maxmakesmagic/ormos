Vintage Masters's Rarities
==========================



 Posted in [ARTICLES](/en/articles)
 on June 4, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 





We're all excited about the opportunity to play with the [Power Nine](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/arcana/432) in **Magic Online**'s upcoming expansion *Vintage Masters*, but there are a ton of players eyeing rarities in the set well below the [special rarity](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/arcana/1491) that will hold **Magic**'s most precious gems.


The [Pauper](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/resources.aspx?x=magic/rules/pauper) community, for example, is eagerly awaiting getting its hands on a number of commons that, prior to *Vintage Masters*, have never been available in the format before. Additionally, plenty of players are looking to get their hands on a number of other cards that will be more plentiful in *Vintage Masters* than at their previous rarities.


So to get you started, here is a complete list of cards for *Vintage Masters*, along with the rarity you'll find them printed at when you start cracking packs on Friday, June 13!
