Top 8 Profiles of Pro Tour Dominaria
====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 3, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











After 16 rounds, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy here at Pro Tour *Dominaria*. Find out more about these people here!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptdom/t8_darby.jpg)


**Name:** Wyatt Darby


**Age:** 23


**Hometown:** Iowa City, IA


**Occupation:** Insurance Agent


**Team:**


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Earned the title of "generic brand Michael Majors" from friends.


**Limited Record:** 6-0


**Standard Record:** 6-3-1


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?**


Paying my own way through college. The responsibility helped me grow a lot as a person.


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


Hitting the two-out [Lightning Strike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Strike) to win Round 15!


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.**


My late father. I owe him everything and miss him every day.


**If you win the Pro Tour, who’s your first call afterwards?**


Jason Kenjar. Love ya, buddy!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptdom/t8_pinto.jpg)


**Name:** Gonçalo Pinto


**Age:** 32


**Hometown:** Barreiro


**Occupation:** Digital Marketing Analyst


**Team:** None, but tested with Hareruya Latin and Cardhoarder Brazil


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Top 8 Grand Prix Birmingham (Standard) 2018


**Limited Record:** 4-1


**Standard Record:** 8-1-1


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?**


Getting my dog and teaching him stuff.


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


Well, this one. Top 8ing with Márcio Carvalho after beating him Round 1.


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.**


My wife. She left her country to come to Portugal.


**If you win the Pro Tour, who’s your first call afterwards?**


My wife, obviously.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptdom/t8_turtenwald.jpg)


**Name:** Owen Turtenwald


**Age:** 29


**Hometown:** Arlington, VA


**Occupation:** *Magic*


**Team:** Ultimate Guard Pro Team


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Five Pro Tour Top 8s, two-time Player of the Year, Pro Tour Hall of Fame


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Standard Record:** 8-0-2


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


12-4 at Pro Tour *Magic 2015* in Portland and getting 8th on tiebreakers, leading to the Seabiscuit gallop up to the stage.


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.**


William Jensen and Jon Finkel, co-heroes.


**If you win the Pro Tour, who’s your first call afterwards?**


Dinner reservation for The Pantheon.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptdom/t8_hendriks.jpg)


**Name:** Thomas Hendriks


**Age:** 25


**Hometown:** Leiden


**Occupation:** Student


**Team:** Revelation (Genesis group)


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir* Top 8, some Grand Prix Top 8s


**Limited Record:** 5-1


**Standard Record:** 7-1-2


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?**


Life's pretty great in general, *Magic* related or not. No specific one.


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


Getting into Top 8 of a Pro Tour without my teammates having any idea I was still live in the tournament.


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.**


This spot's reserved for the card that wins me the tournament tomorrow, if I win it. I hope it's [Rekindling Phoenix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rekindling+Phoenix). It has been treating me well so far.


**If you win the Pro Tour, who’s your first call afterwards?**


Not calling anyone in the United States. Expensive business. I'll text my girlfriend through.
