Fate Reforged Clash Pack
========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 6, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






Remember that time we told you [we were going to wait until after previews](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/arcana/fate-reforged-packaging-2015-01-01) to show you the *Fate Reforged* Clash Pack? Well, we got impatient. This iteration of the Clash Pack—Power and Profit—is just so cool we couldn't wait to show it off.


 


Clash Packs, if you recall, come with two ready-to-play decks built to battle one another *or* to be combined into one super-awesome deck. And each of the two decks also comes with three alternative-art promos that, as you'll see, are gorgeous.


 


Also note that not every card from *Fate Reforged* has been previewed just yet. If you don't recognize a card in one of these lists, hold tight! It will be coming soon.
