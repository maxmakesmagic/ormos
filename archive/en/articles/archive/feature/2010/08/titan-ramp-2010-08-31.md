One of three National Championships held last weekend, Germany crowned its 2010 champion at the same time as Great Britain and the United States. Dennis Johannsen will lead the team, and he won his spot playing Titan Ramp, a deck that saw its debut earlier this summer at the Japan National Championships. Incredibly, the German player was appearing in his *twelfth* Germany National Championships, the most of any player ever. Read full coverage of the event [here](/en/events/coverage/johannsen-ramps-glory-germany).
