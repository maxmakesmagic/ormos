Magic Online Championship Series - 2009 Season 3
================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on July 2, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Players earned QPs during 2009 Season 3 as follows:
---------------------------------------------------

* 3 QPs were earned for finishing in the Top 8 of any Premiere Event or Daily Event.
* 1 QP was earned for being the winner of any queue with 8 or more players (any format).
* The Season 2 LCQs and Championship did not award any QPs for Season 3.
* Magic Online Live Series events awarded 1 QP to the winner.
* Block Constructed events between the June 17 and 24 downtimes awarded 3 QPs to the winner and 1 QP to second place.
