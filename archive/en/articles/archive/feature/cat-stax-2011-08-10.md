With the world's most powerful cards at their fingertips, Vintage players command decks that can kill as early as the first turn. But there's a lot more to the format then meets the eye, as demonstrated in the Top 8 by Ryan Glackin's Cat Stax deck, a mana denial strategy featuring [Slash Panther](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slash+Panther) (yes, [Slash Panther](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slash+Panther)). Read full coverage of the Vintage Championships and check out a picture of the first-prize alternate-art [Time Walk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Walk) in the [official coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/2011-us-national-championship-day-1-blog-2011-08-05).
