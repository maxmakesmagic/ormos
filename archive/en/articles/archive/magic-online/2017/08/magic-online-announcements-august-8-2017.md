**The August MOPR Promo period is underway**


The August MOPR period runs from July 26 to August 30. On August 31, the [MOPR changes](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-player-rewards-changes-2017-07-31) announced last week will be implemented. Here is this month’s promo card:  

 


Event and Store Promo


![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170808185117im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/images/hero/Fatal_Push_Promo.png)


[Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push)


  

Yes, thats right! [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push) is the August promo for BOTH the Event and Store!  
  

Play in one or more events during the August MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push). Play in five or more and you'll get an additional premium [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push). Spend at least $20 total in the *Magic Online* Store during this MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push). By spending $40 or more, you'll get an additional premium [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push).

**Standard PTQ for Pro Tour *Ixalan* Saturday, August 12**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, August 12 at 9 a.m. PT. There is no need to qualify through a Preliminary for Constructed, simply join this event with 30 Event Tickets, 300 Play Points or a spare Blue Constructed PTQ Finals Entry Token!


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

***Magic Online* Format Challenges back this weekend**


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges introduced a new prize structure and recurring weekend events for Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper formats!


Events begin every Saturday at 8:00 a.m. PT, and run through Sunday. Check out [this page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-format-challenges) for further event details as well as the weekend schedule.

**Bug Blog updates**


Store | [Gideon, Martial Paragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Martial+Paragon) *Amonkhet* Planeswalker deck has 1 [Gideon's Intervention](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%27s+Intervention) instead of 1 [Glory-Bound Initiate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glory-Bound+Initiate)


Vintage | With [Solemnity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solemnity) in play, you are still prompted to pay one mana for cards with Cumulative Upkeep that have zero Age counters.


Collection | A warning message is missing when adding cards that are in a deck in a League to a new Trade Binder.


Standard | [Diregraf Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diregraf+Colossus) will in rare cases reset the game.


Duel Scene | If a creature is blocked by two creatures with combined toughness equal to the blocked creature’s power, you are unable to divide combat damage.


Standard | [Sunscourge Champion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunscourge+Champion) will cause you to lose life if it has negative power when its trigger resolves.


Limited | If [Trueheart Duelist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trueheart+Duelist) blocks multiple attackers, as well as another creature as part of a group, damage assignment errors can arise.


Multiplayer | If a commander is chosen for Riches in [Rags // Riches](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rags+%2F%2F+Riches), no other opponents need to choose a creature.


General | *Eventide* Mimics trigger even when a card of only one of its colors is cast.


Standard | [Vizier of Many Faces](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vizier+of+Many+Faces) keeps its abilities when an opponent controls [Overwhelming Splendor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overwhelming+Splendor).
