Looking for a Knight-themed deck now that *Mirrodin Besieged* will soon be available for purchase? Why not try **Magic** R&D head Aaron Forsythe's awesome deck from spellslinging the Prerelease? Aaron was at [Misty Mountain Games](http://www.mistymountaingames.com/) in Minneapolis, Minnesota using a host of new Knights from the latest set in the *Scars of Mirrodin* block. When asked about the deck's inspiration, Aaron replied, "There are a lot of good Knights in *Mirrodin Besieged*." And did you know you can [follow Aaron on Twitter](http://www.twitter.com/MTGAaron)?
