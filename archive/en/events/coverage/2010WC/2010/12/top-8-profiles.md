Top 8 Profiles

By Event Coverage Staff

### Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa


**Age**: 23   
**Hometown**: Brazil   
**Occupation**: Student   
**How did you qualify for Worlds?**  

Pro Level


**Number of Grand Prix/Pro Tour Top 8s:**  
**PT**: 7   
**GP**: 7


**Other previous Magic accomplishments:**  

I finished in the money in my first three Pro Tours!


**What is your record by format?**   
**Standard**: 5-1   
**Booster Draft**: 5-1   
**Extended**: 3-0-3


**How did you prepare for Worlds? Who did you prepare with?**   

Played with the ChannelFireball crew and a bunch of other people (same as Eric Froehlich).


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**   

2-3, for videos


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**   

Blue-black. It is decent against almost everything and I'm comfortable with it.


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic, or if you don't go to FNM anymore, where do you play?**   

I don't anymore, but I used to play in my local store, Jambo.


**Pick a side: Mirran, or Phyrexian?**   

Mirran!


\*I'd like to dedicate this Top 8 to my biggest fan, Brad Nelson

### Love Janse


**Age**: 26   
**Hometown**: Linkoping   
**Occupation**: Unemployed   
**How did you qualify for Worlds?**  

3rd in Nationals


**Number of Grand Prix/Pro Tour Top 8s:**  
**PT**: 0   
**GP**: 0


**Other previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Qualifying for Worlds was my biggest accomplishment three days ago


**What is your record by format?**   
**Standard**: 4-2   
**Booster Draft**: 6-0   
**Extended**: 4-2


**How did you prepare for Worlds? Who did you prepare with?**   

A week of testing Standard with Swedish squad, weekly semi-casual drafts throughout the fall, frantically trying to find out an Extended deck last night (and finally copying Kenny Öberg and Bertil Elfgren's list).


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**   

Zero.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**   

Eldrazi Ramp, because I built it for FNM since I had the Titans so it's the deck I know best.


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic, or if you don't go to FNM anymore, where do you play?**   

Serieborsen, Linkoping


**Pick a side: Mirran, or Phyrexian?**   

Mirran
