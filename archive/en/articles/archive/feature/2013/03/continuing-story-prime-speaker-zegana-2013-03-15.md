The Continuing Story of Prime Speaker Zegana
============================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 15, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







Sometimes, I get hung up on trying to make a card or interaction work. At the moment, it's [Gyre Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gyre+Sage) with [Prime Speaker Zegana](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prime+Speaker+Zegana). 

  
![Prime Speaker Zegana](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Prime+Speaker+Zegana) Zegana and [Gyre Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gyre+Sage) both require that you play a lot of creatures, but Standard requires that you have answers to creatures (most importantly [Olivia Voldaren](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Olivia+Voldaren)) if you're going to play a lot of creatures. This can cause issues both in terms of space and color requirements in a deck. 

 My latest effort to solve these problems is a BUG deck that uses [Sever the Bloodline](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sever+the+Bloodline) as its removal spell, which can allow it to take advantage of the huge amounts of mana [Gyre Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gyre+Sage) can potentially generate in the late game to get extra powerful removal spells. 

 The creatures focus on keeping me alive, smoothly evolving [Gyre Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gyre+Sage), and gumming up the ground while I win with fliers. 

 I avoid mana Elves despite the utility of accelerating [Prime Speaker Zegana](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prime+Speaker+Zegana) because they don't work with [Gyre Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gyre+Sage), make me take much more damage from my lands, and make me more vulnerable to sweepers. Instead, the deck is full of high-impact spells. 

 I've played a handful of matches with it on **Magic Online** and I've been reasonably happy with the results. I'm not ready to take it to a Grand Prix, but between casting your opponent's spells with [Nightveil Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nightveil+Specter) and creating a huge army with [Master Biomancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+Biomancer) there's more than enough fun to be had for [FNM](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm)! 

Seriously, give it a try:
