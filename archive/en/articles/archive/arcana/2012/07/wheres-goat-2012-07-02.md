Where's That Goat?
==================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on July 2, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Last week, we looked at the tokens of ***Magic** 2013*, including this goat token:


![Magic 2013 Goat Token](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1002_token2_t7243updke.jpg)  
At the time, the card that creates Goats wasn't public. But now it is! So let's look at Trading Post.


![Trading Post](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/tcg/products/m13/nu5mx8nxip_en.jpg)  
"Okay, great," you say. "So where are the goats?"


Well, that's a fair question. Let's blow the art up a little.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1006_tradingpost.jpg)Trading Post | Art by [Adam Paquette](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Adam+Paquette%22%5D)


Hmm. That's a big place. But it looks like there might be some goats down at the bottom. In fact, it looks like they might be the very same kind of goat that are on the token! Let's zoom in for a closer look.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1006_zoomin.jpg)  
Hello, goats!
