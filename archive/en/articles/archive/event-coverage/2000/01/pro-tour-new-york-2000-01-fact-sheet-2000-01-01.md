### Masters Series


 Invitation List


The Masters Series is an invitation-only tournament to be held in conjunction with Pro Tour-New York. The 32-person field is composed of the Top 25 players based on the Pro Tour-New York 1999/2000 Pro Standing, the Top 5 DCI rated Extended players (based on the ratings published 8/14/00), and the Top 2 finishers of the Gateway Tournament (see below).


**Gateway Tournament**


The Masters Series Gateway Tournament is open to all players with 6 or more Pro Points as of the Pro Tour-New York 1999/2000 Pro Standing. The Gateway Tournament awards two invitations to the Masters Series which immediately follows the Gateway Tournament. The format of the Gateway Tournament is the same as the format of the Master Series Tournament, which is single-elimination Extended Constructed. 


The Gateway Tournament and the first two rounds of the Master Series Tournament will be held at Neutral Ground New York.


Neutral Ground  

122 W 26th Street

(between 6th and 7th Avenues)

Registration for the Gateway Tournament begins at 8:00 AM on Thursday, September 28 and the tournament begins at 9:00 AM sharp. There is no admission charge for the Gateway Tournament.
