Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 13, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











Learn more about Pro Tour *Amonkhet*'s Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they've done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptakh/t8-fennell.jpg)Name** : Chris Fennell


**Age**: 31


**Hometown**: Tampa, FL


**Team**: [Lingering Souls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lingering+Souls)


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** : Top 8 at Pro Tour *Born of the Gods*. Nine Grand Prix Top 8s.


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 9-1


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?** Black-green and green-blue splashing white. I preferred the black-green deck.


**What's the key to drafting *Amonkhet*?**  Be in the right colors.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?** Black-White Zombies. It wins the mirror and does not lose to a resolved [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel).


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?** [Anguished Unmaking](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anguished+Unmaking)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?** Validation for 20 years of playing. I would always be a Pro Tour Champion.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptakh/t8-yukuhiro.jpg)Name** : Ken Yukuhiro


**Age**: 28


**Hometown**: Tokyo, Japan


**Team**: Musashi, sponsored by Dig.cards


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** : two Pro Tour Top 8s, seven Grand Prix Top 8s


**Limited Record**: 6-0


**Standard Record**: 7-3


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?** Black-red twice


**What's the key to drafting *Amonkhet?*** Studying all the archetypes in the set and not playing green.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?** Black-Green Energy because I have mastered it.


**What card was your MVP this weekend?** [Bone Picker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bone+Picker)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?** It will be the start of my new *Magic* story.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptakh/t8-tobiasch.jpg)Name** : Marc Tobiasch


**Age**: 29


**Hometown**: Benn, Germany


**Team**: EUreka


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** : Managing and building [Doomsday](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomsday) piles in time.


**Limited Record**: 6-0


**Standard Record**: 6-2-2


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?** Black-green and then green-white. The black-green deck was the best.


**What's the key to drafting *Amonkhet?*** Find out what is open and either be aggro or have a good plan to beat it.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?** Temur Marvel. [It's a] midrange deck that has an "I win" button as well as a very good late game.


**What card was your MVP this weekend?** [Tireless Tracker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tireless+Tracker).


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?** Starting the German tradition of winning all the Pro Tours again, finally. Also, Platinum and the World Championship. Come on!




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptakh/t8-calcano.jpg)Name** : Christian Calcano


**Age**: 28


**Hometown**: New York, NY


**Team**: MTG Bent Card


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** : seven Grand Prix Top 8s with two wins, two Pro Tour Top 16s.


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 7-1-2


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?** White-Blue [Slither Blade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slither+Blade), going 3-0. Then Red-White Aggro going 2-1. Favorite was [Slither Blade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slither+Blade) (obviously).


**What's the key to drafting *Amonkhet?*** Don't play four-drops or higher.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?** Mono-Black Zombies, because I trust my teammates.


**What card was your MVP this weekend?** [Liliana's Mastery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%27s+Mastery)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?** Everything I've dreamed of, and one step closer to the Hall of Fame.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptakh/t8-muller.jpg)Name** : Martin Müller


**Age**: 19


**Hometown**: Jyllinge, Denmark


**Team**: Genesis


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** : Top 8 at Pro Tour *Battle for Zendikar*. 2014 World *Magic* Cup Champion.


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 7-2-1


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?** White-black (2-1), red-green (3-0)


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?** Temur [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) with [Chandra, Flamecaller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Flamecaller)


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?** [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) and [Chandra, Flamecaller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Flamecaller)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?** Playing Worlds would be great because I haven't done well at all [in that tournament].




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptakh/t8-thompson.jpg)Name** : Gerry Thompson


**Age**: 32


**Hometown**: Renton, WA


**Team**: Mutiny


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** : Top 8 at Pro Tour *Gatecrash*


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 7-2-1


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?** White-Black Zombies and Blue-Red Spells. The spells deck was my favorite by far.


**What's the key to drafting *Amonkhet*?** Be aggressive or be able to answer aggression.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?** Zombies—it was level 0, but felt good.


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?** [Dark Salvation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Salvation).


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?** Cross that bridge when we get there.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptakh/t8-froehlich.jpg)Name** : Eric Froehlich


**Age**: 33


**Hometown**: Las Vegas, NV


**Team**: Channel Fireball Ice


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** : Pro Tour Hall of Fame. 4 Pro Tour Top 8s, 15 Grand Prix Top 8s.


**Limited Record**: 6-0


**Standard Record**: 6-4


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?** Black-red and black-green. The black-red was good with a bad curve; the black-green deck was just bad.


**What's the key to drafting *Amonkhet*?**  Luck


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?** Temur [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel). It's the best deck and I hoped to avoid the hate.


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?** [Ulamog, the Ceaseless Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ulamog%2C+the+Ceaseless+Hunger)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?** Final piece of the puzzle




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptakh/t8-watanabe.jpg)Name** : Yuuya Watanabe


**Age**: 28


**Hometown**: Tokyo, Japan


**Team**: Musashi


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** : Three Pro Tour Top 8 appearances; 25 Grand Prix Top 8s with seven wins; won both Player of the Year and Rookie of the year; inducted into the Hall of Fame in 2017.


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 7-3


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?** Went 3-0 with white-black and then 2-1 with white-blue. The second was my favorite because it had two [Drake Haven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drake+Haven)s.


**What's the key to drafting *Amonkhet?*** Understanding the table position correctly.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?** Temur [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel). It's the best deck in Standard!


**What card was your MVP this weekend?** [Ulamog, the Ceaseless Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ulamog%2C+the+Ceaseless+Hunger)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?** To achieve my last
