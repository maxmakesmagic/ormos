Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 26, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro










Here are your final eight players who made it through the Texas gauntlet to make it to the top of Grand Prix Dallas/Fort Worth.



### Aryeh Wiznitzer


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpdal15/gpdal15_t8-wiznitzer.jpg)


**Age:** 31


**Hometown:** Houston, Texas


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


Some amount of Top 32s.


**What did you play Saturday, and how did you do?**


Mardu, splashing for [Suppression Bonds](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Suppression+Bonds) for no real reason. 8 – 1.


**What did you draft today, and how did that go?**


White-Blue Rares 3 – 0. Multiple bombs win in draft.


Black-Red Rares 2-0-1 (ID). Greg Orange passed me the nuts in pack two, but it would out OK for him in the end.


**What were your best commons or uncommons this weekend? Why?**


Who needs uncommons when everyone is passing you rares?  



**What do you want open for pack one, pick one? Why?**


Chandra's Parents [[Pia and Kiran Nalaar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pia+and+Kiran+Nalaar)] because it's the best card and red is a strong color.

### Dan Jordan


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpdal15/gpdal15_t8-jordan.jpg)


**Age:** 24


**Hometown:** Albany, New York


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


3 Pro Tour Top 16s  



**What did you play Saturday, and how did you do?**


Blue-Red Aggro, 8-1.


**What did you draft today, and how did that go?**


Red-White both time. 3-0, 2-1.


**What were your best commons or uncommons this weekend? Why?**


[Enshrouding Mist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enshrouding+Mist)


**What do you want open for pack one, pick one? Why?**


The [Siege-Gang Commander](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander) guy [[Pia and Kiran Nalaar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pia+and+Kiran+Nalaar)] because it is the best rare in the set.





---

### Ross Merriam


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpdal15/gpdal15_t8-merriam.jpg)


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** Middletown, Connecticut


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


Top 8 Grand Prix Cleveland 2015  



**What did you play Saturday, and how did you do?**


8-1 with White-Black rares.  
[Tragic Arrogance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tragic+Arrogance), [Kytheon's Irregulars](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kytheon%27s+Irregulars) and [Mage-Ring Responder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mage-Ring+Responder)


**What did you draft today, and how did that go?**


3-0 with U/w Miscreant Aggro (5 [Faerie Miscreant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faerie+Miscreant)s)


2-1 with Blue-Red Thopter Aggro  

Both decks were great.


**What were your best commons or uncommons this weekend? Why?**


Veteran Sidearm, because my decks were full of small creatures.  
[Suppression Bonds](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Suppression+Bonds) was great in my sealed deck.


**What do you want open for pack one, pick one? Why?**


[Hangarback Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hangarback+Walker). A great card that lets me stay open.





---

### Joel Larsson


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpdal15/gpdal15_t8-larsson.jpg)


**Age:** 23


**Hometown:** Stockholm, Sweden


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


2nd place at Pro Tour *Gatecrash*, three Pro Tour Top 16s, 5 Grand Prix Top 8s, Swedish National Team three times (captain twice), and almost Winter Champ.


**What did you play Saturday, and how did you do?**


Red-White Midrange, 8-1.


**What did you draft today, and how did that go?**


Blue-Black with two [Jace, Vryn's Prodigy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+Vryn%27s+Prodigy), [Languish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Languish), [Priest of the Blood Rite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Priest+of+the+Blood+Rite), and sick uncommons.  

Blue-Red-black. Artifacts with really good four-drops.


**What were your best commons or uncommons this weekend? Why?**


Jace, [Pia and Kiran Nalaar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pia+and+Kiran+Nalaar), and [Molten Vortex](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Vortex).


[Those are not commons or uncommons, Joel.]


**What do you want open for pack one, pick one? Why?**


[Jace, Vryn's Prodigy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+Vryn%27s+Prodigy). It dictates the draft around you and is nuts.

### Shuhei Nakamura


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpdal15/gpdal15_t8-nakamura.jpg)


**Age:** 33?


**Hometown:** Don't know anymore.


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** I'm a retired *Magic* player. I forgot.


**What did you play Saturday, and how did you do?** Blue-Red, I went 9-0. [Sword of the Animist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+the+Animist) saved me from a lot of mana screw.


**What did you draft today, and how did that go?** White-Black, 1-2. White-Red, 3-0.


**What were your best commons or uncommons this weekend, and why?** [Alchemist's Vial](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Alchemist%27s+Vial), helped save me from mana screw. [Call of the Full Moon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Full+Moon) also won games, even when I only drew two lands.


**What do you want to open for pack one, pick one? Why?** Planeswalkers, because they're a great signal and very good.

### Gregory Orange


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpdal15/gpdal15_t8-orange.jpg)


**Age:** 22


**Hometown:** Top


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 9th place, Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir*; Top 8 at Grand Prix Minneapolis in 2014, Top 25 at Pro Tour *Magic 2015.*


**What did you play Saturday, and how did you do?** I had a great red-green deck.


**What did you draft today, and how did that go?** My first deck was an awesome white-green deck with a good curve. My second deck was a bad white-blue deck with a bad curve.


**What were your best commons or uncommons this weekend, and why?** I had two [Zendikar Incarnate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zendikar+Incarnate) in my Sealed pool that were really good.


**What do you want to open for pack one, pick one? Why?** I want to open the best card in the set so I don't lose.





---

### Nathaniel “Pandabeast24” Smith


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpdal15/gpdal15_t8-smith.jpg)


**Age:** 20


**Hometown:** Omaha, Nebraska/Houston, Texas


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 2015 *Magic Online* Championship competitor; 29th at Pro Tour *Fate Reforged;* Silver pro now.


**What did you play Saturday, and how did you do?** Black-green rares. I went 8-1 with mythics plus some bad cards.


**What did you draft today, and how did that go?** White-black, the deck was good and I went 2-1. Then red-green beats. I went 3-0 but the deck wasn't very good. [Act of Treason](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Act+of+Treason) is OP though.


**What were your best commons or uncommons this weekend, and why?** [Act of Treason](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Act+of+Treason)… no one plays around it. Also [Deadbridge Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deadbridge+Shaman). Discard one plz!


**What do you want to open for pack one, pick one? Why?** I'm not sure, maybe Jace?

### David Ochoa


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpdal15/gpdal15_t8-ochoa.jpg)


**Age:** 33


**Hometown:** Hayward, California


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Top 8 at Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica;* five Grand Prix Top 8s; Nationals Top 4 and then finalist. Allegedly won a round of a draft video.


**What did you play Saturday, and how did you do?** White-blue, went 7-2 (including three byes).


**What did you draft today, and how did that go?** Blue-red, went 3-0. Then white-black; went 3-0.


**What were your best commons or uncommons this weekend, and why?** [Fiery Impulse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Impulse), [Topan Freeblade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Topan+Freeblade) and [Ringwarden Owl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ringwarden+Owl).


**What do you want to open for pack one, pick one? Why?** [Hangarback Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hangarback+Walker). It's great and colorless.
