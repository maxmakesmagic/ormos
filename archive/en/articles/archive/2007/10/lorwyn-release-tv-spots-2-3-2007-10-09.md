=================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 9, 2007

Another day, another batch of TV spots for the [*Lorwyn* release events](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/releases). *[Goldmeadow Stalwart](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goldmeadow+Stalwart)*


In conclusion, *[Lorwyn release events](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/releases) are this weekend at a store near you!*
