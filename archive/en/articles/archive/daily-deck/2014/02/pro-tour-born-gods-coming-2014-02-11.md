Pro Tour Born of the Gods is Coming
===================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on February 12, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






Next week, February 21-23, 2014, the best **Magic** players in the world will gather in Valencia, Spain, for [Pro Tour *Born of the Gods*](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/bornofthegods14) to battle for their share of the $250,000 prize pool. Coverage will start on our [Twitch.tv](http://www.twitch.tv/magic) at 9 a.m. in Valencia, or midnight Pacific, 3 a.m. Eastern (see the table below for a breakdown).

**Come watch the Pro Tour in person!**

The tournament hall is open to the public starting Friday morning. Spectators are invited to watch the Pro Tour on projection screens showing the live webcast and matches from along the rail in designated viewing areas. The hall will open at 8 a.m. on Friday and Saturday, and 8:30 a.m. on Sunday.

Sunday features a full slate of festivities that begin at 9 a.m. Key activities are scheduled around the Top 8 playoff schedule, making it so you won't have to miss a minute of the action. 

* **Ask Ramp;D Session**: Aaron Forsythe and Tom LaPille answer your questions and talk about what awaits in the **Magic** Multiverse. To be held Sunday between the last quarterfinal match and the first semifinal match.
* **Artist Signings**: Get exclusive foil art prints signed by artists [Peter Mohrbacher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoileramp;method=visualamp;action=advancedamp;artist=+%5b%22Peter+Mohrbacher%22%5d) and [Tyler Jacobson](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advancedamp;output=spoileramp;method=visualamp;artist=+%5B%22Tyler%20Jacobson%22%5D).
* **Tokenize Yourself:** Get your face on a token creature from the world of Theros.
* **Meet Top Pros**: Get autographs from World Champion Shahar Shenhar and other top Pros.
