*Aether Revolt* Championship Qualifiers
=======================================



### *Aether Revolt* Limited Championship Qualifiers


**Start Times**:Every three hours on the half-hour, starting Wednesday, February 15, 12:30 p.m. until Tuesday, February 21, 9:30 p.m. All times are Pacific (UTC -8).  
**Location**:Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Scheduled Events  
**Entry Options**:


* Option 1: 30 Event Tickets
* Option 2: 6 Event Tickets, plus product
* Option 3: 300 Play Points

**Product**: 4 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 2 *Kaladesh* booster packs  
**Size**: 17–64 players  
**Play Style**: Swiss  
**Format**: Sealed  
**Duration**: 20 minutes deck-building time. Five rounds, each round up to 50 minutes  
**Prizes**:




| Match Wins | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5 Wins | An invitation to the *Aether Revolt* Limited Championship Finals, 11 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 5 *Kaladesh* booster packs, and an [alternate-art](#promos) [Yahenni's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yahenni%27s+Expertise) | 3 |
| 4 Wins | An invitation to the *Aether Revolt* Limited Championship Finals, 8 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 4 *Kaladesh* booster packs, and an [alternate-art](#promos) [Yahenni's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yahenni%27s+Expertise) | 1 |
| 3 Wins | 6 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 2 *Kaladesh* booster packs | 0 |

All participants will receive an alternate-art [Trophy Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trophy+Mage) promo.

### *Aether Revolt* Standard Championship Qualifiers


**Start Times**:Every three hours on the half-hour, starting Wednesday, February 22, 12:30 p.m. until Tuesday, February 28, 9:30 p.m. All times are PDT (UTC -8). From November 6, 2:00 a.m. PT through the end of the event, all times are PST (UTC -8).  
**Location**:Play Lobby -> Constructed Tournaments -> Scheduled Events  
**Entry Options**:


* Option 1: 15 Event Tickets
* Option 3: 150 Play Points

**Product**: A Standard Constructed deck  
**Size**: 17–64 players  
**Play Style**: Swiss  
**Format**: Constructed  
**Duration**: Five rounds, each round up to 50 minutes  
**Prizes**:




| Match Wins | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5 Wins | An invitation to the *Kaladesh* Standard Championship Finals, 10 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 5 *Kaladesh* booster packs, and an [alternate-art](#promos) [Yahenni's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yahenni%27s+Expertise) | 3 |
| 4 Wins | An invitation to the *Aether Revolt* Standard Championship Finals, 7 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 3 *Kaladesh* booster packs, and an [alternate-art](#promos) [Yahenni's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yahenni%27s+Expertise) | 1 |
| 3 Wins | 4 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 1 *Kaladesh* booster pack | 0 |

All participants will receive an alternate-art [Trophy Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trophy+Mage) promo.

*Aether Revolt* Championship Finals
===================================



### *Aether Revolt* Limited Championship Finals


**Start Time**:Saturday, February 25, 7:00 a.m PT (UTC -8)  
**Location**:Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Scheduled Events  
**Entry Option**:


* Invitation

**Product**: 4 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 2 *Kaladesh* booster packs. *Magic Online* will provide 2 *Aether Revolt* booster packs and 1 *Kaladesh* booster pack for the Top 8 Draft.  
**Size**: 8-1024 players  
**Play Style**: Swiss, with Top 8 Draft  
**Format**: Sealed  
**Duration**: 20 minutes deck-building time. A number of rounds determined by attendance, each round up to 50 minutes, followed by a Top 8 Draft.  
**Prizes**:




| Place | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | 2 premium *Aether Revolt* sets, 2 nonpremium *Aether Revolt* sets, and 14 *Aether Revolt* boosters packs + 7 *Kaladesh* booster packs | 15 |
| 2nd | 1 premium *Kaladesh* set, 1 nonpremium *Aether Revolt* set, and 14 *Aether Revolt* boosters packs + 7 *Kaladesh* booster packs | 15 |
| 3rd–4th | 1 premium *Aether Revolt* set and 14 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 7 *Kaladesh* booster packs | 15 |
| 5th–8th | 1 nonpremium *Aether Revolt* set and 14 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 7 *Kaladesh* booster packs | 15 |
| 9th–32nd | 10 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 5 *Kaladesh* booster packs | 0 |
| 33rd–128th | 3 *Aether Revolt* booster packs + 2 *Kaladesh* booster packs | 0 |
