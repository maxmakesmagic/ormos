Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX CHIBA 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpchi15)
 on May 31, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











3,551 players was narrowed down to these eight masters. Learn a little bit more about the Grand Prix Chiba Top 8 players!




---


Yuki Matsumoto
==============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpchi15/Top8-Yuki-Matsumoto.jpg)


**Hometown:** Tokyo  
**Occupation:** Office Worker  
**Sealed Record:**8-1  
**Booster Draft #1 Record:** 3-0  
**Booster Draft #2 Record:** 2-0-1


**When did you first begin playing *Magic*?**   
*Scars of Mirrodin*


**What is the best memory/moment that *Magic* has contributed to your life?**   

(N/A)




---


Ichiro Matsubara
================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpchi15/Top8-Ichiro-Matsubara.jpg)


**Hometown:** Tokyo  
**Occupation:** Actor  
**Sealed Record:** 8-1  
**Booster Draft #1 Record:** 3-0 (UG)  
**Booster Draft #2 Record:** 2-0-1(UG)


**When did you first begin playing *Magic*?**   
*Urza's Saga*. I retired once, but I came back to *Magic* when *Zendikar* was released.


**What is the best memory/moment that *Magic* has contributed to your life?**   

Today!




---


Shougo Sunada
=============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpchi15/Top8-Shougo-Sunada.jpg)


**Hometown:** Kanagawa  
**Occupation:** Engineer  
**Sealed Record:** 8-1  
**Booster Draft #1 Record:** 3-0(BG tokens)  
**Booster Draft #2 Record:** 2-0-1(RGwub Domain)


**When did you first begin playing *Magic*?**   

When I was a 2nd grade elementary school student.


**What is the best memory/moment that *Magic* has contributed to your life?**   

Three answers:


1. When I finished 13th place with an original deck
2. When I got the title of ''GOD of MODERN''
3. Now!

Albert Budisanjaya
==================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpchi15/Top8-Albert-Budisanjaya.jpg)


**Hometown:** Surabaya  
**Occupation:** Businessman  
**Sealed Record:** 7-2  
**Booster Draft #1 Record:** 3-0(UG Graft)  
**Booster Draft #2 Record:** 3-0(UG Graft splash red)


**When did you first begin playing *Magic*?**   

2009. I was introduced to the game by my senior high school friend.


**What is the best memory/moment that *Magic* has contributed to your life?**   

Winning a World *Magic* Cup Qualifier, which let me go to Europe last year for the World *Magic* Cup.
