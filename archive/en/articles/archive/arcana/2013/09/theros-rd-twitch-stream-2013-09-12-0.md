*Theros* R&D Twitch Stream
==========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 12, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






andy copied this ![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1196_ino5e8iqv9_theroslogo.jpg)  



Next week we will be hosting a Twitch.tv stream with R&D Developer Dave Humpherys and R&D's Head Designer Mark Rosewater, along with Sean Gibbons (who you might remember from the Wizards team of the Community Cup). 

 They will be talking about *Theros*, answering your questions, and talking about some of the stories from within R&D during the set's design and development. 


**Who**: Sean Gibbons, Mark Rosewater, and Dave Humpherys   
**What**: Twitch stream with R&D.   
**When**: Thursday, September 19, 2013 - 3:00 p.m. PT to 4:00 p.m. PT   
**Where**: [http://www.twitch.tv/magic](/en/events/coverage/pro-tour%E2%80%93amsterdam-standard-qualifier-season-top-8-decklists)
  
**Why:** Because we know you have lots of awesome questions!
