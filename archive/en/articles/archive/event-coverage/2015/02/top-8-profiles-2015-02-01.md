TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 2, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmex15/top8-maynard-2.jpg)


### **Name**: Pascal Maynard


**Age**: 21


**Occupation**: Game Store Manager at Botique Donjon


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Five GP 8


**What colors did you play on Day 1? What were your most important cards?**  

Sultai splashing red. [Sandsteppe Mastodon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sandsteppe+Mastodon).


**What colors did you play in the first draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Blue/Red Tempo. [Treasure Cruise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise).


**What colors did you play in the second draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Red/White Aggro. [Ghostfire Blade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghostfire+Blade).


**What card do you most hope to open from Fate Reforged in the Top 8 draft?**  
[Mistfire Adept](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistfire+Adept).




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmex15/top8-juza-2.jpg)


### **Name**: Martin Jůza


**Age**: 27


**Occupation**: Tourist


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Won a Constructed GP with [Shimmering Grotto](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shimmering+Grotto) in my deck. I also have more than twenty GP Top 8s.


**What colors did you play on Day 1? What were your most important cards?**  

Green/Black. [Sandsteppe Mastodon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sandsteppe+Mastodon).


**What colors did you play in the first draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Black/White/Green. [Sandsteppe Citadel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sandsteppe+Citadel).


**What colors did you play in the second draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Blue/White/Red. [Mantis Rider](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mantis+Rider).


**What card do you most hope to open from Fate Reforged in the Top 8 draft?**   

One of the two color dragons.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmex15/top8-canavati-2.jpg)


### **Name**: Andreas Canavati


**Age**: 26


**Occupation**: Planeswalker


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Two Pro Tours, 1 GP Top 16.


**What colors did you play on Day 1? What were your most important cards?**  

Every color but Black. [Sagu Mauler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sagu+Mauler).


**What colors did you play in the first draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Red/White Aggro. Alesha.


**What colors did you play in the second draft?** **What were your most important cards?**  

Jeskai Tempo. [Master of Pearls](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Pearls).


**What card do you most hope to open from Fate Reforged in the Top 8 draft?**  

One of the rare Khans or Dragons enchantments.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmex15/top8-flores-2.jpg)


### **Name**: Mario Flores


**Age**: 22


**Occupation**: Engineering Student


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: 3 Gp Top 8s.


**What colors did you play on Day 1? What were your most important cards?**

Abzan. [Dromoka, the Eternal](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dromoka%2C+the+Eternal).


**What colors did you play in the second draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Esper. [Silumgar, the Drifting Death](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silumgar%2C+the+Drifting+Death).


**What card do you most hope to open from Fate Reforged in the Top 8 draft?**  
[Dromoka, the Eternal](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dromoka%2C+the+Eternal)!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmex15/top8-vieira-2.jpg)


### **Name**: Eduardo Dos Santos Vieira


**Age**: 25


**Occupation**: *Magic* Grinder


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Winner Gp Santiago. Top 4 Grand Prix Denver. Finals GP Sao Paulo. Top 25 Pro Tour Magic 2015.


**What colors did you play on Day 1? What were your most important cards?**  

Jeskai. [Ojutai, Soul of Winter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ojutai%2C+Soul+of+Winter).


**What colors did you play in the first draft? What were your most important cards?**   

Temur. [Yasova Dragonclaw](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yasova+Dragonclaw).


**What colors did you play in the second draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Temur. [Savage Knuckleblade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savage+Knuckleblade) and [Sagu Mauler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sagu+Mauler).


**What card do you most hope to open from Fate Reforged in the Top 8 draft?**   
[Temur Battle Rage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temur+Battle+Rage) and [Pyrotechnics](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyrotechnics).

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmex15/top8-sim-2.jpg)


### **Name**: Chapman Sim


**Age**: 29


**Occupation**: Event coverage writer and tourist! (Like Martin Jůza)


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Top 8 at Grand Prix Shizouka 2014. Finalist at Grand Prix Singapore 2013. Being on the best team ever, MTG Mint Card!


**What colors did you play on Day 1? What were your most important cards?**  

Abzan. [Crux of Fate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crux+of+Fate). Maindeck [Return to the Earth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Return+to+the+Earth).


**What colors did you play in the first draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Sultai. Double [Icy Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Icy+Blast) and [Sandsteppe Mastodon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sandsteppe+Mastodon)!


**What colors did you play in the second draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Abzan. [Gurmag Angler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gurmag+Angler), a card that Lee Shi Tian, Huang-Hao Shan, and Zhang Meng Qui tipped me onto.


**What card do you most hope to open from Fate Reforged in the Top 8 draft?**  
[Abzan Beastmaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abzan+Beastmaster). The card is insane, and I wouldn’t have made Top 8 without it.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmex15/top8-martinez-2.jpg)


### **Name**: Axel Martinez


**Age**: 32


**Occupation**: Store Owner  


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Mexico National Champion 2008. Played in seven Pro Tours. 9th at the last three Mexican Grand Prix events.


**What colors did you play on Day 1? What were your most important cards?**  

Abzan. [Grim Haruspex](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Haruspex), [Abzan Beastmaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abzan+Beastmaster), [Kheru Bloodsucker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kheru+Bloodsucker), [Wildcall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wildcall), and Dromoka.


**What colors did you play in the first draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Abzan. [Sultai Flayer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sultai+Flayer).


**What colors did you play in the second draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Green/White. Two [Seeker of the Way](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seeker+of+the+Way). [Savage Punch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savage+Punch).


**What card do you most hope to open from Fate Reforged in the Top 8 draft?**  
[Whisperwood Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whisperwood+Elemental).

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmex15/top8_botis-2.jpg)


### **Name**: Juan Carlo Botis


**Age**: 29


**Occupation**: Psychologist


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: None


**What colors did you play on Day 1? What were your most important cards?**   

Abzan. [Palace Siege](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Palace+Siege) was a key card, enabling comebacks from difficult situations.


**What colors did you play in the first draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Jeskai. [Will of the Naga](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Will+of+the+Naga), [Force Away](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+Away), [Alabaster Kirin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Alabaster+Kirin), and [Lotus Path Djinn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Path+Djinn).


**What colors did you play in the second draft? What were your most important cards?**  

Blue/White. [Crippling Chill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crippling+Chill), Slinging Bell Strike, and [Channel Harm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Channel+Harm).


**What card do you most hope to open from Fate Reforged in the Top 8 draft?**  

Any card that will get the job done!
