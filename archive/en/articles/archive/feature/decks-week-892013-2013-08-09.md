Decks of the Week: 8/9/2013
===========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 9, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Last week, Amsterdam played host to Worlds Week, a five-day **event** that combined the [World Championship](/en/events/coverage/youth-served-flash) and [World **Magic** Cup](/en/events/coverage/l%C3%A9vy-du-soleil-en-france)! Congratulations to Shahar Shenhar, our new world champion, and the Raphaël Lévy–led Team France, our new World **Magic** Cup champions! Between those two tournaments, players competed in **seven different formats**. Of particular interest to those of you competing at PTQs this season, Standard was well represented. If you plan on going to a PTQ this season, find one near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros). Good luck; have fun!
