### *[Fallen Empires](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/expert/fallen_empires/fallen_empires.asp)* Information:




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Released:** November 1994**Design & Development Team:** Skaff Elias, Jim Lin, Dave Pettey with design contributions from Chris Page**Art Director:** Sandra Everingham |  | **New Mechanics:** “battery” lands, creature race based theme, extensive use of counters, “sac” lands**Factoid:** *Fallen Empires* was the first "tribal" set. It focused on Elves, Thallids, Soldiers, Dwarves, Orcs, Goblins, Thrulls, Homarids, and Merfolk. |
