The 9-0 Decks of Grand Prix Paris 2015
======================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX PARIS 2015](/en/events/coverage/gppar15)
 on May 10, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











Out of all the 1,426 players who were in the running originally, only four managed to win all nine of Saturday's rounds. And in a testament to the diversity of the current Standard format, all four played very different decks.


Jochen Frenster chose Esper Dragons, Manuel Faber picked a green devotion deck with a splash of red, Amand Dosimont decided to run Abzan Aggro, and Yohan Dudognon build his very own deck: a four-colored monstrosity featuring [Collected Company](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collected+Company) and so many cool things you should probably just check out the decklist yourself!
