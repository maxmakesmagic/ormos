Decks of the Week: 11/19/2009
=============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on November 19, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Tons of material to cover this week, so let's get started. The 2009 Grand Prix season wound to a close at Grand Prix–Minneapolis, where Zohar Bhagat took down a very tough Top 8 to claim the title. In the digital realm, there were tons of **Magic Online** events as players began preparing for the World Championships in Rome (check back here over the next few weeks for all the deck lists!), and a half dozen PTQs got their deck lists in to share with you.


Enjoy!
