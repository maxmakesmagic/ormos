*Ninth Edition* Saproling Token Art
===================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 17, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











As *Ninth Edition* appears in ***Magic** Online* on the 29th, you'll see brand new art for the tokens generated by a few of those new cards.  As you know, the amazing token-generating machine **[Verdant Force](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verdant+Force)** makes its Core Set debut in *Ninth*. 

Here's a sneak peek at the art of the new token art for the Saprolings it pumps out.
