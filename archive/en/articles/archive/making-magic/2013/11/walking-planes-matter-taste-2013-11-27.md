Walking the Planes: A Matter of Taste
=====================================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on November 27, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Nathan Holt and Shawn Kornhauser












What kind of **Magic** player are you? What’s your style? Whether you’re devoted to red, have a penchant for non-English cards, or are attracted to certain card illustrations, chances are the aesthetic of your game matters to you. There’s no "right" way to do it. It’s a matter of taste.
