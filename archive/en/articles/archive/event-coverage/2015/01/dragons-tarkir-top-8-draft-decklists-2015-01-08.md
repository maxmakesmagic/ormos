Dragons of Tarkir: Top 8 Decklists
==================================



 Posted in **event coverage**
 on January 8, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










The Pro Tour Qualifier season for Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir* is under way. This is your chance to test your knowledge of Standard Constructed, because invitations and tickets to Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir* go to PTQ winners!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/ptq_400.jpg)




Look through the events below for Top 8 Standard decklists. Decklists will be updated during the PTQ season. The new season runs December 6, 2014 – February 22, 2015 . For more information about the PTQ season, [click here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/qualifiers-pro-tour-dragons-of-tarkir).
