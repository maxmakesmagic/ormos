In honor of the Hall of Fame ballots being tabulated this week, Daily Decks is examining current Hall of Famers' most famous creations. The Littlest Viking, as Olle Rade was affectionately known during his time on the Pro Tour, used some very unorthodox cards to take home the title at Pro Tour–Columbus. While other players were sweeping their opponent’s board with [Pyroclasm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm), Rade managed to heavily out-metagame the field by employing a bevy of X/3 creatures to finish off his opponents. His [Giant Trap Door Spider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Giant%2BTrap%2BDoor%2BSpider) and [Woolly Spider](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woolly+Spider)s could survive both [Pyroclasm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm) and Stormbind and, in addition to his [Deadly Insect](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deadly+Insect)s, earned his creation the title “Bugbind.”
