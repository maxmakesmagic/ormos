These aren't our best FFL decks per se, but instead a glimpse into what the set has to offer. ![Warren Instigator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Warren+Instigator)With a bunch of land that enters tapped and a few quirky one-ofs (e.g. [Goblin Artillery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Artillery)), the deck had some variance in both the speed of its opening and the way it plays out. It was built to showcase [Warren Instigator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warren+Instigator) and some of the other cards that *Zendikar* offers to the aggro deck. I played about ten games with it, and it seemed pretty competitive against the decks I encountered (White Aggro, Jund, and Five-Color Control). Definitely had a few exciting and powerful plays when the [Warren Instigator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warren+Instigator) came out early.
