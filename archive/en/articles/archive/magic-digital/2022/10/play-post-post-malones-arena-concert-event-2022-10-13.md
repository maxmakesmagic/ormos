Play Like Posty in Post Malone's Arena Concert Event
====================================================



 Posted in **Magic Digital**
 on October 13, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











By now, [you might have heard about](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/news/magic-gathering-and-post-malone-team-year-long-celebration-making-friday-night) or seen [Make Your Friday Night *Magic*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MQUEtJ7NO7g) and Post Malone's collaborations with *Magic: The Gathering*. On top of announcing our *Secret Lair x Post Malone* drop (more details on this coming soon!), we at *MTG Arena* are excited to announce our partnership with Post Malone!


![Post Malone's Arena Concert Event October 20–24](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/azgz6wielt9.jpg)


Post Malone's *Arena* Concert event, running October 20–24, is a no-entry-fee event. All fans will have the opportunity to attend Post Malone's show-stopping concert by playing five preconstructed Historic Brawl decks! Not only do the decks carry the flavor to join this star, but players and fans will also enjoy other aspects of Post throughout the event with avatars, sleeves, and stickers. You can earn the sleeves during the event, and the stickers will be available in the *MTG Arena* Store.


![Squee rim shot sticker](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/squeerimshot.gif)![Karn crowd surfing sticker](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/karncrowdsurf.gif)![Radha head bang sticker](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/radhaheadbang.gif)


Check out the event details and rewards below to find out how you can play like Posty!


![Post Malone wizard avatar](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/92aptnwbk8z.png)


Post Malone's *Arena* Concert Event
===================================


**Dates**: October 20, 8 a.m. PT (15:00 UTC)–October 24, 8 a.m. PT (15:00 UTC)


**Format**: Preconstructed Historic Brawl


**Structure**: Play until event ends


**Entry**: No cost to enter


**Rewards**:

| --- | --- |
| 5 wins | Posty Forest sleeve |
| 4 wins | Posty Mountain sleeve |
| 3 wins | Posty Swamp sleeve |
| 2 wins | Posty Island sleeve |
| 1 win | Posty Plains sleeve |
