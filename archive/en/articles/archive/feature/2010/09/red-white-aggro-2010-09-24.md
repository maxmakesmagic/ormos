Red-white aggressive decks are an old archetype, but the modern world of Standard has given them new life. With *Scars of Mirrodin* soon entering the world of Standard, how will that change things? Will infect, proliferate, or metalcraft improve the red and white decks? Or are there new bombs that were just what the deck was looking for? Find out in the [Visual Spoiler](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/scarsofmirrodin/spoiler) (and don't forget to attend your [Prerelease](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/prerelease-facts) this weekend, your first chance to see the new set!)
