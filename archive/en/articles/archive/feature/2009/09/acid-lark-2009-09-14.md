Naoki Shimizu is a pro player, coverage reporter, and **Magic** writer. The above deck, featuring [Acidic Slime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime) in conjunction with [Reveillark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reveillark), was the one he used to make the Top 8 at his local Game Day in Japan. The 2/2 deathtoucher happens to have exactly the right stats to be abused with [Reveillark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reveillark): a powerful enters-the-battlefield ability and power 2 or less. Look for the green creature to be popping up in [Reveillark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reveillark) decks at a tournament near you soon!
