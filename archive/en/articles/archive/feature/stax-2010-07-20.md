Legacy is one of the most popular formats in **Magic**. In fact, a Grand Prix featuring the format was the largest **Magic** tournament ever held, with over 2,000 competitors showing up to play! STAX is an archetype from the format designed to lock a player out of lands while still having its own thanks to [Crucible of Worlds](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crucible+of+Worlds). Check out this version by FiatLux featuring [Mana Vortex](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Vortex) and [Smokestack](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smokestack) to keep its opponent under the gun!
