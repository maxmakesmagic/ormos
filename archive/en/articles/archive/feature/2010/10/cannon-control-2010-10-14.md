The 2010 [State Championships](http://www.the2009s.com) have been played, and in Indiana Charles Morrison's White-Blue Control deck managed to finish third. What's so neat about Charles's deck? The boost it got from *Scars of Mirrodin*! [Lux Cannon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lux+Cannon) and [Ratchet Bomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ratchet+Bomb) allow Charles to control anything an opponent can throw at the deck. [Trinket Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinket+Mage) even serves to fetch up powerful cards like [Voltaic Key](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key) and [Everflowing Chalice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Everflowing+Chalice). Neat!
