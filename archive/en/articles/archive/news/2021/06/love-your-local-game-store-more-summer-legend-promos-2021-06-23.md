Love Your Local Game Store with More Summer of Legend Promos
============================================================



 Posted in **News**
 on June 23, 2021 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Love Your Local Game Store is back and bigger than before as [the Summer of Legend continues](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/news/welcome-summer-legend-2021-05-06)!


In-Store Retro Frame Promos
===========================


If you've shopped in your local game store recently, you may have received this retro frame promotional version of [Fabled Passage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fabled+Passage).


![Retro Frame Fabled Passage](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/en_w37sdjsywd.png)


Beginning July 23, there are even more retro frame promos coming to your local game store.


![Retro Frame Aven Mindcensor](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/U1QYda7BuS.png)![Retro Frame Dig Through Time](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/8abGGS1Dso.png)


![Retro Frame Bolas's Citadel](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/ffdGhrKu0N.png)![Retro Frame Goblin Guide](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/ryhiPzaiBT.png)![Retro Frame Scavenging Ooze](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/m3LUMjvffU.png)


These five cards ([Aven Mindcensor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Mindcensor), [Dig Through Time](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dig+Through+Time), [Bolas's Citadel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bolas%27s+Citadel), [Goblin Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide), and [Scavenging Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scavenging+Ooze)) will be available by participating in in-store events, plus they come with an *MTG Arena* code to unlock a Japanese alternate-art Planeswalker Sleeve. Check with your local game store for details on how you can enjoy these upcoming cards!


*Adventures in the Forgotten Realms* Ampersand Promos
=====================================================


In addition to [*Mystery Booster: Convention Edition* coming to in-store events this August](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/news/mystery-booster-convention-edition-returns-store-events-2021-06-21), WPN Premium stores will also receive an amazing series of promotional versions of *Adventures in the Forgotten Realms* rares and mythic rares featuring the iconic *Dungeons & Dragons* ampersand.


![Ampersand Tiamat](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/YvnrRxuMwn.png)


These ampersand promos are traditional foil with a glossy D&D ampersand overlaying the entire card. Stores can offer these cards in a variety of ways, so be sure to find how you can receive one once they're available this August.
