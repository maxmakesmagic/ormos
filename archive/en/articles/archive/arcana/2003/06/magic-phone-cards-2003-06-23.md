**Magic** phone cards
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 23, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











During 2002, Wizards and MCI released a few phone card bearing **Magic** artwork. The cards were available primarily at premier stores, and had came 30-, 60-, and 100-minute increments.

Although the cards no longer work (they expired on 12/31/02), they still are interesting collectibles.
