***Magic** 2012* Promo Cards
============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 8, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











The prerelease for the ***Magic** 2012* Core Set will be on July 9-10. When you play in one, asssuming supplies hold out, you'll get this alternate-art promo card:


![Bloodlord of Vaasgoth](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/728_bczqtlbbbk.jpg)  
The launch party will be one week later, July 15-18. And again, while supplies last, there will be a foil alternate-art promo card. And this is it!


![Garruk's Horde](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/728_gdi9pyus0z.jpg)
