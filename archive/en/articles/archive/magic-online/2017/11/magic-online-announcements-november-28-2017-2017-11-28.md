**Vintage Super League tonight**


Vintage Super League (VSL) is live tonight at 5 p.m. PT on [Twitch](https://www.twitch.tv/magic)!


Interested in the decklists being played on VSL? Decklists are updated weekly and can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-season-7-decklists).

***Iconic Masters*now available on *Magic Online*!**


*Iconic Masters*is now available on *Magic Online*! Available in the *Magic Online* store for $6.99 per booster pack, *Iconic Masters*released simultaneously with tabletop *Magic*on Friday, November 17, and will be available until December 6.


*Magic Online*will feature two Draft leagues for this release, regular and phantom.


More information about the *Magic Online*release of *Iconic Masters*can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/iconic-masters-comes-magic-online).

**Standard PTQ this Saturday, December 2**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, December 2 at 9 a.m. PT. There is no need to qualify through a Preliminary—simply join this event with 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).


[Top](#top)

***Iconic Masters*Sealed PTQ Open this Sunday, December 3**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in this PTQ Open on Sunday, December 3 at 7 a.m. PT.


Join this event with 50 Tix or 500 Play Points.


Learn more about *Iconic Masters*on *Magic Online*[here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/iconic-masters-comes-magic-online)*.*

**Throwback Standard Gauntlet 10: Gauntlet of Greatness begins Wednesday, December 6**


Throwback Standard Gauntlet 10: Gauntlet of Greatness begins December 6 and will run until December 13. Expect Randy Buehler's article to be published tomorrow on [MTGO.com](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-products-game-info), including the Gauntlet's decklists.


More information about Throwback Standard Gauntlets can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/flashback-favorites-and-throwback-standard-gauntlets-2016-12-15).
