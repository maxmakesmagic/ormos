Friday Nights: "Politics"
=========================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic-archive)
 on November 6, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/lrrbiopic.png)
By LoadingReadyRun












The Alex Initiative seeks support as the Loading Ready Run crew do battle with Commander. Alex wields [Purphoros, God of the Forge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purphoros%2C+God+of+the+Forge); Kathleen battles with [Oona, Queen of the Fae](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oona%2C+Queen+of+the+Fae); Jeremy uses [Diaochan, Artful Beauty](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diaochan%2C+Artful+Beauty); while both Graham and Paul use the new *Commander* (2013 Edition) [Derevi, Empyrial Tactician](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Derevi%2C+Empyrial+Tactician). Meanwhile, James and Cam decide to make a new Cube!
