Top 8 Players of Grand Prix Barcelona 2016
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 17, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After 15 rounds, with a total of 2,076 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!




---






### Fabrizio Anteri



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpbar16/t8_anteri.jpg)



**Age:** 26




**Occupation:**




**Hometown:** London, England




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Nine Grand Prix Top 8s, four wins.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Wolf of Devil's Breach](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolf+of+Devil%27s+Breach). Red-white. 8-1.



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Town Gossipmonger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Town+Gossipmonger). Red-white. 3-0.



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Call the Bloodline](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+the+Bloodline). Black-green splash white. 2-1.




---






### Mehdi Saadi



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpbar16/t8_saadi.jpg)



**Age:** 24




**Occupation:** Pharmacy student




**Hometown:** Paris, France




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Went 10-4-1 at Grand Prix Madrid in September.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Tireless Tracker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tireless+Tracker). I was playing a green-white Clue deck. 8-1.



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Accursed Witch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accursed+Witch). Black-Green Delirium. 2-0-1.



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Pack Guardian](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Guardian) and [Tireless Tracker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tireless+Tracker). Green-Blue Clue Value. 3-0.




---

**Hometown:** Boston, MA, USA




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Hall of Fame 2013.

[Silverfur Partisan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silverfur+Partisan). Red-green. 2-1.

[Markov Dreadknight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Markov+Dreadknight). White-black. 2-0-1.




---






### Viktors Kazanskis



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpbar16/t8_kazanskis.jpg)



**Age:** 26




**Occupation:** Project manager




**Hometown:** Riga, Latvia




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Attended two Pro Tours.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



White-Black No Rares. Every removal spell was good.



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Black-red splash green for [Arlinn Kord](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arlinn+Kord). She won a couple of games. 3-0.



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



White-red. [Fiery Temper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper). 2-0-1.




---






### Brian Braun-Duin



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpbar16/t8_bbd.jpg)



**Age:** 29




**Occupation:** Writer at ChannelFireball.com




**Hometown:** Roanoke, VA, USA




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Four Grand Prix Top 8s. Two Grand Prix wins.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**




[Accursed Witch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accursed+Witch). I played blue-black and went 9-0.




**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Thing in the Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thing+in+the+Ice). I drafted a really bad blue-green deck and barely squeaked out a 1-2.



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Three copies of [Topplegeist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Topplegeist). I played Green-White Aggro/Delirium and went 3-0.




---






### Shaun McLaren



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpbar16/t8_mclaren.jpg)



**Age:** 28




**Occupation:** Professional *Magic* player and content creator for StarCityGames.com




**Hometown:** Edmonton, Alberta, Canada




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**




 Winner of Pro Tour *Born of the Gods*. Second at Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir*. Three Grand Prix Top 8s.




**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Red-Green Werewolves with two [Pack Guardian](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Guardian)s. 8-1.



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Arlinn Kord](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arlinn+Kord). Red-Green Werewolves. 2-1. Had a bunch of 2-drops.



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Green-Blue Clues with [Jace, Unraveler of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+Unraveler+of+Secrets), [Tamiyo's Journal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tamiyo%27s+Journal), and two [Fleeting Memories](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleeting+Memories).
