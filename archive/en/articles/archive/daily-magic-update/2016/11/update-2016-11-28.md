What People Are Talking About
=============================


1. [We have a new Scale on our hands—the Rabiah Scale!](http://markrosewater.tumblr.com/post/153695449493/so-the-plane-storm-scale-would-be-the-rabiah)

This was a funny one to [gain traction](https://www.reddit.com/r/magicTCG/comments/5f7fy4/we_have_a_new_scale_on_our_hands_the_rabiah_scale/) over the weekend. For those who are confused (I expect that number is high), Mark Rosewater maintains a Storm Scale that measures how likely a mechanic is to return (or how unlikely). So the Rabiah Scale, as it is apparently now called, looks at how likely we are to revisit planes for future sets—as in, "We're probably not going to be returning to Rabiah (*Arabian Nights*), so it's a 10 on the Rabiah Scale."

Deck of the Day
===============


1. Blue-Black Metallurgic Summonings

For as little as I hear about this deck, I sure do play against versions of it online a lot. And usually lose to it.


The key interaction is [Metallurgic Summonings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metallurgic+Summonings) and [Part the Waterveil](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Part+the+Waterveil), which gives you a 6/6 and an extra turn without all of that awakening nonsense. But every part of the deck gets better with [Metallurgic Summonings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metallurgic+Summonings) in play. Suddenly [Glimmer of Genius](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimmer+of+Genius), already a strong control card, comes with a free, instant-speed 4/4. [Essence Extraction](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Essence+Extraction) drops a 3/3 into play. And [Torrential Gearhulk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk) gets downright silly, bringing a friend along with whatever spell you cast.


And if you get in a pickle or can't seem to keep a creature around, Summonings can sacrifice itself to bring back every spell in your graveyard. This particular list went 4-0 at a [Standard Showdown](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/standard-showdown), so I would highly recommend taking it out for a spin.
