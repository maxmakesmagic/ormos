Draft 1: Courtney's Boys vs. Slay-Pillage-Gerard
================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 28, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall




 Brian David-Marshall is a New York–based game designer who has been involved with Magic since 1994, when he started organizing tournaments and ran a Manhattan game store. Since then, he has been a judge, a player, and one of the longest-tenured columnists on DailyMTG.com, as he enters his second decade writing for the site. He is also the Pro Tour Historian and one of the commentators for the Pro Tour. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptbos02/a898.jpg)As reported yesterday, Courtney's Boys spent the last ten days preparing for this event at Bob's house in Wisconsin. When pushed for details about their testing methods, Gary revealed that it largely consisted of Neil and Bob watching over Gary's shoulder while he played Magic Online. Gary felt that his famed limited skillz had atrophied during his time in England and needed to have Bob and Neil mocking his misplays in order to sharpen his game.

"All kidding aside," confided Wise, "If you are asking me if we are prepared for this event...

"We are."

After playtesting against the high quality pool of players at Neutral Ground and Togit, Slay Pillage Fabiano should be well tested in drafting against quality opponents. 

Slay Pillage Fabiano won the coin toss and they elected to have their opponents open their packs first.

***Odyssey* pack one: Neil Reeves**

Pack one offered nothing in the way of bombs and Neil started of with a solid—although underwhelming—[Krosan Archer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Archer). Bob took an [Aven Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Flock) and when the picks came around to SPF there was little left for them. Jon got a Morgue theft, Gerard took a [Scrivener](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrivener) and Scott picked up [Aven Cloudchaser](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Cloudchaser). Gary got to wheel a Fledling Imp and [Auramancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Auramancer). 

***Odyssey* pack two: Bob Maher**

Bob opened a much better pack than the first one and was looking at the traditional U/W cocktail in the C-Seat after he picked up a [Cephalid Retainer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Retainer). Jon grabbed [Morbid Hunger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger) while Gerard and Scott picked up [Treetop Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Sentinel) and [Nantuko Disciple](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Disciple). Gary took a [Cephalid Scout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Scout) and Neil was able to wheel a [Chatter of the Squirrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chatter+of+the+Squirrel) and [Muscle Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Muscle+Burst).

***Odyssey* pack three: Jon Sonne**

The first three picks of this pack would belong to SPF and they were happy with their choices. Jon got [Ghastly Demise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghastly+Demise), Gerard picked up a second [Treetop Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Sentinel), making green a poor proposition for Neil—in fact, Gerard thought the second Sentinel would send Neil out of green, and Scott picked up an [Aven Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Flock). The next three picks were less exciting with Dwarven recruiter, [Leaf Dancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leaf+Dancer), and [Seafloor Debris](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seafloor+Debris).

***Odyssey* pack four: Gerard Fabiano**

Gerard started off with [Dreamwinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder) and Scott picked up another [Aven Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Flock). Gary grabbed a [Diligent Farmhand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diligent+Farmhand), threatening to move intop B/G. Neil got Flameburst and Bob got an [Aven Fisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Fisher) that Gerard pish-poshed when the pack was opened and Jon got to wheel [Fledgling Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Imp) and [Blessed Orator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blessed+Orator).

***Odyssey* pack five: Scott McCord**

Scott gave the first indication that he was thinking about R/W when he first picked a [Pardic Firecat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pardic+Firecat). Gary got the all-important [Whispering Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whispering+Shade), Neil got a [Ghastly Demise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghastly+Demise), and Bob picked up Cephaild Scout. 

***Odyssey* pack six: Gary Wise**

Gary opened the best pack so far and it couldn't come at a better position for his team with the first three picks coming to them. Gary nabbed [Ghastly Demise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghastly+Demise), Neil picked up [Ember Beast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Beast) over [Liquid Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liquid+Fire)--at Bob's insistance, and Bob took [Kirtar's Wrath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kirtar%27s+Wrath). There were still solid picks for SPF and Jon took a [Seton's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Desire), Gerard took [Cephalid Scout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Scout) and Scott took [Reckless Charge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reckless+Charge) and [Liquid Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liquid+Fire) on the wheel. Gerard even got his second [Dreamwinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder) on the way back.

At this point the decks were shaping up to look like this:

**Gary:** B/X where X = either green or white.  
**Neil:** G/R possibly touching black.  
**Bob:** U/W with Retainer and Wrath

**Jon:** B/X where X = either green or white  
**Gerard:** U/X where X = green or—less likely—white.  
**Scott:** W/X where X = red or green.

***Torment* pack one: Gary Wise**

Gary's pick was pretty simple and he took the Butcher. Unfortunately, the rest of the pack was going to get picked over by SPF before his team would get another crack and there were some goodies to be had. Scott followed through on his threat to play red when who chose [Petravark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Petravark). Gerard made a controversial pick when he chose [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) over [Llawan, Cephalid Empress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llawan%2C+Cephalid+Empress). His whole team seemed to be on board with the plan and Jon took an [Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm). Bob grabbed a [Militant Monk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Militant+Monk) and the game breaking Llawan made it all the way around into the hands of the player who was facing a blue deck. The pack was so deep that Bob had to defensively draft a [Carrion Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carrion+Wurm) on the way back.

***Torment* pack two: Scott McCord**

Scott was clearly in red when he took a first pick [Fiery Temper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper). Gerard took a [Soul Scourge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Scourge)—it was unclear at this point whether this was a defensive draft or a contingency plan in case Llawan made an appearance. Jon took a [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade) after lingering over [Sickening Dreams](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sickening+Dreams) for a few moments. The Dreams would make it all the way around to Gary.

***Torment* pack three: Gerard Fabiano**

Gerard took a [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) to open his pack and Jon grabbed a [Crippling Fatigue](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crippling+Fatigue). Bob snapped up a [Teroh's Faithful](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teroh%27s+Faithful) and Neil d-drafted a [Militant Monk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Militant+Monk). Gary took an [Angel of Retribution](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Retribution) and Scott got to wheel [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) and Flash of defiance.

***Torment* pack Four: Jon Sonne**

Jon took a [Chainer's Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainer%27s+Edict) over [Possessed Nomad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Possessed+Nomad) which went to Bob with the next pick. Neil hook dup with a [Skywing Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skywing+Aven) and Gary got a [Waste Away](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Waste+Away). Scott seemed pleased with [Temporary Insanity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temporary+Insanity) and Gerard showed he was thinking about black again when he indicated that he wanted the [Gloomdrifter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gloomdrifter) and [Organ Grinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Organ+Grinder) on the wheel.

***Torment* pack five: Bob Maher**

Courtney's Boys go three solid picks here with Bob taking [Hypochondria](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypochondria), Neil landing a [Fiery Temper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper) and Gary grabbing a [Cabal Torturer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Torturer). Scott reeled in his second [Petravark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Petravark) and Gerard and Jon picked up [Hydromorph Guardian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hydromorph+Guardian) and [Gravegouger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravegouger).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptbos02/a897.jpg)***Torment* pack six: Neil Reeves**

Neil opened a [Centaur Chieftain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Centaur+Chieftain) and seemed happy to pick it first. Gary took a [Soul Scourge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Scourge) and when the cards swung around to his side, Scott picked up a [Fiery Temper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper).

After the second set of pack everyone seemed locked in on their colors with the possible exception of Gary who was still B/X.

***Judgment* pack one: Neil Reeves**

Neil happily took a [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) with his first pick. Bob got to pick up a [Phantom Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Flock) and Jon took [Sudden Strength](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sudden+Strength). Gerard got a Wormfang drake and Scott picked up an [Ember Shot](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Shot).

***Judgment* pack two: Bob Maher**

U/W is always likely to be splashing a third color and Bob seemed perfectly happy to take an Arcane teachings with his first pick. Jon grabbed a [Nullmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nullmage+Advocate) and Gerard took [Elephant Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide). Scott nabbed a [Battlewise Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlewise+Aven). Gary took [Prismatic Strands](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Strands) and Neil took [Wormfang Behemoth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Behemoth).

***Judgment* pack three: Jon Sonne**

Jon took one of the most vital cards in the B on B match-up—[Filth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Filth)! Gerard recruited a [Phantom Tiger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Tiger) and Scott got an [Arcane Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcane+Teachings) of his own. [Earsplitting Rats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earsplitting+Rats) and [Nullmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nullmage+Advocate) were nabbed before Bob nabbed a [Vigilant Sentry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigilant+Sentry).

***Judgment* pack four: Gerard Fabiano**

Gerard nabbed a [Giant Warthog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Giant+Warthog) but not before checking that Jon had a way to deal with [Filth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Filth) that was going to make it to Gary. Jon fanned a handful of [Gravegouger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravegouger)s and Gerard just let it go. Scott took a [Shieldmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shieldmage+Advocate) and Gary did get the [Filth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Filth). Neil picked up barbarian Bully and Jon got to wheel [Sutured Ghoul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sutured+Ghoul) and [Anurid Barkripper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anurid+Barkripper).

***Judgment* pack five: Scott McCord**

Scott took swelter, and Werewolf, [Nullmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nullmage+Advocate), and Wind Drake were all scooped up in that order by Bob's tram.

***Judgment* pack six: Gary Wise**

Gary got a [Battle Screech](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battle+Screech) and seemed thrilled to have it. Neil took breaking Point and Bob got a [Shieldmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shieldmage+Advocate).

Both teams seemed to have a pretty good idea of what they were up against. Everyone agreed that the Fabiano match was going to come down to Llawan—although Gerard was positive he would win.

Jon Sonne and Gary Wise's match was clearly going to come down to [Filth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Filth). Jon felt confident that he could contain the [Filth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Filth) with his three [Gravegouger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravegouger)s.
