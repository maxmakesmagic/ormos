The world of Standard is alive with creativity. Ultimate red mage Patrick Sullivan clearly believes tales of [Squadron Hawk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squadron+Hawk)s and Jaces are overrated. He made the Top 8 of the StarCityGames.com Invitational with this innovative red build. [Furnace Scamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Furnace+Scamp)? That's right; he had it main deck!
