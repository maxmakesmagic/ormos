Mono-Black Infect
=================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 28, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











With *Zendikar* block out of Standard, some under-the-radar archetypes from *Scars of Mirrodin* block have been given a new stage to shine on. Take, for example, Mono-Black Infect. After the archetype was championed by sage deck builder Gerry Thompson all week, former U.S. National Champion Joshua Wagener took it to the Top 8 of the StarCityGames.com Open Series in Baltimore. Try out this powerful budget-friendly option tonight at Friday Night **Magic**!
