It's a Revolution! Chicago area native Chris Boomer is intent on bringing Warp World back into power in Standard. He played this list of the deck to a Top 8 at a recent PTQ. The goal of the deck? Building up as many permanents into play as possible, then blowing the world up with [Warp World](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warp+World). Thanks to token generators and acceleration like [Fertile Ground](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fertile+Ground), he gets to reveal more cards than his opponent, all while fueling his draw engine with [Mulldrifter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter) and [Elvish Visionary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Visionary) and even getting [Warp World](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warp+World) back with [Nucklavee](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nucklavee)!
