DRAFTING WITH SHUHEI NAKAMURA
=============================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX SHANGHAI 2014 - COVERAGE - EVENTS](/en/events/coverage/gpsha14)
 on October 4, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











"Who is the draft master in the room? You?" I asked.


"I don't know how to draft. It's everyone's first time," Shuhei Nakamura professes.


The Hall-of-Famer jokingly proclaimed that he is a semi-retired player, referring to his lackluster season that ended with him achieving "only" Gold Level. Of course, we know that he isn't slowing down at all, since he did well enough yesterday to wind up in Pod 1. Today, we will take a peek at how he approaches the all-new draft format.




*Shuhei Nakamura* 




  
Booster One
===========



 Nakamura almost instantly decided upon [Hooded Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hooded+Hydra) as his very first pick, and agonized over his second. He eventually decided on [Ainok Bond-Kin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ainok+Bond-Kin) over [Alabaster Kirin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Alabaster+Kirin), clearly valuing two-drops highly in the format. He stuck to his colors, picking [Alabaster Kirin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Alabaster+Kirin) and [Timely Hordemate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Timely+Hordemate) next, before deviating momentarily by grabbing a couple of blue cards. A pair of late [Jungle Hollow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jungle+Hollow)s ensued, and it looked like he was poised to move into Abzan despite only having [Rite of the Serpent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rite+of+the+Serpent) as his only black card.




[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Hooded%20Hydra)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hooded%20Hydra)

*A great start to a great draft!*

Booster Two
===========



 When [Armament Corps](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Armament+Corps) stared at him in the second booster, Nakamura confirmed his Abzan inclinations and never looked back. His second booster was not impressive but he did pick up a second copy of [Timely Hordemate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Timely+Hordemate), a card that he values highly due to the card and tempo advantage it offered. His deck looked devoid of any spells at all, and was looking to fill the void in the next booster.

*One of the best uncommons the Abzan player could hope for.*

Booster Three
=============



 What more could the Abzan player ask for other than [Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino)? Not many others apparently, and Nakamura was glad to rip open this bomb. [Sandsteppe Citadel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sandsteppe+Citadel) was his second pick, confirming our suspicions that tri-colored lands are indeed that high of a pick. He was fortunate enough to snap up double [Smite the Monstrous](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smite+the+Monstrous), [Debilitating Injury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Debilitating+Injury) and [Throttle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Throttle) to complete his deck. Since he needed spells in the final booster, it was no surprise to see him ship [Abzan Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abzan+Guide) in favor of [Smite the Monstrous](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smite+the+Monstrous). A duo of [Mardu Skullhunter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mardu+Skullhunter)s would work very well alongside the two copies of [Timely Hordemate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Timely+Hordemate) that he had already drafted.




[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Siege%20Rhino)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege%20Rhino)

*[Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino) adds another sparkle to an awesome deck.*

*Naughty Nakamura pretends to be camera-shy...* 




  
Nakamura seemed to be rather pleased with his deck and said that his draft had turned out rather well. He feels that the first few picks of the draft could be "sacrificed" and players should stay open until a clear signal can be read. This strategy turned out great for him and it wasn't until Pack Two that he settled on a three-color clan.


At the time of press, Nakamura had just won his first two rounds. This bumps him up to 10-1 and will soon face one of the three undefeated players from yesterday's Sealed Deck portion. Will China's National Champion Han Bing be able to withstand the power of the Abzan Houses?
