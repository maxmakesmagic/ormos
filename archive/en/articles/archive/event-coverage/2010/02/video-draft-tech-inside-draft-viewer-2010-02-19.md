Video Draft Tech: Inside the Draft Viewer
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 19, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_46.png)
By Brian David-Marshall and Rich Hagon











Step deep inside the first draft from Pro Tour-San Diego as Rich Hagon and Brian David-Marshall get pick-by-pick analysis from Jeroen Kanis, Patrick Chapin, Gabriel Nassif, and Dennis Stone. The four players were sitting next to each other in Draft 1, Pod 2 on Friday. You can see all their picks in [the Draft Viewer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/magic/draftools/draftviewer.asp?draftid=2_19_2010_1).
