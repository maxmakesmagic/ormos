2005 Canada National Championship
---------------------------------


Jason Olynyk Leads Canadian Rat Pack to Worlds!
===============================================




August 27-28, 2005











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/canat05/fin.jpg)The roster for the Canadian National team has been set. Jason Olynyk (center) will lead a squad that includes J. Evan Dean (right) and 15-year-old Tyler Blum. Jason and Evan engaged in some rat-on-rat violence in the finals while Tyler had to finesse his way through a tricky [Memnarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnarch) mirror match in the 3rd/4th playoff.


*Ninth*  Edition was represented by [Llanowar Wastes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Wastes), [Hypnotic Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypnotic+Specter), [Annex](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Annex), [Tidings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tidings), [Blackmail](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blackmail), and [Nekrataal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nekrataal) in the Top 8. BlueTooth proved it will continue to be a force to be reckoned with throughout the remainder of the Nationals season, while the green-black Viridian Rats deck dominated the Top 8 with six of the eight players wielding it.


Next up is Japanese Nationals! Tune in September 2nd to see how these developments evolve as some of the game's top deck designers tackle the new Standard metagame.
