Magic Online Treasure Chest Information
=======================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on November 15, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Treasure Chests are a type of prize on *Magic Online*! Details about Treasure Chest contents can be found in the dropdowns below. For more information about *Magic Online* Treasure Chests, read the full announcement article [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/announcing-treasure-chests-2016-09-29).


Last Updated: 11/15/2017




Treasure Chest Contents
-----------------------





The following is a breakdown of what you can expect to find in each slot of a *Magic Online* Treasure Chest.




| Slot | Curated | Modern Rare/Mythic | Exclusive | Play Points | Standard Commons/Uncommons |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Slot 1 | 22.0% | 43.0% | 5.0% | 30.0% | 0.0% |
| Slot 2 | 6.5% | 10.0% | 5.0% | 0.0% | 78.5% |
| Slot 3 | 1.0% | 8.0% | 5.0% | 0.0% | 86.0% |

A Play Points pull will be a number of Play Points. The Frequency Ratio in the section below indicates how often a quantity of Play Points appears relative to other quantities.Curated cards are from a list curated by *Magic* R&D. Some curated cards may have multiple versions that appear. The frequency at which a curated card appears relative to others is also curated by *Magic* R&D. The rarity on the card shown reflects only the original printing of the card and is not indicative of its frequency of appearance in Treasure Chests. Curated cards are non-premium, including both *Zendikar Expeditions* and *Kaladesh Inventions*.


Modern-set rare or mythic rares will appear as from those sets. This doesn't include sets that were never Standard-legal, such as *Modern Masters* sets. Regular rares and mythic rares in these sets that have been banned in the Modern format are still included. Each regular rare will appear twice as often as any given mythic rare. This includes regular rares from older sets that lacked mythic rares. Timeshifted cards from *Time Spiral*count as regular rares for this purpose. All cards in Treasure Chests will be non-premium, with the exception of rares from *Eighth Edition* and *Ninth Edition*, which will be premium.


Standard-set commons and uncommons will appear as from those sets. Each common will appear 2.5 times as often as any uncommon. These cards will be non-premium. Exclusives are cards or versions of cards on Magic Online that are released only through Magic Online Treasure Chests rather than through a booster set. They have a Treasure Chest expansion symbol.

Treasure Chest Play Points
--------------------------





The first slot of a *Magic Online* Treasure Chest has a chance at containing a number of Play Points. If this happens, the table below shows the odds of each card appearing. The Frequency Ratio represents their relative frequency over a large number of treasure chests. For example, treasure chests will contain 20 Play Points five times as often as 80 Play Points.
 



|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Play Points** | **Frequency Ratio** |
| 10 | 500 |
| 15 | 500 |
| 20 | 500 |
| 30 | 300 |
| 40 | 300 |
| 80 | 50 |
| 120 | 50 |
| 1000 | 1 |

Treasure Chest Curated Card List
--------------------------------





Some cards have multiple versions in the *Magic Online* Treasure Chest. These are listed separately with the set code for that version listed. Note that some cards can appear as either a Modern-set rare or mythic or as a curated card.


The Frequency Ratio represents their relative frequency over a large number of Treasure Chests. Note that curated cards that are Modern rares and mythic rares can appear either as a curated card or a Modern rare or mythic rare. The below number reflect only when it is chosen as a curated card.


A note on set symbols: PRM and PR2 are promos which use the *Magic Online* promo set symbol. PZ1 cards appeared in the Legendary Cube Prize Packs and use that set symbol. PZ2 cards use the *Magic Online* Treasure Chest symbol. EXP cards are expeditions from *Battle for Zendikar* and *Oath of the Gatewatch*. MS2 cards are masterpieces from *Kaladesh* and *Aether Revolt*.
