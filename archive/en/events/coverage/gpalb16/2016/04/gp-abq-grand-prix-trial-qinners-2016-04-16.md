GP ABQ – Grand Prix Trial Winners
=================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 16, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











Though the main event is technically the first time to play Grand Prix­–level *Shadows over Innistrad* Sealed, it is far from the first time to play Sealed at the event itself. In the Grand Prix Trials on Friday night, players can earn themselves byes for the next day. And in doing so, get the true first cracks at *Shadows* Sealed before Saturday!


Below are all the Grand Prix trial winners. In addition to the nine Sealed trials there was one Standard (complete with a spicy, spicy list), and one Legacy. Enjoy!
