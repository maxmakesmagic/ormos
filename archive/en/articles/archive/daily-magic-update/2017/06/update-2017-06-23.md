The June 23, 2017 Update
========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on June 23, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Nicholas_Wolfram.jpg)
By Nicholas Wolfram




 Nicholas Wolfram fell promptly in love with tabletop and video games when he was only two years old, and he has been writing about them since he figured out how words worked. Now he puts together words about Magic, which he started playing way back in 2000. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on June 23, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by [Sliver Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sliver+Queen), the first tournament-legal creature to publicly welcome the five-color rainbow of *Magic* into her heart with pride.


Today's Previews
================


1. [Resilient Khenra](http://cardshop-serra.com/material/column/8299) | Cardshop Serra

日本語のプリビューか。すごいですね! Er . . . For those of you who have no idea what any of this says, I'd like to introduce Resilient Khenra:


Resilient Khenra

2/2  

When Resilient Khenra enters the battlefield, you may have target creature get +X/+X until end of turn, where X is Resilient Khenra's power.

After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [5 Little Tricks of Team Draft](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/lee-shi-tian/5-little-tricks-of-team-draft) | MTG Mint Card | Lee Shi Tian

If you've been following [Team Draft Super League](https://vintagesuperleague.com/tdsl-schedule/), you have likely been harboring a burgeoning interest in Team Draft. This is the perfect time for such an interest, too, with Team Limited showing up this weekend at both [Grand Prix Sydney](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpsyd17) and [Grand Prix Cleveland](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpcle17). If you need some advice on the difference between regular Draft and Team Draft, Lee has it.


1. [Ben Stark, More Level-Ups, New Block Structure, and HOU Mechanics](http://lrcast.com/limited-resources-393-ben-stark-more-level-ups-new-block-structure-and-hou-mechanics/) | Limited Resources | Marshal Sutcliffe and Luis Scott-Vargas

I'd love to tell you more about the episode, but . . . the title kind of says it all. There's a lot of ground covered here, so make sure you tune in!


1. [Magic the Amateuring: LIVE from Las Vegas!](http://www.magictheamateuring.com/mta-episode-230-live-from-las-vegas/) | Magic the Amateuring | Meghan Wolff and Maria Bartholdi

Graham Stark of LoadingReadyRun and Wizards's own Melissa DeTora join Meghan and Maria in front of a live studio audience for this latest episode of MtA.


1. [Vintage 101: It's Devastation Time!](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/vintage-101-it-s-devastation-time?src=wizards) | MTG Goldfish | Islandswamp

With almost 25 years of *Magic* cards supporting it, Vintage is a format that doesn't get shaken up terribly often. But if there's one thing that the format loves, it's more hate cards, and the destruction-themed *Hour of Devastation* has already shown us plenty of those. Islandswamp brings evaluations of those along with some more supportive cards we've revealed.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Mono-Red Eldrazi Burn](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-06-22-0)

Between the [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) banning just going into effect and the release of *Hour of Devastation* coming in a few weeks, we're in for a bit of a "Wild West" of Standard for the next month or so. Appropriately, The\_Gunslingers managed to pilot an archetype we haven't seen much of lately through this new environment to a 5-0 victory during a recent Standard *Magic Online* League.
