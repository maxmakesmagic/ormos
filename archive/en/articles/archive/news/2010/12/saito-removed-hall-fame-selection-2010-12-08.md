Saito Removed from Hall of Fame Selection
=========================================



 Posted in **News**
 on December 8, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/halloffame.jpg)Tomoharu Saito’s status as a **Magic: The Gathering** Pro Tour Hall of Fame member-elect has been rescinded and he will not be inducted into the Hall of Fame at the 2010 World Championships. 

Wizards of the Coast continually strives to apply consistent standards within the Pro Tour system and throughout all of our organized play programs.
