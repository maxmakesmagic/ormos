Obviously the Best Deck
=======================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on February 6, 2014 










We've seen a few different flavors of [Show and Tell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell) strategies in Legacy. Some are mono-blue with [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) (and win with [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish)), some are blue-red (and win with [Sneak Attack](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sneak+Attack) and fatties), and there are some mish-mashes containing elements of both, with or without [Burning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish).



![Show and Tell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?size=small&type=card&name=Show%20and%20Tell&options=)Today I have another version.

**Magic Online** player "obv" is well-known in the real world as well and is no stranger to combo decks. His update to [Show and Tell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell) includes six discard spells and [Omniscience](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience), with [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) instead of [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), which seems like an upgrade for a couple reasons.


First of all, you get a full fifteen-card sideboard instead of having to play tutor targets. Secondly, [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) functions as a tutor for your combo pieces, which [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) never did. The downside is that you don't win immediately, but you don't have to play [Dream Halls](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dream+Halls) (which is slow and unwieldy) and you have more, better things to [Show and Tell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell), like [Griselbrand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Griselbrand).


There are pros and cons of each version, but obv's version is new, exciting, and potentially the best version.
