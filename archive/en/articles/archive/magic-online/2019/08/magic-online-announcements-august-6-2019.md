You Choose the Flashback
------------------------


Currently in quarterfinals, the [You Choose the Flashback](https://wizardsmtgo.tumblr.com/post/186472835619/you-choose-the-flashback) bracket wraps up on Friday at [twitter.com/magiconline](https://twitter.com/magiconline). The winning format will be played August 21–28. Stay tuned for next week's blog that will announce the winner.

*Dominaria* Phantom Flashback Draft
-----------------------------------


[Call the Cavalry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=442897), because we're having an Dominaria Phantom Flashback Draft League! Dominaria is ridiculously fun to draft and full of possibilities . . . [Seal Away](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+Away) your workday stress and get your draft on August 6–14! Entry options are 10 Event Tickets, 2 *Dominaria* boosters + 2 Event Tickets, or 100 Play Points.




---

Chaos Draft! August 14
----------------------


Flex your deck-building skills during next week's CHAOS DRAFT!, a format that requires players to contemplate meta-archetypes over the curated archetypes typically found in a cube. CHAOS DRAFT! starts August 14 and runs for a week—plenty of time to smash face amidst the [Pandemonium](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pandemonium).

Next Spotlight Cube: *Grixis Cube*
----------------------------------


We know a lot of you really enjoyed the last Spotlight Cube. The next one, launching a week from tomorrow, is a doozy, too. Ryan Overturf ([@RyanOverdrive](https://twitter.com/RyanOverdrive) on Twitter) has a real treat for us: the *Grixis Cube*. Starting August 28, you can pretend that green and white don't exist and draft a Cube with deep archetypes and deeper dives into the three (and a half) supported colors! Expect a full design article talking about the Cube and the details of its awesomeness next Tuesday.

*Core Set 2020*: *Magic Online* Edition
---------------------------------------


Core sets are back! *Core Set 2020* events are well underway on *Magic Online* and [redemption begins today](https://magic.wizards.com/en/gameinfo/products/magiconline/redemption), so now's the time to get in on the action. Seize your chance to play *Magic* in its purest form with this new core set, which features prominently the fiery Planeswalker Chandra Nalaar.


For more info about M20 on MTGO, including event details, check out Alli Medwin's article [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/core-set-2020-magic-online-edition-2019-06-28).




---

*Modern Horizons*—Extra Time Added!
-----------------------------------


*Modern Horizons* events are ongoing and boosters are on sale, *and* we're happy to announce that events will now be available until August 28 (previously August 14). Lots of folks are really enjoying this Limited format, so join the fun and log in to draft today! Draft Leagues, Sealed Leagues, and Phantom Draft Leagues are all available.


Check out [Chris's release article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/state-magic-online-modern-horizons-play-lobby-2019-05-23) for additional info on *Modern Horizons* events, and you can also learn more about the Open Play option changes in *Magic Online*'s new Play Lobby from [his Tumblr post](https://wizardsmtgo.tumblr.com/).

Pauper Premier League
---------------------


Our friends at CoolStuffInc.com are hosting a new Pauper Premier league. You can check out the action every Thursday at 6:00 p.m. PT. Check out the [announcement video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dlzIR12bVOs)!

Team Modern Super League
------------------------


The newest Super League season is here with the return of the Team Modern format! Eight teams (including Wizards' Play Design group) will battle over 13 weeks for bragging rights in Modern, with playoff rounds continuing this week. The broadcast begins at 6 p.m. PT every Tuesday at [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic).


For more information and team rosters, click [here](https://vintagesuperleague.com/).

Limited MCQ Finals Friday
-------------------------


On Friday, August 9, there will be a Limited MCQ Finals at 3 p.m. PT. The set will be *Core Set 2020*, and each event will cost 1 Limited MCQ Finals Token. The winner will receive an invitation to Mythic Championship Richmond.


Learn more about Mythic Championship Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-mythic-championship-qualifiers).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format challenges are the first step toward qualifying for premier play through your favorite *Magic* formats. To learn more about Format Focus events and premier play qualification, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/2019-magic-online-championship-2018-11-12).


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges continue every weekend for Modern, Vintage, Legacy, and Pauper formats except when replaced by Format Playoffs during scheduled Format Playoff dates.
