Looking for a chance to use some of those commons you haven't played with in a while? The Pauper format is just for you! It allows players only cards of the common variety for building their decks. In fact, **Magic Online** recently held a Premier Event for the format. BryTheFryGuy managed to make the Top 8 with this take on the popular [Martyr of Sands](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Martyr+of+Sands) strategy. Using [Grim Harvest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Harvest) and [Disturbed Burial](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disturbed+Burial), Bry could set up a loop with [Martyr of Sands](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Martyr+of+Sands) that would continually generate massive amounts of life gain!
