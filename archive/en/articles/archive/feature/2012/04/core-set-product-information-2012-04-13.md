### Set Information


The ***Magic** 2013* Core Set features 249 black-bordered cards, including randomly inserted premium versions of all cards in the set. The ***Magic** 2013* Core Set is available in booster packs, intro packs, and fat packs


**Set Name**: ****Magic** 2013* Core Set*  
**Number of Cards**: 249  
**Twitter Hashtag**: [#MTGM13](https://twitter.com/#!/search/realtime/%23mtgm13)

### *Face a Greater Challenge*


Play **Magic: The Gathering** and discover why it’s the world’s premier trading card game. Start with a deck of cards filled with potent spells and fearsome creatures. Then take on the mantle of a Planeswalker, a powerful mage, and challenge your friends—and yourself—in the ultimate battle of wits.


**Prereleases**: July 7-8, 2012  
**Release Date**: July 13, 2012  
**Game Day**: August 4-5, 2012


**Design Team:** Doug Beyer (lead), Aaron Forsythe, Graeme Hopkins, Ryan Miller, Mark Purvis


**Development Team:** Zac Hill (lead), Ethan Fleischer, Mark L. Gottlieb, Tom LaPille, Max McCall, Ryan Miller
