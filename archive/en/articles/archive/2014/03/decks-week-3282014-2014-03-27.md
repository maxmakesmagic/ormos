Decks of the Week: 3/28/2014
============================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on March 28, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Last weekend, Kyle Boggemes beat out 1,700+ other players to win in Standard at [Grand Prix Cincinnati](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/gpcin14/welcome). Across the Atlantic, Aniol Alcaraz bested No. 1 ranked Jérémy Dezani in the finals at [Grand Prix Vienna](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/gpvie14/welcome). Congratulations, Kyle and Aniol, who will be going to Pro Tour ***Magic** 2015*  in Portland this August. You can join them by winning a Pro Tour Qualifier! The M15 PTQ season is Sealed Deck. Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#m15). Good luck; have fun!
