The Power Nine
==============



 Posted in [ARTICLES](/en/articles)
 on October 15, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










*Mirrodin* has folded words like "[Mox](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=47446)" and "[Lotus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=373335)" back into common *Magic* parlance. Why is this exciting? Because the original Moxen and Lotus form six of *Magic* 's so-called Power Nine, a nonuple of the most rule-breakingly powerful cards ever to grace cardboard. In case you've heard the term but don't know the cards, here's a rundown of the original Power Nine:
