Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 18, 2008 













![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpatl08/scottvargast8.jpg)

**Name** -Luis Scott-Vargas  
**Age** - 25  
**Hometown** - Oakland  
**Occupation** - Magician


**How long have you been playing competitive Magic** - 5 years


**What are your previous best finishes** - 1st PT Berlin, 1st Nationals '07


**What was your record day 1** - 7-2


**What was your record day 2** - 5-0-1


**What is your favorite Draft format and why** - MD5. Affinity is pretty fun, as is 14 lands!


**What card was the best for you this weekend** - [Tower Gargoyle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tower+Gargoyle)


**What shard would you live on if you lived in Alara** - Naya, since I'm an animal.

**Name** - Chris Pait  
**Age** - 30  
**Hometown** - Toledo, OH  
**Occupation** - Accounting Clerk


**How long have you been playing competitive Magic** - Too long


**What are your previous best finishes** - Top 8 GP KC


**What was your record day 1** - 7-2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpatl08/fennellt8.jpg)

**Age** - 25  
**Hometown** - Fort Myers, FL  
**Occupation** - High School Teacher, GO Tritons!!


**How long have you been playing competitive Magic** - 4/5 years


**What are your previous best finishes** -Winner GP DC trios, Top 4 GP St. Louis

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpatl08/thompsont8.jpg)
