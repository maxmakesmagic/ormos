Top 8 Profiles of Grand Prix Atlanta 2018
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 4, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_FrankKarsten.jpg)
By Frank Karsten











After 15 hard-fought rounds of Modern, with a total of 1,545 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running. Their Top 8 performance came with invitations and airfare to Pro Tour *Ravnica Allegiance* ... and the opportunity to fight for the title and the trophy.


Learn more about Grand Prix Atlanta's Top 8 competitors—including who they are, why they chose their Modern deck, and more—by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpatl18/GP_ATL18_Juza.JPG)


**Martin Jůza**


**Age**: 31


**Occupation**: *Magic* pro player


**Hometown**: Plzen, Czech Republic


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments**: 4 Pro Tour Top 8s, Hall of Fame class of 2017.


**What Modern deck did you play this weekend, and why did you choose it?** Hollow Boys. It's great and it can easily win through the sideboard hate.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)! Just ask my opponents.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?** [Leyline of the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leyline+of+the+Void). It just wins games on its own.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** 4-8 pro points, 1,500-10,000 USD.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpatl18/GP_ATL18_PeiyuanZheng.JPG)


**Peiyuan Zheng**


**Age**: 18


**Occupation**: Student


**Hometown**: Beijing, China


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments**: Lost two consecutive RPTQ win-and-PT. Several GP Top 32s.


**What Modern deck did you play this weekend, and why did you choose it?** Bant Spirits. I like this [Collected Company](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collected+Company) deck.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Collected Company](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collected+Company). I feel excited when using it because you don't know what you will get.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?** [Knight of Autumn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+Autumn), a creature that can handle multitasks.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Going to the PT, and earning money for a plane ticket to GP Portland.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpatl18/GP_ATL18_Larsen.JPG)


**Christoffer Larsen**


**Age**: 30


**Occupation**: Navy/school


**Hometown**: Copenhagen, Denmark


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments**: Got to my plane last weekend :) Playing Nassif in the semis [of Grand Prix Lille].


**What Modern deck did you play this weekend, and why did you choose it?** Played [Hardened Scales](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hardened+Scales).


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Mishra's Bauble](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Bauble). Best card to side out.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?** Not sure what to Claim.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Steak Dinner!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpatl18/GP_Atl18_Negri.JPG)


**Kazu Negri**


**Occupation**: Professional turn-3 killer


**Hometown**: Morgantown, WV


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments**: Won an Open, handful of 12-3s, that's about it.


**What Modern deck did you play this weekend, and why did you choose it?** Infect. After nearly a year of playing Jeskai Control, I didn't feel like working as hard for wins.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Distortion Strike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Distortion+Strike). It makes [Glistener Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glistener+Elf) suck a bit less.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?** [Distortion Strike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Distortion+Strike)! I wish I had 4.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** I'm excited as hell.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpatl18/GP_ATL18_JoaoChoca.JPG)


**Joao Choca**


**Age**: 27


**Occupation**: Marketer


**Hometown**: London, UK


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments**: Grand Prix Turin 2018 champion.


**What Modern deck did you play this weekend, and why did you choose it?** KCI. Rob Catton gave me a 75 with sideboard plans, and I didn't want to waste time thinking about Modern.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Sai, Master Thopterist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sai%2C+Master+Thopterist)—literally won every game I resolved it.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?** Same as above, except also Game 2/3s—Sai won every game she appeared in.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Almost Silver!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpatl18/GP_ATL18_TakumiUtsunomiya.JPG)


**Takumi Utsunomiya** 


**Age**: 23


**Occupation**: Student


**Hometown**: Saitama, Japan


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments**: GP Hongkong 2018 Top 8.


**What Modern deck did you play this weekend, and why did you choose it?** Bridge Vine. It's the best for me.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Vengevine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vengevine). Always helps me.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?** [Assassin's Trophy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Assassin%27s+Trophy). Destroys anything.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Helpful in becoming Gold.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpatl18/GP_ATL18_Utsunomiya.JPG)


**Yoshihiko Ikawa** 


**Age**: 32


**Occupation**: Fleeter


**Hometown**: Tokyo, Japan


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments**: Pro Tour San Diego 2010 Top 8


**What Modern deck did you play this weekend, and why did you choose it?** Tron. I love this deck.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Ulamog, the Ceaseless Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ulamog%2C+the+Ceaseless+Hunger). Three Ulamogs are the best!


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?** [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize). Tron needs one-mana disruption.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** This is my first Top 8 in an individual GP. So happy!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpatl18/GP_ATL18_Glogowski.JPG)


**Piotr Głogowski**


**Age**: 23


**Occupation**: Magician / Student / Streamer


**Hometown**: Poznań, Poland


**Previous Magic accomplishments**: Two Grand Prix Top 8s, Pro Tour Ixalan Top 8, WMC Finalist.


**What Modern deck did you play this weekend, and why did you choose it?** KCI, because there are no bad matchups, and also I like taking copious amounts of game actions.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Mox Opal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Opal) is strong.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?** [Sai, Master Thopterist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sai%2C+Master+Thopterist) is strong.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Internal and external validation, which I crave.
