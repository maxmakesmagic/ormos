Decks of the Week: 8/26/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on August 26, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Three countries, three National Champions, three different winning Standard decks! Last weekend showcased three major National Championships from around the world. Josh Utter-Leyton piloted Mythic Conscription to the winner's circle at U.S. Nationals, Dennis Johannsen used Red-Green Valakut Ramp to win Germany Nationals, and Joe Jackson played Naya Fauna Shaman to take home the Great Britain title. Along with the Top 8 deck lists from each event linked below, you can also find all 6-2 or better Standard lists from the United States and Germany.
