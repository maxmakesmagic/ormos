A week after Tsuyoshi Ikeda did it, Jan Schmidt repeated in Prague using an aggressive blue-white draft deck to take down the title! He had to do it in a Top 8 that featured Yuuya Watanabe, who had Top 8ed two Grand Prixs in back-to-back events leading into Prague. Jan's deck benefited from a healthy horde of flyers backed by solid blockers like [Siege Mastodon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Mastodon) to hold the ground and a triple-set of [Divination](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Divination) to make sure he never ran out of cards.
