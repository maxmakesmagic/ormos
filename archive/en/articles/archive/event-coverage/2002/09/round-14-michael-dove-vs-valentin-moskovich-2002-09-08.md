Round 14: Michael Dove vs. Valentin Moskovich
=============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 8, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_alexshvartsman.jpg)
By Alex Shvartsman











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcle02/a904.jpg)It is do or die time for the two competitors, as the winner of this match will advance to their first Grand Prix top 8. Moskovich is playing the ever-popular mono-black control deck, while Dove brought a white-green creature deck to the table.

Dove opened with [Anurid Brushhopper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anurid+Brushhopper). He phased it out pitching [Glory](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glory) and a land in response to an Edict, and summoned [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). He phased it out yet again in response to [Mutilate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate) but could not stop the Edict out of the graveyard, which allowed Moskovich to stabilize. Having removed two [Tireless Tribe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tireless+Tribe)s via [Innocent Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood)s, Moskovich summoned [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade) and attacked with it for fourteen. Dove top-decked a [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) which, cheered by [Glory](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glory) in the graveyard, could block the Shade indenfinitely, but Moskovich had yet another Edict! Its path clear, Shade delivered the killing blow.

Moskovich 1 - Dove 0

Dove played a turn 2 [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) and was able to pitch a [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) in response to [Innocent Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood). [Anurid Brushhopper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anurid+Brushhopper) followed. Lacking more removal, Moskovich had to contend himself with destroying a [Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) with [Rancid Earth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth). Down to thirteen life points, he summoned [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade) and blocked a [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) with it. It traded in combat with [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel), taking out a card from Dove's hand with it. Moskovich cast [Mind Sludge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Sludge) to get rid of a [Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) and a pair of [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur)s. On the following turn he cast Edict and then used [Haunting Echoes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haunting+Echoes) to remove most of Dove's dangerous cards from the game. 

Shade came out next turn and Moskovich was able to attack for twelve. Dove drew [Glory](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glory) and [Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm) neither of which he could cast with four lands. The match was over with more than thirty minutes remaining in the round. 

**Final Result:** Moskovich 2 - Dove 0
