The recent $5k Starcitygames.com tournament in Nashville awarded a large cash prize as well as shaking up the Standard format! Kali Anderson took the whole thing down with an Eldrazi Green deck designed to hold off Jund and Boros Bushwhacker decks with cards like [Llanowar Elves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) and [Nissa's Chosen](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa%27s+Chosen). It was a powerful choice for the weekend, but perhaps her toughest match was in the Quarterfinals where she squared off against her husband!
