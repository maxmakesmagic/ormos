 Daniel Almeida was all smiles at the end of Grand Prix-Sao Paulo! His Kithkin deck took the whole tournament down, and in resounding fashion. Nearly every card he played seemed to generate some form of card advantage for him, a rare feat for the beatdown deck. You can read more about Grand Prix-Sao Paulo in the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/daniel-dominates-defending-brazil).
