Decks of the Week: 07/06/2012
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 6, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Would you like to play against the best players in the world at [Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica*](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/seattle12-qualifiers) in Seattle, this October? If you would, you can consult [this handy PTQ list](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist&tablesort=1) to locate a Pro Tour Qualifier event near you. And if you do see one you can attend, make sure to have your Standard deck tweaked, honed, and ready to fight. To help you figure out what kinds of decks are popular (and, thus, you're likely to face), check out the Top 8 decklists from some of the recent PTQs (including those on **Magic Online**)!
