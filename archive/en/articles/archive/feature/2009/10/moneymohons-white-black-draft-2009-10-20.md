The first Pro Tour Qualifier, or PTQ, ever to be held on **Magic Online** was recently won by Nick Mohon (a.k.a. Money\_Mohon). He managed to plow his way through two Sealed pools, then a Top 8 draft. His white-black deck got a touch of help from a splashed [Earthquake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake), and Nick now finds himself qualified for Pro Tour–San Diego. All from the comfort of his own home!
