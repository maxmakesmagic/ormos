Decks of the Week: 5/24/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 24, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations to Craig Wescoe, winner of  [Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze*](/en/events/coverage/voice-raises-wescoe-above-all-pro-tour). The pro season concluded with the Pro Tour, which means we can also congratulate Josh Utter-Leyton as our 2012-2013 Player of the Year! And since this is Decks of the Week, it seems appropriate to applaud Rob Castellon, who made it to the Top 8 after an incredible undefeated run on Day One. And how did he get to the PT in the first place? Won a PTQ. You can too! Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros)! Good luck; have fun!
