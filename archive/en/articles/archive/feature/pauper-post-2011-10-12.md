Pauper Post
===========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 12, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Big-mana Post decks have posted fine finishes in a variety of formats. The deck's key cards—[Cloudpost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudpost) and [Glimmerpost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimmerpost)—are both commons, which let the.zzz pilot this EightPost deck to a 3-1 finish in a Pauper Daily Event on **Magic Online**.
