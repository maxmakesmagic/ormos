Factoids
========



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Interesting details and facts from the event, compiled by *Sideboard* staff.


* This is the first major event ever held in Chile.
* The Latin America Championship 2000 is the first Continental Championship held in Latin America. This will now become an annual event.
* It is winter here in Santiago and the temperature drops to around 30 degrees in the evening.
* This is the first big-money event that Juan Del Compare (level 3 certified judge from Argentina) has head-judged.
* The Latin America Championship is being held in Centro Cultural Estacion Mapocho which is a 100 year old train station beautifully restored as a magnificient event hall.
* The next large event in Latin America will be Grand Prix Buenos Aires in November, also head-judged by Juan Del Compare.
