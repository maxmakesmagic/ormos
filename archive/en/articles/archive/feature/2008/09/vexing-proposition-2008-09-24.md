The shard of Esper returns to **Magic** an often under-represented tribe: Sphinxes. That isn't to say the mythic creatures haven't been seen amongst the planes before, however. Iowa player Blake Rasmussen used *Coldsnap's* [Vexing Sphinx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vexing+Sphinx) to put together the above creation for TCGplayer.com. What do the return of Sphinxes to **Magic** mean for the denizens of *Shards of Alara*? You can find out at the *Shards of Alara*
[product page](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/shardsofalara).
