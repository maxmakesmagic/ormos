At first glance, Tomomi Shiraishi's Extended combo deck is very strange looking. [Summoner's Pact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoner%27s+Pact) in a deck with no green creatures? [Hive Mind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hive+Mind)? [Ethereal Usher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ethereal+Usher)? Here's how Tomomi used the innovative deck to go 4-2 on the Extended day at Worlds: [Ethereal Usher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ethereal+Usher) transmutes for [Hive Mind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hive+Mind). [Dark Ritual](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Dark%2BRitual)s allow Tomomi to accelerate out the expensive enchantment, then he simply casts [Pact of the Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pact+of+the+Titan) or [Summoner's Pact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoner%27s+Pact). [Hive Mind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hive+Mind) automatically copies the spell for Tomomi's opponent, who then very likely dies on his or her next upkeep, unable to pay for the Pact!
