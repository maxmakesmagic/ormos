Video Feature: An Extended Roundtable
=====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 3, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_46.png)
By Brian David-Marshall and Rich Hagon











On the eve of Pro Tour-Amsterdam and the debut of the new Extended, Brian David-Marshall sat down with Hall of Famer Frank Karsten, rogue deckbuilder Conley Woods, and R&D developer Zac Hill to dive deep into the format. If you want a leg up on the format or just tips on deckbuilding in general, tune in for exceptional insights from some of Magic's brightest minds.
