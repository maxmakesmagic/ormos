2011 National Championships
===========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 12, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/tournaments/images/nationals.gif)Held each year, National Championships are the highest level competition for a particular country. In 2011, players from 69 countries will compete in **Magic: The Gathering**  National Championships for the right to represent their countries at the 2011 World Championships held in San Francisco, USA on November 17-20, 2011.

National Championships are regulated by the Premier Event Invitation Policy. The latest version can be found in the [**Magic**  Document Center](http://www.wizards.com/wpn/Events/Rules.aspx?category=magic:thegathering).

Closed Nationals Information: 

* All closed Nationals require an invitation to participate. Complete invitation criteria can be found in the Premier Event Invitation Policy (located in the [**Magic**  Document Center](http://www.wizards.com/wpn/Events/Rules.aspx?category=magic:thegathering)).
* All closed Nationals have rating-based invitations. Those invitations are based on the ratings published on June, 2011. Those invitations will be officially awarded on June 1, 2011.
* All closed Nationals award Pro Points to the top finishers. To view the points awarded, [click here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=grandprix/points).

Open Nationals Information: 

* All citizens and residents of that country may participate.

Additional details will be added as countries' National Championship information is finalized.
