Alpha "Oops..." VII
===================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 4, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












One of the most famous misprints in Alpha (the very first edition of Magic) is the poor [Elvish Archers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Archers).


The first printing of the card had the power and toughness reversed, making it a 1/2 first striker for ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220123123600im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/green.gif)! I'm sure there was some consternation among players once they figured out that it was a rare, as even the correct version doesn't knock anyone's socks off.


Like most of the Alpha errors, the Archers were printed the way Richard Garfield intended -- as 2/1 -- in Beta.
