Magic 2014 Gold Bundle Coming to Steam
======================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 21, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1476_d14gold.png)  

This week we're launching our ***Magic** 2014—Duels of the Planeswalkers* bundles on Steam for anyone wants to grab the game before the launch of ***Magic** 2015—Duels of the Planeswalkers*.


Here are the items you'll be able to find: 

* **Gold Complete Bundle** - Containing: base game, its expansion, its three deck packs and all 21 unlocked decks and 21 foil conversions.
* **Gold Game Bundle** - Containing: base game, its expansion, and its three deck packs.
* **Gold Deck Unlocks Bundle** - Unlock all 21 decks at once (requires ***Magic** 2014*, ***Magic** 2014* Expansion, and ***Magic** 2014* Deck Packs 1, 2 & 3 to unlock all 21 decks.)
* **Gold Foils Bundle**  - Convert all 21 decks into foils (***Magic** 2014*, ***Magic** 2014* Expansion, and ***Magic** 2014* Deck Packs 1, 2 & 3 to convert all 21 decks.)
* **Gold Sealed Slot Bundle**  - Receive all 18 available Sealed Play Slots in one package.

Keep an eye on [***Magic** 2014* on Steam](http://store.steampowered.com/app/213850/) for when the bundles go on sale later this week!
