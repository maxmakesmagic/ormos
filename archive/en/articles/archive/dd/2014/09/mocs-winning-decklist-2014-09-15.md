[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Glimpse of Nature)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimpse of Nature)
The *Magic Online* Championship Series is back! Recently, the *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS for short) was won by Lantto, who played his way to the top of the Legacy format with an explosive Elves deck. He even defeated another Elves deck in the finals!


The core of this deck comes from generating lots of mana with [Gaea's Cradle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gaea%27s+Cradle) or the [Nettle Sentinel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel) + [Heritage Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid) combo. With this mana, [Glimpse of Nature](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimpse+of+Nature) allows you to draw lots of cards. Sometimes you can even draw your entire deck. Alternatively, [Natural Order](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Natural+Order) allows [Craterhoof Behemoth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Craterhoof+Behemoth) to provide a lethal attack with even a minimal board presence.


There are lots of subtle combinations between one-mana Elves in this deck, and this deck relies on lots of little tricks when [Glimpse of Nature](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimpse+of+Nature) is not involved. [Wirewood Symbiote](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Symbiote) can return an [Elvish Visionary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Visionary) to recast while untapping a [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman) for an additional activation. [Quirion Ranger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quirion+Ranger) can bounce [Dryad Arbor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dryad+Arbor) back to safety (from [Wasteland](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland) or an attacking creature for example). [Green Sun's Zenith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Green+Sun%27s+Zenith) helps assemble these combinations and mini-combinations, giving the deck lots of different options.
