Ask Wizards
===========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 4, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Ask Wizards is a weekly feature that allows you to ask us questions! If you'd like to submit your question please email it to [AskWizards@wizards.com](mailto:AskWizards@wizards.com). We aren't able to answer every question we receive but if your question is good then it might show up in the coming weeks!


 **Q: Do you have an Official Magic: The Gathering account on Instagram? If so, what is it? —Oscar**  


**A: Mike McArtor, copy editor for DailyMTG:** 


We do indeed! I asked Community Manager Sean Gibbons and he helpfully provided the link:


[instagram.com/wizards\_Magic](http://instagram.com/wizards_Magic#)


If you're on Instagram and like **Magic**, I would be remiss not to also point out Mark Rosewater's account:


[instagram.com/mtgmaro](http://instagram.com/mtgmaro#)
