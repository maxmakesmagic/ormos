9th to 16th Decklists of Grand Prix Birmingham 2018 (Legacy)
============================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 12, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











The Top 8 featured one Dredge, one Mono-Red Prison, and one Steel Stompy. Other than that, though, it was full of Forces, [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)s, Delvers, with a couple of [Leovold, Emissary of Trest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leovold%2C+Emissary+of+Trest) and [Kess, Dissident Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kess%2C+Dissident+Mage) thrown in for good measure.


One step further down in the standings, the picture looked similar, but also included additional archetypes like Stoneblade, Turbo Depths, [Food Chain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Food+Chain), and Sneak and Show:
