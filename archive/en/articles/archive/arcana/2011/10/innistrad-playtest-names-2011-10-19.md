*Innistrad* Playtest Names
==========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 19, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











As you may know, **Magic** cards have different names during playtesting. Why wouldn't they? They don't even have art during that stage! So here's what's going to happen. You'll look at some playtest names, then you'll guess what *Innistrad* card they ended up as. It'll be fun!



|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| False Lycanthropy | Furor of the Bitten |
| Salvaged Guardian | Makeshift Mauler |
| Hunchback | Stitcher's Apprentice |
| Bloodbath | Blasphemous Act |
| Zoologic Mage | Delver of Secrets |
| Sample the Meat | Gnaw to the Bone |
| Vampire's Den | Stensia Bloodhall |
| Necronomicon | Grimoire of the Dead |
| Haphazard Study | Desperate Ravings |
| Frankenstein Dragon | Skaab Ruinator |
