June through August FNM Promo Update
====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 15, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_mikemcartor.jpg)
By Mike McArtor




 Mike first played with Ice Age and became the copy editor for DailyMTG.com in December 2011. Before DailyMTG, he was an editor on D&D's Dragon magazine for four years. 







Here is a look at the FNM Promos you can pick up at your local  [Friday Night **Magic**](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm)  over the next few months. And no, despite my known predilections, I did not pick these promos. But I do hope you enjoy them! :)


### May


The current card, which you can earn tomorrow, is [Tormented Hero](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tormented+Hero).
