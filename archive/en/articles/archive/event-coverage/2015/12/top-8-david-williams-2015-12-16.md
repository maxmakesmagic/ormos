Top 8: David Williams
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPPORTO00/Images/202.jpg)Name: David Williams  
**Age:** 20  
**Location:**  Dallas, TX, USA  
**Occupation:** Medical Doctor  
**Played Magic since:** 95 - Fallen Empires  
**Best accomplishments:** GP top 8s in Cannes, San Diego and Nagoya



Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Porto?  

A: To see Ed Fear dominate.



Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?  

A: To win, because I'm badass.



Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.  

A: Day 1 - Great. Day 2 - Downhill.



Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?  

A: More $.



Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?  

A: Williams over Fuller in top 4, Ruel lose to Pustilnik in top 4 --> Williams over Pustilnik.
