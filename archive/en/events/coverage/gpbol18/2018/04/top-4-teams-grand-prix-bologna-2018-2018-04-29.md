Top 4 Teams of Grand Prix Bologna 2018
======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 29, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After fourteen rounds of *Dominaria* Team Sealed Deck play, with a total of 1,428 other players eliminated, only four teams of three remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophies. Find out more about them, their decks and their tournament so far!





---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpbol18/t4a.jpg)  
*Joonas Eloranta, Jori Hukka, Matti Kuisma (left to right)*


**Name:** Joonas Eloranta


**Age:** 29


**Occupation:** Software engineer


**Hometown:** Tampere, Finland


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


7-2 on a Grand Prix Day 1 with a home brew.


**What was the best card in your Day 1 deck and what color combination did you play?**


Song of Freylise, black and green.


**What was the best card in your Day 2 deck and what color combination did you play?**


[Song of Freyalise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Song+of+Freyalise), red-green.


**What's the sweetest interaction you've seen in *Dominaria* Limited so far?** 


Killing your own [Josu Vess, Lich Knight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Josu+Vess%2C+Lich+Knight) just to return it to your hand and cast it again with kicker.




---

**Name:** Jori Hukka


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Chef


**Hometown:** Helsinki, Finland


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


I won Finnish Highlander champs back in the day.


**What was the best card in your Day 1 deck and what color combination did you play?**


Blue-red. [Tolarian Scholar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tolarian+Scholar), but [Karn, Scion of Urza](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karn%2C+Scion+of+Urza) and [Verix Bladewing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verix+Bladewing) finished people off nicely.


**What was the best card in your Day 2 deck and what color combination did you play?**


White-blue. [Pegasus Courser](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pegasus+Courser) by far. She carried my deck.


**What's the sweetest interaction you've seen in *Dominaria* Limited so far?** 


[Aesthir Glider](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aesthir+Glider) turn three to make [Relic Runner](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Relic+Runner) unblockable. And a bunch of nonsense.
