Looking for something new and fun to play in *Shards of Alara* Block play? A\_Webb managed to take this version of a Grixis control deck to second place in a recent Premier Event on **Magic Online**. [Cruel Ultimatum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Ultimatum), [Grixis Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grixis+Charm), [Sedraxis Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sedraxis+Specter); the deck teems with the very best (or worst, considering their plane of existence!) Grixis has to throw at the world. And check out none other than [Nicol Bolas, Planeswalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+Planeswalker) ready to rumble in the sideboard!
