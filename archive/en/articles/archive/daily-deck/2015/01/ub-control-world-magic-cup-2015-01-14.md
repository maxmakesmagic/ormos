UB Control at the World Magic Cup
=================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on January 14, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/shuhei_authorpic.jpg)
By Shuhei Nakamura




Shuhei Nakamura is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, former Player of the Year, and is one of just six players with more than 400 lifetime pro points. 






The [World Championship](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/2014WC) was not the only place for deck development.



Many of the successful nations' UB control decks at the [World *Magic* Cup](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/2014WMC) had been modified from the original version. I assume this was partially because of the restrictions of Team Constructed.


[![](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Ashiok%2C+Nightmare+Weaver)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashiok%2C+Nightmare+Weaver)

The UB control piloted by Thomas Enevoldsen from the champions, Team Denmark, is a clear example of this.



Shockingly, there are zero creatures in the mainboard. Instead, what you find is four [Ashiok, Nightmare Weaver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashiok%2C+Nightmare+Weaver)s. This Ashiok is the deck's only win condition.



Why was Ashiok chosen? The answer is simple if you list the decks in Standard. Abzan, Jeskai, Mardu, Mono-Green, Mono-Red, etc. All these decks are similar in the sense that they are aggressive decks with many creatures.



To such decks, [Ashiok, Nightmare Weaver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashiok%2C+Nightmare+Weaver) is a threat that must be dealt with immediately. Ashiok also doubles as a source of protection, preserving UB control's precious life total during the early game.



What if they ignore Ashiok?



Activate [Perilous Vault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Perilous+Vault) with Ashiok's minus ability on the stack. I'm sure your opponent will soon change his or her mind.
