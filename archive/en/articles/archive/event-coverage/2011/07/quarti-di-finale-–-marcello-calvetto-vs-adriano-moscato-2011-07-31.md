Quarti di Finale – Marcello Calvetto vs. Adriano Moscato
========================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 31, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Bruno Panara












Marcello Calvetto di Ivrea é al suo secondo top8 ai Nazionali, e non é nuovo a giocare sotto pressione, avendo fatto anche top8 al GP: Lione e partecipato a numerosi Pro Tour. D'altro canto Adriano Moscato é un giovane giocatore di Napoli che si é qualificato una sola volta al Pro Tour. Marcello gioca CawBlade e il suo avversario Valakut Ramp.


Nessun mulligan per i nostri giocatori, e mentre Adriano parte accelerando con [Explore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Explore) e [Rampant Growth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth), Marcello evoce [Spellskite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite) e [Mirran Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirran+Crusader). Moscato arriva a sei terre in un baleno, ma ne ha altrettante in mano e solo removal. Intanto il Crociato attacca e quando arriva [Sword of Feast and Famine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+Feast+and+Famine) un [Dismember](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember) leva di torno [Spellskite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite), e [Beast Within](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Beast+Within) distrugge l'equipaggiamento. Ancora pescate morte per il giovane napoletano, che scende ad 1 vita con l'attacco seguente. Finalmente arriva [Primeval Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan), che con due [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle), e altre 4 montagne potrebbe fare la differenza, ma quando Calvetto mostra [Mana Leak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak) la partita é finita.


Marcello Calvetto 1 – Adriano Moscato 0

Adriano Moscato   

Out: 2 [Dismember](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember) 1 [Avenger of Zendikar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avenger+of+Zendikar) 1 [Green Sun's Zenith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Green+Sun%27s+Zenith) 1 [Harrow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harrow) 1 [Overgrown Battlement](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Battlement)  

In: 4 [Nature's Claim](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nature%27s+Claim) 1 [Summoning Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoning+Trap) 1 [Gaea's Revenge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gaea%27s+Revenge)


Marcello mulliga la sua mano di sei magie e un'Inkmoth Nexus, ma decide mi tenere con 5 terre e [Flashfreeze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flashfreeze), mentre il suo avversario tiene le prime 7. Immediata accelerazione di Moscato con [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) e [Overgrown Battlement](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Battlement), seguiti da un [Primeval Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan). Marcello ha [Flashfreeze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flashfreeze), ma non puó nulla contro il [Summoning Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoning+Trap) che lo segue, e quando l'avversario trova un altro titano, Marcello concede prima di vedere il suo quarto turno.


Marcello Calvetto 1 – Adriano Moscato 1


Terza partita senza mulligan. Adriano prova 2 [Rampant Growth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth) ed un [Explore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Explore), ma solo una di queste risolve grazie a due [Spell Pierce](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Pierce) di Calvetto, che poi appoggia uno [Squadron Hawk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squadron+Hawk). Moscato fa [Solemn Simulacrum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solemn+Simulacrum) e il suo avversario ne approfitta per fare [Sword of Feast and Famine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+Feast+and+Famine) e altri due falchi.


Adriano decide che é il momento di agire e prova [Nature's Claim](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nature%27s+Claim) sulla spada: [Mental Misstep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mental+Misstep), nel suo turno [Primeval Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan): [Flashfreeze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flashfreeze), [Summoning Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoning+Trap): [Flashfreeze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flashfreeze). Quando la prossima pescata é una terra si passa alla quarta


Marcello Calvetto 2 – Adriano Moscato 1

*Marcello Calvetto* |



Piccoli cambi di side: Marcello mette un Day of Judgement per uno [Squadron Hawk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squadron+Hawk), mentre Adriano mette un [Overgrown Battlement](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Battlement) per un [Explore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Explore)


Questa volta tocca ad Adriano mulligare, ma parte forte lo stesso con [Rampant Growth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth) e [Solemn Simulacrum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solemn+Simulacrum), a cui Marcello risponde con [Spellskite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite). Il napoletano accelera ancora con [Overgrown Battlement](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Battlement), ma la mano non é ottima: [Nature's Claim](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nature%27s+Claim) e un'altra terra. Quando l'istantaneo verde prova a bersagliare [Spellskite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite), viene neutralizzato da [Mental Misstep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mental+Misstep). Marcello approfitta della carta in mano solitaria del suo avversario per giocare [Jace Beleren](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace+Beleren) e poi un altro [Spellskite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite), riempiendosi la mano di counter e di falchi, grazie ad uno [Squadron Hawk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squadron+Hawk). Ancora terre per Moscato, che ora ne ha tre in mano e nei turni a seguire arrivano il secondo muro ed il secondo, e poi il terzo, simulacro. Nel frattempo i falchi hanno portato il napoletano a 14, e piuttosto che scartare Marcello usa un [Dismember](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember) su un muro, senza rendersi conto forse che Adriano ha ormai cinque montagne in gioco ed inizia ad attaccare con i simulacri. Uno viene bloccato da [Spellskite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite) che poi viene distrutto grazie ad un [Explore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Explore) che permette a [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle) alla sesta Montagna di entrare in gioco.


Piede sull'acceleratore per Marcello gioca [Sword of Feast and Famine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+Feast+and+Famine) e poi [Mirran Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirran+Crusader). Adriano prova [Green Sun's Zenith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Green+Sun%27s+Zenith) ma Calvetto é pronto con [Flashfreeze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flashfreeze). Dopo un po' di confusione con danni di first strike e abilitá dei simulacri, Moscato non riesce a pescare niente e Marcello Calvetto é il nostro primo semifinalista!
