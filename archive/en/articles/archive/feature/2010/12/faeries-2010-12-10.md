Faeries was the scourge of Standard when Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa used the archetype to make the Top 8 of the 2008 World Championship in Memphis. Paulo is a favorite at this year's champs in Chiba, Japan, which take place this weekend. You'll find coverage of that event on the [front page](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds) of this very website, and the archive of Paulo's win is [here](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/japanese-coverage-2008-magic-world-championships-2008-12-11).
