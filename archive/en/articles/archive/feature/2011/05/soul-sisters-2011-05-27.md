Soul Sisters, so named because of the tandem effects of [Soul Warden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Warden) and [Soul's Attendant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul%27s+Attendant), has reared up in the world of Standard before. Now that the deck also has [Suture Priest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Suture+Priest), brewmeister Conley Woods has taken a stab at resurrecting the archetype. Here's his list from the StarCityGames.com Louisville Open, which you can read more about in StarCity's [coverage](http://www.starcitygames.com/php/news/expandnews.php?Article=21896).
