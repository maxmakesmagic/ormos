Top 8 Profile: Olivier Ruel
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 24, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbar02/a914.jpg)**Name:** Olivier Ruel

**Age:** 20

**Occupation:** Student

**Where do you live?** Aubervilliers

**Since when do you play Magic?** Six years I think ( 4th Edition )

**Best accomplishments:**   

63rd Grand Prix Curitiba

**What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Barcelona; what did you expect to find?**  

Pro Points and money. Pro Points and money!

**What do you think is the main reason for you to make Top 8 in this tournament and why?**  

Luck and Overruns, they both help

**What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

I beat Noah Boeken, the other games don't matter.

**Anything else you'd like to share with the Sideboard Online readers?**  

My love for Winnie the Pooh.
