Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 18, 2015 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbia01/a876.jpg)*Joost Vollebregt***Name:** Joost Vollebregt **Age:** 22


**Where do you live:** The Netherlands


**Occupation:** Web engineer


**Played Magic since:** Ice Age


**Best accomplishments:** 


* Fourth Grand Prix Cologne 2001
* 18th Grand Prix Gothenburg 2001
* 33rd Worlds 2001 (11-0-1 in Constructed)
* 37th Pro Tour Tokyo 2001

**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Biarritz?**  

Bad opponents, good draft decks and not many mana screws


**Which colors did you play and would you change strategy if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

Black-blue, the colors are dictated by the players to your right, so only a limited number of combinations are available


**What do you think about *Odyssey* as a draft format?**  

Thumbs up! **How did you prepare?**  

Drafting weekly in the Hague


**Did you enjoy this Grand Prix?**  

Yes, although I got little sleep


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbia01/a883.jpg)*Alexander Witt***Name:** Alexander Witt


**Age:** 21


**Where do you live:** Amsterdam


**Occupation:** Student


**Played Magic since:** Fallen Empires


**Best accomplishments:** 


* National Champion in 1999
* Top 16 Pro Tour Barcelona
* Top 8 Grand Prix Amsterdam 2001
* Top 32 Grand Prix Prague
* Top 16 Grand Prix Amsterdam 1999

**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Biarritz?**  

I played the same as ever but the level of play around here is lower


**Which colors did you play and would you change strategy if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

Mostly green-blue and if I get those colors in the Top 8 I'll be very happy


**What do you think about *Odyssey* as a draft format?**  

I love it


**How did you prepare?**  

One weekly draft


**Did you enjoy this Grand Prix?**  

YES!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbia01/a880.jpg)*Nicolas Labarre***Name:** Nicolas Labarre


**Age:** 24


**Where do you live:** Rennes, France


**Occupation:** Student


**Played Magic since:** Revised


**Best accomplishments:** 


* Second Pro Tour Rome 1999
* Top 8 Pro Tour New York 1999
* Top 8 World Championship 2000

**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Biarritz:**  

Far from faultless but definitely satisfying


**Which colors did you play and would you change strategy if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

Blue, blue again, always blue... It worked. I wouldn't change a thing


**What do you think about *Odyssey* as a draft format?**  

I like it a lot. The set is skill intensive enough to allow you to outplay a weaker opponent, unlike Invasion for example


**How did you prepare?**  

One draft per week since *Odyssey* was released


**Did you enjoy this Grand Prix?**  

Tremendously


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbia01/a877.jpg)*Alexis Dumay***Name:** Alexis Dumay


**Age:  

Where do you live:** Montpellier, France


**Occupation:** Computers


**Played Magic since:** 1995


**Best accomplishments:** 


* Top 4 French Nationals
* Top 8 Grand Prix Lisbon

**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Biarritz?**  

I'm never happy with my game play as I make too many mistakes


**Which colors did you play and would you change strategy if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

No particular strategy, I just try to get the good cards


**What do you think about *Odyssey* as a draft format?**  

You can play any color combination and that is really good


**How did you prepare?**  

I didn't really prepare. I'm more of a Constructed fan so in normally I'm a tourist in Limited events


**Did you enjoy this Grand Prix?**  

How couldn't I enjoy a Grand Prix where I reached the Top 8?

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbia01/a879.jpg)*Olivier Ruel***Name:** Olivier Ruel


**Age:** 20

**Occupation:** Student

**Best accomplishments:** 


* Winner Grand Prix Cannes
* Winner Team Challenge
* Second Grand Prix Porto
* Second Grand Prix Valencia
* Fourth Grand Shizuoka
* Fifth Grand Prix Madrid
* Beating Kai Budde in Euros with a Rebel-Poacher-Opposition deck

**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Biarritz?**  

Just perfect


**Which colors did you play and would you change strategy if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

Everything but red. No way!


**What do you think about *Odyssey* as a draft format?**  

Pretty cool, but too easy


**How did you prepare?**  

Going to Shizuoka for a Grand Prix a month ago


**Did you enjoy this Grand Prix?**  

Not for a single moment, it was definitely too easy. I'm starting to understand how Kai Budde and Jon Finkel feel when they play on a tournament


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbia01/a882.jpg)*Lucio Moratinos***Name:** Lucio Moratinos

**Played Magic since:** At the start of Revised


**Best accomplishments:** 

* Top 32 at Grand Prix Madrid

**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Biarritz?**  

Less mistakes than usual


**Which colors did you play and would you change strategy if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**

Interesting but too straight
