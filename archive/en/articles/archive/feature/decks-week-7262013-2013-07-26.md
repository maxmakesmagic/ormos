Decks of the Week: 7/26/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 26, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












This weekend, we see the first Standard tournament (Grand Prix Calgary) since the release of ***Magic** 2014*. This weekend is also the ***Magic** 2014* Prerelease weekend **Magic Online** (it's available there on Monday, July 29). Next week is Worlds Week, when both an individual World Champion and a World **Magic** Cup champion will be crowned. There's going to be a lot of exciting **Magic** to watch, and if you plan on going to a PTQ this season, you'll want to take note of the Standard decks there. Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros). Good luck; have fun!
