**Magic Online** is home to constantly firing tournaments of the latest Pro Tour and Pro Tour Qualifier formats like Standard, Draft, and Sealed Deck. But there's a lighter side to the online world as well. Like the recent Pauper Premier Event held. It was won by Sarcasto playing this build of Affinity. Of course, since you're only allowed to use commons in Pauper events, Sarcasto had to do it without the help of rares and uncommon, but who needs those cards when you've got [Somber Hoverguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard) and [Myr Enforcer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Enforcer)?
