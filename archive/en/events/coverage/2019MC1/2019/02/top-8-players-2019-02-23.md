Top 8 Players
=============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 24, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamstyborski.jpg)
By Adam Styborski




 Stybs has played Magic the world over, writing and drafting as part of the event coverage team and slinging Commander everywhere his decks will fit. 






After sixteen rounds, eight players remain in the running for the glory, fortune, and prestige of winning Mythic Championship I! Learn more about them below.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/2019MC1/Top-8-Luis-Scott-Vargas.jpg)


**Name**: Luis Scott-Vargas  
**Age**: 36  
**Hometown**: Oakland, California  
**Occupation**: Vice President, ChannelFireball.com and Game Designer  
**Ravnica Guild Affiliation**: Dimir  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Mulligan to four in the previous Pro Tour finals.  
**Limited Record**: 4-2  
**Standard Record**: 8-1-1  
**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?** Eating an eight-pound burrito (mostly) by myself. Josh Utter-Leyton helped a little bit, if we're being honest.  
**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?** Getting interviewed by Brian David-Marshall at my third Pro Tour.  
**Who is your hero, *Magic* or not?** My parents, for teaching me what really matters.  
**If you could get help from one Planeswalker in the Top 8, who would it be and why?** Ral, because he's served me well so far.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/2019MC1/Top-8-Marcio-Carvalho.jpg)


**Name:** Márcio Carvalho  
**Age:** 33  
**Hometown:** Portugal  
**Occupation:** MPL Player  
**Ravnica Guild Affiliation:** Simic  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 5 Pro Tour Top 8s. In my last four Pro Tours I made Top 8 three times—feels unreal.  
**Limited Record:** 6-0  
**Standard Record:** 7-1-2  
**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?** Being invited to Gonçalo Pinto's wedding.  
**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?** When I made Top 4 of Pro Tour 25h Anniversary with Carlos Romão and Thiago Saporito.  
**Who is your hero, *Magic* or not?** Willy Edel. He's way more than a Hall of Famer—he's just the best person you will ever know.  
**If you could get help from one Planeswalker in the Top 8, who would it be and why?**


[Gideon, Ally of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Ally+of+Zendikar). He never leaves a man down.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/2019MC1/Top-8-Reid-Duke.jpg)


**Name:** Reid Duke  
**Age:** 29  
**Hometown:** Sugar Loaf, NY  
**Occupation:** *Magic: The Gathering*  
**Ravnica Guild Affiliation:** Selesnya  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 2011 *Magic Online* Champion; three previous Pro Tour Top 8s; Team Series Champions with Ultimate Guard Pro Team  
**Limited Record:** 6-0  
**Standard Record:** 7-2-1  
**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?** High School Math Team State Championship Competitor  
**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?** Winning the Team Series Championship with the Ultimate Guard Pro Team  
**Who is your hero, *Magic* or not?**


* Lou Gehrig
* Sir Launcelot of the Lake
* Luis Scott-Vargas

**If you could get help from one Planeswalker in the Top 8, who would it be and why?** Urza. Going to need a powerful blue Planeswalker to get me through this.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/2019MC1/Top-8-Alex-Majlaton.jpg)


**Name:** Alex Majlaton  
**Age:** 34  
**Hometown:** Lanham, Maryland  
**Occupation:** Data Scientist  
**Ravnica Guild Affiliation:** Izzet  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 8 GP Top 8s; played in 34 PTs  
**Limited Record:** 4-2  
**Standard Record:** 8-1-1  
**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?** Personal development; I worked hard on my ability to interact with people in general and treating everyone with respect.  
**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?** PT Portland 2014, playing 3 consecutive tournaments, practicing with Face to Face Games, vacationing in a log cabin playing games for a week afterward.  
**Who is your hero, *Magic* or not?** My dad. I hope I can be half as great as he is.  
**If you could get help from one Planeswalker in the Top 8, who would it be and why?**


Karn—shout out to my robots.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/2019MC1/Top-8-Autumn-Burchett.jpg)


**Name:** Autumn Burchett  
**Age:** 27  
**Hometown:** Langley, England  
**Occupation:** Streamer, writer, professional *Magic* player  
**Ravnica Guild Affiliation:** Temur  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Back-to-back English National Champion  
**Limited Record:** 5-1  
**Standard Record:** 7-1-2  
**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?** I had a few rough years early in my adulthood, and I am proud to have left them stronger, happier, and more confident in myself.  
**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?** Preparing for PT GRN with Jade.  
**Who is your hero, *Magic* or not?** Mike, Charlie, Emma, Jadine, and Aaron all fill my life with so much love and joy.  
**If you could get help from one Planeswalker in the Top 8, who would it be and why?** Narset, because representation matters <3
