The Legacy Cube Returns, Again!
===============================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on January 10, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The *Legacy Cube* returns to *Magic Online* tomorrow (January 11), and with it a handful of changes since the last time you played with it. We’re only swapping 20 cards this time around, mostly incorporating some powerful *Kaladesh* and *Commander (2016 Edition)* cards. But why listen to me? See for yourself below.


Enjoy the newly updated *Legacy Cube*! Get the full list of changes plus event details below.

Legacy Cube Event Details
=========================



### *Legacy Cube* Single Elimination Draft Queue


**Start Times**: Wednesday, January 11, after the downtime until Wednesday, January 25, 4:00 a.m. All times are Pacific (UTC -7).  
**Location**:Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Queues  
**Entry Options**:


* Option 1: 10 Event Tickets
* Option 3: 100 Play Points

**Product**: 3 *Legacy Cube* booster packs. Phantom product will not be added to a player's collection at the conclusion of the event.  
**Size**: 8 players  
**Play Style**: Single-Elimination  
**Format**: Draft  
**Duration**: 10 minutes deck-building time. Three rounds, each round up to 50 minutes  
**Prizes**:




| Place | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | 220 Play Points | 1 |
| 2nd | 160 Play Points | 0 |
| 3rd-4th | 100 Play Points | 0 |



---

### *Legacy Cube* Swiss Draft League


**Start Time**: Wednesday, January 11, following downtime  
**Closed Time**: Wednesday, January 25 at 4:00 a.m. All times are Pacific (UTC -7).  
**End Time**: Wednesday, January 25 at 8:00 p.m. All times are Pacific (UTC -7).  
**Location**:Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Leagues  
**Entry Options**:


* Option 1: 10 Event Tickets
* Option 3: 100 Play Points

**Product**: 3 *Legacy Cube* booster packs  
**Size**: 8 players  
**Play Style**: Swiss  
**Format**: Draft League  
**Duration**: Three matches, each up to 50 minutes  
**Prizes**:




| Match Wins | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 3 Wins | 150 Play Points | 0 |
| 2 Wins | 100 Play Points | 0 |
| 1 Win | 40 Play Points | 0 |
| 0 Wins | 10 Play Points | 0 |

**Please Note**: Since Draft Leagues pair players outside of their Draft pod, you may encounter opponents using cards that are also in your deck.
