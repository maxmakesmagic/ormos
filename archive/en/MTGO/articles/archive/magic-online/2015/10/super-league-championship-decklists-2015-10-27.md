Super League Championship Decklists
===================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on October 27, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Every Tuesday through November 17, at 6:00 p.m. Pacific, the newest episode of the Super League Championship airs live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic).


If you haven't already, check out Randy Buehler's [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/super-league-championship-episode-1-2015-10-27) for a look at what to expect in the Championship!


Follow the links below to take you to each player's decklist—they'll be using these decks up until the Super League Championship playoffs on November 10! There will be new decklists posted for the Championship Playoffs on November 17.
