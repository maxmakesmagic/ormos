**Standard PTQ for Pro Tour *Ixalan* Friday, July 21**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Friday, July 21 at 9 a.m. PT. There is no need to qualify through a Preliminary – simply join this event with 30 Event Tickets, 300 Play Points or a spare Blue Constructed PTQ Finals Entry Token!


Learn more about Magic Online Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

***Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet*** **Sealed MOCS Monthly Saturday, July 22**


A *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) *Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet* Sealed Monthly Event is coming up on July 22! Players can enter the event with 35 Qualifier Points (QPs), and top finishers qualify for the last remaining Playoff Event on November 11.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points for the year. Top players on the [Leaderboard](http://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/leaderboard) at the end of the 2017 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.


We look forward to seeing you on July 22 for the latest Monthly Event! Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online* Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).
