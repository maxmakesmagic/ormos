Cat Aggro
=========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 10, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson












[Brimaz, King of Oreskos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brimaz%2C+King+of+Oreskos) is one of the most powerful cards in *Born of the Gods*. Luis Scott-Vargas gave a pun-filled [review](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/td/282) of the card already, but today I want to showcase what a Brimaz deck might look like. As always, Patrick Chapin has been brewing and he has a solid-looking deck. 

![Brimaz, King of Oreskos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Brimaz%2C+King+of+Oreskos) Slotting Brimaz into Mono-White Aggro is pretty easy, but Chapin was also singing the praises of [Eidolon of Countless Battles](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eidolon+of+Countless+Battles). [Spirit of the Labyrinth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spirit+of+the+Labyrinth) makes a guest appearance as well. 

 Ben Lundquist has been championing [Ajani, Caller of the Pride](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani%2C+Caller+of+the+Pride) instead of [Spear of Heliod](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spear+of+Heliod), but both seem like great options. With the advent of Brimaz, white aggro has a plethora of three-drops to choose from and they are among the best cards in the deck. 

 Mono-White Aggro still has plenty options and this should simply serve as a starting point. You could splash black for [Xathrid Necromancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xathrid+Necromancer) or red for [Boros Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Charm). Either way, you have plenty of options for fighting cards that are good against you, such as [Supreme Verdict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Supreme+Verdict).
