=====================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on July 13, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Let's talk ***Magic** 2010* Intro Packs! As you remember, each Intro Pack contains a ready-to-play 41-card deck featuring a foil premium rare, a sealed 15-card booster pack, a strategy insert, "learn to play" guide. We're guessing you're most interested in the contents of the decks. So let's get to it!

### We Are Legion

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/216_intropack5_3th6zybdzr.jpg)  
The forces of order attack with righteous fury! [Lightwielder Paladin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightwielder+Paladin) leads your charge, flanked by Soldiers, Angels, Griffins, and Pegasus, while your spells power up your creatures and protect you from harm.
