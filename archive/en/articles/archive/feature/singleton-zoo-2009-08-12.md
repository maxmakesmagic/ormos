**Magic Online** is a digital playground for all sorts of preferred **Magic** formats. You've got your tried and true tournament formats like Extended, Classic, and Standard, but you also get the benefit of finding games around the clock in less common formats. What type of formats you ask? Kaleidoscope, Commander, even 100 Card Singleton. That's the format for the featured deck here, with the parameter that each deck has 100 cards and that you can only play one copy of any card other than a basic land. Thekid put those constraints to good effect. Check out [Arc-Slogger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arc-Slogger), already powerful but even more so with 100 cards to eat!
