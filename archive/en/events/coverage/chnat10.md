Lu and behold, China! You've got a new National Champ.


 


While the world was avidly throwing new M11 cards into their old decks, Lu Chao had been busy tinkering with a forgotten archetype to great success. His expeditious Boros Landfall deck miraculously contains no new cards from the Core set and it was good enough to win him the honor to captain Team China at Worlds this fall. Zhang Zhiyang (2009 Worlds National Team Champ) returns to the Team once again, in a bid to defend his title. Lei Qiang, who also made Top 8 at Nationals last year, forms the last member of the team, with Lu Jia Chong as the alternate. Congratulations to all winners!


大家注意，您的国家冠军出炉了！


当全世界忙着将M11的牌往旧套牌里塞时，陆超的波洛斯地落套牌居然连一张M11的新牌都没。称为环境中最快速的套牌之一，如今它的实力是大家有目共睹的。陆超将带领中国国家队，到年尾的世界赛争光。张之洋再次归队，希望可以蝉联世界国家队冠军的宝座。国家队最后一位成员就是去年国冠赛也打入8强的雷强。身为殿军的吕佳翀虽然不在国家队，仍可到日本参赛。2010国冠赛就到此告一个段落，并再次恭喜所有得奖玩家！

Monday, 4:30 p.m. - Quarterfinals Recap
---------------------------------------


*Chapman "randomchap" Sim*
### 星期一, 4:30 p.m. - 八强赛精髓


**First Quarterfinals - Lu Jia Chong (UW Control) VS Jia Bin (Jund)  

第一组：吕佳翀（蓝白控) VS 贾斌 (永得)**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Q1%20-%20Both%20Players.jpg)*Lu Jia Chong VS Jia Bin*
 


Game 1 ended so fast that I missed out on all the action before I could get to the table, but there was a sight to behold in the next game. Game 2 saw Lu Jia Chong with a pretty dominating board, with the only problem that he was at a mere one life. Multiple [Sun Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sun+Titan)s do not save you when facing [Malakir Bloodwitch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Malakir+Bloodwitch) and [Siege-Gang Commander](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander).


 


The deciding match saw Jia Bin having pretty much a Jund god draw but his efforts were erased with a timely [Day of Judgment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Day+of+Judgment). On the turn that Lu was tapped out, Jia chose to drop [Malakir Bloodwitch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Malakir+Bloodwitch) instead of Siege-gang Commander which possibly cost him the match. Lu Jia Chong proceeded to drop [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) to bounce it. Lu Jia Chong managed to stabilize with [Gideon Jura](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon+Jura) and win the match, proceeding to the Semi-finals.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Q1%20-%20Game%202.jpg) 


第一局似乎过得很快，我还没到达就已经完毕。不过，第二局为我们带来精彩的画面。 吕佳翀面上操控着一对旭日泰坦 [[Sun Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sun+Titan)]，好像快赢了的感觉。美中不足的是，他只剩下1点生命。眼看马拉奇血祭师 [[Malakir Bloodwitch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Malakir+Bloodwitch)]和攻城指挥官 [[Siege-Gang Commander](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander)]就杀到城门外了，还是乖乖投降吧！


 


第三局贾斌几乎抓到了"神手"，不过一切的心血都被对手的审判末日 [[Day of Judgment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Day+of+Judgment)]抹除了。就在吕佳翀没有魔法力的时候，贾斌选择了结算马拉奇血祭师 [[Malakir Bloodwitch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Malakir+Bloodwitch)]。吕佳翀利用心灵塑师杰斯 [[Jace, the Mind Sculptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor)]将它弹会手中，然后利用游侠艾紫培 [[Elspeth, Knight-Errant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant)]和基定尤拉 [[Gideon Jura](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon+Jura)]取得胜利。贾斌在赛后说过，选择施放攻城指挥官 [[Siege-Gang Commander](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander)]可能会比反白保护的血祭师来得好，也许因为这个决定输掉这局。


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Q1%20-%20Game%203.jpg)*Lu Jia Chong advances to the semi-finals! // 吕佳翀进入半决赛！*
 


**Second Quarterfinals - Liu Zhuo Ran (Boros Landfall) VS Zhang Zhiyang ([Runeflare Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Runeflare+Trap))  

第二组：刘卓然（波洛斯地落) VS 张之洋 (红蓝陷阱)**


 


The second semi-finals never took place as both players happen to belong to a team named "Little Sheep". Liu concedes to Zhang to put him back on the National Team for the second year running, giving him an opportunity to defend his title of Worlds Team Champion.


**Zhang Zhiyang advances to the semi-finals!**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Q2%20-%20Little%20Sheep.jpg)*Team 'Little Sheep', one of the most dominating teams in China consist of Top 8 competitors Liu Zhuo Ran and Zhang Zhiyang. (both pictured in the middle)*
 


这局八强赛从来没有发生。不巧，两位玩家都来自"小绵羊"。刘卓然决定认输，让张之洋再次归队，希望可以再世界赛蝉联世界赛冠军队伍的荣县。


 


**张之洋进入半决赛！**


**"小绵羊"**


**Third Quarterfinals**  
**Lu Chao (Boros Landfall)VS Xu Liang (Fauna Naya)**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Q3%20-%20Game%201.jpg) 


Lu Chao's draw wasn't not fast enough and Xu Liang managed to assemble this sick board to take down Game 1. It is relatively hard to lose when you've got [Baneslayer Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel) in play with [Knight of the Reliquary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary) backup.


 


The second game ended almost as fast as it started as Lu Chao obtained one of the fastest draws his deck could muster. Look at the photograph below and you'll understand because it looks pretty self-explanatory.


The final game saw Lu Chao receiving a double [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) and double [Path to Exile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) (nuking [Cunning Sparkmage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Sparkmage) and Baneslayer angel) draw but Xu Lianng managed to stabilize with a just single [Fauna Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman) in play. That didn't last for very long, because Lu Chao soon topdecked an [Ajani Vengeant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani+Vengeant) to get rid of it. [Obstinate Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obstinate+Baloth) did not save him either, because a combination of a kicked [Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning) and a topdecked [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) had denied Xu Liang entry to the semi-finals.


**Lu Chao advances to the semi-finals!**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Q3%20-%20Game%202.jpg) 


**第三组：陆超（波洛斯地落) VS 徐亮 (祭师纳雅)**


 


陆超在第一回合的速度欠佳，没法尽快把对手打死，最后让徐亮有机可乘。破邪天使 [[Baneslayer Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel)]加上圣物骑士 [[Knight of the Reliquary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary)]联手合作，想输都难！


第二局比赛三两下就完成，显示出波洛斯地落惊人的速度。 看看图片应该可以猜到大概发生什么事了吧？


  

第三局的对战看见陆超无情的蹂躏，得到各两张鬼怪向导 [[Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide)]和流放之径 [[Path to Exile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile)]，不过徐亮还剩下一位动物群祭师 [[Fauna Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman)]，可能有扭转乾坤的机会。徐亮也不敢示弱，不久后抓到复仇阿耶尼 [[Ajani Vengeant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani+Vengeant)]将她烧死。即使召唤了，顽强巴洛西 [[Obstinate Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obstinate+Baloth)]，还是比不上增幅的闪电迸发 [[Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning)]和掏到的闪电击 [[Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)]。


**陆超进入半决赛！**


**Fourth Quarterfinals - Yu Chao (Jund) VS Lei Qiang (Turboland)**  
**第四组：陆超（永得) VS 雷强 (蓝绿加速地)**


After losing the first game, Yu Chao annihilated Lei Qiang with one of the best draws Jund could offer. A pair of [Putrid Leech](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Putrid+Leech)es alongside [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf) threatened to end the game quicky, but [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) (making sure there were no [Obstinate Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obstinate+Baloth)s in Lei Qiang's hand) and [Blightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blightning) tore up Lei Qiang's hand to ensure that a turnaround was virtually impossible.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Q4%20-%20Game%20Three.jpg) 


The rubber match was pretty interesting, since Lei Qiang mulled to 5 and one of his main kill conditions [Avenger of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avenger+of+Zendikar) vanished from his deck, thanks to Yu Chao's sideboarded [Thought Hemorrhage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought+Hemorrhage). Thank god he had boarded in the "monster-plan" and proceeded to win the game with [Obstinate Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obstinate+Baloth), [Frost Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frost+Titan) and [Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan). Jund is good at killing stuff but not all at the same time! Lei Qiang advances to the semi-finals.


 


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Q4%20-%20Game%20Two.jpg)*Lei Qiang advances to the semi-finals!*
第一局败了之后，路超不惜一切拿回第二局。两只腐臭蛭 [[Putrid Leech](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Putrid+Leech)]和血辫地精 [[Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf)]造成血腥的局面。再加上逼迫[[Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)]和脑电击 [[Blightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blightning)]，雷强肯定没有回头路了。


第三局还挺有意思，雷强调度至五张手牌，而且还雪上加霜，卡色卡了好几回合。备牌里的思想溢血 [[Thought Hemorrhage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought+Hemorrhage)]将雷强所有的赞迪卡复仇者 [[Avenger of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avenger+of+Zendikar)]移除游戏，不过雷强一点儿也不屑，应为他还可以使用顽强巴洛西 [[Obstinate Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obstinate+Baloth)]，冰霜泰坦 [[Frost Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frost+Titan)]和太古泰坦 [[Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan)]取胜。


**雷强进入半决赛！**



 


  



 


Monday, 5:00 p.m. - Semifinals: Lu Chao VS Lei Qiang
----------------------------------------------------


*Chapman "randomchap" Sim*
### 星期一, 5:00 p.m. - 半决赛：陆超 对抗 雷强


**Game 1**


Lu Chao's decision to play something under the radar has obviously paid off. Widely heralded as the fastest deck in the format, it has fell out of favor in recent months for various reasons. Most people think the mana base is unstable, others fear the inclusion of [Obstinate Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obstinate+Baloth) in every other deck out there. His opponent, Lei Qiang happens to be packing some of those, but is about to face one of this worst nightmares. His Turboland deck has no real removal to any of Lu Chao's early creatures and his only plan seems to be focused on powering out a fatty as soon as possible. Unfortunately, Titans generally cost six mana, while [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) and [Steppe Lynx](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steppe+Lynx) cost only one.


陆超的套牌可算是这环境里最快速之一，不过还是有他的绊脚石。有人说它魔法力常常出问题，时常卡色。不仅如此，顽强巴洛西 [[Obstinate Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obstinate+Baloth)]在环境里超过一半的套牌都有使用，难免会影响这套牌受欢迎的程度。不巧，他的对手雷强也有使用此牌。不过，蓝绿加速地的缺点在于没有任何的杀牌，只能尽快施放出庞大的生物作为抵御。


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/SemiFinals.jpg) 


Lu Chao opened with [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) and wasted no time in pointing [Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning) at Lei Qiang's freshly summoned [Lotus Cobra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Cobra). He followed that up with a [Plated Geopede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Geopede) but all Lei Qiang could do was [Ponder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ponder) in search of solutions. He resolved an [Oracle of Mul Daya](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle+of+Mul+Daya) next turn and promised to go crazy on his next turn. Lu Chao wasn't worried though, because he dropped [Elspeth, Knight-Errant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant), and sent his team over to bring Lei Qiang down to 4 life. He tried to stay alive with a [Time Warp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp) but conceded upon seeing his next draw.


 


陆超叫出鬼怪向导 [[Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide)]开始攻打，并使用[[Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning)]将雷强的莲花眼镜蛇 [[Lotus Cobra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Cobra)]除掉。过后，又加上板甲地蜈蚣 [[Plated Geopede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Geopede)]和游侠艾紫培 [[Elspeth, Knight-Errant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant)]。雷强使出慕达雅先知 [[Oracle of Mul Daya](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle+of+Mul+Daya)]，下一回合必定可以开始加速，可是一切都太迟了。


**Lu Chao wins the first game at blazing speed!**  
**陆超第一局获胜！**


**Game 2**  

Lu Chao had no action on turn one, and Lei Qiang tried to get a kick start with [Rampant Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth) on turn two. He shuffled his library and offered it to his opponent when the table judge stopped him. It turns out that Lei Qiang had failed to sufficiently randomize his deck after resolving [Rampant Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth). He had previously been given a warning for such an offence during the course of this tournament, and reminded by the judges in the Quarterfinals as well. A repeated offence like this had to be upgraded into a game loss, resulting in a truly anti-climatic semi-finals.


路超第一回合没有任何生物，雷强便开始利用徒长 [[Rampant Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth)]开始加速。他搓了搓牌库，呈现给陆超切牌。这时，裁判走了过来请他停下。原来，裁判在八强赛事，以提醒他必须充分和彻底将牌库洗好，两三下的搓洗是不足够的。雷强在上轮已经得到一个警告，所以在此被升级为一盘输。就这样，半决赛便结束了。


**Lu Chao wins the match and proceeds to the final table.**



 


  



 


Monday, 6:10 p.m. - Finals: Lu Chao VS Zhang Zhiyang
----------------------------------------------------


*Chapman "randomchap" Sim*
### 星期一, 6:10 p.m. - 决赛: 陆超 对抗 张之洋


Fourteen rounds of play has passed and most of the dust has settled. Both players are clearly glad to make the Nationals Team but would have to battle it out to determine who would be championing the team at Worlds 2010 at Chiba this year.


Zhang Zhiyang is currently undefeated in the constructed portion and hasn't dropped a match since Saturday. His [Runeflare Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Runeflare+Trap) deck had been punishing control players all day, Lu Chao on the other hand had surprised everyone by piloting his lightning quick Boros deck to the finals. Running a deck loaded with low costed cards, early pressure and game-finishing burn, it seemed like this matchup was greatly in his favor.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/chnat10/Finals-Match.jpg) 


**Game 1**  
**第一局**


 


Lu opens with a second turn [Plated Geopede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Geopede), which was Unsummoned as soon as it hit table. When the insect resolved and tried to attack for 5 damage the next turn, it was sent Into the Roil, causing a major tempo setback.


Unfazed, he lays his 4th land and resolves [Temple Bell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+Bell). Ajani continued to tap down Zhang's only [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain), giving him only three [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)s to work with. He then dropped [Plated Geopede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Geopede), [Stoneforge Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic) (receiving [Adventuring Gear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Adventuring+Gear)) and [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) all on the same turn. Facing immense pressure and the potential to lose next turn, Zhang mulled over his options before he decided to send [Ajani Vengeant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani+Vengeant) [Into the Roil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Into+the+Roil) on his main turn.


[Stoneforge Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic) picked up the equipment he searched for, and Lu Chao's 5th fetchland entered play, giving a gigantic boost to both the Geopede and the Mystic. A second [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) joined the team and crashed in for a 14 damage, taking Zhang down to a precarious three life.When Yu revealed two [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)s from his hand, Zhang promptly scooped up his cards without delay.


陆超的板甲地蜈蚣 [[Plated Geopede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Geopede)]在二三回合分别遭反召唤 [[Unsummon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsummon)]和没入狂搅 [[Into the Roil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Into+the+Roil)]，张之洋则顺利在第二回合结算了嚎叫的矿井 [[Howling Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine)]。复仇阿耶尼 [[Ajani Vengeant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani+Vengeant)]进场，将张之洋的唯一山脉 [[Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain)]横置。张之洋下了第四块地后，将庙宇响钟 [[Temple Bell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+Bell)]搬了出来。


路超目前没有对张之洋造成任何伤害，不过在下回合从手中抛出三只生物，分别有鬼怪向导 [[Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide)]，锻石秘教徒 [[Stoneforge Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic)]和板甲地蜈蚣 [[Plated Geopede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Geopede)]。下回合，他将找出的历险配备 [[Adventuring Gear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Adventuring+Gear)]装备在教徒上，才放置了不毛高地 [[Arid Mesa](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arid+Mesa)]，让教徒和蜈蚣"成长"。第二只鬼怪向导 [[Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide)]加入陆超伟大的阵容，联合两张闪电击 [[Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)]，轻而易举拿下第一局。


**Lu Chao wins Game 1!**  
**路超第一局获胜！**


**Game 2**


Zhang was happy with his initial seven, but Lu chose to go down to five. His early [Steppe Lynx](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steppe+Lynx) was struck down by a [Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning) but a second one appeared next turn to replace its fallen comrade. Currently stuck on one land, he was blessed to draw a fetchland the next turn, attacking with the Landfall-loving cat for 4 damage. [Kor Skyfisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Skyfisher)s on the next two consecutive turns ensured that the Lynx would be at least 2/3 for the next few turns.


Zhang dispatched one of the Skyfishers with a kicked [Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning), but went down to seven from the two remaining creatures. The next turn gave Yu a 3rd [Plains](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains) but the attack reduced Zhang to 3 life once again. It is amazing how he had managed to bring his opponent within burn range without any red sources available and with [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) and [Plated Geopede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Geopede) stuck in his hand.


The lack of [Howling Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) and its fellow card-drawing artifacts meant that Zhang's [Runeflare Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Runeflare+Trap)s were utterly useless. Currently holding [Runeflare Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Runeflare+Trap), [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak) and [Time Warp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp), he had to pass with no play. Lu needed a land to win, but he did not draw it this time. [Kor Skyfisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Skyfisher) took Zhang down to one life and and he was forced to cast two [Time Warp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp)s, drawing only one card each turn since he did not have either [Howling Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) or [Temple Bell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+Bell) in play.


Not drawing any removal for the incoming assaulters, he pondered for a brief moment and extended his hand graciously.


张之洋对自己的起手满意，不过陆超却调度了两次。他第一只草原山猫 [[Steppe Lynx](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steppe+Lynx)]迅速遭闪电迸发 [[Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning)]袭击，不过第二只却安然无恙。接着，他连续召唤了两个寇族空渔人 [[Kor Skyfisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Skyfisher)]，确保下两回合都有地落。


张之洋利用增幅的闪电迸发 [[Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning)]解决其中一个空渔人，不过继续被剩余的两只生物攻打。仅剩三点生命的张子洋在没有任何神器的情况下，无法使用手中的符焰陷阱 [[Runeflare Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Runeflare+Trap)]，只好将时间弯曲 [[Time Warp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp)]，看看下张牌是否可以挽救。


不幸，第一个时间弯曲 [[Time Warp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp)]只帮他抓到另一枚时间弯曲 [[Time Warp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp)]。下回合抓牌之后，只好甘拜下风，大方的和对方握握手表示认输。


**Lu Chao beats Zhang Zhiyang to become China National Champion 2010! Congrats!**  

陆超击败张之洋，成为中国国家冠军赛2010年冠军！恭喜！
