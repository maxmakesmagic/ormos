TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 31, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











After many rounds of Swiss and some great games of *Magic*, the Top 8 for Grand Prix Sydney is set! Learn more about this event’s Top 8 competitors, their history at the top tables, and more by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles.  

 




---

  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpsyd16/gpsyd_top8_stark.jpg)


**Name:** Ben Stark  
  
**Hometown:** Coral Springs, Florida  
  
**Age:** 32  
  
**Occupation:** None  
  
**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**  

- Pro Tour Paris Champion  

- Pro Tour Hall of Fame  

- 4 Pro Tour Top 8s; 18 Grand Prix Top 8s  
  
**What color combination was your Sealed Deck? What your best card and why?**  

Black-Red. Collective Defiance–All three modes were really useful.  
  
**What color combinations was your 1st draft deck? What your best card and why?**  

White-Black. [Hope Against Hope](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hope+Against+Hope). Won every game.  
  
**What color combinations was your 2nd draft deck? What your best card and why?**  

Green-Blue-white. [Tamiyo, Field Researcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tamiyo%2C+Field+Researcher). Planeswalkers are good.  
  
**What card surprised you the most this weekend?**  
[Hope Against Hope](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hope+Against+Hope).  
  
**What's at stake for you this week—here and at Pro Tour Eldritch Moon (if applicable)?**  

Platinum. If I get 15 points between two events.




---

  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpsyd16/gpsyd_top8_depaoli.jpg)


**Name:** Santiago De Paoli  
  
**Hometown:** Buenos Aires, Argentina  
  
**Age:** 29  
  
**Occupation:** Customer Service  
  
**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Top 64 PT Paris, 2011 (40th)  
  
**What color combination was your Sealed Deck? What your best card and why?**  

Red-White. My best card was [Mirrorwing Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirrorwing+Dragon), because I had a few pump spells.  
  
**What color combinations was your 1st draft deck? What your best card and why?**  

Green-Black. [Liliana, the Last Hope](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%2C+the+Last+Hope), because she likes to go “Infinite Zombies”.  
  
**What color combinations was your 2nd draft deck? What your best card and why?**  

Red-White. The equipment that gives +2/+2 ([Cultist's Staff](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cultist%27s+Staff)). My deck was mediocre.  
  
**What card surprised you the most this weekend?**  

The equipment I just mentioned.  
  
**What's at stake for you this week—here and at Pro Tour Eldritch Moon (if applicable)?**  

I just want to experience the whole Pro Tour and being able to prepare for it with Team DEX Army makes it even better.




---

  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpsyd16/gpsyd_top8_takahashi.jpg)


**Name:** Zen Takahashi  
  
**Hometown:** Auckland, New Zealand  
  
**Age:** 19  
  
**Occupation:** Student/Writer for MTG Mint Card  
  
**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**  

- Top 4 Grand Prix Sydney 2013  

- Top 8 Grand Prix Sydney 2015  

- Team member for this year's New Zealand World Magic Cup team  
  
**What color combination was your Sealed Deck? What your best card and why?**  

White-Black splash blue, [Bygone Bishop](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bygone+Bishop). The only bomb in my deck.  
  
**What color combinations was your 1st draft deck? What your best card and why?**  

Green-Red Werewolves, [Vildin-Pack Outcast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vildin-Pack+Outcast). My deck had three [Waxing Moon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Waxing+Moon)!  
  
**What color combinations was your 2nd draft deck? What your best card and why?**  

Blue-Red Spells, [Mercurial Geists](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mercurial+Geists). The biggest payoff creature for the archetype.  
  
**What card surprised you the most this weekend?**  
[Graf Harvest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Graf+Harvest)  
  
**What's at stake for you this week—here and at Pro Tour Eldritch Moon (if applicable)?**  

I had 14 Pro points going into this GP, so I needed a Top 8 to lock Silver. I've managed to get there so I'm now qualified for the next three Pro Tours! Now I have to book a new flight for the Pro Tour next weekend!

RB. [Olivia's Dragoon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Olivia%27s+Dragoon), because it's a free Madness enabler, cheap cost, and also sometimes a Delirium enabler.  
  
**What card surprised you the most this weekend?**  
[Vildin-Pack Outcast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vildin-Pack+Outcast)  
  
**What's at stake for you this week—here and at Pro Tour Eldritch Moon (if applicable)?**

White-Black Delirium, [Sanctifier of Souls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sanctifier+of+Souls). It allowed me to come from behind.  
  
**What card surprised you the most this weekend?**  
[Lashweed Lurker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lashweed+Lurker)

I needed an invite to Pro Tour Honolulu, so this will help in chaining some PTs together.
