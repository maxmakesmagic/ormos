### Intoroduction

Each M-fest Feeder Tournament will be open to all participants.

The season runs from April 1st, 2007 through July 25th, 2007 and players can compete in each M-fest Feeder Tournament. Even if you win an M-fest Feeder Tournament in April, you can play and try to win again in May, June and July!

### Entry Fee

Entry fees vary from location to location. Check with the Tournament Organiser for specific information.

### Tournament Locations




* [Great Britain and Ireland](/en/articles/archive/feature/m-fest-feeder-tournament-locations-2007-03-22)

### Tournament Structure

The tournament structures vary from location to location. Check with the Tournament Organiser for specific information.

### Tournament Format

The tournament formats vary from location to location. Check with the Tournament Organiser for specific information.

Tournaments will be run according to the latest DCI Universal Tournament Rules, Penalty Guidelines, and all DCI rules pertaining to **Magic: the Gathering**.

### Prizes

* Each M-fest Feeder Tournament will give M-fest Feeder Series points to its participants according to the table below. These will determine the amount of extra prizes that players will get at [M-Fest](/en/articles/archive/feature/m-fest-artist-profiles-2007-05-31).


|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Finish** | **Points** |
| 1st  | 5 |
| 2nd | 3 |
| 3rd-4th | 2 |
| 5th and lower | 1 |

* All players receive an M-fest Player Giveaway while supplies last. (Please note that this will change monthly.)
* Additional packs of **Magic: The Gathering** boosters to top players, (as determined by the Tournament Organizer).

Every five points a player earns beyond their first fivefrom M-fest Feeder Tournaments will get them 10 boosters when they show up at [M-Fest](/en/articles/archive/feature/m-fest-artist-profiles-2007-05-31).

### Additional Information

* Contact the Tournament Organiser for location and registration information about any particular Junior Series Challenge.
* Players who have general questions about M-fest Feeder Tournaments or the [M-Fest](/en/articles/archive/feature/m-fest-artist-profiles-2007-05-31) should contact Wizards of the Coast customer service at 08457 125 559, or send us email to [wizards@hasbro.co.uk](mailto:wizards@hasbro.co.uk?subject=Junior%20Series%20Challenge%20(magicthegathering.com)).
