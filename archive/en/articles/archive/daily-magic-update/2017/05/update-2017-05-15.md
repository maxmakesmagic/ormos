The May 15, 2017 Update
=======================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on May 15, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on May 15, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by mistakes. [Everyone makes them](#7), and sometimes they win the Pro Tour shortly thereafter.


What You Might Have Missed This Weekend
=======================================


1. [Pro Tour *Amonkhet*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptakh)

I mean, I *hope* you didn’t miss the Pro Tour this weekend. I suppose it’s possible you were trapped under a rock, or maybe your first child was being born, or maybe you were too busy winning the whole thing to watch any coverage (Gerry). If you did miss it, here are the best links to catch up:


* [Twitch archives of all three days](https://www.twitch.tv/magic/videos/all)
* [Top Stories of Pro Tour *Amonkhet*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptakh/top-stories-of-pro-tour-amonkhet-2017-05-14) | Corbin Hosler, Frank Karsten, and Nate Price
* [Top 8 Deck Lists](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptakh/top-8-decklists-2017-05-13)
* [24-27 Point Deck Lists](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptakh/24-27-point-standard-decklists-2017-05-14)
* [Finals: Gerry Thompson Vs. (22) Yuuya Watanabe](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptakh/finals-gerry-thompson-vs-yuuya-watanabe-2017-05-14) | Corbin Hosler
* [Pro Tour *Amonkhet* Day Two Standard Metagame Breakdown](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptakh/pro-tour-amonkhet-day-two-standard-metagame-breakdown-2017-05-13) | Frank Karsten

Those will get you caught up on the newsy parts, but there are a few pieces that showcase the people that are worth reading as well. Like…


1. [Family Fans](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptakh/family-fans-2017-05-12) | Wizards of the Coast | Corbin Hosler

Corbin, king of the *Magic* profile pieces that tug at your heartstrings, goes straight for them in this profile piece on the family of Brand Nelson and Corey Baumeister.


1. [Teeming With Hope](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptakh/teeming-with-hope-2017-05-12) | Wizards of the Coast | Corbin Hosler

Corbin again, though this one is a touch less, well, touching and more of an uplifting look at the Pro Tour Team Series, specifically Team MTG Mint Card.


1. [What does a *Magic* art director do?](http://sheppard-arts.blogspot.com/2017/05/what-does-magic-art-director-do.html?m=1) | Cynthia Sheppard

Now for something totally unrelated to the Pro Tour. Well, mostly unrelated—the cards played there still do have art on them, after all. Cynthia Sheppard, *Magic* Art Director extraordinaire, was recently nominated for a Chesley award for Best Art Director. In the art world, that’s a pretty big deal—but it also requires some explanation for anyone *not* in the art world. So she does that. The explanation, that is.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [What We Did Wrong at Pro Tour *Amonkhet*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J_P5tY4-27I) | ChannelFireball | Paulo Vitor Damo Da Rosa

PVDDR reflects on his team’s testing process and what went wrong for Channel Fireball Ice. They put Eric Froehlich into the Top 8, but the rest of the team floundered. Hear Paulo’s take on why.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/J_P5tY4-27I>


1. [Pro Tour *Amonkhet*: By the Numbers](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/pro-tour-amonkhet-by-the-numbers?src=wizards) | MTGGoldfish | SaffronOlive

This is the earliest, most comprehensive analysis you’ll find online of how various archetypes performed at the Pro Tour. It’s a Spike’s dream, basically.


1. [*Magic: The Gathering* - Everybody Makes Mistakes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d6m1PB0TLms) | Elantris

This is great. A bevy of Pros and Pro-adjacent players talk about their worst plays on camera. Gerry Thompson [Grasp of Darknessing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=407595) a 4/5 for no effect not included. He won, so we can joke about it.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/d6m1PB0TLms>


What People Are Talking About (Mostly Gerry Thompson)
=====================================================


1. > 
> The volume and intensity of love in the Magic community for [@G3RRYT](https://twitter.com/G3RRYT) is more impressive than his win I think. People are so happy!
>

The [congratulations](https://twitter.com/bmkibler/status/863858115334225920) [poured in](https://twitter.com/mtg_lee/status/863858060690718720) [from](https://twitter.com/Boss_MTG/status/863808442875736065) [all corners of the internet](https://www.reddit.com/r/magicTCG/comments/6b65ck/congratulations_to_the_winner_of_pro_tour_amonkhet/). You can view a clip of [his winner’s interview here](https://clips.twitch.tv/InexpensiveLivelyPonyPJSalt).


1. >
