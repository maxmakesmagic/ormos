Grand Prix - Taipei
===================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 13, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












The **Magic: The Gathering** Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro Tour events.


### Tournament Format And Schedule


The format for Grand Prix–Taipei is *Kamigawa* Block Constructed. All DCI Constructed-format rules, Magic Floor Rules, and all subsequent updates will be enforced.


**Friday, August 5**: Onsite registration is from 3:00 – 9:00 P.M.  
**Saturday, August 6**: Late registration from 8:30 – 9:30 A.M. Tournament begins at 10:00 A.M. The Top 64 players advance to competition on Sunday.  
**Sunday, August 7**: The Top 64 players begin play at 9:00 A.M.


### Tournament Location


**Taipei Hero House Friends of Armed forces Association R.O.C**

**Access to Venue**

### Tournament


**Divisions**  

The main tournament at Grand Prix–Taipei is open to **Magic** players of all ages.


* **Head Judge**  

 The Head Judge for Grand Prix–Taipei will be Jacky Yang , Level 3 from Taiwan.
* **Tournament Structure**  

 The structure for Grand Prix–Taipei will be modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds), with the Top 64 players advancing to the second day of competition for prizes and invitations.
* **Amateur Status**  

 A player is considered to have amateur status if he or she has 0 lifetime pro points. If a player has ever been awarded any points from a Grand Prix, Pro Tour, or World Championship event, that player no longer has amateur status, and is not eligible for amateur awards at Grand Prix or other tournaments.
* **Tournament Divisions and Prizes**  

 Grand Prix–Taipei will feature the standard main tournament prize schedule, as well as a special Amateur division prize schedule. Players who have 0 lifetime Pro Points will be eligible for the Amateur prizes. The Amateur prizes will be paid to the highest-finishing amateur players *in addition to any winnings those players receive from the main tournament prize schedule*. All prizes are in U.S. dollars.

* **Pro Tour Invitations**  

 The Top 8 previously uninvited players will each win an invitation to Pro Tour–Los Angeles. *In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher in that Grand Prix. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*
* **Tournament Byes** (*Byes are not cumulative throughout the season or year*)  

 Byes will be given to players in the Grand Prix based on the following criteria: ***Third-Round Byes*** 
	+ Players with 28 or more professional points in the 2005 Philadelphia Pro Standings.
	+ DCI players with a Constructed rating of 1950 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed ratings published on May 11, 2005.***Second-Round Byes*** 
	+ Players with 14 or more professional points in the 2005 Philadelphia Pro Standings.
	+ DCI players with a Constructed rating of 1850 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed ratings published on May 11, 2005.***First-Round Byes*** 
	+ Players with 7 or more professional points in the 2005 Philadelphia Pro Standings.
	+ DCI players with a Constructed rating of 1750 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed ratings published on May 11, 2005.

### Side Events


* **Grand Prix–Taipei Trials** (Full List of Trials)  
**Friday, August 5**: Trial starts as soon as 32 players is fill up. Maximum 32 players per Trial, single-elimination. Trial starts as soon as 32 players is fill up. The top finisher receives a third-round bye at Grand Prix–Taipei and one display of *Saviour of Kamigawa* boosters. The format is *Kamigawa* Block Constructed. Cost: NTD 250, Registration Time: 12noon—6pm
* **Pro Tour– Los Angeles Qualifier**  
**Sunday, August 7**: Registration closes at 9:45 A.M.; play begins at 10:00 A.M. The top finisher will received an invitation and a returned air ticket to Pro Tour -- Los Angeles. The format is *Block Constructed*. Cost: NTD350
* **On Demand Tournaments**  

 Eight-person single-elimination, on-demand tournaments will be offered throughout the weekend. Costs: Constructed NTD250, Draft NTD350, Sealed NTD650
