**Battle of the Planes ends tomorrow, June 21**


As a reminder, Battle of the Planes will be coming to an end tomorrow on *Magic Online*. Event entry will close at 7 a.m. and the event itself will end at 10 a.m. PT on June 21.

***Amonkhet* Sealed MOCS Monthly Friday, June 23**


A *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) *Amonkhet* Sealed Monthly Event is coming up on June 23! Players can spend 35 Qualifier Points (QPs) to enter the event. The series will contain 24 Monthly Events, each of which qualify top players for one of the four Playoff Events. These Playoff Events qualify top players for the *Magic Online* Championship, a Pro Tour, and the Pro Tour Challenge event at that Pro Tour.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points for the year. Top players on the Leaderboard at the end of the 2017 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.


We look forward to seeing you on June 23 for the latest Monthly Event! Learn more about the 2017 Magic Online Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

***Amonkhet* Sealed MOCS Open on Saturday, June 24**


A *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) *Amonkhet* Sealed Open Event is coming up on June 24! MOCS Open events do not require an invitation or Qualifier Points to join. The winner qualifies top players for the *Magic Online* Championship, a Pro Tour, and the Pro Tour Challenge event at that Pro Tour.


Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online* Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).
