Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 8, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro










Though many things have changed over the years with Grand Prix tournaments, the last-chance trials remain a Friday staple. Affectionately called “Grinders” from “Meat Grinders,” winning one of these can earn you two byes for the next morning.


Joseph Sanchez, Arthur Villela, David Situ, Steven Busley, and Jay Scott won Sealed deck trials, and below are the players who won in Standard trials, along with their decklists.


You might want to take special note of Ivo Mednev's Atarka Dragons—complete with both Atarkas!
