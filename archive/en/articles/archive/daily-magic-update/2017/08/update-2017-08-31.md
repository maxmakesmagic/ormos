Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Blue-Black Metallurgic Pyramid](https://twitter.com/utdzac/status/902991852491726850)

[Sunset Pyramid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunset+Pyramid) has been something of a secret star in more controlling *Hour of Devastation* Draft decks, so it's not shocking to see someone try it out in Standard. It's even less surprising that that someone is Zac Elsik, creator of the Modern Lantern deck and all-around offbeat deck brewer.


This one isn't *that* offbeat, as many have tried [Metallurgic Summonings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metallurgic+Summonings) before. But what makes this one neat (beyond the [Sunset Pyramid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunset+Pyramid)s) is that the main deck is virtually rotation-proof. Of the non-lands, just [Grasp of Darkness](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grasp+of+Darkness) is leaving us. The deck does lose [Choked Estuary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Choked+Estuary) and [Sunken Hollow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunken+Hollow) from its mana base, but picks up [Drowned Catacomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drowned+Catacomb) in *Ixalan* to help compensate. Everything else stays, which means we could be demolishing Pyramids in Standard for quite some time.
