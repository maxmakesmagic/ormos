Aggro Loam
==========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 8, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Last weekend's StarCityGames.com Open in Las Vegas saw some old favorites appear in the Top 8… like Aggro Loam! This powerful midrange deck has a deceptively strong land-based engine revolving around [Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam). The *Ravnica* sorcery combines with cycling lands like [Forgotten Cave](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forgotten+Cave) to help [Countryside Crusher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Countryside+Crusher) get big quickly and let you kill out of nowhere with the one-two punch of [Seismic Assault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seismic+Assault). Check out this deck for your next Legacy tournament!
