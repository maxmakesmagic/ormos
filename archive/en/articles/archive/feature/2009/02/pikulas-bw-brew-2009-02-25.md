For many players, Team Deadguy member Chris Pikula will always be the [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage). That's because after winning the Invitational, the 2/2 is the card Chris decided to design as his prize. The nickname didn't stop the East Coaster from coming out of retirement a few years later to Top 8 the Legacy Grand Prix held in Philadelphia. You can read more about the event in [the archives](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/gpphi05/welcomea). If you're interested in Legacy, [visit the Grand Prix-Chicago page](/en/articles/archive/grand-prix%E2%80%93chicago-event-information-2009-01-28) for information on this year's Legacy Grand Prix.
