*Scars of Mirrodin* Game Day has come and gone. Jos Morales took part in the festivities at Legends and Dragons in Guatemala. The end result was this Mono-Black Control deck, which uses hand disruption and creature removal to keep the opponent under wraps. From there, Jos uses powerful attackers like [Abyssal Persecutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abyssal+Persecutor) and [Grave Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grave+Titan) to finish the hapless competition off!
