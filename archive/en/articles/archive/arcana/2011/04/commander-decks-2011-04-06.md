Commander Decks
===============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 6, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











As you may perhaps remember, [***Magic: The Gathering** Commander*](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/productarticle.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/commander/productinfo) is going to be on sale on June 17, and it will consist of five different 100-card singleton decks, each with a suggested retail price of $29.99. You may also remember that between the five decks, there are a total of 51 new **Magic** cards. But what you do not remember is what each of the decks will look like! To be fair, this is probably because we haven't shown them to you. Until now!
