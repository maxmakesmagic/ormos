![Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Astral+Slide)Hall of Famer Bob Maher was semi-retired from **Magic** when Grand Prixâ€“Detroit rolled around in 2003. When his friend and noted deck builder Brian Kowal handed him a powerful red-white Astral Slide deck for the event, Bob's gamer instincts took over and he powered his way to the championship trophy. Using his cyclers in conjunction with [Astral Slide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide), he held off all comers. [Read more about the event in the official event coverage archives](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=sideboard/events/gpdet03).
