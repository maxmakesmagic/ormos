Top 8 Decklists
===============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 19, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











A healthy mix of archetypes made the Top 8 at Grand Prix Porto Alegre. In addition to two Mardu Vehicles making the cut, the Top 8 also featured three *copies* of Copycat, one Temur Tower deck, one Black-Green Delirium deck and one Black-Green Energy deck!


1. Sebastian Pozzo (Mardu Vehicles)
2. Luis Salvatto (Four-Color Copycat)
3. Patricio Roman (Four-Color Copycat)
4. Vagner William Casatti (Mardu Vehicles)
5. Carlos Alexandre Dos Santos Esteve (Four-Color Copycat)
6. Victor Fernando Silva (Temur Tower)
7. Francisco Sifuentes (Black Green Delirium)
8. Rafael Bertolli Parra (Black-Green Energy)

Decklists are displayed according to Swiss standings after Round 15!
