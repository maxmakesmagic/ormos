*Scars of Mirrodin* has hit shelves, and the **Magic** world is busily brewing with the latest set. One slightly unlikely card that's attracted a lot of attention is the ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20210707095534im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless0.gif)-cost [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite). In this deck, piloted by Axel Jensen to a Top 16 finish at last weekend's TCGPlayer Wizard World Series in New York, [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite) and fellow ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20210707095534im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless0.gif)-cost creature [Ornithopter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ornithopter) team up to power out [Glint Hawk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk)s, carry [Adventuring Gear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Adventuring+Gear), and trigger [Quest for the Holy Relic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quest+for+the+Holy+Relic) to search up deadly Equipment like [Argentum Armor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Argentum+Armor) and [Sword of Body and Mind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+Body+and+Mind).
