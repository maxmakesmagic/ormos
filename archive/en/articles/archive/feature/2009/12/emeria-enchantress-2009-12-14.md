The 2009 State and Provincial Championships took place across North America recently. One of the top finishers in Connecticut? Roger Sampson, who used an innovative white Enchantress deck. The deck is filled with powerful enchantments like [Journey to Nowhere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Journey+to+Nowhere), [Oblivion Ring](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Ring), and [Luminarch Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Luminarch+Ascension), and Roger's coup de grace iss [Mesa Enchantress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesa+Enchantress). The plucky 0/2 nets him a free card for each enchantment he casts!
