Decks of the Week: 11/03/2011
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 3, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Congratulations to Martin Juza on his victory at Grand Prix Hiroshima! Juza won the Standard tournament with a Green-white Tokens deck armed with the key metagame choice of [Mirran Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirran+Crusader) against [Kessig Wolf Run](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kessig+Wolf+Run) decks, which had been one of the dominant archetypes heading into the tournament.
