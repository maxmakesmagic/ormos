Team China emerged from the 2009 Team World Championships the victor in Rome, Italy. The team competition featured three member teams competing in one format a piece: Standard, Extended, and Legacy. For China, it was Bo Li battling Legacy and his deck of choice was a Fish build. A powerful strategy featuring cheap blue Merfolk backed by [Lord of Atlantis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Lord%2Bof%2BAtlantis) and counters, he was very successful despite not having played much Legacy prior to the event itself. For more information on the 2009 World Championships, [visit the official event coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12).
