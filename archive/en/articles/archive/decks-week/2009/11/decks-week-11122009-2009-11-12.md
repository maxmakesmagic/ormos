Decks of the Week: 11/12/2009
=============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on November 12, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











And we're back! Adrian Rosada had a busy weekend, winning the largest **Magic**  tournament ever held. Nearly 2,000 players showed up to battle at the [Grand Prix in Paris](/en/events/coverage/trap-secures-rosada%E2%80%99s-monument-paris) and Adrian outlasted the field to take the top slot. Check out his deck list, and the rest of the Top 8, below. Of course, we also have the latest from the world of Pro Tour Qualifiers for San Diego, *Zendikar* Game Day, and of course **Magic Online**.


Decks of the Week rounds up the biggest events from the digital world week in and week out, but if you can't wait for Thursday to roll around, you don't have to! With the debut of [www.mtgonline.com](http://www.mtgonline.com), you can check out all the latest **Magic Online** deck lists each day! Plus, there's tons of announcements and other important information to be found on that page. So what are you waiting for? Check out the lists, and check back here and on the new **Magic Online** site often for more!


Enjoy!
