Announcing From the Vault: Twenty
=================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 18, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












**Twenty Proven Champions!**


![From the Vault: Twenty](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1171_logo_h7wy6g7fip.jpg)For the twentieth birthday of **Magic: The Gathering**, we've got a special twenty-card *From the Vault*! Draw on the power of twenty influential **Magic** cards to give your decks an edge. Each was a key part of a premier tournament-winning deck from each of the past twenty years. Now you can learn their secrets, harness their strengths, and use them to defeat your enemies!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1171_symbol_998tjye2fl.jpg) 


### Contents and Details


* **Twenty** premium foil cards, including some with new art.
* An exclusive Spindown life counter.
* A collector's guide.
* Each card has been printed using a foil process unique to the From the Vault series.
* The twenty cards are all printed in the current card frame. For some, it is for the first time.
* *From the Vault: Twenty* will be available worldwide in English only, and will have an extremely limited print run.
* All cards are black bordered and tournament legal. This means that these cards are legal for use in any tournaments where the original printings are still legal.

Please use the hashtag [#FTV20](http://twitter.com/#!/search/%23ftv20) for discussing this product on Twitter!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1171_art_uo9k10kc2e.jpg)Art by [Izzy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Izzy%22%5D)


**Product Concept and Development Team**: Gavin Verhey and Jeremy Jarvis  
**Release Date**: August 23, 2013  
**MSRP**: $39.99 ***\*Applies to U.S. Only***


Note: From the Vault will go back to fifteen cards and a $34.99 MSRP ***\*Applies to U.S. Only*** in 2014.
