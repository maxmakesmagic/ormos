Round 8: William Jensen vs. Ben Rubin
=====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 18, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a828.jpg) Read about their draft [here](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/draft-3-rubin-thoren-and-jensen-2003-01-18).

Jensen and Rubin are young but experienced veterans of the Pro Tour who grew up with the game. Jensen is best known as a limited expert, and Rubin has had two Pro Tour finals appearances many years back, and also has two Masters championships under his belt. Both players arrived at today's rounds with 6-1 records, and are looking to start their day with a win.

### Game 1

Rubin played [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) on turn two, and Jensen put down a morph creature. [Wirewood Savage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Savage) gave Rubin another great creature, and [Lavamancer's Skill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavamancer%27s+Skill) let Jensen's morph kill [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith). This was followed by [Riptide Biologist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Riptide+Biologist). Rubin used his turn to cycle [Krosan Tusker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Tusker), and [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)ed the Skilled morph creature, which turned out to be [Mistform Shrieker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Shrieker). The Biologist attacked for one point of damage, and Jensen went to the air with [Ascending Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven). The Savage attacked back for two, and was joined by [Symbiotic Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Symbiotic+Elf). Jensen's Aven came over for three damage, and the ground was fortified with [Mistform Wall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Wall) and a morph creature, leaving Jensen with no cards in hand. Rubin used his turn to put two [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher)s on the table, making him capable of gaining eight life a turn with the current board.

[Battering Craghorn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battering+Craghorn) gave Rubin a card when it came into play, and Jensen's Aven was fighting an uphill battle of three damage a turn to Rubin's eight life a turn. [Centaur Glade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Centaur+Glade) gave Rubin a huge threat, and once eight mana was reached the board was flooded with 3/3 creatures.

Jensen- 0 Rubin- 1

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a829.jpg)Jensen started off with a second turn Biologist. It was joined by [Mistform Dreamer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Dreamer), and Rubin summoned [Wirewood Savage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Savage). Jensen played [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift) and cycled a land to kill the Savage, so Rubin played another one. The Dreamer continued to attack through the air, and [Ascending Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven) showed up to increase the clock to five damage. [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher) and [Leery Fogbeast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leery+Fogbeast) gave Rubin a bit more to work with, but Jensen had a significant amount of pressure in the air, and played two morph creatures after an attack. Rubin cycled [Solar Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solar+Blast) to kill the Dreamer, and [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock) brought down the Aven. Both of Jensen's morphs attacked, and [Leery Fogbeast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leery+Fogbeast) and [Wirewood Savage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Savage) teamed up to block one. It was morphed to reveal [Skittish Valesk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skittish+Valesk), and Rubin was left with [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher) as his only creature. 

[Symbiotic Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Symbiotic+Elf) was played, and Jensen attacked with his morph, which was unblocked and morphed before combat to reveal [Battering Craghorn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battering+Craghorn). A Dreamer made the board a little better. The Elf attacked back, and Rubin played a morph. Jensen's Craghorn and Dreamer attacked, and Rubin blocked the first striker with his morph, which was revealed to be a Craghorn as well. Damage was put on the stack, and [Chain of Vapor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+of+Vapor) bounced back Jensen's Craghorn; [Vitality Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vitality+Charm) saved Rubin's. At this point in the game, the ground was even and [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher) was gaining two life a turn to nullify [Mistform Dreamer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Dreamer)'s attacks. [Clone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clone) gave Jensen a second Dreamer, and he was now able to get through for two damage per turn. [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) made Jensen's position even better, but [Threaten](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Threaten) stole the [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith), and Rubin used it to kill a Dreamer. It was then sacrificed to Rubin's [Goblin Sledder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sledder) before it went back to Jensen's side.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a830.jpg)Things were now back to even, with the [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher) and Dreamer keeping each other in check. [Wave of Indifference](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wave+of+Indifference) was used to let [Skirk Commando](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Commando) get through, and it killed the Dreamer. Jensen finally found a cycling card, and used it to target [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher), but Rubin sacrificed two Goblins with the Sledder to keep it alive. A second [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher) came out the turn after, and Jensen used [Choking Tethers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Choking+Tethers) to clear the way for an attack, bringing Rubin to three life points and killing a [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher) thanks to Rift. Morphs butted heads, and the battle turned out to be [Riptide Biologist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Riptide+Biologist) to [Snarling Undorak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snarling+Undorak). Another cycling card killed the remaining [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher), and both Biologists attacked to bring Rubin back to three. This cleared the way for a big Undorak attack, but Rubin was far enough behind to allow the Biologists were able to win the race.

Jensen- 1 Rubin- 1 

### Game 3

Rubin brought out [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher) on turn two, and Jensen had [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift). The Rift took out a morph and [Wirewood Savage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Savage), and Rubin tried to gain back some ground with [Wirewood Herald](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Herald) and a Morph. [Lavamancer's Skill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavamancer%27s+Skill) made [Riptide Biologist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Riptide+Biologist) a machine, and it shot Rubin's morph. Rubin's shrugged and morphed it to reveal an Undorak, but Jensen simply played a second Skill on [Snapping Thragg](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snapping+Thragg) to finish the job. With three points of direct damage available to kill creatures every turn, things weren't looking very good for Rubin. [Mistform Shrieker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Shrieker) followed by a [Clone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clone) of the 3/3 flier let Jensen win in convincing fashion.

**Final Result:** Jensen- 2 Rubin- 1
