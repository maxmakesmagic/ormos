Magic Online *Urza's Destiny* Prerelease News
=============================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 29, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











With the announcement of [*New Phyrexia*](/en/node/661551), now is a great time to go back and revisit the first war against the Phyrexians and see how it all began. In *Urza's Destiny*, the ancient planeswalker seeks to complete his epic struggle across Dominaria and silence the Phyrexian campaign of terror. 


The *Urza's Destiny* prerelease will feature three separate parts: 

* Scheduled Swiss Sealed Flights
* 8-Player Swiss Draft Queues
* Casual Drafts

 See below for more information. 
 ### *URZA'S DESTINY* PRERELEASE SWISS SEALED FLIGHTS


*START TIMES:*  


* Friday, April 1 at 11:00 AM PDT (18:00 UTC)
* Friday, April 1 at 4:00 PM PDT (23:00 UTC)
* Saturday, April 2 at 3:00 AM PDT (10:00 UTC)
* Saturday, April 2 at 3:00 PM PDT (22:00 UTC)
* Sunday, April 3 at 11:00 AM PDT (18:00 UTC)

*LOCATION:*  Premier Events room.   
*ENTRY OPTION(S):*  30 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 2 *Urza's Saga* booster packs, 2 *Urza's Legacy* booster packs, and 2 *Urza's Destiny* booster packs for this event.   
*SIZE:*  33 Players Minimum, 256 maximum.  
*DURATION:* Deck building time plus 6 rounds, Swiss pairings. Play in all six rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 
 

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 18 | 15 *Urza's Destiny* booster packs | 3 |
| 15 | 8 *Urza's Destiny* booster packs | 2 |
| 12 | 4 *Urza's Destiny* booster packs | 1 |
| 9 | 2 *Urza's Destiny* booster packs | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in a *Urza's Destiny* Prerelease Tournament will receive 1 copy of the prerelease [False Prophet](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=False+Prophet) promo card. Players with at least 9 match points at the end of the tournament will also receive a premium foil False Prophet. ### *URZA'S DESTINY* 8-PLAYER SWISS DRAFT QUEUES


*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Saturday, April 2 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC) and ending Sunday, April 3 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC).  
*LOCATION:*  8-Player Draft room.   
*ENTRY OPTIONS:*  20 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 3 *Urza's Destiny* booster packs for this event.   
*SIZE:*  8 Players.  
*DURATION:* Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, Swiss pairings. Play all three rounds, win or lose!  
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 9 | 4 *Urza's Destiny* booster packs | 1 |
| 6 | 2 *Urza's Destiny* booster packs | 0 |
| 3 | 1 *Urza's Destiny* booster pack | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in a *Urza's Destiny* 8-Player Swiss Draft will receive 1 copy of the prerelease [False Prophet](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=False+Prophet) promo card. Players with 9 points will also receive a premium foil False Prophet. ### *URZA'S DESTINY* CASUAL DRAFT QUEUES


*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Sunday, April 3 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC) and ending at the beginning of the scheduled downtime on Wednesday, May 5.  
*LOCATION:*  Casual Draft room.   
*ENTRY OPTIONS:*  Product only.  
*PRODUCT:*  1 *Urza's Saga* booster pack , 1 *Urza's Legacy* booster pack and 1 *Urza's Destiny* booster pack.  
*SIZE:*  4, 6, or 8 Players.  
*DURATION:* Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, Swiss pairings.   
*PRIZES:* None.
