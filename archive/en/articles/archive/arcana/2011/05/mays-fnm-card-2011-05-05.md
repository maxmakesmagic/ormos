May's FNM Card
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 5, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











It's Thursday, which means that tomorrow is Friday. And *that* means that tomorrow night is [Friday Night **Magic**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm)! Since we're now in May, there's a new card being given out at FNM. Check it!


![Squadron Hawk](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/704_squadronhawk.jpg)  
Each month a different foil card will be given out. The cards feature the FNM logo and many have original flavor text and updated wordings.
