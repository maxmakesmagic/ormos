Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 22, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast

*Brad Carpenter* |

**Name:**Brad Carpenter  
**Age:** 18


**Home Town and Country:** St. Petersburg, FL USA


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 2 PTQ Wins, top 100 PT Honolulu ‘09, Top 32 GP Tampa ‘09, T64 GP Chicago ‘09


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**  

Jund with some sick tech by Logan Mize


**Would you change anything about your deck in light of the weekend?**  

No


**Are you playing at PT San Juan, and how much testing have you done so far?**  

Unfortunately, No


**If you knew you were going to win the GP no matter what, which card from Rise of the Eldrazi would you like to use to do it?**  
[Sarkhan the Mad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sarkhan+the+Mad)

*Michael Joseph Stanfar* |

**Name:** Michael Joseph Stanfar  
**Age:** 23


**Home Town and Country:** Fredricksburg, VA USA


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** None


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**  

Super Friends – Most resilient, powerful cards. Felt like I had a lot of control of winning / losing


**Would you change anything about your deck in light of the weekend?**  
[Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) was sub-par. Possibly run a [Sphinx of Jwar Isle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx+of+Jwar+Isle) main, games were taking too long to win


**Are you playing at PT San Juan, and how much testing have you done so far?**  

Nope, testing with friends whom are going


**If you knew you were going to win the GP no matter what, which card from Rise of the Eldrazi would you like to use to do it?**  
[Gideon Jura](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon+Jura), that guy is the real deal!
