Kenny Oberg is the Grand Prix–Gothenburg champion! It was the largest event ever held in Scandinavia, no doubt in part because it was just one weekend prior to Pro Tour-Amsterdam. Oberg bested a tough field that included Hall of Famer Nicolai Herzog, super-pro Anton Jonsson, and regular gravy train members Marijn Lybaert and Sam Black. The combo of colors that got him there? Green and red, with just enough black to support a [Doom Blade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doom+Blade). For more on the event, read the official coverage [here](/en/events/coverage/tezzerator-dominates-gothenburg).
