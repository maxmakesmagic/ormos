### PRO TOUR KHANS OF TARKIR


REGIONAL PTQ LOCATIONS AND PROMO CARD REVEALED
==============================================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/n2KoE-g2_bigger.jpeg)

Mike Rosenberg

Mike Rosenberg is a writer and gamer and has been part of the Magic text coverage team since 2011. These days, he spends less time globetrotting around the world for Grand Prix and more time in Renton, WA, working as the organized play content specialist for Wizards of the Coast.


October 11, 2014
 










Today, during the Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir* coverage, Director of Organized Play Programs and Operations Helene Bergeot announced the locations for the 31 locations for the first round of Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers, set to take place April 25, 2015.


The locations for these Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers, which you can qualify for by winning a Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifier from December 6 – February 22, will take place in the following cities across the world:

Players who compete in a Regional Pro Tour Qualifier during 2015 will receive a [Liliana of the Veil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil) promo cards.


![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/ptktk/rptq_promo.png)

Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifiers, which qualify winners for the Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers, are store-level events. To learn more about what format your local Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifiers will be run in, and to see if your local store is running one, talk to your store. Sanctioning for these events is open now, so be sure to check with your store to see if they are planning to run one!


For more information on the upcoming Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifier season, [click here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/pptqvan15).


For more information on the Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers set to take place on April 25, 2015, [click here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/rptqvan15).
