Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 15, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/hero/wizardslogo_thumb.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler and Marc Calderaro











Learn more about Grand Prix Atlanta's Top 8 competitors by taking a look through their profiles!  

 




---


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpatl15/t8_wiznitzer.jpg)Name:** Aryeh Wiznitzer


**Age:** 32


**Occupation:** Teacher


**Hometown:** Houston, Texas


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Top 8 Grand Prix Dallas 2015; some other money finishes.


**What color combination was your sealed deck, and what was your record?**  

Red-white removal. It had [Planar Outburst](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Planar+Outburst) and other good ones. I went 9-0.


**What was your best card, and why?**  
[Quarantine Field](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quarantine+Field). in Sealed it’s always a 2-for-1 or a 3-for-1.


**What color combination was your first draft deck, and what was your record?**  

Black-Red Devoid, going 2-1.


**What was your best card, and why?**  
[Touch of the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Touch+of+the+Void). It always overperforms. Sometimes it goes to the dome!


**What color combination was your second draft deck, and what was your record?**  

Red-white midrange, going 2-0-1.


**What was your best card, and why?**  
[Turn Against](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turn+Against). It found its way into my hand at opportune moments.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpatl15/t8_martell.jpg)Name:** Tom Martell


**Age:** 33


**Occupation:** Lawyer


**Hometown:** Mountain View, California


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Pro Tour Gatecrash champion; Grand Prixs Indianapolis and Sacramento champion.


**What color combination was your sealed deck, and what was your record?**  

Red-green splashing blue.


**What was your best card, and why?**  
[Grove Rumbler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grove+Rumbler). It hit for a lot and applied lots of pressure. Or [Endless One](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Endless+One), as it filled out my curve.


**What color combination was your first draft deck, and what was your record?**   

Blue-Black Devoid; went 3-0.


**What was your best card, and why?**  
[Fathom Feeder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fathom+Feeder). It always let me turn on my processing.


**What color combination was your second draft deck, and what was your record?**  

Blue-Red Devoid; went 3-0.


**What was your best card, and why?**  

Double [Ruination Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ruination+Guide), since I had a million Devoid guys.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpatl15/t8_eason.jpg)Name:** Brian Eason


**Age:** 23


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Atlanta


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Second place at Grand Prix Charleston.


**What color combination was your sealed deck, and what was your record?**  

White-blue splashing for two Drana’s Emissary. I went 7-2.

Drana’s Emissary because it’s a can’t-lose.

Black-green splashing for Drana’s Emissary again, going 3-0.


**What was your best card, and why?**  
[Woodland Wanderer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woodland+Wanderer), [Greenwarden of Murasa](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Greenwarden+of+Murasa), [From Beyond](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=From+Beyond), [Painful Truths](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Painful+Truths), and [Brood Butcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brood+Butcher) because rares.


**What color combination was your second draft deck, and what was your record?**  

White-Black Allies splashing for [Munda, Ambush Leader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Munda%2C+Ambush+Leader).


**What was your best card, and why?**  
[Tandem Tactics](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tandem+Tactics), because of the lifegain triggers.
