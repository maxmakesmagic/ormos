Robert Cash is a player who loves him some tokens! His Sacramento, California PTQ Top 8 deck featured a whopping *five* different token generators in the maindeck! What did he *do* with his tokens? Why, he used his [Furystoke Giant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Furystoke+Giant)s and [Torrent of Souls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Torrent%2Bof%2BSouls) to annihilate his opponents post haste. Find out which cards from the exciting new set *Shards of Alara* you could add to Robert’s deck with the [*Shards of Alara* Visual Spoiler](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/shardsofalara/spoiler).
