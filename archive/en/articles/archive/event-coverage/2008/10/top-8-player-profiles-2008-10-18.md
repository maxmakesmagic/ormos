Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 18, 2008 










![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpkc08/Top-8.jpg)


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpkc08/T8-Romao.jpg)**Name:**  
Carlos Eduardo Romao


**Occupation:**  
**Magic** player


**Previous High Finishes:**  
 I won Worlds 2002 and I have 5 GP Top 8s.


**What was your record Day 1?**  
 8-1


**What was your record Day 2?**  
 2-1  
 2-0-1


**What was the best card from your Sealed Deck?**  
 My best card was [Sigil of Distinction](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sigil+of+Distinction). The card is amazing! I had it in my sideboard. Big mistake!


**What deck or cards are you hoping to draft in the Top 8?**  
 I hope to draft [Sigil of Distinction](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sigil+of+Distinction), so I can make the right move this time and apologize to the **Magic** gods.
