Nicol Bolas Commander
=====================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 29, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorPic_GavinVerhey.jpg)
By Gavin Verhey




 When Gavin Verhey was eleven, he dreamt of a job making Magic cards—and now as a Magic designer, he's living his dream! Gavin has been writing about Magic since 2005. 






Each week, the crew of [CMDR Decks over on GatheringMagic.com](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/author/CMDRDecks/) puts out an article featuring a new Commander deck and a complete video review of that deck. This week, the focus is on the classic elder dragon [Nicol Bolas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=1672)! Before the dragon was a [Planeswalker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+Planeswalker) he was a creature—and the people behind CMDR Decks have pulled his [original version](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=1672) out yet again to lead a Commander deck into battle!


The deck is a solid three-color control deck—and I'm sure the omnipotent [Nicol Bolas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=178020) would have it no other way. Chock full of card advantage, removal spells, and board sweepers, and a handful of finishers to close out the game, this deck aims to make sure it is in control at all times.


The deck slowly broods and plots, just like the mighty dragon himself, until it is ready to jump into action. When the coast is clear—or enough countermagic is available—[Nicol Bolas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=178020) can come down and start tearing apart opponents' hands. If that doesn't pan out and something dreadful like [Spell Crumple](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Crumple) happens to Bolas, the cavalcade of card advantage followed by a threat like [Consecrated Sphinx](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Consecrated+Sphinx) or [Sheoldred, Whispering One](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sheoldred%2C+Whispering+One) should be able to seal the deal.


If you've been looking at building a Commander control deck, a [Nicol Bolas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=178020) Commander deck, or even both, this is a great place to draw some inspiration! Take a look for yourself, check out the CMDR decks column, and watch their videos for more information on their decks!
