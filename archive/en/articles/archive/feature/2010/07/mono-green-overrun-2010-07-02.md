[Vengevine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vengevine) is the hottest card in Standard right now, but Yoel42 didn't even bother to put it main deck in a recent Pro Tour Qualifier Top 8 on **Magic Online**. Instead the goal of Yoel's deck was generating as many creatures as possible, preferably via Eldrazi Spawn tokens or Wolves from [Wolfbriar Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolfbriar+Elemental), then overwhelming the opponent using [Beastmaster Ascension](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Beastmaster+Ascension), [Eldrazi Monument](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Monument), and [Overrun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overrun). It's the perfect deck to test at your Friday Night Magic!
