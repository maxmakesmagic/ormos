Decks of the Week: 3/11/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on March 11, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Were you one of thousands of **Magic** players participating in *Worldwake* Game Day last Saturday? Decks of the Week is here to showcase Game Day deck lists from some of the top Wizards Play Network locations around the world. Looking for a new Standard deck idea? You might just find one in the more than 25 Top 8s listed below. Along with that rush of Standard tech, the Extended Pro Tour Qualifier season for San Juan rolls along. With only [a month left in the season](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/sanjuan10-qualifiers), there's still time for you to win the invite and airfare to the second Pro Tour of the season—and a nice vacation to sunny Puerto Rico!
