Everyone knows about plainswalk, swampwalk, forestwalk, mountainwalk, and islandwalk. But what about the *other* forms of the ability? You know, the ones that no one talks about these days? If you were to take all of the creatures in the game with a \_\_\_\_walk ability that wasn't keyed to one of the basic land types, this is the deck you'd wind up with. As for what your opponent shouldn't have when they play you, how about [Desert](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desert)s, nonbasic lands, snow lands, legendary lands, or blue jeans? Yes, blue jeans.
