Tainted Lands
=============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 31, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Here are the four "tainted" lands from *Torment*. When the cards were designed, the creative team thought it would be cool if they had the art "mirror" basic lands from *Odyssey*. So the original land artists were commissioned to do darker versions of their original basic lands.


Click on each one to see what the lands look like "un-tainted."
