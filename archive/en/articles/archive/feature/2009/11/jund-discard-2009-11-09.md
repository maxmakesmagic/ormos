The 2009 **Magic** Game Day was a smashing success! All over the world players tried their hands at the new world of *Zendikar* Standard. In Austria, Paul Gelautz opted to battle with Jund, but with an added twist: discard creatures. A twin-set of Specters of the [Sedraxis Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Sedraxis%2BSpecter) and [Hypnotic Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Hypnotic%2BSpecter) variety helped keep an opponent's hand devoid of answers while [Madrush Cyclops](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Madrush+Cyclops) helped make the entire team a turn faster by granting all Paul's creatures haste.
