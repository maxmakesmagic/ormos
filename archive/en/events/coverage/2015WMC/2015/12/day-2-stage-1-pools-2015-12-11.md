DAY TWO STAGE 1 POOLS
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 11, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Below are the pools for the start of Day Two at the 2015 World Magic Cup. Teams will play round robin within their pool during the Battle for Zendikar Team Sealed Deck rounds to start the day.
