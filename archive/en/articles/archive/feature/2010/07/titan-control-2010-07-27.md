Looking for more ways to use your Titans? Kamiya Shogo used this blue, red, and green deck at a recent Standard tournament in Japan. The game plan was to control the flow on the ground by accelerating mana using [Cultivate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cultivate) and [Rampant Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth). [Destructive Force](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Destructive+Force) helped keep things calm, and [Frost Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frost+Titan) or [Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan) batted cleanup to finish the opponent off!
