The September 15, 2017 Update
=============================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on September 15, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Nicholas_Wolfram.jpg)
By Nicholas Wolfram




 Nicholas Wolfram fell promptly in love with tabletop and video games when he was only two years old, and he has been writing about them since he figured out how words worked. Now he puts together words about Magic, which he started playing way back in 2000. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on September 15, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by every *Ixalan* card coming out in two weeks. [All of them](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/ixalan)!


Today's Must
============


1. [A Taste of Pirates](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/lee-shi-tian/a-taste-of-pirates) | MTG Mint Card | Lee Shi Tian

If anyone offers you "a taste of Pirates," you should probably decline politely. Unless it's Lee Shi Tian offering, an *Ixalan* set is coming out soon . . . and it's hopefully not in person.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [*Hour of Devastation* Sunset Show and *Ixalan* Previews](http://lrcast.com/limited-resources-406-hour-of-devastation-sunset-show-and-ixalan-previews/) | Limited Resources | Luis Scott-Vargas and Marshall Sutcliffe

The guys reflect fondly back on *Hour of Devastation* and look forward to *Ixalan* in the near and immediate future.


1. [Everywhere Scars: Jace on Ixalan](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/samkeeper-09142017-everywhere-scars-jace-on-ixalan/) | Gathering Magic | Sam Keeper

Poor Jace has been stranded on Ixalan and is apparently living out his unfortunate fate as a handsome sailor/bodybuilder (at least for now) since he has forgotten his role as a mind mage who does his best to avoid physical activities. Especially fighting. However, his scars and subconscious still hold his history of physical encounters, even if he can't recall them. For those interested in that history, look no further.


1. [Vance's Blasting Cannons in Ramunap Red](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/vances-blasting-cannons-in-ramunap-red/) | ChannelFireball | Mike Sigrist

When a land has a permanent [Open Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Open+Fire) attached to it, burn decks are probably going to want it. However, this land flips from a four-cost enchantment. Mike explores whether this will have a slot in Ramunap Red after rotation.


1. [Eternal Formats, *Ixalan*, and You](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/35836_Eternal-Formats-Ixalan-And-You.html) | StarCityGames.com | Emma Handy

While everyone talks about what Standard will look like after *Ixalan* launches, Emma wants to discuss what will be happening with Modern and Legacy.


1. [Second Chances, Second Choices](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?ID=14166&writer=Corey+Baumeister&articledate=9-15-2017) | TCGplayer | Corey Baumeister

It seems as though playing pro-level *Magic* runs in the family here. Corey reflects on his pro play experiences and his relationship with his brother and fellow pro player Brad Nelson.


1. Full-Art Basic Lands in *Unstable*!

These were previewed at HASCON last week, but we're showing them off again in case you missed the reveal because [John Avon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&output=spoiler&method=visual&artist=+%5b%22John%20Avon%22%5d)'s art is simply that good.

Deck of the Day

1. [BigPrads's God-Pharaoh's Gift](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-09-13)

Soon, the God-Pharaoh will be giving out a lot more Zombie Dinosaurs than he has in the past, which has recently surpassed laughter as the greatest gift one can give. Until then, this is one of the leading builds for the archetype, as it recently went 5-0 in a Competitive Standard League on *Magic Online*.
