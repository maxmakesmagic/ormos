Not a newcomer to the pages of Daily Decks, Conley Woods is well known for using innovative, off the wall decklists to get ahead in tournaments. His foresight served him well at Pro Tour–Honolulu where he was able to take this four-color Aggro deck all the way to the Top 8. For more about Pro Tour–Honolulu, including all the top decklists, [read the official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/mitamura-finds-gold-paradise).
