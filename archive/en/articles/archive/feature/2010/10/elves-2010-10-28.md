Elves aren't a new tribe to the **Magic** world, but Terrance Shields's version used to Top 8 the New York Champs tournament this year has an added twist: it's almost Mirran aligned! What does that mean, and why does it matter? Because *Scars of Mirrodin* Game Day is coming! The global tournament allows you to represent which side of the Mirrodin war you're on, and by doing so you can earn exclusive previews from the upcoming *Mirrodin Besieged* set! To find out how to build your affiliated deck for the official Game Day, visit the [facts page](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/gameday-facts).
