*Onslaught* Block Sealed Deck Tournament V
==========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 9, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











T *he Battle for the Mirari, a mysterious and powerful artifact, has ended. The mighty barbarian Kamahl has been embraced by the Krosan Forest and claims the Mirari in its name - but his trials aren't over yet. The Cabal still holds the continent of Otaria firmly in its merciless grip, and fearsome new threats have appeared along with a new enemy - Ixidor the master illusionist.*


Play with a classic block and compete for great prizes.


### *ONSLAUGHT* BLOCK SEALED DECK TOURNAMENT V


*START TIME:* Saturday, March 20, 2010 11:00 AM PST / 19:00 UTC.   
*LOCATION:*  Premier Events room.   
*ENTRY FEE:*  30 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 2 *Onslaught* (ONS) booster packs, 2 *Legions* (LGN) booster packs, and 2 *Scourge* (SCG) booster packs for this event.  
*SIZE:*  33 players minimum, 512 players maximum.   
*DURATION:* 6 rounds, Swiss Pairings. Play in all six rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 18 |  Complete premium-foil set of any 1 *Onslaught* block expansion, winner's choice | 3 |
| 15 | 15 booster packs (5 ONS, 5 LGN, 5 SCG) | 2 |
| 12 | 9 booster packs (3 ONS, 3 LGN, 3 SCG) | 1 |
| 9 | 3 booster packs (1 ONS, 1 LGN, 1 SCG) | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in the *Onslaught* Block Sealed Deck Tournament V will receive 1 copy of the promotional version of [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift). Players with at least 15 match points at the end of the tournament will also receive a premium foil Lightning Rift.
