*Mirage* Block Sealed Deck Tournament I
=======================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 14, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











W*elcome to Jamuraa, a land steeped in magical traditions that have existed for millennia. The northwest of the huge continent of Jamuraa is occupied by three nations: the militaristic kingdom of the Zhalfirins, the religious state of Femeref, and the trading province of the Suq'Ata empire.*.

### *MIRAGE* BLOCK SEALED DECK TOURNAMENT I

*START TIME:* Sunday, May 2, 2010 11:00 AM PDT / 18:00 UTC.   
*LOCATION:*  Premier Events room.   
*ENTRY FEE:*  30 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 2 *Mirage* booster packs, 2 *Visions* booster packs, and 2 *Weatherlight* booster packs for this event.  
*SIZE:*  33 players minimum, 512 players maximum.   
*DURATION:* 6 rounds, Swiss Pairings. Play in all six rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 18 |  Complete Premium Foil set of any 1 *Mirage* Block expansion, winner's choice. | 3 |
| 15 | 15 booster packs (5 *Mirage*, 5 *Visions*, 5 *Weatherlight*) | 2 |
| 12 | 9 booster packs (3 *Mirage*, 3 *Visions*, 3 *Weatherlight*) | 1 |
| 9 | 3 booster packs (1 *Mirage*, 1 *Visions*, 1 *Weatherlight*) | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in the *Mirage* Block Sealed Deck Tournament I will receive 1 copy of the promotional Arena version of [Impulse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Impulse). Players with at least 15 match points at the end of the tournament will also receive a premium foil Impulse.
