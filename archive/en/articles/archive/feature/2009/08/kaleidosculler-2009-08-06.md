Kaleidoscope is a wacky **Magic Online** format that has one deck building requirement: all cards must be multicolored. Th00mor took that constraint to heart, building this deck which features two of the game's very best two-drops: [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) and [Tidehollow Sculler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tidehollow+Sculler). So, are *you* ready to get started on a Kaleidoscope deck? [**Magic Online**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=Magic/Digital/MagicOnline.aspx) is available 24/7!
