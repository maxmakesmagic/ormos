Adrian Sullivan is a name that has appeared in this space more than a time or two, and the commencement of the Pro Tour Qualifier season for San Juan sees him return in the first weekend of competition! His [Bloodchief Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodchief+Ascension) deck for Extended is truly one of a kind and took him all the way to 3rd place in Minneapolis, Minnesota. This black-red burn deck has powerful backup plans in the form of [Countryside Crusher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Countryside+Crusher), [Bloodchief Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodchief+Ascension), and [Isochron Scepter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Isochron+Scepter).
