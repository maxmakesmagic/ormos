It has been many years since Affinity was the scourge of multiple formats, but with a plethora of powerful hate card for the deck, the Robots have quietly become a solid beatdown deck whose popularity ebbs and flows over an Extended season. For Dan Rodemann, it looks like the tide is in as he used his Affinity list to win a recent PTQ for Honolulu. Make sure to check out the newly minted *Conflux* hit [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) in his sideboard; they're great against [Kataki, War's Wage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kataki%2C+War%27s+Wage)*and* the mirror match too!
