One of the most fascinating new decks to come from Pro Tour-Berlin, Kenny Öberg's innovative control list took him all the way to the Top 8 before losing in the quarterfinals. He focuses on controlling the game with artifacts like [Engineered Explosives](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Explosives), [Vedalken Shackles](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vedalken+Shackles), and [Ensnaring Bridge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ensnaring+Bridge). There's also a host of flexible tutor options, but what does he use to search up the individual artifacts? None other than [Tezzeret the Seeker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret+the+Seeker) himself, who conveniently doubles as a win condition! The deck even received its name from the iconic planeswalker. Read more about Kenny, Tezzeret, and all the other exciting developments from Pro Tour-Berlin on the [official coverage page](/en/node/502416).
