States and Champs Information Update
====================================



 Posted in **News**
 on October 10, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2008/champs_dourbark2.jpg)*Yours for participating (while supplies last).* Get ready to take your **Magic**  game to the next level. Pit yourself against the fiercest competition your state or province has to offer and claim the title of State or Provincial Champion. Step up and see if you have the chops to be the best!

**Facts:**

- Brought to you by Tournament Organizers

- $25 Entry Fee

- Held in all 50 states & 10 Canadian provinces

- Tournament locations in the U.S. and Canada will utilize a Standard format

- All participants will receive a foil DCI [Dauntless Dourbark](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dauntless+Dourbark) promo card (while supplies last)

- Top 8 finishers will be awarded exclusive tournament playmats

- Winners will receive a 2008 Champion plaque, as well as free admission to all premier-level open Constructed events run by all States/Champs participating organizers for one year

For more information regarding States or Champs tournaments in your state or province, please [click here](http://www.mtgchamps.com).
