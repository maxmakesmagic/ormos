National Qualifier season is underway! The first National Qualifiers around the world were held in Japan, and in Okayama Yuuki Wada managed a 2nd place finish with this version of Goblins. The most interesting thing about the list? It was one of FOUR in the Top 8! Take that, [Squadron Hawk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squadron+Hawk)!
