Standard Super League Season 2 Decklists
========================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on August 11, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










Every Tuesday through September 9, at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7) (except for a quick break the week of the August 25), the newest episode of the Standard Super League's second season airs live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic).


If you haven't already, check out Randy Buehler's [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/standard-super-league-episode-9-recap-2015-06-30) that gives an overview of what the Super League schedule is going to look like over the next few months as it builds towards the Super League Championships!


Follow the links below to take you to each player's decklist—they'll be using these decks up until the Standard Super League playoffs on September 8!
