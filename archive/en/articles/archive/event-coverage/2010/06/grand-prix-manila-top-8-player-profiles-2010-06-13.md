Grand Prix-Manila Top 8 Player Profiles
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 13, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/gpman10/T8_Liu.jpg)*Yuchen Liu*


**Name:**  Yuchen Liu  
**Hometown:**  Beijing, China  
**Age:**  18  
**Occupation:**  Student  
**Day One Record:**  7-2  
**Day Two Record:**  6-0  
**How many GP/PT top 8’s have you had?**  This is the first one  
**What deck did you play this weekend?**  Jund  
**What was the best card in your sideboard this weekend?**  [Goblin Ruinblaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ruinblaster)  
**What deck do you NOT want to play against in this Top 8?**  Next Level Bant

**Hometown:**  San Pablo City, Laguna, Philippines  
**Age:**  29  
**Occupation:**  Senior Product Manager, Level Up! Lat’l  
**Day One Record:**  8-0-1  
**Day Two Record:**  4-1-2  
**How many GP/PT top 8’s have you had?**  One

**What deck do you NOT want to play against in this Top 8?**  Blue/White, if there is one.

**What deck do you NOT want to play against in this Top 8?**  Nothing.
