We interrupt this week's parade of Nationals decklists for an update from the world of Pauper, the **Magic Online** format that uses commons only. So, what did sfenixw choose to build in a recent tournament online? Goblins, of course! Even at common, there are plenty of the little red critters to build a deck that's a blast to play competitive as well.
