Decks of the Week: 12/09/2010
=============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on December 9, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Welcome to the week of the 2010 World Championships! Over the next three days, the shape of the Constructed metagame will be set as we head into 2011. Standard kicks off the bash in Chiba with six rounds on Day One, so [check out the coverage](/en/events/coverage/theres-no-jace-guillaume) to see what decks have sprung from the minds of the top players in the game. Then be sure to tune in again on Saturday to see what six rounds of Extended has to offer. The next Pro Tour Qualifier season is Extended and begins in January, so this is your chance to get a head start on earning your trip to the Pro Tour.
