Marlon Egolf is the 2009 Grand Prix-Boston champion! He finished the job in the Top 8 M10 draft with a spicy red-green-blue deck that featured some pretty bomby cards in the form of [Ant Queen](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ant+Queen), [Earthquake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake), and [Shivan Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shivan+Dragon). Of course, his talented play didn't hurt! To read more about Grand Prix-Boston, visit the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/egolf-wins-big-boston).
