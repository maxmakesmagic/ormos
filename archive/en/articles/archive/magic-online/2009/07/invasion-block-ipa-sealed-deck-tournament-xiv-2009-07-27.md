*Invasion* Block (IPA) Sealed Deck Tournament XIV
=================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on July 27, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Starting with IPA XIV, legacy product events no longer require players to qualify to be able to enter, these events are now open to any player that wishes to join. Here is your chance to play with your favorite classic sets while you compete for fun prizes.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/life-death.jpg)


### *Invasion* Block Sealed Deck Tournament XIV


START TIME: Sunday August 2, 2009 11:00 AM PST/1700 UTC.


SIZE: 33 players minimum, 512 players maximum.


DURATION: 6 rounds of Swiss play


LOCATION: "Premier Events" room.


ADMISSION: 30 event tickets (product will be supplied)


PRODUCT PROVIDED: 2 *Invasion* boosters, 2 *Planeshift* Boosters, and 2 *Apocalypse* Boosters.


PRIZE SUPPORT: Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each loss awards 0 points.

All who participate in the IPA XIV tournament will get a Life/Death promo card. Players with 15 or more match points and the end of the tournament will also get a premium foil version of the Life/Death promo card.
