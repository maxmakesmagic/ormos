2004 North American Challenge Quarterfinals Feature Match
=========================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 10, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_alexshvartsman.jpg)
By Alex Shvartsman











### Game 1


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/nac04/aBruce.jpg)*Jacob Bruce*This game was all about mana denial as Jacob Bruce used a second turn [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) to remove his opponent's [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) to see him miss a second land drop.


"I knew it! I read it in your eyes," boasted a satisfied Bruce. He played out a [Rotlung Reanimator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator) and then used a [Death Cloud](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Cloud) for one to destroy one of his opponent's now two lands, and a [Vine Trellis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vine+Trellis) – setting him back to one mana yet again. Bruce himself did not suffer as much, as his [Rotlung Reanimator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator) left a 2/2 Zombie in its wake. 


[Withered Wretch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Withered+Wretch) and a 2/2 Zombie token were busy lowering Minicucci's life total while the [Tooth and Nail](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tooth+and+Nail) player was stuck on two lands and unable to do anything about it.


### Sideboarding


**Dominic Minicucci**  
 +4 [Pyroclasm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm), +1 [Vine Trellis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vine+Trellis), +1 [Symbiotic Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Symbiotic+Wurm)  
 -4 [Vernal Bloom](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vernal+Bloom), -1 [Sundering Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sundering+Titan), -1 Kamahl


**Jacob Bruce**  
 +4 [Echoing Decay](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Echoing+Decay), +1 [Death Cloud](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Cloud)  
 -2 [Headhunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Headhunter), -2 [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother), -1 [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox)


### Game 2


Bruce mulliganed twice but it looked like he might recover early in the game. He slowed his opponent down by using an [Echoing Decay](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Echoing+Decay) on a [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise), then summoned a [Rotlung Reanimator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator) and played a [Grave Pact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grave+Pact) for good measure. [Cabal Archon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Archon) and [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) came out next, making it Bruce look like the favorite in the matchup. 


Before Bruce had the chance to equip either of his creatures, Minicucci cast [Pyroclasm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm) to take out both creatures, then a second [Pyroclasm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm) to remove the Zombie tokens they generated. He lost all of his own creatures too, and the board was now tied, both players in top-deck mode.


Minicucci significantly increased his mana base by playing out a pair of [Wood Elves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wood+Elves) and then drew a perfect card for his situation – a [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp)! With plenty of mana and a pair of useless one-toughness creatures in play he was able to go off, drawing lots of cards, playing a [Wirewood Herald](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Herald), killing it to draw yet more cards and get a Shaman, and more. Soon he had a several creatures in play and enough lands to tap out and play [Darksteel Colossus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Colossus) – without any [Vernal Bloom](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vernal+Bloom)s in play.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/nac04/aMinicucci.jpg)*Domenic Minicucci*It was now or never for Bruce, who tapped out to cast [Death Cloud](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Cloud) for four. It forced his opponent to sacrifice all of his creatures, and killed most of Bruce's lands. Although it removed both hands, Minicucci was able to draw two cards from the [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) ability after [Death Cloud](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Cloud) resolved. His next draw was a [Wirewood Herald](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Herald). With plenty of mana and a [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) in play he was able to start his engine once again. At only two life, Bruce could no longer [Death Cloud](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Cloud) and he packed up his cards once Minicucci summoned a [Symbiotic Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Symbiotic+Wurm). 


### Game 3


Bruce opened with a [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) and followed it up with a turn-two [Rotlung Reanimator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator) (with help from [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox)). He then replenished his hand by playing a [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) and feeding [Disciple of the Vault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+the+Vault) and [Dark Supplicant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Supplicant) to it. 


Meanwhile, Minicucci was working on his mana base. He played out a Bird, then [Wood Elves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wood+Elves) and a Symbiote. His [Viridian Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Shaman) took out the opposing Clamp and was soon joined with a [Vine Trellis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vine+Trellis).


Minicucci took some damage from the Zombies, slowly taking them out in combat. Eventually he found a [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) of his own. With plenty of mana in play he went through several of his smaller creatures to draw cards then cast [Pyroclasm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm), killing three Zombie tokens and a [Withered Wretch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Withered+Wretch). Bruce's answer was to [Death Cloud](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Cloud) for two, keeping his opponent's mana in check.


While unable to cast [Tooth and Nail](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tooth+and+Nail) anytime soon, Minicucci was now in perfect position to take advantage of the [Viridian Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Shaman) / [Wirewood Symbiote](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Symbiote) combo he was holding. He took out Bruce's [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) and a [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), leaving him with nothing but a pair of [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp)s. Then Shaman and its friends attacked several more times, winning the game without the help from any huge creatures.


**Final Result:** Minicucci 2 – Bruce 1
