Decks of the Week: 9/13/2013
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on September 13, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












While players wait with bated breath for the release of *Theros*, the PTQ season for Pro Tour *Born of the Gods* is ramping up both on  [**Magic Online**](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/MagicOnline.aspx?x=mtg/digital/magiconline/download)  and in stores across the world! This PTQ season uses the Sealed format (switching to Draft for the Top 8) with  ***Magic** 2014*  boosters for a couple more weeks; then, beginning on September 27, PTQs will use boosters from *Theros* instead. Make sure to check out the  [*Theros* Card Image Gallery](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/theros/cig#)  for all the previewed cards and find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#bog). Good luck; have fun!
