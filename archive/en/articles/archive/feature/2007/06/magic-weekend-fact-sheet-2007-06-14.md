### July 26-29, Baltimore, Md.


**Magic** Weekend brings all the best parts of the game together in one location for four amazing days. Whether you're hanging out with your friends playing in open events, challenging for a slice of $100,000 in scholarship money, or trying to become the next United States National Champion, there's something for everybody!


Running July 26-29 in Baltimore, Md., **Magic** Weekend is a celebration of **Magic: The Gathering**. The doors to the Baltimore Convention Center open Thursday morning, so start planning your trip to see one of the top [**Magic** Roadside Attractions](http://www.wizards.com/roadsideattractions/splash.htm)â€”including the Game of the Year, the biggest game of **Magic** you've ever seen!


So what's stopping you? Find your way to Baltimore for the most **Magic** you'll find in one place all summer.

### Game of the Year


Ever wanted to see life-sized **Magic**? Come to Baltimore and be a part of the Game of the Year, where fans are paired with **Magic** creator Richard Garfield and Pro Tour Hall of Famer Alan Comer to battle on a giant playmat. Want to know what it feels like to send an army of three-foot-tall cards into the Red Zone? The Game of the Year is for you!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/gameofyear.jpg)### Open Events


Take advantage of five separate open "grinder" tournaments on Thursday to earn a coveted spot in the U.S. National Championship. After that, tackle a Pro Tour Qualfier to win an invitation and a plane ticket to Pro Tour-Valencia, hop in a Grand Prix Trial to earn byes at upcoming Grand Prix, join an 8-man single-elimination queue, or play for cool prizes in one of the many other open events running all weekend. For a full schedule, [click here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/nationals/usa-sideevents). Here's a highlight of just some of the prizes you can win:





* An uncut framed sheet of *Time Spiral* cards.
* An Apple TV + 80gb iPod
* A $200 Gift Certificate to Phillips Seafood
* A laptop computer (minimum value $750
* Your choice of a Japanese *Urza's Destiny* or European *Urza's Legacy* display box
