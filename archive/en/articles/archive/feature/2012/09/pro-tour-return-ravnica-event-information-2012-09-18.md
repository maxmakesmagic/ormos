### Smith Cove Event Center at Pier 91  Seattle, Washington  October 19-21, 2012




* [**Live Coverage of Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica***](/en/events/coverage/ptrtr12)

 The third Pro Tour of 2012 comes to the home of **Magic: The Gathering**  in Seattle and features the debut of the [new *Return to Ravnica*](/en/node/703956) set on the premier-play stage. The top players from around the world will be in Seattle to battle for fame, glory, and cash prizes in the Modern Constructed and *Return to Ravnica* Booster Draft formats. Tune in for the three-day live webcast starting Friday morning for complete access to the Pro Tour: live match coverage, deck techs, the Pro Tour Hall of Fame Class of 2012 induction ceremony, behind-the-scenes interviews with R&D members, and much more!

**Format**
  


*The **Magic: The Gathering**  Tournament Rules and Infraction & Penalty Guide are in effect for this event unless specifically overruled by information in this Format Document.*


* [Modern](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/sfrmodern) and *Return to Ravnica* Booster Draft
* 3 *Return to Ravnica* boosters will be used for drafting
* Deck lists are required
* Check [**Magic**  Tournament Rules](http://www.wizards.com/WPN/Document.aspx?x=Magic_The_Gathering_Tournament_Rules) for Modern Constructed and Booster Draft format rules.

**Tournament Length**
  


* Day 1 will consist of 5 rounds of Modern followed by 3 rounds of Booster Draft
* Day 2 will consist of 3 rounds of Booster Draft followed by 5 rounds of Modern
* 16 Swiss Rounds total, 55 minutes per round
* When time is called, the active player will finish the turn and five additional turns will be taken
* Stalling and slow play rules will be strictly enforced during the tournament.

**Advancement**
  


* All players may participate in all 8 Swiss rounds of Friday's competition.
* Players with 12 or more match points after round 8 will advance to Saturday's competition.
* The top 8 players after round 16 will advance to Sunday’s Top 8 single-elimination finals.

**Top 8**
  


* Top 8 will be Modern Constructed format
* Top 8 matches will be best 3 of 5
* Where necessary, the standings after the Swiss rounds will still be used to determine final order in the standings.
* While there are no time limits in the Top 8, players are expected to play at a reasonable pace and finish their matches within 90 minutes.
* For the first game of each match in the Top 8, the player that finished higher in the Swiss rounds chooses either to play first or to play second. For subsequent games in each Top 8 match, the usual Play/Draw rule applies (loser of the previous game decides whether to play first in the next game.)

**Notes**
  


* Planeswalker Points Multiplier 12x (Lifetime Total only), REL: Professional
* Players are required to use the same Modern Constructed deck for Day 1, Day 2, and the Top 8
* Players are responsible for knowing all DCI rules and [high-level tournament rules](http://www.wizards.com/protour/images/HighlevelRules.pdf).
* Players must bring pen, paper, card sleeves and appropriate counters.
