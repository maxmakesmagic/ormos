The Duels Medallions
====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 13, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











If you've played **Duels of the Planeswalkers** 2013, you've probably noticed that it includes the Medallions from *Tempest*. But unless you were looking closely, you might not have spotted that they have new art!
