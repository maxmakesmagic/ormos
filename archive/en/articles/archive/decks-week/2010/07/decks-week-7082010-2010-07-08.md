Decks of the Week: 7/08/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on July 8, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Japan staked an early claim as the favorite in the 2010 World Team Championship by putting together one of the most fearsome National Teams in recent memory last weekend at the [Japan National Championship](/en/events/coverage/mori-defeats-ikeda-japan-title-0). Led by 2005 World Champion Katsuhiro Mori, the Japan National Team also includes Tsuyoshi Ikeda (and his 307 lifetime Pro Points) and Shou Tagomori. You can see Top 8 deck lists, top Standard deck lists, undefeated Booster Draft deck lists, and winning Standard Grinder deck lists below. National Championship coverage resumes July 24-25 with Canada, France, and Australia Nationals, when ***Magic** 2011* joins the Standard environment!
