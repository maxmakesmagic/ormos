While mono-white decks featuring [Tempered Steel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tempered+Steel) were the talk of the town headed into [Pro Tour Nagoya](/en/events/coverage/sharfman-masters-magic-weekend-nagoya), a group of Belgian pros took a different route. Their mono-white deck was a control build that eschewed aggressive artifact creatures for planeswalkers and board-sweepers. This version was played by Jan Van Nieuwenhove, and you can watch a deck tech with his teammate Marijn Lybaert:
