Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpstu07/joel_calafell_profile.jpg)

**Name:** Joel Calafell  
**Age:** 19  
**Residence:** Barcelona, Spain  
**Occupation:** Student  
  
**Number of GP Top 8s:** 1  
  
**Number of PTs played:** 4  
  
**Biggest Magic success:** Top 16 at GP Bilbao 2005, co-designer of the deck "Cepmacio Breakfast" (not sure if this is a success)  
  
**Tournament result:** 13-2  
  
**How did you prepare for the tournament?** Made two drafts on MTGO the day before leaving






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpstu07/patrizio_golia_profile.jpg)

**Name:** Patrizio Golia  
**Age:** 17  
**Residence:** Forlí, Italy  
**Occupation:** Student  
  
**Number of GP Top 8s:** 0  
  
**Number of PTs played:** 0  
  
**Biggest Magic success:** Day 2 at GP Firenze, Italian "Under 15" National Champion 2005, 5 PTQ Top 8, 20th at Italian Nationals 2006.  
  
**Tournament result:** 13-2, with two byes, and an 11-0 after his 2-2 start.  
  
**How did you prepare for the tournament?** Magic Online






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpstu07/shuuhei_nakamura_profile.jpg)

**Name:** Shuuhei Nakamura  
**Age:** 26  
**Residence:** Edge of Osaka, Japan  
**Occupation:** Pro traveler  
  
**Number of GP Top 8s:** 11?  
  
**Number of PTs played:** 20+?  
  
**Biggest Magic success:** I got 3rd in Limit's qualifier tournament (the 1st and 2nd get invited to the Limit's tournament). [It's the biggest invitation only event in Japan.]  
  
**Tournament result:** Day one: 7-1-1, Day two: 3-0, 2-1  
  
**How did you prepare for the tournament?** I was playing all GPs using Lorwyn. I like blue!






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpstu07/marc_vogt_profile.jpg)

**Name:** Marc Vogt  
**Age:** 20  
**Residence:** Freising, Germany  
**Occupation:** Student  
  
**Number of GP Top 8s:** 1  
  
**Number of PTs played:** 0  
  
**Biggest Magic success:** Second at a Prerelease event with more than 100 people  
  
**Tournament result:** 12-1-2; 3-0 in the first draft with a Goblins/Faeries deck, 0-1-2 in the second draft with W/U Changeling  
  
**How did you prepare for the tournament?** Playing a lot of Etherlords II!






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpstu07/raul_porojan_profile.jpg)

**Name:** Raul Porojan  
**Age:** 19  
**Residence:** Freiburg, Germany  
**Occupation:** Student, Basketball coach  
  
**Number of GP Top 8s:** 2  
  
**Number of PTs played:** 1  
  
**Biggest Magic success:** 9th Edition prerelease Top 9 (and of course the Top 2 finish at GP Strasbourg)  
  
**Tournament result:** 12-1-2; in draft, I played Merfolk because you cannot go worse than 2-1 with them, and red Elementals/Giants because you either go 3-0 or 0-3.  
  
**How did you prepare for the tournament?** MTGO






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpstu07/robert_van_meedevort_profile.jpg)

**Name:** Robert van Meedevort  
**Age:** 21  
**Residence:** The Netherlands  
**Occupation:** Student  
  
**Number of GP Top 8s:** first one  
  
**Number of PTs played:** 10  
  
**Biggest Magic success:** Team Worlds Championship 2006  
  
**Tournament result:** 12-1-2  
  
**How did you prepare for the tournament?** I did a lot of drafts to prepare for Worlds.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpstu07/jonathan_bergstrom_profile.jpg)

**Name:** Jonathan Bergström  
**Age:** 25  
**Residence:** Mullsjö, Sweden  
**Occupation:** Poet in a truck driver's body  
  
**Number of GP Top 8s:** First!  
  
**Number of PTs played:** 4  
  
**Biggest Magic success:** Lots of Nationals Top 8  
  
**Tournament result:** Sealed 8-1, Draft 4-0-2  
  
**How did you prepare for the tournament?** Did a FNM draft. I always build Treefolk Sealed decks, because I love them.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpstu07/fried_meulders_profile.jpg)

**Name:** Fried Meulders  
**Age:** 22  
**Residence:** Rillaar, Belgium  
**Occupation:** Student  
  
**Number of GP Top 8s:** 1  
  
**Number of PTs played:** 12-ish  
  
**Biggest Magic success:** Belgian Champ 2007, 20th at PT Geneva 2007, Level 3 last season (unfortunately, that doesn't mean anything anymore)  
  
**Tournament result:** Sealed 7-2, Draft 6-0. I just went with the flow, and played R/U Elementals in the first draft and R/G Giants in the second draft.  
  
**How did you prepare for the tournament?** MTGO.
