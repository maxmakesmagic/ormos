2001 New Orleans Masters Coverage
---------------------------------


Mike Pustilnik's reputation as a Limited Specialist continues to grow with his decisive victory over Chris Benafel and his red burn deck. Since his victory at Los Angeles last year, he may have fallen off the radar somewhat, but now he's riding high with a huge check in his pocket. Congratulations to Mike Pustilnik, New Orleans Masters Champion!


Follow all the action on the [bracket](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/bracket-2015-10-13)!
