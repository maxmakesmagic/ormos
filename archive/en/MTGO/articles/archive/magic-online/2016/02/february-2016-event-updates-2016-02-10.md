February 2016 Event Updates
===========================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on February 10, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/lee_author_image_0.jpeg)
By Lee Sharpe











Today I'm announcing exciting stuff for Legacy players and championships for *Oath of the Gatewatch*. Let's get started!




---

Legacy Challenge
================


Get ready for a new monthly event: the Legacy Challenge! The Legacy Challenge will be held the second Saturday of every month at 11 a.m. Pacific. The first one will be on March 12. Like the Power Nine Challenge for Vintage, the Top 8 players can expect to win some exciting cards! What cards, you may ask? Well, for starters, everyone in the Top 8 will get one of each of the ten original dual lands. Here's a few more cards in the prize pool:


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/mtgo/card_MM_rishadan_port.png)![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/mtgo/card_PD3_cabal_therapy.png)![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/mtgo/card_TPR_wasteland.png)


The complete event details for the Legacy Challenge are [below](#legacychallenge). There are a lot of exciting cards to win and we're excited to bring a competitive Legacy premier event to Magic Online.


You may be asking yourself "Where am I going to test for the Legacy Challenge?" If so, the answer is in Legacy Leagues of course. Wait, Legacy is getting a League?!




---

Legacy Leagues
==============


That's right. The next *Magic Online* League is Legacy! When we created a League for Pauper, it helped reinvigorate and grow participation in the format, and we believe the Legacy Challenge as well as a new League will grow Legacy as well. We want *Magic Online* to be a great place to play Legacy, and we believe these events will do a lot to grow both formats.


To promote the League, we'll be ending Legacy Daily Events and the eight-player single-elimination queue with that downtime as well, so that we can focus on League play. The transition from these events to the League will happen during the February 17 downtime.




---

*Oath of the Gatewatch* Championships
=====================================


As is our tradition after each set, we will have the *Oath of the Gatewatch* Limited and Constructed championships. The *Oath of the Gatewatch* Limited qualifiers are February 17–23, with the championship on February 27. The Standard qualifiers are February 24–March 1, with the championship on March 6. Just for participating in any qualifier, you'll get an [Immolating Glare](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Immolating+Glare) promo. Qualify for the finals? You'll pick up a [Jori En, Ruin Diver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jori+En%2C+Ruin+Diver) promo as well.


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/mtgo/card_promo_OGW_Champ_Immolating_Glare.png)![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/mtgo/card_promo_OGW_Jori_En_Ruin_Diver.png)


I hope you enjoy these events! You can see the full details of these events [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-oath-gatewatch-championship-event-details-2016-01-31).
