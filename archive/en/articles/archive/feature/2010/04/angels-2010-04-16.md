 Looking for a fun deck for Friday Night **Magic** that casts tons of big Angels and can even return them to the battlefield if they're killed? Look no further! This deck, which user Typhodius took to an undefeated record in a recent **Magic** **Online** Standard tournament, casts [Baneslayer Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel), [Archon of Redemption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archon+of+Redemption), and even [Luminarch Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Luminarch+Ascension) on its Angel theme. And if someone kills your precious flyers? You can use [Marshal's Anthem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Marshal%27s+Anthem) to return them from the beyond, even giving them a power/toughness boost!
