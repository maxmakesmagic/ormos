With the new [Chandra, the Firebrand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+the+Firebrand) available with the printing of ***Magic** 2012*, players around the world are trying to come up with a way to abuse the powerful new card. Elmo the Elk may have done it. This take on a Mono-Red Control deck uses Chandra to copy the powerful [Geosurge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geosurge). From there you have plenty of mana to cast your fatties much faster than you should be able to. [Koth of the Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Koth+of+the+Hammer) will let you double up your mana production as well.
