2006 Legacy Championship Fact Sheet
===================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 7, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/Legacychamps.jpg)


GEN CON Indianapolis
--------------------


### Where


Indiana Convention Center  

TCG Hall  

100 South Capitol Avenue  

Indianapolis, Indiana


### Date/Time


Friday, August 11, 2006  

Registration begins at 9AM  

Tournament begins at 10AM


### Cost


The entry fee for the 2006 **Magic: The Gathering** Legacy Championship is $15.00. Please note that admission to GEN CON is also required. Please visit [www.gencon.com](http://www.gencon.com) for more information.


### Format


* Legacy Format
* No proxies of any kind are allowed
* Open to all players

### Tournament Length


* Swiss rounds, 60 minutes per round
* Number of rounds based on attendance according to the following chart:



|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Attendance** | **Number of Swiss rounds** |
| 17 - 32 | 5 |
| 33 - 64 | 6 |
| 65 - 128 | 7 |
| 129 - 226 | 8 |
| 227 - 409 | 9 |
| 410 or higher | 10 |

### Advancement


* All players may participate in all Swiss rounds.
* The top-8 players after the final Swiss round will advance to the top-8 single-elimination finals.

### Top- 8


* Top 8 matches will be best 2 of 3.
* Top 8 matches will have no time limit.
* Where necessary, the standings after the Swiss rounds will still be used to determine final order in the standings.

### Notes


* 48K, REL 4
* All Universal Tournament Rules and **Magic: the Gathering** Floor Rules are in effect for this event unless specifically overruled by information in this document
* Players must bring pen, paper, card sleeves and appropriate counters.

### Prizes


* All prize payouts will include Italian *Legends*
* Winner receives 4 copies of each *Revised* Edition Dual Lands (40 total Dual Lands), plus 48 boosters
* Second Place receives 1 copy of each *Revised* Edition Dual Lands (10 total Dual Lands), plus 48 boosters
* Third through Eighth Place each receive 48 boosters,
* Ninth through Sixteenth Place each receive 36 boosters,
* Seventeenth through Thirty-Second Place each receives 18 boosters.

### GEN CON Legacy Side Events


* **5PM Thursday: August 10th** – Legacy Championship Prelim  

 $6 Swiss rounds based on attendance with a top-8 single elimination.  

 First Place wins a 2 round bye at the 2006 Legacy Championship.
* **11:30PM Thursday: August 10th** – $500 Legacy Tournament  

 $10.50 Swiss rounds based on attendance with a top-8 single elimination.
* **1PM Friday: August 11th** – Legacy Tournament  

 $6 Straight Swiss rounds based on attendance. No single elimination.
* **11AM Sunday: August 13th** – Legacy Tournament  

 $6 Straight Swiss rounds based on attendance. No single elimination.
