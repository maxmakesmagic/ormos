Pro Tour Ixalan
---------------


Manfield Energizes Albuquerque
==============================




2015 World Champion and Genesis team member Seth Manfield cements his place in modern *Magic* history, winning Pro Tour *Ixalan* to take home $50,000 and another revered trophy to add to his growing collection.

 



Nov 3-5, 2017


Albuquerque Convention Center



Seth Manfield
