GRAND PRIX TRIALS WINNING DECKLISTS
===================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX MANILA 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpman15)
 on January 2, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim










A total of six Standard Grinder and four Sealed deck Grinders took place yesterday, granting all winners Second Round Byes for today's main event. Abzan, Jeskai and Sultai continue to assert their dominance in Standard while it was almost a clean-sweep for Mardu on the Sealed Deck front.


Hearty congratulations to all winners! You've got a head start today!
