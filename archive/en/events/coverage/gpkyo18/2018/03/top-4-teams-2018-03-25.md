Top 4 Teams of Grand Prix Kyoto 2018
====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 25, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











Here are your top 4 teams for Grand Prix Kyoto 2018!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpkyo18/TOP4_yotsumoto_endo_takeishi.jpg)


**Team 1**


**Player A**


**Name:** Yuki Yotsumoto


**Age:** 32


**Occupation:** Company Employee


**Hometown:** Saitama Prefecture


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** 1 Grand Prix Top 8


**How did you choose your teammates?**


They are my best friends!


**Which format and which deck did you play?** 


Standard. Red-Green Monsters.


**Why did you play this format and why did you choose this deck?**


I like five-mana cards which are red. (Editor's note: [Glorybringer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glorybringer)?)




---

**Player B**


**Name:** Ryota Endo


**Age:** 29


**Occupation:** Civil Servant


**Hometown:** Saitama Prefecture


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** 1 Grand Prix Top 8


**How did you choose your teammates?**


I have been friends with Yuki and Koji for over ten years.


**Which format and which deck did you play?** 


Modern. 5-Color Humans.




---

**Player C**


**Name:** Koji Takeishi


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Company Employee


**Hometown:** Saitama Prefecture


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** 1 Grand Prix Top 8


**How did you choose your teammates?**


Ten years of friendship!


**Which format and which deck did you play?** 


Legacy. 4-Color Leovold. It’s an okay matchup against Delver, and I get to play [Force of Will](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will).




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpkyo18/top4_yamamoto_matsubara_takamura.jpg)


**Team 2**


**Player A**


**Name:** Ryouichi Yamamoto


**Age:** 24


**Occupation:** Civil Servant


**Hometown:** Tochigi Prefecture


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** None.


**How did you choose your teammates?**


Matsubara told me that Takamura was a Legacy specialist. Thanks, Matsubara and Takamura!


**Which format and which deck did you play?** 


Standard. Blue-Black Midrange. I like two-color decks.




---

**Player B**


**Name:** Yuusuke Matsubara


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Manufacturing Industry


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** None.


**How did you choose your teammates?**


We live close to each other and get along well.


**Which format and which deck did you play?** 


Modern. Affinity. The deck is currently under the radar.


**What does this Top 4 mean to you?**


It’s always been my dream to play in a Pro Tour.




---

**Player C**


**Name:** Kazuki Takamura


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Company Employee


**Hometown:** Ibaraki Prefecture


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** None.


**How did you choose your teammates?**


We enjoy gaming with each other.


**Which format and which deck did you play?** 


Legacy. [Show and Tell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell), because my "sensei” is a master at the deck.


**What does this Top 4 mean to you?**


I got the experience in playing high-level Magic.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpkyo18/top4_ichihara_uemoto_shibara.jpg)

**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Company Employee


**Hometown:** Hyōgo Prefecture


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** None.


**How did you choose your teammates?**


Friends for many years.

Standard. Mono-Red splash Black. It was recommended to me.

I like five-mana cards which are red. (Editor’s note: [Glorybringer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glorybringer)?)

I won an overseas trip to Richmond!




---

**Player B**


**Name:** Takahiro Uemoto


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Civil Servant


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** None.


**How did you choose your teammates?**


I asked my friends if they wanted to come to Kyoto together, and they said: “let’s play!"


**Which format and which deck did you play?** 


Modern. Mono-Blue [Time Walk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Walk)s. In my testing, it has a good win percentage.


**What does this Top 4 mean to you?**


I will take a vacation from work to travel to the Pro Tour.




---

**Player C**


**Name:** Masataka Shibata


**Age:** 32


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Kyoto Prefecture


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** KMC Legacy Winner


**How did you choose your teammates?**


Mr. Uemoto invited me. =)


**Which format and which deck did you play?** 


Legacy. [Aluren](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aluren). It’s good against Delver decks.


**What does this Top 4 mean to you?**


Thank you to my incredible teammates!

**Player B**


**Name:** Akira Tanaka


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Company Employee


**Hometown:** Tokyo Prefecture


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** 2012 World Magic Cup Team Member


**How did you choose your teammates?**


Party friends forever!


**Which format and which deck did you play?** 


Modern. White-Blue Control. Initially, I played Bogles, but I kept losing. My teammates advised me to play another deck, and that’s how I ended up with White-Blue Control.


**What does this Top 4 mean to you?**


Kato-san took the words out of my mouth.
