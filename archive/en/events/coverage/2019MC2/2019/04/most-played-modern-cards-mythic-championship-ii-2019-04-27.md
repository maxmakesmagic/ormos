The Most-Played Modern Cards at Mythic Championship II
======================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 27, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_FrankKarsten.jpg)
By Frank Karsten

Modern is a fast format, so you have to be mana-efficient. Indeed, the 8 most-played non-land cards were all one-mana spells. This helps explain why [Chalice of the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chalice+of+the+Void) is so powerful in the Modern format right now.


[Surgical Extraction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Surgical+Extraction), [Path to Exile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile), [Nature's Claim](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nature%27s+Claim), and [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) are all hyper-efficient answers. [Ancient Stirrings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Stirrings), [Opt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opt), [Faithless Looting](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faithless+Looting), and [Serum Visions](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serum+Visions) are the most-played card selection spells.

The next part of the list contains half a Tron deck, which makes sense because it was the most popular Modern archetype here in London.


Also found above are [Damping Sphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Damping+Sphere) and [Field of Ruin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Field+of+Ruin), which are good ways to slow down the Tron deck. It's nice that every deck can play them, regardless of color.


Below you can find the rest of the bunch. The most-played *Ravnica Allegiance* cards were [Deputy of Detention](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deputy+of+Detention), [Skewer the Critics](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skewer+the+Critics), [Kaya, Orzhov Usurper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kaya%2C+Orzhov+Usurper), [Light Up the Stage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Light+Up+the+Stage), and [Prime Speaker Vannifar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prime+Speaker+Vannifar), in that order.
