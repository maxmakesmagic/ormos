Top 8 Players of Grand Prix London 2018
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 28, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After fifteen rounds, with a total of 1,971 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gplon18/t8_freeman.jpg)


**Name:** Marcelino Freeman


**Age:** 31


**Occupation:** Transfer pricing manager at Kreston


**Hometown:** Puebla, Mexico


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


Two Grand Prix Top 8s.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-red, [Rekindling Phoenix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rekindling+Phoenix), 8-1, and [Ravenous Chupacabra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Chupacabra)!


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Blue-black, 3-0, [Kitesail Corsair](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitesail+Corsair).


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Green-blue, 2-0-1, two [Kitesail Corsair](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitesail+Corsair)!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gplon18/t8_rubin.jpg)


**Name:** Steve Rubin


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Magic, writing, gaming


**Hometown:** Pittsburgh, PA


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


Eight GP Top 8s, three consecutive Platinum years, two PTQ wins, two SCG wins, one PT win.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Green-blue. [Hadana's Climb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hadana%27s+Climb).


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


2-1. Black-Red Pirates.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


2-1. Blue-Red Pirates splashing black.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gplon18/t8_manfield.jpg)


**Name:** Seth Manfield


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** *Magic* pro


**Hometown:** Columbia, MD


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


Five Grand Prix wins, Pro Tour *Ixalan* winner.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Ravenous Chupacabra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Chupacabra), was better than [Impale](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Impale). Blue-black splash green.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Red-Green Dinos. Two [Charging Monstrosaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Charging+Monstrosaur) and [The Immortal Sun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Immortal+Sun).


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


White-Black Vampires. [Forerunner of the Legion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forerunner+of+the+Legion).




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gplon18/t8_kreuz.jpg)


**Name:** Alexander Kreuz


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Finance/insurance consultant


**Hometown:** Koblenz, Germany


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


Played in six Pro Tours, cashed some, and played one bubble match at GP Amsterdam.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Tetzimoc, Primal Death](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tetzimoc%2C+Primal+Death), 8-1.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


There was no one best card, Green-Blue Merfolk, 2-1.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


White-blue. There was no best card again, 3-0.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gplon18/t8_nelson.jpg)


**Name:** Brad Nelson


**Age:** 31


**Occupation:** Magic


**Hometown:** Roanoke, VA


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


Many.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[The Immortal Sun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Immortal+Sun), white-black splash green, 9-0.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Two [Ravenous Chupacabra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Chupacabra), 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Curious Obsession](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Curious+Obsession), green-blue, 1-1-1.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gplon18/t8_hulten.jpg)


**Name:** Alexander Hulten


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** IT specialist


**Hometown:** Arvika, Sweden


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


Top 20 GP Stockholm.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Profane Procession](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Profane+Procession) was the best card in my white and black deck. 7-1-1.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Zacama, Bant, 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Twilight Prophet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Twilight+Prophet). White-black. 3-0.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gplon18/t8_jacobson.jpg)


**Name:** Mark Jacobson


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Hardware engineer


**Hometown:** Philadelpthia, PA


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


12th at Pro Tour *Magic Origins*, 11-5 at Pro Tour *Kaladesh*, Top 4 at Grand Prix Toronto 2015 and Grand Prix Houston 2016.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


8-1, starting on red-white. Boarded into five different color configurations depending on the matchup. Best card was [Arch of Orazca](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arch+of+Orazca).


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


2-1 with Blue-Red Aggro and [Storm Fleet Sprinter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Storm+Fleet+Sprinter).


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


3-0 Green-Blue Merfolk and two [Merfolk Mistbinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Mistbinder). Honorable mention goes to sideboard [Plummet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plummet). On the penultimate turn of my win-and-in, my opponent put [One With the Wind](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=One+With+the+Wind) on a 3/3 Dino token and then attempted to [Hunt the Weak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hunt+the+Weak) ...




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gplon18/t8_sajgalik.jpg)


**Name:** Eduardo Sajgalik


**Age:** 30 this weekend


**Occupation:** Community consultant


**Hometown:** Montreal, Canada


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 


Two Pro Tour Top 8s (*Return to Ravnica*/*Aether Revolt*). Multiple national teams (UK/England/Canada). Fifth Grand Prix Top 8.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-red. [Charging Monstrosaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Charging+Monstrosaur) was best, [Dark Inquiry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Inquiry) was MVP. 7-2, or 4-2 plus three byes.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-Red Aggro Pirates. Deck was a team effort, but [Dire Fleet Poisoner](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dire+Fleet+Poisoner) was best.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


White-Blue Fliers with three [Expel from Orazca](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Expel+from+Orazca). MVP was [Snubhorn Sentry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snubhorn+Sentry), 3/3s for 1 are great.
