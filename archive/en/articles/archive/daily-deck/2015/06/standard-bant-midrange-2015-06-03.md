Standard Bant Midrange
======================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on June 3, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Melissa_DeTora.jpg)
By Melissa DeTora




 A former Magic Pro player and strategy writer, Melissa's career Top 8s include PT Gatecrash and 2 GPs. She's currently a development intern at WotC. 






Green midrange decks have been popping all over the place. Today's Daily Deck is one of these green midrange decks with quite a few interesting choices. Rosettus Weeks piloted this list to the Top 8 of the [TCGPlayer Diamond event in Flint, Michigan](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/deck_search_result.asp?Location=TCGplayer+Open+5K+Flint,+MI+-+5/23/2015). Like in most green midrange decks, we see the usual suspects: [Deathmist Raptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathmist+Raptor), [Den Protector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Den+Protector), and [Whisperwood Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whisperwood+Elemental). We also see ramp creatures like [Rattleclaw Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rattleclaw+Mystic) and [Elvish Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Mystic).



In addition to green creatures, Rosettus's deck also revolves around [Mastery of the Unseen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mastery+of+the+Unseen). This deck plays a high morph count to get extra value out of the manifests. Usually when your opponent manifests a creature, you have a good idea of what it could be. It's pretty unlikely that it's going to be bigger than 4/4 ([Whisperwood Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whisperwood+Elemental) is usually the biggest creature in these decks). However, you could be in for a shock if that manifested creature is a [Hornet Nest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Nest). Suddenly what looked like a lowly 2/2 is now a massive number of insect tokens with deathtouch! [Stratus Dancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stratus+Dancer) is another great choice for this deck. Opponents will have to think twice about casting that [End Hostilities](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=End+Hostilities) or [Crux of Fate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crux+of+Fate) if they suspect a [Stratus Dancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stratus+Dancer) is face-down.



[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Mastery+of+the+Unseen)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mastery+of+the+Unseen) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Hornet+Nest)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Nest) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Stratus+Dancer)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stratus+Dancer)


One of my favorite interactions in this deck is [Hornet Nest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Nest) and [Dromoka's Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dromoka%27s+Command). If your opponent has a giant creature, just make them fight and you will suddenly have an army of insects. These insects also make any future fighting result in your favor, as you can fight one of your insects to kill any creature due to deathtouch.



[Temur Sabertooth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temur+Sabertooth) is a nice addition to this deck as well. It's a super-late-game card but it does a lot of work. This deck plays many of creatures that you wouldn't mind returning to your hand to recast for extra card advantage, like [Den Protector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Den+Protector) and [Stratus Dancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stratus+Dancer). It also saves your creatures from removal, provided you have the mana.
