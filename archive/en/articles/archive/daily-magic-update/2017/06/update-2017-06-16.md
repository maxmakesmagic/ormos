Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Kyle Gellert's Legacy Miracles](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gplv17/legacy-last-chance-trial-undefeated-decklists-2017-06-15)

Reports of Miracles's demise appear to have been premature, as one Kyle Gellert managed to win a Last Chance Trial with an updated version of the deck. Clearly it can't lean on [Counterbalance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterbalance) to lock up the game anymore, but the deck is still chock-full of powerful cards and control elements that few people are truly preparing for now. Don't be surprised to see [Terminus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terminus) and [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) still making waves in Legacy this weekend.
