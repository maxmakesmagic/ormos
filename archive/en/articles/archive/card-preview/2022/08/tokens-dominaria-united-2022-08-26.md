The Tokens of Dominaria United
==============================



 Posted in **Card Preview**
 on August 26, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/claytonkroh_0.jpg)
By Clayton Kroh




 Clay is a content strategist, writer, and editor. He's a content editor and writer for DailyMTG, and has contributed creative work for *Magic: The Gathering* sets. On the side, he writes science fiction, fantasy, and horror. 






In the coming struggle for the future of Dominaria, every being will be essential, including the tokens! To aid in the fight, [*Dominaria United*](https://magic.wizards.com/products/dominaria-united) brings 36 beautiful, full-art tokens and two Planeswalker emblems to the battlefield. You can find 24 of these tokens plus the two emblems in Draft and Set Boosters; in Collector Boosters you’ll find these as double-sided token in a variety of combinations, all in traditional foil treatment!


There are [two *Dominaria United* Commander decks](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/dominaria-united-commander-decklists-2022-08-19) that bring 12 additional token types. Each deck includes ten double-sided tokens that will ensure you've got the forces necessary to stand your ground against opponents.


Build your forces and your collection! Preorder *Dominaria United* booster display, bundles, Commander decks and more from your [local game store](https://locator.wizards.com) and online retailers like [Amazon](https://gtly.to/HZV8BGWl_).


Visit the [*Dominaria United* Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/dominaria-united) to see the cards in the set, including the ones that create these tokens. You can also stop by the [*Dominaria United* Variant Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/dominaria-united-variants) for a look at the Booster Fun and promo versions of *Dominaria United*cards, and then check out the [*Dominaria United* Commander Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/dominaria-united-commander) to see the cards in the two Commander decks!
