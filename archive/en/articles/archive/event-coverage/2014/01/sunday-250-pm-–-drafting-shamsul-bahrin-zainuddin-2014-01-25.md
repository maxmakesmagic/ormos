Sunday, 2:50 p.m. – Drafting with Shamsul Bahrin Zainuddin
==========================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 25, 2014 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Noel Neo











Shamsul is one of just 6 players sitting on 11-1, having gone 3-0 in his first pod. Is this a fluke of chance or does Shamsul have some secret sauce? Read on to find out.


Shamsul quickly solidified himself in UG, with an initial four picks of [Nimbus Naiad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nimbus+Naiad), [Voyage's End](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyage%27s+End), [Leafcrown Dryad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leafcrown+Dryad) and [Feral Invocation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Feral+Invocation). He stuck to his guns through the three packs and ended up with a strong evasive rush deck.


A number of less conventional picks include [Nylea's Disciple](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nylea%27s+Disciple) over [Horizon Scholar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Horizon+Scholar) – Shamsul tends to err on the side of speed over power; [Bronze Sable](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bronze+Sable) over counter drafting [Underworld Cerberus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underworld+Cerberus); and not picking up a late Warrior's Lesson. A late pick of [Benthic Giant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Benthic+Giant) over [Horizon Scholar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Horizon+Scholar) was made because he has a number of ways of granting his creatures evasion and so contextually values hexproof over flying.

**Deckbuilding**


Having drafting a pool of solid UG, Shamsul then made another unconventional decision in deciding to splash black for the singleton – [Pharika's Mender](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pharika%27s+Mender). "The ability to return [Aqueous Form](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aqueous+Form) is very powerful", he explained.


To enable the splash, Shamsul is running two copies of [Unknown Shores](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unknown+Shores). Would his plan work?
