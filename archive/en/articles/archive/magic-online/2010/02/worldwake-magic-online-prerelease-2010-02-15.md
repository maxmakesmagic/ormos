*Worldwake* Magic Online Prerelease
===================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on February 15, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Play in an *Worldwake* Prerelease event and experience the newest **Magic: the Gathering** set 3 days before it goes on sale! Compete in one of these sealed flights and receive an exclusive prerelease [Comet Storm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Comet+Storm) promo card!

NOTE: *Worldwake* becomes legal in ALL constructed formats following the February 17 downtime. 

### *Worldwake* Prerelease Swiss Sealed Flights

DURATION: Friday February 19, 1:00 pm PST– Saturday February 20, 9:00 pm PST.

FORMAT: Each *Worldwake* Prerelease Sealed Swiss Flight will accommodate 16 players. These 4-round events will be run "on demand" – as soon as 1 event fills, another will open for sign-up.

LOCATION: These Swiss events will take place in the "Sealed Swiss Queues" room. 

PRODUCT: 3 *Zendikar* boosters and 3 *Worldwake* boosters

ADMISSION: 30 Tix (all product will be supplied). 

PRIZE SUPPORT: Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each loss awards 0 points. In addition, players will earn QPs for Season 2 of the [2010 Magic Online Championship Series](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/121509a)



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Points** | ***Worldwake*Boosters** | QPs |
| 12 | 10 | 3 |
| 9 | 4 | 1 |
| 6 | 1 | 0 |

  
All players who participate in a 16-Player Prerelease Swiss Sealed Flight will get a prerelease [Comet Storm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Comet+Storm) promo card. All players who earn 9 or more points will also receive a premium foil version of the prerelease [Comet Storm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Comet+Storm) promo card as well.
