Important Reminder
==================


1. See! I'm already shaking up Blake's precious system. Muahaha.

Today is the release of *Ixalan*! With the release today, Standard is also rotating! A mythical event that hasn't happened in quite a while. So, if your Standard deck contains *Battle for Zendikar*, *Oath of the Gatewatch*, *Shadows over Innistrad*, or *Eldritch Moon* cards, you're going to need to change those out before playing in a Standard event. You probably already knew this, but I care about you (unlike Blake) and I want you to have fun at Friday Night *Magic*, so I wanted to make sure you knew.


What to Watch This Weekend
==========================


1. [Grand Prix Providence](https://magic.wizards.com/en/events/premierplay/grandprix/fact-sheet-gppro17) | [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic) | Saturday at 7:30 a.m. PT/10:30 a.m. ET/2:30 p.m. UTC; and Sunday at 7 a.m. PT/10 a.m. ET/2 p.m. UTC

The best players in the world join forces into teams of three. Why three? Two is too few and four is too many, *obviously*. Will they all end up losing to Peach Garden Oath (Reid Duke, William Jensen, and Owen Turtenwald)? Maybe. I'm not an expert, but that's something that might happen.


1. StarCityGames.com Dallas Open | [twitch.tv/scgtour](http://www.twitch.tv/scgtour) | 8:30 a.m. PT/11:30 a.m. ET/3:30 p.m. UTC

Time to see what *Ixalan* Standard is all about. Will there be Dinosaurs? Probably. Pirates? Likely. Plus you can see some holdovers from pre-rotation Standard, like a Khenra or two. Be sure to check out [Brennan Decandio's Information Overload](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/35918_Information-Overload-For-SCG-Dallas.html) for his predictions of the event.

After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [A First Look at *Ixalan* Standard Results](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?ID=14198&writer=Seth+Manfield&articledate=9-29-2017) | TCGplayer | Seth Manfield

2015 World Champion Seth Manfield looks at the landscape of the new Standard and shares the top Standard decks based on the early *Magic Online* results utilizing *Ixalan* and the rotation.


1. [*Ixalan* Post-Prerelease Thoughts! Sealed Deck Info for *Magic: The Gathering*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a41YoRtVoFs) | Magic the Amateuring

Megan and Maria podcast about Sealed and Draft through the lens of Pirates, Dinosaurs, Vampires, and Merfolk . . . Cats and . . . socks? Seems right on par for these two awesome ladies.


1. [Flavor Tour of *Ixalan*](https://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2017/09/flavor-tour-ixalan/) | Hipsters of the Coast | Beck Holden

Beck Holden throws his tricorn Pirate hat into the ring as a voice for . . . wait for it . . . Vorthos. In his first Vorthos review, he does a stellar job looking at the art and talking about things that art lovers like. Angles of view, and colors, and story. A lightweight and fun read to look closer at the amazing art of *Ixalan*.


1. [*Ixalan* Prerelease Recap](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GlelL_xOiFI) | TapTapConcede

Cam and Serge sit down and ~~host a show on NPR~~ share their stories and thoughts from the *Ixalan* Prerelease. Easy listening for a commute or while you're going about your day. Their calm tones intermix with excitement at cards, and gameplay stories make this quite the enjoyable watch or listen.
