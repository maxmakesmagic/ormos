By modern deckbuilding standards, Zak Dolan's World Championship winning list is "interesting" to say the least. He packed it full of some of the game's most powerful cards (look at how many members of the Power 9 are present!) and locked his opponent out of the match with his Stasis/Time Elemental combo and good ol' Serra Angel for a win condition. You can read more about the first World Champions in [this](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtgcom/feature/226) nifty article by **Magic** web manager Monty Ashley.


As for this year's World Championships? You can live the dream too, by qualifying for the [2008 World Championships](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds), to be held in Memphis, Tennessee December 11-14. How? By participating in the Last Chance Qualifier tournaments being held on [**Magic Online**](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/digital/magiconline.aspx)! The events feature Standard and Limited formats and run from Wednesday, October 15 through Thursday, October 16. For more information, check out the official announcement [here](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/100308).
