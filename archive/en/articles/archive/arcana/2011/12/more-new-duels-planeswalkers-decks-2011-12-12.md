More New Duels of the Planeswalkers Decks!
==========================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 12, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/861_header.jpg)  
Remember the new *Duels of the Planeswalkers* decks we [announced last month](/en/articles/archive/new-duels-planeswalkers-decks-2011-11-14)? The ones that came out on Steam on November 16? Well, another two 60-card decks with unlockable cards are coming out on December 14! There's another pair coming on January 11, but that's so far away we can ignore them for now. If you're on PSN, the first deck pack will be available on December 20.

Each decklist pack will be $2.99 on Steam or 240 Microsoft Points in the United States. On other platforms and in other countries, it will probaby be close to that but not precise. You know how it is.

And now, on with the decklists! This time we've got a green Treefolk deck called "Forest's Fury" and a white-black Angel deck called "Dark Heavens" for your enjoyment.
