The StarCityGames.com Open series is a large tournament circuit hosted and produced by StarCityGames.com. It gives away thousands in dollars of prizes, boasts some 15 events, and features Standard and Legacy competition. the first event of the 2010 season was won by Richard "Aaron" Wayne, who took his Standard build of Burn all the way to the trophy! You can read StarCity's coverage of the event [here](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/18414_Live_Coverage_of_the_StarCityGamescom_10K_Weekend_in_St_Louis.html).
