Magic Panel at SDCC
===================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Last weekend *Magic* hosted a panel at San Diego Comic-Con featuring Aaron Forsythe, senior director of *Magic*R&D; Jenna Helland, design manager of the *Magic* story team; Jeremy Jarvis, *Magic* senior art director; Liz Lamb-Ferro, manager of *Magic*global brand marketing; and Mark Rosewater, *Magic* head designer. Together, they discussed *Magic Origins*, the future of *Magic*storytelling, and some choice art and story call-outs.
