***Magic** 2011* Boosters
=========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 24, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Ahoy! You may not realize this, but ***Magic** 2011* is right around the corner. Before you know it, you'll be able to buy it in booster packs. Booster packs that look like this!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/456_pack1.jpg)  
There's just nothing not to like about a guy with a huge sword.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/456_pack2.jpg)  
This would appear to be a merfolk of some variety. Very interesting.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/456_pack3.jpg)  
This looks like it's probably a force of malignant evil. Something like that, anyway. Now we're getting somewhere!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/456_pack4.jpg)  
I'm not going to lie to you. I already know what card this image goes on. And here's a sneak preview: it's a dragon!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/456_pack5.jpg)  
Rawr! A monster!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/456_display.jpg)
