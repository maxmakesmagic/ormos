Vintage Championships
=====================



 Posted in **MTGO Event Info**
 on July 14, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_davidwhitfield.jpg)
By David Whitfield












![](https://web.archive.org/web/20141030155949im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/NEWSITE_MTGO_Logo_reduced.png)

[Download *****Magic Online*****](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/download ) 
  


You've become a *Vintage Masters* Limited legend! A Constructed colossus! What to do now? How can you prove that you're truly the best on *Magic Online*?


![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150920165531im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/VintageMasters_championship_blk_620.png)
That's right, Championship season is upon us once again. It's time to prove your mastery over the *Vintage Masters* set.


Qualifiers begin on Wednesday, August 13 and run until Tuesday, August 26. [Vintage Limited Championship Qualifiers](#vmaltdqualifier) lead the pack, running ever three hours beginning at 12:00 p.m. on Wednesday, August 13, and concluding on Tuesday, August 19. [Vintage Constructed Championship Qualifiers](#vmaconstqualifier) begin at 12:00 p.m., Wednesday, August 20 and run through Tuesday, August 26. At least four wins in either event will net you an invite to the corresponding Championship Event, not to mention *Vintage Masters* boosters and QPs.


You have your hard-won invitation, you've been playing the queues, reading up on Vintage strategy, meditating at dawn â€” all of that. You're ready. It's time for the [Vintage Championships](#championships)! The [Vintage Limited Championship](#limited) takes place on Saturday, August 23 at 8:00 a.m. Pacific Time. The Limited Championship will be a Swiss Sealed event, followed by a Top 8 Draft. Once the dust settles in our digital arena, the [*Vintage Masters* Constructed Championships](#constructed) will begin on Saturday, August 30 at 8:00 a.m. Constructed Championships will be Single Elimination, with a Vintage Constructed Top 8.


Refer to the tables below for the full details.
