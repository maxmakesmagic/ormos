Decks of the Week for September 26, 2014
========================================



 Posted in [ARCHIVES](/en/articles/archive)
 on September 26, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










While players focused their energy on the *Khans of Tarkir* Prerelease events, the Pro Tour Qualifier season had a relatively slow week. Action will be picking up once again as we move into October.



Once we return to PTQs on a regular basis, the format players will be delving into is *Khans of Tarkir* Limited. For now, we pay farewell to *Magic 2015* Limited. New block, new set, and new cards will be the forefront of the PTQ competitors' minds. Remember to [find a Pro Tour Qualifier near you](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/pro-tour-fate-reforged-qualifiers) to see if you can earn an invitation and airfare to Pro Tour *Fate Reforged* February 6–8, 2015.
