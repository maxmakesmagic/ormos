Alternate Moxen
===============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 10, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Did you know that each year, the winner of the Vintage Championship at Gen Con wins a big, cool, alternate-art version of one of the Power Nine?


From 2003 through 2005, the prizes were new versions of [Black Lotus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Lotus), [Timetwister](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Timetwister), and [Ancestral Recall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancestral+Recall). Then in 2006, we started with the Moxen, each done by Volkan Baga. And perhaps you would like to see the sketches and artwork that led to them!
