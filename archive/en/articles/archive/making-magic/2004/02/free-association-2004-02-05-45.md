Free Association
================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on February 5, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_markrosewater.jpg)
By Mark Rosewater




 Working in R&D since '95, Mark became Magic head designer in '03. His hobbies: spending time with family, writing about Magic in all mediums, and creating short bios. 






I'm curious whether I do too many, too little or just enough offbeat articles. (And whether you want to hear any more oblique jokes involving Descartes.) What do you think? I'd love to hear.

Anyway, it's time to wrap up this column. Thanks for dropping by.

Join me next week when I turn the tables on all of you.

 Until then, may you enjoy the unexpected. Mark Rosewater
