Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 13, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After 15 rounds, with a total of 1,412 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!


### Wenzel Krautmann


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmad15/gpmad15_t8_krautmann.jpg)


**Age:** 33


**Occupation:** Tourist


**Hometown:** T체bingen, Germany


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Member of #TEAMTHOMMO.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-white, with red-green in my sideboard. I ended up 7-2.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Four copies of [Faerie Miscreant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faerie+Miscreant) in Blue-White Flyers. 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Five copies of [Faerie Miscreant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faerie+Miscreant). Blue-Red Flyers. 3-0.

### Aleksa Telarov


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmad15/gpmad15_t8_telarov.jpg)


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Magic: The Gathering and accounting


**Hometown:** Novi Sad, Serbia


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


I think six-time national championship, Grand Prix Vienna Top 4, World Magic Cup Top 8, cashed at some GPs, played in a number of Pro Tours. Also, shook hands with Rich Hagon!


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Mountain. 8-1 with a red-green deck with a good curve.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Mountain. Red with a touch of white. 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Mountain. Red-Blue Artifacts. 2-1.

### Ashraf Abou Omar


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmad15/gpmad15_t8_abouomar.jpg)


**Age:** 26


**Occupation:** Daytrader


**Hometown:** Kiel, Germany


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Member of Team #GPWhatever.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-white. [Grasp of the Hieromancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grasp+of+the+Hieromancer).


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**

### Francesco Giorgio


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmad15/gpmad15_t8_giorgio.jpg)


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** PhD Student


**Hometown:** Oxford, England, originally from Napoli, Italy


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Third place at the 2014 World Magic Cup, Top 75 at two Pro Tours.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-red, 8-1. The deck worked very well as a whole, but if I had to pick one card, then definitely [Cruel Revival](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Revival).


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Blue-black, 3-0. [Sphinx's Tutelage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%27s+Tutelage) was awesome.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Blue-Black, 2-1. Again, [Sphinx's Tutelage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%27s+Tutelage) won the most of my games. Also [Jhessian Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jhessian+Thief) which is one of my favorite cards in the set.

### Fabrizio Anteri


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmad15/gpmad15_t8_anteri.jpg)


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Professional Magic player, part-time Magic store clerk


**Hometown:** London, England


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Won Grand Prix London four weeks ago, finished in the Top 16 at Grand Prix Prague two weeks ago.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Hangarback Walker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hangarback+Walker) ["HangarFAB Walker"] and "Jose and Maria," also known as [Pia and Kiran Nalaar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pia+and+Kiran+Nalaar). Black-red, 9-0.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Knightly Valor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knightly+Valor), green-white, 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Outland Colossus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Outland+Colossus), red-green, 3-0.

### Michael Bonde


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpmad15/gpmad15_t8_bonde.jpg)


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Student and member of Team Rocket


**Hometown:** Aarhus, Denmark


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Top 4 at Grand Prix Strasbourg 2013, tenth on tiebreakers at Grand Prix San Diego 2010, StarCityGames Worchester champion, Bazaar of Moxen Vintage champion 2013.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Green-white, [Topan Freeblade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Topan+Freeblade) and [Outland Colossus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Outland+Colossus), 7-2.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Red-black, [Act of Treason](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Act+of+Treason) and [Volcanic Rambler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Rambler), 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Red-black, [Act of Treason](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Act+of+Treason) and [Chandra's Fury](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%27s+Fury), 3-0.

**Hometown:** Kiev, Ukraine


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Two Grand Prix Top 8s, 2014 Ukrainian national champion.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-green. [Outland Colossus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Outland+Colossus) and [Managorger Hydra](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Managorger+Hydra). 7-2.

Red-White Tempo. Three [Iroas's Champion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iroas%27s+Champion)s. 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Blue-green. Six [Claustrophobia](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Claustrophobia)s. 3-0.
