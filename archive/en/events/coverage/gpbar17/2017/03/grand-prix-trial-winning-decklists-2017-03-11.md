Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 11, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











Grand Prix events typically don't start until Saturday (although one Grand Prix Las Vegas later this year will). However, that doesn't mean it's not worth it to head to the venue on a GP Friday. Most importantly, Friday is the final day for players to earn byes for the weekend's main event. And their final chance is our first: that is, to see which decks might do well over the course of the weekend.


So let's do just that, shall we? Here is a selection of winning lists from yesterday's Trials. Overall, Mardu Ballista and Four-Color Copycat did outstandingly well and we could have listed a few more copies of either. For Temur Tower and Black-Green, on the other hand, really only one list each made its way to us. For something outside of the "Big Four," we recommend checking out the Aetherworks Marvel and Black-Red Aggro decks posted immediately below!
