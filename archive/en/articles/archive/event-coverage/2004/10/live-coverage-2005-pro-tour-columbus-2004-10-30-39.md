Live Coverage of 2005 Pro Tour Columbus
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 30, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff












Eight different decks, five countries represented, two first-time Pro Tour players…truly this Extended format has put together as varied a Top 8 as we've seen in recent memory. Here's a look at each player, in seed order:




![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptcol05/canali.jpg)*Pierre Canali*


### Pierre Canali


**Age:** 22  
**Home:** Aix en Provence, France  
**Occupation:** Salsa teacher  
**Number of Pro Tours:** 1  
**How did you qualify?** PTQ Toulouse  
**Number of GP/PT top 8's:** 0  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments:** Day 2, GP  
**Day 1 record:** 8-0  
**Day 2 record:** 5-3  
**What did you play and why?** Affinity. I think there are a lot of sideboard cards against the deck and everyone thinks its bad as a result. [Meddling Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) made all the difference.  
**Who built your deck?** Guillaume Matignon, Guillaume Wago Tapa and me  
**What decks were you expecting to play against?** Rock, Reanimator, combo  
**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** Pretty good  
**Fun fact from the weekend:** I won against turn 1 [Phantom Nishoba](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nishoba).






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptcol05/west.jpg)*Nicholas West*


### Nicholas West


**Age:** 21  
**Home:** Guildford, England  
**Occupation:** N/A  
**Number of Pro Tours:** 1  
**How did you qualify?** PTQ  
**Number of GP/PT top 8's:** 0  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments:** 6th, English Nationals, 2004  
**Day 1 record:** 7-1  
**Day 2 record:** 6-2  
**What did you play and why?** Scepter/Chant, I built it and thought people would be unprepared for it.  
**Who built your deck?** Me with help from Steve Easton, Tim Hows and Samuel Rager over the last 8 months  
**What decks were you expecting to play against?** Rock, Tog, Deep Dog, [Reanimate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reanimate), RDW  
**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** If I win in the quarterfinals I should make the finals, but really, it's anyone's game on the big day.  
**Fun fact from the weekend:** Stuart Wright will no longer be able to call me really lucky and bad at **Magic**!





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptcol05/szleifer.jpg)*Gadiel Szleifer*


### Gadiel Szleifer


**Age:** 15  
**Home:** Ithaca, NY  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Number of Pro Tours:** 4  
**How did you qualify?** PT Seattle  
**Number of GP/PT top 8's:** 0  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments:** 7th, PT Seattle  
**Day 1 record:** 7-1  
**Day 2 record:** 5-2-1  
**What did you play and why?** Reanimator, so could maximize getting lucky. I did.  
**Who built your deck?** I changed some lists from a Japanese GP  
**What decks were you expecting to play against?** Rock, Goblins, RDW, Reanimator  
**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** I'll win the quarters but lose the semis to a horrible matchup.  
**Fun fact from your Magic career:** I slept through day 2 of GP Oakland





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptcol05/ooiso.jpg)*Masashi Ooiso*


### Masashi Ooiso


**Age:** 20  
**Home:** Hiroshima, Japan  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Number of Pro Tours:** 14  
**How did you qualify?** PT points  
**Number of GP/PT top 8's:** Three each  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments:** None given  
**Day 1 record:** 7-1  
**Day 2 record:** 5-2-1  
**What did you play and why?** Desire, it's a good deck!  
**Who built your deck?** Katsuhiro Mori and other Japanese players.  
**What decks were you expecting to play against?** Reanimator, the Rock.  
**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** I hope I win.  
**Fun fact from the weekend:** I never played against red.





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptcol05/ruel.jpg)*Olivier Ruel*


### Olivier Ruel


**Age:** 23  
**Home:** Paris, France  
**Occupation:** Not quite occupied right now.  
**Number of Pro Tours:** Around 30  
**How did you qualify?** PT points  
**Number of GP/PT top 8's:** 9 GPs, 3 PTs  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments:** 9th Kamigawa Pre-release in Paris  
**Day 1 record:** 6-2  
**Day 2 record:** 6-1-1  
**What did you play and why?** Goblins. It's the only deck I didn't test and I didn't like anything else.  
**Who built your deck?** Yann Hamon and Anon Go  
**What decks were you expecting to play against?** Rock, Goblins, Reanimator  
**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** I really like my first matchup, but even if I lose, it'll be to Oiso, which I don't really mind.  
**Fun fact from the weekend:** In both French Nationals and this tournament, I hated all the decks except for Goblins because I never tested Goblins. Yann gave me the deck the night before and my first round was my first game. Oh and I won French nationals.





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptcol05/nakamura.jpg)*Shuhei Nakamura*

**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** I want to win!!! But, I probably won't.  
**Fun fact from the weekend:** I beat Cephalid Breakfast twice. It was too easy.





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptcol05/arita.jpg)*Ryuichi Arita*

**Home:** Tokyo, Japan  
**Occupation:** Professional Magic player  
**Number of Pro Tours:** 6

**Number of GP/PT top 8's:** One, PT Seattle, Team S.A.I.

**Day 1 record:** 7-1

**What decks were you expecting to play against?** The Rock, Reanimator, RDW and Goblins.  
**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** My quarterfinal match is tough. I lost to him in the swiss.
