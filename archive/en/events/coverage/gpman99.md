Grand Prix-ManilaDecember 4-5, 1999
===================================












![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPMANILA99/images/Winner.jpg)Grand Prix-Manila wrapped up at 11:30 pm Sunday night. The finals were played by two Philippine competitors, Christopher Parreña and Frederick Salazar. Although the hour was late when the Grand Prix concluded, there was still a crowd of spectators watching and cheering the players on.
