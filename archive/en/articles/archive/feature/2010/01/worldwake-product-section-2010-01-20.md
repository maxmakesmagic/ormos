*Worldwake* Product Section
===========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 20, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By magicthegathering.com Staff











Ihe [t's time for another update to the *Worldwake* Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/worldwake)! There's a new puzzle! A new setting preview! And there's the Visual Spoiler, an archive of all preview cards that have been officially released. Have you missed any cards? Here's one we showed on the @[dailymtg](/en/articles/archive/2012-07-10) Twitter feed yesterday:

![Abyssal Persecutor](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/tcg/products/worldwake/rto4ggowya_EN.jpg)  
That card, along with other cards that have been previewed, shows up on the Visual Spoiler today. And over the next week and a half, more and more cards will appear as we count down to the *Worldwake* Prerelease on January 30-31.

[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/features/feature73b_ss_gps5nmkwit.jpg)](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/worldwake)  
So what are you waiting for? Hit the [go to the *Worldwake* Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/worldwake) now!
