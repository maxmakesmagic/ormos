Top 8 Decklists
===============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 28, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











After fifteen rounds of Modern Constructed, we reduced the field of 2802 players down to just 8! An indication of a diverse and healthy metagame, eight different archetypes were represented in the Top 8 of Grand Prix Kobe! Let's see what they played to rise above the rest!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpkob17/gpkob17_TOP8_Big.jpg)


* Tomoya Tsubouchi – Ad Nauseam
* Takeshi Kanagawa – Affinity
* Akio Chiba – Esper Control
* Park Bi-o – Grixis Shadow
* Ryoichi Tamada – Jeskai Control
* Terumasa Kojima – Dredge
* Fumiyasu Suzuike – Eldrazi Tron
* Joe Soh – White-Black Eldrazi
