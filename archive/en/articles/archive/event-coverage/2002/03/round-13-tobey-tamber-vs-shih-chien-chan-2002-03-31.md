Round 13: Tobey Tamber vs. Shih Chien Chan
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 31, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_244.png)
By Ryan Soh











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpkl02/a942.jpg)Grand Prix - Kaohsiung Champion Tobey Tamber's deck is B/G with Chainer and [Roar of the Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm). Shih Chien's deck is a top-heavy R/G with 18 land, featuring copious amounts of burn and [Chainflinger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger). The winner of this match will get to fight it out for the big bucks in the Top 8.

### Game 1

Playing second, Tamber held two swamps, [Werebear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) and some black cards. He drew into the [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) and the Bear was the first to enter the arena, and after a [Flame Burst](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flame+Burst), it was the first to leave. Chan drew and played [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). Tamber had removal of his own in the form of [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood) and used [Morgue Theft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morgue+Theft) to bring the [Werebear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) back to his hand.

A Shih Chien [Chainflinger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger) got [Ghastly Demise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghastly+Demise)d and Tobey made a shortlived [Soul Scourge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Scourge) that got hit by [Acceptable Losses](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acceptable+Losses). Over the course of the next few turns the Scourge was brought back into the game via [Morgue Theft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morgue+Theft), a Farmhand was played and [Mesmeric Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) took away Chan's [Demoralize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demoralize). It was the only non-land spell he had for the rest of the game.

Tamber 1 Chan 0

Sideboarding: Tamber: -1 Chainer -1 [Sandstone Deadfall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sandstone+Deadfall) +2 [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood)  

Chan: -1 [Whipkeeper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whipkeeper) +1 [Krosan Constrictor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Constrictor)

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpkl02/a943.jpg)Chan started with [Skyshooter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyshooter) on turn two and [Krosan Constrictor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Constrictor) on turn four. Combined, they played havoc with Tamber's life total and he was brought down to 12 before he stabilized with [Nantuko Calmer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Calmer), [Nantuko Disciple](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Disciple) and [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood). The advantage still remained with Chan however as these were the only spells he had for about five turns - apparently Chan's mana flood was contagious.

Chan, meanwhile, had business spells and used [Flame Burst](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flame+Burst) to burn the Disciple and cast [Limestone Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Limestone+Golem), which socked Tobey in the face once, and then on the next turn made [Centaur Veteran](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Centaur+Veteran). He cleverly used [Magma Vein](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magma+Vein) to gain Threshold and played [Demoralize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demoralize), attacking Tamber down to three. Tobey found blockers, but [Fledgling Imp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Imp)s and [Werebear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear)s don't stop [Scorching Missile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scorching+Missile).

Tamber 1 Chan 1

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpkl02/a944.jpg)Tobey Tamber's attentive sideboarding was key in this game. He used the two [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood)s in his opening hand to dispose of Shih Chien's [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) and [Seton's Scout](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Scout). He followed up the second Blood with [Mesmeric Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend), hoping to further blunt Chan's attempts at being the aggressor. He chose to remove [Magma Vein](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magma+Vein) over [Elephant Ambush](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Ambush). Knowing Chan's hand, on his next turn he enchanted the Fiend with [Shade's Form](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shade%27s+Form). An elephant token came into play on Chan's side and started the beatdown. Tamber took the damage and played [Werebear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) and [Diligent Farmhand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diligent+Farmhand). He send the Fiend in and pumped it with all the Black mana he had, which was growing by the turn.

The Farmhand held the Elephant off for a turn and went to look for more [Swamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp)s. It was pretty clear after Tamber attacked for the third time with the Fiend that the he was winning the race. Shih Chien's hand was not useful. He held [Demoralize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demoralize) and land. [Scorching Missile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scorching+Missile) was used to try and even out the race but the deity of Mana Flood mananged a hat-trick. The Elephant gave its life to save its master for one more turn, but Chan could not deliver.

**Final Result:** Tamber 2 Chan 1
