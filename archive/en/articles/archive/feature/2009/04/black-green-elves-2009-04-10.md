Black-Green Elves was a popular deck over the course of the past two years, but has cooled off in Standard of late. Now, Mrine brings us a new version of the deck straight from **Magic Online**. This version recently made the Top 8 of a Premier Event there, running unique inclusions like [Leaf Gilder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leaf+Gilder) and [Platinum Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Platinum+Angel). Could Elves be the new tribe back on the block?
