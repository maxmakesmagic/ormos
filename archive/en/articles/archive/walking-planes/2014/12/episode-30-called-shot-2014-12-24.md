Episode 30: "Called Shot"
=========================



 Posted in **Walking the Planes**
 on December 24, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_nathanholtandshawnkornhauser_0.jpg)
By Nathan Holt and Shawn Kornhauser











What is your greatest dream related to *Magic*? To collect every card from every set? To teach all your friends how to play? Or maybe you'd like to win a tournament. Perhaps even the World Championship! Today, we tell the story of Worlds Week 2014, a magical event showcasing the best *Magic* talent in the world. For those involved, it's a fairy tale come to life.
