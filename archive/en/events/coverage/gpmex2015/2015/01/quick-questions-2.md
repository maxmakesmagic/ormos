QUICK QUESTIONS #2 - “What’s your favorite uncommon in Fate Reforged Limited?”
==============================================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 31, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/080214_1291.jpg)
By Jacob Van Lunen




Jacob Van Lunen began playing Magic in 1995. He has participated in organized play at every level of competition and was a member of the winning team at Pro Tour San Diego in 2007, thanks to an innovative draft strategy. As a writer, Van Lunen has had more than three hundred Magic strategy pieces published 





Evaluating the power of individual cards against one another can be difficult when a new set is first released. The ability to establish which cards are most desirable at every rarity level puts a player well ahead of his or her opponents. I sat down with some of the most well-known players here at Grand Prix Mexico city to ask, “What’s your favorite uncommon in *Fate Reforged* Limited?”
