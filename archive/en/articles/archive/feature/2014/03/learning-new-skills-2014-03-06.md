Learning New Skills
===================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 6, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson












I'm still waiting for Hexproof to put up a premier, high-level finish.

![Madcap Skills](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Madcap+Skills) In the meantime, I'll keep posting these tuned-looking lists that continue to 4–0 **Magic Online** Daily Events. [Fleecemane Lion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleecemane+Lion) and [Fiendslayer Paladin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiendslayer+Paladin) aren't perfect to suit up, but they seem to be getting the job done. One of the great things about the deck is that [Skylasher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skylasher) out of the sideboard is exactly the type of dude you want to suit up in certain matchups. If you hate losing to Mono-Blue Devotion, then this might be your deck. 

 The sideboard also looks pretty strong. [Mizzium Mortars](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mizzium+Mortars) takes care of opposing Blood Barons, which is one of the few things that can stand in your way. I love the use of [Mending Touch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mending+Touch) against [Supreme Verdict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Supreme+Verdict), and would consider a third if not for the already-present [Boros Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Charm)s. [Revoke Existence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Revoke+Existence) can get rid of indestructible gods or [Detention Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere) and Gift of the Orzhova is an additional [Unflinching Courage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unflinching+Courage). 

 Hexproof is not exactly a fun deck to play with or against, but it looks pretty good right now. Mono-Black Devotion players appear to be cutting their [Devour Flesh](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devour+Flesh)es as well, so now might be the time to teach your [Gladecover Scout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gladecover+Scout) some [Madcap Skills](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Madcap+Skills).
