Artisan
=======


![](https://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/02_FotF_Artisan.jpg)



***Chapter 1 - The Artisan Market***



*Hidden deep in the Wilds, the Festival of the Fae kicks off with a welcome fete of magical wares. A bustling plaza stretches as far as the eye can see. What's for sale? Mostly junk, of course. But sprinkled among the clutter real treasures await...if you're willing to look.*


**Event Details:** Build a 60-card Standard constructed deck using only commons and uncommons.  
**Event Format:** Artisan  
**Entry Fee:** 2500 Gold or 500 Gems  
**Banned Cards:**


* [Cauldron Familiar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cauldron+Familiar)
* [Cavalcade of Calamity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cavalcade+of+Calamity)
* [Gates Ablaze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gates+Ablaze)
* [Persistent Petitioners](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Persistent+Petitioners)

**Event Record:** Rewards up to 5 wins, losses are not counted  
**Rewards:**


* 1 Win: Merchant of the Vale Showcase card style
* 2 Wins: Beanstalk Giant Showcase card style
* 3 Wins: Order of Midnight Showcase card style
* 4 Wins: Giant Killer Showcase card style
* 5 Wins: Brazen Borrower Showcase card style

Oko’s Madness
=============


![](https://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/03_FotF_Oko_Madness.jpg)


***Chapter 2 - Oko's Madness*** 


*The master of ceremonies has arrived! Now let go the shackles of your courtly Realm and embrace the chaos of the Wilds! The very land shifts under your feet and impossible creatures appear out of thin air to join the fray. You're not dreaming. It's just getting weird.*


**Event Details:** Each player begins the game with a 60-card deck containing only basic lands, and an Oko's Whims emblem that has the following abilities:


* Creatures you control have base power and toughness 3/3 and are Elks in addition to their other types.
* Pay 1, Discard a card: Create a token that's a copy of a random creature with converted mana cost 8 or less. Activate this ability only any time you could cast a sorcery.

**Event Format:** Oko's Madness (A Variant on Momir's Madness)  
**Entry Fee:** 2500 Gold or 500 Gems


**Additional Cards:** The following creature tokens can also appear when creating a token through the Oko’s Whims emblem:


* [Baleful Force](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baleful+Force)
* [Bedlam Reveler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bedlam+Reveler)
* [Borborygmos Enraged](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Borborygmos+Enraged)
* [Crash of Rhinos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crash+of+Rhinos)
* [Gigantomancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gigantomancer)
* [Griselbrand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Griselbrand)
* [Hoverguard Sweepers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hoverguard+Sweepers)

Cascade
=======


![](https://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/04_FotF_Cascade.jpg)



 



***Chapter 3 - Cascading Finale***


*Even Faerie parties must come to an end, but not before going out with a bang. A magical cacophony fills the Wilds as festivalgoers launch spells like fireworks into the night sky, summoning all manner of mayhem. Join in if you dare!*


**Event Details:** Each player begins the game with a Maelstrom Nexus emblem that has the following ability:


* When you cast your first spell each turn, exile cards from the top of your library until you exile a nonland card that costs less. You may cast it without paying its mana cost. Put the exiled cards on the bottom of your library in a random order.

**Event Format:** Cascade  
**Entry Fee:** 2500 Gold or 500 Gems  
**Banned Cards:**


* [Deafening Silence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deafening+Silence)
* [Fae of Wishes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fae+of+Wishes)
* [Field of the Dead](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Field+of+the+Dead)
* [Flaxen Intruder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flaxen+Intruder)
* [Lavinia, Azorius Renegade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavinia%2C+Azorius+Renegade)
* [Lucky Clover](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lucky+Clover)
* [Rule of Law](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rule+of+Law)
* [Teferi, Time Raveler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Time+Raveler)

**Event Record:** Rewards up to 5 wins, losses are not counted  
**Rewards:**


* 1 Win: Rimrock Knight Showcase card style
* 2 Wins: Foulmire Knight Showcase card style

* 4 Wins: Lovestruck Beast Showcase card style
