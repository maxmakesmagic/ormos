Elementals have been an outside force in Standard since *Lorwyn* Block hit the world of Standard. Brandden "McDoogle" Mcdonough took this build to the Finals of a recent PTQ in Seattle, Washington. Fast mana on the backs of [Smokebraider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smokebraider) and [Bloom Tender](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloom+Tender) allow powerful Elementals to hit the battlefield early. [Flamekin Harbinger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flamekin+Harbinger) ties the deck together, ensuring a player can always tutor for the Elemental they need!
