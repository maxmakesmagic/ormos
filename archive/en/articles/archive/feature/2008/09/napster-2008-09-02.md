In honor of the Hall of Fame ballots being tabulated this week, Daily Decks is examining current Hall of Famer's most famous creations. Jon Finkel is famous for many reasons but one of the decks he is best known for is the list he used to claim the [U.S. National champion title in 2000](http://www.wizards.com/sideboard/article.asp?x=USNAT00/welcome). His mono-black list, affectionately entitled "Napster," used a group of singleton cards to abuse the powerful [Vampiric Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor). The instant allowed Jon to search up a card like [Perish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Perish) when playing against a green opponent while reducing the likelihood he would draw it against any other opponent. The gambit paid off and he added one more title to his impressive **Magic** resume.
