If you're like players all across North America, you just finished up a long Regionals weekend. What did you play? Did you see any cool, new decks? If you were at Tokyo Regionals on May 5th, you may have seen this unique creation played by Koji Takanashi. The Japanese player sought to draw both he and his opponent many extra cards each turn, lock out the combat step with fogs, and eventually deck the other player with [Howling Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine)s! Such strategies have done well before, and it won the day for Takanashi who finished in first.
