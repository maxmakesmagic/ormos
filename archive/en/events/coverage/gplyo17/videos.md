Danes Dominate Lyon
===================




In an all-Danish *Ixalan* Team Booster Draft finals, the trio of Thomas Enevoldsen, Christoffer Larsen, Michael Bonde bested Alexander Pasgaard, Kasper Nielsen, Marcus Hensing to become champions of Grand Prix Lyon 2017!






Nov 25-26, 2017


Lyon EurExpo



Thomas Enevoldsen, Christoffer Larsen and Michael Bonde
