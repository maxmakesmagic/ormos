Round 2: Craig Krempels vs. Tom Guevin
======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 27, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a932.jpg)Krempels has had a breakout year in competitive Magic, and was even the leader in the Rookie of the Year race until Masashi Ooiso crept ahead of him and then pulled far ahead by coming in second at Pro Tour Yokohama. This is not to take anything away from Krempels, who came in ninth at the same Pro Tour on tiebreakers. Without a doubt, he's a player to watch this weekend. His opponent, Tom Guevin, is, well...Tom Guevin. "Tommy" made the finals of the second Pro Tour in history on the old Queen Mary, but is better known for his unique personality than anything else.

### Game 1

Guevin cast [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), seeing [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace), two [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse) and lands. [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse) went to the graveyard, Krempels played [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and Guevin brought out [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade). [Rotlung Reanimator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator) followed, and [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) was played, naming [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion). It was flashed back to get a [Wrath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath) that was discarded to [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), but Krempels drew a second one, and cleared the board of creatures. The Rotlung provided a Zombie token when it left, and Guevin summoned [Stronghold Assassin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stronghold+Assassin). Thanks to [Unholy Grotto](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unholy+Grotto), the Rotlung came back out, while Krempels continued to cycle through his deck with [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and drew extra cards with [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a933.jpg)[Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) slowed things down a bit, and a face-down [Exalted Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel) died to [Stronghold Assassin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stronghold+Assassin). A [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) was [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse)d twice, and the third time, Krempels responded with [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) to get a flashed back [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace), and cast it. The Fiend grabbed [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith). Krempels took his turn, and played [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake). The following attack was nullified by [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace), but [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt) finished it before Krempels could untap and go off.

Krempels- 0 Guevin- 1

### Game 2

[Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) revealed a hand of [Exalted Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel), [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake), [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) and [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God)- the [Wrath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath) headed to the graveyard. [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) followed on turn two, getting the Angel, and [Rotlung Reanimator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator) continued the curve on turn three. [Krosan Verge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Verge) accelerated Krempels into [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake) one turn early, and a second Fiend took [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith), leaving Krempels with [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) and [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse). [Withered Wretch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Withered+Wretch) added even more pressure to the board, but [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) at least bought Krempels an extra turn.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a931.jpg)The Wretch removed the Peace from the graveyard to prevent a flash back on the next attack, and [Braids, Cabal Minion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Braids%2C+Cabal+Minion) was [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell)ed (with Krempels burning for two since his only blue source was [Skycloud Expanse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skycloud+Expanse)). With no answers left, Krempels drew [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), and drew [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God) off that. Thanks to the Rotlung, Guevin was left with two Zombie tokens, but Krempels got back [Exalted Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel) and [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith). [Chastise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chastise) took out a token and [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith) was cycled. [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) got the Angel out of the way, and a token was used to flash it back, getting [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) out of Krempels' hand, leaving him with only a [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse). 

[Braids](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Braids) resolved, and Krempels was way ahead on lands in play thanks to his second [Krosan Verge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Verge). Guevin untapped, and sacrificed [Braids](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Braids) right away. [Graveborn Muse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Graveborn+Muse) died to [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God), and a deadly [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt) was [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse)d. This caused Krempels to burn for two, and when Guevin replayed it, Krempels had another [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse), burning once again. The third time, it resolved.

**Final Result:** Krempels- 0 Guevin- 2
