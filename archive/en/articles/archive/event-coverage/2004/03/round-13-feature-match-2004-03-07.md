Round 13 Feature Match:
=======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 7, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall




 Brian David-Marshall is a New York–based game designer who has been involved with Magic since 1994, when he started organizing tournaments and ran a Manhattan game store. Since then, he has been a judge, a player, and one of the longest-tenured columnists on DailyMTG.com, as he enters his second decade writing for the site. He is also the Pro Tour Historian and one of the commentators for the Pro Tour. 







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gphk04/r13fm1_1.jpg)


Both players were thinking about a draw this round but just as Olivier had run through the math for the young Malaysian he stopped himself short and realized, "I am so bad at math, we have to play." In fact, Olivier needed to win out with his deck in order to make Top 8.


### Game 1


Olivier tried to keep the tension out of the match with humor, "You have a die?"


"No."


"That is okay, I will play first…"


Terry laughed and offered Olivier the choice between two face down cards. The highest casting cost would choose. Terry won the "flip" but it was Olivier who led off the action with a [Lifespark Spellbomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lifespark+Spellbomb) and followed with a Talisman. Terry had a turn three [Gold Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gold+Myr). Olivier cycled EOT. Olivier untapped and played [Atog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Atog) and [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp). He equipped the Tog and sent back the turn brimming with confidence.


Terry played a [Rustspore Ram](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rustspore+Ram) and Olivier announced, "I have a response." He sacrificed the equipment to his [Atog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Atog) and tried to make the best of a bad situation, "Sometimes I impress even myself."


He untapped and [Fabricate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fabricate)d for a [Viridian Longbow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Longbow) and played it. Terry cast a [Nim Lasher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Lasher)--perhaps to deflect attention from his Myr. Olivier paid full retail for a [Spire Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spire+Golem) and when he passed the turn Terry [Shatter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shatter)ed the Longbow. He played a Drill Skimmer on his turn.


Olivier paid two for a [Dross Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dross+Golem) and attacked with [Atog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Atog) and [Spire Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spire+Golem). Terry nudged his Ram in the way of the Tog and Olivier ate a land to kill the 1/3. He played a [Hematite Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hematite+Golem) post attack. Suddenly Terry was in danger of being overwhelmed and he pushed his Drill Skimmer and [Nim Lasher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Lasher) into combat. Olivier thought about blocking and eventually put his [Hematite Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hematite+Golem) in front of the 3/1 [Nim Lasher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Lasher). He was tapped out and Terry stunned him with a [Scale of Chiss-Goria](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scale+of+Chiss-Goria) that boosted it's power and toughness. He played a [Vulshok Berserker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vulshok+Berserker) after combat.



![Vulshok Berserker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Vulshok+Berserker)

Olivier got in for five with his golems and was down to one card. It was [Essence Drain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Essence+Drain) and he killed the [Nim Lasher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Lasher) and went back up to twenty. Olivier looked at all the cards in his bin compared to Terry's and sighed, "You know you are playing badly when you have three times as many cards in your yard as they do."


Terry had another Berserker and attacked with both and the [Gold Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gold+Myr). Olivier traded a Talisman for the Berserker when he put his [Atog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Atog) in its path. Drill Skimmer took down the [Dross Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dross+Golem) on Olivier's attack and Terry took out the [Atog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Atog) with [Irradiate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Irradiate) on his own turn.


Olivier laughed as he drew [Arcbound Worker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcbound+Worker). He played it with his best karate sound effects and attacked with his flier. Terry kept plugging away with his two creatures and the Worker jumped in the way of the 3/2 and made the flier a 3/5. He cast [Thoughtcast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtcast) for four mana and found a [Gold Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gold+Myr). Terry fell to two with no flier in sight. He kept up the attack and stayed alive with a [Consume Spirit](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Consume+Spirit) for four.


Olivier cast [Chittering Rats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chittering+Rats) and Terry put a card on top of his deck. The Frenchman swung for three and Terry knew there were no answers awaiting him.


"This is just my impression but I think I played all my good cards while you didn't draw much."


Ruel - 1 Soh - 0


### Game 2


Olivier gnashed his teeth when Terry announced, "I will let you go first."


Olivier had three types of land and an [Atog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Atog) in play on turn three. Terry accelerated into turn three Berserker with a [Gold Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gold+Myr). Olivier tried to keep his foe at bay with his Tog in the upright position and a [Lifespark Spellbomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lifespark+Spellbomb). Terry attacked and Olivier laughed as he adjusted his life totals, "It could have worked."


Terry played [Grimclaw Bats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grimclaw+Bats) and [Emissary of Despair](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emissary+of+Despair). Ruel cycled for a land. He found one and played [Dross Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dross+Golem). Terry drew [Specter's Shroud](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Specter%27s+Shroud) and equipped his Emissary and attacked with everyone. Olivier double blocked the ground creature and took seven in the air and lost a card.


Ruel's draw step yielded nothing he could cast and they moved on to the rubber game of the match.


Ruel - 1 Soh - 1


### Game 3


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gphk04/r13fm1_2.jpg)After much debate, Olivier decided to play. He opened with an [Arcbound Worker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcbound+Worker). Terry took a couple of points and played a Talisman. Olivier had a turn three [Dross Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dross+Golem) but he was not willing to trade it with Terry's Berserker. Olivier grinned and played [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp). He drew two cards off of the Worker and attacked for four. He played [Welding Jar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar) and [Gold Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gold+Myr) to boot.


Terry had the [Disciple of the Vault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+the+Vault) and attacked for three. Olivier went to draw a pair of cards with his [Gold Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gold+Myr) but Terry stopped him with [Irradiate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Irradiate). Olivier's [Pewter Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pewter+Golem) was met with [Consume Spirit](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Consume+Spirit) and he ate the [Welding Jar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar) to save it. Terry packed it in one turn later.


Ruel - 2 Soh -1
