Card of the Day - September, 2012
=================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 3, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley

Ravnica is a place where the guilds are all at war.
