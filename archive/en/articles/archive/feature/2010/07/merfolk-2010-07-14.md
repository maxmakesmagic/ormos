Continuing our series of decks highlighting the decks R&D champions took to ***Magic** 2011* Prereleases all across the land, today we present a blue and white Merfolk deck played by Ken Nagle. He said the deck got a huge boost from the reprinting of [Mana Leak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak) as well as [Scroll Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scroll+Thief). If you're looking to give the new cards a spin, trying building Ken's deck for Friday Night **Magic** following the Launch Party for ***Magic** 2011*! Ken was one of the challengers in Seattle, WA.
