Former Pro Player of the Year Tomoharu Saito was the champion at Grand Prix–Singapore. He used his own take on Naya Zoo to earn the top slot. Cutting burn and [Sulfuric Vortex](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sulfuric+Vortex), he opted to play a slightly slower version that was more powerful in the mirror match and benefited from [Gaddock Teeg](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gaddock+Teeg) maindeck against matchups like Storm. Read more about the Grand Prix [in the official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/saito-slaughters-singapore).
