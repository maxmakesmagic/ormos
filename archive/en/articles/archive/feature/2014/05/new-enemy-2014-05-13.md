The New Enemy
=============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 13, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 







Ladies and Gentlemen, you have a new deck to beat. Black Devotion decks have been at the top of the Standard pecking order for quite some time. Due to the power of [Temples](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&format=+%5B%22Theros+Block%22%5D&type=+%5B%22Land%22%5D&name=+%5BTemple%5D), both white and red have seen success as splashes in Black Devotion. With *Journey into Nyx*, [Temple of Malady](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+of+Malady) opens up the option of splashing green. Andrew Tenjum recently won the [StarCityGames Open in Cincinnati](http://www.starcitygames.com/events/030514_cincinnati.html) with a Black Devotion deck with a light splash of green. 

![Gray Merchant of Asphodel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Gray+Merchant+of+Asphodel) The appeal of a green splash becomes apparent when you look at other new options with *Journey into Nyx*. [Banishing Light](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banishing+Light) has made its way into all sorts of white decks, and combined with [Detention Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere) as an additional target, [Abrupt Decay](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay) looks very appealing as a removal spell that will rarely lack a target. [Vraska the Unseen](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vraska+the+Unseen) and [Golgari Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golgari+Charm) ramp up the enchantment removal, which is important, given the amount of enchantments in Standard now with the introduction of *Journey into Nyx*.
