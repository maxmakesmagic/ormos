Last week it was [announced](/en/articles/archive/latest-developments/welcome-modern-world-2011-08-12) that the upcoming [Pro Tour Philadelphia](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/magicweekend/philadelphia11) will feature the Modern format. So what might a Modern deck look like? Here's one from Wizards of the Coast's own Lee Sharpe, based around [Kuldotha Rebirth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kuldotha+Rebirth). Lee's goal is to plunk as many creatures onto the table as quickly as possible, then use [Haze of Rage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haze+of+Rage) or [Goblin Bushwhacker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Bushwhacker) to hit the opponent for lethal! The deck list comes from the 2011 **Magic Online** Community Cup, where Lee was a competitor on behalf of the Wizards team.
