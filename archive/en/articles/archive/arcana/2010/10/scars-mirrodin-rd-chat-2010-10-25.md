*Scars of Mirrodin* R&D Chat
============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 25, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Want to learn how to design and develop your own card set? Come ask Mark and Mike how brought *Scars of Mirrodin* to life.


**Who:**



> Mark Rosewater (*Scars of Mirrodin* Lead Designer) and Mike Turian (*Scars of Mirrodin* Lead Developer)


**When:**



> Wednesday, October 27, 4:00 PM PDT (7:00 PM EDT)


**How:**



> There are three ways to join the chat.   
>   
> * via widget: <http://bit.ly/9LZ1CN> (Should take you directly to a login screen)
> * via mibbit: [http://mibbit.com/chat](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coveritlive-chat-session-day-one-2012-10-24-0) (Takes you to a screen where you type in your desired username and your channel name (the channel will be #r&dchat )
> * via IRC: If you have an IRC client, you can access it by going to the mibbit IRC and then the channel (#r&dchat).
> 


**NOTE:** the channel is **#r&dchat**, not #mtgdevchat.


See you there!
