It's Shoe Time!
===============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 3, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











### Show your colors. The ultimate design battle has begun.

Ever wanted to see your **Magic** themed artwork on shirts, hats, even SHOES? **Magic: The Gathering** has partnered with RYZ to make that happen. We bring you the "Show Your Colors" competition.

**Its go time!** All you artists and designers with a passion for **Magic** can now submit your best **Magic**-themed work and maybe YOU will [RYZ to the top](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/feature-designing-coverage-2008-05-24)! The winner receives design immortality ... as well as $1000 and an uncut foil sheet of **Magic** cards.

Not an artist or designer but skilled with words? Voice your thoughts by critiquing the submissions and chatting it up [in the forum](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/feature-designing-coverage-2008-05-24).

**DESIGN** – June 3rd-June 17th: Artists/designers submit their best work to be critiqued by the community.

**VOTE**  – June 17th: The submissions get narrowed down and move into the voting cycle. Its up to you, the community to determine the winner and the look for next line of **Magic: The Gathering** gear.

**CELEBRATE** – July 1st: Voting closes and a winner is chosen.

**ON SALE** – September 6th: You have decided and we deliver. Product goes on sale on [RYZwear.com](http://RYZwear.com).
