2003 Japan National Championships
=================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 19, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Each of the following decks was used at a Japan Nationals Open Qualifier on June 18, 2003. With the exception of a Sealed Qualifier (Tounament #10), each of the eleven tournaments was a 64-person single elimination event, with the top two players earning an invitation to Nationals.
