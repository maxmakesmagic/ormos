The largest **Magic** tournament ever was Grand Prix–Madrid, featuring the Legacy format. It saw over 2,000 competitors show up to play. At the end of the first day of competition, one of those players who had managed to escape the Swiss undefeated was Nicola Landoni. The deck? [Survival of the Fittest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Survival+of+the+Fittest) Bant, which uses the old enchantment to fetch up a steady stream of creatures thanks to [Squee, Goblin Nabob](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squee%2C+Goblin+Nabob) recursion. To read more about Grand Prix–Madrid, check out the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/m%C3%BCller-magical-madrid).
