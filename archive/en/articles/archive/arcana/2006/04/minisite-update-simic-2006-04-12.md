 Minisite Update: Simic
=======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 12, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











You've already seen some [secrets of the Azorius](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/dissension/azorius) in the *Dissension* minisite. Now it's time to breach the bio-wombs of Novijen — and if you don't know what we're talking about, you will soon.

[![The Simic](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1055_simicscreen.jpg)](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/dissension/simic) The Blue-Green guild, the [**Simic Combine**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/dissension/simic), hits the *Dissension* minisite today. There you'll find:

* Simic guild lore and exclusive concept art from the style guide
* Simic short fiction by Rei Nakazawa
* Special Simic art preview with bonus wallpaper

Tune in again next Wednesday for the final update — the Cult of Rakdos.

[*Momir Vig is waiting.*
