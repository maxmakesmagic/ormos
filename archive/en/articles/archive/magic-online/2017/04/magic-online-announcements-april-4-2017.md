**Throwback Standard Gauntlet begins tomorrow, April 5**


The Throwback Standard Gauntlet is back on the schedule! Starting tomorrow, you'll be able to enjoy the Standard environment of *Mirage*-*Tempest* with preconstructed decks on *Magic Online*. For more information, [click here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/flashback-standard-gauntlets-mirage-tempest).

**April MOPR Promo cards**


The April MOPR period runs from March 29, 2017, through April 26, 2017. Here are the promo cards:




|
|  |
| Event Promo

[Oracle's Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle%27s+Vault) | Store Promo

[Servo Exhibition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Servo+Exhibition) |

Play in one or more events during the February MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium [Oracle's Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle%27s+Vault). Five or more and you'll get an additional premium [Oracle's Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle%27s+Vault). Spend at least $20 total in the *Magic Online* Store during this MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium [Servo Exhibition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Servo+Exhibition). By spending $40 or more, you'll get an additional premium [Servo Exhibition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Servo+Exhibition).

***Hour of Devastation* Modern Pro Tour Qualifier on Sunday, April 9**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, April 9!


Remember, the Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries now follow a set weekly schedule, thanks to the redesigned PTQ system that *Magic Online* Events Manager Lee Sharpe announced [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/pro-tour-qualifier-preliminary-schedule-changes).


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#HOU_schedule).


After Monday, April 10, Constructed Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries will revert to Standard format.
