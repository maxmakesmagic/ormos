Jund decks have been popular in Standard for some time. But Dev used this list to win a recent Premier Event on Magic Online. The format? Kaleidoscope, which requires all players use cards that are multi-colored. [Savage Twister](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savage+Twister) and [Terminate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terminate) keep the board cleared, while spells like [Anathemancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anathemancer) and Lavalanche punish opponents in the late game. As a backup? No less than [Broodmate Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodmate+Dragon) to sweep things up in the air!
