Playtest names
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 5, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












It's a good thing we have a naming and continuity department here at Wizards of the Coast, otherwise you'd be playing with cards named "Hot Damage" and "Fat Elf." R&D might be great at coming up with abilities and mechanics, but no one ever mistook them for poets or bards.


Here are four *Torment* cards as they would have looked if their original design/playtest names were kept. If you don't know what they really are, click on each card to find out…
