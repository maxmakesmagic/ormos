The God of Token Creatures
==========================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on July 29, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 






While the Gods in *Theros* block are at their most potent when they become indestructible creatures, many players forget that the Gods provide abilities not seen on other *Magic* cards. [Purphoros, God of the Forge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purphoros%2C+God+of+the+Forge) can become a potent attacker with enough devotion, but this deck capitalizes on the non-devotion aspects of the card. In fact, this deck is not capable of providing the requisite five devotion in order to make Purphoros a creature!


![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Purphoros,%20God%20of%20the%20Forge)
This deck takes advantage of [Purphoros, God of the Forge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purphoros%2C+God+of+the+Forge) by making lots of tokens and using Purphoros to supply extra damage with those tokens. While dealing 6 damage each time you activate [Elspeth, Sun's Champion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Sun%27s+Champion)'s +1 ability is nice, don't forget that Purphoros can likely give those creatures +1/+0 a few times.


This deck seems well positioned against the other top decks in Standard while maintaining a high power level. Built-in two-for-one cards like [Voice of Resurgence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence) and [Scion of Vitu-Ghazi](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scion+of+Vitu-Ghazi) seem like a great place to win the attrition game against Mono-Black Devotion. [Mizzium Mortars](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mizzium+Mortars), [Deicide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deicide), and [Mistcutter Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistcutter+Hydra) are some of the strongest cards in the format against Mono-Blue Devotion. Finally, the tag team of [Sylvan Caryatid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Caryatid) and [Courser of Kruphix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courser+of+Kruphix) will make life difficult for the low-to-the-ground aggressive decks.
