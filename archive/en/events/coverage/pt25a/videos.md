Pro Tour 25th Anniversary

Hotsauce Takes Top Honors
=========================




Allen Wu, Ben Hull, and Gregory Orange of Hotsauce Games cemented themselves in Pro Tour history, with their team winning $150,000 and the Pro Tour 25th Anniversary champion title!






Aug 2-5, 2018


Minneapolis Convention Center



Allen Wu, Ben Hull, and Gregory Orange
