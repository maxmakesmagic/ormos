Decks of the Week: 06/01/2012
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 1, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast

Find a [PTQ near you](/en/articles/archive/qualifiers-pro-tour-return-ravnica-seattle-2012-03-15) for a chance to join him in Seattle this October. To give you some ideas of what is being played, check out these Top 8 decklists from some of the recent PTQs (including those on **Magic Online**)!
