Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 28, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











Learn more about Grand Prix Kobe's Top 8 competitors—including who they are, why they chose their Modern deck, and more—by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles!




---


### Joe Soh


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpkob17/gpKobe2017_TOP8_8_soh_EN.jpg)


**Age:** 29


**Hometown:** Malaysia


**Occupation:** Self Employed


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Grand Prix Taipei 2014 Top 8, Grand Prix Kobe 2015 finalist, 2003 and 2009 Malaysia National Champion.


**What deck did you play this weekend and how do you feel about your choice?**


White-Black Eldrazi. I liked Eldrazi but I didn't like the Eldrazi decks in the format, so I brewed my own.


**What is your favorite card in Modern?**


[Wasteland Strangler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland+Strangler) and [Thought-Knot Seer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought-Knot+Seer)


**What was the most interesting memory this weekend?**


Turn 2 [Tidehollow Sculler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tidehollow+Sculler)s into turn 3 [Wasteland Strangler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland+Strangler) has surprised many people, as well as the maindeck [Relic of Progenitus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Relic+of+Progenitus).

### Park Bi-o


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpkob17/gpKobe2017_TOP8_4_park_EN.jpg)


**Age:** 34


**Hometown:** South Korea


**Occupation:** Engineer


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** WMCQ Finalist


**What deck did you play this weekend and how do you feel about your choice?**


Grixis Shadow. I like big and fast creatures plus discard spells to cripple my opponent. [Gurmag Angler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gurmag+Angler) and [Tasigur, the Golden Fang](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tasigur%2C+the+Golden+Fang) on turn 2 is very good.


**What is your favorite card in Modern?**


[Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)


**What was the most interesting memory this weekend?**


I had a very lucky event all week.

### Tomoya Tsubouchi


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpkob17/gpKobe2017_TOP8_1_tsubouchi.jpg)


**Age:** 30


**Hometown:** Aichi, Japan


**Occupation:** Self-employed


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Pro Tour Atlanta competitor, BMO Modern Top 4, Team Legacy champion


**What deck did you play this weekend and how do you feel about your choice?**


Ad Nauseam. I'm really happy with my result because I worked hard to tune this deck with my team, Enukiru. I'll continue playing Ad Nauseam because it is a great deck!


**What is your favorite card in Modern?**


[Pact of Negation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pact+of+Negation)


**What was the most interesting memory this weekend?**


I need to learn how to play [Spoils of the Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spoils+of+the+Vault) better and I'm glad to not play any mirror matches.
