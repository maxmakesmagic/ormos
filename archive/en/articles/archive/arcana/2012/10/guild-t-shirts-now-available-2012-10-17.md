Guild T-Shirts Now Available
============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 17, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











T-shirts! Get your T-shirts!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1083_izzet_tee.jpg)  
The presale is over and you can now order **Magic** T-shirts at [MTGMerch.com](http://www.mtgmerch.com/). There are shirts for all ten guilds, plus a shirt with the Planeswalker symbol, a shirt with the **Magic** logo, and a shirt with Jace.

And your feedback has been heard! There are now women's sizes! And the men's shirts go up to 3XL!

Finally, here's a quote from the FAQ that you might find interesting, especially if you're tired of things that are only available in the United States.
