It's Phyrexia Week! In order to pay homage to our new overlords, we present to you your Daily Deck: the Phyrexians list from *Duel Decks: Phyrexia vs. the Coalition*. That's right, Mirrodin is not the first plane the Phyrexians have invaded. Back in *Invasion* block, the Phyrexians were up to no good in an attempt to overpower the plane of Dominaria. The latest Duel Deck, *Knights vs. Dragons*, is now for sale!
