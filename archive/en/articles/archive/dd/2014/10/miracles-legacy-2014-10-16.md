### DAILY DECK


Miracles in Legacy
==================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/shuhei_authorpic.jpg)

Shuhei Nakamura

Shuhei Nakamura is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, former Player of the Year, and is one of just six players with more than 400 lifetime pro points.


October 16, 2014
 










There is another card with delve that is likely to shake things up in Legacy: [Dig Through Time](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dig+Through+Time). Unlike [Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise), the cost can't be reduced to lower than UU, but its library sifting ability—choosing the best two out of the top seven—is nearly unparalleled. It basically lets you put whatever card in your deck you want into your hand. And, also unlike [Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise), it's an instant, meaning you can wait to use it until after you've seen what your opponent is planning and pick the perfect response. Rarely have control players been given such a powerful draw spell to add to their quiver.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Dig Through Time)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dig Through Time)
When I mentioned to Yūta Takahashi that I was looking for good Legacy decks for this week's series, he sent me this list along with the following comment:


"This goes in the slot that [Snapcaster Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snapcaster+Mage) used to hold. It's the same in that it's a little heavy but gives a lot of advantage, and just like [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) it lets you fire off [Terminus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terminus) or [Entreat the Angels](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Entreat+the+Angels) immediately, and it helps you put together your Top/[Counterbalance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterbalance) combo that much faster as well."
