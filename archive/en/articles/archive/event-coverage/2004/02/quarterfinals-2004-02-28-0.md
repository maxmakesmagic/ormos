Quarterfinals:
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 28, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter












Raffaele Lo Moro vs. Masashiro Kuroda
-------------------------------------


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptkob04/qf2_1.jpg)For Masashiro Kuroda, this is a day of redemption. In the past, he was notorious for going undefeated on day one of a major tournament, but failing to make the Top 8. He did it at Japanese Nationals, Grand Prix Sapporo, and Grand Prix Kyoto. With a bad start yesterday it seemed that the trend was destined to continue, but Kuroda came back to form and is now the only Japanese player in the Top 8. Recently married, Kuroda had his wife cheering him on from the stands along with the local Japanese players.


Kuroda and his opponent, Italy's Raffaele Lo Moro, are running versions of Big Red. The difference maker here may be the fact that Lo Moro's deck is packing a lot more artifact destruction, while Kuroda has more burn. That may make a difference where the metagame is concerned and you want to be able to destroy Ravagers and [Skullclamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp)s, but in a red-on-red matchup, the efficient, massive damage spells tend to be what wins games.


### Game 1


Kuroda took a mulligan, and strangely Lo Moro declared a mulligan of his own as Kuroda started shuffling. Occasionally people will not wait until their opponents declare all their mulligans before taking one of their own in the interest of saving time, but it was odd to see someone do it in the Top 8 of a Pro Tour where you'd theoretically want every advantage you can get.


No action happened until turn four, when Lo Moro played [Solemn Simulacrum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solemn+Simulacrum). Kuroda brought out an [Arc-Slogger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arc-Slogger), and Lo Moro answered back with one of his own. Kuroda's Slogger took out Lo Moro's, with the help of [Barbed Lightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbed+Lightning). Another shot killed the Simulacrum, clearing the path for an attack for four. Lo Moro played another Simulacrum, and Kuroda targeted it with [Detonate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detonate), but Lo Moro responded with [Electrostatic Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrostatic+Bolt), which cost him a card but prevented two damage. This let the Slogger get through for damage again, but it got Flinged at Kuroda on the following turn thanks to Lo Moro's Grab the Reigns. Lo Moro played an [Arc-Slogger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arc-Slogger) of his own soon after, but Kuroda's early damage made it insignificant. He cast [Pulse of the Forge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulse+of+the+Forge) on end step, then used [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) to deal the final points of damage.


**Lo Moro- 0 Kuroda- 1**


### Game 2


Lo Moro kept his opening draw this time, but Kuroda didn't. Lo Moro's Talisman was destroyed with [Echoing Ruin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Echoing+Ruin), and both players missed their fourth land drop. Turns went back and forth with each player hoping to draw land- Lo Moro found one first, playing a Simulacrum. It died to an [Electrostatic Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrostatic+Bolt), and Kuroda still only had three mana sources. A second Simulacrum was killed by Kuroda's [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball), and Lo Moro played a third, along with [Pyrite Spellbomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyrite+Spellbomb). Kuroda finally found a land, and played a Simulacrum of his own, but had a lot of catching up to do if he was going to put up a fight this game. [Detonate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detonate) killed his Simulacrum and Lo Moro's attacked, bringing Kuroda down to fourteen.


[Molten Rain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Rain) destroyed one of Lo Moro's [Stalking Stones](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stalking+Stones), and a Bolt killed the Simulacrum. Lo Moro activated his remaining [Stalking Stones](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stalking+Stones) on end step, and during his attack, Kuroda targeted it with [Barbed Lightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbed+Lightning) with entwine. In response, Lo Moro cast Grab the Reigns to throw it at Kuroda. Massive [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball)s were thrown back and forth, but Kuroda was able to squeak out the win by casting a second [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball), along with [Shrapnel Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shrapnel+Blast).


**Lo Moro- 0 Kuroda- 2**


### Game 3


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptkob04/qf2_2.jpg)Lo Moro summoned a Simulacrum, and Kuroda killed it with a Bolt. He untapped, activated two [Blinkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blinkmoth+Nexus)es, and attacked to deal two damage. Lo Moro summoned an [Arc-Slogger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arc-Slogger), and it got through for damage on the next turn. He then played another Simulacrum, and Kuroda sent [Barbed Lightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbed+Lightning) to the dome. A [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) killed the Slogger, and Lo Moro responded by shooting Kuroda twice. The Simulacrum dealt two damage, and Kuroda killed it with a Bolt on his turn before destroying a Talisman with [Echoing Ruin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Echoing+Ruin). Lo Moro's [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) for seven brought Kuroda down to three, and on end step [Pulse of the Forge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulse+of+the+Forge) brought Lo Moro to ten. With two [Darksteel Citadel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Citadel)s, two [Blinkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blinkmoth+Nexus)es and two mountains, he couldn't maximize efficiency off the Pulse. He attacked with one Nexus, and cast the Pulse again. This brought Lo Moro within [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) range, giving Kuroda the match.


**Lo Moro- 0 Kuroda- 3**
