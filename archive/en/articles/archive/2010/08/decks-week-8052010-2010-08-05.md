Decks of the Week: 8/05/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on August 5, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












It was a weekend of Legacy action across the live and digital **Magic** tournament scene. First at [Grand Prix–Columbus](/en/events/coverage/saitos-legacy-continues), **Magic** superstar Tomoharu Saito took home the trophy with a Merfolk deck, defeating Tom Martell and his CounterTop deck in the finals. Over in the world of **Magic Online**, the Season Seven Championship in MOCS was won by Archer. with Ad Nauseam/Tendrils. If Standard's more your scene, check out the Top 8 lists from Italy Nationals featuring multiple archetypes.
