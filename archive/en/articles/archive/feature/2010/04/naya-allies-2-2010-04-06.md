Naya Allies is continuing to do well in Standard! This week, Toolundertow used a version to go 3-1 in a large event on **Magic Online**. The powerful teamwork ability of the Allies work together to create gigantic [Hada Freeblade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hada+Freeblade)s and [Kazandu Blademaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kazandu+Blademaster)s, gain tons of life with [Ondu Cleric](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ondu+Cleric), become unblockable with protection from [Kabira Evangel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kabira+Evangel), and more. It's a fun breeze of fresh air in Standard, perfect for your upcoming Friday Night **Magic** event!
