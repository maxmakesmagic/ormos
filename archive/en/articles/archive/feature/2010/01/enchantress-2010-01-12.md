Enchantress decks, historically centered around cards like [Verduran Enchantress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verduran+Enchantress), [Argothian Enchantress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Argothian+Enchantress), and [Enchantress's Presence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enchantress%27s+Presence), have been around **Magic** for quite some time. Their popularity has waxed and waned over the year as different metagames have come and gone, but Lou Christopher recently reintroduced the land of Legacy to the concept. He took first place at a [StarCityGames.com Legacy Open event](http://www.starcitygames.com/php/news/article/18542.html) playing this innovative take on the old strategy!
