Day 1 Undefeated Decklists
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 23, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











After nine rounds of *Amonkhet*/*Hour of Devastation* Sealed Deck, we reduced the field of 2,398 players down to just 752! Among them, nine esteemed players achieved the perfect 9-0 record.


* [![Nicol Bolas, God-Pharaoh](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+God-Pharaoh)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+God-Pharaoh)
* [![The Locust God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=The+Locust+God)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Locust+God)
* [![The Scorpion God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=The+Scorpion+God)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scorpion+God)
* [![The Scarab God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=The+Scarab+God)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God)
* [![God-Pharaoh's Gift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=God-Pharaoh%27s+Gift)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=God-Pharaoh%27s+Gift)
* [![Samut, Voice of Dissent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Samut%2C+Voice+of+Dissent)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Samut%2C+Voice+of+Dissent)
* [![Samut, the Tested](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Samut%2C+the+Tested)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Samut%2C+the+Tested)

Interestingly, [Nicol Bolas, God-Pharaoh](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+God-Pharaoh), [The Locust God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Locust+God), [The Scorpion God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scorpion+God), and [God-Pharaoh's Gift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=God-Pharaoh%27s+Gift) all made appearances, while one player even had both [Samut, Voice of Dissent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Samut%2C+Voice+of+Dissent) and [Samut, the Tested](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Samut%2C+the+Tested) in the same deck! Isn't this amazing?
