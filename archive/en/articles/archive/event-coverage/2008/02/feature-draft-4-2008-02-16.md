Feature: Draft #4
=================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 16, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim












Everyone is fascinated by a returning legend. Hall of Famer and two-time PT Champion and Nicolai Herzog is here to shatter dreams once again.


In the fourth draft of the event, Nicolai started out strong (well, perhaps too strong) with a booster which contained [Imperious Perfect](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imperious+Perfect), [Mulldrifter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter) and a much-feared [Shriekmaw](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shriekmaw), which he was only too happy to pick. He was then passed [Sower of Temptation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sower+of+Temptation) as a second pick. There aren't so many uncommons you'd take over Control Magic, and Nicolai mentioned later that his neighbor may well have ripped a [Shriekmaw](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shriekmaw) too.

![Shriekmaw](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Shriekmaw)

The next pack served up [Hostility](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hostility), [Fodder Launch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fodder+Launch) and [Moonglove Extract](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moonglove+Extract) and he went with the unexciting but always dependable "Seal of Fire." He then took a [Wizened Cenn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wizened+Cenn) over the [Cloudthresher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudthresher) (having already passed [Imperious Perfect](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imperious+Perfect)), hoping to keep his options open.


He shook his head as he passed [Seedguide Ash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seedguide+Ash) and [Doran, the Siege Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower), but things were getting out of hand. Blue was clearly starting to dry up and Nicolai practically received nothing of note in the rest of pack 1. Eighth-pick [Changeling Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Changeling+Titan) and tenth-pick [Fertile Ground](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fertile+Ground) nudged Nicolai into green midpack.


[Marsh Flitter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Marsh+Flitter) joined the pile of cards as the first pick of pack 2. The next three picks were pretty much no-brainers, and he snapped up [Briarhorn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Briarhorn), [Wydwen, the Biting Gale](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wydwen%2C+the+Biting+Gale), and [Final Revels](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Final+Revels) in succession. [Rootgrapple](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootgrapple) offered Nicolai a way to deal with any pesky planeswalkers if he ever encountered any in his endeavors.


At this point, Herzog seemed like he was solidified in black and green with a splash of blue.


*Morningtide* provided some much-needed removal in the form of [Pack's Disdain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack%27s+Disdain) as a first pick and [Violet Pall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violet+Pall) as a second. [Wolf-Skull Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolf-Skull+Shaman) was picked third, but Nicolai didn't have many comrades to generate tokens with. He was passed yet another [Wolf-Skull Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolf-Skull+Shaman), but he swiftly scooped up the aptly named [Unstoppable Ash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unstoppable+Ash). [Orchard Warden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Orchard+Warden), [Ambassador Oak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ambassador+Oak) and a pair of [Festercreep](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Festercreep)s rounded up his draft.
