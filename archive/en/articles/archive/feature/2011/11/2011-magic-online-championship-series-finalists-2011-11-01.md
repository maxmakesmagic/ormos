### 2011 Magic Online Championship Details

*START TIME:* The 2011 **Magic Online** Championship begins Thursday, November 17 and takes place over 4 days at the 2011 **Magic: The Gathering** World Championships. Events will be scheduled so registered players can compete in both events.  
*LOCATION:* Live at the **Magic** World Championships in San Francisco.  
*ENTRY FEE:* None â€“ Invitation Only.  
*FORMAT:* The 2011 **Magic Online** Championship features several formats over four days:  


* Thursday, November 17: Standard Constructed, 4 rounds, Swiss pairings
* Friday, November 18: *Innistrad* Booster Draft, 3 rounds, Swiss pairings
* Saturday, November 19: Modern Constructed, 4 rounds, Swiss pairings
* Sunday, November 20: Modern Constructed Championship Finals, 1 round, single elimination

*SIZE:* 12 Players.  
*SCORING:* Each of the first three events will award championship points based on the number of match wins as follows:   


|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| # of Match Wins | # of Championship Points |
| 1 | 4 |
| 2 | 10 |
| 3 | 17 |
| 4 | 25 |

  
Each player's earned championship points will be totaled after the first 3 events. The top 2 players will advance to the Championship Finals on Sunday, while the remaining players' standings will be based on the number of championship points earned. DCI tie-breakers will be aggregated across all three tournaments and used if needed.   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on final standings at the end of the tournament:  




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Place | Prize |
| 1st | $25,000 USD, 1 complete premium foil digital set (players choice), and 1 complete standard digital set (players choice) |
| 2nd | $17,000 USD , 1 complete premium foil digital set (players choice), and 1 complete standard digital set (players choice) |
| 3rd - 4th | $9,000 USD and 1 complete standard digital set (players choice) |
| 5th - 8th | $6,000 USD and 1 complete standard digital set (players choice) |
| 9th - 12th | $4,000 USD and 1 complete standard digital set (players choice) |

  
Participants must play on the **Magic Online** account that they used to qualify for the 2011 **Magic Online** and supply their own decks for the constructed portions of the event. Product will be provided for the *Innistrad* draft. Players must play in the 2011 MOCS Championship to be eligible for the prizes

Good luck to all the **Magic Online** Championship finalists and see you in California!
