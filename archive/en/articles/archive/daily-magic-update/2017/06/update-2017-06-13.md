Deck of the Day
===============


1. Temur Engery

It's your time to shine, Temur Energy! The shell that was formerly used to house [Saheeli Rai](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Saheeli+Rai)/[Felidar Guardian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Felidar+Guardian) first and then [Aetherworks Marvel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel)/[Ulamog, the Ceaseless Hunger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ulamog%2C+the+Ceaseless+Hunger) has had both combos removed from it and—it's still pretty darn good. This is a 5-0 list we used as an example [in today's B&R announcement](http://edit.magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/june-13-2017-banned-and-restricted-announcement-2017-06-13). And the deck continues to put up results. Watch for many Marvel players to transition to something like this in the coming weeks (though there are no major Standard event prior to Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation*).
