Luca Ravagli is the 2009 Italian National Champion! Like American champ Charles Gindy, Luca opted to battle with Cruel Control. ***Magic** 2010* cards like [Great Sable Stag](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Great+Sable+Stag) have helped push the deck over the top. Want to read more about the event? Just check out the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/ravagli-ravages-italy)!
