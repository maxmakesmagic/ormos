On the Mirrodin Site: November 13-19
====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 19, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Welcome back to "On the Mirrodin Site," where we show off some of the recent highlights from the [Mirrodin Site](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/).


On the Phyrexia side, we've got more art for cards that don't exist. We might as well tell you that these are for cards for *Mirrodin Besieged*. This one is [Phyrexian Rebirth](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/#plains,hotspots;W8;0;).


On the Mirran side, Kessla of Temple Might has a [new field report](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/#!blog,655). And there's a wallpaper for the *Scars of Mirrodin* version of [Mindslaver](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/#mountains,hotspots;R6;0;)!
