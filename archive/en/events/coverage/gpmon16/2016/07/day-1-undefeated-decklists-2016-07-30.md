Day 1 Undefeated Decklists
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 31, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett











Nine rounds of competition took their toll on 1,300 players, and at the end only four stood with perfect 9-0 records. Below you’ll find their decks, including the complete pool from which they were built. It’s early days in Eldritch Moon / Shadows Over Innistrad sealed deck, and these four can be your study guide for upcoming builds. See if you would have come to the same conclusions. Ask yourself why certain cards made the cut and others did not. Could these builds be improved upon?
