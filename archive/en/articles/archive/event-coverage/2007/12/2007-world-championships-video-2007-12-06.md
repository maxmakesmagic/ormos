2007 World Championships Video
==============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 6, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations to Uri Peleg and the Switzerland National Team, your [2007 **Magic**  World Champions](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/2007wc). Peleg defeated Patrick Chapin 3-1 in the Individual Final with a green-black Standard deck armed heavy-hitters such as [Doran, the Siege Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower), [Garruk Wildspeaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Garruk+Wildspeaker), and [Liliana Vess](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+Vess), negating Chapin's explosive re-tooled Dragonstorm deck featuring [Spinerock Knoll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spinerock+Knoll). The Swiss took down Austria in two games of the Two-Headed Giant World Team Final.


If you want insight on how to play these decks from the best players in the world, or just want to watch some of the best **Magic** of the year, dive in to the full webcast archives linked below.
