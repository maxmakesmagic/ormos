Changes to Season 2 MOCS Invitations Announced
----------------------------------------------


As part of our [Digital Premier Play announcement](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/digital-premier-play-path-2022-04-21) on Thursday, the following changes have been made to MOCS Season 2:


* Starting with Season 2, both the winner and runner-up of the MOCS Showcase will earn invitations to the World Championship in 2023!
	+ The winner of MOCS Showcase Season 1 in June will instead earn an invitation to the first Pro Tour of 2023.
* Qualifiers and Super Qualifiers will now feed into the Regional Championships. These events are slated to start up again in July when tabletop's qualifying season begins.
* MOCS Showcase Qualifiers and the MOCS Leaderboard will give invitations to the Pro Tour.
* MOCS Showcase Opens will give invitations only to the winner starting with Season 2 *(previously winner and runner-up)*.

Our next Premier Events will be Showcase Challenges on May 7–8. Full details are available at the links at the top of this blog.

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. [Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place.


*NOTES:* 


* *Vintage and Standard Challenges are never pre-empted.*
* *Saturday Legacy Challenges require 32 players and give prizes to Top 32.*
* *Sunday Legacy Challenges require 64 players and give prizes to Top 64.*
