*Shards of Alara* Block Constructed is firing events around the clock on **Magic Online**. Here's a look at a recent Premier Event Top 8 deck featuring the best the plane of Esper has to offer. [Sanctum Gargoyle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sanctum+Gargoyle) combos with both [Courier's Capsule](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Courier%2527s%2BCapsule) and [Executioner's Capsules](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Executioner%27s+Capsules). [Master of Etherium](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Etherium) pumps the team and grows large itself. [Oblivion Ring](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Ring) and [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) take care of opposing creatures; the deck is truly an artifact work of wonder!
