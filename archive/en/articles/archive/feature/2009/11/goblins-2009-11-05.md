*Zendikar* Game Day has come and gone for 2009. Tim Green of [Carrollton, Georgia](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/zendikar-game-day-quest-comic-shop-carrollton-georgia-2009-11-03) took an innovative Standard build of Goblins all the way to the top 4. The deck featured powerful Goblins like [Warren Instigator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warren+Instigator) as well as [Goblin Razerunners](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Razerunners) and [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander). A tiny splash of white allows for [Intimidation Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intimidation+Bolt) and [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) to deal with opponent's blockers. You can see many more Standard deck lists from Game Day [here](/en/events/coverage/zendikar-game-day).
