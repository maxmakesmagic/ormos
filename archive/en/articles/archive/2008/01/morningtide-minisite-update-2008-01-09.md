*Morningtide* Minisite Update
=============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 9, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












It's time for a [*Morningtide* Minisite](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/morningtide/home) update!


Continue to explore *Morningtide* with the new "Seeds of a Plan" piece, which tells the story of Colfenor and Rhys. Got a hankering for some puzzles? We've got both a jigsaw-style puzzle (with a wallpaper reward) and an all-new acrostic by Mark Gottlieb! And of course, the card previews are updated every day!


Stop on by, won't you?
