Decks of the Week: 02/24/2012
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 24, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Multiple formats are finding time under the spotlight this month. Two Grand Prix highlighted the schedule, as a *Dark Ascension/Innistrad* Sealed Deck/Booster Draft event in Kobe was won by Masahiro Hiraga thanks to [Sudden Disappearance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sudden+Disappearance), while Bronson Magnan and his Aggro Loam build took home the trophy at the Modern event in Lincoln. If you're looking for deck variety, six different archetypes were represented in Lincoln's Top 8. Plus, there are seven Modern PTQs to peruse for deck ideas to take to your next Pro Tour Qualifier!
