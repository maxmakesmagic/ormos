TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 5, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/walkinshaw.jpg)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw











After many rounds of Swiss and some great games of *Magic*, the Top 8 for Melbourne is set! Learn more about this event’s Top 8 competitors, their history at the top tables, and more by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles.




---

### David Mines


**Hometown:** Brisbane


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Harm Reduction Counsellor


**What deck did you play?** W/U Eldrazi


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** This Top 8, Top 50 Pro Tour Magic 2015.


**Which *Shadows over Innistrad* preview card is your favourite?** Archangel Avacyn.




---

### Maitland Cameron


**Hometown:** Melbourne


**Age:** 26


**Occupation:** Asset Management Consultant


**What deck did you play?** W/U Eldrazi (the bad guys)


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** 4th GP Sydney 2014, 2nd GP Auckland 2015.


**Which *Shadows over Innistrad* preview card is your favourite?** Archangel Avacyn looks fantastic to play against in draft.




---

### Yuuki Ichikawa


**Hometown:** Japan


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Pro player / Team Cygames


**What deck did you play?** [Living End](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End)


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** Pro Tour Journey into Nyx Top 4.


**Which *Shadows over Innistrad* preview card is your favourite?** Archangel Avacyn.




---

### Louis Thomson-Gregg


**Hometown:** Wellington, New Zealand


**Age:** 22


**Occupation:** McSlave/Student


**What deck did you play?** R/G Eldrazi


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** Day 2 Pro Tour Magic 2015 and this Top 8.


**Which *Shadows over Innistrad* preview card is your favourite?** Haven’t had time to look.




---

### Chris Cousens


**Hometown:** Melvegas (Melbourne)


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Student


**What deck did you play?** Abzan Superfriends (Abzan Company) featuring Anafenza, Melira, and Kataki!


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when**? Winning Game Day for Khans of Tarkir. Number 1, baby!


**Which *Shadows over Innistrad* preview card is your favourite?** The one with Maitland’s face!




---

### Kentaro Yamamoto


**Hometown:** Japan


**Age:** 31


**Occupation:** Team Cygames


**What deck did you play?** [Living End](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End)


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** 2nd place Pro Tour San Diego 2007


**Which *Shadows over Innistrad* preview card is your favourite?** Archangel Avacyn.




---

### Lee Shi Tian


**Hometown:** Hong Kong


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Accountant


**What deck did you play?** [Living End](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End)


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** Top 4 Pro Tour Return to Ravnica playing [Scapeshift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scapeshift).


**Which *Shadows over Innistrad* preview card is your favourite?** Relentless Dead. The zombies in [Endless Ranks of the Dead](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Endless+Ranks+of+the+Dead) finally breaking through the window! Big Walking Dead fans. Btw, can I say happy birthday to your friend?




---

### Jason Chung


**Hometown:** Auckland, New Zealand, and on twitter @sqlut


**Age:** 23


**Occupation:** Taylor Swift fanboy.


**What deck did you play?** Squee’s Revenge (originated/tuned from Raph Levy’s Loam deck)


**What's your best Magic event finish and where/when?** Carried Phoenix Taku in the WMC to a Top 16 finish.


**Which *Shadows over Innistrad* preview card is your favourite?** The one that flips into Chester Swords.
