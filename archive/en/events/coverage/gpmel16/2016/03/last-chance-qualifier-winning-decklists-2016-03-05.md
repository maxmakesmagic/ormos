Last Chance Qualifier Winning Decklists
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 4, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/walkinshaw.jpg)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw











As with every Grand Prix, players flocked to the venue on Friday to play in the Last Chance Qualifiers. Often affectionately referred to as “The Grinders”, and not to be confused with the Stitcher’s Lab Escape the Room event happening at the same time, players will often enter one after the other all day to see if they could win themselves a pair of byes in the Main Event.


There was one limited Grinder, which was won by Wu Hao, but the rest were Modern, and we figured you’d like to see their decklists:
