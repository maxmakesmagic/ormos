**TOURNAMENT**  
 The main tournament at Grand Prix-Reims is open to **Magic** players of all ages, and will award invitations to Pro Tour - Houston to the top 8 players in the tournament. In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher in that Grand Prix. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).

**Amateur Status**  
 A player is considered to have amateur status if he or she has 0 lifetime pro points. If a player has ever been awarded pro points from a Grand Prix, Pro Tour, or World Championship event, that player no longer has amateur status and is not eligible for amateur awards at Grand Prix or other tournaments.

**Tournament Format**  
 The format for Grand Prix - Reims is Extended. All DCI Extended rules and **Magic** Floor Rules will be enforced. 

**Tournament Structure**  
 The tournament will be Modified Swiss style, with 50-minute rounds. The number of rounds will be determined by the number of participants in the tournament.

**PRIZE PAYOUT**  
Grand Prix-Reims will feature the standard "main tournament" prize schedule as well as a special "Amateur" prize schedule. The Amateur prizes will be paid to the highest-finishing amateur players in addition to any winnings those players receive from the main tournament prize schedule . All prizes are in U.S. Dollars.
