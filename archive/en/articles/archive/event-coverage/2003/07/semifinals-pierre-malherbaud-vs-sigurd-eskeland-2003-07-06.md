Semifinals: Pierre Malherbaud vs. Sigurd Eskeland
=================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 6, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_585.png)
By Roger Dane











### Game 1

Malherbaud won the toss and decided to play but a brief pause was caused by Eskeland who asked to change sides of the table so that he could draw from his right hand side. A sacland followed by [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) from the Frenchman showed four creatures, two lands and two spells from which he took [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/euro03/a840.jpg)Turn two [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) from Eskeland was followed by a [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive) for [Anger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anger), [Arcanis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcanis) and [Phantom Nishoba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nishoba) by Malherbaud. After a [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) attack a second dog hit the table but no land was forthcoming. Malherbaud did nothing but lay a land so Eskeland continued the early beatdown taking the Frenchman to thirteen but still couldn't find a third land.

[Doomed Necromancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomed+Necromancer) entered play hasted by the [Anger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anger) in the graveyard so it immediately sacrificed itself to produce a hasted [Phantom Nishoba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nishoba) which managed a fourteen point life swing when it attacked. The life totals were now 13 – 20 in the Frenchman's favor.

Eskeland attacked with one [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) to keep his opponent's life falling. Malherbaud casts [Sickening Dreams](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sickening+Dreams) for three and Eskeland saved both of his creatures by discarding but a [Stitch Together](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stitch+Together) for [Akroma, Angel of Wrath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroma%2C+Angel+of+Wrath) was too much and Eskeland scooped.

Malherbaud 1 – Eskeland 0

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/euro03/a842.jpg)Both players sideboarded and Eskeland decided to play first. Both players kept their opening hands and Eskeland went for the aggro [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) start. Malherbaud duplicated his Game 1 start with [Bloodstained Mire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodstained+Mire) sacrificed for a [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) which in turn was used to cast a [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) taking [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation). Turn two and Malherbaud decided to [Burning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish) for [Zombify](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombify). Eskeland got a [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost) into play but [Last Rites](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Last+Rites) to stock the Graveyard plus the [Zombify](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombify) put Akroma into play and a couple of hits ended the game.

Malherbaud 2 – Eskeland 0

Game 3 

Once again Eskeland decides to play and both keep. Eskeland can only manage an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) turn one but for the third time Malherbaud manages his [Bloodstained Mire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodstained+Mire), [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) trick which raised a round of applause from Eskeland. [Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) and Rootwalla followed while Malherbaud sacced another Mire to get another [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp), cycled a [Barren Moor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barren+Moor) and [Entomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Entomb)ed an Akroma. Eskeland could only attack with a pumped Rootwalla on turn three but Malherbaud cast [Last Rites](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Last+Rites) disposing of [Unsummon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsummon), [Turbulent Dreams](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turbulent+Dreams) and [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell).

Malherbaud was on fourteen life but missed a land drop and had no play. Summoning a [Doomed Necromancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomed+Necromancer) was attempted by the Frenchman but Eskeland used a now active [Merfolk Looter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter) to gain card advantage while saying no to the dangerous black creature with [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic).

Eskeland has attacked Malherbaud to four life keeping in the game using [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost) card advantage combined with Malherbaud mana screw and when the Frenchman starts to cast [Stitch Together](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stitch+Together) on the next turn he realizes that it will make no difference and scoops.

Malherbaud 2 – Eskeland 1

### Game 4

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/euro03/a841.jpg)Malherbaud decided to play first and both players kept their opening hands. After another turn one [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) the players discuss the odds of doing it eight times in a row as the Frenchman had managed so far in the top eight. Malherbaud removed a [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) then after only a land from Eskeland casts [Entomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Entomb) for Akroma on turn two.

Eskeland cast [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation) for [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm), [Krosan Reclamation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Reclamation) and [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Malherbaud summoned a [Doomed Necromancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomed+Necromancer) and passed and then Eskeland flashed back a [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Malherbaud cast a second [Necro](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necro)mancer and attacks with the first. During the end of turn step Eskeland falls to fourteen life by using his [City of Brass](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Brass) to cast [Krosan Reclamation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Reclamation) from the graveyard which returned Akroma to his opponents deck. He then cast [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) next turn to prevent any more cheap shots from Malherbaud's [Necro](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necro)mancers but a last Rites for three sent [Anger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anger) and [Arcanis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcanis) to the Frenchman's graveyard and a [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell), [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic) and a [Turbulent Dreams](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turbulent+Dreams) to the Norwegian's.

Malherbaud has been severely hampered by the fact that he is still on only three land but after a [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) token for Eskeland hit play the Frenchman finds a fourth land. With sufficient mana available Malherbaud cast [Buried Alive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive) stocking the graveyard, leaving one black mana open to reanimate Akroma with one of the [Necro](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necro)mancers barely pausing before he sent it in to attack for six.

Eskeland desperately cast [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) trying to find something to help but all to no avail and as he held out his hand in concession Malherbaud pumped the fist with elation.

**Final Result:** Malherbaud 3 – Eskeland 1
