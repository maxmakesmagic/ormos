Last week we brought you a [Birthing Pod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod) list by Hall of Fame member Olivier Ruel, who made Top 8 at the France National Championship with the archetype. We kick off this week with a second take on the deck, this time by Andrew Jacobs in Australia. Like Olivier's build, Andrew's deck focuses on a creature chain to power [Birthing Pod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod) looking for exactly the right creature for any given situation. Also like Ruel's, Andrew's take on the archetype proved powerful enough to power him to the Top 8! Read the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/nicoll-nails-it) of Australia Nationals for more.
