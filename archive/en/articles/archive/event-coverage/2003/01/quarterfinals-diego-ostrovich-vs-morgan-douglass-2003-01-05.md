Quarterfinals: Diego Ostrovich vs. Morgan Douglass
==================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 5, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_benbleiweiss.jpg)
By Ben Bleiweiss











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a931.jpg)Does it get anymore rogue than this? Morgan Douglass piloted [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker), the most dejected and discarded of potential extended archetypes, to a Top 8 finish. His first round opponent: Diego Ostrovich, whose current [Elves! deck](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=sideboard/gpno03/tech2) quietly slipped to some impressive finishes at PT Houston. Among the cards in their two decks are [Crumbling Sanctuary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crumbling+Sanctuary); [Predator, Flagship](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Predator%2C+Flagship); [Planar Portal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Planar+Portal); [Slate of Ancestry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slate+of+Ancestry); [Skyshroud Poacher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyshroud+Poacher); and [Fecundity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fecundity). If this extended season has proven anything so far, it's that the metagame can accommodate all different types of decks and builds. 

### Game 1

Diego won the die roll, and opened with a first turn [Birds of Paradise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise). Morgan answered with [Voltaic Key](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key), and Diego could only play a land and pass the turn, off to an uncharacteristically slow start. Douglass capitalized on this immediately by playing [Rishadan Port](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishadan+Port), further locking down Diego. The third turn saw no action on the Argentinean side of the board, so Douglas decided to run away with the game by dropping [Ancient Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb), using it to cast [Thran Dynamo](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thran+Dynamo), used the Dynamo to cast two [Voltaic Key](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key)s, untapped the Dynamo twice, and cast [Mishra's Helix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Helix).

Well, at least Diego got to drop [Skyshroud Poacher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyshroud+Poacher) the following turn before things went from bad to worse. Morgan untapped, [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker)ed the Helix for [Masticore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore), and wiped the board clear of creatures. Diego managed a pair of [Ravenous Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth)s, but only because Morgan was busy setting up a [Stroke of Genius](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stroke+of+Genius) for seven. The stroke yielded seven artifacts, including a quickly-played [Metalworker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker). Between the two keys and the [Metalworker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker), Douglass generated an obscene amount of mana, shot down the two Baloths plus a freshly summoned [Masticore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore) on the other side of the board, and still had dozens of mana to spare. Wisely, Diego conceded.

Morgan Douglass 1 – Diego Ostrovich 0

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a934.jpg)Elves! decided to pump out some namesake creatures early this time, with [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) and Rofellos jumping from hand to table on the first turns. [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) accelerated with [Ancient Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb) for a [Tangle Wire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire), which slowed the game down to snail's pace. This game though was all green and no brown, as Diego laid land after land to power up Rofellos, and started dropping [Ravenous Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth) and [Call of the Herd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd). In addition, he found the time to preemptively nab [Uktabi Orangutan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Uktabi+Orangutan) from the sideboard. 

With his life looming precariously low, Morgan stroked himself for seven again, but this time lacked any action cards. He attempted to [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) both [Crumbling Sanctuary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crumbling+Sanctuary) and [Masticore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore) onto the board, but the Orangutan monkeyed around with the damage preventer, and the horde came across through the [Masticore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore). An additional [Deranged Hermit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deranged+Hermit) joined the team, but Morgan couldn't find enough mana to shoot down Diego's board, succumbing to his horde.

Morgan Douglass 1 – Diego Ostrovich 1.

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a935.jpg)Sometimes, the game just doesn't work out for either player. Morgan started out with a mulligan, only to be one-upped by Diego's double mulligan. However, Morgan stalled on two lands until turn six, while Diego went turn four [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves), turn five [Deranged Hermit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deranged+Hermit), [Gaea's Cradle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gaea%27s+Cradle), [Masticore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore). Morgan failed to find the land he needed to do anything, and quickly succumbed.

**Final Result:** Diego Ostrovich 2 – Morgan Douglass 1
