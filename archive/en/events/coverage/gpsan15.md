TOP 8 COVERAGE



GP Santiago has been won by local planeswalker Rodrigo López. His aggressive Red/Green deck overcame Tomoharu Saito’s Blue-Black control deck in the Finals of the Top 8. In fact, the Top 8 included a total of six Chileans, demonstrated Santiago’s commitment to excellence in Magic. With that, GP Santiago comes to a close. ¡Nos vemos luego!

DAY 1 COVERAGE



799 Magic players entered the competition but only 81 have survived to make Day Two of GP Santiago. Leading the pack are two local players, Christian Flores and Jorge Guerra, both running Rakdos decks, both with Chandra, Fire of Kaladesh. Tomorrow the remaining 81 will test their skills at Origins Draft. But for now, these tired planeswalkers will put aside their mana until the mañana.
