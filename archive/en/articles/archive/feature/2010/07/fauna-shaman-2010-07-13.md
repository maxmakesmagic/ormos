Our second installment of R&D champion challenge decks starring ***Magic** 2011* and today's deck comes from former Pro Tour star and R&D member Zac Hill. Zac knows a thing or two about deck building, and one card that caught his attention in the new set was [Fauna Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman). The 2/2 is great for turning cards in hand into just the right creatures, and Zac had [Squadron Hawk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squadron+Hawk) to help provide him plenty of fodder for tutoring with the Shaman. He took on challengers in Atlanta, Georgia.
