Two great tastes that go great together. No, we're not talking peanut butter and chocolate, but instead two powerful strategies that happen to fit into the same deck: [Pyromancer Ascension](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer+Ascension) and [Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch) / [Splinter Twin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Splinter+Twin). The two combos strongly feature both blue and red cards, and Jason Hager included both in his Pyro Twin deck at the recent StarCityGames.com Open in Cincinnati. The decision was good enough to net him a Top 8 finish. Read [coverage of the event](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/22243_The_StarCityGamescom_Open_Series_is_Coming_to_Cincinnati_on_July_1617.html) at StarCitygames.com.
