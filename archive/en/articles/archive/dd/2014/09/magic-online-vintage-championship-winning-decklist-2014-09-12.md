### DAILY DECK


Magic Online Vintage Championship Winning Decklist
==================================================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)

Adam Prosak

From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer.


September 12, 2014
 










[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Mishra's Workshop)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra's Workshop)
Recently, the *Magic Online* Championships featured a championship for both *Vintage Masters* Limited and Vintage Constructed. David Whitfield wrote a recap of both events on the *Magic Online* homepage that [you can read here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online-vintage-championship-winners-2014-09-08).


The winner of the Vintage Championships was JUGEIA, who played a pure colorless deck fueled by [Mishra's Workshop](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Workshop). This deck focuses primarily on mana denial, trying to prevent the opponent from casting any cards at all! The dream start involves playing the restricted [Trinisphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinisphere) with [Mishra's Workshop](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Workshop) on the first turn, meaning your opponent will unlikely to be able to play cards for the first two turns. In addition to [Trinisphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinisphere), [Lodestone Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lodestone+Golem), [Sphere of Resistance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphere+of+Resistance), and [Thorn of Amethyst](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thorn+of+Amethyst) turn lightning-fast Vintage decks into much slower decks.


[Chalice of the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chalice+of+the+Void) and [Phyrexian Revoker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Revoker) help fight against otherwise crucial Moxes from the opponent. Finally, [Tangle Wire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire) and [Smokestack](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smokestack) help maintain the lock on opponents who want to cast threatening spells. If all goes well, the opponent will never cast spells while you win the game with creatures that are helping to establish the lock, like [Lodestone Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lodestone+Golem).


Congrats to JUGEIA for winning the *Magic Online* Vintage Championship!
