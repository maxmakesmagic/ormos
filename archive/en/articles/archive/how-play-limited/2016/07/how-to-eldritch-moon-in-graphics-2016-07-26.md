How to Eldritch Moon—in Graphics
================================



 Posted in **How to Play Limited**
 on July 26, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Diego-Fumagalli.jpg)
By Diego Fumagalli




 Diego is a visual designer and longtime Magic addict. He started from scratch creating infographics on VisualizingMagic.com, and he thinks a great game deserves a great communication tool! 






*Eldritch Moon* is now available and, most importantly, draftable! Diego Fumagalli has put together five examples of some of the cool, fun things you can do in *Eldritch Moon* Limited when the pieces come together.


*(Click on any graphic to view that section on its own.)*




---

Creatures with enters-the-battlefield abilities work wonders with [Displace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Displace). Try out these two to send your opponent scrambling.


[![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/images/daily/PL20160726_01.jpg)](http://media.wizards.com/2016/images/daily/PL20160726_01.jpg)




---

Emerge is an exciting new ability that lets you cast large, terrifying creatures for less mana than normal by sacrificing other creatures. Some creatures, though, actually *want* to be sacrificed.


[![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/images/daily/PL20160726_02.jpg)](http://media.wizards.com/2016/images/daily/PL20160726_02.jpg)




---

[Aberrant Researcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aberrant+Researcher) is a *Shadows over Innistrad* card that looked cool, but was kind of hard to transform. Apparently, we should have been looking in the archives.


[![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/images/daily/PL20160726_03.jpg)](http://media.wizards.com/2016/images/daily/PL20160726_03.jpg)




---

Humans need some help fighting against the horrors of Emrakul. Thankfully, there *is* help.


[![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/images/daily/PL20160726_04.jpg)](http://media.wizards.com/2016/images/daily/PL20160726_04.jpg)

Creating a Vampire army can be such tiring work. Maybe quench your thirst with this combo and deal a ton of damage to an unsuspecting opponent.
