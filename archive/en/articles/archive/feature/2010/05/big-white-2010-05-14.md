Who needs a fancy mana base? Not Skyler Stewart, who managed to make the Top 8 of the PTQ in Salt Lake City, Utah with this decidedly dual-less take on Mono-White. Not an aggressive strategy, Skyler is happy to stall the ground with cards like [Kor Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Cartographer), [Lone Missionary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lone+Missionary), [Pilgrim's Eye](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pilgrim%27s+Eye), and [Wall of Omens](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens) until the late game.   
 That's when [Martial Coup](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Martial+Coup); [Iona, Shield of Emeria](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iona%2C+Shield+of+Emeria); and [Emeria, the Sky Ruin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emeria%2C+the+Sky+Ruin) all take over to dominate. Find a Pro Tour Qualifier near you on the [Amsterdam Qualifiers page](/en/node/639906).
