Round 7 Feature Match:
======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 15, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett












Gerard Fabiano vs. Antoine Ruel
-------------------------------


![Antoine Ruel](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptsd04/fm16_1.jpg)Antoine Ruel is one half of France's Fabulous Ruel Brothers, two Pro Players who have been ripping up the Tour since their humble beginnings as two-thirds of Team Black Ops. Antoine is currently doing the family name proudest, since he's undefeated after two drafts. The slacking Olivier slouched into Day 2 with a 4-2 record, and will have to put the jets on if he wants to catch his brother.


Right now Ruel faces Gerard Fabiano, armed with his ubiquitous hand-towel in a bid to reach 7-0. A Team TOGIT player of some distinction, Fabiano first grabbed the public eye when he, Scott McCord and Jon Sonne (together, Slay-Pillage-Gerard) finished fourth at Pro Tour Boston 2002.


### Game 1


Ruel got on the table first with [Copper Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr), but Fabiano answered with a [Tel-Jilad Chosen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Chosen). Ruel didn't have a third land, and spent his turn making a [Darksteel Ingot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Ingot). Fabiano hit for two and dropped [Krark-Clan Grunt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krark-Clan+Grunt). [Electrostatic Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrostatic+Bolt) took care of the Chosen at end of turn.


Ruel untapped and played out a [Darksteel Pendant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Pendant), and then a criminally cheap [Somber Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard). Fabiano kept swinging and played out [Clockwork Condor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clockwork+Condor). Ruel got his third land after a [Thoughtcast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtcast) and played a [Neurok Spy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Neurok+Spy). Fabiano brought him to eleven attacking with both his men and played a mighty [Ogre Leadfoot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ogre+Leadfoot). Ruel even 'Oooh'ed to make him feel better.


He untapped and bashed for five. [Neurok Prodigy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Neurok+Prodigy) joined his team. Fabiano hit with his Ogre, bringing Ruel to eight, and he played a [Goblin Replica](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Replica). With the help of [Echoing Truth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Echoing+Truth) on the Condor, Ruel's unblockable squad evened the score. He counted his artifacts and played a [Rust Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rust+Elemental). Fabiano wasted no time targeting it with [Shatter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shatter). He untapped and started thinking. The path he chose was to play the Condor and swing with all his creatures.


Unfortunately for him, Ruel had [Shrapnel Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shrapnel+Blast) in hand, and with enough flyers to get past the Condor, the first game was his.


Ruel 1 - Fabiano 0


### Game 2


Fabiano started the carnage in Game 2 with [Goblin Replica](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Replica). Ruel made a [Neurok Spy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Neurok+Spy), but Fabiano went one better with the mighty [Juggernaut](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Juggernaut). Ruel had a [Somber Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard) ready, though, and the two traded when Fabiano failed to have a trick. He had no follow-up, and passed back on five mana. Ruel hit for two and made a [Spire Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spire+Golem).


Fabiano [Deconstruct](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deconstruct)ed and brought out a sizeable [Tangle Spider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Spider). Ruel thought he was safe behind a [Mirror Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Golem) imprinted with [Juggernaut](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Juggernaut), but Fabiano had [Shatter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shatter) and hit for more damage. That noise had to stop when Ruel came up with [Stalking Stones](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stalking+Stones). Fabiano wasn't about to trade his Golem for it with the help of Ruel's Myr.


For a few turns they both drew unhelpful cards. Fabiano made a Condor, but Ruel had [Barbed Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbed+Lightning). [Vedalken Engineer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vedalken+Engineer) for Ruel barely affected the board. Finally, Fabiano drew a breaker: [Loxodon Warhammer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Loxodon+Warhammer). He suited up his Spider and got in there. Ruel lost his Engineer and Stones, and the life totals stood seventeen to four. An [Echoing Ruin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Echoing+Ruin) put away the Warhammer, but the damage had been done. Fabiano played [Arcbound Worker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcbound+Worker), and the next turn checked life totals. He shrugged and spent his [Electrostatic Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrostatic+Bolt) to axe Ruel's 1/1 and allow his worker to dock him a point.


Ruel's next draw? [Spire Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spire+Golem). Fabiano was not pleased.


Fortunately, his deck was forgiving. It served up [Goblin Charbelcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Charbelcher) that took one more point right away and given time would put things away. After all, Ruel was only at two. Ruel hit with his Golem and played a [Somber Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard). Another Belcher activation, and it sputtered. Ruel made a [Cobalt Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cobalt+Golem) and dared dream he could win this one. Fabiano turned on the Belcher one more time. This time it hit two spells, and they were on to Game 3.


Ruel 1 - Fabiano 1


### Game 3


Fabiano had a first-turn [Bonesplitter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bonesplitter), but nothing to follow it, while Ruel busted out [Silver Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silver+Myr) and [Cobalt Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cobalt+Golem). He played another Myr on his third turn and passed it back. Fabiano stopped him cold with [Tel-Jilad Outrider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Outrider). Ruel hit for two in the air, then tapped four and showed him a [Rust Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rust+Elemental).


"Wanna race me?" - Antoine Ruel


Fabiano hit for three and played a [Clockwork Condor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clockwork+Condor) which inherited the axe. Ruel crippled him with [Echoing Ruin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Echoing+Ruin), busting through for eight, having fed Rusty an artifact land. Fabiano stood at just seven. He did some quick counting, then hit for another three and played a post-combat [Vulshok Berserker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vulshok+Berserker) and put the axe on it.


![Gerard Fabiano](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptsd04/fm16_2.jpg)Ruel ate a Myr and hit for six, leaving Fabiano at one. He played a [Darksteel Ingot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Ingot) and was done, in a seeming indomitable position. Fabiano untapped, attacked with his creatures and threw down an entwined [Savage Beating](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savage+Beating)! Before the spectators had time to calculate how much damage that was, Ruel calmly showed him [Electrostatic Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrostatic+Bolt).


Fabiano's face fell. After checking that Ruel had more than twelve life, he picked up his cards.


Antoine Ruel defeats Gerard Fabiano 2-1
