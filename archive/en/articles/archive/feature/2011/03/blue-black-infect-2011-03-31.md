Blue-Black Control may have won the Barcelona Grand Prix, but perhaps the deck that is the most fun is Blue-Black Infect. Championed by pros like Brian Kibler, the deck uses a blue-black control core with cards like [Doom Blade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doom+Blade) and [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak) to control the game, but finishes the opponent off with poison. How? [Skithiryx, the Blight Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skithiryx%2C+the+Blight+Dragon) or [Phyrexian Crusader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Crusader). Look for your control opponents to begin using the strategy against you at Friday Night **Magic**! Read more about Grand Prix Barcelona in the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/scheinin-beats-them-black-and-blue).
