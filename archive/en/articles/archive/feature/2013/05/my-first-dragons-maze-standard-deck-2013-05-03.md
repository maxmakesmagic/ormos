My First *Dragon's Maze* Standard Deck
======================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 3, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







As we prepare for *Dragon's Maze* release today, it's time for me to share a Standard deck for the new format. There's one card in *Dragon's Maze* I absolutely had to build a deck with: [Varolz, the Scar-Striped](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Varolz%2C+the+Scar-Striped). [Varolz](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Varolz%2C+the+Scar-Striped) is kind of like a bigger [Cartel Aristocrat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat), and *Dragon's Maze* brings along a much bigger [Doomed Traveler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomed+Traveler) to go with it in the form of [Voice of Resurgence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence). 

![Voice of Resurgence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Voice+of+Resurgence)This deck will be something like a take on The Aristocrats with green instead of red.

 My favorite play in Standard is [Doomed Traveler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomed+Traveler) into [Skirsdag High Priest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirsdag+High+Priest) into [Cartel Aristocrat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat), making a Demon. Adding green lets me make the same play with [Young Wolf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Young+Wolf), and adding [Varolz, the Scar-Striped](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Varolz%2C+the+Scar-Striped) lets me make the same play with him instead of [Cartel Aristocrat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat). End result: more turn-three Demons. 

 All these creatures that stick around mean that the token created by [Voice of Resurgence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence) is going to be huge, which is particularly good news for me, since I'll be able to sacrifice it to create that token whenever I want. 


[Cartel Aristocrat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat) also has a great interaction with Varolz, in that I can use his third ability (in order of importance, not order printed on the cardâ€”first is "sacrifice a creature," second is "regenerate," and the third is that all your creatures have scavenge) to make [Cartel Aristocrat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat) big, and then sacrifice some random guys to make it unblockable. This is a common play to attack for 2 in other decks with [Cartel Aristocrat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat), but can easily be much bigger here. 

 Speaking of big, protected [Cartel Aristocrat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat)s, this deck is an excellent home for the new [Armadillo Cloak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Armadillo+Cloak), [Unflinching Courage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unflinching+Courage). I only really want it against people I need to race, but how does an aggro deck beat turn-one [Young Wolf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Young+Wolf)/[Doomed Traveler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomed+Traveler), turn two [Cartel Aristocrat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat), turn three [Unflinching Courage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unflinching+Courage)? 

 I've said on twitter that [Maw of the Obzedat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Maw+of+the+Obzedat) is too expensive for me, but when I build the perfect home for it, I have to give it a chance. [Demonlord of Ashmouth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demonlord+of+Ashmouth) is a bit of a stretch, but I like having another sac outlet and I thought it might be powerful.
