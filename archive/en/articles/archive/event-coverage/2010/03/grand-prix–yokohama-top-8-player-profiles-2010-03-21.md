**Name**  

 Masashiro Kuroda

 Company employee  
**Record**   
**Byes**  

 2

 6-1  
**Day 2**  

 5-1  
**Where do you usually play *Magic*?**  

 I play **Magic** Online a lot, and I also practice at the Amenity Dream store in Nanba.  
**Tell us about the deck you used this weekend.**  
**Deck Name/Type**  
[Hypergenesis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypergenesis)
  
**Designer**  

 Me  
**Key points**  

 I don’t like
 [Blood Moon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon)
 so I have two
 [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest)
 s. I also put in an Akroma’s Memorial.  
**Tell us about a memorable match you played this weekend.**  

 I have three stand-out memories. Once, I was going to lose a game if I didn’t draw a land; I got a gorilla instead. Another time, I had to burn a gorilla with Borgardan Hellkite to remove a
 [Bridge from Below](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bridge+from+Below)
 . Finally, I was going to lose a game when I didn’t have any blockers, and I top-decked a gorilla. It’s all about the gorillas.  
**What other *Magic* achievements do you have?**  

 PT Kobe 2004 champion, Masters Venice champion, The Finals champion  
**How do you see yourself playing *Magic* after this?**  

 As long as I can keep using angels, dragons, and gorillas, I’ll keep playing.  
**Do you have a Twitter account? How do you use it?**  

 今後登録します。  

 I don’t have one now, but I’m planning on signing up.
