Ravnica Revealed
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 16, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











### The City of Guilds


I***n the largest metropolis of a city-covered plane, perspectives will clash and laws will be tested. From the depths under the city to its lofty spires above, Ravnica's citizens eke out an existence even as all around them ancient guilds vie for control and power.***


The first set in the block codenamed "Control - Alt - Delete" is called *Ravnica: City of Guilds*, and will release on October 7, 2005. For more information, be sure to watch **magicthegathering.com** as we approach the set's release date.


  
*Ravnica (RAV-ni-kah) style guide image by Greg Staples*
