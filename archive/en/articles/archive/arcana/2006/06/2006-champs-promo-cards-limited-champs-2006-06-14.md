2006 Champs Promo Cards: Limited Champs
=======================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 14, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/champs06.jpg)Maybe you got your promo [Electrolyze and Niv-Mizzet](/en/articles/archive/2006-champs-promo-cards-2006-02-16) at the 2006 Limited Two-Headed Giant Champs, or maybe you didn't. Maybe you're admiring your promo cards right now and wondering how you can get more, or maybe you're staring at your empty hands right now and blinking back a tear.

Either way, Champs are coming back this month, and this time around it's all *Ravnica* limited, all the time. Throw down in *Ravnica/Guildpact/Dissension* sealed deck and then test your draft skills and guild theories in the Top 8.

Attend this round of Champs, and you get your hands on this:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1099_GuildmagePromo.jpg)Top 8 at this round of Champs, and you get a foily one of these:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1099_VoidslimePromo.jpg)Top 8 finishers also get one of these handy Magic CD cases:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2006/champs_case.jpg)Limited Champs are less than two weeks away! Head [here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/magic/champs) for locations near you and for the full download of specifics. Because whose Simic deck doesn't need a foil promo [Voidslime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voidslime)?
