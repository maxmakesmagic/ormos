Invitations
-----------


#### Last Updated: May 24, 2016


[Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon* Event Information](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/premierplay/protour/ptemn) 


Invitations to Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon* are awarded in the following manner:


* The top four (4) finishing players from each Regional Pro Tour Qualifier with attendance of 128 or fewer players that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*.
* The top eight (8) finishing players from each Regional Pro Tour Qualifier with attendance of 129 or more players that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*.
* The top eight (8) finishing players from each *Magic Online* Regional Pro Tour Qualifier that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*.
* The winner of each *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifier Final that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*.
* The winner of each *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) Quarterly Playoff tournament that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*.
* Players that finished with 33 or more match points at Pro Tour *Shadows over Innistrad*.
* The Top eight (8) finishing players plus any other players that finished with 39 or more match points from each individual-format Grand Prix that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*.
* Team members from the top four (4) finishing teams plus any team members from a team that finished with 36 or more match points from each team-format Grand Prix that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*.
* The winner of each "Series-Style" events that that awards invitations to Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*.
* Players that have advanced to Pro Tour Players Club Silver level after the start of a Professional Points Season are invited to the next Pro Tour (following their promotion) of that Professional Points Season. In the event that a player already has an invitation to that Pro Tour, or chooses not to attend that Pro Tour, the invitation will be deferred to the next Pro Tour of that Professional Season, and so on. Invitations will not be deferred beyond the end of the Professional Season in which the player earned the invitation in this manner. Silver level members do not receive their invitations until all qualifying tournaments for Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon* have concluded.
* Players that start a Professional Points season at Pro Tour Players Club Silver level are invited to the first Pro Tour of that season. In the event that a player already has an invitation to that Pro Tour, or chooses not to attend that Pro Tour, the invitation will be deferred to the next Pro Tour of that Professional Season, and so on. Invitations will not be deferred beyond the end of the Professional Season in which the player earned the invitation in this manner. Silver level members do not receive their invitations until all qualifying tournaments for Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon* have concluded.
* Players with a Pro Tour Players Club Gold or Platinum level as of the start date of Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*.
* Players that are members of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame.

Note: The Pro Tour invitation criteria listed above do not generate a pass down of invitations to other players.


If you believe that your finish at a Regional Pro Tour Qualifier or Grand Prix is in error, please visit [www.wizards.com/Magic/PlaneswalkerPoints](http://magic.wizards.com/en/block/pro-tour-shadows-over-innistra-0/www.wizards.com/Magic/PlaneswalkerPoints). After logging in, click the "Appeals Form" link at the bottom of the page and fill out the form.
