Conley Woods is a player known for innovating formats. Check out his newest PTQ winning creating for Extended! He pairs the powerful [Knight of the Reliquary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary) with some forgotten land destruction spells. [Boom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Boom)? The [Armageddon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Armageddon) retread ensures Conley's graveyard is stocked with mana and, out of lands to do anything about it, his opponent is helpless to watch as the Knight rampages through the red zone.
