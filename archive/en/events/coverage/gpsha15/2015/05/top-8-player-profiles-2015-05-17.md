Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX SHANGHAI 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpsha15)
 on May 17, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











These are the Top 8 Players of Grand Prix Shanghai 2015.




---


Xie Hao Chen
============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsha15/Top8XieHaoChen.jpg)


**Hometown:** Beijing, China  
**Age:** 25  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Day 1 Score:** 7-0-2 (2 Byes)  
**Day 2 Score:** 5-0-1


**What Standard deck are you playing today?**   

Green Red Devotion


**What are the best cards in your maindeck and sideboard respectively?**   
[Xenagos, the Reveler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos%2C+the+Reveler) and [Rending Volley](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rending+Volley)


**What is your favorite dragon in Magic history?**  
[Dragonlord Atarka](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonlord+Atarka)

=============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsha15/Top8NamSungWook.jpg)
