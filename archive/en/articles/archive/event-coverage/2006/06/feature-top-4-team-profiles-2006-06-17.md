**Age:**18

**Hometown:** Porto Allegre

**Occupation:** Physics Student

**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Charleston?** PTQ

**Number of PT/GP Top 8s?** 1

**Other Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Top 32 Prague and Hawaii

**What was your individual record on Day 1?** 6-1 **Day 2?** 6-1

[Orzhov Pontiff](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Orzhov+Pontiff)
