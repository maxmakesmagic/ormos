Arena Fireball
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 12, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












For the first few years of the Arena League, alternate art [Disenchant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disenchant)s and [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball)s were given away as prizes. We've shown the Disenchant as a previous "Card of the Day," but we haven't put up the Fireball yet. So here it is (on the left). The wording hadn't be changed on the card yet to support the "![](https://web.archive.org/web/20160829100442im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/x.gif)Y![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130159im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/red.gif)" mana cost, but there is a version of Fireball printed like that. The *Beatdown* box set from a couple years ago contained a real Fireball with the XY mana cost (right).
