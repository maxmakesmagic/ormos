*Gatecrash* Lexicon
===================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 7, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Welcome to the *Gatecrash* edition of the Lexicon, in which we explore the meanings of some of the more unusual words we use in the names of those cards. While many offbeat names in **Magic** (like "[rubblehulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=rubblehulk)") are simply made up, we often dig around dictionaries for real little-used and archaic words for inspiration.

![Borborygmos Enraged](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Borborygmos+Enraged)**bor•bo•ryg•mus** [bawr-b*uh*-**rig**-m*uh* s]   
*noun*

1. a rumbling or gurgling sound caused by the movement of gas in the intestines.

***Origin:***
  
 1710–20;  Neo-Latin  Greek *borborygmós* intestinal rumbling

![Diluvian Primordial](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Diluvian+Primordial)**di•lu•vi•al** [dih-**loo**-vee-*uh* l]   
*adjective*

1. pertaining to or caused by a flood or deluge.

![Luminate Primordial](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Luminate+Primordial)**pri•mor•di•al** [prahy-**mawr**-dee-*uh* l]   
*adjective*

1. constituting a beginning; giving origin to something derived or developed; original; elementary: *primordial forms of life.*
2. *Embryology*. first formed.
3. pertaining to or existing at or from the very beginning: *primordial matter.*

![Sepulchral Primordial](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Sepulchral+Primordial)**se•pul•chral** [s*uh*-**puhl**-kr*uh* l]   
*adjective*

1. of, pertaining to, or serving as a tomb.
2. of or pertaining to burial.
3. proper to or suggestive of a tomb; funereal or dismal.
4. hollow and deep: *sepulchral tones.*

![Sylvan Primordial](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Sylvan+Primordial)**syl•van** [**sil**-v*uh* n]   
*adjective*

1. of, pertaining to, or inhabiting the woods.
2. consisting of or abounding in woods or trees; wooded; woody: *a shady, sylvan glade.*
3. made of trees, branches, boughs, etc.

*Source: dictionary.com*
