Ken Nagle, Wizards R&D member, used this deck for spellslinging at the *New Phyrexia* Prerelease this past weekend. How does it work? Says Ken, "This deck defends itself with [Contagion Clasp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contagion+Clasp)s and [Tumble Magnet](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tumble+Magnet)s until a planeswalker, a [Shrine of Burning Rage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shrine+of+Burning+Rage), or an [Inkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkmoth+Nexus) poison counter gets enough proliferation to kill the opponent." Ken's favorite *New Phyrexia* art is [Elesh Norn, Grand Cenobite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elesh+Norn%2C+Grand+Cenobite), and you can follow him on his Twitter feed, @[NorrYtt](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=twitter.com/NorrYtt). Interested in playing the set yourself? It goes on sale [this weekend](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/release-facts)!
