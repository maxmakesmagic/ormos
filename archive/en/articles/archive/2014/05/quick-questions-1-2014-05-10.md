QUICK QUESTIONS #1
==================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on May 10, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











**Which Color Gained the Most from the Introduction of Journey into Nyx into the Limited Mix?** 



![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/qqlevy.jpg)***(24) Raphaël Lévy**: In Sealed it's definitely green. I've played a couple and I was green every time.* 

![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/qqkrautmann.jpg)***Wenzel Krautmann**: Green. There's more mana ramp now. [Golden Hind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Hind) is particularly good.* 



![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/qqrachid.jpg)***Denniz Rachid**: Black. It was good in *Theros*, then terrible in *Born of the Gods*, and now it's good again.* 

![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/qqdezani.jpg)***(1) Jérémy Dezani**: Green.* 



![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/qqjuza.jpg)***(25) Martin Jůza**: There's a Pro Tour next week, so I'll have to say, no comment. Sorry.* 

![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/qqseverin.jpg)***Thoralf Severin**: People seem to like black more, and I don't like that because I like black myself. But what actually gained the most is probably white.*
