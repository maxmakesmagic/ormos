In the War Zone
===============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 9, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Have you taken a good look at [Contested War Zone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+War+Zone) from *Mirrodin Besieged*? If you haven't, here's your chance!

![Contested War Zone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Contested+War+Zone)  
Okay, so you know it portrays a war zone. That's kind of basic to the concept. Take a closer look.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/643_contestedwarzone.jpg)[Contested War Zone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+War+Zone) | Art by [Scott Chou](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Scott+Chou%22%5D)

Although the whole scene is a war zone, the actual combat is currently happening over on the right. Let's zoom in on that!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/643_warzonedetail.jpg)
