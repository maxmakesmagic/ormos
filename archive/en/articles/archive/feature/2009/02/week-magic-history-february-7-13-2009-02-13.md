This Week in Magic History: February 7-13
=========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 13, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











### February 9-11, 2007: Mike Hron Wins Pro Tour–Geneva 2007


![Mike Hron, winner of Pro Tourâ€“Geneva](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptgen07/F_Trophy_Hron.jpg)It had been over six years since an American had won a Limited Pro Tour. But Wisconsin's Mike Hron turned that around with his virtuoso performance in *Time Spiral* Booster Draft.


The final match against Takuya Osawa was one for the ages, culminating in a rare Osawa misplay when he appeared to misread [Spectral Force](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spectral+Force).


### Also This Week


* February 4-February 7, 1999: Mike Long takes down the *Duelist* Invitational in Barcelona, defeating Sturla Bingen in the finals. The scene was set for [Rootwater Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootwater+Thief) to enter the *Nemesis* set!
* February 4-February 7, 1999: Kai Budde wins Grand Prix–Barcelona. Another time a Grand Prix run at the same time as the Invitational was won by a future Invitationalist!
