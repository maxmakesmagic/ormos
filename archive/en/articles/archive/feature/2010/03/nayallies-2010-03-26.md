 Many players have tried to build an Allies deck since the tribe first came into existence with the printing of *Zendikar*. Shirai Taiga is one who managed to do it! The Japanese player relied on the colors of Naya and some help from the Allies to make the Top 8 of the **Magic** Game Day event celebrating *Worldwake* in Japan. For more decks, perfect for inspiration for your Friday Night **Magic** tournaments, check out all the [*Worldwake* Game Day Standard deck lists](/en/events/coverage/worldwake-game-day).
