Meet the Champions Update: Iname and You Decide! Winner
=======================================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 29, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Ready for another story about the heroes of Kamigawa? Check out the [*Champions of Kamigawa* site](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/displayexpansion.asp?set=chk&page=1) for an update in the "Meet the Champions" area.


This week's story is "**[Thankless Child](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/chk/iname)**" by Rei Nakazawa. It's the story of Iname, a legendary kami with two opposing halves: one side representing [Iname, Life Aspect](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Iname%252C%2BLife%2BAspect), the other [Iname, Death Aspect](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Iname%252C%2BDeath%2BAspect). Enjoy!
