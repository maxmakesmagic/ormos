Pro Tour–Berlin Qualifying Season: Top 8 Decklists
==================================================












 


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/protour/images/qt_lg2.jpg)Get your *Lorwyn/Shadowmoor*  Block Constructed deck ready, because tickets to Berlin are up for grabs in this summer's Pro Tour Qualifier season!


You can test each decklist thanks to our [Sample Hand Generator](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/feature/294). Just click on the card fan in the upper right of each decklist and you can see what your opening hand could be, along with mulligans and the first three cards you'd draw.


Decklists will be updated weekly during the PTQ season. The season runs from June 7 to August 31. To find a PTQ near you, [click here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/berlin08-qualifiers). [Pro Tour–Berlin](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/berlin08) is scheduled for October 31-November 2, and the format is Extended.
