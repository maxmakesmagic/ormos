Meet the Champions Update: Azami
================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 1, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Ready for another story about the heroes of Kamigawa? Check out the [*Champions of Kamigawa* site](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/displayexpansion.asp?set=chk&page=1) for an update in the "Meet the Champions" area.


This week's story is "**[Everything](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/chk/azami)**" by Gwendolyn Kestrel. It's the story of Azami, a woman so dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge that she was willing to sacrifice -- everything. Enjoy.
