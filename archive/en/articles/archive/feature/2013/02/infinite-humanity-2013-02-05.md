Infinite Humanity
=================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 5, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samstoddard.jpg)
By Sam Stoddard




Sam Stoddard came to Wizards of the Coast as an intern in May 2012. He is currently a game designer working on final design and development for Magic: The Gathering.
 







G*atecrash* brought more than just the obvious heavy hitters to the field; it also brought cards that may have gone under the radar for most people, including [Cartel Aristocrat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat). When placed in the already strong Human Reanimator deck, [Cartel Aristocrat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat) both gives another sacrifice outlet to allow for "infinite" combos with [Angel of Glory's Rise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Glory%27s+Rise) and also comes on a Human, which means the combo will now work without requiring a creature like [Falkenrath Aristocrat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Falkenrath+Aristocrat) in play before it begins. 


![Angel of Glory's Rise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Angel+of+Glory%27s+Rise)
 If you are unaware of how the combo works, the idea is to return, cast, or reanimate an [Angel of Glory's Rise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Glory%27s+Rise) with a [Cartel Aristocrat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat), a [Fiend Hunter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiend+Hunter), and a [Huntmaster of the Fells](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells) in your graveyard. The Angel returns all three, and you resolve their triggers, putting a 2/2 Wolf onto the battlefield, gaining 3 life, and exiling the [Angel of Glory's Rise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Glory%27s+Rise). Sacrifice your Huntmaster and the [Fiend Hunter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiend+Hunter) to the [Cartel Aristocrat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartel+Aristocrat), and when the Angel returns to play, it will return both the [Fiend Hunter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiend+Hunter) and the Huntmaster to play, allowing you to loop the combo, gain a huge amount of life, and put a lot of tokens onto the battlefield. 


If you are playing in events this weekend, you may want to make sure your sideboard includes a few answers for graveyard decks like this one.
