==========================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX SALT LAKE CITY 2014 - COVERAGE - EVENTS](/en/events/coverage/gpslc14) 
 on September 7, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











 From the King of the Hill, Nathan Holiday, to Pro Tour *Gatecrash* winner No. 11 Shaun McLaren, to Allen Sun and Ian Robertson, both of whom avoided the spotlight most of the first day, four players ended the inaugural nine rounds with nary a loss. A huge advantage going into the two draft pods, these players are in the best position to earn themselves a Top-8 berth. But that means preciously little when it gets down to brass tacks; there's still six rounds of *Magic* to be played. And now, even the ones who dodged the lights yesterday have big targets on their backs.
