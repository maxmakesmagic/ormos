Spellslingers Has Arrived
=========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 19, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1324_geekandsundry.png)Teased on Monday, the new [Geek & Sundry](http://www.geekandsundry.com/) show "[Spellslingers](http://spellslingers.geekandsundry.com/)" launched on [their YouTube channel](http://www.youtube.com/geekandsundry) yesterday and it is awesome!

In this week's episode it is [Sean "Day[9]" Plott](http://twitter.com/day9tv) vs. E-Sports caster and personlity [Rob Simpson](https://twitter.com/robpsimpson). They battle with a green-blue Simic deck and a white-black Orzhov deck. There's nothing I enjoy more than watching two people have fun playing **Magic**.

This series is great to show friends who are thinking about getting into **Magic**. They don't dive deep into the theory or rules and instead just focus on having fun.

If you enjoy the episode be sure to let Geek & Sundry know! Subscribe to [their YouTube channel](http://www.youtube.com/geekandsundry), [tweet them](http://www.twitter.com/geekandsundry) and [Day[9]](http://www.twitter.com/day9tv) on... Twitter, or share it on Facebook! I really enjoyed it and hope you do too!

They'll be back next week with another episode featuring NASA's Mohawk guy: [Bobak Ferdowsi](http://twitter.com/tweetsoutloud)!

<http://www.youtube.com/embed/KmouDgc9hD8>
