### GRAND PRIX SHANGHAI 2014


GRAND PRIX TRIALS WINNING DECKLISTS
===================================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)

Chapman Sim




October 3, 2014
 











[Zurgo Helmsmasher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zurgo+Helmsmasher) would be so proud. A total of sixteen *Khans of Tarkir* Sealed Deck Trials took place yesterday, granting all winners Second Round Byes for today's main event. Apparently, Mardu seems to be the dominant force yesterday and only time will tell if it is indeed the best clan of all! Do check out the Standard decklist right at the end, Mardu won the only Standard GPT as well!
