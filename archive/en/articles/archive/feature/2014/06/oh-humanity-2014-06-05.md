Oh, the Humanity
================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 5, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 






![Young Pyromancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Young+Pyromancer)
Modern is home to many cheap, efficient instants and sorceries. [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt), [Serum Visions](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serum+Visions), and [Remand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Remand) provide effective ways to take control of the early stages of the game without losing their effectiveness as the game wears on. **Magic Online** user MR\_Thompson takes advantage of these with creatures that want a number of spells to be played. Each creature in the deck gets stronger as the number of instants and sorceries grows. 

 This deck also uses its sideboard well to shore up different matchups without changing the core of the deck. [Molten Rain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Rain) is a perfect example. [Molten Rain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Rain) is effective against [Urza's Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Tower) and [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle), while still allowing this deck to maintain its high spell count and positive tempo. [Shattering Spree](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shattering+Spree) gives additional fuel against [Cranial Plating](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cranial+Plating) and friends while [Combust](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Combust) is strong against [Deceiver Exarch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch) and [Pestermite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pestermite).
