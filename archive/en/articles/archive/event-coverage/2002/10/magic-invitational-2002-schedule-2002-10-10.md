Magic Invitational 2002 Schedule
================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 10, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











NOTE: ALL TIMES ARE TENTATIVE AS ROUNDS ARE SOMEWHAT FLEXIBLE. WHEN ALL PLAYERS FINISH, WE WILL GO STRAIGHT TO THE NEXT ROUND.

### Friday, October 18

9:00am - Round 1 Deck-Build Begins (The format for rounds 1-3 is Two-Headed Giant)  
 9:30am - Round 1 Begins  
 11:00am - Round 2 Deck-Build Begins  
 11:30 - Round 2 Begins  
 1:00pm - Lunch Break (after Round 2)  
 2:00pm - Round 3 Deck-Build Begins  
 2:30pm - Round 3 Begins  
 4:00pm - Round 4 Begins (The format for rounds 4-6 is Future Extended)  
 5:30pm - Round 5 Begins  
 7:00pm - Round 6 Begins

### Saturday, October 19

9:00am - Auction of the People Begins  
 9:30am - Round 7 Begins (The format for rounds 7-9 is Auction of the People)  
 11:00am - Round 8 Begins  
 12:30pm - Lunch Break (after Round 8)  
 1:30pm - Round 9 Begins  
 3:00pm - Onslaught Draft Begins (The format for rounds 9-12 is Onslaught Rochester Draft)  
 4:00pm - Deck-Build Begins  
 4:30pm - Round 10 Begins  
 6:00pm - Round 11 Begins  
 7:30pm - Round 12 Begins

### Sunday, October 20

9:00am - Round 13 Begins (The format for rounds 13-15 is Future Standard)  
 10:30am - Round 14 Begins  
 12:00pm - Round 15 Begins  
 1:30pm - Lunch Break Begins  
 2:30pm - Finals Begin (Best two out of three match of all three constructed formats)  
 2:30pm - Gunslinging Begins
