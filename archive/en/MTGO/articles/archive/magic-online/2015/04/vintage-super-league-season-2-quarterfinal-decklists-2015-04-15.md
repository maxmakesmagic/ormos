Vintage Super League Season 2: Quarterfinal Decklists
=====================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on April 15, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











As described in Randy Buehler's playoff [preview](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-episode-11-2015-04-14), each player in a Vintage Super League playoff match brings three decks, labeled "A," "B," and "C," then pit corresponding decks against each other. Below are the decks that Randy Buehler and Eric Froehlich brought to their [quarterfinal match](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dlADuKt4jXQ).


You can follow the links below to see the different sets of decklists players used through the Vintage Super League season.


Watch the Vintage Super League live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic) Tuesday nights at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7).
