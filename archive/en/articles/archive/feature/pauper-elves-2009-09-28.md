Elves was all the rage in last year's Extended season, but it wasn't the first time the tribe had seen success. No, since the beginning Elves have been with us from the lowly [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Llanowar%2BElves) all the way up to the [Elvish Archdruid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Archdruid). Now you can even play them in Pauper! JJonasN used this list to some success in a recent **Magic Online** tournament in that very format.
