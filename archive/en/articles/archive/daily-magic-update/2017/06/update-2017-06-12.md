Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Todd Stevens's Eldrazi Tron](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/displaydeck.php?DeckID=114104)

This was certainly a breakout performance for the entirely colorless (ish) deck in Modern, as not one but two copies made the Top 8 of the StarCityGames.com Open, this one piloted by champion Todd Stevens.


It's actually kind of amazing what a well-rounded deck you can put together without producing any non-colorless mana. [Walking Ballista](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista) provides creature control, [Thought-Knot Seer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought-Knot+Seer), [Chalice of the Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chalice+of+the+Void) and [Relic of Progenitus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Relic+of+Progenitus) provide disruption, and the deck can even go over the top with [Karn Liberated](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karn+Liberated), [Endbringer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Endbringer), and [Dismember](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember).


The real star of the deck is [Chalice of the Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chalice+of+the+Void), almost always set to shut off spells that cost one mana. After that, the deck's mana kicks it into another level. [Eldrazi Temple](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Temple) enables fast [Thought-Knot Seer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought-Knot+Seer)s, the Urza's lands enable Karns and large [Walking Ballista](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista)s, and [Expedition Map](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Expedition+Map) ties it all together in a neat little package.


As for the "ish" above, the deck cheats a little bit on the colorless thing by dipping into Phyrexian mana for a pair of [Dismember](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember)s, which virtually always cost 4 life in this deck (the "almost" coming from the corner case of [Urborg, Tomb of Yawgmoth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urborg%2C+Tomb+of+Yawgmoth) being in play).
