The Pro Tour Qualifier season for Honolulu has drawn to a close, and what a season! There were tons of great decks, and Joshua Wittenmore proved that even in the final week of the season, there was still room for innovation. He decided to take all his favorite multi-color cards, put them together, and make the mana work out with oft forgotten *Ravnica* Block card [Pillar of the Paruns](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pillar+of+the+Paruns). The non-basic land makes sure whatever Joshua tries to cast, he has the right mana to do so!
