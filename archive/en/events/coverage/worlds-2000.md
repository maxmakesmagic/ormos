* [Finals: Jon Finkel (USA) vs. Bob Maher, Jr. (USA)](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/world-championship-finals-jon-finkel-usa-vs-bob-maher-jr-usa-2000-01-01)  In a battle of Magic titans, Jon Finkel, the greatest player in the history of Magic: the Gathering, defeated Bob Maher three games to two. Playing nearly identical decks, the two friends, both of whom had previously won Pro Tours, played the maximum number of games to decide who would join Tommi Hovi as the only players to ever win more than one premiere level event. * [Team Results](javascript:void(0);) After the four individual matches ended with each team winning two, the team championship came down to a final match between team captains Jon Finkel and Ryan Fuller. Finkel won, 2-0, capturing the championship for the United States.
