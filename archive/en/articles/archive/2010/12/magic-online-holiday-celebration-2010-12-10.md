Magic Online Holiday Celebration
================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 10, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












With winter in full swing, why not stay inside and celebrate the holidays with some great **Magic** events. Join us on **Magic Online** from December 15 through January 5 for our three week Holiday Celebration.


This year's Holiday Celebration includes:


* [Gold Constructed Queues return, now for all core and specialty formats!](#gold)
* [*Masters Edition* 64-Player Drafts let you warm up for the *Masters Edition IV* release.](#me1)
* [*Premium Deck Series:* Fire & Lightning heats up the Daily and Premier Event prize structures.](#prizes)
* [Momir Basic Daily Events return and bring a Nix Tix Momir Basic PE on the 25th!](#momir)
* [*Mirage* Block and *Tempest* Block Nix Tix and Nix Pax drafts return!](#mirage)
* [Double MOPR for the holidays!](#mopr)

**Gold Queues Return**  

Back by popular demand, our Gold Constructed 2-Player Queues are returning for the Holiday Celebration. This time, however, we will be offering Gold Queues for Block, Extended, Legacy, Standard Singleton, 100-Card Singleton, Pauper and Classic along with the Standard Gold Queue. Grab a deck and jump into a queue for your favorite format!


*START TIME:* On demand starting after the scheduled downtime on Wednesday, December 15 until the beginning of the scheduled downtime on Wednesday, January 5.  
*LOCATION:* 8-Player Constructed room.  
*ENTRY REQUIREMENT:* 10 Event Tickets.  
*PRODUCT:* A [format legal constructed deck](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/banned).  
*SIZE:* 2 Players.  
*DURATION:* One single elimination match.  
*PRIZES:* Prizes for Single Elimination events are based on placement at the end of the tournament.




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Place** | **Prizes** |
| 1st | 5 booster packs |


 

***Masters Edition* 64-Player "Top 8" Drafts**  

With the release of *Masters Edition IV* just around the corner, here is your chance to play the first *Masters Edition* one more time.  
*START TIME:* Scheduled start times on Wednesday, December 22 and end on Wednesday, December 29. Two events scheduled during each time slot at 6:00 AM PST (14:00 UTC), 12:00 PM PST (20:00 UTC), 5:00 PM PST (01:00 UTC), and 9:00 PM PST (05:00 UTC).  
*LOCATION:* Premier Events room.  
*ENTRY REQUIREMENT:* 16 Event Tickets.  
*PRODUCT:* **Magic Online** will provide 3 *Masters Edition* booster packs for this event. **Magic Online** will provide 3 *Masters Edition* boosters for the "Top 8" draft.  
*SIZE:* 64 players. *DURATION:* Drafting and deck building time plus 3 Rounds, Single elimination. The "Top 8" will consist of drafting and deck building time plus 3 Rounds, Single elimination.  
*PRIZES:* Prizes for "Top 8" events are based on placement at the end of the tournament.

**Premier and Daily Event Bonuses**  

Starting after the downtime on December 15, 2010 *Premium Deck Series:* Fire & Lightning will be added to our Daily and Premier Event prize structures in addition to the normal booster prizes! Each player who finishes with either a record of 4-0 in a Daily Event or reaches the "Top 8" playoff of a Premier Event will receive a copy of the deck. These bonuses will last until the scheduled downtime on January 5, 2011.


**Momir Returns (Again)**  

Momir Basic Daily Events Return during the entire Holiday Celebration! In addition, join us Saturday, December 25 for our giant Nix Tix Momir Basic Premier Event where all you need is your Momir Basic deck!

*ENTRY REQUIREMENT:* 6 Event Tickets.

*SIZE:* Minimum 16 players, maximum 128 players.  
*DURATION:* 4 rounds, Swiss Pairings.  
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points.

**DoubleMagic Online Player Rewards**  

During the Holiday Celebration, all tournament play and all store purchases will grant double the **Magic Online** Player Rewards points! The double points period begins after the downtime on December 15 and lasts until the downtime on January 5!
