Legacy Cube Releases Wednesday, November 19
On Wednesday, November 19, the Legacy Cube and paired *Vintage Masters* [Events](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online-announcements-november-4-2014#item3) are released into the wild! If you can't wait to see how the Cube plays, check out some of the [streamer events](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online-eternal-stream-stravaganza-2014-11-13) that ran last week.


[Read more](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/new-magic-online-legacy-cube-2014-11-3) about the Legacy Cube.



 



Vintage Super League
It all comes down to this! The [Vintage Super League](http://vintagesuperleague.com/) Championship match will be held Tuesday at 7:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-8), live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic). Luis Scott-Vargas and Stephen Menendian have traded first place back and forth all season long and now they play one final best three out of five matches showdown for the title and the glory.



 



*Khans of Tarkir* Constructed Championship Qualifiers Begin Tomorrow
The *Khans of Tarkir* Constructed Championship Qualifiers start soon! Beginning tomorrow, November 19, *Magic Online* players can begin making their way towards the *Khans of Tarkir* Standard Championship.


For a full schedule of events, refer to the *Khans of Tarkir* Championship [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/khans-tarkir-championship-events-2014-09-29).

*Theros* 4-3-2-2 Queues Leave *Magic Online*
After the downtime on Wednesday, November 26, *Theros* 4-3-2-2 Queues will be retired from *Magic Online*.



 



Downtime for November 19, 2014
**Downtime this week will be extended by two hours, setting our estimated return at 2:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC -8).** 



* All tournaments will close Wednesday, November 19, at 5:00 a.m. Pacific (13:00 UTC)
* Store and trade activity will be suspended at approximately 7:45 a.m. Pacific(15:45 UTC)
* The system will be down from 8:00 a.m. Pacific (16:00 UTC) until 2:00 p.m. Pacific (22:00 UTC)
