***Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet* Sealed PTQs for Pro Tour *Ixalan* Friday andSunday, August 4 and 6**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: Two Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) are approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Friday, August 4 and Sunday, August 6 at 7 a.m. PT. Remember to plan ahead and earn enough tokens for both events!


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

**Upcoming Standard MOCS Playoff on Saturday, August 5**


The third quarter of competition in the *Magic Online*Championship Series (MOCS) culminates on August 5 for the third [Playoff Event](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series#PlayoffEvents) of the series! Big prizes, the chance to move up the [MOCS Leaderboard](http://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/leaderboard), plus invitations to the *Magic Online*Championship and *Pro Tour Ixalan*are all on the line in this event.


Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online*Championship Series and how to qualify for future Playoff Events [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

***Magic Online*Format Challenges back this weekend**


The *Magic Online*Format Challenges introduced a new prize structure and recurring weekend events for Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper formats! Check out Lee Sharpe's [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-format-challenges) for specific details.


Events begin this Saturday, August 5 at 8:00 a.m. PT.

**No downtime tomorrow,  August 2 - events unavailable during maintenance window**


There will be no downtime tomorrow, August 2. However, events will be unavailable during these maintenance times:




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Time | Event |
| 3 a.m. PT | Queues Disabled |
| 5 a.m. PT | Leagues Paused, Trade and Store Disabled |
| 6:30 a.m. PT | Estimated Completion |

The next two downtimes will be August 23 and September 20, both of which will be extended (3 a.m. PT/10:00 p.m. UTC).


Follow *Magic Online*'s [Twitter](https://twitter.com/MagicOnline) account for live updates during downtime.
