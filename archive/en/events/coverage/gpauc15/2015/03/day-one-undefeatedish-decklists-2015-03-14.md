Day One Undefeatedish Decklists
===============================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX AUCKLAND 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpauc15) 
 on March 14, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/walkinshaw.jpg)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw










If we'd cut to Day 2 after Round 8, we'd have had three undefeated players, which is about right these days, I've found. However, we played one more round after that, and Jason Chung got paired down and lost to Maitland Cameron, and Luke Mulcahy lost to Richard Tasker, so Tasker was our last undefeated player heading into Day 2.


But not to sell you all short, here are the decklists for the three players who were undefeated after Round 8 instead.
