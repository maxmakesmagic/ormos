DAY 1 COVERAGE



Witness over 20 years of *Magic* in action as players at Grand Prix Seattle-Tacoma compete in the timeless Legacy Constructed format. With a diverse field of unique and extremely powerful decks at their disposal, who will emerge triumphant? Tune in to find out!
