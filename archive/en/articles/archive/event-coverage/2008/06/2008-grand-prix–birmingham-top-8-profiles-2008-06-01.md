2008 Grand Prix–Birmingham Top 8 Profiles
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 1, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbir08/t8_wiegersma.jpg)

**Name:** Jelger Wiegersma  
**Age:** 26  
**Occupation:** Tourist  
**Hometown:** Eindhoven, the Netherlands


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


Winner PT–Seattle


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 


[Spectral Procession](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spectral+Procession)


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?**


Every match-up is fifty-fifty.


**If you were to play your deck in the upcoming PTQs, would you change anything?**


The sideboard is pretty bad.





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbir08/t8_kuenzler.jpg)

**Name:** Matthias Künzler  
**Age:** 27  
**Occupation:** Engineer in microtechnics, co-owning a game store  
**Hometown:** Bern, Switherland


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


Top 8 GP–Vienna 1999  

Top 16 PT–Yokohama 2007


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 


[Cryptic Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command)


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?**


Quite okay. Kithkin isn’t that bad a match-up.


**If you were to play your deck in the upcoming PTQs, would you change anything?**


Yes, switch one [Sower of Temptation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sower+of+Temptation) for a [Familiar’s Ruse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Familiar%E2%80%99s+Ruse) in the sideboard.





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbir08/t8_bucher.jpg)

**Name:** Manuel Bucher  
**Age:** 21  
**Occupation:** Magic Player  
**Hometown:** Luzern, Switzerland


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


Quick’n’Toast creator


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 


[Mind Spring](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Spring)


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?**


Depends on my match-up against WW, never actually played that so far.


**If you were to play your deck in the upcoming PTQs, would you change anything?**


I would probably play a win condition.





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbir08/t8_tian.jpg)

**Name:** Lee Shi Tian  
**Age:** 20  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Hometown:** Hongkong


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


Err... none.


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 


[Oblivion Ring](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Ring)


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?**


Don’t know.


**If you were to play your deck in the upcoming PTQs, would you change anything?**


Complete change. I got bored with White Weenie...





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbir08/t8_malin.jpg)

**Name:** Antti Malin  
**Age:** 25  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Hometown:** Helsinki, Finland


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


4th at PT–London  
*Unhinged* Prerelease champion


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 


Actually, the deck overall... I’d say [Spellstutter Sprite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellstutter+Sprite).


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?**


Decent... Happy to be here.


**If you were to play your deck in the upcoming PTQs, would you change anything?**


I’d add one or two [Peppersmoke](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peppersmoke) to the sideboard.





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbir08/t8_levy.jpg)

**Name:** Raphael Levy  
**Age:** 26  
**Hometown:** Toulouse, France


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 


PT Hall of Fame


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 


[Horde of Notions](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Horde+of+Notions)


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?**


If I win my quarterfinal, I probably win the whole thing.


**If you were to play your deck in the upcoming PTQs, would you change anything?**


I would probably change a couple of cards, depending on how popular the elemental deck becomes. If I were to play the tournament again, I would play the same 75 cards.
