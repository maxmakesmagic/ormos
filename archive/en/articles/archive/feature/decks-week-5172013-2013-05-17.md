Decks of the Week: 5/17/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 17, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations to Quan Zhou for his win at [Grand Prix Beijing](/en/events/coverage/zhou-brings-back-beijing) and to Sam Pardee for his victory at [Grand Prix Portland](/en/events/coverage/its-pardee-time)! Both Quan and Sam are now invited to Pro Tour *Theros*, and if you would like to compete against them there you can do so by winning a Pro Tour Qualifier event! Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros)! Good luck; have fun!
