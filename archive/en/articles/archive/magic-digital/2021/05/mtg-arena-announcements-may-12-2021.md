Recent Server Issues
====================


Unfortunately, *MTG Arena* experienced server issues on Saturday, May 8. We apologize for the impact it had on people's attempts to play. In response to this issue, we've made the code "ExperimentalOverload" valid through July 6 for receiving 2,000 XP. Enter the code on [your Wizards Account](https://myaccounts.wizards.com/) page or on a PC or Mac in the Store tab.



> 
> We're aware the issues on Saturday may have limited your ability to play and make progress toward your quests and mastery pass, so enter code "ExperimentalOverload" to receive 2000 XP to help get you back on track.  
>   
> 
> You can enter the code on <https://t.co/KuFwZ5yQ1m>
> 
>

College Cup Exam Cram—Don't Forget Your Supplies!
=================================================


The [College Cup](https://admin.magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/college-cup-here-strixhaven-2021-05-05) begins May 13 and continues through May 31. Do you have all your school supplies ready? You get extra credit by earning bonus points for your college team when you show off your college spirit by having these when you participate:


* **College sleeves**: Take that Strixhaven quiz to get the email containing your sleeve code, then redeem it in [your Wizards Account](https://myaccounts.wizards.com/) or through the Store on PC or Mac.
* **College avatar**: Each college's avatar is available in the second position in the *Strixhaven* Mastery web.
* **College pet**: Each college has a tome pet available within the first ten levels of the *Strixhaven* Mastery Pass rewards levels.

There are three events, each testing skills every effective mage must have, and each offering rewards in the form of Japanese Mystical Archive card styles!


Practice makes perfect, so play as many games as you want.


**Entry**: 2,500 gold or 500 gems


**May 13–18**: Test of Knowledge


**Format**: Singleton with Archmage Emeritus emblem


* 1st win: [Cultivate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518294)
* 2nd win: [Thrill of Possibility](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518289)
* 3rd win: [Opt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518262)
* 4th win: [Revitalize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518252)

**May 20–25**: Test of Wit


**Format**: 100-Card Standard with Treasure Token Factory emblem


* 1st win: [Strategic Planning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518263)
* 2nd win: [Shock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518287)
* 3rd win: [Defiant Strike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518246)
* 4th win: [Eliminate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518273)

**May 27–31**: Test of Leadership


**Format**: *Strixhaven* Brawl—Standard Brawl, but only Strixhaven legendary creatures and planeswalkers are legal (and can be your commander)


* 1st win: [Adventurous Impulse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518292)
* 2nd win: [Village Rites](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518278)
* 3rd win: [Divine Gambit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518247)
* 4th win: [Claim the Firstborn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=518280)

You can find all the [details on earning points in the College Cup](https://admin.magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/college-cup-here-strixhaven-2021-05-05) in David McDarby's article.


When the College Cup has concluded and the point totals have been tallied, the winning college will change the face of *MTG Arena* itself! For one week following the College Cup event, *MTG Arena* will celebrate the winning college with a makeover.


For example, if Prismari wins, you might see this:


![College Theme](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/cjxquMOlxE.jpg)


Plus, log in during this week and get a special card sleeve featuring the winning college's emblem!


![Winning Sleeves](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/YTRjSmKxuB.png)


*Strixhaven* Draft Challenge
============================


*MTG Arena*'s expansion into high-level Limited play continues with the *Strixhaven* Draft Challenge.


**Dates**: May 22–25


**Format**: Player Draft, Best-of-Three matches


**Entry**: 20,000 Gold or 3,000 Gems


**Length**: Play until six match wins or two match losses


**Rewards**: Based on player feedback from the last Draft Challenge, we're shifting rewards by adding Player Draft Tokens and slightly reducing the number of packs. Note that Player Draft tokens cannot be used to join this event but can be used for any Premier Draft or Traditional Draft.





| Best-of-Three |
| --- |
| 0 wins | No reward |
| 1 win | 1 Player Draft Token |
| 2 wins | 1 Player Draft Token + 3 *Strixhaven* Packs |
| 3 wins | 2 Player Draft Tokens + 6 *Strixhaven* Packs |
| 4 wins | 3 Player Draft Tokens + 10 *Strixhaven* Packs |
| 5 wins | 3 Player Draft Tokens + 15 *Strixhaven* Packs |
| 6 wins | 4 Player Draft Tokens + 20 *Strixhaven* Packs |


Historic Anthology 5 on Sale May 27
===================================


Historic Anthology 5 goes on sale May 27, introducing new cards to Historic and adding new dimensions to game strategies. Players will also be able to craft these cards using Wildcards of the appropriate rarity after this date.


Bundles may be purchased for 25,000 Gold or 4,000 gems. Depth art card styles of all 25 cards will be available in assorted bundles in the store also on May 27.


Historic Anthology 5 cards are legal in the Historic format upon release.


Previews began on May 6—take a look at where you'll find more cards in Historic Anthology 5 all this week:


* May 6: [Wizards Summer of Legend article](https://admin.magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/news/welcome-summer-legend-2021-05-06/#historic-anthology-5-preview)
* May 10: [Bleeding Cool](https://bleedingcool.com/games/historic-anthology-v-on-magic-arena-adds-these-four-new-cards/)
* May 11: [Destructoid](https://www.destructoid.com/stories/exclusive-here-s-a-look-at-some-upcoming-magic-arena-historic-anthology-v-cards-628930.phtml)

* May 13: [Comicbook.com](https://comicbook.com/)
* May 14: [CBR](https://www.cbr.com/)

We'll share the complete card list for Historic Anthology 5 through an article here at DailyMTG on Monday, May 17.
