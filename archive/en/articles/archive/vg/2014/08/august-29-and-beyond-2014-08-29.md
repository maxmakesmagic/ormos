August 29 and Beyond
====================

Mike Rosenberg

Mike Rosenberg is a writer and gamer and has been part of the Magic text coverage team since 2011. These days, he spends less time globetrotting around the world for Grand Prix and more time in Renton, WA, working as the organized play content specialist for Wizards of the Coast.


August 29, 2014
 










There's a lot of *Magic* to watch this weekend around the web, so let's dive right in to the kind of content you can find on YouTube and Twitch.TV over the next few days.


*Spellslingers* Featuring Luis Scott-Vargas


*Magic*Pro Tour [Hall of Fame](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/luis-scott-vargas) member and [ChannelFireball.com](http://store.channelfireball.com/landing) founder Luis Scott-Vargas *clashes* with professional video gamer and *Spellslingers* host Sean Plott with the *Magic 2015*Clash Packs in this week's episode. The two have played against each other before, back at [Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze*](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/ptdgm13/welcome). Will Sean get the upper hand with the Fury deck, or will Scott-Vargas score the victory with the Fate deck?


You can watch this week's episode of *Spellslingers* below.


<//www.youtube.com/embed/artBJGT7MrQ>

StarCityGames.com Open Series Featuring the Invitational in New Jersey



[StarCityGames.com](http://www.starcitygames.com/) is hosting its quarterly Invitational tournament this weekend. In this multi-day event, qualified players will compete in a grand total of fourteen rounds, including seven rounds each of Standard and Legacy. The Top 8 competitors will then battle it out with the Legacy decks they brought for the event.


In addition to the Invitational, SCG will also be running its Open Series tournaments alongside the big spectacle.


Hosts Patrick Sullivan, Mattias Hunt, and Cedric Phillips will be bringing you all the latest from the tournament floor for all three days, starting today at 12 p.m. ET/7 p.m. GMT.


You can watch the Invitational Friday, Saturday, and Sunday on the [SCGLive Twitch.TV channel](http://www.twitch.tv/scglive).
