Innistrad Roundtable: Winning Thoughts
======================================



 Posted in [The Week That Was](/en/articles/columns/the-week-that-was-archive)
 on September 29, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Brian David-Marshall










*Innistrad* is finally here. [Launch Parties](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/release-facts) are taking place the world wide and there are even even some big Standard events with the [SCG Open in Indianapolis](http://www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=179921392051822) and the [Grudge Match](http://www.mtggrudgematch.com/) in Philadelphia. Last weekend I had a chance to play in a Prerelease tournament at New World Manga in Livingston, NJ and went 4-0 but would have had a great time regardless of the circumstances. The store was great and all of the people I met there were fantastic, and I look forward to playing there again.

Here is the Sealed Deck pool that I opened and at the end of the article I will share the deck that I ended up building out of it. What would you do with the following selection of cards?
