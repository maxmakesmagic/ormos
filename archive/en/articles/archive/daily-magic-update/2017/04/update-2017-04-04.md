1. Lay Bare the Heart | [Riot Pixels](http://nukes.riotpixels.com/mtg-1/)

Another powerful discard spell to add to black's arsenal. Let's do the math. There are [16,391 cards legal in Vintage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?format=%5b%22Vintage%22%5d). Of those, [572 are lands](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?format=%5b%22Vintage%22%5d&type=+%5bLand%5d) and [784 are Legendary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?format=%5b%22Vintage%22%5d&type=+%5bLegendary%5d), and [40 are both](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&type=+%5b%22Legendary%22%5d+%5b%22Land%22%5d&format=+%5b%22Vintage%22%5d). That means the hit rate on this card across all legal *Magic* cards is nearly 92%, just a touch worse than [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) at 95%. Not bad. Also, math.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Black-Red Zombies](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/displaydeck.php?DeckID=112467)

So, we know *Amonkhet* is bringing some Zombies with it and we know there will be creatures that turn into Zombie tokens (embalm) from the graveyard. Hmm, if only we had a graveyard-focused Zombie deck waiting to . . . oh, what's this?! Exactly the thing I was just describing! How weird.


Matt Megaw finished 5th at a StarCityGames.com Invitational Qualifier with this list, and it's pretty close to the stock list prior to *Amonkhet*. [Haunted Dead](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haunted+Dead), [Cryptbreaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptbreaker), and [Voldaren Pariah](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voldaren+Pariah) all make excellent discard outlets for creatures with embalm, and [Insolent Neonate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Insolent+Neonate) is certainly replaceable. This deck would also make a ton of sense being headed by [Hazoret the Fervent](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/making-magic/amonkhet-down-business-part-1-2017-04-03), the recently revealed red God.
