**Leagues on the Horizon!**


Chris Kiritz, Digital Product Manager for *Magic Online*, returns to talk about when we're launching Leagues (it's very soon!) and what we're launching (Constructed Leagues!). It's been a long journey, and we're excited to finally be able to release Leagues to the public.


Check out Chris's [full article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-leagues-update-2015-08-17) for all of the details about our plan to roll out Leagues.

***Magic Origins* Standard Championship Qualifiers Begin August 19**


Today, August 18, is the last day of the [*Magic Origins* Limited Championship Qualifiers](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-origins-championship-events-2015-08-03#ltdqualifier). After tomorrow's downtime, *Magic Origins* Standard Championship Qualifiers begin at 12:30 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7)! Four or five wins in a Qualifier Event will get you an invitation to the *Magic Origins* Standard Championship Finals on August 29!


Check out the *Magic Origins* Championship [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-origins-championship-events-2015-08-03) for full event details.

At the upcoming 2015 *Magic* World Championship, one of the formats is *Modern Masters (2015 Edition)* Draft. Celebrate the upcoming Championship by Drafting *Modern Masters (2015 Edition)* alongside World Championship competitors on *Magic Online* with a *Modern Masters (2015 Edition)* queue running the week of August 19-26!


See below for the event details. Enjoy them and best of luck!

**Community Cup Team Rosters Revealed**


It's tough to pick just eight people from our awesome *Magic* community to be on the Community Team but, after much deliberation, we've chosen [eight people](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/2015-community-cup-2015-08-12) to stand as paragons, representing what makes the *Magic* community so great to be a part of. Their competitors, the dastardly Wizards Team, have also announced a team roster and are currently mid-training montage at an undisclosed location deep within the bowels of Wizards HQ.


If you missed the roster for the Community teams, get brief bios and learn more about their contributions to the community [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/2015-community-cup-2015-08-12) on DailyMTG!

**New Episode of Standard Super League Tonight**


Every Tuesday through September 9, at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7) (except for a quick break the week of the August 25), the newest episode of the Standard Super League's second season airs live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic). Check out Randy Buehler's latest article for a preview at tonight's matches!


If you missed it the first time around, Randy Buehler wrote an [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/standard-super-league-episode-9-recap-2015-06-30) that details how three Super League mini-seasons will qualify players for a Super League Championship later this year. Who will emerge as the Super League grand champion? Find out by watching along!

**Sign Up for the *Magic Online* Newsletter**


The *Magic Online* Newsletter contains a wealth of personalized information you can't find anywhere else. Published monthly, the newsletter is a quick snapshot of your *Magic Online* history, and a top news and announcements list of info you shouldn't miss.


In each issue you'll find personalized stats, such as:



* A lifetime total of booster packs you've won
* MOPR promo cards you've earned through both store and event activity
* Current MOCS season qualifier points
* A personalized factoid
* Year-to-Date MOCS Qualifier Points, by format


And we're always looking for ways to improve this resource! Get directions on enrolling [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online-general-resources/magic-onlines-news-and-help-resources-2013-10-14).

***Magic Online* Judge Open on Saturday, August 29**


The *Magic Online* Judge Open (MOJO) pits the folks who know *Magic*'s rules the best—*Magic* judges—in a head-to-head competition. Applications for the final MOJO event of the year, the Iwazaru MOJO, are still open. If there's a judge in your life (including yourself) who hasn't signed up yet, apply now!


[Apply for the upcoming MOJO.](http://apps.magicjudges.org/events/3496/)


Check out the decklists from the [August 8, 2015](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/sealed-ori-block-judge-open-2015-08-10) and the [August 14, 2015](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/sealed-ori-block-judge-open-2015-08-17) *Magic Online* Judge Open.


[Learn more](http://blogs.magicjudges.org/) about being a *Magic* judge.


### *Magic Online* Judge Open



**Start Time:** Saturday, August 29, 11:00 a.m. All times are Pacific (for UTC, add 7 hours).  
**Location:** Scheduled Events  
**Entry Options:**


* Option 1: Invite Only

**Product:** *Magic Online* will provide 6 Phantom *Magic Origins* booster packs  

All Boosters are Phantom product and will not be added to a player's collection at the conclusion of the event  
**Size:** 8-1024 players  
**Play Style:** Swiss  
**Format:** Sealed  
**Duration:** 20 minutes deck-building time. Five rounds, each round up to 50 minutes.  
**Prizes:**





| Match Wins | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5 Wins | 5 *Magic Origins* booster packs and a non-premium [Moat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moat) promo | 0 |
| 4 Wins | 4 *Magic Origins* booster packs and a non-premium [Moat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moat) promo | 0 |
| 3 Wins | 3 *Magic Origins* booster packs | 0 |
| 2 Wins | 2 *Magic Origins* booster packs | 0 |
| 1 Win | 1 *Magic Origins* booster pack | 0 |

**Note:** All participants will receive a Grand Arbiter Augustin IV Avatar.

***Magic Origins* Redemption Information**


*Magic Origins* becomes available for redemption on Wednesday, August 26, about one month after its digital release. If you collect the whole set on *Magic Online*, you can exchange it for a full, paper version! If you're interested in redeeming *Magic Origins*, don't wait too long—because the set won't be available to redeem forever.


[Learn more](http://magic.wizards.com/en/gameinfo/products/magiconline/redemption) about *Magic Online* redemption.

**Upcoming Extended Downtime on Wednesday, August 26**


There will be an extended downtime on Wednesday, August 26, for *Magic Online*. Events close at 12:00 a.m. Pacific (07:00 UTC), midnight, and store activity and trades end at 2:45 a.m. Pacific (09:45 UTC) leading up to the system coming down at 3:00 a.m. Pacific (10:00 UTC). The system is estimated to return from downtime at 1:00 p.m. Pacific (20:00 UTC).

* All tournaments will close Wednesday, August 19, at 4:00 a.m. Pacific (11:00 UTC)
* Store and trade activity will be suspended at approximately 7:45 a.m. Pacific (14:45 UTC)
