Nationals Showcase: United States, France, Italy
================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 25, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast













|  |
| --- |
| Nationals |
| Magic 2010 |

The Road to Worlds begins in earnest for three major **Magic**-playing countries this weekend. Invitations to Rome and the opportunity to represent your country on the biggest **Magic** stage are up for grabs. How will ***Magic** 2010* change Standard? With Pro Points on the line for top finishers, these are sure to showcase exicting play by some of the biggest names in **Magic**. Follow along all weekend for the top stories from the feature match pits, the shifts in the metagame, and much more!


### 
[Gindy Gets U.S. Lead!](/en/events/coverage/gindy-gets-us-lead)


Charles Gindy defeated Adam Yurchick in the finals at the United States National Championship, adding a new trophy next to his Pro Tour-Hollywood hardware. Joining Gindy and Yurchick on the national team is Todd Anderson, who beat **Magic Online** sensation Brad "Fffreak" Nelson in the 3rd/4th playoff. 


### [Mongilardi, Elves Victorious in France](/en/events/coverage/mongilardi-leads-france)


Gilles Mongilardi defeated Antoine Menard in the finals to capture the title of France national champion. The three-man team is rounded out by Guillaume Matignon.


### [Ravagli Ravages Italy](/en/events/coverage/ravagli-ravages-italy)


Host country for this year's World Championship, Italy determined who would defend the home turf come November. Luca Ravagli will be heading up the team as national champion, and is joined by Marco Monetto and Samuele Estratti.
