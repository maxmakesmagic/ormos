The 8-0 Decks of Grand Prix Lyon 2018
=====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 18, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











2,058 players had entered the tournament in total, but only thirteen of them escaped the first day's eight rounds with perfect records of 8-0: Arthur Brocsko, Matic Penko, (20) Márcio Carvalho, Malte Smits, Kim Ströh, Florian Koch, Davide Cappiello, and Mehdi Chelil (pictured below, left to right) as well as Leonardo Dalmasso, Julien Henry, Vjeran Horvat, Raul Vallejo, and Ricardo Sanchez (not pictured).


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gplyo18/LYO_8-0decks.jpg)


Burn, Lantern, Tron, and Mardu Pyromancer each were played by two of them, but singletons of White-Blue Control, Abzan, Grixis Death's Shadow, Titan Breach, and Jeskai Breach still brought the total to nine different archetypes represented!
