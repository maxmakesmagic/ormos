Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Zombie Emerge](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-07-05)

Another deck that was overshadowed by [Aetherworks Marvel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) making a comeback, the blue-red Zombie Emerge deck (fueled by [Fevered Visions](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fevered+Visions)) has started making its presence felt again.


For those not familiar, the deck utilizes the Stitchwings from Innistrad to power out [Prized Amalgam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam)s and provide food for [Elder Deep-Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elder+Deep-Fiend)s. [Cathartic Reunion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cathartic+Reunion) and [Tormenting Voice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tormenting+Voice) drop cards in the graveyard while providing fuel for later. [Fiery Temper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper) serves as the deck's [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt), since you always have discard outlets.


[Fevered Visions](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fevered+Visions) is the engine that makes it all go, providing extra cards to pitch, extra damage, and free wins versus control decks. It also goes really well with [Elder Deep-Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elder+Deep-Fiend) tapping down lands during their upkeep. It's difficult to get rid of cards in your hand when your lands are all tapped down.


The kicker is that the deck probably gets to use [Kozilek's Return](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kozilek%27s+Return) better than any other deck. One, it drops it neatly into the graveyard at will. And two, [Elder Deep-Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elder+Deep-Fiend) makes frequent appearances thanks to all the card draw. And, finally, the [Sanctum of Ugin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sanctum+of+Ugin)s mean you get a relatively steady stream of Eldrazi.


My favorite part of this deck is the story it tells. A [Tormenting Voice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tormenting+Voice) maddens you, you experience [Fevered Visions](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fevered+Visions), and, suddenly, you get the idea to reanimate the dead. But they bring more dead with them, maybe more than you intended. And all that reanimating catches the attention of an [Elder Deep-Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elder+Deep-Fiend), who, depending on its temperament, burns everything down. Then . . . you do it again.


I have no idea how that story starts if you're having a [Cathartic Reunion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cathartic+Reunion).
