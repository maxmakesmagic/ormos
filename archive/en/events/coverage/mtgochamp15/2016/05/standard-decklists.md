Standard Decklists
==================



 Posted in [2015 MAGIC ONLINE CHAMPIONSHIP](/en/events/coverage/mtgochamp15)
 on May 13, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











It was not long ago when the community looked at the potential combo of [Eldrazi Displacer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Displacer), [Brood Monitor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brood+Monitor), and [Zulaport Cutthroat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zulaport+Cutthroat) and thought of it as merely cute. Now, it has established itself as one of the big decks of the Standard format. Four of this year's competitors brought 4-Color Rite, the deck that uses [Cryptolith Rite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptolith+Rite) alongside [Brood Monitor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brood+Monitor) and [Eldrazi Displacer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Displacer), for Friday's four rounds of Standard.


Not to be left in the shadows is what Luis Scott-Vargas has referred to as the best deck in Standard in the LSV's Keys segment in our live video: Green-White Tokens. Three players have arrived with that. From there, choices start to get a bit more off the wall, with Sam Black packing perhaps the most interesting deck of the tournament, followed closely behind Jonathan Anghelescu who went with an unusual selection to tackle the expected metagame.
