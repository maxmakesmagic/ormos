==================================================

 on May 1, 2014

Standard Format Recap
---------------------


[Top](#top)

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/ss_standard.jpg)  

Having a smart sideboard provided Mini\_Gnarls access to potent threats in the control matchup.
Player Mini\_Gnarls won the Standard event’s top spot playing a mono-red aggro deck. His final round opponent, Vault2, played an Esper control deck that proved to be just a step slower.


**Standard Game One**  

Early in game one, Mini\_Gnarls saw both a [Firedrinker Satyr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firedrinker+Satyr) and a [Rakdos Cackler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rakdos+Cackler) take their [Last Breath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Last+Breath) before they could deal any damage. This didn’t give Vault2 too much breathing room, though, as a second Cackler and back-to-back copies of [Ash Zealot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ash+Zealot) quickly reduced his life total to 8. Digging for an answer, Vault2 spent consecutive turns on casting [Sphinx’s Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%E2%80%99s+Revelation). Hoping to finally take control of the game, Vault2 then put his faith in the soldier-generating powers of [Elspeth, Sun’s Champion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Sun%E2%80%99s+Champion). Seeing his opponent tap out to summon this powerful ally, Mini\_Gnarls seized the opportunity and unleashed two [Lightning Strike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Strike)s targeting Vault2. This meant that next turn using [Firefist Striker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firefist+Striker)’s battalion trigger, Mini\_Gnarls could easily take the game.


**Standard Game Two**  

Game two got off to a similar start with [Firedrinker Satyr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firedrinker+Satyr) drawing a [Last Breath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Last+Breath). However, Mini\_Gnarls dropped in a [Hammer of Purphoros](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hammer+of+Purphoros) and wasn’t shy at all about turning mountains into 3/3 threats for Vault2 to deal with. Smartly playing around [Detention Sphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere), Mini\_Gnarls didn't over extend with Golems tokens. Vault2 was already at 8 life before he could successfully land his first creature of the match, which helpfully had lifelink, [Blood Baron of Vizkopa](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Baron+of+Vizkopa). However, Mini\_Gnarls had more than what was necessary to win the game on turn 6: [Burning-Tree Emissary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning-Tree+Emissary), [Lightning Strike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Strike), and a sideboarded-in [Skullcrack](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullcrack).

Limited Format Recap
--------------------


[Top](#top)

**Limited Game One**  

Player fukusin777 won out in the Limited event with an evasive white/black deck. His opponent in the finals, Esrin, drafted a blue/green deck with a splash of red for [Xenagos, God of Revels](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos%2C+God+of+Revels). Esrin started strong in game one with [Sedge Scorpion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sedge+Scorpion), [Satyr Wayfinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Satyr+Wayfinder), and [Divination](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Divination). Fukusin777 then tried hard to race his opponent’s growing [Pheres-Band Tromper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pheres-Band+Tromper) with [Heliod’s Emissary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heliod%E2%80%99s+Emissary) backed by [Whip of Erebros](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whip+of+Erebros). However, Esrin was ready with an [Unravel the AEther](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unravel+the+AEther) to shuffle the whip back into their opponent’s library. From there Esrin was able to easily win the game on the back of his growing creature horde.


**Limited Game Two**  

In the second game of the match, fukusin777 was the one with the strong start helping his [Cavalry Pegasus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cavalry+Pegasus) successfully complete the [Ordeal of Heliod](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ordeal+of+Heliod) by removing Esrin’s [Vaporkin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vaporkin) with an [Eye Gouge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eye+Gouge). Things looked progressively darker for Esrin as the life total gap widened to 35-1 before he was able to stabilize behind creatures like the monstrous [Nessian Asp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nessian+Asp) and the life gain of [Horizon Chimera](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Horizon+Chimera). The life totals even closed to a much more reasonable 18-2 before fukusin777 was able to put the game away with several well timed activations of [Scholar of Athreos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scholar+of+Athreos).


**Standard Final Game**  

In the final game of the match, fukusin777 seemed ready to take control again early with a [Fleshmad Steed](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleshmad+Steed) enchanted with [Ordeal of Heliod](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ordeal+of+Heliod). Though this time we saw Esrin’s [Griptide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Griptide) used to solve the early big-creature problem. Undeterred, fukusin777 was able to get an airborne assault going courtesy of [Ornitharch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ornitharch) and [Cavalry Pegasus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cavalry+Pegasus). On turn 10, the fliers proved too much for Esrin to hold off, and he conceded, making Fukusin777 the *Born of the Gods*  Limited Championship Winner!
