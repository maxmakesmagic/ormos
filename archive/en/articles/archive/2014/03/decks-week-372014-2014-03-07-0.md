Decks of the Week: 3/7/2014
===========================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on March 7, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












What a great couple weeks it's been for Christian Seibold, who not only Top 8ed [Pro Tour *Born of the Gods*](/en/events/coverage/mclaren-weaves-victory-valencia) on February 23, but last weekend he and the Gräfensteiner brothers, Daniel and Tobias, took down Team Limited [Grand Prix Barcelona](/en/events/coverage/bold-band-brothers-best-barcelona). Across the globe, more than 900 players descended on Melbourne to compete in the [largest-ever Grand Prix in Australia](/en/events/coverage/nam-annihilates-melbourne), which was ultimately won by Nam Sung-Wook. Congratulations, Christian, Daniel, Tobias, and Nam! The format for GP Melbourne was Standard, the same format as the Pro Tour *Journey into Nyx* Qualifier season, which ends this weekend. Find out if one of the last PTQs for the season is near you with [this handy link](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#jou). Good luck; have fun!
