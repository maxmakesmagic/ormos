![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/gpnj19/t8-dylan-gellis.jpg)


**Name:** Dylan Gellis


**Age:** 20


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Fulton, Maryland


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Top 8ed Grand Prix Sacramento, back-to-back Limited Grand Prix Top 8s


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Theater of Horror was nuts, played Rakdos splashing White


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Blue White splashing Black, [Gateway Sneak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gateway+Sneak) was my favorite card, 3-0


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Lawmage's Binding](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lawmage%27s+Binding) was my favorite. In fact, I liked it so much I played four.


**What is your preferred draft strategy for this set?**


I mean, clearly Azorius. Why else would I draft it twice?

**Name:** Bryan Mabie


**Age:** 38


**Occupation:** Private investigator


**Hometown:** Raleigh, North Carolina


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Pro Tour Kyoto competitor, PPTQ winner


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


I played Jund, went 8-1, good cards were [Electrodominance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrodominance), [Rakdos Firewheeler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rakdos+Firewheeler)


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


I played Simic, went 3-0, MVPs were [Zegana, Utopian Speaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zegana%2C+Utopian+Speaker), [Guardian Project](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Guardian+Project), [Biomancer's Familiar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Biomancer%27s+Familiar)


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


I played Rakdos, went 2-0-1, MVPs were [Sphinx of the Guildpact](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx+of+the+Guildpact), 4 [Goblin Gathering](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Gathering)s, and a bunch of Py-off cards


**What is your preferred draft strategy for this set?**


Pick a guild and try to stick with it, if possible.
