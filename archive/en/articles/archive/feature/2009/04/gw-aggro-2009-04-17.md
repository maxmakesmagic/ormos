NimbleRooster teamed the best green and white had together to create this deck and ride it to the Top 8 of the **Magic Online** Premier Event circuit. Featuring a healthy dosage of early beaters that are both green and white, Rooster was able to capitalize with cards like [Sigil Blessing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sigil+Blessing) to put his deck over the top. Check out those [Shield of the Oversoul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Shield%2Bof%2Bthe%2BOversoul) in the sideboard! They'll make nearly any target in the deck a hard to deal with monster!
