Card Submissions
================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 19, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/tiago_chan.jpg)

**Tiago Chan**  

 Denying Channel  

 Land  

 T: Add ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif) to your mana pool.  

 2UU, Discard Denying Channel: Counter target spell.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/paulo_vitor.jpg)

**Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa**  

 Prolific Visionary  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220123123600im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/green.gif)  

 Creature -- Human Wizard  

 2/2  

 Play with the top card of your library revealed.  

 If the top card of your library is a land, you may play that card.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/willy_edel.jpg)

**Willy Edel**  

 Gambling Mage  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/Symbols/Symbol_BR_mana.gif)![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/Symbols/Symbol_BR_mana.gif)  

 Creature -- Human Wizard  

 2/2  

 Protection from white  

 As an additional cost to play Gambling Mage, discard a card at random.  

 When Gambling Mage comes into play, search your library for a card, then shuffle your library and put that card on top of it. Flip a coin. If you win the flip, draw a card.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/evan_erwin.jpg)

**Evan Erwin**  

 Cap’n Tickles  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220123123600im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/green.gif)![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/Symbols/Symbol_rg_mana.gif)![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/Symbols/Symbol_rg_mana.gif)  

 Creature -- Human Pirate  

 2/1  

 Haste  

 When Cap’n Tickles comes into play, put two 1/1 red and green Citizen creature tokens into play.  

 Citizen creatures get +1/+0 and have haste.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/rich_hoaen.jpg)

**Rich Hoaen**  

 Inebriation  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)  

 Enchantment  

 Whenever you play a spell, return that card to your hand and put a random card from your hand onto the stack.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/craig_jones.jpg)

**Craig Jones**  

 Psychotic Professor  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130159im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/red.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130159im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/red.gif)  

 Creature -- Human Wizard  

 2/1  

 Whenever an instant or sorcery spell you control deals damage to an opponent, search your library for a red instant card and reveal it. Shuffle your library, then put the revealed card on top of it.  

 Sacrifice Psychotic Professor: Shuffle your library, then reveal the top card of your library. Psychotic Professor deals damage to target opponent equal to the converted mana cost of the revealed card. You lose the game at end of turn.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/frank_karsten.jpg)

**Frank Karsten**  

 Eye of the Beholder  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)  

 Creature -- Human Wizard  

 2/1  

 Each player plays with the top card of his or her library revealed and with his or her hand revealed.  

 At the beginning of your upkeep, target player puts the top card of his or her library on the bottom of his or her library.  

 Sacrifice Eye of the Beholder: Target player shuffles his or her library.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/raphael_levy.jpg)

**Raphael Levy**  

 Mindsear Battlemage  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227195556im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/black.gif)  

 Creature -- Human Wizard  

 2/1  

 When Mindsear Battlemage comes into play, you may pay ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227195556im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/black.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130159im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/red.gif)and name a card. If you do, that player reveals his or her hand and discards all cards with that name, then Mindsear Battlemage deals 2 damage to that player for each card he or she discarded this way. Otherwise, that player reveals his or her hand.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/stephen_menendian.jpg)

**Steve Menendian**  

 Twilight Tutor  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227195556im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/black.gif)  

 Sorcery  

 If ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227125855im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/white.gif) was spent to play Twilight Tutor, search your library for an enchantment card and reveal it. If ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif) was spent, search your library for an instant card and reveal it. If ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130159im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/red.gif) was spent, search your library for a sorcery card and reveal it. If ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220123123600im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/green.gif) was spent, search your library for a creature card and reveal it. Put a card revealed this way into your hand, then shuffle your library.






 >![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/gabriel_nassif.jpg)

**Gabriel Nassif**  

 Bob the Dog  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161225105850im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless2.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)  

 Creature -- Hound Wizard  

 1/3  

 Flash  

 Flying  

 When Bob the Dog comes into play, draw a card, then discard a card. If ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227195556im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/black.gif) was spent to play Bob the Dog, target player discards a card. If ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227125855im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/white.gif) was spent to play Bob the Dog, target creature gains protection from the color of your choice until end of turn.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/shuhei_nakamura.jpg)

**Shuhei Nakamura**  

 Kou-Bash  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)  

 Creature - Human Jester  

 0/0  

 As Kou-Bash comes into play, you may reveal a creature card from your hand with converted mana cost 3 or less or choose a creature card in a graveyard with a converted mana cost 3 or less.  

 Kou-Bash comes into play as a copy of the revealed or chosen card






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/antoine_ruel.jpg)

**Antoine Ruel**  

 Reivilo  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220123123600im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/green.gif)  

 Creature -- Frog  

 1/1  

 When Reivilo comes into play, draw two cards for each other permanent named Reivilo. If you draw six or more cards this way, you win the game.  

 Reivilo gets +1/+1 for each other permanent named Reivilo.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/kenji_tsumura.jpg)

**Kenji Tsumura**  

 I Hate Pacts  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)  

 Enchantment  

 Players skip their upkeep steps.  

 At the beginning of your draw step, put a kogamo counter on I Hate Pacts.  

 Remove three kogamo counters from I Hate Pacts: Draw a card.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/guillaume_wafotapa.jpg)

**Guillaume Wafo-Tapa**  

 Tropical Faerie  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220123123600im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/green.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)  

 Creature -- Faerie Wizard  

 1/1  

 Flash  

 Flying  

 Whenever a Forest comes into play under your control, you may put a 1/1 blue and green Faerie creature token into play.  

 Whenever an Island comes into play under your control, you may untap up to X target permanents, where X is the number of Faeries you control.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/jelger_wiegersma.jpg)

**Jelger Wiegersma**  

 Greatest Gargadon  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130050im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless3.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)  

 Creature -- Human Wizard  

 2/4  

 When Greatest Gargadon comes into play, choose instant or sorcery. Your opponents can’t play cards of the chosen type. You can’t play cards of the type that wasn’t chosen.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/mi07/shota_yasooka.jpg)

**Shota Yasooka**  

 Avatar of Fire and Ice  
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130159im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/red.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130159im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/red.gif)  

 Creature -- Avatar  

 4/4  

 Discard a blue card, Discard a card, Pay 1 life: Counter target spell.  

 Sacrifice two mountains: Avatar of Fire and Ice deals 4 damage to target creature or player.
