Minotaur in the House!
======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 16, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











As you know, we have a [Shivan Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shivan+Dragon) in our lobby. It looks like this:


  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana300/Dragon0.jpg)  
(Well, that's what it looked like in our old building; we had to [move it across the street](/en/articles/archive/wotc-dragon-moves-2005-10-17) a few years ago.)


But here's something you might not know. That dragon was sculpted by Kim Gram, who stopped by the office last week. And here's what *she* looks like:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/146_KimGram.jpg)  
So our dragon appears to have been sculpted by a minotaur. Neat!
