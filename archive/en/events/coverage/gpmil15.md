TOP 8 COVERAGE



Swedish *Magic Online* superstar Magnus Lantto claims his first live title and joins Dimitriy Butakov among the top level of players making the step from online to live *Magic*. His Birthing Pod deck took him to victory defeating Jeskai Ascendancy and Primeval Titan alike on his way to the title. In the end not even Italian Niccolò Bellini and the Birthing Pod mirror match could stand in his way.


Another exciting weekend of *Magic* is over, and the Modern format has gotten it's new star players and decks. In a field flooded with Delver of Secrets, Scapeshift and Affinity it was Birthing Pod that took down the title in the hands of Magnus Lantto of Sweden.


An impressive 1760 players came to enjoy the Modern format, and surely speculate about what cards we will see in *Modern Masters* 2 in May. 210 of them made the second day of competition, where a top 8 finally fought for the title late on Sunday evening.


This marks the end of the Grand Prix year in Europe, and *Magic* fans and Swedes alike will celebrate X-mas with a big smile on their face, having enjoyed some of the best *Magic* Europe has to offer. 


With that we thank those who followed our coverage this weekend, and one final time we congratulate Swede Magnus Lantto on his victory. Or as the victory howl sounds in the south of Sweden where he lives:


Ijaaaaaadåååååååååååå!

DAY 1 COVERAGE



Day 1 of Grand Prix Milan 2015 is in the books, and even through all the vicissitudes of life it did turn out to be a great day for *Magic*! We've seen interesting new takes on familiar deck archetypes and in the feature match area we've been witness to close calls, difficult plays, hilarious blowouts, and exciting turnarounds all day. 


Modern continues to be as vibrant a format as ever and the massive turnout only gave further evidence to its ongoing popularity.
