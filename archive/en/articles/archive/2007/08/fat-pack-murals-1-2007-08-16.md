Fat Pack Murals #1
==================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 16, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1399_fatpack_pointatmural.jpg)The Fat Pack, that chunky little miracle of a **Magic** product, is always chock full of goodies. On top of all the **Magic** cards and storage, the fat pack contains a piece of original art that captures some of the highlights of the set.


Every Fat Pack since *Ninth Edition* has had a mural that helps you understand some of the themes and mechanics of the set—HA. No, who are we kidding? They're pictures of giant monsters... who (in general) just FIGHT! Today we take a look at a few of these murals.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1399_fatpackart_9e.jpg)*Ninth Edition fat pack mural: [Serra Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serra+Angel) vs. [Hypnotic Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypnotic+Specter)  
Art by Greg Staples*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1399_fatpackart_rav.jpg)*Ravnica: City of Guilds fat pack mural: [Savra, Queen of the Golgari](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Savra%252C%2BQueen%2Bof%2Bthe%2BGolgari) vs. [Savra, Boros Archangel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Savra%252C%2BBoros%2BArchangel) vs. [Szadek, Lord of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Szadek%252C%2BLord%2Bof%2BSecrets) vs. [Tolsimir Wolfblood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Tolsimir%2BWolfblood)  

Art by Zoltan Boros & Gabor Szikszai* 
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1399_fatpackart_gpt.jpg)*Guildpact fat pack mural: [Niv-Mizzet, the Firemind](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Niv-Mizzet%252C%2Bthe%2BFiremind), [Angel of Despair](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Despair), and [Borborygmos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Borborygmos)  
Art by Parente*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1399_fatpackart_dis.jpg)*Dissension fat pack mural: [Rakdos the Defiler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Rakdos%2Bthe%2BDefiler), [Grand Arbiter Augustin IV](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Grand%2BArbiter%2BAugustin%2BIV), and [Momir Vig, Simic Visionary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Momir%2BVig%252C%2BSimic%2BVisionary)  

Art by Zoltan Boros & Gabor Szikszai*
