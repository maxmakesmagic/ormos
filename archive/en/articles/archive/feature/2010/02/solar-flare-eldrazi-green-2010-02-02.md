*From **Magic** R&D intern Zac Hill:*


The first is basically a Solar Flare deck that wants to throw as many huge guys at you as possible, while staying alive with Walls and two-mana blue disruption. The Elf list is basically Eldrazi Green with more hyper-combo-finish Growths. The Solar Flare deck was a little soft against any deck able to deploy a flurry of creatures early but performed well against Jund. The Eldrazi Green deck felt very powerful and was loads of fun to play!
