Round Thirteen Feature Match:
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 14, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_589.png)
By Neil Parker











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpana03/fm9_1.jpg)Gabe Walls is running a Rock deck featuring everybody's favorite [Troll Ascetic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Troll+Ascetic) while Mike Abraham is playing the [Hermit Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hermit+Druid) deck capable of a turn two kill. Both players were 9-3 at the start of the round with a loss knocking the loser out of Top 8 contention. Gabe won the all-decisive die roll and decided to play first. After shuffling each other's deck and a brief dispute about where on the playmat Mike should keep his library, the match was underway.

### Game 1

Gabe played out a [Llanowar Wastes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Wastes) and cast [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) for [Hermit Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hermit+Druid). Mike revealed a hand of [Sutured Ghoul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sutured+Ghoul), [Krosan Cloudscraper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Cloudscraper), [Vampiric Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor), [Mystical Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor), [Ancient Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb), [City of Traitors](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Traitors), and [Underground River](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underground+River). Mike drew his card, dropped the [Underground River](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underground+River) on the table and sent the turn back to Gabe.

Gabe played a [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) off of another [Llanowar Wastes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Wastes). At the end of Gabe's turn Mike cast his [Mystical Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor), fetching up a buried alive. Mike accidentally put the [Mystical Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor) on top of his library, and the [Buried Alive](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive) in his graveyard.

![Troll Ascetic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Troll+Ascetic)\*Judge goes to fix the situation\*  
 "No, let it goâ€¦ it'll be funny" - Gabe Walls

 \*everybody laughs\*

When played the [Ancient Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb), cast the buried alive and passed the turn before searching his deck Gabe's eyes lit up, however the judge allowed Mike to search his deck for a pair of [Krosan Cloudscraper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Cloudscraper)s and a [Sutured Ghoul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sutured+Ghoul). At the end of Mike's turn Gabe cast a [Vampiric Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor).

"I'm gonna actually use it though" - Gabe Walls

Gabe drew his card off of the Tutor and sent his elf into the red zone, then flashed back a [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) and knocked the [Vampiric Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor) from Mike's hand, which now also had a [Dragon Breath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon+Breath) in it. He then played a [Phyrexian Negator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Negator) and passed the turn.

Mike's next turn saw him play a [City of Traitors](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Traitors), play a morph tapping his [City of Traitors](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Traitors) and Ancient tomb leaving his [Underground River](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underground+River) untapped.

Gabe drew for his turn and played out a [Troll Ascetic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Troll+Ascetic). He hit with his Negator and passed the turn. During his end step Mike cast [Mystical Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor) to fetch a [Corpse Dance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corpse+Dance), at which point Gabe scooped up his cards.

Mike Abraham-1 Gabe Walls-0

After joking about Mike's thorough shuffling after Game 1, Gabe decided to play first for game 2.

### Game 2

After both players kept their opening hands, Gabe played a [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest), and [Birds of Paradise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) and passed the turn. Mike had a monster of a first turn by playing a [Mox Diamond](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Diamond), [Underground River](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underground+River), and [Hermit Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hermit+Druid).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpana03/fm9_2.jpg)Gabe drew then cast [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), taking a [Reanimate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reanimate) out of Mike's hand, leaving him with [Mystical Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor), [Dragon Breath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon+Breath), and a Krosan Cloudscaper. [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) joined Gabe's board, then it was Mike's turn.

Mike decided to cast [Mystical Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor) for [Reanimate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reanimate) during his upkeep, drawing a "How terrible!" from spectator and Latin Dance champion Osyp Lebedowicz.

Gabe drew for his turn and scooped

Final result: Mike Abraham defeats Gabe Walls 2-0

After the match Mike asked Gabe how tutoring for the [Reanimate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reanimate) on his upkeep was the wrong play, and Gabe told him that it left [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), and [Krosan Reclamation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Reclamation) as outs for Gabe. If Mike had waited until Gabe's end step, Gabe's only out would have been [Krosan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan).
