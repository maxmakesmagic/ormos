Magic Comic Book
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 8, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Here's what you need to know: there's going to be a **Magic: The Gathering** comic book published by IDW. And there will be exclusive, playable, alternate-art cards that you can only get through select issues! For more information, check out [IDW's official announcement](http://idwpublishing.com/news/article/1938).
