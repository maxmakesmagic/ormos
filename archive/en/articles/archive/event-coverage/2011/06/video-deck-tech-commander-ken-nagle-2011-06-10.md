Video Deck Tech: Commander with Ken Nagle
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 11, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 






*Magic: The Gathering Commander*  lead designer Ken Nagle walks Rich Hagon through the new "Political Puppets" deck, one of five 100-card Commander decks going on sale June 17. The deck is built on the idea of giving your permanents to your opponents to your advantage, taking them back at just the right time to deliver the finishing blow.
