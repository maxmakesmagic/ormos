European Preview Streams
========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 11, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Hello, *Magic* fans! *Guilds of Ravnica* is a very special set, and, as such, we'd like to do something different for this preview season in Europe.


We're going to have a variety of streamers from different countries broadcasting *Guilds of Ravnica* card previews!


Below, you'll find all the information you need from our selected streamers so that you don't miss any of the new cards being revealed this week.


Choose your guild, and we'll see you on Twitch!


* Tuesday, September 11 (9:30 p.m. CEST/7:30 p.m. UTC/12:30 p.m. PT) – [Budilicious](http://www.twitch.tv/budilicious) (in Italian)
* Wednesday, September 12 (3:30 p.m. CEST/1:30 p.m. UTC/6:30 a.m. PT) – [La Taberna de Dam](http://www.twitch.tv/damkalloh) (in Spanish)
* Thursday, September 13 (5:30 p.m. CEST/3:30 p.m. UTC/8:30 a.m. PT) – [Igromania](http://www.twitch.tv/igromania) (in Russian)
* Saturday, September 15 (10:00 a.m. CEST/8:00 a.m. UTC/1:00 a.m. PT) – [BestMarmotte @JVTV](http://www.twitch.tv/jvtv) (in French)
* Monday, September 17 (8:00 p.m. CEST/6:00 p.m. UTC/11:00 a.m. PT) – [DoktorFroid](http://www.twitch.tv/DoktorFroid) (in German)
* Tuesday, September 18 (5:00 p.m. CEST/3:00 p.m. UTC/8:00 a.m. PT)– [Evangelion](http://www.twitch.tv/evangelion0) (in Spanish)
