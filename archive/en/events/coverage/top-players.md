TOP PLAYERS
===========


















You’ve heard their names. You’ve seen their decks. You’ve watched them vie for trophies and history. They are the best of the best, the world’s top Magic players who traverse the globe to compete for prizes and their spot among the game’s elite. They live, eat, and sleep Magic, all for the chance to be recognized among the greatest ever to play the game.

The Pro Tour Team Series spotlights the players that have formed teams around the Pro Tour as they compete for Pro Tour invitations, prize money, and a chance to declare themselves the best in a special team finals at the *Magic*World Championship!
