=========================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX LYON 2015](/en/events/coverage/gplyo15)
 on November 1, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke

Did you try and construct your own 40-card decks? Do you think you managed to find a combination that would allow a player to avoid any and all losses?



Well, check your results against these lists. These were the main decks Guillaume Matignon, Vincent Lemoine, Branco Neyrinck, Marc Lazaro, Laurent Favier, and Damien Mace registered yesterday morning:
