In 2006, the Pro Tour made its first venture to the beautiful Honolulu, Hawaii. The winner of that event? Mark "Heezy" Herberholz rocking this red-green aggro deck called "Gruul." This season marks the return of the Pro Tour to the tropical paradise. Make sure to tune in to **magicthegathering.com** in just a few short weeks to catch all the action live!
