Vintage Super League Season 2: Semifinal Decklists
==================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on April 22, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










As described in Randy Buehler's most recent [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-episode-12-2015-04-21), each player in a Vintage Super League playoff match brings three decks, labeled "A," "B," and "C," then pit corresponding decks against each other. Below are the decks that Luis Scott-Vargas and Eric Froehlich brought to their [semifinal match](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cbgJjmncx0I).


You can follow the links below to see the different sets of decklists players used through the Vintage Super League season.


Watch the Vintage Super League finals live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic) Sunday, May 26, at 1:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7). The Standard Super League premieres Tuesday, May 28, on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic). The Standard Super League will air on the same schedule as the Vintage Super League, Tuesday nights at 6:00 p.m. Pacific.
