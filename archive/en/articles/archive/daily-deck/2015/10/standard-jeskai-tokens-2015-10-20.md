Standard Jeskai Tokens
======================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on October 20, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






This week, we are looking at some of the deck techs shown over the weekend during Pro Tour *Battle for Zendikar*. I could tell you all about these decks, how sweet they are, and how they work, but it's probably best you hear it from the experts—the pros who designed and played these exciting decks. First up, the revival of Jeskai Tokens from Team Eureka and Matej Zatlkaj.
