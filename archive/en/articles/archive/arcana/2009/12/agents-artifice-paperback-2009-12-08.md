Agents of Artifice in Paperback
===============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 8, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











So you say you want to read Ari Marmell's *Agents of Artifice*, but you don't want to buy a hardback book? Even one as shiny and nice looking as this?


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/96_book.jpg)  
Well, we have some good news! *Agents of Artifice* is now out in paperback form! It's smaller, lighter, and more portable! And it's only $7.99 in the US ($10.99 in Canada). What a bargain!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/337_books.jpg)
