Semifinals: Olivier Ruel vs. Farid Meraghni
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 4, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_92.png)
By Mark Wraith











Both of these players were already qualified for the World Championship before the event started, so they have more reason than anyone to want to make the World's team. To do so they must win now or in the 3rd/4th play off. Of course, there is also the small matter of 3500 euros and the National Championship at stake.

### Game 1

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natfra03/a920.jpg)Olivier played a second turn [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and Farid had yet another good draw with the [Opposition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opposition) deck, Speculating for [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), and [Roar of the Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) with a Bird of Paradise and a Llanowar Elf in play. The [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) hit two [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell)s and left Olivier with just two [Mirari](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari)s in hand. Both players were in high spirits despite Ruel being in a horrible position in a horrible matchup already.

With nothing in hand of any relevance, Olivier could only watch as Farid played [Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) and [Opposition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opposition), ending the game rather rapidly.

Farid Meraghni 1 – Olivier Ruel 0

Amiel Tenenbaum was obviously an Olivier Ruel supporter as he came up during sideboarding and asked for his [Birds of Paradise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) back, but Farid decided to keep them. Farid jokes about how bad the [Static Orb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Static+Orb) in his sideboard was, as he'd only brought it in once in the whole event against Florent's Slide deck, and it was absolutely useless there as well.

### Game 2

![Quiet Speculation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Quiet+Speculation)Farid played [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) on turn one, taking [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion). There was a debate on turn three when Meraghni made [Call of the Herd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) and then Olivier sacrificed his [Krosan Verge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Verge). Farid tried to flashback his [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), but Olivier claimed that he had used the Verge in his opponent's end step. The judge ruled that Farid hadn't said 'go', and so he was allowed to steal [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel) as well.Olivier used [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) to fetch [Opportunity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opportunity). Meraghni had another [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) and left Olivier holding only lands and [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God). A third Therapy left him with absolutely nothing. Farid didn't have a creature in play, but had drawn lots of cards from [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Olivier drew yet another land Farid cast [Quiet Speculation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation) for three [Call of the Herd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd)s.

Ruel drew another land and took six from the two 3/3s, then an [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel). This didn't help at all as Farid had [Opposition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opposition) so he could tap it with a [Birds of Paradise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) – Farid Meraghni advances to the final.

**Final Result:** Farid Meraghni defeats Olivier Ruel 2 – 0
