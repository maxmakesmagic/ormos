Decks of the Week: 7/22/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on July 22, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











M***agic** 2011* is now legal in Constructed play! With the world focused on M11 Launch Parties last weekend we're just starting to see glimpses of new Standard tech at premier events, but that desire will be met this weekend with not one, not two, but three National Championships featured on **magicthegathering.com**. Tune in starting Friday night for coverage from Australia, France, and Canada to see what the top players in those countries have come up with for the new Standard environment. If you're interested in new formats, the first Daily Event featuring the new Extended format happened this week, so be sure to check it out. And don't forget, ***Magic** 2011* comes to **Magic Online** with prereleases starting July 30 and goes on sale August 2!
