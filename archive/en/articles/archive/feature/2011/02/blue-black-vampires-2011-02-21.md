Mono-black and black-red have been popular color combinations for Vampire decks over the past few years in Standard. For American Christian Calcano, working with a group of European players to come up with something a bit more unique led to the addition of the color blue. With this new take on the deck, Calcano and company can rely on [Unified Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unified+Will) to counter spells while [Creeping Tar Pit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Creeping+Tar+Pit) attacks unblocked. For more information on Pro Tour Paris, where Calcano debuted this version of the deck, visit [here](/en/events/coverage/stark-flies-high-planeswalkers-paris).
