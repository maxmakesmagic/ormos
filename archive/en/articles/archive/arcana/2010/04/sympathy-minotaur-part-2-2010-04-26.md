Sympathy for the Minotaur, Part 2
=================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 26, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Tom Jenkot











Power struggles shift control back and forth from good to evil, worlds literally collide, and the Multiverse rolls on. That is, for everyone except the minotaur.

Here we see the mighty Tahngarth's punch being caught, Steven Seagal style, by none other than Gerrard. Karn sits idly by, clearly not a fan of any minotaur.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_brawl.jpg)[Brawl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brawl) | Illustration by [Edward P. Beard, Jr.](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5Bedward%5D+%5Bbeard%5D+%5Bjr%5D)

Banished! Darkly!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_darkBanishing.jpg)[Dark Banishing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Banishing) | Illustration by [John Matson](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5B&quot;John%20Matson&quot;%5D)

Dominated in the art and [Hand to Hand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Hand%2Bto%2BHand).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_handToHand.jpg)[Hand to Hand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hand+to+Hand) | Illustration by [Carl Frank](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5B&quot;Carl%20Frank&quot;%5D)

Proving that all minotaurs are treated equally badly.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_equalTreatment.jpg)[Equal Treatment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Equal+Treatment) | Illustration by [Greg & Tim Hildebrandt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5Bhildebrandt%5D)

Recipient of a magical shove off a cliff by a horned brother.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_rakaDisciple.jpg)[Raka Disciple](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raka+Disciple) | Illustration by [Arnie Swekel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5B&quot;Arnie%20Swekel&quot;%5D)

When you think about it, the boomerang had to be thrown at a minotaur. There never really was a choice. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_razor.jpg)[Razor Boomerang](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Razor+Boomerang) | Illustration by [Franz Vohwinkel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5B&quot;Franz%20Vohwinkel&quot;%5D)

Suffering through a Phyrexian minefield of hurty purple bolts while dodging hurty Phyrexian swords.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_powerstoneMinefield.jpg)[Powerstone Minefield](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Powerstone+Minefield) | Illustration by [Greg & Tim Hildebrandt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5Bhildebrandt%5D)

Finding out the hard way that dragons are not music fans.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_piper.jpg)[Talruum Piper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Talruum+Piper) | Illustration by [Pete Venters](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5Bpete%5D+%5Bventers%5D)

Ooo, sparkly!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_fleeting.jpg)[Fleeting Distraction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleeting+Distraction) | Illustration by [Kieran Yanner](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5B&quot;Kieran%20Yanner&quot;%5D)

Tethered by a Kor, to a Kor, and impaled by his own sword being ridden, like a surf board, by a Kor.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/436_smite.jpg)[Smite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smite) | Illustration by [Cyril Van Der Haegen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&artist=+%5B&quot;Cyril%20Van%20Der%20Haegen&quot;%5D)

O, [woe is the minotaur](/en/articles/archive/sympathy-minotaur-2009-06-23).
