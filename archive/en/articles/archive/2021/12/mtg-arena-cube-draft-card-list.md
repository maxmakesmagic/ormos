MTG Arena Cube Draft Card List
==============================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on December 8, 2021 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*Updated December 8, 2021*


[Alchemy](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/introducing-alchemy-new-way-play-mtg-arena-2021-12-02) brings new digital-only cards and rebalanced older cards to *MTG Arena*, and in this iteration of the *Arena Cube* you'll find four rebalanced cards appearing in the lists:


* A-Luminarch Aspirant
* A-Alrund's Epiphany
* A-Esika's Chariot
* A-Cosmos Elixir

These cards include the "A-" prefix in their card names to identify them as *MTG Arena*rebalanced versions. You can see these cards and find out the reasoning behind their rebalancing by visiting Donald Smith's article [Alchemy Rebalancing Philosophy](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/alchemy-rebalancing-philosophy-2021-12-02).


Arena Cube Draft Event Details
==============================


Best-of-One
-----------


**Dates**: January 6–20, 2021


**Entry**: 4,000 gold or 600 gems


**Structure**: 7 wins or 3 losses (whichever comes first)


Best-of-Three (Traditional)
---------------------------


**Dates**: June 6–20, 2021


**Entry**: 4,000 gold or 600 gems


**Structure**: Three matches (regardless of wins/losses)
