*Eighth Edition* Rollout - *Alliances*
======================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 23, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












[![Eighth Edition Rollout](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/articles/8EtopBar.gif)](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=mtgcom/8erollout/welcome)  

 
*Discard just got a little riskier.*


### *[Alliances](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/expert/alliances/alliances.asp)* Information:




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Released:** June 1996**Design Team:** Skaff Elias (lead), Jim Lin, Chris Page, Dave Petty**Development Team:** Charlie Catripino (lead), Skaff Elias, William Jockusch, Joel Mick, Bill Rose, Paul Peterson, Mark Rosewater**Art Director:** Sue-Ann Harkey |  | **New Mechanics:** “choice spells” (you had two effects to choose from), pitch spells, “sac” lands (this term has now gone on to represent a different mechanic)**Factoid:** *Alliances* was the last set to feature multiple pieces of art on each common card. |
