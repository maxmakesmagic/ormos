World Magic Cup Qualifier Schedule Set
======================================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on February 27, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Qualifiers for the 2013 World **Magic** Cup will be run across the world on three weekends in the coming months, providing thousands of players the opportunity to represent their countries on **Magic**'s biggest stage. To make the experience even better, an alternate-art [Vengevine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vengevine) will be given to all participants (while supplies last) and Top 8 players will earn an exclusive World **Magic** Cup Qualifier Top 8 playmat. 


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/features/feat236b_promo.jpg) World **Magic** Cup Qualifiers will be run the weekends of April 6–7, April 20–21, and June 1–2, in seventy-one countries. Each country's three World **Magic** Cup Qualifiers will be held on separate weekends. You can find qualifiers for your country by visiting the [Event Locator](http://locator.wizards.com/). 

 All World **Magic** Cup Qualifiers will be run in the Standard format. 

 A full list of countries running World **Magic** Cup Qualifiers, as well as the 2013 Yearly Award Planeswalker Points needed to earn an invitation to the qualifiers, is listed below. All 2012–13 Pro Tour Players Club Silver, Gold, and Platinum members and Pro Tour Hall of Famers are automatically invited to their country's World **Magic** Cup Qualifiers. 

 Winners of World **Magic** Cup qualifiers earn an invitation and airfare to the World **Magic** Cup, to be held August 2-4, in Amsterdam, Netherlands. The overall prize pool for the World **Magic** Cup has been increased to $250,000 this year. 

 For complete information about 2013 World **Magic** Cup Qualifiers,  [visit this page](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/worldmagiccup/qualifiers) .
