The Community Cup is at hand, and a new format has been announced for the online bash: Modern. The format will feature all card sets released since the debut of the "modern" card frame in *Eighth Edition*. Here's a look at an early example of a list for such a format, a Modern take on Affinity. Read the official announcement [here](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/05192011d).
