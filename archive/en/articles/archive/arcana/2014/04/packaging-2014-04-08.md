 Packaging
==========



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 8, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






Today we are diving into all the different packaging for *Journey into Nyx*. With *Theros* we had a green style, *Born of the Gods* had an orange-red hue, and with *Journey* we get purple.

### Boosters

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/jw37liwo9i_arc_b2.jpg)  
Pharika makes her appearance on a booster pack!

### Fat Pack

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/jw37liwo9i_arc_fs.jpg)  
And here's what is shown in the picture above: 

* Player’s Guide with complete visual encyclopedia
* *Journey into Nyx* card box
* Nine booster packs
* Eighty-card basic land pack
* Special edition Spindown™ life counter
* Two deck boxes
