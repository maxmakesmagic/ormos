*Modern Horizons 2* Leagues and Premier Play Events Return
----------------------------------------------------------


* The *Modern Horizons 2* Sealed League and Draft League returned on Wednesday, July 21, and run until *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* events launch on Thursday, September 16 (extended one week due to downtime change).
* Those Leagues are returning to act as support for the four Limited Premier Events currently slated between now and *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt*—the plan is to use *Modern Horizons 2* for all of them instead of *Adventures in the Forgotten Realms*.
	+ Increased entry options of 50 Event Tickets or 500 Play Points will be used for the two *Modern Horizons 2* Super Qualifiers
	+ The QP entry option will remain at 40 for both Qualifiers and Super Qualifiers
* *Modern Horizons 2* will be used as the Limited Preliminary format starting July 21 at 4 p.m. instead of *Adventures in the Forgotten Realms*.
	+ Entry options: 45 Event Tickets or 450 Play Points
	+ See next item for updates to prize payout



---

*Modern Horizons 2* Limited Preliminary Prize Increase
------------------------------------------------------


Starting with last week's *Modern Horizons 2* Limited Preliminary event and lasting until the season ends with *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt*, we've increased the prizes of 4–0 and 3–1 finishes in *Modern Horizons 2* Limited Preliminaries by 150 Play Points each.


**Entry**: 45 Event Tickets or 450 Play Points


**Prizes**:


* 4–0: **750** **Play Points** and 40 Qualifier Points
* 3–1: **450** **Play Points** and 20 Qualifier Points
* 2–2: 150 Play Points and 10 Qualifier Points

August Is Spotlight Cube Series Month
-------------------------------------


For the next 3 weeks, *Magic Online* will be featuring a new cube from the community each week! And at the culmination, we'll bring *Legacy Cube* back. It's a good time to be a cube fan! The three Spotlight Cube Series cubes you'll have the pleasure of drafting with starting tomorrow are:


* August 4–11: Autumn Burchett's *Bant Cube*
* August 11–18: Ellie Rice's *Temur Cube 2.0*
* August 18–25: Ryan Saxe's *Classic Cube*



---

Spotlight Cube Series: *Temur Cube 2.0*
---------------------------------------


The next community entry during Spotlight Cube Series Month comes to MTGO this week! Packed with even more combos and potential degeneracy to be had, *Temur Cube 2.0* comes to *Magic Online* this Wednesday, August 11, and lasts for a full week. Cube designer Elizabeth Rice has more to share about *Temur Cube 2.0*, as well as a full card list, available in the [announcement article here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/spotlight-cube-series-temur-cube-20-2021-08-10).

On Sunday, August 15, there will be a Modern Super Qualifier with a start time of 7 a.m. PT! The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points (Season 3), 40 Event Tickets, or 400 Play Points. The top two finishers qualify for an upcoming Set Championship on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Super Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021#sq).


*Note: This is a change from the original announcement—the October 17 event that was formerly a Super Qualifier is now a Qualifier instead.*

Showcase Qualifier Weekends
---------------------------


Players who earned entry into the Showcase Qualifier will be squaring off for an invitation to the Champions Showcase. The entry fee for Showcase Qualifiers is a format token that matches the format of the Showcase. As with QPs, after the season's Showcase Qualifiers are finished, all unused Format Tokens will be removed from players' accounts. Each Showcase Qualifier is a scheduled Swiss-style event with a Top 8 playoff. The winner of each Showcase Qualifier earns a spot in the final Showcase event on October 30 and a chance to earn their share of $70,000 and an upcoming Premier Event invitation.


Showcase Qualifiers are scheduled as follows:

MOCS Last Chance Events August 8–11
-----------------------------------


Following Showcase Challenge Weekend, your next opportunity to continue toward qualification for the Season 2 MOCS are the Last Chance Events. From Sunday afternoon through Wednesday morning Pacific time, Preliminaries in the four Showcase Qualifier formats *(Modern, Pioneer, Legacy, and Vintage)* are replaced with Last Chance tournaments.


These five-round events cost either 30 Event Tickets plus 40 Qualifier Points, or 300 Play Points plus 40 QPs to enter. If you win all five matches, you will earn an Entry Token good for that format's Showcase Qualifier later this month.


Note that Limited, Standard, and Pauper Preliminaries during this period will fire normally.


Learn more about Last Chance events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021#slce).

[Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.
