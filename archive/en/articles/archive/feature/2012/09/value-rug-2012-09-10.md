Value RUG
=========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 10, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_conleywoods.jpg)
By Conley Woods











Almost two weeks ago, Yuuya Watanabe won the first-ever [**Magic** Players Championship](/en/events/coverage/watanabe-wins-magic-players-championship) in Seattle, Washington. With the World Championships changing to an all-team format with the [World **Magic** Cup](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/welcome-world-magic-cup-2012-08-17-0), and the Player of the Year race seemingly becoming a thing of the past, this sixteen-person, invite-only tournament looks to be the only remaining event to crown a year-long champion. The event combined three different formats across three days to crown the first ever players champion.


![Aether Vial](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Aether+Vial)Shouta Yasooka was one of the event's participants. If you know Shouta, you know that means a brew of a Constructed deck. Shouta has been seen casting [Cryptic Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command) atop a [Doran, the Siege Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower) he had in play. I even remember losing to a [Yosei, the Morning Star](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yosei%2C+the+Morning+Star) lock via [Miren, the Moaning Well](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Miren%2C+the+Moaning+Well) in Extended, so he can certainly show up to an event with just about anything.


For the Modern portion of the **Magic** Players Championship, as well as the Top 4, Shouta showed up with an innovative—yet tame, by his standards—RUG Aggro Control list. The deck is one of the first in the format to abuse [Æther Vial](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=%C3%86ther+Vial), similar to how Merfolk uses the engine in Legacy. With every one of his creatures being an incredible rare, often with utility, Shouta is able to turn [Æther Vial](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=%C3%86ther+Vial) into a card-advantage, and mana-advantage, engine. With a [Modern Pro Tour](/en/articles/archive/qualifiers-pro-tour-return-ravnica-seattle-2012-03-15) around the corner, keep an eye on this RUG list to help define the new format!
