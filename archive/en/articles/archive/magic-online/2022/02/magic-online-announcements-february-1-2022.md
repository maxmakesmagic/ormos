*Innistrad: Double Feature* January 26–February 9
-------------------------------------------------


*Innistrad: Double Feature* is here! This spooky mashup of *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* and *Innistrad: Crimson Vow* is coming to *Magic Online* from January 26 to February 9. This initial two-week run is the only place you'll be able to find the sweet Dracula series treatments on *Magic Online*, so this is a frighteningly fun event you won't want to miss!


You can find out all the details on [*Innistrad: Double Feature* here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/innistrad-double-feature-product-overview-2021-11-15). For *Magic Online*, the Endless Ranks of the Dead promo card will be found in Treasure Chests with the release of *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty*!


### Event Details


**Draft League**


* **Entry Options**: 12 Event Tickets or 120 Play Points
* **Prizes**:
	+ 3 Wins – 240 Play Points, 3 MOCS Season 1 Qualifier Points, 1 Competitive Trophy
	+ 2 Wins – 80 Play Points

**Sealed League**


* **Entry Options**: 24 Event Tickets or 240 Play Points
* **Prizes**:
	+ 5 Wins – 540 Play Points, 5 MOCS Season 1 QP, 1 Competitive Trophy
	+ 4 Wins – 360 Play Points, 2 MOCS Season 1 QP
	+ 3 Wins – 150 Play Points, 1 MOCS Season 1 QP

**Single-Elimination Draft Queue**


* **Entry Options**: 15 Event Tickets or 150 Play Points
* **Prizes**:
	+ 1st Place – 480 Play Points, 3 MOCS Season 1 QP, 1 Competitive Trophy
	+ 2nd Place – 240 Play Points, 1 MOCS Season 1 QP

*Black Is Magic* Celebration
----------------------------


Celebrate Black History and excellence during February and every month with us here on *Magic Online*! Read on to find out how you can score the entire *Secret Lair: Black Is Magic* drop by simply playing *Magic*!


![Black Is Magic on MTGO](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/2nsd2Dweu_BIM.png)


For each week in February, we'll be keeping tabs on accounts that simply play *and finish* an entire game of *Magic* each week. Whether that's a round in the Pauper League, a match playing *Innistrad: Double Feature* or *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* draft, or just a Commander game with friends, you'll eventually be able to earn all these cards on March 1.


Playing games during these weeks will earn you the following cards. But if you just play a game each week in February, you're in the clear to receive the entire *Secret Lair* drop on *Magic Online*!


* **Week 1**: Shalai, Voice of Plenty
* **Week 2**: Cultivate
* **Week 3**: Path of Ancestry
* **Week 4**: Ponder
* **Two-Week Combo**: Kaya, Ghost Assassin
* **Three-Week Combo**: Sol Ring
* **Four-Week Combo**: Teferi, Hero of Dominaria

[Read more about the *Secret Lair: Black Is Magic* drop.](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/news/black-is-magic-product-details-2021-02-04)

Legacy Super Qualifier Saturday
-------------------------------


On Saturday, February 5, there will be a Legacy Super Qualifier with a special start time of 9 a.m. PT (17:00 UTC). The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points, 40 Event Tickets, or 400 Play Points. The top two finishers qualify for the New Capenna Championship on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Super Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/mtgo-qualifiers-super-qualifiers).

Limited Preliminaries Remain *Modern Horizons 2*
------------------------------------------------


Limited Preliminaries will continue to feature the *Modern Horizons 2* format until *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* events go live on February 10. Entry options will remain 45 Event Tickets and 450 Play Points until that time.

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. [Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place.


*NOTES:* 


* *Pauper and Standard Challenges are never pre-empted.*
* *Saturday Legacy Challenges require 32 players and give prizes to Top 32.*
* *Sunday Legacy Challenges require 64 players and give prizes to Top 64.*
