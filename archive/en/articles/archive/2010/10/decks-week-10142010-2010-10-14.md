Decks of the Week: 10/14/2010
=============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on October 14, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast













S*cars of Mirrodin* has hit the streets and the Limited environment looks much deeper than simply metalcraft vs. infect. Jeremy Neeman [won Grand Prix–Sydney](/en/events/coverage/neeman-shatters-opposition-sydney) on the backs of an aggressive red-white draft deck, defeating Luis Scott-Vargas. The qualifier season for Pro Tour–Paris heated up with two more events featuring one winning decklist running the [Furnace Celebration](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Furnace+Celebration+)  gambit while the other flexed the might of [Kuldotha Forgemaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kuldotha+Forgemaster+)  and [Venser, the Sojourner](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Venser%2C+the+Sojourner). Find a qualifier near you and get your ticket punched to Pro Tour–Paris and the first **Magic Weekend**  of 2011!
