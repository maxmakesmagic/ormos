### DAILY DECK


Green with Envy
===============



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)

Adam Prosak

From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer.


September 18, 2014
 










[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Leatherback Baloth)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leatherback Baloth)
While there is an appeal to building powerful decks with crazy interactions, there is also value in building straightforward decks that are incredibly consistent. Today's deck is an exercise in consistency. Typically, monocolor decks succeed due to their consistency rather than their power. However, in a format as large as Modern, it's easy to find enough cards for your strategy even within a single color.


This is a focused beatdown deck that really wants to win in combat and plays combat tricks that are not otherwise seen in Constructed decks. [Vines of Vastwood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vines+of+Vastwood), [Slaughterhorn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slaughterhorn), and [Aspect of Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aspect+of+Hydra) are rarely seen cards that help this deck maintain its aggression. [Aspect of Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aspect+of+Hydra) is particularly interesting due to the creatures in this deck providing ample devotion to green.


[Strangleroot Geist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strangleroot+Geist), [Leatherback Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leatherback+Baloth), and [Kalonian Tusker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kalonian+Tusker) are particularly useful in a deck like this with a nearly all-Forest mana base. The Modern card pool even allows green access to a removal spell in [Dismember](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember), which can be essential in breaking up a [Splinter Twin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Splinter+Twin) combo or a [Cranial Plating](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cranial+Plating) kill.
