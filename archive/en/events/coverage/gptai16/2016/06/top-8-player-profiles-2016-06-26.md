Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX TAIPEI 2016](/en/events/coverage/gptai16)
 on June 26, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











Learn more about Grand Prix Taipei's Top 8 competitors, their history at the top tables, and more by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles.




---


### Yuuki Ichikawa


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gptai16/T8-YuukiIchikawa.jpg)


**Age:** 28  
**Occupation:** Team Cygames Pro Player  
**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 2 Pro Tour Top 8s, 6 Grand Prix Top 8s with 1 win.


**Number of Byes:** 3  
**Score in Day 1:** 8-1  
**Score in Day 2:** 4-1-1


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Brian Braun-Duin's Bant Company. Best Standard deck!


**What is the best card in Standard, and why?**  
[Collected Company](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collected+Company). I love it!


**What is your worst matchup?**  

Any Ramp decks.




---


### Huang Yung-Ming


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gptai16/T8-HuangYungMing.jpg)


**Age:** 35  
**Occupation:** Engineer  
**Hometown:** Tainan, Chinese Taipei


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** World *Magic* Cup 2014 Team Member, World *Magic* Cup 2016 Team Member


**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Score in Day 1:** 8-1  
**Score in Day 2:** 4-0-2


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Green-White tokens, because I just won a WMCQ with it last weekend with the same 75 cards. I've spent almost a month to find the best build after Grand Prix Tokyo.


**What is the best card in Standard, and why?**  
[Dromoka's Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dromoka%27s+Command). Maybe they should have banned it a year ago for the sake of diversity.


**What is your worst matchup?**  

Red-Green Ramp




---


### Lee Shi Tian


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gptai16/T8-LeeShiTian.jpg)


**Age:** 29  
**Occupation:** Accountant and MTG Mint Card Pro Player  
**Hometown:** Hong Kong


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 4 Pro Tour Top 8s, 6 Grand Prix Top 8s with 1 win.


**Number of Byes:** 3  
**Score in Day 1:** 9-0  
**Score in Day 2:** 4-1-1


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Bant Company, because I am most familiar with it. Also, while practicing at the Hong Kong WMCQ, I found the build that I was looking for.


**What is the best card in Standard, and why?**  
[Collected Company](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collected+Company). Tempo, card advantage, tricks, all rolled up in a single card.


**What is your worst matchup?**  

White-Red Humans. I need a good draw to beat it.

### Lien Chien-Hung


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gptai16/T8-LienChienHung.jpg)


**Age:** 32  
**Occupation:** Engineer  
**Hometown:** Chia-Yi, Chinese Taipei


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** MOCS Top 8


**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Score in Day 1:** 8-1  
**Score in Day 2:** 4-1-1


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Yuuya Watanabe's version of Bant Humans. I'm playing it simply because Watanabe plays it.


**What is the best card in Standard, and why?**  
[Tireless Tracker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tireless+Tracker). It's a good creature that generates you card advantage.


**What is your worst matchup?**  

The mirror match.

**Hometown:** Bangkok, Thailand

**Score in Day 2:** 4-1-1

### Liu Yuchen


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gptai16/T8-LiuYuchen.jpg)


**Age:** 25 (Today is my birthday!)  
**Occupation:** MTG Sheep Pro Player  
**Hometown:** Beijing, China


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 4 Grand Prix Top 8s


**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Score in Day 1:** 8-1  
**Score in Day 2:** 4-1-1


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Green-White Tokens, because I love to cast [Gideon, Ally of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Ally+of+Zendikar).


**What is the best card in Standard, and why?**  
[Gideon, Ally of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Ally+of+Zendikar). It helped me win a lot of games!


**What is your worst matchup?**  

Perhaps Bant Company, I don't have much experience against that deck.
