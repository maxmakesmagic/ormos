**Magic Online** is home to many unique formats with tournaments running around the clock. One popular format is Vanguard, which allows players to use a special avatar to give their deck extra oomph. William\_ won the recent event with the above list. Using the Nekrataal avatar, William\_ was able to play each creature in the deck for one black mana less than usual. That allowed the creation to outlast the rest of the competition! For more information on the Vanguard format, visit [this link](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Article.aspx?x=magic/magiconline/vanguard). For more information on **Magic Online**, visit [this link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/Default.aspx).
