An Abridged History of Magic by Mark Rosewater
==============================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 22, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







Mark Rosewater talked with Pro Tour commentator and Limited Information author Marshall Sutcliffe, during which time he dove into the twenty year history of Magic. This segment originally aired during the World **Magic** Cup coverage.
