Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX SÃO PAULO 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpsao15)
 on May 3, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











After two days of competition, only eight players remained. Let's get to know them a bit!




---






Claudio Barriento Ochoa
=======================



![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsao15/Top-8-Claudio-Barriento.jpg)



**Hometown:**  Temuco, Calama, Chile  
**Age:**  29  
**Occupation:**  Civil Industrial Engineer  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Day 2, Grand Prix Buenos Aires 2014; Day 2, Grand Prix Santiago 2014




**What Standard deck are you playing, and why did you choose it?**  

 Abzan Aggro, because my brother and my teammates convinced me to play it! I always play “Rock” decks!




**What card won you the most matches this weekend and how?**  
[Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino).




**What are your best and worst matchups?**  

 My best matchups are the combat-based ones, and the worst matchups are Control in general.




**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

 I will move [Ultimate Price](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ultimate+Price) to the sideboard and add something to the maindeck for the Control matchups.





---






Mateus Batista de Melo
======================



![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsao15/Top-8-Mateus-Batista.jpg)



**Hometown:**  Belo Horinzont, Brazil  
**Age:**  23  
**Occupation:**  Entrepreneur  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 7-2 at São Paulo PTQ; Grand Prix Trial Winner




**What Standard deck are you playing, and why did you choose it?**  

 Esper Dragons. It's the best deck of the metagame and I am used to playing Control.




**What card won you the most matches this weekend and how?**  
[Dragonlord Ojutai](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonlord+Ojutai) from the maindeck and [Dragonlord Silumgar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonlord+Silumgar) from the sideboard.




**What are your best and worst matchups?**  

 GW Company




**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

 One more [Bile Blight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bile+Blight), one less [Ultimate Price](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ultimate+Price).

Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa
========================



![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsao15/Top-8-Paulo-Vitor-Damo.jpg)



**Hometown:** Porto Alegre, Brazil  
**Age:** 27  
**Occupation:** *Magic* Player and Writer at ChannelFireball.com  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 9 Pro Tour Top 8, three-time National Champion, Hall of Fame!




**What Standard deck are you playing, and why did you choose it?**  

 Esper Dragons. I think it's very good and I've played a lot with it.




**What card won you the most matches this weekend and how?**  

 Ojutai, attacking! Ashiok was pretty good too.




**What are your best and worst matchups?**  

 The best matchups are the slow non-blue decks, like Abzan Control and bees. The worst matchups are the super-fast aggro decks, like Green/White and Mono-Red (though I'm 8-0 versus Mono Red with Esper!)




**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

 Right now, nothing.

Maximiliano Sanchez Pinnilo
===========================



![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsao15/Top-8-Maximiliano-Ramir.jpg)



**Hometown:** Chile  
**Age:** 32  
**Occupation:** Right end in Club Football, Lawyer  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** World *Magic* Cup 2012




**What Standard deck are you playing, and why did you choose it?**  

 RG Dragons. I like Dragons because they are very massive and red.




**What card won you the most matches this weekend and how?**  
[Stormbreath Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormbreath+Dragon) and [Crater's Claws](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crater%27s+Claws). [Crater's Claws](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crater%27s+Claws) is my best card for winning games in the Grand Prix.




**What are your best and worst matchups?**  

 I think the best matchup is Esper Dragons and the bad matchup is Sidisi-Whip.




**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

 I would cut 1 [Boon Satyr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boon+Satyr) for 1 more Mountain.

Mariano Cartechino
==================



![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsao15/Top-8-Mariano-Cartechino.jpg)



**Hometown:** Liniers, Buenos Aires, Argentina  
**Age:** 29  
**Occupation:** Employee  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 11 lifetime pro points; 10th at Grand Prix Valencia 2013; and father of last Super Sunday Series Champion.




**What Standard deck are you playing, and why did you choose it?**  

 Abzan Aggro because it doesn't have bad matchups.




**What card won you the most matches this weekend and how?**  
[Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino), the best card in Standard




**What are your best and worst matchups?**  

 50% against the field




**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

 -1 [Wingmate Roc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingmate+Roc) in the main deck and -1 Nissa in the 
sideboard. +1 [Ultimate Price](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ultimate+Price) in the sideboard. I am not sure what to add to the main.

 Best matchup: Megamorph. Worst matchup: Esper Dragons

 None.
