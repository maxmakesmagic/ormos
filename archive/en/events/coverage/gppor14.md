TOP 8 COVERAGE
--------------


There is just no stopping No. 2 Ranked Player Owen Turtenwald, No. 3 Ranked Player Reid Duke, or No. 4 Ranked Player William Jensen.


Coming off of what can only be described as a historical season for the three players, the trio of teammates and friends, commonly referred to in social media as the Peach Garden Oath brothers, have started off the new Premier Play season with another Grand Prix trophy to add to their wall.


Going into this weekend, nobody would have scoffed at picking the ChannelFireball Pantheon team members as their favorite to win the event. Sure enough, it turns out that the trio are not slowing down this year. After dispatching No. 23 Ranked Player Eric Froehlich, Hall of Famer Luis Scott-Vargas, and Paul Cheon in the Semifinals, the terrific trio took down the crew of Eric Severson, Benjamin Weitz, and Josiah Skallerup in order to claim their trophy.


For Jensen, this weekend is yet another notch to the reasons why he is in the *Magic* Hall of Fame. For Turtenwald and Duke, today represents their third Grand Prix victory, with all of their wins taking place in just a few short years.


But for them, this win represents a dream come true. Where all three players, simultaneously, can share in the glory of their win. Today, the Pantheon stands strong.
