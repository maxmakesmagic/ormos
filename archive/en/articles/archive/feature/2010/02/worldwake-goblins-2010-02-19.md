It's everyone's favorite red tribe, Goblins! Michael Rose took this list to a Top 8 at a large Standard event held recently in Kansas. He won some brand-new packs of *Worldwake* with a new take on an old tribe. [Quest for the Goblin Lord](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quest+for+the+Goblin+Lord) is almost assured to go active thanks to [Dragon Fodder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon+Fodder), [Warren Instigator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warren+Instigator), and [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander), not to mention a host of support Gobbos waiting in the wings. It's the perfect concoction to test out *Worldwake* at Friday Night **Magic** this weekend!
