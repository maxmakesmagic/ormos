If you are a fan of the Knight class, John Thorson’s Vermont States Top 4 list is exactly the deck you’re looking for. He played, as the deck’s name implies, twenty Knights maindeck. He also managed to squeeze in a whole boatload of planeswalkers with [Elspeth, Knight-Errant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Elspeth%252C%2BKnight-Errant), [Ajani Vengeant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani+Vengeant), and [Sarkhan Vol](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sarkhan+Vol) all helping to push the Knights over the top. You can find John’s list and more in the [2008 State Championship archive](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/event/champs08/all).
