Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 6, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











The Top 8 is more than just a pile of cards. It's the players that piloted those decks and weathered the ebbs and flows of a two-day tournament. Here are the players who performed well enough this weekend to make the Top 8 of Grand Prix Minneapolis.



**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmin17/gpMinn17_CoreyBaumeister.jpg)Name:**  Corey Baumeister




**Age:** 26




**Hometown:** Fargo, North Dakota




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Better Brother




**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Black-Green Constrictor; Brad told me to.




**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Winding Constrictor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Winding+Constrictor), it's gas.




**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Skysovereign, Consul Flagship](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skysovereign%2C+Consul+Flagship) (The boat). T-Pain agrees.




**What was your plan to beat Ramunap Red this weekend?** Cast 2/3s when they play 2/1s.




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** First Top 8 with my big bro!!! I couldn't be happier.





---


  
**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmin17/gpMinn17_BradNelson.jpg)Name:**  Brad Nelson




**Age:** 31




**Hometown:** Roanoke, Virginia




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  ~~Better Brother.~~ That's not true.




**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Black-Green Constrictor, because Corey told me to.




**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** Whatever came off the top.




**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** I'm on a boat!




**What was your plan to beat Ramunap Red this weekend?** Have early draws to dodge it.




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** My brother and I have dreamed of being in the same Top 8 since we started playing *Magic*. This means everything!





---


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmin17/gpMinn17_RayHuang.jpg)Name:**  Ray Huang




**Age:** 26




**Hometown:** Madison, Wisconsin




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Three Pro Tour appearances




**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Mardu Vehicles




**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Thraben Inspector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thraben+Inspector). It does everything.




**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Skysovereign, Consul Flagship](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skysovereign%2C+Consul+Flagship). Black decks can't kill it.




**What was your plan to beat Ramunap Red this weekend?** Race with [Aethersphere Harvester](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aethersphere+Harvester), have good blockers.




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Back on the Pro Tour!





---


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmin17/gpMinn17_DanielWeiser.jpg)Name:**  Daniel Weiser




**Age:** 22




**Hometown:** Minneapolis, Minnesota




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  One Grand Prix Top 16, one Top 32.




**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Black-Green, because it's nice.




**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Blooming Marsh](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blooming+Marsh), because [Bayou](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bayou) is strong.




**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Ishkanah, Grafwidow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ishkanah%2C+Grafwidow). It's tutorable and has a variety of applications.




**What was your plan to beat Ramunap Red this weekend?** Win. The matchup is good.




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** The payoff of a lot of hard work, and a trip to the Pro Tour.





---


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmin17/gpMinn17_RobCastro.jpg)Name:**  Rob Castro




**Age:** 41




**Hometown:** San Salvador, El Savador. I live in Brookfield, Wisconsin.




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  1997 Wisconsin Standard State Champion.




**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Mardu Vehicles. I asked Matt Severa how he liked the deck at the Pro Tour. He loved it and sent me the latest version. I was the only deck I had played.




**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** There is not one card, the entire deck was great.




**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Gideon, Ally of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Ally+of+Zendikar), because I didn't run four main.




**What was your plan to beat Ramunap Red this weekend?** Play [Aethersphere Harvester](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aethersphere+Harvester).




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** It means that Dan Bock was not there to stop me from making the Pro Tour. I believe I'm in the first Savadoran to make Top 8 of a Grand Prix.
