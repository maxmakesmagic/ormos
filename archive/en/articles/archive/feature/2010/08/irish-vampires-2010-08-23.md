The 2010 Ireland National Championship concluded with a repeat champion: Chris Black! But his opponent in the Finals, Simon Carmichael, had a most interesting deck. In stark opposition to the conventional wisdom, Simon brought Vampires to the field of battle. The archetype has fallen out of favor of late, but he didn't let that affect his decision for the tournament. Doing so netted him a trip to compete at the 2010 World Championships in Chiba, Japan in December!
