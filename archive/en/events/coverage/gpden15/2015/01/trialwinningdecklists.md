TRIAL WINNING DECKLISTS
=======================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on January 3, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










A well-known rite of passage in the *Magic* tournament scene is the last-chance Grand Prix trials (or, affectionately, “Grinders”). Late into Friday night, players battled for a some bye coming into Saturday. There sixteen trials last night—four were Sealed, and were won by Lucas Kiefer, Cory Reinking, Robert Phillips, and Andrew Feinberg. The rest were all Standard, baby. Here are the lists that were good enough to take down a trial last night.
