**Upcoming Standard PTQ for Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation*  on Saturday, April 29**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! This week, due to Monday's start of *Amonkhet* events adding new cards to Standard, we've added two Standard PTQ Preliminaries tomorrow, April 26, at 8 a.m. and 6 p.m. PT (UTC -7). The weekly suite of Preliminaries on Thursday through Tuesday, April 27 through May 2, will take place at their normal times. We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, April 29!


Remember, you are no longer required to enter into the PTQ Finals immediately following the Preliminary that you qualified through, thanks to the redesigned PTQ system that *Magic Online* Events Manager Lee Sharpe announced [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/pro-tour-qualifier-changes-2015-11-16).


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#HOU_schedule).

**Upcoming Sealed PTQ for Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation*  on Sunday, April 30**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! The *Hour of Devastation* Sealed Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries begin Thursday, April 27, and run through Tuesday, May 2. We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, April 30!


Remember, you are no longer required to enter into the PTQ Finals immediately following the Preliminary that you qualified through, thanks to the redesigned PTQ system that *Magic Online* Events Manager Lee Sharpe announced [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/pro-tour-qualifier-changes-2015-11-16).


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#HOU_schedule).

**The May MOPR Promo period begins tomorrow**


The May MOPR period runs from April 26 to May 31. Here are the promo cards:




|
|  |
| Event Promo

 Archfiend of Ifnir | Store Promo

Unlicensed Disintegration |

Play in one or more events during the May MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium Archfiend of Ifnir. Five or more and you'll get an additional premium Archfiend of Ifnir. Spend at least $20 total in the *Magic Online* Store during this MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium Unlicensed Disintegration. By spending $40 or more, you'll get an additional premium Unlicensed Disintegration.
