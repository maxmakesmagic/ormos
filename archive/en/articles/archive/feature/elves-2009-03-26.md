Turbo Elf decks have been featured on Daily Decks before, but Luckygoblin4711's list deserves special mention. Despite an unfortunate moniker considering the tribe picked for the deck, the plucky player took the above list to the Top 8 of a Premier Event on **Magic Online**. Considering the recent Pro Tour-Kyoto which saw nearly a half dozen different archetypes in the Top 8 of the event, Luckygoblin adds yet another fun deck to the testing gauntlet.
