TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 11, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











Learn more about Grand Prix Milwaukee's Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they’ve done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!


 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmil16/t8_severson2.jpg)Name:** Eric Severson


**Age:** 24


**Hometown:** Davis, CA


**Occupation:** Math PHD student


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Gold pro with Team East-West Bowl; three Grand Prix Top 8s


**What did your Sealed Deck look like?** **What was the best card?** Red-White Aggro. I had [Veteran Motorist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Veteran+Motorist); Scry 2 makes every hand with it, a Mountain and a Plains great. I went 7-2.


**What colors were your draft decks, and what was your record? What were the best cards?** Red-Green Aggro and then Red-White Aggro; 3-0 with both. The best two cards in those drafts were [Renegade Freighter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renegade+Freighter) and then [Key to the City](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Key+to+the+City).


**What card do you most want to open in this Top 8?** Skysovereign, Flagship Consul, but a powerful *Invention* would be more fun.


**What was your favorite moment of 2016?** Narrowly surviving a car crash two months ago and now rallying back to another great finish.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmil16/t8_weitz2.jpg)Name:** Benjamin Weitz


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** St. Paul, MN


**Occupation:** Graduate Student


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Three Grand Prix Top 8. Gold Pro and qualified for 2017 *Magic Online* Championship Series. I also cohost the podcast [Allied Strategies](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Allied+Strategies)!


**What did your Sealed Deck look like?** What was the best card? Black-Red Aggro, Inventor’s Goggles was the best card.


**What colors were your draft decks, and what was your record? What were the best cards?** I went 3-0 in my first draft with Blue-Black Energy. [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) is great, especially when you hit [Noxious Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noxious+Gearhulk). Realistically, [Gearseeker Serpent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gearseeker+Serpent) is how I actually won.


Second draft I went Black-Red Aggro. [Night Market Lookout](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Night+Market+Lookout) plus [Sky Skiff](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sky+Skiff) is a good combo. Chandra helped too.


**What card do you most want to open in this Top 8?** [Skysovereign, Consul Flagship](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skysovereign%2C+Consul+Flagship). It’s just the best card.


**What was your favorite moment of 2016?** Making Gold in the penultimate round of Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon* after going 4-4 on Day 1. Also, my entire team made gold or platinum.

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmil16/t8_petre2.jpg)Name:** Evan Petre


**Age:** 24


**Hometown:** White Plains, NY


**Occupation:** Shift Supervisor at CVS


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Grand Prix Pittsburgh 2016 champion.


**What did your Sealed Deck look like?** What was the best card? White-Blue good stuff. I had double [Cloudblazer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudblazer), [Confiscation Coup](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Confiscation+Coup) and [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel).


**What colors were your draft decks, and what was your record?** What were the best cards? Went 3-0 with Blue-Black with double [Scrapheap Scrounger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrapheap+Scrounger), and then 2-0-1 with Red-White with triple [Aviary Mechanic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aviary+Mechanic).


**What card do you most want to open in this Top 8?** [Hangarback Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hangarback+Walker).


**What was your favorite moment of 2016?** Winning the Grand Prix in Pittsburgh.

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmil16/t8_fischer3.jpg)Name:** Brandon Fischer


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** San Francisco


**Occupation:** Software Engineer


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Grand Prix Costa Rica finalist.


**What did your Sealed Deck look like? What was the best card?** Green-Blue Energy with [Animation Module](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Animation+Module) and [Durable Handicraft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Durable+Handicraft) combo.


**What colors were your draft decks, and what was your record?** What were the best cards? 3-0 in both with Temur Energy. [Minister of Inquiries](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Minister+of+Inquiries) in my first deck and [Fabrication Module](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fabrication+Module) in my second.


**What card do you most want to open in this Top 8?** [Whirler Virtuoso](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whirler+Virtuoso).


**What was your favorite moment of 2016?** Playtesting with the rest of Team East-West Bowl in Honolulu.

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmil16/t8_carter2.jpg)Name:** Steve Carter


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** Aurora, IL


**Occupation:** Airbrush Painter


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Won a couple PTQs


**What did your Sealed Deck look like? What was the best card?** Went 9-0 with Black-Red-Green Midrange, [Peema Outrider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peema+Outrider) was my best card.


**What colors were your draft decks, and what was your record? What were the best cards?** White-Black with [Eliminate the Competition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eliminate+the+Competition) and then Temur with Cultivator’s Caravan.


**What card do you most want to open in this Top 8?** [Sol Ring](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sol+Ring).


**What was your favorite moment of 2016?** Cubs winning the World Series!

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmil16/t8_lax2.jpg)Name:** Ari Lax


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** ~~Detroit. Boston. DC.~~ Who knows?


**Occupation:** Engineer


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir* champion. Eight other Grand Prix Top 8s with one win.


**What did your Sealed Deck look like? What was the best card?** I had white-blue stuff, Smuggler’s Copter was my best card that I never drew.


**What colors were your draft decks, and what was your record? What were the best cards?** 3-0 with Black-Red; [Renegade Freighter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renegade+Freighter) was best. Then went 2-0-1 with Bant with [Minister of Inquiries](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Minister+of+Inquiries).


**What card do you most want to open in this Top 8?** [Sword of Fire and Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+Fire+and+Ice), just because I haven’t had it yet.


**What was your favorite moment of 2016?** Everyone from East-West Bowl hitting Gold in Sydney!

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmil16/t8_burkhart2.jpg)Name:** Corey Burkhart


**Age:** 24


**Hometown:** Alta Loma, CA


**Occupation:** Game Designer


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Five Grand Prix Top 8s; my last three Grand Prix I’ve finished 2nd, 9th and now Top 8 here. Four weeks and three formats.


**What did your Sealed Deck look like? What was the best card?** I had a solid black-green deck. I played poorly and ended 7-2. Best card was [Verdurous Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verdurous+Gearhulk).


**What colors were your draft decks, and what was your record? What were the best cards?** Blue-Black Aggro (a very bad deck with two [Torch Gauntlet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torch+Gauntlet)s). Best card was [Ovalchase Daredevil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ovalchase+Daredevil). Second draft was Jeskai [Panharmonicon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Panharmonicon). It had a bad curve but powerful cards. The best card was [Whirler Virtuoso](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whirler+Virtuoso).


**What card do you most want to open in this Top 8?** [Skysovereign, Consul Flagship](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skysovereign%2C+Consul+Flagship). That card is messed up in Limited.


**What was your favorite moment of 2016?** Watching Luis Scott-Vargas Top 8 his third Pro Tour in a row – it was better than my three Grand Prix Top 8s this season.
