======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 17, 2010

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/tcg/products/DBT/m14core_deckbuildertoolkit.jpg)### Further Information


**Original Release Date:** May 21st, 2010  
**Refreshed Version Release Date:** July 19, 2013  
**Refreshed Version Languages Available:** English, French, German, and Spanish  
**MSRP:** $19.99


Jump-start your collection with 285 battle-tested cards, perfect for building several different decks! This box also includes a guide containing dueling tips and expert advice on how to custom-build the deadliest and most effective decks possible! And since no two Toolkits are the same, you and an opponent can each pick one up and duel.


The *Deck Builder's Toolkit* was refreshed for ***Magic** 2014* and contains boosters from a variety of recent **Magic: The Gathering** sets. It is available in English, French, German, and Spanish.


**Each box contains:**


* 125 semi-randomized cards suitable for building several different **Magic** decks
* Four 15-card booster packs from a variety of recent **Magic: The Gathering** sets
