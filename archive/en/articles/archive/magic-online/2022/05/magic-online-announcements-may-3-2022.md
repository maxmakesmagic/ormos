Alternative Play for *Streets of New Capenna* Season
----------------------------------------------------


Here's the schedule for the alternative play experiences you can expect during the *Streets of New Capenna* season*.* All events run for a week, except where noted:


* May 4: **Caleb Gannon's *Powered Synergy Cube*** – This cube from popular content creator Caleb Gannon features *Vintage Cube*-level strategies with even sweeter synergies!
* May 11: ***Modern Horizons 2*** – We're bringing back *Modern Horizons 2* Draft for two weeks! This will be non-phantom, so it's your chance to nab a ritzy [Ragavan, Nimble Pilferer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ragavan%2C+Nimble+Pilferer), [Urza's Saga](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Saga), or even a [Solitude](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solitude)!
* May 25: ***Proliferate Cube*** – Our very own Carmen Handy is back with another energizing update to *Proliferate Cube*! You can count on this being one infecting experience!
* June 1: ***Vintage Cube*** – We've bookended this calendar with Moxen! *Vintage Cube* is back, powered up with *Streets of New Capenna*, and will run for four awesome weeks of cubing action.

Caleb Gannon's *Powered Synergy Cube* Starts Wednesday
------------------------------------------------------


Popular *Magic* Content Creator Caleb Gannon has brought his very own cube to *Magic Online*! *Powered Synergy Cube* combines The Power 9 with a plethora of synergistic and flexible combo pieces that come together to create some amazing experiences! Caleb's *Powered Synergy Cube* runs from May 4–11 on *Magic Online*, and you can read all about it and check out the full card list in [Caleb's announcement article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/spotlight-cube-series-caleb-gannons-powered-synergy-cube-2022-05-03).

Showcase Challenge Weekends
---------------------------


Players looking to prove their mettle against the best players in *Magic Online* will want to use their QPs to enter *Magic Online* Champions Showcase (MOCS) events such as the returning Showcase Challenges this weekend. These MOCS events are a set of linked events designed to highlight the best players on *Magic Online* each season, culminating in the Champions Showcase and its prize pool of $70,000 and a future Championship invitation.


First up on Saturday, May 7, we have the Modern Showcase Challenge at 8 a.m. PT (15:00 UTC), and on Sunday, May 8, we have the Pioneer Showcase Challenge at 6 a.m. PT (13:00 UTC). Then next week on Saturday, May 14, we have the Pauper Showcase Challenge at 8 a.m. PT (15:00 UTC), and on Sunday, May 15, we finish things off with the Legacy Showcase Challenge at 8 a.m. PT (15:00). Each of these events requires 40 QPs to enter. The Top 8 finishers from each event will earn a qualification token to enter the appropriate Showcase Qualifier in August.


Learn more about Showcase Challenge events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/mocs-step-1-showcase-challenges-last-chance-events).

Changes to Season 2 MOCS Invitations Announced
----------------------------------------------


As part of our [Digital Premier Play announcement](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/digital-premier-play-path-2022-04-21) on Thursday, the following changes have been made to MOCS Season 2:


* Starting with Season 2, both the winner and runner-up of the MOCS Showcase will earn invitations to the World Championship in 2023!
	+ The winner of MOCS Showcase Season 1 in June will instead earn an invitation to the first Pro Tour of 2023.
* Qualifiers and Super Qualifiers will now feed into the Regional Championships. These events are slated to start up again in July when tabletop's qualifying season begins.
* MOCS Showcase Qualifiers and the MOCS Leaderboard will give invitations to the Pro Tour.
* MOCS Showcase Opens will give invitations only to the winner starting with Season 2 *(previously winner and runner-up)*.

Our next Premier Events will be Showcase Challenges on May 7–8. Full details are available at the links at the top of this blog.

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. [Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place.


*NOTES:* 


* *Vintage and Standard Challenges are never pre-empted.*
* *Saturday Legacy Challenges require 32 players and give prizes to Top 32.*
* *Sunday Legacy Challenges require 64 players and give prizes to Top 64.*
