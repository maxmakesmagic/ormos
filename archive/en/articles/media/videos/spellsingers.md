SPELLSLINGERS
-------------


In partnership with our friends at Geek & Sundry comes Spellslinger! Presented by Sean Plott of Day9TV, prepare to experience fun-filled, fast-talking and adrenaline-paced battles that highlight the latest MTG cards. In each episode geek icons will take on Sean with the hope of emerging victorious in an all-or-nothing match, while you learn the tips and tricks to become a MTG expert! Visit the [Geek & Sundry website](http://geekandsundry.com/)!
