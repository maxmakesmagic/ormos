Round 8: Gary Wise vs. Antoine Ruel
===================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 14, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











### Game 1

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a870.jpg)Wise started off with [Hapless Researcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hapless+Researcher), while Ruel had [Seton's Scout](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Scout). The Researcher got through for a point, while [Pilgrim of Virtue](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pilgrim+of+Virtue) stayed back on defense. Ruel killed it off with [Sonic Seizure](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sonic+Seizure), and Wise asked for a die to roll rather than picking a card. He borrowed one from Itaru Ishida at a nearby feature match table and rolled, revealing [Fiery Temper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper). "Hey Itaru, next time lend me a different die." Wise build up his side with [Mystic Zealot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+Zealot) and [Aven Flock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Flock), while Ruel had [Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). With Wise tapped out, Ruel burned away the Flock with [Sonic Seizure](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sonic+Seizure), and Wise borrowed another die from Ishida. This time, [Anger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anger) was randomly discarded, which got a good laugh out of the onlooking crowd. 

The path was now clear for the Centaur, and it dealt five damage. Wise summoned [Battlewise Aven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlewise+Aven), and Ruel played [Longhorn Firebeast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Longhorn+Firebeast). Wise countered it with [Rites of Refusal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rites+of+Refusal), discarding an island. This left him with no cards in hand, but he now had threshold, making his Aven bigger. The Zealot and Aven double-blocked the Centaur, bringing it down to 3/1 while the Aven died. Wise peeled [Hydromorph Gull](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hydromorph+Gull) off the top, with a "I was holding it!" Ruel summoned [Springing Tiger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Springing+Tiger), and was two cards away from threshold. The game was now at a standstill, with both players hesitant to attack. Wise surprised everyone watching when he played [Coral Net](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coral+Net) on [Springing Tiger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Springing+Tiger).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a873.jpg)Wise- "I like it maindeck."  
 Ruel- "I don't."

If [Coral Net](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coral+Net) was a surprise, Ruel had a better one. He tapped four mana, and summoned another [Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur).

Wise- "Who was passing to you?"  
 Ruel- "Fourth pick!"  
 Wise- "Mark (Rosewater), who designed that card?"  
 Mark- "Brian Tinsman."  
 Me- "How do you spell his last name?"

Wise then suggested his own spelling, which was not appropriate for all ages.

Wise- 0 Ruel- 1

### Game 2

Ruel opened with [Chatter of the Squirrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chatter+of+the+Squirrel), followed by [Seton's Scout](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Scout). The Scout was enchanted with [Coral Net](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coral+Net), and headed right to the graveyard on upkeep. Wise then played [Aven Smokeweaver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Smokeweaver) to hold back Ruel's squirrels. When the fourth turn came around, Ruel brought out [Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a872.jpg)Wise- (Sigh)  
 Ruel- "I do have two in my deck."  
 Wise- "Shut up!"

On the next turn, Ruel played another Centaur.

Wise- "When you said you had two, I thought you meant you had two in your deck!"

At this point, Ruel got the attention of Kai Budde, who was at the Feature Match table next to him.

Kai- "Holy @#$%! Gary, did you win the first game?"  
 Wise- "No, he got both the first game too."

**Final Result:** Wise- 0 Ruel- 2
