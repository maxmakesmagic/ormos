Top 8 Decklists of Players Tour Brussels
========================================



 Posted in **Competitive Gaming**
 on February 1, 2020 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After two Drafts and ten rounds of Pioneer, the Top 8 of the very first Players Tour event ended up with seven different archetypes: one each of Mono-Red Aggro, Mono-Black Aggro, Lotus Breach, Dimir Inverter, Niv to Light, Sultai Delirium as well as two copies of Bant Spirits.


Check out the full decklists below!
