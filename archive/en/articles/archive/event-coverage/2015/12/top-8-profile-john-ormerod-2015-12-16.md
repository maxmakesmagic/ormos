Top 8 Profile - John Ormerod
============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPMAN00/Images/T8OrmerodPose.jpg)**Name:** John Ormerod  
**Age:** 30  
**Location:** London, UK  
**Occupation:** Computer programmer  
**Played Magic since:** 1995, Fallen Empires  
**Best accomplishments:** 5th Pro Tour Mainz, 9th 1999 Pro Tour Chicago, 9th 2000 Pro Tour New York  


**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Manchester?**  

A: Friends were driving here.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Make second day, three byes.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: Many mistakes but lucky.



**Q: What do you think about the tournament location-/organisation-wise?**  

A: Location = too far from London. Organisation = OK.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Drafted well.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: Q for LA.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: Noah will win.
