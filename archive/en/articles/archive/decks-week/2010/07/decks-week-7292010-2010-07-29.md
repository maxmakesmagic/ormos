Decks of the Week: 7/29/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on July 29, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The floodgates have opened for new Standard decks. No fewer than three major national championships and 10 North American Pro Tour Qualifiers showcased the format last weekend with ***Magic** 2011* cards greatly impacting play. From the Titan-fueled Blue-white Control decks in France, [Fauna Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman)s and [Primeval Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan)s in Australia, and mana ramp decks in Canada to more than 1,800 players battling in PTQs, this week is bursting with deck ideas. And don't forget, [***Magic** 2011* Prerelease events begin Friday on **Magic Online**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/07142010e), with the set going on sale Monday, August 2. For those with a mind toward Pro Tour–Amsterdam and the new Extended format, you can delve into the daily Extended decks from **Magic Online**.
