Meloku's Gallery
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 20, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











M**irror Gallery** (a *Betrayers of Kamigawa* card [previewed today](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/daily/mg159) by Mark Gottlieb) depicts the interior of a grand, Soratami cloud palace. It's filled with mirrors reflecting a regal-looking moonfolk in the foreground.

  
*[Mirror Gallery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Gallery) art by Scott M. Fischer*


That moonfolk is none other than the legendary [Meloku the Clouded Mirror](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meloku+the+Clouded+Mirror), also illustrated by Scott M. Fischer.
