If you like playing big mana spells early by using mana accelerants, you're going to love Kettle's Big Mana Block deck. Used to Top 8 a **Magic Online** Premier in the *Shards of Alara* Block Format, the deck plays cards like [Keeper of Progenitus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Keeper+of+Progenitus) and [Knotvine Mystic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knotvine+Mystic) to hasten big mana spells like [Titanic Ultimatum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Titanic+Ultimatum). Imagine the horror on an opponent's face when they pass the turn, comfortably protected with a [Cancel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cancel) in hand only to find a blazing X of uncounterable doom coming right at them from an over-sized [Banefire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banefire)!
