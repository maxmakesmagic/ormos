Magic Online *Magic 2012* Prerelease Events
===========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 12, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











T**he Battle Rages On!**


The **Magic: The Gathering** game expands every year, introducing new worlds filled with deadly perils, ancient adversaries, and fantastic new adventures. At its center is the **Magic** core set, the game in its purest form: evocative spells, menacing creatures, and infinite possibilities. But even at its core, the experience is ever-changing. The ***Magic** 2012* core set boldly continues this tradition. 

***Magic** 2012* will be available in the **Magic Online** store on August 1. The ***Magic** 2012* prerelease will feature three separate parts: Swiss Sealed Flights, 8-Player Swiss Draft Queues, and Casual Drafts. See below for more information. 

***MAGIC 2012* PRERELEASE SWISS SEALED FLIGHTS**

*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Thursday, July 28 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC) and ending Saturday, July 30 at 10:00 PM PDT (Sunday 05:00 UTC).  
*LOCATION:*  Sealed Swiss Queues room.   
*ENTRY OPTION(S):*  30 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 6 ***Magic** 2012* booster packs for this event.   
*SIZE:*  16 Players.   
*DURATION:*  Deck building time plus 4 rounds, Swiss pairings. Play in all four rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:*  Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 12 | 10 ***Magic** 2012* booster packs | 3 |
| 9 | 4 ***Magic** 2012* booster packs | 1 |
| 6 | 1 ***Magic** 2012* booster pack | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in a ***Magic** 2012* Prerelease Tournament will receive 1 copy of the prerelease Bloodlord of Vaasgoth promo card. Players with at least 9 match points at the end of the tournament will also receive a premium foil Bloodlord of Vaasgoth.

***MAGIC 2012* 8-PLAYER SWISS DRAFT QUEUES**

*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Saturday, July 30 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC) and ending Sunday, July 31 at 10:00 PM PDT (Monday 05:00 UTC).   
*LOCATION:*  8-Player Draft room.   
*ENTRY OPTION(S):* 20 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 3 ***Magic** 2012* booster packs for this event.   
*SIZE:*  8 Players.   
*DURATION:*  Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, Swiss pairings. Play all three rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:*  Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 9 | 4 ***Magic** 2012* booster packs | 1 |
| 6 | 2 ***Magic** 2012* booster packs | 0 |
| 3 | 1 ***Magic** 2012* booster pack | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in a ***Magic** 2012* 8-Player Swiss Draft will receive 1 copy of the prerelease Bloodlord of Vaasgoth promo card. Players with 9 points will also receive a premium foil Bloodlord of Vaasgoth. 

***Magic 2012* BLOCK CASUAL DRAFT QUEUES**

*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Thursday, July 28 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC) and ending at the beginning of the scheduled downtime on Wednesday, August 18.   
*LOCATION:*  Casual Draft room.   
*ENTRY OPTION(S):*  Product.   
*PRODUCT:*  3 ***Magic** 2012* booster packs.   
*SIZE:*  4, 6, or 8 Players.   
*DURATION:*  Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, Swiss pairings.   
*PRIZES:*  None.
