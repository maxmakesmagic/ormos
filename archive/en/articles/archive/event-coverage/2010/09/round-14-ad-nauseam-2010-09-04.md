Round 14: Ad Nauseam...
=======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 4, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_43.png)
By Tobias Henke











The Netherlands' Kamiel Cornelissen has five Pro Tour Top 8s and six Grand Prix Top 8s to his name. Standing in the way of yet another Top 8 this round was Kenny Öberg who's currently on fire with a Grand Prix win in Gothenburg, Sweden on home soil just last week. This being the famous—or infamous—[Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam) mirror match, the audience was sure in for an interesting match.


### Game 1


Both players had [Preordain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Preordain) on their first turns, but Öberg also had [Lotus Bloom](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Bloom) to go along with it. Meanwhile, Cornelissen made a second-turn [Coalition Relic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coalition+Relic) off [Simian Spirit Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Simian+Spirit+Guide). Öberg missed a third land and went looking with another [Preordain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Preordain) and [Ponder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ponder), while Cornelissen chained [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) into [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) and more [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings).


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptams10/r14_cornelissen_oberg.jpg)*Kamiel Cornelissen and Kenny Öberg face off with Ad Nauseam decks. Sick.*[Lotus Bloom](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Bloom) came out of suspension, but with the [Coalition Relic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coalition+Relic) still untapped on Cornelissen's side, threatening [Angel's Grace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel%27s+Grace), Öberg couldn't go off. Instead he simply summoned [Simian Spirit Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Simian+Spirit+Guide) to start the beatdown. The Ape got in twice, while the Dutchman flashbacked one of his [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) to go get [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam). Then he cast [Conflagrate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conflagrate) to kill the Ape.


On the following turn, Cornelissen went for [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam), not going off, but rather trying to get a new grip of cards. [Ponder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ponder), [Slaughter Pact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slaughter+Pact), [Preordain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Preordain), [Plains](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains), and [Pact of Negation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pact+of+Negation) went to his hand for the bargain price of just 2 life. He played the land, and discarded [Slaughter Pact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slaughter+Pact) and [Angel's Grace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel%27s+Grace) during his end step.


More [Ponder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ponder), [Preordain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Preordain), more lands, and more [Coalition Relic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coalition+Relic) were being played on both sides, but nothing of actual consequence happened. For a long, long time...


With thirty minutes left in the round, the real action began. At the end of Öberg's turn, Cornelissen tried for [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam). In response Öberg cast [Teferi, Mage of Zhalfir](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Mage+of+Zhalfir). Cornelissen responded with [Pact of Negation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pact+of+Negation), which Öberg matched. After his Teferi resolved, Öberg had another Pact for the [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam).


On his turn, Cornelissen cast [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) and went for [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam). [Teferi, Mage of Zhalfir](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Mage+of+Zhalfir) had to be dealt with and, with his [Slaughter Pact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slaughter+Pact) already discarded, it was unclear what Cornelissen was looking for. Öberg cracked his [Lotus Bloom](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Bloom) and cast [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) in response, got yet another [Pact of Negation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pact+of+Negation), and stopped whatever Cornelissen's plan might have been.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptams10/r14_oberg.jpg)*The Tezzerator.*With his [Lotus Bloom](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Bloom) gone, Öberg was not able to pay for the three Pacts he had cast since the beginning of his last turn, but fortunately, he didn't have to. He just cast [Angel's Grace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel%27s+Grace) instead. Then he followed it up with an [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam) of himself. That would allow him to draw the entirety of his deck and finally kill Cornelissen through [Conflagrate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conflagrate), but the Dutch player didn't make his opponent go through the motions.


**Cornelissen 1, Öberg 0**


### Game 2


The second game started much worse for Cornelissen. He took a mulligan down to six, and kept a hand with [Dreadship Reef](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreadship+Reef) and [Plains](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains) as his only lands. He did have two [Coalition Relic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coalition+Relic)s, but one was taken out of his hand by Öberg's [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), another one was countered by [Pact of Negation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pact+of+Negation). Öberg was developing his mana fast with three [Coalition Relic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coalition+Relic)s all in all, but when Cornelissen finally drew a land and a third [Coalition Relic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coalition+Relic) of his own—this time cast successfully—and had [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), Öberg was suddenly left with no cards in hand at all!


On the other side of the table, Cornelissen still had a full grip of cards even if he was still a little tight on mana. It certainly sufficed for [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) and, soon after, [Teferi, Mage of Zhalfir](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Mage+of+Zhalfir). Öberg didn't even put up a fight. Instead he cast [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) in his main phase and searched up [Vendilion Clique](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique). He saw a hand of [Vendilion Clique](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique), [Slaughter Pact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slaughter+Pact) and [Pact of Negation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pact+of+Negation), and took the latter.


Cornelissen attacked and passed the turn. Öberg, with the help of his three [Coalition Relic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coalition+Relic)s, cast [Conflagrate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conflagrate) for nine mana and 4 damage to Teferi. Cornelissen responded with [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam), presumably to find [Pact of Negation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pact+of+Negation). Unfortunately, he didn't, and had to stop early when the first three cards he flipped included another [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam) as well as [Simian Spirit Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Simian+Spirit+Guide). This little adventure left him with just 5 life, and the [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) he played afterwards turned that to 3.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptams10/r14_cornelissen.jpg)*Kamiel gets in there.*Öberg once again had no cards in hand, although he did have [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) left in his graveyard. Cornelissen also summoned [Simian Spirit Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Simian+Spirit+Guide), which started attacking and was soon joined by Cornelissen's [Vendilion Clique](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique). Under pressure, Öberg found himself in a position were he needed to flashback [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) to get another of the legendary Faeries, and tap out during his own end step to cast it. 


He disliked what the [Vendilion Clique](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique) showed him. There were two [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam)s in Cornelissen's hand. Tapped out, the Swede could only look in dismay as Cornelissen searched up [Angel's Grace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel%27s+Grace) with a spare copy of [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings) and combined that with [Ad Nauseam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam).


"[Pact of Negation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pact+of+Negation)!" he announced, and flipped the last card in his hand—which was in fact an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island), and the admission of defeat.


**Cornelissen 1, Öberg 1**


Unfortunately, time had run out and the current score turned into the final result.
