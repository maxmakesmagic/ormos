Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 24, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











Learn more about Grand Prix Toronto's Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they've done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!


 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gptor17/t8_potter.jpg)Name:** Doug Potter


**Age:** 30


**Hometown:** Edmonton, Alberta


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top of Grand Prix Toronto 2016 while also making Top 8 of the Sunday Super Series at the same time.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** Green-white splashing red. 9-0.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Oasis Ritualist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oasis+Ritualist)


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** Blue-Red, 1-2; Blue-Red 3-0.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** My podcast cohosts on First Strike did a lot of prep, and I feel this Top 8 validates their findings. Without them I wouldn’t be here so I am very thankful.  

 




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gptor17/t8_rashid.jpg)Name:** Taimur Rashid


**Age:** 21


**Hometown:** Toronto, Ontario


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** None


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** The Three-Color Jund Special. 8-1


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** Oasist Ritualist; it lets you put anything into your deck.


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** Green-White Exert, 3-0. Then 1-1-1 with white-black.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** It’s been a goal for a while. And being better than MTGO grinder Proltilary.  

 




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gptor17/t8_nickolai.jpg)Name:** Matthew Nickolai


**Age:** 25


**Hometown:** Waterford, MI


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** JSS super star.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** White-blue splashing green for [River Hoopoe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=River+Hoopoe). It teamed up with [Crested Sunmare](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crested+Sunmare) to make a LOT of Horses. I went 8-1.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** Despite what I just said, the best card was definitely Royal Caracal.


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** 2-1 with blue-red aggro with a lot of reach, and then 3-0 with black-green.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** It means I get to go win a Pro Tour :) Shoutouts to Kyle, Jennifer, Jarod, Tiny Rick, the Cranberry Boiz and the Haolophiles.  

 




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gptor17/t8_an.jpg)Name:** You Cheng An


**Age:** 30


**Hometown:** Beijing, China


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** FNM Top 8


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** Red-white, 7-1-1.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Gust Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gust+Walker).


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** Went 5-1 with white-red and then white-black.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** I can’t get back home on time.  

 




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gptor17/t8_cuneo.jpg)Name:** Andrew Cuneo


**Age:** 42


**Hometown:** Philadelphia


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8 at GP Las Vegas.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** White, blue and black.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** Sideboarded in [Shatter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shatter)storm to destroy [Edifice of Authority](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Edifice+of+Authority) in one round. Also got Rhonis’s Monument and [Sunset Pyramid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunset+Pyramid) in another round.


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** Esper, 3-0. Black-red 2-0-1.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Locked Gold.  

 




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gptor17/t8_sun.jpg)Name:** Allen Sun


**Age:** 39


**Hometown:** Alexandria, Virginia


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8 at Grand Prix Vancouver


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** 8-1 with Blue-White splash black.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Hour of Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hour+of+Revelation) because it won my every game I cast it. The second best was [Cartouche of Ambition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartouche+of+Ambition) because I played against five red-white decks.


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** White-black splash green, 3-0. Four-Color Ramp, 1-1-1.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** It means the world to me because I’ve been trying for the past year to get back on the Pro Tour. Shoutout to all the great *Magic* players in the north DC metro area.  

 




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gptor17/t8_anderson.jpg)Name:** Robert Anderson


**Age:** 32


**Hometown:** Montreal, Quebec


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 64 at Pro Tour Philadelphia at the first Modern Pro Tour. I am old.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** Blue-black with one [Trial of Zeal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trial+of+Zeal) that I never drew. I went 8-1.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Torment of Hailfire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torment+of+Hailfire).


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** Red-white terrible, went 2-1. White-blue great, went 3-0.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** So happy to finally get there and share this with this great community.  

 




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gptor17/t8_weitz.jpg)Name:** Benjamin Weitz


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** St. Paul, MN


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** four Grand Prix Top 8, 2017 MOCS competitor. Gold pro.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** White-black, went 9-0.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [The Scarab God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God). Actually unbeatable.


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** Black-red with sideboarded [Nicol Bolas, God-Pharaoh](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+God-Pharaoh), went 3-0. Then drafted White-Blue [Anointed Procession](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anointed+Procession) and went 1-1-1.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Winning the tournament would get me Gold for next year in the Pro Players Club and mean my first trophy. That’s what I’ve got my sights set on.
