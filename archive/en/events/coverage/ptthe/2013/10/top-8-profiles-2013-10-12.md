### Guillame Wafo-Tapa


**Age:** 32  
**Team:** Revolution  
**Hometown:** Nantes, France  
**Occupation:** Card Player


**What was your record by format?**


**Standard Constructed:**  

8-0-2


**Booster Draft:**  

4-2 (1-2 and 3-0)


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour *Theros*? Who did you prepare with?**  

I playtested some in my hometown with friends, then went to Dublin a couple of weeks early to prepare with Team Revolution.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

I'm playing Esper because it's the kind of deck I like.


**How many  **Theros**  drafts did you do this week? How many online?**  

Three in person and seven online.


**Which  **Theros**  card is your favorite for its flavor?**   
[Hundred-Handed One](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hundred-Handed+One)


**What were you playing for this weekend and what did you achieve?**  

Nothing specific. I'm just giving it my best every game and having fun.

**Age:** 32  
**Hometown:** Enschede, the Netherlands

**What was your record by format?**


**Standard Constructed:**  

8-1-1


**Booster Draft:**  

4-2


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour *Theros*? Who did you prepare with?**   

I played in the  **Theros**  prerelease and in-between rounds with Frank Karsten.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

Monored Devotion. It was the best-performing deck from the prerelease test session, and I didn't have time to test afterwards.


**How many  **Theros**  drafts did you do this week? How many online?**   

None. The Pro Tour drafts were my only  **Theros**  drafts.


**What is your favorite myth or legend from Earth?**  

The myth of Cuchalain.


**Which  **Theros**  card is your favorite for its flavor?**   
[Rescue from the Underworld](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rescue+from+the+Underworld).


**What were you playing for this weekend and what did you achieve?**  

I had no expectations since I didn't know whether or not my deck was any good, so I'm obviously very happy to have made the Top 8.
