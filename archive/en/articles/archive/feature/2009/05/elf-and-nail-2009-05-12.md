For months [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) was the scourge of Standard, but while most players used it to make their Affinity decks more powerful, a group of Seattle players pioneered its use alongside Elves. The powerful green deck stormed through the Regionals tournament, including the top spot in Seattle, Washington. This year's Regionals are close at hand! Find out more information by [visiting the Regionals page](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/regionals).
