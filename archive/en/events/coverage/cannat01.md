2001 Canadian National Championship Coverage
--------------------------------------------


![National Championships Logo](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/nationals_sm.jpg)![Canadian Flag](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/worlds/images/flag_canada.jpg)Terry Tsang enjoyed quick victories with his Skies deck, becoming the 2001 Canadian National Champion. Joining him on the Canadian National Team are former Junior Pro-Tour great Elijah Pollock and newcomer Sam Lount, playing Counter-Rebels and Dark Fires respectively. Look for great success from this talented team at Worlds, returning the Sideboard spotlight back to Canada a few weeks from now.
