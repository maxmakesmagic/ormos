**Vintage Super League Finals Tonight!**


Vintage Super League (VSL) is live tonight at 5 p.m. PT on [Twitch](https://www.twitch.tv/magic)! And this week is the finals, where Reid Duke and Kevin Cron will face off to see who will walk away as the season's winner.


Interested in the decklists being played on VSL? Decklists are updated weekly and can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-season-7-decklists).

**Best of Throwback Standard Gauntlet**


The Best of Throwback Standard Gauntlet starts tomorrow, December 6. Yep, we made a Gauntlet out of our other Gauntlets, and this is your chance to pick up the best decks from different Standard eras and pit them against each other!


For detailed information on the schedule, prizes, decklists, and more, check out [Randy Buehler's article here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/best-throwback-standard-gauntlet-2017-11-29).


More information about Throwback Standard Gauntlets can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/flashback-favorites-and-throwback-standard-gauntlets-2016-12-15).

**Two PTQs This Weekend**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: two Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) are approaching! If you're aiming for a seat at Pro Tour Dominaria, one of these tournaments might just get you there.


Saturday, December 9, at 9 a.m., the format is Standard.


Sunday, December 10, at 9 a.m., the format is Modern


You can join either event for 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points. Learn more about Magic Online Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

***Vintage Cube*** **Returns**


*Vintage Cube* is back, and this time you'll have longer than ever before to try it out! Events start December 15 and will run all the way until January 15, when **Rivals of Ixalan** events become available.


To read about the tweaks to the card list and get more details about the events, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/vintage-masters-flashbacks-and-vintage-cube-2017-12-05).


*[Top](#top)*

****Amonkhet**** **Redemption Update**


Players who unsuccessfully attempted to purchase one or more Amonkhet (Regular) Redemption Sets between Monday, November 20, and the cutoff date of 10 a.m. PT on Wednesday, November 22, should contact Game Support [via our Help Form](http://wizards.custhelp.com/) no later than 10 a.m. PT on December 11 regarding their order. When contacting Game Support, please make sure to include the user name and contact information for the account attempting the purchase.

**Special** ****Vintage Masters**** **Flashbacks Next Week**


Starting next Wednesday, December 13, we're doing a week of **Vintage Masters** flashbacks—with an even-cooler-than-usual twist: all of the Power Nine with original art and card frames! Events will run December 13–20, with both regular and phantom drafts available.


For more information, check out [this article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/vintage-masters-flashbacks-and-vintage-cube-2017-12-05).
