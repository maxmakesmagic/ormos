The **Magic: The Gathering**® Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro TourTM events.


**TOURNAMENT FORMAT AND SCHEDULE**


* The format for Grand Prix - Buenos Aires is Extended.
* **Friday, November 24:** Registration from 7:00 - 9:00 P.M.
* **Saturday, November 25:** Registration from 8:30 - 9:30 A.M.
* **Saturday, November 25:** Tournament begins at 10:00 A.M. The Top 64 players advance to Sunday.
* **Sunday, November 26:** Finals begin at 9:30 A.M.

**TOURNAMENT**


* **Divisions**  

The main tournament at Grand Prix - Buenos Aires is open to **Magic**® players of all ages.

*If the player who wins an invitation to Pro Tour-Tokyo at Grand Prix-Buenos Aires has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher. Previous qualification may occur through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCITM player (valid on the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*
* **Tournament Format**  

The format for Grand Prix - Buenos Aires is Extended. All DCI-Extended rules and **Magic** Floor Rules announced on September 1, 2000, along with all subsequent updates, will be enforced.
* **Tournament Structure**  

The structure for Grand Prix - Buenos Aires is modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds, with the number of rounds determined by the number of participants), with the Top 64 players advancing to Day 2 of competition for prizes and invitations.
