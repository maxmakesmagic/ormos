Exclusive Cards in the Amonkhet Planeswalker Decks
==================================================



 Posted in **Card Preview**
 on April 6, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






Once again, we have two Planeswalker Decks being released with *Amonkhet*, this time featuring the visages of Gideon, Martial Paragon and Liliana, Death Wielder. Should you go looking for one of these decks, these are the two cards you'll see staring back at you from the front.


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_dZGIK04peP.png)![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_D5IulOvOh1.png)


If you pick up the package featuring Liliana gracing its cover, you'll get a full deck of Standard-legal cards, including these four cards that are found only in the Planeswalker Deck:


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_56ySiCdtoi.png)![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_fEaY9jHDFz.png)


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_mbVorMDziL.png)![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_olEf5iXy9L.png)


If you pick up the Gideon, Martial ~~Beefslab~~ Paragon deck, you'll receive, among other cards, these four:


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_LRAdMU66VH.png)![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_6oZ3HyFmjc.png)


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_2N7Yi2uqi1.png)![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/dw466ytu5_akh/en_UF2RIm3huj.png)


Planeswalker Decks will be available with *Amonkhet* at [a local store near you](http://locator.wizards.com/). We'll release the full decklists the week leading up to the [*Amonkhet* Prerelease](http://magic.wizards.com/en/event-types/prerelease) (which is April 22!).
