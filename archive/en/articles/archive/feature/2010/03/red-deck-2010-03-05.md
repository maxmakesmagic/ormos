Ben Lundquist is a former U.S. Nationals team member. At Pro Tour–San Diego, he managed to notch up six wins with his aggressive red deck in the Standard portion of the tournament. One of his biggest hitters, no pun intended, was [Stone Idol Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Idol+Trap). Not only is it a huge attacker, but it can even take out those pesky [Kor Firewalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Firewalker)s! Could this be the perfect deck for your Friday Night **Magic** tonight? For the full story of Pro Tour–San Diego, visit the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/g%C3%B6rtzen-goes-all-way-san-diego).
