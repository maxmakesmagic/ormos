Top 4 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 11, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











Learn more about Grand Prix Louisville's Top 4 teams—including who they are, what they’ve done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!


### 


### **Rubin-Hayne-Sigrist**


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gplou16/GP_LOU16_SiggyTeam.jpg)**


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gplou16/t8_hayne.jpg)Name:** Alexander Hayne


**Age:** 28


**Hometown:** Westmount, Quebec.


**Occupation:** Dark Wanderer


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* champion; Four Grand Prix wins.


**How did your team come together for this tournament?** I couldn't find anyone else who could put up with me.


**What were your best cards this weekend?** [Drag Under](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drag+Under), [It of the Horrid Swarm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=It+of+the+Horrid+Swarm) and [Wharf Infiltrator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wharf+Infiltrator) needs some money because it went to work overtime.  
  
**Does your team have a name? Any story to it?** “WE DEM BOYZ!”” Steve and Siggy were all about it.


**What was the most helpful thing your teammates did for you this tournament?** They gave great advice and played fantastically (no, really!)




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gplou16/t8_steve.jpg)Name:** Steve Rubin


**Age:** 24


**Hometown:** Pittsburgh, PA


**Occupation:** Magic player, high school coach


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Pro Tour *Shadows Over Innistrad* champion


**How did your team come together for this tournament?** We were previously teammates on the Pro Tour.


**What were your best cards this weekend?** [Lunarch Mantle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lunarch+Mantle) and Bloodbriat.  
  
**Does your team have a name? Any story to it?** We dem boyz. It's a Wiz Khalifa song. I'm Taylor Gang.


**What was the most helpful thing your teammates did for you this tournament?** They won matches!




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gplou16/t8_sigrist.jpg)Name:** Mike Sigrist


**Age:** 33


**Hometown:** Foxboro, MA


**Occupation:** Magic player/content provider


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** 2014-15 Player of the Year


**How did your team come together for this tournament?** We test together for Pro Tours.


**What were your best cards this weekend?** [Distended Mindbender](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Distended+Mindbender) — I had it in both my decks.  
  
**Does your team have a name? Any story to it?** We Dem Boyz. Me and Steve would sing it a lot during testing.


**What was the most helpful thing your teammates did for you this tournament?** Hayne helped on tough decisions and Steve ordered the Ubers.
