### Jadine Klomparens


**Age:** 24  
**Hometown:** Kalamazoo, MI  
**Occupation:** Student. Also pizza.  
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** 
  
Nothing of note.
 



**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**
  
Black Devotion. Every matchup is winnable.
 



**What would you change about your deck after this weekend?**
  
Nothing, really.
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend and why?**
  
[Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize). Have you read it?




**What will getting to play at the Pro Tour in Hawaii mean to you?**
  
A dream finally realized.
 



  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpchi14/t8_boettcher.jpg)


### Jared Boettcher


**Age:** 22  
**Hometown:** Rensselaer, NY  
**Occupation:** MTG Pro  
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** 
  
PT *Born of the Gods* 9th, PT *Journey into Nyx* 10th, GP DC 2013 2nd, GP Cincinnati 2014 3rd
 



**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**
  
Black Devotion splashing red. It's a consistent deck and has a lot of powerful cards.
 



**What would you change about your deck after this weekend?**
  
Would have to think about it more. Maybe change [Silence the Believers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silence+the+Believers) to a second [Erebos, God of the Dead](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Erebos%2C+God+of+the+Dead).
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend and why?**
  
[Whip of Erebos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whip+of+Erebos) was there when I needed to stabilize against the red decks I played this weekend.




**What will getting to play at the Pro Tour in Hawaii mean to you?**
  
I was already qualified, but, either way, it means a new place to add to my list of travels and the start of a fresh Pro Tour season.
 



  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpchi14/t8_rubin.jpg)


### Steve Rubin


**Age:** 22  
**Hometown:** Pittsburgh, PA  
**Occupation:** Student  
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** 
  
None
 



**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**
  
Black Devotion splashing green. It's the deck I'm the most comfortable playing.
 



**What would you change about your deck after this weekend?**
  
I would find room for an additional [Brindle Boar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brindle+Boar) or [Staff of the Death Magus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Staff+of+the+Death+Magus) in the sideboard.
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend and why?**
  
[Pack Rat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat). It helped me race against fast decks and win mirrors.




**What will getting to play at the Pro Tour in Hawaii mean to you?**
  
It will be nice to be qualified for the first Pro Tour of the season for the first time.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpchi14/t8_blum.jpg)


### Tyler Blum


**Age:** 24  
**Hometown:** Calgary, Alberta Canada  
**Occupation:** Student  
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** 
  
3rd Canadian Nationals 2005, played in 3 Pro Tours
 



**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**
  
Black Devotion. At 11:45 p.m. Friday night I was losing a lot with Blue/White Control. Neal Oliver had a backup Black Devotion deck! Thanks to Jon Stern for the list and Francis Toussaint and Alexander Hayne for coaching me.
 



**What would you change about your deck after this weekend?**
  
-2 [Temple of Malady](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+of+Malady) +2 [Temple of Deceit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+of+Deceit)




**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend and why?**
  
[Pack Rat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat). So many free wins. Very vital in going 6-0 vs. Blue Devotion.




**What will getting to play at the Pro Tour in Hawaii mean to you?**
  
Everything. I play this game to play on the Pro Tour!
