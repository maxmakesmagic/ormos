Decks of the Week: 6/14/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 14, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Last weekend saw two Limited Grand Prix events! So, congratulations to New York trio Andrew Longo, Eric Phillipps, and Eric Berger, who beat out 585 other teams to win the Team Limited GP in [Providence](/en/events/coverage/old-friends-new-champions-providence). Meanwhile, across the Atlantic, Danish newcomer Oscar Christensen beat out a field of 1,006 players in [GP Gothenburg](/en/events/coverage/christensen-breaks-through-gothenburg). Those four players will be heading to Dublin in October to compete at Pro Tour *Theros*, and you can join them by winning a PTQ! The current PTQ season is Standard, so check out these Top 8 Standard decks from recent events and find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros). Good luck; have fun!
