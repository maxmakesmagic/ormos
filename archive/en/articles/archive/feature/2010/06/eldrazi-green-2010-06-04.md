The deck name "Eldrazi Green" has a whole new meaning after Pro Tour–San Juan. Originally referring to a token deck that used [Eldrazi Monument](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Monument), Kotaro Otsuka took a version that used Spawn tokens to actually cast Eldrazi. The creation was good enough to put him firmly into the Top 8, but can you add cards from Magic 2010 and the Alara Block to make the deck ready for Standard? Read the official coverage of Pro Tour–San Juan [here](/en/events/coverage/paulo-celebrates-six-beach-san-juan).
