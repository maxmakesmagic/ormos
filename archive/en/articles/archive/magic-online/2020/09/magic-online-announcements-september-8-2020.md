*Double Masters* on *Magic Online*
----------------------------------


[As announced previously](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/double-masters-magic-online-edition-and-gen-con-online-2020-07-23), *Double Masters* events are ongoing with Sealed Deck, Draft, and Phantom Draft Leagues available. If you haven't checked out the updated *Magic* Premier Play schedule yet and what Super Qualifiers qualify you for, go [read that announcement](https://magic.gg/news/announcing-the-2020-2021-magic-esports-season).

Cube Spotlight: *Live the Dream*
--------------------------------


David McDarby, operations administrator and game designer at WotC, brings us the latest entry in MTGO's Cube Spotlight Series: *Live the Dream*. Here, cards cost more mana, have more versatility, and are more . . . *mythic*. If you haven't yet, check out [David's article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/cube-spotlight-series-live-dream-2020-09-07) which covers all eleven archetypes from the cube and includes a full card list.


*Live the Dream* runs from tomorrow through September 16, and entry options are 10 Event Tickets or 100 Play Points.

Legacy Split Championship Qualifier Saturday
--------------------------------------------


On Saturday, September 12, there will be a Legacy Split Championship Qualifier at 7 a.m. PT! The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points. The winner qualifies for the *Zendikar Rising* Split Championship on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Split Championship Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#ptq).




---

Modern Super Qualifier Sunday
-----------------------------


On Sunday, September 13, there will be a Modern Super Qualifier at 3 a.m. PT! The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points, 40 Event Tickets, or 400 Play Points. The winner qualifies for the *Zendikar Rising* Split Championship on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Super Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#sq).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats. To learn more about Premier Play qualification, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020).


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges now run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place. *NOTE: The Pauper Challenge is never pre-empted**.*
