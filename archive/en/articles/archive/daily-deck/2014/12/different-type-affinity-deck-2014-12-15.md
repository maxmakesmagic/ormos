A Different Type of Affinity Deck
=================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**

[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Ensoul%20Artifact)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ensoul%20Artifact)


The basic Affinity shell is very powerful in Modern. Play as many cheap artifacts as you can, relying on a few powerful cards such as [Cranial Plating](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cranial+Plating), [Steel Overseer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steel+Overseer), or [Arcbound Ravager](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcbound+Ravager) to do much of the heavy lifting. Yuusei Gotou felt that the deck was a little too reliant on these cards but would benefit from pulling back on the heavy artifact theme for some other hard-hitting cards.



Instead of playing cards like [Arcbound Ravager](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcbound+Ravager) or [Steel Overseer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steel+Overseer) that require many artifacts, Yuusei's deck focused on cards that only need a small number of artifacts to be effective. This led Yuusei to [Ensoul Artifact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ensoul+Artifact) and [Shrapnel Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shrapnel+Blast). While [Cranial Plating](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cranial+Plating) is still around, it is still a very effective card with only three or four artifacts in play.


With less burden on artifacts, Yuusei gains the ability to play nonartifact cards more liberally. [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) and [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) are his main-deck choices, with [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) and [Whipflare](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whipflare) in the sideboard. These cards provide a degree of resilience against cards that are normally potent against a classic Affinity deck. After all, no amount of [Ancient Grudge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Grudge)s will stop a [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) from attacking you.
