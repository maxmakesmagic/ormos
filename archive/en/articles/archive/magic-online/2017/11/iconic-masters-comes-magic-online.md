Iconic Masters Comes to Magic Online on November 17!
====================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on November 9, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/lee_author_image_0.jpeg)
By Lee Sharpe











*Iconic Masters* is almost here, bringing some of the most famous *Magic* cards of all time back to draft in an all-new format! Available in the *Magic Online* store for $6.99 per booster pack, *Iconic Masters* releases simultaneously with tabletop *Magic* on Friday, November 17, and will be available until December 6. *Magic Online* will feature two Draft leagues for this release, regular and phantom. In case that isn't enough, we’ve also added two open *Iconic Masters* Sealed Deck PTQs to our PTQ schedule.


This release also features three new limited-release Play Point Bundles and a Treasure Chest update that includes cards from *Commander (2017 Edition)*. Check out all the details below!


**Introducing Play Point Bundles (for a limited time)**


Starting November 15, *Magic Online* will offer three new Play Point Bundles in the *Magic Online* store:




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Store Item | Price |
|  | $24 |
|  | $45 |
|  | $100 |

You can use the Bundles either to round out your Play Point collection or to buy in bulk and save some money. Just purchase, open, and play. And speaking of saving money, if you don’t open the Silver (240) Play Point Bundle, you can use it to join an *Iconic Masters* draft between November 17 and December 6 to save 10 Play Points. See below for more on that.


These Bundles are only available through the holidays, and will disappear from the store when *Rivals of Ixalan* releases on January 19, 2018.


***Iconic Masters* Drafts**


*Magic Online* will offer both regular and phantom *Iconic Masters* Draft leagues from November 17 until December 6.


*ICONIC MASTERS* DRAFT LEAGUE


* Start Time: Friday, November 17, at 10 a.m. Pacific
* Closed Time: Wednesday, December 6, at 10 a.m. Pacific
* End Time: Wednesday, December 6, at 2 p.m. Pacific
* Entry Options:
	+ 25 Event Tickets
	+ 3 *Iconic Masters* boosters + 4 Event Tickets
	+ 250 Play Points
	+ 1 Silver (240) Play Point Bundle
* Structure: Draft, then up to 3 rounds at your convenience
* Prizes:
	+ 3 wins: 6 *Iconic Masters* boosters + 1 Qualifier Point
	+ 2 wins: 2 *Iconic Masters* boosters

*ICONIC MASTERS* PHANTOM DRAFT LEAGUE


* Start Time: Friday, November 17, at 10 a.m. Pacific
* Closed Time: Wednesday, December 6, at 10 a.m. Pacific
* End Time: Wednesday, December 6, at 2 p.m. Pacific
* Entry Options:
	+ 10 Event Tickets
	+ 100 Play Points
* Structure: Draft, then up to 3 rounds at your convenience
* Prizes:
	+ 3 wins: 150 Play Points
	+ 2 wins: 100 Play Points
	+ 1 win: 40 Play Points
	+ 0 wins: 10 Play Points

Once you feel like these drafts have given you a handle on *Iconic Masters* Limited, want to try to qualify for the Pro Tour with it?


***Iconic Masters* PTQ Sealed Open**


We’ve added two extra Sealed Deck PTQs to the schedule to celebrate *Iconic Masters*. Unlike most Sealed PTQs, no qualification is required. Just join and play! Please note that these bonus PTQ events will not accept Limited PTQ entry tokens, but players still have the same number of opportunities to use their tokens in other events this season.


The winner of each *Iconic Masters* PTQ will qualify for Pro Tour *Dominaria* and the Pro Tour Challenge at that Pro Tour. All rules, terms, and conditions for *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers (found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers)) apply to these events.


The events will be on Friday, November 24, and Sunday, December 3, at 7 a.m. Pacific. The full event structure is available below:


* Event Times: Friday, November 24, and Sunday, December 3, at 7 a.m. Pacific
* Entry Options:
	+ 50 Event Tickets
	+ 500 Play Points
* Structure: Sealed rounds based on attendance, then Top 8 players draft and play single-elimination rounds
* Prizes:
	+ 1st Place: Invitation to Pro Tour *Dominaria* + invitation to Pro Tour Challenge + 1 complete premium set of *Iconic Masters* + 108 *Iconic Masters* boosters + 6 QPs
	+ 2nd Place: 1 complete premium set of *Iconic Masters* + 108 *Iconic Masters* boosters + 5 QPs
	+ 3rd–4th Place: 1 complete regular set of *Iconic Masters* + 54 *Iconic Masters* boosters + 4 QPs
	+ 5th–8th Place: 54 *Iconic Masters* boosters + 3 QPs
	+ 9th–32nd Place: 24 *Iconic Masters* boosters + 1 QP

***Magic Online* Treasure Chest Update**


Two big changes to Treasure Chests arrive with the November 15 maintenance. First, select cards from *Commander (2017 Edition)* and other sets are making their *Magic Online* debut! Second, we’re adding complete Standard-legal, non-foil sets to Treasure Chests. If you get one of these sets, you’ll need to open it before you can use the cards for your *Magic Online* Constructed decks, redeem them (if the awarded set is still eligible for redemption), or trade them!


We are adding a new sheet to Treasure Chests, the Exclusive sheet. These are for cards that Magic Online Treasure Chests are the only way to get these cards. This includes cards from Commander 2017, as well as promo cards with new art! Here is how likely each of the 3 Treasure Chest slots is to pull from each sheet:




| Slot | Curated | Modern R/M | Exclusive | Play Points | Standard C/U |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 22% | 43% | 5% | 30% | 0% |
| 2 | 6.50% | 10% | 5% | 0% | 78.50% |
| 3 | 1% | 8% | 5% | 0% | 86% |
