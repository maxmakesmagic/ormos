Magic at San Diego Comic Con
============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 27, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1003_sdcc.jpg)](http://www.comic-con.org/)It's time for San Diego Comic Con! Wizards of the Coast will be in attendance, and we'll be bringing all manner of **Magic: The Gathering** events. We even have a site in the Gaslamp Quarter this year! SDCC runs from July 12-15, with a preview night July 11. Remember, panels at SDCC fill up quickly and space is VERY limited this year, so plan appropriately!

**Magic: The Gathering** Highlights 

* Check out the MTG Experience at 431 J Street in the Gaslamp District!   

	+ Demo **Magic: The Gathering** on traditional and digital platforms – including iPad!
	+ Get in on special evening events.
	+ Purchase your favorite licensed merchandise and latest Magic product.
	+ Find a store near you with the WPN Store Locator.
	+ Be a part of the **Magic** community!
	+ Pick up **Magic** swag including a **Duels of the Planeswalkers** 2013 promo code, which will unlock one new playable card in each of your decks accessed through game play.

The MTG Experience will be open noon–10pm on July 12–14 and 9am–6pm on July 15. Check in at the MTG Experience on Foursquare or Facebook and you may receive a random alternate art Euro land card.\*

\* While supplies last. One per person.

* Play in **Magic** events all weekend, including four different leagues and Mini-Master events at the Marriott, Ballroom F. [Click here](http://www.cascadegames.com/events/919) for a list of all tournaments.
* Attend the **Magic** panel "What's New With **Magic: The Gathering**" Friday, July 13, from 6 to 7pm in room 24ABC.

Panel description:   
 On the heels of **Magic**'s most successful year in its 19-year history, some of the game's top creators share details on upcoming products as well as answer your questions. The panel, moderated by Mark Rosewater (head **Magic** designer), includes Aaron Forsythe (director, **Magic** R&D), Matt Cavotta (senior creative **Magic** art director), Mark Purvis (**Magic** senior brand manager), and Scott Larabee (**Magic** organized play program manager).

* Say hello to our Street Team, who will be roaming the Gaslamp District, handing out information and possibly other goodies as well!

The street team will be demoing **Duels of the Planeswalkers** 2013 on iPad as well.

For complete scheduling information about San Diego Comic Con, visit [www.comic-con.org](http://www.comic-con.org/).
