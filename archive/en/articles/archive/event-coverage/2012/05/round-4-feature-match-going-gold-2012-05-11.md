=========================================

 on May 11, 2012

Long time pro and three-time Grand Prix Top 8 competitor Florian Pils has a lot on the line this weekend. Pils came into this event with sixteen Pro Points and is in need of a Top 25 finish in order to reach Gold level in the Pro Player's Club, which would guarantee that he will be invited to every Pro Tour during the 2012-2013 season.


His opponent this round, Grand Prix Santiago Top 8 competitor Melissa DeTora, has an even tougher road ahead of her. DeTora has fourteen Pro Points coming into this event, and needs a Top 16 finish to reach the greatly coveted Gold level.



![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptavr12/r4_detora_vs_pils.jpg)
*Both Melissa DeTora and Florian Pils need a strong finish this weekend to lock up Gold level in the Pro Player's Club.*

While Pils and DeTora certainly have their work cut out for them, they've already gotten some of their heavy lifting done by starting off 3-0.


### Game 1


Melissa DeTora had a [Mayor of Avabruck](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mayor+of+Avabruck), a [Champion of the Parish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Champion+of+the+Parish), and a [Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells) to start off the match. But a [Pillar of Flame](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pillar+of+Flame), a [Brimstone Volley](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brimstone+Volley), and a [Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells) of his own gave Florian Pils a distinct early lead.



![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptavr12/r4_pils.jpg)
*[Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells) is put to use by both players, but removal made sure that the one Pils played was supreme.*

At this point, DeTora found herself without any relevant spells, while Pils's [Wolfir Silverheart](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolfir+Silverheart) ensured that she would have no time to recover.


Melissa DeTora 0, Florian Pils 1


### Game 2


Pils mulliganed to start the second game, but that didn't seem to set him back at all as he opened on [Avacyn's Pilgrim](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avacyn%27s+Pilgrim), [Borderland Ranger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Borderland+Ranger), and [Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells). DeTora looked to keep up with an [Ambush Viper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ambush+Viper) and a [Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells) of her own, but a [Bonfire of the Damned](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bonfire+of+the+Damned) for two left her without a board


Pils was able to deal with DeTora's next two copies of [Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells), but he had no answer for the [Wolfir Silverheart](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolfir+Silverheart) that followed.



![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptavr12/r4_detora.jpg)
*DeTora presses on after losing her board to a [Bonfire of the Damned](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bonfire+of+the+Damned) with some more wolf-powered action.*

But the giant sized [Wolfir Silverheart](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolfir+Silverheart) wouldn't be quite enough for DeTora to stabilize, as a [Kessig Wolf Run](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kessig+Wolf+Run),plus a [Geistflame](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geistflame) allowed Pils to knock DeTora down to exactly 0 life before she could take over the game with her overwhelmingly large creatures.


Melissa DeTora 0, Florian Pils 2
