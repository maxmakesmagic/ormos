*Core Set 2021*: MTGO Edition
-----------------------------


*Core Set 2021* is arriving soon on *Magic Online* after this week's update, and although events start a few days later, we're going into downtime on Tuesday, June 23 to push the new build live. Yes, you read that right: this time we've got a Tuesday downtime instead of Wednesday. As usual, we're planning to start around 9 a.m. Pacific time (UTC-7) and come back up by noon.


You can read more about *Core Set 2021* on MTGO in [Alli's release article here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/core-set-2021-magic-online-edition-2020-06-19), and you can check out the updated Treasure Chest contents [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/treasure-chest-card-list-info).

*Core Set 2021* Launch Super Event
----------------------------------


So, you've scoured the previews and are ready to test your mettle against the best players around, eh? Here's your chance! We've added two special *Core Set 2021* Launch Premier Events in addition to our regular release events. Similar to the Super Qualifiers, these large scheduled events feature "double" prizes for the Top 8 qualifiers (though there are no invitations awarded for this event), including complete *Core Set 2021* sets. If you want to brush up on the set before joining, Leagues start on Thursday morning. Have fun!


**Start Times**:


* Saturday, June 27, at 7:00 a.m. PT
* Friday, July 3, at 7:00 a.m. PT

**Format**: *Core Set 2021* Sealed Deck  
**Product**: 6 *Core Set 2021* Booster Packs. Product opened in Limited events *will* be added to players' collections.  
**Location**: The Limited lobby under Premier Events  
**Entry Options**:


* 40 Event Tickets
* 400 Play Points
* 40 *Magic Online* Qualifier Points

**Size**: 129–672 players  
**Duration**: A number of rounds determined by entries, with Swiss pairings. Each round plays up to 50 minutes, followed by a single-elimination Top 8.  
**Prizes**:





| **Place** | **Prizes** |
| --- | --- |
| 1st–2nd | 900 Play Points, 200 Treasure Chests, and 1 premium foil *Core Set 2021* set |
| 3rd–4th | 750 Play Points, 150 Treasure Chests, and 1 premium foil *Core Set 2021* set |
| 5th–8th | 600 Play Points, 100 Treasure Chests, and 1 non-foil *Core Set 2021* set |
| 9th–16th | 400 Play Points and 50 Treasure Chests |
| 17th–32nd | 400 Play Points and 25 Treasure Chests |
| 33rd–64th | 200 Play Points and 10 Treasure Chests |
| 65th–128th | 100 Play Points |

*Legacy Cube* Extended to July 1
--------------------------------


Do you like that one card, from that one set, and you just never see it anymore? Fear not, *Legacy Cube* returns June 10, and you may see it again! *Legacy Cube* is full of some of the best cards from across *Magic*'s history, working together in ways you can only experience in a cube. And the fun doesn't stop until July 1. Then, following right behind the *Legacy Cube*, the fan-favorite *Vintage Cube* returns, running from July 1 through July 29.


Full information on the contents of the *Legacy Cube* can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/legacy-cube-cardlist).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats. To learn more about Premier Play qualification, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020).


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges now run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place. *NOTE: The Pauper Challenge is never pre-empted**.*
