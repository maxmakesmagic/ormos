**The *Magic Online* Championship starts Friday, March 3!**


It's time to start getting hyped! From Friday, March 3, through Sunday, March 5, the *Magic Online* Championship will be streaming live from Wizards HQ. Watch 16 of the world's top *Magic* players battle it out for online supremacy. [Find out more details here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/magic-online-championship-approaches-2017-02-07)!

**Full *Invasion* Block Flashback Drafts begin tomorrow, March 1**


The Flashback Favorites Draft series continues tomorrow with *Invasion* block drafts starting Wednesday, March 1! Draft *Invasion*-*Planeshift*-*Apocalypse* with seven other players through March 8. For more information, [click here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/flashback-favorites-and-throwback-standard-gauntlets-2016-12-15).

***Aether Revolt* Standard Championship on Sunday, March 5**


The *Magic Online* *Aether Revolt* Standard Championship begins Sunday, March 5! Qualifying players will be able to complete for awesome prizes like premium *Aether Revolt* sets! [Find out more details here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-aether-revolt-championship-event-details)!

***Amonkhet* Regional Pro Tour Qualifier on Saturday, March 4**


A Standard Regional Pro Tour Qualifier (RPTQ) is coming up on March 4. Please note, these events are only open to players who have qualified through an in-store qualifier event.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/instoreplay/rptqakh).


[Top](#top)

***Amonkhet* Sealed Pro Tour Qualifer on Sunday, March 5**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! The Sealed Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries begin Thursday, March 2, and run through Tuesday, March 7. We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, March 5!


Remember, you are no longer required to enter into the PTQ Finals immediately following the Preliminary that you qualified through, thanks to the redesigned PTQ system that *Magic Online* Events Manager Lee Sharpe announced [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/pro-tour-qualifier-changes-2015-11-16).


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#AKH_schedule).
