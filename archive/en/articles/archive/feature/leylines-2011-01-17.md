Looking for a hilariously fun way of winning on the first turn in Vintage and/or Legacy? Try Leylines! Here's how the deck works: first, mulligan/[Serum Powder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serum+Powder) your opening hand into a ton of Leylines, [Serra's Sanctum](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serra%27s+Sanctum), and [Opalescence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opalescence). Cast all of your Leylines for free at the beginning of the game. Play the Sanctum, which generates a lot of mana thanks to all your Leylines, then tap it to cast [Opalescence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opalescence). Because your Leylines were on the battlefield at the start of the turn, they aren't affected by summoning sickness, and you can attack for the win! Awesome!
