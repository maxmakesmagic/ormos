Where to Find Commander Legends Previews
========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 23, 2020 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*Commander Legends* previews are almost upon us!


It can be a bit daunting to try to follow along with everything, so to help out, we've created this guide to preview season


This page will help you follow along with everything coming on every day by giving you the outlets we have planned on each day. The goal is to help you find what you're looking for every day and avoid engaging in leaks if you so choose.


Below, you'll find a list of dates as well as any outlets previewing a card on that date. Once the previews are live, we'll update this very article so that you can easily find previews once they're up. If the link simply goes to the front of a website or social media site, that means they probably haven't previewed the card yet.


And if you just want to see all the cards that have been officially previewed in one place, you can always check out our [Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/commander-legends) and our bonus [Variant Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/commander-legends-variants) (which contains alternate versions of *Commander Legends* cards). Note that the CIGs are updated every morning, so they contain all cards previewed the previous day (and earlier), but not the day of. For the most up-to-date listings, you can check this page!


So bookmark this page and check back regularly for all the latest previews from *Commander Legends*!
