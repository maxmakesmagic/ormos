While the rest of the world's pros were battling with [Gifts Ungiven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven) style control decks, Antonino De Rosa was following the path of a different drummer. His innovative Mono-Blue Control deck was the perfect foil to the [Hana Kami](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hana+Kami) engine from Gifts Ungiven, and he managed to take his deck all the way to the title slot at Grand Prixâ€“Salt Lake City.
