Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013 Expansion
=========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 19, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/digital/d13/D13_Headers.jpg)### Magic: The Gathering — Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013 Expansion


In the Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013 Expansion, enjoy five new playable decks whose colors correspond to the guilds in Return to Ravnica, five new puzzling challenges, and ten new single-player campaign levels. Put your spell-slinging skills to the test and try to defeat the guilds and unlock their new decks!


* Five new playable decks: Take on your opponents with new two-color decks that correspond to the Azorius, Golgari, Izzet, Rakdos, and Selesnya guilds.
* New campaign levels: The expansion adds on five new single-player campaign levels and five single-player revenge campaign levels.
* Five new challenges: New puzzles for you to solve.
* Plus even more: Ten new persona portraits to unlock, new [achievements/trophies], and more!

### Aura Servants


On the city-world of Ravnica, the Azorius Senate creates magically binding laws to enforce peace. The “Aura Servants” deck uses Auras to improve the strength and ferocity of its creatures. Endlessly customize your forces with different magical enhancements!

### Sepulchral Strength


The city-world of Ravnica relies on the black- and green-aligned Golgari Swarm to oversee the messier aspects of life and death. The “Sepulchral Strength” deck harnesses the power of death for its own purposes. Sap vitality from enemy creatures and add it to your own en route to crushing all opposition!

On the city-world of Ravnica, magical research is the chief domain of the Izzet League, often with explosive results. The “Mindstorms” deck is one such result. Dangerously unstable elementals and weirds take the fight to the enemy, while lightning strikes rain destruction from above. Use the Izzet’s deadliest discoveries against your enemies!
