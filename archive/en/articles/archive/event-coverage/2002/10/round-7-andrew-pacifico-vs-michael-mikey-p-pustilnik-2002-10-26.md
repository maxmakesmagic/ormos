Round 7: Andrew Pacifico vs. Michael "Mikey P" Pustilnik
========================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 26, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_602.png)
By Matt Urban











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpphi02/a935.jpg)New York City's Mikey P is a long time professional player. In fact, he is the only player with a Pro Tour win, a Grand Prix win, and a Masters Series win. Andrew Pacifico is a name that hasn't surfaced recently, but some may remember the Florida resident as JSS National Champion in 1998. Coming to Philadelphia with one bye and a dream, both he and Mikey P are in the one more loss and you're out bracket at 5-1.

Mikey P was not extremely happy with his B/R/W deck, saying "My deck is unremarkable, but we'll give it a shot." Andrew just smiled wanly, knowing his R/G deck is full of hits.

### Game 1

Andrew won the die roll and elected to play first. Both players started out slowly, with the only early spell being Mikey P's turn 2 [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift). He followed this with a turn 3 Morphed creature. Andrew did nothing but take damage from the Morph until he summoned a turn 5 [Barkhide Mauler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barkhide+Mauler). He followed this up with a turn 6 [Rorix Bladewing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rorix+Bladewing). 

With his life total at a lofty 16, Andrew was able to cast [Crown of Vigor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crown+of+Vigor) on the Dragon Legend and attack with his entire team, following that up with a [Thoughtbound Primoc](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtbound+Primoc). Mikey P managed to cycle [Lay Waste](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lay+Waste) and cast [Death Pulse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Pulse) to kill Rorix, but Andrew kept summoning huge monsters, including a second [Barkhide Mauler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barkhide+Mauler) and a [Butcher Orgg](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher+Orgg). Mikey just looked at his next card and packed them up.

Pacifico - 1, Mikey P - 0

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpphi02/a936.jpg)Mikey P chose to play, hoping to get enough damage in before the bombs in Andrew's deck show. Again he led with a turn 2 [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift), but Andrew had a play before turn 5 in the form of [Wellwisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher). The elf was promptly killed by Mikey through cycling [Swat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swat) and activating [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift). The [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift) was activated using an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island), as Mike had switched from white to blue. Andrew cycled [Krosan Tusker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Tusker) and took 2 to the head for his efforts thanks to the [Lightning Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift).

After untapping, Andrew summoned [Thoughtbound Primoc](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtbound+Primoc) and used [Erratic Explosion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Erratic+Explosion) to deal lethal damage to Mikey's recently summoned Morph creature. After passing the turn in which he only played a land, Andrew again summoned [Rorix Bladewing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rorix+Bladewing), hitting Mikey down to 12. Mike tried to put up a fight, including killing Rorix through cycling [Solar Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solar+Blast) and casting [Death Pulse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Pulse), but it wasn't enough. Andrew cast [Crown of Fury](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crown+of+Fury) on the [Thoughtbound Primoc](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtbound+Primoc), increasing the speed of the clock by one turn and winning the race easily.

**Final Result:** Pacifico - 2, Mikey P - 0
