The **Magic: The Gathering** Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro Tour events.

### TOURNAMENT


* **Divisions**  

 The main tournament at Grand Prix - Utsunomiya is open to **Magic** players of all ages.


If a player that wins an invitation to Pro Tour - Chicago at Grand Prix - Utsunomiya is already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher. Previous qualification may occur through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid on the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).
* **Tournament Structure**  

 The structure for Grand Prix - Utsunomiya is modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds, with the number of rounds determined by the number of participants), with the Top 64 players advancing to the Sunday finals for prizes and invitations.
* **Tournament Byes**  *(Byes are not cumulative through the season or year.)*  

 Byes for Grand Prix - Utsunomiya are based on the 2002 World Championship professional standings and DCI ratings as of August 21, 2002. Please refer to the [Grand Prix - Utsunomiya Bye List](/en/articles/archive/feature/grand-prix-utsunomiya-byes-2002-08-29) for details.
