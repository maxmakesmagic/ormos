May 17-19, 2013












Pro Tour Dragon's Maze in San Diego
-----------------------------------


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/ptq_2012.jpg)Get your Modern Constructed skills warmed up, because invitations and tickets to Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze* in San Diego are up for grabs this Pro Tour Qualifier season!


Look through the events below for Top 8 Modern decklists. You can test each decklist thanks to our [Sample Hand Generator](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/feature/294). Just click on the card fan in the upper right of each decklist and you can see what your opening hand could be, along with mulligans and the first three cards you'd draw.


Deck lists will be updated weekly during the PTQ season. The season runs from December 22, 2012 - March 17, 2013. For more information about the PTQ season, [click here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/protour/qualifiers/dragonsmaze13). You can also  [qualify on **Magic Online**](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/events/ptqmtgo/dragonsmaze/13) !
