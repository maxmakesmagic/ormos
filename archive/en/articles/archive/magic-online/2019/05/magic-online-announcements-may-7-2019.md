New Play Lobby Beta
-------------------


The time has finally come—be the first to use our new Play Lobby Beta to join events! Our goal with this update is simple: we want to make it much quicker to find and join the events and games you want without asking you to work through inscrutable menus and option lists.


To sign up for the Beta, click [here](https://mtgo-support.wizards.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000941903-Magic-Online-Closed-Beta-Requirements). The deadline for New Play Lobby Beta signup is 12 p.m. on Wednesday, May 8.

Vintage Cube Returns May 15
---------------------------


That's right! Vintage Cube is almost back, and it's nothing like any other cube out there. It's simply overflowing with iconic cards from *Magic*'s entire 25-year history, and you can draft the super-deck of your dreams when it arrives May 15. Until then, enjoy plenty of play with *War of the Spark*.




---

*Modern Horizons* June 6
------------------------


*Modern Horizons* is on the horizon. Expect more details on the arrival of this new set in the coming weeks and as soon as previews start on May 19.




---

*Magic Online* Championship
---------------------------


The wait is almost over—24 qualified players from the 2018 *Magic Online* Championship Series will battle for a $200,000 prize pool! The *Magic Online* Championship takes place Friday–Sunday, May 17–19 at Wizards' HQ in Renton, Washington.


Formats for the event include *War of the Spark* Draft, plus both Standard and Modern Constructed. Live video coverage will be provided by the Vintage Super League team of Randy Buehler and Athena Froehlich at [twitch.tv/magic](https://twitch.tv/magic) at 10 a.m. Friday, 8 a.m. Saturday, and 10 a.m. Sunday.

Afternoon Limited MCQ Friday
----------------------------


On Friday, May 10, there will be a Limited MCQ Finals at 3 p.m. PT. This time change is a test for our ongoing effort to identify how best to serve our player base and accommodate different schedules. If the event does well, we'll likely continue to run events in this time slot occasionally going forward.


The set will be *War of the Spark*, and the event will cost 1 Limited MCQ Finals Token. The winner will receive an invitation to Mythic Championship Barcelona.


Learn more about Mythic Championship Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-mythic-championship-qualifiers).




---

Standard MCQ Finals Saturday
----------------------------


On Saturday, May 11, there will be a Standard MCQ Finals at 9 a.m. PT. The event will cost 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points. The winner will receive an invitation to Mythic Championship Barcelona.


Learn more about Mythic Championship Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-mythic-championship-qualifiers).




---

Modern MOCS Preliminary Sunday
------------------------------


On Sunday, May 12, there will be a *Magic Online* Championship Series Preliminary event for the 2019 MOCS! The format will be Modern, and things will kick off at 7 a.m. PT. MOCS Preliminaries are eight rounds of Swiss and require 35 Qualifier Points to enter. Winners will receive (among other prizes) an invitation to the MOCS Playoff event on July 13.


Learn more about MOCS events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2019-magic-online-championship-series#MOCSPrelims2019).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format challenges are the first step toward qualifying for premier play through your favorite *Magic* formats. The *Magic Online* Format Challenges continue every weekend for Modern, Vintage, Legacy, and Pauper formats except when replaced by Format Playoffs during scheduled Format Playoff dates.

Build Notes
-----------


Standard | [Cry of the Carnarium](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cry+of+the+Carnarium) can no longer exile creatures that died many turns ago.


Replay | The game no longer crashes for a spectator when a player submits their sideboard.


Collection | The load time for planeswalker art assets has been improved.


General | Attacking with a creature that has provoke can no longer reset the game.


Limited | [Band Together](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Band+Together) no longer gives incorrect text in the prompt box if only one friendly creature is targeted.


Duel Scene | Auto-Pass (F8/8) no longer passes priority when there is floating mana and a spell with unique ways of paying costs such as delve in the player's hand.
