Pro Tour–San Diego 2007 Format and Schedule
===========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 5, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











### Tournament Format


*All Universal Tournament Rules and Magic: the Gathering Floor Rules are in effect for this event unless specifically overruled by information in this Format Document.*


**Format**

- Two-Headed Giant Booster Draft

- All teams must designate an A & B player. During play, the A player is the primary player and sits to the right of the B player (who is the secondary player):

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2hgseatingchart.jpg)
- 2 *Time Spiral*, 2 *Planar Chaos*, and 2 *Future Sight* booster packs will be used by each 2-person team for drafting.

- Competition will consist of 7 Booster Drafts, each followed by 2 rounds of competition.

- All matches will be single-game matches.

- Check [www.thedci.com](http://www.thedci.com) for Two-Headed Giant Booster Draft rules.

**Round Length**  


- 14 Swiss Rounds total, 60 minutes per round.

- The deck building period after each draft will be 40 minutes.

- Top 4 single-elimination finals have no time limit.

- When time is called, the active team will finish the turn and three additional turns will be taken.

- Stalling and slow play rules will be strictly enforced during the tournament.

**Advancement**  


- All teams may participate in the first 3 drafts and Swiss rounds of Friday's competition.

- Teams with 12 or more match points after round 6 will advance to draft 4 on day 2.

- The top 16 teams after round 12 will advance to draft 7 on day 3.

- The top 4 teams after round 14 will advance to Sunday's top 4 single-elimination finals.

**Top 4**  


- Top 4 matches will be single-game matches.

- The Top 4 teams will be seated randomly at the draft table immediately prior to drafting.

- The Top 4 bracket will be determined using this random seating.

- Where necessary, the standings after the Swiss rounds will still be used to determine final order in the standings.

- While there are no time limits in the Top 4, teams are expected to play at a reasonable pace and finish their matches within 60 minutes.

**Notes**  


- 48K, REL: Professional

- Players are responsible for knowing all DCI rules and [high-level tournament rules](http://www.wizards.com/protour/images/HighlevelRules.pdf).

- Players must bring pen, paper, card sleeves and appropriate counters.

- Players will be drafting from boosters that have had the foils cards replaced. Foils from *Time Spiral* Block boosters are replaced as follows:

- Common, uncommon, and rare foils are replaced by a random, unduplicated common card.

- "Timeshifted" cards from *Time Spiral* are replaced by a random, unduplicated "timeshifted" card.

**Friday Schedule**  
 8:55 AM Seating for player meeting posted.  
 9:00 AM Mandatory Player meeting begins.  
 9:15 AM Day 1 play begins.


**Saturday Schedule**  
 8:55 AM Day 2 pairings posted.  
 9:00 AM Day 2 play begins.


**Sunday Schedule**  
 8:55 AM Day 3 pairings posted.  
 9:00 AM Day 3 play begins.  
 1:30 PM Top 4 teams check in at feature match area.  
 2:15 PM Single-elimination Finals draft begins.
