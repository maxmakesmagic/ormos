Announcing "From the Vault: Dragons"
====================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 13, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![From the Vault: Dragons](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1589_1.jpg)Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of **Magic: the Gathering** with the **From the Vault: Dragons** Limited Edition boxed set. Inside, you'll encounter 15 dragon cards printed in distinctive foil, including a sneak preview dragon from the upcoming *Shards of Alara* expansion.


 


* Each card has been printed using a foil process never before used on **Magic: The Gathering** cards. Some cards in the set appear in foil for the first time.
* The 15 dragon cards are all printed in the current card frame – for several, it is for the first time.
* 6 classic dragon cards have new artwork commissioned from artists including Justin Sweet, Todd Lockwood and D. Alexander Gregory.
* All cards are black-bordered and tournament legal.
* **From the Vault: Dragons** will be available in English only, and will have an extremely limited print run.
* It will be sold in hobby-game stores worldwide, and on **Magic Online** for a limited time and in limited quantities.
