TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 1, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











After many rounds of Swiss and some great games of *Magic*, the Top 8 for Toronto is set! Learn more about this event's Top 8 competitors, their history at the top tables, and more by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles.




---

### Josh Buitenhuis


**Age:** 19


**Occupation:** Warehouse Worker


**Hometown:** Cambridge, Ontario


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**   

None to note really, other than local hobby shop matches and tournaments.


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Black-White Eldrazi. I've been playing it for months and felt comfortable with it. I was also confident it was a good metagame choice.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  
[Thought-Knot Seer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought-Knot+Seer)


**What was the best deck you played against this weekend?**  

Grixis Control


**If you could, what would you transform into?**  

The Profane Prince




---

### Michael Sheng


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Frederickton, New Brunswick


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**   

I went to a Pro Tour once?


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Green-White Tokens splashing Chandra and [Needle Spires](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Needle+Spires). Advocate was bad without a creature land and Chandra is basically free thanks to Oath.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  
[Tragic Arrogance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tragic+Arrogance)


**What was the best deck you played against this weekend?**  

Green-White Tokens


**If you could, what would you transform into?**  

Jace.

### Doug Potter


**Age:** 29


**Occupation:** Young Life Staff (Non-Profit)


**Hometown:** Edmonton, Alberta


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**   

Qualified for the Sunday Super Series Top 8 draft, that starts in 1 hour.


**What deck did you play and why?**  

Green-White Tokens, because Sammy T shipped me an INCREDIBLE list.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  

4 [Den Protector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Den+Protector) main. It's unblockable and kills planeswalkers.


**What was the best deck you played against this weekend?**  

Green-White mirrors.


**If you could, what would you transform into?**  

A Durdle Turtle.

### Oliver Tiu


**Age:** 18


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Cambridge, Massachusetts


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**   

Top 16 Pro Tour Oath of the Gatewatch, Top 16 Pro Tour Shadows over Innistrad, Top 8 Grand Prix Providence 2015

Grixis Control, because I went 8-2 with it at the Pro Tour last week. I feel confident playing the deck.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**

4 Color Rite Thing.

Noah Walker

### Bradley Robinson


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Application Engineer


**Hometown:** West Chester, Pennsylvania


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**   

Top 16 GP Charlotte 2015, Competed at PT Born of the Gods. Top 100 at GP (forgot name)


**What deck did you play and why?**  

White Weenie. It's fast, consistent, and punishes mulligans and tap lands.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  
[Thalia's Lieutenant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thalia%27s+Lieutenant). Honorable mention: Plains.


**What was the best deck you played against this weekend?**  

4 Color Rites. [Brood Monitor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brood+Monitor) FTW!


**If you could, what would you transform into?**  

Something with wings. Flying best superpower.
