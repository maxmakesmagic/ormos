Damien's Demon Delivers Victory In Lyon
=======================================




Harnessing the power of Ob Nixilis Reignited, Damien Bouillot defeated all in *Battle for Zendikar* Limited, including finalist Christian Seibold, to become champion of Grand Prix Lyon 2015!

TOP 8 COVERAGE




 It was a great weekend full of exciting Magic here in Lyon. 1,952 players entered the tournament in total and, after nine rounds of *Battle for Zendikar* Sealed, 217 made it to the second day. Finally, after another six rounds of *Battle for Zendikar* Booster Draft, a strong Top 8 gathered in the feature match area, including Branco Neirynck in his third GP Top 8 this season, Tomoharu Saito in the 22nd of his career, and Christian Seibold in his fourth.



Seibold even managed to make it all the way to the finals, dispatching Saito along the way. But in the end it was little-known Damien Bouillot from Lyon who not only kept the trophy within the country but within city limits.


Over the course of the weekend, we saw Allies fight Eldrazi, lands awaken and join the battle, and various colors converging on green. Between all the titans in this Limited format, Damien Bouillot proved the most formidable. Congratulations to the champion of Grand Prix Lyon 2015!

DAY 2 COVERAGE



Welcome back to Day 2 of Grand Prix Lyon 2015!


Initially, 1,952 players were in the running, but through yesterday's nine grueling rounds of *Battle for Zendikar* Sealed play the number was reduced to a mere 217. 217 would now battle through two *Battle for Zendikar* Booster Drafts, for a shot at the Top 8, the fame and the fortune, and, eventually, the trophy and the title.


We already saw some exciting games yesterday and picked the brains of some of the game's best and brightest for insights into this Limited format. Today, we have even more chance to do so: more action, more excitement, and more strategy, as the tournament enters its homestretch.


Follow us as we bring you all the latest straight from the battlefield of Grand Prix Lyon 2015!

DAY 1 COVERAGE



What had started with 1,952 players in the morning was reduced to a much more manageable 217 by midnight. 217 players succeeded in winning at least seven of their rounds with their Sealed Decks and earned the right to return for the Booster Draft rounds on Day 2.


Among them, six players stood above all the others with perfect records of 9-0: Guillaume Matignon, Vincent Lemoine, Branco Neyrinck, Marc Lazaro, Laurent Favier, and Damien Mace. But fierce competition wasn't far behind with such luminaries as Marcio Carvalho, Joel Larsson, Tomoharu Saito, Louis Deltour, and Christian Seibold all ending the day on 8-1.


Tune back in tomorrow for more Battle for Zendikar Limited action, for two drafts, for the Top 8, and for the epic conclusion of Grand Prix Lyon 2015!
