Standard Super League Decklists
===============================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on July 1, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Every Tuesday at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7), the newest episode of the Standard Super League airs live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic). For the Standard Super League, we want to get league decklists into the hands of viewers as quickly as possible. Now, also on Tuesdays at 6:00 p.m. Pacific, viewers can stop by this article to find a link to the Standard Super League decklists for that night's episode!


Follow the links below to take you to the corresponding week's decklists. Remember to check in next Tuesday at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7) for the Standard Super League's next set of decks!
