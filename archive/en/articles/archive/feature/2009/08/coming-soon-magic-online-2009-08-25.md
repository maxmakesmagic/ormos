Coming Soon to Magic Online
===========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 25, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











### From the Vault: Exiled

It is said there were once ﬁfteen **Magic: The Gathering** cards so potent, they were banned or restricted from the known realms of competition. But in the years that have passed, the game has evolved and these elder cards are once again in play. Master these weapons from ages past and give your opponent a beating of historic proportions.

This exciting collection features several cards that are brand new to **Magic Online**. Each *From the Vault: Exiled* contains one of each of the 15 cards listed below. All cards are premium (foil) and in the new **Magic** card frame.

* [Balance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Balance)
* [Berserk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Berserk)
* [Channel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Channel)
* [Gifts Ungiven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven)
* [Goblin Lackey](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Lackey)
* [Kird Ape](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kird+Ape)
* [Lotus Petal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Petal)
* [Mystical Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor)
* [Necropotence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necropotence)
* [Sensei's Divining Top](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top)
* [Serendib Efreet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serendib+Efreet)
* [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp)
* [Strip Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strip+Mine)
* [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker)
* [Trinisphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinisphere)

*From the Vault: Exiled* will be available in the **Magic Online** store on August 31, 2009 for $34.99.

### Commander Theme Decks

We are pleased to announce the “Enchantress Rubinia” and “Deathdancer Xira” Commander Theme Decks. These decks feature cards drawn from throughout **Magic**'s rich history. Each 100-card deck includes the foil version of the legendary commander it is named after as well as seven other rare cards. In addition, each deck will contain cards from the upcoming *Masters Edition III* set and several cards that have never before been released on **Magic Online**.

Each deck will be available for $19.99 in the **Magic Online** store on August 26, 2009.
