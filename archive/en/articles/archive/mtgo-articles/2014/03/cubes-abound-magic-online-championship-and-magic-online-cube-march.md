Cubes Abound! Magic Online Championship and Magic Online Cube in March
======================================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on March 3, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_MikeTurian.jpg)
By Mike Turian




Before joining Magic Online as its digital product manager, Mike worked as a producer for Wizards's technology department, a product manager for organized play, and as an R&D lead developer. He has played Magic Online since it came out in 2002 and Magic since The Dark. 








[![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/arcana/927_cubebooster.jpg)](https://accounts.onlinegaming.wizards.com/)
[Download **Magic Online**](https://accounts.onlinegaming.wizards.com/)


The **Magic Online**  Championship features sixteen players battling for a share of more than $100,000 USD in cash prizes in the Standard, Modern, Cube Draft, and *Born of the Gods/Theros*  Draft formats. Not only will the winner take home a cool $25,000 USD, but he or she will also earn a coveted invitation to the 2014 **Magic**  World Championship in December.


This **Magic Online**  Championship will feature a Cube Draft, and, beginning March 19, we will bring the same Cube that the competitors will be drafting to **Magic Online** so you can enjoy the same experience!


The **Magic Online**  Championship Event takes place in San Francisco, CA, March 28-30, 2014. You can find complete details on the event on the [2013 Magic Online Championship Event Information](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/event/mtgo/mocs2013#viewer) page.
