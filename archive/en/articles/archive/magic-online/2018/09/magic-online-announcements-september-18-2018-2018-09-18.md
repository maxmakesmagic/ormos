Announcement Time!
------------------



*Guilds of Ravnica* is almost here! This means that we'll be rolling out some fun things on MTGO to celebrate. Keep an eye on the website this week for an article about GRN happenings by Alli Medwin!





---

Legacy Cube
-----------



For those who have never played Legacy before, Legacy Cube is a great way to try out all of those super old cards you've heard about. If you haven't gotten a chance to try it out yet or are just absolutely loving it, Legacy Cube will be around until September 27!





---

M19 Redemption Deck
-------------------


M19 Regular redemption sets are currently out of stock and we will be starting the reprint process, which will take several weeks. We will announce the restock here the week before they are available. Thanks for your patience.




---

Sealed PTQ Finals Saturday
--------------------------



There will be a *Core Set 2019* Limited PTQ Finals event this Saturday at 7 a.m. PT. Players can enter with 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points. The winner qualifies for Pro Tour Cleveland and its associated Pro Tour Challenge.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).





---

Standard MOCS Monthly
---------------------



There will be a MOCS Monthly Standard event this Sunday at 7am PT. Players can enter with 35 Qualifier Points, and those who finish with six or more wins qualify for the MOCS Playoff on December 22nd.


To earn more about the 2018 *Magic Online* Championship Series, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2018-magic-online-championship-series).

Downtimes September 26 and October 24
-------------------------------------



The next two downtimes will be September 26, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC and October 24, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC. Please note that there are no more 'extended' or 'normal' downtimes; in the new world with fewer downtimes, they're all the same length of time.
