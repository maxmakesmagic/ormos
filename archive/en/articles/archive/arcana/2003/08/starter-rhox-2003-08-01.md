Starter Rhox
============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 1, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The great monster [Rhox](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhox) from the *Nemesis* expansion has been reprinted in the Core Set with its original art.


A rarer premium version of [Rhox](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhox)—with art based on how he was presented in television ads—was included in the [2000 Starter set](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/starter/starter/welcome.asp).
