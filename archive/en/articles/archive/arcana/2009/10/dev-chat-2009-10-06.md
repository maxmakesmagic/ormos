Dev Chat!
=========



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 6, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Do you ever wish you could sit down and talk with the people who create **Magic: The Gathering**? Well, we've got the next best thing: a *Zendikar* Dev Chat!

**Who:**



> **Magic** R&D director Aaron Forsythe (*Zendikar* Development Team) and **Magic** creative writer Doug Beyer (*Zendikar* Design Team)

**When:**



> Thursday, October 8, 2:00 PM PDT (5:00 PM EDT)

**How:**



> There are three ways to join the chat.  
>   
> * via widget: <http://tinyurl.com/devchat> (Should take you directly to a login screen)
> * via mibbit: [http://mibbit.com/chat](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coveritlive-chat-session-day-one-2012-10-24-0) (Takes you to a screen where you type in your desired username and your channel name (the channel will be #devchat )
> * via IRC: If you have an IRC client, you can access it by going to the mibbit IRC and then the channel (#devchat).
> 

See you there!
