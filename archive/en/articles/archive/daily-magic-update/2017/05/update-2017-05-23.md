The May 23, 2017 Update
=======================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on May 23, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on May 23, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by HASCON, which, well, read below.


HASCON Tickets on Sale
======================


1. [And by that we mean *Iconic Masters* Prerelease Event Tickets on Sale](http://hascon.hasbro.com/)

Remember when we announced that HASCON—Hasbro's first-ever convention—was going to be kicking off our 25th Anniversary celebration in September? And remember how we also told you we were running a Prerelease experience for [*Iconic Masters*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/products/iconic-masters), which doesn't release till November?


[Tickets went on sale last night](http://hascon.hasbro.com/). Just saying.


I'm joking a little bit about the focus on *Iconic Masters*, which is certainly a cool event, but we'll also have panels, other play experiences, *Ixalan* previews, and more, not to mention all the other Hasbro toys and universes that will have their own things going on. I mean, it's not like you need a reason to head to Providence, RI, in September, but here's one anyway.


After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [Channel LSV – 1v1 Commander: Baral, Chief of Compliance](https://www.channelfireball.com/videos/channel-lsv-1v1-commander-baral-chief-of-compliance/) | ChannelFireball | Luis Scott-Vargas

It's everything you didn't know you wanted. LSV jumps into 1v1 Commander with, clearly, a [Baral, Chief of Compliance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baral%2C+Chief+of+Compliance) deck. In fact, we're going to make the deck today's [Deck of the Day](#10) as well, since it is *the* de facto 1v1 Commander deck.


In totally not-at-all-related-to-this-deck news, look for updates to the 1v1 Commander B&R list tomorrow on the [*Magic Online* Tumblr](http://wizardsmtgo.tumblr.com/).


1. [Casual Content You Don't Want to Miss](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?ID=13963&writer=Bruce+Richard&articledate=5-23-2017) | TCGPlayer | Bruce Richard

Man, Bruce doing my job for me. In this article, Bruce highlights some of the best casual-minded *Magic* content on the interwebs. Most of these we've looked at here at one time or another, so this one gets my stamp of content reviewing approval.


1. [Mono Black Zombies Matchup Guide](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/zen-takahashi/mono-black-zombies-matchup-guide) | MTG Mint Card | Zen Takahashi

I'm actually surprised we haven't seen more guides to playing Mono-Black Zombies, given that it's actually a relatively new deck and there are a ton of nuances to playing with it. Takahashi remedies that with a thoroughly thorough matchup guide to basically anything you could possibly play against in Standard.


1. [Martin Müller PT *Amonkhet* Top 4 Report](http://www.hareruyamtg.com/article/en/category/detail/120) | Hareruya English | Martin Müller

Speaking of the Pro Tour, while we were all caught up in Gerry Thompson's win and Christian Calcano's first Top 8, Martin Müller quietly grabbed his second Pro Tour Top 8 in his young career. Here he explains how he came around to [Chandra, Flamecaller](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Flamecaller) and how he made it all the way to the Top 4.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [1v1 Baral, Chief of Compliance](https://www.channelfireball.com/videos/channel-lsv-1v1-commander-baral-chief-of-compliance/)

While players play to their taste in 1v1 Commander, Baral decks are, far and away, the tastiest on *Magic Online* right now. It's a mono-blue deck that aims to counter all of the things before finishing the game with a [Polymorph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polymorph)ed (or other similar spell) [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Aeons+Torn).


It's really that simple. Super powerful, but quite simple.
