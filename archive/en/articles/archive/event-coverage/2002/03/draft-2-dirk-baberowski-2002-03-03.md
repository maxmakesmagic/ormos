Draft 2: Dirk Baberowski
========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 3, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_242.png)
By Stan van der Velden












Dirk Baberowski entered this draft with a 9-1-1 record, needing a 2-1 performance to make top 8. He was being fed by Arjan van Leeuwen and passing to Franck Canu.


The following are the picks Dirk made:


### Pack 1


1. **Cephalid****Looter**, Ivy Elemental


Not a hard first pick for Dirk. Cephalid Looter is widely considered the better card of the two.


2. **Mystic Zealot**, Painbringer, Patriarch's Desire, Scrivener


This is a very interesting pack as it forces Dirk to pick his second color almost immediately. Dirk decides to gamble that white will be underdrafted this draft and takes the Zealot.


3. **Resilient Wanderer**, Hallowed Healer, Syncopate  

4. **Aven Windreader**, Dirty Wererat, Hallowed Healer, Blessed Orator  

5. **Mystic Zealot**, Repel, Aether Burst  

6. **Pilgrim of Virtue**  

7. **Embolden**, Angelic Wall, Words of Wisdom  

8-15. **Patrol Hound, Mystic Visionary**


### Pack 2


1. Aven Flock, **Psionic Gift**, Ghastly Demise


This is where Dirk decides to forget about splashing and just draft white/blue, valuing mana consistency more than sheer power, although Psionic Gift is about as good as demise in blue/white.


2. **Shelter**, Aether Burst, Psionic Gift  

3. Millikin, **Angelic Wall**, Cephalid Scout  

4. **Mystic Zealot**  

5. Scrivener, **Second Thoughts**  

6. Embolden, **Aether Burst**  

7. **Scrivener**


Had he known beforehand, Dirk could have drafted three Aether Bursts and two Scriveners, ofcourse hindsight really is 20/20.


### Pack 3


1. **Deep Analysis**, Violent Eruption


Another not-quite-splash possibility for Dirk, one he once again chose not to take.


2. **Skywing Aven**, Hypochondria  

3. **Stupefying Touch**, Vengeful Dreams


Dirk chose to pick Stupefying Touch over Vengeful Dreams. Although Vengeful Dreams probably is the better card, blue/white needs a way to shut down Chainflingers and Cabal Torturers, so the pick is more than justified.


4. **Vengeful Dreams**, Stern Judge  

5. **Obsessive Search**, Spirit Flare  

6. **Teroh's Faithful**, Militant Monk, Hypochondria  

7-15. Nothing much, Torment is not very friendly to blue/white players..


Dirk ended up with a pretty good blue/white deck that has a good chance of getting him the 2-1 record he needs. I said the same about Jan Doise's Blue/White deck last time though and he went 0-3, so we'll just have to wait and see.
