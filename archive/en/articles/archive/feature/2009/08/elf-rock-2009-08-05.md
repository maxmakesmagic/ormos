The story out of Japanese Nationals centered around an explosive Elf deck that generated fast mana and combed [Regal Force](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Regal+Force) and [Primal Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Command) to win the game. It was Elves that were again the big story out of French Nationals, but Mongilardi Gilles eschewed all the fancy bells and whistles of the green-white versions for the tried and true Rock approach. To read more about the event, visit the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/mongilardi-leads-france).
