R&D Google+ Hangout
===================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 17, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











How would you like to ask questions of Mark Rosewater and Dave Humpherys about *Gatecrash*? And how would you like to see them answer your questions live, in glorious streaming video?


Well, good news! On January 24, that's just what's going to happen! We're having a Google+ Hangout where Mark Rosewater (co-lead designer of *Gatecrash*) and Dave Humpherys (lead developer of *Gatecrash*) will answer select questions asked on any of the following venues:


* Our [Facebook page](http://www.facebook.com/MagicTheGathering/posts/518728521482411)
* Our [Google+ page](https://plus.google.com/115867258402597168527/posts?gpinv=AMIXal8_E0YSD8DwefIvFaSBR6elglYFN4z7gGd65s3Nc5OJ9sFMTZpmGC_L_l8F7FsLaGkObvpHWdKqlurQCeP44QUVvct5Btgg8MojNYtAW0k8k-27yZY&cfem=1)
* On Twitter (with the hashtag [#mtghangout](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23MTGHANGOUT&src=hash))

Here's what else you need to know:


* **[Sign up for the Hangout](https://plus.google.com/events/cvsthjfvnag2lcgc4n0n7duh584?gpinv=AMIXal8_E0YSD8DwefIvFaSBR6elglYFN4z7gGd65s3Nc5OJ9sFMTZpmGC_L_l8F7FsLaGkObvpHWdKqlurQCeP44QUVvct5Btgg8MojNYtAW0k8k-27yZY&cfem=1)**
* **[Show up](https://plus.google.com/115867258402597168527)** from 11:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. PT, January 24, and see Mark Rosewater and Dave Humpherys answer your questions about *Gatecrash*!

And if you miss it, it'll be on YouTube immediately. Futuristic!
