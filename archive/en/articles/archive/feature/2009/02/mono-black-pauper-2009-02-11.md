Looking for a fun format or a break from the usual **Magic** you play with your friends or local playgroup? Then try Pauper **Magic**! An alternative format you can find on **Magic Online**, Pauper generally eschews rares and focuses on commons and uncommon for deck building. That means you get to try building with some cards you might never have considered! SlashRus used this Mono-Black build to win a recent Premier Event online.
