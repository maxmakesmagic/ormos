Looking for something heavy to play at your next Pro Tour Qualifier? Scott Bielick used [Primalcrux](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primalcrux) to power his way into the Top 8, with no less than [Oversoul of Dusk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oversoul+of+Dusk), [Spellbreaker Behemoth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellbreaker+Behemoth), and [Cloudthresher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudthresher) along for the ride! The cards combined quite well with the [Phosphorescent Feast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phosphorescent+Feast)s in his sideboard, any red mage's worst nightmare!
