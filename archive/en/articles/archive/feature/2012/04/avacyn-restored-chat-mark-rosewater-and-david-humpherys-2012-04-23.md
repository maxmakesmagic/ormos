Avacyn Restored Chat with Mark Rosewater and David Humpherys
============================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 23, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Join us for a live chat with *Avacyn Restored* R&D Members Mark Rosewater (Design Team) and David Humpherys (Lead Developer). They’ll be taking your questions and giving you some information on what it was like to help create the final set in the *Innistrad* Block!

Please note that this is a moderated chat, so your questions and comments will not automatically show up in the chat when you submit them.
