Video Feature: Return to the Magic Beach House
==============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 5, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











Three years after coming to the tropical paradise of Hawaii, the **Magic**  Pro Tour has returned. We check in on a group of European pros who are living the life of a globe-trotting gamer as they enjoy some fun in the sun at a beach house on Oahu in the days leading up to the Pro Tour.
