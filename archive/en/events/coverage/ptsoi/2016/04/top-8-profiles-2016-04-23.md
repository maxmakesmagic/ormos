TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 23, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff












**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/ptsoi/t8_finkel.jpg)Name:** Jon Finkel


**Age:** 37


**Hometown:** Fanwood, NJ


**Occupation:** Hedge Fund Manager


**Limited Record:** 6-0


**Constructed Record:** 8-1-1


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 16 Pro Tour Top 8s, 3 wins.


**Did You Team up with anyone to practice for this Pro Tour? Who?**


CFB: The Pantheon


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Green-Blue Clues, White-Black Vampires. The second deck was better.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


[Seasons Past](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seasons+Past). Because my teammates thought it was better than I did.


**What is the most terrifying card from *Shadows over Innistrad* to see on the other side of the battlefield?**


No idea.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


I'd only need three more to catch Kai for wins.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/ptsoi/t8_mengucci.jpg)Name:** Andrea Mengucci


**Age:** 23


**Hometown:** Senigallia (Italy)


**Occupation:** Student / Writer for ChannelFireball


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Constructed Record:** 8-2


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8 PT *Journey into Nyx*, Top 16 PT *Dragons of Tarkir*, World *Magic* Cup Winner 2015


**Did You Team up with anyone to practice for this Pro Tour? Who?**


Yes, MTGMintcard. I have to thank Katsuhiro Mori more than anyone else.


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Red-Black and Red-White. I liked my Red-White with five [Thraben Inspector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thraben+Inspector).


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Bant Company, because it was the best deck and my team and I haven't come up with anything new.


**What is the most terrifying card from *Shadows over Innistrad* to see on the other side of the battlefield?**


[Sorin, Grim Nemesis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sorin%2C+Grim+Nemesis), because it is the best new Planeswalker.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


A dream come true, and seeing all my efforts realized.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/ptsoi/t8_nelson.jpg)Name:** Brad Nelson


**Age:** 29


**Hometown:** Roanoke, VA


**Occupation:** Starcitygames.com Content Producer


**Limited Record:** 5-1


**Constructed Record:** 8-2


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Player of the Year 2010, 2 PT Top 8s, 12 GP Top 8s. Winner of all Pie Bets.


**Did You Team up with anyone to practice for this Pro Tour? Who?**


Team EUreka.


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Blue-Black Zombies and Black-Green-White Humans. 40-card formats are never may favorite.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Red-Green Goggles. The [Pyromancer's Goggles](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer%27s+Goggles) shell is amazing at beating creature decks, but blue doesn't add much. The green gives the deck more consistency and a better sideboard.


**What is the most terrifying card from *Shadows over Innistrad* to see on the other side of the battlefield?**


[Seasons Past](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seasons+Past).


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


Everything.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/ptsoi/t8_salvatto.jpg)Name:** Luis Salvatto


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** Lujan, Argentina


**Occupation:** Chicken Businessman


**Limited Record:** 6-0


**Constructed Record:** 6-4


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments: Top 8 Grand Prix Santiago 2013; Super Sunday Series Championship Champion 2014, SSS Semifinalist 2015**


**Did You Team up with anyone to practice for this Pro Tour? Who?** First time with DEX Army. Tulio Jaudi created the deck, Marcio Carvalho gave me some advice and taught me a lot about the draft format. And Sebastian Pozzo played with me all the time before.


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Green-White Human-Werewolves


White-Blue Spirits Old School: Flyers and Walls


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Red-White Goggles. When Tulio showed me the deck, I was fascinated. Then I tested other decks, but didn't find anything I liked, so I took Tulio's deck.


**What is the most terrifying card from *Shadows over Innistrad* to see on the other side of the battlefield?**


Avacyn...


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


Everything. I've always dreamt about this. It took a few years to become qualified, but now, I can't believe this!


Special Thanks to all my team from Argentina, especially to Martin Quiroga, my brother in *Magic*!

Getting 1st is a large part of why I play, so it would mean a ton.
