Weather Alert for United States Prereleases
===========================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on January 28, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Because of the developing winter storm moving across much of the United States, Wizards of the Coast recommends that all players check with their local organizers for any possible delays or postponements for this weekend's [*Worldwake* Prerelease events](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/prerelease-facts).

The [Regional Prerelease](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/prerelease-regional) scheduled for Richmond, Virginia has been cancelled.

To find contact information for a Prerelease near you, use the Event Locator tool to the right.
