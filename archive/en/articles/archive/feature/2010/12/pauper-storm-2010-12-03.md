Pauper is a fun format that allows players to build decks using only commons. On **Magic Online**, The\_Raging\_Flump came up with a storm strategy powerful enough to do well in a recent Pauper Challenge. The combo: using *Ravnica* block bounce lands like [Azorious Chancery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Azorious+Chancery) and [Dimir Aqueduct](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dimir+Aqueduct), the Flump builds up a number of spells by casting [Cloud of Faeries](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloud+of+Faeries), using [Snap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snap) to return the 1/1, and drawing cards with [Frantic Research](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frantic+Research). 

When all is said and done, you have extra mana in your mana pool and a storm count that allows you to cast [Temporal Fissure](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temporal+Fissure). That lets you return most of your opponent's permanents to his or her hand, and you should be able to win easily from there!
