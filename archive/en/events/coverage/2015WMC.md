2015 WORLD MAGIC CUP
====================




Live video coverage of the 2015 World *Magic* Cup begins on Friday, December 11 at 10 a.m. local time (CET)/1 a.m. PT/4 a.m. ET/9 a.m. UTC.

### 




Watch LIVE coverage of the 2015 World *Magic*Cup on [DailyMTG.com](http://www.dailymtg.com) and [Twitch](http://www.twitch.tv/magic) December 11-13! Coverage begins on December 11 and 12 at 10 a.m. local time (CET)/1 a.m. PT/4 a.m. ET/9 a.m. UTC. Coverage of Day Three will begin on December 13 at 9 a.m. local time (CET)/12 a.m. PT/3 a.m. ET/8 a.m. UTC.
