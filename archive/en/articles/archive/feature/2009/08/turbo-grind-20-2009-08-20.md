Sanity Grinding made a brief blip on the radar screen of the tournament circuit just a few months back, but one John Jackson has pulled it back into the mainstream with a win at a PTQ in Lincoln, Nebraska. The deck uses [Sanity Grinding](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sanity+Grinding), combined with cards like [Cryptic Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command) and [Twincast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Twincast), to deck an opponent. The engine is fueled by [Howling Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine), [Jace Beleren](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace+Beleren), and [Time Warp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp).
