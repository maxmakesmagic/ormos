UMA Flashback Drafts Tomorrow
-----------------------------


*Lorwyn* Flashback Drafts are winding down to make room for *Ultimate Masters* Drafts, back by popular demand! If you missed the action last time, not to worry—UMA Flashback Drafts start March 6 and end March 13—a whole week to draft your favorite bombs.


Players may enter with any of the following options: 10 Event Tickets; 1 *Ultimate Masters* booster + 3 Event Tickets; or 100 Play Points.




---

Ravnica Remix Drafts March 13
-----------------------------


Available March 13, Ravnica Remix is a digital-exclusive Phantom experience made from two lists of cards from *Guilds of Ravnica* and *Ravnica Allegiance*, curated by our very own *Magic* R&D. The two sets are mixed into two different packs. The set will be drafted in A, B, B style, meaning the second and third packs are the same as one another and different than the first pack.


The contents of the first pack include a higher density of multicolored cards. The second and third packs have more monocolored cards and mana fixing. Ravnica Remix encourages players to mix and match guilds and discover playstyles not possible in either GRN or RNA draft.


The Ravnica Remix league entry options will match our other Phantom offerings: entry will be 10 Event Tickets or 100 Play Points.


Some cards from those sets have been omitted from Ravnica Remix packs. The full list of cards found in Ravnica Remix packs is below.

Standard Regional MCQ Sunday
----------------------------


On Sunday, March 10, there will be a Standard Regional Mythic Championship Qualifier (RMCQ) at 5 a.m. PT (12:00 UTC). Regional MCQs are invitation-only events. An invitation to participate in a Regional MCQ for Mythic Championship London 2019 is offered to the following:


* Players with a Mythic Championship Players Club Bronze or Silver level for the *Ravnica Allegiance* Cycle
* Players that won a Preliminary Mythic Championship Qualifier from October 6, 2018 until January 6, 2019.
* Players that received an invitation to this round of Regional Mythic Championship Qualifiers for their top finish at a Regional Mythic Championship Qualifier for Mythic Championship Cleveland 2019.
* Players that won a Regional Last Chance Qualifier that ties to that Regional Mythic Championship Qualifier.

Pre-registration for the event is required and only open to players who have not competed in a paper-world Regional MCQ event since February 2. The event is limited to 212 players, and registration closes tomorrow at 11:59 p.m. PT.


**IMPORTANT**: Players in North America & Caribbean—remember to set your clocks forward on Saturday night. Players outside North America—start times will automatically change in the game after 10:00 UTC Sunday. Late entries cannot be accommodated.
