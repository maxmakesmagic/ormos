A Mana Saved is a Mana Earned
=============================



 Posted in [Top Decks](/en/articles/columns/top-decks-archive)
 on September 23, 2009 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Mike Flores










Many of you probably know about this card [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash). Or at least you have heard about the card [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) in the context of what can only be considered the finest, most powerful, and most perfectly tuned (main) deck in the history of competitive **Magic** ... Hulk Flash, as envisioned by Billy Moreno and executed by [Limited Information](/Magic/Magazine/Archive.aspx?tag=Limited%20Informationamp;description=Limited%20Information) columnist Steve Sadin (making his brief but brilliant foray into the shoes of a Constructed Grand Prix champion).
