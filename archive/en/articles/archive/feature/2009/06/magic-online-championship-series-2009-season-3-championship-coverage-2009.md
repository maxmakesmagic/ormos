======================================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 30, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast

The 2009 Season 3 Championship was held on June 27. Click below to launch the draft viewer to see how they drafted.
