Second Draft: Kasuyuki Momose
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 1, 2002 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff

* Akira Asahara is a member of the Hachiouji Clan, the designer of the Zombie Prison deck Kazuaki Arahori used to win GP Sendai. His best performance to date is making Top 8 of the 2001 Finals. His speciality is Constructed.
* Katushiro Mori: Fresh off his GP Nagoya win, the Rookie of the Year is looking for another title.
* Kazuyuki Momose: The only member of Panzer Hunter left withing striking distance of the Top 8, Momose is doing especially well today, in spite of his claim of not having practiced for the event.
* Tsuyoshi Ikeda: The highest-ranking Japanese player at PT Osaka, Ikeda is one of the members of the old guard looking for a title.
* Shinsuke Hayashi: Made Top 4 at GP Hong Kong, his first premiere event.

### Odyssey

1st pick: [Second Thoughts](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Second+Thoughts). Other choices: [Dreamwinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder), [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp) (All in all, not a very strong pack.)  
 2nd pick: [Aven Cloudchaser](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Cloudchaser). Other choices: [Afflict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Afflict), [Cephalid Scout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Scout), [Elephant Ambush](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Ambush)  
 3rd pick: [Lithatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lithatog). Other choices: [Aven Fisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Fisher), [Embolden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embolden)[Morbid Hunger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger)  
 4th pick: [Shelter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shelter). Other choices: [Chamber of Manupulation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chamber+of+Manupulation), [AEther Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=AEther+Burst), [Syncopate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Syncopate). As one might guess from his card selection, Momose seems to deliberately be avoiding blue.  
 5th pick: [Angelic Wall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelic+Wall). Other choices: [Barbarian Lunatic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbarian+Lunatic), [Dwarven Strike Force](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dwarven+Strike+Force). Momose later said that since at this point he was planning on moving away from white, he should have taken one of the red cards.  
 6th pick: [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt). Other choices: [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), [Dreamwinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder)  
 7th pick: [Zombie Assassin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Assassin)  
 8th pick: [Aven Cloudchaser](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Cloudchaser). Other choices: [Pilgrim of Virtue](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pilgrim+of+Virtue) 9th pick: [Dwarven Recruiter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dwarven+Recruiter)  
 10th pick: [Ravaged Highlands](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravaged+Highlands)  
 11th pick: [Escape Artist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Escape+Artist)  
 12th pick: [Thermal Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thermal+Blast)  
 13th pick: [Touch of Invisibility](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Touch+of+Invisibility)  
 14th pick: [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek)  
 15th pick: [Tremble](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tremble)

Although before the draft he said UW was his primary choice, Momose seems to be actively avoiding those cards, instead chosing RGW. Is he planning to concentrate on RG in *Torment* and avoid a fight over blue?

### Torment

1st pick: [Major Teroh](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Major+Teroh). Other choices: [Centaur Chieftain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Centaur+Chieftain), [Crippling Fatigue](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crippling+Fatigue). Momose continues to pick white over any other color. Perhaps he thinks he can succeed where Zvi failed?  
 2nd pick: [Nantuko Cultivator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=+Nantuko+Cultivator). Other choices: [Soul Scourge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Scourge). By picking the Cultivator, it looks like Momose has lost any strategy he may have had, and is just going after good cards from the pack.  
 3rd pick: [Fiery Temper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper). Other choices: [Skywing Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skywing+Aven)  
 4th pick: [Fiery Temper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper)  
 5th pick: [Longhorn Firebeast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Longhorn+Firebeast)  
 6th pick: [Crackling Club](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crackling+Club). Other choices: [Flash of Defiance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash+of+Defiance), [Ghostly Wings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghostly+Wings)  
 7th pick: [Flash of Defiance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash+of+Defiance)  
 8th pick: [Militant Monk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Militant+Monk)  
 9th pick: [Militant Monk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Militant+Monk)  
 10th pick: [Floating Shield](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Floating+Shield)  
 11th pick: [Pay No Heed](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pay+No+Heed)  
 12th pick: [Pay No Heed](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pay+No+Heed)  
 13th pick: [Accelerate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accelerate)  
 14th pick: [Equal Treatment](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Equal+Treatment)  
 15th pick: [Invigorating Falls](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Invigorating+Falls)

Altogether, it's hard to fathom what Momose is trying to do. Of couse, two [Fiery Temper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper)s are strong in any deck, but RW is not exactly one of the optimum combinations in *Odyssey* block. With Wakisaka, Mori, and Masui all going black-green, the two people at the table drafting white and red--including Momose--should expect big things from *Judgment*.

### Judgment

1st pick: [Phantom Nomad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nomad). Other choices: [Wormfang Drake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Drake)  
 2nd pick: [Unquestioned Authority](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unquestioned+Authority). Other choices: [Wormfang Drake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Drake)  
 3rd pick: [Vigilant Sentry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigilant+Sentry)  
 4th pick: [Vigilant Sentry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigilant+Sentry). Picking up two of these is an interesting choice--sure, once you hit threshold they're powerful, but RW is generally not set up to be an enabler of threshold.  
 5th pick: [Phantom Nomad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nomad)  
 6th pick: [Vigilant Sentry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigilant+Sentry)  
 7th pick: [Prismatic Strands](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Strands). Other choices: [Guided Strike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Guided+Strike) 8th pick: [Phantom Nomad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nomad). Is he trying to build a constructed deck with multiples of the same card?  
 9th pick: [Ember Shot](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Shot)  
 10th pick: [Ember Shot](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Shot)  
 11th pick: [Web of Inertia](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Web+of+Inertia)  
 12th pick: [Benevolent Bodyguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Benevolent+Bodyguard)  
 13th pick: [Liberated Dwarf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liberated+Dwarf)  
 14th pick: [Flaring Pain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flaring+Pain)  
 15th pick: [Wormfang Newt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Newt)

Faced with few options, Momose has essentially drafted a monowhite deck. He'll want to aim for threshold as much as he can to fully reallize the potential of his multiple [Vigilant Sentries](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigilant+Sentries), but without [Lithatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lithatog), that's a tall order.
