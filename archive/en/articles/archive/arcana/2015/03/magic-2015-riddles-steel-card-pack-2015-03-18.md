Magic 2015—Riddles of Steel Card Pack
=====================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 18, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 





Do you play [*Magic 2015—Duels of the Planeswalkers*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/game-info/products/duels-of-the-planeswalkers)? Do you like new things? Do you like answering rhetorical questions? Do you like bonus content? Of course you do!



And just for you, starting March 18—today!—you'll receive, as a bonus, the Riddles of Steel card pack. Different systems update at different times, but look for this bonus content in your account soon!



![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/F15B_swrve.jpg)



So what is this Riddles of Steel card pack? Keep reading!



*Magic 2015* Riddles of Steel Card Pack includes three new ten-card all-foil sets for use with your collection (30 cards in all):



* "Might and Majesty" Set—Bolster your ranks with this collection of powerful white cards. Raise an army with [Captain of the Watch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Captain+of+the+Watch) or win in majestic style with [Felidar Sovereign](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Felidar+Sovereign).
* "Brain Power" Set—Bend the rules to your advantage with this collection of blue cards. Command the seas to rise with [Master of Waves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Waves) or use [Laboratory Maniac](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Laboratory+Maniac) to invent a new way to win.
* "Doomsday Machine" Set—Unleash your unstoppable mechanical creations with this collection of cards that empower your artifacts. [Darksteel Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Colossus) towers over other creatures while [Master of Etherium](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Etherium) makes your artifact creatures into an overwhelming clockwork army.


A client update is required for content to appear in your collection.
