Ben Rubin was just 15 years old when he found himself all the way in the finals of the 1998 World Championships in Seattle, and that was just the beginning of a long, prosperous professional career. Ben did well at the '98 Championships with the tried and true strategy of red creatures and burn. Powerful support from the likes of Wasteland and [Cursed Scroll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cursed+Scroll) backed by [Fireblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireblast), one of the most powerful direct damage spells of all time, worked wonders for the phenom. You can watch the 2008 World Championships live from Memphis as Ben is inducted into the Pro Tour [Hall of Fame](/en/node/574031) with [the webcast of the Top 8](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/webcast/home) set to kick off from Memphis at 9:45 a.m. ET on December 14th.
