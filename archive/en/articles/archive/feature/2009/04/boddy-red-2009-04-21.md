A semi-pro based in the Seattle area, Kyle Boddy is no doubt looking forward to the upcoming Grand Prix to be held in his own home city! Of course, he's no doubt *really* excited now that he's won a Grand Prix Trial with the above decklist. Eschewing the more expensive but fat red creatures of many other Standard red decks, Kyle opted to play aggressive one-drops like [Tattermunge Maniac](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tattermunge+Maniac) and back them up with burn to force through. It's a tried and true strategy, and it worked for Kyle, earning him at least three wins to kick off the Grand Prix!
