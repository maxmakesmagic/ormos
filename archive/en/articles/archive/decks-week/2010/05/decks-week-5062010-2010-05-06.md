Decks of the Week: 5/06/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on May 6, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Welcome to the new Standard metagame! *Rise of the Eldrazi* saw wide use throughout the world as new deck types emerged showing off the new cards. Want to turn a humble 0/1 plant token into [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Aeons+Torn)? Check out the winning deck from Montreal's PTQ. How about dropping an [Eldrazi Conscription](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Conscription) on a [Baneslayer Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel)? Then Salt Lake City and San Diego have the deck for you. With the [Pro Tour–Amsterdam qualifier season](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/amsterdam10-qualifiers) in full swing plus [National Qualifiers](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/events/nationalqualifiers) being run around the world, there are lots of opportunities to put the new tech to work.
