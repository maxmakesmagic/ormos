### ARCANA


Magic 2015 Clash Pack
=====================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)

Blake Rasmussen

Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever.


July 7, 2014
 







Back in March, we announced something called a [Clash Pack](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/arcana/1443) for *[Magic 2015](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-2015-core-set-card-set-archive-products-game-info)*, a two-player product that contains two 60-card decks—Fate and Fury—designed to be played against each other. It's a great way to introduce new players to *Magic*. And it also comes with six premium foil cards with alternative art.


Clash Packs are not replacing Event Decks, but instead will alternate with them for each release. That means you'll see a Clash Pack for *Magic 2015* and an Event Deck for *Khans of Tarkir*. The Clash Pack will then return for the second set in the *Khans of Tarkir* block.



We've kept the lists secret while we went through previews, but now that the entire set is previewed, we can show you just what you'll get when you pick up these:
