### 2008 Legacy Championship

2008 Legacy Championship comprises a number of rounds according to the table below, followed by a Top 8 single-elimination Playoff.  
  




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Number of Players** | **Number of Rounds** |
| 8 – 16 | 4 |
| 17 – 32 | 5 |
| 33 - 64  | 6 |
| 65+ | 7 |

### Grand Prix Trial - Paris

Trials for individual-format Grand Prix tournaments award a three-round bye and a travel award to a specific Grand Prix tournament for one or more of the top-finishing competitors. The number of three-round byes and travel awards will be determined by the number of players in the Trial as shown in the table below.  
  




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Number of Players** | **Number of 3-Round Byes**  |
| 8 – 32 | 1 |
| 33 – 64 | 2  |
| 65 - 128  | 4  |
| 128+  | 8  |
