![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpind08/top8-jelger.jpg)

**Name:** Jelger Wiegersma  
**Age:** 26  
**Occupation:** Tourist  
**Hometown:** Eindohoven, the Netherlands


**Previous magic accomplishments:** Won Pro Tour Seatle and Grand Prix Gothenburg


**Non-Magic accomplishments:** Tontie Master


**What decks did you draft today?** GW all good cards and BW all bad cards and Incremental Blight


**Record in Sealed:** 8-1


**Record in Draft:** 3-0, 1-0-2


**Most overrated card in Shadowmoor:** Giant Baiting


**Most underrated card in Shadowmoor:** Juvenile Gloomwidow


**Who do you least want to play in the Top 8?** Tim Aten
