Draft 2 Color Breakdown
=======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 3, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_benbleiweiss.jpg)
By Ben Bleiweiss











Finally a Green/White deck showed up at this Pro Tour. In fact, there were two and neither performed very well. Of the original 331 competitors, 325 continued onto the second draft, where once again Red/Green reigned as the most popular deck choice. Black/Green variants (including Black/Green/Red) picked up a good number of drafters.

Here are the decks that were played during the second draft, with all percentages rounded off to the nearest tenth of a percent. There were a total of 325 deck lists available (out of 325 competitors)

Black: 199 decks, 61.1% of all decks.  
 Blue: 168 decks, 51.5% of all decks.  
 Green: 170 decks, 52.2% of all decks.  
 Red: 147 decks, 45.1% of all decks.  
 White: 106 decks, 32.5% of all decks.

1) G/R-----45 Decks, 13.8%  
 2) B/U-----35 Decks, 10.7%  
 3) B/U/W---31 Decks, 9.5%  
 4) B/G------30 Decks, 9.2%  
 5) B/G/R---29 Decks, 8.9%  
 6) U/W-----24 Decks, 7.4%  
 7) B/R------23 Decks, 7.1%  
 8) B/U/G---14 Decks, 4.3%  
 9) B/U/R---13 Decks, 4.0%  
 10) U/G-----12 Decks, 3.7%  
 10) U/G/W---12 Decks, 3.7%  
 10) U/G/R---12 Decks, 3.7%

 14) B/W------11 Decks, 3.4%  
 15) B/G/W---6 Decks, 1.9%  
 16) R/U/W---5 Decks, 1.5%  
 16) R/W------5 Decks, 1.5%
