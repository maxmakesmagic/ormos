Top 8 Player Profiles of Grand Prix Sacramento 2018
===================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 23, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_meghanwolff_0.jpg)
By Meghan Wolff




 Meghan is one half of the podcast Magic the Amateuring and an adjunct professor at Tolarian Community College. She loves Limited, likes Modern, and dips her toes into each Standard season. She's decidedly blue and is the #1 hater of Siege Rhino in the Multiverse. 






After 15 rounds of *Core Set Magic 2019* Sealed deck and Draft, only eight players remain looking to become a Grand Prix champion. Let's take a moment to get to know our top 8 players as they prepare themselves for the final draft of the weekend at Grant Prix Sacramento 2018.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpsac18/t8_gellis.jpg)


**Name**: Dylan Gellis


**Hometown**: Fulton, MD


**Occupation**: Student at University of Maryland


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** A few PPTQ Wins


**What color combination did you play on Day 1, and what was your best card?**


Blue-Red. [Patient Rebuilding](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Patient+Rebuilding).


**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best card?**


Draft 1 – White-Blue; [Resplendent Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Resplendent+Angel)


Draft 2 – Black-Red; [Act of Treason](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Act+of+Treason)


**What's the most exciting play you've made in *Magic 2019* Limited?**


I got to reanimate a [Meteor Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meteor+Golem) with Berath.


**What would winning GP Sacramento mean to you?**


EVERYTHING.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpsac18/t8_luong.jpg)


**Name**: Marcus Luong


**Hometown**: San Francisco, California


**Occupation**: Student


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Founder of the Eggplant Garden Oath


**What color combination did you play on Day 1, and what was your best card?**


Black-Red – [Volley Veteran](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volley+Veteran)


**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best card?**


Draft 1 – White-Black Aggro – [Pegasus Courser](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pegasus+Courser)


Draft 2 – White-Red – [Volley Veteran](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volley+Veteran) (I had three of them)


**What's the most exciting play you've made in *Magic 2019* Limited?**


Putting a [Knight's Pledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight%27s+Pledge) on [Novice Knight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Novice+Knight)


**What would winning GP Sacramento mean to you?**


It would mean that I'm worth of being Oliver Tomajko's 8th father.

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** GP Santa Clara 2017 Top 8.


2 Pro Tour Appearances.

**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best card?**


Draft 1 – White-Blue - Trusty Camel


Draft 2 – Blue-Black – Mystic Archeologist


**What's the most exciting play you've made in *Magic 2019* Limited?**


[Uncomfortable Chill](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Uncomfortable+Chill) in an unfavorable spot clearing my opponent's entire battlefield.


**What would winning GP Sacramento mean to you?**


That practice pays off. Everyone should be prepared for the day that lady luck visits you. Shout to the NYC crew, the Cali crew and my awesome girlfriend who supports me playing *Magic*!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpsac18/t8_walski.jpg)


**Name**: John Walski


**Hometown**: Cicero, IL


**Occupation:** D.J.


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Won Find You Battlefield – A local *Magic* tournament my friend runs in Los Angeles!


**What color combination did you play on Day 1, and what was your best card?**


Black – Green Splashing Chromium off a Gift of Paradise…


**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best card?**


Draft 1 – Blue-Green - Sleep


Draft 2 – White-Blue Fliers – Sleep


**What's the most exciting play you've made in *Magic 2019* Limited?**


Flashing in Chromium with no Islands or Plains on the battlefield.


**What would winning GP Sacramento mean to you?**


That I got extremely lucky. And that maybe I'm semi-decent at *Magic the Gathering*!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpsac18/t8_mick.jpg)


**Name:** Max Mick


**Hometown:** San Diego, California


**Occupation:** Teenager


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 2 Grand Prix Top 8s: Vegas 2018 and Portland 2016.


**What color combination did you play on Day 1, and what was your best card?**


Grixis Control. My best card was Mystic Archeologist. My most impressive card was [Chaos Wand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chaos+Wand).


**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best card?**


Draft 1 – Blue-Green Midrange – [Rabid Bite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rabid+Bite) and [Departed Deckhand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Departed+Deckhand) were my best cards.


Draft 2 – White-Red Aggro – [Star-Crowned Stag](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Star-Crowned+Stag) and [Siegebreaker Giant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siegebreaker+Giant) were my best cards.


**What's the most exciting play you've made in *Magic 2019* Limited?**


My opponent played [Switcheroo](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Switcheroo) giving me a [Fell Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fell+Specter) and taking my Mystic Archeologist. I then used [Skeleton Archer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skeleton+Archer) to kill the Mystic Archeologist, and my [Chaos Wand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chaos+Wand) found a [Mind Rot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Rot) to eliminate my opponent's hand and the [Fell Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fell+Specter) dealt them 4 damage!


**What would winning GP Sacramento mean to you?**


I would reach Silver! Plus I would have endless bragging rights.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpsac18/t8_liu.jpg)


**Name:** Richard Liu


**Hometown**: San Jose, California


**Occupation**: Software Engineer


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Final at WMCQ


**What color combination did you play on Day 1, and what was your best card?**


White-Blue splashing Red. [Cleansing Nova](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cleansing+Nova) was my best card.


**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best card?**


Draft 1 – White-Black – [Regal Bloodlord](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Regal+Bloodlord)


Draft 2 – Black-Green – Vivian Reid


**What's the most exciting play you've made in *Magic 2019* Limited?**


I killed four of my opponent's creatures with a [Cleansing Nova](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cleansing+Nova)!


**What would winning GP Sacramento mean to you?**


I've been playing *Magic* off and on for over twenty years. I've had so many near misses in my life that just making it to the top 8 vindicates all the hours I've poured into this game. Obviously, winning would also be great!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpsac18/t8_burgess.jpg)


**Name**: Brian Burgess


**Hometown:** San Luis Obispo, California


**Occupation:** CFO/CPA


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Junior Super Series Championships Qualifier


**What color combination did you play on Day 1, and what was your best card?**


Blue-Red splashing Black.


Either [Nicol Bolas, the Ravager](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+the+Ravager) or [Banefire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banefire).


**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best card?**


Draft 1 – Blue-Green – [Skyrider Patrol](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyrider+Patrol)


Draft 2 – White-Black – [Cleansing Nova](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cleansing+Nova)


**What's the most exciting play you've made in *Magic 2019* Limited?**


Chump blocking with a flier, going to 2 life. My opponent played a spell that wasn't [Giant Spider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Giant+Spider), I attacked with [Volcanic Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Dragon), putting them to 8, and then played [Banefire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banefire) for 9 to finish them off!


**What would winning GP Sacramento mean to you?**


It would be an excellent way to temporarily retire from playing Grand Prixs, as my first child will be born in November!
