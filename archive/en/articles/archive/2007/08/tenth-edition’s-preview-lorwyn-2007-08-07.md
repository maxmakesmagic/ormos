*Tenth Edition’s*  Preview of *Lorwyn*
======================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 7, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












If you’ve seen the [*Tenth Edition* Starter Game](/en/articles/archive/tenth-edition-starter-game-2007-06-14), you know it comes bundled with a sneak peek of *Lorwyn*, **Magic**’s all-new large set coming in October. In the starter game you’ll find some beautiful basic lands ported directly from the *Lorwyn* set. Today we show off these lands to you, to give you a glimpse of what’s coming in the world of Lorwyn.

Note that some of the pieces of *Lorwyn* basic land art contain a secret that can give you an immersive insight into the *Lorwyn* setting. Lay your Wayne Reynolds Swamp next to your Wayne Reynolds Mountain, and you might get a surprise.
