Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Blue-Red Burn](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/standard-mocs-2016-09-11)

There was a [Standard MOCS](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/standard-mocs-2016-09-11) (*Magic Online* Championship Series) tournament this past weekend, and the second place deck by MikeyRooRoo (fantastic name) struck me because nearly the entire deck survives rotation intact. That means we might have an early frontrunner for the Deck to Beat come *Kaladesh*'s early weeks.


The deck is the same concept we've been seeing for a bit now—red burn spells backed by [Fevered Visions](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fevered+Visions), with [Thermo-Alchemist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thermo-Alchemist) spitting out damage. MikeyRooRoo went the [Stormchaser Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormchaser+Mage) route, and even added a bit of spice with [Unsubstantiate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsubstantiate), which is killer with [Fevered Visions](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fevered+Visions).


So what does the deck lose? Not much. This list has a single [Jace, Vryn's Prodigy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+Vryn%27s+Prodigy), but that isn't even standard. [Fiery Impulse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Impulse) is pretty easily replaced by [Galvanic Bombardment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Galvanic+Bombardment). And [Shivan Reef](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shivan+Reef) gets a (maybe?) upgrade with the new blue-red dual land in *Kaladesh*. The sideboard loses a few more cards, but all of them are basically replaceable/metagame calls anyway.


So get look below and start getting a (fevered) vision of what could be headlining Standard soon.
