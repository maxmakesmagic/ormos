Some call it the most powerful archetype in the *Scars of Mirrodin* Draft format. Conrad Kolos certainly put it to good use at Grand Prix–Nashville. What is it? Infect of course! Usually seen in black and green combinations, the archetype uses the powerful new keyword ability to kill opponents with poison counters. Aggressive weenies like [Ichorclaw Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ichorclaw+Myr) allow you to put the hurt on early, while evasive creatures like [Plague Stinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plague+Stinger) can combine with [Untamed Might](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Untamed+Might) for a big win out of nowhere. To read more about Conrad's deck and Grand Prix–Nashville, click [here](/en/events/coverage/xmas-comes-early-gerry-t).
