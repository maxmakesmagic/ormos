Quick Interview
===============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 16, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_KeitaMori.jpg)
By Keita Mori











**Question: What is your philosophy as a Magic player?**

Always give 100%, and never give up.  

-**Kouichirou Maki**

In order to defeat your opponent, you must first reflect on your own weaknesses.  

-**Ken'ichi Fujita**

To keep practicing and work at improving yourself. There is luck in **Magic**, but I believe that in the long run, skill will always cancel out any bad luck you might have.  

-**Alex Shvartsman**
