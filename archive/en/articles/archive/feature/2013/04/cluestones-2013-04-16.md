The Cluestones
==============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 16, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







With *Dragon's Maze* we introduce a new cycle of guild mana fixing: the Cluestones. A Ravnican might think of the Cluestones as an "in case of emergency break glass," providing useful magic to the members of their guild.


In the first *Ravnica* block, we had [the Signets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&name=+%5bsignet%5d&block=%7c%5b%22Ravnica+Block%22%5d):



![Boros Signet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Boros+Signet)

 

Then in *Return to Ravnica* and *Gatecrash* we were given [the Keyrunes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&name=+%5bkeyrune%5d):



![Boros Keyrune](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Boros+Keyrune)

 

And now we have the Cluestones:
