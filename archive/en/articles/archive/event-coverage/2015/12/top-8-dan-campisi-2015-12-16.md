Top 8: Dan Campisi
==================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*by Omeed Dariani*


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/AMCHAMP00/Images/b22.JPG)Sideboard (S): Where are you from?


Buffalo, NY


S: How long have you been playing?


Unlimited, Six years. Gave it up for a little while.


S: How long have you been playing competitively?


Five years. I enjoyed the social aspect of the competition. 


S: What are your best tournament accomplishments?


Today.


S: What are you playing and why?


Sneak Attack. Very good in playtesting. Won a few tournaments.


S: How does it stack up against the field?


Pretty good v. creature decks. Not easy to disrupt. 


S: How do you feel about your chances in the Top 8? 


Could go any way. I'm not overly confident.


S: What's your favorite card?


Sneak Attack.


S: What's your favorite format? 


Type II.


S: What's your favorite card mechanic?


Buyback.


S: What's your favorite movie?


The Exorcist. 


S: What's your favorite TV show?


The Practice.


S: What's your favorite food?


Pizza.
