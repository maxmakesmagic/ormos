Top 8: John Stevens
===================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*by Omeed Dariani*


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/AMCHAMP00/Images/b24.JPG)Sideboard (S): Where are you from?


Cookeville, TN.


S: How long have you been playing?


Dark.


S: How long have you been playing competitively?


Fallen Empires.


S: What are your best tournament accomplishments?


Today, Top 8 PTQ in Knoxville, Extended.


S: What are you playing and why?


Modified Angry Hermit (no Hermits - Earthquakes and Blastoderms). We metagamed it against the popular decks from the championships.


S: How does it stack up against the field?


Stompy can be a bad matchup, but the deck is solid against the field. 


S: How do you feel about your chances in the Top 8? 


I don't like speculating. It just gets me stressed.


S: What's your favorite card?


Counterspell.



S: What's your favorite format? 


Extended.



S: What's your favorite card mechanic?


Protection from a color/untargetability. 


S: What's your favorite movie?


Aliens


S: What's your favorite TV show?


Law & Order


S: What's your favorite food?


Spaghetti
