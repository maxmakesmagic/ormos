Spring All-Access Concludes This Week
-------------------------------------


All-Access Weeks are back! From March 23 to April 6, the Mythic Event Token will be available in the *Magic Online* Store and will allow players to play with almost every card on *Magic Online*.


![](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/MTGO-Mythic_Generic.png)


To use the Mythic Event Token, simply purchase it in the *Magic Online* store and enter a game or event. No cards will be added to your collection, but you'll be able to play with a deck even if you don't have all the cards in your collection. In fact, you don't need any!


The token is $25.00 USD, and the access will last until 10 a.m. (17:00 UTC) on April 6. We'll remove all the Mythic Event Tokens from accounts at that time.


For those of you who have QPs saved up, you can enter Showcase Last Chance Events as normal, but purchasing the Mythic Event Token will allow you to easily try out new formats!

----------------------------

Entry options include 5 Event Tickets or 50 Play Points, and the event runs from March 23 through April 6. MOCS Last Chance Events April 3–6
---------------------------------


Following Showcase Challenge Weekend, your next opportunity to continue toward qualification for the Season MOCS are the Last Chance Events. From Sunday afternoon through Wednesday morning Pacific time, Preliminaries in the four Showcase Qualifier formats (Modern, Pioneer, Legacy, and Vintage) are replaced with Last Chance tournaments.


These five-round events cost either 30 Event Tickets plus 40 Qualifier Points, or 300 Play Points plus 40 QPs to enter. If you win all five matches, you will earn an Entry Token good for that format's Showcase Qualifier later this month.


Note that Limited, Standard, and Pauper Preliminaries during this period will fire normally.


Learn more about Last Chance events  [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/mocs-step-1-showcase-challenges-last-chance-events)  .

2022 MOCS Season 1 Showcase Qualifiers This Weekend
---------------------------------------------------


Players who earned entry into the Showcase Qualifier will be squaring off for an invitation to the 2021 Season 3 Champions Showcase!


The entry fee for Showcase Qualifiers is a format token that matches the format of the Showcase. As with QPs, after the season's Showcase Qualifiers are finished, all unused Format Tokens will be removed from players' accounts.


Each Showcase Qualifier is a scheduled Swiss-style event with a Top 8 playoff. The winner of each Showcase Qualifier earns a spot in the final Showcase event and a chance to earn their share of $70,000 and an invitation to the first Pro Tour of 2023.
