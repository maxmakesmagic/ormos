*Coldsnap* Theme Decks
======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on July 11, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1117_swords.jpg)Want to get hip-deep in *Coldsna*p immediately? Check out the *Coldsnap* theme decks, hitting shelves along with booster packs and Fat Packs on [July 21](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/releases). 

### *Ice Age* and *Alliances* cards?!

Yep. Since *Coldsnap* is part of the *Ice Age* Block, its cards of course play nicely with cards from the *Ice Age* and *Alliances* sets. And just like other third sets' theme decks, *Coldsnap's* theme decks contain cards from its venerable predecessors, *Ice Age* and *Alliances*.

### Online *Ice Age* and *Alliances* cards?!

Yep. The contents of these theme decks is particularly big news for the ***Magic** Online* crowd. As soon as these theme decks hit ***Magic** Online*, *Ice Age* and *Alliances* cards that have never been available online will become available. That means your Online Classic decks can feature such true classics as [Gorilla Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gorilla+Shaman), [Bounty of the Hunt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bounty+of+the+Hunt), [Giant Trap Door Spider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Giant+Trap+Door+Spider), [Kjeldoran Dead](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kjeldoran+Dead), [Death Spark](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Spark), [Ashen Ghoul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashen+Ghoul), [Scars of the Veteran](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scars+of+the+Veteran), [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), [Storm Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Storm+Elemental), [Browse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Browse), and good old [Swords to Plowshares](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swords+to+Plowshares).
