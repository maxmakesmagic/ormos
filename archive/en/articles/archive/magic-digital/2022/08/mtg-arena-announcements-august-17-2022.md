Tomorrow will be bursting with previews with the first Wizards Presents set to begin at 9 a.m. PT on the official [*Magic: The Gathering* YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC8ZGymAvfP97qJabgqUkz4A) and [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic).

Qualifier Play-In Best-of-Three This Friday, August 19!
=======================================================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/logo_play-in_blue_850.jpg)


Want to get into the August Qualifier Weekend so you can play your way into Arena Championship 1? If you haven't already qualified, this [Friday's Best-of-Three Play-In event](https://magic.gg/events/qualifier-play-in) is your last chance to step onto that path to *Magic* glory!


Friday, August 19, beginning at 6 a.m. PT (13:00 UTC), you can enter the Best-of-Three Play-In and compete in Alchemy matches. But don't wait, because entry closes on August 20, at 3 a.m. PT (10:00 UTC).


Perform well in the Play-In and you can earn an invitation to [the August Qualifier Weekend](#qualifierweekend), which kicks off right after the Play-In event ends, on August 20 at 6 a.m. PT (13:00 UTC)!


Qualifier Weekend Begins This Saturday, August 20!
==================================================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/logo_qualifier_weekend_blue_850.png)


This is the final [Qualifier Weekend](https://magic.gg/events/qualifier-weekend) in which you can earn the coveted invitation to [the first Arena Championship](#arenachampionship) in September, where competition is fierce, players are top-tier, and the prizes are serious!


The August Qualifier Weekend is this weekend, with Day One entry opening on Saturday, August 20, at 6 a.m. PT (13:00 UTC) and closing two hours later at 8 a.m. PT (15:00 UTC)—don't be late! Get the maximum wins, and you'll receive an invitation to the Day Two event.


The Day Two event opens to entries on Sunday, August 21, at 6 a.m. PT (13:00 UTC), and closes two hours later at 8 a.m. PT (15:00 UTC).


The format for both days is Traditional Alchemy, so you'll be facing opponents in Best-of-Three matches.


Not yet qualified for the August Qualifier Weekend? You're in luck, because you can still squeeze into the running by entering [the Best-of-Three Qualifier Play-In happening this weekend](https://magic.gg/events/qualifier-play-in), immediately preceding the August Qualifier Weekend event!


Arena Championship 1, September 24–25
=====================================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/logo_arena_championship_blue_850.jpg)


This is it! You've faced down your competition and scaled the Premier Play mountain to reach the peak, where the air is rarified and the playing field is arrayed with the very best players in *MTG Arena*!


September 24 and 25, the game is on during [Arena Championship 1](https://magic.gg/events/arena-championship-1). This invitation-only, two-day event features major bragging rights, a massive total prize pool of $200,000, and a shot at an invitation to the *Magic: The Gathering* World Championship in 2023!


Check out [the current leaderboard and invitation list for Arena Championship 1](https://magic.gg/standings/Arena%20Championship%201) to see who's qualified to compete, and then prepare for the fiercest showdown in *MTG Arena*!

Qualifier Weekend Events
------------------------


[Qualifier Weekend events](https://magic.gg/events/qualifier-weekend) are two-day events in which eligible players compete for invitations to the Arena Championship 1 held in the fourth quarter of 2022.


*Qualifier Weekend Day One and Day Two events open to entries at 6 a.m. PT (13:00 UTC) and close to new entries at 8 a.m. PT (UTC 15:00) each day. You must enter within this two-hour window—late entries cannot be accommodated.*


### August

 August 20, 6 a.m.–August 21, 4 p.m.
	+ Format: Alchemy (Best-of-Three)

* **Qualifier Weekend**  

 September 17, 6 a.m.–September 18, 4 p.m.
