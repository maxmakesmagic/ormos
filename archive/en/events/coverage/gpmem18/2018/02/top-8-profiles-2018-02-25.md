Top 8 Profiles of Grand Prix Memphis 2018
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 26, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











After fifteen rounds of Swiss, the field of 1082 was down to just eight! Let's get to know them a little, and have them tell us about their amazing weekend.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmem18/gpmem18_miller.jpg)


**Name:** Blake Miller


**Hometown:** Hartselle, Alabama


**Age:** 27


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Top 32 at Grand Prix Atlanta


**Day 1 Record:** 7-1 (1 bye)


**Day 2 Record:** 6-0-1


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Mardu Vehicles. I know the deck better than any other in the format. [Walking Ballista](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista) was the best card all weekend.


**Did any particular card or deck surprise you this weekend?**


[Profane Procession](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Profane+Procession).


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


This is only my second Grand Prix, so it still hasn’t sunk in yet!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmem18/gpmem18_tenjum.jpg)


**Name:** Andrew Tenjum


**Hometown:** Madison, Wisconsin


**Age:** 24


**Occupation:** Student


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** 23rd at Pro Tour Dragons of Tarkir and 2nd at SCG Invitational.


**Day 1 Record:** 7-1 (2 byes)


**Day 2 Record:** 6-1


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Grixis Energy. It is the best [The Scarab God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God) deck.


**Did any particular card or deck surprise you this weekend?**


[Confiscation Coup](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Confiscation+Coup) stealing my [The Scarab God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God) in the mirror match.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


A chance to play on the Pro Tour again.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmem18/gpmem18_schroeder.jpg)


**Name:** Tyler Schroeder


**Hometown:** Iowa City, Iowa


**Age:** 26


**Occupation:** Mathematician


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** [Stubborn Denial](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stubborn+Denial) targeting [Violent Outburst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Outburst)


**Day 1 Record:** 7-1 (2 byes)


**Day 2 Record:** 6-1


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Red-Green Monsters. It’s resilient and abrasive.


**Did any particular card or deck surprise you this weekend?**


Some crazy five-color ramp deck.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmem18/gpmem18_heath.jpg)


**Name:** Heath Vance


**Hometown:** Clarksville, Tennessee


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Between jobs, just got out of the Army.


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** PPTQ win!


**Day 1 Record:** 6-2 (2 byes)


**Day 2 Record:** 7-0


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Blue-Black Control, because I feel it is the strongest deck and it is also able to beat Mono-Red. [The Scarab God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God) and Vraska’s [Contempt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contempt) were my best cards this weekend.


**Did any particular card or deck surprise you this weekend?**


Mastermind Ramp with main deck [Carnage Tyrant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carnage+Tyrant).


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


I qualified for my first Pro Tour.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmem18/gpmem18_barich.jpg)


**Name:** Aaron Barich


**Hometown:** Gulfport, Mississippi


**Age:** 21


**Occupation:** Bum


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** 5 SCG Open Top 8s


**Day 1 Record:** 8-0 (2 byes)


**Day 2 Record:** 4-1-2


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Sultai Constrictor. Overall, the matchups are good and it is great against midrange decks with [The Scarab God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God). Hadana’s Climb and [Blossoming Defense](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blossoming+Defense) were great!


**Did any particular card or deck surprise you this weekend?**


Mardu with [Snubhorn Sentry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snubhorn+Sentry)


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


Another chance to win a Pro Tour.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmem18/gpmem18_froehlich.jpg)


**Name:** Eric Froehlich


**Hometown:** Las Vegas, Nevada, USA


**Age:** 34


**Occupation:** I don’t believe in jobs.


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** Pro Tour Hall of Fame, 5 Pro Tour Top 8s, 16 previous Grand Prix Top 8s.


**Day 1 Record:** 7-1 (3 byes)


**Day 2 Record:** 5-0-2


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Blue-Black Control. In a world where most things are even, a better mana base and card drawing is king. [Torrential Gearhulk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk) was my best card!


**Did any particular card or deck surprise you this weekend?**


[Scrapheap Scrounger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrapheap+Scrounger) was tough to deal with.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


I’m going to the Pro Tour!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmem18/gpmem18_kling.jpg)


**Name:** Matthew Kling


**Hometown:** Salt Lake City, Utah


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** [Oasis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oasis) Games Employee


**Day 1 Record:** 8-0 (2 byes)


**Day 2 Record:** 4-2-1


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Grimes Energy, I listened to Michael Hinderaker! [Commit // Memory](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Commit+%2F%2F+Memory) was the MVP.


**Did any particular card or deck surprise you this weekend?**


Nothing much.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


My first Pro Tour qualification!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmem18/gpmem18_cochran.jpg)


**Name:** Hunter Cochran


**Hometown:** Stony Brook, New York


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Physics Lab Slave


**Day 1 Record:** 7-0-1 (2 byes)


**Day 2 Record:** 5-2


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Grimes Energy, because Nathan Smith told me to. He’s very smart!


**Did any particular card or deck surprise you this weekend?**


The “Turbo Ascend” deck.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


Widening the gap between myself and one chat ELO king, Allen Wu.
