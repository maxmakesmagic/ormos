Qualifiers for Pro Tour in Barcelona
====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 7, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














|  |
| --- |
|  |

  
Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* in Barcelona is May 11-13, 2012.


Qualifiers for Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* start December 26 and run until April 1, 2012. Check the Event Locator to find an event near you that could earn you an invitation to Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored*. Pro Tour Qualifiers offer a [Planeswalker Points](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/PlaneswalkerPoints/Information) multiplier of 5x, making them prime opportunities to add to your Planeswalker Point totals.


**Magic Online** Pro Tour Qualifiers will be run as well, so if you can't make it to an event near you, there's still a chance for you to qualify! Complete information on **Magic Online** can be found [here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/events/ptqmtgo/barcelona/12).


* [Invitation List for Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored*](/en/articles/archive/invitation-list-pro-tour-avacyn-restored-2012-2012-02-03)
* [Deck Lists from Pro Tour Qualifier Season](/en/events/coverage/pro-tour-avacyn-restored-qualifier-season-top-8-modern-decklists)

### Entry Fee


Entry fee varies from location to location. Check with local organizer for specific information.


### Tournament Structure


The structure for qualifier tournaments feeding Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* will be modified Swiss-style, with the Top 8 players playing in a single-elimination format for prizes and invitation(s).


### Tournament Format


* [Modern Constructed](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/sfrmodern)
* The *Dark Ascension*  expansion is not legal for use in the Modern format until February 4, 2012. Events held prior to this date will not use the Dark Ascension expansion
* Deck lists are required

### Tournament Length


* Swiss rounds - 50 minutes per round
* Top 8 matches will have no time limit.
* Number of Swiss rounds based on attendance according to the following chart:  


|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Attendance | Number of
 Swiss rounds |
| 17 - 32 | 5 |
| 33 - 64 | 6 |
| 65 - 128 | 7 |
| 129 - 226 | 8 |
| 227 - 410 | 9 |
| 411 or higher | 10 |

### Advancement


* All players may participate in all Swiss rounds.
* The Top 8 players after the final Swiss round will advance to the Top 8 single-elimination finals.

### Top 8


* Top 8 matches will be best 2 of 3
* Top 8 matches will have no time limit
* Where necessary, the standings after the Swiss rounds will still be used to determine final order in the standings.

### Notes


* [Planeswalker Points](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/PlaneswalkerPoints/Information) multiplier 5x, REL: Competitive
* The [**Magic: The Gathering**  Tournament Rules](http://www.wizards.com/wpn/Document.aspx?x=Magic_The_Gathering_Tournament_Rules) and [**Magic: The Gathering** Infraction Procedure Guide](http://www.wizards.com/wpn/Document.aspx?x=Magic_The_Gathering_Infraction_Procedure_Guide) are in effect for this event unless specifically overruled by information in this Format Document
* Players must bring pen, paper, card sleeves and appropriate counters.

### Eligibility


* Players who have won a Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* Qualifier may not compete in further Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* Qualifiers (including **Magic Online** Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* Qualifiers)
* Players with [2012 Pro Tour Players Club](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=protour/standings/proplayersclub11) level 7 or higher may not compete in Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* Qualifiers
* All other players may participate in Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* Qualifiers

Complete information about invitations to Pro Tours can be found in the [2012 **Magic: The Gathering** Premier Invitation Policy](http://www.wizards.com/wpn/Document.aspx?x=Magic_The_Gathering_Premier_Event_Invitation_Policy).


### Prizes


* The winner of each event will receive an invitation and airfare to Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* in Barcelona.
* Product prizes to be determined by the organizer.

### Registration Information


* Players who want more specific local tournament or registration information should call their local contact phone number.
* Players who have general questions about the **Magic: The Gathering**® trading card game, Wizards of the Coast LLC, the **Magic: The Gathering**  Pro Tour, DCI players’ organization, Qualifier tournaments, the **Magic**® World Championships, National Championships, or Regionals should call Wizards of the Coast at (800) 324-6496, or access [the Knowledge Base](http://wizards.custhelp.com/).
