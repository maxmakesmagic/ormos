Hulk Flash Smashes Columbus!
============================




May 19-20, 2007











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcol07/champ.jpg)If you happen to be in New York City this week, the ice cream cake is going to be on Steve Sadin! 883 players descended on Columbus, Ohio with Goblins, Affinity, Threshold, MonoBlack, Slivers, and just about every deck you can name, but Steve piloted a Billy Moreno-designed version of Hulk [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) to victory at the third-ever Legacy Grand Prix.


Sadin was thrilled when he realized he had qualified for a Pro Tour by a means other than PTQ earlier in the day. When he took the three-game match against Owen Turtenwald's Goblin build in the finals he hoisted the trophy and did a literal lap around the room.

### Bill Stark


**Age:** 24  
**Hometown:** Iowa City  
**Occupation:** Student, writer, editor (at Londes.com!)


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Personal friend of Gerry Thompson and Tim Aten


**Record Day 1:** 7-2  
**Record Day 2:** 6-0


**How would you identify yourself as a Magic player? (Constructed specialist, Limited Specialist, Vintage player, etc.)**  

I love limited but have been more successful at constructed (did beat H-Ron @ PTQ finals in sealed though)  
**How much work did you put in for the format?**  

A lot of theorizing/arguing with my Iowa guys, a medium amount of testing time (hours each week).  
**How big a factor was Hulk [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) in your testing?**  

It [was](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=was) the factor. I started with Powder & Leyline main.  
**Do you think [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) needs to be banned in Legacy?**  

Mmmm…I would probably punt and say "Let's see how [Future Sight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Future+Sight) affects things"  
**Were there any deck strategies that you think were overlooked for this weekend?**  

Obviously aggro black with a nod to Pro:White guys for Fish & DI handkill for Hulk.  
**What is the best Constructed deck of all time and why?**


An iteration of Trix. No other archetype has ever dominated for as long in varous forms. There may be/ have been more broken decks but we don't remember one-hit wonders, only repeat champions.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcol07/T8Nicolo.jpg)

### Paul Nicolo


**Age:** 20  
**Hometown:** Oak Park, MI  
**Occupation:** Student


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

2 PTQ top 8's, top 8 at Vintage Championship this year


**Record Day 1:** 7-2  
**Record Day 2:** 5-0-1


**How would you identify yourself as a Magic player? (Constructed specialist, Limited Specialist, Vintage player, etc.)**  

I play everything, but prefer constructed. I enjoy playing vintage.  
**How much work did you put in for the format?**  

Several weeks both before and after the flash errata.  
**How big a factor was Hulk [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) in your testing?**  

It was challenging to make a deck that did well against both [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) and everything else. I focused on the other match-ups, but I still have some cards for [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) .  
**Do you think [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) needs to be banned in Legacy?**  

Probably. The Pacts in [Future Sight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Future+Sight) make the deck much better.  
**Were there any deck strategies that you think were overlooked for this weekend?**  

Aggressive decks were required to play [Force of Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will) , [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) and [Stifle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stifle) to keep up with [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash) . Non-blue aggressive decks are at a disadvantage.


**What is the best Constructed deck of all time and why?**


The one with 4 [Burning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish) and 4 Lion's Eye Diamond.

Sunday, May 20: 5:35 p.m. - Quarterfinals: Gadiel Szleifer vs. Max Tietze
-------------------------------------------------------------------------


*by Brian David-Marshall*

  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcol07/qfSzleifer.jpg)*Gadiel Szleifer*
Gadiel Szleifer was easily the most experienced player in the room in terms of Pro Tour level play. Max Tietze was his polar opposite. While I know Max pretty well as a regular on the Gray Matter PTQ circuit on the East Coast this was certainly his best performance of any kind.


### Game 1


First turn [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) on the play seems like a solid game plan for a deck coming into a tournament where everyone was fearing turn one kills - can't help you on the turn zero kill but no seemed to running that version. Tietze took [Force of Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will) from Gadiel leaving [Daze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze) , [Mystical Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor) , and [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) among the eligibles. Interestingly he left the [Mystical Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Tutor) despite seeing a [Protean Hulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Protean+Hulk) in hand.


Gadiel dug into his deck with [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) and decided to see what was up with [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) of his own revealing two different forms of combo disruption - Swords and [Stifle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stifle) . He took the blue card. Tietze tapped out to play [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) and [Mother of Runes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mother+of+Runes) and Gadiel killed him.


Just like that.


Gadiel - 1 Max - 0


### Game 2


[Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) resolved through Gadiel's [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) on the second turn of the game. It was unclear if that was because Gadiel could not find a counter or if he had the kill assembled in hand.


"How many cards do you have?"


"Five."



![Force of Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Force+of+Will)

That was too many for Gadiel's liking and he played [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) naming Sword to Plowshares. He saw two [Meddling Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) s, [Daze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze) , and [Stifle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stifle) . When Max drew for the turn he also Force of Wiil which took a five point chunk off of Max's life total. Max offered up one of the two Mages on his turn and named [Massacre](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Massacre) . Gadiel attempted to take it out with Red Elemental Blast and Max chose to fight with [Force of Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will) pitching [Stifle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stifle) .


Max attacked for four and dug up another Mage with [Serum Visions](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serum+Visions) and played it naming [Massacre](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Massacre) . Gadiel took a stab at Max's hand with [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) and Max responded with [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) . Gadiel thought for awhile and finally allowed it - he only had two mana in play. Max then responded with [Daze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze) and Gadiel chose not to pay and Red Blasted the first [Meddling Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) with his other land.


Gadiel took four and tried to stop the beats by Tutoring [Pyroblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroblast) but he did not have the mana to pay for the second [Daze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze) Max had ready to punish the land light Szleifer.


Gadiel - 1 Max - 1


### Game 3
