The Tokens of Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty
====================================



 Posted in **Card Preview**
 on February 10, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/claytonkroh_0.jpg)
By Clayton Kroh




 Clay is a content strategist, writer, and editor. He's a content editor and writer for DailyMTG, and has contributed creative work for *Magic: The Gathering* sets. On the side, he writes science fiction, fantasy, and horror. 






Tokens get a glow up when technology and spirits meet in the neon-suffused [*Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/kamigawa-neon-dynasty). There are 29 full-art tokens and two emblems gleaming across *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* and its Commander decks.


In *Kamigawa: Neon Destiny* Draft and Set Boosters, there are 17 tokens plus two emblems to be found, and each Collector Booster glitters with a traditional foil double-sided token. The two Commander decks each include ten double-sided tokens to support your Commander games, with 12 tokens being unique to *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* Commander decks.


You'll find all the cards that create these tokens in the [*Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/kamigawa-neon-dynasty), the [Variant Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/kamigawa-neon-dynasty-variants), and the [*Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* Commander Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/kamigawa-neon-dynasty-commander)!
