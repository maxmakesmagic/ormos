Sealed Grand Prix Trial-Winning Decklists
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 6, 2017 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Marc Calderaro and Adam Styborski











*Amonkhet* is upon us! The suns are shining bright, so thank the Gods! And though we’re on a distant plane, one thing remains ever-constant: Grand Prix Trials. As sure as the suns rise in the ... whichever direction(s) they rise from on Amonkhet ... so will players converge on convention center on Fridays to earn byes for their Saturday.


As this is a new format, you should have as many deck lists as your bare-lain hearts can stand. So below are all the decklists from last-night’s trials and tribulations (sans Oscar Hurtado’s, which was lost to the annals of time).
