[Cruel Ultimatum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Ultimatum) and [Violent Ultimatum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Ultimatum) are both cards that have seen play in Standard since they saw print during *Shards of Alara*. But [Brilliant Ultimatum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brilliant+Ultimatum)? Not so much. Or at least, no so much until Alex Viksnins managed to pull the feat off at the TCGPlayer $5K in Boston. It turns out the card is very good at letting you cast [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Aeons+Torn) for free. And if you don't flip an [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Emrakul%252C%2Bthe%2BAeons%2BTorn)? Perhaps any of your four different types of planeswalker, or even a second copy of the Ultimatum!
