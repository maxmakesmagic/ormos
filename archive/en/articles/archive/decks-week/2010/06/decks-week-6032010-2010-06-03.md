Decks of the Week: 6/03/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on June 3, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Congratulations to Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa, your newest Pro Tour champion. Paulo made his sixth Top 8 at [Pro Tour-San Juan](/en/events/coverage/paulo-celebrates-six-beach-san-juan) thanks to an 8-2 record with his Blue-Red-Green *Zendikar* Block Constructed deck and his 5-1 record in Booster Draft. Check out the Block Constructed decks of each player who recorded at least 18 points over the 10 rounds of Swiss, along with the Top 8 Booster Draft decks and a few San Juan Public Event deck lists from the weekend as well.
