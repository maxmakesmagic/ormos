Images of Planechase
====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on July 16, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Remmember when we [told you about *Planechase*](/en/articles/archive/announcing-planechase-2009-05-12)? Well, we were thinking that you might possibly be interested in seeing what the four *Planechase* deck packs look like. So here you go!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/234_elementalthunder.jpg)  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/234_metallicdreams.jpg)  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/234_strikeforce.jpg)  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/234_zombieempire.jpg)  
Each of these game packs will be released September 4, 2009 and will have an MSRP of $19.99. *Planechase* release events will be held September 4-6, 2009.
