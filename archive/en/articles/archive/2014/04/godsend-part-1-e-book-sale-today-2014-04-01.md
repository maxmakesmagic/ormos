Godsend Part 1 E-Book On Sale Today!
====================================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on April 1, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






Posting anything serious on April Fools day is a difficult task. I have to convince you all there isn't any trick involved (other than myself of course.) So, let me try to be perfectly clear here: **This is not a prank.**


**There is no prank here.**


**Seriously. This is 100% real.**


Now that that's through and you are all fully convinced I am telling you the truth. I can begin telling you about Jenna Helland's book *Godsend*, which has Part 1 going on sale today through [Amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com/Theros-Godsend-Part-Jenna-Helland-ebook/dp/B00FUZNL7O/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1389991470&sr=8-1&keywords=jenna+helland) and [Barnes & Noble](http://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/theros-jenna-helland/1117135286?ean=9780786965564) for just $1.99.


Your seventh, eigth, and ninth steps on the [Hero's Path](http://www.mtgherospath.com) are fast approaching, read the E-book to learn about Elspeth's journey and discover what she has faced on her own hero's path. Check back tomorrow for a sample from the E-book!


Part 2 of *Godsend* will release on May 13, 2014.
