Worlds 2009 was held in Rome, Italy and the last Italian left defending the title in the host nation was William Cavaglieri. The deck he used to make the Top 8? Mono-White Tokens. The powerful strategy makes good use of the combo between [Conqueror's Pledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conqueror%27s+Pledge) and [Honor of the Pure](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Honor+of+the+Pure)! For more information on the 2009 World Championships, [visit the official event coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12).
