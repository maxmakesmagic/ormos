Video Quick Questions: Origin Stories
=====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 17, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











Brian David-Marshall prowled the hall in Austin to get insights from some of the game's top players about the biggest influences in their **Magic** careers, the first decks they built, and when they felt like they had the "stuff" to compete on the Pro Tour. Included in the segment are Hall of Famer Raphael Levy, Player of the Year hopeful Martin Juza, Rookie of the Year frontrunner Brian Robinson, Matej Zatlkaj, and Zack Hall.
