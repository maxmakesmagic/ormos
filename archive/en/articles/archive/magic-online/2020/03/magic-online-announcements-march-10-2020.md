Cube Spotlight Series: *Pauper Cube* March 11–18
------------------------------------------------


Welcome back to the Cube Spotlight Series! After over 10 years maintaining the cube, Adam "Stybs" Styborski, member of the [Commander Advisory Group](https://mtgcommander.net/index.php/about-us/) (CAG) and *Magic* esports content and coverage teams, is ready to share *Pauper Cube* on MTGO. Entry options include both League and Single-Elimination Queue, and events cost 10 Event Tickets or 100 Play Points. *Pauper Cube* runs from March 11–18.


All undefeated players get their trophy *and* a copy of Pauper staple [Thorn of the Black Rose](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thorn+of+the+Black+Rose)! And because this is a bit of a special occasion, don't forget to login to *Magic Online* by March 18 to redeem the code "PauperCube2020" for your copy of [Standard Bearer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standard+Bearer) from *Apocalypse*. Even free accounts can get in on this promotion!


For more *Pauper Cube* information, check out Tolarian Community College's [announcement video](https://youtu.be/oOSAdOyMfaw) and go behind the scenes with Adam's [*Pauper Cube* article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/cube-spotlight-series-pauper-cube-2020-03-10).

Pioneer PTQ Saturday
--------------------


On Sunday, March 14, there will be a Pioneer PTQ at a special start time of 4 a.m. PT (11:00 UTC)! The entry fee is 1 Red PTQ Finals Token or 40 [MOCS Qualifier Points](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020). The winner qualifies for their choice of events in Series 2 of the [2020 Players Tour](https://magic.gg/players-tour) in May.


Learn more about Players Tour Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#ptq).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats. To learn more about Premier Play qualification, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020).


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges continue every weekend for Standard, Pioneer, Modern, Vintage, Legacy, and Pauper formats except when replaced by Showcase Challenges during scheduled Showcase Challenge dates. *NOTE: The Vintage Challenge happens every weekend regardless of scheduled Showcase Challenges.*
