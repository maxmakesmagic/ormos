Meet the Champions Update: The Brothers Yamazaki
================================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 6, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Ready for another story about the heroes of Kamigawa? Check out the [*Champions of Kamigawa* site](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/displayexpansion.asp?set=chk&page=1) for an update in the "Meet the Champions" area.




This week's story is "**[Bonds of Ice and Fire](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/chk/yamazaki)**" by Jay Moldenhauer-Salazar. It's the story of how the legendary warriors, the [Brothers Yamazaki](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Brothers%2BYamazaki)
[Brothers Yamazaki2](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Brothers%2BYamazaki2), became revered in their own time, and yet why their accomplishments were never recorded in Kamigawa's written history. Enjoy!
