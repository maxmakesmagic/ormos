Grand Prix Nottingham Byes
==========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 12, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**Rating used**: Limited, September 7th, 2005  
**Pro Point Standings Used**: **Magic the Gathering** Pro Players Club. These byes are not represented in the tables following, but are awarded as follows:

Level 1 members get a 1-round bye  
 Level 2 members get a 2-round bye  
 Level 3 or higher members get a 3-round bye

To find the number of byes you have for Grand Prix-Nottingham, go to the appropriate list below (sorted by last name):
