Friday Night Countdown: Pro Tour Battle for Zendikar
====================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 23, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










Rich Hagon and Brian David-Marshall bring you this Eldrazi-sized edition of Friday Night Countdown from Pro Tour *Battle for Zendikar*. Not only can you get fresh advice from top pros on what to play in your next Standard or Draft event, but this episode also features two exclusives not seen during Pro Tour coverage: a deck tech from all-time great Jon Finkel on Dark Jeskai, and *Battle for Zendikar* Draft pick order analysis from reigning World Champion Seth Manfield.
