======================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 1, 2010

By Wizards of the Coast

### Release date:


* November 3, 2010 on XBox LIVE Arcade
* November 3, 2010 on PC
* November 23, 2010 on Playstation 3

### Features:


* Three new playable decks, including Niv-Mizzet's "Root of the Firemind" Planeswalker deck
* Three new unlockable cards for each of the eight original decks plus the three decks from expansion 1 and the three decks from expansion 2
* Five new puzzling challenge levels
* Three new achievements

### Achievements




|  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
|  | **Name** | **Description** | **Points** |
|  | Tsunami of Pain | Deal 12 combat damage to an opponent in a single attack with one blue creature | 15 |
|  | Salt in the Wound | An opponent's last permanent leaves the battlefield | 15 |
|  | Madness Suppressed | Defeat Sarkhan the Mad in the single-player campaign (Expansion 3) | 20 |
