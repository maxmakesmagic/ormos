Top 8 Profile - Neil Rigby
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPMAN00/Images/T8Rigby.jpg)**Name:** Neil Rigby  
**Age:** 23  
**Location:** Home  
**Occupation:** Job  
**Played Magic since:** Dawn of time  
**Best accomplishments:** Being me.



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Manchester?**  

A: Because it's 600 yards from my house.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Winning, because nobody can beat me.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: Perfect day one, slightly better day two.



**Q: What do you think about the tournament location-/organisation-wise?**  

A: Brilliant, I can walk home (good old blighty).



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Because I'm Neil Rigby.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: £££$$$ Holiday to LA



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: Neil 3-0 Neil 3-0 Neil 3-0, spank you very much!
