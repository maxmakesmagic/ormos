GP Trial Winning Decklists
==========================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on February 14, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_FrankKarsten.jpg)
By Frank Karsten










Yesterday, loads of players competed in the Grand Prix Trials to earn two byes in the main event. The winning and second-place decks, listed below, traditionally give a first impression of what to expect over the course of the weekend. Judging by these deck lists, the early frontrunners are:


* R/W Aggro, which mana-efficient removal spells like Valorous Stance and Wild Slash from Fate Reforged;
* R/G Aggro, which aims to ramp into the well-positioned Stormbreath Dragon; and
* Abzan Midrange, featuring Tasigur, the Golden Fang as a new standout card.
