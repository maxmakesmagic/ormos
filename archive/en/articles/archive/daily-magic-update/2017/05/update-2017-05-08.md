The May 8, 2017 Update
======================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on May 8, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)

Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Blue-Red Mindful Control](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-05-07)

Zayoh, who piloted the below list to a 5-0 record in a *Magic Online* Standard Competitive League, is just the latest to find success with a mostly blue control deck that only touches on red for some cheap removal.


In this case, [Magma Spray](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magma+Spray) and [Harnessed Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harnessed+Lightning) are all you need, as the rest of the deck fills up with counterspells ([Censor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Censor), [Disallow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disallow), [Essence Scatter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Essence+Scatter), and [Void Shatter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Void+Shatter)), card draw ([Glimmer of Genius](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimmer+of+Genius), [Pull from Tomorrow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pull+from+Tomorrow), and [Kefnet the Mindful](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kefnet+the+Mindful)), plus the super versatile [Commit // Memory](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Commit+%2F%2F+Memory) and control-daddy [Torrential Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk).


And that's it, that's the full deck. Control what's cast, prune the board with cheap removal, and finish things off with Kefnet and [Torrential Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk). It's a simple but effective plan that's almost certain to show up in some capacity at Pro Tour *Amonkhet* this weekend.
