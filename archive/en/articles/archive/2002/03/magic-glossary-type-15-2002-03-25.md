Magic Glossary: Type 1.5
========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 25, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












**Type 1.5**


Type 1.5 is a cross between Type 1 and Extended in that it allows the use of all card sets, but the most powerful cards (like Time Walk and Necropotence) are banned.


**Type 1.5 Legal Sets**


* All basic sets
* All extensions of the basic set
* All promotional cards released by Wizards of the Coast
* All limited-edition or large expansion sets
