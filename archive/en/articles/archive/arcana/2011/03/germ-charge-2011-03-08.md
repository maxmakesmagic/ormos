The Germ in Charge
==================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 8, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Hey, wanna see something cool? Here's the Germ token that comes in some *Mirrodin Besieged* packs.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/tcg/products/mbs/07he2j6tbi_en.jpg)  
And here's that Germ blown up. Do not be afraid! It is not the Germ itself! It is only a pictorial representation!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/662_germ.jpg)  
Okay, now look at that Germ. Get its image in your brain. Really get to know it. And now, check out [Flayer Husk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flayer+Husk).

![Flayer Husk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Flayer+Husk)If we blow up the [Flayer Husk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flayer+Husk) art, you might see something cool. So let's try it!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/662_flayerhusk.jpg)
