A Sneak Peek at Coldsnap
========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 18, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












*Coldsnap* previews don't start for another week, and it's a month until the set goes on sale. But we couldn't wait that long to share a little with our loyal players, so here's a sneak peek at a card from the new set. Without further ado...


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptcha06/sunscour.jpg)Coldsnap [Prerelease Events](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/prerelease/coldsnap) are scheduled for July 8-9, and the set goes on sale July 21. Tune in to **magicthegathering.com**  on Monday for the Orb of Insight, and then full *Coldsnap* previews start the week of June 26.
