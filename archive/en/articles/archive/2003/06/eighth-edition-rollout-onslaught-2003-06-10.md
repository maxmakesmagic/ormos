### *[Onslaught](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=magic/expansion/onslaught)* Information:




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Released:** October 2002**Design Team:** Mike Elliott (lead), Mike Donais**Development Team:** Randy Buehler (lead), Elaine Chase, Mike Donais, Brian Schneider, Henry Stern, Worth Wollpert**Art Director:** Jeremy Cranford & Dana Knutson |  | **New Mechanics:** cycling triggers, morph, “tribal” cards**Factoid:** *Onslaught* marked the first time a keyword ability (cycling) returned to the game after previously being phased out. Cycling was first introduced in *Urza's Saga* in 1998. |
