*Brian Kibler* |

**Name:** Brian Kibler  
**Hometown:** San Diego  
**Age:** 29  
**Occupation:** Game designer. Look for Ascension, coming out this August!  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Three Pro Tour Top 8s, including one win. I’ve been in the Top 8 of a Grand Prix 10 times. I’ve made it to Nationals Top 8 twice before, with no win. =(  
**Record (including byes):**  

Byes: 3  

Day 1: 9-0  

Day 2: 4-1-  
**Where do you usually play *Magic*?** I mostly just play Premier events - when I play FNM I go to Sky High Comics in San Marcos, CA, I test usually with Ben Rubin.  
**What deck did you use? Who designed it?** Next Level Bant. Originally designed by Pat Chapin - I’ve tuned it a lot since GPDC.  
**Did you make any changes to the design yourself?** Vengevine, Jace, Elspeth  
**Any memorable matches?** Come back from one life to beat Mori in round one today!  
**Any memorable decks?** Jund and U/W are both strong decks, but there’s nothing as quiet as this deck!  
**If you were to play in Japan Nationals, which deck would you use?** I’d play the same deck with a few sideboard tweaks.  
**What’s your favorite format?** Block constructed - fresh constructed formats with a limited card pool are always interesting.

*Hiroyuki Shimoya* |

**Name:** Hiroyuki Shimoya  
**Hometown:** Sapporo  
**Age:** 26  
**Occupation:** Auction buyer  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** I’ve played in Nationals, and gone 3-0 at an FNM.  
**Record (including byes):**  

Byes: 0  

Day 1: 8-1  

Day 2: 4-0-2  
**Where do you usually play *Magic*?** BJ Shop (Sapporo)  
**What deck did you use? Who designed it?** I played a Bant deck of my own design.  
**Did you make any changes to the design yourself?** I think the solution is 4 Jaces.  
**Any memorable matches?** My first match on Day 2 was against a non-Japanese player. It was my first time to play a foreigner, and we had some communication problems.  
**Any memorable decks?** I think the Dredge deck that got 3 byes is amazing.  
**If you were to play in Japan Nationals, which deck would you use?** Either Next Level Bant, or the same deck I used today.  
**What’s your favorite format?** Legacy. I like the rough-and-tumble feel of classic *Magic*.

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Finalist, Grand Prix Yamagata

*Ryou Tasaki* |

**Name:** Ryou Tasaki  
**Hometown:** Tokyo  
**Age:** 25  
**Occupation:** Doctor  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** First champion of The Limits  
**Record (including byes):**  

Byes: 3  

Day 1: 5-1  

Day 2: 4-2  
**Where do you usually play *Magic*?** TASAKI HOUSE  
**What deck did you use? Who designed it?** UW Control, designed by myself and my friends Kimura and Yamakawa.  
**Did you make any changes to the design yourself?**   
**Any memorable matches?** I was playing Saito. I had a Sovereigns and a 1/3 with Conscription on it, and was on the clock to lose the next turn, but I won because the 1/3 had Path played on it during upkeep.  
**Any memorable decks?** Jund was stronger than I expected.  
**If you were to play in Japan Nationals, which deck would you use?** Same as today.  
**What’s your favorite format?** Limited. It’s simple—you just need to open packs and you can play. I play it with my friends all the time.
