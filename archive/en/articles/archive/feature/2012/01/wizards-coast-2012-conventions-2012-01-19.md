Wizards of the Coast 2012 Conventions
=====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 19, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Wizards of the Coast is excited to attend the following events as part of the 2012 convention season:


[PAX East](http://east.paxsite.com/) - Boston, MA   
 April 6 – 8, 2012


[San Diego Comic Con](http://www.comic-con.org/cci/) - San Diego, CA   
 July 11 – 15, 2012 (the 11th is preview night!)


[Gen Con](http://www.gencon.com/2012/indy/default.aspx) - Indianapolis, IN   
 August 16 – 19, 2012


[PAX Prime](http://www.paxsite.com) - Seattle, WA   
 August 31 – September 2, 2012   
*The site currently redirects to the PAX East site.*


A full slate of seminars and events is on the schedule for all of our 2012 events. Be sure to stop by the booth to check out new activities, get sneak peeks of new content, and receive promotional giveaways (while supplies last). Don't forget to participate in the great **Magic: The Gathering** and **Dungeons & Dragons** tournaments and programming!


We look forward to seeing you in 2012!
