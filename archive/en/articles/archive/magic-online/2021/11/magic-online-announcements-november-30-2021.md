Modern Banned Gauntlet
----------------------


The Modern Banned Gauntlet arrives tomorrow! From December 1–8, players can choose a preconstructed Modern deck with a card we've banned in it and see how these "too good for Modern" decks fare against each other. Make sure to check out all the decklists in [the accompanying article here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-banned-gauntlet-2021-11-30).

*Live the Dream Cube* Season 3 Ends Tomorrow
--------------------------------------------


The Spotlight Cube Series returns with Season 3 updates to David McDarby's *Live the Dream Cube*. Since its last showing, David has added cards from *Strixhaven: School of Mages*, *Dungeons & Dragons: Adventures in the Forgotten Realms*, *Modern Horizons 2*, *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt*, and *Innistrad: Crimson Vow*! The cube ends tomorrow, December 1, and you can check out the full card list and read all about the archetypes in [the update article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/spotlight-cube-series-live-dream-cube-season-3-2021-11-23).

*Modern Horizons 2* Returns
---------------------------


While we currently have *Vintage Cube* up and running, it's time to bring back another fan favorite: *Modern Horizons 2* Draft! We're bringing back the non-Phantom Draft League now through January 5 with the same prize structure and chance to open a Ragavan, Nimble Pilferer you know and love!


* Entry Options:
	+ 25 Event Tickets
	+ 3 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters and 4 Event Tickets
	+ 250 Play Points
* Prizes:
	+ 3 Wins – 6 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters, 2 MOCS Season 3 QPs, and a Competitive Trophy
	+ 2 Wins – 2 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters

Limited Super Qualifier Friday
------------------------------


On Friday, December 3, there will be an *Innistrad: Crimson Vow* Sealed Super Qualifier with a special start time of 2 p.m. PT (22:00 UTC). The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points (Season 3), 40 Event Tickets, or 400 Play Points. The top two finishers qualify for the Neon Dynasty Championship on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Super Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021#sq).

Showcase Challenge Weekends
---------------------------


Players looking to prove their mettle against the best players in *Magic Online* will want to use their QPs to enter *Magic Online* Champions Showcase (MOCS) events such as the returning Showcase Challenges this weekend. These MOCS events are a set of linked events designed to highlight the best players on *Magic Online* each season, culminating in the Champions Showcase and its prize pool of $70,000 and a Set Championship invitation.


First up on Saturday, December 4, we have the Pauper Showcase Challenge at 8 a.m. PT (16:00 UTC), and on Sunday, December 5, we have the Legacy Showcase Challenge at 8 a.m. PT (16:00 UTC). Then next week on Saturday, December 11, we have the Modern Showcase Challenge at 8 a.m. PT (16:00 UTC), and on Sunday, December 12, we finish things off with the Pioneer Showcase Challenge at 6 a.m. PT (14:00). Each of these events requires 40 QPs to enter. The Top 8 finishers from each event will earn a qualification token to enter the appropriate Showcase Qualifier in December.


Learn more about Showcase Challenge events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021#schallenges).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. [Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place. *NOTE: Pauper, Vintage, and Standard Challenges are never pre-empted**.*
