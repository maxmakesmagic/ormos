Inside R&D at Pro Tour Khans of Tarkir
======================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 14, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_mikemcartor.jpg)
By Mike McArtor




 Mike first played with Ice Age and became the copy editor for DailyMTG.com in December 2011. Before DailyMTG, he was an editor on D&D's Dragon magazine for four years. 





In case you hadn't heard, this past weekend was [Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptktk)! First off, congratulations to Ari Lax for his victory over a really tough Top 8!


There's a lot of great coverage at the link above, but for this Arcana we're going to single out a few videos made ahead of time called “Inside R&D.” Want to know what R&D was thinking while making *Khans of Tarkir*? Watch away!
