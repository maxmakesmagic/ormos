===========================

Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on December 7, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by the [art of Jesper Ejsing](#2).

If that's not a recipe to make the Daily Update without mentioning [Cloudblazer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudblazer) or [Panharmonicon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Panharmonicon), I don't know what is.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. (Nearly) Mono-Blue Artifacts

Here's another Standard Showdown submission that made me smile. The deck is essentially a [Metalwork Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalwork+Colossus) deck that sticks to almost entirely just blue, but it has a few twists.


The spiciest is the not mono-blue option, [Saheeli Rai](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Saheeli+Rai), who can give you hasty a [Metalwork Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalwork+Colossus), scry to set up [Glint-Nest Crane](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint-Nest+Crane), double-up on [Foundry Inspector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Foundry+Inspector) to let you cheat costs for a turn, or let you draw cards with copies of [Hedron Archive](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hedron+Archive). R&D member Sam Stoddard thinks Saheeli is one of the most underplayed cards in Standard, and this deck shows off a way to make the card shine.


Additionally, the nearly mono-blue nature of the deck makes [Engulf the Shore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engulf+the+Shore) a viable way to keep the board clear, and [Gearseeker Serpent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gearseeker+Serpent) can be basically [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) under the right circumstances (just don't draw too many—hence the single copy).


The deck is super fun and sweet, and just the right kind of awesome for a Standard Showdown.
