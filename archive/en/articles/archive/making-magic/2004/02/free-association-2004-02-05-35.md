Free Association
================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on February 5, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_markrosewater.jpg)
By Mark Rosewater




 Working in R&D since '95, Mark became Magic head designer in '03. His hobbies: spending time with family, writing about Magic in all mediums, and creating short bios. 






I'm a [giant fan](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/free-association-2004-02-05-24) of light bulb jokes. As such, I've spent a great time trying to come up with a good answer to:


How many **Magic** players does it take to change a lightbulb?


Got a good answer? I'd love to hear it.
