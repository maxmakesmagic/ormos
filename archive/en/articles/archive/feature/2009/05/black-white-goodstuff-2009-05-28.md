Black-White is a pretty common color combination in Standard these days with the powerful Black-White Tokens strategy dominating tournaments. Christopher Murray, however, wasn't content playing the same ole' stock Tokens list. Instead, he opted to take the most powerful cards offered by black and white and smash them together. [Nyxathid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nyxathid)? Goes well with the handkill the deck also plays. [Stillmoon Cavalier](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stillmoon+Cavalier)? Peculiarly good against those aforementioned Black-White Tokens decks. Christopher's build was good enough to get him into the Top 8 in Winnipeg, Manitoba. To read about more Regionals Top 8 decklists, visit [the Regionals decklists page](/en/events/coverage/2009-regionals).
