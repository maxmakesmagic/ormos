Already a successful Standard deck, Fish is starting to see a resurgence in the Extended format as well. Sandydogmtg recently used this list to make the Top 8 of a premier event on Magic Online. Cursecatcher is a solid play against the TEPS/Storm decks popularized by Luis Scott-Vargas' win in Grand Prix-Los Angeles while the usual support staff of [Lord of Atlantis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Lord%2Bof%2BAtlantis)s, [Silvergill Adept](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Silvergill%2BAdept)s, and [Merrow Reejerey](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Merrow%2BReejerey)s cleaned up. Want to take sandydog's deck for a spin at the next PTQ for Honolulu? Find one near you [here](/en/node/640716).
