========================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 22, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast

If you weren't aware of that, we apologize for springing this information on you like that. You can get [the whole story here](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/they%E2%80%99re-alive-%E2%80%93-robot-chicken-and-phyrexia-vs-coalition-2010-02-20).
