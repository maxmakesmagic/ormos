**Banned and Restricted changes in effect after downtime tomorrow, June 14**


As [announced today](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/june-13-2017-banned-and-restricted-announcement-2017-06-13), [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) is banned in Standard. All Standard leagues will be reset with tomorrow’s downtime as scheduled. In addition, the Commander 1v1 league will be reset so we can return [Food Chain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Food+Chain) and [Erayo, Soratami Ascendant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Erayo%2C+Soratami+Ascendant) to the banned list.


New Standard and Commander 1v1 leagues will be created with updated banned lists and available to join after the downtime. In addition, all Standard and Commander 1v1 events that begin after the downtime will use the updated banned lists.


All other constructed leagues will have their durations extended until early July.

**Battle of the Planes begin tomorrow, June 14**


Tomorrow, Battle of the Planes begins in celebration of MTGO’s 15 year anniversary! Choose your favorite among five of *Magic*'s most iconic planes, get a Sealed deck from it, and play! This league will be available through June 21.


Additional information about Battle of the Planes can be found in the anniversary article [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/magic-onlines-fifteenth-anniversary-2017-06-07).

**New *Magic Online* features launch with downtime tomorrow, June 14**


Last week Lee Sharpe and Chris Kiritz introduced some well received anniversary events as well as new features being added to *Magic Online*. The Full Trade Binder in addition to the client’s connectivity update will both be implemented during the downtime tomorrow.


More information about these features can be found [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/magic-onlines-fifteenth-anniversary-2017-06-07).

***Amonkhet* Standard Championship Qualifiers begin tomorrow, June 14**


*Amonkhet* Standard Championship Qualifiers begin tomorrow, June 14, after downtime. Start times will be every three hours on the half-hour, and can be found in the Scheduled Events section on *Magic Online*.


Refer to this [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-amonkhet-championship-event-details-2017-05-31) for Amonkhet Championship specifics as well as updated information regarding Constructed PTQs.

***Magic Online* RPTQ for Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation* on Saturday, June 17** 


*Magic Online* Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers (RPTQ) for Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation* will take place on Saturday, June 17. These events are only open to players who qualified via in-store Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifier events – pre-registration is also required.


Additional details about Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers can be found [here.](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/regionalqualifiers)
