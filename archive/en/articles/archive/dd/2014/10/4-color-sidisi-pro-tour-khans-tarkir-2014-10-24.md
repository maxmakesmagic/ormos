4-Color Sidisi at Pro Tour Khans of Tarkir
==========================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on October 24, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_shuheinakamura.jpg)
By Shuhei Nakamura











Do you like strong creatures? How about lifegain? Or a little combo? Perhaps you just like to play offbeat decks?


If you answered yes to any of those questions, then I have the deck for you today—Ken Yukuhiro's [Pro Tour](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptktk) deck.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Sidisi,%20Brood%20Tyrant)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sidisi,%20Brood%20Tyrant)
While it can follow the well-beaten Abzan path of using mana creatures to pull out a [Siege Rhino](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino), it also breaks new ground by ramping into [Sidisi, Brood Tyrant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sidisi%2C+Brood+Tyrant).


Sidisi is much more than a gold [Hill Giant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hill+Giant). With enough creatures in the deck, she can act like a miniature [Grave Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grave+Titan), and she turns [Satyr Wayfinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Satyr+Wayfinder) into an amazing two-mana creature that not only gives you a land but makes 2/2 Zombies.


But that's not the only gimmick Yukuhiro thought of. What would happen if Sidisi or the Wayfinder were to turn over a [Soul of Theros](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+of+Theros)? You know, the one that gives everything +2+2, first strike, and lifelink?


Do you like decks that turn [Hill Giant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hill+Giant)s into [Baneslayer Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel)s?
