The Jan. 12, 2016 Update
========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on January 12, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know for today, January 12, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by [glowy eyes and some of the most beautiful *Magic* cards you will ever see](#number1).


Today's Must See
================


1. [*Oath of the Gatewatch* Limited Set Review: White](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/oath-of-the-gatewatch-limited-set-review-white/) | ChannelFireball | Luis Scott-Vargas

When Luis-Scott Vargas reviews a set, the people listen. Even if what they're listening to is a metric ton of puns. Check out the first entry today and then follow along as he runs through the entire set over the next week or six.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [The Top 5 Cards from *Oath of the Gatewatch* BEST for Modern for *Magic: The Gathering*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9i8CcGehexU) | Tolarian Community College | The Professor

The Professor's latest video tackles the best five cards for Modern from *Oath of the Gatewatch* and a nasty case of the [Kor Firewalker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Firewalker)s. With [Pro Tour *Oath of the Gatewatch*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/ogw) featuring Modern, you might as well start brainstorming at a college. Even a community one.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/9i8CcGehexU>


1. [Sleeper Hold](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/seanuy-011316-sleeper-hold/) | Gathering Magic | Sean Uy

Board-state puzzles have a long history in *Magic*, and Sean Uy does that tradition proud with a perplexing puzzle featuring the long loved, and oft-discussed "Sleeper" preconstructed deck from *Urza's Saga*, everyone's favorite preconstructed deck featuring enchantments that turn into creatures. Right? Just me? Anyway, the puzzle is fun.


1. [*Oath of the Gatewatch* Tricks](http://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/oath-the-gatewatch-tricks) | MTGGoldfish | Sameer Merchant

If you like being surprised at the Prerelease, avert your mouse. But if you like knowing exactly what your opponent might have for tricks at every mana cost—in a handy table format—then click no further as Sameer Merchant goes through Literally. Every. Trick. In. The. Book. The book being *Oath of the Gatewatch*.


1. [Brew Corner—Stone Haven Drawing](http://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2016/01/brew-corner-stone-haven-drawing/) | Hipsters of the Coast| Aaron Gazzaniga

Aaron Gazzaniga "doesn't know Standard very well," but he knows when a card has brewability (brewability—adj., the quality of leading to brews), and today he's all over Stone Haven Outfitter, the "wannabe [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp)."


1. [Tobias Andrion](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/cmdrdecks-011316-tobias-andrion/) | Gathering Magic | CMDR Decks

Another fun entry from Gathering Magic, CMDR decks delivers a Pauper Commander deck headed by the mighty (not really) and powerful (not at all) [Tobias Andrion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tobias+Andrion), selected specifically for the synergies (it's a common) and strategies (it's a common) it brings to the table. Looking to add even more restrictions to your next Commander deck? Get some inspiration from Tobias and friends.
