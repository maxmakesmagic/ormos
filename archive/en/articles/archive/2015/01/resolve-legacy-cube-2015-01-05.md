Resolve To Legacy Cube
======================



 Posted in **MTGO Articles**
 on January 5, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_davidwhitfield.jpg)
By David Whitfield










Happy New Year, *Magic Online*! The new year is a time of looking forward, of plotting where we want to go in the fresh twelve months ahead. It's also a time of retrospection, though. How can we know where we want to go if we don't assess where we came from? You might say that it's a time to take a long look at one's...


*Legacy*.


That's right! The *Magic Online* Legacy Cube is returning for your gaming pleasure.


Beginning Wednesday, January 14, following the downtime, the [Legacy Cube](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/new-magic-online-legacy-cube-2014-11-3) will make its triumphant return to *Magic Online*. Accompanying the Legacy Cube will be triple *Odyssey* Flashback Drafts. Both *Odyssey* and Legacy Cube Events run in Swiss and Single Elimination Draft. Both sets of queues conclude with the Wednesday, January 28, downtime.
