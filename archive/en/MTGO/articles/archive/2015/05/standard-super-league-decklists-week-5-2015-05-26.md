By Wizards of the Coast











Every Tuesday at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7), the newest episode of the Standard Super League airs live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic). For the Standard Super League, we want to get league decklists into the hands of viewers as quickly as possible. Now, also on Tuesdays at 6:00 p.m. Pacific, viewers can go [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/standard-super-league-decklists) to find a link to the Standard Super League decklists for that night's episode!


The below decklists are for week 5 of the Standard Super League, a special Modern week! Click on player names below to bring you right to their deck.
