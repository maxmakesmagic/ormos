Inside R&D: Magic Origins—Liliana
=================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 30, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










Five Planeswalkers, five origin stories. *Magic Origins* chronicles the growth of five iconic Planeswalkers through the cards, and this edition of Inside R&D dives into the stories of the set, the Planeswalkers, and designing the cards that tell their tales.




---

Today, we explore the mysterious origins of Liliana Vess, the healer-turned-necromancer whose origins are tied to Dominaria—but whose ascension to powerful master of the undead took place on Innistrad.
