Grand Prix - Singapore
======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 9, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The Magic: The Gathering® Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro Tour™ events.


**TOURNAMENT FORMAT AND SCHEDULE**  
The format for Grand Prix–Singapore is *Onslaught*™ block Sealed Deck, with day 2 Booster Draft. All DCI™ Limited-format rules, **Magic**® Floor Rules, and all subsequent updates will be enforced.


**Friday, March 28:** Onsite registration is from 4:00 – 9:00 P.M. at Meeting Room 306.  
**Saturday, March 29:** Late registration from 8:30 – 9:30 A.M. Tournament begins at 10:00 A.M. The Top 64 players advance to competition on Sunday.  
**Sunday, March 30:** The Top 64 players begin play at 9:00 A.M.

**TOURNAMENT**


**Divisions**  
 The main tournament at Grand Prix–Singapore is open to **Magic** players of all ages.


* **Head Judge**  
 The Head Judge for Grand Prix–Singapore will be Mark Brown, Level 3 from Australia.
* **Tournament Structure**  
 The structure for Grand Prix–Singapore will be modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds), with the Top 64 players advancing to the second day of competition for prizes and invitations.
* **Amateur Status**  
 A player is considered to have amateur status if he or she has 0 lifetime pro points. If a player has ever been awarded any points from a Grand Prix, Pro Tour, or World Championship event, that player no longer has amateur status, and is not eligible for amateur awards at Grand Prix or other tournaments.
* **Tournament Divisions and Prizes**  
 Grand Prix–Singapore will feature the standard main tournament prize schedule, as well as a special Amateur division prize schedule. Players who have 0 lifetime Pro Points will be eligible for the Amateur prizes. The Amateur prizes will be paid to the highest-finishing amateur players *in addition to any winnings those players receive from the main tournament prize schedule*. All prizes are in U.S. dollars.

* **Pro Tour Invitations**  
The Top 8 previously uninvited players will each win an invitation to Pro Tour–Yokohama. *In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher in that Grand Prix. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*

**SIDE EVENTS**


* **Grand Prix–Singapore Trials** ([Full List of Trials](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=grandprix/singapore03/trials)  
**Friday, March 28:** Trials start at 12:00 P.M., 2:00 P.M., 4:00 P.M., and 6:00 P.M. Registration closes 15 minutes prior to each Trial. Maximum 32 players per Trial, single-elimination. The top finisher receives a third-round bye at Grand Prix–Singapore and one display of *Legions*™ boosters. The format is *Onslaught* block Sealed Deck. Cost: S$25
* **Pro Tour–Yokohama Qualifier**  
**Sunday, March 30:** Registration closes at 9:15 A.M.; play begins at 9:30 A.M. The top finisher receives US$375 and an invitation to Pro Tour–Yokohama. The format is *Onslaught* block Sealed Deck with Booster Draft finals. Cost: S$30
* **On Demand Tournaments**  
 Eight-person single-elimination, on-demand tournaments will be offered throughout the weekend. Costs: Constructed S$5, Draft S$15, Sealed S$25
* **Win an Xbox™**  
 Whenever a player enters the main event or a side event at Grand Prix–Singapore, that player's registration form will be entered to win assorted product prizes. Winners will be randomly selected during the Top 8 finals on Sunday, March 30, 2003 from all eligible entries received. The Grand Prize is an Xbox video game system. *Terms and conditions apply.*
