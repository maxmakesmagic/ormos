**Throwback Standard Gauntlet: *Worldwake*begins tomorrow, August 30**


Tomorrow Throwback Standard Gauntlet:*Worldwake begins*! You will be able to enter this event tomorrow and the event will run through September 6. 


For more details about Throwback Standard Gauntlet: *Worldwake,* as well as decklists, please refer to this article [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/gauntlet-7-worldwake-2017).

Vintage Leagues are finally here! Check out Lee Sharpe's article [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-leagues-begin-august) for all of the exciting details!

**Vintage B&R update to cause brief downtime tomorrow, August 30**


Starting at 8 a.m. PT, *Magic Online* will go into a brief downtime lasting until roughly 9 a.m. PT. During this time the *Magic Online* team will be implementing the necessary B&R updates affecting Vintage. Please be aware that redemptions will not be processed during this downtime.

***Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet*Sealed PTQ for Pro Tour *Rivals of Ixalan*on Saturday, September 2**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, September 2 at 7 a.m. PT. Qualify for this event by earning a PTQ Entry Token in a Preliminary.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).


[Top](#top)

**Modern PTQ for Pro Tour *Rivals of* *Ixalan*Sunday, September 3**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, September 3 at 9 a.m. PT.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

***Magic Online* Format Challenges back this weekend**


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges introduced a new prize structure and recurring weekend events for Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper formats!


Events begin every Saturday at 8:00 a.m. PT, and run through Sunday. Check out [this page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-format-challenges) for further event details as well as the weekend schedule.
