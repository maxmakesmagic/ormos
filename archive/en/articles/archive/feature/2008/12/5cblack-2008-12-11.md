Jakub Slemr had a very successful career on the **Magic** Pro Tour, with the crown in his resume being his World Champion title in 1997. He used a combination of rainbow lands to supplement a powerful black weenie strategy with the best splash cards the other four colors could provide. With Five Color Control now a popular strategy in the world of modern Standard, there is the real possibility a deck playing all five colors could regain the championship again in 2008. You can find out all weekend long on **magicthegathering.com** as the World Championships kick off today, with [the live webcast](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/webcast/home) of the Top 8 set to begin from Memphis at 9:45 a.m. ET on December 14th.
