Yohan Allouche
--------------


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/EURO00/Images/215.JPG)First Name: Yohan 


Last Name: Allouche


Date of Birth: August 19th, 1981


Country of Residence: France


Occupation: Student


Since when have you been doing reporting for Magic?


1999 Regional Championships


Why do you like doing it?


I like to report information especially when it's about Magic or computers or science.


What part of the coverage do you like best/which articles do you prefer to write?


Draft reports. The best Magic Type!!


Besides being a Sideboard reporter what else do you do related to Magic?


Many tournaments.


And finally, who's your personal favorite for this weekend?


Lucien Bui

Monty Ashley
------------


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/EURO00/Images/Monty.JPG)First Name: Monty 


Last Name: Ashley


Date of Birth: May 8th, 1970


Country of Residence: USA


Occupation: Sideboard Guy


Since when have you been doing reporting for Magic?


Since Pro Tour-New York 1998 (the one Jon Finkel won)


Why do you like doing it?


It gives me a warm feeling inside. And it's my job.


What part of the coverage do you like best/which articles do you prefer to write?


I like to do the detailed analysis of which decks did the best against which other decks at tournaments. It entertains me that I spend so much time on those articles and people hardly look at them.


Besides being a Sideboard reporter what else do you do related to Magic?


Well, I'm also a Sideboard editor. And I've played Magic since Alpha.


And finally, who's your personal favorite for this weekend?


Noah Boeken, although I should point out that I'm writing this just after Boeken won his semifinal match.

Jeff Donais
-----------


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/EURO00/Images/245.JPG)First Name: Jeff 


Last Name: Donais


Date of Birth: May 14th, 1971


Country of Residence: USA


Occupation: WotC guy


Since when have you been doing reporting for Magic?


Since early 1997. I wrote Key Plays for the Duelist and did coverage for Duelist Online for the old Pro Tour web casts.


Why do you like doing it?


It's nice to help Magic players become celebrities and provide a way for people to learn about the Magic tournament community.


What part of the coverage do you like best/which articles do you prefer to write?


I like to read feature matches, player interviews and player profiles. I like to write everything.


Besides being a Sideboard reporter what else do you do related to Magic?


I do some work in the DCI and some other stuff for Wizards of the Coast.


And finally, who's your personal favorite for this weekend?


I think Noah Boeken will win.

Kim Eikefet
-----------


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/EURO00/Images/300.JPG)First Name: Kim 


Last Name: Eikefet


Date of Birth: July 10, 1973


Country of Residence: Norway


Occupation: Sports reporter


Since when have you been doing reporting for Magic?


Since Grand Prix Stockholm. I started writing for the written European Sideboard at that time.


Why do you like doing it?


I like going to events and meeting people.


What part of the coverage do you like best/which articles do you prefer to write?


Interviews. I like talking to people and getting to know them better. Going under the surface of the player, get to know the person behind the player.


Besides being a Sideboard reporter what else do you do related to Magic?


I play but not very often since there's no place to play in Bergen where I live. And if there's a tournament, most of the time I judge instead of play in it.


And finally, who's your personal favorite for this weekend?


A Norwegian. "We kind of agreed that Bjorn Petter, the current national champion, would win, because the national champion also won last year. But I wouldn't mind if any other Norwegian won."

Alex Shvartsman
---------------


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/EURO00/Images/184.JPG)First Name: Alex 


Last Name: Shvartsman


Date of Birth: November 19th, 1975


Country of Residence: USA


Occupation: Writer


Since when have you been doing reporting for Magic?


Inquest articles since 1996 Sideboard magazine and web coverage since 1997.


Why do you like doing it?


My favorite hobby is playing Magic. My second favorite is observing and writing about the game. I like the competition aspect of it a lot and enjoy reporting on the tournament scene.


What part of the coverage do you like best/which articles do you prefer to write?


Strategy content - deck analysis, overviews, feature matches. Also player-based articles such as discussing the teams/play testing, competitors previous accomplishments, etc.


Besides being a Sideboard reporter what else do you do related to Magic?


Play on the Pro Tour level, write for several MTG related magazines and trade/sell singles.


And finally, who's your personal favorite for this weekend?


I'm voting for my teammates from the European Alliance. I would also like to see Nicolas Labarre and Bram Snepvangers do well.
