The October 17, 2016 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on October 17, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Nicholas_Wolfram.jpg)
By Nicholas Wolfram




 Nicholas Wolfram fell promptly in love with tabletop and video games when he was only two years old, and he has been writing about them since he figured out how words worked. Now he puts together words about Magic, which he started playing way back in 2000. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on October 17, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by [Thing in the Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thing+in+the+Ice), the surprise *breakout* card of the [Pro Tour *Kaladesh*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptkld/) finals!

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Shota Yasooka's Grixis Control](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptkld/top-8-decklists-2016-10-15)

This complex brew of a deck is what led Yasooka to take home the title of Pro Tour *Kaladesh* Champion! With only two nonland four-ofs and four one-ofs in the main deck, this deck relies on a broad variety of counterspells, creature removal and burn spells, and draw spells to keep the opponent off the board long enough to hit them hard with [Thing in the Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thing+in+the+Ice) and [Torrential Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk).
