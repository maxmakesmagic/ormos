One of the breakout decks from [Pro Tourâ€“Kyoto](/en/events/coverage/nassif-greater-power), the Swans combo deck in Standard isn't quite like the Swan decks of old. Instead of killing a player in just one turn, the Blue-Red control deck is happy to plunk [Swans of Bryn Argoll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swans+of+Bryn+Argoll) onto the board to slow attackers, then drop [Seismic Assault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seismic+Assault) to seal the deal. By pitching one land targeting the 4/3, its controller gets to draw two cards, potentially fueling an onslaught of direct damage. Cco brings us the newest incarnation of the deck, straight from the Premier Events stage on **Magic Online**!
