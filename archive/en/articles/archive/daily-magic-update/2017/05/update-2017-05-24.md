The May 24, 2017 Update
=======================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on May 24, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on May 24, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by every creature entering the battlefield at the same time.


Today's Must
============


1. [What If All Creatures in *Magic* Would Enter the Battlefield at the Same Time?](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/what-if-all-creatures-in-magic-would-enter-the-battlefield-at-the-same-time/) | ChannelFireball | Frank Karsten

I want to live in Frank Karsten's brain, where not only do these questions get raised, but actually answered. The comments are great, [the Reddit thread is great](https://www.reddit.com/r/magicTCG/comments/6d0cm7/theorycrafting_what_if_all_creatures_in_magic/), everything about this is great.


After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [And a Pomme de Terre to You, Kind Sir](http://www.magictheamateuring.com/mta-episode-226-and-a-pomme-de-terre-to-you-kind-sir/) | Magic the Amateuring | Maria Bartholdi and Meghan Wolff

Grand Prix, Canadian border patrols, and the newfound love of a true-blue Commander. All that in just a touch over an hour. Sounds like a perfect lunch break.


1. [Top 10 Black and Green Cards in EDH – Revisited](https://collected.company/2017/05/23/top-10-black-green-cards-edh-revisited-159/) | The Command Zone | Jimmy Wong and Josh Lee Kwai

Well, they're not blue, but these colors do go well with blue, so I'll allow it. Jimmy and Josh both give their lists for the best cards in Golgari colors. [Mulldrifter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter) was not eligible.


Community Spotlight
===================


1. [The Content Creators' Cup - TastySnackies vs. Nizzahon Magic - Quarterfinal Match #1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B2jnP8u9z5E&feature=youtu.be) | Card Bazaar

Card Bazaar is a YouTube channel that creates content pretty regularly. The latest offering is a Content Creators' Cup pitting a pair of *Magic* content creators against one another in 1v1 Commander. They also do a series called Card Anthology, where they discuss old *Magic* sets for just the right touch of nostalgia.


1. [The Loregoyfs](https://soundcloud.com/user-438748113)

A group of four story-loving friends get together and talk about *Magic Story*. It's a simple formula, but the quartet are friendly, funny, and engaging, which makes it an enjoyable listen. They've got 15 episodes under their belt and don't show any signs of slowing down.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [White-Blue Flash](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-05-23)

There was a time when White-Blue Flash was the best deck in Standard, particularly since it was so strong against [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel).


Sound familiar?


*Magic Online* user McWinSauce piloted their updated version of the deck to a 5-0 record in a Competitive Standard League recently, and it has all the makings of being powerful once more. [Spell Queller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Queller) is still one of the best ways to hold up an [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel). [Archangel Avacyn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+Avacyn) is excellent against Zombies, especially when [Eldrazi Skyspawner](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Skyspawner) and [Walking Ballista](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista) can trigger Avacyn at will.


The list even has multiple ways to deal with an [Ulamog, the Ceaseless Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ulamog%2C+the+Ceaseless+Hunger) if it does end up in play—[Declaration in Stone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Declaration+in+Stone) and [Stasis Snare](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stasis+Snare).


Plus, [Gideon, Ally of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Ally+of+Zendikar) and [Archangel Avacyn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+Avacyn) are just plain powerful cards. That's not a bad place to be.
