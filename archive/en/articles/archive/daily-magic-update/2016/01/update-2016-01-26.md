The January 26, 2016 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on January 26, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on January 26, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by [puzzling your way to a 1-2 finish](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/seanuy-012616-sucker-punch/).


Today's Must
============


1. [Interview: Kimberly Kreines & Melissa Li of *Magic: The Gathering*](http://www.gadgette.com/2016/01/25/interview-kimberly-kreines-melissa-li-of-magic-the-gathering/) | Gadgette | Sammy Maine

Two of our creative designers—Kimberly Kreines and Melissa Li—talk work, science, *Oath of the Gatewatch*, working as women in the gaming industry, their process for creating and working with *Magic*'s characters, and the Green Bay Packers.


The Packers suck (Skol Vikings!), but this article most certainly does not.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [This Is That: *Oath of the Gatewatch*](http://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/this-is-that-oath-of-the-gatewatch) | MTGGoldfish | Jake Stiles

I've been reading *Magic* content a long time. Longer than some of you have been alive. So it's rare I find something unique. Now, maybe things like this have been done before—it is a pretty simple concept after all—but if so, I haven't seen them. "This Is That" looks at the design space *Oath of the Gatewatch* has explored through reprints, cards that are generally better, generally bigger, generally smaller, and generally worse, and those that are wholly unique. It's a good way to give you a baseline of what a card is capable of, and a handy way to creatively spur some ideas.


1. [U/R Prowess Standard Deck Tech—*Oath of the Gatewatch*—MTG!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uJnhRwwu4ko) | The Mana Source | Wedge

This video gets my vote for calling Atarka Red the cockroach of the format. It also happens to highlight a new entrant into the Standard format with Blue-Red Prowess, a deck we saw perform well at the [StarCityGames.com Atlanta Open](http://www.starcitygames.com/events/230116_atlanta.html). For more on the deck, you can see another [deck tech from that tournament featuring Ross Merriam](http://www.starcitygames.com/events/coverage/ur_prowess_with_ross_merriam.html).


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/uJnhRwwu4ko>


1. [*Shadows over Innistrad*: Theory](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WROPefVpF5Q)| Seibenlore

What, Release events for *Oath* are over so we're already on to *Shadows over Innistrad*? We haven't even had the Pro Tour yet! *Oath* only comes out on *Magic Online* this week!


Oh well. If you're not the type to slow your roll and smell the roses and all that jazz, and you *really* want to start speculating on *Shadows over Innistrad*, we won't stop you.


So how much did Seibenlore get right? Ha, nice try. I will give you this one, exclusive bit of information—[he got the name of the set correct](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/arcana/announcing-shadows-over-innistrad-2015-11-02). Shh. Don't tell anyone.


1. [The Diary of a Pro Player—Limited Prep](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/the-diary-of-a-pro-player-limited-prep/) | ChannelFireball | Pascal Maynard

Pascal Maynard is giving you a look deep inside his Pro Tour preparations. None of it involves a [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf).

What to Watch Tonight
=====================


1. [Vintage Super League](http://vintagesuperleague.com/)

It's Week 6 (of 9) in the Vintage Super League, and some of the best players in the world will be slinging some Vintage spells. [Sea's Claim](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sea%27s+Claim) just came *thiiiiis* close to winning a StarCityGames.com Modern Classic. Like, really close. Second place, close. Temur Delver [might have taken home the trophy](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/displaydeck.php?DeckID=98005), but the Fish got a moral victory with second and third place finishes. Does this foretell a fishy finish for someone at [Pro Tour *Oath of the Gatewatch*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/ogw)?
