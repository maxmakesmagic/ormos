*Innistrad* Game Day
====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 12, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











I*nnistrad* Game Day is coming! On October 29-30, **Magic** Game Day gives you a shot at glory by becoming a local champion! Run in local game and hobby stores around the world a month after a new card set is available in stores, **Magic** Game Day is a great way to interact with your local players and see how the new cards shape the Constructed metagame. 

### What Happens There?

Game Days are [Standard-format tournaments](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/sfrstandard). Contact the organizer of the event for further tournament details.

### What Do I Get?

All participants receive an exclusive Diregraf Ghoul card (while supplies last). The Top 8 players also each receive an exclusive Elite Inquisitor extended-art prize card (while supplies last). These cards are awarded to the Top 8 players even if the tournament cuts only to the Top 4.
