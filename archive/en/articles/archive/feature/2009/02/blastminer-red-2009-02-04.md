Renowned deck builder Adrian Sullivan recently got 2nd at a Pro Tour Qualifier in his native Wisconsin. The deck he was piloting was a standard Sullivan issue Rogue Red, featuring cards as yet unseen in the format like [Dwarven Blastminer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dwarven+Blastminer) and [Blistering Firecat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blistering+Firecat). But will *Conflux* change everything? To find out how to get a chance to play with the brand new set this weekend, the first it becomes available for sale, visit [this page](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/releases).
