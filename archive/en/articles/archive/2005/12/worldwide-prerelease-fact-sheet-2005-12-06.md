### When


All Tournaments will be held on the weekend of January 21-22, 2006


### Entry Fee & Product Mix


* Check with your local tournament organizer for specific entry fee information.
* Cost of entry will provide you one (1) *Ravnica: City of Guilds* tournament pack and three (3) *Guildpact* boosters.
* In addition, some locations may offer 3-person Team & 2-person Two-Headed Giant formats.
	+ Each 3-person team will receive two (2) *Ravnica: City of Guilds* tournament packs & six (6) *Guildpact* boosters to build 3 decks.
	+ Each 2-person Two Headed Giant team will receive one (1) *Ravnica: City of Guilds* tournament pack & five (5) *Guildpact* boosters to build two decks. For further information about Two Headed Giant format, please go to the [Two-Headed Giant FAQ](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=dci/announce/dci20050805a).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2006/GPTPrereleaseDjinn_small.jpg)*Your prize for attending, while supplies last.*
### Tournament Structure


* The structure of the main *Guildpact* Prerelease tournament is modified Swiss-style.
* *Guildpact* Prerelease side tournaments will be offered throughout the day and will consist of single-elimination rounds.

### Tournament Formats


The format for each Prerelease tournament is:


* DCI sanctioned, *Guildpact*, Sealed Deck.
* Select locations, DCI sanctioned, *Guildpact*, Team Sealed Deck.
* Select locations, DCI sanctioned, *Guildpact*, Two-Headed Giant Sealed Deck.

### Prizes


* Select locations, check with organizer, will offer Door Prizes of 3 *Guildpact* Booster Packs to be given out at random each hour.
* Special commemorative *Guildpact* prerelease foil card for each tournament participant, ***while supplies last***.
* Additional packs of *Guildpact* cards to top players (as determined by your local prerelease Tournament Organizer).
* Other items for winners of the main tournament and side tournaments may be awarded (as determined by your local prerelease Tournament Organizer).
