Arguably the most important development in combo decks to date, Cocoa Pebbles functioned much like the Fruity Pebbles decks it compete alongside. Like those versions it sought to deal an arbitrarily large amount of damage to its opponent using [Goblin Bombardment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Bombardment), [Enduring Renewal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enduring+Renewal), and a zero-cost artifact creature. The important difference, and one that would send ripples through the **Magic** space/time continuum? Tony Dobson's Pro Tour-Chicago deck used [Necropotence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necropotence) to find his combo.
