The story in Standard lately has focused on Caw-Blade and Blue-Red-Green, but plenty of players are innovating outside of the box. Like Robert Moody. He made the Top 8 of the TCGPlayer.com Wizards World Series in Anaheim, California recently with this take on Black-Red Vampires. The key addition to the deck? A twin set of [Hero of Oxid Ridge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero+of+Oxid+Ridge), which prevent pesky [Squadron Hawk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squadron+Hawk)s from getting in the way of his Vampire attacks.
