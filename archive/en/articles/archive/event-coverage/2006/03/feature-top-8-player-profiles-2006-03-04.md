Feature: The Top 8 Player Profiles
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 4, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pthon06/t8_jones.jpg)*Craig Jones*


**Craig Jones**


**Age:** 30


**Hometown:** Preston


**Occupation:** Student


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** "More than Ruud Warmenhoven, but I'm getting pretty old. My memory might be fading.


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Honolulu?** Top 32 from PT LA


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 1 GP Top 8


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** Winner GP Birmingham 1998


**What was your record on Day 1:** 7-1  **Day 2:** 5-1


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**Not as much as I would have liked. Tested with a European team and the UK qualifieds. Also met up with Billy Moreno, which tipped my deck choice.


**Where did you learn to play Magic? Is there a store that you play most of your Magic at now?** At a friend's house. I play at a local store in Manchester.


**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** I may beat Antoine.


**What was your reaction when you heard the Pro Tour was coming to Honolulu? What activities have you done in Hawaii outside of playing Magic?** Pretty good. It's nice to see such exotic locations. I got to walk up and down Diamond Head.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pthon06/t8_osyp.jpg)*Osyp Lebedowicz*


**Osyp Lebedowicz**


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** Union, NJ


**Occupation:** MBA Student, former Latin Dance Champion


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** 18???


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Honolulu?** Players Club


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 4 GP/3 PT


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** Voted Best Dressed by Gabe Walls and Paul Reitzl


**What was your record on Day 1:** 8-0 **Day 2:** 4-2-2


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?** Practiced on MTGO, Apprentice, and IRL with close friends and Mike Flores.


**Where did you learn to play Magic? Is there a store that you play most of your Magic at now?** The Only Game in Town


**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** No clue…probably decent.


**What was your reaction when you heard the Pro Tour was coming to Honolulu? What activities have you done in Hawaii outside of playing Magic?** I was excited. I hung out on the beach and macked it to some honeys.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pthon06/t8_chan.jpg)*Tiago Chan*


**Tiago Chan**


**Age:** 22


**Hometown:** Lisbon


**Occupation:** Graduated in Journalism. Looking for a job.


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** 20-25


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Honolulu?** Level 3 Players Club


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 0/0


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** 2003 Portuguese National Champion


**What was your record on Day 1:** 5-2-1 **Day 2:** 7-0-1


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?** I didn't. I built the deck the morning before the Pro Tour because they told me it was a good gamble.


**Where did you learn to play Magic? Is there a store that you play most of your Magic at now?** I am still learning every now and then.


**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** If I avoid the Zoos it's good.


**What was your reaction when you heard the Pro Tour was coming to Honolulu?** I didn't like it because it was too far and I don't like to take many days off.


**What activities have you done in Hawaii outside of playing Magic?** I went to the Waikiki Beach.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pthon06/t8_bracht.jpg)*Max Bracht*


**Max Bracht**


**Age:** 17


**Hometown:** Regensburg (near Munich)


**Occupation:** Professional card gamer, student


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** 5


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Honolulu?** Copenhagen Top 16, Lille Top 8, and Level 3


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** Two GP


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** GP London Top 3, Top 8, Top 16, Top 32. PT London 12th.


**What was your record on Day 1:** 6-2 **Day 2:** 6-1-1


**How did you prepare for the event?** Who did you prepare with? Tested some Magic Online with Johan Meyvis and some Germans.


**Where did you learn to play Magic? Is there a store that you play most of your Magic at now?** I started at the age of 7. My older brother Chris taught me and played sometimes in [www.dastraumwerk.ole](http://www.dastraumwerk.ole) in Regensberg.


**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** Semifinals


**What was your reaction when you heard the Pro Tour was coming to Honolulu? What activities have you done in Hawaii outside of playing Magic?** I was quite happy because of the nice place and one week of school vacation before the event. We were just hanging around at the beach.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pthon06/t8_oruel.jpg)*Olivier Ruel*


**Olivier Ruel**


**Age:** 24


**Hometown:** Lille


**Occupation:** Journalist


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** ~35


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Honolulu?** Pro points, GP Bilbao, GP Beijing, GP Copenhagen


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 16/5


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** 3 GP Wins (16 Top 8) 5th PT Top 8, 2nd in the Player of the Year race 2005


**What was your record on Day 1:** 6-2 **Day 2:** 6-1-1


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?** I played on the Magic Online beta for a week with Maxime Hermes and Arnost Zidek. Then I practiced for two days with Piere Canali in Honolulu.


**Where did you learn to play Magic? Is there a store that you play most of your Magic at now?**  At home with my brother. The "Xop Cobra ca roule?"


**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** 50/50 every match, so 1/8 I'd say.


**What was your reaction when you heard the Pro Tour was coming to Honolulu? What activities have you done in Hawaii outside of playing Magic?** - "Yeeee! Woooo! Youpi!" - I wrote postcards to mom, Alex, and Emile (hi all!) and went to the beach.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pthon06/t8_ruud.jpg)*Ruud Warmenhoven*


**Ruud Warmenhoven**


**Age:** 23


**Hometown:** Tilberg


**Occupation:** Student


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** More than Craig Jones


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Honolulu?** Level 3


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** One….now


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** Split 15% With Frank Karsten when he had second place at Worlds.


**What was your record on Day 1:** 5-3 **Day 2:** 7-0-1


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?** I traveled through Thailand and got handed a deck by Wessel Oomens.


**Where did you learn to play Magic? Is there a store that you play most of your Magic at now?** A friend in school taught me during class. I haven't seen any Magic stores in Thailand yet.


**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** Quite bad - I have always been afraid of Owls.


**What was your reaction when you heard the Pro Tour was coming to Honolulu?** YAUSSSSS!!!


**What activities have you done in Hawaii outside of playing Magic?** Surfling, playing Volleyball, flirting with the Subway girl.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pthon06/t8_herberholz.jpg)*Mark Herberholz*


**Mark Herberholz**


**Age:** 22


**Hometown:** Detroit, MI


**Occupation:** Student


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** 15+


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Honolulu?** Player's Club Level 3


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** GP:2 PT:3 (counting this one)


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** GP Minneapolis finalist, GP Detroit T8, PT San Diego & Philly Top 8


**What was your record on Day 1:** 6-2 **Day 2:** 6-1


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?** Tested a bit on Magic Online beta, got here a few days early to test with the beach house guys.


**Where did you learn to play Magic? Is there a store that you play most of your Magic at now?** With friends. R/W Hobbies Livonia MI


**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** No clue, but I am pretty lucky.


**What was your reaction when you heard the Pro Tour was coming to Honolulu? What activities have you done in Hawaii outside of playing Magic?** Sweet! Booze, beaches, and babes. What could be better? Went to a few bars, I plan to do more in the following week.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pthon06/t8_aruel.jpg)*Antoine Ruel*


**Antoine Ruel**


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** Montpellier


**Occupation:** Pro Player


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** ~35


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Honolulu?** Level 5


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 13/4


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** Champion PTLA


**What was your record on Day 1:** 7-1  **Day 2:** 5-3


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?** I stayed one week with friends, including Canu, who is the nuts. The day before, my good friend Fred Courtois told me about the deck. I loved the deck. Levy and Bertrand gave me this very good list. Thank you guys!


**Where did you learn to play Magic? Is there a store that you play most of your Magic at now?** I play mostly online, with my friends.


**What do you think of your chances in the Top 8?** "0" - I can't win my first match even if he mulligans down to two cards. (I'm serious)


**What was your reaction when you heard the Pro Tour was coming to Honolulu? What activities have you done in Hawaii outside of playing Magic?** I was really happy about going to such an exotic place. I've been to the beach and enjoyed having as much fun as someone can. There hasn't been any sun though.
