Round 7: Courtney's Boys vs. Slay Pillage Gerard
================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 28, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptbos02/a879.jpg)



|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Courtney's Boys | Slay Pillage Gerard |
| Gary Wise | Jonathan Sonne |
| Neil Reeves | Gerard Fabiano |
| Bob Maher, Jr. | Scott McCord |

Maher started his game with [Hypochondria](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypochondria), and a few turns later [Petravark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Petravark) took away the only island "The Great One" had. McCord took full advantage of the situation, playing two [Aven Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Flock) and destroying [Hypochondria](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypochondria) with [Aven Cloudchaser](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Cloudchaser). [Battlewise Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlewise+Aven) made the air even more crowded, and [Shieldmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shieldmage+Advocate) let him get past Maher's [Phantom Flock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Flock) for the win.

Wise used [Last Rites](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Last+Rites) to take away [Seton's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Desire) and [Gravegouger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravegouger) from Sonne, leaving him with [Sutured Ghoul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sutured+Ghoul) and lands. The game soon became a battle of Shades, as Sonne had the Nantuko variety while Wise had an unblockable Whispering one, as well as [Soul Scourge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Scourge). [Sutured Ghoul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sutured+Ghoul) came out a while later, giving Sonne a 6/6 monster. [Morgue Theft](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morgue+Theft) helped put Sonne ahead. Wise summoned [Filth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Filth), which was less impressive than usual since both of his creatures couldn't be blocked by Sonne anyway. The resulting attack required Wise to chump block to stay alive, and he scooped after drawing his next card.

Reeves had [Barbarian Bully](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbarian+Bully), [Skywing Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skywing+Aven), [Liberated Dwarf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liberated+Dwarf) and [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) to Fabiano's [Wormfang Drake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Drake) and [Soul Scourge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Scourge). Reeves eventually won the game, as his creatures were simply superior to Fabiano's. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptbos02/a887.jpg)Game 2 saw Maher summon [Mystic Familiar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+Familiar), [Shieldmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shieldmage+Advocate), [Possessed Nomad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Possessed+Nomad) and [Vigilant Sentry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigilant+Sentry). Once again, McCord used [Petravark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Petravark) to take away Maher's only island. Maher found another island, but McCord played a second [Petravark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Petravark), and also cast [Arcane Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcane+Teachings) on his first one. Maher was now stuck at three plains, while McCord built his forces with every passing turn. Things didn't improve much, and McCord was able to take the game and the match. 

Sonne played a turn two [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade), followed by a turn three [Gravegouger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravegouger). Wise used [Prismatic Strands](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Strands) and [Embolden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embolden) in the following combat exchanges, and both were taken out with a second [Gravegouger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravegouger). The Shade was dominating at this point, and Wise told his teammates it wasn't looking good. Sonne used [Sudden Strength](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sudden+Strength) to make things even worse. [Waste Away](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Waste+Away) killed the [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade), and Sonne summoned [Nullmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nullmage+Advocate). [Morbid Hunger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger) brought Wise down to two, and Sonne attacked with both of his creatures. [Fledgling Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Imp) blocked and traded with [Gravegouger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravegouger), and [Toxic Stench](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Toxic+Stench) made the Advocate one power. This let Wise survive the attack at one life, and put Strands and [Embolden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embolden) back in his graveyard. Wise untapped, and played [Stitch Together](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stitch+Together) to get back [Soul Scourge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Scourge). [Morgue Theft](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morgue+Theft) brought back [Gravegouger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravegouger), and it took [Prismatic Strands](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Strands). [Psychotic Haze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychotic+Haze) dealt the final point of damage, and gave Slay Pillage Gerard the match. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptbos02/a886.jpg)Fabiano opened Game 2 with [Hydromorph Guardian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hydromorph+Guardian) and [Elephant Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide), while Reeves had [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). [Nullmage Advocate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nullmage+Advocate) eventually destroyed the powerful enchantment, and [Krosan Archer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Archer) provided some defense. [Treetop Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Sentinel) gave Gerard a great blocker, but Reeves had three 2/3 creatures, and was able to attack through for the win.

**Final Result:** Courtney's Boys- 1 Slay Pillage Gerard- 2
