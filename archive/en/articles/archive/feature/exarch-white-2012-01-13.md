Exarch White
============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 13, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











TCGPlayer.com columnist Justin Vizaro is all about putting spins on established archetypes. This week, he looked at the white-blue [Moorland Haunt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moorland+Haunt) deck in Standard and puts his own spin on it. If you're looking for a fun, budget-friendly deck for your FNM tonight, give this new take on an established favorite a try!
