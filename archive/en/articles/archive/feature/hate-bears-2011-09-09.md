While combo decks got a lot of attention at this past weekend's Pro Tour Philadelphia (part of **Magic** Weekend Philadelphia), plenty of players opted for a different strategy. Check out Sascha Thomsen's take on the archetype known as "Hate Bears." The game plan? Beat opponents down with 2-power creatures that disrupt their game plans. [Aven Mindcensor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Mindcensor) makes it hard for Twelvepost players to search their libraries, [Qasali Pridemage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Qasali+Pridemage) causes all sorts of problems for players using artifacts or enchantments, [Leonin Arbiter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Arbiter) can shut off fetch lands... and all of that while continuing to attack. For more, visit [full coverage](/en/events/coverage/estratti-starts-modern-revolution-philly) of Pro Tour Philadelphia.
