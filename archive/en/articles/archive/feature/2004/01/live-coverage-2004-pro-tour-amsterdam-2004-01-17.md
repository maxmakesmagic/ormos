Live Coverage of 2004 Pro Tour Amsterdam
========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 17, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Nicolai Herzog is the Pro Tour - Amsterdam champion! After fifteen rounds of competition he and seven others got to draft one more time for all the marbles. Herzog was in first seat, and his prayers were answered when he saw [Molder Slug](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molder+Slug) staring back at him from his pack. He never looked back. .
