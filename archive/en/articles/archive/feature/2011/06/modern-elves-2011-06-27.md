Luis Scott-Vargas won Pro Tour Berlin playing a powerful Extended deck based around mana-producing Elves and the card draw spell [Glimpse of Nature](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimpse+of+Nature). When given the opportunity to help debut a brand new format, Modern, at the 2011 Community Cup, Luis tried his hand at the deck once more. It proved to be a good move, as Luis's deck, and the Community team, proved victorious! Read all about the new Modern format in the [Community Cup coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/magic-online-community-cup-thursday-2011-06-16).
