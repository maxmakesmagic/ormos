2014 Holiday Cube Card List
===========================



 Posted in 
 on December 8, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_davidwhitfield.jpg)
By David Whitfield











Like jolly old Saint Nick, the Holiday Cube has returned for another holiday season. Unlike Saint Nick, we don't keep the exact same eight, exhausted reindeer that we had last year in the queue. I'm sure Comet or Blitzen would be delighted if Sarkhan, the Dragonspeaker volunteered to guide Santa's sleigh for a night. Yes, the 2014 Holiday Cube is a different animal than the 2013 version that you know and love. Refer to the table below for a full Cube list and details on what's changed since the 2013 Holiday Cube!


Holiday Cube [events](http://wotc-mx1.bkom.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online-holiday-festival-2014-11-12#swissholidaycube) begin Wednesday, December 17, after downtime and run through Monday, January 5, at 10:00 a.m. Pacific (UTC-8).
