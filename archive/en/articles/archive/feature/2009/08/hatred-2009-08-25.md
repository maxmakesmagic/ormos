On first read, many players thought [Hatred](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hatred) was a junk rare. It didn't take long for people to realize that a first-turn [Carnophage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carnophage) followed by two [Dark Ritual](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Ritual)s into [Hatred](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hatred) meant a second-turn kill! [Suicide Black](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Suicide+Black), already a popular aggro deck of the day, quickly incorporated the windfall of a strategy into its main deck. In no time, players all over the world were trying to put together a playset of the powerful rare.
