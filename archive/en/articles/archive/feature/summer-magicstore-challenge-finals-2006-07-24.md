Summer of MagicStore Challenge Finals
=====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 24, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*Updated 2007-09-06*

### Store Challenge Final

### 

### Tournament Location

**Hilton Birmingham Metropole**  
National Exhibition Centre  
Birmingham  
B40 1PP  
[www.hilton.co.uk/birminghammet](http://www.hilton.co.uk/birminghammet)

### Tournament Schedule

**October 20, 2007**: Registration from 9:00 A.M – 10:00 A.M. Tournament begins at 10:45 A.M.  


### Entry Fee

FREE. Invitation only.

### Invitations

* [Store Champions](/en/articles/archive/feature/summer-magicstore-challenge-champions-2006-10-02)
* [Challenge Series Points](/en/articles/archive/feature/summer-magicstore-challenge-points-2006-10-02) (5+ points = invitation)

### Format

* Swiss Rounds: Standard
* Swiss Rounds: *Lorwyn* Booster Draft
* Playoff: Standard
* Decklists will be required.

### 
Tournament Length

* Swiss rounds - minutes per round
* Number of Swiss rounds based on attendance according to the following chart:




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Attendance** | **Number of Swiss rounds** |
| 64 or less | 6 |
| 65 - 128 | 7 |
| 129 - 256 | 8 |
| 257 or higher | 9 |

### Notes

* 16K, REL 3
* All Universal Tournament Rules and **Magic: the Gathering** Floor Rules are in effect for this event unless specifically overruled by information in this Format Document
* Players must bring pen, paper, card sleeves and appropriate counters.

### Prizes

* To be confirmed

* Please note that the invitation lists contains only those Store Champions and points awarded, from reported 8+ player tournaments only. If your name does not appear on the lists then please contact the tournament organiser to arrange for the tournament to be uploaded.
