Pro Tour Hall of Fame member Mike Turian was the gunslinger at the Denver, Colorado Prerelease for *Scars of Mirrodin*. His Standard deck of choice? Whitecraft—a White Weenie strategy powered by the new keyword metalcraft. Mike plays white's familiar bear-type creatures backed by equipment, but metalcraft allows him to explode onto the battlefield with cards like [Mox Opal](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Opal) and [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite). From there it's all downhill for his opponents!
