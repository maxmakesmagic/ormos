Alpha "Oops…" IV
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 15, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











A few cards in Alpha were printed with what turned out to be incorrect casting costs (see this old [Magic Arcana](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=mtgcom/arcana/38)), but one card was printed without a casting cost at all!

Check out the Alpha [Cyclopean Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cyclopean+Tomb) below. Nothing in the corner, not even a ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20210707095534im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless0.gif) like [Black Lotus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Lotus) had.

The cost -- ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20160830042801im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless4.gif) -- was added in the Beta printing.
