DAY 1 COVERAGE



More than 800 players have made the trek to Omaha to compete in the final Standard event of *Amonkhet.* Temur Marvel is certainly the favorite coming in, but word amongst the pro community is that the metagame may be open for other decks to make an impact as the field has narrowed its focus on Marvel and Ulamog, the Ceaseless Hunger.


We’ll find out by the end of the weekend if that’s the case, or if the Temur Marvel deck that won Grand Prix Montreal has what it takes to stand up to the scrutiny of being the top dog in Standard.


There are plenty of big names here to figure that out. From Reid Duke and Luis Scott-Vargas in the coverage booth to Hall of Famers Paul Reitzl, Eric Froehlich, and Owen Turtenwald in the field, the best minds in the game are here to close the door on *Amonkhet* Standard.


You can find coverage all weekend at [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic).
