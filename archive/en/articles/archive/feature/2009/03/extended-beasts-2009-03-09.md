Beasts aren't exactly a tribe known for the prowess in the Extended format, but Corbett Gray didn't let that stop him from Top 8ing a recent PTQ with the monsters. He took his deck all the way to the finals on the back of powerhouse creatures like [Chameleon Colossus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chameleon+Colossus) and [Ravenous Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth) as well as some sneaky hits like [Taurean Mauler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Taurean+Mauler). Can the deck remain good throughout the rest of the PTQ-Honolulu season? The world waits with baited breath!
