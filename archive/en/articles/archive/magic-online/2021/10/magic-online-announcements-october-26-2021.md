*Vintage Cube* October 2021 Update
----------------------------------

This time, we're looking at expanding the parts of the sandbox that include build-arounds and reactive cards. 2021 *Magic Online* Champions Showcase Season 2
-----------------------------------------------


The second *Magic Online* Champions Showcase of 2021 is almost here, featuring eight players battling for their share of $70,000 and the title of the 2021 Season 2 Showcase Champion!


The 2021 *Magic Online* Champions Showcase Season 2 will be streamed live beginning at 10 a.m. PT on October 30 at [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic).


You will find decklists for the event at the [2021 *Magic Online* Champions Showcase Season 2 event page](https://magic.gg/events/2021-magic-online-champions-showcase-season-2), with round-by-round standings and updates provided through the live broadcast.


For the more info on the event, make sure to check out the [viewers guide here](https://magic.gg/news/2021-magic-online-champions-showcase-season-2-viewers-guide).

Limited Qualifiers Saturday and Sunday
--------------------------------------


On Saturday, October 30, there will be an *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* Sealed Qualifier with a special start time of 1 a.m. PT (08:00 UTC). On Sunday, October 31, there will be another *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* Sealed Qualifier at 7 a.m. PT (14:00 UTC). The events cost 40 Season 3 QPs, and the winner will earn an invitation to the *Innistrad* Championship event on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021#ptq).




---

Limited Super Qualifier Saturday
--------------------------------


On Saturday, October 30, there will be an *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* Sealed Super Qualifier with a special start time of 2 p.m. PT (21:00 UTC). The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points (Season 3), 40 Event Tickets, or 400 Play Points. The top two finishers qualify for the *Innistrad* Championship on *MTG Arena*.


The normally scheduled Saturday Limited Preliminaries are cancelled. Learn more about Super Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021#sq).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. [Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format!
