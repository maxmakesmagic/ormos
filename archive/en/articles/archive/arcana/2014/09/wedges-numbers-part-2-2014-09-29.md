Wedges by the Numbers, Part 2
=============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 29, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






[A while back](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/arcana/wedges-numbers-2014-08-27), we looked at the (small) assortment of wedge cards available in *Magic* before the release of *Khans of Tarkir*. To say the choices were meager would be a bit of an understatement, as some wedges had as few as four cards to their name. Heck, a "robust" number was five.


But with the actual release of *Khans of Tarkir* this past week, those numbers have swelled considerably. So while we won't be showing off every single card in every wedge this time around, we can look at the constitution of the three color cards for each.

[Doran, the Siege Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower) (0/5) sort of. Next? [Karador, Ghost Chieftain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karador%2C+Ghost+Chieftain) (3/4)  
**Not an Ascendancy:** [Overgrown Estate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Estate)  
**Possible Commanders:** 5
