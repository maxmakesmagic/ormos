Pro Tour Guilds of Ravnica Top 8 Profiles
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 11, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff















[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/Arena_Banner_850x90_1.jpg)![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/Arena_Banner_300x90_0.jpg)![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/Arena_Banner_748x90_0.jpg)](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgarena?utm_source=MTGArena&utm_medium=Banner&utm_campaign=1358606)

After sixteen rounds, eight players remain in the running for glory, fortune, and the title of Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica* Champion! Learn more about them below.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptgrn/mok.jpg)


**Name**: Wilson Mok


**Age**: 27


**Hometown**: Vancouver, Canada


**Occupation**: Sales


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation**: Izzet?


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: :-(


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 8-1-1


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?** This!


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?** Opening a foil [Chrome Mox](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) in my very first booster pack!


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.** Elias Pettersson, of the Vancouver Canucks. Maybe Jeff Fung.


**If you win the Pro Tour, who is the first person you'll call?** My mom! Then my girlfriend!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptgrn/nielsen.jpg)


**Name**: Kasper Nielsen


**Age**: 31


**Hometown**: Copenhagen, Denmark


**Occupation:** Office manager


**Team**: Bolt the Bird


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation**: Boros


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Top 8 Grand Prix Utrecht 2017; second at Grand Prix Lille 2017 (team)


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 8-2


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?**


Getting healthy after a serious illness.


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


Playing the Grand Prix Lille finals versus longtime friend Chris Larsen.


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.** Chris Larsen.


**If you win the Pro Tour, who is the first person you'll call?**


My boss (my dad) to tell him I will need more free time to travel.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptgrn/watanabe.jpg)


**Name**: Yuuya Watanabe


**Age**: 29


**Hometown**: Tokyo, Japan


**Occupation**: Pro *Magic* player


**Team**: Musashi


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation**: Izzet


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Player of the Year; Rookie of the Year; won last World *Magic* Cup; won Players Championship in 2012; seven Grand Prix wins; and Hall of Fame


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 7-2-1


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?** None. All of me is made of *Magic*


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?** Winning the Players Championship


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.** Luis Scott-Vargas


**If you win the Pro Tour, who is the first person you'll call?** Azusa Nakano

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptgrn/tay.jpg)


**Name**: Tay Jun Hao


**Age**: 30


**Hometown**: Singapore


**Occupation**: Card store owner


**Team**: Grey Ogre Games


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation**: Boros?


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Two Grand Prix Top 8s


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 7-2-1


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?** Finishing in the Top 8 of the Junior National Chess Championship.


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?** Watching my teammate Top 8 Pro Tour *Dominaria* and then doing it myself here.


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.** My guys back home who are taking care of the store while I am away for Pro Tours and Grand Prix.


**If you win the Pro Tour, who is the first person you'll call?** My mum :)

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptgrn/dezani.jpg)


**Name**: Jérémy Dezani


**Age**: 28


**Hometown**: Paris/Tokyo


**Occupation**: *Magic* pro player


**Team**: Hareruya Sword


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation**: Boros


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Pro Tour *Theros* Champion; 14 Grand Prix Top 8s


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 7-2-1


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?** I survive to party with Kenji Tsumura and Tomoharu Saito.


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?** Winning Pro Tour Dublin. I didn't realize what I had done and it took me five years to Top 8 a Pro Tour again.


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.** Rafael Nadal and Raphaël Lévy


**If you win the Pro Tour, who is the first person you'll call?** My mum and my father

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptgrn/scottvargas.jpg)


**Name**: Luis Scott-Vargas


**Age**: 35


**Hometown**: Oakland, California


**Occupation**: Game Designer // ChannelFireball.com


**Team**: Team ChannelFireball


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation**: Dimir


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: Forming Team ChannelFireball. Making the Top 8 in a lot of tournaments.


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Standard Record**: 7-2-1


**What is your proudest non-*Magic* accomplishment?**


Helping build cool things and teach people (in various ways).


**What is your favorite *Magic* memory?**


Too many to count, but teaming with Paul Cheon and Ben Lundquist as the 2006 US National team is high on the list.


**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.**


My parents, for teaching me to pursue my passions.


**If you win the Pro Tour, who is the first person you'll call?**


United Airlines

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/ptgrn/elenbogen.jpg)


**Name**: Andrew Elenbogen


**Age**: 25


**Hometown**: Ann Arbor, Michigan


**Occupation**: Software developer


**Team**: Cardboard Live


**Ravnica Guild Affiliation**: Dimir


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**: 3.5 Grand Prix Top 8s (one 13-2 miss on tiebreakers)

Winning the Michigan High School Chess Team tournament in back-to-back years.

**Who is your hero? *Magic*-related or not.**


I'm not big on the concept of heroes.


**If you win the Pro Tour, who is the first person you'll call?**


My mom.
