Our final **Magic** Game Day deck this week comes from Saitama, Japan. Shun Ikeda played what looked like an ordinary Bant deck. Of course, when the opponent least expected it, [Time Warp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Warp) or [Savor the Moment](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savor+the+Moment) hit the stack and Ikeda happily took an extra turn while a hapless opponent could only watch in horror. Add to that the interaction of [Fertile Ground](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fertile+Ground) and [Garruk Wildspeaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Garruk+Wildspeaker)'s untap ability, and the "downside" of [Savor the Moment](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savor+the+Moment) didn't bring him down!
