Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on March 8, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke










After 15 rounds, the original field of 1,772 players has been whittled down to but eight people. These eight are the best of the best, the cream of the crop, the people … you really should know something about. If you don't, check out their profiles below!




---


Andreas Jonsson
===============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpliv15/t8-jonsson.jpg)


**Age:** 33


**Occupation:** Student/social worker


**Hometown:** Malmö, Sweden


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Top 8 at Grand Prix Birmingham 1998, Top 32 at Pro Tour Rome 1998.


**What colors was your sealed deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Jeskai, [Torrent Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrent+Elemental), 9-0.


**What colors was your first draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Abzan, [Daghatar the Adamant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daghatar+the+Adamant), 2-0-1.


**What colors was your second draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Blue-green-white, no good cards, 1-0-2.


**What's your favorite *Dragons of Tarkir* card previewed so far?** 


Ojutai's Command.




---


Kenneth Ellingsen
=================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpliv15/t8-ellingsen.jpg)


**Age:** 37


**Occupation:** Teacher


**Hometown:** Raufoss, Norway


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Won a PTQ, Top 16 at Grand Prix Moscow '00-'01.


**What colors was your sealed deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


7-2 with five-color. No card in particular, I just had to play all my best cards.


**What colors was your first draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


3-0 with Abzan. All my lifelink creatures were good against aggressive decks.


**What colors was your second draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


3-0 with Abzan. My 39 random cards to support [Mastery of the Unseen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mastery+of+the+Unseen) were great.


**What's your favorite *Dragons of Tarkir* card previewed so far?** 


Thunderbreak Regent.




---


Daniel Oppliger
===============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpliv15/t8-oppliger.jpg)


**Age:** 35


**Occupation:** Supermarket manager


**Hometown:** Lupfig, Switzerland


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Swiss National champion 2005, played in three Pro Tours.


**What colors was your sealed deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


7-2 with Sultai. Can't remember the cards.


**What colors was your first draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Blue-green with [Torrent Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrent+Elemental). 3-0.


**What colors was your second draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Blue-green with [Become Immense](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Become+Immense). 3-0.




---


Lukas Blohon
============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpliv15/t8-blohon.jpg)


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Tourist


**Hometown:** Prague, Czech Republic


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


One Pro Tour Top 8, three Grand Prix Top 8s.


**What colors was your sealed deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


I played Abzan with [Palace Siege](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Palace+Siege) and [Duneblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast), but I should have played Jeskai with two copies each of [Goblin Heelcutter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Heelcutter), [Act of Treason](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Act+of+Treason), and [Collateral Damage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collateral+Damage). Had [Dragon-Style Twins](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon-Style+Twins) and [Flying Crane Technique](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flying+Crane+Technique) too.


**What colors was your first draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Mardu. [Outpost Siege](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Outpost+Siege), [Butcher of the Horde](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher+of+the+Horde). 3-0.


**What colors was your second draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Red-blue. [Outpost Siege](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Outpost+Siege), [Ashcloud Phoenix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashcloud+Phoenix), three [Goblin Heelcutter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Heelcutter)s.


**What's your favorite *Dragons of Tarkir* card previewed so far?** 


Narset Transcendent.

===============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpliv15/t8-carvalho.jpg)


**Age:** 29


**Occupation:** Grand Prix Top 8er


**Hometown:** Portugal


**What colors was your sealed deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


7-2, black-white splashing blue for [Torrent Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrent+Elemental). Best card was [Torrent Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrent+Elemental).


**What colors was your first draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Temur with three [Goblin Heelcutter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Heelcutter)s. 3-0.


**What colors was your second draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Temur. [Pyrotechnics](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyrotechnics). 3-0.


**What's your favorite *Dragons of Tarkir* card previewed so far?** 


Dragonlord Silumgar.

Martin Dang
===========


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpliv15/t8-dang.jpg)


**Age:** 33


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Aarhus, Denmark


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Won a MOCs online for 2015, played in more than ten Pro Tours.


**What colors was your sealed deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


[Duneblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast), played Abzan, went 8-1.


**What colors was your first draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


2-1 with Jeskai, had two [Elite Scaleguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elite+Scaleguard).


**What colors was your second draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


3-0 with [Incremental Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Incremental+Growth), played white-blue-green.


**What's your favorite *Dragons of Tarkir* card previewed so far?** 


Narset Transcendet looks awesome.

================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpliv15/t8-simonot.jpg)


**Age:** 26


**Occupation:** Marketing


**Hometown:** Paris, France


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**

Black-green. [Temur Sabertooth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temur+Sabertooth). 3-0.


**What colors was your second draft deck, what was the best card in it, and what was your record?**


Black-green splashing blue for [Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise) which also was the best card.


**What's your favorite *Dragons of Tarkir* card previewed so far?** 


Narset Transcendent.
