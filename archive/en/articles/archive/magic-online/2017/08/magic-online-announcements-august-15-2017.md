**No Team Draft Super League tonight**


There will be no Team Draft Super League (TDSL) tonight, however TDSL will be back next week on August 22!


Team Draft Super League is a live streamed event Tuesday nights on our Twitch [channel](https://www.twitch.tv/magic). You can also watch past Team Draft Super Leagues on our YouTube [channel](https://www.youtube.com/user/wizardsmtg).

**Throwback Standard Gauntlet 6: Tribal *Lorwyn*****coming August 17**


Decklists and other details regarding Throwback Standard Gauntlet 6: Tribal *Lorwyn* are available [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/gauntlet-6-release-tribes-2017-08-15)! Throwback Standard Gauntlet 6: Tribal *Lorwyn* will begin on August 17 and run through August 23.  
  

More details about Throwback Standard Gauntlets can be found [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/flashback-favorites-and-throwback-standard-gauntlets-2016-12-15).

**Paul Cheon *Magic Online* Stream this Friday, August 18**


Watch [Paul Cheon](https://twitter.com/haumph?lang=en) stream *Magic Online* on our Twitch [channel](http://www.twitch.tv/magic) this Friday at 1 p.m. PT! Paul Cheon (aka [Haumph](https://www.twitch.tv/haumph)) will start off the stream with a HOU/AKH draft, playing against various Wizards of the Coast employees! You will definitely want to tune in for this fun and entertaining event.

***Magic Online* StandardRPTQ for Pro Tour*Ixalan*on Saturday, August 19**


A *Magic Online*Regional Pro Tour Qualifier (RPTQ) for Pro Tour*Ixalan*will take place on Saturday, August 19. These events are only open to players who qualified via in-store Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifier events – pre-registration is also required.


Additional details about Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers can be found [here.](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/regionalqualifiers)


[Top](#top)





---

***Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet* Sealed PTQ for Pro Tour *Rivals of Ixalan* on Sunday, August 20**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, August 20 at 7 a.m. PT. Qualify for this event by earning a PTQ Entry Token in a Preliminary.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).


[Top](#top)

***Magic Online* Format Challenges back this weekend**


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges introduced a new prize structure and recurring weekend events for Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper formats!


Events begin every Saturday at 8:00 a.m. PT, and run through Sunday. Check out [this page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-format-challenges) for further event details as well as the weekend schedule.


[Top](#top)

**No downtime tomorrow, August 16**


There will be no downtime tomorrow, August 16. The next two downtimes will be August 23 and September 20, both of which will be extended (3 a.m. PT/10:00 p.m. UTC).


Follow *Magic Online*'s [Twitter](https://twitter.com/MagicOnline) account for live updates during downtime.
