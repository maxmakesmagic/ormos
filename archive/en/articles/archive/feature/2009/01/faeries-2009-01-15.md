In honor of the start of the Pro Tour Qualifier-Honolulu season, Daily Decks is bringing you some of the hottest Extended decks all week long! American superstar Paul Cheon may have seen the twilight fall on his professional career at the 2008 World Championships, but he made sure to go out with a bang. Using this Wizards/Faeries hybrid he dominated the Extended portion and turned many a head with the innovative build (allegedly created with the help of wunderpros Gabriel Nassif and Mark Herberholz). To find an Extended PTQ for Honolulu near you, visit [this link](/en/node/640716).
