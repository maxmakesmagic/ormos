Decks of the Week: 2/22/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 22, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations to Tom Martell for leading Aristocrats to victory at [Pro Tour *Gatecrash*](/en/events/coverage/martell-calls-down-reckoning-pro-tour) this past weekend! He will be back for Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze* and you could face him! One way is to win at a Grand Prix—and tomorrow brings us back to Canada and Standard with [Grand Prix Québec City](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/event/grandprix/quebeccity13). If you plan to compete there, it behooves you to study Tom's Aristocrats deck as well as the decklists of all the Top 8 finishers. The other way to get to the Pro Tour is to win a PTQ. Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#dgm) and check out recent PTQ results to see what you might face! Good luck; have fun!
