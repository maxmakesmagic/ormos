Top 8 Players of Grand Prix Prague 2019
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 13, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After fifteen rounds, with 2,492 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/gppra19/t8_chabot.jpg)


**Name:** Yves Chabot


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Consultant


**Hometown:** Paris, France


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Grand Prix teammate of now MPL player Jean-Emmanuel Depraz.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Blue-Black Control splash white. Best card was [Death Denied](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Denied). 8-1.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Groundskeeper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Groundskeeper). Red-green with two [Seismic Assault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seismic+Assault) and four [Faithless Looting](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faithless+Looting).


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Green-White Auras with three [Phalanx Leader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phalanx+Leader). Best card was [Gods Willing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gods+Willing).




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/gppra19/t8_huschenbeth.jpg)


**Name:** Arne Huschenbeth


**Age:** 21


**Occupation:** *Magic*


**Hometown:** Berlin, Germany


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Five Grand Prix Top 8s. Beating Alex Kotlenko in an FNM Draft.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Karn Liberated](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karn+Liberated). Sultai. I went 9-0. I boarded into red-green a lot. Red-green didn't drop a game, probably should have played red-green.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Unburial Rites](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unburial+Rites). White-Black Reanimator. 2-1.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Laboratory Maniac](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Laboratory+Maniac). Green-blue splashing red and white. 2-0-1.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/gppra19/t8_dehaan.jpg)


**Name:** Rob de Haan


**Age:** 31


**Occupation:** Aftersales


**Hometown:** Zwolle, Netherlands


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Three Grand Prix Top 32s, Day 2 at Pro Tour San Diego, Nationals Top 8.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Green-blue-black splash white. I won several games on [Rise from the Tides](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rise+from+the+Tides) with [Temporal Manipulation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temporal+Manipulation).


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-Red Trainwreck. Managed to 2-1 with [Rogue's Passage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rogue%27s+Passage).


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Green-Blue Madness (thanks LSV). 3-0. Splashed [Reanimate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reanimate) for the win.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/gppra19/t8_vinci.jpg)


**Name:** Stefano Vinci


**Age:** 26


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Turin, Italy


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Played two Pro Tours.


**What was the best card in your Sealed Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


I had a solid black-green splashing white and blue. Best card: [Shriekmaw](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shriekmaw).


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-Red Madness, three [Fiery Temper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper), 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Red-White Aggro, three [Warleader's Helix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warleader%27s+Helix), 2-1.

**Name:** Anton Morgenstern

**Occupation:** Poker


**Hometown:** Berlin, Germany

Team captain Malta 2018.

**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Sovereigns of Lost Alara](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sovereigns+of+Lost+Alara). Bant. 2-1.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


White-blue. 3-0. [Miraculous Recovery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Miraculous+Recovery).

**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Product owner

3-0. Red-green splashing white and black. [Spider Spawning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spider+Spawning).


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


1-1-1. Blue-black. [Shriekmaw](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shriekmaw).
