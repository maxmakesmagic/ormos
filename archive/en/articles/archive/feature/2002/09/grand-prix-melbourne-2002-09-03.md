Grand Prix - Melbourne
======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 3, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The Magic: The Gathering Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro Tour events.

**TOURNAMENT FORMAT AND SCHEDULE**  
 The format for Grand Prix–Melbourne is *Onslaught* block Limited, with Day 2 Rochester Draft. All DCI Limited rules and **Magic** Floor Rules will be enforced.

**Friday, November 22:** Registration from 4:00-9:00 P.M.  
**Saturday, November 23:** Late registration from 8:00-9:00 A.M.  
 Tournament begins at 9:30 A.M. The Top 64 players advance to competition on Sunday  
**Sunday, November 24:** The Top 64 players begin play at 9:00 A.M. Finals will immediately follow.

**TOURNAMENT LOCATION**  
 The Melbourne Convention Centre  
 Cnr of Flinders & Spencer Streets  
 Melbourne Victoria 3000

**ENTRY FEE**  
 AU$40 Preregistration on Friday  
 AU$45 Late Registration Saturday Morning

**TOURNAMENT**

 The main tournament at Grand Prix-Melbourne is open to **Magic** players of all ages.

*If the player who wins an invitation to Pro Tour–Chicago at Grand Prix–Melbourne has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be passed down to the next-best finisher. Previous qualification may occur through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI™ player (valid on the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*

**Amateur Status**  
 A player is considered to have amateur status if he or she has 0 lifetime pro points. If a player has ever been awarded any points from a Grand Prix, Pro Tour, or World Championship event, that player no longer has amateur status, may not participate in the Amateur Championships or Junior Super Series tournaments, and is not eligible for amateur awards at Grand Prix or other tournaments.

**Tournament Format**  
 The format for Grand Prix - Melbourne is *Onslaught™* block Limited, with Day 2 Rochester Draft. All DCI™ Limited rules, **Magic**® Floor Rules announced on September 1, 2002, and all subsequent updates will be enforced. 

**Tournament Structure**  
 The structure for Grand Prix - Melbourne is modified Swiss (50-minute rounds, with the number of rounds determined by the number of participants), with the Top 64 players advancing to competition on Sunday for prizes and invitations.

### Grand Prix Melbourne – Side Event Schedule


Unless explicitly stated, all side events at GP Melbourne have a standard price arrangement.  
 Prices are:


Draft: $15  
 Constructed: $10  
 Sealed: $30


Prize structure for most events depends on the number of players and will be announced at the start of each event.


All events are sanctioned unless an exception is clearly stated.


### Friday 22 November


3 PM to 11 PM (Last sealed will start between 7 and 8 PM).


**GP Trials**. All trials will be *Onslaught* sealed. All trials will be single elimination with prizes for top 16. Top 4 get a box and top 2 receive a 3rd round bye for GP Melbourne.


Each trial will start as soon as there is 32 players.


**2 and 3 Bye Player Practice Events**.: These events will be open only to players who have 2 or 3 byes. Each will be *Onslaught* sealed with a minimum of three rounds. Minimum 8 players, maximum of 32. Prizes determined by numbers. 


**Drafts on Request**.: Anytime there are 8 players ready there will be a draft. Signup sheets will be located at the side event area. 6 boosters for 1st, 3 for 2nd.


### Saturday 23 November


10 AM  
**Metagame Sealed**.: This is a limited event where each player receives 2 x 7th Ed boosters, 1 *Odyssey* booster, 1 *Torment* booster, 1 *Judgment* booster and 1 *Onslaught* booster. 1st place wins a box. Prizes for top half of the field.


12 Noon  
**Type II.**: *Onslaught* legal. Prizes for all players.


2 PM  
**Sealed Two Headed Giant**. (Not sanctioned): Each team gets 2 *Onslaught* starters and 1 booster each of *Odyssey*, *Torment*, *Judgment* and 7th Ed. Mix in 40 lifepoints and spells going everywhere and you have the mayhem that is two headed giant. Team cost is $60. Prizes for top half of the field.


4 PM  
**Masques Block Draft**.: Full block draft with one pack each of Mercadian Masques, Nemesis and Prophecy.


20 Minutes after final round  
**Type 1**. Get out all the old cards. 1st will get a box if there is 12 or more players. Prizes for top half of the field.


20 Minutes after final round  
***Onslaught* mini master**. (Not sanctioned) Single elimination Booster draft. If you win you get all your opponents cards and then get to remake your deck for the next round. Winner takes all.


**Drafts on Request**.: Anytime there are 8 players ready there will be a draft. Signup sheets will be located at the side event area. 6 boosters for 1st, 3 for 2nd.


### Sunday 24 November


10 AM  
***Onslaught* Backdraft**. (Not sanctioned): Draft the worst deck you can, as this is what your opponent will use. At the start of each match you give your opponent the cards you drafted and they make a deck. You make a deck from what they drafted and play with that. Either 8 or 16 players. 1st gets 16 or 32 boosters, depending on numbers.


10AM  
**Type II.**: *Onslaught* legal. Prizes for all players.


12 Noon  
**PTQ – Pro Tour Chicago, *Onslaught* Sealed with draft final**. Normal sealed PTQ with a top 8 rochester draft final. 1st place wins $US375 and an invite to PT Chicago. 2nd place wins a box.


3.30 PM  
***Onslaught* Team Sealed.**: Get to do the first *Onslaught* team sealed. Each team of 3 gets 2 *Onslaught* starters and 4 boosters. Modified double elimination. 1st place gets 3 boxes, 2nd get 1.5 boxes. Prizes for top 3rd of the field. Cost is $80 per team.


**Drafts on Request**.: Anytime there are 8 players ready there will be a draft. Signup sheets will be located at the side event area. 6 boosters for 1st, 3 for 2nd.
