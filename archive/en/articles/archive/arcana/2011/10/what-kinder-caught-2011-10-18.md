What the Kinder Caught
======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 18, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Would you like to see something neat about [Kindercatch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kindercatch)? If not, stop now! Look away, I implore you! Because if you stay, you may never see [Kindercatch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kindercatch) the same way again!


Okay, now that we've gotten rid of the timid and weak of mind, we can continue. Here's [Kindercatch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kindercatch):


![Kindercatch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Kindercatch)  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/822_kindercatch.jpg)[Kindercatch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kindercatch) | Art by [Terese Nielsen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5B%22Terese+Nielsen%22%5D)


That's pretty cool, right? Well, let's zoom in and take a closer look.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/822_kindercatch_zoomin_2.jpg)  
Oh, hey! A bag full of children! Creepy! However, that's not what we're here to look at. Let's try again.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/822_kindercatch_zoomin_1.jpg)  
That puppet ... it looks somehow familiar, doesn't it? If not, Click here to see it in another context.


![Creepy Doll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Creepy+Doll)  
No, not the one in the foreground with the scissors. The one in the back. The marionette.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/822_creepydoll.jpg)[Creepy Doll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Creepy+Doll) | Art by [Matt Stewart](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5B%22Matt+Stewart%22%5D)


Wow!
