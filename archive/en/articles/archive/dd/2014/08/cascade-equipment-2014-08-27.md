A Cascade of Equipment
======================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on August 27, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 





[Stoneforge Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic), [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), and [Force of Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will) have been seen together for many years, but today's deck puts a heavy spin on the classic archetype. Paul Lynch was able to take his take on the archetype to a 4th-place finish at the [StarCityGames Legacy Open](http://www.starcitygames.com/events/230814_washingtondc.html) in Washington DC.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Stoneforge Mystic)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge Mystic)
The first major difference was incorporating [Shardless Agent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shardless+Agent) and [Ancestral Vision](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancestral+Vision) into the deck. The cards in this deck are selected to be powerful at all stages of the game, so [Shardless Agent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shardless+Agent) can be cast liberally. The countermagic in the main deck is only [Force of Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will) and a [Misdirection](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misdirection), both of which cannot be cascaded into by [Shardless Agent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shardless+Agent).


The other major change is the inclusion of the [Sword of the Meek](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+the+Meek) and [Thopter Foundry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thopter+Foundry) combo. When combined, a single mana turns into gaining 1 life and making a 1/1 flier, and this can be done repeatedly. What makes this function well in the deck is that [Stoneforge Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic) can search up [Sword of the Meek](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+the+Meek)â€”being an Equipment comes in handy!
