Limited PTQ Friday
------------------


On Friday, January 24, there will be a Limited PTQ at a special start time of 1 p.m. PT! The set will be *Theros Beyond Death*, and the event will cost 1 Red Limited PTQ Token. You can also join with 40 of the new year's [MOCS Qualifier Points](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020). The winner qualifies for their choice of events in Series 2 of the [2020 Players Tour](https://magic.gg/players-tour) in May.


Learn more about Players Tour Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-players-tour-qualifiers#limitedprelims).

Showcase Challenge Weekend
--------------------------


Players looking to prove their mettle against the best players in *Magic Online* will want to use their QPs to enter *Magic Online* Champions Showcase (MOCS) events, starting with the Showcase Challenges this weekend. These MOCS events are a set of linked events designed to highlight the best players on *Magic Online* each season, culminating in the Champions Showcase and its prize pool of $70,000 and a Players Tour Finals invitation.


First up on Saturday, we have the Standard (6 a.m. PT), Modern (8 a.m. PT), and *Theros Beyond Death* Sealed (9 a.m. PT) Showcase Challenges. Then on Sunday, we finish off with the Pioneer (6 a.m. PT), Legacy (8 a.m. PT), and Pauper (10 a.m. PT) Showcase Challenges. Each of these events require 40 QPs to enter. The Top 8 finishers from each event will earn a qualification token to enter the appropriate [Showcase Qualifier](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#sq).


Learn more about Showcase Challenges events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#schallenges).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Back Next Weekend

Build Notes
-----------


Standard, Limited | Issues with [Furious Rise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Furious+Rise) not allowing players to cast cards exiled by it have been corrected.


Standard, Limited | [Whirlwind Denial](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whirlwind+Denial) now asks players to pay for spells consistently in the same order.


Standard, Limited | Protection from specific mana costs, as seen on cards such as Haktos, the Unscarred, now properly interact with X spells such as [Purphoros's Intervention](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purphoros%27s+Intervention).


Standard, Limited | Issues with permanents that are copies of [Bronzehide Lion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bronzehide+Lion) returning to the battlefield have been fixed.


Limited | Creatures enchanted with [Heliod's Punishment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heliod%27s+Punishment) are now granted the correct blue rules text.


Pioneer, Cube, Commander | Cards taken hostage by [Hostage Taker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hostage+Taker) can now be cast.


Pauper | Cards are now correctly flagged as legal or banned for Pauper.
