Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists
==================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX TAIPEI 2016](/en/events/coverage/gptai16)
 on June 25, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim












 Despite the exciting tourist attractions and enticing night markets, we're all Magic players and what we really want to do is to play some *Magic*!



Yesterday, players attended the Grand Prix Trials to earn a couple of byes, and five players earned the coveted prize! To sweeten things a little, they also received complimentary Sleep-in Special, which allowed them to show up at the tournament venue a little later! You know what they say about beauty sleep. It's the closest thing there is to a [Fountain of Youth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fountain+of+Youth)!


From the looks of it, Human variants continue to be popular, while Green-White Tokens and 4-Color Rites won the other two Trials. Will these patterns conform to expectations in the main event?
