Looking for something new to try out at your next Standard tournament? Give William Cavaglieri's Mono-White Tokens list a try! The powerful deck relies on a host of solid white creatures to give it an edge against decks like Grixis and Jund. William managed to take the deck to a Top 8 at the 2009 World Championships. You can read more about his performance as well as the rest of the event in the official [Worlds 2009 event coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12).
