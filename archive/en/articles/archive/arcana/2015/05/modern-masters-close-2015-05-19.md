Modern Masters Close Up
=======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 19, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Bio_Yichao.jpg)
By Michael Yichao




 Yichao is a writer of words for plays, television, theme parks, and—most recently—Magic: The Gathering. He loves Cube Draft and corgis. 






Sure, you're probably familiar with some of the big, splashy, mythic rares of *Modern Masters 2015 Edition*. After all, who isn't excited about [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) or [Dark Confidant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant)? But how well do you know the commons of *Modern Masters 2015 Edition*? Below is a closer look at the art from some noteworthy commons. (In some cases, it's a REALLY close look!) Can you name them just from the art?



1. This little guy isn't so little, but he's *common*.


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_zoomed_UlamogsCrusher.jpg)





---

1. If you look closely, you can see the guild these guys are from! That gives a hint about the ability found on this card, which might give a hint of its name….


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_zoomed_ConclavePhalanx.jpg)





---

1. Did you ever notice this poor little guy? He probably was just minding his business when that rude giant Keldon came a-smashing through!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_zoomed_BruteForce.jpg)





---

1. I'm worried for her safety…mostly because I can't tell how gravity is working here.


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_zoomed_FieryFall.jpg)

**Answers:**



> 
> 1. [Ulamog's Crusher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ulamog%27s+Crusher)
> 2. [Conclave Phalanx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conclave+Phalanx)
> 3. [Brute Force](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brute+Force)
> 4. [Fiery Fall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Fall)
> 





---

How many did you know? If you didn't do so hot, no worries! You're sure to get to know these commons much better as you draft or played in sealed events with *Modern Masters 2015 Edition*. And you're sure to know them intimately if you play in [*Modern Masters* Weekend](http://magic.wizards.com/en/game-info/products/card-set-archive/modern-masters-2015-edition)!
