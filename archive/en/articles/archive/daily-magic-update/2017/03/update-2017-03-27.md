The March 27, 2017 Update
=========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on March 27, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Jund Midrange](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/displaydeck.php?DeckID=112468)

When in doubt, Jund 'em out.


James Rector did just that at a StarCityGames.com Invitational Qualifier where he finished 6th with a grindy Jund deck in Standard that was reminiscent of things we've seen in the not-too-distant past. [Goblin Dark-Dwellers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Dark-Dwellers) does a lot of the heavy lifting in this deck, flashing back [Unlicensed Disintegration](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unlicensed+Disintegration), [To the Slaughter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=To+the+Slaughter), [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push), or the underrated [Pulse of Murasa](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulse+of+Murasa).


You haven't lived until you've [Pulse of Murasa](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulse+of+Murasa)-ed back a [Goblin Dark-Dwellers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Dark-Dwellers) which then gets to [Pulse of Murasa](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulse+of+Murasa)-ed again. It's a cascade of card advantage and life that's hard to come back from.
