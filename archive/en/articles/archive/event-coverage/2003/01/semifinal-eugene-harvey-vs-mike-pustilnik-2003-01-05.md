Semifinal: Eugene Harvey vs. Mike Pustilnik
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 5, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_benbleiweiss.jpg)
By Ben Bleiweiss











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a925.jpg)Many players joked before the tournament that straight-laced Mike Pustilnik would be a shoo-in to win GP New Orleans, as he would be apt to party less than many of the other more rambunctious Magic players. This joke turned into reality as a well-rested Mikey P slept off the after effects of a cold with good food and early bedtimes to cruise to the top of the field at the end of the Swiss rounds. By contrast, Eugene Harvey arrived in New Orleans and immediately began partying. He hadn't let up a bit, going out each night and living La Vida Loca. In a contest featuring these two Grand Prix champions, would the laid-back Mike Pustilnik emerge victorious, or would party animals extraordinaire Eugene Harvey two-fist his way to the finals?

### Game 1

The first few turns came and went fairly uneventfully, with each of the control players dropping lands and casting minor card-drawing spells to thin through their decks. Eugene stalled on lands first (at five), and attempted to resolve a [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), but Mikey P had [Circular Logic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic) to stop Eugene from digging for more lands. Pustilnik couldn't capitalize that turn, but on the following turn he cycled two [Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire)/[Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ice) on Harvey's lands and resolved [Morphling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morphling) through a lone [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell).

Eugene cast a main-phase [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), which Mike [Disrupt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disrupt)ed to stifle Eugene's mana further. This netted two [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)s and a [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), and allowed CMU based Harvey to discard [Wonder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) from his now eight-card hand at end of turn.

Mike swung for five with [Morphling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morphling), bringing the life totals to 19-14 in Mike's favor (the extra point on each side being due to sacrificed [Polluted Delta](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta)s). Eugene drew and attempted to pass the turn. With plenty of mana to spare, Mike dug into his sideboard with [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), and attempted to cast the five cost spell after untapping. Eugene responded with [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell). Mike cast [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) targeting the [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell). Eugene then cast [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), and was given the choice of [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak) or three [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)s + [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge). Eugene chose the [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak), and countered back on the [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell), winning the war and sending [Sunder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunder) helplessly to the graveyard. Mike got [Morphling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morphling) in for another five, bringing Harvey down to nine.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a927.jpg)Eugene untapped, and then quickly retapped all eight of his lands, floated a black and a blue, cast [Upheaval](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval), and then dropped a land and [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog). With seven cards in hand and nineteen cards in graveyard, the game was over and Mikey P graciously conceded.

### Game 2

On the second turn, Eugene plopped down [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), which was promptly counterspelled. On turn third turn, Eugene plopped down [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), which was promptly counterspelled.  
 On the fourth turn, Mike declined to play a fourth land, though not voluntarily. Eugene simply played land, which gave Pustilnik the opportunity to cast [Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire)/[Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ice) and [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), which finally found him more land. He dropped [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) successfully, and Eugene still had no plays.

Looking to dig deeper into his deck, Mike tapped two blue into his pool and returned two [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)s to [Gush](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gush). With this mana he fuelled [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), volunteering [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) for front line duty. After attacking with his creature, Mike passed the turn, but was met with the very same spell from Eugene. Luckily, the spell turned up four lands and a [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), so Eugene ended up settling for an [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) and a [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm).

After another turn of nothing, Eugene cast an end-of-turn [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) for two, cast [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), put his two worst cards on top of deck, used a [Polluted Delta](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta) to shuffle up the whole shebang, and then cast a third [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge). On his own turn, Eugene played a land, and went to discard. Mike attempted to cast [Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire)/[Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ice), which allowed Harvey to kill [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) with [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother). 

Another turn went by uneventfully, with Mike casting [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) and Eugene's attempt at [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) meeting with [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell). Mike found a fifth land the following turn, and Eugene found yet another [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), a [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), and ten cards in hand. By the end of his turn, Eugene's discard pile towered higher than the remainder of his deck, and his land count dominated by a score of nine to six. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a928.jpg)This time, the other side of the jungle resolved [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction). [Sunder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunder) went away, two lands, [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) and [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) came to play. Confusion briefly ensued about the word "Discard", but Mike quickly realized that Eugene was asking to discard, not commanding the same of him.

At the end of Pustilnik's turn, Eugene finally moved towards the big 'it' by grabbing [Mana Short](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Short) from his sideboard via [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) and casting his millionth [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) of the game. This time the split ended up pretty dire, with Eugene getting multiple [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell)s instead of a fourth [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) plus a [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak). Mike joined in the infinite [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) fest adding [Misdirection](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misdirection) to his hand. 

The fun finally began. Eugene cast [Mana Short](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Short), which Mike attempted to misdirect. Eugene [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell)ed the [Misdirection](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misdirection), as Pustilnik added two to his pool, [Gush](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gush)ed, and cast [Circular Logic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic) on the [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell). Eugene tried to [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) the [Circular Logic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic), as Mike floated yet another blue and [Gush](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gush)ed for a second time. He drew nothing, and as Eugene went to discard [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) and [Upheaval](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval) as his eighth and ninth cards, Mike laughed and extended his hand.

**Final Result:** Eugene Harvey 2 Mike Pustilnik 0
