Huang's Win Elementary at Eternal Weekend!
==========================================




Bob Huang managed his information well to defeat Akash Naidu in the finals of the 2015 Legacy Championship.
















2015 Legacy Championship
------------------------




* [COVERAGE](#tabs-0)


COVERAGE



From the 744 players that started the Eternal Weekend with Legacy, Bob Huang remained standing in the end to become the 2015 Legacy Champion. Piloting a powerful Four-Color Delver of Secrets deck through plenty similar decks in the Top 8, it took patience and planning to overwhelm Akash Naidu's potent Omni-Tell deck in the finals.


While the Top 8 was filled with several Legacy's staples - Lands using Life from the Loam, Delver of Secrets of many colors, and even Menfolk making a splash - the real standout was Jeskai Splinter Twin jumping from Modern into another Eternal scene. Max Ansbro may have fallen in the quarterfinals, but his performance rallying from a first round loss all the way into the Top 8 will stand out for the thousands of Moderns fans that are suddenly interested in another format. Another note from the weekend is that Stoneforge Mystic was absent from the Top 8, making it clear Legacy is moving in new directions.


And wherever the format goes, the 2015 Legacy Champion will follow: Congratulations again to Bob Huang, winner of the 2015 Legacy Championship at Eternal Weekend!
