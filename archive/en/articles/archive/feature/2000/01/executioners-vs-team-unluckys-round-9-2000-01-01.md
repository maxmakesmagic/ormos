Executioners vs. Team Unluckys - Round 9
========================================



 Posted in **Feature**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












**Team Executioners is a top Japanese team, comprised of:**


Satoshi Nakamura (1998 APAC Champion, 1996 Japanese National Team, regular Pro Tour player)


Koichiro Maki (2000 Magic Invitational competitor, highly rated APAC player)


Kenichi Fujita (Grand Prix Taipei 1998 winner, Grand Prix Tokyo winner, 1999 Japanese National Team, regarded as one of the best drafters in Japan)


**During the Rochester Draft portion of round 9, Executioners had the following matchups:**


**Player A:** Satoshi Nakamura (blue/black) versus Osamu Fujita (green/red) Result: 1-2 (Nakamura loses)


**Player B:** Koichiro Maki (blue/white) versus Ayumi Hidaka (blue/white) Result: 0-1-1 (Maki loses)


**Player C:** Kenichi Fujita (green/black) versus Takayuki Nagaoka (green/black/red) Result: 2-1 (Fujita wins)


[View a mini-movie of Executioners versus Team Unluckys.](http://archive.wizards.com/sideboard/gpnagoya00/images/exec_movie9.MPG)
