[Terastodon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terastodon) is a fascinating new rare from *Worldwake* that players have initially been excited about, but have struggled to find a place for in their decks. Former Player of the Year Tomoharu Saito had no problems, swapping the fatty into his [Hypergenesis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypergenesis) combo deck, then taking the deck to the Top 8 of Grand Prix–Oakland. When he plopped the 9/9 onto the battlefield, he usually blew up his own lands, giving him a whopping 18 power worth of creatures! After all, once he had gone off, he no longer needed his lands. Read the full coverage from the event [here](/en/events/coverage/g%C3%B6rtzen-goes-all-way-san-diego).
