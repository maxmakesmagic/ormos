Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Standard White-Blue God-Pharaoh's Gift](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/standard-ptq-2017-10-29)

We've come almost full-circle with this [God-Pharaoh's Gift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=God-Pharaoh%27s+Gift) thing, as the latest variations ditch trying to search it up with [Gate to the Afterlife](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gate+to+the+Afterlife) and go straight to [Refurbish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Refurbish)-ing it out of the graveyard. That lets the deck play fewer enablers ([Minister of Inquiries](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Minister+of+Inquiries) is no one's idea of a good *Magic* card) and more—[Sacred Cat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sacred+Cat)s? You know, for a 1/1 lifelink creature, [Sacred Cat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sacred+Cat) sure is seeing a lot of play these days between this and token strategies.
