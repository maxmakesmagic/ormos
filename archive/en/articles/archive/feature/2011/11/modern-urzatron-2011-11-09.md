Modern Urzatron
===============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 9, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











With [Cloudpost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudpost) out of the Modern picture, some players are turning to another way to achieve fast mana: the Urzatron! Francis Toussaint of Manadeprived.com [wrote about](http://manadeprived.com/2011/10/fran-tic-search-ub-urzatron-in-modern.html) his take on the archetype. Could this be a major player in Modern going forward? With the Modern segment of the [World Championships](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds) only two weeks away, stay tuned to **DailyMTG.com** to find out!
