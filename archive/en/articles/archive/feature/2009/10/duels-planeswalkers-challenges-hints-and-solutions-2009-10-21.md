**Hint #1:**

**Double Strike**
  
 Attacking and blocking creatures that have first strike and double strike deal combat damage before other creatures. Then, all creatures except those with first strike deal combat damage.


Creatures that have double strike deal combat damage twice each combat.


It's possible for a creature to be destroyed before it can deal its combat damage.




**Hint #2:**

Double Strike also doubles the effect of creature enhancements.

**Solution:**

1. Tap six [Plains](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains) for mana. Play [Angelic Blessing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelic+Blessing) targeting [Skyhunter Skirmisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyhunter+Skirmisher). Play another [Angelic Blessing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelic+Blessing) targeting [Skyhunter Skirmisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyhunter+Skirmisher).
2. Attack with [Skyhunter Skirmisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyhunter+Skirmisher). It's 7/7 and has flying and double strike. It deals 14 damage to your opponent.


### Challenge #2 – Elspeth vs. Chandra*Hands of Flame Challenge*

*You've been playing a fevered game against Elspeth, and it's all come down to this. Although you've knocked Elspeth down to just 1 life, you're in grave danger of losing next turn thanks to the [Serra Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serra+Angel) that's been taking big chunks out of your life total and the [Voice of All](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+All) with protection from red that just joined it. It's your turn, and you've already drawn your card. How can you win the game?*

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/digital/dotp/EN/dotpchallenges_2.jpg)  
**Hint #1:**

Sometimes a creature's abilities are more important than their power and toughness.

**Hint #2:**

A creature under your control, even temporarily, uses his abilities to benefit you – not his owner.

**Solution:**

1. Tap three [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain)s and play [Threaten](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Threaten) targeting [Soul Warden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Warden). You gain control of the [Soul Warden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Warden), and it gains haste.
2. Tap one [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) and play [Raging Goblin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin). Since you now control [Soul Warden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Warden), you gain 1 life. (It's not important that you gain the life – but it's very important that Elspeth doesn't!)
3. Attack with [Raging Goblin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin), [Soul Warden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Warden), and [Earth Elemental](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earth+Elemental). Dr. Wombat can block only two of them, meaning that at least one will be unblocked and deal 1 damage to Elspeth. You win!

**Hint #1:**

**Trample**
  
 If a creature with trample would deal enough combat damage to its blockers to destroy them, it deals the rest of its damage to the defending player.

**Hint #2:**

Put all your power behind the point of least resistance.

**Solution:**

1. Tap 1 [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) for mana and play [Elvish Piper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Piper)'s ability to put [Roughshod Mentor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roughshod+Mentor) into play.
2. Attack with all 3 [Grizzly Bears](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grizzly+Bears).
3. The creature that is blocked by anything except the enchanted [Wall of Wood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Wood) (ie civic wayfinders) is [Giant Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Giant+Growth)ed 3 times and deals lethal damage

7. Your opponent is dealt 2 damage from [Abyssal Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abyssal+Specter), 8 damage from [Crowd of Cinders](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crowd+of+Cinders), and 1 damage from [Drudge Skeletons](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drudge+Skeletons). She's at 4.
8. [Abyssal Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abyssal+Specter)'s ability causes your opponent to discard a card.
9. Each [Megrim](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Megrim) triggers and deals 2 damage to your opponent. She's at 0.

[Lys Alana Huntmaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lys+Alana+Huntmaster)'s ability triggers and you get an Elf token.
3. Tap two [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest)s for mana. Play [Elvish Warrior](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Warrior).
