Top 8 Profile - Jens Thoren
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPHEL00/163.jpg)**Name:** Jens Thorén  
**Age:** 20  
**Location:** Umeå, Sweden  
**Occupation:** Umeå, computer stuff  
**Played Magic since:** Ice Age  
**Best accomplishments:** 43rd 2000 Pro Tour New York, 17th 2000 Team Pro Tour New York, 20th Grand Prix Stockholm



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Helsinki?**  

A: Not very far from home.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Top 32, I wanted to win money.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: Good deck day one, less mana screws than my opponents day two.



**Q: What do you think about the formats used in this tournament, and what was your strategy?**  

A: I think the formats are good, draft B, U, R or G, W, U.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Good tiebreakers.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: It's fun to finally go good enough to make top 8. Means I will be continue playing.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: Dominik wins.
