Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX PARIS 2015](/en/events/coverage/gppar15)
 on May 10, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Through fifteen rounds, the original field of 1,426 players had been reduced to a mere eight who would now duke it out in the Top 8—for the fame, the glory, and potentially the trophy. Time to learn a bit more about each of those eight!




---


Christian Hauck
===============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/t8_hauck.jpg)


**Age:** 30  
**Hometown:** Mutterstadt, Germany  
**Occupation:** Controller  
**Favorite dinosaur:** Stegosaurus which kinda looks like [Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino)  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8 at Grand Prix Barcelona, Top 8 at Nationals 2010.


**What deck did you play and why did you choose it?**  

Abzan Aggro, the most solid choice in a wide-open field.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

Cut one [Hero's Downfall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero%27s+Downfall) for the fourth [Abzan Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abzan+Charm) perhaps.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  
[Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino), and after sideboarding, [Den Protector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Den+Protector).




---


Antonio Del Moral León
======================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/t8_delmoralleon.jpg)


**Age:** 20  
**Hometown:** Córdoba, Spain  
**Occupation:** *Magic* player  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Won a Pro Tour, won a MOCS, cash at various Grand Prix.


**What deck did you play and why did you choose it?**  

Abzan Control. I think it's good in the metagame.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

None.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  
[Elspeth, Sun's Champion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Sun%27s+Champion)




---


Hugo Diniz
==========


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/t8_diniz.jpg)


**Age:** 25  
**Hometown:** Funchal, Madeira, Portugal  
**Occupation:** Nephrology Resident at Hospital in São João  
**Favorite dinosaur:** [Deathmist Raptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathmist+Raptor)  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 23rd at the 2014 World *Magic* Cup with Team Portugal. 0-6 at one WMCQ before qualifying.


**What deck did you play and why did you choose it?**  

Green Devotion with a splash of red. I was going to play Abzan Control until Thursday, when I lost six matches in a row and decided to switch to this because of its good matchups against green-white decks like Abzan, Bant, et cetera.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

I like the main deck, maybe play a third [Den Protector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Den+Protector) or [Arc Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arc+Lightning) in the sideboard.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  

Vespa Rainha! Or, in English, [Hornet Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Queen)

Tristan Pölzl
=============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/t8_polzl.jpg)


**Age:** 27  
**Hometown:** Toulouse, France  
**Occupation:** Chemistry technician  
**Favorite dinosaur:** Old Fossile  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Won MKM Rome Standard, won Ovinospring Legacy.


**What deck did you play and why did you choose it?**  

Mono-Red Goblins. It's the most stable deck in the format.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

None.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  

All the 1-drops.




---


Zan Syed
========


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/t8_syed.jpg)


**Age:** 23  
**Hometown:** Atlanta, GA, USA  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Favorite dinosaur:** Raptor/Chris/Mike  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8 at GP Miami 2015.


**What deck did you play and why did you choose it?**  

Green/White Collected Company.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

Probably cut the Nykthos for consistency.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  
[Mastery of the Unseen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mastery+of+the+Unseen)/[Collected Company](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collected+Company).




---


Jasper Grimmer
==============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/t8_grimmer.jpg)


**Age:** Jasper Grimmer  
**Hometown:** 26  
**Occupation:** Berlin, Germany  
**Favorite dinosaur:** Educator  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8 at GP Malmö 2012.


**What deck did you play and why did you choose it?**  

Abzan Aggro Morphs. I've been listening to Standard Master Brad Nelson on the SSL.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

Cut the [Warden of the First Tree](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warden+of+the+First+Tree) in the sideboard for probably another [Drown in Sorrow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drown+in+Sorrow). Otherwise it's great.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  

Still [Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino). Although every hand with [Fleecemane Lion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleecemane+Lion) is great.




---


Amand Dosimont
==============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/t8_dosimont.jpg)


**Age:** 32  
**Hometown:** La Louviere, Belgium  
**Favorite dinosaur:** Raptors if they are in my opponent's deck  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Grand Prix Prague Top 16.


**What deck did you play and why did you choose it?**  

Abzan Aggro because I had not finished my Atarka Red's decklist during sitting.


**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

I would add more Anafenzas and [Abzan Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abzan+Charm)s but that will make my deck illegal.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  
[Abzan Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abzan+Charm).




---


Kyoungsoo Kim
=============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/t8_kyoungsoo.jpg)


**Age:** 33  
**Hometown:** Pusan, South Korea  
**Occupation:** Financial planner  
**Favorite dinosaur:** What?  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Korea National Team finalist in 2002 and 2009. Took part in the last World Championship in San Francisco. Top 32 at Grand Prix Taipei 2013.


**What deck did you play and why did you choose it?**  

Mono-Red. I'm just sightseeing with my wife. So I have to sightsee with my wife at night. I never expected my Top 8!


**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?**  

I want to have one more [Searing Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Searing+Blood) in my sideboard. It is very good against morphs.


**What is the most important card in your deck?**  
[Stoke the Flames](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoke+the+Flames).
