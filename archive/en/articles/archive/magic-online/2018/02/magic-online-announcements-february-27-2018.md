*Magic Online* Championship This Weekend!
-----------------------------------------


After a year of preparation and competition, it's finally here! The *Magic Online* Championship takes place March 2–4 right here at Wizards HQ in Renton, Washington. Featuring 24 of the best MTGO players in the world, a $200,000 prize pool, and a roster of great coverage personalities, this really is the biggest MTGO moment of the year.


All three days of the tournament will stream live on [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic). Check out [Mike Rosenberg's article here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/organized-play/magic-online-championship-soon-2018-02-15) for more information.

Legacy Cube Returns
-------------------


Starting tomorrow (Wednesday, February 28), Legacy Cube returns to *Magic Online*, giving you the chance to draft a carefully cultivated collection of some of the most powerful cards from *Magic*'s history. Whether you have fond memories of casting the original Counterspell or you've always been curious about *Magic*'s oldest sets, you'll find something to get excited about.


Legacy Cube runs February 28 through March 16, and can be found in the Limited Leagues area of the Play Lobby. To see the full card list, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/legacy-cube-cardlist). For a complete list of recent changes to the card list, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/legacy-cube-returns-feb-28-2018-02-26). Event details below.


***LEGACY CUBE*LEAGUE**


**Start Time**: Wednesday, February 28, at 10 a.m. PT  
**Close Time**: Friday, March 16, at 10 a.m. PT  
**End Time**: Friday, March 16, at 2 p.m. PT  
**Entry Options**:


* 10 Event Tickets
* 100 Play Points

**Structure**: Draft, then up to three matches played at your convenience  
**Prizes**:


* 3 wins: 150 Play Points
* 2 wins: 100 Play Points
* 1 win: 40 Play Points
* 0 wins: 10 Play Points

***LEGACY CUBE*SINGLE-ELIMINATION QUEUE**


**Start Time**: Wednesday, February 28, at 10 a.m. PT  
**End Time**: Friday, March 16, at 10, a.m. PT  
**Entry Options**:


* 10 Event Tickets
* 100 Play Points

**Structure**: Draft, then three rounds of single-elimination matches  
**Prizes**:


* 1st place: 220 Play Points + 1 QP
* 2nd place: 160 Play Points
* 3rd–4th place: 100 Play Points

MOCS Monthly Event March 3
--------------------------


On Saturday, March 3, a MOCS Monthly Event will kick off at 7 a.m. PT. The format will be Standard. Players can enter with 35 Qualifier Points (QPs), and top finishers qualify for the Playoff Event on May 5.


Learn more about MOCS events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2018-magic-online-championship-series).




---

PTQ Finals Event March 4
------------------------


A Pro Tour Qualifier Finals Event will kick off this Sunday, March 4, at 7 a.m. The format will be *Rivals of Ixalan-Ixalan* Sealed. A red or blue PTQ Finals Entry Token is required to join this event, and the winner will qualify for Pro Tour *Dominaria*.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).
