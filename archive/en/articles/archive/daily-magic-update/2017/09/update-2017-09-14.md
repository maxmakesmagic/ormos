The September 14, 2017 Update
=============================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on September 14, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Nicholas_Wolfram.jpg)
By Nicholas Wolfram




 Nicholas Wolfram fell promptly in love with tabletop and video games when he was only two years old, and he has been writing about them since he figured out how words worked. Now he puts together words about Magic, which he started playing way back in 2000. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on September 14, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by puns, good and bad, clever and cringeworthy.


Today's Must
============


1. [*Magic* Open House: What You Need to Know (and Why You Should Participate!)](http://www.manaleak.com/mtguk/2017/09/magic-open-house-need-know-participate-joseph-dunlap/) | Mana Leak | Joseph Dunlap

The *Ixalan* *Magic* Open House will be taking place this Saturday and Sunday at local stores around the world. Not only do you get an excuse to play more *Magic* (as if you need an excuse), but you can also bring home an *Ixalan* card a week before even Prereleases happen! So throw on your tricorn, grab your favorite matey, and sail on over to your local shop this weekend for a swashbuckling good time. And remember: this is a casual event, so don't be afraid to bring your own brew instead of just parroting the most dominant decks in the metagame!


Today's Previews
================


1. [Fleet Swallower](https://www.wired.it/gadget/videogiochi/2017/09/14/spoiler-anteprima-divoratore-di-flotte-fleet-swallower-magic/) | WIRED Italy | Simone "AKirA" Trimarchi

![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/xln/en_dEquoVXWFQ.png)


That's a big Fish. In fact, this Fish is tied for the second-largest stats we've ever printed with the creature type. For a big flavor win, hit it with [Spreading Flames](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spreading+Flames) and then a boatload of lemons. Because roasted fish is delicious.


1. [Repeating Barrage](http://www.hobbyconsolas.com/noticias/nueva-carta-exclusiva-magic-ixalan-descarga-redoblada-164738) | Hobby Consolas | Jes√∫s Delgado

![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/xln/en_5NhGLNtay1.png)


[Hammer of Bogardan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hammer+of+Bogardan) returns as double-barreled ballista. Not a bad upgrade, but I would probably be aiming closer to that Dragon if were this guy.


1. [Axis of Mortality](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=obwekjqpYMQ) | The Command Zone | Jimmy Wong and Josh Lee Kwai

Chaos Commander players, it looks like you might want to start leaning into Jeskai to keep terrorizing your friends with the most random board states possible. This is your chance to make [Zedruu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=420848) into the Greathated among your playgroup.


1. [Perilous Voyage](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/snacktimewithmikeandant-09142017-snack-time-57-ixalan-preview/) | Snack Time with Mike and Ant | Mike Linnemann and Ant Tessitore

I tried to tell Jace that falling for a gorgon wasn't a good idea, but ever the contrarian, he took his defiance a little too literally. Not what I meant, Jace.


1. More to come!

Be on the lookout for preview cards from [The Mana Source](https://www.youtube.com/user/TheManaSource) and Ken Nagle ([@norrytt](https://twitter.com/norrytt)) as we finish strong before the full [*Ixalan* Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/ixalan) is revealed tomorrow!
