Top 8 Player Profiles

### Andrejs Prost


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsea15/top8_prost.jpg)


**Hometown:** Anchorage, AK  
**Age:** 23  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Two Pro Tour Top 8s; one Grand Prix Top 8; Latvian National Champion.


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**  

Sharless Sultai. I didn't have enough time to get reacquainted with Miracles.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**  
[Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) because it's [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm).


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**  
[Meddling Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage). It's very versatile.


**What was the most surprising deck you played against this weekend?**  

AIR (All-in Red).


**When did you play your first Legacy Constructed event, and how did that go?**  

Online. I won a Daily event.

### Brian DeMars


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsea15/top8_demars.jpg)


**Hometown:** Detroit, MI  
**Age:** 31  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Grand Prix Boston champion; StarCityGames Indianapolis champion; StarCityGames Richmond Power 9 champion, etc.


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**  

Miracles because [Sensei's Divining Top](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top) is incredible.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**  
[Sensei's Divining Top](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top).


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**  
[Blood Moon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon). It punishes greedy mana bases and provides free wins.


**What was the most surprising deck you played against this weekend?**  

I didn't play against any "brews" - just real decks.


**When did you play your first Legacy Constructed event, and how did that go?**  

I've played since 1995. I was casting [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) since *Ice Age* came out!

### Chase Hansen


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsea15/top8_hansen.jpg)


**Hometown:** Snohomish, WA  
**Age:** 24  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 20th GP Denver; several Top 16s at StarCityGames Opens


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**  

Reanimator because it's what I've played for over six years.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**  
[Archetype of Endurance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archetype+of+Endurance) because Death and Taxes can't beat it.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**  
[Abrupt Decay](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay) because [Grafdigger's Cage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grafdigger%27s+Cage) and [Rest in Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rest+in+Peace) exist.


**What was the most surprising deck you played against this weekend?**  

There weren't any surprising decks that I played against.


**When did you play your first Legacy Constructed event, and how did that go?**  

Many years ago, and it went poorly. I didn't have [Daze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daze)s, dual lands, or [Force of Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will)s.

### Gary Wong


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsea15/top8_wong.jpg)


**Hometown:** Glendale, AZ  
**Age:** 27  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 4 Grand Prix Phoenix, Top 32 at Grand Prix San Diego and Los Angeles


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**  

Four-Color Delver. [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman), [Young Pyromancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Young+Pyromancer), and [Delver of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets) are the best.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**  
[Gurmag Angler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gurmag+Angler). It's a one-mana [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) that can't be [Abrupt Decay](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay)ed.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**  
[Abrupt Decay](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay)


**What was the most surprising deck you played against this weekend?**  

I didn't play a matchup I was surprised against.


**When did you play your first Legacy Constructed event, and how did that go?**  

I played Legacy when it was called Type 1.5 and I lost because I was playing a Standard deck.

### Martin Goldman-Kirst


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsea15/top8_goldman-kirst.jpg)


**Hometown:** Seattle, WA  
**Age:** 25  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Played in six Pro Tours


**What did you play this weekend, and why?**  
[Aluren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aluren) because nobody knows how to play against it and it's a great deck.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?**  

Alurne because it's why the deck works.


**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**  
[Carpet of Flowers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carpet+of+Flowers) because I played against blue all day long.


**What was the most surprising deck you played against this weekend?**  

A midrange [Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam) deck but it wasn't that surprising.


**When did you play your first Legacy Constructed event, and how did that go?**  

A StarCityGames Open with Merfolk. I went 5-3 I think.

### Xin Sui


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsea15/top8_xui.jpg)

**Age:** 23  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 2012 National Champion; two Grand Prix Top 8s

Shardless Sultai. I sent 20-0 on Magic Online two-player queues.

**What was the best card out of your sideboard, and why?**  
[Sower of Temptation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sower+of+Temptation). It's great in multiple matchups.


**What was the most surprising deck you played against this weekend?**  

Tin Fins/Reanimator?


**When did you play your first Legacy Constructed event, and how did that go?**  

It was three years ago at a StarCityGames Open. I made Top 8.

**When did you play your first Legacy Constructed event, and how did that go?**  

Most likly Grand Prix Philadelphia 2005.
