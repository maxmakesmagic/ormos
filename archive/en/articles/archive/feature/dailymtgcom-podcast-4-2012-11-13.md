DailyMTG.com Podcast #4
=======================



 Posted in **Feature**

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












This is the biweekly audio podcast for DailyMTG.com. Intro music by Bern Menth.


[**Episode 4 - "Unlimited Resources"**](http://media.wizards.com/podcasts/magic/DailyMTGEp4UnlimitedResources.mp3): *(51:34)* This episode’s guest is Ryan Spain, who, along with Tifa, Mike, and Trick discuss **Magic Online** and Ryan’s role with it as well as the Wide Beta which is underway. Along with **Magic Online** the discussion turns to *Return to Ravnica* and to Ryan’s favorite format: Draft. (24 MB)

[**Episode 3 - "Mystical Web Producer"**](http://media.wizards.com/podcasts/magic/DailyMTGEp3MysticalWebProducer.mp3): *(36:38)* In this episode, Trick, Tifa, and Mike talk with Web Producer (and Arcana author) [Monty Ashley](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/archive.aspx?author=monty%20ashley). They talk about *Return to Ravnica* Game Day, the **Magic Online** Community Cup, and Halloween! (13 MB)


[**Episode 2 - "Uh oh, Rhino!"**](http://media.wizards.com/podcasts/magic/DailyMTGEp2Rhinos.mp3): *(55:21)* In this episode, Trick, Tifa, and Mike talk with R&D Designer and DailyMTG Columnist [Gavin Verhey](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/archive.aspx?author=gavin%20verhey). They talk about *Return to Ravnica*, the upcoming Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica*, and their [Fantasy Pro Tour](https://apps.facebook.com/magicfantasyprotour/) rosters for the event! (25 MB)


[**Episode 1**](http://media.wizards.com/podcasts/magic/DailyMTGEp1.mp3): Trick, Tifa, and Mike gather for the first time to launch a new podcast for DailyMTG.com. They introduce themselves, then dive into *Return to Ravnica*, **Magic**, stories from their Prerelease, and looking at how *Return to Ravnica* might change their beloved formats and decks. (20.8 MB)

### The Guest:



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/activity/da1102_ryan.jpg)

Ryan Spain is a **Magic** Digital Designer in R&D. His primary responsibilities are the ongoing design of features and game play presentation for the **Magic Online** beta client and the smooth transition of new card sets from paper to **Magic Online**. He also does his share of playtesting for upcoming sets, and recently finished his first stint on the design team of an upcoming **Magic** set. Follow him on Twitter: [@ryanspain](https://twitter.com/ryanspain).




### The Hosts:



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/activity/da1092_trick.jpg)

Patrick "Trick" Jarrett is the Editor-in-Chief of DailyMTG.com. Born in Atlanta, GA, he moved his wife and their two dogs to Seattle to work for Wizards of the Coast. He's a big fan of the Commander format and loves his [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiki-Jiki%2C+Mirror+Breaker) mono-red Commander deck. Follow him on Twitter: [@TrickMTG](https://twitter.com/TrickMTG).





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/activity/da1072_tifa.jpg)

Jennifer "Tifa" Meyen is the Assistant Brand Manager for **Magic: The Gathering** as well as the founder and organizer for [The Lady Planeswalker Society](http://www.facebook.com/pages/Lady-Planeswalkers-Society/206021052816060), a welcoming and friendly environment for women **Magic** players of all skill levels. Jennifer has been an avid gamer since birth, and **Magic** became her life source during ***Magic** 2011*. Follow her on Twitter: [@TifaMeyen](https://twitter.com/TifaMeyen) and [@MTGLadySociety](https://twitter.com/MTGLadySociety).





![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/activity/da1072_michael.jpg)

Michael "Mike" Robles is the Community Manager for **Magic: The Gathering** and creates content for the [official **Magic: The Gathering** Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/MagicTheGathering) as well as the [official Twitter account](https://twitter.com/wizards_magic)! If you see him at conventions he's probably got plenty of free stuff to give you. He is fluent in Facebook, Twitter, and Internet. You can follow Mike and his escapades on Twitter: [@michaelrobles](https://twitter.com/michaelrobles).
