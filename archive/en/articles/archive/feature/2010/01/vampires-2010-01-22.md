Looking for something to play at this week's Friday Night **Magic** (FNM)? Eric Palmerduca has you covered! He won a StarCityGames.com Standard Open event with this version of the dark tribe Vampires. You just can't keep the ol' Vamps down; just when everyone thought they were simply worse than Jund and other options in the format, Eric proved them all wrong! Now you can wow your friends with his powerful deck at [Friday Night **Magic**](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm).
