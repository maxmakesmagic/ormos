*Magic Online* Stream Event Extravaganza
Across three days, November 11, 12, and 13, you'll be able to find *Magic Online* being streamed across the web. The festivities start Tuesday with another edition of the [Vintage Super League](http://vintagesuperleague.com/). Make sure you stick around through the duration of Vintage Super League, as there will be a special announcement mid-stream. After that, players can look forward to an early preview of the Legacy Cube in the form of two, 8-player Swiss Draft events on November 12, and 13, at 5:30 p.m. Check out the full [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online-eternal-stream-stravaganza-2014-11-13) for all of the details.



 



*Khans of Tarkir* Championship Qualifiers Begin Tomorrow
The *Khans of Tarkir* Championship Qualifiers are coming! Beginning tomorrow, November 12, the *Khans of Tarkir* Limited Championship Qualifiers open on *Magic Online*. The more Standard-minded amongst you won't have to wait much longerâ€”*Khans of Tarkir* Standard Championship Qualifiers begin Wednesday, November 19.


For a full schedule of events, refer to the *Khans of Tarkir* Championship [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/khans-tarkir-championship-events-2014-09-29).



 



Legacy Cube Releases Wednesday, November 19
On Wednesday, November 19, the Legacy Cube is released into the wild! If you can't wait to see how the Cube plays, check out some of our ongoing [streamer events](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online-eternal-stream-stravaganza-2014-11-13).


[Read more](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/new-magic-online-legacy-cube-2014-11-3) about the Legacy Cube.



 



Get Your *Commander 2014* Decklists
*Commander 2014* made its debut on *Magic Online* on Friday, November 7. If you've ever wanted to take command with a Planeswalker, now's your chance! Haven't picked your commander? Want to see what makes the decks tick? Head on over to [this page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/download-your-commander-2014-decklists-2014-11-07) for decklists and instructions on how to download them for *Magic Online*.



 



Downtime for November 12, 2014
**Downtime for Wednesday, November 12, will be extended by one hour.**



* All tournaments will close Wednesday, October 29, at 5:00 a.m. Pacific (13:00 UTC)
* Store and trade activity will be suspended at approximately 7:45 a.m. Pacific(15:45 UTC)
* The system will be down from 8:00 a.m. Pacific (16:00 UTC) until 1:00 p.m. Pacific (21:00 UTC)
