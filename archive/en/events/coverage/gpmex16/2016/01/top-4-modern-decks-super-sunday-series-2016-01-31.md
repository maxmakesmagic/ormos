Top 4 Modern decks – Super Sunday Series
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 31, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











With Pro Tour *Oath of the Gatewatch* less than a week away, people are talking about little else besides the impact of the new cards from *Oath,* and what they think the Modern metagame will look like after the removal of Splinter Twin and Amulet Bloom.


We got a little peek into that on Sunday at Grand Prix Mexico City, as the Modern side event for the Super Sunday Series showcased a little bit of that new Modern world. Here are the top four decks, in order, from the event.
