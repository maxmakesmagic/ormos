The Heron in the Moon
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 17, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Have you wondered what the deal is with [Moon Heron](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moon+Heron)?

![Moon Heron](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Moon+Heron)  
Well, the fact is, this is a ghostly heron that's associated with the legendary "Heron in the Moon" that some people say they can see on the moon of *Innistrad*. Have you ever noticed it? Let's look at one of the moon-heavy cards:

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/844_paraselene.jpg)[Paraselene](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Paraselene) | Art by [Ryan Yee](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Ryan+Yee%22%5D)

Still having trouble seeing it? Well, let's pull in an image from the *Innistrad* Style Guide!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/844_styleguidemoon.jpg)
