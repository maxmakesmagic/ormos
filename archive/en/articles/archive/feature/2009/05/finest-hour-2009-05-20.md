[Finest Hour](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Finest+Hour) is an exciting new card from Alara Reborn, and a fitting title for Tommy Ashton's performance a few weekends ago at a Pro Tour Qualifier in Richmond, Virginia. He used this build of Bant to take the top slot, mowing down the competition along the way. Not your average Bant deck he combined cards like [Rafiq of the Many](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Rafiq%2Bof%2Bthe%2BMany) and [Jhessian Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jhessian+Infiltrator) with [Finest Hour](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Finest+Hour) to attack for massive amounts of damage each turn. It was no doubt Tommy's finest hour!
