TOP 8 PLAYER PROFILES
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 29, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/080214_1291.jpg)
By Jacob Van Lunen




Jacob Van Lunen began playing Magic in 1995. He has participated in organized play at every level of competition and was a member of the winning team at Pro Tour San Diego in 2007, thanks to an innovative draft strategy. As a writer, Van Lunen has had more than three hundred Magic strategy pieces published 






After many rounds of Swiss and some great games of *Magic*, the Top 8 for Grand Prix Minneapolis is set! Learn more about this event’s Top 8 competitors, their history at the top tables, and more by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles.  

 




---

 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmin16/gpmin16_t8_turtenwald.jpg)


### Owen Turtenwald


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** Las Vegas  
**Occupation:** Channelfireball.com


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


3 Pro Tour Top 8, Player of the Year 2011


**What Deck are you playing and why?**


White-Black Control


**What card are you most excited about playing with in Eternal Masters?**


[Volunteer Reserves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volunteer+Reserves)


**What's the most underrated card in Standard?**


[Read the Bones](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Read+the+Bones)

### Max McVety


**Age:** 22


**Hometown:** Ann Arbor, MI  
**Occupation:** Mechanical Engineer


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


2 Pro Tour Top 50s, Columbus SCG Invitational Winner, 4-1ed a competitive Standard league with Humans


**What Deck are you playing and why?**


Humans. I have won every tournament I played it in.


**What card are you most excited about playing with in Eternal Masters?**


[Chain Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+Lightning)


**What's the most underrated card in Standard?**


[Tandem Tactics](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tandem+Tactics)

### Alex Johnson


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** Hopkins, MN  
**Occupation:** Health Services Administrator


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


2nd SCG Classic, PT Battle for Zendikar competitor


**What Deck are you playing and why?**


Green-White Tokens. The matchups for this deck are mostly favorable. Dodging Four-Color Rites most of the tournament helps.


**What's the most underrated card in Standard?**


[Lambholt Pacifist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lambholt+Pacifist)

**Age:** 36


**Hometown:** Madison, WI  
**Occupation:** Software Engineer


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Top 8 GP Detroit 2003


Top 4 GP Nashville 2014


Winner GP DC 2016

### Reed Hartman


**Age:** 28


**Hometown:** Dekalb, IL  
**Occupation:** Electrician


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Played in Pro Tour Dragon's Maze. 2nd Legacy Champs 2012


**What Deck are you playing and why?**


Green-White Tokens. It's a consistent deck that I played in GP New York. I made a few changes and tried it again.


**What card are you most excited about playing with in Eternal Masters?**


All the cards in foreign languages! [Mana Crypt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Crypt), [Hymn to Tourach](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hymn+to+Tourach), [Karakas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karakas). I can upgrade my cube!

### Andrew Elenbogen


**Age:** 22


**Hometown:** Ann Arbor, MI  
**Occupation:** Software Engineer


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


GP Omaha Top 8


13-2 GP Boston


Many GP Top 16s and Top 32s


**What Deck are you playing and why?**


Bant Humans with [Eldrazi Displacer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Displacer). I wanted to play Bant Company, but it wasn't proactive enough to beat rites and Green-White Tokens. This deck really ups the pressure to have mostly favorable matchups. Displacer is great against everything.


**What card are you most excited about playing with in Eternal Masters?**


[Winter Orb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Winter+Orb)


**What's the most underrated card in Standard?**


Max McVety's Clue Token
