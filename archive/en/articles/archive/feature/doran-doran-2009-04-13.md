[Doran, the Siege Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower)? Orie Guo managed to ride a number of Standard stalwarts all the way to the Top 8 of a recent Pro Tour Qualifier for Honolulu. Of course, there were plenty of Extended staples riding the pine too, but one has to imagine there were a few surprised opponents beaten down by some of the very best from the world of Standard.
