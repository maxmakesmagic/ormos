Modern Loam at Pro Tour Fate Reforged
=====================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on March 12, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/shuhei_authorpic.jpg)
By Shuhei Nakamura




Shuhei Nakamura is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, former Player of the Year, and is one of just six players with more than 400 lifetime pro points. 






There have been a wide variety of [Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam) decks in the history of *Magic*. It's hard to define "a Loam deck" due to its wide usage over many archetypes. It depends a lot on what cards it's paired up with, and sometimes the deck is called something else if Loam is only a part of the deck. Dredge is a good example.



I dug up as much information as I could on Modern Loam and discovered it has shifted from its "old fashioned" Aggro Loam form. Instead, I would call it a reversion of the original "Loam" deck. Raphaël Lévy's deck has hardly any aggro aspect.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Life+from+the+Loam)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam)

It's built to slowly choke the opponent using [Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam) over and over again. The deck stretches out the game with [Smallpox](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smallpox) and [Liliana of the Veil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil), and digs through its library with [Faithless Looting](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faithless+Looting) and dredge cards. Whenever it hits [Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam), it assures a replenished hand. From there, the deck focuses on drowning the opponent in card advantage. Dredging [Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam) gives the deck flashback and replenish spells, and redundant [Tectonic Edge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tectonic+Edge) is a win condition in itself.



Any token put on the battlefield in the proses works as a finisher.
