2015 Magic Online Player Rewards
================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on December 15, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_davidwhitfield.jpg)
By David Whitfield




 David works on the Magic community team as a content specialist. He spends his days writing about Magic Online and trying to play too many colors at once in Limited. 






The [2015 *Magic Online* Player Rewards (MOPR) page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2015-magic-online-player-rewards) is now live!


The *Magic: The Gathering Online* Player Rewards program offers players the chance to earn digital promo cards for their monthly *Magic Online* activity. This activity includes playing in tournaments of a sufficient size, as well as making purchases in the *Magic Online* Store. For the current season, November 26, 2014 through January 5, 2015, [Nekusar, the Mindrazer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nekusar%2C+the+Mindrazer) is the event promo, and [Stoke the Flames](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoke+the+Flames) is the store activity promo.


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mtgo/MTGO_12-15-2014_img1.png)
You can earn both standard non-foil versions, as well as premium foil versions of the Magic Online Player Rewards program cards. Check out the table below for the run-down on what activities award which cards.


Promo cards are updated each monthly MOPR period so make sure to add the [2015 page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2015-magic-online-player-rewards) to your favorites to keep in-the-know on the latest *Magic Online* Player Rewards!





|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Activity** | **Reward** |
| Participate in at least one (1) Constructed, Sealed Deck, or Draft Tournament with a minimum of eight (8) players | Regular Event Participation promotional card |
| Participate in at least five (5) Constructed, Sealed Deck, or Draft Tournaments with a minimum of eight (8) players | Premium Event Participation promotional card |
| Full $20.00 USD spent in the **Magic Online** Store (excluding coupons, shipping, and taxes. | Regular Store Activity promotional card |
| Full $40.00 USD spent in the **Magic Online** Store (excluding coupons, shipping, and taxes) | Premium Store Activity promotional card |
