Sliver art gallery 2
====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 9, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Y[esterday](/en/articles/archive/sliver-art-gallery-2004-03-08) we looked at the art of *Stronghold's* cycle of multicolored Slivers. Get set for more: today we look at some of *Tempest's* adaptable nasties.


*Tempest* has two cycles of Slivers: a common cycle of 1/1 Slivers for converted mana cost of ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161225105850im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless2.gif) (plus [Metallic Sliver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metallic+Sliver) for ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)), and an uncommon cycle of 2/2 Slivers for ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130050im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless3.gif). Today we present the art for the 2/2 uncommons. Enjoy.
