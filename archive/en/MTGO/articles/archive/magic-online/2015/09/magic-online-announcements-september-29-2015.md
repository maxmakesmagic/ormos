**Modern Leagues and Other *Magic Online* Event Updates**


Lee Sharpe, Digital Product Manager for *Magic Online* Events, returns to bring players the latest news and event updates. Get *Magic Online* updates across a wide variet of formats, including an updated Daily Event schedule, changes to eight-player queue prize structure, and more.


For all of you Modern fans out there, the biggest news is going to be our new Modern League that begins on September 30, following the downtime!


Read the full article [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/september-2015-event-changes-2015-09-28).

**Newly Updated Legacy Cube Events Running Now**


Randy Buehler's [latest article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/legacy-cube-update-2015-09-14) details the second round of updates to the *Magic Online* Legacy Cube. Check it out for a list of what's changed and a link to the full Cube list!


Events for the [newly updated](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/legacy-cube-update-2015-09-14) Legacy Cube run until Friday, October 9 at 10:00 a.m. Pacific (UTC -7). You can check out the full event details [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-announcements-september-15-2015-2015-09-15#item3)!

**Modern Super League Champion Crowned Tonight!**


Watch the final episode of the Modern Super League tonight at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7) live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic)! Check out Randy Buehler's latest [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-super-league-episode-3-2015-09-29) for a preview of tonight's matches.

Who will emerge as the Super League grand champion? Find out by watching along!

**Mulligan Rule Update**


The new mulligan rule takes effect on *Magic Online* next Wednesday, October 7, just in time for *Battle for Zendikar* [Prerelease events](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-battle-zendikar-prerelease-and-release-events-2015-09-28#preswissdraft)!


Get more information on the new mulligan rule [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/arcana/reminder-new-mulligan-rule-starts-bfz-2015-09-23).

**Double Pro Tour Qualifier Weekend**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls, there's a double Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) weekend coming up! Modern Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries and the *Magic Origins* Sealed Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries begin tomorrow, September 30, and run through the day preceding each Finals Event.


Learn more about PTQs on *Magic Online* and get the full schedule [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers-pro-tour-oath-of-the-gatewatch).


[Top](#top)


 




---

**Downtime for September 30, 2015**


* All tournaments will close Wednesday, September 30, at 5:00 a.m. Pacific (12:00 UTC)
* Store and trade activity will be suspended at approximately 7:45 a.m. Pacific (14:45 UTC)
* The system will be down from 8:00 a.m. Pacific (15:00 UTC) until 12:00 p.m. Pacific (19:00 UTC)
