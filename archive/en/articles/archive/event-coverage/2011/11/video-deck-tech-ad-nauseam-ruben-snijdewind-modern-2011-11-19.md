Video Deck Tech: Ad Nauseam with Ruben Snijdewind (Modern)
==========================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 18, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 





Rich Hagon catches up with Ruben Snijdewind, the Dutch National Champion, on Day Three at the World Championships. Snijdewind's [Ad Nauseam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ad+Nauseam+)  deck has him on the cusp of the Top 8 and a spot for his team in the National Team final. A deck list will be posted after the event.
