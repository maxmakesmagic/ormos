Vintage Super League Returns!
=============================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on January 23, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The [Vintage Super League](http://vintagesuperleague.com/) returns next week, kicking off on Tuesday, January 27, at 6 p.m. PT (one hour earlier than Season 1). In honor of the return of VSL, we thought we'd run one of the decks VSL Season 1 champion Stephen Menendian used to win the championship.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Delver+of+Secrets)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets)[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Insectile+Aberration)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Insectile+Aberration)Transform

Tune in next Tuesday to see how a restricted [Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise) and unrestricted [Gifts Ungiven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven) affects the format.
