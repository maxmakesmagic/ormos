With the **Magic** world's focus on Extended and the PTQ Honolulu season, times call for a look at Extended past. In 2007, French player Remi Fortier played a [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) control deck featuring the powerful combination of [Counterbalance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterbalance) and [Sensei's Divining Top](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top). With the enchantment in play, Remi could use the Divining Top to make sure he could lock out the majority of his opponent's relevant spells. He managed to win a one-of-a-kind weekend, but you can join Remi on the Pro Tour. To find a Pro Tour Qualifier near you, visit [this link](/en/node/640716).
