Looking for a fun Extended deck with a wacky combo? How about trying Chester Li's [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Kiki-Jiki%252C%2BMirror%2BBreaker) deck from the Top 8 of a Pro Tour Qualifier held in Boston? The combo is simple: cast [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiki-Jiki%2C+Mirror+Breaker) and use it to copy [Pestermite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pestermite). The token untaps a permanent—[Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Kiki-Jiki%252C%2BMirror%2BBreaker)—and the 2/2 Goblin is free to make another copy of [Pestermite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pestermite). Repeat ad nauseam to create infinite [Pestermite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pestermite) tokens with haste and attack!
