Free Association
================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on February 5, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_markrosewater.jpg)
By Mark Rosewater




 Working in R&D since '95, Mark became Magic head designer in '03. His hobbies: spending time with family, writing about Magic in all mediums, and creating short bios. 






![Orcish Artillery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Orcish+Artillery)Orcs are one of those races that I believe have gotten short shrift in the game. I think the popularity of goblins has squeezed out space that in the past had been filled by orcs and [dwarves](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/free-association-2004-02-05-17). Mental note: look for more design space for orcs. And bleeder cards. Oh, and a new version of Feedback.
