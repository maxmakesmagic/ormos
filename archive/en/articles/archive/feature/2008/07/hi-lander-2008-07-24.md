Hi, Lander!
===========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 24, 2008 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By The Mimic











*Welcome to Mimic Week! All this week on **magicthegathering.com**, the regular columns will appear as usual… but with a twist. Your eight regular writers, plus at least two guest authors who've written for the site before, are hiding in the ten regular column slots—maybe even their own—under a clever pseudonym: The Mimic! Can you figure out who actually wrote each article? Tune in Monday, July 28 for the answers!*

There comes a time in every young man’s life when he asks, “Mom, have I gone through puberty yet?” And if your family is anything like my family, she’ll answer with a laugh. “Of course you have! And now that you’re a man, you have special new tasks around the house.” Then she stabbed me through the eye with a baking roller. So began one man’s journey into the Australian Outback. A lifelong obsession with wombats was born out of terrible fear and fury. Rabies shots ensued. It was all that, and a bag of chips.


This has absolutely nothing to do with anything. I’m just tapping the words, so you don’t have to!


Did you know that I was recently offered employment with Wizards of the Coast? It’s true! (No, it’s not). Am I arguing with myself? (Yes, you are.) GET THEE GONE, VOICES IN MY HEAD! (No.) Evil parenthetical spirits. (![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211201201921im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless1.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161115194122im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/blue.gif), 2/2. When Evil Parenthetical Spirits come into play, bracket target creature. It can’t split infinitives.)


Hark, the angels sing! These are not yon voices in mine head, no! It is the original Gathering, the time of the [Quicken](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quicken)ing. It is not madness, but mass-decapitations in the name of childbirth. I speak of [Highlander](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Highlander_%28film%29). There can only be one!


[![quicken](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/cardart/GPT/quicken.jpg)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=quicken)For further reading about Highlander, you can enjoy these tasty tidbits from your local library:

I loves me some Highlander, yes I do! (Yes, he does!) I also loves me a sammich! (Yes, he does!) Sandwiches have layers. HIghlander has layers. (Stretched analogy!) How many layers can we put on Highlander?
