Decks of the Week: 9/30/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on September 30, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












We hope you all had a fantastic time at the *Scars of Mirrodin* Prereleases last weekend, whetting your appetite for the new set and triggering all sorts of deckbuilding ideas. Your first chance to buy the new set is Friday, so make your way to a store near you … and while you're there, you might as well join in the fun by playing in a [Launch Party](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/release-facts)! While you're waiting for the weekend to arrive, take a gander at the rest of the Extended decks from the record-setting Pro Tour-Amsterdam and the cavalcade of decks tearing up the **Magic Online** metagames.
