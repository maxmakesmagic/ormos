Free Association
================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on February 5, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_markrosewater.jpg)
By Mark Rosewater




 Working in R&D since '95, Mark became Magic head designer in '03. His hobbies: spending time with family, writing about Magic in all mediums, and creating short bios. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/fcpics/making/110_maro.jpg)If you ever have a chance to have me sign a Maro (and it's the number one card I sign; #2 is Look at Me, I'm the DCI and #3 is Multani, Maro-sorcerer) you'll notice that I always draw something on the face. I've signed thousands of Maros so I've come up with a [variety](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/mr110.asp) of face accoutrements: glasses, facial hair, scars, various hats, eye patches, arrow through the head.
