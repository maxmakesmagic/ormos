Decks of the Week: 10/25/2013
=============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on October 25, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Pro Tour Hall of Famer Shuhei Nakamura defeated fellow world traveler and [Team ChannelFireball](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=%20http://www.channelfireball.com/) teammate Martin Juza in the finals of [Grand Prix Hong Kong](/en/events/coverage/nakamura-masters-hong-kong) last weekend, bringing his GP victory total to six. Meanwhile, across the Pacific, Brian Braun-Duin proved his devotion to black was up to the task by winning [Grand Prix Louisville](/en/events/coverage/braun-duin-leader-pack-louisville). Congratulations Shuhei and Brian! Want to play against them at Pro Tour *Born of the Gods* in Valencia, Spain? One way is to win a PTQ! This season's format is *Theros* Limited, just like GP Hong Kong! Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#bog)! Good luck; have fun!
