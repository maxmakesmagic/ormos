Noorlander’s New Legacy
=======================




Niels Noorlander showcased mastery across multiple formats to become the *Magic Online* Champion, winning $25,000 and earning a seat at this year’s *Magic: The Gathering* World Championship.

Top 4 Coverage



Niels Noorlander started off the tournament in rough shape, picking up two losses early and needing to compensate for the early fumble with multiple wins in the other three formats. While his Booster Draft results left him short, it was his performance in both Standard and Modern that propelled him into a Cinderella story Top 4 berth.



 There, he showcased mastery in format selection for the best two-out-of-three matches playoffs by blasted past his competition, taking the win in the semifinals against first-seed Jonathan Anghelescu in two matches, including a first-match upset that did not favor him in Legacy. From there, he did the same in the finals against Alexander Ivanov, taking the first match in Standard when it was Ivanov’s selection and a format that favored him. Noorlander locked up his win in two games with an incredibly favorable Legacy match, earning him the $25,000, Platinum-status in the Pro Tour Players Club, and an invitation to the 2016 *Magic: The Gathering* World Championship.




 Congratulations to Niels Noorlander on his victory. We’ll see him next time at Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon* in Sydney, Australia!

DAY 2 COVERAGE



The Top 4 of this year's *Magic Online* Championship features over a combined 12,000 matches of event-based experience in Daily Events, Leagues, Premier Events, and more, with each player showcasing a strength that shined this weekend. Jonathan Anghelescu, Gerry Thompson, Alexander Ivanov, and Niels Noorlander all played brilliantly, with each match mattering even into the final round of Swiss standing.


Which of these four players will be crowned the *Magic Online* Champion, winning the $25,000 first place prize and the seat at the 2016 *Magic: The Gathering* World Championship that comes with it? Tune in to [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic) on Sunday at 10 a.m. PT/1 p.m. ET/6 p.m. UTC to find out!
