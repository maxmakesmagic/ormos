Round 3: Frank Gilson vs. Peter Szigeti
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 27, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











Frank Gilson was a member of the famed Pacific Coast Legends back in the day, and was considered one of the best deckbuilders in the world. He slowly fell off the radar like most of the team, but came back in recent years. Yesterday he grinded into Nationals through a Limited grinder. His opponent, Peter Szigeti, has taken the mantle of Magic's villain, and ran with it for all it's worth. To his credit, Szigeti has recently written some very entertaining articles for TOGIT's website. An interesting aspect of this match is that it features the clich√© of the old veteran against the "young punk"- both from California.

### Game 1

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a916.jpg)Szigeti had a second turn [Nantuko Shade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade), and Gilson played [Withered Wretch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Withered+Wretch). Szigeti summoned [Rotlung Reanimator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator). Gilson's [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) got a [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), and Szigeti attacked with his team- the Wretch blocked the Reanimator, forcing a trade.

Szigeti, to a judge- "What happens? I get two guys?"  
 Gilson- "I get a guy and you get a guy."  
 Szigeti- "No, I'm the only one who gets them."

Gilson seemed a little thrown off by the fact that he didn't understand the card properly. Szigeti continued to build his side with [Graveborn Muse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Graveborn+Muse), and Gilson cleared everything with [Mutilate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate). Szigeti came right back with two [Withered Wretch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Withered+Wretch)es, one of which died to [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother). A Rotlung gave Szigeti another threat to work with and [Corrupt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt) killed it, giving Gilson seven life and Szigeti a 2/2 token. Szigeti attacked for four damage a turn, and eventually brought out [Visara](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara).

Gilson- 0 Szigeti- 1

Szigeti- "Hey Mark, if I win Nationals, I'm going to turn the trophy into a crown. My mom can help me figure something out, she made this (the plaque necklace)."

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a914.jpg)Gilson played a first turn [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), naming [Rotlung Reanimator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator). He saw two swamps, [Unholy Grotto](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unholy+Grotto), double [Corrupt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt), [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) and [Nantuko Shade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade). Szigeti's [Mesmeric Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) took [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother), leaving Gilson with an unimpressive hand containing lands and [Shambling Swarm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shambling+Swarm). The Swarm went away thanks to [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), and just when things seemed hopeless, Gilson drew [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother). It killed the Fiend to get back the other [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother), which killed the Shade. Gilson untapped and played [Rotlung Reanimator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator), sacrificing it to flash back [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) and take the two [Corrupt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt)s out of Szigeti's hand. This left him with a 2/2 Zombie token, which Szigeti killed with [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother).

Szigeti played a morph creature followed by [Mesmeric Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend), which saw only a land in Gilson's hand. [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) killed the morph ([Headhunter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Headhunter)), and Szigeti summoned [Braids, Cabal Minion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Braids%2C+Cabal+Minion). It traded with a Rotlung in combat, and Gilson's Shade died to [Smother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother). Szigeti played another morph creature, and it traded with Gilson's token. Another morph creature came out, and once again Szigeti had [Visara](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara). Gilson played two [Engineered Plague](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Plague)s set to Legends, but they merely delayed the inevitable.

**Final Result:** Gilson- 0 Szigeti- 2
