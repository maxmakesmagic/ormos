Perilous Research
=================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 26, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Focused on exploring the **Magic Online** metagame, this column looks at the digital world of **Magic** and how the decks online might impact the decks at your next **Magic** tournament. Metagame research is deep and fraught with perils, but with this column you’ll be better equipped and your deck will be better built.
