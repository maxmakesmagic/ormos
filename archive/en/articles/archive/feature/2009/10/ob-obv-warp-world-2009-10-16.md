Pro Tour coverage reporter and The Week That Was columnist Brian David-Marshall has been brewing all sorts of decks for the world of new Standard. The above list is his take on a [Warp World](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warp+World) deck that features [Ob Nixilis, the Fallen](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ob+Nixilis%2C+the+Fallen). When the legendary black creature enters the battlefield after [Warp World](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warp+World) resolves, he forces the opponent to lose 3 life for each land entering with him. That generally leads to a very dead opponent! Brian posted the list on his **Magic** blog, Top8Magic.com.
