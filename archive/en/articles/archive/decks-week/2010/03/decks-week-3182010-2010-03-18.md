Decks of the Week: 3/18/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on March 18, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Formats continue to evolve as deck builders around the world work *Worldwake* into their plans. This week we showcase every Day 2 Standard deck from Grand Prix–Kuala Lumpur along with a few new Game Day locations. But that's not the only format we have covered here—the Extended PTQ season for San Juan has three weeks left, including six **Magic Online** qualifiers. One of those happens to be [today at 5 p.m. PT](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/events/ptqmtgo/sanjuan10), so comb through the lists from previous PTQs and **Magic Online** events and find the right deck for you!
