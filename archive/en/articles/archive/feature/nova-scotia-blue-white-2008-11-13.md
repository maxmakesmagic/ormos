Blue-White Control is one of **Magic**'s most classic archetypes. Brett Ripley offered up his take on the deck at his Champs tournament in Nova Scotia, Canada. New additions to the deck include [Knight-Captain of Eos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight-Captain+of+Eos), [Tidings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tidings), and [Mistmeadow Witch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistmeadow+Witch). That unassuming 1/1 works wonders when combined with [Mulldrifter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter), [Reveillark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reveillark), the Knight-Captain, and [Kitchen Finks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks). Congrats to Brett, and keep it tuned to Daily Decks all week long for a look at what Champs tournaments are bringing to the Standard format!
