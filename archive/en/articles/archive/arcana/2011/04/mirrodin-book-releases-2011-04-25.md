Mirrodin Book Releases
======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 25, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Do you enjoy the story line of **Magic**? Do you wish that you knew more about what was happening *right now* in the *Scars of Mirrodin* story? Well, good news! The latest **Magic** novel, *The Quest for Karn*, is coming out on April 26, 2011. That's tomorrow!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/696_thequestforkarn.jpg)  
Written by Robert B. Wintermute, *The Quest for Karn* covers Koth, Elspeth, and Venser on Mirrodin as they, well, quest. For Karn. It will be available in both paper and e-book form.

Now, let's say you're not 100% familiar with Mirrodin's backstory. Well, good news! Because April 26 is also going to see the release of the original Mirrodin Cycle books as e-books! Relive the adventures of Glissa and Slobad in *The Moons of Mirrodin, The Darksteel Eye,* and *The Fifth Dawn* on your convenient electronic device!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/696_mirrodincycle.jpg)  
Whether you want to follow the story of Mirrodin from its introduction in 2003 or just read the latest developments, April 26 is your day to remember.
