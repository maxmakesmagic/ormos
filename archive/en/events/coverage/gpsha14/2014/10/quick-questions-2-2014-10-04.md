QUICK QUESTIONS #2
==================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on October 4, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











 What is the best Common, Uncommon and Rare/Mythic Rare for *Khans of Tarkir* Limited?







![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Kuo-Tzu-Ching.jpg)

***Kuo Tzu Ching:** [Feat of Resistance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Feat+of+Resistance), [Abzan Battle Priest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abzan+Battle+Priest), and [High Sentinels of Arashin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Sentinels+of+Arashin)*




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Lee-Shi-Tian.jpg)

***Lee Shi Tian:** [Leaping Master](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leaping+Master), Tri-Colored Lands, and [Wingmate Roc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingmate+Roc)*










![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Tomoharu-Saito.jpg)

***Tomoharu Saito:** [Sultai Scavenger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sultai+Scavenger) or [Woolly Loxodon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woolly+Loxodon), [Murderous Cut](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Murderous+Cut), and [Ashcloud Phoenix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashcloud+Phoenix)*




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Nam-Sung-Wook.jpg)

***Nam Sung Wook:** [Feat of Resistance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Feat+of+Resistance), [Raiders' Spoils](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raiders%27+Spoils), and [Duneblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast)*










![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Ken-Yukuhiro2.jpg)

***Ken Yukuhiro:** [Debilitating Injury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Debilitating+Injury), [Murderous Cut](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Murderous+Cut), and [Duneblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast)*




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Tiago-Chan.jpg)

***Tiago Chan:** [Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise) for Sealed, [Ruthless Ripper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ruthless+Ripper), and [Ghostfire Blade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghostfire+Blade)*










![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Park-Jun-Young.jpg)

***Park Jun Young:** [Mardu Hordechief](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mardu+Hordechief) , [Suspension Field](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Suspension+Field), and [Flying Crane Technique](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flying+Crane+Technique)*




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpsha14/QQ-Han-Bing.jpg)

***Han Bing:** [Mardu Hordechief](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mardu+Hordechief), Tri-Colored Lands, and [Sorin, Solemn Visitor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sorin%2C+Solemn+Visitor)*
