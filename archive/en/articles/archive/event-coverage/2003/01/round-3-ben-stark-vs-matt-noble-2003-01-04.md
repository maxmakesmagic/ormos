Round 3: Ben Stark vs. Matt Noble
=================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 4, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_benbleiweiss.jpg)
By Ben Bleiweiss











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a975.jpg)The eternal question: Oath versus.....Ghoul Burst? It's the battle of the deck that won PT Houston versus the deck that had an awful showing at PT Houston. Ben Stark himself piloted the [Academy Rector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Academy+Rector) based deck to a 33rd place finish at that tournament, and he swears that the deck performs better than its history would suggest. Matt won the die roll and chose to play first.

### Game 1

Ben made the first play of the game, a turn one [Birds of Paradise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise). Matt answered with [Oath of Druids](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oath+of+Druids). Stark cast a second turn [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), naming [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell). This nailed one copy of the signature blue card, while revealing a hand of [Krosan Reclamation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Reclamation), [Oath of Druids](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oath+of+Druids), [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) and [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm). He then summoned [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves), played [Windswept Heath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Windswept+Heath), and passed the turn.

Matt took this opportunity to activate the namesake enchantment of his deck, milling into a 6/6 [Cognivore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cognivore). Ben returned fire by enchanting his flyer with [Pattern of Rebirth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pattern+of+Rebirth), allowing Noble to bring out another [Cognivore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cognivore). This pumped the first flyer to 15/15—and people used to think [Morphling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morphling) was huge! 

Ben pondered his board position. He laid down a swamp, giving him the Patterned Bird, a Llanowar Elf, a [Swamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp), a [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) and a [Plains](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains). His first play: hard-casting a second [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy). As it successfully resolved, he picked up his opponent's graveyard, and counted each copy of the cards. Finally, he named [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak)—revealing a hand of [Krosan Reclamation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Reclamation), [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), and two other inconsequential cards. When Ben attempted to flash back his [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) to kill his Rector, Matt responded with [Krosan Reclamation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Reclamation) to shuffle both the second Therapy and the Rector into the library. With no recourse left in the game, Ben scooped.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a974.jpg)Matt Noble 1 – Ben Stark 0

### Game 2

Both players kept, and Ben came out swinging with a first turn [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), naming [Oath of Druids](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oath+of+Druids). It was a swing and a miss, as Matt's hand contained [Foil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Foil), [Moment's Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace), [Polluted Delta](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta), [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge), [Yavimaya Coast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yavimaya+Coast), [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), and [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest). On the very next turn, with the board clear of non-land permanents, Matt ripped Oath off the top of the deck. Ben slumped back into his chair slightly demoralized. He untapped, cast [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), and found the [Ray of Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation) he needed to get rid of the enemy enchantment.

Ben came out full throttle next turn, summoning [Nantuko Husk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Husk) and [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) with the game-winning [Academy Rector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Academy+Rector) in hand. He flashed back his Therapy, sacrificing his Elves after tapping them for a mana. In response, Noble cast one of his two [Accumulated Knowledge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge)s, and lost the second one to the discard spell. Stark attempted to cast [Academy Rector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Academy+Rector) with a green floating, hoping to draw his opponent in an ill-advised [Force Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+Spike)—but Matt caught this quickly. He instead responded with [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), which netted him the [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) he needed to [Foil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Foil) the white creature.

Matt: "So you've got a green floating, right?"  
 Ben: (Smiling) "I was hoping to be the [Trickster Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trickster+Mage). I hope that one point of mana burn doesn't come back to bite me."

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a973.jpg)Stark kept on drawing lands, emptying his hand to play both [Phyrexian Ghoul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Ghoul) and [Nantuko Husk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Husk), against a full grip including [Moment's Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) and several card drawers. Ben's second [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) of the game revealed the choices of [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), [Birds of Paradise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) and flotsam, none of which would help him over the next few turns.

[Pernicious Deed](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pernicious+Deed) wiped the board clear of zombies, and then the control player cast [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) to nab a [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell). Ben used [Windswept Heath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Windswept+Heath) to get rid of the his upcoming useless cards, and promptly drew two straight lands. He attempted to cast his Therapy, but it was countered—as Ben extended his hand to concede, Matt flashed me a hand of double [Forbid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forbid) and [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak).

**Final Result:** Matt Noble 2 – Ben Stark 0
