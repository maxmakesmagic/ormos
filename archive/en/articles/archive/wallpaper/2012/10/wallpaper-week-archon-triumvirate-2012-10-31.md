Wallpaper of the Week: Archon of the Triumvirate
================================================



 Posted in **Wallpaper**
 on October 31, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Welcome to **Wallpaper of the Week**! It's Azorius Week, and we've got an Azorius wallpaper for you! And if that's not enough, [the Azorius page](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/productarticle.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/guilds/azorius) has a fan kit that includes wallpapers for [Isperia, Supreme Judge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Isperia%2C+Supreme+Judge), [New Prahv Guildmage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=New+Prahv+Guildmage), and [Hallowed Fountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hallowed+Fountain)! But first, we've got David Rapoza's majestic [Archon of the Triumvirate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archon+of+the+Triumvirate). Enjoy.


Check out the [Azorius Fan Kit](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/productarticle.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/guilds/azorius) for the other wallpapers!
