Pro Tour-Kyoto is the latest Pro Tour to hit the books, and what a record setter! Former Player of the Year and Pro Tour superstar Gabriel Nassif of France took the tournament down after a grueling finals match against one of the hottest players in the game right now, Luis Scott-Vargas. The deck Gabe decided to play? His own take on Five-Color Control! Make sure to [check out all the coverage from the event](/en/events/coverage/nassif-greater-power), including a video interview with Gabriel about his deck.
