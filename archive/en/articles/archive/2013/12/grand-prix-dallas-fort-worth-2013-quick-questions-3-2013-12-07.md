GRAND PRIX DALLAS-FORT WORTH 2013 - QUICK QUESTIONS #3
======================================================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on December 8, 2013 











![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpdfw13/melissa_qh.jpg)***Pro Tour *Gatecrash* Quarterfinalist Melissa DeTora** : I would probably say Mono-Blue.* 

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpdfw13/owen_qh.jpg)***No. 12 Ranked Player Owen Turtenwald**: Mono-Black for sure.* 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 



![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpdfw13/stern_qh.jpg)***Grand Prix Atlantic City 2013 Champion Jon Stern**: I think the best deck in Standard is Mono-Black. But it's close with Mono-Blue.* 

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpdfw13/fennell_qh.jpg)***Grand Prix Indianapolis 2012 Finalist Chris Fennell**: I consider Mono-Black Devotion to be the best right now. I think it's the most consistent.* 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 



![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpdfw13/shahar_qh.jpg)***No. 4 Ranked Player Shahar Shenhar**: That's a close one. I think it's between Mono-Blue and Mono-Black and I can't honestly tell you which one. It matters so much what you're playing against in the actual tournament.* 

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpdfw13/huey_qh.jpg)***Hall of Famer William “Huey” Jensen**: Mono-Black Devotion.*
