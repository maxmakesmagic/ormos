Video Deck Tech: White Weenie with Pat Cox
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 11, 2011 










Fresh off his victory at the Star City Games Invitational, Pat Cox shares his deck choice for Pro Tour Nagoya: White Weenie featuring [Puresteel Paladin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Puresteel+Paladin). Designed by PT champion Mark Herberholz, Cox aims to equip a [Sword of War and Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+War+and+Peace+)  as soon as possible.
