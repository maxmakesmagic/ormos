*Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* Set Mastery
======================================


* 24x *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* boosters
* 5 Mastery Orbs (each can be redeemed for a card style on the *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* Mastery Tree)

How many levels are there in *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* Set Mastery?
====================================================================


The *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* Set Mastery goes up to level 60. All players receive rewards through level 48, while players with the Mastery Pass will receive rewards up to level 60, and beyond!
