Extended is a format known for crazy combo decks, and the recent Pro Tour–Austin didn't disappoint! Kuan Tian, who already has a respectable deck design pedigree, combined [Thopter Foundry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thopter+Foundry) and [Sword of the Meek](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+the+Meek) to gain tons of 1/1 flyers. He spliced the combo into a [Gifts Ungiven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven) control skeleton and managed to finish in Day 2! Read more from Pro Tour–Austin in the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/kibler-completes-comeback-austin-victory).
