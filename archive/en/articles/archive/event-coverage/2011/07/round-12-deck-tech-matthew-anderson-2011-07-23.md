Round 12: Deck Tech Matthew Anderson
====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 23, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170727014735im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Walkinshaw-Profile.jpg?itok=d6FJTN90)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw











In the [Mass Polymorph](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mass+Polymorph) Deck Tech with Dylan Brown, I mentioned that he had won a Grinder on Friday with a Goblin deck that featured [Pyretic Ritual](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyretic+Ritual), which prompted this tweet:


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/aunat11/02_r12_twitter.jpg)
 


So this morning, I found Dylan and asked him. "Haha, it's his deck!" Dylan smiled, pointing to the pony-tailed gent he'd been conversing with, "ask him."


Matthew Anderson was sitting at 6-1 at the end of day one, and was more than happy to talk about [Pyretic Ritual](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyretic+Ritual). "It's amazing. There's nothing better than turn one [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide), turn two Ritual, [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide), and a kicked [Goblin Bushwhacker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Bushwhacker). You're on 10!" he cackled. I got the feeling more than one player had been defeated in this manner already this weekend, however unlikely that sequence of events seemed. "Turn three [Hero of Oxid Ridge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero+of+Oxid+Ridge) is great too, sending the team past any [Spellskite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite)s or [Overgrown Battlement](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Battlement)s."





|  |

| 
*Matt Anderson is looking like a very solid lock for the top 8 going into the last round.* |


 


"I've paid for [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak) with [Pyretic Ritual](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyretic+Ritual), and it's also great with [Shrine of Burning Rage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shrine+of+Burning+Rage). I even managed to activate a Shrine with only two lands untapped when someone tried to kill it, it's awesome."







#### Matthew Anderson
