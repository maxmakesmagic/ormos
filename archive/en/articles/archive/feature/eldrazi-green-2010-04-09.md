 Named *before* the Eldrazi became a known entity, Eldrazi Green is all about the fatties. Well, that is to say, fatties you can make yourself. Tons of tokens combine to become unkillable threats after the resolution of [Eldrazi Monument](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Monument). [Leatherback Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leatherback+Baloth) can simply beat down in a pinch, and [Wolfbriar Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolfbriar+Elemental) and [Nissa Revane](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa+Revane) help keep Jund on the back foot by acting as problematic threats the black-red-green deck struggles to deal with. It's the perfect deck for your Friday Night **Magic**, and who knows how good it becomes when [the *real* Eldrazi](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/riseoftheeldrazi) show up beginning April 17!
