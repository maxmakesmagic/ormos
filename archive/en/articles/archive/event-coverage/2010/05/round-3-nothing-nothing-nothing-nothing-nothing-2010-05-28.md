Round 3: "Nothing Nothing Nothing Nothing Nothing ...."
=======================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 28, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett











### Gabe Walls (Green-Blue Control) vs. Travis Woo (Red-Green Mana Denial)


It was Brew versus Brew in Round 3. Perpetually jolly Gabe Walls was playing a mostly blue green-blue deck concocted with Gab Nassif, stocked with permission, an Oracle eight-pack ([Sea Gate Oracle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Sea%2BGate%2BOracle) AND [Mul Daya Oracle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Mul%2BDaya%2BOracle)) and backed up with six creature-stealing enchantments. Travis Woo (best known for his breakout [Living End](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End) deck at GP–Oakland), was piloting an unorthodox red-green mana-denial deck. Woo's deck aims to punish the mana-hungry big players of the format through [Roiling Terrain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roiling+Terrain), [Goblin Ruinblaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ruinblaster) and [Lodestone Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lodestone+Golem), accelerated out by [Lotus Cobra](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Cobra), [Nest Invader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nest+Invader), and [Overgrown Battlement](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Battlement). On the play it is capable of some cruel starts.


### Game 1


Walls won the die roll and led with [Khalni Garden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Khalni+Garden). He had [Spreading Seas](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spreading+Seas) for Woo's [Raging Ravine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine). Woo made a [Lotus Cobra](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Cobra), and after Walls played [Halimar Depths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Halimar+Depths), dropped [Roiling Terrain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roiling+Terrain) on the Garden. Walls replaced it with an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) and passed. Woo made a second Cobra, and with the extra mana tried a [Lodestone Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lodestone+Golem), but Walls had the [Cancel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cancel).


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptsj10/r3_walls_woo.jpg)*Gabe Walls and Travis Woo: brew vs. brew.*  
[Sea Gate Oracle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sea+Gate+Oracle) netted Walls a card, and he played an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island). Woo tapped six and swung in with [Hellkite Charger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hellkite+Charger), dropping Walls to ten. All Walls could do was play a [Halimar Depths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Halimar+Depths) and send the Charger back with [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor). Woo brought it back for an encore and swung in, killing Jace. This time, however, Walls had access to five mana, and was able to [Vapor Snare](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vapor+Snare) the Charger to his side. Woo had a second waiting in the wings, and suddenly Walls was down to five.


Walls picked up his island for [Vapor Snare](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vapor+Snare) and Domesticated one of Woo's Cobras. Woo still had a ton of gas in hand. He counted his mana, then played [Scalding Tarn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scalding+Tarn) and [Vengevine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vengevine). With his leftover mana he was able to activate [Raging Ravine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine) and swing with the whole team. Walls pushed the stolen Hellkite in front of its brother, the Plant token blocked the Ravine, and the Oracle and Cobra teamed up on [Vengevine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vengevine).


Walls was left alive with an empty board. He untapped, drew, and then played another [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) and [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed: Nothing that could stop the now-lethal Ravine.


Travis Woo 1, Gabe Walls 0


### Game 2


Two fetches got Woo a mountain and forest, enabling a [Lotus Cobra](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Cobra). Walls played his third land and passed with three blue open. Woo tried [Lodestone Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lodestone+Golem), but was [Cancel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cancel)led. Walls untapped and brought the Cobra over to his side with [Domestication](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domestication).


Woo kicked up a [Goblin Ruinblaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ruinblaster) on [Halimar Depths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Halimar+Depths) and dropped Walls to 16. Now hidden behind the Cobra, Walls cast [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) and [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed. Woo traded the Ruinblaster for the traitorous Cobra, then knocked two loyalty off Jace with [Roiling Terrain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roiling+Terrain) and played a [Raging Ravine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine). 


Walls [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed and brought out more speedbumps: [Sea Gate Oracle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sea+Gate+Oracle) and a plant from [Khalni Garden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Khalni+Garden). Woo animated his [Raging Ravine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine), ate the Plant, and played a second Ravine, but without more pressure he was giving Walls a lot of time. Jace peeked at the top of Woo's library and let him keep the card. Walls played a [Tectonic Edge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tectonic+Edge), further tightening his grip.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptsj10/r3_walls.jpg)*Walls—sorry, that's 'creatures with defender.'*  
Woo tried to break through with [Hellkite Charger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hellkite+Charger), but this time Walls had [Deprive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deprive) ready, picking up his Garden for an added bonus. [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) continued to fateseal Woo, keeping him off dangerous cards. He animated his Ravine and Walls destroyed it with the Edge. Walls did, however, let him enjoy a [Nest Invader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nest+Invader). So that was something.


Walls had to put his next fateseal on the bottom of Woo's library, giving him a live draw, but had the [Cancel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cancel) in hand for what turned out to be a second [Hellkite Charger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hellkite+Charger). Wall restocked with [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) from Jace and an [Oracle of Mul Daya](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle+of+Mul+Daya), getting a second [Tectonic Edge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tectonic+Edge) from the top of his library. Woo struggled to find any action. His Ruinblaster forced Walls to use his Edge, and then the following turn used [Burst Lightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning) to put Jace down to three loyalty, but the game was firmly under Walls's control. Soon [Avenger of Zendikar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avenger+of+Zendikar) made an appearance, and it was on to Game 3.


Travis Woo 1, Gabe Walls 1


"That's a really cool deck," said Woo.


"Yours too," grinned Walls.


### Game 3


As Woo played his second land Walls started chanting "Nothing nothing nothing nothing nothing ..." and when Woo paused, "No Cobra, no Cobra, [Nest Invader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nest+Invader)." Woo broke his heart by showing him a Cobra. Walls played a second island. Woo cracked a fetchland and spent two on [Everflowing Chalice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Everflowing+Chalice), then tapped out for [Roiling Terrain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roiling+Terrain), but Walls had [Spell Pierce](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Pierce).


Walls played a [Misty Rainforest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misty+Rainforest) and passed the turn. Woo hit for two and played [Nest Invader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nest+Invader). Walls gummed up the ground with [Sea Gate Oracle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sea+Gate+Oracle) and [Khalni Garden](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Khalni+Garden). That was fine by Woo, who went overhead with [Hellkite Charger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hellkite+Charger), dropping Walls to ten.


Walls frowed at his options. He played Jace and bounced the Charger, playing an untapped [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island). Woo gamely replayed it and offed the planeswalker. Walls had a replacement in hand, and made the same play, this time ending with two untapped [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)s. That made Woo pause, and reconsider his plan.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptsj10/r3_woo.jpg)*Woo closes in.*  
He came out of the tank and kicked up [Goblin Ruinblaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ruinblaster), then animated his [Raging Ravine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine) and sent everyone charging at Jace. Walls picked off the [Lotus Cobra](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Cobra) with his [Sea Gate Oracle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sea+Gate+Oracle) and put Jace in the grumper. He untapped and played [Oracle of Mul Daya](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle+of+Mul+Daya). The [Everflowing Chalice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Everflowing+Chalice) on top of his deck didn't interest him, so he shuffled it away with [Misty Rainforest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misty+Rainforest). He was rewarded with [Tectonic Edge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tectonic+Edge), and then [Halimar Depths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Halimar+Depths).


Woo looked at the board and decided it was time to try his Charger again. To his surprise, Walls had been bluffing the [Deprive](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deprive), and since he knew the top of his deck, he scooped up his cards, dead to the incoming Charger in two attacks.


Travis Woo 2, Gabe Walls 1


Afterwards Travis Woo explained that he's always had a soft spot for land destruction, and can't help but try it in any format. Luckily, this time, his testing showed it to be good. [Lodestone Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lodestone+Golem) in particular is brutal to so many decks, and even dodges [All Is Dust](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=All+Is+Dust).
