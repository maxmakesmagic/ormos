Draft 1: Richard Grace
======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 4, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_1.png)
By Dan Gow











Start in seat 6 in between Chris Nadebaum and Domonic Lo, Richard's first 2 picks ([Mask of Memory](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mask+of+Memory) and [Myr Mindservant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Mindservant)) didn't commit him to a specific colour. After noting that both player either side him were committing to Green, settled nicely in black white, picking up [Slith Ascendant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slith+Ascendant) and [Terror](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terror). He continued to take solid white picks with a [Leonin Skyhunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Skyhunter), [Leonin Sun Standard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Sun+Standard), and [Titanium Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Titanium+Golem).

In the second pack he picked up an [Arrest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrest) x2, an [Icy Manipulator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Icy+Manipulator), [Pewter Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pewter+Golem), and a very late [Relic Bane](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Relic+Bane). Nadebaum seemed to move into white (or at the very least wanted to keep his options open), which saw Grace return the favour by countering with [Turn to Dust](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turn+to+Dust), [Elf Replica](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elf+Replica), and [Deconstruct](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deconstruct). This caused his picks to thin a little in the third pack only managing an [Irradiate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Irradiate), a [Skyhunter Cub](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyhunter+Cub) and a second [Leonin Den-Guard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Den-Guard).
