The captain of the U.S. National Team, Josh Utter-Leyton had quite the World Championships for 2010. In the Extended portion of the event, Josh, who is nicknamed "Wrapter" for his **Magic Online** name, decided to run Five-Color Cruel Control. Named for its powerful [Cruel Ultimatum](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Ultimatum)s and somewhat neurotic mana base, the deck has proved powerful in previous formats. Read more Worlds in the archive [here](/en/events/coverage/theres-no-jace-guillaume).
