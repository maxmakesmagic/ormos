### DAILY DECK


Esper Control from Hareru-ya
============================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/shuhei_authorpic.jpg)

Shuhei Nakamura

Shuhei Nakamura is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, former Player of the Year, and is one of just six players with more than 400 lifetime pro points.


October 7, 2014
 










Standard! Standard!! Standard!!!


With the release of *Khans of Tarkir*, we have entered a new environment.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Elspeth, Sun's Champion)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth, Sun's Champion)
From now on, we need not worry about getting our [Mutavault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutavault) accidentally killed, or despairing in the face of a plague of [Pack Rat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat)s, or watching helplessly as our creatures are mercilessly judged with a [Supreme Verdict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Supreme+Verdict). Oh, and no more sudden [Sphinx's Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%27s+Revelation), either.


To coincide with the rollout of a new Standard, I plan on focusing this week on new decks from that format. Today's deck is one from Card Shop Hareru-ya (more popularly known as "Saitō's shop") in Tōkyō.


At Hareru-ya, they run three FNM tournaments, at 14:00, 17:00, and 20:00. This deck, an Esper control deck by Hiroaki Yamakawa, went [6–0 in games at the 14:00 tournament](http://www.happymtg.com/decks/view/D070268) last week.


I guess this is what Esper looks like in the new Standard world: removal, draw spells, and a little countermagic. The win condition, [Elspeth, Sun's Champion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Sun%27s+Champion), is strong in any environment.


One thing to note is Yamakawa's fresh perspective in including [Singing Bell Strike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Singing+Bell+Strike) as removal. Oh, and [Empty the Pits](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Empty+the+Pits). Combined with all the draw spells in the deck, it can lead to an explosive end step.
