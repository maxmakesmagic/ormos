THE SHEER SIZE OF IT
====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX NEW JERSEY 2014 - COVERAGE - EVENTS](/en/events/coverage/gpnj14)
 on November 15, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/080214_1291.jpg)
By Jacob Van Lunen




Jacob Van Lunen began playing Magic in 1995. He has participated in organized play at every level of competition and was a member of the winning team at Pro Tour San Diego in 2007, thanks to an innovative draft strategy. As a writer, Van Lunen has had more than three hundred Magic strategy pieces published 





With nearly four thousand players in the main event, and likely another thousand in the room, Grand Prix New Jersey is an impressively huge event. I took a stroll around the convention center during the fourth round in an attempt to fully capture the sheer size of this weekend's Legacy Grand Prix!


I started my mid afternoon walk in front of the stage.
