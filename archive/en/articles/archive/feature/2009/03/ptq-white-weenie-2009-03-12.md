What's that? You didn't think White Weenie could be a viable Extended deck for the Pro Tour Qualifier-Honolulu season? Mr. Andrew Wagner would ask you to guess again as he took his list of the age-old deck to a Top 8 at a recent PTQ. With plenty of maindeck hate for the format in the likes of [Ethersworn Canonist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ethersworn+Canonist), [Kataki, War's Wage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kataki%2C+War%27s+Wage), and [Burrenton Forge-Tender](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burrenton+Forge-Tender), Andrew charged right through the naysayers all day long.
