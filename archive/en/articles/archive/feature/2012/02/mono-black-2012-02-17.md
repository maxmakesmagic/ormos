Mono Black
==========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 17, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











A lot of players were glued to Pro Tour *Dark Ascension* coverage to see if a Zombie deck would show up in Standard. Pro Tour newcomer Jasper Johnson-Epstein finally unleashed the Zombie horde people had been waiting for, placing in the Top 50 using several cards from the shambling tribe.

Jasper's deck can quickly push a lot of power onto the table with eleven one-drop creatures, threatening a swarm early. His deck backs that up with [Porcelain Legionnaire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Porcelain+Legionnaire) and [Geralf's Messenger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geralf%27s+Messenger) to continue beating down.

One of the huge role players here is the underused [Skirsdag High Priest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirsdag+High+Priest). The High Priest gives the deck some long-game power alongside the recursive [Gravecrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravecrawler), and making sure the morbid ability is active is easy with [Mortarpod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mortarpod) and [Fume Spitter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fume+Spitter) around. It doesn't take much effort for this deck to spit out a legion of 5/5 demons that will quickly devour unprepared opponents.

Between aggressive creatures, quality removal, and a strong long game, Jasper Johnson-Epstein has assembled something fearsomeâ€”and fun to play, too! If you've been looking for a fun and powerful deck for tonight's Friday Night **Magic**, listen to [what Jasper has to say on the deck](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LhkS7JiQGUI) and give this a try! And don't miss the [Friday Night Countdown](http://youtu.be/DiaORgqnoTU) for plenty of decklists and advice from the best in the game!
