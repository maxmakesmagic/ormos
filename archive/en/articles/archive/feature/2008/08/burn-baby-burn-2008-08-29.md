Serious about being a burn deck? Matteo Orsini-Jones took that concept to the highest level at the 2008 Great Britain National Championships. Matteo's Top 8 finishing list featured a whopping ZERO creatures maindeck; his primary focus was on comboing his opponent using burn spells and [Pyromancer's Swath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer%27s+Swath)! Now THAT'S a burn deck!
