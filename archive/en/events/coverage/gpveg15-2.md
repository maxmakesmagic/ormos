TOP 8 COVERAGE



There’s never been another weekend like this one. Thousands of players around the world joined together in Chiba, Utrecht, and here, in Las Vegas, to celebrate *Magic*. Yes, there was a tournament—two, in fact—of historic proportions. But there were also dozens of artists, cosplayers, fans, art lovers, fathers and sons, husbands and wives, brothers and sisters who came to Las Vegas to play, trade, discuss, and enjoy the game that binds us all.


At the end of the weekend, two trophies were raised. In the second Grand Prix, Scott Markeson, a Minnesotan who cruised through Day 1, also cruised through the Top 8. He was one of three players counting on the Affinity archetype to carry him to victory, and carry him it did.


Markeson will certainly be remembered for his victory, but the memories that nearly 8,000 players—not to mention judges, staff, visitors, and the curious—will stay with us a lifetime. We met the *Magic* community up close and personal. Just 20,000 of our closest friends, hanging out and playing *Magic* for the weekend.
