Looking at Takenuma
===================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 24, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Here's Takenuma, the new planar card previewed in [Tuesday's Serious Fun](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/sf/196).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/tcg/products/pc2/x82u3qdcaj_lg.jpg)  
And here's that art without that pesky frame in the way.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/979_takenuma.jpg)  
Takenuma is a bamboo swamp on the plane of Kamigawa. And that brings up something you might not know about the Kamigawa swamps.

They're done as murals! If you had the art all together, you could see one continuous piece of art. So here are the four separate *Champions of Kamigawa* [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=79065) illustrations. Note that this also appears to be some kind of bamboo-based swamp.
