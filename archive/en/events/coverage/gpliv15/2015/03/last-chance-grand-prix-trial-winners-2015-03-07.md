Last-Chance Grand Prix Trial Winners
====================================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on March 7, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











 As always, Friday saw a number of Grand Prix Trials being held. Those grueling 32-player single-elimination events marked the last chance for players to earn two byes for the weekend's main event—and the first chance for us to get an impression of what it takes nowadays to win in the *Fate Reforged*/*Khans of Tarkir* Sealed Deck format.



Check out the following decklists. Each took their respective pilot to a 5-0 finish, but the similarities stop there: We have four-color decks, straight clan decks, and even one blue-white deck here!
