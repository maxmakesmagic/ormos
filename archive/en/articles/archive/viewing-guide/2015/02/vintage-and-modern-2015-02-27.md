After a few weeks packed with Twitch content, we enter a brief lull for a week before returning with a minimum of three major entries.



![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/MatchupsSlide_Week5.jpg)



Week 5 of the [Vintage Super League](http://VintageSuperLeague.com/) once again features the new lineup of decks in action, as well as a matchup between the league's two remaining undefeated players. Chris "Meddling Mage" Pikula and Eric "EFro" Froehlich face off to see who will retain their status of all wins and no losses.



As always, the full decklists for the players in this round of league action can be found [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/vintage-super-league-season-2-decklists).



Join host Randy Buehler and the rest of the Vintage Super League crew on the [*Magic* Twitch channel](http://www.twitch.tv/magic) on Tuesday, March 3 at 6 p.m. PT/9 p.m. ET/2 a.m. GMT to catch all of Week 5's matches!



![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/images/daily/openSeriesBanner.png)



Meanwhile, Modern mayhem continues, this time on the [StarCityGames.Com Open Series](http://www.starcitygames.com/pages/scgop/open_series), which makes a stop in Baltimore this weekend. It's the first Open Series event to highlight Modern as the premier event, so it's sure to be an exciting one!



Join hosts Patrick Sullivan and Matthias Hunt for all things Modern on the [SCGLive Twitch channel](http://www.twitch.tv/scglive) starting Saturday at 7:30 a.m. PT/10:30 a.m. ET/3:30 a.m. GMT. Day Two coverage begins Sunday at 6 a.m. PT/9 a.m. ET/2 p.m. GMT.
