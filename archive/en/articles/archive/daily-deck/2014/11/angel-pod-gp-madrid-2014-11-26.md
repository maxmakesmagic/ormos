### DAILY DECK


Angel Pod at GP Madrid
======================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/shuhei_authorpic.jpg)

Shuhei Nakamura

Shuhei Nakamura is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, former Player of the Year, and is one of just six players with more than 400 lifetime pro points.


November 26, 2014
 










While it was overwhelmed in the news by the 4,000-player [Grand Prix New Jersey](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpnj14) held the same weekend, [Grand Prix Madrid](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpmad14) was no slouch with 1,900 players participatingâ€”1,900 players!


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Archangel of Thune)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel of Thune)


More importantly, Madrid was run using one of the same formats that will be on display at next week's World Championship: Modern. For the next few days, we'll be looking at some of the top decks from Madrid. First off is Kevin Grove's [Birthing Pod](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod) deck, the archetype most represented in that event's Top 8.


While we talk about "[Birthing Pod](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod) decks" a lot, there are in fact a lot of variations on the deck. What they have in common is a decent number of mana creatures, plus a large variety of creatures. Obviously, they all have the eponymous card. Finally, there is usually some form of "infinite" combo, which is triggered off of two or three different creatures, such as [Restoration Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Restoration+Angel) and [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiki-Jiki%2C+Mirror+Breaker), or [Melira, Sylvok Outcast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Melira%2C+Sylvok+Outcast) plus [Viscera Seer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viscera+Seer) and creature with persist.


The combo in this version is an infinite life combo, achieved through the combination of [Spike Feeder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spike+Feeder) and [Archangel of Thune](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+of+Thune). But, as you can see from the list, that's just a side dish. The main dish of this deck is actually midrange beatdown. I personally like this sort of deck, where there is a game-winning strategy separate from the combo. If you manage to get the combo, great, but the deck can still function without it.
