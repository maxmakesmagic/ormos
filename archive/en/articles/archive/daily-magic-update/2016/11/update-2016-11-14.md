The November 14, 2016 Update
============================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**

Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever.

What You Might Have Missed This Weekend
=======================================


1. [Top 5 Moments of Grand Prix Rotterdam 2016](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gprot16/top-5-moments-of-grand-prix-rotterdam-2016-11-13) | Wizards of the Coast | Tobi Henke

Draft mastery, a storm combo kill, and a Hall of Fame pro returning to the finals stage—with two of his best friends. Team Grand Prix are the best, and this article captures the best of the weekend.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Abzan Delirium](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/displaydeck.php?DeckID=109540)

James Gibbs finished in 5th place with this host of one-ofs at the StarCityGames.com Standard Classic in Columbus this past weekend. It is, for the most part, a Black-Green Delirium deck like the ones that have been on top of Standard for a while, only spiced up with a bunch of singletons and two-ofs and some powerful white cards.


No one is sleeping on [Archangel Avacyn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+Avacyn), but the first time its flipped with [Grapple with the Past](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grapple+with+the+Past), your opponent's eyes are going to bug out. And [Anguished Unmaking](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anguished+Unmaking) is just the best possible removal spell in all worlds for a deck that wants the best possible removal spells.


The deck is kind of like mashing together the White-Black [Thalia's Lancers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thalia%27s+Lancers) deck and Delirium, and then splitting [Painful Truths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Painful+Truths) and [Live Fast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Live+Fast) just because. It's a bit Wild West, but it is clearly effective.
