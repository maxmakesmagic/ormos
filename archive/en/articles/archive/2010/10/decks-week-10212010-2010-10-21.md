Decks of the Week: 10/21/2010
=============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on October 21, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast













The qualifier season for Pro Tour–Paris continues, and *Scars of Mirrodin* Limited is proving to be a format that asks some tough—and unusual—questions, from the role of non-infect creatures in an infect-heavy to deck to something as fundamental as how many lands to play. In three different North America PTQs, Top 8 drafters gave different answers to these questions.


Find a qualifier near you and get your invitation to Pro Tour–Paris and the first **Magic** Weekend of 2011!
