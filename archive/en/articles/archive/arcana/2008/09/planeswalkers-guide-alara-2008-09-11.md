*A Planeswalker's Guide to Alara*
=================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 11, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











We'd like to take a moment to introduce you to the authors of *A Planeswalker's Guide to Alara*. You might know them already. Say hello to Doug Beyer and Jenna Helland!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1675_both.jpg)  
Naturally, we can't just give you the whole book for free, but Doug and Jenna were willing to give you a peek at the contents. Doug, show 'em some of Grixis!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1675_doug.jpg)  
Ooh, demony! Jenna, I believe you wanted to show off some of Naya?

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1675_jenna.jpg)  
Delicious! To see these pages in full size, along with the rest of the book, we encourage you to rush out to your local bookstore and buy a copy. More information about the book is [available here](http://ww2.wizards.com/books/Wizards/Products/?doc=219957400).
