The 2014 Holiday Card
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 2, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_mikemcartor.jpg)
By Mike McArtor




 Mike first played with Ice Age and became the copy editor for DailyMTG.com in December 2011. Before DailyMTG, he was an editor on D&D's Dragon magazine for four years. 






Ever since 2006, we have distributed silver-bordered Holiday Cards to employees and certain partners of Wizards of the Coast. This year's card is Mishra’s Toy Workshop.


 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/Mishras-Toy-Workshop.png)
