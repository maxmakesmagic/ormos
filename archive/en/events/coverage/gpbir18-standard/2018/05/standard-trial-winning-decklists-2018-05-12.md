Standard Trial Winning Decklists
================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 12, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke

Their final chance was our first, that is, to see which decks might be successful over the course of the weekend. Check out the winning lists of Standard here!
