Anton Jonsson, who was whispered as a possible inclusion for the 2010 Hall of Fame, came out of retirement in his native Sweden to battle at the recent Grand Prix–Gothenburg. Always famous as a drafter, he proved he still had it ripping off a Finals appearance in the hands of his white-blue draft deck before losing to eventual champion Kenny Oberg. Read full coverage of the event [here](/en/events/coverage/tezzerator-dominates-gothenburg).
