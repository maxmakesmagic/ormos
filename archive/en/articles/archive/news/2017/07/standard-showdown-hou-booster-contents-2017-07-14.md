Standard Showdown: HOU Booster Contents
=======================================



 Posted in **News**
 on July 14, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Standard Showdown: *Hour of Devastation* packs contain one more card than previously announced, including 1 premium foil rare or mythic rare from a currently legal Standard set.


Each **5-card** *Hour of Devastation* Standard Showdown booster includes:


* 2 rare or mythic rare cards from a currently legal Standard set
* 1 *Hour of Devastation* full-art land
* 1 premium common or uncommon from a currently legal Standard set or *Hour of Devastation* full-art land
* 1 premium foil rare or mythic rare from a currently legal Standard set

\*Standard Showdown: *Hour of Devastation* booster packs do not include double-faced cards


The five-card boosters are only for the *Hour of Devastation* Standard Showdown boosters, so be sure to check with your local store to learn more about [Standard Showdown events in your area](http://magic.wizards.com/en/event-types/standard-showdown), which kick off July 22!
