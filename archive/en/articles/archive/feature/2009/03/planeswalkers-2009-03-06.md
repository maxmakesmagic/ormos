Planeswalker Control was one of the more exciting decks to come from the 2008 State Championships tournaments, and at least one player saw fit to play the deck at Pro Tour-Kyoto. His name? AJ Sacher, and he posted a very respectable 55th place finish with the deck. Want to learn how to play it? Easy! Just make sure to [check out all the coverage from the event](/en/events/coverage/nassif-greater-power), which includes a video interview from AJ on how to play Planeswalker Control!
