Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 27, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











Learn more about Grand Prix Indianapolis' Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they've done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!


  
**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpind17/t8_boggemes.jpg)Name:**Kyle Boggemes


**Age:** 28


**Hometown:** Ann Arbor, Michigan


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


2nd Pro Tour San Diego 2010, 1st Grand Prix Cincinnati, 4th Grand Prix D.C. 2010, 9th Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir*


**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?**


Black-Green splash Red, 7-2


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck and why?** 


[Hapatra, Vizier of Poisons](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hapatra%2C+Vizier+of+Poisons)


**What were your draft decks, and records with them?**


Red-Green Aggro, three [Ambuscade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ambuscade), two Synchronous Strike  

Mono-Red, 16 Mountains


**What was the best card in either of your Draft decks and why?**


[Ambuscade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ambuscade) in the first deck; [Firebrand Archer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebrand+Archer) in the second deck.


**What’s your story? What should people know about you?**


I work as a financial analyst so I don’t travel a ton. I’m not great at Limited. Used to play professionally.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


I wasn’t Silver for not showing up to Japan, so it’s cool to requalify. It’s also my frist Limited Premier Event Top 8. I consider myself a Constructed player.
