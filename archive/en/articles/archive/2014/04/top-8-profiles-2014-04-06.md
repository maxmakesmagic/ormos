**Age:** 36  
**Hometown:** Mesa, AZ  
**Occupation:** Engineer (Software/Electrical)

Jund Good Stuff - It matches well against the field with Esper/Black Devotion/RG Monsters so prevalent. It is also a lot of fun.

Playing with friends and at FNM.
