Preparing your Standard deck for the upcoming Regionals tournaments? Check out Michal Swierczynski's aggressive Bant list from a recent Nationals Qualifier in Poland. [Jhessian Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jhessian+Infiltrator), [Rhox War Monk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhox+War+Monk), and [Rafiq of the Many](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rafiq+of+the+Many) are some of the most powerful creatures in their colors. [Snakeform](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snakeform) and [Behemoth Sledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Behemoth+Sledge) provide spell support, and the list was good enough to finish in the Top 8. For more information on Regionals, [visit the Regionals page](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/regionals).
