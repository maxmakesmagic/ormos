The US National Championship has concluded for 2011! American standout Ali Aintrazi took the top title with a powerful Blue-Black Control list. Aintrazi will lead a team that includes David Ochoa (in his second appearance representing America), Haibing Hu, and Brandon Nelson as the alternate. Read more in the [official coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/2011-us-national-championship-alternate-style-2011-08-06-0) of the event from the **DailyMTG.com** coverage team.
