**Standard PTQ for Pro Tour *Ixalan* Saturday, July 29**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, July 29 at 9 a.m. PT. There is no need to qualify through a Preliminary – simply join this event with 30 Event Tickets, 300 Play Points or a spare Blue Constructed PTQ Finals Entry Token!


Learn more about Magic Online Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

***Magic Online* Format Challenges back this weekend**


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges introduced a new prize structure and recurring weekend events for Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper formats! Check out Lee Sharpe's [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-format-challenges) for specific details.


Events begin this Saturday, July 29 at 8:00 a.m. PT.

**Extended downtime tomorrow, July 26**


There will be an extended downtime tomorrow, July 26 (3 a.m. PT/10:00 p.m. UTC). The next two downtimes will be August 23 and September 20, both of which will be extended (3 a.m. PT/10:00 p.m. UTC).


Follow *Magic Online*'s [Twitter](https://twitter.com/MagicOnline) account for live updates during downtime.

**Build Notes**


Client | A rare crash issue with Windows 10 Creator has been fixed.


General | Magic Online player rewards for July have been granted. Those cards are listed in [this article.](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-announcements-july-11-2017)


Client | Various issues that caused game crashes have been fixed.


Duel Scene | Emerge creatures can successfully be canceled in the middle of casting.


Limited | [Imaginary Threats](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imaginary+Threats)' effect shield for your opponent’s creatures is no longer imaginary.


Duel Scene | Eternalize and Embalm Tokens no longer have art issues.


Commander | The [Brothers Yamazaki](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brothers+Yamazaki) no longer disable the Legendary check for all of your creatures.


Commander | [Windfall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Windfall) no longer sees split cards as 3 separate cards.


Limited | [Ruination Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ruination+Guide) can now ingest again.


General | Various issues with Kamigawa Flip cards are now fixed.


Commander | Cards that have a cost of putting a card from your hand to your library now function correctly.


Commander | [Darksteel Mutation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Mutation) now works correctly when being put into play from zones other than your hand.


General | When your opponent is sacrificing a creature before you do, that creature is now highlighted for you.


Limited | A token copy of [Jeskai Infiltrator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jeskai+Infiltrator) now does not infiltrate your library with its trigger.


Limited | [Aphetto Dredging](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aphetto+Dredging) now correctly only returns creatures of a single creature type.


Duel Scene | Turn Ending effects now correctly removed delayed triggers. Forever.


General | [Kongming, "Sleeping Dragon"](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kongming%2C+%22Sleeping+Dragon%22) can now sleep soundly with his name being spelled correctly.


General | Planeswalkers that enter the battlefield after [The Chain Veil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Chain+Veil)'s activation can now correctly double tap.


General | [Chorus of the Conclave](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chorus+of+the+Conclave)'s static ability no longer causes a game reset.


Duel Scene | [Prized Amalgam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam)'s delayed trigger will no longer leave the Effects zone sometimes.
