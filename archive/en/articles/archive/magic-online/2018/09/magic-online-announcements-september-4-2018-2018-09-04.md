Legacy Cube Starts Tomorrow!
----------------------------



Do you like that one card, from that one set, and you just never see it anymore? Fear not, Legacy Cube starts tomorrow and you may see it again! Legacy Cube is full of awesome cards from all legal sets and gives you a chance to really diversify your play time. This starts tomorrow and ends September 27.

PTQ Saturday
------------



The Modern PTQ Finals originally scheduled for this Sunday will now be held this Saturday, September 8 at 9 a.m. PT. The winner qualifies for Pro Tour *Ravnica Allegiance*. How exciting! The entry options are 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).





---

RPTQ Sunday
-----------



A *Magic Online* Regional Pro Tour Qualifier (RPTQ) will take place this Sunday, September 9 at 5 a.m. PT and will use Standard format. These events are only open to players who qualified via in-store Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifier events, and pre-registration is required.


For more information on RPTQ events, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/regionalqualifiers).

Downtimes September 26 and October 24
-------------------------------------



The next two downtimes will be September 26, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC and October 24, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC. Please note that there are no more 'extended' or 'normal' downtimes; in the new world with fewer downtimes, they're all the same length of time.
