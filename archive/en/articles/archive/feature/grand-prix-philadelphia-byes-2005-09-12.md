Grand Prix Philadelphia Byes
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 12, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**Rating used**: Constructed and Eternal (Higher) Rating, September 7th, 2005  
**Pro Point Standings Used**: **Magic the Gathering** Pro Players Club. These byes are not represented in the tables following, but are awarded as follows:


***Third-Round Byes***

* Players with a Players Club level of 3 or higher
* DCI players with a Constructed or Eternal rating of 2025 or higher. Players receive the bye amount for the higher of the two ratings. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed and Eternal ratings published on September 7, 2005.

***Second-Round Byes***

* Players with a Players Club level of 2
* DCI players with a Constructed or Eternal rating of 1925 or higher. Players receive the bye amount for the higher of the two ratings. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed and Eternal ratings published on September 7, 2005.

***First-Round Byes***

* Players with a Players Club level of 1
* DCI players with a Constructed or Eternal rating of 1850 or higher. Players receive the bye amount for the higher of the two ratings. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI Constructed and Eternal ratings published on September 7, 2005.
