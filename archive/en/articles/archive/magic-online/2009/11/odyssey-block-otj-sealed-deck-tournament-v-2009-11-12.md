*Odyssey* Block (OTJ) Sealed Deck Tournament V
==============================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on November 12, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_352.png)
By Mike Gills











T*he meek shall inherit the Earth. But not Dominaria.*

Play with a favorite classic block and compete for great prizes!

### *Odyssey* Block Sealed Deck Tournament V

START TIME: Saturday November 21, 2009 11:00 AM PDT / 1800 UTC. 

SIZE: 33 players minimum, 512 players maximum.

DURATION: 6 rounds. Play in all six rounds, win or lose!

LOCATION: Premier Events room. 

ADMISSION: 30 Event Tickets (tournament product will be supplied) 

PRODUCT PROVIDED: 2 *Odyssey*™ (ODY) boosters, 2 *Torment™* (TOR) boosters, and 2 *Judgement™* (JUD) boosters. 

PRIZE SUPPORT: Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each loss awards 0 points.



|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Points** | **Product Prize** |
| 18 | 1 complete premium foil set – winner’s choice of any *Odyssey* block expansion |
| 15 |  15 booster packs (5 ODY, 5 TOR, 5 JUD) |
| 12 | 9 booster packs (3 ODY, 3 TOR, 3 JUD) |
| 9 | 3 booster packs (1 ODY, 1 TOR, 1 JUD) |

  
All players who participate in the *Odyssey* Block Sealed Deck Tournament V will receive a copy of the following previously released avatars: Flametongue Kavu (MTGO 2nd Anniversary), Akroma, Angel of Wrath (Chuck’s Virtual Party), and Fallen Angel (2004 Holiday Events).
