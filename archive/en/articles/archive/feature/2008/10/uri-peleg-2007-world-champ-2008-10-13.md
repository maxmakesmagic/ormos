Uri Peleg was a well respected Israeli pro who had worked with some of the game's biggest and brightest. Prior to the [2007 World Championships](/en/events/coverage/historic-wins-peleg-swiss-worlds), however, he hadn't managed a breakthrough performance. That all changed as he bested three separate formats on the weekend before hoisting the title of 2007 **Magic** World Champion. The deck that took him to the title through the Top 8? The above Doran list!


You can live the dream too, by qualifying for the [2008 World Championships](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds), to be held in Memphis, Tennessee December 11-14. How? By participating in the Last Chance Qualifier tournaments being held on [**Magic Online**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/digital/magiconline.aspx)! The events feature Standard and Limited formats and run from Wednesday, October 15 through Thursday, October 16. For more information, check out the official announcement [here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/100308).
