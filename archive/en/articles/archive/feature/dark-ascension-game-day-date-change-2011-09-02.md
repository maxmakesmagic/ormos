Dark Ascension Game Day Date Change
===================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 2, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Game Day events for the *Dark Ascension* set will take place on February 25-26, 2012, rather than March 3-4, 2012 as previously announced. More information about the *Dark Ascension* release is available in the [product announcement](/en/articles/archive/announcing-dark-ascension-2011-07-25).
