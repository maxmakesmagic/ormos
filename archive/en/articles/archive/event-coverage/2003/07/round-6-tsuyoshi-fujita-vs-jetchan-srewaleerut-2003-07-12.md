Round 6: Tsuyoshi Fujita vs. Jetchan Srewaleerut
================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 12, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall




 Brian David-Marshall is a New York–based game designer who has been involved with Magic since 1994, when he started organizing tournaments and ran a Manhattan game store. Since then, he has been a judge, a player, and one of the longest-tenured columnists on DailyMTG.com, as he enters his second decade writing for the site. He is also the Pro Tour Historian and one of the commentators for the Pro Tour. 






Fujita's deck has carried him to a 5-0 record so far. The key to the deck for him are the [Goblin Matron](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Matron)s which can him anything he needs. He does not think the deck will translate well to Block Constructed because of the lack of the matrons as well as not having the [Sulfurous Springs](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sulfurous+Springs)—its straight Goblins for Fujita in block!

### Game 1

At international events such as these there are some communication issues that need to be addressed to determine the heads and tails of the coins. Finally, Fujita won the flip and elected to play.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds02/a965.jpg)Fujita made a dramatic performance of looking at each card and elected to keep. He opened with a Prospector but Jetchan drew first blood with [Raging Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin). A Sledder joined the party but there was no blocking the Rager. [Goblin Warchief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Warchief) came down on turn three and Fujita attacked for four. 

Jetchan played a Burrows and passed the turn without attack. Fujita played a hasty [Goblin Sharpshooter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sharpshooter) and killed the [Raging Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin). A [Goblin Matron](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Matron) found a [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander) and Fujita passed back the turn. [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption) did little but force Fujita to sacrifice two goblins and get two activations for his Sharpshooter. 

When he attacked with Siege-Gang and everyone else, Jetchan conceded.

Fujita – 1 Jetchan – 0

### Game 2

![Gempalm Incinerator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Gempalm+Incinerator)Jetchan brought in close to a dozen cards. They included [Gempalm Incinerator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gempalm+Incinerator), [Clickslither](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clickslither), [Earthquake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake), [Starstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Starstorm) and [Slice and Dice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slice+and+Dice). He took out an assortment of cards including [Ensnaring Bridge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ensnaring+Bridge)s and [Grafted Skullcap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grafted+Skullcap)s. Jetchan mulliganed while Fujita smiled at the two Matrons in his hand—no doubt planning on getting Gempalms of his own.[Raging Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin) came out on turn one and his next draw yielded the second land he was hoping for, which he used to cycle a Gempalm when Fujita attacked with his turn one Sledder. [Goblin Warchief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Warchief) was [Volcanic Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer)ed and a [Goblin Matron](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Matron) found an Incinerator. Another [Raging Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin) came down and it traded with the Matron. Gempalm cycled on the next turn to kill the remaining Portal goblin. 

Fujita played a [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander) but Jetchan had an [Earthquake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake) and cast it for three to do the extra damage. Fujita had another Siege-Gang but Jetchan was out of gas. He attacked for five and Matroned up another Commander which loomed in his hand should Jetchan deal with the present one. A [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer) was [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother)ed and Fujita was able to finish his opponent off any number of ways.

**Final Result:** Fujita – 2 Jetchan – 0
