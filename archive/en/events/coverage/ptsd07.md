Pro Tour San Diego
------------------


Sliver Kids Stun San Diego
==========================




June 29-July 1, 2007












[日本語の取材へ](/en/node/539731)


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptsdg07/SD_banner.jpg)Chris Lachmann and Jacob Van Lunen came to Pro Tour-San Diego with a Sliver plan that flew in the face of nearly every other established draft strategy. But who’s telling these first-time Pro Tour players they’re crazy now? The New Jersey duo steamrolled through the Top 4 with their Sliver-rific concoction in record time, dispatching Kentaro Yamamoto and Yuuta Takahashi in five rounds in the finals after besting semifinal opponents Genki Taru and Masami Kaneko in four rounds.


Lachmann and Van Lunen started drawing attention after defeating Hall of Famers Jon Finkel and Dave Humpherys in back-to-back rounds during Day One with poison counters (thanks to many [Virulent Slivers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Virulent+Slivers) ). They stuck with their plan all the way to the championship and $50,000 first prize. So the next time you think the metagame’s all figured out, remember Lachmann and Van Lunen…and the power of Slivers!
