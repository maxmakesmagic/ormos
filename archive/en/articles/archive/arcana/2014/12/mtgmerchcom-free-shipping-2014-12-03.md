MTGMerch.com Free Shipping!
===========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 3, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_mikemcartor.jpg)
By Mike McArtor




 Mike first played with Ice Age and became the copy editor for DailyMTG.com in December 2011. Before DailyMTG, he was an editor on D&D's Dragon magazine for four years. 







The holiday season means holiday deals! To wit:


From now through December 15, 2014, get free FedEx Smartpost shipping to US locations on orders over $50 at [MTGMerch.com](http://www.mtgmerch.com/)!
