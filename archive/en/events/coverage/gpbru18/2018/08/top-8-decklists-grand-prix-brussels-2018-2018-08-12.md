Top 8 Decklists of Grand Prix Brussels 2018
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 12, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After fifteen rounds of Standard, with a total of 1,101 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. To everyone's surprise, their decks included a scant eight copies of [Goblin Chainwhirler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Chainwhirler)s. Instead, the most represented archetype was Esper Control. Indeed, the three most accomplished players in this Top 8 all chose to run three [Teferi, Hero of Dominaria](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Hero+of+Dominaria) and four each of [Torrential Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk) and [Vraska's Contempt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vraska%27s+Contempt).
