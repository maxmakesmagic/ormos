 Q. What is ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers*? 

***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* is our latest version of the digital game in the **Magic: The Gathering** franchise. Building on the success of the original *Duels of the Planeswalkers*, **Magic** 2012 and **Magic** 2013, ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* will introduce a new sealed play game mode, Planeswalker opponents, decks, puzzle challenges, and other exciting new features on Xbox LIVE® Arcade, PC/Steam®, PlayStation® Network, iPad® and for the first time on Android™ tablet. ***Magic** 2014*  – *Duels of the Planeswalkers* is the best way to introduce new players to **Magic**. 

 Q. What is the storyline of ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers*? 

 A. In ***Magic** 2014* , players join Chandra Nalaar in a quest for revenge, as they track down the dangerous and manipulative Planeswalker, Ramaz. In doing so, they discover new and exciting cards from ***Magic** 2014 Core Set* . 

 Q. Which platforms are ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* available for? 

 A. ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* is currently available for Android tablets via Google Play and the Amazon App Store, the Xbox LIVE® Arcade system, the PS3 through PlayStation® Network, the iPad® through the App Store®, and for your PC through Steam®. 

 Q. What ages are appropriate for ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers*? 

 A. ***Magic** 2014*  has the following ratings: 

[ESRB](http://www.esrb.org/index-js.jsp) (US): T, Blood, Violence, Suggestive Themes, Online Interactions Not Rated by the ESRB  
 PEGI (EU): 7 (6 in Portugal), PEGI Online, Fear   
 GRB (Korea): 12+, Fear/Horror/Threatening   
 CERO (Japan): 15+, Crime  
 USK: 6  
 iOS: 9+  
 COB (Australia): Rating Pending  


 Q. Where can I purchase ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers*? 

 A. You can purchase ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* from the Xbox LIVE® Marketplace on your Xbox and through the Web [here](http://marketplace.xbox.com/en-us/product/magic-2013/66acd000-77fe-1000-9115-d80258411213), for your PS3 on the PlayStation® Network, and for your PC through Steam® [here](http://store.steampowered.com/). The iPad® version is available on the App Store. For Android Tablets, you can purchase via Google Play and the Amazon App Store. Availability may vary by region and platform. 

 Q. In which countries can I purchase ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers*? 

 A. ***Magic** 2014*  is being made available worldwide. [Click here for the complete list of countries for Xbox LIVE® Arcade and PlayStation® Network](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/digital/duelsoftheplaneswalkers.aspx?x=mtg/digital/d14/xboxpsncountries). 

 Q. How much does ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* cost? 

 A. The ***Magic** 2014*  base game is priced competitively at MSRP 800 MS Points for Xbox LIVE® or MSRP $9.99 for your PS3 through the PlayStation® Network, for your iPad® through the App Store, for your PC through Steam®, and for Android Tablet on Google Play and Amazon App Store. Prices may vary by platform and region. Go to the specific sites to verify. 

Q. Is additional downloadable content available?

 A. Yes. ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* offers a variety of downloadable game content, as well as smaller DLC items, such as deck unlocks and foils conversions as well as additional slots for sealed play decks. 

Q. Can I purchase additional cards for my decks?

 A. Yes. In ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers*, all the cards in the ***Magic** 2014*  set are available by defeating opponents or through purchasing deck unlocks. 

Q. What game modes are available?

 A. ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* features a full single-player campaign game vs. AI opponents. ***Magic** 2014*  also offers online multiplayer play with Two-Headed Giant; three- and four-player free-for-all play; and new sealed play mode, where players build decks from randomized booster packs.\* 

\*Android tablet features Ad Hoc multiplayer. iPad and Android tablet support up to two-player multiplayer.

 Q. Is there trading in ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers*? 

 A. ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* does not support trading; you can unlock all the cards in the game by simply playing or through purchasing deck unlocks. 

Q. What are the multiplayer free-for-all rules?

 A. ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* features free-for-all play, meaning the last player remaining wins. You can choose to play ranked games against random opponents or unranked games against your friends. 

Q. How many players can play in a multiplayer game?

A. For Xbox, PSN, and Steam versions, up to four players can play online and up to four players can play in Two-Headed Giant or free-for-all games. The iPad and Android tablet versions support two-player matches with human players or you can play with three AI partners.

 Q. Is ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* suitable for gamers unfamiliar with **Magic: The Gathering** or trading card games? 

 A. Yes! ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* is ideal for gamers regardless of their familiarity with **Magic: The Gathering** or trading card games. ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* is the best way to learn how to play the **Magic: The Gathering** trading card game. 

Q. What features are there for new players?

 A. ***Magic** 2014* —*Duels of the Planeswalkers* includes a tutorial where you learn while you play, in-game hints, and even an in-game rulebook and glossary. 

Q. Can I create new decks or modify my deck?

 A. ***Magic** 2014*  allows players to customize their decks by removing cards from their original pool and adding in cards they have unlocked through game play. Land cards are added automatically by the AI. ***Magic** 2014*  introduces the sealed play game mode, which for the first time allows players to open boosters and build decks for play in a sealed play campaign against the AI and in PVP. 

Q. What cards are featured in the game?

 A. ***Magic** 2014*  features select cards from throughout the history of **Magic: The Gathering** up through the ***Magic** 2014 Core Set* . 

Q. Are there achievements or trophies and, if so, what type?

A. There are thirty achievements to be earned in the base game for XBLA and Steam®, fifteen trophies for PSN, and thirty achievements on iPad. Each achievement/trophy is earned in a different way, including ranked match wins and meeting unique victory conditions. The thirty achievements on Xbox LIVE® are worth a total of 400 Gamerscore. The thirty achievements on iPad are worth a total of 400 points. The fifteen trophies on PSN are worth up to 315 points. 

 Q. Is ***Magic** 2014*  challenging for the experienced players? 

 A. ***Magic** 2014*  has an advanced AI that can be adjusted to provide challenging game play for even skilled **Magic: The Gathering** players. Also, ***Magic** 2014*  has ranked head-to-head competitive play on the XBLA, PSN, iPad, and Steam platforms. 

 Q. There's a promotional six-card booster pack available for purchasing ***Magic** 2014*  on XBLA, PSN, iPad, Android, or Steam®. How do I determine if I'm eligible? Where can I get it? 

 A. First, please check your eligibility to participate and the restrictions by checking out the promotion Terms amp; Conditions [here](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/digital/duelsoftheplaneswalkers.aspx?x=dotp/redemption14/tos). If you are eligible you will need to follow the directions to register on the [redemption web site](http://archive.wizards.com/promo/duelsoftheplaneswalkers14/) and get your voucher. Take this voucher to a local participating retail location to collect your promo card. You can find participating stores [here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/digital/d14/promostores). 

Q. What cards are available through this promotion?

A. There are three alternate-art cards available: Scavenging Ooze (iPad, Android, PC), Bonescythe Sliver (Xbox), and Ogre Battledriver (PSN), each of which comes in a different six-Card Booster Pack.

Q. When does this promotion end?

A. This promotion runs through December 13, 2013, or while supplies last.

***Magic** 2014*  Expansion FAQ 


Q. Are there titles, achievements or trophies available with the expansion and, if so, what type?

 A: There are eight earnable achievements in the ***Magic** 2014*  Expansion for Xbox LIVE® Arcade, PC/Steam®, and iPad®. Seven trophies will be available for PlayStation® Network. Each achievement or trophy is earned in a different way, including ranked match wins and meeting unique victory conditions. 

The eight achievements available for Xbox LIVE® Arcade are worth a total of 100 Gamerscore. The eight achievements on iPad® are worth a total of 100 points. The seven trophies on PlayStation® Network are worth up to 180 points. The Expansion will also feature five new Titles that will be available across all platforms. 

 Q. Will the ***Magic** 2014*  Expansion include new decks? How many? 

 A. Yes, the ***Magic** 2014*  Expansion will include five new decks: 

 Dodge and Burn, a blue-red spell-based 'Counterburn' deck that is associated with Dack Fayden 

 Lords of Darkness, a mono-black deck full of Demons and other infernal creatures 

Hall of Champions, a white-green-blue deck that includes plenty of creatures with the exalted mechanic associated with Elspeth

 Sylvan Might, a mono-green Elf deck that is associated with Nissa 

Sword of the Saumrai, a red-white deck full of Kamigawa Samurai

Q. Will Sealed play receive more potential cards? 

 A. The ***Magic** 2014*  Expansion does not contain any additional content for the Sealed Play game mode. 

 Q. Will the ***Magic** 2014*  Expansion give us extra Sealed slots? 

 A. The ***Magic** 2014*  Expansion will not include additional Sealed slots. 

Q. Will the Expansion add online multiplayer to the Android tablet version of the game?

 A. Just like the base game, the ***Magic** 2014*  Expansion on Android tablet will feature Ad Hoc multiplayer.
