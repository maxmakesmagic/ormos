*Rise of the Eldrazi* Launch Party Card
=======================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 19, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











R*ise of the Eldrazi* had its prerelease last weekend. That means that it's going be officially on sale this Friday. And *that* means that it's [Launch Party](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/releases) time! And if you play in a *Rise of the Eldrazi* Launch Party (and supplies hold out), you'll get one of these!
