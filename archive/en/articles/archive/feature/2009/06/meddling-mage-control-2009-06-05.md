[Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) was one of the most exciting cards released in *Alara Reborn*, and David Daniel has made good on using the powerful 2/2 in Standard! He earned a Regionals Top 8 in Albuquerque with a Blue-White control strategy. The deck drops [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) into play, then hides behind the 2/2 while protecting itself with [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile), [Negate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Negate), and [Remove Soul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Remove+Soul). You can even use [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek) to make sure you lock down cards in the opponent's hand with the Mage! No doubt Chris Pikula would be proud...
