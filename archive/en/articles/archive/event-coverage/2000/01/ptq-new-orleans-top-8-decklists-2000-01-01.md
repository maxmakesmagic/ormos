PTQ New Orleans Top 8 Decklists
===============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_KeitaMori.jpg)
By Keita Mori











Tempo, tempo, tempo, that's the story of Invasion Block Constructed. A format defined by awkward mana and powerful but expensive spells tends to support the quick beats, and the proof is in this, the Top 8 decks from the PTQ for New Orleans held at Pro Tour - New York. Three Green-Blue-Red decks, packed with bears, bounce and burn, two Red-Green decks to smash opponents slow out of the gates, two aggressive and flexible red-white-blue decks with the beast 2/2's available, and only one real control deck. The card of the day is clearly Prophetic Bolt.
