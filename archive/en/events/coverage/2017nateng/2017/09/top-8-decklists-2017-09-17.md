![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Craig-Jones-Author-Photo-150x150_0.jpg)
By Craig Jones

After twelve rounds only 8 were left standing. Here are the decks they chose for the Constructed portion of the tournament.
