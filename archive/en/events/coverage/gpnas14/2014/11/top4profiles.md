### Jesse Hampton, (19) Jacob Wilson, Matt Nass




  




  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpnas14/top4_hampton_solo.jpg)
### Jess Hampton


**Age:** 25  
**Hometown:** Seattle, Washington


 

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

Played on the Pro Tour, Platinum Pro, Pro Tour Philly Top 8


**What colors was your first Sealed deck, what was your record, and what was your best card?**  

Abzan. 7-2. [Anafenza, the Foremost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anafenza%2C+the+Foremost) and 12 good uncommons.


**What colors was your second Sealed deck, what was your record, and what was your best card?**  

Abzan. 4-0. [Duneblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast), or maybe the second [Duneblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast).


**What was a card that you wanted for a deck, but your teammate needed to play?**  

Jacob took [Sorin, Solemn Visitor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sorin%2C+Solemn+Visitor), and [High Sentinels of Arashin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Sentinels+of+Arashin) in the second sealed deck.


**What was the most helpful thing your teammates did for you in the tournament?**  

Helped me write and build deck after I drove to Nashville at 3 a.m. the night before. Got snowed in at the Charlotte airport.







  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpnas14/top4_wilson_solo.jpg)
### Jacob Wilson


**Age:** 19  
**Hometown:** San Francisco, California


 

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

Platinum level Pro. Pro Tour Top 8. 4 Grand Prix Top 8s – Cycle of formats (Standard, Modern, Legacy, Limited)


**What colors was your first Sealed deck, what was your record, and what was your best card?**  

Mardu, 7-2. [Trumpet Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trumpet+Blast)


**What colors was your second Sealed deck, what was your record, and what was your best card?**  

Mardu, 4-0-1. 1st: [High Sentinels of Arashin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Sentinels+of+Arashin); 2nd: [Sorin, Solemn Visitor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sorin%2C+Solemn+Visitor).


**What was a card that you wanted for a deck, but your teammate needed to play?**  
[Wind-Scarred Crag](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wind-Scarred+Crag).


**What was the most helpful thing your teammates did for you in the tournament?**  

They let me ignore their advice.

### Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa


**Age:** 27  
**Hometown:** Porto Alegre, Brazil


 

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

Nine Pro Tour Top 8s!


**What colors was your first Sealed deck, what was your record, and what was your best card?**  

UWR [Jeskai], 4-2 (we went 8-1). [Dragon-Style Twins](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon-Style+Twins).

They won all the matches on Day 1!







  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpnas14/top4_martell_solo.jpg)
### Tom Martell


**Age:** 32  
**Hometown:** Mountain View, California


 

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

Pro Tour *Gatecrash* champion, Two-time Grand Prix Champion, 2013 Player of the Year runner-up


**What colors was your first Sealed deck, what was your record, and what was your best card?**  

White-Black splash Red, 8-1. Best card was [Butcher of the Horde](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher+of+the+Horde).


**What colors was your second Sealed deck, what was your record, and what was your best card?**  

Black-Green splash White, 2-0-1 (two incompletes). Best card was [Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino).


**What was a card that you wanted for a deck, but your teammate needed to play?**  

For my first deck, I was actively giving away anything teammates wanted. My second deck could have used the [Abzan Falconer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abzan+Falconer) we gave to Shahar.


**What was the most helpful thing your teammates did for you in the tournament?**  

They won a lot of matches. We cooperated extremely well during deck construction.
