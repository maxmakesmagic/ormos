The 2010 Magic Online Community Cup is going on right now, and what better time to take a stroll down memory lane? Last year the Community participants narrowly defeated Magic R&D. One of those champions? Peter Jahn, who in addition to being a member of the tournament-winning team is also a very active judge. The above list is his Zendikar draft list that he used at the event.
