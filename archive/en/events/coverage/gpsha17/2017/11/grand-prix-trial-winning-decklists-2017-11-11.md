Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 11, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











The Grand Prix Trials which take place on Friday usually provide a good preview of Saturday's metagame. Aside from the usual suspects - Temur Energy, Sultai Energy, Four-Color Energy, White-Blue Approach, and Ramunap Red - a couple of interesting decks also showed up to surprise the competition. Check out Yan Haowen’s Sultai Control deck with [Shefet Monitor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shefet+Monitor) as well as Nobuhiro Yamada’s innovative White-Blue Aggro deck featuring [Heart of Kiran](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heart+of+Kiran) and eight Planeswalkers!
