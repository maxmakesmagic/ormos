Izzvergg was the *Masters Edition 2* champion on **Magic Online** last week, piloting this particular draft special through a tough field to emerge victorious. *Masters Edition 2*, released exclusively for use on **Magic Online**, brings a number of the game's most famous and most cherished cards to the world of digital **Magic**, most for the first time ever. It also creates an interesting Limited format allowing [Whirling Catapult](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whirling+Catapult), [Combat Medic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Combat+Medic), and [Ironclaw Orcs](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ironclaw+Orcs) to do battle side-by-side for the first time in **Magic**'s history!

Adding to the excitement of new releases on **Magic Online** comes this week's release of *Shards of Alara* for digital play! Casual drafts are happening right now with Release Events just around the corner, and you can join in the fun.
