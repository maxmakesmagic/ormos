Ignite Your Spark Winner
========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 30, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







Out of over 1,100 individual entries from around the world, today we are announcing the two winners of our Ignite Your Spark contest.


We encouraged entries to put together the best cosplay of their favorite Planeswalker. From Jace to Tamiyo, Liliana to Ajani, we saw all sorts of Planeswalkers cosplayed in our contest. The judges from among Wizards' staff chose the top ten entries allowing the community to cast their vote, and they chose from among them the grand prize winner.


### Fan Favorite Winner


The fan favorite proved to Christine S., with her beautiful Elspeth cosplay. Christine wins a new laptop and a **Magic** swag bag.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1331_zofj_christine.jpg)
