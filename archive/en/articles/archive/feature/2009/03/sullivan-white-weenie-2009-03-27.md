Adrian Sullivan is a well known deck innovator and **Magic** writer. His latest creation? This innovative White-Green aggro deck, just in time for the Pro Tour Qualifier-Honolulu season! It features some standard players like [Wild Nacatl](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Nacatl) and [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) alongside some more surprising choices. [O-Naginata](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=O-Naginata)? [Lightning Greaves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Greaves)? The [Steelshaper's Gift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steelshaper%27s+Gift) to fetch up the equipment on a whim? If you're looking for something truly innovative this Extended season, this deck is for you.
