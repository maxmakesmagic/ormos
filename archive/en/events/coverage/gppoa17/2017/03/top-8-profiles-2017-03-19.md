Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 19, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim












**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppoa17/t8-pezzo.jpg)Name:**  Sebastian Pozzo




**Hometown:** Buenos Aires, Argentina




**Age:** 27




**Occupation:** *Magic* Player




**Score for Day 1:** 9 – 0 (2 Byes)




**Score for Day 2:** 3 – 2 - 1




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  

 Top 8 at Grand Prix Buenos Aires 2008



MOCS 2014 Competitor



**What deck are you playing today, and why?**



Mardu Vehicles, because it's good, fits my play style, and I practiced a lot on *Magic Online*.



**What is the most interesting thing you encountered this weekend?**



Three non-Mardu, non-Saheeli decks in the Top 8. It impressed me!



**What is your favorite memory this weekend?**



I had to beat Mardu Master (Lucas Esper Berthoud) by winning Game 2 and Game 3 in order to make the Top 8.



**What is your favorite card in *Modern Masters 2017*?**



[Gifts Ungiven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven). Easy pick!




---


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppoa17/t8-salvatto.jpg)Name:**  Luis Salvatto




**Hometown:** Buenos Aires, Argentina




**Age:** 28




**Occupation:** Chicken Businessman and *Magic* Player




**Score for Day 1:** 8 – 1 (2 byes)




**Score for Day 2:** 4 – 1 - 1




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**




 Pro Tour Shadows over Innistrad Top 8  

 Super Sunday Series 2015 Champion



Three Grand Prix Top 8s, 1 win



**What deck are you playing today, and why?**



Four-Color Saheeli. It's so good and a lot of fun to play.



**What is the most interesting thing you encountered this weekend?**



There were surprisingly a lot of Black-Green players.



**What is your favorite memory this weekend?**



After 2 byes, I lost Round and then went 10-0. Also, during Round 6, I lost Game 1 and mulliganed to 5 in Game 2. I won that game and then made a good bluff to win Game 3! Phew!



**What is your favorite card in *Modern Masters 2017*?**



I didn't pay much attention to it yet.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppoa17/t8-roman.jpg)Name:**  Patricio Roman




**Hometown:** Santiago, Chile




**Age:** 30




**Occupation:** Father




**Score for Day 1:** 7 – 1 – 1 (2 Byes)




**Score for Day 2:** 5 - 1




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**



I made some Top 16's at a few Grand Prix and also played a few World *Magic* Cups on Team Chile.



**What deck are you playing today, and why?**



Four-Color Saheeli. I've been playing it for a few weeks and felt very comfortable with it.



**What is the most interesting thing you encountered this weekend?**



I played against a [Saheeli Rai](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Saheeli+Rai) + [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) combo deck in Day 1.



**What is your favorite memory this weekend?**



The hugs from all my friends after I made the Top 8.



**What is your favorite card in *Modern Masters 2017*?**



[Wall of Frost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Frost).

![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppoa17/t8-bertolli.jpg)**Name:** Rafael Bertolli Parra




**Hometown:** Sao Paulo, Brazil




**Age:** 26




**Occupation:** Project Manager




**Score for Day 1:** 8 – 1 (2 Byes)




**Score for Day 2:** 4 - 2




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**



1st place at a Modern side event at Grand Prix Sao Paulo last year.


Went 5 – 0 at Friday Night *Magic*.



**What deck are you playing today, and why?**



Black-Green Energy. It was the only deck which I had cards to build. Also, I like Greenbelt Rampager because it is good against Mardu Vehicles and Four-Color Saheeli.



**What is the most interesting thing you encountered this weekend?**



I saw a Four-Color Saheeli deck with Worldbreaker, otherwise most of the lists were pretty standard.



**What is your favorite memory this weekend?**



My Round 15 match against Mardu Vehicles was very long and intense. I won Game 3 with less than 2 minutes on the clock.


What is your favorite card in *Modern Masters 2017*?


[Liliana of the Veil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil). She is amazing in my Modern Jund deck!
