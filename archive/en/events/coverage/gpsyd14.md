TOP 8 COVERAGE
--------------


Not afraid of doing things the hard way, Paul Jackson from Perth soldiered through Day 1 of Grand Prix Sydney with no byes, easily making the cut to Day 2 at 8-1. Jackson rattled off another four wins to lock up his spot in an impressive Top 8.


Faced with the daunting task of drafting *Magic 2015* alongside two-time Australian GP Champ James Zhang, 2011 Australian National Team member Maitland Cameron, 2009 New Zealand National Team member Matt Griffin, GP Minneapolis 2014 champion Park Jun Young, and Japanese Legend Tomoharu Saito, (Saito in his 20th Grand Prix Top 8!) Jackson simply rolled up his sleeves, spun his cap, and set to drafting an impressive White/Blue deck around Triplicate Spirits and Spectra Ward.


The first person from Western Australia to ever win a Grand Prix, Jackson will look to improve again on this finish at Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir* in Honolulu later this year. Until then, good luck at your World *Magic* Cup Qualifiers next week, and join us here in two weeks for Grand Prix Salt Lake City!

DAY 2 COVERAGE
--------------


Day 1's *Magic 2015* Sealed rounds turned out to be a format George Jackson would approve of coz you gotta have soul (you young kids might have to look that one up).. Seven of the winning Sealed Grinders had at least one of the 6/6 M15 Souls in their deck and both Day 1 undefeated players, Matt Griffin and Chris Sparks, had three Souls between them. The six-mana Avatars dominated the field, reminding many of the much-loved *Magic 2010* format.


Draft, however, is a very different beast to Sealed, where synergy and speed can trump size and strength. With 84 players remaining in the tournament competition will be fierce as strategy shifts during the battle towards the Top 8.

DAY 1 COVERAGE
--------------


Our marathon is half-way through with competitors falling to misplays, mental fatigue, and not quite making it to seven wins. The field has been cut from 813 to 84 with two undefeated players left, New Zealander Matt Griffin with his bomb-laden double-Soul and Avacyn, Guardian Angel deck, and Chris Sparks from Sydney, with his single-Soul triple-Lightning Strike deck.


Sunday will see a new day dawn for our *Magic 2015* Draft runners. With the finish line in sight will Matt or Chris retain the top spot before the cut to Top 8, or will they be overtaken by the pack? Only time (and six rounds of draft) will tell.
