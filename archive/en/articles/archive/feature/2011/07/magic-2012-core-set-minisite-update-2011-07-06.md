***Magic 2012*** Core Set Minisite Update
=========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 6, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Have you been enjoying the ***Magic** 2012* Core Set preview season? The reason we ask is that today is one of the biggest days on the calendar. The [***Magic** 2012* Minisite](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/magic2012) has another exclusive Wallpaper up today, and the Card Image Gallery has every card. Not just every card that's been officially previewed, you understand. Every single card in the ***Magic** 2012* Core Set.

[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/features/feature148b_thumb.jpg)](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/magic2012)  
What are you waiting for? The [***Magic** 2012* Minisite](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/magic2012) is ready for you! And the [prerelease](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/prerelease-facts) is this weekend!
