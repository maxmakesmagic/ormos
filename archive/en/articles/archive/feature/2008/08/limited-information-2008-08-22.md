By Wizards of the Coast












Limited Information is a column dedicated specifically to Limited Magic in all its forms. Here you'll learn strategies for improving your game at Sealed Deck, Booster Draft, and other 40-card formats. Limited Information is written by coverage writer and podcaster Marshall Sutcliffe.
