Round 14: Double Your Fun
=========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 2, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_rashadmiller.jpg)
By Rashad Miller










Joshua Hakakian was playing in his second Pro Tour here in Philadelphia and had an impressive 30 points heading into the round. Brian Demars, also with 30 points, is a much more seasoned veteran and was relatively fresh off the Top 16 finish at Grand Prix Providence that qualified him for this event. Both players were on course to make a Sunday appearance. Who would continue the trend?



### Game One



Hakakian won the roll and chose to play first. He kept his opening seven cards, while Demars mulliganed to six.




![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptphi11/r14_demars.jpg)

*Brian Demars fights for a shot at the Top 8.*

Hakakian started with a turn-one [Preordain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Preordain). Demars had a tapped [Stomping Ground](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stomping+Ground) on his turn. Hakakian used [Scalding Tarn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scalding+Tarn) to search for [Steam Vents](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steam+Vents), then cast [Boomerang](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boomerang) on Demars's [Stomping Ground](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stomping+Ground) to set him back a turn. Demars replayed the [Stomping Ground](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stomping+Ground) untapped and cast a [Noble Hierarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noble+Hierarch). Hakakian played [Misty Rainforest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misty+Rainforest) and passed the turn. Demars played a [Misty Rainforest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misty+Rainforest) of his own, but had targeted it with [Boom & Bust](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boom+%2F%2F+Bust)]Boom along with Hakakian's [Steam Vents](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steam+Vents). Demars sacrificed his [Misty Rainforest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misty+Rainforest) in response to put [Temple Garden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+Garden) onto the battlefield untapped. Hakakian floated one red mana, and before Demars moved to the next phase, Hakakian fetched [Steam Vents](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steam+Vents) and cast [Thirst](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thirst) For Knowledge.


Hakakian's next actions were playing a [Steam Vents](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steam+Vents) tapped alongside [Spellskite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite). Demars summoned [Kavu Predator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kavu+Predator) before targeting [Spellskite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite) with [Oust](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oust). [Oust](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oust) resolved, Hakakian gained 3 life and [Kavu Predator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kavu+Predator) gained three +1/+1 counters. Hakarian played his fourth land and ended his turn. Demars attacked Hakakian down to 11 with [Kavu Predator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kavu+Predator), then attempted a postcombat [Green Sun's Zenith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Green+Sun%27s+Zenith) for two. Hakakian flashed in [Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch), untapped an [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) when it entered the battlefield, then used [Remand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Remand) to send the Zenith back to Demars's hand. Demars had to settle for [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) instead. Hakakian untapped, drew his card and played [Splinter Twin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Splinter+Twin) on his [Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exarch). With the game-winning combo successfully deployed, we were on to


### Game Two



Hakakian 1, Demars 0



### Game Two



Demars chose to play first. Both players kept their opening hands.


Demars and Hakakian started with [Misty Rainforest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misty+Rainforest) and [Scalding Tarn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scalding+Tarn) respectively. Demars sacrificed his [Misty Rainforest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misty+Rainforest) for untapped [Stomping Ground](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stomping+Ground), played an untapped [Temple Garden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+Garden), then cast a [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf). Hakakian laid a second [Scalding Tarn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scalding+Tarn) and ended his turn. Demars added [Horizon Canopy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Horizon+Canopy) and [Seal of Primordium](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Primordium) to the battlefield. An attack from a 1/2 [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) set the life totals to 15 and 19 in Hakakian's favor. At the end of the turn, Hakakian double searched with his two fetch lands for two [Steam Vents](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steam+Vents). Hakakian, generally content to do nothing on his turn, played a [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) and passed. On Demars's next turn, Hakakian flashed in [Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch), depriving Demars of his only red mana source. Demars cast [Green Sun's Zenith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Green+Sun%27s+Zenith) for zero retrieving [Dryad Arbor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dryad+Arbor) from his library. Hakakian's first sorcery of the match turned out to be [Slagstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slagstorm). Demars used [Path to Exile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) to exile [Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch) keep his [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) alive. Demars attacked Hakakian down to 11 with a 4/5 [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf). [Green Sun's Zenith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Green+Sun%27s+Zenith) for two added a second [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) to Demars's team. Hakakian laid a [Misty Rainforest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misty+Rainforest) and passed the turn with six mana available.




![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptphi11/r14_hakakian.jpg)

*Joshua Hakakian finds his [Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch)'s many long-lost twins.*

On Demars's next turn, Hakakian cast [Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch) to again deprive Demars of his only red mana source. Demars had [Beast Within](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Beast+Within) in response to destroy the [Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exarch) and leave a 3/3 [Beast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Beast) token in its place. Still in Demars's upkeep, a second [Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch) hit the battlefield for Hakakian and untapped [Steam Vents](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steam+Vents). Demars attacked with both [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf)s , one of which was blocked by a beast token, sending Hakakian down to 6 life. Hakakian life had dwindled pretty low, but that didn't matter, as he had [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiki-Jiki%2C+Mirror+Breaker) to complete the combo.


**Hakakian 2, Demars 0**
