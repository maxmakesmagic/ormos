In newly minted Constructed formats, like Standard with the addition of *Zendikar*, it is often challenging for solid control decks to appear during the first few weeks of play. Often the decks need to determine the most potent threats they're solving for to learn which answers they'll need to rely on. Calosso Fuentes paid that adage no heed as he used his take on a [Luminarch Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Luminarch+Ascension) / planeswalker deck to take himself all the way to the Top 16 of the recent Standard event hosted in Philadelphia by Starcitygames.com. What do you think of Calosso's suite of answers, and how will you build control decks in the weeks to come?
