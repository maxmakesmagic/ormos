Magic Glossary: Extended
========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 11, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












**Extended Format (also known as Type 1.x)**


Extended allow the use of older cards, dating back to *Fifth Edition* and *Ice Age*. A few cards have been banned because their power-level is problematic for the format. Every few years, the DCI makes an announcement about whether or not sets will be rotating out of Extended.


The Extended-legal sets are:


* *Fifth Edition*
* *Classic* (*Sixth Edition*)
* *7th Edition*
* *Ice Age*
* *Homelands*
* *Alliances*
* *Mirage*
* *Visions*
* *Weatherlight*
* *Tempest*
* *Stronghold*
* *Exodus*
* *Urza's Saga*
* *Urza's Legacy*
* *Urza's Destiny*
* *Mercadian Masques*
* *Nemesis*
* *Prophecy*
* *Invasion*
* *Planeshift*
* *Apocalypse*
* *Odyssey*
* *Torment* (effective March 1st, 2002)
* *Judgment* (effective July 1st, 2002)

In addition, the ten cards listed below, commonly referred to as "dual lands," are legal in Extended:

[Savannah](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savannah)  
[Scrubland](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrubland)  
[Taiga](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Taiga)  
[Tropical Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tropical+Island)  
[Tundra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tundra)  
[Underground Sea](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underground+Sea)  
[Volcanic Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Island)
