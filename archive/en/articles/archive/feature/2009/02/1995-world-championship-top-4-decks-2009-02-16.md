1995 World Championship Top 4 Decks
===================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 16, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Note: we don't have complete records of the decklists from 1995. Here's what we were able to piece together from various sources, including a [post on Usenet from Henry Stern](http://groups.google.com/group/rec.games.trading-cards.magic.misc/msg/b65880d10748ed78?hl=en&dmode=source)

Still wondering what was in everyone's deck? Well, we've got a little game for you. Back in 1996, when the Pro Tour was just beginning, there were a variety of ways to get invited. For the first one, players called a 1-900 number. For the second, one of the ways was a puzzle in *The Duelist* where readers were challenged to match up sideboards with 1995 Worlds Top 8 players. [Give it a try](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/images/daily/features/26e_puzzle.jpg), but keep in mind that you're about twelve years too late to qualify for the Pro Tour this way.

And here are the three decks we're sure of!
