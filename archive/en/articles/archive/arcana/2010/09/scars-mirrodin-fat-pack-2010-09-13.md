*Scars of Mirrodin* Fat Pack
============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 13, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Psst! Hey you! Yeah, *you*. That's right, the one reading these words. You wanna see the *Scars of Mirrodin* Fat Pack? Of course you do! Well, peep this! No, wait. Does anyone still "peep" things? That seemed a little strained. For now, just look at it. You can peep it later, on your own time.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/536_fatpack_closed.jpg)  
Oh, now that's a lovely box. Isn't that a lovely box? No question about it, that box is lovely.


Let's look inside!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/536_fatpack_contents.jpg)  
Behold, the mysteries of the *Scars of Mirrodin* Fat Pack! It contains:


* Player's guide with complete visual encyclopedia
* Card box with panoramic art
* Eight *Scars of Mirrodin* 15-card booster packs
* 40-card basic land pack
* Special edition *Scars of Mirrodin* Spindown life counter, available only in the fat pack
