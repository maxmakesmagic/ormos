His white-blue deck was just powerful enough to get him there, with double [Pacifism](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pacifism) and [Excommunicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Excommunicate) containing his opponent's creatures while heavy hitters like [Serra Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serra+Angel) got there in the air. For more about Shingou's win and the rest of the details from the event, visit the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/kurihara-crushes-bangkok).
