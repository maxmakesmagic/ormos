**Qualify for the Holiday Festival Vintage Championships Beginning Tomorrow**


Beginning after the downtime on Wednesday, December 10, through Friday, December 19, players will be able to qualify for the upcoming [Holiday Festival Vintage Championships](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online-holiday-festival-2014-11-12#holidayvintagechampionship) by going 4-0 or 3-1 in any of the Vintage Daily Events, or by going 4-0 in a Legacy Daily Event.


In order to provide more weekday opportunities to qualify for the Holiday Festival Vintage Championship, we are adding some additional Vintage Daily Events that will qualify for the championship. These events will run at 11:30 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. Pacific (UTC-8) on the following days:


* Thursday, December 11
* Tuesday, December 16
* Thursday, December 18

Check out the Daily Event [schedule](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/events-schedule-2014-08-27#calendar) to start planning your path to the Holiday Festival Vintage Championship.

**Upcoming R&D Challenge Event on Friday, December 19**


Think you have the *Magic* chops to beat the folks in R&D at their own game? Good, I like the cut of your jib. You're in. The time? High noon Pacific (UTC-8). The place? The Scheduled events room. The format? Modern Constructed. The prize? *Khans of Tarkir* booster packs. Except for R&D. They don't get the booster packs. Instead, those booster packs will be distributed amongst players who clock in at 15 match points. Full event details are below.






---

**R&D Challenge**



* **START TIMES**—Friday, December 19, 12:00 p.m. All times are Pacific (for UTC, add 8 hours)
* **LOCATION**—Scheduled Events
* **ENTRY OPTION**—Option 1: 10 Event Tickets
* **PRODUCT**—A Modern Constructed Deck
* **SIZE**—16-128 players
* **PLAY STYLE**—Swiss
* **FORMAT**—CONSTRUCTED
* **DURATION**—Five rounds, each round up to 50 minutes
* **PRIZES**
	+ 5 Match Wins
		- **Prizes**—18 *Khans of Tarkir* booster packs
		- **QPs**—3
	+ 4 Match Wins
		- **Prizes**—9 Khans of Tarkir booster packs
		- **QPs**—1
	+ 3 Match Wins
		- **Prizes**—5 Khans of Tarkir booster packs
		- **QPs**—0


**Note:** Magic R&D members are not eligible to win prizes but their matches will still affect standings. Any prizes R&D members would have won will be distributed evenly among the players with 15 match points within 5 business days of the event's competition.

***Magic Online* Player of the Year Event** 


The *Magic Online* Player of the Year Event gathers together the top 150 players in both the [Constructed](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/player-year-constructed-standings) and [Limited](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/player-year-limited-standings) standings to compete in a tournament that will not only crown the first place finisher as *Magic Online* Player of the Year, but will also net them an invitation to Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir*. The Player of the Year Event takes place Saturday, December 13.


Refer to the *Magic Online* Championship Series schedule page for the full [details](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/content/magic-online-championship-series#playeroftheyear).

**Holiday Schedule Changes for *Magic Online*** 


Due to the upcoming Holiday Season, some *Magic Online* services and regularly scheduled maintenances will be delayed or rescheduled as follows. All times are Pacific (UTC -8).


* ***Magic Online* Holiday Downtime schedule**
* **Monday, December 22:** Specially scheduled *Magic Online* Downtime 8:00 a.m. – 12 p.m.
* **Wednesday, December 24:** No *Magic Online* Scheduled Downtime
* **Wednesday, December 31:** No *Magic Online* Scheduled Downtime
* **Monday, January 5, 2015:** Specially schedule *Magic Online* Downtime 8 a.m. - 12 p.m.
* **Wednesday, January 14:** Regularly scheduled *Magic Online* Wednesday downtimes resume.

Please note that redemption tokens will only be processed and removed during downtimes. Redemption orders processed during the 12/22 and 1/5 downtimes will be shipped after Thursday, January 8, due to holiday warehouse closures. Please plan accordingly! There will be no downtime on Wednesday, January 7. Downtimes will return to their regular Wednesday schedule on Wednesday, January 14.


 




---

**Extended Downtime On Wednesday, December 17**


There will be an extended downtime on Wednesday, December 17, for *Magic Online*. Events close at 12:00 a.m. Pacific (08:00 UTC), midnight, and store activity and trades end at 2:45 a.m. Pacific (10:45 UTC) leading up to the system coming down at 3:00 a.m. Pacific (11:00 UTC).


 




---

**Downtime for December 10, 2014**


Downtime this week will be extended by one hour, setting our estimated return at 1:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC -8).


* All tournaments will close Wednesday, December 10, at 5:00 a.m. Pacific (13:00 UTC)
* Store and trade activity will be suspended at approximately 7:45 a.m. Pacific(15:45 UTC)
* The system will be down from 8:00 a.m. Pacific (16:00 UTC) until 1:00 p.m. Pacific (21:00 UTC)
