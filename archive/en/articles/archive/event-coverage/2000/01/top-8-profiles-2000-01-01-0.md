Top 8 Profiles

By Wizards of the Coast











### Holger Meinecke


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPVIE01/886.jpg)*Holger Meinecke***Age:** 26


**Where do you live:** Rutesheim, Germany


**Occupation:** Student


**Played **Magic** Since:** 1994


**Best Accomplishments:**  

- 23rd at Pro tour New York 2000  

- Eight at Euro's 1999


**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Vienna?**  

I made some mistakes because of tiredness


**What do you think about *Odyssey* as a draft format?**  

OK


**How did you prepare?**  

I did two drafts and two sealed decks.


### Gabor Papp


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPVIE01/888.jpg)*Gabor Papp***Age:** 23


**Where do you live:** Budapest, Hungary


**Occupation:** I work in the family business


**Played **Magic** Since:** *Revised*


**Best Accomplishments:**  

- Euro's top 8  

- National Champion  

- Two Grand Prix top 16's and some Grand Prix top 32's


**What draft strategy did you use and would you change this strategy if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

I drafted green/black and red/black


**What do you think about *Odyssey* as a draft format?**  

I don't really know it yet


**How did you prepare?**  

I played two times on Netdraft.


### Name: Eivind Nitter


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPVIE01/889.jpg)*Eivind Nitter***Age:** 22


**Where do you live:** Oslo, Norway


**Occupation:** Student


**Played **Magic** Since:** July '94


**Best Accomplishments:**  

- Winner Euro's 2001  

- Fifth at the team Pro Tour in New York, 2000


**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Vienna?**  

Worse than for a long time. Much of this was because of the short time limit.


**What draft strategy did you use and would you change this strategy if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

I drafted blue/white both drafts


**What do you think about *Odyssey* as a draft format?**  

Fun to play but needs to be more than 50 minutes.


**How did you prepare?**  

Six drafts in Oslo and some sealed deck practise with Sigurd [Eskeland] on the plane


### Matthias Künzler


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPVIE01/882.jpg)*Matthias Künzler***Age:** 21


**Where do you live:** Lausanne, Switzerland


**Occupation:** Student


**Played **Magic** Since:** 1995 (summer)


**Best Accomplishments:**  

- 33rd Grand Prix Amsterdam '95 (?)


**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Vienna?**  

Tired and with headaches on Saturday


**What draft strategy did you use and would you change this strategy if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

No I wouldn't change them. I played with 3 different decks.


**What do you think about *Odyssey* as a draft format?**  

Two colors again! Great!!


**How did you prepare?**  

Not much at all. Two Pre-release and one draft.
