Buy a Box Promotion
===================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on January 16, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/tcg/products/bng/EN_BNG_BuyABox_Booster_Display.jpg) Here's an offer sure to appeal to every player: The first 20 (30 for Advanced locations) people that preorder or purchase a **Magic: The Gathering**® *Born of the Gods*™ booster box in your retail store, will get an Alternate Art Premium **FATED CONFLAGRATION** promo card! But hurry, this offer is limited to **ONLY** the first 20 (or 30) people per store who have either preordered or purchase a full booster box! 

  
![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/tcg/products/bng/EN_BNG_BuyABox_Promo_Card.jpg)* [List of participating stores (North amp; Latin America)](/Magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/daily/events/bngbuyaboxpromolocations)
