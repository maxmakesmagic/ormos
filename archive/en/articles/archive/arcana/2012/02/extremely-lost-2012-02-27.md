Extremely Lost
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 27, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Here is the art for the *Dark Ascension* card [Lost in the Woods](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lost+in+the+Woods).


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/916_lostinthewoods.jpg)[Lost in the Woods](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lost+in+the+Woods) | Art by [Matt Stewart](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Matt+Stewart%22%5D)


Does something look odd here? Consider the way the flames of the torch look a little strange. Look at the top of the picture. Do those look like the *tops* of trees? Wait a minute... these people aren't just lost! They're *upside-down*! No wonder that guy on the right looks so disturbed!
