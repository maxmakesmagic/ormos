*Morningtide*'s Re-Cycle
========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 7, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)

Re-deem the Lost. Re-search the Deep. Re-vive the Fallen. Re-lease the Ants. Re-cross the Paths. Five *Morningtide* cards that re-cursively re-ward you for re-peatedly clashing.
