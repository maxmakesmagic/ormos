The 9-0 Decklists
=================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 28, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











Yesterday, out of 1,818 competitors, six players managed to go 9-0: Gold-level pro Thomas Hendriks, GP Lille 2015 quarterfinalist Thomas van der Paelt, multiple GP Top 8er Michael Bonde, Manuel Menges, Alessandro Casamenti, and first-time GP player Mike Boulinguiez (pictured here, left to right, with Boulinguiez in front).


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gplil16/9-0.jpg)


Here are their decklists, none like the other, a testament to the diversity of the format!
