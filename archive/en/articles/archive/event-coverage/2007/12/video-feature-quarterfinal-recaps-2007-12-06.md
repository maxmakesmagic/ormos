### Quarterfinal Recap: Nassif vs. van Heeswijk


Gabriel Nassif dispatched Roel van Heeswijk in the quarterfinals of the 2007 World Championships. Ben Coleman and Evan Erwin bring you a recap of the match and a post-game interview with the winner.




### Quarterfinal Recap: Mori vs. Peleg


Uri Peleg defeated 2005 World Champ Katsuhiro Mori in the quarterfinals of the 2007 World Championships. Randy Buehler and Evan Erwin bring you a recap of the match and a post-game interview with the winner.




### Quarterfinal Recap: Chapin vs. Nakano


Patrick Chapin emerged victorious over Yoshitaka Nakano in the quarterfinals of the 2007 World Championships. Brian David-Marshall and Evan Erwin bring you a recap of the match and a post-game interview with the winner.




### Quarterfinal Recap: Huber vs. Otsuka


Kotaro Otsuka defeated Christoph Huber in the quarterfinals of the 2007 World Championships. Rich Hagon and Evan Erwin bring you a recap of the match and a post-game interview with the winner.
