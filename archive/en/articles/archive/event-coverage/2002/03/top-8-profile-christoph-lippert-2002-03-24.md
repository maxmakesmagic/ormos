Top 8 Profile: Christoph Lippert
================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 24, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbar02/a915.jpg)**Name:** Christoph Lippert

**Age:** 22

**Occupation:** Student

**Where do you live?** Munich (Germany)

**Since when do you play Magic?** 1995

**Best accomplishments:** Several Grand Prix Top 8's

**What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Barcelona; what did you expect to find?**  

I skipped on Osaka and wanted to spend my holiday playing Limited Grand Prix tournaments and Barcelona is sooo beautiful!

**What do you think is the main reason for you to make Top 8 in this tournament and why?**  

I had a bad sealed deck but I played five rounds against people from Spain or Portugal...  

And I beat Noah Boeken (of course).

**What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

Reinhard Blech will beat 'em all.

**Anything else you'd like to share with the Sideboard Online readers?**  

A lotta love...
