With the ability to grant its wielder dozens of life per activation, the [Martyr of Sands](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Martyr+of+Sands), [Proclamation of Rebirth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Proclamation+of+Rebirth) combo has been a potent mixture since it first came on the scene. Could it be the breakout hit of this Extended season? Scott Honigmann seems to think so, using his white washed version to score the top slot at his Louisville, KY PTQ. You can find the entire Lousiville Top 8 [here](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/pro-tour%E2%80%93honolulu-qualifying-season-top-8-decklists-2009-01-13-0), or check out all the PTQ Top 8s [here](/en/events/coverage/pro-tour%E2%80%93honolulu-qualifying-season-top-8-decklists). And of course, find a PTQ near you [here](/en/node/640716).
