Round Fourteen Feature Match:
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 14, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall




 Brian David-Marshall is a New York–based game designer who has been involved with Magic since 1994, when he started organizing tournaments and ran a Manhattan game store. Since then, he has been a judge, a player, and one of the longest-tenured columnists on DailyMTG.com, as he enters his second decade writing for the site. He is also the Pro Tour Historian and one of the commentators for the Pro Tour. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpana03/fm10_1.jpg)Terry Tsang, or Terry T as his Legion of supporters call him, is one of the most popular players in the room. Like many a Magic veteran, Terry has drifted in and out of the game. He is currently trying to get back onto the gravy train. He and his opponent both had outside shots at making the Top 8 if they won this round. Terry was playing Twiddle Desire. His opponent was David Ochoa, or the Web as his friends from the San Diego area know him. More at home playing with Type 1 cards than anything else, David had piloted Red Deck Wins with abundant artifact hate to within one match of a shot at the Top 8.

**Game 1**

Terry held up a Canadian coin and offered to flip, "[Call](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call) it…Moose or Queen!"

"Moose."

"Moose it is."

David led off with a land and a [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox). Terry had only an [Ancient Tomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb) and that was promptly [Wasteland](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland)ed on David's second turn. Terry had no second land and played a [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) imprinting [Trade Secrets](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trade+Secrets).

![Grim Monolith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Grim+Monolith)David put out a clock in the form of a [Jackal Pup](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jackal+Pup) and used the [Shattering Pulse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shattering+Pulse) in his hand to smoke the [Grim Monolith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Monolith) that Terry played on the next turn. David played a morph. Terry played a Sapprazan Skerry but it was fated to be [Pillage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pillage)d.

Finally Terry played a [Seat of the Synod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seat+of+the+Synod) and was able to [Tinker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) it away. It was obvious that he though he was going to win since he started smiling broadly and counted off his spells. He used [Burst of Energy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+of+Energy) and [Dream's Grip](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dream%27s+Grip) to generate enough mana to cast [Mind's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind%27s+Desire) with one mana floating.

Terry asked if he could just flip up four cards but David said, "You can just shuffle." Terry played a [Gilded Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gilded+Lotus) and a Monolith for free and cast [Diminishing Returns](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diminishing+Returns) with two blue floating. Terry twiddled and Energy Bursted his [Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus) to have enough mana to cast Tendrils as the tenth spell of the turn.

Remember Terry's *Legions* of supporters? After seeing Terry pull off the combo they wished him luck and wandered away to play Highlander.

**Game 2**

Terry did not keep his hand, which contained a pair of Tendrils. He kept his next one but he David came out armed for bear this game. He opened with Port and [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) and had a second turn [Stone Rain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Rain) for Terry's Sapprazan Skerry. Terry had another. David played a [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer) and tapped the Skerry on his upkeep. Terry had no second land.

![Chrome Mox](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Chrome+Mox)David made a [Slith Firewalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slith+Firewalker) and sent it into the red zone with the Lavamancer. He then [Wasteland](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland)ed the Skerry. Terry discarded on his next turn and took three on David's next attack step.

He discarded again.

David swung and reinforced with [Jackal Pup](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jackal+Pup).

Terry played a Skerry and a [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) but David responded to the imprint with [Shattering Pulse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shattering+Pulse) and whatever slim hope Terry had of going off were shattered.

**Game 3**

"C'mon turn one kill!" Terry didn't draw the kill but he liked what he saw enough to keep it. David did not and sent his cards back for six fresh ones. Terry opened with Skerry. Dave had the [Jackal Pup](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jackal+Pup).

Terry untapped and played his Monolith and [Tinker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker)ed it away for [Gilded Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gilded+Lotus). He then [Tinker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker)ed away Seat of Synod for another [Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus). Terry then announced he would take mana burn and pass the turn.

David played a [Mountain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) and [Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) to cast [Rack and Ruin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rack+and+Ruin) on both the [Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus)es. There was a collective groan from the crowd as it happened. Terry played another Skerry and said go with his head shaking.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpana03/fm10_2.jpg)Terry took repeated beatings from the Pup as he tried to build back his hand. When he played a Seat it was [Shattering Pulse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shattering+Pulse)d with Buyback. On the next turn David had the [Wasteland](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland) for the Skerry with two counters. He followed that with a Port.

To make matters worse he drew another [Wasteland](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland).

Terry shook his head, "I figured the worst that could happen-"

Ochoa finished his sentence, "-[Chrome Mox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) - [Rack and Ruin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rack+and+Ruin)?"

**Final result:** David Ochoa - 2 Terry Tsang - 1
