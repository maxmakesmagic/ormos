Standard, Historic, and Alchemy Changes
=======================================


If you missed the news yesterday, there were many changes announced for formats on *MTG Arena*.


Here's the [latest Banned and Restricted announcement](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/news/january-25-2022-banned-and-restricted-announcement):


**Standard**:


[Alrund's Epiphany](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Alrund%27s+Epiphany) is banned.


[Divide by Zero](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Divide+by+Zero) is banned.


[Faceless Haven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Haven) is banned.


**Historic**:


[Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse) is banned (from suspended).


[Teferi, Time Raveler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Time+Raveler) [has been rebalanced and unbanned](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/alchemy-rebalancing-january-27-2022).


Plus, [the following cards were rebalanced for Alchemy and Historic](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/alchemy-rebalancing-january-27-2022):


* Acererak the Archlich
* Assemble from Parts
* Bloodrage Alpha
* Cloister Gargoyle
* Divide By Zero
* Dungeon Descent
* Ellywick Tumblestrum
* Fearsome Whelp
* Fate's Reversal
* Find the Path
* Hullbreaker Horror
* Inquisitor Captain
* Lier, Disciple of the Drowned
* Puppet Raiser
* Precipitous Drop
* Sanguine Brushstroke
* Teferi, Time Raveler
* Town-Razer Tyrant
* Triumphant Adventurer

[Check out these updates in the dedicated article breaking them down card by card](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/alchemy-rebalancing-january-27-2022) before they're available to play this Thursday, January 27!


Mad Science Is Happening Now!
=============================


Don't miss your chance to fill out your collection of *Innistrad: Crimson Vow* Eternal Night card styles by playing in the Mad Science event going on right now until February 1!


This Alchemy event features the Zombies! special emblem with the text: "Whenever you cast an instant or sorcery spell, amass X, where X is that spell's mana value."


Check out the [complete details on the Mad Science event page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/mtg-arena-mad-science-event-2022-01-10).


Double Feature Draft
====================


From January 28 through February 10, join in a monster of a draft event, featuring a special blend of both *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* and *Innistrad: Crimson Vow* cards. You can also score a Dracula card style you don't already own each time you enter!
