Elves weren’t the only tribe represented at the top tables of Pro Tour–Berlin. Johan Sadeghpour took his list of Goblins all the way to the Top 16, narrowly missing an opportunity to play in the Top 8. His maindeck [Mogg Fanatic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogg+Fanatic)s and [Goblin Sharpshooter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sharpshooter)s worked wonders against a field of small Elves, and his ingenuity will no doubt see many players trying to copy his success come the PTQ season.

For more on Pro Tour–Berlin including blogs, feature matches, podcasts, and video coverage, check out [the official Pro Tour–Berlin event coverage](/en/node/502416)!
