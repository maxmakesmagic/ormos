After setting an attendance record just one year prior, with 1,800+ competitors, Grand Prix Paris 2009 broke its previous record by pulling in over 1,900 competitors to try their hand at *Zendikar* Sealed and Booster Draft. Adrian Rosada was the eventual champion. The Pro Tour returns to Paris this weekend as **Magic** Weekend kicks off the 2011 Pro Tour season! Will Paris once again break the attendance record?
