The January 19, 2016 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on January 19, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know for today, January 19, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by [Vintage Super League](#vintage), and [*West Wing* references](#westwing).


Today's Must See
================


1. [Legacy Dredge In Our Schools! Protect Your *Magic: The Gathering* Values](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=owKYK7yXBzI)!| Tolarian Community College

One of these days, the Professor will post a deck tech or preview video that isn't awesome. Probably. Maybe. I mean, it's possible, right?


Well, that day isn't today. This Legacy Dredge primer is something non-Legacy players might normally skip, but the first three minutes alone are worth viewing for anyone who's a fan of *Magic*. Come for the old-timey social satire, stay for a solid primer on one of the scariest decks in Legacy.


I should also point out that the Professor and Jimmy Wong (he of [The Command Zone](http://www.rocketjump.com/podcasts#thecommandzone) podcast) recently joined forces for Kitchen Table Fables, short videos about the imaginary world behind kitchen table battles. The first two videos can be viewed [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qElhy9kShpM) and [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ww7AKj7Xq0I).

After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These

Pauper and Commander Are Formats Too
====================================


1. [E032—The Commander Rules Update](http://www.commanderinmtg.com/2016/01/18/e032-the-commander-rules-update-commanderin-mtg-podcast.html) | Commanderin' MTG Podcast

Not discussed: why is the Prophet of Kruphix way, way better than Kruphix himself? Seems like Kruphix placed a lot of trust in his prophets, and then just kind of sat at home accumulating mana.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Vintage Super League](http://vintagesuperleague.com/) | Randy Buehler Goes Green

If you haven't already checked out the [Vintage Super League](http://vintagesuperleague.com/) streaming weekly on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic), then why not tonight? I mean, it's Tuesday. What else is there to do on a Tuesday? Tacos? You can eat those *while* you watch the VSL. It's a two-for-Taco-Tuesday!


At any rate, every week a bunch of Vintage stalwarts battle in the most powerful *Magic* format around. Tonight is the halfway point of the season, and it's still a pretty tight race up and down.


And if you tune in to the Vintage Super League tonight, you might just catch Randy Buehler battling, once again, with Goblin Charbelcher—though this time, he's gone green. The Living Wish sideboard gives him greater access to a few lands without dampening his ability to Charbelcher for lethal. Is it good? He's currently 2-2 in the League, so the answer is a resounding...maybe?
