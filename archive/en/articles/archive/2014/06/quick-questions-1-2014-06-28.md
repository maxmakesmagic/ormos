### GRAND PRIX MILAN 2014


Quick Questions #1
==================



![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)

Olle Rade




June 28, 2014
 








 What will you miss most about *Theros* Block Limited?







![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpmil14/QQAnteri.jpg)

***Fabrizio Anteri:** – I'll really miss all the ways to play around combat tricks. There are so many tricks in this format that it takes a lot of skill to play around them. Which makes combat very skill intense.* 




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpmil14/QQAlkio.jpg)

***Anssi Alkio:** – Scry. It's a good mechanic that really smooths out your draws. What I won't miss are the too agressive mechanics that are only good when you are ahead.* 






![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpmil14/QQMackl.jpg)

***Valentin Mackl:** – I'll really miss all the ways to play around combat tricks. There are so many tricks in this format that it takes a lot of skill to play around them. Which makes combat very skill intense.* 




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpmil14/QQDagen.jpg)

***Pierre Dagen:** – Scry. It's a good mechanic that really smooths out your draws. What I won't miss are the too agressive mechanics that are only good when you are ahead.*
