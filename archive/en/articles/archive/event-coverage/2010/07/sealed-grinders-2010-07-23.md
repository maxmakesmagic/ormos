Sealed Grinders
===============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 23, 2010 










The Last Chance Qualifiers, or "Grinders" as their affectionately known, are always popular before the National Championships, but that has never been as true as it was yesterday. 42 grinders were run, making the 28 we had last year look like a walk in the park. But were our judges worried? Not in the slightest. With almost military precision, the commandeered a nearby white board and managed the seating of the tournaments flawlessly. Tom LaPille would have been proud.
