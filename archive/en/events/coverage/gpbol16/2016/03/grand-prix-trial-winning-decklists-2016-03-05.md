Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists
==================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX BOLOGNA 2016](/en/events/coverage/gpbol16)
 on March 5, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_FrankKarsten.jpg)
By Frank Karsten











Yesterday, loads of players competed in the Grand Prix Trials to earn byes in the main event. In total, as many as 50 Grand Prix Trials fired! This morning, I took a random sample of 10 of winning decklists to get a first impression of what to expect over this weekend.


The sample size of 10 is small, but the Eldrazi villain was nowhere to be seen. Instead, players won Grand Prix Trials by going under the lumbering Eldrazi with nimble Elves or Robots, by sweeping their board with [Living End](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End) or [Supreme Verdict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Supreme+Verdict), or by tutoring for [Magus of the Moon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magus+of+the+Moon), [Big Game Hunter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Big+Game+Hunter), or [Platinum Emperion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Platinum+Emperion).
