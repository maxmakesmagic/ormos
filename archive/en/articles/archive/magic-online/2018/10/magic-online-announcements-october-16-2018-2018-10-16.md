MOCS Monthly Saturday
---------------------


On Saturday, October 20, at 7 a.m. PT, we're having a MOCS Monthly event for the December 22 MOCS Playoff. The event will feature *Guilds of Ravnica* Phantom Sealed and costs 35 MOCS Qualifier Points to enter.


To find out more information, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-announcements-august-21-2018-2018-08-21).




---

PTQ Sunday
----------



On Sunday, October 21st at 7 a.m. PT, we're having a Limited PTQ Finals event featuring *Guilds of Ravnica* Sealed. Enter this event with a Chandra or Jace Limited PTQ Finals Entry Token.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

Downtimes October 24 and December 5
-----------------------------------



The next two downtimes will be October 24, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC and December 5, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC. Please note that there are no more 'extended' or 'normal' downtimes; in the new world with fewer downtimes, they're all the same length of time.
