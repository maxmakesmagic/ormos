Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX SYDNEY 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpsyd15)
 on October 11, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Walkinshaw-Profile.jpg)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw











After many rounds of Swiss and some great games of *Magic*, the Top 8 for Grand Prix Sydney is set! Learn more about this event’s Top 8 competitors, their history at the top tables, and more by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles.

### John Seaton


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsyd15/GPSydney_T8_Profile_Seaton.jpg)


**Hometown:** Christchurch, New Zealand  
**Age:** 36  
**Occupation:** Cable Maker


**What colours did you play on Day 1?**  

White-Blue-Red.


**What were your colours in draft 1?**  

White-Black-Red.


**What were your colours in draft 2?**  

White-Black.


**What's your best *Magic* event finish and where/when?**  

11/10/2015 Grand Prix Sydney, top of the Swiss standings of a 996 players GP.


**Are you Team Allies of Zendikar, or Team Eldrazi?**  

Allies, allies, allies!
