 Looking for something new to try at your Friday Night **Magic** this week? Check out Jeroen Aga's super fun Naya Allies deck. It powers out tons of powerful Allies with [Harabaz Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harabaz+Druid), and can make a gigantic [Hada Freeblade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hada+Freeblade) very quickly. And you know Jeroen's deck is good, because he managed to go undefeated at Grand Prix–Brussels on Day 1, the largest Standard tournament in history. For more from the Grand Prix, visit the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/thrinaxes-sprout-giusti-brussels).
