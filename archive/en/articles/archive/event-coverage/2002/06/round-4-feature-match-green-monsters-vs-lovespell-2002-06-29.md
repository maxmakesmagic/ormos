Round 4 Feature Match: Green Monsters vs. Lovespell
===================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 29, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











This matchup features three of New England's finest- Chris Manning, Joel Frank and Bruce Cowley against the impressive trio of Eric Froelich, Alex Borteh and Chris Benafel. Both teams went into this match with perfect 3-0 records, and are looking to go into the second sealed deck with an undefeated record. 

Frank started off strong against Borteh, and had [Skywing Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skywing+Aven), [Ironshell Beetle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ironshell+Beetle) and [Cephalid Retainer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Retainer) out before Borteh had summoned any creatures. However, thanks to [Afflict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Afflict) forcing the Aven to bounce and an [Aven Smokeweaver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Smokeweaver), things were looking better for Borteh. This was before Frank used [Seton's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Desire) to make his Beetle 4/4, and followed up with a [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill) that Boreth would have to break. However, he did break it with [Treetop Sentinel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Sentinel), which put a stop to the 4/4 Beetle. [Soul Scourge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Scourge) gave Borteh a third flier, but Frank had [Gurzigost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gurzigost) to get through for massive damage, and took the first game.

Meanwhile, Manning had a full assortment of creatures, uncluding [Border Patrol](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Border+Patrol), [Hallowed Healer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hallowed+Healer), [Militant Monk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Militant+Monk), [Mystic Visionary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+Visionary), [Thaumatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thaumatog) and [Aven Fogbringer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Fogbringer). Benafel simply had a [Mystic Familiar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+Familiar). All was not lost, as Benafel summoned [Wayward Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wayward+Angel). [Thaumatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thaumatog) was still able to attack through it, and the game was busted wide open thanks to Manning's [Silver Seraph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silver+Seraph). Benafel's lone [Wayward Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wayward+Angel) was horribly outnumbered, and Manning won to put Green Monsters up 3-0 in games.

Cowley's aggressive black/red deck won the first game against Eric Froelich. In the second game, Froelich pounded the ground with [Elephant Ambush](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Ambush), a 5/5 [Springing Tiger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Springing+Tiger) and [Narcissism](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Narcissism) to give Lovespell their first game win. 

In his second game, Borteh had a pair of [Skywing Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skywing+Aven)s, but Frank summoned [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). If that wasn't good enough, he enchanted the monster with [Seton's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Desire) to make it huge and invincible. Still, Borteh was able to fly over for the win in time to force their match to a third game. 

Benafel and Manning went into a white on white stalemate in game two, with various high toughness creatures staring each other down. Manning's [Silver Seraph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silver+Seraph) and [Thaumatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thaumatog) formed a vicious tag team, but the board was still at a bit of a standstill, with Manning slowly chipping away at Benafel's life total. 

The third game between Cowley and Froelich started off with [Wormfang Drake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Drake) and [Elephant Ambush](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Ambush) smashing Cowley, while he had only a [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp). [Sandstone Deadfall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sandstone+Deadfall) gave Cowley some defense, but Froelich improved his board position even more wuth [Cephalid Looter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Looter) and [Nantuko Tracer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Tracer). Regardless, Cowley had [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption) and used it to kill the Tracer and Looter, and dealt two damage to Froelich. [Morbid Hunger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger) followed to kill the Elephant token, and all of a sudden Cowley was back in the game, A second Elephant Ambush put the pressure back on, and if Froelich ever got to eight lands there would be two more Elephants breathing down Cowley's neck. [Flame Burst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flame+Burst) and [Toxic Stench](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Toxic+Stench) killed the Elephant before it did any damage. [Narcissism](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Narcissism) and [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) made Froelich's board even better, but a flashed back [Morbid Hunger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger) was able to kill the Mongrel since Froelich only had one card in hand. Still, [Anurid Barkripper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anurid+Barkripper) and Milikin eventually filled up Froelich's side, and the power of [Narcissism](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Narcissism) was simply too much. Froelich won the game, putting Lovespell up in matches 1-0.

Frank started off his third game with [Thought Nibbler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought+Nibbler) and [Cephalid Scout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Scout), but missed his third land drop. He drew land in time, and after some trading, the game was essentially at a point of parity except for [Screams of the Damned](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Screams+of+the+Damned) on Borteh's side. The enchantment eventually cleared the board, and Borteh came back with [Skywing Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skywing+Aven) while Frank had [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). [Screams of the Damned](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Screams+of+the+Damned) kept clearing away Frank's threats, but Borteh had to be careful since he was at a much lower life total. [Acorn Harvest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acorn+Harvest) allowed [Seton's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Desire) to make a squirrel 3/3 with Lure. However, Borteh had [Aura Graft](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aura+Graft) to move the enchantment to his [Skywing Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skywing+Aven). That, along with [Soul Scourge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Scourge), gave Borteh the game, and Lovespell the match. Manning won his game and match against Benafel.

Lovespell- 2 Green Monsters- 1
