Players' Card Submissions
=========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 2, 2015 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff











**Chris Benafel**  

DoDo Keeper of Mue  

UB  

Creature - Wizard  

When DoDo Keeper of Mue comes into play, you may return target creature which does not share a creature type with another creature in play to its owner's hand.  

DoDo Keeper of Mue cannot be the target of spells or abilities.  

If DoDo Keeper of Mue is put into a graveyard from play, return it to its owner's hand instead.


**Kai Budde**  

Wisedrafter's Will  

U  

Enchantment  

Opponents play with their hand revealed.  

U, Sacrifice Wisedrafter's Will: Draw a card.  

UU, Sacrifice Wisedrafter's Will: Counter target spell.


**Dan Clegg**  

Clegg's Bauble  

0  

Artifact  

Clegg's Bauble comes into play tapped.  

T, sacrifice Clegg's Bauble: Add one colorless mana to your mana pool and draw a card.  

Sacrifice Clegg's Bauble: Draw a card.


**Kamiel Cornelissen**  

Doomsday Clock  

4  

Artifact  

At the beginning of your upkeep, put an omen counter on Doomsday Clock.  

At the beginning of your upkeep, for each omen counter on Doomsday Clock, sacrifice a permanent and draw two cards.


**Jon Finkel**  

Lightmage  

1WU  

Creature - Wizard  

2/3  

UU, T: Draw a card.  

WW, T: Target creature gains protection from the color of your choice until end of turn.


**Tsuyoshi Fujita**  

Master of Hokuo-Shinken  

BB  

Creature - Karate Master  

2/2  

At the beginning of your upkeep, put a karate counter on each creature with a karate counter on it.  

T: Put a karater counter on target creature.  

T: Destroy target creature with two or more karate counters on it.  

"You are already dead"


**Scott Johns**  

Dusk Owl  

1UB  

Creature - Bird  

3/1  

Flying.  

When Dusk Owl comes into play, look at the top four cards of target opponent's library and replace them in any order.


**Brian Kibler**  

Apocalyptic Dragon-Kin  

3RGW  

Creature - Dragon Angel  

4/4  

Flying  

When Apocalyptic Dragon-Kin comes into play, discard your hand and destroy all other permanents in play.


**Chris Pikula**  

Plaguewalker  

BR  

Creature - Zombie Wizards  

2/2  

Protection from white  

Whenever a creature comes into play, that creature gets -2/-2 until end of turn.


**Dave Price**  

Goblin Operative  

UW  

Creature - Merfolk Soldier Goblin  

2/1  

Protection from red  

Instead of paying this card's mana cost, you may instead discard a mountain.  

You may play Goblin Operative as an instant.


**Mike Pustilnik**  

Woodland Scholar  

1GG  

Creature - Druid  

2/3  

U, Pay 1 life: Draw a card.


**Scott Richards**  

Odungo, Aura Master  

1W  

Creature - Cleric Legend  

2/1  

Protection from black  

When Odungo, Aura Master comes into play, search your library for an enchantment, reveal it to all players, and put it into your hand. Skip your next draw phase.


**Antoine Ruel**  

Morphlure  

2WUU  

Creature - Shapeshifter  

3/3  

Whenever Morphlure leaves play, return all creatures in play to owner's hand.  

W: Morphlure gains protection from the color of your choice until end of turn.  

U: Untap Morphlure  

1: Morphlure gets -1/+1 until end of turn.  

1: Morphlure gets +1/-1 until end of turn.


**Olivier Ruel**  

Penguinscape  

U  

Enchantment  

All penguins becomes blue.  

"Take care, I taught them how to bite" - Oh, Penguin Master


**Tom van de Logt**  

Wall o'Walla  

UU  

Creature - Wall  

0/4  

As Wall o'Walla comes into play, choose a creature card.  

T: Return a creature of the chosen type to its owner's hand.


**Gary Wise**  

Artifact of Doom  

2  

Artifact  

As Artifact of Doom comes into play, choose a number.  

Spells with a converted mana cost of the chosen number cannot be played.
