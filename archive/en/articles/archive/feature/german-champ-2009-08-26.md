Sebastian Thaler is a solid pro who recently added the title of National Champion to his resume. He will helm the German National team at the World Championships in Rome this fall. The deck that got him there? Blue-White Merfolk, a popular strategy that has a powerful game against Five-Color Control. Read more about the event [from the official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/thaler-takes-title).
