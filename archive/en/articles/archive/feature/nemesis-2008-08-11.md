E**dge out the competition** 


...with the *Nemesis* expansion for the **Magic: The Gatherin**g trading card game. 


Check out the [Laccolith Warrior](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Laccolith%2BWarrior), a new creature type that hacks apart the weak links in your opponent's defense. Blocking them is only the start of your opponent's troubles. 


And then there are [Blastoderm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Blastoderm), a fine assortment of card types that give you the edge - ahead of schedule. 


Featuring 143 all-new, black-bordered cards, the *Nemesis* set is sold in four theme decks, and 15-card booster packs. 


**Give it to them. Get *Nemesis*.**
