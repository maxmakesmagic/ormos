United States WMCQ Winning Decklist
===================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on August 18, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 





World *Magic* Cup Qualifiers happened all over the world this past weekend. One of the first to punch his ticket to the World *Magic* Cup was Isaac Sears, who won his WMCQ in the United States with Rabblemaster Red. Congratulations to Isaac! See the full schedule of World *Magic* Cup Qualifers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/wmcq/schedule).
