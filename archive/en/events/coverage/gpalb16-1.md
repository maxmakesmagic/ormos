TOP 8 COVERAGE



Allen Wu is your Grand Prix Albuquerque 2016 Champion!


1266 descended upon Albuquerque, New Mexico to try their hand at *Shadows Over Innistrad* Limited. Nine rounds of Sealed Deck on Saturday thinned the herd as we saw powerful cards like Archangel Avacyn and Sorin, Grim Nemesis dominate the top tables. 352 players earned the right to return to battle their way through two drafts on Sunday. When the dust settled, only 8 remained.


Christian Keeth, Jacob Thiessen, William Craddock, Rick Wong, Allen Wu, Michael Simon, (3) Seth Manfield, and Jiachen Tao made the cut.


Allen Wu’s Blue-Black deck overcame Michael Simon in the quarterfinals. He would face his fiercest opponent yet in the semifinals; Seth Manfield tried to defeat Wu with an aggressive Red-Green werewolf strategy. Wu had different plans, though, and the champ found himself out-valued in two long games. William Craddock proved to be a formidable opponent, but in the third game of the finals a quickly established Delirium let Wu operate on all cylinders to secure victory and be crowned Grand Prix Albuquerque Champion!

DAY 1 COVERAGE



This morning, 1266 players descended upon Albuquerque, New Mexico with hopes of hoisting a trophy into the air. After nine rounds of *Shadows over Innistrad* Sealed Deck, only players with an X-3 record or better will be returning tomorrow for a chance at glory. Leading the pack with undefeated records are Michael Simon, Patrick Stein, Jared Carrier, Michael Scovazzo, and our reigning World Champion Seth Manfield!


*Shadows over Innistrad* introduces an exciting new Limited format where werewolves, vampires, zombies, and humans clash while trying to solve the mystery of victory. Our foray into the new Limited environment continues tomorrow with six rounds of *Shadows over* Innistrad Draft before a final Top 8 draft to decide the eventual champion! Stay tuned to continuing coverage of Grand Prix Albuquerque!
