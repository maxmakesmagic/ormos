Duel the Xbox Developers: June 27
=================================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on June 24, 2009 










### Xbox LIVE® Arcade – Game with Developers – June 27, 2009


Team members from Stainless Games, Ltd., Wizards of the Coast, Microsoft, and other **Magic**  developers behind the new [**Magic: The Gathering Duels of the Planeswalkers**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/DuelsOfThePlaneswalkers.aspx) will play Xbox LIVE® Gold Members on Saturday, June 27.


Join us [Saturday, June 27 from 2–5 p.m. ET](http://ww2.wizards.com/Site/LeavingWizards.aspx?url=http://www.xbox.com/en-US/community/calendar/&origin=) for a classic gaming session. Plus, this is your chance to get the XBLA **Spellslinger Achievement**  directly from the source by defeating **Duels of the Planeswalkers**  developers!
