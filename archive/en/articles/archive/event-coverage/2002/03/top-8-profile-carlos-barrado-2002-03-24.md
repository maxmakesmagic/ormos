Top 8 Profile: Carlos Barrado
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 24, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbar02/a912.jpg)**Name:** Carlos Barrado

**Age:** 25

**Occupation:** Computer Analyst 

**Where do you live?** Madrid

**Since when do you play Magic?** Dark

**Best accomplishments:**   

2000 Grand Prix Madrid Champion  

2000 Nationals Champion  

Third Pro Tour top 64

**What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Barcelona; what did you expect to find?**  

See some friends and make some money

**What do you think is the main reason for you to make Top 8 in this tournament and why?**  

Three byes yes and a good sealed deck

**What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

Noah Boeken will win - just because he told me
