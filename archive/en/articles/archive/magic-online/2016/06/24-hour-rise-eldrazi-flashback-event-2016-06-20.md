24-Hour Rise of the Eldrazi Flashback Event
===========================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on June 20, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











It's confirmed—Emrakul is the source of Innistrad's descent into madness. For the next 24 hours, Emrakul will be bringing a little madness to *Magic Online* as well, with *Rise of the Eldrazi* Flashback Draft Events!


### 24-Hour *Rise of the Eldrazi* Flashback Draft


**Start Times**:Monday, June 20, 8:00 a.m. until Tuesday, June 21, 8:00 a.m. All times are Pacific (UTC -7).  
**Location**:Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Queues  
**Entry Options**:


* Option 1: 10 Event Tickets
* Option 2: 2 Event Tickets, plus product
* Option 3: 100 Play Points

**Product**: 3 *Rise of the Eldrazi* booster packs  
**Size**: 8 players  
**Play Style**: Single-Elimination  
**Format**: Draft  
**Duration**: 10 minutes deck-building time. Three rounds, each round up to 50 minutes  
**Prizes**:




| Place | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | 200 Play Points | 1 |
| 2nd | 100 Play Points | 0 |
| 3rd-4th | 50 Play Points | 0 |
