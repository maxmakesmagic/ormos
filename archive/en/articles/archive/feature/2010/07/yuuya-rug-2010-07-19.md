While Katsuhiro Mori was the eventual champion, Pro Tour Player of the Year Yuuya Watanabe cooked up an innovative deck for the 2010 [Japan National Championship](/en/events/coverage/mori-defeats-ikeda-japan-title-0). The star opted to hybridize a blue-red-green deck to utilize [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander) and [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor). With the addition of ***Magic** 2011* to the format, heralding the exit of ***Magic** 2010* in the near future, what changes do you think the deck would benefit from for the new world of Standard?
