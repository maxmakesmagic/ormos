The Grinders—single elimination qualifier tournaments held before U.S. Nationals each year—have yielded some of the most innovative decks in **Magic**'s history. Perhaps the most famous was the first deck to utilize [Oath of Druids](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oath+of+Druids) in a competitive format. This version was played by Adrian Sullivan of the Cabal Rogue. Now recognized as one of the game's most powerful cards, [Oath of Druids](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oath+of+Druids) had just been released in the *Exodus* set. What innovative new decks will you cook up for your National Qualifiers this year? For more information on National Qualifiers, visit the [official site](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/events/nationalqualifiers).
