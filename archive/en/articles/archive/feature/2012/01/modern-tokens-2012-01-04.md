Modern Tokens
=============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 4, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











In preparation for the Modern Pro Tour Qualifier season, Modern Daily Events on **Magic Online** have been firing and showing off some exciting new decks. **Magic Online** player Bjcollins brought his take on the format to the table, going 3–1 in a recent event with a unique tokens deck. Bjcollins's deck features everything from old token making standbys like [Raise the Alarm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raise+the+Alarm) to new hits like [Midnight Haunting](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Midnight+Haunting), backed up by [Leyline of the Meek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leyline+of+the+Meek) and [Intangible Virtue](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intangible+Virtue)! Check out the deck and keep it in mind for the Modern PTQ season, starting this weekend.
