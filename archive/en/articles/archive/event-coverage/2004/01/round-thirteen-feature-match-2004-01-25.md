Round Thirteen Feature Match:
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 25, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











Tsuyoshi Ikeda made Top 4 at Pro Tour Yokohama, which was a draft event. Showing that he has talent in multiple formats, he's got two matches to go and a shot at the Top 8 of an Extended Grand Prix. He's playing Dancing Ghoul, while his opponent, Asuka Doi, is the lone day two player running [Aluren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aluren). ### Game 1

![maggot carrier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=maggot+carrier)Ikeda's [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) revealed two [Living Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+Wish), two lands and two [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)s. Doi's only green source of mana was the [Hickory Woodlot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hickory+Woodlot) he had played on turn one, so Ikeda had him lose a [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm). He had another [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) on turn two, and this time Doi responded with [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), which let him protect the cards he wanted to keep. The hand revealed was [Polluted Delta](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta), [Raven Familiar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raven+Familiar), two [Living Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+Wish) and [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition)- the [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) headed to the graveyard. Doi had [Aluren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aluren) waiting on top of his library and played it, summoned [Raven Familiar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raven+Familiar) for free, and had nothing past that. Ikeda cast [Vampiric Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor) on end step, getting [Burning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish). He untapped and cast it, getting [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy). It was cast naming [Living Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+Wish), getting both copies out of Doi's hand and leaving him with only a [Chrome Mox](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chrome+Mox) in his hand. With the [Aluren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aluren) and seemingly no pieces to go with it, Doi was stalled and Ikeda cast [Buried Alive](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive) to get [Sutured Ghoul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sutured+Ghoul) and two Cloudscrapers. It seemed the game was ready to end, but Doi had drawn [Vampiric Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor), and used it to get [Cavern Harpy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cavern+Harpy). He then went off with the Familiars, getting the Savage and explained that he would win by getting [Soul Warden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+Warden) and casting [Living Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+Wish) to get [Maggot Carrier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Maggot+Carrier).

Ikeda- 0 Doi- 1

### Game 2

Ikeda looked at his opening draw and rubbed his forehead with his hand, staring at the cards as he took a deep breath or two. After a few moments, he quickly sent it back and shuffled up to draw six. He played [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), seeing a hand of Savage, Familiar, four lands, and a [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) that headed to the graveyard. Doi played [Hickory Woodlot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hickory+Woodlot), and Ikeda played another [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), looking at Doi with a "Please tell me you drew something this can hit" expression. Sure enough, Doi had drawn [Vampiric Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor), and Ikeda was extremely vocal expressing how happy it was to have it sent to the graveyard. Doi went searching with the Familiar, and Ikeda cast [Vampiric Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor) on end step. He then cast [Buried Alive](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Alive), getting the Cloudscrapers and Ghoul. This left him with only one card in hand, and Doi confidently cast [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)- Ikeda showed him [City of Traitors](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Traitors) and they both laughed. With Ikeda's strategy stalled, Doi cast a Tutor of his own, and played [Aluren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aluren). This led to [Wirewood Savage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Savage) and [Cavern Harpy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cavern+Harpy), and Ikeda conceded.

Ikeda- 0 Doi- 2
