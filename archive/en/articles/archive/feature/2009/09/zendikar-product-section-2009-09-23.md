*Zendikar* Product Section
==========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 23, 2009 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By magicthegathering.com Staff











The [*Zendikar* Prerelease](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/prereleases) is this weekend! Only a few scant days away! And the [Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/zendikar) has its final update today! There's a new puzzle, a new wallpaper, and a new setting preview: *The World of Zendikar*! And the Visual Spoiler, an archive of all preview cards that have been officially released. Watch the Visual Spoiler and the Product Section intently, because it's going to be updated frequently as we count down to the [*Zendikar* Prerelease](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/prereleases). Which is on September 26-27.


[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/features/feature54a_ss.jpg)](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/zendikar)
  
That's pretty much all we can say about it, so why don't you just [go to the *Zendikar* Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/zendikar) now?
