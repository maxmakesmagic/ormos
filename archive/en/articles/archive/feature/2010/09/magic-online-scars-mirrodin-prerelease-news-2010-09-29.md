Magic Online *Scars of Mirrodin* Prerelease News
================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 29, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Return to the metal plane of Mirrodin and experience the newest **Magic: The Gathering** expansion before it goes on sale… and now prereleases start earlier than ever before! All *Scars of Mirrodin* prerelease players will also earn a [Wurmcoil Engine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wurmcoil+Engine) prerelease card! 

The *Scars of Mirrodin* prerelease will feature three separate parts: Sealed Swiss Flights, Booster Draft Flights, and Casual Drafts. See below for details on these great events. 

 NOTE: *Scars of Mirrodin* becomes legal in ALL constructed formats following the October 13 downtime. 

### *SCARS OF MIRRODIN* PRERELEASE SWISS SEALED FLIGHTS

*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Thursday, October 14 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC) and ending Saturday, October 16 at 10:00 PM PDT (Sunday 5:00 UTC).  
*LOCATION:*  Sealed Swiss Queues room.   
*ENTRY REQUIREMENT:*  30 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 6 *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs for this event.  
*SIZE:*  16 Players.  
*DURATION:* Deck building time plus 4 rounds, Swiss Pairings. Play in all four rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 12 | 10 *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs | 3 |
| 9 | 4 *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs | 1 |
| 6 | 1 *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in a *Scars of Mirrodin* Prerelease Tournament will receive 1 copy of the prerelease [Wurmcoil Engine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wurmcoil+Engine). Players with at least 9 match points at the end of the tournament will also receive a premium foil Wurmcoil Engine. ### *SCARS OF MIRRODIN* PRERELEASE DRAFT FLIGHTS

*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Saturday, October 16 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC) and ending Saturday, October 16 at 10:00 PM PDT (Sunday 5:00 UTC).  
*LOCATION:*  8 Player Draft room.   
*ENTRY REQUIREMENT:*  20 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 3 *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs for this event.  
*SIZE:*  8 Players.  
*DURATION:* Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, single elimination.   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the final standings at the end of the tournament.



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Place** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 1st | 4 *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs | 1 |
| 2nd | 3 *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs | 0 |
| 3rd-4th | 2 *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in a *Scars of Mirrodin* Prerelease Draft will receive 1 copy of the prerelease [Wurmcoil Engine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wurmcoil+Engine). The winner of the Prerelease Draft will also receive a premium foil Wurmcoil Engine. ### *SCARS OF MIRRODIN* CASUAL DRAFT FLIGHTS

*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Sunday, October 17 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC) and ending at the beginning of the scheduled downtime on Wednesday, November 3.  
*LOCATION:*  Casual Draft room.   
*ENTRY REQUIREMENT:*  Product  
*PRODUCT:*  3 *Scars of Mirrodin* booster packs.  
*SIZE:*  2, 4, or 8 Players.  
*DURATION:* Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, Swiss style pairings.   
*PRIZES:* No Prizes for this event.
