More Than One Way to Infect
===========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 7, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 







Infect decks have been a part of Modern since its inception. Sam Black used the currently banned [Blazing Shoal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blazing+Shoal) to get a 10-power infect creature as early as the second turn to a semifinal appearance at [Pro Tour Philadelphia](/en/events/coverage/estratti-starts-modern-revolution-philly). Ari Lax made a deep run at [Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica*](/en/events/coverage/cifka-savors-pro-tour-breakfast-champions) with a BUG version that used green pump spells to quickly assemble ten poison counters. 

![Phyrexian Crusader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Phyrexian+Crusader) Now, Waterd1 has found success with a slower, mono-black version of the deck, winning a Premier Event on **Magic Online**. Waterd1's deck features lots of black disruption in the form of discard and removal spells, relying on only a few threats to win the game. This allows Waterd1's deck to focus on the poison route, with strong creatures such as [Phyrexian Crusader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Crusader) and [Phyrexian Vatmother](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Vatmother) doing the dirty work. These creatures are immune to [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt), which is hugely desirable given [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)'s popularity in Modern. These creatures also essentially get a double bonus from [Runechanter's Pike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Runechanter%27s+Pike). 

 One excellent card for this deck is [Funeral Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Funeral+Charm). It is a removal spell against decks that have a ton of creatures to kill, and it's a discard spell when you need lots of discard. Also, giving one of your infect creatures 2 extra power might often provide a lethal attack, and I'm sure Waterd1 used swampwalk at least once to make a creature unblockable. What a versatile card for a single mana!
