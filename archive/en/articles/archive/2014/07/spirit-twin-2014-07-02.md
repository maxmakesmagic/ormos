Spirit Twin
===========



 Posted in [ARTICLES HOME](/en/articles)
 on July 2, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 






With Modern PTQ season underway, plenty of players are innovating new ways to combine the thousands of Modern legal cards at their disposal. *Magic Online* user coughl has found a deck that is capable of doing something few other Modern decks are capable of.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Geist%20of%20Saint%20Traft)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist%20of%20Saint%20Traft)
Win on the third turn.



[Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch) + [Splinter Twin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Splinter+Twin) is one of the defining combinations in Modern, but is often thought about as a turn-four combo. After all, [Splinter Twin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Splinter+Twin) costs four mana. However, this deck features [Noble Hierarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noble+Hierarch) and [Birds of Paradise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) to accelerate the combo by a full turn. Like many great Modern combo decks, this deck comes with a potent backup planâ€”[Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft). With the mana accelerants in this deck, a turn-two [Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft) provides a potent threat, and often a flurry of [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)s, [Remand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Remand)s, and [Snapcaster Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snapcaster+Mage)s is enough for [Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft) to attack for 20 damage quickly. Of course, when the opponent commits heavily to stopping [Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft), a [Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch) or [Pestermite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pestermite) comes down and picks up a [Splinter Twin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Splinter+Twin), ending the game in a hurry.
