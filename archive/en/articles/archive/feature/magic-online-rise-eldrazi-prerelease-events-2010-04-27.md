**Magic** Online *Rise of the Eldrazi* Prerelease Events
========================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 27, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











I*n their quest to uncover ancient treasures, Planeswalkers have awakened the Eldrazi—parasitic titans of the Æther, imprisoned on Zendikar for thousands of years. Seemingly unstoppable, these world-eaters threaten the entire Multiverse, and their very presence has changed everything you know about Zendikar. To survive, you’ll have to be at the very top of your game.* 

Play in a *Rise of the Eldrazi*  Prerelease Event and experience this the newest set before it goes on sale. Players will also earn an [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Aeons+Torn) prerelease card! 

 NOTE: *Rise of the Eldrazi* becomes legal in ALL constructed formats following the May 6 downtime. 

### *RISE OF THE ELDRAZI* PRERELEASE SWISS SEALED FLIGHTS

*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Friday, May 7 at 10:00 AM PDT (17:00 UTC) and ending Saturday, May 8 at 9:00 PM PDT (Sunday 4:00 UTC). *LOCATION:*  Sealed Swiss Queues room.   
*ENTRY FEE:*  30 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 6 *Rise of the Eldrazi* booster packs for this event.  
*SIZE:*  16 Players.  
*DURATION:* Deck building time plus 4 rounds, Swiss Pairings. Play in all four rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 12 | 10 *Rise of the Eldrazi* booster packs | 3 |
| 9 | 4*Rise of the Eldrazi* booster packs | 1 |
| 6 | 1*Rise of the Eldrazi* booster packs | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in an *Rise of the Eldrazi* Prerelease Tournament will receive 1 copy of the prerelease [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Aeons+Torn). Players with at least 9 match points at the end of the tournament will also receive a premium foil Emrakul, the Aeons Torn.
