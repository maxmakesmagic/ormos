Day 1 Undefeated Decklists
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 20, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











Of all the 1,510 players who took part in the Grand Prix this weekend only four managed to escape the carnage of the first day with undefeated records: Four-time Grand Prix champion Martin Jůza was on Abzan, Pro Tour *Battle for Zendikar* finalist Ryoichi Tamada brought Bant Company, while newcomers Olivier Gheysen and Steve Bains played Grixis Control and Dark Jeskai, respectively.


Curiously absent? Rally the Ancestors. In fact, there wasn't even a single Rally player among the nine who played for a 9-0 record in Round 9. Instead, enjoy the following lists:
