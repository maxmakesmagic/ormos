Amulet Blood at GP Omaha
========================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on January 29, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/shuhei_authorpic.jpg)
By Shuhei Nakamura




Shuhei Nakamura is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, former Player of the Year, and is one of just six players with more than 400 lifetime pro points. 






You have gone turn-one [Gemstone Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gemstone+Mine) into [Amulet of Vigor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Amulet+of+Vigor), and just played a land to cast [Summer Bloom](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summer+Bloom) the second turn. [Simic Growth Chamber](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Simic+Growth+Chamber) and [Primeval Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan) are your only cards in-hand.



What else can you do this turn?



The answer is 8 damage plus six mana's worth of lands this turn, and 16 more damage next turn.



How? Easy.


[![](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Amulet+of+Vigor)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Amulet+of+Vigor)

You can play three more lands thanks to [Summer Bloom](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summer+Bloom), so play [Simic Growth Chamber](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Simic+Growth+Chamber). The Chamber enters the battlefield untapped due to [Amulet of Vigor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Amulet+of+Vigor), but you must return one land to your hand. Tap the Chamber for GU and return it to your hand. Doing this three time gives you six mana of GGGUUU. Summon [Primeval Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan), searching for two lands.



Search for [Boros Garrison](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Garrison) and [Slayers' Stronghold](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slayers%27+Stronghold). (Returning a land from the Garrison.)



These enter the battlefield untapped, so pump your Titan into an 8/6 haste vigilance and attack, bring two more lands. including [Sunhome, Fortress of the Legion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunhome%2C+Fortress+of+the+Legion). You're all set.



With the lands you play next turn and search from your Titan attack, you can activate Sunhome and Stronghold to deal 16 damage that turn, for 24 damage total. Easy, huh?



If you don't want to be fancy, you can always win the simple route and cast [Hive Mind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hive+Mind) into [Summoner's Pact](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoner%27s+Pact).
