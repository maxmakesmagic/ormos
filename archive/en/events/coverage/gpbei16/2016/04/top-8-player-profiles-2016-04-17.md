Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX BEIJING 2016](/en/events/coverage/gpbei16)
 on April 17, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/benswa-author.jpg)
By Ben Swartz











After 15 rounds, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!




---






### Hironobu Sugaya



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpbei16/gpBeijing16_top8_sugaya.jpg)



**Hometown:** Chiba, Japan  
**Age:** 36  
**Occupation:** Producer  
**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Day 1 Record:** 8-1  
**Day 2 Draft 1 Record:** 3-0 (U/B)  
**Day 2 Draft 2 Record:** 2-0-1 (B/R)  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 2008 GP Manila Champion




**What draft archetype do you think is best and why?**  

 White-Green Humans. The Green uncommon creatures are very strong.




**What is your favorite uncommon in draft and why?**  
[Tooth Collector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tooth+Collector), I used it in both Day 1 and in Day 2!

**Hometown:** Beijing  
**Age:** 45  
**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Day 1 Record:** 8-1  
**Day 2 Draft 1 Record:** 2-1  
**Day 2 Draft 2 Record:** 2-0-1

 R/G Wolf




**What is your favorite uncommon in draft and why?**  
[Duskwatch Recruiter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duskwatch+Recruiter).





---






### Kazuaki Fujimura



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpbei16/gpBeijing16_top8_fujimura.jpg)



**Hometown:** Osaka, Japan  
**Age:** 25  
**Occupation:** Freelancer  
**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Day 1 Record:** 7-2 (W/G Humans)  
**Day 2 Draft 1 Record:** 3-0 (W/G Humans)  
**Day 2 Draft 2 Record:** 3-0 (U/G Aggro)  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Next Pro Tour Champion




**What draft archetype do you think is best and why?**  

 W/G Humans, it's easy and strong. You don't need rares.




**What is your favorite uncommon in draft and why?**  
[Duskwatch Recruiter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duskwatch+Recruiter). You can take it without hesitation. It's like a mythic rare, but it's uncommon.

### Gao Zhen Xing



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpbei16/gpBeijing16_top8_gao.jpg)



**Hometown:** Beijing  
**Age:** 33  
**Occupation:** Manager  
**Number of Byes:** 0  
**Day 1 Record:** 9-0-0  
**Day 2 Draft 1 Record:** 1-1-1 (W/B)  
**Day 2 Draft 2 Record:** 2-0-1(B/R)  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 2004 GP Kuala Lumpur 3rd place. 2013 GP Singapore Top 8




**What draft archetype do you think is best and why?**  

 Madness. It's very beatdown and fast.




**What is your favorite uncommon in draft and why?**  
[Kindly Stranger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kindly+Stranger). She is a solid creature with a removal ability.
