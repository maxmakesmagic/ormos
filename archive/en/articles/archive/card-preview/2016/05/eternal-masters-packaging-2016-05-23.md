Eternal Masters Packaging
=========================



 Posted in **Card Preview**
 on May 23, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






One of the cool things about the otherwise rote job of revealing the set packaging to all you fine folks is that sometimes that means I get to also reveal preview cards to you that happen to be on the packaging.


And we've got some doozies on the *Eternal Masters* packaging.


First up, it's banned in Modern, incredibly powerful in Legacy, and one of the great Cube all-stars, let's hear it for *Deeeeeeaaaaattttthhhhhhh-riiiiiiiiiiite Shaaaaaaaaa-maaaaaan*.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/images/daily/XC6NgZgpGL.png)
