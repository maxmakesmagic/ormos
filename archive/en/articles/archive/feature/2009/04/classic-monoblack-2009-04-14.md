Fans of "old" formats can enjoy them on **Magic Online** just as they do in the real world! This list managed a recent Premier Event Top 8 on the back of powerful black creatures like [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade) and [Tombstalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tombstalker). Joining the team is anti-aggro artifact creature [Epochrasite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Epochrasite). Of course, creatures alone aren't enough as [Crucible of Worlds](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crucible+of+Worlds), [Hymn to Tourach](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hymn+to+Tourach), [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), and a host of other powerful spells pitch in to keep opponents from fighting fair.
