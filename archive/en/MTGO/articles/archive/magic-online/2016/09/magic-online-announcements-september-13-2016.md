**The Special Full Innistrad Sealed League Begins Tomorrow**


Visit Innistrad in the Full Innistrad Sealed League Event, running on *Magic Online* September 14–21!


Players can enter the Full Innistrad Sealed League starting September 14, 10:00 a.m. PT/5:00 p.m. UTC. Entrants get two *Innistrad*, one *Dark Ascension*, two *Shadows over Innistrad*, and one *Eldritch Moon* booster pack to build from. Once your deck is built, play five matches before the League closes on September 20, 7:00 p.m. PT/2:00 a.m. UTC.


Enjoy your visit to Innistrad, and don’t forget a [Jar of Eyeballs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jar+of+Eyeballs) to commemorate your trip!

**The Submission Deadline for the "You Make the Cube" Contest is September 14**


Calling all aspiring Cube crafters! We're looking for a new Cube to compliment the Vintage and Legacy Cubes, but we want to know what kind of Cube *you* would like to play. Submit your pet Cube project to the You Make the Cube contest, *Magic* R&D will pick their favorites, and we'll put them to a vote! The deadline for submissions is **September 14, 11:59 p.m. PT/September 15 6:59 a.m. UTC**!


The winning Cube designer will get sweet prizes and will go down in history as the designer of the next great *Magic Online* Cube! Get the full details [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/announcing-you-make-cube-contest-2016-08-24).

**The Year of Modern Flashbacks Continues with *Scars of Mirrodin***


It's 2016: The Year of Modern Flashbacks! If you want to build up a collection for Modern, these Flashback Drafts add everything you draft to you collection once the event is over. Even if Modern isn't your thing, you can enjoy a leisurely Draft through the planes of the past with rotating formats each week!


*Magic 2011* Flashback Drafts run until September 14, 10:00 a.m. PT/5:00 p.m. UTC, and are followed up with triple *Scars of Mirrodin* Drafts. Get the full schedule [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/content/magic-online-limited-events#YearOfModernFlashbacks) and mark your calendar for the formats you're most excited about!

**Double Pro Tour Qualifier Weekend**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: there's a double Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) weekend coming up! Standard Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries and the *Shadows over Innistrad*-*Eldritch Moon* Sealed Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries begin Thursday, September 15, and run through Tuesday, September 20. The Standard Finals will take place on Saturday, September 17, at 9:00 a.m. PT/4:00 p.m. UTC and the *Shadows over Innistrad*-*Eldritch Moon* Sealed Finals are Sunday, September 18, at 7:00 a.m. PT/2:00 p.m. UTC.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#AER_schedule).

**Reminder: No Downtime on September 14**


In an effort to make sure *Magic Online* is available when you want to play *Magic*, we’re working on ways to reduce the number of times we have to bring the system offline for maintenance. As a first step we’re simply going to eliminate at least one downtime each month.


Here are the dates of the exact downtimes we’ll be skipping in the second half of 2016:


* September 14
* October 12
* November 2
* November 9
* November 30
* December 14
