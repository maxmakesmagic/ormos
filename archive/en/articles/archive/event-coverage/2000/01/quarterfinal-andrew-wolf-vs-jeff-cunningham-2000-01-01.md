Quarterfinal: Andrew Wolf vs. Jeff Cunningham
=============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptsd02/a538.jpg)*Jeff Cunningham*The last draft of the swiss rounds saw Jeff Cunningham sitting next to Don Gallitz in pod 1. Cunningham came in with the intention of drafting black/blue, and Gallitz wanted white/blue. They ended up fighting over their common color, but both did well enough to find their way to today. Ironically, Cunningham and Gallitz sat next to each other during the Top 8 of this draft, and once again, both players fought over blue. During the swiss rounds, Cunningham easily had the better deck of the two, but this time Gallitz cut off quite a bit of blue from Cunningham.


On the other hand, Wolf is running a very solid red/green deck, with all the usual suspects:


### Game 1


Wolf summoned a second turn Mongrel, while Cunningham had [Crypt Creeper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Creeper). The Mongrel came through for two damage, and [Barbarian Lunatic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbarian+Lunatic) followed. Crypt Creeper swung back for two, and Cunningham brought out [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp). Wolf's team then attacked for four, and [Chainflinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger) came out. Cunningham would need to kill off the pinger, or his two creatures would soon die. He cast [Concentrate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Concentrate), and said go. [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) took down [Crypt Creeper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Creeper), and [Chainflinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger) pinged away the [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp). This left Cunningham with no creatures on the board, and the Lunatic and Mongrel attacked for another four points of damage.


Cunningham then played another Creeper and [Millikin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Millikin). [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) was flashed back on the Creeper, and Wolf's [Chainflinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger), Mongrel, and Lunatic all attacked. The [Millikin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Millikin) chump blocked, and Cunningham went to six life. [Morbid Hunger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger) killed off the [Chainflinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger), but Wolf had another Firebolt to finish up the job after an attack.


Wolf- 1 Cunningham- 0 


### Game 2


Yet again, Wolf had a second turn Mongrel, but this time Cunningham had [Rites of Refusal](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rites+of+Refusal). He discarded island, and the super-bear was countered. He followed up with [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp). The Imp died to [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt), and Cunningham summoned [Cephalid Scout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Scout). Wolf then brought out [Krosan Archer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Archer), while Cunningham had [Fledgling Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Imp). [Halberdier](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Halberdier) hit the ground running thanks to [Reckless Charge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reckless+Charge), and both of Wolf's creatures attacked. Cunningham blocked the Archer with the Imp and Scout since Wolf was left tapped out. The result was Cunningham taking six damage, while the Archer and Imp traded. [Dematerialize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dematerialize) bounced back the [Halberdier](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Halberdier), giving Cunningham a little more room to breathe.


The [Halberdier](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Halberdier) came back out, and [Blazing Salvo](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blazing+Salvo) targeted the Scout. Cunningham chose to take five damage, going down to nine life points. [Patriarch's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Patriarch%27s+Desire) then killed off the [Halberdier](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Halberdier). Wolf came right back with [Krosan Archer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Archer), and Cunningham brought out [Bloodcurdler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodcurdler). [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) was flashed back to burn away the Scout, and the Archer came through for damage. Cunningham brought out [Aven Fisher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Fisher), and Wolf flashed back [Reckless Charge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reckless+Charge) on the Archer, and attacked. The Fisher took one for the team, saving Cunningham from taking five damage. 


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptsd02/a534.jpg)*Andrew Wolf*Firebolt took out Cunningham's [Crypt Creeper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Creeper), and Wolf played [Seton's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Desire) on the Archer to deal more damage. [Morbid Hunger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger) went to Wolf's dome, but the damage had been done. Cunningham tried to dig into an answer with Concentrate, and summoned [Crypt Creeper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Creeper). Wolf brought out [Dwarven Strike Force](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dwarven+Strike+Force), and that was enough to put him up 2-0.


Wolf- 2 Cunningham- 0


### Game 3


Cunningham brought out [Crypt Creeper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Creeper), while Wolf commented, "I'll tell you one thing...I'm one lucky man today" and brought out [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). [Millikin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Millikin) showed up on Cunningham's side, and Wolf summoned [Krosan Avenger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Avenger). [Patriarch's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Patriarch%27s+Desire) took down the Mongrel, and Wolf attacked with the Avenger. It traded with the Creeper, and Wolf cast [Chainflinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger). It shot down the [Millikin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Millikin) on the following turn, while Cunningham brought out [Fledgling Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Imp). [Frenetic Ogre](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frenetic+Ogre) was countered with [Rites of Refusal](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rites+of+Refusal), and Cunningham attacked with [Fledgling Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Imp) for two point of damage. He then brought out [Rotting Giant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotting+Giant) to help stabilize the board.


Now was Cunningham's chance to become the aggressor, and he attacked for five with both his creatures. Wolf saw that Cunningham's defenses were down, double [Reckless Charge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reckless+Charge)d his [Chainflinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger), and attacked for eight damage. [Rotting Giant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotting+Giant) attacked for three, [Bloodcurdler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodcurdler) came out, and [Psionic Gift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psionic+Gift) enchanted [Fledgling Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Imp). Wolf then played [Overrun](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overrun) and [Reckless Charge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reckless+Charge)d his [Chainflinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger), which was his only creature. Cunningham blocked with [Bloodcurdler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodcurdler) and the Imp, and went down to two. [Scorching Missile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scorching+Missile) finished the job.


**Final Result:** Wolf- 3 Cunningham- 0
