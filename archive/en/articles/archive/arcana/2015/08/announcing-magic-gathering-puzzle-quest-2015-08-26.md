Announcing Magic: The Gathering—Puzzle Quest
============================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 26, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/MTG-PQ-LOGO-New.png)


*Magic: The Gathering—Puzzle Quest* blends the hypnotic match-three puzzle elements of the popular *Puzzle Quest* franchise with the strategic gameplay and expansive lore of the groundbreaking *Magic: The Gathering* trading card game.


**Platforms**: iOS (iPad and iPhone) and Android (Amazon and Google)


**Cost**: Free to download and play. In-app purchases available.


**Languages**: English, Japanese, Spanish, French upon launch.


**Publisher**: D3 Go!


For more information on the upcoming worldwide launch of *Magic: The Gathering—Puzzle Quest*, please follow us on Twitter [@MtGPuzzleQuest](https://twitter.com/MtGPuzzleQuest) or like us on Facebook at [facebook.com/MagicPuzzleQuest](https://www.facebook.com/MagicPuzzleQuest) for further updates and details.
