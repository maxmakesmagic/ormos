Get Your Nicol Bolas Playmat
============================



 Posted in **News**
 on March 2, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Get your hands on the new limited edition Nicol Bolas playmat for the **Magic: The Gathering** trading card game, available exclusively at a qualifying Wizards of the Coast Premier Store near you. Supplies are very limited, so you'd better act fast. Contact your [local participating Gold Tier Premier Store](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/030209goldtier) to find out how to get yours today!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/bolas_playmat.jpg)
