The powerful Legacy format is full of interesting combos you won't find anywhere else in the game. At the recent StarCityGames.com Legacy Open in [San Jose](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/20916_Live_Coverage_of_StarCityGamescom_San_Jose.html), Nick Spagnolo made the Top 16 using just such a combo: [Painter's Servant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Painter%27s+Servant) and [Grindstone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grindstone). By casting the Servant and turning *all* cards into one color, Nick can activated the [Grindstone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grindstone) to mill his opponent's entire library in one fell swoop. Cool!
