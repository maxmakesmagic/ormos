*Ice Age* Factoids and Statistics
=================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 4, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Gentlemen and ladies, start your calculators. Today's Magic Arcana takes a look at some of details of the *Ice Age* set from a numerical or factoid perspective.

[![Ice Age](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/global/images/magic_expansion_iceage_expansionLogo_en.jpg)](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/expansion/iceage)* **Release date:** June, 1995.
* **Number of cards:** 383.
* **New mechanics:** Cumulative upkeep, cantrips, and snow-covered lands.
* **Alphabetical first and last cards:** 
[Abyssal Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Abyssal%2BSpecter); [zuran spellcaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=zuran%2Bspellcaster).
![Elkin Bottle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Elkin+Bottle)* **White mana symbol:** 
[Redesigned](/en/articles/archive/white-mana-symbol-2003-02-06).
* **Plains art:**  A [mural](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/arcana/68).
* **Number of cards involving single-surface topology:** 
[1](/en/articles/archive/single-surface-topology-2002-03-29).
* **Number of instants:**  53.
* **Number of sorceries:**  29.
* **Number of lands:**  33 (including 3 of each basic land).
* **Number of enchantments:**  95 (including enchant creatures and enchant lands).
* **Number of non-creature artifacts:**  39.
* **Number of artifact creatures:**  6.
* **Number of those artifact creatures that count as walls:**  3.
![Polar Kraken](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Polar+Kraken)* **Number of creatures that aren't artifacts:**  128.
* **Largest creature:** 
[Polar kraken](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Polar%2Bkraken).
* **Card with the highest converted mana cost:** 
[Polar kraken](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Polar%2Bkraken).
* **Number of cards with "snow" in the name:**  11.
* **Number of cards with "ice" in the name:**  4 ([Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Justice) and [Fiery Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Justice) don't count).
* **Number of cards with "winter," "glacier" or "blizzard" in the name:**  1 each ([Winter's Chill](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Winter%27s+Chill), [Glaciers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glaciers), and [Blizzard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blizzard)).
* **Number of cards that mention banding:**  11.
* **Number of cards that mention cumulative upkeep:**  30.
![Skeleton Ship](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Skeleton+Ship)* **Number of legends:**  4.
* **Number of cards printed in *Ice Age* that were also printed in *Eighth Edition*:**  25.
* **Number of *Ice Age* cards currently restricted in Type 1:**  2 ([Demonic Consultation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demonic+Consultation) and [Necropotence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necropotence)).
* **Number of *Ice Age* cards currently banned in Type 1:**  1 ([Amulet of Quoz](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Amulet+of+Quoz)).
