Mono-Black Creatureless Control
===============================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on September 1, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 





When you ask most players what they think the best deck in Standard is, Mono-Black will likely be the most popular answer. However, these players are generally referring to a devotion deck featuring [Desecration Demon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desecration+Demon) and [Gray Merchant of Asphodel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gray+Merchant+of+Asphodel). Today's deck, while still mono-black, is strategically much different than the Black Devotion decks that have had so much success in Standard for the past year.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Corrupt)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt)
This deck has no creatures! No [Pack Rat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat). No [Lifebane Zombie](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lifebane+Zombie). No [Desecration Demon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desecration+Demon). These staples of Mono-Black Devotion are nowhere to be found. Instead, this deck is fueled by the card drawing provided by both [Sign in Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sign+in+Blood) and [Underworld Connections](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underworld+Connections). However, these cards cost life points, as does [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize). This makes the lifegain provided by [Corrupt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt) vitally important, and is the reason you see [Elixir of Immortality](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elixir+of+Immortality) and [Pharika's Cure](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pharika%27s+Cure) in the main deck. [Liliana Vess](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+Vess) is a very important card in this deck, if only because she finds these important [Corrupt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt)s for you.
