Parallel Lives
==============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 16, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











At the StarCityGames.com Invitational this past weekend, Jonathan Job showcased an exciting new Standard deck featuring [Parallel Lives](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Parallel+Lives)! Duplicating everything from Golem tokens to [Mimic Vat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mimic+Vat) tokens, the enchantment works overtime in this strategy. Check it out, and bring it to your Friday Night **Magic** tournament tonight!
