### Deck Pack 3: Rogue's Gallery & Mana Mastery


The blue and black “Rogues’ Gallery” deck disrupts your enemies’ plans with spells, then summons stealthy mages and shadowy undead to deliver the killing blows. Wield all five colors of magic with the “Mana Mystery” deck. A pentacolor array of spells will help you keep the battlefield clear while you set the stage for towering, indomitable creatures!
