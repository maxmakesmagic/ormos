The January 21, 2016 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on January 21, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know for today, January 21, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by [#mtgcosplay](#cosplay) and [tea time with Jacob and Alex](#tea).


Today's Must See
================


1. [Against the Odds: Realm Razer](http://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/against-the-odds-realm-razer) | MTGGoldfish | Saffron Olive

The Against the Odds series, if you haven't checked it out, is a series where Saffron Olive lets players vote on what card he'll play the next week—usually wacky or offbeat—and then watch and read along as he does just that. It's wacky fun, but it's also a cool exercise in "Is this good?" and "Can we really do that?" This week, oddly enough, he kind of can.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [Inside the Deck: *Oath of the Gatewatch* Prerelease](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/insidethedeck-012116-inside-the-deck-oath-of-the-gatewatch-prerelease/) | Gathering Magic

Inside the Deck goes inside an *Oath* Prerelease in [Orlandoooooo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9osskTGhb2s).


1. [*Oath of the Gatewatch* Set Review: Rare and Mythic Rare](http://lrcast.com/limited-resources-320-oath-of-the-gatewatch-set-review-rare-and-mythic-rare/) | Limited Resources | Marshall Sutcliffe and Luis Scott-Vargas

The boys are back with their second *Oath of the Gatewatch* set review, this time covering all of the rare and mythic rare cards in the set, and even the Expeditions! Which is good, because at a scant three hours long, this one felt a little light, guys.


You can catch their [common and uncommon review](http://lrcast.com/limited-resources-319-oath-of-the-gatewatch-set-review-commons-and-uncommons/) from last week, and if you're looking for even more *Oath* drafting reviews, there are [plenty](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/oath-of-the-gatewatch-limited-set-review-colorless-lands-and-gold/) [more](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?ID=13076&writer=Conley+Woods) from [other sources](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d23xLqYDipg).


1. [Your Ultimate Guide to European Eternal 2016](http://www.mindsportsacademy.com/News/Index/248) | Mind Sports Academy

If you're European, a Legacy aficionado, or you've just got a bunch of travel coming your way you just don't know what to do with, Mind Sports Academy—a European site dedicated to Scrabble, Chess, Go, and *Magic*—posted the slate of European *Legacy* and *Vintage* tournaments for 2016. So grab a suitcase and bring your Bazaar of Baghdads to Brussels, pack your Ponders for Paris, or make for Madrid with your Moxen, because Europe has all kinds of Eternal format options in 2016.


1. [Cards Got Banned and Stuff](https://soundcloud.com/cedtalks/cedtalks-12016-cards-got-banned-and-stuff-mtg) | CEDTalks | Cedric Phillips and Patrick Sullivan

Cedric Phillips returns with Patrick Sullivan to recap much of the news that's happened while the podcast has been on hiatus, including something about the Sacramento Kings, which I gather is a group of men who [pretend to be](http://espn.go.com/nba/team/_/name/sac) professional basketball players.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Hedron Alignment Combo by Carlos Gutierrez](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/carlosgutierrez-01202016-daily-deck-list-hedron-alignment-combo/)

Ah, living the Johnny dream. Hedron Alignment is tailor-made for someone—or many someones—to tackle the complex question it poses. People have tried wacky ways to do it, but Carlos Gutierrez at Gathering Magic has the Spikiest take on it I've seen so far. Is it good? Who knows. But it doesn't look bad. And when it comes to aligning the hedrons, this deck puts Carlos well ahead. [Read his article](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/carlosgutierrez-01202016-daily-deck-list-hedron-alignment-combo/) for the full explanation.
