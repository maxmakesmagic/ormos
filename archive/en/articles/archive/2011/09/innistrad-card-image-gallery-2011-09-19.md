*Innistrad* Card Image Gallery
==============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 19, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By magicthegathering.com Staff












Worldwide *Innistrad* Prereleases are this coming weekend, and if you're going to set out into a world full of monsters lurking in the darkness, you'll want to be ready.


Luckily for you, weary traveler, we've got you covered. The whole set is now visible in the official [Card Image Gallery](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/innistrad/cig), and we have many other resources for about the set, the world, and the rules.



> 
> * Learn about the world of Innistrad in the [Planeswalker's Guide](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/stf/157).
> * Peruse Tim Willoughby's [Prerelease Primer](/en/articles/archive/innistrad-prerelease-primer-2011-09-12) for a crash course (or a reminder) on what to expect, what to bring, and how to get the most out of your Prerelease experience.
> * Check out the [Mechanics Article](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/innistrad/mechanics) for an overview on the new and returning mechanics of *Innistrad*.
> * For more technical rules detail about the mechanics and cards of *Innistrad*, consult the [FAQ page](/en/articles/archive/innistrad-frequently-asked-questions-2011-09-16).
> 


So check out the set, check out the world, delve into the rules if that's your thing, and we'll see you at the Prerelease!
