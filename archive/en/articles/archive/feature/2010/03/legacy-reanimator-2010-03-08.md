It was the largest **Magic** tournament in the history of the game. Grand Prix–Madrid clocked in at an astonishing 2,227 players, all there to battle Legacy! German Andreas Müller was the only one left standing. His deck was a new direction on an old favorite: Reanimator. Simply dump a fatty into the graveyard then cheat it onto the battlefield with [Reanimate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reanimate) or [Exhume](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exhume). For more on Grand Prix–Madrid, visit the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/m%C3%BCller-magical-madrid).
