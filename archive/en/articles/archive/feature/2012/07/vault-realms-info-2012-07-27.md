From the Vault: Realms Info
===========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 27, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












As we [announced here](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/arcana/918) (and reminded people at San Diego Comic-Con), *From the Vault: Realms* will be released on August 31, 2012 with an MSRP of $34.99. It will contain fifteen powerful lands from the history of **Magic**. And here's a look at the box!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1025_ftvrealmsbox.jpg)As long as we're on the subject, here are all the cards that were revealed at the aforementioned San Diego Comic-Con:




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Dryad Arbor | Forbidden Orchard |
| Glacial Chasm | Grove of the Burnwillows |
| Murmuring Bosk | Urborg, Tomb of Yawgmoth |
