Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Grand Prix Kuala Lumpur Champion: Mardu Vehicles](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpkl16/top-8-decklists-2016-10-23)

White-Blue Flash might have dominated [Grand Prix Kuala Lumpur](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpkl16), but it was Mardu Vehicles in the hands of Fumiya Matsumoto that won the whole thing. And why not? The deck is brutally aggressive, and its three-color mana base both runs smoothly and gives the deck a ton of options. [Cultivator's Caravan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cultivator%27s+Caravan) is a workhorse, both giving a beefy Vehicle to run people over and enabling the deck's splashes and higher-curve cards. And while the deck is called "Mardu Vehicles," you'll note the touch of blue mana—from [Aether Hub](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Hub), [Spirebluff Canal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spirebluff+Canal), and [Cultivator's Caravan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cultivator%27s+Caravan)—to go with the [Ceremonious Rejection](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ceremonious+Rejection)s in the sideboard.
