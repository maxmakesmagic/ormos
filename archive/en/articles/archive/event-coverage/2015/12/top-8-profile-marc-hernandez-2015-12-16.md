Top 8 Profile - Marc Hernandez
==============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**Name:** Marc Hernandez  
**Age:** 25  
**Location:** Montigny - le - bretonneuse, France  
**Occupation:** Chief Editor for [www.Ankou.com](http://www.Ankou.com) (French Magazine)  
**Played Magic since:** 1994 (before Antiquities)  
**Best accomplishments:** 2nd 1995 World Championship, 6th Pro Tour London  


**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Manchester?**  

A: I like the idea of a pre-release Grand Prix. Plus Invasion seems fun.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Making day two.



**Q: What do you think about the tournament location-/organisation-wise?**  

A: Invasion is better than Mask Block so the tournament was cool. But 250 participants is few. There was a lot of high quality players though.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Luck. Because it's Magic.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: Money.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: I expect an all-European final.
