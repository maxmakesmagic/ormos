Live Coverage of the 2004 Grand Prix Austin

 on October 10, 2004

By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpaus04/sonne.jpg)
 **Name:** Jonathan Sonne


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** Columbia, NJ


**Occupation:** Lab Analyst


**What was your record yesterday?** 7-1


**How many byes?** 3


**What was in your sealed deck?** Black-white…nothing memorable. I thought the deck was bad.


**What colors did you draft today?** Blue-white then red-black

**What is currently on your MP3 player?** Rap and techno mixes. And some Slayer/
