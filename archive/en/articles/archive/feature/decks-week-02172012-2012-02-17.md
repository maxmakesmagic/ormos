Decks of the Week: 02/17/2012
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 17, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Congratulations to Brian Kibler, the Pro Tour *Dark Ascension* champion. Kibler piloted a Wolf Run Ramp deck featuring [Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells) to victory over Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa in the Finals, after defeating Jon Finkel in a legendary semifinal showdown. You can find the Top 8 decks, along with all decks that recorded at least 18 points in the ten Standard rounds during Swiss competition, below.
