K.I.S.S. is the acronym for "Keep It Simple, Stupid," and Misha Gurevich kept his Naya deck for Extended stripped down to the basics: mana, the best creatures red, green, and white have to offer, and a smattering of removal. Making his list standout from his fellow PTQers is the fact he has put four [Cunning Sparkmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Sparkmage)s into the main deck. The hasty 0/1 allows him to keep his opponent's mana Elves, Faeries, and other small creatures *off* the battlefield.
