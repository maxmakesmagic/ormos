TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on March 24, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpvie14/t8_jurkovic.jpg) 


### Robert Jurkovič


**Age:** 36  
**Hometown:** Bratislava, Slovakia  
**Occupation:** Professional **Magic Online** player

 


 

**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**   

2010 World Team Champion


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Fabled Hero](javascript:void()); I went 8 - 1 with a red-white deck.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

I had two copies of [Kragma Warcaller](javascript:void()) and I ended up 3 - 0 with a black-red deck.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

I picked a [Fabled Hero](javascript:void()) again and this time it carried me to a 2 - 1 with a red-white deck.



  
  

 



  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpvie14/t8_rachid.jpg) 


### Denniz Rachid


**Age:** I feel old  
**Hometown:** Kungsbacka, Sweden  
**Occupation:** Teacher

 


 

**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**   

Top 8 Pro Tour *Dark Ascension* and Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored*Top 8 Grand Prix Warsaw 2013


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Polukranos, World Eater](javascript:void()) was the best card, but a honorable mention goes out to [Noble Quarry](javascript:void()). I piloted my green-white deck to an 8 - 1 finish.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Ordeal of Thassa](javascript:void()) was the best card in a green-blue deck. I finished 1 - 1 - 1 thanks to an unintentional draw.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Portent of Betrayal](javascript:void()) helped me to finish 3 - 0 with a red-green deck.



  
  

 



  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpvie14/t8_dezani.jpg)

**Age:** 23  
**Hometown:** Orléans (somewhat) close to Paris, France.  
**Occupation:****Magic** Professional that also works for MTG Madness.

 


 

**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**   

Pro Tour *Theros* ChampionSix Grand Prix Top 8 (Lyon, Vienna, Warsaw, Prague, Verona and Paris)Another one with my team in Barcelona 3 weeks ago.

I played Jund (red-black-green) with [Stormbreath Dragon](javascript:void()) to a 9 - 0 finish.

**Occupation:** Software Developer

![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpvie14/t8_alcaraz.jpg) 


### Aniol Alcaraz Coca


**Age:** 28  
**Hometown:** Sabadell, which is close to Barcelona, Spain  
**Occupation:** [Nihilumbra](http://www.nihilumbra.com/)!)

 


 

**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**   

A long time ago, I reached the Top 8 of Grand Prix Barcelona and Madrid, which also happened to be Limited events.I won my National Champonship in 2004.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

I really underestimated [Courser of Kruphix](javascript:void()) at first, but it turned out to be a real star in my deck. My blue-green deck featured a lot of solid and big creatures. I lost the first round, but I won the following eight, so 8 - 1!


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

I played a very regular Boros (white-red) deck. I managed to finish 2 - 1. Nothing stood out particularly, but the best card was still [Wingsteed Rider](javascript:void()).


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Probably [Ember Swallower](javascript:void()), though [Portent of Betrayal](javascript:void()) (in my main deck) gave me a couple of close games. It was mono red with some blue cards that I splashed. I was very lucky to finish 3 - 0 with it.



  
  

 



  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/gpvie14/t8_lesch.jpg) 


### Andreas Lesch


**Age:** 23  
**Hometown:** Cologne, Germany  
**Occupation:** Student and **Magic** Grinder in Hiveworld Cologne.

 


 

**Previous **Magic** accomplishments:**   

I ended up in the Top 8 of a Pro Tour Qualifier two weeks ago and I reached Day 2 at 3 Grand Prix before.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Sudden Storm](javascript:void()); I played Blue-Green Critters because all the other colors were weak and my pool was missing any spoilers. I piloted it to a 8 - 1 finish.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Mono black devotion with a splash of blue; [Rescue from the Underworld](javascript:void()) was my best card and I won my draft pod (3 - 0).


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


 

[Raised by Wolves](javascript:void()) was the best card; it was a heavy green deck with a splash of white and it was enough to end up on 2 - 1.
