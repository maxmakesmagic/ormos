October through December FNM Promos
===================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 27, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







T*heros* releases today! So before we dive into the upcoming Friday Night **Magic** promos, let's first go over everything you can get at tonight's FNM.

First up, you can still get the September FNM Promo: [Experiment One](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Experiment+One).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1265_experiment.jpg)  
Then you also can get the *Theros* release promo: [Bident of Thassa](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bident+of+Thassa).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1309_3_zgf64zarxt.jpg)  
If you seek the aid of the gods and [solve the puzzle](/en/articles/archive/checking-out-nykthos-2013-09-24) you'll get the new Hero card to help you on the Hero's Path:

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1330_harvester.jpg)  
Not a bad haul for a night of playing **Magic** with your friends!
