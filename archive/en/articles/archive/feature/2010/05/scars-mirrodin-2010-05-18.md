### Collector's Highlights


* *Scars of Mirrodin* is the first set in the *Scars of Mirrodin* block.
* The set features 249 black bordered cards, including randomly inserted premium versions of all cards in the set.
* It will be available in intro packs, booster packs and fat packs.
* The set introduces new mechanics including: Metalcraft, Infect, and Proliferate.
* New Planeswalker cards are included.
