Unstable Variants
=================



 Posted in **News**
 on December 6, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











As many of you have noticed by now, the "216-ish" cards listed for *Unstable* were heavy on the "ish." A number of cards in the set have different variationsâ€”variations that have different art, flavor text, watermarks, or even abilities.


We can now tell you that thirteen different cards have four to six variations each. The cards are generally listed as "A" version, "B" version, etc. internally, so that's how we'll mark the differences here.
