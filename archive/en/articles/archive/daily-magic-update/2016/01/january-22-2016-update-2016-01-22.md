The January 22, 2016 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on January 22, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know for today, January 22, 2016, and today, we go to eleven.


Today's Update is brought to you by [Ragnar's mighty beard](#ragnar) and [Tibalt, the best card in Modern](#tibalt).


Today's Must See
================


1. [#KenjiWayfinder](https://www.instagram.com/hareruya_mtg/) | Hareruya | Kenji Tsumura

Yes, today's must see is at least partially in Japanese. But the words around the pictures (many of which are in English) aren't even the point. It's the pictures themselves. Every time a new set is released, a few Japanese players—in this case Kenji Tsumura—like to post a slew of brews for Standard. Kenji posted pictures of twelve decks, some more out there than others. Here Kenji has posted everything from Abzan Superfriends to Boros Equipment to Temur Prowess to, of course, several Eldrazi-based decks. If you're looking for ideas, this is the place to start.

After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [64](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/64-cards/) | ChannelFireball | Ben Rubin

This just missed the cut to make the Update yesterday, mostly because clicking on the link [went nowhere](https://twitter.com/bmkibler/status/690226978230632448). But, ultimately Rubin does the most important thing: he answers the question "Why 64?" Welcome to 2016, Ben.


1. [*Oath of the Gatewatch* Set Review](http://www.crcast.com/podcasts/constructed-resources-41-oath-of-the-gatewatch-set-review/) | Constructed Resources | Marshall Sutcliffe and Eric Froehlich

Just in time for the release of *Oath of the Gatewatch*, Marshall and Efro sit down to discuss constructed implications for *Oath of the Gatewatch*. At least I assume they're sitting down. It's a podcast, I can't see them.


1. [Thunder Dome](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/erincampbell-podcast-012216-the-girlfriend-bracket-43-thrunder-dome/) | The Girlfriend Bracket

Erin Campbell, Hallie Santo, Katie Neal, and Kriz Schultz push through the plague to discuss *Oath* Prereleases, bans, and a [VICE article about women on the grind in *Magic*](https://broadly.vice.com/en_us/article/the-women-of-magic-the-gathering).
