Following Pro Tour-Berlin, Elves! has been on the tip of every tongue speaking about the Extended format. With the 2008 World Championships happening right now, we will see whether Elves! are the real deal or whether the pro community is able to come up with enough solutions to them. TuSaisPas is striking out in that direction with this rendition of a Blue-Black Tron deck. The combination of Urza's lands, powerful artifacts, blue control, and black removal took the player to a second place finish in a recent **Magic Online** premier event. You can check out all the Worlds coverage as it happens on **magicthegathering.com**, and make sure to tune in to [the live webcast](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/webcast/home) of the Top 8 set to begin from Memphis at 9:45 a.m. ET on December 14th.
