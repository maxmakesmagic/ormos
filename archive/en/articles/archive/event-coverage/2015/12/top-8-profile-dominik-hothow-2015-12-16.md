Top 8 Profile - Dominik Hothow
==============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPHEL00/165.jpg)**Name:** Dominik Hothow  
**Age:** 18  
**Location:** Winsen, Germany  
**Occupation:** Student (school)  
**Played Magic since:** 1995, The Dark  
**Best accomplishments:** 14th 1999 Euros, 22nd 2000 Euros, 3rd 2000 Worlds



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Helsinki?**  

A: PT Points



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Top 32 because of three byes.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: SMASH!



**Q: What do you think about the formats used in this tournament, and what was your strategy?**  

A: I like the Draft portion, but not the Sealed Deck, it's too luck dependent.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Seems like I can win despite getting trash-talked.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: Some PT Points, save qualification for LA.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: -
