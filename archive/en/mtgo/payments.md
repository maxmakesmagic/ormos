Magic Online: Payments













*Magic Online* lets you play the *Magic: The Gathering* trading card game seven days a week from the comfort of your own home. Build your favorite decks and enjoy the fun of casual games or face the competition in *Magic Online* events held every day. *Magic Online* lets you play what you want, when you want.


The basic *Magic Online* account is free to download and includes everything you need to get started, including over 800 cards. Play as many casual games as you like using decks built with the cards that come with your account. *Magic Online* does not have any monthly fees, however, not all *Magic Online* features are available with the basic account.


To get the most out of *Magic Online*, you’ll need to upgrade to a full account. Upgrading costs $9.99, adds even more cards to your account, and unlocks all *Magic Online* account features, including Trading, Chat, the complete *Magic Online* Store, and more.


Events are available at all hours of the day and typically require a number Event Tickets to enter, which are available for purchase in the *Magic Online* Store for $1.00 each. Other entry requirements may also be necessary based on the type of event. For example, a booster draft may also require booster packs in addition to Event Tickets.


Most booster packs cost $3.99. Deck products and specialty products can vary in price but will typically range from $4.99 to $24.99. The *Magic Online* Store inside the game client will have the most recent prices and you can click on each product to learn more about it. Notwithstanding the foregoing, Wizards of the Coast may modify, suspend, and/or cancel an event, substitute prizes, or change the costs and/or schedule for an event without notice, at its sole discretion.


If you’re unhappy with a purchase, we’re able to refund the purchase price of most objects sold in the *Magic Online* store if the products meet the eligibility requirements below.


Unopened deck products, booster packs, and bundles may be returned up to 30 days from the date of purchase. To be eligible for a refund, these objects must be in the account that originally purchased them from the Magic Online store, can never have been traded, and must be unopened.


Account upgrade fees and Event Tickets are non-refundable. Items purchased more than 30 days prior to contacting Wizards of the Coast are also non-refundable.


To request the refund of a purchase that meets these requirements, please [click here](https://mtgo-support.wizards.com/hc/en-us).
