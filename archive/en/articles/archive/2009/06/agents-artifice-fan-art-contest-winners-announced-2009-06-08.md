### Agents of Artifice Fan Art Contest


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/global/images/mtg_novels_product_agentsofartifice_picMain_en.jpg)They say a picture’s worth a thousand words. Give us your story and we could end up giving you $2,000!


Simply create a color work, either digitally or traditionally, depicting your favorite scene from the book [*Agents of Artifice*](/en/articles/archive/agents-artifice-2009-01-15)  by Ari Marmell. It can be a comic, a drawing, a painting ... use your imagination – and have fun with it! Plus, there are prizes for second and third place, too!


Here is the [entry form](http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/novels/AofA_contestentryform.doc) (173k Word doc). You can email your entry to us at bookpromos@wizards.com, send it to us on a CD or DVD, or mail the actual piece to us. The deadline for submission is May 6, 2009.


For complete contest rules and eligibility, [click here](/en/articles/archive/agents-artifice-art-contest-official-rules-2009-03-02).
