**Throwback Standard Gauntlet 8: *Innistrad*era ends tomorrow, November 8**


Throwback Standard Gauntlet 8: *Innistrad*era will close at 7 a.m. PT tomorrow. Join this event in the Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Leagues. Entry options include 10 Tix, or 100 Play Points.


Decklists and additional information about Throwback Standard Gauntlet 8: *Innistrad*era can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/throwback-standard-gauntlet-8-innistrad-standard).

**Throwback Standard Gauntlet 9: Recent Past begins tomorrow, November 8**


Throwback Standard Gauntlet 9: Recent Past begins tomorrow, November 8 and will run until November 17. That's right, this Gauntlet will run an extra two days! Join this event in the Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Leagues. Entry options include 10 Tix, or 100 Play Points.


Decklists and additional information about Throwback Standard Gauntlet 9: Recent Past can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/throwback-standard-gauntlet-9-recent-past).

***Iconic Masters*comes to *Magic Online*November 17!**


*Iconic Masters* comes to *Magic Online* November 17! Exepct an article this Thursday, November 9 on [MTGO.com](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-products-game-info) going over the details surrounding *Iconic Masters* as well as other *Magic Online* updates.

**Standard MOCS Playoff this Saturday, November 11**


The fourth quarter of competition in the *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) culminates on November 11 for the final [Playoff Event](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series#PlayoffEvents) of the 2017 series! Big prizes, the chance to move up the [MOCS Leaderboard](http://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/leaderboard), plus invitations to the *Magic Online*Championship and *Pro Tour Rivals of Ixalan*are all on the line in this event.


Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online*Championship Series and how to qualify for future Playoff Events [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).


[Top](#top)

**Modern PTQ this Sunday, November 12**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! There is no need to qualify through a Preliminary– simply join this event with 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points. We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, November 12 at 9 a.m. PT.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

**No downtime tomorrow, November 8**


There will be no downtime tomorrow, November 8. The next two downtimes will be on November 15 (regular, 6 a.m-10 a.m.. PT/-8 for UTC) and November 29 (extended, 3 a.m. PT/-8 for UTC).


Follow *Magic Online*'s [Twitter](https://twitter.com/MagicOnline) account for live updates during downtime.
