Pro Tour Born of the Gods Photo Essay
=====================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on February 22, 2014 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Craig Gibson and Mike Rosenberg











  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo1.jpg)*Pro Tour *Born of the Gods* is taking place at the Museum of Arts and Sciences which, aside from being an awesome display of architecture, is particularly gorgeous at night.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo2.jpg)*Video coverage commentator Tim Willoughy (left) and Pro Tour Prague 2006 Quarterfinalist Quentin Martin were making waves at the coverage team's first dinner in Valencia.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo3.jpg)*The Pro Tour venue is beautiful during the day too. The weather is also perfect; a very welcome change from the last time we ventured into Valencia.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo4.jpg)*Welcome to the Pro Tour! Please sign these forms.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo5.jpg)*Aside from some cool trophies, the winner and finalist for this weekend also get to take home a lot of prize money.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo6.jpg)*Alex Sittner's opponent is braving the [Desperate Ravings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desperate+Ravings).* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo7.jpg)*Chris Fennell's storm is brewing.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo8.jpg)*Hall of Famer Jon Finkel is attempting to keep his head up after a shaky game.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo9.jpg)*No! Bad [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf)! Go attack your owner! Not me!* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo10.jpg)*Reid Duke made a pact that he couldn't refuse. So he didn't, and then he won.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo11.jpg)*It's easy for Hall of Famer Kenji Tsumura to be all smiles when he has resolved a [Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft).* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo12.jpg)*Team MTG Mint Card swept into this event with the innovative Blue Moon deck.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo13.jpg)*HEY! LANDS ARE OVER HERE!* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo14.jpg)*A fine team of judges assembled here in Valencia to ensure top-level play at Pro Tour *Born of the Gods*.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo15.jpg)*Ah, the good ol' morning ritual. Get a cup of coffee. Pick up a pen and clipboard. Watch some people draft from Pod 1 of the Pro Tour. Record their picks. Assemble the draft viewer. Good times.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo16.jpg)*The hall for this Pro Tour is nicely sprawled out, giving spectators plenty of matches to watch from the sidelines.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo17.jpg)*Martin Juza, beating you in a feature match with a smile and a handshake.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo18.jpg)*Some of the card names in *Theros* could use a closer examination when recording picks for the draft viewers.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo19.jpg)*It's important to stretch ones muscles before beating people with red and white creatures.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo20.jpg)*Three fine gentlemen eager and ready to report the latest Pro Tour tidbits from the news desk.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo21.jpg)*Esther Morena Morales, 67-year old **Magic** fan, visiting her hometown Pro Tour.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo22.jpg)*Morales is an avid collector, and is still working on a foil set of *Born of the Gods*.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo23.jpg)*Artists are on site and displaying their amazing work.* 
  
  
![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptbng14/photo24.jpg)*Seriously. That Xenagos piece is truly badass.*
