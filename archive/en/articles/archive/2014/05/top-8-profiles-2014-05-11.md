TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on May 11, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff












 


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/t8_anteri.jpg)
 


**Sergiy Sushalskyy** **Age:** 30  
**Hometown:** Kiev, Ukraine  
**Occupation:** Sales manager


 


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Won a couple of FNMs.


 


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

I played Green-White Heroic with a lot of creatures and combat tricks. 7-2.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

In the first draft I played a black-green midrange deck with [Reaper of the Wilds](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reaper+of+the+Wilds). 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

An aggressive red-green deck with [Harness by Force](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harness+by+Force) being the best card. 3-0.


 


  



 


 


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/t8_vanderhallen.jpg)
 


**Nicolas Vanderhallen** **Age:** 24  
**Hometown:** Antwerp, Belgium  
**Occupation:** Sales executive


 


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

A PTQ win and a PTQ Top 8, and Day 2 at Pro Tour Born of the Gods.


 


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Green-Blue Midrange. My best cards were [Noble Quarry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noble+Quarry), [Fated Intervention](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fated+Intervention), and [Wavecrash Triton](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wavecrash+Triton), but [Noble Quarry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noble+Quarry) won me most of my games.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Red-Black Aggro, 3-0. Lots of removal but my best card was [Spiteful Returned](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spiteful+Returned).


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Blue-Black Tempo, 1-1-1. Best card was [Shipwreck Singer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shipwreck+Singer) which almost single-handedly won my second round.


 


  



 


 


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/t8_fior.jpg)
 


**Daniel Fior** **Age:** 28  
**Hometown:** Caracas, Venezuela  
**Occupation:** Wanderer


 


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Three Grand Prix Top 8s, never cashed at a Pro Tour.


 


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Black-white, [Whip of Erebos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whip+of+Erebos), 8-1.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Red-white, [Lightning Diadem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Diadem), 2-1. Deck was bad!


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Black-red, two Ordeals, 3-0.


 


  



 


 


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/t8_lehner.jpg)
 


**Bernhard Lehner** **Age:** 24  
**Hometown:** Vienna, Austria.  
**Occupation:** Student, Magic player


 


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Austrian National champion 2009, Team Worlds finals 2009, Grand Prix Top 8s in Verona, Valencia … and a couple of Top 32 finishes.


 


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Kiora's Follower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiora%27s+Follower), [Retraction Helix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Retraction+Helix). 9-0.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Same as in Sealed. 2-1.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Same as before. 1-0-2.


 


  



 


 


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/t8_dominguez.jpg)
 


**Javier Dominguez** **Age:** 27  
**Hometown:** Palau, de Plegamans, Spain  
**Occupation:** Grinder


 


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Won Grand Prix Paris 2014.


 


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Polukranos, World Eater](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polukranos%2C+World+Eater), green-red, 8-1.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Prophet of Kruphix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prophet+of+Kruphix), blue-green, 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Nyx-Fleec Ram](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nyx-Fleec+Ram), 1-0.


 


  



 


 


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/t8_anteri.jpg)
 


**Fabrizio Anteri** **Age:** 24  
**Hometown:** Venezuelan living in London, England.  
**Occupation:**


 


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

White-red, [Celestial Archon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Celestial+Archon), 4-2.


 


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

White-black walls and removal, [Scholar of Athreos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scholar+of+Athreos), 3-0.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

White-black walls and removal, [Scholar of Athreos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scholar+of+Athreos), 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

White-red, [Mogis's Warhound](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogis%27s+Warhound), 3-0.


 


  



 


 


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/t8_yu.jpg)
 


**Ben Yu** **Age:** 28  
**Hometown:** Las Vegas, Nevada  
**Occupation:** Poker player


 


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Qualified for my first Pro Tour (Born of the Gods) after only playing Magic for one year. It's the reason I'm still in Europe and here at this Grand Prix.


 


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Red-Black Control, [Sigiled Skink](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sigiled+Skink), 7-1-1.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Green-White Aggro, [Nyx-Fleece Ram](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nyx-Fleece+Ram) (Baaaa!), 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Green-black, [Eidolon of Blossoms](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eidolon+of+Blossoms), 2-1. Eidolon was so good Ivan Floch used a card to kill it, then [Fated Returned](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fated+Returned) it.


 


  



 


 


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/events/2014/gpwar14/t8_floch.jpg)
 


**Ivan Floch** **Age:** 27  
**Hometown:** Bratislava, Slovakia  
**Occupation:** Professional tourist


 


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

2010 Team World champion, winner Grand Prix Lisbon 2012.


 


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Black-green, [Courser of Kruphix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courser+of+Kruphix), 9-0.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Red-Green Aggro, [Fall of the Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fall+of+the+Hammer), 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Blue-Black Control, [Daring Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daring+Thief)—the more junk you have the better card [Daring Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Daring+Thief) is. And he was insane in this deck … 1-1-1.
