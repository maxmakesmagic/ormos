How to Stream Magic
===================



 Posted in **Magic Lifestyle**
 on February 11, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gabyspartz_0.jpg)
By Gaby Spartz




 By night, Gaby streams Magic on Twitch. By day, she's co-founder and VP of Content at Dose, the Chicago-based media company behind Dose.com and OMGFacts.com. 






*In her debut video for DailyMTG, Gaby Spartz teaches you—yes, YOU—how you can start streaming* Magic. *Part 1 covers the basics of setting up your stream, and Part 2 teaches you how to optimize the stream.*
