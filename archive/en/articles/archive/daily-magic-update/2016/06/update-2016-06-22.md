The June 22, 2016 Update
========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on June 22, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_KatieAllison.jpg)
By Katie Allison




 Katie is a copy editor and occasional writer for DailyMTG, as well as a longtime Magic player with a fondness for giant green creatures. She especially loves Commander, despite having the patience of a fruit fly, and might glare at you if your turn takes too long. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on June 22, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by the one coworker who I'm pretty sure has realized I'm not Blake. She keeps looking at me funny, and my costume is falling apart. I think the jig is up, guys.


Today's Must
============


1. [Against the Odds (Bonus): Worldgorger Combo Draft](http://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/against-the-odds-bonus-worldgorger-combo-draft) | MTGGoldfish | SaffronOlive

I'm pretty sure this is the apotheosis of drafting, and we should all be glad to witness it. Off the back of a first-pick [Worldgorger Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Worldgorger+Dragon) and second-pick [Animate Dead](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Animate+Dead), SaffronOlive managed to assemble an infinite combo so powerful it was once banned in legacy...in *Eternal Masters* Draft! Just watch it. It's...it's beautiful.

What People Are Talking About
=============================


1. [#HanweirChronicle concludes](https://twitter.com/hashtag/HanweirChronicle?src=hash)

For months, the Hanweir Chronicle has been issuing cryptic dispatches from the slowly warping Innistrad, giving fans and players around the world a firsthand glimpse into the madness overtaking the plane. At long last, the Chronicle has concluded, as the now-collective consciousness of the town's citizens has no need for such communications. If you've missed any parts of the Hanweir Chronicle, or just want to revisit your favorites, they're collected at [HanweirChronicle.com](http://hanweirchronicle.com/).


Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Modern Dredge](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-modern-constructed-league-2016-06-21)

This looks pretty different from a typical Legacy Dredge deck, but what really caught my eye is the full set of four [Prized Amalgam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam)s. There's a card that I literally do not think I've seen played in a Limited environment once—but in this particular Constructed strategy, it's an obvious all-star. If you play a lot of Limited, like me, you can easily start to disregard cards like [Prized Amalgam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam) that don't do anything notable in their particular Limited environment, and it's always fun to see those cards find a home.


Congratulations to *Magic Online* user MTGMulligan for taking this list to a 5-0 finish in a Competitive Modern Constructed League. I'm looking forward to seeing what other sleeper cards from *Shadows over Innistrad* and *Eldritch Moon* will make their way into older formats!
