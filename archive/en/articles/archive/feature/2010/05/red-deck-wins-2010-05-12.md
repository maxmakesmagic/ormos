Red Deck Wins has been around for quite some time, but there's a reason they say it can win! Gabriel Willmon took a look at the new *Rise of the Eldrazi* and immediately made some changes to the archetype for Standard. [Kargan Dragonlord](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kargan+Dragonlord) and [Kiln Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiln+Fiend) now occupy the two-drop slot, and [Staggershock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Staggershock) has been added to the cadre of burn spells in the deck. But the truly interesting addition? [Devastating Summons](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devastating+Summons). On the fourth turn Gabriel can cast it to make two 4/4 creatures while floating two additional mana. What to do with that? How about casting [Goblin Bushwhacker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Bushwhacker) with kicker? Take 12, thanks! Find a Pro Tour Qualifier near you on the [Amsterdam Qualifiers page](/en/node/639906).
