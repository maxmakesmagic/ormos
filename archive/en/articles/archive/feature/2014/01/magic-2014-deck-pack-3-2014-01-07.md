***Magic** 2014* Deck Pack 3
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 7, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1402_dp3.jpg)Later today we start rolling out Deck Pack 3 for ***Magic** 2014—Duels of the Planeswalkers* across all platforms and regions. As is usual this will roll out with an update to the game and once you are updated you will be able to purchase the new Deck Pack in your copy of the game.


The update will roll out at some time during the day listed below, based on your platform and region:


* January 7 - PSN in North America
* January 8 - PSN in Europe, XBLA, iOS, Android (Amazon and Google Play), and Steam
* January 9 - PSN in Asia and Japan



---

 
 



> *Seeking to expand your mastery and defeat your foes? This Magic 2014 Deck Pack includes two brand-new decks for Campaign and Multiplayer play. In the red and white “Warsmith” deck, the mere presence of your artifacts on the battlefield will bolster your unstoppable forces and empower your spells. Forge your army, and march to war! With the green “Hunting Season” deck, the creatures at your command will become unnaturally large, and if that should prove insufficient to crush your enemy, make more creatures. Call the hunt!*


Here are the two deck lists for this Deck Pack. After each deck you'll see your list of thirty unlockable cards, and then the ten promotional unlocks that require codes that have been distributed to the community and online. I'm sure you all can find some codes to unlock the goodies.
