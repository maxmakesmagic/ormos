The February 27, 2017 Update
============================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on February 27, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on February 27, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by [Damnation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Damnation). We did it. We finally did it. You know it's quite the day when reprinting the *Zendikar* fetch lands is the second coolest reprint.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Samuel Vuillot's Mardu Ballista](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gputr17/event-coverage/top-8-decklists-2017-02-26)

Add possibly the best card in the format to possibly the best deck in the format, and the result is a Grand Prix victory for Samuel Vuillot, who, like a lot of players, found a way to put [Walking Ballista](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista) into Mardu Vehicles. Vuillot, however, was the last player standing at Grand Prix Utrecht.


But there was much more to the Grand Prix than robots driving air ships—and you can read all about it [on the coverage page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gputr17), check out the [Top Moments from Frank Karsten](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gputr17/top-5-moments-2017-02-26), and/or check out our Utrecht video coverage on our [Twitch archive page.](https://www.twitch.tv/magic/videos/all)
