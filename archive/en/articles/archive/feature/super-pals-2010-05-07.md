 Looking for a fun new deck for your Friday Night **Magic** starring some of the new power players from *Rise of the Eldrazi*? Try Lewis Laskin's tournament-winning control strategy. He uses twelve planeswalkers, including [Gideon Jura](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon+Jura), to overwhelm his opponent's forces. [Wall of Omens](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens) does blocking duty, while board-sweepers, spot removal, and [Spreading Seas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spreading+Seas) keep opponents off balance. It's a great new deck to try for your big tournament this week!
