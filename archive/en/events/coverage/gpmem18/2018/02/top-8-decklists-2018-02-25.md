Top 8 Decklists
===============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 25, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











What did the Top 8 players bring to the tables at Grand Prix Memphis this weekend?


Five archetypes – and all five colors - were represented in the Top 8. Despite fears that little red men might torch the field, none of them made it through to the elimination rounds!


* Blake Miller (Mardu Vehicles)
* Andrew Tenjum (Grixis Energy)
* Tyler Schroeder (Red-Green Monsters)
* Heath Vance (Blue-Black Control)
* Aaron Barich (Sultai Constrictor)
* Eric Froehlich (Blue-Black Control)
* Matthew Kling (Grixis Energy)
* Michael Cochran (Grixis Energy)
