Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 17, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











Learn more about Grand Prix Las Vegas Limited's 2017 Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they’ve done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!


 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gplv17/t8_lim_herr.jpg)Name:** Paul Herr


**Age:** 32


**Hometown:** Irvine, California


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** None


**What was your record on Day 1, and what did you play?**


9-0, Black-Green


**What were your records on Day 2, and what did you play?**


3-0 Red-White splash Black


1-2 White-Black Zombies


**Which cards performed well for you this weekend, and why?**


[Sandwurm Convergence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sandwurm+Convergence) – I never lost when I played it in the Sealed portion.


[Insult // Injury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Insult+%2F%2F+Injury) – It works great with trample.


[Unburden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unburden) – I cast it when my opponents have two cards left.


**What's been the best part of the Las Vegas weekend so far?**


Spending time with my friends John Balla and Greg Hatch


**What *Amonkhet* God best describes you?**


Oketra
