The *Zendikar* cycle of rare lands such as [Crypt of Agadeem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+of+Agadeem) and [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle) has drawn a lot of interested looks from deck builders all over the web. John Treviranus of Magic.TCGplayer.com recently wrote an article featuring this control strategy starring the Crypt. He abuses the cycling black creatures from *Shards of Alara* block to power his land, allowing him to [Consume Spirit](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Consume+Spirit) his opponent's to death!
