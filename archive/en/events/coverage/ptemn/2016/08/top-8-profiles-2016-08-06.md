TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 6, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Learn more about Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon's* Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they've done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/ptemn/t8-blohon.jpg)


### Lukas Blohon


**Age**: 26


**Hometown**: Prague, Czech Republic


**Occupation**: *Magic* player


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Constructed Record**: 7-2-1


**Previous Magic Accomplishments**: Pro Tour Top 8, Grand Prix Win


**Did you team up with anyone to practice for this Pro Tour? Who?**


Team Cabin Crew


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Green-White Humans twice


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


White-Black Control with creatures. I thought it would be solid, but we misjudged the metagame and I don't have many good matchups.


**What breakout card from *Eldritch Moon* most impressed you this weekend?** 


[Emrakul, the Promised End](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Promised+End)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


One step closer to the Hall of Fame

![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/ptemn/t8-pardee.jpg)


### Samuel Pardee


**Age**: 27


**Hometown**: Berkeley, CA


**Occupation**: *Magic* player


**Limited Record**: 6-0


**Constructed Record**: **6-2-2**


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 


2 Grand Prix wins, 9 Grand Prix Top 8s


**Did you team up with anyone to practice for this Pro Tour? Who?**


Team Face to Face Games! They are the reason the Pro Tour is fun for me.


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Black-red in Draft 1, green-white in Draft 2. Black-red was my preference going into the event.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Black-Green [Grim Flayer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Flayer) Delirium. Lots of solid matchups and takes good advantage of a lot of the new cards.


**What breakout card from *Eldritch Moon* most impressed you this weekend?** 


[Liliana, the Last Hope](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%2C+the+Last+Hope). She just offers so much card advantage as well as board control.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


The fulfillment of a dream I've had since I found out there was a Pro Tour.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/ptemn/t8-lsv.jpg)


### Luis Scott-Vargas


**Age**: 33


**Hometown**: Oakland, CA


**Occupation**: Vice President, ChannelFireball.com


**Limited Record**: 5-1


**Constructed Record**: 7-1-2


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments** : Top 8, Pro Tour *Oath of the Gatewatch*


Top 8, Pro Tour *Shadows over Innistrad*


**Did you team up with anyone to practice for this Pro Tour? Who?**


Team ChannelFireball and Team Ultra PRO


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Red-white midrange and Red-Green Werewolves. Red-white was definitely more fun.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Bant Company, because I want to Bant people.


**What breakout card from *Eldritch Moon* most impressed you this weekend?** 


[Tamiyo, Field Researcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tamiyo%2C+Field+Researcher), after defeating Emrakul and more with her.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


That I would barely lose the Player of the Year race to Owen Turtenwald.

### Andrew Brown


**Age**: 24


**Hometown**: Los Angeles, CA


**Occupation**: Game Designer


**Limited Record**: 4-2


**Constructed Record**: 8-2


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 


Pro Tour *Oath of the Gatewatch*, Top 8


Grand Prix Denver Champion


**Did you team up with anyone to practice for this Pro Tour? Who?**


Team East-West Bowl!


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Mono-Black, splashing Arlinn Cord


White-Black, love melding the Rats


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Temurge; [Elder Deep-Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elder+Deep-Fiend) is a powerful *Magic* card


**What breakout card from *Eldritch Moon* most impressed you this weekend?** 


[Grapple with the Past](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grapple+with+the+Past). You get infinite Deep-Fiends, and [Sanctum of Ugin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sanctum+of+Ugin) to [Time Walk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Walk) your opponents ad nauseum.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


That my love and dedication to the game will pay off.
