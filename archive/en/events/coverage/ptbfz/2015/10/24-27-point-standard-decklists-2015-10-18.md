24-27 Point Standard Decklists
==============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 18, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










Twenty-three players earned 24 point or more from their Standard rounds at Pro Tour *Magic Origins*. Sixteen hit 24 points (8-2), five got to 25 points (8-1-1), one player—Ricky Chin—received 26 points (8-0-2), and just one managed to run her records to 27 points (9-1) in the Standard rounds—Autumn Burchett.
