Godo's Warrior tokens
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 21, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The *Saviors of Kamigawa* sorcery **[Rally the Horde](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rally+the+Horde)** adds a potentially enormous horde of angry Warrior tokens to your team. 


![Rally the Horde](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Rally+the+Horde)
Notice who's doing the rallying in that art -- it's [Godo, Bandit Warlord](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Godo%252C%2BBandit%2BWarlord) (also illustrated by Paolo Parente), riding his water-buffalo-like beast and brandishing [Tenza, Godo's Maul](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Tenza%252C%2BGodo%2527s%2BMaul) (*also* illustrated by Parente):



Next Monday, June 27th (the day after [Regionals weekend](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/magic/regionals)), *Saviors of Kamigawa*  releases in ***Magic** Online* (with [release events](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/magiconline/article061705) shortly thereafter). At that point you'll be able to see this art for the Warrior tokens you create with [Rally the Horde](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rally+the+Horde), illustrated by Mark Brill:
