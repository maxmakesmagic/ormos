Decks of the Week: 4/15/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on April 15, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Let us take a moment to look back at the Extended Pro Tour–San Juan Qualifier season which concluded last weekend…OK, time's up! *Rise of the Eldrazi* arrives this weekend at [Prerelease events](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/prerelease-facts) around the world, and the complete set is now posted in our [Visual Spoiler](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/riseoftheeldrazi/spoiler)! So get cracking on those deckbuilding ideas and have a great time this weekend with the debut of *Rise of the Eldrazi*.
