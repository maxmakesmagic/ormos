**Eligibility**  

The **Magic: the Gathering®** Scholarship Series Championship is an invitation-only event, open to college-bound DCI members in good standing who have won an invitation through DCI ratings or from Scholarship Series qualifying events. To see the current invite list, please [click here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/mss-2007invitations).


**Registration Requirements**


* We are required to have the following to complete tournament registration:
* **Bring your Parents:** If you are 17 or under, you must have a parent or guardian present at registration.
* **Consent Form**: filed with Wizards of the Coast. If you are 17 or under, the form requires parental signature. The consent form is available online at the Scholarship Series frontpage.
* **Proof of identity and age**: If you don't have a photo ID, please bring appropriate government-issued proof of age documentation (examples: birth certificate, passport, state ID card, student body card that contains date of birth, drivers permit).

 


**Registration Locations and Times**


* There will be signs posted directing you to the registration location. If you have questions, one of the Wizards of the Coast staff members will happily assist you. Scholarship Series players can register at the following times and locations:

 


**4:30 pm - 8 pm Friday, July 27th inside the tournament hall.**


**Format**


* Standard Constructed
* Deck lists are required and will be collected at the 9:00 AM player meeting on Saturday
* Check [www.thedci.com](http://www.thedci.com/) for Standard-format rules, including possible banned cards

 


**Round Length**


* 13 Swiss Rounds total, 50 minutes per round
* Saturday will have 9 Swiss-format rounds of competition
* Sunday will have 4 Swiss-format rounds of competition
* Top 8 single-elimination finals have no time limit

 


**Advancement**


* All players may participate in all Swiss rounds on Saturday
* Players with 15 or more match points after round 9 will advance to Sunday's competition
* The top 8 players after round 13 will advance to Sunday's top 8 single-elimination finals

 


**Top 8**


* Standard Format
* Top 8 matches will be best 2 of 3

 


**Notes**


* 40K, REL 3
* All Universal Tournament Rules and Magic: the Gathering Floor Rules are in effect for this event unless specifically overruled by information in this Format Document
* Players are required to use the same Standard deck for Saturday and Sunday
* Players are responsible for knowing all DCI rules and [high-level tournament rules](http://www.wizards.com/protour/images/highlevelrules.pdf)
* Players must bring pen, paper, card sleeves and appropriate counters

 


**Saturday Schedule**  

9:00 AM: Seating for player meeting posted  

9:15 AM: Mandatory Player meeting begins, deck lists are collected  

9:30 AM: Round 1 begins  

12:00 PM: Scholarship Series Parent learn-to-play begins


**Sunday Schedule**  

8:45 AM: Seating for player meeting posted  

9:00 AM: Mandatory Player meeting begins  

9:15 AM: Round 10 begins


* The top 8 single-elimination will begin shortly after the completion of round 13 (est. 2:30 PM)
* An award ceremony will take place immediately following the single-elimination top 8
