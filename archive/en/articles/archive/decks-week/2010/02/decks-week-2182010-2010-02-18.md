Decks of the Week: 2/18/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on February 18, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











W*orldwake* is here! The first major events featuring the new set are in the books, and we're already seeing the impact. The 2010 Grand Prix season kicked off last weekend in Oakland and the Extended format Top 8 saw [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor), [Loam Lion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Loam+Lion), [Searing Blaze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Searing+Blaze), [Terastodon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terastodon), and [Stoneforge Mystic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic) see play from the new set. *Worldwake* gets its Standard debut this weekend at Pro Tour–San Diego, with coverage starting on this site Friday!
