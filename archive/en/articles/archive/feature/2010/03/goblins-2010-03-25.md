Interested in trying out the Legacy format? Now you can! Legacy will soon be available to play in tournaments and casually on **Magic Online**. The format, which allows cards from all of **Magic**'s expansions (minus a list of banned cards), allows for all sorts of innovative decks. Take Nick Montaquila's, for example. He recently made the Top 8 of a large Legacy tournament, the StarCityGames.com Open in Indianapolis. He relied on the Goblin tribe to take him to 2nd place, with hits like [Goblin Piledriver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Piledriver), [Goblin Warchief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Warchief), [Stingscourger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stingscourger), and [Goblin Ringleader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ringleader) to reload.
