Standard Grand Prix Richmond Trial Decklists
============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 6, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro

Mardu Vehicles has been a good standby for people before the Pro Tour—it’s still a good deck, for sure—but a couple other plants sprouted up, making cracks in the racetrack. The Mono-Black Zombie list is certainly on the rise ... from the grave.
