Grand Prix Houston Trial-Winning Decklists
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 27, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











On Friday, players have their last chance to win byes going into the weekend's tournament. These Last Chance Qualifiers (often called “Grinders”) can be a great look into what people think will do well at the tournament, and what might be a great deck to bring to your local FNM! All the lists from the Standard Last Chance Qualifier are below. And there are some spicy ones. Eldrazi might not just be for Modern anymore...


In addition to both John McIntosh and Seth Morton, who won Sealed grinders to earn their byes, here are all the people who went 5-0 in their respective tournaments, earning the right to sleep in a little longer going into Saturday.
