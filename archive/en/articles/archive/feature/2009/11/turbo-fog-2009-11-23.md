![Angelsong](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Angelsong)Joel Calafell started the 2009 World Championships off strong with a 6-0 start using a Turbo Fog deck designed to take advantage of a field filled with Jund. Early card draw through things like [Howling Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) and [Jace Beleren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace+Beleren) keep his hand stocked with [Fog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fog)s in the form of [Safe Passage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Safe+Passage) and [Angelsong](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelsong). Before long his opponent is low on cards, and a timely [Archive Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archive+Trap) is all it takes to send the challenger packing! For more on the 2009 World Championships, [visit the official event coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12).
