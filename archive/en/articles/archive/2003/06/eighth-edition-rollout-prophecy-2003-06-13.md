*Eighth Edition* Rollout - *Prophecy*
=====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 13, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












[![Eighth Edition Rollout](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/articles/8EtopBar.gif)](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=mtgcom/8erollout/welcome)  

 
*[Avatar of Hope](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avatar+of+Hope) moves into* Eighth Edition *without the other four.*


### *[Prophecy](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=magic/expansion/prophecy)* Information:




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Released:** May 2000**Design Team:** William Jockusch (lead) with contributions from Bill Rose and Mark Rosewater**Development Team:** Charlie Hasbrino (lead), Mike Elliott, William Jockusch, Paul Peterson, Henry Stern, Teeuwynn Woodruff with contributions from Beth Moursund**Art Director:** Dana Knutson & Ron Spears |  | **New Mechanics:** “avatars”, rhystic spells, “winds”**Factoid:** *Prophecy* is most well known for its three rare cycles: Avatars, Winds, and Spellshaper Legends |
