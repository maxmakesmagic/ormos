Hour of Devastation comes to Magic Online July 10!
==================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on June 29, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/lee_author_image_0.jpeg)
By Lee Sharpe











Like *Amonkhet* before, *Hour of Devastation* will be available on *Magic Online* on July 10, the day after paper Preleases in your [local game store](http://locator.wizards.com/). *Hour of Devastation* will feature our usual complement of Draft, Sealed, and Constructed Leagues!


You can see the full details below!


Timeline
========


Events will change as follows:


* July 5: Commander 1v1 Banned-List Change Announcement
* July 5 Downtime: *Hour of Devastation* Build
	+ Constructed Leagues end and are recreated for the new set
	+ Commander and Commander 1v1 split into two different formats/banned lists
	+ Commander 1v1 banned-list changes implemented
	+ *Hour of Devastation* cards become Constructed-legal
* July 8-9: Play in *Hour of Devastation* Prereleases in Your [local game store](http://locator.wizards.com/)
* July 10: ***Hour of Devastation* Events Begin on *Magic Online***
	+ *Amonkhet*-only Limited events end
	+ *Vintage Cube* events end
* July 11: Team Draft Super League begins at 6 p.m. Pacific Time
	+ Exciting look at HOU-HOU-AKH draft
	+ Team Channel Fireball (Luis Scott-Vargas, Ben Stark, and Eric Froehlich) vs. Team Limited Resources (Marshall Sutcliffe, Kenji Egashira, and Paul Cheon)

New to *Magic Online* Events
============================


We currently offer Phantom Draft and Standard queues for new player events. We weren't satisfied with the Standard experience and are replacing that queue with the New to *Magic Online* Standard Gauntlet. This is a two-player queue where you'll use a random Planeswalker Deck and be paired against an opponent also using a Planeswalker Deck.


The cards in the deck won't be added to your collection, but you'll get to look over it for a few minutes and then play a match. The event details for these events are unchanged. Each player will join the queue with 2 New Play Points, and the player who wins the match will receive 5 Play Points.


Hopefully these new events will allow players to get used to *Magic Online*'s interface in a Constructed match without requiring you to have gotten a deck together in advance!


Prestige Avatars
================


During *Hour of Devastation* season (starting July 5 for constructed and July 10 for limited), you'll be able to earn these prestige avatars by earning enough trophies in leagues on *Magic Online*. You can earn the Silver, Gold, and Mythic avatars as follows:


These prestige avatars can be earned in any of the following ways for *Hour of Devastation* season:


* Silver Prestige Avatar— Samut, The Tested
	+ 5 trophies in the Friendly *Hour of Devastation / Amonkhet* Draft League
	+ 5 trophies in the Friendly *Hour of Devastation / Amonkhet* Sealed League
	+ 2 trophies in any single Friendly Constructed League
* Gold Prestige Avatar— The Locust God
	+ 4 trophies in the Competitive *Hour of Devastation / Amonkhet* Draft League
	+ 4 trophies in the Competitive *Hour of Devastation / Amonkhet* Sealed League
	+ 4 trophies in any single Competitive Constructed League
* Mythic Prestige Avatar— Nicol Bolas, God-Pharaoh
	+ 12 trophies in the Competitive *Hour of Devastation / Amonkhet* Draft League
	+ 6 trophies in the Competitive *Hour of Devastation / Amonkhet* Sealed League
	+ 6 trophies in any single Competitive Constructed League

Finishing with six or more wins in any single MOCS Monthly event during *Hour of Devastation* season

*Hour of Devastation* Event Structures
======================================


**Friendly Draft League**


* Product: 2 *Hour of Devastation* Boosters + 1 *Amonkhet* Booster
* Structure: Draft, then single-game matches at your convenience until either 7 wins or 4 losses, whichever comes first
* Entry Options:
	+ 12 Event Tickets
	+ 2 *Hour of Devastation* Boosters + 1 *Amonkhet* Booster + 2 Event Tickets
	+ 120 Play Points
* Prizes:
	+ 7 wins: 120 Play Points
	+ 6 wins: 100 Play Points
	+ 5 wins: 80 Play Points
	+ 4 wins: 40 Play Points
	+ 3 wins: 20 Play Points
	+ 2 wins: 10 Play Points

**Intermediate Draft League**


* Product: 2 *Hour of Devastation* Boosters + 1 *Amonkhet* Booster
* Structure: Draft, then up to 3 matches at your convenience
* Entry Options:
	+ 12 Event Tickets
	+ 2 *Hour of Devastation Boosters* + 1 *Amonkhet* Booster + 2 Event Tickets
	+ 120 Play Points
* Prizes:
	+ 3 wins: 5 *Hour of Devastation* Boosters + 1 *Amonkhet* Booster + 1 Qualifier Point
	+ 2 wins: 1 *Hour of Devastation* Booster + 1 *Amonkhet* Booster

**Competitive Draft League**


* Product: 2 *Hour of Devastation* Boosters + 1 *Amonkhet* Booster
* Structure: Draft, then play matches at your convenience until either 3 wins or 1 loss
* Entry Options:
	+ 15 Event Tickets
	+ 2 *Hour of Devastation* Boosters + 1 *Amonkhet* Booster + 5 Event Tickets
	+ 150 Play Points
	+ 2 *Hour of Devastation* Boosters + 1 *Amonkhet* Booster + 50 Play Points
* Prizes:
	+ 3 wins: 4 *Hour of Devastation* Boosters + 2 *Amonkhet* Boosters + 150 Play Points + 2 Qualifier Points
