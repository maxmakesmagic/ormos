Walking the Promenade
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 29, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











This is [Transguild Promenade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Transguild+Promenade).

![Transguild Promenade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Transguild+Promenade)It's a walkway in Ravnica that's used by people of all guilds. It even has different guild symbols in the pillars!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1114_transguildpromenade.jpg)[Transguild Promenade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Transguild+Promenade) | Art by [Noah Bradley](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Noah%20Bradley%22%5D)

In fact, if you look closely, you can see that people from multiple guilds are actually walking on the promenade right now! This person is definitely from the Izzet League.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1114_figure1.jpg)  
And over on the left, there's someone from the Rakdos Cult talking to someone from the Selesnya Conclave!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1114_figure2.jpg)
