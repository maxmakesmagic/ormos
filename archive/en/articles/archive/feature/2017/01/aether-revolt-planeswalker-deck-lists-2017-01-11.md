Aether Revolt Planeswalker Deck Lists
=====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 11, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Starting this weekend at your *Aether Revolt* [Prerelease](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/prerelease-events-types-events), you can start picking up the new Planeswalker Decks featuring unique versions of Ajani and Tezzeret. The decks are meant for newer players, but can be fun for anyone to battle them against one another.


You can check out the [exclusive cards found in these decks here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/exclusive-cards-aether-revolt-planeswalker-decks-2017-01-05). Enjoy!
