2002 Grand Prix Copenhagen Top 8 Profiles
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 13, 2002 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcop02/a859.jpg)**Name?**  
 Jens Krause

**Age?**  
 19

**Where are you from?**  
 Germany

**Occupation?**  
 Zivi / Social Service

**What are your other achievements with Magic the Gathering?**  
 3rd GP Hamburg  
  
**What do you think of the *Onslaught* Limited Format?**  
 It's just perfect

**What colour combination do you like most in *Onslaught* draft?**  
 B/W

**Why?**  
 Clerics are good

**What is your favourite card in *Onslaught*?**  
[Voidmage Prodigy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voidmage+Prodigy)!

**What is your most memorable play from the tournament?**  
 Killing all 3 of my opponents creatures (all morphs) with [Harsh Mercy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harsh+Mercy)

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcop02/a858.jpg)**Name?**

**Age?**  
 24

**Where are you from?**  
 Germany

**Occupation?**  
 Travel Agent

**What are your other achievements with Magic the Gathering?**  
 3 PT's blah blah....

 **What do you think of the *Onslaught* Limited Format?**  
 As evidenced by the quality of the Top 8 - Good

**What colour combination do you like most in *Onslaught* draft?**  
 R/W

**Why?**  
 Most aggressive

**What is your favourite card in *Onslaught*?**  
[Glory Seeker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glory+Seeker)/[Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)

**What is your most memorable play from the tournament?**  
 Going Bear, [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock), Bear by turn 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcop02/a856.jpg)**Name?**  
 Robert Maher

**Age?**  
 24

**Where are you from?**  
 Madison, WI, USA

**Occupation?**  
 Student

**What are your other achievements with Magic the Gathering?**  
 Player of the Year, PT Chicago '99 Champion, World's 2000 finalist, Team PT 2002 3rd Place. Other top 8 finishes at GP's

 **What do you think of the *Onslaught* Limited Format?**  
 I think it is a lot of fun. But it is new, sets are always fun when they are new.

**What colour combination do you like most in *Onslaught* draft?**  
 R/W

**Why?**  
 It is an aggressive deck with the least number of situational cards. Just solid all round

**What is your favourite card in *Onslaught*?**  
[Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith)

**What is your most memorable play from the tournament?**  
 Making 7/7 Elementals with [Voice of the Woods](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+the+Woods)

I also mana-burned twice due to not knowing the cards.
