*Scars of Mirrodin* is now available for Standard play at your local retailers! Michael Lapine managed to do well at one of the first events starring the new format, taking an Elf deck to the Top 8 of a TCGPlayer.com tournament in New York. The deck stars [Ezuri, Renegade Leader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ezuri%2C+Renegade+Leader) heading an army of Elves and is also powered by [Genesis Wave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Genesis+Wave). With many of your creatures providing the mana to fuel the ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20160829100442im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/x.gif) spell, it's sure to yield lots of juicy goodies for your side of the battlefield!
