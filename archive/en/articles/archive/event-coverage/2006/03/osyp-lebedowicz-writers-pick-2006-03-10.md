Osyp Lebedowicz is the Writers' Pick!
=====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 10, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2006/invitational/banner2.jpg)](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/2006-magic-invitational-voting-2006-02-10)
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2006/invitational/osyplebedowicz.jpg)If anyone ever doubted it, Osyp Lebedowicz's larger-than-life persona transcends borders.


Dominating a field of 15 worthy candidates, Osyp earned a spot at the 2006 Magic Invitational by securing 63 total points. He appeared on 20 of the 24 possible ballots from an international voting body.


Expanding on last year's Writers' vote, the 2006 voting was opened to a global community of **Magic**  writers. Internet sites and magazines were included in this year's voting, and 24 voters took part in determining a slot for the Invitational based on "Which player has the best combination of talent and personality?"

The writers had to pick five names from the following list:


Tim Aten, Jeff Cunningham, Antonino De Rosa, Gerard Fabiano, Tsuyoshi Fujita, Sam Gomersall, Mark Herberholz, Craig Krempels, Osyp Lebedowicz, Masashi Oiso, Neil Reeves, Jeroen Remie, Tomoharu Saitou, Tomi Walamies, Gabe Walls
