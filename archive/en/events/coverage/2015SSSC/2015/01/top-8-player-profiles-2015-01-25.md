Jérémie Ross-Latour
===================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/2015SSSC/top8-rosslatour.jpg)

**Hometown:** Montréal




**Which Grand Prix did you win your Super Sunday Series at?**   

 Montréal!




**What was your record in Sealed Draft (and what did you play)?**   

 2-1. Temur. I played 4/1 guys and removal. It worked well!




**What was your record in Standard Constructed (and what did you play)?**   

 3-0. [Jeskai Ascendancy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jeskai+Ascendancy). The version that plays green creatures and in which you combo out by drawing your deck. On MTGO, I call it [Jeskai Ascendancy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jeskai+Ascendancy) Clicks.




**What was your record in Booster Draft (and what did you play)?**   

 2-1. Jeskai. I won thanks to [End Hostilities](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=End+Hostilities) and [Crater's Claws](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crater%27s+Claws) to the dome.




**What has been your favorite part about this trip?**   

 The social part was great! Getting to casually talk over a meal with the very people who make the game was something unique.

 Melbourne

 2-1, I played a Jeskai tempo deck with lots of Prowess and tap/bounce effects.
