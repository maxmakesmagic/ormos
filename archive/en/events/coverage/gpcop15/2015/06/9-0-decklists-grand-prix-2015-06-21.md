The 9-0 Decklists of Grand Prix
===============================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX COPENHAGEN 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpcop15)
 on June 21, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_FrankKarsten.jpg)
By Frank Karsten











1,345 hopefuls had entered the tournament in the beginning. While 158 of them managed to win seven of their rounds on Day 1 to qualify for the second day of competition, only four players won all nine. And they did so with four different decks, a testament to the diversity of Modern.
