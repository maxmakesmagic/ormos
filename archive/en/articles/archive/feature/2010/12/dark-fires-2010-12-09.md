The 2001 World Championships in Toronto, Canada saw Jan Tomcani make the Top 8 playing Dark Fires. Based on [Fires of Yavimaya](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fires+of+Yavimaya) and adding a splash of black, Jan's deck was the same archetype that had dominated Standard for months. The 2010 World Championships start this weekend in Chiba, Japan and you can find live coverage [here](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds). To find archived coverage of 2001's event visit [here](http://www.wizards.com/sideboard/event.asp?event=Worlds2001).
