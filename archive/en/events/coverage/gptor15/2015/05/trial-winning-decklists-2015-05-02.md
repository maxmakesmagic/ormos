Grand Prix Toronto – Trial-Winning Decklists
============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 2, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro










The night before a Grand Prix there is always a flurry of activity. Players clamor to put the finishing touches on their decks, while others play in events to kick off the fun weekend. A Friday mainstay is the Grand Prix Trials, where the winners can earn coveted byes going into the main event on Saturday.


There were three Sealed grinders, won by Nate Cutler, Chris Ball, and Lucas Giuliani. Here are the eleven Standard-winning decklists:
