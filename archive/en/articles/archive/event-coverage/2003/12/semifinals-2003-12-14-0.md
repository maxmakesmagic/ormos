Semifinals:
===========



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 14, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_591.png)
By By Neil Parker











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpana03/sf2_1.jpg)The convention center was packing up at the start of this match, so the players were moved to a back corner of the hall. Ben and Peter shuffled quietly as the match begun. Peter won the die roll and chose to play. Both players mulliganed to six, then the game began. ### Game 1

Both players started out playing lands and saying go, with PTR dropping a [Rishadan Port](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishadan+Port) to keep Ben off of his mana. On Ben's third turn he [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)ed Peter seeing [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire), [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire), [Metalworker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker), [Gilded Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gilded+Lotus), and [Tinker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker). Ben took the [Tinker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) and said go.

Peter untapped and dropped a [Metalworker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker), then passed the turn back. Ben bounced right back with a [Diabolic Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Edict) to get rid of the [Metalworker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker).

Peter's next turn saw him play a [Seat of the Synod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seat+of+the+Synod) and a [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire). Ben had to tap all four of his lands during his upkeep, then played a [Skycloud Expanse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skycloud+Expanse) and sent the turn back.

Peter untapped, drew, played a [Voltaic Key](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key), and said go. On Ben's upkeep he Ported Ben's island with [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire) on the stack, tapping Ben down to one land. Ben then shipped back the turn without playing a land.

Peter taps down a [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire) and a [Voltaic Key](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key), then plays a [Thran Dynamo](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thran+Dynamo) and shiped the turn to Ben. After tapping to [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire), Ben destroyed PTR's Dynamo with a [Vindicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vindicate).

On Peter's next turn, he played out [Ancient Tomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb), [Thran Dynamo](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thran+Dynamo), [Gilded Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gilded+Lotus), and [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire). Ben floated mana with the [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire)s on the stack and [Disenchant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disenchant)ed PTR's [Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus). He then played a [Polluted Delta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta) and sent the turn back.

PTR's next turn is uneventful as he simply ships it back to Ben. Ben taps down to [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire)s, then [Vindicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vindicate)s the Dynamo in his main phase.

Peter comes back hard with a [Seat of the Synod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seat+of+the+Synod), [Tinker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) sacrificing [Voltaic Key](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key) to get [Myr Incubator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Incubator), then plays out a [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire). Ben taps down his land on his upkeep and send the turn back. Peter then sacrifices the Incubator with [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire) on the stack, and ben concedes.   
 "Did you ever play Humphries at masters or something untimed?" - Paul Rietzl  
 "Are you poking fun at me?" Ben Rubin

### Game 2

![Shadowmage Infiltrator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator)Ben played first and mulliganed to six. Peter then mulliganed as well, and kept his six.

Ben started out the game by playing a [Polluted Delta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta). Peter played an island and passed the turn. On Peter's end step, Ben cast [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek) seeing two [Rishadan Port](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishadan+Port)s, Chalice of the [Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Void), [Myr Incubator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Incubator), and two [Grim Monolith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Monolith)s. He then played a Flooded Stand and was done.

Peter played a [Rishadan Port](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishadan+Port) and sent the turn back, Porting an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) on Ben's upkeep. Peter played out [Rishadan Port](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishadan+Port), [Grim Monolith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Monolith), another [Grim Monolith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Monolith), [Thran Dynamo](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thran+Dynamo), and Chalice of the [Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Void) set on two. 

Ben untapped and destroyed the Dynamo with a [Vindicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vindicate). After a dispute about whether a Chalice set on one would be countered by his Chalice on Two, PTR played a [Metalworker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker) and passed the turn.

On Ben's next turn he cast a [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) followed by a [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), knocking out a [Myr Incubator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Incubator), leaving PTR with a Chalice of the [Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Void). PTR simply passed the turn back after drawing. Ben then flashed back [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), played a [Shadowmage Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator). Peter untapped his [Grim Monolith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Monolith) on Ben's end step.

Peter drew and passed the turn back. Ben attacked with Shadowmage, only to be blocked by [Metalworker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker). In his second mainphase he cast a [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), then went to [Vindicate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vindicate) PTR's Chalice. In response PTR cast [Stroke of Genius](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stroke+of+Genius) for seven cards. Ben then cast edict to kill the [Metalworker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker), and passed the turn.

[Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion) came out on PTR's side and it was Ben's turn. Ben [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed on his turn, then dropped a [Seal of Cleansing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Cleansing) to kill the [Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion), which shot down the Shadowmage before dying. Ben then cast a [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) taking [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval), leaving Peter with [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island), [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island), [City of Traitors](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Traitors), [Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore), [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire), and Chalice of the [Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Void). Ben then played a [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) set on [Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masticore). Peter's only response was a Chalice of the [Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Void) set on two.

The next two turns Ben attacked with [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) to knock Peter down to 14. Peter drew and played a [Mindslaver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslaver) and immediately activated it. On Ben's next turn, he showed a hand of [Caves of Koilos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Caves+of+Koilos) and [Disenchant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disenchant). Ben drew another [Caves of Koilos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Caves+of+Koilos). PTR made him play a caves, tap for 3 pain dropping him to 11, then cast the [Disenchant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disenchant) so it would be countered by the Chalice.

PTR didn't have any action to follow it up on his turn and sent it back. Ben then drew, played and flashed back a [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) on his turn and shipped the turn back to PTR. PTR then got up and left to use the bathroom. 

When he got back he drew and passed the turn. Ben continued to beat him down with [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) and found an [Energy Flux](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Energy+Flux) that shut down PTR long enough for the win.

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpana03/sf2_2.jpg)Both players kept their opening hands with Peter playing first.

Ben opened with a [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) taking a Chalice of the [Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Void) seeing [Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion), [Voltaic Key](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key), [Metalworker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker), [City of Traitors](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Traitors), and [Seat of the Synod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seat+of+the+Synod). Peter cam back with a [Metalworker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalworker) which Ben [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother)ed on his turn. Peter played a [Voltaic Key](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Key) while Ben [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage)ed for [Triskelion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Triskelion). 

After coming in for two with the Mage, Ben cast [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Peter came back with a Chalice of the [Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Void) set on two. Ben flahed back the [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and played a [Shadowmage Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator). In three short turns Peter succumbs to the Invitational army

Ben Rubin defeats Peter Szigeti 2-1
