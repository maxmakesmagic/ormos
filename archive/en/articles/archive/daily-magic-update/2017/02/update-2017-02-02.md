The February 2, 2017 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on February 2, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Nicholas_Wolfram.jpg)
By Nicholas Wolfram




 Nicholas Wolfram fell promptly in love with tabletop and video games when he was only two years old, and he has been writing about them since he figured out how words worked. Now he puts together words about Magic, which he started playing way back in 2000. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on February 2, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by [Walking Ballista](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista), a mobile murder machine.


Today's Must
============


1. [*Magic: The Gathering* expands its Twitch presence to 36 Pro Tour and other events in 2017](http://venturebeat.com/2017/02/01/magic-the-gathering-expands-its-twitch-presence-to-36-pro-tour-and-other-events-in-2017/) | Venture Beat | Jason Wilson

If you haven't already heard, we're greatly expanding our presence on Twitch this year. We want everyone to experience the intensity of as many competitive *Magic* events as possible, whether they're able to travel to the event or not. With an extra 22 events coming to Twitch this year compared to last year, it looks like that will become reality!


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [Beyond the Game: Understanding Other Skills in *Magic*](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?ID=13772&writer=Brian+Braun-Duin&articledate=2-2-2017) | TCG Player | Brian Braun-Duin

BBD shines a light on skills that he sees as essential to performing well at *Magic* that don't get enough discussion. He believes there is a lot more to being "good at *Magic*" than just piloting a game well. And as one of the world's top players, he's probably right.


1. [Holistic Wisdom: Top 7 Standard Decks to Watch](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/freyalise/holistic-wisdom-top-7-standard-decks-to-watch) | MTG Mint Card | Freyalise

With [Pro Tour *Aether Revolt*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptaer) starting tomorrow, everyone is buzzing about which decks will rise to the Top 8 and which will crash early. Freyalise weighs in on seven archetypes she thinks are strong enough to snuff out the competition.


1. [Walking Ballista Is the Most Important Card in Standard](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/walking-ballista-is-the-most-important-card-in-standard/) | ChannelFireball | Andrea Mengucci

Not content to just make bold predictions, Andrea would rather hit us with a bold declaration: [Walking Ballista](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista) is the card that will have the greatest impact on the Standard metagame in the coming months.


1. [Mechanized Production](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/34488_Mechanized-Production.html) | StarCityGames.com | Matt Higgs

It's inevitable: each new card with an alternate win condition brings a new wave of brewers trying to break the game with it. With *Aether Revolt*, we have [Mechanized Production](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mechanized+Production) trying to make eight identical artifacts and begin a turn with one of them enchanted. Matt offers a few different ways to make this happen in mono-blue, Esper, and Bant.


1. [The Top 8 Standard Decks You Haven't Seen](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?ID=13773&writer=Adam+Yurchick&articledate=2-2-2017) | TCG Player | Adam Yurchik

Adam joins the prediction party with eight new decks that he thinks may still have the chance to take the top spot this weekend at Pro Tour *Aether Revolt* in Dublin.


1. [End of SeibenLore—Start of Something New!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Fncqw1OSd1o) | Aether Hub | SeibenLore

Relax, Vorthoses! SeibenLore isn't *really* ending; it's expanding! The channel will now be functioning as Aether Hub, with a focus that expands beyond (but still includes) *Magic* lore videos.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/Fncqw1OSd1o>

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [John Asbach's Blue-Black *Aether Revolt* Draft](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpsj17/top-8-decklists-2017-01-29)

[Tezzeret's Touch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret%27s+Touch) is not a Draft all-star. Not only is it two colors, but it's also an Aura, which are generally harder to squeeze value out of in Limited. However, John managed to put the card to work for him during [Grand Prix San Jose](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpsj17)'s Top 8 last weekend and clinch first place. Backed by one of *Aether Revolt*'s strongest Limited rares, [Aethersphere Harvester](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aethersphere+Harvester), John brought his own touch to the game and dominated the competition.
