Brandon Lau, 2008 Australian Nationals finalist, knows all about that feeling. He used a half dozen token generators, including the easy-to-overlook [Kher Keep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kher+Keep), to flood the field with innocuous 1/1s. Shortly thereafter said 1/1s would turn into +2/+2 bonuses for the [Nantuko Husk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Husk), hasten a suspended Gargadon into play, or start [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)ing everything in sight after having their fury stoked.
