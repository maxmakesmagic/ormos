The *Magic Online* Season 1 Champions Showcase June 26
------------------------------------------------------


The final event of the first season of the 2021 MOCS is happening this Saturday! Eight of the best *Magic Online* players will be battling it out in *Modern Horizons 2* Limited and Modern Constructed for their share of a $70,000 prize pool! Your guide to this event can be found [here](https://magic.gg/news/2021-magic-online-champions-showcase-season-1-viewers-guide), and this high-level event will be streamed live beginning at 10 a.m. PT June 26 at [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic).

*Modern Horizons 2* on MTGO
---------------------------


We're excited to be continuing the amazing play in *Modern Horizons 2*! Sealed, Draft, and Phantom Draft events we've been running will continue, as well as our special Over the Horizons Series (discussed below). Those Leagues will run until the release of *Adventures in the Forgotten Realms* in July.


Again, cards from the set are now legal in all formats and this set is non-redeemable.


***Modern Horizons 2*** **Draft League**


**Entry Options**:


* 25 Event Tickets
* 3 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 4 Event Tickets
* 250 Play Points

**Prizes**:


* 3 wins: 6 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 2 Qualifier Points
* 2 wins: 2 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters

***Modern Horizons 2*** **Sealed League**


**Entry Options**:


* 45 Event Tickets
* 6 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 3 Event Tickets
* 450 Play Points

**Prizes**:


* 5 wins: 6 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 675 Play Points + 5 Qualifier Points
* 4 wins: 3 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 450 Play Points + 2 Qualifier Points
* 3 wins: 1 *Modern Horizons 2* booster + 225 Play Points + 1 Qualifier Point

***Modern Horizons 2*** **Phantom Draft League**


**Entry Options**:


* 10 Event Tickets
* 100 Play Points

**Prizes**:


* 3 wins: 150 Play Points + 1 Treasure Chest + 2 Qualifier Points
* 2 wins: 100 Play Points
* 1 win: 50 Play Points

Over the Horizon Championship
-----------------------------


The Over the Horizon Series, a special group of high-level *Modern Horizons 2* Limited events, wraps up with the Over the Horizon Championship!  
  

Those who earned six wins in one of the Over the Horizon Series tournaments have earned a Token good for entry into the Over the Horizon Championship on Saturday, June 26, at 7 a.m. Pacific (14:00 UTC)!


Top finishers in the Championship will earn sets of both the original *Modern Horizons* and *Modern Horizons 2*, while the winner walks away with four sets of each!


**Over the Horizon Championship**


**Start Time**: Saturday, June 26, at 7 a.m. Pacific (14:00 UTC)


**Entry Options**: 1 Limited Championship Entry Token


* *NOTE: Unused or extra Limited Championship Entry Tokens will be removed from collections at the next downtime.*

**Product**: 6 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters, plus 3 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters in the Top 8 draft. Cards opened will be added to players' collections.


**Format**: Sealed Deck—20-minute deck-building time, followed by a number of Swiss rounds based on attendance, followed by a Top 8 draft with single-elimination playoff.


**Round Length**: 25 minutes per player, first to win two matches


**Prizes**:


* 1st: 1,000 Play Points, 200 Treasure Chests, 4 regular *Modern Horizons* sets, and 4 regular *Modern Horizons 2* sets
* 2nd: 750 Play Points, 150 Treasure Chests, 2 regular *Modern Horizons* sets, and 2 regular *Modern Horizons 2* sets
* 3rd–4th: 600 Play Points, 100 Treasure Chests, 1 regular *Modern Horizons* set, and 1 regular *Modern Horizons 2* set
* 5th–8th: 450 Play Points, 50 Treasure Chests, 1 regular *Modern Horizons* set, and 1 regular *Modern Horizons 2* set
* 9th–16th: 300 Play Points, 25 Treasure Chests, and 1 regular *Modern Horizons 2* set
* 17th–32nd: 300 Play Points, 15 Treasure Chests
* 33rd–64th: 150 Play Points, 5 Treasure Chests

  
*Please note: Qualifier and Super Qualifier events will return to MTGO in late July or early August before the end of MOCS Season 2.*

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. Learn more about [Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021).


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place. *NOTE: Pauper and Standard Challenges are never pre-empted**.*
