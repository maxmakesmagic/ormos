When people say "red mage," three names should come to mind: David Price, Dan Paskins, and Patrick Sullivan. These three players have helped to make red decks what they are today. Patrick Sullivan continues to protect the red deck's honor by championing it in modern times. That was exactly what he was doing this weekend when he won the StarCityGames.com Standard Open in Edison, New Jersey playing this modern version of his favorite deck.
