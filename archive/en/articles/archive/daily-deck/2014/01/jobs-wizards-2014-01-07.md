Jobs at Wizards
===============



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on January 8, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1403_wotclogo.jpg)Welcome to 2014! Today's Arcana is a reminder that we are always looking to hire people. I do not know of a single day where we did not have open positions listed on our website.

As of this writing there were fifteen listings on the Wizards careers page, ranging from a [content manager](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/content-manager-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1) (working for me!) to a [Japanese support member](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/japanese-support-csr-work-home-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1) to art director roles for both **[Magic](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/art-director-magic-rd-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** and **[Damp;D](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/sr-marketing-art-director-dungeons-dragons-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)**.

To see the full list of positions we're hiring for, visit [our Careers page](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers) on the company website.

Here's a listing of positions available to work on **Magic** (note, positions may be filled by the time you're reading this).

* **[Associate Brand Manager](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/associate-brand-manager-magic-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - The brand team is looking to hire a new associate brand manager to join the team. You'd be part of the decision-making process and help drive the vision for **Magic**.
* **[Sr. Business Manager, Magic Online](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/sr-business-manager-magic-online-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - Help drive the business for **Magic Online**â€”working with Worth Wollpert and his team on the popular platform.
* **[Digital Art Director, Magic Online](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/digital-art-director-magic-online-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - You would be responsible for guiding the aesthetic quality of **Magic Online**.
* **[Sr. Marketing Art Director](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/sr-marketing-art-director-magic-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - You would be developing, executing, and maintaining the marketing campaign visuals and style guides for **Magic**!
* **[Art Director](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/art-director-magic-rd-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - You would be working with Jarvis and the Creative team to world-build and commission illustrations for **Magic**.
* **[Software Developer](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/software-developer-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - Work on the **Magic Online** game and help us continue to improve it!
* **[Sr. Product Manager](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/sr-product-manager-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - You would determine the strategic direction, business requirements, and critical feature sets of **Magic** digital products.
* **[Localization Manager](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/localization-project-manager-analog-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - While not directly working on **Magic**, the localization team is a vital part of our system that enables **Magic** to be printed in eleven languages!
* **[Content Manager](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/content-manager-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - This position is working for me, helping drive **Magic**'s content forward. You'd take on responsibilities over DailyMTG and other projects.
* **[Content Manager, Trade Marketing](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/content-manager-trade-marketing-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - Like the role above, this one focuses on the content we use to communicate with our trade partners, such as stores in the WPN network.
* **[Japanese Support CSR, Remote Job](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/japanese-support-csr-work-home-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - Work remotely as a member of our game support team.
* **[Technical Producer, MTGO Live](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/technical-producer-mtgo-live-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1)** - Work on **Magic Online** development setting clear production and technology direction, leading cross-functional teams to deliver an excellent customer experience on the **Magic Online** Game Service.
