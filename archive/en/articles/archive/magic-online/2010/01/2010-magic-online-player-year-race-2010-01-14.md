2010 Magic Online Player of the Year Race
=========================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on January 14, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











***Magic Online Player of the Year***…has a nice ring to it. Earn the most Qualifier Points (QPs) over the course of the [2010 Magic Online Championship Series](http://archive.wizards.com/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/121509a) and the title is yours!

In addition to the Player of the Year and individual Format titles, high finishers in the various races will earn prizes and qualify for the **Magic Online** Player of the Year MOCS tournament held on October 30, 2010.

### Magic Online Player of the Year

All QPs earned in all formats will be totaled for the MTGO Player of the Year race and updated [online](/en/articles/archive/building-budget/2011-magic-online-player-year-standings-2009-04-09) each week.

SEASON DATES: January 6 through October 13

PRIZE SUPPORT: Prizes are based on QP totals earned at the end of the 2010 MOCS Season.



|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Place | Product Prize |
| 1 | 4 complete premium (foil) sets |
| 2 | 3 complete premium (foil) sets |
| 3 | 2 complete premium (foil) sets |
| 4 | 1 complete premium (foil) set, 1 complete regular set |
| 5 | 1 complete regular set |
| 6 | 36 boosters |
| 7 | 27 boosters |
| 8 | 18 boosters |
| 9 | 12 boosters |
| 10 | 6 boosters |

  
The top 50 players will qualify for the MTGO Player of the Year Championship!

All prizes represent Magic Online digital objects. For all prizes (sets and boosters), the recipient may choose any previously released expansion as of October 30, 2010 or any combination of expansions. For example, the 2nd place finisher in the Player of the Year race could select two foil *Torment* sets and a foil *Conflux* set.

### Magic Online Format Champions of the Year

All QPs earned in each of the following categories will be totaled for the MTGO Format Champions races and updated [online](/en/articles/archive/building-budget/2011-magic-online-player-year-standings-2009-04-09) each week.

The categories that will be tracked and awarded are:

* Standard
* Block Constructed\*
* Extended
* Classic
* Specialty\*\*
* Sealed\*\*
* Draft\*\*

\*Block Constructed format means the most recent block as of when points are earned (excluding older sets being released on **Magic Online**. Currently this is only tracking *Zendikar* Block Constructed, but this will switch to "Lights" block once "Lights" is released. There is only one overall winner for this category.

\*\*Play across all product mixes and formats will be aggregated for each of these titles. There will only be one overall champion for each of the Sealed, Draft, and Specialty Format play categories.

SEASON DATES: January 6 through October 13

PRIZE SUPPORT: Prizes are based on QP totals earned in each specific category earned at the end of the 2010 MOCS Season.



|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Place | Product Prize |
| 1 | 36 boosters |
| 2 | 27 boosters |
| 3 | 18 boosters |
| 4 | 12 boosters |
| 5 | 6 boosters |

  
The top 16 players will qualify for the MTGO Player of the Year Championship!

All prizes represent **Magic Online** digital objects. For all prizes, the recipient may choose boosters from any previously released expansion as of October 30, 2010 or any combination of expansions. For example, the 2nd place finisher in a Format Championship race could select 15 *Saviors of Kamigawa* boosters, 9 *Darksteel* boosters, and 3 *Masters Edition* boosters.
