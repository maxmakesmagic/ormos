June 20 2011 DCI Banned & Restricted List Announcement
======================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 20, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**Announcement Date:** June 20, 2011  
**Effective Date:** July 1, 2011  
**Magic Online Effective Date:** June 29, 2011

**Standard**  
[Jace, the Mind Sculptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) is banned.  
[Stoneforge Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic) is banned.

*Exception:* The deck list for the "[War of Attrition](/en/articles/archive/new-phyrexia-event-decks-deck-lists-2011-05-19)" Event Deck will be legal in Standard if kept completely intact. That deck, which went on sale on June 10, contains two copies of [Stoneforge Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic).

On **Magic Online**, [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) and [Stoneforge Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic) will both be banned in Standard as of the June 29, 2011 downtime. There will be no exceptions made for the Event Deck containing [Stoneforge Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic), as that deck will not be sold online.
