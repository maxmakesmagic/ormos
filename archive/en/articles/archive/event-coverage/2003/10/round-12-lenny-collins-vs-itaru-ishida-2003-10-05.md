Round 12: Lenny Collins vs. Itaru Ishida
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 5, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_1.png)
By Dan Gow











With both players sitting on 30 points, it was likely that this match was going to put someone into the top 8. 2-time Australian national team member, Lenny Collins, who had drafted a very aggressive red/green deck, was now facing Pro Tour veteran Itaru Ishida with a menacing black/green deck.

### Game 1

![Nuisance Engine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Nuisance+Engine)Ishida plays and begins with a [Scythe of the Wretched](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scythe+of+the+Wretched) followed by a [Krark-Clan Grunt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krark-Clan+Grunt) - Collins responding with an [Elf Replica](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elf+Replica). Ishida wasted no time swinging with the Goblin for 4 a turn in first 2 turns.A [Nuisance Engine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nuisance+Engine) on Ishida's side of the table proved to be a... nuisance for Collins as the Krark Grunt had infinite first strike. The first of what could be 4 [Nim Shriekers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Shriekers) was met with a [Grab the Reins](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grab+the+Reins) from Collins, who was more than happy to trade his [Elf Replica](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elf+Replica).

The following turn another [Nim Shrieker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Shrieker) came out, and with Lenny having already taken 12 from the Scythed Goblin (whose Scythe was [Deconstruct](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deconstruct)ed by Lenny), and facing a 5/1 flier who was only going to get larger ([Nuisance Engine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nuisance+Engine)), Collins headed to game 2 in a very timely fashion.

Collins 0 - Ishida 1

### Game 2

![Slith Firewalker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Slith+Firewalker)Lenny plays and opens with a [Slith Firewalker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slith+Firewalker), for a 2 early hits, putting Ishida on 17. Ishida's situation looks grim with no 3rd turn drop, taking another 3 from the dervish-like slith, and also allowing Collins to play a rearguard [Tel-Jilad Exile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Exile). It's not often they go for 4 in a row unchecked, by Ishida's [Nim Shrieker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Shrieker) has to be replace with a second due to Collins' [Electrostatic Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrostatic+Bolt).Now on 8 and needing to keep his Shrieker alive to equip next turn, Ishida take one more hit of 7 (a 5/5 slith and the [Tel-Jilad Exile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Exile)) proved to be his down fall as Collins showed the [Battlegrowth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlegrowth) that sealed the game.

Collins 1 - Ishida 1

### Game 3

With lightning fast games like this it was no wonder Ishida elected to play, allowing Collins to mulligan to 6 cards. Ishida, opening with a turn #3 Nuisance and a turn #4 Shrieker was always going to put Collins on the back foot, especially coming off a mulligan. But Lenny wouldn't be denied tempo with a [Goblin Striker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Striker) and a [Vulshok Berserker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vulshok+Berserker), which gave him the early lead in life scores.

![Deconstruct](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Deconstruct)No attack came from Ishida as he was willing to trade his [Nim Shrieker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Shrieker) with the Berserker. A [Predator's Strike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Predator%27s+Strike) prevented that however, dropping Ishida to a precarious 8.With another Shrieker, which could not commit as a block Lenny put him on 5 the next turn and followed with an ever resilient [Duskworker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duskworker).

Nothing on the 7th turn for Ishida, who had drawn a ridiculous amount of land was always going to spell the end - even letting Collins burn for 3 as he [Deconstruct](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deconstruct)ed the [Nuisance Engine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nuisance+Engine).

Collins 2 defeats Ishida 1.
