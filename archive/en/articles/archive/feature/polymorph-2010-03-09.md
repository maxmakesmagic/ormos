Pro Tour–San Diego saw a lot of different archetypes make their mark in Standard. One you may not have heard much about was Tim Sussino's take on red-white-blue control. Using [Dragon Fodder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon+Fodder) and [Goblin Assault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Assault), Tim was free to cast [Polymorph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polymorph) knowing that the blue sorcery would inevitably reveal the only creature in his deck: [Iona, Shield of Emeria](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iona%2C+Shield+of+Emeria). You can read about all the decklists from Pro Tour–San Diego in last week's installment of [Decks of the Week](/en/articles/archive/decks-week/decks-week-342010-2010-03-04).
