Solar Flare
===========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 15, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorPic_GavinVerhey.jpg)
By Gavin Verhey




 When Gavin Verhey was eleven, he dreamt of a job making Magic cards—and now as a Magic designer, he's living his dream! Gavin has been writing about Magic since 2005. 






Jeremy Dombek snapped [Delver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets)'s mean streak at the StarCityGames.com Open Series in Massachusetts, taking the tournament down with his take on Solar Flare! The white, blue, and black control deck hasn't seen a lot of play in the past few months, but Jeremy tweaked it for this metagame and found success. Beating three Delver decks in the Top 8 en route to the trophy, could this deck be the Delver killer that everybody has been waiting for?

Jeremy's deck kicks off the early game with a slew of removal spells like [Doom Blade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doom+Blade) and [Dead Weight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dead+Weight), as well as [Lingering Souls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lingering+Souls). Even [Geist of Saint Traft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft)—Delver's trump card against removal—falls prey to [Phantasmal Image](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantasmal+Image) and [Day of Judgment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Day+of+Judgment).

This build of Solar Flare takes hold in the early game—and never releases the lead. [Gideon Jura](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon+Jura) and [Tamiyo, the Moon Sage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tamiyo%2C+the+Moon+Sage) control the game and eventually turn offensive, forcing the opponent to act or be overwhelmed. [Sun Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sun+Titan) and [Elesh Norn, Grand Cenobite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elesh+Norn%2C+Grand+Cenobite) alongside [Unburial Rites](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unburial+Rites) can present a huge threat quickly, locking out some of the more aggressive decks in Standard.

The combination of removal spells, board sweepers, and midrange threats can put down opponents in a hurry. Take a look at the decklist below and give it a try for yourself at Friday Night **Magic** tonight!
