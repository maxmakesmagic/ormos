Pauper Fiend
============



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on December 18, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Pauper is **Magic Online**'s format that uses only commons, but that doesn't mean it isn't a format capable of quick wins. \_Shaper\_ finished 2nd in a Pauper Premier Event with a Pauper version of a deck that current **Magic** Ramp;D intern [Gerry Thompson played at Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica*](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/ptrtr12/video19). This deck's plan is to get a [Kiln Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiln+Fiend) or [Nivix Cyclops](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nivix+Cyclops) on the battlefield and then play a lot of cheap instants and sorceries to quickly make its power huge.
