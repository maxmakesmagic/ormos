### Lukas Parson


**Age:** 22  
**Hometown:** Madisonville, KY   
**Occupation:** Student/*Magic* Grinder
 
   

**Pro Tour/Grand Prix Top 8s:**
  
First
 



**Other *Magic* Achievements:** 
  
Three PTQ Wins, Top 8 Indy WMCQ
 



**Record on Day 1/Day 2?**
  
8-1, 4-1-1
 



**What deck are you playing today and why?**
  
Red/Green Aggro. Most consistent mana base with having an excellent nut draw. [Stormbreath Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormbreath+Dragon) is very well-positioned.
 



**What was your MVP this weekend? Maindeck? Sideboard?**
  
[Goblin Rabblemaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Rabblemaster)/[Xenagos, the Reveler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos%2C+the+Reveler)




**What are you most excited about for Fate Reforged?**
  
Crux of Fate
