Birthing a New Archetype
========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 23, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson












There might be a lot of Mono-Blue and Mono-Black in Standard right now, but there are also plenty of hidden gems.

 Did you ever imagine that [Molten Birth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Birth) was one of them? 

![Molten Birth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Molten+Birth) We've seen some Naya decks making a splash in Standard in the hands of Brad Nelson and Willy Edel, but G0D\_L1k3's deck is whole new animal. The deck might come out of the gates slowly, but its damage potential on turn five is huge. [Hammer of Purphoros](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hammer+of+Purphoros); [Purphoros, God of the Forge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purphoros%2C+God+of+the+Forge); [Xenagos, the Reveler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos%2C+the+Reveler); and [Burning-Tree Emissary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning-Tree+Emissary) are incredibly explosive cards. 

 You might think you're safe to set up a bunch of [Pack Rat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat)s, but what happens when your opponent casts [Burning-Tree Emissary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning-Tree+Emissary) into [Molten Birth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Birth) plus [Molten Birth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Birth) with [Purphoros, God of the Forge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Purphoros%252C%2BGod%2Bof%2Bthe%2BForge) in play? 


[Assemble the Legion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Assemble+the+Legion) is the go-to hate card against Mono-Black while [Skylasher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skylasher) and [Mistcutter Hydra](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistcutter+Hydra) are clearly there for Mono-Blue, so I like this sideboard already. [Plummet](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plummet) deals with [Stormbreath Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormbreath+Dragon) while [Boros Reckoner](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Reckoner) and [Flames of the Firebrand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flames+of+the+Firebrand) cover you against red or white aggro. Overall, G0D\_L1k3's deck looks well-built, and I wouldn't be surprised if it matched up decently with most of the decks in Standard.
