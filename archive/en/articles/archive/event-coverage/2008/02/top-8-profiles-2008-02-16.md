### Guillaume Wafo-Tapa


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** Nantes, France


**Occupation:** Pro Magic Player


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** around 12


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Kuala Lumpur?** Pro Club level


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** Three GP Top 8s, two PT Top 8s


**Other previous Magic accomplishments:** Won PT Yokohama


**What was your record by draft?**  

Draft 1: 3-0  

Draft 2: 2-1  

Draft 3: 3-0  

Draft 4: 2-1  

Draft 5: 2-1


**What is your draft strategy?**  

Avoid green and red if you can. Blue-white or blue-black are what you're looking for (Merfolk and/or Wizards)


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour-Kuala Lumpur? Who did you prepare with?**  

I rested the previous week with the other French pros. We rented two apartments in Penang so we could enjoy the country and prepare at the same time. Testing partners were the Ruel brothers, Amiel Tenenbaum, Gabriel Nassif, Maxime Hermes, and Pierre Baussaron.


**What are your three favorite commons in *Lorwyn* and *Morningtide*?**  
*Lorwyn*: [Mulldrifter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter), [Silvergill Douser](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silvergill+Douser), [Nameless Inversion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nameless+Inversion), [Peppersmoke](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peppersmoke).  
*Morningtide*: [Stonybrook Schoolmaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stonybrook+Schoolmaster), [Stream of Unconsciousness](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stream+of+Unconsciousness)


**Interesting non-Magic facts about you:**  

(According to the Ruels and Pierre Canali)  

Young French man VVWM looking for a GF


If interested please contact [guillaume@wafo-tapa.com](mailto:guillaume@wafo-tapa.com)


I love pasta, particularly since winning 40k in Yokohama allowed me to buy cheese.
