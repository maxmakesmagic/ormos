Gateway Round 6: David Jafari vs. Oyvind Odegaard
=================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/masterssd02/a946.jpg)*David Jafari vs. Oyvind Odegaard*
The winner of this match would walk away with a guaranteed $2000 payout, and the chance to play for even more. Jafari is running black/blue [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), while Odegaard has green/blue/red with maindeck [Blurred Mongoose](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blurred+Mongoose). Jafari will need to contain this untargetable, uncounterable creature, and force through his [Shadowmage Infiltrators](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrators).


### Game 1


Odegaard started off strong with two [Blurred Mongooses](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blurred+Mongooses), but Jafari played [Shadowmage Infiltrator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator), which stayed back on defense to hold off Odegaard's army. To make matters worse, Odegaard was stuck at three lands. Jafari played a second Finkel, but it was [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell)ed. [Urza's Rage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Rage) was sent towards the Infiltrator, but Jafari saved it with [Repulse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Repulse). This cleared the way for the Mongooses to attack for four points of damage, and Finkel came back down on Jafari's next turn.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/masterssd02/a945.jpg)*Oyvind Odegaard*
A total of three [Memory Lapses](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapses) were played by Jafari in the next few turns, setting Odegaard back even further from getting out of his mana stall. A [Nightscape Familiar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nightscape+Familiar) showed up to hold off the Mongooses, allowing Finkel to get to work. [Thieving Magpie](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thieving+Magpie) soon joined the team, giving Jafari a significant amount of card advantage. Jafari played [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) on end step, and a counter war resulted. A second [Fact or Fiction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) was added to the stack by Jafari, and Odegaard conceded.


Jafari- 1 Odegaard- 0


### Game 2


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/masterssd02/a944.jpg)*David Jafari*
Interestingly enough, Odegaard chose to draw first. He had a turn two Mongoose while Jafari had [Nightscape Familiar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nightscape+Familiar), but Odegaard had [Repulse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Repulse). The Familiar came back out, and Odegaard had a [Flametongue Kavu](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flametongue+Kavu), which was [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell)ed. Odegaard attacked with the Mongoose, and brought out [Bearscape](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bearscape). Jafari then brought out [Shadowmage Infiltrator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator), and was able to attack thanks to his Familiar holding back the Mongoose. Odegaard tried to apply some pressure by activating [Bearscape](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bearscape), but Jafari had [Hibernation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hibernation) waiting.


A few turns later, Jafari played [Lobotomy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lobotomy) and responded with [Hibernation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hibernation). This brought [Bearscape](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bearscape) back to Odegaard's hand, and [Lobotomy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lobotomy) resolved, removing the enchantment from Odegaard's deck. [Shadowmage Infiltrator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator) continued its attack, and [Psychatog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) was soon summoned to finish up the job.


**Final Result:** Jafari- 2 Odegaard- 0
