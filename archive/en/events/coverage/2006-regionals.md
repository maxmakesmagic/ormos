2006 Regionals
==============











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/protour/images/reg_lg.jpg)*Dissension* made its Standard debut format on May 20 at U.S. and Canada Regionals. How did the new cards impact the format? Let the decklists from Regionals be your guide as you explore the new Standard environment.


You can test each decklist thanks to our [Sample Hand Generator](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/feature/294). Just click on the card fan in the upper right of each decklist and you can see what your opening hand could be, along with mulligans and the first three cards you'd draw.


[U.S. Nationals](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/nationals/usa) will be held July 28-30 in Atlanta, Ga. [Canada Nationals](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/nationals/canada) will be held August 4-6 in Ottawa, Ontario. For more information on all Nationals, [click here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/magic/nationals).
