### GRAND PRIX MILAN 2014


Top 8 Profiles
==============



![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)

Olle Rade




June 29, 2014
 







Some of Europe's finest players conquered their way into the Top 8 of Grand Prix Milan 2014. You can learn a little more about these players below.



  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpmil14/t8_simonot.jpg)
### Timothée Simonot


**Age:** 25  
**Hometown:** Paris, France  
**Occupation:** Magic writer  
**Magic Online Username:** Magicovore


 

**Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Won World Magic Cup 2013 with the French team. Won GP London 2013.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Arbor Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arbor+Colossus), Blue/Green, 9-0


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

3 x [Font of Fertility](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Font+of+Fertility), Blue/Green splashing Red and Black, 2-1


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

Maybe [Dissolve](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dissolve), Blue/Black, 1-2


**What are you most going to miss about *Theros* Block Limited?**   
[Dakra Mystic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dakra+Mystic)
