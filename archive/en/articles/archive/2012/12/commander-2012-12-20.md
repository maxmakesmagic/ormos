Commander
=========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 20, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By magicthegathering.com Staff












The Commander format acts as a perfect example of the interaction of **Magic** fans and Wizards of the Coast. Originally created by fans (and [still maintained](http://mtgcommander.net/rules.php) by an external group), Commander was adopted by Wizards as a fully supported format both in paper **Magic** and on **Magic Online**. Commander is a popular, high-powered, casual multiplayer format where huge creatures battle against powerful spells from throughout **Magic**'s history.


### Deck Construction Rules


The basic points of deck construction for [Commander](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Article.aspx?x=magic/rules/100cardsingleton-commander) are:



* 100-card deck (absolute)
* 1-copy limit (except basic lands)
* No sideboard
* 1 legendary creature in the command zone

A Commander deck is like no other. Instead of "at least sixty cards" (the norm for Constructed formats) you build a deck of *exactly* one hundred cards (no more, no less). Instead of being able to have "up to four copies" of any card (except basic lands) you may only have *exactly* one copy of each. One of those hundred cards must be a legendary creature (your commander), which defines your deck's color identity—i.e., the colors you can include in your deck (this isn't limited to just casting cost, but includes anywhere mana colors appear on a card).


Building a Commander deck on **Magic Online** is a slightly different experience as you build the deck by putting your deck's commander in the sideboard and the other 99 cards go in your main deck.


### Finding Commander on **Magic Online** Wide Beta


 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/features/format226d_commander.jpg)

### Details and B&R List


Your commander resides in the command zone (where things like Planeswalker emblems also live), and may be cast at any time you have the mana. Whenever your commander would die or be exiled, you can send it to the command zone instead, but each time your commander returns to that zone its casting cost increases by ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161225105850im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless2.gif).


Commander follows both the [Vintage](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/sfrvintage) banned list and its own banned list.
