Draft Viewer: 3/4/2009
======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 4, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Welcome back to the Draft Viewer! This time, we're going to temporarily leave the world of **Magic Online** and journey to Japan for two drafts from Pro Tour–Kyoto!


First up is Draft One, Table Three: Marcio Carvalho, Shouta Yasooka, Peter Wimmer, Joel Calafell, Cedric Phillips, Taichi Fujimoto, Yuuichi Kishimoto, and Luis Scott-Vargas. This is the draft that happened before Round Five. Click on the screenshot to launch the Draft Viewer!
