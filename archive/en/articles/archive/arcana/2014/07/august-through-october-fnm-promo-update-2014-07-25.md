August through October FNM Promo Update
=======================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on July 25, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_mikemcartor.jpg)
By Mike McArtor




 Mike first played with Ice Age and became the copy editor for DailyMTG.com in December 2011. Before DailyMTG, he was an editor on D&D's Dragon magazine for four years. 






Here is a look at the FNM Promos you can pick up at your local [Friday Night *Magic*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/event-types/friday-night-magic) over the next few months.


August


In August, black mages get another creepy-cool promo!
