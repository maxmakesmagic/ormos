Quick Questions #2
==================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX PARIS 2015](/en/events/coverage/gppar15)
 on May 10, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_FrankKarsten.jpg)
By Frank Karsten











 Early on Saturday, we asked the Twitterverse for interesting questions to be answered by the pros here at Grand Prix Paris. We selected the following one, because *Modern Masters* certainly is one of the big topics this weekend:





> 
> 
> [@magicprotour](https://twitter.com/magicprotour) what is the best way to beat Esper Dragons? Or: Which card excites you the most of Modern Masters 2015?
>  
> 
> — Danny de Rooij (@One2WhoopWhoop) [May 9, 2015](https://twitter.com/One2WhoopWhoop/status/596953962173091840)
> 




 And well, we already tackled [Esper Dragons recently](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpsao15/seven-ways-beat-esper-dragons-2015-05-02), so we took a look at the [*Modern Masters 2015* card image gallery](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/modernmasters2015edition) and asked the pros for their thoughts.





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/gppar15_qq_juza.jpg)  




![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mm2_9vgauji43t9a/en_zZAEqwgRAp.png)  





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/gppar15_qq_blohon.jpg)  




![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mm2_9vgauji43t9a/en_rZIxVRAbYe.png)  
*[Karn Liberated](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karn+Liberated). It's one of the best planeswalkers of all time.*





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/gppar15_qq_larsson.jpg)  
**Pro Tour *Gatecrash* finalist Joel Larsson**




![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mm2_9vgauji43t9a/en_0gYrhYs23Y.png)  
*[Bitterblossom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bitterblossom). I've had some fun with that. I used to play Faeries back in the days.*





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/gppar15_qq_melis.jpg)  
**2010 Dutch National Champion Bas Melis**




![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mm2_9vgauji43t9a/en_wqrhwi3REi.png)  
*[Cryptic Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command). It's a great card, and I love to play it.*





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/gppar15_qq_dolar.jpg)  
**Grand Prix Kraków 2015 finalist Robin Dolar**




![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mm2_9vgauji43t9a/en_4sRXIOzwPJ.png)  
*[Vendilion Clique](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique). It's a 3/1 flier with flash for 1UU that can put a card from a player's hand on the bottom of his or her library. That's why it's good.*





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppar15/gppar15_qq_anteri.jpg)  
**Gold level pro Fabrizio Anteri**




![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mm2_9vgauji43t9a/en_rZIxVRAbYe.png)  
*[Karn Liberated](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karn+Liberated). I've played with it in Modern, and I'd like to play with it in Limited.*
