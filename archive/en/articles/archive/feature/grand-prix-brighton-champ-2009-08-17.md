Olivier Ruel was elevated to the **Magic** Pro Tour Hall of Fame last year, and he's still going strong. This past weekend in Brighton he won his fifth Grand Prix Champion title, in his 26th GP Top 8, at his 107th Grand Prix. His champion deck had both [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball)*and* [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander). You can read more from [the official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/%E2%80%9Chere-i-ruel%E2%80%9D).
