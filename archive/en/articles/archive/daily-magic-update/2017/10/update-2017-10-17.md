Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Standard Blue-Black Chart a Course](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-10-16)

*Magic Online* user Jaberwocki was the one who inspired Lucas Esper Berthoud to pick up a Pirate-flavored Blue-Black Control deck and, you know, merely make the Brazilian National team his first time playing the deck.


Since then, Jaberwocki has again gone 5-0 with the deck, helping prove none of it was a fluke.


This deck, certainly more than other Blue-Black Control lists, is powered by [The Scarab God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God). [Kitesail Freebooter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitesail+Freebooter) is an amazing card to eternalize, as are [Gonti, Lord of Luxury](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gonti%2C+Lord+of+Luxury) and [Torrential Gearhulk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk). [Gifted Aetherborn](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifted+Aetherborn) is a concession to the red matchup—and a strong one at that. If you ever bring one of those back with [The Scarab God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scarab+God), you've pretty much already won against any red opponent. Heck, you've probably pretty much won against almost anyone.
