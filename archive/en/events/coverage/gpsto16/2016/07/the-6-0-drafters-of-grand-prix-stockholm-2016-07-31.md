The 6-0 Drafters of Grand Prix Stockholm 2016
=============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 31, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











1,244 players had been in the running originally. 352 made it to the second day and got to draft. Four of them managed to win all three rounds of their first draft as well as all three rounds of their second draft: Filippo Kratter, Karo Jaakkola, Matti Kuisma, and most notably Hall of Famer Olle Råde.


Check out all of their decklists below!
