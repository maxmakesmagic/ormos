2008 World Championships Invitation List
========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 3, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Invitations to the 2008 World Championships are awarded in the following manner:

* The current World Champion.
* The Top 8 finishers from the previous World Championship.
* The current Pro Tour Player of the Year.
* For countries that hold an invitation-only National Championship, the members of each national team, including that team's designated alternate, if any (see "National Team Composition" below).
* For countries that hold an open National Championship, the winner of that National Championship (see "Open Nationals Invitations to Worlds" below).
* Players with Pro Tour Players Club level 3 or higher. Level 3 players may only attend one Pro Tour and/or Worlds of their choice in a season.
* The Top 50 DCI Composite–ranked players from the APAC region (published on October 29, 2008).
* The Top 50 DCI Composite–ranked players from the Europe region (published on October 29, 2008).
* The Top 50 DCI Composite–ranked players from the Latin America region (published on October 29, 2008).
* The Top 50 DCI Composite–ranked players from the North America region (published on October 29, 2008).

If you believe that your rating, Pro Club level, or finish at a National Championship is in error, please Visit <https://webapp.wizards.com/login.aspx?target=personal>. After logging in, click the "Appeals" link and fill out the form.
