Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 31, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/olle_rade_author.jpg)
By Olle Rade













After fifteen rounds but eight players remained in the running for the title of champion of Grand Prix Utrecht 2015, to be enshrined among the stars of Modern Masters Weekend. It's about time to get to know them a little better, don't you think?




**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gputr15/t8_ahlstrom.jpg)Name:** Christian Åhlström


**Age:** 24


**Occupation:** Teacher


**Hometown:** Järna, Sweden



**When did you start playing *Magic*?**


Zendikar



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Mutagenic Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutagenic+Growth) and [Ant Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ant+Queen). Green-white, 7-2



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Kami of Ancient Law](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kami+of+Ancient+Law), black-white, 3-0



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning), red-black, 3-0



**What's your happiest memory from playing *Magic*?**


When my wife introduced me to the game and pushed me to follow my dreams. Also playing EDH at home with my friends.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gputr15/t8_seibold.jpg)Name:** Christian Seibold


**Age:** 26


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Nuremburg, Germany



**When did you start playing *Magic*?**


2002



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Wildfire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wildfire) and [Savage Twister](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savage+Twister), red-green ramp, 9-0



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Ghost Council of Orzhova](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghost+Council+of+Orzhova), black-white-blue, 3-0



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Battlegrace Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlegrace+Angel), black-white, 1-2



**What's your happiest memory from playing *Magic*?**


Making Top 8 at Pro Tour Born of the Gods in Valencia.  

 




---

  



**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gputr15/t8_smit.jpg)Name:** Iwan Smit


**Age:** 26


**Occupation:** Artist/Illustrator


**Hometown:** Rotterdam, The Netherlands



**When did you start playing *Magic*?**


When I was 12.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Banefire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banefire)! I played red-black-white, but sideboarded into red-blue-black. 7-1-1.



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Nameless Inversion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nameless+Inversion), black-white spirits, 3-0



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak), blue-white artifacts, 3-0.



**What's your happiest memory from playing *Magic*?**


Hanging out at *Magic* United and playing at home with De Tovenaars van de Binnenweg.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gputr15/t8_neirynck.jpg)Name:** Branco Neirynck


**Age:** 24


**Occupation:** Unemployed


**Hometown:** Koekelare, Belgium



**When did you start playing *Magic*?**


2008



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Pelakka Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pelakka+Wurm). Green-blue-white, 9-0


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Steady progress, blue-green, 2-1


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Ant Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ant+Queen). Green-black-red, 2-0-1


**What's your happiest memory from playing *Magic*?**


Winning the Belgian National Championships 6 years ago.  

 




---

  



**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gputr15/t8_dickmann.jpg)Name:** Fabian Dickmann


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Bochum, Germany



**When did you start playing *Magic*?**


1996 when I was 6 or 7 years old.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Two copies of [Apocalypse Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Apocalypse+Hydra) . Green-white-red, 8-1.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Precursor Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Precursor+Golem), Red-black Bloodthirst. 2-1.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Six [Glint Hawk Idol](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk+Idol)  and two [Cranial Plating](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cranial+Plating). Mono white, 3-0.


**What's your happiest memory from playing *Magic*?**


Making my first GP Top 8 in my hometown and the German/Austrian testing group for Pro Tour Montreal.  

 




---

  



**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gputr15/t8_joachim.jpg)Name:** David Joachim


**Age:** 23


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Münster, Germany



**When did you start playing *Magic*?**


At the age of 7 with mu uncle in Ireland on a picnic blanket. We were highlandering International Edition. He had a set of each card.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Two [Selesnya Guildmage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Selesnya+Guildmage) along with [Overwhelming Stampede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overwhelming+Stampede). With an honorable mention to [Kozilek's Predator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kozilek%27s+Predator). Green-white, 9-0.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


A foil [Mirror Entity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirror+Entity), green-white, 1-2.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Spikeshot Elder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spikeshot+Elder), [Sunforger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunforger) and [Cranial Plating](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cranial+Plating) with double-strike creatures. Red-white, 3-0.


**What's your happiest memory from playing *Magic*?**


Playing *Magic* with my uncle on a vacation in Italy. I could never beat him, but I was still eager to play with him for hours in the evenings. I was fascinated by his stories about how you were a Mage that chose spells from your arsenal to outwit your opponent.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gputr15/t8_vergoni.jpg)Name:** Davide Vergoni


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Architect


**Hometown:** Perugia, Italy



**When did you start playing *Magic*?**


1995, thanks to my brother who taught me the game.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Cryptic Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command) gave me lots of wins, but two [Mulldrifter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter) were the cards that made the deck work. I played 5 colors, 8-1.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Cranial Plating](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cranial+Plating), blue-white Affinity, 3-0


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Mighty Leap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mighty+Leap) that won me some games, and Mirran Crusader. 2-0-1.


**What's your happiest memory from playing *Magic*?**


Definitely my first two Italian nationals back in 2001 and 2002. The first one was in my hometown when I was a kid, and the second one qualified by meat grinder.
