The StarCityGames.com Invitational for the first half of 2011 was this past weekend, and SCG Open standout Patrick Cox was the victor. The split-format event meant that in order to win, Pat had to excel in both Standard and Legacy. His choice for the Standard portion, which he also played in the Top 8 of the event, was RUG. You can read [full coverage](http://www.starcitygames.com/php/news/expandnews.php?Article=21948) of the event at StarCityGames.com.
