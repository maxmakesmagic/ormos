They are a tribe as old as the game itself, and at Pro Tour–Amsterdam the Netherlands's own Robert Van Medevoort managed to use Goblins to make his mark on the tournament. Thanks to [Simian Spirit Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Simian+Spirit+Guide) he can start with [Warren Instigator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warren+Instigator) on his very first turn, and [Punishing Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Punishing+Fire) / [Grove of the Burnwillows](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grove+of+the+Burnwillows) turns his late land flood into tons of extra damage. Powerful! Read more from the official coverage [here](/en/events/coverage/rietzl-sweeps-victory-amsterdam).
