Feature Match Round Four - Brian Hegstad vs Joel Frank
======================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_480.png)
By David Jafari











The round four feature match paired US Nationals' Finalist and Team ABU member Brian Hegstad with a red/green speed deck splashing blue for [Prophetic Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prophetic+Bolt) versus Massachusetts' native Joel Frank playing No-Mar.


### Game 1


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPMINN01/980.jpg)Brian won the flip and opted to play first, leading with a first turn [Shivan Oasis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shivan+Oasis). Joel matched this with a [Coastal Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coastal+Tower) on the other side of the table. By the time Joel made his third land drop, Brian was already beating him senseless with a [Raging Kavu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Kavu) and a [Blurred Mongoose](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blurred+Mongoose). Joel made a second [Gerrard's Verdict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gerrard%27s+Verdict), but had nothing for the creature ambush. Joel missed his fourth land drop, but had a [Recoil](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Recoil) for the [Raging Kavu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Kavu) to slow down the onslaught. On Joel's next turn, a moan from the spectators signaled his inability to draw land; he was able to put an [Emblazoned Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emblazoned+Golem) on the board to act as a speed bump, but Brian simply laid a [Raging Kavu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Kavu) at the end of the turn followed by a [Skizzik](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skizzik) without kicker on his turn. The Golem stepped in front of the [Raging Kavu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Kavu), but with Joel now at one life and with only two swamps and a [Coastal Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coastal+Tower) in play, there was nothing Joel could do about the [Blurred Mongoose](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blurred+Mongoose) and conceded.


Hegstad 1 - Frank 0


### Game 2


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPMINN01/979.jpg)Interestingly enough for this block, this game started without any taplands. In a change of pace, Brian missed his fourth land drop and lacked access to red mana. Joel moated Brian's offense with a [Spectral Lynx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spectral+Lynx), which was currently standing guard over a turn two [Blurred Mongoose](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blurred+Mongoose). To compound Brian's problems, Joel used an end step [Recoil](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Recoil) when he realized that Brian was about to discard. On Joel's next turn, he began attacking with his [Spectral Lynx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spectral+Lynx) and dropped a second one. All of the sudden, Joel became the aggressive deck while Brian was regrouping from his mana inconsistencies. Joel, on the other hand, was drawing a little bit too much mana, leaving him open to Brian's unkicked [Skizzik](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skizzik) that evened out the damage race. Another unkicked [Skizzik](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skizzik) put Joel at a precarious six life, but he casually untapped, sent his two bears to reduce Brian to five, and showed a [Death Grasp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Grasp) for the game.


Hegstad 1 - Frank 1


### Game 3


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPMINN01/978.jpg)
The third game mirrored the first with both players opening with taplands: [Shivan Oasis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shivan+Oasis) and [Coastal Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coastal+Tower). Brian didn't have a second turn drop, but he did have an Ice to lock up Joel's mana on the next turn. While Joel didn't have a direct answer for the [Thornscape Familiar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thornscape+Familiar) that resolved on Brian's next turn, he did have an [Addle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Addle) to proactively remove a threat. Joel didn't see wasn't very happy when a [Raging Kavu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Kavu), a [Skizzik](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skizzik), a [Prophetic Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prophetic+Bolt), a [Ghitu Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghitu+Fire), and a land were revealed, but he was even more displeased with Brian topped a [Skizzik](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skizzik) to replace the [Addle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Addle)d one and served for seven on the fourth turn. Joel dropped to six during Brian's next attack phase with the same creatures. Feeling the heat, Joel played a [Dromar's Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dromar%27s+Charm) for five life during Brian's endstep after nothing was cast in his opponent's main phase. Joel followed up with a topdecked [Death Grasp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Grasp) on the [Skizzik](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skizzik) to try to regain control, but an end step [Prophetic Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prophetic+Bolt) from Brian followed by a [Raging Kavu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Kavu) on his turn which sent five more points collectively reduced Joel back to five life. Joel cast [Gerrard's Verdict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gerrard%27s+Verdict), feature two creatures, and [Spectral Lynx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spectral+Lynx) on his next turn, but was helpless to stop the [Ghitu Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghitu+Fire) he knew he had left Brian after the [Addle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Addle).


**Final Result:** Hegstad 2 - Frank 1
