Pro Tour-Berlin has barely wrapped up and Luis Scott-Vargas has been crowned a champion! He played the breakout hit of the tournament in the form of Elves. The deck seeks to generate huge amounts of card and mana advantage through a combination of [Glimpse of Nature](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimpse+of+Nature), [Heritage Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid), [Nettle Sentinel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel), and cheap Elves. Six players took varying versions of the concoction to the Top 8, but it was Luis Scott-Vargas who reigned supreme.
