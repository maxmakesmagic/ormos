### DAILY DECK


Magic 2015 Mono-Green
=====================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)

Adam Prosak

From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer.


July 23, 2014
 







When a new set is introduced into Standard, some players opt to continue to play their existing decks. Charley Murdock is not one of those people. At the [StarCityGames Open in Baltimore](http://www.starcitygames.com/events/190714_baltimore.html) this past weekend, Charley put many of *Magic 2015*'s most exciting cards into his Mono-Green Devotion deck, falling just short of the Top 8 due to tiebreakers.



![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Nissa, Worldwaker)

*Magic 2015* cards are all over the place in this deck, each with a unique impact on the deck. [Nissa, Worldwaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa%2C+Worldwaker) is a fantastic card for this deck, as she fuels a relatively mana-hungry deck or provides a constant stream of 4/4s. The [Darksteel Citadel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Citadel) looks very strange until you realize that [Nissa, Worldwaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa%2C+Worldwaker) can turn it into an indestructible creature. Given that it is still a land, there are very few cards that can remove it from play. One of the cards that Nissa can provide extra mana for is [Genesis Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Genesis+Hydra). [Genesis Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Genesis+Hydra) is a potent threat that often brings another threat alongside it.



[Chord of Calling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling) is another great *Magic 2015* addition that allows the deck to play a little bit of a toolbox of creatures, effectively finding the right one for each situation. [Hornet Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Queen) is often a backbreaker against black decks focused on one-for-one removal, while [Reclamation Sage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reclamation+Sage) is an excellent tool against the many enchantments of *Theros* block. Rounding out the *Magic 2015* singletons, [Phyrexian Revoker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Revoker) provides an answer to Planeswalkers that green can often have a tough time with.
