Free Association
================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on February 5, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_markrosewater.jpg)
By Mark Rosewater




 Working in R&D since '95, Mark became Magic head designer in '03. His hobbies: spending time with family, writing about Magic in all mediums, and creating short bios. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/global/images/bigwotclogo.jpg)For a long time employees referred to Wizards of the Coast as “watzee”. This came from pronouncing the first letter in each word (WotC). Eventually, we realized that “watzee” was [confusing](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/mr110.asp) to the public and started shortening Wizards of the Coast to just Wizards.
