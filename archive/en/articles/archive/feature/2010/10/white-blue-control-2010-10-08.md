White-Blue Control has been around for years, and *Scars of Mirrodin* is providing some new tools for the deck! The latest **Magic** set is now available for sale and use in your Standard decks. Gerard Fabiano recently did well with his tech on the archetype at a large tournament in New York presented by TCGPlayer.com. [Sunblast Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunblast+Angel) is a powerful win condition that also happens to wipe out your opponent's team. If your opponent won't tap one of their his or her creatures to allow you to blow it up? Steal it with [Volition Reins](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volition+Reins)!
