Top 8 Players of Grand Prix Metz 2017
=====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 27, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After fifteen rounds, with a total of 1,477 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!




---


### Martin Jůza


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmet17/t8_juza.jpg)


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Professional tourist


**Hometown:** Plzen, Czech Republic


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


I was voted into the Hall of Fame!


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


9-0, black-red, nothing excelled in particular, it was a combination of good curve, good removal, and some rares.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


White-Blue Aggro, 2-1, [Aerial Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aerial+Guide).


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Blue-Green Ramp splashing white, 2-0-1, [Weaver of Currents](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Weaver+of+Currents).




---


### Christoffer Larsen


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmet17/t8_larsen.jpg)


**Age:** 29


**Occupation:** Navy


**Hometown:** Copenhagen, Denmark


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Back-to-back Two-Headed Giant champion and some Grand Prix Top 8s. Playing with Team Genesis.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


White-black critters and spells. Mirror was great, though.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


More white-black critters and spells with friendly neighborhood [Gust Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gust+Walker).


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


The God! ([The Locust God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Locust+God).)




---


### Theophile Delavy


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmet17/t8_delavy.jpg)


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Due diligence analyst


**Hometown:** Geneva, Switzerland


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Nothing relevant.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Angel of Condemnation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Condemnation). Red-white, sometimes sideboarding into green-white. 8-1.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Majestic Myriarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Majestic+Myriarch). Mono black splashing green. 2-1.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Dreamstealer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamstealer). Black-green. 3-0.




---


### Francesco Fritto Hugony


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmet17/t8_hugony.jpg)


**Age:** 37


**Occupation:** Gamification manager


**Hometown:** Milan, Italy


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Some Grand Prix Top 16s and Pro Tour Day 2s.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Bounty of the Luxa](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bounty+of+the+Luxa), G/u/b/w four-color base green, 9-0.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Grind // Dust](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grind+%2F%2F+Dust), black-red splashing white, 1-2.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Unconventional Tactics](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unconventional+Tactics), White-Black Zombies, 3-0.

### Wolfgang Bach


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmet17/t8_bach.jpg)


**Age:** 35


**Occupation:** Ben Stark fanboy/Limited teacher at Club Remata—shoutouts!


**Hometown:** Regensburg, Germany


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Top 32 Grand Prix Prague 2015.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Farm // Market](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Farm+%2F%2F+Market), red-green, 7-2.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Angel of Condemnation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Condemnation), red-white, 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Ominous Sphinx](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ominous+Sphinx) or [Dreamstealer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamstealer), blue-black, 3-0.

### Heiko Lehnert


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmet17/t8_lehnert.jpg)


**Age:** 43


**Occupation:** Product owner


**Hometown:** Duisburg, Germany


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Drachental Invitational runner-up.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-red with [The Scorpion God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Scorpion+God). 8-1.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


White-Black Zombies, all Zombies, 3-0.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Chaos Maw](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chaos+Maw), Black-Red Removal, 2-1.




---


### Mitchell Manders


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmet17/t8_manders.jpg)


**Age:** 26


**Occupation:** Student/*Magic Online* grinder


**Hometown:** Dordrecht, the Netherlands


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Grand Prix Bilbao 2013 winner, World *Magic* Cup 2016 competitor, *Magic Online* Player of the Year 2015–2016.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Black-Red Aggro. Three [Open Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Open+Fire) and a bringer of glory.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Green-white splashing blue, 2-1, two [Overwhelming Splendor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overwhelming+Splendor).


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Blue-red, 2-1, two Anger Drake.

### Elias Klocker


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmet17/t8_klocker.jpg)


**Age:** 23


**Occupation:** *Magic: The Gathering* trader


**Hometown:** Schwarzach, Austria


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**


Top 8 Grand Prix Ghent 2012, Top 33 Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation*.


**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Ammit Eternal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ammit+Eternal). Black-Red Aggro. 9-0.


**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


[Ammit Eternal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ammit+Eternal) and [Supernatural Stamina](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Supernatural+Stamina). Black-Red Aggro. 2-0-1.


**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**


Red-Green Aggro. [Samut, the Tested](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Samut%2C+the+Tested). 2-0-1.
