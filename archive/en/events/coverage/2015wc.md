2015 WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP COMPETITORS
-----------------------------------


* ![Shahar Shenhar](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042904im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_shenhar_newinlineA.png)

### Shahar Shenhar




Winner - 2014 *Magic*World Championship


Shahar Shenhar claimed the unprecedented honor of winning back-to-back World Championship titles with his victory at the 2014 World Championship. 


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/shahar-shenhars-origin-story-2015-08-03)
* ![Ari Lax](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042853im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_Lax_newinlineA.png)

### Ari Lax




Winner - Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir*


Ari Lax's victory at Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir*made him the first confirmed competitor at the 2015 *Magic* World Championship.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/ari-laxs-origin-story-2015-08-04)
* ![Martin Müller](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042812im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_muller_newinlineA.png)

### Martin Müller




Team Captain - 2014 World *Magic*Cup Winning Team


It was at the 2014 World *Magic*Cup, the event in which Martin Müller earned his invitation to the 2015 *Magic*World Championship, where the beginning of the Danish *Magic* explosion began.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/martin-mullers-origin-story-2015-08-24)
* ![Antonio Del Moral León](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042720im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_delmoralleon_newinlineA.png)

### Antonio Del Moral León




Winner - Pro Tour *Fate Reforged*


Antonio Del Moral León made *Magic*history when he became the first player from Spain to win a Pro Tour after his victory at Pro Tour *Fate Reforged*. 


[2015 Worlds Info](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/2015WC/event-information)
* ![Martin Dang](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042843im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_dang_newinlineA.png)

### Martin Dang




Winner - Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir*


Danish *Magic*player Martin Dang exploded onto the pro player scene early in 2015, first with his win at Grand Prix Liverpool and then his victory at Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir*a month later.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/martin-dangs-origin-story-2015-08-24)
* ![Magnus Lantto](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042537im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_lantto_newinlineA.png)

### Magnus Lantto




Winner - 2014 *Magic Online*Championship


Longtime player Magnus Lantto saw some of his first big successes in *Magic*tournament play with his win at Grand Prix Milan and the 2014 *Magic Online*Championship.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/magnus-lanttos-origin-story-2015-08-18)
* ![Alexander Hayne](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827043015im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_hayne_newinlineA.png)

### Alexander Hayne




Grand Prix Pro Points Leader 


After a tight race with fellow Quebecois and friend Pascal Maynard, Alexander Hayne locked up his 2015 World Championship seat by earning 42 Professional Points through Grand Prix finishes. 


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/alexander-haynes-origin-story-2015-08-04)
* ![Joel Larsson](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042833im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_larsson_newinlineA.png)

### Joel Larsson




Winner - Pro Tour *Magic Origins*


Joel Larsson has been close to a Pro Tour title before, but in 2013 it eluded him. This time, at Pro Tour *Magic Origins*, Larsson was able to claim the trophy. 


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/joel-larssons-origin-story-2015-08-18)
* ![Mike Sigrist](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042618im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_sigrist_newinlineA.png)

### Mike Sigrist




2014-15 Player of the Year, Top Pro Points - North America


Mike Sigrist narrowly edged out Eric Froehlich with a finals finish at Pro Tour *Magic Origins*to claim the 2014-15 Player of the Year title. 


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/mike-sigrists-origin-story-2015-08-07)
* ![Eric Froehlich](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042558im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_Froehlich_newinlineA.png)

### Eric Froehlich




Top Pro Points - North America


Recent Pro Tour Hall of Fame elect Eric Froehlich put up a stellar performance this season, including a win at Grand Prix San Jose 2015 and a Top 8 finish at Pro Tour *Fate Reforged*. 


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/eric-froehlichs-origin-story-2015-08-12)
* ![Samuel Black](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827052449im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_black_newinlineA.png)

### Samuel Black




Top Pro Points - North America


Samuel Black put up multiple consistent finishes this season, including a very deep run in the 2014 World Championship. 


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/samuel-blacks-origin-story-2015-08-17)
* ![Brad Nelson](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827052358im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_nelson_newinlineA.png)

### Brad Nelson




Top Pro Points - North America


Brad Nelson put up a solid 2014-15, backed by a jaw-dropping win percentage in Standard featuring three Grand Prix Top 8s.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/brad-nelsons-origin-story-2015-08-21)
* ![Ondřej Stráský](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042730im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_strasky_newinlineA.png)

### Ondřej Stráský




Top Pro Points - Europe


Young Czech Republic player Ondřej Stráský exploded onto the Pro Tour scene this season with Sunday stage appearances at Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir* and Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir*.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/ondrej-straskys-origin-story-2015-08-17)
* ![Lee Shi Tian](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042955im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_lee_newinlineA.png)

### Lee Shi Tian




Top Pro Points - Asia-Pacific


Hong Kong player Lee Shi Tian added to his impressive resume with Top 8 finishes at Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir* and Pro Tour *Fate Reforged* this season.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/lee-shi-tians-origin-story-2015-08-13)
* ![Yuuya Watanabe](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042822im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_Watanabe_newinlineA.png)

### Yuuya Watanabe




Top Pro Points - Asia-Pacific


One of Japan's legends and a two-time Player of the Year, Yuuya Watanabe's stellar season has earned him a seat in his fourth World Championship appearance.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/yuuya-watanabes-origin-story-2015-08-19)
* ![Kentaro Yamamoto](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042608im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_Yamamoto_newinlineA.png)

### Kentaro Yamamoto




Top Pro Points - Asia-Pacific


Kentaro Yamamoto's impressive run at Pro Tour *Magic Origins* gave him the necessary lead to lock Asia-Pacific's third seat in the 2015 World Championship.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/kentaro-yamamotos-origin-story-2015-08-19)
* ![Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042934im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_pvddr_newinlineA.png)

### Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa




Top Pro Points - Latin America


Hall of Famer Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa cemented his seat in the spring on multiple solid finishes with Esper Dragons in Standard, including a win at Grand Prix São Paulo 2015.


[2015 Worlds Info](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/2015WC/event-information)
* ![Thiago Saporito](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042710im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_saporito_newinlineA.png)

### Thiago Saporito




Top Pro Points - Latin America


Thiago Saporito's breakout performance took place at Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir*, and he didn't slow down on earning Pro Points all season following that event.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/thiago-saporitos-origin-story-2015-08-10)
* ![Seth Manfield](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827052439im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_manfield_newinlineA.png)

### Seth Manfield




Top Pro Points - At Large


Seth Manfield had a stellar season, with his third Grand Prix victory in Ottawa last November and his first Pro Tour Top 8 at Pro Tour *Fate Reforged*.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/seth-manfields-origin-story-2015-08-21)
* ![Paul Rietzl](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042517im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_rietzl_newinlineA.png)

### Paul Rietzl




Top Pro Points - At Large


Hall of Famer Paul Rietzl picked up plenty of Pro Points with consistent Pro Tour finishes this season and two Grand Prix Top 8s.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/paul-rietzls-origin-story-2015-08-06)
* ![Shaun McLaren](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042659im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_mclaren_newinlineA.png)

### Shaun McLaren




Top Pro Points - At Large


Shaun McLaren started off his season right with a finals finish at Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir*, and a multitude of solid finishes following that cemented him among the top of the Pro Point standings all year long. 


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/shaun-mclarens-origin-story-2015-08-20)
* ![Steve Rubin](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827052428im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_rubin_newinlineA.png)

### Steve Rubin




Top Pro Points - At Large


Dark horse Steve Rubin has been a machine all season, quietly racking up very solid Pro Tour finishes and amassing Pro Points to earn his seat.


[2015 Worlds Info](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/2015WC/event-information)
* ![Owen Turtenwald](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042527im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_turtenwald_newinlineA.png)

### Owen Turtenwald




Top Pro Points - At Large


Owen Turtenwald started off the season right with a team win at Grand Prix Portland 2014, and he's been the shining example of consistency since.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/owen-turtenwalds-origin-story-2015-08-20)
* ![Jacob Wilson](https://web.archive.org/web/20150827042548im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/2015_wilson_newinlineA.png)

### Jacob Wilson




Top Pro Points - At Large


Jacob Wilson's season highlights include a Grand Prix win last year in Nashville as well as his Top 8 finish at Pro Tour *Fate Reforged*.


[LEARN MORE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/jacob-wilsons-origin-story-2015-08-14)
