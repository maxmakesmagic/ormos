The October 7, 2016 Update
==========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on October 7, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on October 7, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by the VIP area at Prakhata Club. It's where the Consulate's most famous go to let loose.

What to Watch This Weekend
==========================


1. [Grand Prix Atlanta](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpatl16)

Grand Prix Atlanta is going to be our first look at the *Kaladesh* Limited format on a big stage. Well, bigger than the [Pre-Prerelease](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VgcDP03OnyY) that is. Coverage starts at 11 a.m. ET/8 a.m. PT on [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic).


After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [*Kaladesh*: Top 10 Commander Cards](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/kaladesh-top-10-commander-cards) | MTGGoldfish | SaffronOlive and Tomer Abramovici

SaffronOlive and Tomer talk Commander, as they figure out the ten best cards for Commander to come out of *Kaladesh*. And maybe some other alliteration.


Want more *Kaladesh* Commander? The Mana Source also published a video yesterday showing off some sweet, sweet *Kaladesh* Commander action, which you can see below.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/SlHzqBVYLK0>


1. [No Copter, No Copter, No Copter!](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/lee-shi-tian/no-copter-no-copter-no-copter) | MTGMintCard | Lee Shi Tian

You better believe there will be a reaction in the metagame to [Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter)'s ubiquity, and Lee Shi Tian has the decks that don't need no stinkin' Copter. Wait, that doesn't really work. Get to the Copter? That's kind of the opposite of what we're going for. Maybe there just is no movie quote for this.


1. [Play Any Smuggler's Copter Deck You Want](http://www.toplevelpodcast.com/smugglers-copter/) | Next Level Podcast | Patrick Chapin and Mike Flores

Or you can join 'em rather than try to beat 'em. Chapin and Flores give you the good news about [Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter)—it can go in almost any deck!


1. [Budget Brews: Aetherflux Reservoir](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/budget-brews-aetherflux-reservoir/) | ChannelFireball | Neal Oliver

Looking for a sweet deck that won't be too hard to collect or trade for? Oliver has a few [Aetherflux Reservoir](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherflux+Reservoir) decks with low rare counts but powerful finishes. This one just happens to finish by dealing your opponent 50 damage after casting a dozen or so spells in a single turn. Welcome to *Kaladesh*!


1. [How to Keep Playing Control When Aggro Is Too Good](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/33815_How-To-Keep-Playing-Control-When-Aggro-Is-Too-Good.html) | StarCityGames.com | Shaheen Soorani

I feel your pain, Shaheen. I feel your pain. He thinks, however, that he has the answer to all of these Vehicles running around Standard. (By the way, Kaladesh must have really loose Vehicle property laws, if just anyone can jump in any Vehicle and motor around.)

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Red-White Artificers](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/displaydeck.php?DeckID=108268)

[Inventor's Goggles](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inventor%27s+Goggles) was on exactly no one's radar when *Kaladesh* was released, but it's a key cog in David Ortiz's (not that David Ortiz) Red-White Artificers deck that hinges on the sneaky Artificer, [Stone Haven Outfitter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Haven+Outfitter). The deck is incredibly aggressive with a low curve and just nineteen lands, making it one of the fastest decks around. It utilizes a number of cards that key off Equipment and artifacts, and that Equipment serves to boost the creatures even further. And because many of them are Artificers, the [Inventor's Goggles](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inventor%27s+Goggles) fit just perfectly.
