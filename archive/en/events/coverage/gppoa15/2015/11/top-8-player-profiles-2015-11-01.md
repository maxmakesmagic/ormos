Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 1, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim












Presenting... the Top 8 of Porto Alegre!


### Thiago Saporito


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppoa15/gppoa15_t8_saporito.jpg)


**Hometown:** Santo André, São Paulo, Brazil


**Age:** 22


**Occupation:** MTG Player


**Day 1 Score:** 8–1 (3 Byes)


**Day 2 Score:** 5–0–1


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** 


Pro Tour Khans of Tarkir Top 8, World Magic Cup 2014 Top 8 (Team Brazil)


**What deck did you play today, and why did you choose this deck?**  

White-Green Hexproof Auras, because Daniel Ward gave me the deck!


**What was your MVP all weekend?**   
[Ethereal Armor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ethereal+Armor)





---

### Marcos Paulo De Jesus Freitas


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppoa15/gppoa15_t8_de-jesus-freitas.jpg)


**Hometown:** Rio de Janeiro, Brazil


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Systems Analyst


**Day 1 Score:** 9–0 (2 Byes)


**Day 2 Score:** 4–1–1 (conceding to Thiago Saporito!)


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Grand Prix Buenos Aires 2015 Finalist


**What deck did you play today, and why did you choose this deck?**  

Naya Zoo, because “Joao Da Pochete” told me to. It's not really favored against in the first game, but it has a great sideboard!


**What was your MVP all weekend?**   
[Blood Moon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon)





---

### Gabriel Fehr


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppoa15/gppoa15_t8_fehr.jpg)


**Hometown:** Salvador, Bahia, Brazil


**Age:** 23


**Occupation:** Lawyer


**Day 1 Score:** 8–0–1 (1 Bye)


**Day 2 Score:** 4-1-1


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


World Magic Cup 2014 Top 8 (Team Brazil)


**What deck did you play today, and why did you choose this deck?**  

Blue-Red Twin. Modern is about knowing your deck, and I've been playing [Splinter Twin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Splinter+Twin) since it was introduced.


**What was your MVP all weekend?**   
[Snapcaster Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snapcaster+Mage), but Keranos, did a lot of the work too!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppoa15/gppoa15_t8_amaral.jpg)


**Hometown:** Niteroi, Rio de Janerio, Brazil


**Age:** 22


**Occupation:** Student


**Day 1 Score:** 7-1-1 (2 Byes)


**Day 2 Score:** 5-1


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Grand Prix São Paulo Top 32


**What deck did you play today, and why did you choose this deck?**  

Bant Zoo


**What was your MVP all weekend?**

### Guilherme Merjam


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppoa15/gppoa15_t8_merjam.jpg)


**Hometown:** São Paulo, Brazil


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Salesman at LETS COLLECT Store


**Day 1 Score:** 7-2 (2 Byes)


**Day 2 Score:** 5-0-1


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Pro Tour San Juan 2010 Top 16, Brazil Nationals Top 4, Pro Tour Seattle 2012 Top 64, Pro Tour Battle for Zendikar Top 32, Grand Prix São Paulo 2014 Champion


**What deck did you play today, and why did you choose this deck?**  
[Living End](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End), because I've been practicing with it for three months on Magic Online.


**What was your MVP all weekend?**   
[Living End](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End), and [Sudden Death](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sudden+Death)!

### Diego Marquez


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gppoa15/gppoa15_t8_marquez.jpg)


**Hometown:** Buenos Aires, Argentina


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Civil Servant


**Day 1 Score:** 7-2 (No Byes)


**Day 2 Score:** 5-1


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


None.


**What deck did you play today, and why did you choose this deck?**  

Green-Red Tron, because it's easy to play and there is little room for mistakes. And also because [Karn Liberated](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karn+Liberated) is one of the best cards in the format.
