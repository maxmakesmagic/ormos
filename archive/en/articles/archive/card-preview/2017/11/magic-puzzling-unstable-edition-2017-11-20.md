Puzzle A6 – Logic Puzzle
========================


Five female Greek Titans are trying to figure out where their missing sister is. Can you figure out when and where they each saw her last?


1. Mnemosyne was alone in her home all day Thursday and Friday.
2. Neither Tethys nor Theia has been to the amphitheater all week.
3. Mnemosyne and Themis saw their missing sister on consecutive days.
4. Rhea saw her missing sister at the temple.
5. The missing sister was spotted in the amphitheater on Wednesday.
6. Themis saw her missing sister on the street, but not on Thursday.
7. Tethys saw her missing sister on Monday, but not at the market.
8. The missing sister was not seen at home or the temple on Tuesday.
9. Theia saw her missing sister on Friday.
