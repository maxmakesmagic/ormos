Wallpaper of the Week: Look at Me, I'm the DCI
==============================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 1, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











This week's wallpaper features a card from an artist who's only illustrated one **Magic** card: Mark Rosewater's [Look at Me, I'm the DCI](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Look+at+Me%2C+I%27m+the+DCI) artwork from *Unglued*. It's like having Mark Rosewater himself drawing on your monitor with crayons! Enjoy. If you can.
