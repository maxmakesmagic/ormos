 Posted in [GRAND PRIX AUCKLAND 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpauc15)

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/walkinshaw.jpg)

What is the best *Fate Reforged* common or uncommon in sealed deck?
===================================================================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/QQ_Wilfy_Horig.jpg)  
**Wilfy Horig:** [Aven Surveyor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Surveyor).




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/QQ_Anthony_Hallett.jpg)  
**Anthony “Rangor” Hallett:** [Sultai Emissary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sultai+Emissary).




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/QQ_Don_Van_Ravenzwaaij.jpg)  
**Don van Ravenswaaij:** [Mistfire Adept](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistfire+Adept).




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/QQ_Hao-Shan_Huang.jpg)  
**Huang Hao-shan:** Common: [Goblin Heelcutter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Heelcutter), uncommon: [Elite Scaleguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elite+Scaleguard).




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/QQ_Nam_Sung_Wook.jpg)  
**Nam Sung Wook:** Common: [Rakshasa's Secret](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rakshasa%27s+Secret), uncommon: [Arc Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arc+Lightning).




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpauc15/QQ_Luke_Mulcahy.jpg)  
**Luke Mulcahy:** [Temur Sabertooth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temur+Sabertooth).
