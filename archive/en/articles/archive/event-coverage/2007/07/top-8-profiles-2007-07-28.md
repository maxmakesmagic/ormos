### Kotaro Otsuka


**Hometown:** Kanagawa  
**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** 12  
**How many Worlds have you played in?**4  
**How did you qualify for 2007 Worlds?** Pro points  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:**GP 4 / PT 0  
**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

2003 Japanese National Champion


**Standard Record:**  4-1  
**What Standard deck did you play and why did you play it?**   

U/B Mannequin because it always won on **Magic Online**


**Booster Draft Record:**  5-1  
**What was your draft strategy?**   

Kithkin. I was taught my strategy by Shota Yasooka


**Legacy Record:**  3-2  
**What Legacy deck did you play and why did you play it?**  

2 Land Belcher, because it's easy to play!


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**  

Practiced on **Magic Online** and with many friends who live in Kobuchi







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//sideboard/images/worlds07/top8_chapin.jpg)

### Patrick Chapin


**Hometown:** Detroit, Michigan USA  
**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** Oodles  
**How many Worlds have you played in?**Several  
**How did you qualify for 2007 Worlds?** Rating  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:**3 PT Top 8s, 1 GP Finals  
**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

Former Vintage champion, two-time state champion, regional champion


**Standard Record:**  4-1  
**What Standard deck did you play and why did you play it?**   

Dragonstorm. It is very strong and I didn't think most people would be prepared for combo.


**Booster Draft Record:**  5-1  
**What was your draft strategy?**   

I didn't try to force anything this time and my drafts worked out much better. I ended up R/G giants and B/G elves, so I am glad that I was willing to be flexible. Thank you Herberholz, Rich Hoaen and Gabe Walls for advice.


**Legacy Record:**  3-1-1  
**What Legacy deck did you play and why did you play it?**  

Four-color threshold with [Counterbalance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterbalance), [Sensei's Divining Top](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top), [Dark Confidant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant+)  and [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize). It is just so consistent and my build is well equipped for the mirror.


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**  

I mostly worked with Mark Herberholz and Gabriel Nassif, as well as Paul Nizolo, Josh Wludyka and DJ Katsner.







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//sideboard/images/worlds07/top8_peleg.jpg)

### Uri Peleg


**Hometown:** Mevasseret, Israel  
**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** 8  
**How many Worlds have you played in?**5  
**How did you qualify for 2007 Worlds?** National Team  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:**0  
**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

31st PT Philadelphia, 37th Worlds 2000


**Standard Record:**  4-1  
**What Standard deck did you play and why did you play it?**   

BGw agro splashing white for Doran the fat Siege Tower. ("He who shelters the wild")


**Booster Draft Record:**  5-1  
**What was your draft strategy?**   

One draft I got lucky, and the other I opened [Profane Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Profane+Command+)  and [Dread](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dread).


**Legacy Record:**  3-1-1  
**What Legacy deck did you play and why did you play it?**  

Landstill designed by Gabriel Nassif, because Antonino De Rosa and Nassif were nice enough to give me a list (thank you!!!)


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**  

Playtested some standard with Eviatar Olpiner, Elisha Amivr and Orr Bildner (from my nats top 8). Also tested Legacy with them but when I got Nassif's list I switched.

### Gabriel Nassif


**Hometown:** Paris, France  
**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** (not listed)  
**How many Worlds have you played in?**6-7  
**How did you qualify for 2007 Worlds?** Level 4  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:**Grand Prix: 3, Pro Tour: 8  
**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:**  

2004 Player of the Year  
  
**Standard Record:**  4-1  
**What Standard deck did you play and why did you play it?**  

Dragonstorm because I thought it was the best deck by far.


**Booster Draft Record:**  5-1  
**What was your draft strategy?**   

Pick the best card in the first pack and go from there. Try to avoid Kithkin.


**Legacy Record:**  3-0-2  
**What Legacy deck did you play and why did you play it?**  

Landstill because I thought it had a good/ok matchup against Gro, Goblins, and Breakfast and an overall solid deck that had made its proofs.


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**  

Going to East Lansing, Michigan to hang out with Herberheezy, go out drinking, learning *Kingpin*  punch lines and mising decklists from random GP side events.
