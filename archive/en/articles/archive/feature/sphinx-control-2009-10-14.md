*Zendikar* has brought new strategies to Standard for everyone, and that includes control players! Gavin Verhey wrote about his favorite control strategy for the new format on StarCityGames.com. The deck features card draw in [Courier's Capsule](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courier%27s+Capsule), control in [Day of Judgment](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Day+of+Judgment), and a powerful win condition that doubles as more card draw in [Sphinx of Lost Truths](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx+of+Lost+Truths).
