Joel Calafell already had some Grand Prix and Pro Tour successes to his name when he took on the largest tournament field of all time at Grand Prix-Barcelona. After two length days of play, he emerged victorious at the hands of his Cascade Swans deck! The innovative deck plays spells with cascade, but only one spell they can cascade into: [Seismic Assault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seismic+Assault)! When they're not getting the enchantment, they hit [Swans of Bryn Argoll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swans+of+Bryn+Argoll), which is the perfect combination of cards for Joel to use his 40+ lands in order to burn his opponent to death. To read more about Grand Prix-Barcelona, [visit the official event coverage page](/en/events/coverage/calafell-cascades-victory).
