Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Mardu Planeswalkers](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2016-11-09)

I'm just fathoming a guess here, but could *Magic Online* player TheShaheenSoorani maybe be control master Shaheen Soorani? Maybe?


If so, Soorani has cooked up another brew here that should get your control blood boiling. This planeswalker-centric Mardu deck packs a ton of punch, plus Soorani pet-card [Goblin Dark-Dwellers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Dark-Dwellers). He's been advocating a black-red-based approach to control since *Kaladesh* was released, and this looks like his latest iteration (oh, and it went 5-0 in a Competitive Standard League). The deck features five different planeswalkers up and down the curve, plus removal, card draw, and powerful creatures.


This might just be the Sooraniest deck I've ever seen that didn't contain blue cards. I can imagine this being a blast to play at FNM, just piling up the dice on your makeshift Mardu Gatewatch and cackling as they tick ever higher.
