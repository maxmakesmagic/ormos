80,000 Words
============



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on December 25, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_markrosewater.jpg)
By Mark Rosewater




 Working in R&D since '95, Mark became Magic head designer in '03. His hobbies: spending time with family, writing about Magic in all mediums, and creating short bios. 







*My second favorite column of the year was probably the fan favorite of the year. And ironically enough, it was the one with the fewest words.*


*If you haven't had the chance to "read" "80,000 Words", I'm excited to present it again. If you've already had a chance to see it, I suggest taking some time looking around in the backgrounds of some of the pictures as there was a lot of stuff there that some people missed the first time around.*


*This article originally appeared on August 7, 2006*
