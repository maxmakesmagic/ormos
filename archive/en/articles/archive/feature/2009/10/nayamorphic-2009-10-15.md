Aggressive decks are often a great strategy for a new Standard format as the rest of the world tries to figure out what to do with all the new cards from the latest set. Alexander Shearer of ChannelFireball.com wasted no time in proposing this Zoo-like list featuring [Steppe Lynx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steppe+Lynx), [Burst Lightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning), and powered by the new fetch lands from *Zendikar*, in this case [Arid Mesa](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arid+Mesa). It's just the type of strategy to give control players nightmares!
