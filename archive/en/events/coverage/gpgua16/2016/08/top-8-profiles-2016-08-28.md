Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX GUANGZHOU 2016](/en/events/coverage/gpgua16)
 on August 28, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











After many great games of *Magic*, the Top 8 of Grand Prix Guangzhou is set! Learn more about Guangzhou's Top 8 competitors, including their previous accomplishments, below.




---


### Jia Hao


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpgua16/Top-8-Jia.jpg)


**Age:** 32  
**Occupation:** Businessman  
**Hometown:** Beijing, China


**Previous Accomplishments:** Two Super Sunday Series Top 8s


**Score on Day 1:** 9-0 (2 byes)  
**Score on Day 2:** 4-2


**What deck are you playing and why?**  

Naya Burn without [Wild Nacatl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Nacatl), because I am the most familiar with this deck.


**What is the most interesting deck you saw all weekend?**  

None. Burn is the best!




---


### Ryoichi Tamada


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpgua16/Top-8-Tamada.jpg)


**Age:** 31  
**Occupation:** Shop Owner  
**Hometown:** Osaka, Japan


**Previous Accomplishments:** Pro Tour Battle for Zendikar finalist and 3 Grand Prix Top 8s


**Score on Day 1:** 9-0 (3 byes)  
**Score on Day 2:** 4-2


**What deck are you playing and why?**  

Jeskai [Pyromancer Ascension](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer+Ascension). I wanted to play with [Lightning Helix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Helix) and that's why I splashed white.


**What is the most interesting deck you saw all weekend?**  

In Round 13, my opponent was playing a White-Blue Spirits “flash” deck with [Spell Queller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Queller) and [Restoration Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Restoration+Angel).




---


### Wu Kon Fai


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpgua16/Top-8-Wu.jpg)


**Age:** 27  
**Occupation:** Bank Employee  
**Hometown:** Hong Kong


**Previous Accomplishments:** World Magic Cup 2012 participant


**Score on Day 1:** 8-1 (2 byes)  
**Score on Day 2:** 5-1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  

Naya Burn (with [Wild Nacatl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Nacatl)). It is good in the metagame now and doesn't have a lot of bad matchups.


**What is the most interesting deck you saw all weekend?**  

The Sliver deck running around in Day 2 was pretty fun!




---


### Lim Zhong Yi


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpgua16/Top-8-Lim.jpg)


**Age:** 29  
**Occupation:** Information Technology  
**Hometown:** Singapore


**Previous Accomplishments:** Singapore Nationals Top 4


**Score on Day 1:** 9-0 (2 byes)  
**Score on Day 2:** 3-2-1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  
[Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) Aggro. I like the deck and it has [Steppe Lynx](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steppe+Lynx) in it.


**What is the most interesting deck you saw all weekend?**  

Nothing much. Most of it were as I had expected.




---


### Kentaro Yamamoto


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpgua16/Top-8-Yamamoto.jpg)


**Age:** 31  
**Occupation:** Team Cygames pro player  
**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan


**Previous Accomplishments:** Three Pro Tour Top 8s


**Score on Day 1:** 8-1 (3 byes)  
**Score on Day 2:** 4-1-1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  
[Goryo's Vengeance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goryo%27s+Vengeance). I really like [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Aeons+Torn)!

### Keita Kawasaki


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpgua16/Top-8-Kawasaki.jpg)


**Age:** 30  
**Occupation:** Office Worker  
**Hometown:** Osaka, Japan


**Previous Accomplishments:** Top 4 at Grand Prix Singapore 2015


**Score on Day 1:** 8-1 (2 byes)  
**Score on Day 2:** 4-1-1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  

Jund with both [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) and [Grim Flayer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Flayer). I always play Jund!

### Kelvin Chew


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpgua16/Top-8-Chew.jpg)


**Age:** 30  
**Occupation:** Games Haven Manager & MTG Mint Card pro player  
**Hometown:** Singapore


**Previous Accomplishments:** Pro Tour Return to Ravnica Top 8 and Grand Prix Sydney 2015 Top 4


**Score on Day 1:** 8-1 (2 byes)  
**Score on Day 2:** 4-1-1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  

Bant Knightfall, because [Spell Queller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Queller) is broken in Modern.


**What is the most interesting deck you saw all weekend?**  

The Open the Vault deck was most interesting by far.

### Albertus Law


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpgua16/Top-8-Law.jpg)


**Age:** 35  
**Occupation:** Self-Employed  
**Hometown:** Singapore


**Previous Accomplishments:** Pro Tour Barcelona 2001 Top 8 and Grand Prix Singapore 2001 Champion


**Score on Day 1:** 8-1 (2 byes)  
**Score on Day 2:** 4-1-1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  

Grixis Delver. It's consistent and fairly aggressive.

Jason Chung's and Huang Hao-Shan's Green-White Tron.
