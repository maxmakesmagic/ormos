New T-Shirts!
=============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 11, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











There are two new T-shirts at [mtgmerch.com](http://www.mtgmerch.com/)! Each one is $20 (in US dollars) and combines an iconic Planeswalker with a mana symbol. If that description isn't clear enough, perhaps these pictures will make things clearer!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1166_jace.jpg)  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1166_liliana.jpg)  
The two new shirts join the guild shirts and hats as the official **Magic: The Gathering** merchandise at [mtgmerch.com](http://www.mtgmerch.com/).
