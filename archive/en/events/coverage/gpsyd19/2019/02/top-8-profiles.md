Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 3, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Walkinshaw-Profile.jpg)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw











After fifteen rounds of Limited, only eight competitors could continue on. Meet your Grand Prix Sydney Top 8 players!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2019/events/gpsyd19/T8_Kakumae.jpg)


**Name:** Teruya Kakumae


**Age:** 32.


**Occupation:** Pro Player.


**Where do you live:** Osaka, Japan.


**How long have you been playing *Magic*?** 7 years.


**Best finish at a *Magic* event:** GP Champion.


**What was the best card in your Day 1 Deck, what color combination did you play, and what was**


**your record?** Hydoid Krasis, Simic, 8-1.


**What was the best card in your first Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?** [High Alert](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Alert), Azorius, 2-1.


**What was the best card in your second Booster Draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?** [Sharktocrab](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sharktocrab), Simic, 3-0.


**What is your preferred draft strategy for this set?** Aggro!
