Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 18, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds11/top8_woods.jpg)



### Conley Woods



**Age:** 24  




**How did you qualify for Worlds?**  Level 6 benefits


**Number of Grand Prix/Pro Tour Top 8s:**   



**Other previous Magic Accomplishments:**   

Pro Tour Honolulu 2009 Top 8


**What is your record by format?**  
**Standard:** 6-0  
**Booster Draft:** 6-0  
**Modern:** 4-2\*


**How did you prepare for Worlds? Who did you prepare with?**  

We came out to San Francisco two weeks early to test with team ChannelFireball. LSV, PV, Wrapter, Web, Owen Turtenwald, Brian Kibler, Shuhei Nakamura, Martin Juza, Ben Stark, Brad Nelson, Eric Froelich, Lucas Blohon.


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**   

20-30.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

Tempered Steel. It attacked the metagame from a good position.


  

 


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds11/top8_darosa.jpg)

### Paulo Vito Damo Da Rosa


**Age:** 24  
**Hometown:**Brazil  
**Occupation:** Student/Player


**How did you qualify for Worlds?**  PT Points


**Number of Grand Prix/Pro Tour Top 8s:**   

PT: 8  

GP: 10?


**Other previous Magic Accomplishments:**   

Top 64 my first eight Pro Tours! Also 4 Worlds Top 8s in eight tries.


**What is your record by format?**  
**Standard:** 4-2  
**Booster Draft:** 6-0  
**Modern:** 5-1


**How did you prepare for Worlds? Who did you prepare with?**  

Went to Oakland for a week to play with the ChannelFireball people.


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**   

Maybe two…I play only when I do videos.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

Tempered Steel, we thought it had a good match against other aggro decks and figured it'd be relatively unexpected.


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic, or if you don't go to FNM anymore, where do you play?**  

I don't go to FNM anymore, I play online and before tournaments when we meet.



  

 


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds11/top8_scottvargas.jpg)

### Luis Scott-Vargas


**Age:** 28  
**Hometown:** Oakland  
**Occupation:** Editor, Channelfireball.com


**How did you qualify for Worlds?**  Pro Club


**Number of Grand Prix/Pro Tour Top 8s:**   

PT: 5  

GP: 7


**Other previous Magic Accomplishments:**   

Went 16-0 at PT San Diego, which Conley has no chance of beating.


**What is your record by format?**  
**Standard:** 5-1  
**Booster Draft:** 5-1  
**Modern:** 4-2


**How did you prepare for Worlds? Who did you prepare with?**  

Tested with Team Fireball by borrowing my dad's house in Oakland. Thanks, papa! The team included Conley, PV, Josh, Shuhei, Kibler, Owen, Ben S, Martin Juza, Lukas Blohon, Brad, Web, and Eric Froehlich.


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**   

Lately it's been closer to five than anything else, but often none when sets release.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

Tempered Steel, because its good against everything but Red-Green.


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic, or if you don't go to FNM anymore, where do you play?**  

Superstars in San Jose!
