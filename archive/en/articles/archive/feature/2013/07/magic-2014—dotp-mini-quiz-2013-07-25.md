Magic 2014—DotP Mini Quiz
=========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 25, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






Today's quiz is all about ***Magic** 2014—Duels of the Planeswalkers*—you don't have to have played it to be able to answer these questions about it, but... playing it will obviously help. There is also a prize at the end, but it's only useful to those who have already bought the game.


Q**1.** What was the new platform introduced with this year's release of *Duels of the Planeswalkers*?


* [iPad](#)  - WRONG - We introduced the iPad with last year's *Duels of the Planeswalkers*.
* [XBox Live Arcade](#)  - WRONG - This has been one of the platforms since we originally launched the game.
* [Playstation](#)  - WRONG - This has been one of the platforms since we originally launched the game.
* [Android](#)  - RIGHT - Here you go! This year we launched Android as the new platform for the game, for sale on both Google Play and Amazon App Market.
* [Steam](#)  - WRONG - This has been one of the platforms since we originally launched the game.

Q**2.** Which of the following is the new play mode introduce this year?


* [Archenemy](#)  - WRONG - *Archenemy* was our game mode in *Duels of the Planeswalkers* 2012.
* [Sealed](#)  - RIGHT - That's right! This year's game allows you to crack packs and build your very own deck from a sealed pool.
* [Planechase](#)  - WRONG - We introduced Planechase in *Duels of the Planeswalkers* 2013.

Q**3.** Which of the following cards is in ***Magic** 2014—Duels of the Planeswalkers*?


* [Archangel of Thune](#)  - RIGHT - As one of the new mythic rares in ***Magic** 2014* this card makes an appearance in ***Magic** 2014—Duels of the Planeswalkers*.
* [Worldfire](#)  - WRONG - Worldfire appeared in last year's *Duels of the Planeswalker* game.

Did you answer all three questions?
