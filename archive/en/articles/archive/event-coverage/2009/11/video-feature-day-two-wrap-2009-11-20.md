Video Feature: Day Two Wrap-up
==============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 20, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_84.png)
By Randy Buehler and Brian David-Marshall











Twelve individual rounds are finished here at Worlds 2009, and two players sit atop the standings. Hall of Famer Randy Buehler and Pro Tour Historian Brian David-Marshall run down the results from Friday's six rounds of Zendikar Booster Draft, the Player of the Year and Rookie of the Year standings, and set the stage for the final two rounds of Team competition and six rounds of Extended happening Saturday morning.
