Brian Robinson may not have been the most famous American to Top 8 Pro Tour-Kyoto, but his deck was certainly one of the most interesting. He played a Doran/Rafiq aggro deck that was capable of some *blisteringly* fast starts. During one game in the Top 8 he attacked for a whopping 16 damage on the third turn! Make sure to [check out all the coverage from the event](/en/events/coverage/nassif-greater-power) including decklists, videos, and more.
