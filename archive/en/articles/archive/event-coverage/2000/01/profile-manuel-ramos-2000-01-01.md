Profile: Manuel Ramos
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_562.png)
By by Sideboard Staff











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPVAL01/858.jpg)**Name:** Manuel José Ramos Das Faustiso 
  
**Age:** 24 
  
**Location:** Almada 
  
**Occupation:** Informatic/Guitar player 
  
**Played Magic since:** 94 
  
**Best accomplishments:** 22nd GP Porto 2000 




**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Valencia?**  

A: Win the slot to PT Barcelona




**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Make top 8 and win the slot




**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: I made solid decks/sealed brought me a lot of good cards that help me a lot (Urza's Rage rules).




**Q: What do you think about the Invasion Block Limited metagame, and why did you choose to play the deck you did?**  

A: I think there is ga ood metagame, we can do lots of powerful combinations and decks.




**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Playing wisely and careful, and taking the right decisions at the right moment.




**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: It's a valuable goal, I'm the fifth Portuguese player making top 8 at a GP.




**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: To win and have lots of fun.




**Q: Anything else you'd like to share with us?**  

A: yeah. Peace and love to all of you around the world. Hug people!
