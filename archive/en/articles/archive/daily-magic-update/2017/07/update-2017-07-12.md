Deck of the Day/Community Spotlight
===================================


1. [Green-White Energy Cats](http://www.mtgcanada.com/standard/standard-deck-tech-green-white-energy-cats-part-2/) | MTG Canada | Joey Hoffer

We've got two reasons for picking today's deck.


First, it will make my wife happy. Her only *Magic* deck is a Cat deck, and it's really the only one she's interested in. This is a version that might actually be competitive in Standard.


Second, it shines a bit of a light on MTG Canada, which is not an outlet we've talked about much before. Sorry! [MTG Canada](http://www.mtgcanada.com/) has a super slick-looking website, daily content, and, most importantly, articles about Cat decks.


So the deck itself—it has Cats. But the good ones. [Longtusk Cub](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Longtusk+Cub) has already proven itself in Standard, [Regal Caracal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Regal+Caracal) does a strong [Cloudgoat Ranger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudgoat+Ranger) impression, [Pride Sovereign](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pride+Sovereign) is the new kitty on the block, and [Adorned Pouncer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Adorned+Pouncer) is adorn-able, plus just begging to be pumped with [Rhonas the Indomitable](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhonas+the+Indomitable), [Gideon, Ally of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Ally+of+Zendikar), and [Prepare // Fight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prepare+%2F%2F+Fight).


By the way, [Prepare // Fight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prepare+%2F%2F+Fight) is an amazing addition to the deck, not just because it's a strong green-white spell, but because it's perfectly on theme for Cats. Particularly when they get on the counter and don't appreciate being pushed off. Like right now—Pepper!
