TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 4, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/n2KoE-g2_bigger.jpeg)
By Mike Rosenberg




 Mike Rosenberg is a writer and gamer and has been part of the Magic text coverage team since 2011. He joined Wizards as organized play’s content specialist in June 2014. 







Learn more about Pro Tour *Aether Revolt*'s Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they’ve done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!


 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptaer/t8_carvalho.jpg)Name:** Marcio Carvalho


**Age:** 31


**Team:** DEX Army


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 2 Pro Tour Top 8s. 13 Grand Prix Top 8s, with 3 wins. Finals of the 2016 World Championship.


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Standard Record:** 9-0-1


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Blue-Red Improvise and Black-Red Aggro. I preferred the Black-Red deck.


**What's the key to drafting *Aether Revolt*/*Kaladesh*?**


Curve them out. Don't draft white.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Mardu Vehicles! I was going to play Black-red, but Thiago Saporito told me that Mardu was awesome. I played some games and I was sold!


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?**


[Release the Gremlins](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Release+the+Gremlins)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


A dream come true!

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptaer/t8_smith.jpg)Name:** Donald Smith


**Age:** 21


**Hometown:** Cut Off, Louisiana


**Team:** [Lingering Souls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lingering+Souls)


**Twitter Handle:** @donaldwsjr


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Top 25 Pro Tour *Battle for Zendikar*, Top 25 *Pro Tour Kaladesh*, Silver pro.


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Standard Record:** 8-0-2


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Green-blue with [Baral's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baral%27s+Expertise), then black-red with two [Weldfast Engineer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Weldfast+Engineer), my favorite.


**What's the key to drafting *Aether Revolt*/*Kaladesh*?**


Read signals carefully in Pack 1 to get a payoff in *Kaladesh*.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Mardu Vehicles. Amazing versus [Saheeli Rai](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Saheeli+Rai) and its "bad" matchup (Black-Green) is close.


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?**


[Depala, Pilot Exemplar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Depala%2C+Pilot+Exemplar).


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


I would be able to start a career playing *Magic*—my dream.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptaer/t8_ksandr.jpg)Name:** Jan Ksandr


**Age:** 24


**Hometown:** Prague, Czech Republic


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Finals of Grand Prix Sydney.


**Limited Record:** 5-1


**Standard Record**: 7-2-1


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


First draft, I played a four-color deck, green-blue splashing white and black. In the first two packs I took four [Unbridled Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unbridled+Growth). Then I got two [Torrential Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk) in the third pack. Second draft was Blue-Black Improvise.


**What's the key to drafting *Aether Revolt*/*Kaladesh*?**


Stay open, focus on archetypes.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Black-Green. My teammates Niels Molle and Kayure Patel built the deck and told me to play it.


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?**


[Scrapheap Scrounger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrapheap+Scrounger).


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


It would mean a lot to me. I think I would focus on *Magic* even more, travel around the world, doing what I really like.

**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


(Everything.) There is not enough space here to say it all.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptaer/t8_sagalik2.jpg)Name:** Eduardo Sajgalik


**Age:** 29


**Hometown:** Montreal, Canada


**Team:** MTG Mint Card


**Twitter Handle:** @Walaoumpa


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8 at Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica* (2012). Four Grand Prix Top 8, four National Team appearances, including twice as captain.


**Limited Record:** 5-1


**Standard Record:** 7-2-1


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Black-Red Midrange—Super solid, removal and aggressive creatures.


Blue-Green Anything Energy—Combine bad cards and hope. ([Shipwreck Moray](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shipwreck+Moray) and Larger than Life)


**What's the key to drafting *Aether Revolt*/*Kaladesh*?**


Stay open, pick the late archetype cards. Also, Moray and Larger than Life.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Mardu Vehicles—The team believe the deck was good. I went with it.


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?**


[Unlicensed Disintegration](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unlicensed+Disintegration)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


No idea. It sounds insane to me! I'm just very happy at the moment.

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/ptaer/t8_damo.jpg)Name:** Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa


**Age:** 29


**Hometown:** Porto Alegre, Brazil


**Team:** Channel [Fireball](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) Ice

Second most Pro Tour Top 8s of all time.


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Standard Record:** 8-2


**What archetypes did you draft? Which was your favorite?**


Green-blue, red-white. I liked green-blue more.


**What's the key to drafting *Aether Revolt*/*Kaladesh*?**


I think all the colors are good, but I like to take enablers before payoff.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**


Mardu Vehicles, the only strategy that doesn't need to sacrifice much to beat Jeskai Copy Cat.


**What card was your MVP for the weekend?**


[Toolcraft Exemplar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Toolcraft+Exemplar), it's so much better than any other one-drop.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


I'd be able to have a wedding party!
