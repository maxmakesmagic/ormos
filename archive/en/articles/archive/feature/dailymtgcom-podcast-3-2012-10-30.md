This is the biweekly audio podcast for DailyMTG.com.


[**Episode 3 - "Mystical Web Producer"**](http://media.wizards.com/podcasts/magic/DailyMTGEp3MysticalWebProducer.mp3): *(55:21)* In this episode Trick, Tifa, and Mike talk with Web Producer (and Arcana author) [Monty Ashley](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/archive.aspx?author=monty%20ashley). They talk about *Return to Ravnica* Game Day, the **Magic Online** Community Cup, and Halloween! (13 MB)


[**Episode 2 - "Uh oh, Rhino!"**](http://media.wizards.com/podcasts/magic/DailyMTGEp2Rhinos.mp3): *(55:21)* In this episode Trick, Tifa, and Mike talk with R&D Designer & DailyMTG Columnist [Gavin Verhey](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/archive.aspx?author=gavin%20verhey). They talk about *Return to Ravnica*, the upcoming Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica*, and go over their [Fantasy Pro Tour](https://apps.facebook.com/magicfantasyprotour/) rosters for the event! (25 MB)


[**Episode 1**](http://media.wizards.com/podcasts/magic/DailyMTGEp1.mp3): Trick, Tifa, and Mike gather for the first time to launch a new podcast for DailyMTG.com. They introduce themselves, then dive into *Return to Ravnica*, **Magic**, stories from their Prerelease, and looking at how *Return to Ravnica* might change their beloved formats and decks. (20.8 MB)

### The Guest:




![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/activity/da1092_monty.jpg)

Monty Ashley is a **Magic** Web Producer, which means he's one of the people who brings this website to you. In addition to various behind-the-scenes tasks, Monty writes the daily [**Magic** Arcana](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Archive.aspx?tag=arcana&description=Arcana) and [Card of the Day](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/cardoftheday/1012) columns. Outside of Wizards of the Coast, Monty does television recaps (for *Game of Thrones*, *Elementary*, and *Arrow*, among others) for [Television Without Pity](http://www.televisionwithoutpity.com). Follow him on Twitter: [@monty\_ashley](https://twitter.com/monty_ashley).




### The Hosts:




![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/activity/da1092_trick.jpg)

Patrick "Trick" Jarrett is the Editor-in-Chief of DailyMTG.com. Born in Atlanta, GA, he moved his wife and their two dogs to Seattle to work for Wizards of the Coast. He's a big fan of the Commander format and loves his [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiki-Jiki%2C+Mirror+Breaker) mono-red Commander deck. Follow him on Twitter: [@TrickMTG](https://twitter.com/TrickMTG).






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/activity/da1072_tifa.jpg)

Jennifer "Tifa" Meyen is the Assistant Brand Manager for **Magic: The Gathering** as well as the founder and organizer for [The Lady Planeswalker Society](http://www.facebook.com/pages/Lady-Planeswalkers-Society/206021052816060), a welcoming and friendly environment for women **Magic** players of all skill levels. Jennifer has been an avid gamer since birth, and **Magic** became her life source during ***Magic** 2011*. Follow her on Twitter: [@TifaMeyen](https://twitter.com/TifaMeyen) and [@MTGLadySociety](https://twitter.com/MTGLadySociety).






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/activity/da1072_michael.jpg)

Michael "Mike" Robles is the Community Manager for **Magic: The Gathering** and creates content for the [official **Magic: The Gathering** Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/MagicTheGathering) as well as the [official Twitter account](https://twitter.com/wizards_magic)! If you see him at conventions he's probably got plenty of free stuff to give you. He is fluent in Facebook, Twitter, and Internet. You can follow Mike and his escapades on Twitter: [@michaelrobles](https://twitter.com/michaelrobles).
