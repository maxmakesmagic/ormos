Magic Online Championship Series - 2009 Season 6 Championship Coverage
======================================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 21, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The [Magic Online Championship Series](/en/articles/archive/feature/magic-online-championship-series-2009-05-07) awards invites to the [Magic World Championships](/en/node/642286) as well as a cash tournament for players who qualify through the series. The 2009 Season 6 Championship was held on September 20. The format was *Masters Edition III* sealed deck with a Top 8 *Masters Edition III* draft. Click below to launch the draft viewer to see how they drafted.

[### Launch the Draft Viewer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/magic/draftools/draftviewer.asp?draftid=mocs2009s6)
