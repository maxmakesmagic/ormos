Round 3: Phil Freneau vs. Ben Rubin
===================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 27, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett











Both these players are riding high at 2-0. Phil Freneau was crowned Grand Prix – Los Angeles Champion at the beginning of the year, and is looking for a big follow up. Everyone knows that Ben Rubin is great, but he now that the Masters is gone, it's unclear where he'll be putting up his top finishes. Hopefully since Nationals isn't a Pro Tour, he'll be able to pour on the pressure.

### Game 1

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a918.jpg)Rubin mulled his first hand, and seemed unimpressed with his second. He laid a [Krosan Verge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Verge) and passed it back. Freneau [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)ed him, and stole [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic), leaving him with only lands in hand. Freneau got in with [Withered Wretch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Withered+Wretch), but had nothing to speed up the beats. Rubin popped his Verge and continued to play lands.

Freneau didn't manage any other pressure until four turns later, a [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade). Rubin had meanwhile drawn into the [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God) to clear the table. Freneau regrew his Wretch at end of turn and put it back into play. The net turn he tried a [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt), but Rubin had [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) in hand.

Soon Rubin was at five. He made two bears with [Grizzly Fate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grizzly+Fate). Freneau tried to [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) both of them, but the second [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) was countered. He had the third [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother), and Rubin fell to three. He also had [Graveborn Muse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Graveborn+Muse). Rubin bought a turn with [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace). Freneau did not forget to remove it, and they were on to Game 2.

"Action packed, that game." – Ben Rubin

Freneau 1 – Rubin 0

### Game 2

Rubin had the Verge to start the second game, and followed up with a pair of islands. Freneau's [Hollow Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hollow+Specter) on turn three had him making a disgusted face. 

"That's good beats. It resolves." – Ben Rubin

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a919.jpg)He Verged, then untapped and dropped the Wake, five cards still in his hand. Freneau played a Grotto, and peeked at four cards with his Specter. They were [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse) and three lands. He took the Lapse. Rubin untapped and played a pair of [Merfolk Looter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter)s, removing all mystery. Freneau stole a forest and played [Graveborn Muse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Graveborn+Muse).

Rubin took a big turn. [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and his two Looters gave him options. He then [Wrath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath)ed the board and played another Looter. Freneau came back with another Muse. Rubin dropped [Kirtar's Wrath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kirtar%27s+Wrath) while at threshold, then flashed back his Analysis. Freneau tried to get a Muse on the board, but Rubin had [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse) and then [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell), all the while beating down with his spirit tokens.

Finally, Freneau got a Muse to stick. Rubin hit again and he fell to seven. Freneau hit back, and added Rotlung and [Hollow Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hollow+Specter) to the board. Rubin outclassed him with a second Wake, forcing the Specter to chump. Freneau hit back, and turned over [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt) with a shrug. Rubin shrugged back, and extended the hand.

**Final Result:** Phil Freneau defeats Ben Rubin 2-0
