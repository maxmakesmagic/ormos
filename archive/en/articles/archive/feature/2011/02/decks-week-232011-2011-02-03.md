Decks of the Week: 2/3/2011
===========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 3, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











It's the calm before the new Standard storm hits the **Magic** tournament world. *Mirrodin Besieged* becomes legal to play on Friday and there will be a ton of major tournament action immediately showcasing the new set. The Star City Games Open Series hits Indianapolis this weekend for a taste of the new Standard environment, and then the top players from around the world gather for [Pro Tour Paris](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/event/magicweekend/paris11) February 10-13. Standard will be on full display in Paris, including the [2010 Player of the Year Showdown](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/news/012111) between Brad Nelson and Guillaume Matignon. All of this Standard excitement will come to **Magic Online** on February 21 when *Mirrodin Besieged* hits the digital shelves.
