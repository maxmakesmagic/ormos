### DAILY DECK


Titans in Modern
================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)

Adam Prosak

From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer.


August 29, 2014
 










[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Primeval Titan)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval Titan)
[Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle) was once banned from Modern, largely due to its nature as a one-card combo kill with [Scapeshift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scapeshift). Simply playing seven lands and then Scapeshifting will deal 18 damage if you have six Mountains and a Valakut in your library. Since its unbanning, [Scapeshift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scapeshift) has existed largely on the fringe of the Modern format, capable of winning tournaments but rarely the most popular deck.


Shogo Yamasaki took advantage of this, unleashing the power of the combination while avoiding the hate cards that were targeted at other archetypes. Shogo's deck featured many different ways to harness the power of [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle) in addition to the classic [Scapeshift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scapeshift). [Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan) will allow you to quickly assemble enough Mountains to deal lethal damage, much like it did when the pair were Standard legal. [Prismatic Omen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Omen) also works wonders with Valakut, making the land a Mountain itself! Usually, you have to wait until your seventh land at minimum to start dealing damage, but [Prismatic Omen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Omen) makes it so that your sixth land can deal 3 damage. [Prismatic Omen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Omen) allows your Misty Rainforests and Scalding Tarns to trigger twice.


Congratulation to Shogo Yamasaki for winning an invitation to Pro Tour *Khans of Tarkir* at [Legends Warehouse in Toronto, Canada](http://www.legendswarehouse.ca/)!
