**Throwback to *Combo Winter* Gauntlet starts tomorrow, May 17**


Randy Buehler's [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/throwback-standard-gauntlet-2-combo-winter-urza-block-2017-05-04) last week introduced the details surrounding Throwback Standard Gauntlet 2: *Combo Winter*. With decks created as brutal weapons and promising no apologies, this event is sure to be an exciting venture for any *Magic Online* fan.


Find this event in the Limited Leagues area of the *Magic Online* play lobby and start playing May 17th at 10:00 a.m. (PT).

**Team Draft Super League begins tonight!**


Team Draft Super League is a live streamed [event](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/schedule-magic-online-products-game-info) every Tuesday night, beginning tonight at 5:00 p.m. (PT). Randy Buehler and Matt Sperling explain the format, upcoming teams, and reveal round one's schedule in this convenient [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5YDr6B1v6v4).


You can watch Team Draft Super League every Tuesday night on our [Twitch](https://www.twitch.tv/magic) channel!

**New *Magic Online* Format Challenges begin Saturday, May 20**


Last week Lee Sharpe [announced](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-format-challenges-2017-05-04) the Format Challenges taking place on *Magic Online* starting May 20th. With a new prize structure and a recurring weekend [schedule](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-format-challenges), formats Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper are even more enticing to play!


Events begin this Saturday, May 20th at 8:00 a.m. (PT).


  
[Top](#top)

**Upcoming Standard MOCS Playoffon Saturday, May 20**


The second quarter of competition in the *Magic Online*Championship Series (MOCS) culminates on May 20th for the second [Playoff Event](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series#PlayoffEvents) of the series! Big prizes, the chance to move up the [MOCS Leaderboard](http://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/leaderboard), plus invitations to the *Magic Online*Championship and *Pro Tour Hour of Devastation* are all on the line in this event.


Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online*Championship Series and how to qualify for future Playoff Events [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

**Saturday Noon Sealed PTQ Preliminaries moved to 1 p.m. starting Saturday, May 20**


Attention: the Sealed PTQ Preliminary held each Saturday, have been permanently rescheduled from noon to 1:00 p.m. (PT).


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#HOU_schedule).


[Top](#top)

**Upcoming Sealed PTQ for Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation* on Sunday, May 21**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! The weekly suite of Preliminaries on Thursday through Tuesday, May 18 through May 22, will take place at their normal times. We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, May 21!


Remember, you are no longer required to enter into the PTQ Finals immediately following the Preliminary that you qualified through, thanks to the redesigned PTQ system that *Magic Online*Events Manager Lee Sharpe announced [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/pro-tour-qualifier-changes-2015-11-16).


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#HOU_schedule).

***Khans of Tarkir*, *Fate Reforged*, *Kaladesh*, and *Aether Revolt* Redemption ending soon**


Take advantage of *Magic Online*'s Redemption feature and trade in your complete *Khans of Takir*/*Fate Reforged*card sets before 9:00 a.m. (PT) on May 31st and *Aether Revolt*/*Kaladesh*card sets before 9:00 a.m. (PT) on June 7th.


Redemptions purchased after downtime on May 24th will be processed on Wednesday, June 14th. 


Learn more about *Magic Online*Redemption [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/gameinfo/products/magiconline/redemption).
