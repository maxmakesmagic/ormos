The first major live tournament utilizing *Zendikar* has come and gone! The Philadelphia event, hosted by Starcitygames.com, saw hundreds of players show up to compete with the brand-new set. It was a new twist on an established favorite, Jund, that took Jack Wang to the top spot. He had *Zendikar* hits like [Goblin Ruinblaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ruinblaster) and [Verdant Catacombs](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verdant+Catacombs) to shore up a traditional black-green-red build.
