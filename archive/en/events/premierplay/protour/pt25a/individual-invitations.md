Individual Invitations
----------------------


#### [Pro Tour 25th Anniversary Event Information](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/premierplay/protour/pt25a)


Individual invitations to Pro Tour 25th Anniversary are awarded in the following manner:​


* Players that finished with 33 or more match points at Pro Tour *Dominaria*.
* Players that have advanced to Pro Tour Players Club Silver level after the start of a Professional Points Season are invited to the next Pro Tour (following their promotion) of that Professional Points Season. In the event that a player already has an invitation to that Pro Tour, or chooses not to attend that Pro Tour, the invitation will be deferred to the next Pro Tour of that Professional Season, and so on. Invitations will not be deferred beyond the end of the Professional Season in which the player earned the invitation in this manner. Silver level members do not receive their invitations until all qualifying tournaments for Pro Tour 25th Anniversary have concluded.
* Players that start a Professional Points season at Pro Tour Players Club Silver level are invited to the first Pro Tour of that season. In the event that a player already has an invitation to that Pro Tour, or chooses not to attend that Pro Tour, the invitation will be deferred to the next Pro Tour of that Professional Season, and so on. Invitations will not be deferred beyond the end of the Professional Season in which the player earned the invitation in this manner. Silver level members do not receive their invitations until all qualifying tournaments for Pro Tour *Rivals of Ixalan* have concluded.
* Players with a Pro Tour Players Club Gold or Platinum level.
* Players that are members of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame.

Note: The Pro Tour invitation criteria listed above do not generate a pass down of invitations to other players.


All individual invitations for Pro Tour 25th Anniversary do *not*guarantee the ability for a player to compete at Pro Tour 25th Anniversary. Players that qualify as individuals must form teams of three with other players on the Individual Invitations list and submit to [premierplay@wizards.com](mailto:premierplay@wizards.com) their team of three players that they will be forming a team with no later than **July 5, 2018** (no exceptions). Players on a submitted team must be included on the email and must confirm via email that they agree to play with as a team with the players on the submitted roster.


If you believe that your current listed standing in the Pro Club is in error, please contact [premierplay@wizards.com](mailto:premierplay@wizards.com) as soon as possible.


The full list of individual invitations for Pro Tour 25th Anniversary will be posted on June 4, 2018 and will be updated every Monday until July 30, 2018.
