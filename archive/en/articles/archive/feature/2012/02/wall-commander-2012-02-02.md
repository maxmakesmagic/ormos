Wall Commander
==============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 2, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorPic_GavinVerhey.jpg)
By Gavin Verhey




 When Gavin Verhey was eleven, he dreamt of a job making Magic cards—and now as a Magic designer, he's living his dream! Gavin has been writing about Magic since 2005. 






There are 224 different creature types in **Magic**, but last week TCGPlayer.com columnist Cassidy Silver chose a rather unusual one to build a Commander deck around: Walls! This unique Commander deck plans to lay down [Doran, the Siege Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower) and then turn all of its creatures with defender—mostly walls—into attacking threats thanks to [Rolling Stones](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rolling+Stones), [Wakestone Gargoyle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wakestone+Gargoyle), and [Warmonger's Chariot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warmonger%27s+Chariot). Themed Commander decks are always a lot of fun to play, and this one seems to be no exception! Which unusual creature type could you try and build a Commander deck around? Try building one and see what happens!
