2017 World Championship
-----------------------


Jensen’s Hometown Victory
=========================




William Jensen breaks the curse of his Peach Garden Oath teammates, winning $100,000 and the title of 2017 *Magic: The Gathering* World Champion in his hometown of Boston.






Oct 6-8, 2017


The Castle at Park Plaza & Revere Hotel



William Jensen
