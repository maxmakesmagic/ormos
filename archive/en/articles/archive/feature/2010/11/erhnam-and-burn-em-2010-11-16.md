In honor of Burn Week, Daily Decks brings you some of the game's most famous burn decks! John Mortimer took the popular "Erhnam and Burn 'Em" strategy to the 1996 Northwest Regionals. His game plan, like many other players playing at that time, was to accelerate out a beefy threat like [Erhnam Djinn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Erhnam+Djinn). After that he relied on burn spells to clear the way for his creature to attack on the ground for the win! Learn more about *Premium Decks: Fire & Lightning*
[here](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/ProductArticle.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/pdsfirelightning/productinfo).
