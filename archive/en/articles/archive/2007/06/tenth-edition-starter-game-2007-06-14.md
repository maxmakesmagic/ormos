*Tenth Edition* Starter Game
============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 14, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












[**Magic** Game Day](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/07gameday), if you haven't heard, is coming July 14th. It's a great day to kick back, take in some *Tenth Edition*, smash face with black-bordered versions of your old favorites, and celebrate the Big X.


It's also an awesome time to introduce a friend or two to **Magic**. This year we've created these two-player starter products. Here, choose one. Go ahead, click on whichever one appeals to you.
