[Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle) has fast become a fan favorite since it was first printed in *Zendikar*. At the 2009 Texas State Championship, Justin Corbett made it the centerpiece of his Top 8 deck. He accelerates out [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain)s while tutoring for Valakut via [Expedition Map](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Expedition+Map). From there on out, virtually every land drop he makes sends a [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) at something!
