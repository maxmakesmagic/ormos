### GRAND PRIX NEW JERSEY 2014


ADVENTURES IN BIG MAGIC
=======================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamstyborski.jpg)

Adam Styborski

Adam "Stybs" Styborski joined DailyMTG.com in 2009 to take over Serious Fun, before switching over to begin Command Tower in 2013. With his passion for Commander and community inclusion, you'll find plenty of opportunity each week to share your thoughts about everyone's favorite casual format.


November 15, 2014
 











 There was once a time when 2,000 players at a Grand Prix was record shattering. The massive scale of modern *Magic* events makes those days seem small. Nearly 4,000 players turned out for the main event at Grand Prix New Jersey, and it was Legacy that players were ready to play.



"I'm 0-and-2 already."





*Brian Coval was off to a slow start at the Grand Prix of his favorite format.*
