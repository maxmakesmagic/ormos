=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 5, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











All the PTQ tickets have been punched for Pro Tour *Dark Ascension*, but the new year has brought us a new Pro Tour Qualifier season! PTQs for Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored* are [going on now in the Modern format](/en/node/640181). Take a look at the Modern lists from the prestigious Finals tournament from Japan held in December plus lists from **Magic Online** Daily Events, then find a PTQ near you to earn a trip to Barcelona in May!
