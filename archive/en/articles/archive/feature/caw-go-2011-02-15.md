Brad Nelson is the 2010 Player of the Year! He beat France's Guillaume Matignon in a playoff at the recent Pro Tour Paris, part of **Magic** Weekend Paris. The Standard deck that helped take him to the title? "Caw Go," a blue and white control strategy that leans heavily on the utility and card advantage gained from [Squadron Hawk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squadron+Hawk). Read full event coverage of Pro Tour Paris [here](/en/events/coverage/stark-flies-high-planeswalkers-paris).
