German WMCQ 7th-Place Decklist

[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Hornet Nest)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet Nest)
Gunnar Geißler didn't quite make the German World *Magic* Cup team with his Mono-Green Devotion, but 7th place was pretty good anyway, and his deck was certainly noteworthy.


Eschewing a second color, Geißler played some pretty spicy cards in both his main deck and sideboard, including [Sylvan Primordial](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Primordial), [Hornet Nest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Nest), and main-deck [Reclamation Sage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reclamation+Sage). Instead, he created a sleek, mono-colored machine that nearly took him to the title.


He also didn't fall for many of the shiny new toys in *Magic 2015*. No [Genesis Hydra;](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Genesis+Hydra%3B) no [Chord of Calling;](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling%3B) and just one [Nissa, Worldwaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa%2C+Worldwaker) in the sideboard.


There's still one more weekend of [World *Magic* Cup Qualifiers](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/world-magic-cup-qualifiers-event-types-events), so if you want to qualify for your national team, it's time to get on it!
