Magic: The Gathering Graphic Novels
===================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on September 13, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/novels/pathoftheplaneswalker.jpg)The *Path of the Planeswalker* graphic novels collect the popular [Planeswalker comics](http://staging.wizards.com/magic/multiverse/planeswalkers.aspx?x=mtg/multiverse/webcomics/main) from magicthegathering.com. These graphic novels combine a compelling look into Planeswalkers' motivations with art from **Magic** artists including Mark Texeira, Christopher Moeller, Jason Shawn Alexander, Aleksi Briclot, Kev Walker, Alex Horley-Orlandelli, and more.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/novels/pathoftheplaneswalker2.jpg)
