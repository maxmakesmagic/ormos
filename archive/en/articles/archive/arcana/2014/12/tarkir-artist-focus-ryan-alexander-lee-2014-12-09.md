Tarkir Artist Focus: Ryan Alexander Lee
=======================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 9, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_mikemcartor.jpg)
By Mike McArtor




 Mike first played with Ice Age and became the copy editor for DailyMTG.com in December 2011. Before DailyMTG, he was an editor on D&D's Dragon magazine for four years. 






The [2014 World *Magic* Cup](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/2014WMC) concluded with what the community has dubbed Daneblastâ€”an auspicious [Duneblast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast) topdeck that let Simon Nielsen snatch victory from the jaws of defeat. While I've looked at [Duneblast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast)'s art in [an Arcana](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/arcana/art-making-friends-multiplayer) already, this gave me the opportunity to not only give it another look but to bring back a series started by Trick last year: the artist focus.



Today, let's look at the eight pieces of Tarkir art done by [Ryan Alexander Lee](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5B%22Ryan+Alexander+Lee%22%5D).
