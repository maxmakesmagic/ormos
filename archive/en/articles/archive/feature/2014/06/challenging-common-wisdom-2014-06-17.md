Challenging Common Wisdom
=========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 17, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 







A[Azorius Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Azorius%2BCharm), [Supreme Verdict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Supreme+Verdict), [Sphinx's Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%27s+Revelation), [Detention Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere). Those have been the pillars of white-blue-based control decks for the entirety of their existence in Standard. If you want to play White-Blue Control, with or without a splash, don't leave home without those cards. 

 Apparently, Jim Davis didn't get the memo. Jim built a deck without format staple [Detention Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere) or its *Journey into Nyx* cousin, [Banishing Light](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banishing+Light). The reason for this is twofold. First, with all three sets of *Theros* block in Standard, enchantments are at an all-time high. This means that enchantment removal is also more prevalent, and [Detention Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere) is not the permanent removal spell it once was. You can see the increased amount of enchantment removal in a main-deck [Deicide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deicide). 

 The other major reason Jim cut [Detention Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere) is that he wanted to play [Planar Cleansing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Planar+Cleansing). [Planar Cleansing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Planar+Cleansing) would destroy any [Detention Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere)s in play, unlocking the permanents that he wanted to remove in the first place! Another powerful card that is enabled by removing [Detention Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere) is [Renounce the Guilds](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renounce+the+Guilds). Many of Standard's strongest permanents are multicolored, including [Blood Baron of Vizkopa](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Baron+of+Vizkopa), [Reaper of the Wilds](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reaper+of+the+Wilds), and a variety of Planeswalkers. 

Sometimes, challenging common wisdom can have its rewards!
