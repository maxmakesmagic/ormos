The *Rise of the Eldrazi* is near! Prerelease weekends are just days away, but before the Eldrazi, large creatures have been a powerful force (pun intended) throughout **Magic**'s history. One of the most famous large creatures was [Verdant Force](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verdant+Force), dubbed "Best Fattie Ever" by its champion Jamie Wakefield. Jamie even managed to qualify for the Pro Tour using a version of the deck like the one listed here. It benefited from fast mana through [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) and [Fyndhorn Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fyndhorn+Elves), additional tricks from creatures like [Spike Weaver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spike+Weaver) and [Uktabi Orangutan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Uktabi+Orangutan), and [Natural Order](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Natural+Order) to cheat the [Verdant Force](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Verdant+Force) onto the battlefield. From there the army of 1/1 creatures it created alongside its hefty 7/7 body were enough to get the job done!
