Brian "The Dragonmaster" Kibler finally managed to add a Pro Tour title to his already impressive resume! He did the deed at Pro Tour–Austin, taking an innovative Zoo list to the title. The deck was built by Hall of Famer Ben Rubin and featured [Baneslayer Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel) and the combo of [Grove of the Burnwillows](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grove+of+the+Burnwillows) and [Punishing Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Punishing+Fire). You can read all about Brian's win in the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/kibler-completes-comeback-austin-victory).
