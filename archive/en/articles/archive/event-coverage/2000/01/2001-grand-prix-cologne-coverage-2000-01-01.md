**The Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to the Pro TourTM.**

 The Castle at Park Plaza  

 The Boston Park Plaza Hotel & Tower  

 64 Arlington St.  

 Boston, MA  

 (617) 426-2000

 $25 (Advance registration)  

 $30 (On site)


For advance registration, contact **Your Move Games at (617) 666-5799**. Advance registration will be available until 5:00 P.M. on Wednesday, January 21, 2001. For hotel reservations, call The Park Plaza Hotel at (617) 426-2000. Mention "Grand Prix-New Boston" for $129/night rate.


For complete Grand Prix-Boston information, please visit <http://www.yourmovegames.com>


**TOURNAMENT**


* **Divisions**
*The main tournament at Grand Prix-Boston is open to **Magic**® players of all ages.  

 In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher in that Grand Prix. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, **or** by being an invited DCITM player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*
* **Tournament Format**  

 The format for Grand Prix-Boston will be *Invasion*TM Block Limited-format, with day 2 Booster Draft. All DCI Limited-format rules and Magic Floor Rules announced on September 1, 2000, along with all subsequent updates, will be enforced.
* **Tournament Structure**  

 The structure for Grand Prix-Boston will be modified Swiss-style (60-minute rounds, number of rounds determined by number of participants), with the Top 64 players advancing to the second day of competition for prizes and invitations.
* **Tournament Byes** *(Byes are not cumulative throughout the season or year)* Byes will be given to players in the Grand Prix based on the following criteria:  
***Third-Round Byes***


	+ Players with 24 or more professional points in the Chicago 2000 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Limited rating of 2025 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI ratings published approximately six weeks prior to the Grand Prix.***Second-Round Byes***


	+ Players with 12 or more professional points in the Chicago 2000 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Limited rating of 1900 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI ratings published approximately six weeks prior to the Grand Prix.***First-Round Byes***


	+ Players with 6 or more professional points in the Chicago 2000 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Limited rating of 1800 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI ratings published approximately six weeks prior to the Grand Prix.

**PAYOUT**


Grand Prix-Boston will feature the standard "main tournament" prize schedule as well as a special "1700 division" prize schedule. DCI members who have a lifetime highest Limited rating of no greater than 1700 will be eligible for the 1700 division. The 1700 division prizes will be paid to the highest-finishing players *regardless of their standing in the Grand Prix*.
