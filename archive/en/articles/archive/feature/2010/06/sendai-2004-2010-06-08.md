Grand Prix–Sendai 2010 was this past weekend, but the city has played host to the Grand Prix circuit before. In 2004 it was Ichirou Shimura who managed to take the trophy. The format was Mirrodin Limited, and his deck was an aggressive red-white strategy. But the true bomb that served him well in the Top 8? [Loxodon Warhammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Loxodon+Warhammer)!
