Feature Match Round Six - Pierre Malherbaud versus Yuri Kolomeyko
=================================================================



 Posted in [ARTICLES HOME](/en/articles)











Pierre Malherbaud is the two-time French Champion and came twenty-seventh at this year's Worlds in Toronto. Most people who can name a Ukrainian **Magic** player would probably say Alex Shvartsman, but that's cheating, and Yuri burst onto the scene with a top eight finish at Pro Tour Barcelona, back in May.


Yuri is playing the old-school red-green speed deck, and Pierre has a more original deck, combining elements of both land destruction and Domain.


### Game One


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPWARSAW01/954.jpg)Yuri obviously doesn't like this match-up, as he shook his head and sighed as early as turn two, when Pierre played a [Fertile Ground](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fertile+Ground) on his Swamp. Kolomeyko's hand wasn't too good either, as it had only burn spells and no creatures.


Pierre's third turn [Harrow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harrow) allowed him to play a [Sabertooth Nishoba](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sabertooth+Nishoba) on his fourth turn. With no creatures large enough to block it, Yuri had to concede fairly soon after, as he couldn't find anything [Kavu Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kavu+Titan) sized to be able to trade.


Pierre Malherbaud 1 - Yuri Kolomeyko 0


Pierre brought in more sizeable creatures, taking out the pointless [Destructive Flow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Destructive+Flow)s for [Rakavolver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rakavolver)s and [Rith, the Awakener](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rith%2C+the+Awakener). The Ukrainian brought in some Masks of Intolerance.


### Game Two


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPWARSAW01/955.jpg)Pierre had to mulligan a one-forest hand, and at least Yuri's draw was a little better this time. He summoned a turn two [Thornscape Familiar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thornscape+Familiar), and followed it up with [Raging Kavu](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Kavu) allowing him to attack for three on the third turn, and then a turn four [Skizzik](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skizzik) putting Pierre on five life. Even a [Collective Restraint](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collective+Restraint) wasn't enough to help, as even though Yuri didn't have a fifth land and Pierre had the Domain, the Ukrainian did have a [Thunderscape Battlemage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thunderscape+Battlemage) to destroy the enchantment.


Yuri Kolomeyko 1 - Pierre Malherbaud 1


### Game Three


Pierre managed to find an early creature of his own this game, and his 2/3 [Emblazoned Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emblazoned+Golem) slowed Yuri down immensely as he only had two 2/2 [Kavu Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kavu+Titan)s. The Frenchman then followed up with a slightly larger Golem on his fourth turn.


Kolomeyko played a [Jade Leech](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jade+Leech) hoping to at least get rid of the larger Golem. Pierre then killed one of the [Kavu Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kavu+Titan)s with a [Flametongue Kavu](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flametongue+Kavu). Yuri used [Ghitu Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghitu+Fire) to kill the larger Golem, and attacked with his Leech, which Pierre had to lose both of his creatures to kill.


This attack cleared the board of all but Yuri's [Kavu Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kavu+Titan), but Pierre tilted the game his way once more with [Rith, the Awakener](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rith%2C+the+Awakener), and then a second Flametongue killed the other [Kavu Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kavu+Titan). Yuri played a third [Kavu Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kavu+Titan), this time with kicker, but he had no way of stopping the Dragon, and the match went to Pierre.


Pierre Malherbaud defeats Yuri Kolomeyko 2 - 1
