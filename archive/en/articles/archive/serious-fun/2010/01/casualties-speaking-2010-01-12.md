Casualties Speaking
===================



 Posted in **Serious Fun**
 on January 12, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/astybs_authorpic.png)
By Adam Styborski




 Stybs has played Magic the world over, writing and drafting as part of the event coverage team and slinging Commander everywhere his decks will fit. 






Right before the holiday break it was [Goblin Week](/en/articles/archive/serious-fun/goblin-compass-2009-12-15). Since I'm a devout EDH enthusiast I, naturally, reviewed some of the more entertaining options for using a legendary Goblin as your general. But more importantly, I explained my boggart nature of "borrowing" the most interesting things around me for my own personal use, challenging you to share a themed (Goblin or not) EDH deck.

Shared you did.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/sf/sf73_wortSplash.jpg)

> Basically, what I was trying to do is get a ton of goblins out, hopefully by obtaining a doubling season and then casting one of the token generators, especially [Mogg Infestation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogg+Infestation). From there, this deck has two options: either attack in massive waves, possibly with the help of [Overrun](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overrun) or [Coat of Arms](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coat+of+Arms), or combine [Boggart Shenanigans](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boggart+Shenanigans) with sacrifice outlets like [Goblin Bombardment](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Bombardment) (for direct damage) or [Altar of Dementia](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Altar+of+Dementia) (milling.) As general, [Wort, the Raidmother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wort%2C+the+Raidmother) helps with the token creation, and also turns spells like [Goblin Grenade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Grenade) or [Goblin War Strike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+War+Strike) lethal, particularly when you have a field full of goblins to conspire with.

I've seen a [Wort, the Raidmother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wort%2C+the+Raidmother) deck before, and she lends herself to the general approached RavenoftheBlack was going for: make Goblins then make Goblins blow up opponents and such. While [Doubling Season](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doubling+Season) and anything that generates tokens is generally considered a combo, it's Wort allowing you to conspire spells like [Overrun](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overrun) and [Harmonize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harmonize) that makes all those token work when many decks in the format drop big, flying fatties.

Several of you didn't send in or post any decks, instead opting just to point out something cool. The most common one I received was a surprising Goblin combo of instant death. 

With [Goblin Lackey](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Lackey) and [Goblin Wizard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Wizard) on the battlefield, cast a [Goblin Recruiter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Recruiter) to put [Goblin Ringleader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ringleader); [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiki-Jiki%2C+Mirror+Breaker); [Lightning Crafter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Crafter); and [Skirk Prospector](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Prospector), in that order, on top of your library. On the next turn, swing with [Goblin Lackey](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Lackey) to allow you to put [Goblin Ringleader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ringleader) onto the battlefield, drawing you at least the next three cards on your library, then tap the [Goblin Wizard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Wizard) to put [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Kiki-Jiki%252C%2BMirror%2BBreaker) onto the battlefield. [Play Skirk Prospector](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Play+Skirk+Prospector), then sacrifice any Goblins except [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Kiki-Jiki%252C%2BMirror%2BBreaker) and the Prospector to generate enough mana to cast [Lightning Crafter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Crafter). It doesn't matter what you would champion, as you can have [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Kiki-Jiki%252C%2BMirror%2BBreaker) copy the [Lightning Crafter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Crafter) in response to the trigger. When the token Crafter comes into play, let *that* champion trigger resolve to remove [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Kiki-Jiki%252C%2BMirror%2BBreaker). You can then tap the token Crafter to deal damage, then sacrifice it to the Prospector ... which will put a trigger on the stack to bring [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Kiki-Jiki%252C%2BMirror%2BBreaker) back untapped. You can repeat as necessary to kill anything (including players) or, if you feel frisky, generate an arbitrarily large amount of mana. Either way it's pretty classy for smelly green dudes!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/sf/sf73_boom.jpg)
