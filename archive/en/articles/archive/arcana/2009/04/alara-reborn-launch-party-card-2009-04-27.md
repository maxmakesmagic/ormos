*Alara Reborn* Launch Party Card
================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 27, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast

Anyway, because the Launch Parties are a day earlier than usual, we're showing you the Launch Party promo card... *right now!*
