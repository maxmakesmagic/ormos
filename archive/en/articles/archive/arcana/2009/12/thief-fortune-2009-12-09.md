Thief of Fortune
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 9, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Come with us now to the long-ago days of *Time Spiral*! Remember [Fortune Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fortune+Thief)?


![Fortune Thief](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Fortune+Thief)
  
The card, of course, is a reference to [Ali from Cairo](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ali+from+Cairo).


![Ali from Cairo](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Ali+from+Cairo)
  
You might even recognize the turban. But there's something else. First, let's blow up the [Ali from Cairo](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ali+from+Cairo) art a bit.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/338_alifromcairo.jpg)[Ali from Cairo](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ali+from+Cairo) | Illustration by [Mark Poole](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Mark+Poole%22%5D)


  
Did you spot the other art reference on [Fortune Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fortune+Thief)?


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/338_fortunethief.jpg)[Fortune Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fortune+Thief) | Illustration by [Christopher Moeller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Christopher+Moeller%22%5D)


  
Check out the necklace around the [Fortune Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fortune+Thief)'s neck!
