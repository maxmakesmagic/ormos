The Next Masters Series Event
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












*by Sideboard Staff*


The second Masters Series will be held at Pro Tour Chicago from November 30 to December 3.


For information about Pro Tour Chicago 2000, [click here](http://archive.wizards.com/sideboard/article.asp?x=protour/PTChicago2000_Facts.asp).


### Location


Hyatt Regency Chicago  

On the Riverwalk  

151 East Wacker Drive  

Chicago, IL 60601  

(312) 565-1234  

Columbus Rooms I & J for Thursday Masters and Gateway, Pro Tour Feature Match area all other days.


### Invited Players


* Top 5 Limited players based on the DCI ratings published October 23, 2000
* Top 25 players in the PT New York 2000 Pro Standing (posted after Pro Tour New York, October 1)
* Top 2 players from the Masters Gateway Tournament, held November 30 in Chicago.

### Format


* Single-Elimination Invasion Booster Draft
* All players will draft, play 2 rounds in their pod (cutting to 8 players) then draft again for the final 3 rounds of play.

### Prize Payout




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| 1st | $25,000 |
| 2nd | $15,000 |
| 3rd-4th | $10,000 |
| 5-8th | $6,500 |
| 9th-16th | $4,000 |
| 17th-32nd | $2,000 |
| **Total Payout** | **$150,000** |

### Schedule


**Thursday, November 30, 2000**


**8:00 AM** Registration for Gateway tournament (only open to players with 6 or more Pro Points in the PT New York 2000 Pro Standing)  
**9:00 AM** Gateway Tournament Begins (Invasion sealed deck or booster draft - to be determined)  
**5:00 PM** Registration for Masters Series (only open to 32 invited players)  
**6:00 PM** Draft #1 of Masters for 32 players (Invasion booster draft)  
**7:00 PM** Round 1 of Masters begins (no time limit) - 32 players  
**9:00 PM** Round 2 of Masters begins (no time limit) - 16 players


**Friday, December 1, 2000**


**9:00 AM** Pro Tour Chicago Player Meeting (Pro Tour Chicago is Standard format)  
**5:00 PM** Draft #2 of Masters for 8 players (Invasion booster draft)  
**6:00 PM** Round 3 of Masters begins (no time limit) - 8 players


**Saturday, December 2, 2000**


**9:00 AM** Day Two of Pro Tour Chicago  
**5:00 PM** Round 4 of Masters begins (no time limit) - 4 players


**Sunday, December 3, 2000**


**9:00 AM** Finals of Pro Tour Chicago and Round 5 of Masters begins (no time limit) - top 2 players
