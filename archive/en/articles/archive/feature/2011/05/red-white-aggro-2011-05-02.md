Now that *New Phyrexia* has been fully revealed, it's time to look at how the new set might impact *Scars of Mirrodin* Block Constructed. This list, piloted by qj\_wong to a 4-0 record in a Daily Event on **Magic Online**, is a throwback to red-white aggro decks of old. What new cards in red and white will help push the deck over the top? What artifacts will help shore up its game plan? Find out by checking out the *New Phyrexia* [Card Image Gallery](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/newphyrexia/spoiler).
