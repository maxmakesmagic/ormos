The State and Provincial championships are just around the corner. Are you ready? Sandydogmtg is trying, with this fascinating take on White Weenie. Utilizing traditional weenie threats in the form of Burrenton Forge-Tender, Figure of Destiny, and Wizened Cenn, Sandydog's deck puts the early beatdown on. Then, thanks to Ajani Vengeant from *Shards of Alara*, the deck has a nice follow-up in the late game! To find out more information about States (including how to get special unreleased promo cards for attending as well as the nearest location to you) visit [www.mtgchamps.com](http://mtgchamps.com/). For more Standard tech for the event, keep your browser tuned to the Daily Decks all week long!
