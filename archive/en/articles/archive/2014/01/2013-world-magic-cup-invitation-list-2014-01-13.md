2013 World Magic Cup Invitation List
====================================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on January 14, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Each national team invited to the 2013 World Magic Cup will consist of the following players:


* The winner of each of the three (3) [2013 World Magic Cup Qualifiers](/node/116774) run in that country.
* The top ranked player at the conclusion of the 2012-2013 Professional Points Season from that country that is not already invited based on the above criteria. If two or more players are tied, ties will be broken as follows:
	+ The tied player with the greatest number of Pro Tour Top 8\* finishes during the 2012-13 Professional Points Season, or
	+ The tied player with the greatest number of Grand Prix Top 8 finishes during the 2012-13 Professional Points Season, or
	+ The tied player with the highest Pro Tour\* finish during the 2012-13 Professional Points Season, or
	+ The tied player with the highest point total at the conclusion of the 2013 Seasonal Yearly Award Season.

\* Note: The Magic: The Gathering World Championship and the World Magic Cup are not Pro Tour events.


If you believe that your finish at a World Magic Cup Qualifier is in error, please visit [this page](/node/46389). After logging in, click the "Appeals Form" link at the bottom of the page and fill out the form.


Players who have (1) won a 2013 World Magic Cup Qualifier or (2) are the top Professional Points player invited to the World Magic Cup (invitations awarded on May 19, 2013) may not compete in further 2013 World Magic Cup Qualifiers.


All invitations are subject to the requirements limitations set forth in the [Magic Premier Event](http://archive.wizards.com/wpn/document.aspx?x=magic_the_gathering_premier_event_invitation_policy) Invitation Policy.
