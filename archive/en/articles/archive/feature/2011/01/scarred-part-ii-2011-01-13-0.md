Scarred, Part II
================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 13, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











It's time to return to the adventures of [Koth of the Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Koth+of+the+Hammer); [Venser, the Sojourner](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Venser%2C+the+Sojourner); and [Elspeth Tirel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth+Tirel)! Venser and Koth are trekking through the Mephidross, while Elspeth has an encounter with one of the Vulshok. Follow all the action in [Scarred, Part II](/en/node/626381)!
