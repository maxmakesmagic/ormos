The October 4, 2017 Update
==========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on October 4, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on October 4, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by [Admiral Beckett Brass](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Admiral+Beckett+Brass). Because she made me say so.


Today's Must
============


1. [Commander VS: *Ixalan* Season Kickoff!](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/35949_Commander-VS-Ixalan-Season-Kickoff.html) | StarCityGames.com | Justin Parnell and Stephen Green

It's the first episode of a new season of Commander VS, and, as they so often do, the crew is kicking things off with the legends of the latest set. It's [Gishath, Sun's Avatar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gishath%2C+Sun%27s+Avatar) vs. [Tishana, Voice of Thunder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tishana%2C+Voice+of+Thunder) vs. [Admiral Beckett Brass](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Admiral+Beckett+Brass) vs. [Vona, Butcher of Magan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vona%2C+Butcher+of+Magan)—and each deck is tribal themed. Watch to see who comes out on top—and it better be Tishana or Admiral Brass.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [Preparation, Expectation, and Reconciliation: Lucas Berthoud's Journey to Worlds](https://mtg.one/preparations-expectations-and-reconciliation/) | MTG.one | Lucas Berthoud

The [*Magic* World Championship](https://magic.wizards.com/en/events/premierplay/2017WC) is *this weekend*. I know, right?! I'll be there watching Berthoud and 23 others vie for the title of World Champion and $100,000. Berthoud goes way back—like 1996 way back—to talk about his journey from someone who just loved coverage to someone on *Magic*'s biggest stage.


1. [The Right Way to Netdeck](https://collected.company/2017/10/03/right-way-netdeck-181/) | The Command Zone | Josh Lee Kwai and Jimmy Wong

Look, we all do it. There's a wealth of information out there, it's hard not to look at at least some of it. Like when you think you've got a sweet [Nekusar, the Mindrazer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nekusar%2C+the+Mindrazer) deck, but you want to check to see if you missed something. Or you really want to build around [Mairsil, the Pretender](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mairsil%2C+the+Pretender), but aren't sure where to start. It's not a bad thing—and Josh and Jimmy show you how to do it right in Commander.


1. [4 Lessons from GP Providence](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/4-lessons-from-gp-providence/) | ChannelFireball | Riley Knight

What did we learn from Grand Prix Providence? Besides the fact that [Rich Hagon puns](https://www.reddit.com/r/magicTCG/comments/73nk6r/i_got_to_meet_mr_chair_himself_today/) are a national treasure, I mean.


1. [Commander Review: *Ixalan* Part 2 (Red, Green, Multicolor, Colorless, Lands)](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/commander-review-ixalan-part-2-red-green-multicolor-colorless-lands?src=wizards) | MTGGoldfish | Tomer Abramovici

There was a part 1, so clearly there had to be a part 2. This is that part 2. It's long, it's thorough, and it's worth your time.


1. [Vampire Pirates Abound in This Gorgeous New *Magic: The Gathering* Art](https://io9.gizmodo.com/vampire-pirates-abound-in-this-gorgeous-new-magic-the-1819110033?rev=1507061791885) | io9 | James Whitbrook

In which io9 calls *Ixalan* "a beautiful mess of the internet's favorite things, blended together and pressed into a set of cards." I mean, all we really need are cats and memes and this is pretty spot on.


1. [Voice](https://www.hipstersofthecoast.com/2017/10/dear-azami-voice-mtg-story/) | Hipsters of the Coast | Levi Byrne

Byrne uses a recent *Magic* Story as a jumping-off point to discuss the concept of voice in *Magic* Story—and comes away feeling good about how the characters are differentiated and fleshed out in *Ixalan*.
