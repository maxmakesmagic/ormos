*Modern Horizons 2* Leagues and Premier Play Events Return
----------------------------------------------------------


* The *Modern Horizons 2* Sealed League and Draft League returned on Wednesday, July 21, and run until *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt* events launch on Thursday, September 16 (extended one week due to downtime change).
* Those Leagues are returning to act as support for the four Limited Premier Events currently slated between now and *Innistrad: Midnight Hunt*—the plan is to use *Modern Horizons 2* for all of them instead of *Adventures in the Forgotten Realms*.
	+ Increased entry options of 50 Event Tickets or 500 Play Points will be used for the two *Modern Horizons 2* Super Qualifiers
	+ The QP entry option will remain at 40 for both Qualifiers and Super Qualifiers
* *Modern Horizons 2* will be used as the Limited Preliminary format starting July 21 at 4 p.m. instead of *Adventures in the Forgotten Realms*.
	+ Entry options: 45 Event Tickets or 450 Play Points
	+ See next item for updates to prize payout

August Is Spotlight Cube Series Month
-------------------------------------


For the next three weeks, *Magic Online* will be featuring a new cube from the community each week! And at the culmination, we'll bring *Legacy Cube* back. It's a good time to be a cube fan! The three Spotlight Cube Series cubes you'll have the pleasure of drafting with starting tomorrow are:


* August 4–11: Autumn Burchett's *Bant Cube*
* August 11–18: Ellie Rice's *Temur Cube 2.0*
* August 18–25: Ryan Saxe's *Classic Cube*



---

Spotlight Cube Series: *Classic Cube*
-------------------------------------


We're wrapping up Spotlight Cube Series Month with Ryan Saxe's *Classic Cube*, running from August 18–25. *Classic Cube* has tons of combos and archetypes to discover, and games are intense right from the get-go. Check out the full list of cards below and read more about Ryan's vision for the cube in tomorrow's article.
