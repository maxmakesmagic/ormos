January's FNM Promo
===================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 25, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 









There is one night that is clearly the best night of the week. That night is Friday. Head down to your local card shop and get together with friends to battle in **Magic**! Just for playing you could get a very cool premium promo!


Here's a peek at the upcoming promos for Friday Night **Magic**:


### November 2013


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1305_oleu_grislysalvage.jpg)### December 2013


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1330_lkjda_collector.jpg)### January 2014


January's Friday Night Magic promo is Warleader's Helix! This is another stunning piece of art that I cannot wait to play during a game.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1350_-klashjd_helix.jpg)Let's take a closer look at the art by [Wesley Burt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Wesley%20Burt%22%5D). He first did art for **Magic** with *Avacyn Restored* and has really brought some wonderful pieces to the game, including this one!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1350_-klashjd_art.jpg)[Warleader's Helix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warleader%27s+Helix) | Art by [Wesley Burt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Wesley%20Burt%22%5D)
