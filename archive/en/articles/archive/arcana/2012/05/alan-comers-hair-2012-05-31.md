Alan Comer's Hair
=================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 31, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











When the [**Magic** Pro Tour Hall of Fame](/en/node/574031) inducted its first class in 2005, the inductees were Jon Finkel, Darwin Kastle, Tommi Hovi, Olle Råde, and Alan Comer. Alan Comer's [Hall of Fame profile](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/2005-11-22-2) includes his five Top 8s and numerous innovative decks. But it doesn't mention his heart.

Here's what Alan Comer looked like a few days ago:

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/984_long.gif)  
He's been growing his hair long, intending to donate it to Locks of Love. So here's what he looks like now:

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/984_short.gif)  
Nice work, Alan!
