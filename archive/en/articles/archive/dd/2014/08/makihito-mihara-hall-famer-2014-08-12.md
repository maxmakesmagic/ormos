### DAILY DECK


Makihito Mihara, Hall of Famer
==============================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)

Adam Prosak

From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer.


August 12, 2014
 







[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Dragonstorm)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonstorm)
Over the course of his career, Makihito Mihara has had many impressive finishes. Mihara has made the Top 8 of the Pro Tour five times, in addition to five appearances on the Japanese national team. He was the captain of the Japanese national team that won the World Team Championship in 2011, but his crowning achievement was his World Championship title in 2006.


Makihito Mihara's Standard deck of choice was a [Dragonstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonstorm) combo deck. Harnessing the power of [Seething Song](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seething+Song), [Rite of Flame](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rite+of+Flame), and [Lotus Bloom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Bloom), Mihara could get to nine mana to cast a [Dragonstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonstorm) with a storm count of four. Then, four [Bogardan Hellkite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bogardan+Hellkite)s would enter the battlefield, dealing 20 damage to the opponent. [Hunted Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hunted+Dragon) provided a backup in case more than 20 damage was needed or one of the [Bogardan Hellkite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bogardan+Hellkite)s were stuck in Mihara's hand.
