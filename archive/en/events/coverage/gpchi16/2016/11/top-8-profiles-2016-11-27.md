### Kentaro Yamamoto


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpchi16/gpChiba16_T8_yamamoto.jpg)


**Age:** 32  
**Occupation:** Team Cygames Pro Player  
**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Three Pro Tour Top 8s  
**Where do you usually play *Magic*?**   
*Magic Online*


**Number of Byes:** 3  
**Score in Day 1:** 8-1  
**Score in Day 2:** 5-0-1


**What deck did you play and what is the most important card?**  

Sneak and Show


**What is your favorite card in Legacy?**  
[Griselbrand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Griselbrand)


**Did you see any cool decks or good plays this weekend?**  

I saw someone playing [Gideon, Ally of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Ally+of+Zendikar) in the sideboard of Miracles.


**Everyone has different habits and styles when it comes to their own deck. Do you have any particular requirements?**  

Nothing special.

### Keisuke Sato


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpchi16/gpChiba16_T8_sato.jpg)


**Age:** 27  
**Occupation:** System Engineer  
**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Grand Prix Shizuoka 2014 Top 8  
**Where do you usually play *Magic*?**   

Hareruya, Card of Paradice Sakuragi and Gamebeat


**Number of Byes:** 2  
**Score in Day 1:** 8-1  
**Score in Day 2:** 5-0-1


What deck did you play?  

Sneak and Show.


**What is your favorite card in Legacy?**  
[Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) or [Knight of the Reliquary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary)


**Did you see any cool decks or good plays this weekend?**  

The [Food Chain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Food+Chain) combo deck was awesome. You can generate infinite mana and use [Tidespout Tyrant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tidespout+Tyrant) to bounce everything.


**Everyone has different habits and styles when it comes to their own deck. Do you have any particular requirements?**  

I like to use promo cards or Premium foil cards whenever possible. I also try to use Japanese language cards as much as I can.

### Kouichi Miyabe


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpchi16/gpChiba16_T8_miyabe.jpg)


**Age:** 34  
**Occupation:** Employee  
**Hometown:** Matsumoto, Nagano, Japan  
**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Nagano Cup 2011 and 2015 Champion.  
**Where do you usually play *Magic*?**   

Chagamo [Fireball](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball), Hama-ya Matsumoto, Touch Matsumoto and Dogs Cup.


**Number of Byes:** 1  
**Score in Day 1:** 9-0  
**Score in Day 2:** 4-1-1


**What deck did you play and what is the most important card?**  

Miracles (but without red, because I play 2 [Back to Basics](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Back+to+Basics)).


**What is your favorite card in Legacy?**  
[Back to Basics](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Back+to+Basics)


**Did you see any cool decks or good plays this weekend?**  
[Misdirection](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Misdirection) and [Stifle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stifle) are very useful cards.


**Everyone has different habits and styles when it comes to their own deck. Do you have any particular requirements?**  

I always use Premium foil cards as well as Premium foil Unhinged basic lands. My deck is all foil where possible, except [Force of Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will)!
