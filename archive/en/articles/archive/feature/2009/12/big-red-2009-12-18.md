Everyone is familiar with Boros Bushwhacker and other aggressive red strategies in Standard, but for Davin Frankosky, the 2009 State and Provincial Championships were all about large red threats. His deck used [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle) and [Armillary Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Armillary+Sphere) to power out fatties like [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander) and [Lavaball Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavaball+Trap). The strategy was good enough to net Davin a Top 8 appearance!
