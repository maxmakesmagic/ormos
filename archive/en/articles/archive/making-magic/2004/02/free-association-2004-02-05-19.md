Free Association
================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on February 5, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_markrosewater.jpg)
By Mark Rosewater




 Working in R&D since '95, Mark became Magic head designer in '03. His hobbies: spending time with family, writing about Magic in all mediums, and creating short bios. 






![Elvish Impersonators](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Elvish+Impersonators)When [Claymore J. Flapdoodle](/en/articles/archive/making-magic/free-association-2004-02-05-9) first turned in the art for Elvish Impersonators, he chose to represent "young" Elvis. I made him change it to "old" Elvis. My rationale? Old Elvis was funnier.
