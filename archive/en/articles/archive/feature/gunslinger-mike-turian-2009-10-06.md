These aren't our best FFL decks per se, but instead a glimpse into what the set has to offer. ![Vampire Nocturnus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Vampire+Nocturnus)There are many ways to make a Vampire deck, but this is the simplest approach: mono-black with four copies of ***Magic** 2010*'s [Vampire Nocturnus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampire+Nocturnus). This deck is fast and can be unforgiving. The biggest surprise was how good the one [Mind Sludge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Sludge) was; it was constantly catching people by surprise and taking them completely out of the game.
