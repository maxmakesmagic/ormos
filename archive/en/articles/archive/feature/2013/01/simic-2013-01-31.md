Simic
=====



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 31, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







Simic is all about growth and getting tricky with creatures, and winning a game of **Magic** should always come as something of an afterthought. As a player with deep roots in casual multiplayer games, I can really identify with this sensibility, and I've worn a Simic pin on my jacket to **Magic** tournaments since it was given out at [Grand Prix San Jose](/en/events/coverage/sperling-williams-and-rietzl-win-san-jose). 


![Master Biomancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Master+Biomancer)
Here, I'm just going to play some little creatures and let them evolve, and maybe draw some cards, and eventually I expect to have enough gigantic creatures that I'll have won the game somehow.


 This deck is mostly built to exploit [Master Biomancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+Biomancer). My early creatures make mana and build up +1/+1 counters, which should allow me to hold off my opponent's creatures long enough that I can play [Master Biomancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+Biomancer) and start making huge creatures. [Master Biomancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+Biomancer) by itself makes all of my creatures pretty big, but not too ridiculous; however, if I play a second one, the first makes the second bigger, and then the two of them together make the rest of my creatures crazy. Alternatively, I can get even bigger even faster by using [Bioshift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bioshift) to transfer counters from one of my cheap evolve creatures onto [Master Biomancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+Biomancer). [Prime Speaker Zegana](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prime+Speaker+Zegana) will draw an absurd number of cards if [Master Biomancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+Biomancer) is in play, and [Somberwald Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somberwald+Sage) and [Gyre Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gyre+Sage) will make sure that I can actually cast them all, at which point I'll find [Craterhoof Behemoth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Craterhoof+Behemoth) pretty quickly to win no matter what my opponent's doing.
