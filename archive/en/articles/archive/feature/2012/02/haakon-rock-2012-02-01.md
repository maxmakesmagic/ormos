Haakon Rock
===========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 1, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorPic_GavinVerhey.jpg)
By Gavin Verhey




 When Gavin Verhey was eleven, he dreamt of a job making Magic cards—and now as a Magic designer, he's living his dream! Gavin has been writing about Magic since 2005. 






In a recent **Magic Online** PTQ, Wirecat brought a crazy new Modern deck to the (virtual) table that has stirred up a lot of discussion. While [Burning Vengeance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Vengeance) was made to work with flashback, Wirecat discovered it *also* works with the *Eventide* retrace mechanic and the *Coldsnap* rare [Haakon, Stromgald Scourge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haakon%2C+Stromgald+Scourge)! This unique innovation has drawn the attention of several noted players, and both [Mike Flores](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/td/179) and [Brian David-Marshall](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/twtw/179) wrote about it last week. Wirecat made the Top 8 of his PTQ with this deck—try it for yourself and [see if you can do the same](/en/node/640181)!
