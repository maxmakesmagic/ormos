### Andre Mueller


**Age:**  27  
**Hometown:**  Getseukirchen


**Occupation:**  Dealer and gamer


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** 20


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Valencia?** Grand Prix-Stockholm Top 16


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:**  2 GP, 2 PT


**Other previous Magic accomplishments:**  Isn't that enough?


**What deck are you playing and why?**


Enduring Ideal, because it beats Aggro and doesn't lose to graveyard hate.


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour-Valencia? Who did you prepare with?**


Most qualified Germans from the area met at my place for a five-day boot camp. Klaus Joens and Aaron Brackmann contributed to the actual building of the deck while the rest played the field against us.
