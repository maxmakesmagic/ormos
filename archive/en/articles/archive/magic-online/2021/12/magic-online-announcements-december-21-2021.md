*Vintage Cube* Is Back: December 22–January 12
----------------------------------------------


*Vintage Cube* is back for a three-week run starting tomorrow and continuing through January 12. To see the full cube list and the list of card changes, make sure to check out [the updated *Vintage Cube* card list page](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/vintage-cube-cardlist).


All paid accounts on file as of last week's downtime have been given a Vintage Point—good for a free entry into the *Vintage Cube* Draft League during this three-week run.


![Vintage Cube Entry](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/img_vintagecubeentry.png)


*Note: Unused Vintage Points will be removed from accounts on January 12.*

2021 Season 3 Showcase Qualifiers
---------------------------------


Players who earned entry into the Showcase Qualifier will be squaring off for an invitation to the 2021 Season 3 Champions Showcase!


The entry fee for Showcase Qualifiers is a format token that matches the format of the Showcase. As with QPs, after the season's Showcase Qualifiers are finished, all unused Format Tokens will be removed from players' accounts.


Each Showcase Qualifier is a scheduled Swiss-style event with a Top 8 playoff. The winner of each Showcase Qualifier earns a spot in the final Showcase event and a chance to earn their share of $70,000 and an upcoming Premier Event invitation.


Showcase Qualifiers are scheduled as follows:





| DATE | TIME | FORMAT | INVITATION |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sunday, December 26, 2021 | 10:00 a.m. | Pauper | MOCS Showcase & Neon Dynasty Championship |
| Sunday, January 2, 2021 | 6:00 a.m. | Pioneer | MOCS Showcase & Neon Dynasty Championship |


Learn more about Showcase Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#shq).

Limited Super Qualifier Sunday
------------------------------


On Sunday, December 26, there will be an *Innistrad: Crimson Vow* Sealed Super Qualifier with a start time of 7 a.m. PT (15:00 UTC). The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points (Season 1), 40 Event Tickets, or 400 Play Points. The top two finishers qualify for the Neon Dynasty Championship on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Super Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/mtgo-qualifiers-super-qualifiers).

Convention Championship – January 1
-----------------------------------


Did you go 3–0 in any of the Convention Preliminary events held on MTGO for PAX West, Gen Con, Origins, or PAX Unplugged? If so, you have a Wizards Ticket on your account—your ticket to compete in the Convention Championship event on New Year's Day at 7:00 a.m. Pacific time (15:00 UTC Saturday, January 1)!


![Wizards Ticket](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/img_wizardsticket.png)


The format will be *Modern Horizons 2* Sealed Deck with Top 8 Draft. The winner will receive 4 copies of each digital set in Modern, and the Top 8 each walk away with at least 1 copy of each digital set in Modern. There are also booster prizes down to 64th place.


Full details on this event can be found at our convention partner Pastimes Events's [official website](https://pastimesevents.com/mtgo-convention-series-championship/).


*Note: Unused Wizards Tickets will be removed from accounts on January 5.*

Format Change for MOCS Showcase Opens
-------------------------------------


We have updated the format for the 2021 Season 3-ending MOCS Showcase Open events on January 8–9. These events will now use *Modern Horizons 2* product. The entry option for each event will be 50 Event Tickets or 500 Play Points, and prizes will contain *Modern Horizons 2* product. Our [event schedule page](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/schedule-magic-online-products-game-info) and [2021 MOCS info page](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) now reflect this change.

Pauper Super Qualifier Rescheduled
----------------------------------


The Pauper Super Qualifier that did not fire on December 18 has been rescheduled. It will now be held Friday, January 28 at 2:00 p.m. Pacific time (15:00 UTC).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. [Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place. *NOTE: Pauper, Vintage, and Standard Challenges are never pre-empted**.*

*Modern Horizons 2* Returns (Including Preliminaries)
-----------------------------------------------------


It's the season for another fan favorite: *Modern Horizons 2* Draft! We're bringing back the non-Phantom Draft League now through January 5 with the same prize structure and chance to open a [Ragavan, Nimble Pilferer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ragavan%2C+Nimble+Pilferer) you know and love!


* Entry Options:
	+ 25 Event Tickets
	+ 3 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters and 4 Event Tickets
	+ 250 Play Points
* Prizes:
	+ 3 Wins – 6 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters, 2 MOCS Season 3 QPs, and a Competitive Trophy
	+ 2 Wins – 2 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters

**UPDATE**: Limited Preliminary events will use *Modern Horizons 2* product until Wednesday, January 12*. (This is a change from last week)*


* Entry Options:
	+ 45 Event Tickets
	+ 450 Play Points
* Prizes:
	+ 4 Wins – 750 Play Points and 40 QP
	+ 3 Wins – 450 Play Points and 20 QP
	+ 2 Wins – 150 Play Points and 10 QP
