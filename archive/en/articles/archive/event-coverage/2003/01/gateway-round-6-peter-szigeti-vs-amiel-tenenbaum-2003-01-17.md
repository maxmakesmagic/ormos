Gateway Round 6: Peter Szigeti vs. Amiel Tenenbaum
==================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 17, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











"PTR" is best known for being the Pro Tour's antagonist element, but lately he's been proving that his game is nothing to scoff at either. He fought his way through the Gateway at Houston to play in the Masters, and is now one match away from repeating that achievement in Chicago. 

### Game 1

![Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Deep+Analysis)Szigeti mulliganed his opening hand. Tenenbaum took away an [Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm) with [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend), leaving Szigeti with many blue cards, and lacking any blue mana to cast them. [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) took out two [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation) from Szigeti's hand, while the Fiend kept chipping away for damage. The hand disruption continued with [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), which took [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic), leaving Szigeti with [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder), [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) and [Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm). Another [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) took a second [Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm) away, and increased Tenenbaum's clock to two points per turn. Szigeti's bad draw combined with Tenenbaum's discard spells meant that even if he drew an island, it would be too late. [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) drew Tenenbaum into more cards, and Szigeti conceded soon after.Szigeti- 0 Tenenbaum- 1

### Game 2

 This game worked out much better for Szigeti, as he played a forest and an island, summoning a turn two [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel). Tenenbaum played [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) to take away another [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel). Szigeti attacked for two, and added [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) to his side. Tenenbaum cast [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek), and saw that Szigeti merely had two lands in his hand. [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) died to [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother), and another [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek) confirmed that Szigeti was out of action cards. However, he drew into [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation) the following turn, and got three [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), flashing one back that same turn. The [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) died to [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother), leaving Szigeti with no creatures in play. A second [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) took away a potentially devastating [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval). Szigeti gained some more card advantage with [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), but still had nothing on the board. A [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) was countered with [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic), and Szigeti summoned [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba), flashed back the third Analysis, and got two [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla)s in play.

![Engineered Plague](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Engineered+Plague)[Ghastly Demise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghastly+Demise) took out [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba), and at seven lands Szigeti was able to play [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) from his hand, which was [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell)ed. The Rootwallas got past the Fiends for damage, and on the following turn the [Roar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar) was flashed back. It died to [Ghastly Demise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghastly+Demise), but Szigeti still had an offense going thanks to his two Rootwallas. A third one joined, and Tenenbaum pounded on his deck and top-decked [Engineered Plague](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Plague). This got a gasp out of the crowd, but Szigeti had [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic).Szigeti- 1 Tenenbaum- 1

### Game 3

Szigeti mulliganed to start for the second time this match. [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) put [Ray of Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation) and an island into Szigeti's graveyard. A second turn [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost) would have been bad news for Tenenbaum, but [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) stopped it. [Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) followed [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), and took [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), leaving Szigeti with only lands in hand. Tenenbaum cast [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), getting further ahead. A second Fiend took [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm), and [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) died to [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother). With no resources left, Szigeti soon fell to the [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog).

**Final Result:** Szigeti- 1 Tenenbaum- 2
