Walking the Planes: Redemption
==============================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on August 22, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Nathan Holt and Shawn Kornhauser












Worlds Week 2013 in Amsterdam was the most spectacular event in the history of **Magic**. With epic showdowns between world class players every round, emotions ran high. Walking the Planes Episode #16: "Redemption" is about revenge, conquering the past, an ascending star, a colossal battle of nations, and a tyrannical monster gone mad with power. 

 Follow Walking the Planes on Twitter ([@WalkThePlanes](https://twitter.com/walktheplanes)).
