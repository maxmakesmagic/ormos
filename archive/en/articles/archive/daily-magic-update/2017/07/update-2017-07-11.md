The July 11, 2017 Update
========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on July 11, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on July 11, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by six-year-olds who are better at *Magic* than you are.


Today's Musts
=============


1. [Dana Fischer is an amazing (and adorable) 6-year-old *Magic* pro](http://mashable.com/2017/07/10/magic-gathering-dana-fischer-6-year-old) | Mashable | Adam Rosenberg

We've talked about Dana Fischer before, but now others are catching on—and by others I mean Mashable. Still, it has all the ingredients of a story that goes viral: adorable child with photos in an oversized hat, loving father, and pop culture hook. What else do you need?


1. [HareruyaWayfinder *Hour of Devastation*](http://www.hareruyamtg.com/article/en/category/detail/146) | Hareruya EN | Kenji Tsumura

In what has become a quarterly tradition, Kenji Tsumura spews out decklist after decklist (not all decklists are by Tsumura, he's just the one doing the sharing). It's almost entirely Standard, but there is one spicy Pauper list in there as well—for Pauper is the most broken of formats.


After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [*Hour of Devastation* Constructed Set Review: White](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/hour-of-devastation-constructed-set-review-white/) | Channel Fireball | Luis Scott-Vargas

Constructed reviews are underway as LSV starts punning his way through each color in *Hour of Devastation*. These are basically required reading.


1. [Top Ten *Hour of Devastation* Cards](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/abesargent-07112017-top-ten-hour-of-devastation-cards/) | Gathering Magic | Abe Sargent

There are going to be a lot of top cards lists coming out, but you've gotta love one by Sargent. Enjoy the more casual bent of this one before everyone starts picking the next Standard all-stars.


1. [What's the Pick? *Hour of Devastation* Pack 1 Pick 1 with PV](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/whats-the-pick-hour-of-devastation-pack-1-pick-1-with-pv/) | Channel Fireball | Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa

We're just getting started on this Limited format, so who knows what the "correct" pick is at this point—and who cares? Start learning from the best now.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Red-Green Disco Beats](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/hollow-one-might-be-hour-of-devastations-most-broken-card/) | Channel Fireball | Joel Larsson

Trying to break [Hollow One](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hollow+One) in a cycling deck might be asking a bit much—you are, after all, still paying a bunch of mana to cycle things—but what about in a deck that likes to discard its hand?


That deck already existed on the fringes of Standard, thanks to cards like [Noose Constrictor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noose+Constrictor), [Bloodrage Brawler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodrage+Brawler), [Hazoret the Fervent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hazoret+the+Fervent), and [Fiery Temper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper)—so why not add another inexpensive beater to the team?


Imagine leading with a turn-two [Noose Constrictor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noose+Constrictor). On turn three, you play [Bloodrage Brawler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodrage+Brawler), discarding [Fiery Temper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper) to clear out a blocker, then you use Constrictor to discard [Honored Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Honored+Hydra) and maybe a land. Then, boom—free [Hollow One](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hollow+One). If there was a [Bomat Courier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bomat+Courier) on the first turn, you're probably ready to refill your hand. Or try replacing [Bloodrage Brawler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodrage+Brawler) with [Cathartic Reunion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cathartic+Reunion), which leaves you even on cards, but makes [Hollow One](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hollow+One) two mana cheaper.


This deck can hit hard, which is definitely going to leave your opponent feeling hollow.
