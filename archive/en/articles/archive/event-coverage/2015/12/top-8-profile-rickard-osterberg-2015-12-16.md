Top 8 Profile - Rickard Osterberg
=================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPHEL00/167.jpg)**Name:** Rickard Österberg  
**Age:** 24  
**Location:** Gothenburg, Sweden  
**Occupation:** -  
**Played Magic since:** Revised  
**Best accomplishments:** 4th place 2000 Euro Championship, 68th 1999 Pro Tour Chicago, 9th Grand Prix Copenhagen, won some Pro Tour Qualifiers, West Swedish Champion



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Helsinki?**  

A: My feeling that it would go well.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: I thought I would at least be in top 32.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: I had fever both days, so I made some small mistakes but played pretty okay overall.



**Q: What do you think about the formats used in this tournament, and what was your strategy?**  

A: My strategy was to build defensive decks, especially blue, white, black.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: My feelings that it would go my way.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: It means well deserved money.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: I really don't know.
