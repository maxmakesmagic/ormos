Top 8 Profile - Benedikt Klauser
================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*By Sideboard Staff*


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPFLO00/180.jpg)**Name:** Benedikt Klauser  
**Age:** 19  
**Location:** Vienna, Austria  
**Occupation:** Law student at the University of Austria  
**Played Magic since:** 6 years  
**Best accomplishments:** 4th at Pro Tour Chicago 1998, 4th at Worlds 2000



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Florence?**  

A: Cuz I need PT points for the Masters Series.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: I was hoping to make the second day and possibly top 16.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: I had two byes, played a lot of mirror matches and then T8.



**Q: What do you think about the Extended metagame, and why did you choose to play the deck you did?**  

A: It's very skill-intensive, that's why I like it.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Playing the best deck.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: PT points that bring me closer to Q'ing for the Masters Series.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: Kai will defeat the bye. Trix will win the tourney!
