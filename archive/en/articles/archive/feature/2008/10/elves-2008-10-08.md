Looking for a jumping off point for the upcoming State Championships using the new Standard format? ImACloud offers up an innovative concept straight from the digital realm of **Magic Online**. His concoction, which is almost entirely new Standard ready, powers out a horde of Elf tokens, pumped by cards like [Bramblewood Paragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bramblewood+Paragon), [Imperious Perfect](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imperious+Perfect), and [Elvish Champion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Champion). How does Cloud manage to do it so fast? By giving all Elves a mana ability thanks to [Heritage Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid)! And if the opponent has a mass removal spell? No worries; a pre-emptive [Regal Force](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Regal+Force) will make sure Cloud's hand stays full for post-[Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Wrath%2Bof%2BGod) good times. It's up to innovative players all over the world to determine which new *Shards of Alara* cards best replace the few rotations from ImACloud's ELVES! list.
