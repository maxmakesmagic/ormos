Magic Online Banned and Restricted List - August 2009
=====================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 20, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The following cards are banned or restricted in the listed **Magic Online** formats as of the August 26 downtime.

### 100 Card Singleton

* [Balance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Balance) is banned.
* [Strip Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strip+Mine) is banned.
* [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) is banned.

### Classic

* [Balance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Balance) is restricted.
* [Channel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Channel) is restricted.
* [Strip Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strip+Mine) is restricted.
* [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) is restricted.

### Commander

* [Balance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Balance) is banned.
* [Karakas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karakas) is banned.
* [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) is banned.

### Prismatic

* [Balance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Balance) is banned.
* [Grim Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Tutor) is banned.
* [Strip Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strip+Mine) is banned.
* [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) is banned.

### Tribal Wars (Classic)

* [Arboria](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arboria) is banned.
* [Balance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Balance) is banned.
* [Channel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Channel) is banned.
* [Strip Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strip+Mine) is banned.
* [The Abyss](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=The+Abyss) is banned.
* [Tinker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tinker) is banned.
