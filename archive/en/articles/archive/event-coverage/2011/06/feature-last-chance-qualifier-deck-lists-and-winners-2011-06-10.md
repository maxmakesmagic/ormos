White and blue continue to be dominant colors in Standard, but New Phyrexia is making its presence known. [Gitaxian Probe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gitaxian+Probe), [Dismember](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember), [Batterskull](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Batterskull), [Spellskite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellskite), [Mutagenic Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutagenic+Growth), and [Deceiver Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch) are just some of the cards from the new set that are having a big impact on the world of Standard. Unfortunately for Jun'ya, Sean, Justin, and Ryou, the Pro Tour itself is Scars Block Constructed, meaning they need to have tested an additional format, as well as the Limited format, to have a shot this weekend. It's a challenge for an LCQ winner to make the Top 8 of a Pro Tour, so for these four players' sakes let's hope they got a good night's sleep and are ready with some tech today!
