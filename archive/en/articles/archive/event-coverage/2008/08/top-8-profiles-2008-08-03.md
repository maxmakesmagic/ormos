Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 3, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpkob08/T8Tanahashi.jpg)*Masayasu Tanahashi*


**Masayasu Tanahashi** 


**Hometown:** Niigata  
**Age:** 24  
**Profession:** Company employee


**What deck did you play, who designed it, and what was the best card in it?**  

I played a Kithkin deck designed by Aikora. My best card was [Windbrisk Heights](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Windbrisk+Heights).


**Which card do you think is currently the most undervalued in *Eventide*?**  
[Drain the Well](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drain+the+Well).


**In your opinion, which *Eventide* card has had the most influence on Constructed?**  
[Figure of Destiny](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Figure+of+Destiny).


**What interests do you have other than *Magic*?**  

I like the Japanese comedy duo “Sekai no Nabeatsu”.


**What is your most lasting impression from Grand Prix Kobe?**  

Not getting [Mirrorweave](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirrorweave) off even once in the Swiss.


**What are your other major *Magic* achievements?**  

None.


**What are your other major non-*Magic* achievements?**  

None.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpkob08/T8Yoshimori.jpg)*Shou Yoshimori*


**Shou Yoshimori**


**Hometown:** Chiba  
**Age:** 24  
**Profession:** Company employee


**What deck did you play, who designed it, and what was the best card in it?**  

I played a traditional [Doran, the Siege Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower) I designed myself. My best combo was [Leaf-Crowned Elder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leaf-Crowned+Elder) with [Doran, the Siege Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower).


**Which card do you think is currently the most undervalued in *Eventide*?**  

I don’t know yet.


**In your opinion, which *Eventide* card has had the most influence on Constructed?**  
[Figure of Destiny](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Figure+of+Destiny) and [Unmake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unmake).


**What interests do you have other than *Magic*?**  

Acapella and games in general.


**What is your most lasting impression from Grand Prix Kobe?**  

In the 8th round, I was playing against Quick and Toast, and managed to pull out a win after getting hit by [Profane Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Profane+Command).


**What are your other major *Magic* achievements?**  

I made Top 8 at three different GP Kobe Trials. I’ve also played in the Finals.


**What are your other major non-*Magic* achievements?**  

Performing acapella throughout university.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpkob08/T8Yoshida.jpg)*Hiroshi Yoshida*


**Hiroshi Yoshida**


**Hometown:** Mizushima  
**Age:** 20

**Which card do you think is currently the most undervalued in *Eventide*?**  
[Flame Jab](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flame+Jab).

[Raven’s Crime](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raven%E2%80%99s+Crime).


**What interests do you have other than *Magic*?**  

I’m a wine connoisseur. I also like following Matsumoto, one of my local players.


**What is your most lasting impression from Grand Prix Kobe?**  

Getting a game loss for tardiness in the last round, which I ended up ID’ing anyway.


**What are your other major *Magic* achievements?**  

Winning the Book [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) Mizushima Cup (a local **Magic** tournament).


**What are your other major non-*Magic* achievements?**  

(no answer)

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpkob08/T8Takahashi.jpg)*Yuuta Takahashi*


**Yuuta Takahashi**


**Hometown:** Tokyo  
**Age:** 22  
**Profession:** Student


**What deck did you play, who designed it, and what was the best card in it?**  

I used a UB Faerie deck of my own design. All 15 of my sideboard cards were strong.


**Which card do you think is currently the most undervalued in *Eventide*?**  
[Endless Horizons](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Endless+Horizons).


**In your opinion, which *Eventide* card has had the most influence on Constructed?**  

For Block Constructed, [Stillmoon Cavalier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stillmoon+Cavalier). For Standard, [Figure of Destiny](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Figure+of+Destiny).


**What interests do you have other than *Magic*?**  

Jazz, the works of Yukio Mishima.


**What is your most lasting impression from Grand Prix Kobe?**  

I was playing Shintarou Ishimura. He went first and played [Devoted Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devoted+Druid) followed by [Chameleon Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chameleon+Colossus), then [Cloudthresher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudthresher), but I still won.


**What are your other major *Magic* achievements?**  

I’ve lost a lot.  

(Editor’s Note: Takahashi won Grand Prix Shizuoka 2008 and was a Finalist at PT San Diego.)


**What are your other major non-*Magic* achievements?**  

None...says a friend of mine.
