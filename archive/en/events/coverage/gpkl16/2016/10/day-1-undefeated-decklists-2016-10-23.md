Day 1 Undefeated Decklists
==========================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX KUALA LUMPUR 2016](/en/events/coverage/gpkl16)
 on October 23, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











After 9 rounds of contest, there are three 9-0 players, namely Anthony Lee from Australia, Marcus Oh from Singapore, and Mark Lawrence Tubola from the Philippines.


Coincidentally, all three players have chosen to play White-Blue [Flash](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash). Each of them had slightly different takes on their main deck, deviating by a handful of cards while maintaining the core of [Spell Queller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Queller), [Selfless Spirit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Selfless+Spirit), [Reflector Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reflector+Mage), and [Archangel Avacyn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+Avacyn).


For example, while Tubola's version was very close to the stock list, Lee chose to add black to the deck to support [Scrapheap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrapheap) Scounger for attrition and value. On the other side of the spectrum, Oh has decided to move [Gideon, Ally of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Ally+of+Zendikar) to the sideboard to house [Gisela, the Broken Blade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gisela%2C+the+Broken+Blade) in the main. And since we're already playing some angels, [Bruna, the Fading Light](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bruna%2C+the+Fading+Light) might just be the best tool for games that go longer. Lastly, [Thalia, Heretic Cathar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thalia%2C+Heretic+Cathar) is also pretty good in the mirror!
