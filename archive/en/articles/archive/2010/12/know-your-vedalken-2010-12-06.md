Know Your Vedalken
==================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 6, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












There are Vedalken on Mirrodin, which means that they show up in both *Mirrodin* and *Scars of Mirrodin.* But they exist in other planes, sometimes in radically different forms. So they've been in a lot of sets.


How many of these Vedalken can you identify?
