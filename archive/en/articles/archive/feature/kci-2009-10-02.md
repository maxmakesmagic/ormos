![Krark-Clan Ironworks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Krark-Clan+Ironworks)Artifacts were the word of the day for Standard at the World Championships in 2004; Affinity was all the rage. Manuel Bevand opted for a different plan of attack, eschewing the red zone entirely for a combo-based artifact deck. His build centered around generating fast artifact mana, funneling it all into [Krark-Clan Ironworks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krark-Clan+Ironworks), then generating even more mana with [Myr Incubator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Incubator) and ending the game with a fiery [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) or [Goblin Charbelcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Charbelcher) activation. For more on his performance, read the official coverage from [the event archive](/en/events/coverage/nuijten-nassif-dominate-worlds).
