Magic at Gen Con
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on July 22, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Gen Con is fast approaching and **Magic: The Gathering** will have a giant presence this year, and we aren’t just talking about the oversized **Magic** game that will be featured each day! 


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/gencon_logo.jpg)With a plethora of cool foil promo cards, a photo scavenger hunt, limited advanced sales of *From the Vault: Relics* (details below), a **Duels of the Planeswalkers**  lounge, **Magic**  tournaments, and other activities, this Gen Con will be one to remember! The action starts Thursday, August 5 in Indianapolis.


### Magic Highlights


* [**Magic Online** Live](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/012510) event leading to **Worlds** in Chiba
* [Vintage Championship](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=events/magic/vintagechamps): Friday, 10 a.m.
* [Legacy Championship](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/legacychamps): Saturday, 10 a.m.
* *Zendikar* Block Championship: Thursday, 5 p.m.
* **Duels of the Planeswalkers** Lounge: Powered by Alienware Thursday-Saturday, 10 a.m. – 6 p.m. and Sunday 10 a.m. – 4 p.m.
* Hands-on demo of **Magic: The Gathering – Tactics**
* Multiplayer Massive **Magic**, featuring Wizards R&D members Tom LaPille and Mark Gottlieb playing with members of the audience, plus head judge Sheldon Menery and master of ceremonies Richard Hagon. Thursday-Saturday, 11 a.m. - 12 p.m. and 3 - 4 p.m.; Sunday 11 a.m. - 12 p.m.
* Pro Tour–Amsterdam Qualifier: Saturday, 11 a.m.
* Mega **FNM**: Friday, 7 p.m.
* Grand Prix Trial: Friday, 9 p.m.
* Multiplayer events all weekend: *Planechase*  (Limited), *Archenemy*  (Limited and Constructed), Two-Headed Giant (Limited) and EDH!

### *From the Vault: Relics* Convention Promotion




|  |
| --- |
|  |

Wizards of the Coast will once again be selling the popular From the Vault series at Gen Con 2010. A limited quantity of the new set **Magic: The Gathering** 
*From the Vault: Relics* card boxes will be sold at Gen Con Indy 2010.


* Only 400 units are for sale for the duration of the show, 100 boxes will be sold each day.
* Attendees wishing to purchase *From the Vault: Relics* are required obtain a ticket. The ticket card allows you to purchase *From the Vault: Relics*.
* 100 tickets will be handed out each day from 10 - 11 a.m.
* Limit **one (1)** ticket per person.
* Tickets can be obtained from any one of 4 Wizards of the Coast representatives wearing a red FTV t-shirt. Representatives can be found in and around the TCG Hall/Hall G and the Exhibition Hall.
* Tickets can be redeemed for *From the Vault: Relics*  at the **Wizards of the Coast Booth** 1721 beginning at 1 p.m. and ending at the close of the exhibit hall each day. You must turn in your ticket in order to purchase your copy of *From the Vault: Relics*. No Ticket, No Purchase, No Exceptions
* Tickets must be redeemed the day they are issued. Tickets will not be allowed to roll over to the following day.
* The limit for *From the Vault: Relics*  is one per person for the duration of the show. Attendee badges will be marked to verify purchase. Badges should be presented out of the badge holder to help in this process.
* *From the Vault: Relics*  will retail for MSRP ($34.99) plus applicable IN state sales tax.
* All retail exclusives are available in limited quantities, while supplies last.
* **All sales are debit or credit card only; and all sales are final.**

### Magic Artists


Noted Magic card artists Steve Prescott and Kekai Kotaki will be in the TCG Hall Artists Booth signing cards. Steve's work has been featured on **Magic** cards since *Time Spiral* block and some of his most recognizable pieces include [Rhys the Exiled](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhys+the+Exiled), [Rhys the Redeemed](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhys+the+Redeemed), [Knollspine Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knollspine+Dragon), [Berserk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Berserk+)  from *From the Vault: Exiled*, and the new M11 card [Fauna Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman). Kekai's art was featured prominently in the *Zendikar* block, including [Lorthos, the Tidemaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lorthos%2C+the+Tidemaker), [Shepherd of the Lost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shepherd+of+the+Lost), [Lord of Shatterskull Pass](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lord+of+Shatterskull+Pass).
