---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/ptogw/ptogw_t8_brown.jpg)


### Andrew Brown


**Age:** 23


**Hometown:** Los Angeles, California


**Occupation:** Game Designer


**Limited Record:** 4-2


**Constructed Record:** 8-1-1


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir* 18th Place


Grand Prix Denver Champion 2015


Silver Pro


**Did You Team up with anyone to practice for this Pro Tour? Who?**


Team East West Bowl


Special shouts to Eugene Hwang and Ben Weitz


**What Modern deck are you playing and why?**


Eldraggo [Blue-Red Eldrazi]


It uses [Eye of Ugin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eye+of+Ugin) to power out powerful starts, plus [Vile Aggregate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vile+Aggregate) = [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf).


**Are you on the side of the Eldrazi or the Zendikari?**


Eldrazi, not close. Get dunked Zendikar clowns.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**


Dream come true, and validation for countless hours of play.
