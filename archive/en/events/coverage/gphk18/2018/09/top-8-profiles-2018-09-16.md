Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 16, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











Only 8 players out of 908 remained standing after fifteen rounds of Swiss! Let's get to know them a little, and have them tell us about their amazing Modern weekend!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gphk18/gpHK2018_Top8_1UtsunomiyaTakumi.jpg)


**Name:** Takumi Utsunomiya


**Hometown:** Saitama, Japan


**Age:** 23


**Occupation:** Student


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


I participated in Pro Tour *Dominaria* and Pro Tour 25th Anniversary.


**Day 1 Record:** 8 - 0 (2 byes)


**Day 2 Record:** 4 - 1 - 2


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


BridgeVine. It is a very fast deck.


**What is your favorite memory of the weekend?**


Winning Round 13 to secure the Top 8.


**What is your favorite previewed card from *Guilds of Ravnica*?**


Assassin's Trophy. It's the best card for Bridge Vine.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gphk18/gpHK2018_Top8_4ShotaTakao.jpg)


**Name:** Shota Takao


**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan


**Age:** 29


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


4 other Grand Prix Top 8s


**Day 1 Record:** 8 - 0 (2 byes)


**Day 2 Record:** 4 - 2 - 1


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Jeskai Tempo. I love decks with “clock” and “permission”.


**What is your favorite memory of the weekend?**


The typhoon!


**What is your favorite previewed card from *Guilds of Ravnica*?**


Goblin Cratermaker. It's similar to [Abrade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrade), and it's a great card!




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gphk18/gpHK2018_Top8_5TanLunYi.jpg)


**Name:** Tan Lun Yi


**Hometown:** Singapore


**Age:** 29


**Occupation:** Healthcare Executive


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Competed in Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation*.


**Day 1 Record:** 6 - 2 (1 bye)


**Day 2 Record:** 6 - 0 - 1


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


5-Color Humans. The deck is strong, and it was recommended to me by my friend, David Tan. Thanks!


**What is your favorite memory of the weekend?**


Beating [Hardened Scales](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hardened+Scales) Affinity in Round 9 to make Day 2. The matchup is brutal for 5-Color Humans.


**What is your favorite previewed card from *Guilds of Ravnica*?**


Assassin's Trophy. I'm a fan of Black-Green decks in both Standard and Modern.
