Grand Prix Amsterdam 2017
=========================




Five 9-0 players, none of whom are running Aetherworks Marvel, lead the Grand Prix Amsterdam standings overnight.
















Grand Prix Amsterdam 2017
-------------------------




* [DAY 1 COVERAGE](#tabs-0)


DAY 1 COVERAGE



Earlier this morning, 1,170 players arrived at the RAI in Amsterdam to show off their skills and decks in Standard. After nine rounds of Swiss, 376 of them made the cut for Day 2, including five players at 9-0, pictured above. From left to right, they are:


-Allard Stellingwerf (Red-Green Energy Gods)  

-Petr Sochurek (Four-color Vehicles)  

-Micheal Maurici (Esper Aggro)  

-Thomas Hendriks (Black-Green Delirium)  

-Samuel Vuillot (Four-color Vehicles)


Tune back to the livestream tomorrow at 9 a.m. local time (CEST)—-thats 12 a.m. PT/3 a.m. ET/7 a.m. UTC—-for six more rounds of Swiss and the Top 8 of Grand Prix Amsterdam 2017!
