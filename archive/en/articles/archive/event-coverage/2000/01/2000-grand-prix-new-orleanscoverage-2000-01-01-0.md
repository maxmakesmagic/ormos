### Magic: The Gathering® Pro Tour Barcelona Qualifier


January 5-7, 2001


**The Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to the Pro TourTM.** 


**WHERE**  

 New Orleans Morial Convention Center  

 900 Convention Center Blvd.  

 New Orleans, LA  

 (504) 582-3000


**WHEN**  

 January 5-7, 2001  

 Side events begin Friday evening.


**COST**  

 $25 (Advance registration and on site)


For advance registration, contact **Event Horizons at (713) 569-3936**. Advance registration will be available until 5:00 P.M. on Wednesday, January 3, 2001. For hotel reservations, call the Holiday Inn Select Convention Center at (504) 524-1881. Ask for Gena Boudreaux. Mention "Grand Prix-New Orleans" for $99/night rate. Players have until **December 5, 2000** to get this rate.


For complete Grand Prix-New Orleans information, please visit <http://www.GRANDPRIXNEWORLEANS.com>


**TOURNAMENT**
* **Divisions**
*The main tournament at Grand Prix-New Orleans is open to **Magic**® players of all ages. In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher in that Grand Prix. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCITM player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*
* **Tournament Format**  

 The format for Grand Prix-New Orleans will be *Invasion*TM Block Limited-format, with day 2 Booster Draft. All DCI Limited-format rules and **Magic** Floor Rules announced on September 1, 2000, along with all subsequent updates, will be enforced.
* **Tournament Structure**  

 The structure for Grand Prix-New Orleans will be modified Swiss-style (60-minute rounds, number of rounds determined by number of participants), with the Top 64 players advancing to the second day of competition for prizes and invitations.
* **Tournament Byes** *(Byes are not cumulative throughout the season or year)*  

 Byes will be given to players in the Grand Prix based on the following criteria: 
 ***Third-Round Byes*** 


	+ Players with 24 or more professional points in the New York 2000 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Limited rating of 2025 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI ratings published approximately six weeks prior to the Grand Prix.***Second-Round Byes*** 


	+ Players with 12 or more professional points in the New York 2000 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Limited rating of 1900 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI ratings published approximately six weeks prior to the Grand Prix.***First-Round Byes*** 


	+ Players with 6 or more professional points in the New York 2000 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Limited rating of 1800 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI ratings published approximately six weeks prior to the Grand Prix.

**PAYOUT**  

 Grand Prix-New Orleans will feature the standard "main tournament" prize schedule as well as a special "1700 division" prize schedule. DCI members who have a lifetime highest Limited rating of no greater than 1700 will be eligible for the 1700 division. The 1700 division prizes will be paid to the highest-finishing players *regardless of their standing in the Grand Prix.*
