Wallpaper of the Week: Havengul Lich
====================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 3, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











This week's wallpaper features James Ryman's undead [Havengul Lich](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Havengul+Lich), from *[Dark Ascension](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/darkascension)*. And we'd like to call your attention to the new wallpapers dimensions we're offering! The new ones are 1920x1080 (which is widescreen and will also work for 1366x768), 1024x1024 (for iPad), and 851x315, for Facebook covers. That's for those of you that have switched to the Facebook timeline look! Enjoy.
