Recommended Restaurants in New York
===================================



 Posted in **The Week That Was**
 on November 30, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall




 Brian David-Marshall is a New Yorkâ€“based game designer who has been involved with Magic since 1994, when he started organizing tournaments and ran a Manhattan game store. If you're not a New York native (or just want to eat at Jon Finkel's favorite stops), here's a guide for while you're in town for the 2007 World Championships.

| Lombardis | 32 Spring Street (btwn Mulberry and Mott)  | (212) 941-7994 | Little Italy | $ | Best pizza in NYC. Reservation recommended. |
| Johns Pizzeria | 278 Bleecker Street (btwn 6th and 7th Avenue) | (212) 243-1680  | West Village | $$ | 2nd best pizza in NYC. Original location. |
| H&H Bagels | 2239 Broadway (on W 80th Street) | (212) 595-8000  | Upper West Side | $ | Best bagel in NYC. 24-7. Original location. |
| Zabars | 245 W 80th Street (on Broadway) | (212) 787-2000  | Upper West Side | $ - $$$ | World famous gourmet grocery with great coffee. |

| RUB | 208 W 23rd St (btwn 7th and 8th Avenue) | (212) 524-4300  | Chelsea | $$ | One of our favorite BBQ places. Great pulled pork.  |
