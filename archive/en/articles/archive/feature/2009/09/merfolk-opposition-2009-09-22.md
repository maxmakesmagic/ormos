![Opposition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Opposition)Alex Borteh made the Top 8 of U.S. Nationals, then a few months later repeated the feat at the World Championships with his innovative Merfolk Opposition deck. Early Merfolk backed by a smattering of counters allowed him to control the battlefield. His end game featured [Opposition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opposition) to lock his opponent out of the game. [Read more about the event in the official event coverage archives](http://www.wizards.com/sideboard/event.asp?event=Worlds2001).
