Round 14: A Floozie with an Oozie
=================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 11, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











### Eric Froehlich (Ooze) vs. Christopher Wolf (Five-Color Control)


Christopher Wolf's first feature match ever was in [Round 9](/node/348596) of this very tournament. And here he is again! He's sitting at third in the standings after thirteen rounds. His opponent, Eric Froehlich, is at the top, although they have identical 11-2 records.


**Game 1**


Wolf started with a [Vivid Marsh](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Marsh) and a [Sunken Ruins](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunken+Ruins) before casting a [Wall of Omens](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens) and sitting back in his chair. Froehlich contented himself with playing lands for the first few turns, since Wolf didn't appear to be in any hurry.


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds10/r14_froehlich_wolf.jpg)  
*Newcomer Christopher Wolf and old-timer Eric Froehlich (left to right, the monitor display not withstanding).*


Froehlich's first play was a [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage) that got [Mana Leak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak)ed, after which Wolf played his third Vivid land of the game. A second [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage) hit the board and killed a [Vivid Creek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Creek), but not before Wolf used its mana to draw cards with [Esper Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Esper+Charm). Froehlich [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize)d away a [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile), leaving [Cruel Ultimatum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Ultimatum).


Froehlich played his deck's namesake: [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze)


[![](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Necrotic+Ooze)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze)
The Ooze killed a [Vivid Meadow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Meadow) with the [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage)'s ability and took up residence in Froehlich's graveyard. Another [NecroticÂ Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic%C2%A0Ooze) was cast and sacrificed for the same effect. Froehlich cast [Acidic Slime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime) to destroy [Cascade Bluffs](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cascade+Bluffs). [Reveillark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reveillark) brought back both [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage)s, which promptly sacrificed themselves to destroy [Vivid Marsh](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Marsh) and [Vivid Creek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Creek).


Wolf cast [Volcanic Fallout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Fallout) to deal with the [Acidic Slime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime).


Froehlich asked how many cards Wolf was holding, and decided that "four" was a safe answer. He cast [Thornling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thornling), and it was not countered. The next turn, it attacked, killing a [Wall of Omens](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens). [Primal Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Command) dealt with a [Reflecting Pool](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reflecting+Pool) temporarily, and once it was back, Froehlich used a new [Acidic Slime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime) to kill it. The [Thornling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thornling) killed Wolf's remaining [Wall of Omens](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens), and Wolf was down to six lands and no creatures.


A Froehlich [Tectonic Edge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tectonic+Edge) reduced that to five lands, but it was still enough to [Cryptic Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command) the [Thornling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thornling) back to Froehlich's hand after he'd used too much mana to recast it. The [Acidic Slime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime) still got in there, though, and it was joined by a [Devoted Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devoted+Druid).


Wolf looked through his graveyard, possibly checking on how many lands he'd gone through and wondering if he'd ever have enough mana for the [Cruel Ultimatum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Ultimatum). Froehlich used a [Makeshift Mannequin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Makeshift+Mannequin) to bring back an [Acidic Slime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime) and kept Wolf's lands-in-play count down to five. When the creatures kept coming, Wolf had had enough.


Froehlich 1, Wolf 0


**Game 2**


Wolf chose to go first and led with a [Vivid Creek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Creek). Since it entered the battlefield tapped, he had no way to stop Froehlich's first-turn [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), which stripped an [Esper Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Esper+Charm). Froehlich allowed a [Mana Leak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak) to stay, so he wasn't surprised when his turn-two [Devoted Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devoted+Druid) didn't resolve. On the third turn, Froehlich kept up the pressure on Wolf's hand, [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)ing a [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) away and leaving two [Cryptic Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command)s.


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds10/r14_froehlich.jpg)  
*The man they call E-Fro ....*


Froehlich's fourth-turn [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze) got in for 4 on turn five, after which Froehlich tried a [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage). Wolf thought about his options and decided to counter it and draw a card. The next turn, the Ooze again attacked (it is, after all, a 4/3) and Froehlich used [Tectonic Edge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tectonic+Edge) to reduce Wolf's array of Vivid lands by one. At the end of Froehlich's turn, Wolf bounced the [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze) with the second [Cryptic Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command) that Froehlich had seen. But when Froehlich, on his next turn, tried a [Primal Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Command), it turned out that Wold had one more [Cryptic Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command) available.


Wolf played a land that could do more that "just" give him any land he wanted, but the [Celestial Colonnade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Celestial+Colonnade) fell victim to a [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage). A pair of [Esper Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Esper+Charm)s refilled Wolf's hand, but he didn't counter Froehlich's [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze) when he replayed it. Instead, he hit it with [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile). Froehlich used the [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage)'s borrowed ability to kill a land and get the [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze) into the graveyard instead of losing it to the exile zone.


Both players had an impressive assortment of land, but Froehlich didn't have enough mana to force his [Acidic Slime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime) through [Mana Leak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak). Wolf got a [Wall of Omens](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens) out, then, on Froehlich's Upkeep, cast [Vendilion Clique](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique). He saw [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage), [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise), and Twisted Mire. After a bit of thought, he decided not to make Froehlich trade either of those cards away. Froehlich played the Mage, but saved the [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) for the next turn.


Wolf's [Vendilion Clique](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique) started to attack through the air which Wolf bolstered his defenses with a second [Wall of Omens](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens) and a [Pithing Needle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pithing+Needle). The [Pithing Needle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pithing+Needle) immediately died to an [Acidic Slime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime), and Froehlich blocked the [Vendilion Clique](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique) with his [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise). But then Wolf cast [Cruel Ultimatum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Ultimatum), and Froehlich saw no point in continuing the game.


Froehlich 1, Wolf 1


**Game 3**


Froehlich led off and got a [Fauna Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman) on the table before Wolf's [Vivid Meadow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Meadow) had even untapped. Wolf waited until Froehlich's Upkeep before sending the Elf on a [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile), and Froehlich used his turn to not only replace the [Fauna Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman) but play a [Tectonic Edge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tectonic+Edge) to threaten Wolf's five-color land base. By waiting until Froehlich's Upkeep, Wolf kept his opponent from using the land that turn, since [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) gives a land that's tapped.


Froehlich tried an [Acidic Slime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime),to whittle down Wolf's four lands, but it got [Mana Leak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak)ed. His [Tectonic Edge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tectonic+Edge) took out a [Vivid Meadow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Meadow), but Wolf [Esper Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Esper+Charm)ed himself a couple of cards.


Froehlich started to employ the awesome power of the [Fauna Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman), discarding a [Quillspike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quillspike) for a [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage), which went down to a [Cryptic Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command). Wolf dissuaded any attacks from the [Fauna Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman) by casting a [Wall of Omens](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens), but Froehlich had other shamanic plans. He discarded a [Reveillark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reveillark) for another [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage).


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds10/r14_wolf.jpg)  
*... and the man they call C-Wolf. (Disclaimer: No one actually calls him C-Wolf ... yet.)*


The new [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage) hit the table and destroyed a [Vivid Creek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Creek). But the [Vivid Creek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vivid+Creek) gave Wolf the mana for a [Vendilion Clique](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique), and Froehlich had to reveal [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze), [Murmuring Bosk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Murmuring+Bosk), [Fauna Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman), and [Makeshift Mannequin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Makeshift+Mannequin). The Mannequin went away.


Wolf attacked with his [Vendilion Clique](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vendilion+Clique), and Froehlich disposed of his superfluous [Fauna Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman) for a [Devoted Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devoted+Druid). He played the [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze), using all of his mana. Wolf [Esper Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Esper+Charm)ed to get two cards, leaving two lands untapped, but if he had a [Mana Leak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak), he did not use it.


Froehlich passed the turn and read [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze). He asked a judge how much time was left, and the answer was nine minutes, forty seconds. Froehlich sent the [Devoted Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devoted+Druid) in his hand away and replaced it with a [Molten-Tail Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten-Tail+Masticore). Wolf took a moment to glance at the [Quillspike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quillspike) in Froehlich's graveyard.


Wolf slumped in his chair, because he knew the end was near. Froehlich tapped the [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze) to add Green Mana to his pool, then added a -1/-1 counter to [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze) to untap it, both using the ability of the [Devoted Druid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devoted+Druid). Then he used the mana and the [Quillspike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quillspike)'s ability to remove the -1/-1 counter and give the [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze) +3/+3. Then he did it again, a million times.


[![](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Devoted+Druid)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devoted+Druid) [![](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Quillspike)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quillspike)
Armed with an arbitrarily large [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze), Froehlich now discarded [Molten-Tail Masticore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten-Tail+Masticore) to get a [Thornling](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thornling). He used the Masticore's ability to deal four damage to Wolf's lone [Wall of Omens](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens), and sent the [Necrotic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necrotic+Ooze) across for a win.


Froehlich 2, Wolf 1
