Sealed Deck: Hugo Machado
=========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 18, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_103.png)
By Rui Oliveira











Did you ever open a Sealed deck, or review or draft cards, and thought "Now, why didn't I get a second Hallowed Healer? It's a common!" Well, strange things happen when you play Sealed deck with only one set. Since all your cards come from the same pool, the print-run Gods sometime play cruel tricks on you. But they can also help you out.


Some players get decks with very few creatures, others get a boatload of extra copies of bad cards. Sometimes you get a couple of duplicate commons. Sometimes you even get three copies of one common. What don't you get is the deck Hugo Machado got.


Prepare to enter the Sealed deck twilight zone....

No, it's not a typo. Yes, you read it right. Nine tripled commons, four doubled commons, making a grand total of twenty-two repeated cards (including Chainflinger, Hallowed Healer, Ghastly Demise and Flame Burst). In case you are wondering here is what Hugo Machado built with it. As I type this, he is heading into round six at 5-0 (with one bye) with a 8-2 game score.
