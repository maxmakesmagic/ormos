With the addition of a number of powerful new black cards over the past few sets, many players are looking forward to the return of Mono-Black Control. Powerful creatures clean up the field after early removal keeps your life total high! Here's a first look at the deck from Frank Lepore, who posted it in an article on TCGPlayer.com.
