Mono-black Control has been a popular archetype even before it entered the mainstream with the release of the set *Torment* from the *Odyssey* block. Dunkle\_stille brings the latest update to the deck for use in recent **Magic Online** Premier Events. The deck was good enough to make the Top 4 of the event. With [Innocent Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood), [Hymn to Tourach](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hymn+to+Tourach), [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), [Smallpox](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smallpox), and heavy hitters like [Tombstalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tombstalker) and [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade), that should come as no surprise!
