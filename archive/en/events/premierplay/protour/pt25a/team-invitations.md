Invited Teams
-------------


[Pro Tour 25th Anniversary Event Information](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/premierplay/protour/pt25a)


Team Invitations to Pro Tour 25th Anniversary are awarded in the following manner:


1) The team members from the top sixteen (16) teams in the 2017-2018 Pro Tour Team Series Leaderboard after Pro Tour *Dominaria*.


1a) Players who qualify via this method must have their team captains submit to [premierplay@wizards.com](mailto:premierplay@wizards.com) the two teams of three that will be comprise of their Team Series roster no later than July 5, 2018.


2) The three (3) team members from a top four (4) finishing team plus any team members from a team that finishes with 36 or more match points from each team-format Grand Prix that awards invitations to Pro Tour 25th Anniversary.


2a) If one of the three team members from a top four (4) finishing team that qualifies for Pro Tour 25th Anniversary is on a Pro Tour Team Series roster, that player may instead choose to compete in a team of three comprised only of other members of their Pro Tour Team Series roster. If they choose to do this, the remaining team members from that top four (4) finishing team will be removed from the Team Invitations List and added to the Individuals Invitation List.


3) The three (3) team members from the winning team from a Team Pro Tour Qualifier (held at Grand Prix) that awards invitations to Pro Tour 25th Anniversary.


3a) If one of the three team members from a Team Pro Tour Qualifier (held at Grand Prix) winning team that qualifies for Pro Tour 25th Anniversary is on a Pro Tour Team Series roster, that player may instead choose to compete in a team of three comprised only of other members of their Pro Tour Team Series roster. If they choose to do this, the remaining team members from that winning team will be removed from the Team Invitations List and added to the Individuals Invitation List.


4) The three (3) team members from a top two (2) finishing team from each Team-format Regional Pro Tour Qualifier that awards invitations to Pro Tour 25th Anniversary.


Note: The Pro Tour invitation criteria listed above do not generate a pass down of invitations to other players.


The Individuals Invitation list will be posted in its entirety shortly after the conclusion of Pro Tour *Dominaria*.


If you believe that your finish at a Grand Prix, Pro Tour Qualifier held at Grand Prix, or Regional Pro Tour Qualifier is in error, please visit <http://www.wizards.com/Magic/PlaneswalkerPoints>. After logging in, click the "Appeals Form" link at the bottom of the page and fill out the form.
