Saturday, 4:45 p.m. - Looking into the LCQ
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 17, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 






Fifteen players are here in the main event today thanks to rattling off five straight wins in the viciously hard-to-win contest that is the Last Chance Qualifier. While two players relied on the vagaries of Sealed Deck, the other thirteen were giving their possible Nationals Standard decks a run out. Here's how the west was won:




|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| 
Tempered Steel

**Grinder 1** - Marcio Araujo got the ball rolling with [Tempered Steel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tempered+Steel). You know the drill - [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite), [Glint Hawk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk), [Glint Hawk Idol](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk+Idol), [Steel Overseer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steel+Overseer)... | 
Pyromancer Ascension

**Grinder 2** - Diogo Oliveira won with [Pyromancer Ascension](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer+Ascension). Lightning Bursts and [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)s stack up to a lot of damage when you're copying them over and over. |
| 
Hero of Oxid Ridge

**Grinder 3** - Guilherme Moura took down grinder 3 with mono-red, featuring the card that Paul Rietzl wanted to see so badly in the final of Pro Tour Paris, [Hero of Oxid Ridge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero+of+Oxid+Ridge). Lots of burn spells got the job done for Guilherme. | 
Grave Titan

**Grinder 4** - Another Guilherme, this time Guilherme Cruz, winning with a Blue-Black control list that looks like Worlds 2010. [Grave Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grave+Titan) very prominent, ways to sweep the board, spot removal, [Into the Roil](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Into+the+Roil), plus things like [Solemn Simulacrum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solemn+Simulacrum) and a singleton [Karn Liberated](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karn+Liberated). |
| 
Splinter Twin

**Grinder 5** - The first [Splinter Twin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Splinter+Twin) to get the job done, in the hands of Andr√© Franco, who we featured earlier in the day. | 
Chandra, the Firebrand

**Grinder 6** - Hugo Terra brings us our first sighting of [Plated Geopede](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Geopede), making a welcome reappearance at the top tables in a mono-Red concoction featuring [Chandra, the Firebrand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+the+Firebrand). |

**Grinder 7** - [Birthing Pod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod) decks are always interesting, so here's the winning list from grinder 7, courtesy of Rafael Quadros:
