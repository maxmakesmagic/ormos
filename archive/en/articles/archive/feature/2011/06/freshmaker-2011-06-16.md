For Christian Valenti, Pro Tour Nagoya marked a move from the ranks of the StarCityGames.com Open Series to the grand stage of the Pro Tour. His red-green deck drew the notice of the coverage team and stood out against a field of White Weenie [Tempered Steel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tempered+Steel) decks. Rather than try to out-aggro his opponents, however, Christian wanted to control the pace of the game while building his mana base until powerful elbow-drops like [Karn Liberated](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Karn+Liberated) or [Red Sun's Zenith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Red+Sun%27s+Zenith) could take over.
