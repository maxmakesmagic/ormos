Saint-Raphael, France brings us our second **Magic** Game Day deck of the week! Adrien Peudecoeur used this innovative, mostly Naya build to make the Top 8. [Magma Phoenix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magma+Phoenix) and a smattering of spot-removal burn spells held the ground while [Swans of Bryn Argoll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swans+of+Bryn+Argoll) attacked in the air. And if Adrien needed more cards to get by? A timely [Intimidation Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intimidation+Bolt) on the 4/3 flyer on the opponent's turn not only stopped the attack, but drew three cards!
