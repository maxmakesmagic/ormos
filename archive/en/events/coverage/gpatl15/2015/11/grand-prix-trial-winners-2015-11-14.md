Grand Prix Trial Winners
========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 14, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











At a large event like this, there are always plenty of things to do Friday night—the night before the mayhem begins. Though there's tons of fun and low-stakes *Magic* to be played, the Grand Prix Trials are a great way to warm up for the Grand Prix, while earning precious byes for the next day. Below are the decklists from all the "grinder" winners last night. Chances are, they're sleeping in today—and don't have to be ready to battle until Round 3.


Here are the Standard Trial winners:
