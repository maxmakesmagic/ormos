Looking for a fun deck for Standard? Ken Yukuhiro took this interesting list to a solid finish at Pro Tour-Kyoto. He sought to abuse Mistmeadow Witch with numerous comes-into-play creatures like [Reveillark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reveillark), [Sower of Temptation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sower+of+Temptation), and [Mulldrifter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter). Once you get the Witch going, your creatures are virtually unkillable, providing you with bonus after bonus as they flip in and out of play!
