2018 US Nationals Top 8 Profiles
================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 1, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_meghanwolff_0.jpg)
By Meghan Wolff




 Meghan is one half of the podcast Magic the Amateuring and an adjunct professor at Tolarian Community College. She loves Limited, likes Modern, and dips her toes into each Standard season. She's decidedly blue and is the #1 hater of Siege Rhino in the Multiverse. 






Congratulations to the Top 8 players from US Nationals 2018!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/2018natus/natgroup.jpg)




---

**Name:** Kevin Jones


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/2018natus/t8_jones.jpg)


**Age:** 30


**Hometown:** Kingston, NY


Occupation: content producer @ The Bearded Dragon Games


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


GP Montreal Champion 2017


US Nationals Team 2016


2 SCG wins!


PT Dominaria Top 17!


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Jeskai Control - good against UB and Red Decks. Ryoichi Tamada is the best.


**What was the best card in your main deck and why?**


[Glimmer of Genius](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glimmer+of+Genius) - helps hit land drops


[Teferi, Hero of Dominaria](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Hero+of+Dominaria) - does everything


**What card from Magic 2019 are you most excited for?**


Nicol Bolas!


**What would winning US Nationals mean to you?**


It would be fulfilling one of my Magic career goals!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/2018natus/t8_andrus.jpg)


**Name:** Justin Andrus


**Age:** 29


**Hometown:** St. Louis


Occupation: Financial analyst


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


two for two on GPs for Day 2


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Esper Control. I enjoy value decks with toolbox styles. There's a lot of cheap card filter.


**What was the best card in your main deck and why?**


[Yahenni's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yahenni%27s+Expertise). 4 mana board wipe combos with [Doomfall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomfall) for a strong tempo play


**What card from Magic 2019 are you most excited for?**


Bolas. Value town.


**What would winning US Nationals mean to you?**


I'd love to win with my brew. I've been brewing for years and this is my best deck.
