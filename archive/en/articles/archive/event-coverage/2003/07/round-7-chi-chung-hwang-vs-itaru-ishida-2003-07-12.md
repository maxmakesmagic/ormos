Round 7: Chi-Chung Hwang vs. Itaru Ishida
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 12, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall




 Brian David-Marshall is a New York–based game designer who has been involved with Magic since 1994, when he started organizing tournaments and ran a Manhattan game store. Since then, he has been a judge, a player, and one of the longest-tenured columnists on DailyMTG.com, as he enters his second decade writing for the site. He is also the Pro Tour Historian and one of the commentators for the Pro Tour. 






### Game 1

The final match of the day was a classic battle between Chi-Chung with Blue Green and Japanese star Itaru Ishida with the Red-Green beats. Each player made traditional openings with [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) on one side and a Llanowar Elf on the other. [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation) for two [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) and a [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) put the early game in Chi-Chung's favor.

![Roar of the Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Roar+of+the+Wurm)Ishida was undaunted and got in for one with his Elf and played a [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). Chi-Chang had no attack with Walla and played an [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) and passed turn with [Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Logic) mana up. When Ishida attempted to [Volcanic Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer) the [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba), Chi-Chung did just that, which cleared the way for another [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel).Chi-Chung played his fourth land and [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm). The game started to shift toward Ishida when he enchanted his [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) with [Elephant Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide) and swung into the Wurm but Hwang chumped with his Walla.

He got in for six with his token and made another. [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) chump blocked the enchanted [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) and Ishida made a [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). He double blocked a [Roar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar) with his second [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) and the Centaur and fell to a precarious four. Hwang Speculated for two [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and a [Ray of Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation) and played another [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel).

Ishida attacked with the [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) and Centaur. Hwang disenchanted the Guide and had no blocks. Ishida discarded a [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption)—killing the [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel)—and a basking Rootwalla. All told, Chi-Chung took nine. He took three more when he flashed back [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and went to two to flash back another. He was looking for a [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder). [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) did not find it for him either and he passed the turn back to Ishida with four creatures and only a lone [Roar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar) to hold them off.

Chi-Chung – 0 Itaru – 1

### Game 2

Both players kept their openers. Chi had the careful Study double [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) opening and Ishida [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt)ed one of them. [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) came down on turn two and Ishida [Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hammer)ed it. He took three from the Rootwalla and Chi-Chung passed back the turn holding [Unsummon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsummon) for the [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) that Ishida played.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpkyo03/a904.jpg)He took three more from the Walla and [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) came down. Ishida flashed back the [Call](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call) and chose not to block the Rootwalla on the next turn. The Walla was [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt)ed on the following turn and when Chi-Chung ripped a [Ravenous Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth) a turn later it was 'killed' with a [Threaten](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Threaten).

The [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) ate a [Call](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call) token in combat but a flashed back [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) finished it off. Ishida's [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) continued to attack and was eventually joined by a Centaur. Chi ripped a [Ravenous Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth) but Ishida had the [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption). He followed up with a [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer).

Chi had been holding back land for a [Turbulent Dreams](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turbulent+Dreams) in hand but when he drew a [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation) and could not cast it and flash back a [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) that plan seemed less wise. He found two [Roar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar)s and a [Ray of Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation) and used [Turbulent Dreams](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turbulent+Dreams) to return the Centaur but it did not matter. The [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer) started to eat away Ishida's graveyard and the game was quickly over.

**Final Result:** Chi-Chung – 0 Itaru – 2
