[Kuldotha Forgemaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kuldotha+Forgemaster) is a surprising hit that has seen play in Standard and in Legacy. Edwin Garcia recently used the five-cost artifact at the StarCityGames.com Standard Open in Los Angeles. What can the Forgemaster forge up in Edwin's deck? All manner of good stuff. [Blightsteel Colossus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blightsteel+Colossus) is a massive whooping stick that no doubt is the principal cause of victories for the deck. [Mindslaver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslaver) is a blast from the past that lets you take your opponent's turn. Finally, in a pinch [Myr Battlesphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Battlesphere) and [Wurmcoil Engine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wurmcoil+Engine) will clutter the battlefield with blockers and gain you life. Read more on the StarCityGames.com Open in the [event coverage](http://www.starcitygames.com/php/news/expandnews.php?Article=21462).
