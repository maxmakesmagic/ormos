Decks of the Week: 3/29/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 29, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Boston played host last weekend to both PAX East and the [**Magic Online** Championship](/en/events/coverage/butakov-tramples-his-way-past-opposition), won by Russia's Dmitriy "Butakov" Butakov! Up the road a ways, Brock Parker returned to the pro stage by winning [Grand Prix Pittsburgh](/en/events/coverage/parker-makes-proud-return-pittsburgh). Congratulations to both! The Modern Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze* qualifier season has wrapped up, but we still have some results coming in. The new Pro Tour *Theros* season is in Standard, so be sure to start paying attention to Standard decks (like the ones linked to below!) to get a grasp on the current metagame. And find one of those nifty Standard PTQs near you with a[this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros)! Good luck; have fun!
