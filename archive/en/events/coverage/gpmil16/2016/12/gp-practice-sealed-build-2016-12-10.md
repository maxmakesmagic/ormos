GP Practice Sealed Build
========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 10, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro












 It's time to get crackin', folks—pack-crackin' that is. We've been learning a lot throughout this *Kaladesh* Limited format, and many things have changed. But some things have stayed the same—value, synergy, and [Renegade Freighter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renegade+Freighter).



Sadly, our practice Sealed pool has no such Freighters, and precious little value and synergy.



 Throughout the morning I'll be talking with *Magic* pros about how they would build this paltry-powered pool, but you have a leg up. I'm giving you the pool now. How would you build this?




![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmil16/gpmil_sealed_riki.jpg)  
*Head Judge Riki Hayashi can't seem to make heads or tails of it either!*



The rares don't really draw you in too much, and the commons and uncommons want you to split. How about Green-White? It's a few cards short, but seems consistent. The [Unlicensed Disintegration](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unlicensed+Disintegration) and [Whirler Virtuoso](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whirler+Virtuoso) pull you to Red, but there are not many good red cards to go around. And Black brings us two—count em', two—[Midnight Oil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Midnight+Oil) for sweet, sweet card advantage.


Do you try for an ambitiously colored build with [Wild Wanderer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Wanderer) and [Servant of the Conduit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Servant+of+the+Conduit)s to maximize the power?


I'll be getting more opinions soon, but how would you build this sticky wicket?
