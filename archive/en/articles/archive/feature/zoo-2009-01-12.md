In honor of the start of the Pro Tour Qualifier-Honolulu season, Daily Decks is bringing you some of the hottest Extended decks all week long! Sebastian Thaler, who earned a Top 8 playing Extended at the recent Pro Tour-Berlin, took this innovative twist on Zoo to a strong finish in Memphis at Worlds. He teched his build with [Shadow Guildmage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadow+Guildmage) over [Tidehollow Sculler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tidehollow+Sculler), a solid maneuver against a field filled with 1/1s be they Elves, Faeries, or Goblins. To find an Extended PTQ for Honolulu near you, visit [this link](/en/node/640716).
