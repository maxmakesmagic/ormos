Looking for a fun take on Standard? You can always try Standard Singleton! All the cards legal in Standard are legal with one caveat: you can't play more than one copy of anything except for basic lands! That leads to a lot of variety and a lot of deck-building creativity. Karakusk recently went 6-0 with this list during a Standard Singleton Challenge on **Magic Online**! Find out more [here](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/resources.aspx?x=magic/rules/standardsingleton).
