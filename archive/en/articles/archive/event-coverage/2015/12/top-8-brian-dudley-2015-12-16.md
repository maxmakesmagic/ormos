Top 8: Brian Dudley
===================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*by Omeed Dariani*


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/AMCHAMP00/Images/b25.JPG)Sideboard (S): Where are you from?


Milford, Mass.


S: How long have you been playing?


Antiquities


S: How long have you been playing competitively?


Since around Revised.


S: What are your best tournament accomplishments?


Today, top 8 at a 500$ tournament.


S: What are you playing and why?


Modified Flores Black. I removed the Ports, added a maindeck Rapid Decay and tweaked the sideboard a lot.


S: How does it stack up against the field?


Islands are very bad for me. Forests, good.


S: How do you feel about your chances in the Top 8? 


Getting better all the time.


S: What's your favorite card?


Cursed Scroll


S: What's your favorite format? 


Sealed


S: What's your favorite card mechanic?


Shadow


S: What's your favorite movie?


Clerks, Chasing Amy, Mallrats, anything funny.


S: What's your favorite TV show?


The Simpsons


S: What's your favorite food?


Hot Dogs
