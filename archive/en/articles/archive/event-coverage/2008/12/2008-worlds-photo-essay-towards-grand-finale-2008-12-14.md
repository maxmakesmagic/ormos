2008 Worlds Photo Essay: Towards the Grand Finale
=================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 14, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/080314_1954.jpg)
By Craig Gibson and Josh Bennett












![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_01.jpg)*It's not ALL tournaments at Wizards. Here we see Rich Hagon hosting his popular **Magic** Game Show, free to enter, with prizes up to a stack of 1,000 rares.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_02.jpg)*Saturday night Hagon hosted a round table discussion with the biggest names in making **Magic**: Matt Place, Mike Turian, Aaron Forsythe, Mark Rosewater, and of course, Dr. Richard Garfield, Ph.D.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_03.jpg)*"Agents of Artifice," coming to a bookseller near you! Some lucky people got advance copies, like this one, and we will be posting their reviews of it online!*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_04.jpg)*A lonely portrait: Masashi Oiso ponders the mistake that earned him a game loss, losing him the match.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_05.jpg)*Supplemental Ruel Antoine mugs for the camera. Olivier is in the grip of total focus.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_06.jpg)*Kenji Tsumura is bringing dandy back.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_07.jpg)*Shuhei Nakamura clinches 2008 Player of the Year!*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_08.jpg)*"And the last member of our Top 8, in 8th place on tiebreakers... JAMIE PARKE!"*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_09.jpg)*Lord Kenji finally gets his throne.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_10.jpg)*The glittering prizes. (Huge sums of cash, fame, and glory not pictured.)*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_11.jpg)*Top level play requires total concentration.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_12.jpg)*The Command Center: You get to see The Big Board.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_13.jpg)*Head of Japanese language coverage Keita Mori. Women want him, men want to *be* him.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_14.jpg)*It's like Tootsie, except she sets people on fire.*
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/worlds08/Sunday_15.jpg)*And of course, The King.*
