Video Feature: Day Three Wrap
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 30, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/hero/wizardslogo_thumb.jpg)
By Rich Hagon and Brian David-Marshall











The sixth time was the charm for Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa. Playing in his sixth Pro Tour Top 8, the Brazilian superstar defeated Guillaume Matignon of France for his first PT victory. Pro Tour Historian Brian David-Marshall and Statistician Rich Hagon bring Pro Tour-San Juan to a close with a recap of the event and an interview with Damo da Rosa moments after his historic victory.
