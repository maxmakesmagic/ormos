Players Tour Phoenix Top 8 Decklists
====================================



 Posted in **Competitive Gaming**
 on February 9, 2020 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











After a tournament seemingly overrun by the potent new [Lotus Breach deck](https://magic.gg/news/players-tour-phoenix-day-2-pioneer-metagame), the Top 8 of Players Tour Phoenix turned out to represent a diverse slice of the quickly evolving format. Here's the Top 8 lists of the tournament.


* 2 Dimir Inverter
* 2 Lotus Breach
* Mono-Red Aggro
* Sultai Delirium
* Bant Spirits
* White-Blue Control
