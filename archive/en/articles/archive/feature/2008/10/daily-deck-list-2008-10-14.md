Daily Deck List
===============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 14, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Enjoy a trip down **Magic**’s memory lane, find the latest in new tech, discover crazy for-fun concoctions, and do it every weekday! The Daily Deck Lists feature examines all manner of decks from throughout **Magic**’s history through the present day. You’ll find a new list each weekday from a variety of formats (including some you may have never heard of!), perfect for competitive and non-competitive players alike.
