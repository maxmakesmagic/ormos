Junk Tokens
===========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 13, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







If you liked the idea of tokens with [Voice of Resurgence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence) to make the Elementals big, deel on **Magic Online** finished 4–0 in a Standard Daily with a list you might be interested in. 


![Voice of Resurgence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Voice+of+Resurgence)
 deel uses [Call of the Conclave](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Conclave); [Voice of Resurgence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence); [Selesnya Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Selesnya+Charm); [Lingering Souls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lingering+Souls); [Midnight Haunting](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Midnight+Haunting); [Advent of the Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Advent+of+the+Wurm); [Increasing Devotion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Increasing+Devotion); and [Sorin, Lord of Innistrad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sorin%2C+Lord+of+Innistrad) to make tokens, with [Intangible Virtue](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intangible+Virtue); [Collective Blessing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Collective+Blessing); [Gavony Township](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gavony+Township); and the same [Sorin, Lord of Innistrad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sorin%2C+Lord+of+Innistrad)s to make them bigger. In other words, deel makes a huge number of very large tokens. In fact, every card in the deck makes mana, makes tokens, or makes tokens bigger, except for two [Putrefy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Putrefy)s. 


 Aggressive decks are under a ton of pressure to kill this deck, as it won't take long to get all the pieces in play to completely dominate the board. Decks that are relying on creatures like [Thragtusk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thragtusk) to block should also find their defenses overwhelmed. Sweepers are effective but fairly quickly, each card demands its own, and [Lingering Souls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lingering+Souls); [Increasing Devotion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Increasing+Devotion); [Sorin, Lord of Innistrad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sorin%2C+Lord+of+Innistrad); and [Voice of Resurgence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence) are even better. 


 I like the [Renounce the Guilds](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renounce+the+Guilds) in the sideboard, especially since there will be times when you want to sacrifice your own [Voice of Resurgence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence), but I'd definitely want more [Ray of Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation)s, since I see hexproof as one of the more threatening decks for this strategy. I'm also surprised not to see any [Rootborn Defenses](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootborn+Defenses).
