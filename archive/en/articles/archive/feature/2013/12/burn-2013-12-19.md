Burn!!!
=======



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 19, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Modern isn't really where red burn decks are popular, but mmmcgill1232 took this one to a 4–0 victory in a recent Modern Daily Event on **Magic Online**. The interesting thing about this deck is the main deck is clearly just designed to burn the opponent from 20–0 as quickly as possible, but the sideboard is well-tuned in order to allow the deck to slow down the burn if it needs answers for specific metagame threats. Even to the point of splashing blue for a counterspell!

 Lee Sharpe   
 Sr. Business Intelligence Analyst—**Magic Online**
