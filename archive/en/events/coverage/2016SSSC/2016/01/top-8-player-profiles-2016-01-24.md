By Event Coverage Staff












Samuel Black
============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/2016sssc/2016sssc_t8_sam-black.jpg)


**Hometown:** Madison, Wisconsin


**Occupation:** Plaer/Writer for StarCityGames.com


**Booster Draft Archetype and Record:** White-Black-red Control: 3-0


**Standard Archetype and Record:** 4-Color Rally: 2-0-2


**What is your favorite card from *Oath of the Gatewatch*?** 


[Reflector Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reflector+Mage)


**What has been your favorite part about this weekend?**


Drafting!

Gustavo Coraiola
================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/2016sssc/2016sssc_t8_coraiola.jpg)


**Hometown:** Curitiba, Brazil


**Occupation:** Lawyer


**Booster Draft Archetype and Record:** Black Green Good-Stuff: 3-0


**Standard Archetype and Record:** 4-Color Rally: 2-0-2


**What is your favorite card from *Oath of the Gatewatch*?** 


[Hedron Alignment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hedron+Alignment). It's the best card since [Sigiled Starfish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sigiled+Starfish)—the best card ever printed—but with a win condition attached.


**What has been your favorite part about this weekend?**


There was no favorite part. The whole weekend has been perfect.

Archibal Raziel Sánchez Peralta
===============================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/2016sssc/2016sssc_t8_peralta.jpg)


**Hometown:** Mexico City, Mexico


**Occupation:** Logistics on gastronomy courses


**Booster Draft Archetype and Record:** Black-Green Devoid: 2-1


**Standard Archetype and Record:** 4-Color Rally: 3-0-1


**What is your favorite card from *Oath of the Gatewatch*?** 


[Reflector Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reflector+Mage)


**What has been your favorite part about this weekend?**


It's been such a great weekend. Lots of *Magic*, food, friends from other countries. It's been a nice experience, and it has been great preparation for Grand Prix Mexico City.




---


Alex Black
==========


![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/2016sssc/2016sssc_t8_alex-black.jpg)


**Hometown:** Phoenix, Arizona


**Occupation:** Accounting


**Booster Draft Archetype and Record:** White-Blue Tempo Allies: 2-1


**Standard Archetype and Record:** Abzan Aggro: 3-0-1


**What is your favorite card from *Oath of the Gatewatch*?** 


[Inverter of Truth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inverter+of+Truth)s. I'd like to see it find a deck.


**What has been your favorite part about this weekend?**


Making the Top 8. The whole weekend has been fun. The tour of the Wizards offices was interesting, and it was good to get to know everybody at the meet and greet Friday and at dinner Saturday. The near constant opportunities to draft has also been nice.
