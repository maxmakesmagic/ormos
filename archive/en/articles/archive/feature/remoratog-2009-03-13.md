Luis Scott-Vargas is a Pro Tour champion and even managed to Top 8 the recent Pro Tour-Kyoto. But just because he's busy playing in the Top 8 of an event doesn't mean he's done winning on the weekend. He handed this Vintage deck off to his good friend Gerry Thompson, who soon found himself playing the final match for the championship. Featuring the surprisingly good [Mystic Remora](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+Remora) and *Conflux* fattie [Inkwell Leviathan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkwell+Leviathan), Gerry found himself a champion while his pal battled it out in the Top 8.
