Face the Hydra Sneak Peek
=========================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic-archive)
 on October 10, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Nathan Holt and Shawn Kornhauser












Continue your quest down the Hero's Path at *Theros* Game Day on October 19–20. Gather the Hero Cards that you collected from the *Theros* Prerelease and Launch Weekend, and get ready for a brand-new play experience—Face the Hydra! Here, a few players from the **Magic Online**  [Community Cup team](/en/events/coverage/2013-magic-online-community-cup) got a sneak peek at the experience and survived to tell the tale!
