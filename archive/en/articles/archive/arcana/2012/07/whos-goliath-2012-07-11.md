Who's a Goliath?
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on July 11, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Consider [Zombie Goliath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Goliath).


![Zombie Goliath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Zombie+Goliath)
  
At first glance, it's just a zombie. So what makes it a Zombie Giant? Well, take a closer look.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1013_zombiegoliath.jpg)[Zombie Goliath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Goliath) | Art by [E.M. Gist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22E.+M.Gist%22%5D)


See its head? See how there are a couple of things in there? *Those* are regular-size zombies. They're riding in the [Zombie Goliath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Goliath)'s cranium! Now the flavor text makes more sense:
