From the land of Japan comes Erika Sudou’s Top 8 [Rampant Growth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth) control deck. Featuring some of the game’s most powerful spells, the deck is able to power them onto the table early thanks to a triple set of both [Rampant Growth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampant+Growth) and [Fertile Ground](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fertile+Ground). After using [Broodmate Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodmate+Dragon), [Primal Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Command), or [Cloudthresher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudthresher)? Why, [Vengeful Rebirth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vengeful+Rebirth) just allows Erika to reload and do it again!
