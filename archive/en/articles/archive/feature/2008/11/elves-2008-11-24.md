Elves were all the rage coming out of [Pro Tour-Berlin](/en/node/502416) but headed to North Carolina States, Nathaniel Muesing had the tribe in mind for Standard. His build, running a shy 16 lands, overwhelmed opponents with efficient green creatures like [Wolf-Skull Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolf-Skull+Shaman), [Nettle Sentinel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel), and [Imperious Perfect](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imperious+Perfect). His late game was shored up with [Overrun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overrun), huge amounts of Elf tokens via [Elvish Promenade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Promenade), and even drawing a ton of cards with [Regal Force](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Regal+Force).
