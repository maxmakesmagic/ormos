Top 8 Player Profiles
=====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX HONG KONG 2015](/en/events/coverage/gphk15)
 on August 9, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim










Here are the final eight players who made it through the sea of 843 other aspirants to make the Top 8 at Grand Prix Hong Kong 2015!


A final booster draft and three more rounds separate them from a glittering Champion trophy!




---


### Yam Wing Chun


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gphk15/gpHK_T8_Yam_Wing_Chun.jpg)


**Hometown:** Hong Kong  



**Score, Colors, and MVP of Day 1 Sealed**  



**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 1**  

3-0. Blue Red Artifacts with triple [Chief of the Foundry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chief+of+the+Foundry).


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 2**  

0-2-1. I drafted a White Black pile of cards with double [Languish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Languish).


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**  

3rd at Grand Prix Washington D.C., 15th at Pro Tour *Fate Reforged*. I went 0-3 drop at three Grand Prix but also went 9-0 three times!


**What are your *Magic* origins?**  

When I first started playing *Magic*, I met a lot of lifelong friends. Today, there are also here with me at Grand Prix Hong Kong and it would be great to hoist the trophy in their presence and in my hometown.




---


### Chye Yian Hsiang


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gphk15/gpHK_T8_Chye_Yian_Hsiang.jpg)


**Hometown:** Petaling Jaya, Malaysia  
**Age:** 32  
**Occupation:** Liquidation Manager


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Day 1 Sealed**  

9-0 (2 byes). White Red Aggro that transforms into White Black after sideboard.


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 1**  

2-1. White Blue with a bunch of [Faerie Miscreants](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faerie+Miscreants).


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 2**  

1-1-1. I drafted Blue Black "garbage". It was a very bad deck with no good cards.


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**  

Won an FNM at Eternal *Magic* and completed a set of Power 9 on MTGO solely by drafting *Vintage Masters*!


**What are your *Magic* origins?**  

I started to play *Magic* during High School during the times of *Zendikar* and since then, I've loved playing any deck that has a lot of interaction.




---


### Victoriano Lim


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gphk15/gpHK_T8_Victoriano_Lim.jpg)


**Hometown:** Quezon City, Philippines  
**Age:** 38  
**Occupation:** Collections Manager


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Day 1 Sealed**  

9-0 (2 byes). I played White Red and I had [Kytheon's Irregulars](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kytheon%27s+Irregulars) and [Pia and Kiran Nalaar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pia+and+Kiran+Nalaar)!


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 1**  

1-2. Blue Red with [Ravaging Blaze](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravaging+Blaze) and [Chandra's Fury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%27s+Fury).


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 2**  

2-0-1. White Red with [Blessed Spirits](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blessed+Spirits) and 5 enchantments!


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**  

I came in Top 32 at Grand Prix Hong Kong 2004! That's more than a decade ago!


**What are your *Magic* origins?**  

My classmates back in University taught me how to play the game. 17 years later, I'm still playing *Magic* and loving every moment of it!




---


### Liu Yuchen


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gphk15/gpHK_T8_Liu_Yuchen.jpg)


**Hometown:** Beijing, China  
**Age:** 24  
**Occupation:** MTG Sheep


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Day 1 Sealed**  

7-2 (2 byes). I was in White Red, but my deck is nothing special.


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 1**  

3-0. I drafted White Red and won the pod with [Tragic Arrogance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tragic+Arrogance) and [Chandra's Ignition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%27s+Ignition)!


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 2**  

2-0-1. White Green. [Sentinel of the Eternal Watch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sentinel+of+the+Eternal+Watch) and [Skysnare Spider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skysnare+Spider) were my best cards.


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**  

Grand Prix Manila 2010 Top 8, Grand Prix Shanghai 2014 Top 8.


**What are your *Magic* origins?**  

When I was a kid, I had the honor of playing a lot of *Magic* with Zhang Zhiyang. Maybe that is why I have improved a lot over these years!




---


### Cheung Lap Chung


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gphk15/gpHK_T8_Cheung_Lap_Chung.jpg)


**Hometown:** Hong Kong  
**Age:** 23  
**Occupation:** Actuarial Analyst


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Day 1 Sealed**  

7-1-1 (2 byes). Black Green.


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 1**  

3-0. I had a Black Red deck with [Chandra's Ignition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%27s+Ignition) and [Priest of the Blood Rite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Priest+of+the+Blood+Rite).


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 2**  

2-0-1. Black Red once again, with yet another [Chandra's Ignition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%27s+Ignition)! Also had [Graveblade Marauder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Graveblade+Marauder)!


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**  

None


**What are your *Magic* origins?**  

My most memorable tournament was Nationals 2011, when I had two copies of [Gideon Jura](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon+Jura) in my deck. I later found out there were four copies of the same Planeswalker in the draft pod! I love to play Boros Burn and my favorite color is Red.




---


### Kwok Suen Wang


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gphk15/gpHK_T8_Kwok_Suen_Wang.jpg)


**Hometown:** Hong Kong  
**Age:** 27  
**Occupation:** Accountant


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Day 1 Sealed**  

8-1 (1 bye). I had an average White Blue deck.


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 1**  

1-1-1. Blue Red Aggro with [Willbreaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Willbreaker).


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 2**  

3-0. White Blue Control with [Tragic Arrogance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tragic+Arrogance).


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**  

Around 50 FNM Top 8s. =)


**What are your *Magic* origins?**  

I originally played another card game but decided to switch over to *Magic* because it seemed more exciting. I want to thank all my teammates from our local store "Battleground" for the good times over the years.

### Wu Songwei


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gphk15/gpHK_T8_Wu_Songwei.jpg)


**Hometown:** Chengdu, China  
**Age:** 31  
**Occupation:** Sales


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Day 1 Sealed**  

8-1 (0 byes). Black Green.


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 1**  

Black Green. [Languish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Languish).


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 2**  

Black Green. [Gilt-Leaf Winnower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gilt-Leaf+Winnower).


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**  

Grand Prix Shanghai 2011 Top 16


**What are your *Magic* origins?**

### Jan Ang


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gphk15/gpHK_T8_Jan_Ang.jpg)


**Hometown:** Quezon City, Philippines  
**Age:** 30  
**Occupation:** Manager


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Day 1 Sealed**  

8-1 (2 byes). White Green.


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 1**  

3-0. White Green with [Somberwald Alpha](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somberwald+Alpha) and pump spells.


**Score, Colors, and MVP of Draft 2**  

2-1. Red Green with [Somberwald Alpha](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somberwald+Alpha) and pump spells, again!


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments**  

Philippines National Champion 2011.


**What are your *Magic* origins?**  

I started playing *Magic* around *4th Edition*. I bought myself a Starter and I remember opening [Force of Nature](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Nature), [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves), and some Forests to help me cast it. Those were the times!
