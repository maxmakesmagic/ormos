THEROS BLOCK SAMPLE SEALED POOL #1
==================================



 Posted in [ARTICLES](/en/articles)
 on June 28, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











When this Sealed pool was opened, it was hard not to marvel at the sheer power of some of the rares.


[Polukranos, World Eater](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polukranos%2C+World+Eater). [Hydra Broodmaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hydra+Broodmaster). ">[autocard]Hunter's Prowess. A trio of powerful green cards that have been known to end games all on their own. And here they were, grouped together for some lucky pilot to play. It's the start to the kind of deck that can propel a player to a dominant record on Day 1 of a Grand Prix.


But it's just that: a start. It takes more than a few bomb rares to make a good Sealed deck, and there are undoubtedly a few ways to approach this Sealed pool, especially considering the [Temple of Malady](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+of+Malady) and the [Temple of Abandon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+of+Abandon).


How would you build your deck if you opened this pool? Construct your build and we'll check back later in the day to see how a featured pro decided to piece it together.
