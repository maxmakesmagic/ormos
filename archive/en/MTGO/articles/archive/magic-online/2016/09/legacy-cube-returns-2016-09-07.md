The Legacy Cube Returns
=======================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on September 7, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The *Legacy Cube* returns to *Magic Online* on September 21, after the downtime, and runs until October 7 at 10:00 a.m. PT/5:00 p.m. UTC! There aren't any major shifts in design philosophy since the last large update—mostly tagging in some of the latest and greatest cards! The entirety of the *Legacy Cube* card list can be found [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/legacy-cube-cardlist).


For the first round of *Legacy Cube* since the release of Draft Leagues, we're going to try running our first Cube Draft League. Due to the singleton nature of the *Legacy Cube*, we understand that cross-pod play could have a larger impact on the experience than it would on a regular set Draft. We're trying this as an experiment and will be listening to player feedback to evaluate whether we continue with Cube Leagues in the future or go back to Draft Queues for both Swiss and Single-Elimination Cube Events.


Enjoy the newly updated *Legacy Cube*! Get the full list of changes plus event details below.

Legacy Cube Event Details
=========================



### *Legacy Cube* Single Elimination Draft Queue


**Start Times**: Wednesday, September 21, after the downtime until Friday, October 7, 10:00 a.m. All times are Pacific (UTC -7).  
**Location**:Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Queues  
**Entry Options**:


* Option 1: 10 Event Tickets
* Option 3: 100 Play Points

**Product**: 3 *Legacy Cube* booster packs. Phantom product will not be added to a player's collection at the conclusion of the event.  
**Size**: 8 players  
**Play Style**: Single-Elimination  
**Format**: Draft  
**Duration**: 10 minutes deck-building time. Three rounds, each round up to 50 minutes  
**Prizes**:




| Place | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | 220 Play Points | 1 |
| 2nd | 160 Play Points | 0 |
| 3rd-4th | 100 Play Points | 0 |
