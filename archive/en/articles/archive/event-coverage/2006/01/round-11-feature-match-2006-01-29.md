Round 11 Feature Match:
=======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 29, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_159.png)
By Jörn Martin Hajek












![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gphass06/fm11_1.jpg)*Ruud Warmenhoven doesn't let lack of sleep stop him from having a great time!*


Both players probably needed to win the rest of their matches to have a shot at the top 8.


Julien was playing an interesting aura deck. Among his auras were two [Moldervine Cloak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moldervine+Cloak), and he had multiple ways of finding them.


Julien drew both Cloaks, but no creatures in his opening hand. He decided to keep, however, Ruud got a Cloak of his own into play before Julien drew a creature. We Julien finally did, he dumped both Cloaks on it, and attacked for eight. Ruud showed [Boros Fury-Shield](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Fury-Shield), and Julien couldn't win the race.


1-0, Ruud


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gphass06/fm11_2.jpg)*Julien Nuijten reflects on his World Championship win*
In Game 2, Julien revealed his secret weapon: [Three Dreams](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Three+Dreams). He found a Cloak, [Faith's Fetters](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faith%27s+Fetters) and [Flickerform](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flickerform). A judge was called to clear up the interaction between [Flickerform](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flickerform) and [Bramble Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bramble+Elemental). After checking the oracle, it was ruled that more tokens will be generated when the Elemental and the aura return to play. [Flickerform](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flickerform) on the [Bramble Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bramble+Elemental) gave the opportunity to make quite a lot of tokens, but it didn't even matter, as a 7/7 that could be phased out was more than enough.


1-1


Julien took a mulligan in the last game, and Ruud wished him bad luck with the mana. His wish came through, and [Transluminant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Transluminant) and [Shambling Shell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shambling+Shell) ended the game quickly.
