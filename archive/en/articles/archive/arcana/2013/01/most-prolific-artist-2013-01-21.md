The Most Prolific Artist
========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 21, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











It's a simple question: who's the most prolific **Magic** artist?


Or is it? When Trick Jarrett came up with this question over the holiday break, he had to define his terms. So what we're looking for is the artist with the most individual pieces of art. Robert Bliss's [Pacifism](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=259712) first appeared in *Mirage* and has appeared in eight core sets, but it should only count once. On the other hand, we'd like to count alternate art made for promo cards.


So we dove into our internal card database and came back with some comprehensive answers! And then we realized that what we were looking at was a little *too* comprehensive, because it included all art that had been done, regardless of whether it had been released yet. And we didn't bother changing the art credits for *Unhinged*, so Pete Venters doesn't get credited for [Erase (Not the Urza's Legacy One)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=74275) because the official artist is Pete "Yes the *Urza's Legacy* One" Venters.


Okay. So, with those caveats, here's a list of the top **Magic** artists that we think is mostly correct. It's pretty close.
