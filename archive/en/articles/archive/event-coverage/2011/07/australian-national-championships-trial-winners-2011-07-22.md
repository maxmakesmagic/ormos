Australian National Championships Trial Winners
===============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 22, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Walkinshaw-Profile.jpg)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw











Every year people qualify for the National Champs in one way or another, be it on the strength of their ranking, or their play at a Regional Qualifier event. But just to be fair, we offer them one last chance the day before the Big Event.


The Last Chance Qualifiers are affectionately known as “The Grinders”. Single elimination events that start as soon as they have 32 players signed up. You lose in one? Sign up for another. More than a few budding mages have been driven mad beating their heads against The Grinders in a last ditch effort to qualify. Last night, 32 players survived the gauntlet playing in constructed Grinders, and 6 made it through playing limited. I hope you’ll pardon my not typing up the sealed decks, but I do have all 32 standard lists for you here.
