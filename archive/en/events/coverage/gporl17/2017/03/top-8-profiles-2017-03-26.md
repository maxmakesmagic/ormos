Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 27, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler












 Eight competitors fought through a field of nearly 1,300 to reach the Top 8, and a final draft of *Aether Revolt-Kaladesh* decided it all. Here's some more about the eight players who reached the top.





---






### Joel Larsson



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gporl17/GP_ORL16_JoelLarsson.jpg)



**Age:** 25




**Hometown:** Stockholm, Sweden




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Winner at Pro Tour *Magic Origins;* six Grand Prix Top 8s, national champion four times.




**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** Sultai colors (black-blue-green), 8-1.




**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Cowl Prowler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cowl+Prowler).




**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** Red-White 3-0, then 2-1 with Jund color (green-red-black).




**What common or uncommon do you most want to open in the Top 8?** [Ridgescale Tusker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ridgescale+Tusker) and [Chandra's Revolution](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%27s+Revolution).




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** A lot. I haven't made Top 8 in a while and I need the Pro Points.





---






### Noah Walker



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gporl17/GP_ORL16_NoahWalker.jpg)



**Age:** 20




**Hometown:** Amherst, Massachusetts




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Two Grand Prix Top 8s, and Game Day Champion!




**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** Green-White, went 8-1.




**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Aethersphere Harvester](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aethersphere+Harvester).




**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** Two blue-red decks, 2-1 and then 3-0.




**What common or uncommon do you most want to open in the Top 8?** [Ridgescale Tusker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ridgescale+Tusker) or [Aether Swooper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Swooper).




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Wouldn't have even been able to show up if not for my boy Alex S. Now I'm super close to Gold and going to Japan!!





---






### Chris Pikula



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gporl17/GP_ORL16_ChrisPikula.jpg)



**Age:** 42




**Hometown:** Bryn Maw, PA




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Three Pro Tour Top 8s, Invitational Winner.




**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** Red-White, went 9-0.




**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Quicksmith Rebel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quicksmith+Rebel).




**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** 3-0 with Black-Red, and then 0-1-2 with Red-Green.




**What common or uncommon do you most want to open in the Top 8?** [Untethered Express](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Untethered+Express).




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** A ton. It has been a long time.





---






### Zile Yao



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gporl17/GP_ORL16_ZileYao.jpg)



**Age:** 16




**Hometown:** Anhui Heiei, China. Now studying at Ohio State University in Columbus.




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  31st at SCG Columbus; 10-5 at Grand Prix Pittsburgh.




**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** Black-Green, went 8-1, lost one because my opponent with two byes had [Duplicant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duplicant).




**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter) and [Walking Ballista](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista). They're some good!




**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** 2-1 with White-Blue, and then 3-0 with Black-Green.




**What common or uncommon do you most want to open in the Top 8?** [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push).




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** My dream was going to the Pro Tour during my time in university – I can't believe I made it when I was a freshman!





---






### Jorge Mantilla



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gporl17/GP_ORL16_JorgeMantilla.jpg)



**Age:** 28




**Hometown:** Miami, Florida




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Day Two at Grand Prix Atlanta (barely).




**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** Red-White, 9-0.




**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Walking Ballista](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista).




**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** First was mainly green with some blue, and I went 1-1-1. Second was white-black and I went 2-0-1.




**What common or uncommon do you most want to open in the Top 8?** [Walking Ballista](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista).




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Everything. Since I started playing *Magic*, I've just fallen in love with the game – Playing and watching *Magic* whenever I can. Feels really good to put in the work and get good results.





---






### Jack Dobbin



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gporl17/GP_ORL16_JackDobbin.jpg)



**Age:** 30




**Hometown:** Chicago




**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**  Best *Magic* player at basketball.




**What colors was your Sealed Deck? Record?** Blue-Black, went 7-2.




**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?** [Universal Solvent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Universal+Solvent).




**What were your draft decks, and records with them?** Red-Green [Animation Module](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Animation+Module) went 3-0, and then I went 3-0 with mono-blue splashing [Noxious Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noxious+Gearhulk).




**What common or uncommon do you most want to open in the Top 8?** [Shipwreck Moray](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shipwreck+Moray).




**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Shoutout to my Chicago people!
