ADJUSTMENT TO SUNDAY VIEWING SCHEDULE
=====================================



 Posted in [COVERAGE ARCHIVE](/en/events/coverage)
 on December 6, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










The viewing schedule for Sunday's World *Magic* Cup Top 8 and World Championship Top 4 has been slightly adjusted to accommodate for the format and to ensure timely resolution of the matches. The webcast will begin at 9 a.m. local time ([click here for local time conversions](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/fact-sheet-2014-magic-world-championship)) and matches will be shown as follows:


**World *Magic* Cup Quarterfinals:** All four matches played simultaneously, with one on camera


**World *Magic* Cup Semifinals:** Both matches played simultaneously, with one on camera


**World *Magic* Cup Finals:** One match played on camera


The World *Magic* Cup Finals will be followed by an award ceremony and product announcement.


**World Championship Semifinals:** Both matches played simultaneously, with video coverage switching back and forth as appropriate


**World Championship Finals:** One match played on camera


The World Championship Awards Ceremony will take place live.
