Draft Viewer: 11/24/2010
========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 24, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Welcome back to the Draft Viewer! Today we've got a classic viewer from the 2009 World Championships. This was the second of two triple-*Zendikar* Booster Drafts from the second day of Worlds and featured not one but two 2010 Pro Tour Hall of Fame inductees: Tomoharu Saito and Bram Snepvangers. The [2010 World Championships](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds) begin December 9 in Chiba, Japan.
