The September 1, 2016 update
============================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on September 1, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on September 1, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by PAX West, which is our everything for the next five beautiful days.


What does this mean for you, Daily Update reader? Well, for one, this is the last Update you'll read until next Tuesday. Basically, Friday's would only be a single entry that says "PAAAAAAAAAAAXXXXX. KAAAAAAALLLLLLAAAAAADESSSSSSHHHHHHH," and that's it. Seems like we can get that out of the way now. Then, on Monday, it's a holiday in the United States, so we'll be celebrating. Also look for some pinch-hitters on the Update next week, as I go on vacation at a crucial juncture in our preview schedule. As one does.


What to Watch This Weekend
==========================


1. [*Kaladesh* and the World Championship at PAX West](http://magic.wizards.com/en/home/kaladesh)

It's here! It's finally here. After a long Pro Tour season capped with Owen Turtenwald's stunning Player of the Year win, we're finally to the World Championship. And after a year of doom and gloom under the gaze (do they gaze?) of the Eldrazi, we're moving on to the bright, sunny world of possibility—*Kaladesh*!


We'll be at PAX West all weekend, where we've brought a bit of Kaladesh to this world. We'll be covering the World Championship, broadcasting panels, and holding an Inventors' Fair right on the street outside! You can find [the whole schedule here](http://media.wizards.com/2016/downloads/Mobile_Schedule_v2.pdf), and you can watch everything on [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic)!


1. [StarCityGames.com Richmond Open Weekend](http://www.starcitygames.com/events/030916_richmond_fact_sheet.html)

If you need a break from the world of *Kaladesh* (What?! We just got here!), there are still denizens back on Tarkir, Zendikar, and Innistrad during the Richmond Open Weekend, where Standard is the main name of the game. Coverage starts Saturday at 10 a.m. ET/7 a.m. PT on [twitch.tv/scglive](https://www.twitch.tv/scglive).

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Bant Company](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2016-08-31)

Here's the open question for this weekend: how much Bant Company will be at the World Championships? Many of these players picked something else for the last Pro Tour, but since then tournaments have gravitated back to the four-mana creature-fetching instant.


The list below is a 5-0 Competitive Standard League list from MUNDISV, and is relatively stock. I would fully expect the best to innovate some. Or maybe this is as good as it gets.


Will the best of the best beat them or join them? Tune in today to find out.
