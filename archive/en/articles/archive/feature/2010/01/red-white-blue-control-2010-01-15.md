Looking for a fun and exciting way to beat Jund at your upcoming Friday Night **Magic**? Pro Tour superstar Luis Scott-Vargas has exactly what you're looking for! He dispatched more than a few copies of the black-red-green deck on his way to the top slot at a recent [StarCityGames.com Open tournament](http://www.starcitygames.com/php/news/article/18542.html) in Los Angeles. His strategy was to control the game with removal and counters, wipe the board with [Earthquake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake), and beat opponents up with powerful planeswalkers [Ajani Vengeant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani+Vengeant) and [Jace Beleren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace+Beleren). And to make sure he never ran out of cards? How about massive [Mind Spring](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Spring)s! Make sure to head to your Wizards Play Network location for [Friday Night **Magic**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm), where you can earn a premium [Tidehollow Sculler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tidehollow+Sculler) throughout January.
