Looking for a fun, relaxing format to try on any random afternoon? Try Singleton, available on **Magic Online**. The format requires players use no more than a single copy of each card (other than basic lands) in their deck. BryTheFryGuy managed to Top 8 a recent Premier Event in the digital world using this deck. Notice a theme? Even with the one card restriction, Bry put together a whopper of an Elf deck!
