[Astral Slide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) is back! Zac Ricketts took this list to a free trip to Hawaii at his PTQ in Lincoln, Nebraska. Abusing the name-sake enchantment, Zac uses his cycling cards to gain advantage by flipping [Cloudthresher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudthresher)s in and out of play (or [Kitchen Finks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), or [Loxodon Hierarch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Loxodon+Hierarch)...). When he gets tired of that, he can always turn the [Astral Slide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) activations on attacking creatures to keep them from connecting!
