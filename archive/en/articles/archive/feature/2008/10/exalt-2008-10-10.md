*Shards of Alara* is finally available for all, and players everywhere are reporting in about fun matches and awesome new cards and abilities. Our very own [Limited Information](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Archive.aspx?tag=Limited%20Information&description=Limited%20Information) columnist Steve Sadin has shown a particular interest in the Bant mechanic of exalted. His very first draft in the format centered around creatures with the ability, and he's written about the deck not once but TWICE. You can find his first article on the subject [here](/en/articles/archive/limited-information/tales-prerelease-2008-09-30) and his second article [here](/en/articles/archive/limited-information/i-bant-attack-one-creature-2008-10-07).
