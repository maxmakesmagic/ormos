Adding a new twist to the Cascade Land Destruction decks that have begun popping up, Derrick Steel added [Boomerang](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boomerang) to his build, meaning he can start the hurt as early as the second turn. Before long, [Cryptic Command](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command), [Rain of Tears](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rain+of+Tears), and [Fulminator Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage) are online, and shortly thereafter, opponents are feeling the [Cruel Ultimatum](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Cruel%2BUltimatum)!
