Decks of the Week: 1/17/2014
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on January 17, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations to Croatia's Vjeran Horvat, who battled through a Modern field of nearly 1,400 to win [Grand Prix Prague](/en/events/coverage/hrvatskas-horvat-crowned-master-modern-prague)! And if you're interested in Modern decklists, you might like to know that the buzz in Prague was for Jan van der Vegt's [Tin Fists](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/day-1-coverage-grand-prix-prague-2014-01-11) deck. This season's Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) format is Standard, though, so be sure to check out the  [*Born of the Gods* Card Image Gallery](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/bornofthegods/cig#)  to get an early view of what might change in February! Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#jou)! Good luck; have fun!
