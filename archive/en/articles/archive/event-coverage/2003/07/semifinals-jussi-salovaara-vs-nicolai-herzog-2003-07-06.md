Semifinals: Jussi Salovaara vs. Nicolai Herzog
==============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 6, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_103.png)
By Rui Oliveira











Yup, two Nordic players playing in an Euros' semifinals. Surprising isn't it? Not really. They know each other quite well and had no qualms discussing their options before the game started. They certainly had enough jokes to tell.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/euro03/a843.jpg)Jussi: "This is the kind of mood I like when I'm playing for this amount of money."

### Game 1

Herzog was not happy with his hand, but after some headshaking he decided to keep it. Jussi got a peek with [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) taking [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic) and seeing another [Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Logic) and [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba).

[Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) entered play and when Salovaara tried to Edict away the Norwegian showed him his topdecked Rootwalla. The Beast stayed and attacked the next turn.

[Mesmeric Fiend](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mesmeric+Fiend) got hit with other [Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Logic) and Nicolai attacked discarding [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm). He flashed it back.

Nicolai: "Not bad."

Jussi's freshly-cast [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade) looked quite small across the table but it kept [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) back. The [Roar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar) brought him crashing down to eight. The purpled haired player played a fourth [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) and sat behind his Shade.

But Nicolai was in a hurry. An [Unsummon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsummon) got the blocker out of the way and the [Roar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar)-Moeba duo finished the game.

Jussi: "Nice Topdecks."  
 Nicolai: "Each draw just got better."  
 Jussi: "Well, that's Magic.

Nicolai 1-0 Jussi.

Nicolai's sideboard:  
 In: Three [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur), three [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost), two [Stupefying Touch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stupefying+Touch), one [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval), three [Ravenous Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth), one [Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) and one [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell).  
 Out: Four [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm)s, four [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic), two [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder), two [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and two [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation).

Jussi's sideboard:  
 In: Two [Chainer's Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainer%27s+Edict), two [Faceless Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher), two [Cabal Coffers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Coffers), two [Visara](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara)s, one [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt) and one [Engineered Plague](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Plague).  
 Out: Four [Rotlung Reanimator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotlung+Reanimator)s, two [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), two [Unholy Grotto](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unholy+Grotto) and two [Withered Wretch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Withered+Wretch).

Jussi: "Why do you Norwegians keep playing Blue-Green?"  
 Nicolai: "It's just the best deck."  
 Jussi: "It might be the worst best deck ever."  
 Nicolai: "Well, it CAN be inconsistent."

### Game 2

Jussi: "This is a good hand."  
 Nicolai: "I think Rune would keep this hand. I'll mulligan."

He kept his second hand and Salovaara played [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy).

Jussi: "I'll go for [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost)."  
 Nicolai: "You should have gone for [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). I do have two of those."

The Finnish player followed that with Wretch and [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) on the first [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel). Herzog played [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) with an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) open but Jussi called his bluff. As you all know Herzog doesn't have any [Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Logic)s now, so [Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher) took [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba).

A second [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) and the second [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) entered the game and Jussi Salovaara was forced to stop swinging. Instead he played out his second Wretch and [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade).

Herzog used [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) to find help and discarded two lands. The Shade pushed him down to eight points. Nicolai played a sixth land and showed the last card in his hand: [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval). Things were back to square zero.

Well, almost. The [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) was released from [Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher) and thus stayed on the table. Two turns later the Shade returned. Nicolai played [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) with four cards in hand and Jussi took a few minutes to work out his new plan.

He stared at Herzog for a few seconds and sent the Shade in. The [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) stepped forward and they traded Black mana for cards. In the end both creatures died. In came another [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) but [Faceless Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) took it away.

The [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) brought Salovaara down to twelve and Herzog again dropped the elbow with his last card in hand. This time it was [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur).

Jussi was NOT happy about that. A second [Faceless Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) took care of the [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) but Herzog topdecked another one to keep the Centaur save from any Edicts.

At seven, Jussi tried to swarm Herzog. He played two [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade)s, signaling that Nicolai Herzog couldn't swing with his Centaur unless he could find another chump-blocker. And even then he would be taking a huge risk ([Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher) or Edict could cost him the game).

He couldn't, so he sent the turn over. Jussi again slumped back in his chair and stared at his cards. The game situation was quite tense and the difference between 0-2 and 1-1 is so huge in a five-game match. He settled for playing Wretch and sending his whole team in. (pumping both Shades once).

Herzog's creatures stopped the Shades and the two [Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher)s brought Herzog to two. Again Herzog found no help. He passed the turn back and scooped when Jussi Salovaara pushed the whole army into the Red Zone.

Nicolai 1-1 Jussi.

Jussi: " I almost messed that up."

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/euro03/a844.jpg)No sideboard changes, although Salovaara pushed the whole sideboard into the deck before taking out exactly the same fifteen cards.

### Game 3

First turn Rootwalla and Jussi Therapied for [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost) again. Again he missed. [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) joined the fray on the second turn. Salovaara had no turn two play so the Norwegian discarded another Rootwalla and attacked.

When the Fin finally moved it hurt: [Engineered Plague](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Plague) on Lizard. Out went the two [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla)s. Herzog discarded [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval) to [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) and kept attacking with [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). Salovaara's [Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher) took the lone creature away.

Nicolai: "This game took a turn for the worst."

Another [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) gave Nicolai Herzog some help in the shape of [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost). But Jussi likes extra cards as well, so he played [Graveborn Muse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Graveborn+Muse). Edict got an [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) out of the way and Herzog went down to thirteen.

He played a second [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost) and [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). But Jussi Salovaara was in a hurry. His Muse was eating his life total. He flashed back the Edict and dropped his opponent to eight.

Still, Nicolai found [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). That can certainly slow down Black creatures. Of course Jussi was drawing like a madman and had Edict. An [Unsummon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsummon) released the [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel), which traded with the Muse.

Herzog kept fighting back but Salovaara's hand was just scary. He played [Visara](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara) that Herzog bounced. A first [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt) left him at one and the second one finished the job.

Jussi 2-1 Nicolai.

Nicolai: "Want to up the split?"  
 Jussi: "No comment."

This time Jussi changed a card. He took out a third [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) and added [Unholy Grotto](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unholy+Grotto).

Jussi: "Let's get this over with."  
 Nicolai: "I'll play first, then."

### Game 4

Nicolai called his hand "interesting" which turned out to be just another word for mulligan. He peeked at the top cards and found out he should have kept the hand. His six-card hand seemed good enough.

Nicolai: "I'll take a first turn Mulligan."

He played [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study). Whatever he drew it sure made him think. He eventually settled on discarding an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) and [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla). The second turn [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) got [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother)ed before it could do anything.

Nicolai: "Triple Mulligan!"

Yup, another [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study), this time discarding [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) and [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba). The Rootwalla was soon outmatched by [Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade). To compensate for it Nicolai Herzog dropped [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) drawing a collective sigh from the crowd.

[Faceless Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) stole [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) and the Centaur attacked while [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) kept it safe from Edicts. A second [Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher) stole the blue Beast and Salovaara tried to race.

[Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm) entered game with obvious "Edict-Protection" duties and Salovaara shook his head. He was one swing away from losing the game and eight point behind on the damage race. He did the math a couple of times and conceded.

Jussi 2-2 Nicolai.

Nicolai's sideboard:  
 In: One [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm).   
 Out: One [Ray of Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation).

### Game 5

Going into the decisive match Jussi Salovaara wasn't too happy. He clearly didn't enjoy getting bashed in by [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). Nicolai, on the other hand, seemed very relieved.

Jussi: "Why are we shuffling in silence?"

And... they spent the rest of the time discussing in which restaurant they would have dinner tonight.

Nicolai nodded over his seven cards before deciding to keep it. He did get the first play in and it was quite good: [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/euro03/a845.jpg)Nicolai: "It was a calculated risk."  
 Jussi & Nicolai: "Sooooooo... lucky!" 

He followed that with [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) as Salovaara played [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) after [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp). He agonized over [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) for a few seconds, with several deep sighs and a lot of shuffling around in his chair. He started [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother)ed the [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) to start things and then unleashed [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy).

Jussi: "Right on time, just before your fourth mana. [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur)."  
 Nicolai: "Nice one."

He got two of them and the crowd reacted loudly. [Ravenous Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth) joined [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) but [Faceless Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) immediately showed up to push it out of table. Nicolai needed some extra help so he used [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) to draw two cards. A [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) helped as well.

[Graveborn Muse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Graveborn+Muse) entered the game and it seemed that despite the pending threat of [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost) Jussi was taking control. [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) got flashed back but all Nicolai Herzog could find was another [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost).

A second [Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher) tried to take the Rootwalla out of the way but Herzog had [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell). He got two extra cards but the attack dropped him to six. He untapped, attacked and hit the safety button: [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval).

Salovaara returned the Baloth, smiled and picked up his cards. At sixteen he had some breathing room to work with but he was surely in trouble. Nicolai's discard step got two [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla)s into play and Jussi gave him a "high five".

The game was as good as over. Salovaara played a [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) and sank to ten, now facing [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) as well. When your only permanents are two [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp)s there isn't much you can do. A [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) kept him alive one more turn but he was forced to concede.

**Final Result:** Herzog 3, Salovaara 2
