Day Three: Intro
================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 18, 2004 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff











It all comes down to this. The weeks of preparation, fifteen rounds of swiss, five drafts, all of it is just prologue to this, the final draft to determine the champion of Pro Tour - Amsterdam. And you can watch it all, live!That's right, just tune in to our Live Streaming Webcast and see all of Sunday's action unfold as it happens! Watch the final Rochester, every controversial pick and hate draft. Then stay tuned for all the elimination action! See a champion crowned!

Will Osamu Fujita become the first Japanese player to win a Pro Tour? Will Mike Turian get the best retirement present a mage could ask for? Will Nicolai Herzog take the title he just missed at Chicago? These are the best of the best. Expect sparks to fly!
