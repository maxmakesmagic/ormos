=======================================



 Posted in 
 on June 30, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Every Tuesday at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7), the newest episode of the Super League airs live on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic). We want to get league decklists into the hands of viewers as quickly as possible. Now, also on Tuesdays at 6:00 p.m. Pacific, viewers can go [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/standard-super-league-decklists) to find a link to the Super League decklists for that night's episode!


The below decklists are for the finals of the Standard Super League. Click on player names below to bring you right to their deck.
