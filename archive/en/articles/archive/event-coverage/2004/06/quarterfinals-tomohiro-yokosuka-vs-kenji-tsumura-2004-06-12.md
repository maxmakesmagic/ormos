Quarterfinals: Tomohiro Yokosuka vs. Kenji Tsumura
==================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 12, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/jpnat04/qf1_1.jpg)Tomohiro Yokosuka put together his Standard deck on Friday morning, simply throwing together a strategy that he thought would best handle Affinity. Apparently it worked well for Yokosuka, who has a Pro Tour Top 8 to his credit. Odds are he's happy with his first round pairing, since he's up against Kenji Tsumura's Affinity deck. ### Game 1


Tsumura started with [Arcbound Worker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcbound+Worker). It was joined by a Ravager, followed by a free [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite). In response, Yokosuka [Naturalize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize)d the Ravager, and Tsumura didn't give its counter to the Worker. He played another free [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite), and ended his turn. Yokosuka played a Silver Knight, and Tsumura put [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) on the Worker to draw two cards, and make a [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite) bigger. The other [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite) took the [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), and bith attacked past Yokosuka's Knight to deal six damage. Yokosuka played a second Knight, and Tsumura played a Ravager. He sacrificed the Clamped [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite) to draw two cards, then switched the [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) to the other [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite). Yokosuka cycled Renewed Faith on end step, untapped, and played [Solemn Simulacrum](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solemn+Simulacrum). Tsumura summoned [Disciple of the Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+the+Vault), and got to work sacrificing Clamped creatures to the Ravager. He played two [Myr Enforcer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Enforcer)s for free, and sent his 3/3 Ravager in for an attack- Yokosuka simply chump blocked with a Knight. Tsumura then played [Pyrite Spellbomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyrite+Spellbomb), and ended his turn. Yokosuka's [Viridian Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Shaman) caused Tsumura to lose his [Welding Jar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar), and on the following turn he had enough artifacts to sacrifice to end the game.


Yokosuka- 0 Tsumura- 1


### Game 2


Tsumura played a second turn Ravager, and a third turn [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite). He missed his third land drop, however, although Yokosuka was doing nothing more than playing lands and saying go. Tsumura played a [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) and tried to give it to the [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite), but in response Yokosuka destroyed it with [Purge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purge). When Tsumura tried the same play once more on the following turn, Yokosuka had the [Purge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purge) again. Tsumura drew some more cards with [Thoughtcast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtcast), and sent his Ravager in for an attack- it died to Wing Shards, and suddenly it seemed that Yokosuka would take control of the game. Tsumura's [Disciple of the Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+the+Vault) died to [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God), and Tsumura played [Welding Jar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar), a second [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), and [Myr Enforcer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Enforcer). Yokosuka had a third [Purge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purge) to kill the 4/4, and Tsumura played [Somber Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard). Tsumura gave it the [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), and when it attacked, Yokosuka [Oxidize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oxidize)d the [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), then cast Wing Shards. 


Despite all this, Tsumura was still charging on offense, playing two [Disciple of the Vault](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+the+Vault) and [Somber Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard). Yokosuka cast [Pulse of the Fields](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulse+of+the+Fields) on end step, but Tsumura cast [Shrapnel Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shrapnel+Blast) in response to make sure he didn't get it back. Yokosuka cast [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God), and Tsumura drew two cards off [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp). He untapped, played [Somber Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard), and gave it the [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp). He then played [Arcbound Ravager](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcbound+Ravager) followed by a second [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), and Yokosuka cycled Krosan Tusker to get an island. [Viridian Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Shaman) drew a [Welding Jar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar) sacrifice, and Tsumura gained more resources with another [Thoughtcast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtcast). The Hoverguard attacked for five, and Tsumura put the other [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) on it to draw tons of cards. A turn later, Yokosuka conceded.


Yokosuka- 0 Tsumura- 2


### Game 3


Yokosuka decided to draw first. Tsumura played a first turn [Chromatic Sphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chromatic+Sphere), then a Disciple, [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite) and [Welding Jar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar) on turn two. Tsumura played a [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp) and tried to put it on his [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite), drawing a [Purge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purge) out of Yokosuka. Naturally, Tsumura let it die, keeping the [Welding Jar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar) to protect the [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp). A second [Purge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Purge) killed the Disciple, but Tsumura simply played another. Tsumura played another [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite) and gave it the [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), then played one more [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp). With only two lands Tsumura wasn't able to make the best of this situation by drawing cards off the [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite) immediately, but he was certainly gaining a lot of momentum despite his lack of mana. The [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite) attacked, and Yokosuka [Oxidize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oxidize)d the [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp). Tsumura didn't sacrifice his [Welding Jar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar), and instead put the other Clamp on it after the attack was over. Yokosuka cast [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God), and Tsumura played [Shrapnel Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shrapnel+Blast) in response, sacrificing his [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite). He drew two cards in the process, and played a [Somber Hoverguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard) on his turn via [Chromatic Sphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chromatic+Sphere). Tsumura had only three lands, and none of them produced blue mana.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/jpnat04/qf1_2.jpg)The Hoverguard took the [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp), and when it attacked, Yokosuka had Wing Shards. Tsumura came right back with a [Myr Enforcer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Enforcer), along with another [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp). He had a second [Welding Jar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar), and when Yokosuka attempted disruption via [Naturalize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize) he gladly sacrificed it. The Enforcer took both [Skullclamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullclamp)s and attacked for six, while Tsumura had a Ravager out as well. [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God) cleared the board, but gave Tsumura plenty of cards in the process. He played a Hoverguard off a Sphere, followed by a [Frogmite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite). Tsumura continued to bring out threats, and when Yokosuka tried to stay in the game with [Pulse of the Fields](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulse+of+the+Fields), Tsumura cast [Shrapnel Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shrapnel+Blast) in response to advance to the Semifinals.


Yokosuka- 0 Tsumura- 3
