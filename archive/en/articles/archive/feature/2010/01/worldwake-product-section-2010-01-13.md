*Worldwake* Product Section
===========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 13, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By magicthegathering.com Staff











The [*Worldwake* Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/worldwake) is here! And something *new* has been added! In addition to all the usual goodies (a puzzle, a wallpaper, product information, a setting preview, and the **Orb of Insight**), there's an introduction to the mechanics you'll find in *Worldwake*! Look for it in the "Explore *Worldwake*" section.


And, of course, there's the Visual Spoiler, an archive of all preview cards that have been officially released. You might want to keep an eye on that one, because it's going to be updated frequently over the next two and a half weeks as we count down to the *Worldwake* Prerelease on January 30-31.


[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/features/feature73b_ss_gps5nmkwit.jpg)](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/worldwake)
  
So what are you waiting for? Hit the [go to the *Worldwake* Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/worldwake) now!
