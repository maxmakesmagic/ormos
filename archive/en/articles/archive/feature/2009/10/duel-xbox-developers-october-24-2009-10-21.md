Duel the Xbox Developers: October 24
====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 21, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/features/feature43c_dotpLogo.jpg)### Xbox LIVE® Arcade – Game with Developers – October 24, 2009

Team members from Stainless Games, Ltd., Wizards of the Coast, Microsoft, and other **Magic**  developers behind [**Magic: The Gathering Duels of the Planeswalkers**](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/DuelsOfThePlaneswalkers.aspx) Expansion Pack 1 will play Xbox LIVE® Gold Members on Saturday, October 24. 

Join us [Saturday, October 24 from 6-9 p.m. ET](http://www.xbox.com/en-US/community/calendar/) for a classic gaming session. Plus, this is your chance to get the XBLA **Spellslinger Achievement**  directly from the source by defeating **Duels of the Planeswalkers**  developers!
