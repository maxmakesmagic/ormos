Vintage Super League Season 2: Finals Decklists
===============================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on April 27, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











In the Vintage Super League finals, each player brings three decks, labeled "A," "B," and "C," then pit the corresponding decks against each other. Below are the decks that Chris Pikula and Eric Froehlich brought to their [finals match](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VVlwId3wneo).


You can follow the links below to see the different sets of decklists players used through the Vintage Super League season.


The Standard Super League premieres Tuesday, May 28, on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic)! The Standard Super League will air on the same schedule as the Vintage Super League, Tuesday nights at 6:00 p.m. Pacific.
