==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 25, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler

That starts with the last-chance trials on Friday, when players compete in single-elimination tournaments to try and earn byes in the main event. Here are the players and decks who took down trials on Friday.
