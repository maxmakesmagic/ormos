Top 4 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 1, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











After fourteen rounds of Swiss, only four teams made it to the top from among 558 others. From 1674 players, we are down to the final 12 players. Which of these teams will win it all?


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpshi17-2/gpshi17-2_TOP4_Group.jpg)  
*The Top 4 teams of Grand Prix Shizuoka (Autumn) 2017!*




---


Team Abe-Hayakawa-Tachibana
===========================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpshi17-2/gpshi17-2_TOP4-Abe_Hayakawa_Tachibana.jpg)  
*(from left to right) Michio Abe, Syo Hayakawa, Kentarou Tachibana*


**Seat:** A


**Name:** Michio Abe


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Company Employee


**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Nothing previously. This is my first Grand Prix Top 8!


**Day 1 Archetype:** White-Black Vampires with [Deathless Ancient](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathless+Ancient) and 2 [Call to the Feast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+to+the+Feast).


**Day 2 Archetype:** White-Red Aggro with [Captivating Crew](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Captivating+Crew).


**Among your team, who is most likely to survive in Ixalan?**


Tachibana-kun.


**Among your team, who is most likely to become dinosaur food?**


Our captain, Mr Hayakawa, will sacrifice himself to protect us.




---

**Seat:** B


**Name:** Syo Hayakawa


**Age:** 26


**Occupation:** Company Employee


**Hometown:** Kanagawa, Japan


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** WMCQ Top 8


**Day 1 Archetype:** Blue-Green Menfolk. My key cards were [Tempest Caller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tempest+Caller) and [River's Rebuke](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=River%27s+Rebuke).


**Day 2 Archetype:** Green-White Dinosaurs with Wakening Sun's Avatar.


**Among your team, who is most likely to survive in Ixalan?**


Mr. Tachibana.


**Among your team, who is most likely to become dinosaur food?**


Michio Abe, but I think he will try to run away.




---

**Seat:** C


**Name:** Kentarou Tachibana


**Age:** 24


**Occupation:** Company Employee


**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** A few Grand Prix Day 2s.


**Day 1 Archetype:** Black-Red Pirates with Pirate's Cutlass.


**Day 2 Archetype:** Blue-Black Pirates with Pirate's Cutlass.


**Among your team, who is most likely to survive in Ixalan?**


Myself.


**Among your team, who is most likely to become dinosaur food?**


Hopefully one of my teammates instead of me.

Team Ishii-Hironobu-Kozakai
===========================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpshi17-2/gpshi17-2_TOP4-Ishii_Sugaya_Kozakai.jpg)  
*(from left to right) Daisuke Ishii, Sugaya Hironobu, Yukio Kozakai*


**Seat:** A


**Name:** Taisuke Ishii


**Age:** 39


**Occupation:** System Engineer


**Hometown:** Kanagawa, Japan


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Grand Prix Kitakyushu 2009 Champion


**Day 1 Archetype:** Black-White Vampires with [Boneyard Parley](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boneyard+Parley).


**Day 2 Archetype:** Blue-Black Pirates splash [Vona, Butcher of Magan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vona%2C+Butcher+of+Magan)


**Among your team, who is most likely to survive in Ixalan?**


Myself.


**Among your team, who is most likely to become dinosaur food?**


When you spell his name in Japanese characters, the last few alphabets sounds like "little fish". Since he is little fish, he is likely to be eaten up.

Team Chew-Lee-Dezani
====================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpshi17-2/gpshi17-2_TOP4-Chew_Lee_Dezani.jpg)  
*(from left to right) Kelvin Chew, Lee Shi Tian, Jérémy Dezani*


**Seat:** A


**Name:** Kelvin Chew


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** MTG Mint Card Pro Player


**Hometown:** Singapore


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 1 Pro Tour Top 8 and 3 other Grand Prix Top 8s


**Day 1 Archetype:** Blue-Green Menfolk with River's [Rebuke](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rebuke) and [Entrancing Melody](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Entrancing+Melody).


**Day 2 Archetype:** Blue-Green Merfolk with One with the Wind and [Dive Down](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dive+Down).


**Among your team, who is most likely to survive in Ixalan?**


Myself.


**Among your team, who is most likely to become dinosaur food?**


Lee Shi Tian. Nice appetizer.




---

**Seat:** B


**Name:** Lee Shi Tian


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Accountant


**Hometown:** Hong Kong


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 5 Pro Tour Top 8s, 7 other Grand Prix Top 8s


**Day 1 Archetype:** Red-White Dinosaurs with 1 [Charging Monstrosaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Charging+Monstrosaur).


**Day 2 Archetype:** Red-White Dinosaurs with 2 [Charging Monstrosaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Charging+Monstrosaur)s. However, this deck was terrible.


**Among your team, who is most likely to survive in Ixalan?**


Kelvin Chew. He has a stone cold face and has a good character to be a pirate.


**Among your team, who is most likely to become dinosaur food?**


Not me, I hope. (But I know Kelvin and Jérémy will point at me.)




---

**Seat:** C


**Name:** Jérémy Dezani


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Hareruya Writer


**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 2014 Player of the Year, 12 other Grand Prix Top 8s, Pro Tour Theros Champion


**Day 1 Archetype:** Black-White Vampires. Bishop's Soldier is so strong.


**Day 2 Archetype:** Black-White Vampires. I sacrificed Rutless Knave in order to cast spells.


**Among your team, who is most likely to survive in Ixalan?**


Kelvin Chew. He is a robot who doesn't care about dinosaurs. Also, robots have 0% flesh.


**Among your team, who is most likely to become dinosaur food?**


Lee Shi Tian. Nice appetizer. (Editor's note: Both Chew and Dezani completed this questionnaire without consulting each other and came up with the exact same answer.)
