===========================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on September 3, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast

Unfortunately for him, this time it was his opponent's time to shine, and Tsuyoshi Ikeda was crowned champion. We have the Top 8 deck lists from that event, the latest PTQs, the **Magic Online** Championship Series Season 5 finals, and everything happening on **Magic Online**. Check it out!
