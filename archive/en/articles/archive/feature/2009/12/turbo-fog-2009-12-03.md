Headed into Rome and the 2009 World Championships, many players feared the Standard format would be rife with Jund on Jund matchups. For Spain's Joel Calafell, there was no such concern. He brought a Turbo Fog deck of his own design, a build tuned to beat Jund and much of the rest of the format. And beat them he did! At the end of the Standard competition, Joel was undefeated at 6-0. For more information on the 2009 World Championships, [visit the official event coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12).
