The first PTQs for Honolulu have started rolling in, and Bradley Carpenter earned a ticket to the tropical Hawaii playing the tried and true Zoo. His build used [Tidehollow Sculler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tidehollow+Sculler) instead of the sometimes popular [Shadow Guildmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadow+Guildmage), but the truly innovative card he added to the mix was a full playset of [Lightning Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Angel)! Read Mike Flores' [discuss this innovation in the most recent Top Decks](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/td/21). Check out the entire Atlanta Top 8 [here](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/pro-tour%E2%80%93honolulu-qualifying-season-top-8-decklists-2009-01-13), or check out all the PTQ Top 8s [here](/en/events/coverage/pro-tour%E2%80%93honolulu-qualifying-season-top-8-decklists). And of course, find a PTQ near you [here](/en/node/640716).
