Magic Online *Masters Edition IV* Prerelease News
=================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 4, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Some of the best and most beloved cards from **Magic**'s history come to **Magic Online** with *Masters Edition IV*, many for the first time, and you can play with them before they go on sale during the *Masters Edition IV* Prerelease. All *Masters Edition IV* prerelease players will also earn an exclusive [Nalathni Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nalathni+Dragon) prerelease card… the only place this card will be available! 

The *Masters Edition IV* prerelease will feature three separate parts: Sealed Swiss Flights, 64-Player "Top 8" Booster Drafts , and Casual Drafts. See below for details on these great events. 

### *MASTERS EDITION IV* PRERELEASE SWISS SEALED FLIGHTS

*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Friday, January 7 at 9:00 AM PST (17:00 UTC) and ending Saturday, January 8 at 10:00 PM PST (Sunday 6:00 UTC).  
*LOCATION:*  Sealed Swiss Queues room.   
*ENTRY REQUIREMENT:*  30 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 6 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs for this event.  
*SIZE:*  16 Players.  
*DURATION:* Deck building time plus 4 rounds, Swiss Pairings. Play in all four rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 12 | 10 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs | 3 |
| 9 | 4 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs | 1 |
| 6 | 1 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in a *Masters Edition IV* Prerelease Tournament will receive 1 copy of the prerelease [Nalathni Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nalathni+Dragon). Players with at least 9 match points at the end of the tournament will also receive a premium foil Nalathni Dragon. ### *MASTERS EDITION IV* 64-PLAYER "TOP 8" PRERELEASE DRAFTS

*START TIMES:* Scheduled start times beginning Friday, January 7 at 9:00 AM PST (17:00 UTC) and ending Sunday, January 9 at 9:00 AM PST (17:00 UTC).  
*LOCATION:*  Premier Events room.   
*ENTRY REQUIREMENT:*  20 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 3 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs for this event. **Magic Online** will provide an additional 3 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs for the "Top 8" draft.   
*SIZE:*  64 Players.  
*DURATION:* Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, single elimination. The "Top 8" draft consists of drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, single elimination.   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the final standings at the end of the tournament.



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Place** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 1st | 12 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs | 6 |
| 2nd | 8 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs | 5 |
| 3rd-4th | 6 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs | 4 |
| 5th-8th | 4 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs | 3 |
| 9th-16th | 3 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs | 0 |
| 16th-32nd | 2 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in a *Masters Edition IV* 64-Player "Top 8" Draft will receive 1 copy of the prerelease [Nalathni Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nalathni+Dragon). The "Top 8" finalists of the Prerelease Draft will also receive a premium foil Nalathni Dragon. ### *MASTERS EDITION IV* CASUAL DRAFT FLIGHTS

*START TIMES:* On demand beginning Sunday, January 9 at 10:00 AM PST (17:00 UTC) and ending at the beginning of the scheduled downtime on Wednesday, January 26.  
*LOCATION:*  Casual Draft room.   
*ENTRY REQUIREMENT:*  Product  
*PRODUCT:*  3 *Masters Edition IV* booster packs.  
*SIZE:*  2, 4, or 8 Players.  
*DURATION:* Drafting and deck building time plus 3 rounds, Swiss style pairings.   
*PRIZES:* No Prizes for this event.
