Owling Mine was a Pro Tour Top 8ing anti-control deck from Pro Tour-Honolulu. **Magic Online** user 30to1 has brought the archetype back for a modern world of Standard. [Kederekt Parasite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=+Kederekt+Parasite) serves as a damage source while [Jace Beleren](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace+Beleren) and [Howling Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) keep both player's hands stocked. Add a small amount of countermagic and some spot removal, and you have a winner!
