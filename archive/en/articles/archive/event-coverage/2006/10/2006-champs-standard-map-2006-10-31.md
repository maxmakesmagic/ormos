2006 Champs: Standard Map
=========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 31, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations to 2006 Champs Triple Crown winners Thomas Evaristo (Delaware), Brett McCleaf (Kansas), Guillaume Daoust (Quebec), Robert Kadlec (Indiana), and Wilbert Yeung (Rhode Island). Each Triple Crown winner will be sent a case of *Time Spiral* booster packs and a bevy of foil promotional cards, including [Sol Ring](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sol+Ring), [Regrowth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Regrowth), [Vampiric Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor), and [Ball Lightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ball+Lightning).
