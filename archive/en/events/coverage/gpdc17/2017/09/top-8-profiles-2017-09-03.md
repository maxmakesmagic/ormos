Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 3, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











The Top 8 is more than just a pile of cards. It's the players that piloted those decks and weathered the ebbs and flows of a two-day tournament. Here are the players who performed well enough this weekend to make the Top 8 of Grand Prix Washington D.C.


 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpdc17/t8_zhu.jpg)Name:** Yuxuan Zhu


**Age:** 18


**Hometown:** China


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** None


**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Mardu Vehicles. I’ve been playing it for five months.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Gideon, Ally of Zendikar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon%2C+Ally+of+Zendikar). It’s very strong to create tokens.


**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Declaration in Stone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Declaration+in+Stone). It deals with [Hazoret the Fervent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hazoret+the+Fervent).


**What are you most excited about for *Ixalan?*** Vehicles


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** I can go the Pro Tour meet Reid Duke!




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpdc17/t8_mullen.jpg)Name:** Collins Mullen


**Age:** 22


**Hometown:** Durham, North Carolina


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Star City Games Invitation Top 8, two SCG Open Top 8s


**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Green-White Ramp, because it beats Temur Energy.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Hour of Promise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hour+of+Promise). RAMP RAMP RAMP.


**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Oblivion Sower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Sower). It makes [Negate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Negate) look silly post-board.


**What are you most excited about for *Ixalan?*** DINOSAURS


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** It’s my first Pro Tour. I’ve mainly played on the SCG circuit and haven’t had many chances to qualify for the Pro Tour. It means a lot to finally get there.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpdc17/t8_yoo.jpg)Name:** Bradley Yoo


**Age:** 28


**Hometown:** Palo Alto, CA


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 18th place at Pro Tour London during *Kamigawa* block.


**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Temur Energy, because I forgot to bring my Ramunap Red.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Whirler Virtuoso](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whirler+Virtuoso) won me so many matches I thought I had no chance in, especially against Red.


**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Chandra, Torch of Defiance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Torch+of+Defiance)


**What are you most excited about for *Ixalan?*** Merfolk


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** That I get to go to Barcelona!




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpdc17/t8_dimaggio.jpg)Name:** Robert DiMaggio


**Age:** 29


**Hometown:** Charlotte, NC


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** None – it’s my first one!


**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Mono-Black Zombies, it’s a great all-around deck against the meta.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** Liliana’s Mastery. It’s a huge tempo swing to bring you back in the game.


**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Fleetwheel Cruiser](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleetwheel+Cruiser) – it’s a great way to pressure control and ramp decks. It’s also aggressive on the play against Red.


**What are you most excited about for *Ixalan?*** Merfolk!


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** It’s my first-ever Day 2 and Top 8. My team and I spent a ton of time practicing and preparing. It’s an awesome feeling!




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpdc17/t8_severa.jpg)Name:** Matt Severa


**Age:** 38


**Hometown:** Madison, WI


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Winner of Grand Prix DC, Louisville and Denver in 2016.


**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Mardu, because it’s all I’ve been playin for like nine months.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Archangel Avacyn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+Avacyn).


**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Aethersphere Harvester](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aethersphere+Harvester) because it gives you outs to beat Zombies.


**What are you most excited about for *Ixalan?*** Nothing in particular, just a change of pace.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Pro Points and $$$.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpdc17/t8_zhang.jpg)Name:** Bolun Zhang


**Age:** 23


**Hometown:** China


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Top 8 at GP Vegas earlier this year


**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Black-Green Constrictor. I hate mirrors, so I just played something weird.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Glint-Sleeve Siphoner](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint-Sleeve+Siphoner). You play it on the second turn and it doesn’t get removed, you win.


**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Dreamstealer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamstealer). It helps me crush the control decks – my worst matchup.


**What are you most excited about for *Ixalan?*** Dinosaurs and Pirates!


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** It means I get my second Top 8. It gives me more confidence and helps me make more friends all over the world.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpdc17/t8_baumeister.jpg)Name:** Corey Baumeister


**Age:** 26


**Hometown:** Fargo, ND


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Third Top 8 in a row!


**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Mono-White Eldrazi; the deck is so resilient.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Archangel Avacyn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel+Avacyn). It’s busted!


**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Skysovereign, Consul Flagship](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skysovereign%2C+Consul+Flagship) for the third tournament in a row!


**What are you most excited about for *Ixalan?*** Watching brother crush Worlds!


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** It means I pass Brad in the Player of the Year race!




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpdc17/t8_neris.jpg)Name:** Jose Neris Hernandez


**Age:** 27


**Hometown:** San Juan, Puerto Rico


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** Game Day Champion for *Amonkhet!*


**What did you play this weekend, and why?** Ramunap Red.


**What was the best card in your main deck, and why?** [Hazoret the Fervent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hazoret+the+Fervent) is hard to deal with.


**What was the best card in your sideboard, and why?** [Warping Wail](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warping+Wail), to counter [Approach of the Second Sun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Approach+of+the+Second+Sun) and [Fumigate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fumigate).


**What are you most excited about for *Ixalan?*** Dinosaurs


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?** Everything, because my grandmother said I would never win any money playing this game.
