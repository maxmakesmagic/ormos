Announcing *Morningtide*
========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 29, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












The second set in the [*Lorwyn*](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/arcana/1256) Block, code-named “Butter,” is called *Morningtide.*




| **Set Name** | *Morningtide* |
| **Release Date** | February 1, 2008 |
| **Prerelease Events** | January 19-20, 2008 |

Keep watching **magicthegathering.com** for further details on *Morningtide* in the months to come.
