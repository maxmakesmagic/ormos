Special Event: Limited Time Full Innistrad Sealed League
========================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on September 7, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_davidwhitfield.jpg)
By David Whitfield




 David works on the Magic community team as a content specialist. He spends his days writing about Magic Online and trying to play too many colors at once in Limited. 






Visit Innistrad in the special Full Innistrad Sealed League Event, running on *Magic Online* September 14–21!


Looking for ideas for your next vacation destination? Plan a trip to the beautiful plane of Innistrad! Bask in scenic vistas, from the [Nephalia Drownyard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nephalia+Drownyard) all the way to [Stensia Bloodhall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stensia+Bloodhall). Feeling outdoorsey? Take a trip to Kessig and you just might catch a glimpse of local Spirits, like the [Kindercatch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kindercatch) or the [Permeating Mass](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Permeating+Mass)! Whether you’ve come to observe the majestic [Maw of the Mire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Maw+of+the+Mire) or are just trying to keep up on the latest in abomination innovation ([Ludevic has been busy!](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=221173)), there’s a little something for everyone on Innistrad.


Players can enter the Full Innistrad Sealed League starting September 14, 10:00 a.m. PT/5:00 p.m. UTC. Entrants get two *Innistrad*, one *Dark Ascension*, two *Shadows over Innistrad*, and one *Eldritch Moon* booster pack to build from. Once your deck is built, play five matches before the League ends on September 21, 7:00 p.m. PT/2:00 a.m. UTC. Remember, this event will be available for one week only, so play while it's still around!


Enjoy your visit to Innistrad, and don’t forget a [Jar of Eyeballs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jar+of+Eyeballs) to commemorate your trip!

### Full Innistrad Sealed League Event Details


**Start Time**: September 14, 10:00 a.m. All times are Pacific (UTC -7).  
**Closed Time**: September 20 at 7:00 p.m.  
**End Time**: September 21 at 7:00 a.m.  
**Location**:Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Leagues  
**Entry Options**:


* 28 Event Tickets
* 2 *Innistrad* booster packs, 1 *Dark Ascension* booster pack, 2 *Shadows over Innistrad* booster packs, 1 *Eldritch Moon* booster pack, and 4 Event Tickets
* 280 Play Points

**Product**: 2 *Innistrad* booster packs, 1 *Dark Ascension* booster pack, 2 *Shadows over Innistrad* booster packs, and 1 *Eldritch Moon* boosters  
**Format**: Sealed League  
**Structure**: Five matches, each up to 50 minutes  
**Prizes**:




| Match Wins | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 5 Wins | 3 *Eldritch Moon* booster packs, 2 *Shadows over Innistrad* booster packs, and 280 Play Points | 3 |
| 4 Wins | 2 *Eldritch Moon* booster packs, 2 *Shadows over Innistrad* booster packs, and 140 Play Points | 1 |
| 3 Wins | 2 *Eldritch Moon* booster packs, 1 *Shadows over Innistrad* booster pack, and 70 Play Points | 0 |
| 2 Wins | 1 *Eldritch Moon* booster pack and 1 *Shadows over Innistrad* booster pack | 0 |
| 1 Win | 1 *Eldritch Moon* booster pack | 0 |
