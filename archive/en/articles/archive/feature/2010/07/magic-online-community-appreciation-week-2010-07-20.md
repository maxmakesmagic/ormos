Magic Online Community Appreciation Week
========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 20, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











We just want to say congratulations on your [2010 Community Cup win](/en/events/coverage/community-team-captures-cup) once again! Players who contributed to the Community Cup strategy threads will be receiving their *Mirrodin* Block draft set shortly and the Community Cup *Mirrodin* Block Draft Queues will start on Friday, July 23. 

For those of you who were not able participate in the Community Cup threads, don't fret...we have decided to make this whole week Community Appreciation Week! Beginning after the downtime Wednesday, July 21, we will have a bunch of events planned just for you.

The **Magic Online** Community Appreciation Week will consist of 5 parts: 

* Community Cup *Mirrodin* Block Draft Queues
* 12-Ticket *Masters Edition I*, *Masters Edition II*, and *Masters Edition III* Nix Pax Draft Queues
* 9-Ticket *Shards of Alara* Block Booster Nix Pax Draft Queues
* *Urza's Saga* and *Urza's Legacy* Return
* Double **Magic Online** Player Rewards!!

We hope you have fun this week and thanks for your support! 

**COMMUNITY APPRECIATION WEEK DRAFT QUEUES**

This week we will have three special draft queues set up to celebrate your contributions to **Magic Online**. The Community Cup *Mirrodin* Block draft queue will be available for the weekend as a place to use the *Mirrodin* Block draft set you received as part of the 2010 Community Cup. In addition, we will have reduced Event Ticket *Masters Edition* and *Shards of Alara* Block drafts available for you to enjoy.

COMMUNITY CUP *MIRRODIN* BLOCK DRAFTS  
*START TIME:* On demand beginning Friday, July 23, at 9am PDT (16:00 UTC) to Monday, July 26, at 9am PDT (16:00 UTC).  
*LOCATION:* 8-Player Draft room.

*SIZE:* 8 Players.

***URZA'S SAGA* AND *URZA'S LEGACY* RETURN**

These classic sets return for an encore week in the **Magic Online** Store, complete with release-style Sealed Deck Events! There is one small difference from the earlier release events, however. These events only require 2 Event Tickets to join! Check the **Magic Online** Premier Events room for times. 

*URZA'S SAGA* BLOCK "TOP 8" SWISS SEALED EVENTS   
*START TIME:* Scheduled start times beginning after the downtime on Wednesday, July 21. See the **Magic Online** Premier Event Room for start times.   
*LOCATION:* Premier Event room.

**DOUBLE MAGIC ONLINE PLAYER REWARDS POINTS FOR ALL EVENTS**

Finally, during the Community Appreciation Week we will double all MOPR points you earn for playing in events. That's right, each event you play in (beginning after the downtime July 21 until the downtime on July 28) will give you twice as many points! Have fun and thank you!
