Top 25
======


1. Romão Leaps into Top 25

When just 3 Pro points separate No. 13 from No. 24, you can expect any strong Grand Prix finish to send anyone in that range shooting up the charts—in this case Carlos Romão's Top 8 finish at [Grand Prix São Paulo](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpsao17) sent him skyrocketing up from No. 25 to No. 13—a whopping twelve-place leap.


Beyond Romão, there were few changes. Javier Dominguez went up three spots and Thiago Saporito and Martin Jůza got slight bumps, while a whole slew of folks stepped back to let those three rise.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [AndyGrey's Jeskai Gift](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-08-15)

We've clearly reached the "What if we add another color?" stage of brewing with [God-Pharaoh's Gift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=God-Pharaoh%27s+Gift), now sporting red. Red grants the deck a few things—[Cathartic Reunion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cathartic+Reunion) to fill the graveyard, [Insolent Neonate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Insolent+Neonate) to also fill the graveyard, and a semi-transformational sideboard that lets the deck go all [Glorybringer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glorybringer) and [Chandra, Torch of Defiance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Torch+of+Defiance) on some people.


But there are a few non-red innovations as well. [Trophy Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trophy+Mage) can helpfully search up [Gate to the Afterlife](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gate+to+the+Afterlife) while standing in as a serviceable body (and blocking fodder), and [Walking Ballista](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista), previously not seen in this deck, can be played for zero to fill the yard quickly, can just do regular [Walking Ballista](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista) things, or it can even come back as a 4/4 with [God-Pharaoh's Gift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=God-Pharaoh%27s+Gift), ready to load up with counters.
