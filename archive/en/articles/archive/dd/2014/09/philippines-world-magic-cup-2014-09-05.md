Philippines World Magic Cup
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on September 5, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 







[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Gray%20Merchant%20of%20Asphodel)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gray%20Merchant%20of%20Asphodel)


 This past weekend, the second round of WMCQs took place around the world. Spots on all countries' World *Magic* Cup teams were won, and now there is only one spot for each country left up for grabs. If you would like to earn one of these spots, check out [the WMCQ schedule](http://magic.wizards.com/en/wmcq/schedule) and make plans to attend your country's final WMCQ for the year!




 In the Philippines, Philip Rama won his way into the World *Magic* Cup playing the boogeyman of Standard, Mono-Black Devotion. Over the past year, Mono-Black Devotion has won more tournaments and has been more popular than any other deck. Drawing on the strength of [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), [Pack Rat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat), and [Mutavault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutavault), this black deck is excellent at exchanging resources while coming out ahead. It seemingly has the answers for nearly every archetype, and has some of the toughest threats to deal with.




 However, many of the key cards in the deck are going to rotate once *Khans of Tarkir* enters Standard. Its key threats in [Pack Rat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat) and [Desecration Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desecration+Demon) are rotating out. Its main card drawing in [Underworld Connections](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underworld+Connections) is also rotating. Will *Khans of Tarkir* have the tools needed to keep [Gray Merchant of Asphodel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gray+Merchant+of+Asphodel) on top of Standard?
