Earlier this week we brought you news of a resurgence in the Elves strategy for Extended from ages past. Adding to the list of refurbished hits shaking the dust off and making the final tables of [Pro Tour Qualifiers](/en/node/641451) is Death Cloud. Coomaster took this version to the Top 8 of a [Magic Online](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=Magic/Digital/MagicOnline.aspx) PTQ, where another copy of the deck also managed to make the single-elimination rounds.
