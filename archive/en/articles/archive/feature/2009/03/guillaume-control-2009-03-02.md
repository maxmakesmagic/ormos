 Prior to Pro Tour-Kyoto this past weekend, Yokohama was the last Japanese city to host Magic's biggest tournament series. The player who proved victorious then? None other than French superstar Guillaume Wafo-Tapa. He ground his opponents out on the back of a brutal control deck that could take over nearly every aspect of the game. Of course, to read more about what happened in Kyoto, make sure to [check out the event coverage](/en/node/640936).
