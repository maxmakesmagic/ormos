Magic in Spectrum 15
====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 13, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











If you spend any time in the Fantasy and Science Fiction section of your local book store, we're not a bit surprised. And you might have seen the lavish *Spectrum 15,* the annual compilation of the best in Fantastic Art.


![Spectrum 15](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/102_spectrum.jpg)  
We're proud to announce that the jury of Daren Bader, Tim Bodendistel, Frank Cho, Kelley Seda, and Justin Sweet selected several pieces of **Magic** art. Here they are in reasonably large sizes, but you'll have to get the book to see them in their true magnificence!
