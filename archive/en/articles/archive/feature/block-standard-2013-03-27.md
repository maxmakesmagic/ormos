From Block to Standard
======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 27, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 






 Yesterday, [I wrote about](/en/articles/archive/jund-all-formats-2013-03-26) a strategy's successful port from Standard to Block Constructed. Today, I'm going to examine a deck that has recently exploded on **Magic Online** despite not yet having physical results that can be seen as an updated port of a Block deck. 

![Unburial Rites](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Unburial+Rites) The Block deck is the WBR deck [I've previously written about](/en/articles/archive/spark-trooper-control-2013-03-20) — what I consider Block's answer to midrange Jund strategies. 

 It's unusual to see several copies of a deck 4–0 daily events on **Magic Online** without previous tournament success, so I asked Prolepsis9 where this deck came from. This deck was reportedly designed by Good Gamery's NicotineJones, who's been responsible for a number of other innovative decks recently, and was reportedly popularized by cjlack92's MOCS [Top 4 finish](http://www.wizards.com/magic/Digital/MagicOnlineTourn.aspx?x=mtg/digital/magiconline/tourn/5088522) with the deck. 

 Like its Block predecessor, this deck uses [Boros Reckoner](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Reckoner); [Aurelia, the Warleader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aurelia%2C+the+Warleader); and [Obzedat, Ghost Council](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obzedat%2C+Ghost+Council) as its core creatures, and supports them with removal and [Rakdos's Return](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rakdos%27s+Return). 

 Unlike the Block version, this deck gets to take advantage of the *Innistrad* Block trio of flashback spells [Lingering Souls](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lingering+Souls), [Unburial Rites](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unburial+Rites), and [Faithless Looting](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faithless+Looting), which all play marvelously together. 

This is cjlack92's more recent version from a later Standard Daily Event.
