**Upcoming Standard MOCS Monthly on Saturday, May 6**


A *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) Standard Monthly Event is coming up on May 6! Players can spend 35 Qualifier Points (QPs) to enter the event. Players with 6 or more match wins in the 8 rounds advance to a MOCS Playoff event on August 5. MOCS Playoff events qualify top finishers for the Magic Online Championship, a Pro Tour, and the Pro Tour Challenge event at that Pro Tour.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points for the year as a whole. Top players on the Leaderboard at the end of the 2017 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.


We look forward to seeing you on May 6 for the latest Monthly Event! Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online* Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

**Upcoming *Amonkhet* Sealed MOCS Monthly on Sunday, May 7**


A *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) *Amonkhet* Sealed Monthly Event is coming up on May 7! Players can spend 35 Qualifier Points (QPs) to enter the event. Players with 6 or more match wins in the 8 rounds advance to a MOCS Playoff event on August 5. MOCS Playoff events qualify top finishers for the *Magic Online* Championship, a Pro Tour, and the Pro Tour Challenge event at that Pro Tour.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points for the year as a whole. Top players on the Leaderboard at the end of the 2017 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.


We look forward to seeing you on May 7 for the latest Monthly Event! Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online* Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

**The May MOPR Promo period is underway**


The May MOPR period runs from April 26 to May 31. Here are the promo cards:




|
|  |
| Event Promo

Archfiend of Ifnir | Store Promo

Unlicensed Disintegration |

Play in one or more events during the May MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium Archfiend of Ifnir. Five or more and you'll get an additional premium Archfiend of Ifnir. Spend at least $20 total in the *Magic Online* Store during this MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium Unlicensed Disintegration. By spending $40 or more, you'll get an additional premium Unlicensed Disintegration.
