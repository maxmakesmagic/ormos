*Masters Edition IV* is coming to **Magic Online**! The product page is available now, and a few cards have already been confirmed as being included. One triumvirate that will be of particular interest to players who have been around the block a time or two is the Urzatron: [Urza's Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Tower), [Urza's Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Urza%2527s%2BMine), and [Urza's Power Plant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Urza%2527s%2BPower%2BPlant). Together those three lands have powered some of the game's most powerful decks, like the above build of Izzetron created and played by Pro Tour champion Osyp Lebedowicz. Find out more on the new set at the official product [page](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/ProductArticle.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/mastersed4/productinfo).
