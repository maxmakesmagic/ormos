### MAGIC ONLINE EVENTS


Magic Online Journey into Nyx Championship Winners
==================================================


June 16, 2014
 










UglyCat


FilippoK



Thanks for the introductions, Iroas. Congratulations to the winners of the *Journey into Nyx* Championship! UglyCat was victorious in the Constructed Championship, while FilippoK claimed the title of Limited Champion!

Block Constructed Format Recap
[Top](#top)

**Block Constructed Game One**


The finals saw UglyCat's Red/Green/White ramp deck face off versus krom321's Black/Green/White [Eidolon of Blossoms](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eidolon+of+Blossoms) deck.


UglyCat started the first game with a [Sylvan Caryatid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Caryatid) into [Xenagos, the Reveler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos%2C+the+Reveler). The 2/2 Satyr was all that remained of [Xenagos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos) as krom321 cast a [Banishing Light](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banishing+Light).


![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150920165952im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/JOU_Const_Champ_Game_One_Turn_3-2.png)  

UglyCat built his mana with a second [Sylvan Caryatid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Caryatid) and a [Voyaging Satyr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyaging+Satyr) on the following turn, while krom321 brought out the powerful [Courser of Kruphix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courser+of+Kruphix). The mana ramp of UglyCat allowed him to cast a turn 5 [Revel of the Fallen God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Revel+of+the+Fallen+God), a turn 6 [Elspeth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth) (killing krom321's just cast) [Doomwake Giant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomwake+Giant), and a second [Revel of the Fallen God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Revel+of+the+Fallen+God) on turn 7. The band of Satyrs frolicked all over krom321's life total to allow UglyCat to take game one.


**Block Constructed Game Two**


In the second game, Krom321 started off with a turn 3 [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) taking a [Banishing Light](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banishing+Light). UglyCat again ramped with a [Voyaging Satyr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyaging+Satyr) into a turn 3 [Xenagos, the Reveler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos%2C+the+Reveler). A [Brain Maggot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brain+Maggot) took a second [Banishing Light](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banishing+Light) from UglyCat. With only a second [Sylvan Caryatid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Caryatid), UglyCat attacked with two Satyrs and a [Voyaging Satyr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyaging+Satyr) taking krom321 down to 11.


With Krom321 missing a land play on turn 4, krom321 conceded when UglyCat cast a [Stormbreath Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormbreath+Dragon) and made a third Satyr.


![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150920214956im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/JOU_Const_Champ_Game_Two_Turn_5.png)  

Congrats to UglyCat for winning the *Journey into Nyx* Block Constructed Championship!


You can view the brackets and the event details for the Constructed Championship [here](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/MagicOnlineTourn.aspx?x=mtg/digital/magiconline/tourn/7195038).

Limited Format Recap
[Top](#top)

The finals of the *Journey into Nyx* Limited Championship saw FilippoK's white-red aggro deck facing off versus BennyBoy2525's green-black Constellation deck.


**Limited Game One**


FilippoK came out fast in the first game with his first picks from *Journey into Nyx* and *Born of the Gods* in the first three turns of the game. The [Stonewise Fortifier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stonewise+Fortifier) followed by [Brimaz, King of Oreskos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brimaz%2C+King+of+Oreskos) were joined by an [Akroan Mastiff](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Mastiff) before BennyBoy2525 conceded game one of the finals without casting a single spell.


![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150920215044im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/JOU_Champ_Game_One_Screen_v2.png)  

  



![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150923131351im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/JOU_Champ_Game_Two_Screen_3.png)  

While game two did not go much better for BennyBoy2525, he did manage to cast a few spells. A turn 2 [Golden Hind](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Hind) traded with a [Oreskos Swiftclaw](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oreskos+Swiftclaw). FilippoK was missing land drops but did manage to cast a [Traveling Philosopher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Traveling+Philosopher) and [Akroan Line Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Line+Breaker). BennyBoy2525 got to six mana and played a [Archetype of Finality](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archetype+of+Finality). The [Archetype](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archetype) blocked and killed the [Akroan Line Breaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Line+Breaker) before dying to a final point of damage from [Forgeborn Oreads](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forgeborn+Oreads). The final blow of the match came when BennyBoy2525 cast a [Grim Guardian](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Guardian) and enchanted it with [Nyx Infusion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nyx+Infusion). FilippoK saw fit to [Chained to the Rocks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chained+to+the+Rocks) the [Grim Guardian](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Guardian) at which point BennyBoy2525 conceded the match.




Congratulations to FilippoK for winning the title of *Journey into Nyx* Champion!
