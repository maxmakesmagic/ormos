What starts off as a simple Bant deck quickly becomes anything but! Krazy Kirby is happy to use a mill strategy on opponents if the efficient beatdown of green, white, and blue's best creatures doesn't work. [Archive Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archive+Trap) and [Hedron Crab](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hedron+Crab) will get the job down. Or turn them on yourself to make [Knight of the Reliquary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary) lethally huge out of nowhere. It's a great plan for this week's [Friday Night Magic](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm)!
