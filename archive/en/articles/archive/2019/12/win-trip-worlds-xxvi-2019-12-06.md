Win a Trip to Worlds XXVI
=========================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles?source=MX_Nav2020)
 on December 6, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/update_worlds_contest.jpg)


Ready for World Championship XXVI? We here at Wizards can't wait, and we are eager for our fans to experience it themselves! We're so eager in fact, that we're hosting a contest for two full trips for two to the event.


Want to go? All you have to do is design your own *Magic*-themed Hawaiian shirt and submit it to us at [MagicCommunityTeam@wizards.com](mailto:MagicCommunityTeam@wizards.com). Please note, **submissions must be a JPG or PNG and cannot contain third party copyrights or violations**.


Gee, that sounds great Wizards, but what else do we get if we win? Great question! We will have two grand prize winners, and each grand prize will include:


* Round trip airfare for 2 to the World Championship in Honolulu, Hawaii
* Car service pickup and drop-off from airport
* Four nights of hotel accommodations
* 2 tickets for the after party
* Two signed play mats of all 16 participants in Worlds
* Dinner with casters and players
* A 15-minute meet and greet with a key member of Studio X
* Lounge seating for Friday and Saturday, and reserved seating for Sunday
* A backstage tour and event swag for two at the Championships
* Two uncut foil mythic sheets of *Theros Beyond Death*
* 2 full foil sets of *Theros Beyond Death*

So get your creative juices flowing and start designing some awesome *Magic*-themed Hawaiian shirts. We're excited to see what you come up with, and can't wait to greet you at the event next year!
