The September 1, 2017 Update
============================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on September 1, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on September 1, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by [Pirates vs. Dinosaurs](https://youtu.be/bjbGRkRWVRo).


Also, a note. Monday is a holiday in the United States, so there won't be a Daily *Magic* Update. There will, however, be normal articles—and previews start in earnest on Monday as well. Enjoy!


What to Watch This Weekend
==========================


1. Grand Prix Turin/Grand Prix Washington, DC, Viewing Extrabonanza | [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic) | Saturday 1 a.m. PT/4 a.m. ET/8 a.m. UTC

So here's what's going on. Grand Prix Turin and Grand Prix Washington, DC, are sending Standard out in style. We'll start with Turin, because they live slightly more in the future, and then once coverage of Turin wraps, we'll throw to the crew in Washington, DC, for basically round-the weekend coverage of two Standard Grand Prix. Monday is a holiday in the US—might as well use the weekend to watch all of the *Magic*.


1. [Exploring *Ixalan*: A Look Inside *Magic: The Gathering* and the Best Worlds in Fantasy Gaming](http://west.paxsite.com/schedule/panel/exploring-ixalan-a-look-inside-magic-the-gathering-and-the-best-worlds-in-f) | [twitch.tv/pax3](https://www.twitch.tv/pax3) | Saturday, 1:30 p.m. PT/4:30 p.m. ET/8:30 p.m. UTC

To answer your first question—yes, there will be preview cards. To answer your second question, yes, the Twitch channel is pax3. Make sure you get that 3 in there. If you're on-site at PAX, these panels are taking place in the Wyvern Theater. Panelists will include Franchise Creative Director Jeremy Jarvis, Senior Art Director Cynthia Shepard, Senior Game Designer James Wyatt, and artist Tyler Jacobson.


After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [The Mechanics of *Ixalan*: Explore, Raid, Enrage, Vehicles, and Double-Faced Cards](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/the-mechanics-of-ixalan-explore-raid-enrage-vehicles-and-double-faced-cards/) | ChannelFireball | Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa

PVDDR explores *Ixalan* (I'm going to be using that one a lot). And since he's No.-1-in-the-world-Player-of-the-Year-Hall-of-Fame-Pro Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa, that should be enough to get you interested.


1. [*Ixalan*!](http://www.toplevelpodcast.com/ixalan/) | Top Level Podcast | Mike Flores and Patrick Chapin

The title is certainly to the point. Flores and Chapin talk previews thus far and, naturally, compare everything to cards that came before. There's even a [Gnarled Mass](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gnarled+Mass) reference!


1. [*Commander 2017* Design with Gavin Verhey pt 1](http://www.commanderinmtg.com/2017/09/01/e107-commander-2017-design-with-gavin-verhey-pt-1.html) | Commanderin' | Phil DeLuca, Sean Whatson, and Shivam Bhatt

I know we're all excited about *Ixalan*, but *Commander (2017 Edition)* *did* just come out. Gavin Verhey—he makes *Magic* cards, don't ya know?—talks about the design of the latest *Commander* decks and also about why Wizards are the best. Maybe not that last part. But maybe.


1. [Planeswalker Rule and You](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/aliaintrazi-09012017-planeswalker-rule-and-you/) | Gathering Magic | Ali Aintrazi

We didn't just get new cards this week—we also got an updated planeswalker rule, which is just that they function like legendary everything now. Aintrazi dives in to what that means across all formats. There are a number of surprising implications in here.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Approach of the Second Sun](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-08-31)

With Temur Energy out in full force this weekend, it could be a good time for [Fumigate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fumigate) and, as a result, a good time for the [Approach of the Second Sun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Approach+of+the+Second+Sun). The deck has put up results here and there, and with two Grand Prix this weekend, we could see one of them grab an elusive Top 8. The list below went 5-0 in a Competitive Standard Constructed League in the hands of GriffGriff, so it's certainly possible to run through a gauntlet filled with Temur.


The deck itself is not great against control decks like Blue-Red Control, so its success might vary based on how many [Torrential Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk)s show themselves this weekend. After all, resolving a seven-mana sorcery is no easy feat.
