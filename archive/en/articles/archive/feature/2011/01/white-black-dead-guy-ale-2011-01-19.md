The latest StarCityGames.com Open has concluded, and this time the event was in [San Jose](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/20916_Live_Coverage_of_StarCityGamescom_San_Jose.html). The Sunday portion of the competition featured the Legacy format, and Ricky Wen managed to get the 4th place finish using a white-black deck called "Dead Guy Ale." The first iteration of the deck was created by Chris Pikula, a founding member of the legendary **Magic** team "the Deadguys."
