Top 8 Profile - Darwin Kastle
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPMAN00/Images/T8Kastle.jpg)**Name:** Darwin Kastle  
**Age:** 29  
**Location:** Medford, MA, USA  
**Occupation:** Professional Magic player  
**Played Magic since:** The Dark  
**Best accomplishments:** 1st Rio Invitational, 1st Pro Tour DC, 2nd Pro Tour Atlanta, 3rd Pro Tour Paris, 3rd (at the first ) Pro Tour LA.



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Manchester?**  

A: I like England and this is how I make my living.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: My expectations were very high because I spend a lot of time preparing.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: I feel that I've been playing well. I have yet to lose a game where I had a good mix of land and spells.



**Q: What do you think about the tournament location-/organisation-wise?**  

A: I'm quite pleased. There should be more premier events in England.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: I'm a very strong limited player and I was well prepared.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: It means a good start to making my living playing Magic.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: This is an elite top eight, anything can happen.
