Round 3: Rich Frangiosa vs. Valentin Moskovich
==============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 26, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_602.png)
By Matt Urban











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpphi02/a977.jpg)Valentin Moskovich is the hot player of the match, coming off his GP Cleveland victory and looking to make it two American Grand Prix in a row. Rich Frangiosa is a local boy, growing up only 15 minutes from the tournament site. He has an impressive resume, including a 6th place finish at Pro Tour DC and several other high finishes. He doesn't play as often as he used to, now focusing on outside concerns. 

### Game 1

Rich won the die roll and elected to play first. Both players avoided the dreaded trip across the Atlantic and the action began. Rich's black/red deck opened quickly with a turn 1 [Goblin Taskmaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Taskmaster). It got in for 2 damage before trading with Valentin's [Disciple of Malice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+Malice). Valentin third land play revealed him to be 3 colors: red, black, and green. With the 3 lands, Valentin summoned a [Leery Fogbeast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leery+Fogbeast). Not the best card in a 3 color deck, but it could be problematic against Rich's empty board.

Rich came back with successive [Screeching Buzzard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Screeching+Buzzard)s over the next two turns, serving to both hold off the Fogbeast and attack through the air. After playing his fifth land, Valentin cast a powerful card: [Centaur Glade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Centaur+Glade). Rich, realizing the pressure was on, kept attacking with the Buzzards and increased the evasion pressure with [Gluttonous Zombie](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gluttonous+Zombie). The Zombie was answered with a [Nantuko Husk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Husk), but Valentin had no other play than cycle an off color creature. Rich kept attacking with the Buzzards, eventually casting [Solar Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solar+Blast) and [Erratic Explosion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Erratic+Explosion) targeting Valentin's nugget for the win.

Frangiosa - 1, Moskovich - 0

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpphi02/a978.jpg)Valentin elected to play and no one took a mulligan. His deck contained numerous cycling cards allowing him to play first despite the 3 color mana base. Playing only [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp)s for the first 2 turns did, Valentin cycled another off color creature in response to Rich's [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith). Unfortunately for Valentin, his 3rd turn land play came into play tapped, and Rich took advantage of this by summoning a 3/1 [Spined Basher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spined+Basher) after attacking with the [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith). Fortunately for Valentin, he drew a second red source, this time a mountain, to cycle [Solar Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solar+Blast) and kill the [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith).

Rich kept the attack going with a [Nosy Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nosy+Goblin), but when Valentin summoned [Disciple of Malice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+Malice) and [Nantuko Husk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Husk), it stabilized the board. Valentin followed up by summoning [Treespring Lorian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treespring+Lorian) and playing [Contested Cliffs](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+Cliffs), planning on just picking off Rich's team one at a time. Rich managed to kill the Lorian, but Valentin came through with another bomb in [Oversold Cemetery](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oversold+Cemetery). With recurring beasts, [Contested Cliffs](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+Cliffs), and 13 life, Valentin was able to take the game going away.

Frangiosa - 1, Moskovich - 1

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpphi02/a979.jpg)Rich elected to play, and mulliganed down to 6. Valentin seemed happy with his 7. Rich again led off with a turn 2 [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith), and that one play dominated the entire game. A turn 3 [Goblin Sky Raider](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sky+Raider) let Rich kill a Morph, and [Embermage Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embermage+Goblin) fell the next turn. It seemed like Valentin was trying to use the [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith)'s drawback to kill Rich, and it did manage to get Rich down to 13. While keeping Valentin's side of the board clear, Rich was summoning evasion creatures in the form of [Screeching Buzzard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Screeching+Buzzard) and [Gluttonous Zombie](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gluttonous+Zombie).

At 9 life, Valentin began trying to stabilize with [Treespring Lorian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treespring+Lorian) and [Contested Cliffs](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+Cliffs). He used the Cliffs to kill [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith), but Rich managed to kill the beast by shooting it with the [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) for 2, and cycling [Solar Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solar+Blast) to finish the job. Eventually, Rich's Buzzard and Goblin air force brought Valentin down to 3, when [Chain of Plasma](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+of+Plasma) finished the job.

**Final Result:** Frangiosa defeated Moskovich 2-1
