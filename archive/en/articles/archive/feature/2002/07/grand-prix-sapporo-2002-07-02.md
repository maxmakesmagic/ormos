The **Magic: The Gathering** Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro Tour events.

### TOURNAMENT FORMAT AND SCHEDULE

The format for Grand Prix - Sapporo is *Odyssey* Block Constructed. All DCI rules and **Magic** Floor Rules announced on October 1, 2001, along with all subsequent updates, will be enforced.

For the latest edition of the DCI and **Magic** Floor Rules, please refer to the [DCI Rules](http://archive.wizards.com/dci/main.asp?x=UTR_Intro) web site. 

* **Friday, August 23:** Registration from 15:00 - 21:00. All teams must register by 21:00 Friday night. LATE REGISTRATION WILL NOT BE ALLOWED. Teams that cannot meet this deadline should contact the organizer at the mail address listed below no later than August 22.
* **Saturday, August 24:** Tournament begins at 09:00. The Top 64 players advance to the finals on Sunday.
* **Sunday, August 25:** Finals begin at 08:30.

### TOURNAMENT

* **Divisions**  
 The main tournament at Grand Prix - Sapporo is open to **Magic** players of all ages.


If a player that wins an invitation to Pro Tour - Houston at Grand Prix - Sapporo is already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher. Previous qualification may occur through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid on the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).
* **Tournament Structure**  
 The structure for Grand Prix - Sapporo is modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds, with the number of rounds determined by the number of participants), with the Top 64 players advancing to the Sunday finals for prizes and invitations.
* **Tournament Byes**
 *(Byes are not cumulative through the season or year.)*  
 Byes for Grand Prix - Sapporo are based on the Pro Tour - Nice 2002 professional standings and DCI ratings as of June 19, 2002. Please refer to the [Grand Prix - Sapporo Bye List](/en/articles/archive/feature/grand-prix-sapporo-byes-2002-07-02) for details.
