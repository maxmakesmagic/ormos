**Announcing the *Fate Reforged* Championship Winners**


The *Fate Reforged* Championship winners have been decided! Want to see how a champion handles a Draft? How about some Championship-level strategy? Relive the twists and turns of both the Limited and Standard Championship Finals and find out who walked away with the title of *Fate Reforged* Champion.


Full winning decklists as well as match play-by-plays are available in Monday's [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-fate-reforged-championship-winners-2015-03-23).

***Dragons of Tarkir* Streaming Events**


To give players a first look at *Dragons of Tarkir* on *Magic Online*, we will be hosting a *Dragons of Tarkir* Preview Draft! To show off the new set, we've invited popular streamers from far and wide to put it through its paces.


We'll be holding two events, one on Wednesday, April 1, at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7), and the second on Thursday, April 2, at 1:00 p.m. Pacific. A booster pack of *Dragons of Tarkir* is on the line for each match win, awarded with the start of Prerelease on Friday, April 3.


For information about which streamers are playing which days, as well as links to their Twitch channels, check out the table below. Luis Scott-Vargas and Kenji Egashira's streams will be hosted on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic)!

**Legacy Cube and *Rise of the Eldrazi* 6-2-2-2 Drafts are Available Now**


The Legacy Cube recently received an [update](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/legacy-cube-update-2015-03-02) in response to its first run of Events, leaving it better than ever. You can try out the updated Legacy Cube for yourself right now! Play in some Cube Events while you have the chance because they're only around until the Wednesday, April 1 downtime.


As a bonus for the reality-devouring titan enthusiasts amongst you, we'll be running *Rise of the Eldrazi* 6-2-2-2 Drafts throughout the same time period! You can find full event details [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-announcements-march-10-2015#item4)

**New Episode of Vintage Super League Tonight**


The [Vintage Super League](http://vintagesuperleague.com/) pits *Magic* celebrities against one another with *Magic*'s oldest and most powerful cards as their weapons of choice. Check it out tonight at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7) on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic)!


For a review of the current state of the league, as well as look at what's coming up in tonight's episode, take a look at Randy Buehler's latest [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-episode-8-2015-03-24).


You can also see the league's decklists [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-season-2-decklists). As of Wednesday, March 18, the page has new decklists for the third phase of the Vintage Super League!

**Double Pro Tour Qualifier Weekend**


There's a double Pro Tour Qualifier Finals weekend coming up! Players can qualify for the Saturday, March 28, Modern PTQ Finals from Wednesday, March 25, through Friday, March 27.


Not much of a Modern player? Qualify for the Sunday, March 29, *Khans of Tarkir/Fate Reforged* Sealed PTQ Finals from Wednesday, March 25, through Saturday, March 28.


Not familiar with Pro Tour Qualifiers on *Magic Online*? Learn how to qualify for Pro Tour *Magic Origins* from the comfort of your computer [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/content/pro-tour-qualifiers-magic-origins).

**Extended Downtime for March 25, 2015**


**No download is required this week.**


[MOCS](http://magic.wizards.com/en/2015-magic-online-championship-series#2015schedule) and [MOPR](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2015-magic-online-player-rewards) promo cards will be granted during the downtime.



* All tournaments will close Wednesday, March 25, at 12:00 a.m. Pacific (7:00 UTC)
* Store and trade activity will be suspended at approximately 2:45 a.m. Pacific(9:45 UTC)
* The system will be down from 3:00 a.m. Pacific (10:00 UTC) until 12:00 p.m. Pacific (19:00 UTC)
