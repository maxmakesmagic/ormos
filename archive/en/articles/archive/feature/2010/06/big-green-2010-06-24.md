A deck that saw some play in the *Zendikar* Block Constructed format is Big Green. It seeks to stall the early game, then explode with a giant Eldrazi or [Summoning Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoning+Trap). Paul Ernest Lazaro made the second day of competition at Grand Prixâ€“Manila with this updated-for-Standard take on the deck. Splashing white for [Wall of Omens](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Omens) and [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile), Paul does an even better job stalling. Either cast [Summoning Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summoning+Trap) or hard-cast a giant Eldrazi and voila! Dead opponent.
