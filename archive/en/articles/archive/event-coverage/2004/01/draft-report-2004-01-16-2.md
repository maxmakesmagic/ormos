Draft Report:
=============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 16, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_paulsottosanti.jpg)
By Paul Sottosanti











TableSix's draft order went a little something like this; 1 Nicolai Herzog [NOR]  
 2 Christophe Haim [FRA]  
 3 Osamu Fujita [JPN]  
 4 Pab Galache Fernandez [ESP]  
 5 David Brucker [DEU]   
 6 Mark Zadjner [CAN]  
 7 Jose Pabon [USA]  
 8 Marc Le Campion [ESP] 

Herzog kicked off the draft with a pack containing the following playable cards: [Goblin Dirigible](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Dirigible), [Domineer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domineer), [Annul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Annul), [Goblin Replica](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Replica), [Skeleton Shard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skeleton+Shard), [Blinding Beam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blinding+Beam), [Copper Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr), [Viridian Longbow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Longbow), and [Seat of the Synod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seat+of+the+Synod). He chose the shard and Christophe selected the powerful [Domineer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domineer). Fujita, to Haim's left, strangely chose the Longbow over all of the colored cards. On Herzog's right, Le Campion took [Goblin Dirigible](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Dirigible) and then [Power Conduit](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Power+Conduit), signaling that he wasn't interested in Blue as he left both Seat and [Annul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Annul) on the table. 

Christophe first picked [Fangren Hunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fangren+Hunter) and showed his intent to go Green/Blue. Herzog wheeled [Disciple of the Vault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+the+Vault) and [Wall of Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Blood). To his right, Le Campion seemed unsure of each and every pick, but at least was staying out of Black.

Out of Osamu's pack, Herzog picked up a Talisman and a second [Disciple of the Vault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+the+Vault), both staples of an affinity based deck. .Haim received only a [Battlegrowth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlegrowth).

![Oblivion Stone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Oblivion+Stone)Fernandez opened a pack with both [Oblivion Stone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Stone) and [Icy Manipulator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Icy+Manipulator), which went in the obvious order. Zadjner took a [Fangren Hunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fangren+Hunter), seemingly ignoring the fact that Brucker was already Green directly in front of him. Herzog picked up a [Pewter Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pewter+Golem) and a [Welding Jar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Welding+Jar).

Out of Brucker's pack Herzog got [Rustspore Ram](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rustspore+Ram) and Haim picked up a Talisman and a [Wurmskin Forger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wurmskin+Forger). Le Campion seemed to have decided on Red as one of his colors, but it didn't seem like he had chosen another and Herzog was in no hurry to commit either.

Zadjner opened and took another [Fangren Hunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fangren+Hunter), which prompted raised eyebrows from Brucker, who had just first picked [Plated Slagwurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Slagwurm). Nicolai then took [Vulshok Berserker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vulshok+Berserker) after Le Campion selected [Serum Tank](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serum+Tank) in front of him, signaling a possible fight for Red. Haim picked up a [Cobalt Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cobalt+Golem).

Out of Pabon's pack Warhammer of course went first. Le Campion stayed mono-Red with [Lodestone Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lodestone+Myr), giving [Terror](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terror) to Nicolai and [Deconstruct](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deconstruct) to Haim. Brucker then wheeled [Blinding Beam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blinding+Beam), which was either a hate draft a signal that he was looking to move into White.

Le Campion, in seat eight, opened and took [Shatter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shatter). Herzog grabbed a [Bonesplitter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bonesplitter) and Haim received his second [Domineer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domineer) (over [Somber Hoverguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard)).

At the end of the first round of packs, the color distribution looked something like this:

1 Nicolai Herzog - B  
 2 Christophe Haim - G/U  
 3 Osamu Fujita - B/R  
 4 Pab Galache Fernandez - W/U  
 5 David Brucker - G/w  
 6 Mark Zadjner - G  
 7 Jose Pabon - U/W/r  
 8 Marc Le Campion - R

Marc took [Crystal Shard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crystal+Shard) out of his second pack and Pabon was happy to receive a [Clockwork Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clockwork+Dragon). Herzog received a gift in the form of eighth pick [Irradiate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Irradiate) along with a perfect counterpart of [Great Furnace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Great+Furnace). Haim, on the other hand, seemed to be in trouble, as he was consistently getting nothing out of his late picks.

![Platinum Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Platinum+Angel)Pabon took [Domineer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domineer) over [Quicksilver Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quicksilver+Elemental), leaving Zadjner to scoop up [Deconstruct](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deconstruct) in front of a frustrated Brucker. Haim finally got a reprieve in the form of the sixth pick [Quicksilver Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quicksilver+Elemental), while Herzog only got an off-color artifact land. Le Campion started showing thoughts towards Green with [Turn to Dust](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turn+to+Dust) and [Tel-Jilad Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Exile).

Zadjner was forced to first pick a Myr out of his pack, while Haim got [Cobalt Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cobalt+Golem) and Nicolai received a [Bottle Gnomes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bottle+Gnomes) to go with his [Skeleton Shard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skeleton+Shard).

Brucker opened yet another [Fangren Hunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fangren+Hunter) and took it without much hesitation. Haim was stuck with [Clockwork Vorrac](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clockwork+Vorrac). Nicolai hesitated between [Krark-Clan Grunt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krark-Clan+Grunt) and Myr Retreiver, but finally took the Grunt while looking rather agitated.

[Platinum Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Platinum+Angel) was taken by Fernandez from an otherwise uninspiring pack. Haim picked up [Tel-Jilad Archers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Archers) and Herzog got only a [Copper Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Copper+Myr) with his fourth pick.

Osamu was happy to receive a [Shatter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shatter), and Haim picked up yet another [Domineer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domineer). With this being his third, it was now at the point where his opponents might sideboard out as many artifact creatures as possible. Still, his only other choice was [Wizard Replica](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wizard+Replica), so the pick made sense.

Haim opened [Broodstar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodstar) and takes a quick look through his pile. The other options were [Deconstruct](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deconstruct), [Bonesplitter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bonesplitter), [Tel-Jilad Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Exile) and [Somber Hoverguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard). He doesn't see enough artifacts and ended up taking the [Deconstruct](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deconstruct). Nicolai was happy to take his second [Bonesplitter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bonesplitter), and Le Campion selected the [Broodstar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodstar), finally cementing himself in Blue. At this point he was probably regretting not taking the [Seat of the Synod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seat+of+the+Synod) earlier.

Herzog's pack presented him with the options of [Pyrite Spellbomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyrite+Spellbomb), [Leaden Myr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leaden+Myr), [Vault of Whispers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vault+of+Whispers), [Duskworker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duskworker), or [Nim Replica](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Replica). He took the fiery potion and Haim received a [Turn to Dust](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turn+to+Dust) to finish things up for the second round.

Interestingly, not a single [Spikeshot Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spikeshot+Goblin) had been opened yet.

 2 Christophe Haim - G/U  
 3 Osamu Fujita - B/R  
 4 Pab Galache Fernandez - W/U  
 5 David Brucker - G/w  
 6 Mark Zadjner - G  
 7 Jose Pabon - W/U/r  
 8 Marc Le Campion - R/U

![Irradiate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Irradiate)Herzog quickly remedied that by busting one open in his third pack. He also opened a gift for Haim in the form of [Viridian Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Shaman). Osamu seems happy to receive [Granite Shard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Granite+Shard) and Zadjner picked up an [Elf Replica](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elf+Replica) while staring pointedly at Haim and his three [Domineer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domineer)s.

Haim had the choice between [Neurok Spy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Neurok+Spy) and [Predator's Strike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Predator%27s+Strike) and selected the blue card. Brucker surprisingly took [Clockwork Condor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clockwork+Condor) over the [Predator's Strike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Predator%27s+Strike) so it landed in the hands of Zadjner. Herzog wheeled [Great Furnace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Great+Furnace) and [Moriok Scavenger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moriok+Scavenger).

Osamu looked unhappy with his pack and made an unorthodox pick of [Granite Shard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Granite+Shard) over [Bonesplitter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bonesplitter). Fernandez, of course, couldn't be happier as the equipment fits right into his White deck. Zadjner then took [Somber Hoverguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Somber+Hoverguard) and seems to be thinking about adding blue to his deck.

Out of Fernandez's pack Herzog has the choice of [Nim Shrieker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nim+Shrieker) or [Irradiate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Irradiate) and quickly selected the Shrieker as it's an important part of Black-based affinity decks. Haim's bad luck continued as he only finds another Talisman.

Brucker opened a boatload of White and Black cards, but stuck to his guns and took a [Tel-Jilad Chosen](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Chosen). [Promise of Power](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Promise+of+Power) makes its way into Herzog's deck fifth, while Haim again only gets a mediocre card. Osamu selected [Woebearer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woebearer), hoping to wheel [Consume Spirit](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Consume+Spirit) off of Pab. It worked and he gets it back ninth.

Zadjner made a rude gesture or two towards his final pack after he saw the contents. He took [Neurok Spy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Neurok+Spy). Herzog takes [Irradiate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Irradiate) over the powerful but situational [Barter in Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barter+in+Blood), perhaps thinking of his two Disciples. Osamu then took [Vulshok Berserker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vulshok+Berserker) over it. With Fernandez and Brucker both respectfully leaving it alone, Osamu finally takes it tenth. Insanity

Speaking of insanity, Pabon's pack included [Crystal Shard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crystal+Shard), [Pentavus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pentavus), and [Spikeshot Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spikeshot+Goblin). He took the consistently ridiculous Shard. Marc thought as long as he could but finally took [Pentavus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pentavus) and Herzog happily kept his monopoly on [Spikeshot Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spikeshot+Goblin)s. Haim got a [Tel-Jilad Chosen](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Chosen). Osamu is rewarded with a late [Relic Bane](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Relic+Bane) that should fit nicely into his aggressive Black/Red deck.

The final booster saw Marc selecting [Thirst for Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thirst+for+Knowledge) and Herzog taking [Frogmite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite). Haim found a much-needed [Neurok Spy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Neurok+Spy) waiting for him.

 2 Christophe Haim - G/U  
 3 Osamu Fujita - B/R  
 4 Pab Galache Fernandez - W/U  
 5 David Brucker - G/w/r  
 6 Mark Zadjner - G/u  
 7 Jose Pabon - W/U/r  
 8 Marc Le Campion - R/U

![Pentavus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Pentavus)"A bad day for affinity," said Nicolai at the close of the draft. No [Myr Enforcer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Enforcer)s, few [Frogmite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frogmite)s, no Shriekers, and few Spellbombs meant that the potential for that strategy was definitely hurting. Still, double [Bonesplitter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bonesplitter) and double Spikeshot with solid support should pave the way to at least a 2-1 record.

Haim seemingly had nothing going his way except for his ability to take control of enemy artifact creatures. His deck is far from exciting and probably won't result in better than 1-2.

As for the rest, both of the White/Blue drafters seemed to pick up solid decks complete with bombs: [Crystal Shard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crystal+Shard) and [Clockwork Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clockwork+Dragon) for Pabon, [Oblivion Stone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Stone) and [Platinum Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Platinum+Angel) for Fernandez. Sharing Green worked out for the most part for Brucker and Zadjner, so both of them should have the chance to pick up a couple wins. The only player whose deck may not have come together was Le Campion, as he seemed unsure and unfocused with many of his picks. Still, [Broodstar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodstar) and [Pentavus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pentavus) certainly have a way of winning games.

Only time (and three rounds of **Magic**) will tell who wins this table.







#### Nicolai Herzog
