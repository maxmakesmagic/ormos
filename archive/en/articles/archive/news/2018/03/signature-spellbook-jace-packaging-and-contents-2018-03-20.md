Signature Spellbook: Jace Packaging and Contents
================================================



 Posted in **News**
 on March 20, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*Signature Spellbook: Jace* is a collection of eight cards plus one random premium foil version of one of those eight cards for the serious blue mage. Each card has Jace-flavored art and special frames just for this set. Check out the packaging and the eight cards below!


*Signature Spellbook: Jace* releases June 15, 2018.
