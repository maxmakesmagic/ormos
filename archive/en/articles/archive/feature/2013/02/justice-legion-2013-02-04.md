Justice of the Legion
=====================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 4, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samstoddard.jpg)
By Sam Stoddard




Sam Stoddard came to Wizards of the Coast as an intern in May 2012. He is currently a game designer working on final design and development for Magic: The Gathering.
 







It's Boros Week, and the Boros Legion wants you!... to play it in Modern. While traditionally a deck that has existed just on the fringes of competitiveness in the format, the recent updates to the [banned and restricted list](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/feature/232) for Modern of [Bloodbraid Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf) and [Seething Song](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seething+Song), combined with the added goodies in *Gatecrash*, may just give the deck the extra oomph needed to start seeing the top tables. 

![Boros Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Boros+Charm) This particular deck comes from a **Magic Online** Daily, where it 4â€“0ed right around [Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica*](/en/events/coverage/cifka-savors-pro-tour-breakfast-champions). The deck features some of the hardest-hitting one-drops in the format, [Steppe Lynx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steppe+Lynx) and [Figure of Destiny](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Figure+of+Destiny), as well as two of the most versatile removal spells in [Lightning Helix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Helix) and [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt). If games do go long (if you consider the fourth turn long), a cracked fetchland and an Elspeth pumping either a [Steppe Lynx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steppe+Lynx) or a [Plated Geopede](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plated+Geopede) should be enough to take out most opponents. 

 What will this deck get from *Gatecrash*? It's hard to say for sure, but there are plenty of options. Certainly, [Boros Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Charm) is an early candidate, providing some protection against removal as well as another way to close the gap between your opponent's life total and 0. [Legion Loyalist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Legion+Loyalist) and [Boros Elite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Elite) (combined with adding [Goblin Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide) to the deck) are options for pushing down the deck's converted mana cost and speeding up the clock even more, and [Skullcrack](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skullcrack) is a perfect sideboard card for decks that attempt to resort to lifegain to escape the justice of the Legion.
