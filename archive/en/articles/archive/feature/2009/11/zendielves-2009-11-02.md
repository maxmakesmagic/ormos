If you thought Elves in Standard were a thing of the past with the rotation of *Lorwyn* and *Shadowmoor*, think again! Pedestrian used this list successfully on **Magic Online**. Check out the powerful interactions with [Nissa Revane](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa+Revane) and the way [Oran-Rief, the Vastwood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oran-Rief%2C+the+Vastwood) makes an army of tiny monsters into a far more imposing force. Toss in [Great Sable Stag](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Great+Sable+Stag) and a touch of white for the sideboard, and you've got a brand new Standard masterpiece!
