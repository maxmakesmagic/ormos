*Lorwyn* and *Shadowmoor* have brought about a resurgence in the popularity of the Faeries tribe, but did you know Faeries have been around **Magic** since the very beginning? All the way back at the very first World Championships in 1994, French player Bertrand Lestree managed to take this deck, featuring Fae representative [Argothian Pixies](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Argothian+Pixies), to the final table before losing to Zak Dolan. Why did Bertrand play the Pixies over more popular cards of the day like [Elvish Archers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Archers)? Because it couldn't be killed by [Mishra's Factory](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Factory)*or* [Juggernaut](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Juggernaut), both common threats of the era! Find out which cards from the exciting new set *Shards of Alara* you could add to Bertrand's deck with the [*Shards of Alara* Visual Spoiler](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=mtg/tcg/shardsofalara/spoiler).
