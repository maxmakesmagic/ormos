Round 1 Feature Match: Hearts of Silver
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 11, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_SteveSadin.jpg)
By Steve Sadin












Antonino De Rosa (Wolves and Angels) vs. Luis Scott-Vargas (White-Green Humans)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


[Gabriel Nassif vs. Elie Pichon](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/ptavr12/fm2)


The last time Antonino De Rosa and Luis Scott-Vargas (LSV) played against each other was in the quarterfinal of U.S. Nationals in 2006. At the time, De Rosa was one of the top American Pros and a heavy favorite to win the event, while LSV was virtually unknown outside of the **Magic** Online community.


LSV managed to pull off (what was then considered) a stunning upset, defeating De Rosa, and ultimately earning himself a spot on the U.S. National Team.



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptavr12/r1_derosa_vs_lsv.jpg)
*The last time Antonino De Rosa and Luis Scott-Vargas played was in the 2006 U.S. Nationals, where LSV claimed victory. Will history repeat itself?*

Seven years after their last match, their roles have been reversed, with De Rosa now playing the role of the underdog against the utterly dominant LSV.


### Game 1


LSV opened on [Avacyn's Pilgrim](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avacyn%27s+Pilgrim), [Silverblade Paladin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silverblade+Paladin), and [Mayor of Avabruck](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mayor+of+Avabruck), while De Rosa found himself stuck on only two lands, dying before he got the chance to play a single spell.


Antonino De Rosa 0, Luis Scott-Vargas 1



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptavr12/r1_derosa.jpg)
*De Rosa: "Sorry I couldn't make that game more exciting."*

### Game 2


De Rosa began the second game with a [Scorned Villager](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scorned+Villager), while LSV got off to another fast start with his White-Green Humans deck, playing a [Champion of the Parish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Champion+of+the+Parish) and a [Avacyn's Pilgrim](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avacyn%27s+Pilgrim).


A miraculous [Bonfire of the Damned](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bonfire+of+the+Damned) wiped LSV's board, and [Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells) plus [Wolfir Silverheart](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolfir+Silverheart) allowed De Rosa to even the match up at one game apiece.


Antonino De Rosa 1, Luis Scott-Vargas 1


### Game 3


LSV took a mulligan, but he was able to get off to quick lead with two copies of [Avacyn's Pilgrim](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avacyn%27s+Pilgrim), [Champion of the Parish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Champion+of+the+Parish), and a [Slayer of the Wicked](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slayer+of+the+Wicked) (which took out De Rosa's [Scorned Villager](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scorned+Villager)).



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptavr12/r1_lsv.jpg)
*"LSV starts the game out with a lead..."*

However, that lead would prove to be short-lived as a [Fiend Hunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiend+Hunter), a [Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells), and a [Garruk Relentless](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Garruk+Relentless) allowed De Rosa to take over the game.


A [Fiend Hunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiend+Hunter) of his own bought LSV a bit of time, but a [Wolfir Silverheart](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wolfir+Silverheart) and a [Huntmaster of the Fells](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Huntmaster+of+the+Fells) were more than enough for De Rosa to pull off the upset against the captain of team ChannelFireball.


Antonino De Rosa 2, Luis Scott-Vargas 1
