Grand Prix Atlantic City Video Delay
====================================



 Posted in **News**
 on May 8, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Due to shipping issues with the Grand Prix video equipment, the live video coverage of Grand Prix Atlantic City will be delayed. The new start time for Saturday video coverage is 6 p.m local time (ET)/3 p.m. PT/10 p.m. UTC. If there are any further updates on the start time, information will be communicated via the *Magic*Pro Tour Twitter account, [@magicprotour](http://www.twitter.com/magicprotour).



Wizards of the Coast apologizes for this inconvenience.
