What are you doing on September 10, 2011? Do you have plans? Cancel them. Go to **Magic Celebration** instead!

**Magic Celebration** is a one-day **Magic** event that's going to happen simultaneously across the world. Here are the general details:

Each Magic Celebration player receives one ***Magic** 2012* Booster Pack (while supplies last) and then plays a Mini-Master event, adding a booster for every match won.

The first four customers at a participating retail location who purchase a Deckbuilderâ€™s Toolkit at a **Magic** Celebration event will receive a t-shirt! *(T-shirt sizes are L and XL. While supplies last. Void where prohibited. Offer good one per person.)*

And not only *that*, but there will be codes for cool digital items from **Duels of the Planeswalkers** 2012\*.

\* While supplies last. May require purchase of **Duels of the Planeswalkers** 2012 and respective game account. Offer valid through 3/31/2012. Offer good in select countries.
