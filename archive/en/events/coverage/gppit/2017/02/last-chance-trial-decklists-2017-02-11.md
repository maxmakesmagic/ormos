Last Chance Trial Decklists
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 11, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











Before every Grand Prix, there are the tournaments before the tournament. In the quest to secure byes before the main event starts, players compete in Last Chance Trials, commonly known as "grinders," a single-elimination, winner-take-all (the byes) format. Lance Hutton, Shawn Doherty and Michael Swarlis won Sealed grinders, while Christopher Chichy's Affinity deck took down a Modern event.


That leaves the Standard events, where players got a jump start on the weekend with these lists.
