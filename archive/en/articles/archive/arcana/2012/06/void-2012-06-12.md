In the Void
===========



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 12, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Here's [Into the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Into+the+Void) from *Avacyn Restored*.

![Into the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Into+the+Void)  
There's something interesting about this card. Which is a good thing, because otherwise this Arcana would have a problem. Let's blow the art up and take a closer look.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/992_intothevoid.jpg)[Into the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Into+the+Void) | Art by [Daarken](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Daarken%22%5D)

See those two things being swooped up into what is presumably the void? You've seen them before. Click here to see where!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/992_hellrider.jpg)[Hellrider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hellrider) | Art by [Svetlin Velinov](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Svetlin+Velinov%22%5D)


It's the devil and its mount from [Hellrider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hellrider), as seen in *Dark Ascension*!
