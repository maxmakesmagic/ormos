Fan of playing Goblins? Then **magicthegathering.com** forum user HKDizzy has the deck for you! Combining some of *Shards of Alara*'s coolest new cards with some that already existed in Standard, this black-red-green concoction is a great starting point for the upcoming States and Provincial championships. [Sarkhan Vol](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sarkhan+Vol), [Goblin Assault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Assault), and [Blightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blightning) team with [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander), [Chameleon Colossus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chameleon+Colossus), and [Murderous Redcap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Murderous+Redcap) to dominate the combat step. To find out more information about States (including how to get special unreleased promo cards for attending as well as the nearest location to you) visit [www.mtgchamps.com](http://mtgchamps.com/).
