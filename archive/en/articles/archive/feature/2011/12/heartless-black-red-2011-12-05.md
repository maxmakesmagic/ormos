Heartless Black-Red
===================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 5, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The quirky *Innistrad* rare [Heartless Summoning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heartless+Summoning) pops up in Standard decklists here and there, but usually it appears alongside green fatties or blue control elements. User Octop on **Magic Online** bucked this trend entirely, running a black-red take on the enchantment. Powering out [Solemn Simulacrum](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solemn+Simulacrum), [Olivia Voldaren](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Olivia+Voldaren), and even [Bloodgift Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodgift+Demon), Octop cruised this deck all the way to a 4-0 finish in a recent Daily Event. Could this finally be the breakout [Heartless Summoning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heartless+Summoning) deck we've been waiting for? Shuffle it up and see for yourself!
