Nakamura Gifts
==============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 23, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The Magic World Championships this past weekend featured the relatively new Modern format. No one was quite sure how the metagame was going to look, but 2011 Hall of Fame inductee Shuhei Nakamura certainly had put some work into the format. After going 6-0 in Modern with this highly innovative [Gifts Ungiven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven) deck, the famous Japanese deckbuilder has put a brand new archetype on the radar. For more on Worlds, check out the [full coverage](/en/events/coverage/japan-stands-tall-worlds-again) page!
