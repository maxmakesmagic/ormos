An Old Favorite
===============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 12, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson











Ok, so maybe "favorite" isn't the right word.

![Unflinching Courage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Unflinching+Courage) I'm once against featuring Patrick Chapin's decklist this week, since he did an amazing job cranking out decklists for *Born of the Gods* Standard. One of his lists was a Bant Hexproof redux, although this version looks much fairer than previous versions with [Invisible Stalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Invisible+Stalker) and [Geist of Saint Traft](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft). Instead, this deck features [Witchstalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Witchstalker) and [Ascended Lawmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascended+Lawmage). 

 As it turns out, even when Bant Hexproof isn't fully powered, it's still pretty good. Decks like Mono-Blue Devotion are largely about racing, and that's tough to do against a hexproof [Baneslayer Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel). [Supreme Verdict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Supreme+Verdict) is an issue, but [Voice of Resurgence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence), [Eidolon of Countless Battles](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eidolon+of+Countless+Battles), and [Mending Touch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mending+Touch) in the sideboard help fight it. 

 There is not a lot of hate, which is understandable considering Bant Hexproof isn't on the radar. Also, Mono-Black Devotion will likely cut [Devour Flesh](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Devour+Flesh)es for [Bile Blight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bile+Blight)s, which makes things a little easier. I'm not sure about the validity of Bant Hexproof in Standard, but I wouldn't be surprised if it made a splash.
