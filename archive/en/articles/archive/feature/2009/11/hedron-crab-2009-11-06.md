The global phenomenon of **Magic** Game Day has come and gone for 2009. In the British Isles, Glenn Goldsworthy opted for a different path to victory: decking the opponent. Using [Hedron Crab](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hedron+Crab), [Mind Funeral](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Funeral), and [Archive Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archive+Trap),cards quickly spin from the top of the opponent's library to the graveyard. [Angelsong](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelsong) and [Day of Judgment](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Day+of+Judgment) keep the ground protected long enough to finish the job!
