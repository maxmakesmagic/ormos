Pro Tour Badges
===============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 6, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Do you know how many Pro Tours there have been? A lot, that's how many! And a lot of them had laminated badges. That many badges would probably look pretty cool all together, right?

![Pro Tour Badges](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/726_badges.jpg)  
Now here's the kicker: we didn't have all these badges lying around the office. They belong to Pro Tour Hall of Famer Raphael Levy. All of them.

![Raphael Levy](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/726_levy1.jpg)  
That's a guy who's been to a *lot* of Pro Tours.
