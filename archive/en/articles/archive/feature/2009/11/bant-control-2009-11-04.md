Your 2009 **Magic** Game Day has come and gone! Matthew Cerino had a very successful run in Doylestown, Pennsylvania with a new interpretation on Bant. The blue-green-white deck played powerful spells like [Rhox War Monk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhox+War+Monk) and [Baneslayer Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel). [Bant Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bant+Charm) and [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) keep creatures off the board, and when you need to refill your hand? [Mind Spring](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Spring) is all you need to rebuild!
