Looking for a fun deck on a theme that has existed in **Magic** since the very first set? We present to you: Spiders! Featuring every creepy crawly member of the tribe (changelings not included), including a few wannabes like [Orbweaver Kumo](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Orbweaver+Kumo) and [Spinneret Sliver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spinneret+Sliver), Spiders! is a walk down an arachnidan memory lane. Plus, there are even a few Spider-themed spells to help you out of any tight spots you get into!
