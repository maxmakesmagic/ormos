Pro Tour–Honolulu Photo Essay: Hawaii Highlights
================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 5, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_45.png)
By Bill Stark and Craig Gibson











Things are on a roll here at Pro Tour–Honolulu! Here’s a look at some of the sights from the latest (and tropicaliest) addition to the Pro Tour family...

**The Good**

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/PTHon.jpg)*Ah, Hawaii ...*  
  
  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/landscape.jpg)*Now taking votes for making this a yearly event*  
  
  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/ConCenter.jpg)*The lovely Hawaii Convention Center, home of Pro Tour–Honolulu*  
  
  
**The Bad**

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/JanDoise.jpg)*Jan Doise shows off his souvenir from the beaches of Oahu*  
  
  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/HJTobyElliot.jpg)*Head Judge Toby Elliott reminds players: NO SLOW PLAY!*  
  
  
**Something Free**

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/DraftSet.jpg)*Brandon Scheel receives his free draft set from Mark Purvis at the player party*  
  
  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/Cheon.jpg)*Paul Cheon got pretty worked up about the free Pro Tour apparel*  
  
  
**Something New**

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/Malin.jpg)*Reigning world champ Antti Malin enjoys a pork luau*  
  
  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/Band.jpg)*Local musical flavor at the player’s dinner*  
  
  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/New.jpg)*Welcome to the Pro Tour, fellas!*  
  
  
**Something “Old”**

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/OldPros.jpg)*Some of the veteran faces in attendance this weekend: Finkel, Wafo-Tapa, Herberholz, Ruel, Rubin, Phillips, Williams, and Levy*  
  
  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/Turian.jpg)*Mike Turian is back in the Champion Challenge seat*  
  
  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/pthon09/Players.jpg)*Nearly 400 players battle for the championship title*  
  
More to come all weekend long, so stay tuned!
