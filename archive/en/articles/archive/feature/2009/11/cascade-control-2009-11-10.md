How do you beat a deck like Jund where nearly all of its cards are more than one card? If you're Andy Hanson at the Hiawatha, Iowa **Magic** Game Day, you do it by playing more such spells! Most of the cards in Andy's deck have cascade, meaning when he casts [Bloodbraid Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf) he's almost assured to turn up [Esper Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Esper+Charm) or [Blightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blightning), which are plus two cards either way you slice it. A powerful strategy for the new world of Standard.
