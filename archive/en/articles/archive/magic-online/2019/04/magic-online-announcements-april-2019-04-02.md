Legacy Cube Until April 25
--------------------------


Do you like that one card, from that one set, and you just never see it anymore? Fear not, *Legacy Cube* is in full swing, and you may see it again! *Legacy Cube* is full of some of the best cards from across Magic's history, working together in ways you can only experience in a Cube. And the fun doesn't stop for nearly a full month, ending April 25.


For more information on this update of the *Legacy Cube*, check out [designer Paul Cheon's article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/march-2019-legacy-cube-overhaul-2019-03-19).

MTGO *Guilds of Ravnica* Redemption
-----------------------------------


Redemptions for regular *Guilds of Ravnica* sets return tomorrow, April 3. Sets will be available for redemption until Wednesday, May 5, or while supplies last. Premium sets will have their cutoff extended to this date as well. Thank you for your patience.




---

Vintage Super League Finals!
----------------------------


After nine weeks of fierce competition, only two players are left standing: Rachel Agnes ([@baetog\_](https://twitter.com/baetog_)) and Andreas Petersen ([@ecobaronen](https://twitter.com/ecobaronen)). Each player has submitted three decks and, tonight, will battle to be the first to win three matches. Tune in tonight for the finale at 6 p.m. PT on [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic)!

*Magic Online* Championship Formats
-----------------------------------


The 2018 MOCS is coming up in the middle of May (the weekend of May 18 to be exact!), and it's time to reveal the formats that our competitors will be playing! Our 24 competitors will be playing Standard, Modern, and *War of the Spark* Draft over the weekend to determine who earns the right to be called this year's *Magic Online* Champion.




---

Legacy MCQ Finals Saturday
--------------------------


On Saturday, April 6, there will be a Legacy Mythic Qualifier Finals at 9 a.m. PT. The event will cost 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points, and the winner will receive an invitation to Mythic Championship Barcelona.


Learn more about Mythic Championship Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-mythic-championship-qualifiers).




---

Limited MCQ Finals Sunday
-------------------------


On Sunday, April 7, there will be a Limited MCQ Finals at 7 a.m. PT. The set will be *Ravnica Allegiance*, and the event will cost 1 Limited MCQ Finals Token. The winner will receive an invitation to Mythic Championship Barcelona.


Learn more about Mythic Championship Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-mythic-championship-qualifiers).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format challenges are the first step toward qualifying for premier play through your favorite *Magic* formats. To learn more about Format Focus events and premier play qualification, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/2019-magic-online-championship-2018-11-12).


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges continue every weekend for Modern, Vintage, Legacy, and Pauper formats except when replaced by Format Playoffs during scheduled Format Playoff dates.
