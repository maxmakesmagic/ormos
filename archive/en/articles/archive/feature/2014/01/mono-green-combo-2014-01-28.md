Mono-Green Combo
================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 28, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson











![Abundant Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Abundant+Growth) This is one of the strangest and most delightful decks I've seen in quite some time. Your lands only produce green mana on their own, but the rest of your deck features cards from every other color. As it turns out, [Snap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snap) and/or [Cloud of Faeries](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloud+of+Faeries) combined with any land that makes more than one mana allows you to do a lot of crazy stuff. You just have to get a little creative. 

 The goal is to enchant a Forest or two with as many [Fertile Ground](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fertile+Ground)s and [Utopia Sprawl](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Utopia+Sprawl)s as possible, continually untap them with [Cloud of Faeries](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloud+of+Faeries) and [Snap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snap), and eventually finish the game with a large [Rolling Thunder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rolling+Thunder). In the meantime, you get to draw cards with [Mulldrifter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter), [Sea Gate Oracle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sea+Gate+Oracle), and [Coiling Oracle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coiling+Oracle). Should you need a little help getting some damage through, [Aura Gnarlid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aura+Gnarlid) can be your finisher. 

PigNorton's deck is an amazing example of thinking outside the box, and I expect decks like this to remain a staple of Pauper.
