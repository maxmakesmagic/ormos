No, we're not talking about the film by James Cameron. Gunslinger Dave Guskin, who took this beauty to the Prerelease in Seattle, WA, was all about Titans. He played both [Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan) and [Inferno Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inferno+Titan) as well as the mana acceleration and stall to make sure he could cast them. Once he did? He had a dead opponent in no time, sometimes by combo'ing [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle) with [Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan)!
