![Emeria, the Sky Ruin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Emeria%2C+the+Sky+Ruin)A fan of White Weenie? At the Nashville $5k, hosted by StarCityGames.com, Shane Giachetti took this build to a top performance. Abusing [Emeria, the Sky Ruin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emeria%2C+the+Sky+Ruin) by playing both [Kor Cartographer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kor+Cartographer) and [Knight of the White Orchid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+White+Orchid), the deck almost always has enough [Plains](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains) on the battlefield to trigger the *Zendikar* rare. Add powerful spells like [Baneslayer Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel), [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile), and [Ajani Goldmane](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani+Goldmane) and you have a competitive masterpiece!
