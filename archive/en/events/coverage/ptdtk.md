PRE-SHOW COVERAGE



Much like the landscape of Tarkir, the Standard Constructed format has been re-forged. What sorts of surprises will the world’s top players bring with them to Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir*? Who will overcome three days of Standard and Booster Draft to emerge with the Pro Tour trophy, the $40,000 first prize, and Platinum status in the Pro Tour Players Club? Find out April 10-12, as we bring you live coverage of all three days of *Magic*!
