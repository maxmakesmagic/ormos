Decks of the Week: 04/20/2012
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 20, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Congratulations to Marco Orsini Jones, one of the first players to earn a spot on his country's World **Magic** Cup team. Orsini Jones won a World **Magic** Cup Qualifier in London, England, and will be representing England at the World **Magic** Cup this summer at Gen Con. World **Magic** Cup Qualifiers are being held around the world from now until July 1, and you can find more information on these Standard-format events [here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/worldmagiccup/qualifiers).
