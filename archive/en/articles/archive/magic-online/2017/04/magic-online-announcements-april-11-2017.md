***Legacy Cube* starts tomorrow, April 12**


That's right! *Legacy Cube* is coming back for another round. Check out Jules Robins's article [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/legacy-uphold-2017-03-30) for more details on structure and cardlist changes, or go straight to the full cardlist [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/legacy-cube-cardlist).

***Aether Revolt*-*Kaladesh* Sealed MOCS Open on Saturday, April 15**


A Sealed *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) Open Event is coming up on Saturday, April 15! No Qualifier Points are needed; players use Event Tickets or Play Points to enter instead. The winner of the tournament will receive an invitation to Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation* in Kyoto and an invitation to the *Magic Online* Championship!


We look forward to seeing you on April 8 for the latest event! Learn more about the 2017 Magic Online Championship Series here.


[Top](#top)

***Kaladesh*-*Aether Revolt* Sealed Pro Tour Qualifier on Sunday, April 16**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, April 16!


Remember, the Pro Tour Qualifier Preliminaries now follow a set weekly schedule, thanks to the redesigned PTQ system that *Magic Online* Events Manager Lee Sharpe announced [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/pro-tour-qualifier-preliminary-schedule-changes).


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#HOU_schedule).

***Modern Masters 2017* and Throwback Standard events end tomorrow, April 12**


*Modern Masters 2017* and the Throwback Standard Gauntlet events end tomorrow! This is your last chance to jump in a queue and draft like a master or relive the days of *Mirage*-*Tempest* Standard! [Find out more details on *Modern Masters 2017* here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/modern-masters-2017-edition-events-constructed-event-changes-2017-03-15) and details on [Throwback Standard Gauntlets here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/flashback-standard-gauntlets-mirage-tempest).
