Fans of the Grand Melee format will be excited to see Dylan Austin’s decklist from GenCon 2008’s Grand Melee tournament. He bested over a hundred other players to repeat as champion from 2007! Using a combination of [Rites of Flourishing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rites+of+Flourishing), [Crucible of Worlds](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crucible+of+Worlds), and [Walk the Aeons](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walk+the+Aeons) Austin attempted to take an arbitrarily large amount of turns, then kill his opponents with [Oona, Queen of the Fae](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oona%2C+Queen+of+the+Fae). The plan worked, and he emerged victorious! Find out which cards from the exciting new set Shards of Alara you could add to Dylan’s deck with the [Shards of Alara Visual Spoiler](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/shardsofalara/spoiler).
