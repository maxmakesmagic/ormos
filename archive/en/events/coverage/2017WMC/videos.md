2017 World Magic Cup
--------------------


The Rising Suns
===============




Team Japan—comprised of Kenta Harane, Yuuya Watanabe, and Shota Yasooka—wins the 2017 World *Magic* Cup!

 



Dec 1-3, 2017


Palais des Congrès Nice Acropolis



Japan
