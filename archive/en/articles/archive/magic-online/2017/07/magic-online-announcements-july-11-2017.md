**Standard PTQ for Pro Tour *Ixalan* Saturday, July 15**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, July 15 at 9 a.m. PT. There is no need to qualify through a Preliminary game – simply join this event with 30 Event Tickets, 300 Play Points or a spare Blue Constructed PTQ Finals Entry Token!


Learn more about Magic Online Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

**Build Notes**


The following notes are from the July 5 build:


Limited | [Naga Vitalist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naga+Vitalist) is now able to add mana of any color when you also control a [Cascading Cataracts](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cascading+Cataracts).


Limited | [Scarscale Ritual](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scarscale+Ritual) can now correctly only put -1/-1 counters on creatures.


Commander | [Lifeblood Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lifeblood+Hydra) no longer hurts you for having negative power.


Limited | [Vastwood Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vastwood+Hydra) distributes the correct number of +1/+1 counters upon death.


Collection | Flip cards can again be searched for by name.


Commander | [Phyrexian Soulgorger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Soulgorger) will now correctly gain more than 1 age counter.


Standard | [Kalitas, Traitor of Ghet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kalitas%2C+Traitor+of+Ghet) will make the correct number of tokens with [Anointed Procession](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anointed+Procession).


Limited | [Watchers of the Dead](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Watchers+of+the+Dead) will correctly leave split cards alone if not chosen.


Commander | [Profane Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Profane+Command) now respects the X in its cost both in terms of reanimating, and giving all its legal targets fear.


General | You can now correctly redirect spells that can target a player but originally did not.


Commander | [Strionic Resonator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strionic+Resonator) can now be resonated. Strionically.


Commander | If you’re lucky enough to control multiple Planeswalker emblems, they will now all display their correct art.


Commander | [Panoptic Mirror](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Panoptic+Mirror) now correctly reflects instants and sorceries.


Standard | [Tezzeret the Schemer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret+the+Schemer)’s Etherium Cells now properly indicate if they were made this turn by right clicking and looking for “Newly Controlled.”


Commander | [Grinning Ignus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grinning+Ignus) can now correctly only grin at sorcery speed.


General | Damage Prevention shields now display immediately on the permanent or player they are protecting.


Legacy | [Doomsday](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomsday) now functions correctly with undo. Good luck, Have fun.


General | Stabwhisker is a little less Odious and will no longer crash the game.


Legacy | [Dismember](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember) no longer gets around [Trinisphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinisphere) without paying 3 mana.


Duel Scene | Yield All (F8) now respects zero mana plays.


Commander | [Mana Bloom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Bloom) now functions correctly.


General | [Suffer the Past](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Suffer+the+Past) no longer suffers from incorrect mana costs.


General | [Dragonlair Spider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonlair+Spider)’s tokens now use the correct art.


Commander | [Ooze Flux](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ooze+Flux) now correctly secretes oozes.
