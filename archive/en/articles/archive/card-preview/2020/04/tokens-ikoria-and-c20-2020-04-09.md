The Tokens of Ikoria and C20
============================



 Posted in **Card Preview**
 on April 9, 2020 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Kendall Pepple











A set filled with monsters deserves a menagerie of too-cool tokens with eye-popping art. Check out this gigantic collection of a combined 31 full-art tokens and one emblem that you can create with cards from *Ikoria: Lair of Behemoths* and *Commander (2020 Edition)* just below.


If you haven't yet seen the cards that make these tokens, step cautiously through these creature-packed [Card Image Galleries for IKO](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/ikoria-lair-behemoths) and [C20](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/commander-2020-edition)â€”just watch out for giant footprints!
