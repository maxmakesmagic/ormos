Decks of the Week: 4/22/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on April 22, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Welcome to the calm before the Eldrazi storm. The new set [hits shelves on Friday](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/release-facts) and is immediately legal to play in Constructed formats. Get a jump on your deckbuilding by perusing the top decks across mutliple formats in **Magic Online** and see where new [*Rise of the Eldrazi* cards](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/riseoftheeldrazi/spoiler) might fit in to push a deck over the top!
