Round 6 Feature Match: Illuminati vs. JBL
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 29, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











Zvi built up his defense right from the start, with [Patrol Hound](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Patrol+Hound) and [Hypochondria](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypochondria). [Phantom Nomad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nomad) made things even better, and Berkowitz only had two gray ogres out as defense. They swung back and forth for four damage total, and Berkowitz brought out a strong defender in [Dreamwinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder). Zvi summoned a second [Phantom Nomad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nomad) and ended his turn, content to halt Berkowitz's attack. [Frightcrawler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler) was able to get through Zvi's defenses a bit, and was joined by [Zombie Assassin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Assassin). Zvi brought out [Dwarven Driller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dwarven+Driller), which is either great, horrible or underrated depending on who you talk to. Berkowitz took the first game.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpnj02/916.jpg)Landis opened against Gary with [Stern Judge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stern+Judge) and [Militant Monk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Militant+Monk), while Gary cast [Chatter of the Squirrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chatter+of+the+Squirrel) twice, followed by [Rabid Elephant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rabid+Elephant). Landis' board became more impressive, as he brought out [Teroh's Faithful](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teroh%27s+Faithful), [Cephalid Inkshrouder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Inkshrouder) and [Mystic Zealot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+Zealot). [Prismatic Strands](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Strands) stopped a [Morbid Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger)'s attempt at removal, and [Kirtar's Desire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kirtar%27s+Desire) nullified the Elephant. Landis' defense locked up the board, and he easily took the first game.

Meanwhile, Bridy had [Jeska, Warrior Adept](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jeska%2C+Warrior+Adept) and an Elephant token beating down Shvartsman. [Wormfang Turtle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Turtle) held down the fort for Shvartsman, [Kirtar's Desire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kirtar%27s+Desire) slowed down Jeska's attack, and [Flame Burst](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flame+Burst) burned away Shvartsman's [Puppeteer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Puppeteer). Bridy's [Barbarian Bully](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbarian+Bully) got [Lost in Thought](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lost+in+Thought), while Shvartsman summoned [Thought Eater](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought+Eater). Bridy was able to pound through for the win.

Alex- "I wish I had drawn more land"  
 Pikula- "What? Who makes an excuse like that?"  
 Alex- "Maybe you should have been watching the game from the start instead of making comments like a jackass"  
 Pikula- "I saw you, you went land, land land..."

Pikula then walked away and talked a bit with Dave Price. 

At this point, JBL was 3-0 in games.

Zvi started his second game with [Patchwork Gnomes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Patchwork+Gnomes), [Militant Monk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Militant+Monk) and [Phantom Nomad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nomad). Berkowitz summoned [Dreamwinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder), and Zvi enchanted the Gnomes with [Crackling Club](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crackling+Club), which allowed him to kill off Berkowitz's blocker. Zvi then summoned yet another [Phantom Nomad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nomad), which got a few groans and complaints out of Berkowitz. [Morgue Theft](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morgue+Theft) brought back the [Dreamwinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder), [Shelter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shelter) saved the blocked creature, and Berkowitz scooped.

Berkowitz- "That wasn't even a game. What was that?"  
 Zvi- "That was Plan A."

Gary started off the next game with a turn two [Werebear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear), which was enchanted with [Coral Net](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coral+Net). It stayed alive for a while, and Landis summoned [Mystic Zealot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+Zealot). It went to the air thanks to [Ghostly Wings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghostly+Wings), and [Teroh's Faithful](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teroh%27s+Faithful) hit the ground. Gary made his army a bit bigger with another [Werebear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear), [Krosan Avenger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Avenger) and [Gravedigger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravedigger) to bring back the dead [Werebear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear). He attacked with his whole team, and the Avenger fell to the Faithful. [Faceless Butcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) was summoned, and the Zealot was bounced in response, which took out the Faithful. Landis summoned a Looter on his turn, while Gary built up his army even further. A second Butcher gave Gary even more of an advantage.

Shvartsman started off his second game with [Pilgrim of Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pilgrim+of+Justice) to Bridy's four mountains and [Anger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anger). [Puppeteer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Puppeteer) made Alex's side a bit more impressive, and Bridy was obviously looking for a forest. Eventually he did find one, and [Rabid Elephant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rabid+Elephant) and Jeska followed. [Aven Flock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Flock) gave Shvartsman some defense, and [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) provided a little card advantage. Peek revealed that the only card in Bridy's hand was [Rites of Initiation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rites+of+Initiation). 

Zvi built up defense in game three with [Hallowed Healer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hallowed+Healer) and [Militant Monk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Militant+Monk), while Berkowitz had [Gravedigger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravedigger) and [Organ Grinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Organ+Grinder). [Shower of Coals](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shower+of+Coals) cleared away Berkowitz's creatures, but he came back strong with [Skywing Aven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skywing+Aven) and [Faceless Butcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher). The Butcher died to [Fierey Temper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fierey+Temper). [Anarchist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anarchist) brought back [Shower of Coals](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shower+of+Coals), which got a huge groan out of Berkowitz. This gave Zvi the match, and put Illuminati up 1-0. 

Gary- "Most of the time when Zvi wins it's like 'What the hell just happened there?' They get beaten by clearly inferior cards."

Landis had a [Stern Judge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stern+Judge), and played [Chainmail](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainmail) on it. Gary swung with his team and the huge Judge blocked a Butcher, so Gary cast [Sudden Strength](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sudden+Strength). Gary pitched two cards to [Fledgling Imp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Imp) to get threshold, and Landis used [Embolden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embolden) to stop some of the damage. Still, the board was too much to handle, and Gary took the second game.

Gary- "Two [Faceless Butcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher)s is pretty good. He tends to win games."

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpnj02/917.jpg)[Wormfang Turtle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Turtle) held down the ground even more for Shvartsman, and was followed by a huge [Breakthrough](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Breakthrough). [Wormfang Drake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Drake) and [Aven Windreader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Windreader) came out soon after. Bridy busted through with an attack, and Shvartsman lost both of them, and was left with [Aven Flock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Flock). However, he untapped and summoned [Mystic Zealot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+Zealot) and [Vigilant Sentry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vigilant+Sentry), and had threshold. Bridy scooped soon after.

Gary mulliganed down to four, yet he put up a fight. His [Nantuko Calmer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Calmer) held down the ground, and [Faceless Butcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) removed [Angelic Wall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelic+Wall). [Thought Eater](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought+Eater) left Landis with no cards in hand.

Berkowitz- "He mulliganed to four? How are you losing?"

[Morbid Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger) and Gary's offense was rendered a bit weaker thanks to [Prismatic Strands](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prismatic+Strands) and [Embolden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embolden). Landis seemed to be winning until Gary cast [Caustic Tar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Caustic+Tar). 

Bridy had a strong start with turn three [Ember Beast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Beast), turn four [Hell-Bent Raider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hell-Bent+Raider). Shvartsman used [Aether Burst](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aether+Burst) to bounce back the Raider, untapped, and summoned [Dreamwinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder). Bridy summoned [Mad Dog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mad+Dog), and Shvartsman halted the Beast with [Kirtar's Desire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kirtar%27s+Desire), and followed with [Wormfang Drake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Drake). The Dog charged into the Turtle, and [Firebolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) finished the job. [Chatter of the Squirrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chatter+of+the+Squirrel) gave Bridy two dorks, while Shvartsman built up the board with [Teroh's Faithful](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teroh%27s+Faithful), and his hand with [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). [Thermal Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thermal+Blast) took down [Dreamwinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder) and [Psionic Gift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psionic+Gift) enchanted the Faithful, making a bad situation even worse for Bridy. An attack, [Flame Burst](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flame+Burst) and [Crackling Club](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crackling+Club) took it down. [Breakthrough](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Breakthrough) put Shvartsman ahead even further. [Hell-Bent Raider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hell-Bent+Raider) hit the ground running, and brought him down to 21 life points. [Wormfang Drake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wormfang+Drake) followed, and Bridy summoned [Nantuko Disciple](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Disciple). Simplify took out [Kirtar's Desire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kirtar%27s+Desire), allowing [Ember Beast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ember+Beast) to attack. [Barbarian Bully](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbarian+Bully) was [Syncopate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Syncopate)d, and Shvartsman was able to attack for lethal damage for the win. This gave Illuminati the match. 

Gary seemed to have the game within his grasp thanks to a flashed back [Morbid Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Morbid+Hunger), but Landis topdecked a Zealot. That game ended in a draw, but Illuminati took the match 2-0-1.
