Round 10: Peter Szigeti vs. Dirk Baberowski
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 4, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











### Game 1

![Skeletal Scrying](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Skeletal+Scrying)Szigeti started off with [Crypt Creeper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Creeper), while Baberowski played his first creature in a fourth turn [Aven Cloudchaser](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Cloudchaser), which blocked and traded with the Creeper. [Petravark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Petravark) took out Baberowski's only plains, and a [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek) revealed [Skeletal Scrying](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skeletal+Scrying), [Faceless Butcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher), and two Cripping Fatigues in Szigeti's hand. The German could only respond with "That's pretty impressive". Baberowski brought out an [Aven Windreader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Windreader) which was taken out by the Butcher, and [Hydromorph Gull](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hydromorph+Gull) died to [Crippling Fatigue](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crippling+Fatigue). Monsters were falling out of the sky left and right, while Szigeti's creatures kept attacking for damage. Baberowski summoned [Dreamwinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder), and Szigeti played [Chainflinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger). [Breakthrough](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Breakthrough) got Baberowski ahead a few cards, but Szigeti definitely had the edge on tempo. [Chainflinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger) shot the [Dreamwinder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreamwinder), Fatigue finished the job, and another four points of damage came through. Baberowski came back with some blockers, but Szigeti played [Caustic Tar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Caustic+Tar), commenting, "It's a blue/white nightmare." Baberowski conceded soon after. The game lasted about six minutes total.Szigeti- 1 Baberowski- 0

### Game 2

![Caustic Tar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Caustic+Tar)Baberowski mulliganed his opening hand, proclaiming that it was the fifth one today. [Peek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek) revealed [Caustic Tar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Caustic+Tar), [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp), [Sonic Seizure](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sonic+Seizure), and [Petravark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Petravark). Baberowski said, "That's more like a hand I'd rather see" but Szigeti noted "It's still not bad." The [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp) came into play turn three, and Baberowski brought out [Hydromorph Guardian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hydromorph+Guardian). [Petravark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Petravark) took out one of Baberowski's lands, and an [Aven Smokeweaver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Smokeweaver) would have been a problem if not for Szigeti using [Crippling Fatigue](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crippling+Fatigue) twice to kill it. [Hydromorph Gull](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hydromorph+Gull) was summoned but died to [Thermal Blast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thermal+Blast), and [Petravark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Petravark) traded with the Guardian, leaving [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp) as the only creature on the table. Baberowski then played [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion).Szigeti now went for it, playing [Caustic Tar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Caustic+Tar). Baberowski would need to cycle into an answer to the enchantment, or die trying. He played [Aven Windreader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Windreader) to stop the [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp), but it died to [Sonic Seizure](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sonic+Seizure). Meanwhile, Szigeti added more pressure with a second [Dusk Imp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dusk+Imp). Baberowski had an [Angelic Wall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelic+Wall), but Szigeti simply added insult to injury with a Butcher, and took the game.

**Final Result:** Szigeti- 2 Baberowski- 0
