*Theros* Hits **Magic Online** Tomorrow
=======================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 3, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1334_mtgotheros.jpg)](https://accounts.onlinegaming.wizards.com/)
  

[Download **Magic Online**](https://accounts.onlinegaming.wizards.com/)


Tomorrow the world ends. Well, no, it doesn't really. I mean, that's a little dramatic. Tomorrow, the world could end and thousands of players wouldn't know because they will be attached to their computers playing in *Theros* Prereleases on **Magic Online**.

At 10 a.m. Pacific tomorrow (Friday, October 4, 2013) the events will come online. Good luck and have fun!

### Prerelease Events - October 4 to 7


**Magic Online *Theros* Prerelease Sealed Deck Queues**


|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| START TIMES | On demand beginning Friday, October 4, until 10:00 AM PDT on Monday, October 7. |
| LOCATION | Release Queues room. |
| ENTRY OPTIONS | 20 Event Tickets plus 1 Hero's Path entry object. |
| PRODUCT | **Magic Online** will provide 5 *Theros* booster packs and 1 seeded Hero's Path booster pack for this event. |
| SIZE | 16 players. |
| DURATION | Deck building time plus four rounds, [Swiss pairings](http://wizards.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/465/). Play in all four rounds, win or lose! |
| PRIZES | Prizes are based on the total Match Wins at the end of the tournament. Each win awards 1 Match Win and each loss awards 0 Match Wins. |




| **Match  Wins** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 4 | 10 *Theros* booster packs | 3 |
| 3 | 4 *Theros* booster packs | 1 |
| 2 | 1 *Theros* booster pack | 0 |
