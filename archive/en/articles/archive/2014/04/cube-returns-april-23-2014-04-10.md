The Cube Returns April 23
=========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 10, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_MikeTurian.jpg)
By Mike Turian




Before joining Magic Online as its digital product manager, Mike worked as a producer for Wizards's technology department, a product manager for organized play, and as an R&D lead developer. He has played Magic Online since it came out in 2002 and Magic since The Dark. 










If you missed the Cube that the top players drafted during the [**Magic Online**  Championship](/en/events/coverage/dam-crowned-magic-online-champion), now is your chance to have the experience before it's gone: The **Magic Online**  Cube returns April 23 through May 7.


For new players, and as a refresher for others, a Cube is a special selection of 720 single **Magic**  cards with no duplicates, which is assembled by R&D. At the end of this article, you will find a [full Cube card list](#cardlist). These cards are randomly divided into Cube booster packs that are used to run Draft and Sealed events in **Magic Online**.


This is an exciting format that is popular among **Magic Online**  players, and only runs for short periods during the year.


Each returning Cube has accompanying Flashback Drafts that allow players to relive the heyday of their favorite sets and blocks.


There are two Flashback Drafts that will run simultaneously with this Cube:


April 23-30: *Lorwyn/Morningtide*  

April 30-May 7: *Shadowmoor/Eventide*
