Round 2: Carlos Romao vs. Kai Budde
===================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 17, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a238.jpg)This is the kind of battle of the titans that the Masters Series was designed for. At one end of the table, you have the current World Champion in Carlos Romao. Across from him is Kai Budde, the undisputed best Magic player in history. Needless to say, a match like this will gather a large crowd. Romao is playing what he carried to a World Championship- [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog). His version is running [Shadowmage Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator) and [Thieving Magpie](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thieving+Magpie) to gain card advantage, as well as [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Playing these creatures also lets Romao use [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy). Budde is playing [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake).

### Game 1

Romao started off stuck at two lands, and played a blind [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) on [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake), and missed. He saw Budde's full hand of [Mirari](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari), [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse), [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell), [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) and [Plains](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains). [Krosan Verge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Verge) was sacrificed, and Budde had five lands to Romao's two. Budde's [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion) resolved easily, and Romao eventually found a third land (his second blue source). Budde tapped out and cast [Mirari](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari), which resolved. Romao found a fourth land and played [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), leaving one mana untapped. Budde flashed back a [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), and Romao did the same, but Budde had [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse). The [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) began to attack, but would need a little help to become a significant threat; [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) in Budde's graveyard wasn't helping either. [Shadowmage Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator) was played; Budde cast [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) on end step, and made a copy to get [Flash of Insight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash+of+Insight) and [Krosan Reclamation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Reclamation).

Budde untapped, played [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God), and copied it, clearing the board. Another [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) came into play. Budde started to go off with copying [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), and Romao conceded.

Romao- 0 Budde- 1

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a240.jpg)A first turn [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) stripped [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) From Budde's hand, leaving the German with nothing but land. That got a smile of surprise and confidence out of the World Champion. Another [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) was played on turn three, and Budde had drawn [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God), [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and [Chastise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chastise); Analysis was sent to the graveyard. Budde's draw was less than stellar, but Romao's wasn't much better as he missed his fourth land drop. Meanwhile, Budde sacrificed a Verge and flashed back [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) to get some more resources. Romao found a fourth land, and tapped out for his own Analysis, which gave Budde an opportunity to resolve [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion).

Romao untapped and played [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) to take [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God). He then flashed back Analysis. He played [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) on the following turn, and Budde had [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell). [Shadowmage Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator) followed this, which was [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse)d. Budde attempted [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake), and Romao had [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell). Romao took his turn and tapped out for [Haunting Echoes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haunting+Echoes), to make victory a seemingly impossible goal for Kai. His expression didn't change, and he played [Mirari](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari). [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) took [Chastise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chastise), and Budde was left with only [Seedtime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seedtime) in hand. [Shadowmage Infiltrator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadowmage+Infiltrator) came into play, and was joined by a Magpie. Budde cycled through some cards and cast [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) on Romao, adding a copy to the stack. It wasn't the most optimal victory condition, but Echoes had left Kai without many options. Regardless, [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) reared its ugly head to finish the job, and it was off to Game 3.

Romao- 1 Budde- 1

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a239.jpg)Romao mulliganed his opening hand, and had a first turn [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress). He saw two [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), [Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest), [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake), [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God) and [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Budde jokingly pointed to Analysis, but Romao sent Wake to the graveyard. Budde's [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion) resolved on turn two, and [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) took care of [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God). Once again, Romao had mana problems and was stuck at two lands. Budde cycled with [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion) and drew cards with Analysis. [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) took away [Mirari](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari), leaving Budde with only lands in hand for the moment. Romao finally found a third land, and cast the third [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) of the game, taking [Elephant Ambush](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Ambush). On the following turn Romao was desperate for lands and targeted himself with [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) to discard and cast [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). This left him tapped out, and Budde took full advantage, playing [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake) followed by [Mirari](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari). 

[Haunting Echoes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haunting+Echoes) was attempted but shrugged off with [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse). On the next turn Romao flashed back an Analysis, and tapped out to hardcast another, leaving him with only a swamp left. This gave Budde the green light to start going off, and he got two elephants into play with [Elephant Ambush](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Ambush). [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) wasn't going to help, and Romao conceded.

**Final Result:** Romao- 1 Budde- 2
