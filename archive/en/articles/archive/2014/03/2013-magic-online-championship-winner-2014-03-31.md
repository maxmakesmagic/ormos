2013 Magic Online Championship Winner
=====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 31, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/claytonkroh_0.jpg)
By Clayton Kroh




 Clay is a content strategist, writer, and editor. He's a content editor and writer for DailyMTG, and has contributed creative work for *Magic: The Gathering* sets. On the side, he writes science fiction, fantasy, horror, and romance. 







Lars Dam
--------



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/winner_larsdam.jpg)

Congratulations to Lars Dam, known in **Magic Online**  as Malavi, for his victory in the **Magic Online**  Championship! He earns the title of Champion, as well as a seat at the 2014 World Championship, an invitation to next year's **Magic Online**  Championship, and the $25,000 first prize.


Lars challenged fifteen of the very best **Magic Online**  players in San Francisco over three days, until finally facing off against Cory Lack in the Finals. The match pitted Lars's black-splashing-red devotion deck against Cory's blue-splashing-white devotion deck. The movers and shakers of the two Finals matches included [Pack Rat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat), [Underworld Connections](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underworld+Connections), [Nightveil Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nightveil+Specter), [Thassa, God of the Sea](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thassa%2C+God+of+the+Sea), and ultimately [Gray Merchant of Asphodel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gray+Merchant+of+Asphodel). Check out [Lars Dam's winning deck](#lars) below, as well as [video of the Final](#video).


[Check out complete details of the three-day event](/en/events/coverage/dam-crowned-magic-online-champion).


Congratulations once again to Lars Dam, **Magic Online**  Champion!
