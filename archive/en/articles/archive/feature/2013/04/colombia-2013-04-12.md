Colombia
========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 12, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 






[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/wmcq_small.jpg)](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/worldmagiccup/qualifiers)

Vladimir Mayoral won the Colombian [WMCQ](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/event/worldmagiccup/qualifiers) with his take on Prime Speaker Bant. 


 Vladimir's deck is mostly what one would expect to see—acceleration, [Loxodon Smiter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Loxodon+Smiter), [Restoration Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Restoration+Angel), [Thragtusk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thragtusk), [Angel of Serenity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Serenity), and [Prime Speaker Zegana](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prime+Speaker+Zegana), with Garruk to supplement the Prime Speaker as additional card draw. His most interesting additions are his singletons—[Clone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clone), [Garruk Relentless](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Garruk+Relentless), and [Sleep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sleep). [Clone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clone) is remarkably good in Standard right now, as many people are playing excellent creatures that cost five or more mana. It's the perfect answer to [Angel of Serenity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel+of+Serenity) and a great way to deal with [Olivia Voldaren](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Olivia+Voldaren), a card this kind of deck can have a serious problem with. 



[Garruk Relentless](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Garruk+Relentless) lets this deck get ahead of other decks that use [Avacyn's Pilgrim](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avacyn%27s+Pilgrim) by accelerating him out before they can cast their big spells and killing one of their "Elves," at which point Garruk the Veil Cursed can make Wolves or search for any of the awesome creatures in this deck. 



[Sleep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sleep) is the most exciting addition, and I'm told Vladimir actually used it to win the finals and become the national champion. Other players have used [Cyclonic Rift](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cyclonic+Rift) for a similar effect—allowing them to break through after filling the board with large green creatures—but [Sleep](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sleep) lets Vladimir get much more aggressive much earlier to win some races, or similarly buys the deck time against aggressive decks to start casting [Thragtusk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thragtusk)s. 


 His sideboard offers the usual assortment of cards that slow aggressive decks; [Negate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Negate)s and [Jace, Memory Adept](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+Memory+Adept) to try to trump control strategies; and [Rest in Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rest+in+Peace) and [Selesnya Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Selesnya+Charm) to fight Reanimator. 


 I hope you've enjoyed this week of champions, and as a final reminder, you can try to join them in the next round of [WMCQs on April 20–21](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/worldmagiccup/qualifierlist) .
