Looking for a blast from the past? Try your hand at *Tempest* Block Constructed on [**Magic Online**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=Magic/Digital/MagicOnline.aspx)! Whether you're nostalgically looking back or experiencing these cards for the first time, this is your chance to get in on a format from the early days of the Pro Tour. DoctorPenick managed to take one *Tempest* Block Constructed event down with a mono-blue aggro deck!
