*Oath of the Gatewatch* Prerelease Events
=========================================



***Oath of the Gatewatch* Prerelease Swiss Draft**


 


**Start Times:** Friday, January 29, 10:00 a.m. until Monday, February 1, 10:00 a.m. All times are Pacific (for UTC, add 8 hours)  
**Location:** Limited Queues  
**Entry Option:**


* 15 Event Tickets
* 150 Play Points

**Product:** 2 *Oath of the Gatewatch* booster packs and 1 *Battle for Zendikar* booster pack.  
**Size:** 8 players  
**Play Style:** Swiss  
**Format:** Draft  
**Duration:** 10 minutes deck-building time. Three rounds, each round up to 50 minutes.  
**Prizes:**


 




| Match Wins | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 3 Wins | 4 *Oath of the Gatewatch* booster packs | 1 |
| 2 Wins | 2 *Oath of the Gatewatch* booster packs | 0 |
| 1 Win | 1 *Oath of the Gatewatch* booster pack | 0 |

[**Participation Prize:**](#avatars) All participating players will receive a Kozilek, the Great Distortion Avatar.


**NOTE:** Wizards of the Coast employees and other persons affiliated with it are eligible to play in this event. For more information, please see this [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/wizards-announces-wizards-play-policy-2015-01-30) and this [FAQ](https://wizards.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/2312) about this policy.

**Start Times:** Monday, February 1, 10:30 a.m. until Tuesday, February 10, 8:30 p.m. Event runs every 2 hours, on the half hour. All times are Pacific (for UTC, add 8 hours)  
**Location:** Scheduled Events  
**Entry Options:**


* Option 1: 20 Event Tickets
* Option 2: 200 Play Points
* Option 3: 8 Event Tickets, plus product

**Size:** 64 players  
**Play Style:** Single Elimination, with Top 8  
**Format:** Draft

**Prizes:**




| Place | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |

| 9th-16th | 3 *Oath of the Gatewatch* booster packs and 1 *Battle for Zendikar* booster pack | 0 |
