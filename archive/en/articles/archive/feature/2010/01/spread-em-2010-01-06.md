Spread 'Em is a new archetype that has been popularized by the 2009s and the StarCityGames.com Open series. Tim Hunt took it to the Top 8 of the first StarCityGames.com Standard Open. the deck's strategy focuses on turning its opponent's lands into unusable [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)s (or otherwise). to do that, it plays a host of cascade spells, each of which must end in a two-mana enchantment, either [Spreading Seas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spreading+Seas) or [Convincing Mirage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Convincing+Mirage). From there, you simply win with any creature as your opponent struggles through being unable to cast any spells! You can read StarCity's coverage of the event [here](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/18414_Live_Coverage_of_the_StarCityGamescom_10K_Weekend_in_St_Louis.html).
