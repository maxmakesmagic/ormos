Info: Event Information
=======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 29, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











The 2012 Magic Players Championship showcases the best players in the world battling against one another for the title of Player of the Year. Sixteen players will be invited to compete across three formats for the title and $40,000 first prize, with a total cash purse exceeding $100,000. In addition to the prize money, players earn one Pro Point for each match win during the first two days and two points per match win on the final day.

| Players earn one Pro Point per match win in the Swiss rounds and two Pro Points per match win in the Playoffs. |

**Day 1 (Wednesday, August 29)**


* *Magic* "Cube" Booster Draft – 3 Swiss rounds.
* [Modern](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/sfrmodern) constructed – 3 Swiss rounds.


**Day 2 (Thursday, August 30)**



* *Magic 2013*  Booster Draft – 3 Swiss rounds.
* Modern constructed – 3 Swiss rounds.


**Advancement to Playoff**


* All players may participate in all 12 Swiss rounds of Wednesday and Thursday's competition.
* The top 4 players after round 12 will advance to Friday's Playoffs.


**Day 3 (Friday, August 31) - Playoffs**


* Playoff matches will be Modern constructed format.
* Playoff matches will be single-elimination.
* Playoff matches will be best 3 of 5.
* Where necessary, the standings after the Swiss rounds will still be used to determine final order in the standings.


**Round Times**


* All rounds during days 1 and 2 will be 55 minutes per round.
* Day 3 single-elimination payoff matches have no time limit; however, players are expected to play at a reasonable pace and finish their matches within 90 minutes.


**Other Tournament Information**


* Planeswalker Points multiplier: 12x (Lifetime only), REL Professional.
* Players are required to use the same Modern constructed deck for Day 1, Day 2, and Playoffs.
* The [**Magic: The Gathering**  Tournament Rules](http://www.wizards.com/wpn/Document.aspx?x=Magic_The_Gathering_Tournament_Rules) and the [**Magic: The Gathering**  Infraction Procedure Guide](http://www.wizards.com/wpn/Document.aspx?x=Magic_The_Gathering_Infraction_Procedure_Guide) are in effect for this event unless specifically overruled by information in this Fact Sheet.
