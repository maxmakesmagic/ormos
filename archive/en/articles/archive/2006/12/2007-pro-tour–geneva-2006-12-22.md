2007 Pro Tour–Geneva
====================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 22, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












*Updated 2/2/2007*


Invitations to Pro Tour-Geneva are awarded in the following manner:


* The 2006 World Champion.
* The 2006 Pro Player of the Year.
* The Top 50 players from Pro Tour-Kobe.
* The Top 50 players from the 2006 World Championships.
* Players on the Top 4 Teams (including alternate) from the 2006 World Championships
* Players with 2007 Pro Tour Players Club level 3 or higher.
* The Top 16 finishers of each Grand Prix tournament that feeds Pro Tour-Geneva.
* Players who earn invitations through Pro Tour Qualifier tournaments that feed Pro Tour-Geneva.
* The Top 100 DCI-ranked players in the Limited on December 27, 2006.

If you believe that your rating or finish at a Pro Tour Qualifier or Grand Prix is in error, please email [dciappeals@wizards.com](mailto:dciappeals@wizards.com).
