### Tom van de Logt


Tom van de Logt's aggressive, black-red "Machine Head" deck used the good ol' "blow stuff up" method. While creatures like [Plague Spitter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plague+Spitter) wiped out his opponents' smaller creatures, more ferocious beasts like [Flametongue Kavu](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flametongue+Kavu) aggressively cleared the board of larger threats.

### Alex Borteh


Alex Borteh's monoblue combo deck contained twelve 1-toughness creatures. These helped hold the board until his [Static Orb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Static+Orb)-[Opposition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opposition) combo could lock down his opponents' permanents--clearing the way for a horde of Merfolk to pour through for the victory.
