Looking for a fun new deck for Extended? Check out this version of an artifact-themed deck called "Steel Artifact." Pascal Maynard played this version to a flawless 6-0 record at the 2010 World Championships in Japan. It features tons of cheap artifacts powered by [Tempered Steel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tempered+Steel). Jonathan Smithers, who also played the deck, was even featured in the above video deck tech discussing how the deck plays. Read more Worlds in the archive [here](/en/events/coverage/theres-no-jace-guillaume).
