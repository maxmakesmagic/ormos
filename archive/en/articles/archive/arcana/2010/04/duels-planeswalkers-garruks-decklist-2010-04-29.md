 Posted in **Arcana**

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast

Today, we're going to use Arcana to show you the decklist for one of them. Behold, **Teeth of the Predator** – the Garruk Wildspeaker Deck!
