Decks of the Week: 5/30/2014
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on May 30, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Atlanta played host to pro-level **Magic** for the second weekend in a row, and nearly 1,300 players showed up for the *Theros*-block Limited [Grand Prix](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/gpatl14/welcome) there. Jon Stern battled his way through a Top 8 that included longtime Pro Tomoharu Saito and three players from the [Top 25 Rankings](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/top25/20140529) to claim another Grand Prix trophy. Congratulations, Jon! The coming weekend brings the last Pro Tour Qualifiers (PTQs) and the last Grand Prix (in Manchester, England, and SÃ£o Paulo, Brazil) for the season that feeds into Pro Tour ***Magic** 2015* . Find one of the last PTQs this season with [this handy link](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#m15). Good luck; have fun!
