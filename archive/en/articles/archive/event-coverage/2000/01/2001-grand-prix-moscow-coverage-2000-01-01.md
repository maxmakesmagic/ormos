### Grand Prix Moscow
Pro Tour New Orleans Qualifier
21-22 April, 2001
Tournament Details



The Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro Tour events.


### Tournament Format & Schedule


The format for Grand Prix - Moscow will be Standard.

* **Friday, April 20** : Grand Prix registration, 4:00 - 9:00 P.M. at the lobby of Novi Manesj
* **Saturday, April 21** : Grand Prix registration, 8:00 - 9:00 A.M.
* **Saturday, April 21** : Grand Prix tournament begins at 10:00 A.M. Top 64 players advance to Sunday.
* **Sunday, April 22** : Top 64 begin at 9:00 A.M.

### Tournament


* **Divisions**  

 The main tournament at Grand Prix - Cologne is open to Magic® players of all ages. In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher in that Grand Prix. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).
* **Tournament Format**  

 The format for Grand Prix - Cologne will be Standard. All DCI Standard-format rules and Magic Floor Rules announced on September 1, 2000, along with all subsequent updates, will be enforced.  
**Day 1** : Constructed - Invasion™ Block  
**Day 2** : Constructed - Invasion™ Block
* **Tournament Structure**  

 The structure for Grand Prix - Moscow will be modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds, number of rounds determined by number of participants), with the Top 64 players advancing to the second day of competition for prizes and invitations.
* **Tournament Byes** (Byes are not cumulative throughout the season or year.)  

 Byes will be given to players in the Grand Prix based on the following criteria:  
**Third-Round Byes**
	+ Players with 24 or more professional points in the Worlds 2000 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Limited rating of 1875 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI ratings published approximately six weeks prior to the Grand Prix.**Second-Round Byes**
	+ Players with 12 or more professional points in the Worlds 2000 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Limited rating of 1775 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI ratings published approximately six weeks prior to the Grand Prix.**First-Round Byes**
	+ Players with 6 or more professional points in the Worlds 2000 professional standing.
	+ DCI players with a Limited rating of 1700 or higher. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Byes are based on DCI ratings published approximately six weeks prior to the Grand Prix.

Important : Because of strict laws in Russia, 35 % of the prize money will be deducted as taxes.
