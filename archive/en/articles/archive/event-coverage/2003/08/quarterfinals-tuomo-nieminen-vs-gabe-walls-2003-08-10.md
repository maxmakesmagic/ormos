Quarterfinals: Tuomo Nieminen vs. Gabe Walls
============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 10, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds03/a515.jpg)As soon as I got to the feature match table, I saw two Euro in coins on each side. Walls explained, "It's the split. It's all I could get out of him. He upped it to two this morning, it was at one." Nieminen is obviously very confident, but was nice enough to offer two Euros to Walls as a consolation prize.

### Game 1

Nieminen mulliganed his opening hand, getting a raise of the eyebrow and a smile out of Walls.

Walls- "You're just making it interesting, aren't you? I appreciate it. I'm a heavy favorite."

Nieminen- "For what?"  
 Walls- "The Team Rochester."

Both players were set to play each other right after this match was over, in the team finals.

Nieminen started with [Krosan Verge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Verge). [Lightning Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift) was [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak)ed, but Nieminen missed his third land drop.

Walls- "That's what I like to see. This is Magic!"

He resolved an [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide), and Nieminen found his third land. [Eternal Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon) was cycled to get a plains, and Nieminen sacrificed [Krosan Verge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Verge). He untapped, and got to work gaining card advantage with [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Nieminen played [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and Walls brought back the [Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon) on his upkeep, tapping one too many mana and taking a point of burn. Nieminen got to work putting cards through [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and flashed back [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). The [Eternal Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon) came out, and Nieminen didn't seem very intimidated. He tapped five mana, and played [Mirari's Wake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake). Walls started some banter, asking "Ooh, I wonder what that is!" but then subdued his tone as he realized he was in trouble. A second and third Wake came down in a row on the following turn.

Walls- "You think you're good or something?"

A few turns later, Nieminen cycled [Decree of Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice), and Walls conceded before X was declared.

Nieminen- 1 Walls- 0

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds03/a528.jpg)Nieminen played [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and Walls played a face-down [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel). Nieminen cycled a card through [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion) and missed his third land drop, which got some excitement out of Walls. He flipped over [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel), and attacked for four. Nieminen found the lands he needed with the help of [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and Walls cycled [Decree of Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice) for two on end step- the spotter was out of [Soldier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soldier) tokens since all the other decks were running the Decree as well, so Walls had to use beads. The next attack was nullified by [Moment's Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace), and Nieminen tapped out to activate [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion) on end step. Before he could do anything else, Walls cycled [Decree of Annihilation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Annihilation), and with [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel) and two 1/1's on the landless board, won easily.

Nieminen- 1 Walls- 1

Walls (leans over to Dave Humpherys)- "Hey Dave, I won! I won my one game!"

### Game 3

Nieminen mulliganed his opening hand.

Walls- "Do you go to church, Tuomo?"  
 Nieminen- "No."  
 Walls- "I do!"

Even after that statement, Walls mulliganed his hand as well. 

Walls- "I guess you do go to church."  
 Nieminen- "I don't believe in God, so I guess he's on your side."  
 Walls- "Yep."  
 Nieminen (sarcastically)- "Yeah, God is always on the side of the Americans."

Walls played a second turn [Burning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish), and Nieminen countered it with [Mana Leak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak). Walls resolved [Lightning Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift) a turn later, and Nieminen cast [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) to get [Ray of Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation) on end step. He untapped, and drew three cards with [Concentrate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Concentrate). [Eternal Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon) was cycled, and Walls activated the Rift to deal two to Nieminen. [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) provided even more card advantage for the Finn, and a turn later, Nieminen stopped to think.

Walls did his best to try and break his concentration, with comments ranging from, "You act like you're playing a control deck over there" to "If I untap, you're dead." Finally, Nieminen decided to cast [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and play [Star Compass](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Star+Compass), leaving him tapped out. Walls cycled [Eternal Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon) and [Renewed Faith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith) on end step, dealing four damage total with the Rift. The [Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon) was brought back on upkeep, and Walls ended his turn. [Mirari's Wake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake) came out, and [Ray of Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation) destroyed [Lightning Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift). 

[Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) was flashed back, and followed by [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion). Walls cycled [Decree of Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice) on end step, making six [Soldier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soldier)s. They attacked, and Nieminen played [Moment's Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace). He cycled cards through [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), as Walls attempted to entertain the crowd. "This is the most boring thing I've ever done in my entire life. Actually, the most boring thing was playtesting, but this is second." Nieminen drew cards with [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), and played [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God).

Walls- "I thought you don't believe in God. You'll use his wrath though? Very sketchy."

Walls cycled two [Renewed Faith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith), brought a [Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon) back on upkeep, and played [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide). Nieminen continued to cycle away cards with [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and had plenty of mana to work with. When Walls played [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel) face-down, Nieminen responded with [Ray of Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation), destroying [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide). The morph resolved, and Nieminen cycled [Decree of Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice) on end step, making fourteen [Soldier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soldier)s. They were 2/2 thanks to the Wake, and Walls conceded.

Nieminen- 2 Walls- 1

### Game 4

Nieminen played a first turn Verge, and Walls had a turn three morph. It flipped over on turn four as the Angel got to work, and Nieminen sacrificed the Verge. He didn't have any lands left, however, and was lacking a blue source of mana. 

Nieminen- "I'm going to have to discard. So lucky."  
 Walls- "No! It's *pure skill*."

[Ray of Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation) went to the graveyard, and the Angel attacked once more. [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) came into play, and the Ray destroyed it. On the following turn, Walls played [Lightning Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift), and cycled [Renewed Faith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith) to deal two to Nieminen and bring him to eight. An island finally showed up at this point, but it was too late. Nieminen tapped out for Wake, but Walls attacked, and cycled two cards to finish it.

Nieminen- 2 Walls- 2

### Game 5

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds03/a513.jpg)A second turn [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion) was matched by [Lightning Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift). On turn three, Nieminen sacrificed [Krosan Verge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Verge), then untapped and cast [Concentrate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Concentrate). Walls used the opportunity to cast [Burning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish), getting [Overmaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overmaster). Walls would need to wait a few turns until he could get a spell to force through with it, but in the meanwhile, Nieminen was setting up with [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion). Nieminen played [Mirari's Wake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake), and Walls decided to go for it.

He cast [Overmaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overmaster), cycled [Eternal Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon) to deal two damage, then cast [Cleansing Meditation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cleansing+Meditation). This was devastating, and destroyed [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion) and Wake. Nieminen responded by discarding two cards before it went away, and although Walls lost his Rift in the exchange, it set the Finn back significantly. Still, Nieminen had plenty of resources in his hand. He cast [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) to gain more of an advantage, and said go. 

Walls played [Obliterate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obliterate).

The game was now back to zero, although Nieminen had more cards. In fact, he had no trouble recovering, playing two lands followed by [Star Compass](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Star+Compass). He then flashed back [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), and played [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion). Walls played [Lightning Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift), but Nieminen had [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) to go get [Ray of Revelation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ray+of+Revelation) and handle it. From this point, Walls didn't have anything going on, while Nieminen continued to activate [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion).

Walls tapped out on upkeep to bring back [Eternal Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon), and Nieminen cast [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) on end step, getting [Stifle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stifle) as insurance for [Decree of Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice) and [Decree of Annihilation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Annihilation). Nieminen had the game under his control but was lacking a green source, as Walls mockingly speculated that there were none left in the deck. 

Walls- "If I lose this game, I know that Bob Maher would have won it. That always consoles me."

He summoned [Eternal Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon), and Nieminen countered it by putting [Circular Logic](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic) through [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion). [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) drew more cards, and Nieminen had the game completely under his control, but needed a way to win before his deck ran out. He was holding [Decree of Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice), but with Walls at 22, he would need a lot of mana to make it work. Finally, he found a green source in [Sungrass Prairie](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sungrass+Prairie). [Mirari's Wake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake) followed, and [Lightning Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Rift) came out and was promptly destroyed by a Ray. Nieminen played a second Wake, and Walls' second [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) didn't hang around for long, as Nieminen flashed back both Rays to destroy the enchantments. [Decree of Justice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice) was cycled after, and Walls conceded.

**Final Result:** Nieminen- 3 Walls- 2






#### Tuomo Nieminen, 6-0
