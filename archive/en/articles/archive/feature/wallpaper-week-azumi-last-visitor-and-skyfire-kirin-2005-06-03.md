Wallpaper of the Week: Azumi, the Last Visitor and Skyfire Kirin
================================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 3, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












This week we have a special **double batch** of *Saviors of Kamigawa* desktop wallpaper (thanks to Jen Page, magicthegathering.com's graphic designer whiz)!


First, in honor of our final [legend vignette](/en/articles/archive/meet-saviors-ayumi-last-visitor-2005-06-01), there's **[Ayumi, the Last Visitor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ayumi%2C+the+Last+Visitor)** by rk post.


And second, in honor of Fire Week, it's **[Skyfire Kirin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyfire+Kirin)** by Tsutomu Kawade. Enjoy.
