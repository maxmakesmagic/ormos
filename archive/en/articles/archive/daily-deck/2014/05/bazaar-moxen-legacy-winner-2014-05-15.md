Bazaar of Moxen Legacy Winner
=============================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on May 15, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 





The [Bazaar of Moxen](http://www.bazaar-of-moxen.com/en/bazaar-of-moxen-coverage-bom9,24.html) in Annecy, France, started as a Vintage tournament, but today, the Legacy main event has become just as important to the event. Recently, at the Bazaar of Moxen, 535 players came out to play Legacy for fabulous prizes, including a full set of the [Power Nine](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/arcana/432) to 1st place! When the dust had settled, Niklas Kronberger won the tournament with an innovative deck featuring [Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam).



![Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?size=small&type=card&name=Life%20from%20the%20Loam&options=)Niklas's deck focuses on the power of its lands. Between [Life from the Loam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam) and [Knight of the Reliquary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary), powerful lands such as [Wasteland](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland) can be used repeatedly. [Grove of the Burnwillows](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grove+of+the+Burnwillows) and [Punishing Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Punishing+Fire) provide a potent combination against small-creature decks, while [Chalice of the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chalice+of+the+Void) and [Gaddock Teeg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gaddock+Teeg) are the weapons of choice against many of the combo decks available in Legacy.
