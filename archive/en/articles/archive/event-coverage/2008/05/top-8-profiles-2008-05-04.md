Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 4, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbru08/pic_cornelissen_top8.jpg)

**Name:** Kamiel Cornelissen  
**Age:** 27  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Hometown:** Enschede, Netherlands


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 5 PT Top8, Dutch national champion 2006, World team champion 2006


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 
[Mistmeadow Witch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistmeadow+Witch)



**What are you going to draft? Any preferences?** I am open to all color combinations.


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?** I’m confident, but there are a lot of good players in the top8, so we will have to see.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbru08/pic_levy2_top8.jpg)

**Name:** Raphael Levy  
**Age:** 26  
**Hometown:** Toulouse


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Magic PT Hall of Fame


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 
[Steel of the Godhead](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steel+of+the+Godhead)
 !


**What are you going to draft? Any preferences?** I’m going to try to draft white. I’ve been quite successful with it this weekend.


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?** I’ve never won a limited GP top8... Hopefully now is the time!!






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbru08/pic_nassif_top8.jpg)

**Name:** Gabriel Nassif  
**Age:** 24  
**Occupation:** Poker/Magic for a living  
**Hometown:** Paris


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 3 GP top8s, 8 PT top8s, ever heard of them?


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 
[Twilight Shepherd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Twilight+Shepherd)
 (x2 in my sealed)


**What are you going to draft? Any preferences?** Not really, pick the best card, go from there and try to get three preferably.


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?** Must be 80:1






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbru08/pic_ruel_top8.jpg)

**Name:** Antoine Ruel (Go Peset Go!)  
**Age:** 28  
**Occupation:** Player  
**Hometown:** Aubervilliers


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** PT LA champion and Invitational champion


**What was your most valuable card this weekend**?
 [Reflecting Pool](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reflecting+Pool)



**What are you going to draft? Any preferences?** It’s all about the first picks and signals, if I had to choose I’d go on white weenie.  
**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?** Good






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbru08/pic_peset_top8.jpg)

**Name:** Alexandre Peset “COBRA”  
**Age:** 31  
**Occupation:** Poker and Magic  
**Hometown:** Lille


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 2 GP top8, 3rd PT Kobe and 2x Top8 at France Championship


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 
[Scuttlemutt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scuttlemutt)



**What are you going to draft? Any preferences?** White with a few green or blue


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?** So many good players... I don’t know... I want to send a kiss to Maja =)






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbru08/pic_lefebvre_top8.jpg)

**Name:** Gaetan Lefebvre  
**Age:** 26  
**Hometown:** Maugeuge


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Top16 GP Stockholm, Top32 PT Valence and Top16 GP Krakow


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 
[Burn Trail](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burn+Trail)



**What are you going to draft? Any preferences?** Blue and black with cheap creatures, removal and fliers. Splash red for a little more removal.


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?** Many pros to beat, but with a 5-0 today everything is possible.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbru08/pic_kleij_top8.jpg)

**Name:** Rogier Kleij  
**Age:** 33  
**Occupation:** Senior IT field support engineer  
**Hometown:** Utrecht, Netherlands


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Attend Pro Tour Kobe


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 
[Sootstoke Kindler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sootstoke+Kindler)



**What are you going to draft? Any preferences?** Red, if available


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?** It’s Magic... there is always a chance!






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbru08/pic_lange_top8.jpg)

**Name:** Holger Lange  
**Age:** 36  
**Occupation:** Working for a Bank  
**Hometown:** Hamburg


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Won 5x Amateur money!


**What was your most valuable card this weekend?** 
[Jaws of Stone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jaws+of+Stone)



**What are you going to draft? Any preferences?** Blue and white, red and black, green and red, I don’t know...


**What do you think about your chances for the top 8?** It’s my first time, so I’m just happy to be there =)
