Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Esper Dragons—new top of the food chain?](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/displaydeck.php?DeckID=99937)

Over the weekend, Esper Dragons, in the hands of Robert Wright, came *thiiis* close to winning the [StarCityGames.com Standard Open in Indianapolis](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/deckshow.php?event_ID=19&t%5bT1%5d=1&start_date=2016-03-19&end_date=2016-03-19&state=IN&city=Indianapolis&order_1=finish&limit=8&t_num=1&action=Show+Decks). He ultimately fell to Andrew Funkhouser's [Rally the Ancestors](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rally+the+Ancestors) deck, but that deck is soon to rotate. And Esper Dragons? It mostly stays intact.


From the main deck, Wright's list loses [Disdainful Stroke](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disdainful+Stroke), fetch lands, a single copy of [Utter End](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Utter+End), and a pair of [Crux of Fate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crux+of+Fate)s. That's it. Well, almost it. Those are all pretty replaceable in some form or fashion (except maybe [Crux of Fate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crux+of+Fate)). The big loss, as with many of these blue decks, is Dig Through time. Esper Dragons can look toward [Dragonlord's Prerogative](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonlord%27s+Prerogative) to fill that gap, but it's not the same.


But there's a very solid core of [Dragonlord Ojutai](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonlord+Ojutai), [Dragonlord Silumgar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonlord+Silumgar), [Jace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=398434), [Silumgar's Scorn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silumgar%27s+Scorn), and [Foul-Tongue Invocation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Foul-Tongue+Invocation) that still exists and will likely continue to be a player in the new Standard.
