2009 PLAY THE GAME, SEE THE WORLD Championship Tournament Official Rules
========================================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 9, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/worldslogo.jpg)](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds)The winner of the **PLAY THE GAME, SEE THE WORLD** Championship tournament will win the prize described below. The championship tournament will take place Sunday, November 22, 2009 at the **Magic: The Gathering** World Championship Tournament at the Palazzo Dei Congressi in Rome, Italy. The format of the Championship tournament is *Zendikar* Sealed Deck. There will be no entry fee for the Championship tournament. To play you must have a valid passport, and sign a Participant Agreement stating you will abide by these official Rules. If you are a minor, your parent or guardian must also sign the Participation Agreement. 

This tournament is sponsored by the Wizards of the Coast LLC, with a principal place of business at 1600 Lind Ave. SW, Suite 400, Renton, WA 98057 ("Sponsor" or "we" or "Wizards").

* [More Information on 2009 World Championships Public Events](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds#publicevents)

**HOW TO PLAY:** Players will need to qualify to play in this championship tournament. Players may qualify in 1 of 3 ways:

1. 3 Public events at Worlds (run November 19-21, 2009) will be designated as events which will qualify players for the **PLAY THE GAME, SEE THE WORLD** Championship tournament. The top 2 finishers at each of these events (Top 2 teams from the 2HG event) will qualify to play in the Championship tournament. (8 players) 


2. In the summer and fall of 2009 we will run a [number of tournaments in the selected markets within Europe in retail stores](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds09-ptgqualifiers). The winner from each of these events will be qualified to play in the **PLAY THE GAME, SEE THE WORLD** Championship tournament. (# of players TBD, dependent on # of events). Stores will be solicited by the Wizards Play Network. 


3. From June to September 2009, in Italy approximately 100 stores will organize 10-20 events each, known as the "[PLAY THE GAME, SEE THE WORLD ITALIAN CHALLENGE SERIES](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds09-ptgchallenges)." These events will lead to final events that qualify those winners to participate in the **PLAY THE GAME, SEE THE WORLD** Championship tournament. 



**PRIZE PACKAGE:**  
 The winner of the Championship tournament will receive one (1) roundtrip coach air ticket from the international airport nearest the player's home to the host city of each of three (3) *Magic: The Gathering* Pro Tours to be held in 2010 and the *Magic: The Gathering* World Championships; and four (4) night's accommodation (single occupancy, room, and tax only) at each Pro Tour location and at Worlds. Winner will receive instructions from Sponsor on whom he must contact and the deadline by which he must arrange travel. Sponsor will not refund travel made outside this service. All travel itineraries must include all the dates of that event. No portion of the prize will be substituted if winner is unable to accept it; and such portion of the prize will be forfeited. All other expenses are the responsibility of the winner and may include, without limitation, ground transportation, taxes, itinerary change fees, passport and visa fees. Approximate retail value of travel package is €5000 Euros. Actual value of trip may vary depending on winner's residence and seasonal rates.  
 The winner will also receive a package of travel accessories as determined by Wizards. Approximate retail value of travel accessories is €215 Euros.  
 Wizards of the Coast reserves the right to award a cash equivalent prize in lieu of the prize package in its sole discretion.   
 Winner must arrange his travel itinerary no later than (30) days before departure. Failure to complete travel arrangements as directed by Sponsor prior to this deadline will result in forfeiture of the award. Every member receiving a travel award must provide to Sponsor an executed Prize Winner Agreement or liability release, any applicable or requested taxation forms, and proof of ID and passport/visa (when required by international destination). In all cases, all such documentation must be provided to Wizards of the Coast, or previously on file, at least thirty (30) days before departure. Failure to timely provide such documentation will result in forfeiture of the award. Winner is solely responsible for obtaining and meeting all requirements of passports and visas necessary to travel internationally.  
 Odds of winning prize depend on number of eligible entries received and the skill of the participants. Winner may select either prize so long as the selection is made immediately upon being named a potential winner in the Championship Tournament. Potential winner may be required to sign an affidavit of eligibility and publicity and liability release.
