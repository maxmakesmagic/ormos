The Finals at Croatian Nationals saw a nearly identical mirror match. Grgur Petric Maretic and Niko Bacic squared off playing a Faeries list they had worked on together. Grgur's build wasn't quite enough to take down his friend, however, and he was forced to walk home with a 2nd place finish. Still, both Grgur and Niko earned the chance to represent Croatia at the World Championships in Rome to be held later this Fall.
