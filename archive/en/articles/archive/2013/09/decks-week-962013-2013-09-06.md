Decks of the Week: 9/6/2013
===========================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on September 6, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Russian player Anatoly Chuhwichov navigated a tough Top 8 that included Hall of Famer Raphaël Lévy and GP powerhouse Martin Jůza to win [Grand Prix Prague](/en/events/coverage/anatoly-beats-them-black-and-blue-prague) last weekend. Congratulations, Anatoly! Meanwhile, here in Washington State, the **Magic Online** Community team [won back the Community Cup](/en/events/coverage/2013-magic-online-community-cup) etc. etc. congratulations Community team, moving on. Like GP Prague, this PTQ season uses the Limited formats of Sealed and Draft. For now, tournaments are using  ***Magic** 2014*  boosters; beginning on September 27, they will use boosters from *Theros*, so make sure to check in with the *Theros*  [Card Image Gallery](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/theros/cig#) to know what to expect! Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#bog). Good luck; have fun!
