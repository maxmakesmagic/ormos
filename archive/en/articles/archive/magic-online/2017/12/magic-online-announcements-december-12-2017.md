Vintage Masters Events Start Tomorrow!
--------------------------------------


Tomorrow (Wednesday, December 13) marks the first day of a week of *Vintage Masters* events. Events will run through Wednesday, December 20, and include both regular and phantom Draft options. And in case you haven't heard, there's something extra to get excited about this time: this iteration of *Vintage Masters* will contain all of the Power Nine with original art and card frames! Yes, including the creepy almost-skeletons on [Time Walk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=426).



For more information on this iteration of *Vintage Masters*, [check out Lee Sharpe's article here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/vintage-masters-flashbacks-and-vintage-cube-2017-12-05).

Standard MOCS Monthly Event on Saturday
---------------------------------------


A *Magic Online* Championship Series Event will take place on Saturday, December 16. The format will be Standard. Players can enter the event with 35 Qualifier Points (QPs), and top finishers qualify forPlayoff Events in the 2018 series.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points for the year. Top players on the [Leaderboard](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/leaderboard) at the end of the 2018 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.



Click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2018-magic-online-championship-series) for more information about the 2018 MOCS events.

Modern PTQ on Sunday
--------------------


This Sunday, December 17, a Modern Pro Tour Qualifier will take place on *Magic Online*. There is no need to qualify through a Preliminary—simply join this event with 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points. If you're aiming for a seat at Pro Tour *Dominaria*, this tournament is a great first step!


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

Build Notes
-----------


Vintage, Legacy | [Solemnity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solemnity) now interacts correctly with all of the commonly played cumulative upkeep cards.


Commander | [Scourge of the Throne](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scourge+of+the+Throne) can now properly grant you an additional combat phase.


Commander | The command zone now stays where it should.


Collection | [Metalwork Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalwork+Colossus) is now grouped with its fellow artifacts when sorted by color.
