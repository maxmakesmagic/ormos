Grand Prix Trial Winners' Decklists
===================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX PARIS 2015](/en/events/coverage/gppar15)
 on May 9, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke












 The actual Grand Prix may not start until Saturday, but that doesn't mean people weren't playing *Magic* on Friday. As is tradition, the day before the big day saw hundreds of competitors competing in a number of last-chance Grand Prix Trials. These tournaments were the last chance for players to earn two byes for the main event, and our first chance to see what's good in Standard this week. So let's dive right in!
