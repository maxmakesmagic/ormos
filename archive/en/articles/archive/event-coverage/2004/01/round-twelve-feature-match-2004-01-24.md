Round Twelve Feature Match:
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 24, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpoka04/r12fm2ja1.jpg)Those who follow competitive Magic certainly recognize Tsuyoshi Fujita, the first Japanese player to make a Pro Tour Top 8. His opponent this round is Masahiro Nishiwaki, an old school player who has been around since the beginning of Japanese Magic. It's a battle of fast red decks with different strategies, as Nishiwaki is running Red Deck Wins, while Fujita has Goblins. ### Game 1

Fujita opened with a [Mogg Fanatic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogg+Fanatic) that Nishiwaki killed with [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt). Turn two produced [Goblin Piledriver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Piledriver), and Nishiwaki frowned as he played [Slith Firewalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slith+Firewalker) and said go. Fujita killed it with [Gempalm Incinerator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gempalm+Incinerator), and sent the Piledriver in for a point of damage. Nishiwaki killed it with [Volcanic Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer), and played [Seal of Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Fire). [Goblin Matron](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Matron) let Fujita search up [Mogg Fanatic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogg+Fanatic), and the Matron died to [Flametongue Kavu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flametongue+Kavu). Fujita played [Goblin Warchief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Warchief) and a Fanatic, and the Seal killed the Fanatic as the parity war continued. Nishiwaki pulled ahead by killing the Warchief with another Flametongue, and Fujita started pedaling back, playing a Warchief and keeping it on defense. Nishiwaki used his advantage to its fullest potential, summoning [Blistering Firecat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blistering+Firecat) and sending it in for an attack along with both Flametongues. A turn later, the game was over.

Nishiwaki- 1 Fujita- 0

### Game 2

Fujita mulliganed down to six, and seemed none too happy about it. He had a first turn [Mogg Fanatic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogg+Fanatic), which was thrown at a [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer). Fujita's [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) attacked for a few turns, as Nishiwaki used [Rishadan Port](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishadan+Port) to slow down Fujita's two land draw. Nishiwaki was stuck at three land himself, and played a Crused Scroll after Fujita had drawn into more land. A Matron provided [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander), and for lack of any other plays, Nishiwaki sent a [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) at the [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith). Fujita played a second, which died to [Flametongue Kavu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flametongue+Kavu). Since Nishiwaki was now tapped out, Fujita was free to summon the Commander, which died right away to [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) as could be expected. [Flametongue Kavu](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flametongue+Kavu) stayed back on defense as Fujita charged with his 1/1 Goblins, and it died to [Blood Frenzy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Frenzy). With seven cards in his graveyard, Fujita dropped a game shifting bomb on the table in [Fledgling Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Dragon), which convinced Nishiwaki to concede.

Nishiwaki- 1 Fujita- 1

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpoka04/r12fm2ja2.jpg)Nishiwaki had a first turn Pup, followed by [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer) and [Seal of Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Fire), while Fujita went through turns one and two with no plays. Nishiwaki's board improved with [Rishadan Port](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishadan+Port) and [Seal of Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Fire), while Fujita could only muster a third turn [Skirk Prospector](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Prospector). It died to [Seal of Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Fire) and Nishiwaki summoned [Fledgling Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fledgling+Dragon), leaving him with one card in hand and a surefire Scroll. [Goblin Matron](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Matron) searched out a [Mogg Fanatic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogg+Fanatic) that came out right away, as Fujita struggled to stabilize the situation. The Lavamancer shot the Fanatic (it shot back for a trade), allowing [Blistering Firecat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blistering+Firecat) to get through for seven damage. Fujita drew, thought for a bit, and then sighed in frustration as he quickly collected his cards.

Nishiwaki- 2 Fujita- 1
