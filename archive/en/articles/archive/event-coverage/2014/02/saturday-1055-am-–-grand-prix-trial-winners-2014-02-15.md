Saturday, 10:55 a.m. – Grand Prix Trial Winners
===============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 16, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke

earn two byes for the Grand Prix and the first chance for us to get an impression of how the field may look like 
at this Legacy event. The following is a sampling of 24 decklists whose pilots went 5-0 to win one of these Trial 
tournaments.
