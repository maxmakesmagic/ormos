[Joining the 2008 Hall of Fame](/en/articles/archive/week-was/remembering-itaru-ishida-2013-01-25) class is French pro Olivier Ruel. Despite having numerous accolades to his name, Olivier continues to play every tournament for which he gets the opportunity. He even managed to earn himself a finalist spot at this year’s 2008 France National Championships. His deck was a five-color control build similar to the ones French players have been championing all year. His “big” win condition? None other than [Platinum Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Platinum+Angel)! Check out all the France Nationals Coverage [here](/en/events/coverage/2008-france-national-championship), and tune into DailyMTG.com all weekend as we bring you live coverage of the 2008 Japan National Championships.
