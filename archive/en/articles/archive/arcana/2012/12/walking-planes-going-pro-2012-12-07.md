 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 7, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)

It's time for another Walking the Planes! In "Going Pro," Nate Holt and Shawn Kornhauser continue their journey through the **Magic** tournament Multiverse at San Antonio for that city's [first Grand Prix](/en/events/coverage/lytles-zombies-big-texas).
