Decks of the Week: 3/4/2010
===========================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on March 4, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The largest **Magic** tournament ever took place last weekend at [Grand Prix–Madrid](/en/events/coverage/m%C3%BCller-magical-madrid), and it's only fitting that it featured Legacy and the more than 10,000 cards legal in that format. Check out the Top 8 decks plus the decks that survived the 2,227-player Day One unscathed! Decks of the Week also closes the book on Pro Tour–San Diego with the rest of those Standard lists, shows off *Worldwake*'s impact on Extended in the San Juan PTQ season, and welcomes *Worldwake* to **Magic Online**. Enjoy!
