The Latest Spellbombs
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 20, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











It will come as no surprise to you that the [Spellbombs of *Scars of Mirrodin*](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&name=+%5BSpellbomb%5D&set=%7C%5B%22Scars%20of%20Mirrodin%22%5D) are descendants of the [artifacts of a similar name](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&name=+%5BSpellbomb%5D&set=%7C%5B%22Mirrodin%22%5D) in *Mirrodin*. We're not going to pretend to be telling you something new on that score.

However, it could still be fun to look at Jim Nelson's five spellbomb illustrations juxtaposed with Franz Vohwinkel's new ones. Let's do that!
