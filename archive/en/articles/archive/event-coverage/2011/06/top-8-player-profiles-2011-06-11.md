Top 8: Player Profiles
======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 11, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptnag11/top8_620.jpg)
 


### David Sharfman


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptnag11/ppsh.jpg)


**Age:** 21  
**Hometown:** Orlando, Florida  
**Occupation:** Medical Assistance  
**How did you quality for Pro Tour Nagoya?**  

Level 4 and Grand Prix Paris.


**Number of Grand Prix/Pro Tour Top 8s:**  

PT: first one!  

GP: 1, Grand Prix Paris 1st


**Other previous Magic accomplishments:**  

1 Nationals Top 8  

2 GP Top 16, StarCityGames.com Legacy Orlando 1st


**Record by format:**  

Block Constructed: 7-2-1  

Booster Draft: 5-1


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour Nagoya? Who did you prepare with?**  

Testing Block with Pat Cox, Antonino de Rosa, Billy Postlethwaite, Orrin Beasley. Playing a deck designed by Mark Herberholz. Drafting a *lot* on *Magic Online*.


**How many hours did you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**  

12 hours.


**What Block Constructed deck did you play and why?**  

Puresteel Paladin. It crushes Tempered Steel and Mono-Red, which were the most popular decks.


**What is your Top 8 draft strategy?**  

Open a mythic for the first time this weekend...


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic, or, if you don’t go to FNM anymore, where did you play?**  

The Game Academy (check them out!).

I typically do not, but I used to play at Dogs of War Gaming in Palin Bay, FL.
