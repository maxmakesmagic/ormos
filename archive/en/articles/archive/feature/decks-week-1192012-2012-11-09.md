Decks of the Week: 11/9/2012
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 9, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations to Jérémy Dezani for his victory at [Grand Prix Lyon](/en/events/coverage/jund-jolts-jeremy-top) and Walker MacMurdo for his at [Grand Prix Auckland](/en/events/coverage/macmurdos-opponents-get-their-pineapple-lumps)! While both of those Grand Prix were Constructed, the Pro Tour *Gatecrash* PTQ season is *Return to Ravnica* Limited—Sealed and Draft. Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#gtc). In the meantime, see what other people are putting together in this season's Limited environments with the decklists here. Enjoy!
