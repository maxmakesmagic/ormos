Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 23, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











Learn more about Grand Prix Kyoto's Top 8 competitors by taking a look through their profiles!




---


### Martin Müller


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpkyo17/gpKyoto2017_TOP8_3_Muller.jpg)


**Age:** 19


**Hometown:** Jyllinge, Denmark


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** 2 Pro Tour Top 8s, World *Magic* Cup 2014 Champion


**What was your record this weekend and what decks did you play?**


**Day 1 Sealed Deck:** 9-0 (3 byes) with Blue-Red


**Day 2 Draft 1:** 3-0 with White-Blue


**Day 2 Draft 2:** 1-1-1 with White-Black


**What is your favorite card from Hour of Devastation, and why?**


I don't really have a favorite card, but I really like how the Limited format is playing out so far.


**What was the happiest moment of your weekend?**


When I won Round 12 to lock Platinum.




---


### William Jensen


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpkyo17/gpKyoto2017_TOP8_5_Jensen.jpg)


**Age:** 34


**Hometown:** Boston, Massachusetts, USA


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** Hall of Fame 2013, 5 Pro Tour Top 8s with 1 win, 5-time Grand Prix Champion


**What was your record this weekend and what decks did you play?**


**Day 1 Sealed Deck:** 9-0 (3 byes) with Grixis Control and [Nicol Bolas, God-Pharaoh](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+God-Pharaoh)


**Day 2 Draft 1:** 1-2 with Blue-Red Prowess


**Day 2 Draft 2:** 3-0 with 4-Color Green


**What is your favorite card from Hour of Devastation, and why?**


[Nicol Bolas, God-Pharaoh](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+God-Pharaoh), because it is the most powerful card.


**What was the happiest moment of your weekend?**


It's nice to see Jon Finkel make an individual Top 8 after such a long hiatus.




---


### Qi Wentao


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpkyo17/gpKyoto2017_TOP8_1_Qi.jpg)


**Age:** 29


**Hometown:** Beijing, China


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** Grand Prix Shizuoka 2017 finalist, Grand Prix Manila 2017 finalist


**What was your record this weekend and what decks did you play?**


**Day 1 Sealed Deck:** 8-1 (2 byes) with White-Blue


**Day 2 Draft 1:** 3-0 with Blue-Red


**Day 2 Draft 2:** 3-0 with Blue-Red


**What is your favorite card from Hour of Devastation, and why?**


[Blur of Blades](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blur+of+Blades). It helped me win a lot of games.


**What was the happiest moment of your weekend?**


I got the opportunity to draft with the top players in the world, such as Martin Muller, Marcio Carvalho and Jon Finkel.

### Jon Finkel


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpkyo17/gpKyoto2017_TOP8_6_Finkel.jpg)


**Age:** 39


**Hometown:** Fanwood, New Jersey, USA


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** Pro Tour New York 1998 Champion, 2000 World Champion, Pro Tour Kuala Lumpur 2008 Champion, Winner of 2000 Invitational, 16 Pro Tour Top 8s.


**What was your record this weekend and what decks did you play?**


**Day 1 Sealed Deck:** 8-1 (3 byes) with 4-Color Green


**Day 2 Draft 1:** 3-0 with White-Blue splash Red


**Day 2 Draft 2:** 2-1 with White-Black


**What is your favorite card from Hour of Devastation, and why?**


[Unquenchable Thirst](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unquenchable+Thirst). It's my favorite common.


**What was the happiest moment of your weekend?**


Billy Jensen making the Top 8. Nice to see the old guys getting it done.

### Yang Xiao Yu (Roy)


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpkyo17/gpKyoto2017_TOP8_4_Roy.jpg)


**Age:** 23


**Hometown:** Vancouver, Canada


**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** Top 32 at Grand Prix Vancouver 2016, Top 64 at Grand Prix Vancouver 2017.


**What was your record this weekend and what decks did you play?**


**Day 1 Sealed Deck:** 8-1 (2 byes) with White-Black Life Gain


**Day 2 Draft 1:** 2-1. White-Green Midrange splash Samut


**Day 2 Draft 2:** 3-0 with Jund Midrange


**What is your favorite card from Hour of Devastation, and why?**


[Oasis Ritualist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oasis+Ritualist). It allows you to splash bombs of all colors.


**What was the happiest moment of your weekend?**


Both the Grand Prix and Pro Tour being in the same city, Kyoto! I love the city and all the good food!
