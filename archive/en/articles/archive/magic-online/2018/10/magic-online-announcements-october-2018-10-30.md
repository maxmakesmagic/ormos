*Innistrad* Flashback Draft
---------------------------


We're kicking off a triple *Innistrad* Flashback Draft to celebrate Halloween! This draft will actually start ***on*** Halloween, making it all the more spooky. Triple *Innistrad* Draft runs through November 7, so you'll have plenty of time to enjoy the creepiest of creatures.




---

*Core Set Cube*
---------------



The next event in the Cube Spotlight Series is almost upon us! A full week of *Core Set Cube* draft starts on November 7. This Cube features iconic cards from every core set on *Magic Online*, from *Seventh Edition* to *Core Set 2019*. Cube designer Kyle O'Neill has more on the cube in his article, available [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/cube-spotlight-series-core-set-cube-2018-10-30)!




---


Saturday MOCS Monthly
---------------------


On Saturday, November 3, at 7 a.m. PT, we're having a MOCS Monthly event for the December 22 MOCS Playoff. The event will feature Standard and costs 35 MOCS Qualifier Points to enter.


To find out more information, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-announcements-august-21-2018-2018-08-21).




---

Sunday PTQ
----------



On Sunday, November 4, at 7 a.m. PT, we're having a Limited PTQ Finals event featuring *Guilds of Ravnica* Sealed. Enter this event with a Chandra or Jace Limited PTQ Finals Entry Token.


Players in North America take note: remember to set your clocks back one hour for Daylight Savings Time on Saturday night. For everyone else, the event starts at 15:00 UTC on Sunday.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

Downtime December 5
-------------------



The next downtime will be December 5, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC. Please note that there are no more 'extended' or 'normal' downtimes; in the new world with fewer downtimes, they're all the same length of time.

Build Notes
-----------


General | Various typos in the client have been corrected.


General | Colorless permanents now correctly lose static abilities when all their abilities are removed.


General | [Nicol Bolas, Planeswalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+Planeswalker) is back to his full strength and now correctly resolves his ultimate ability.


Modern, Legacy | [Angel's Grace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angel%27s+Grace) correctly wears off during the first upkeep step.


Limited | [Fireball](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) correctly no longer allows zero targets when X is larger than zero.


Limited | [Invent](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Invert+%2F%2F+Invent) can now correctly choose split cards.


Commander, Limited | [Back from the Brink](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Back+from+the+Brink) is back to being able to use floating mana.


Commander | [Mystifying Maze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystifying+Maze) now amazingly returns the creature to the battlefield tapped.


Store | Sales tax has been updated for Kentucky.
