So You Want to Go to a Grand Prix?
==================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 7, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












More people than ever are going to Grand Prix events. And you could be one of them! Here's how:




![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220216050541im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/img/interface/video-place-holder.png)[.](http://www.youtube.com/embed/fiakR7NgtF0)

 

 

And now that you know all about how to attend a Grand Prix, you might be interested in knowing that there are Grand Prix events in [Verona, Italy](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/grandprix/verona13), and [Rio de Janeiro](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/grandprix/riodejaneiro13) this weekend. In fact, let's just take a look at the whole schedule for the rest of the year.


### 2013 Season


The Grand Prix experience is held over three days starting on Friday, with the main event starting Saturday morning.
