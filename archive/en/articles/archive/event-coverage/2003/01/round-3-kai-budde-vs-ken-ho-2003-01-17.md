Round 3: Kai Budde vs. Ken Ho
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 17, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a243.jpg)Budde had previously developed a reputation for losing in the first round of Masters tournaments, but he's obviously now past that. Kai has played [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake) to the third round, and as a result, has already earned a significant amount of money for the weekend. In his way this round is Ken Ho, another Pro Tour Champion. Ho is running green/blue, the same archetype that brought him that Championship at Osaka.

### Game 1

Ho summoned a second turn [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel), and followed with [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill); Budde had [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse). [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion) was played on the following turn, and Ho played [Merfolk Looter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter) before getting [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill) into play. Budde started to cycle away cards, attempting to set up an optimal hand for when he would break [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill). With each turn the [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) would attack for two and Budde would cycle more cards with [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), or cycle [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith) to gain life. When he dropped to six life Budde broke [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill) with [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), getting [Seedtime](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seedtime). On the following turn he went for it and cast [Mirari's Wake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari%27s+Wake), which resolved. Ho played [Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm) on end step, getting easy lethal damage on the board. He took his turn and attacked, drawing [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) out of Budde's graveyard. Ho responded by activating [Merfolk Looter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter), and his body language showed the he wasn't happy with where the game was headed. [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) resolved, Ho stopped to think, and tapped out for [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval), floating two blue mana. A counterwar resulted, and Budde came out on top. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a250.jpg)With Ho tapped out, Budde took his turn and played [Mirari](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari). [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) saw Ho discard two land, and a second put [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) and a land into the graveyard. The [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) and Wurm attacked, and Budde used [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) to stay alive. Yet another [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) put [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) into the graveyard, and a [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) into play. This was followed by [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill), which was countered with [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic) discarded to [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion). Budde took his turn and flashed back [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) for more options, going down to one life point. Ho's next attack was nullified with [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace), and he asked Budde to count his library, which was at nine cards. Budde started to go off with [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), grabbing [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic). He took his turn and played [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God), clearing the board. 

[Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) was flashed back to give Ho some pressure. [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) was played and copied, getting the other Wish and [Krosan Reclamation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Reclamation). He used the Reclamation on himself twice to prevent decking, copying it each time. This was enough to convince Ho to concede.

Budde- 1 Ho- 0 

### Game 2

Ho mulliganed down to five. He still made four straight land drops, and had [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) and [Ravenous Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth) as threats. With Ho tapped out Budde played [Mirari](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari), and the Baloth and Rootwalla attacked for seven points of damage, bringing Budde down to twelve. Kai simply played [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and ended his turn. [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) slowed down Ho's attack, and [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval) reset the board, but from a bad position where Ho could not make the first land drop. The Rootwalla was in play, and Ho used his turn to play [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study), discarding [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and a land. Budde replayed [Compulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion). Ho looked to increase his pressure on the board with [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear), but Budde had [Memory Lapse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memory+Lapse). Kai tapped out for [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), and Ho used the opportunity to play [Werebear](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) and [Merfolk Looter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+Looter). Budde still had [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace)s to spare to stay alive, and Ho brought out more pressure with [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill) made the board even better, and Ho's next attack was just enough to defeat Budde, but the German cycled [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith) to stay alive another turn. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a249.jpg)Budde- "I've been really good at drawing this [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God) card in this match."  
 Ben Ronaldson- "You're playing [Wrath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath)?"

On the next turn Budde had to break [Standstill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill) with [Moment's Peace](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) to stay alive, and the resulting counterwar was won by Ho to force a third game.

Budde- 1 Ho- 1

### Game 3

Both players mulliganed their opening hands. Ho had a second turn Looter, and Budde had Complusion. Things weren't looking too good for the German, and he actually had to pitch [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith) to Complusion because he didn't have a white mana to cycle it regularly. This was done in an attempt to find a third land, and was not successful. Meanwhile, it was business as usual for Ho, who used [Careful Study](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Careful+Study) to get a Rootwalla into play, and [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) into the graveyard. The Looter went about its job as well, putting a [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) into the graveyard, which was flashed back soon after. Budde eventually found lands, but the tempo advantage lost due to the mana screw was too much.

**Final Result:** Budde- 1 Ho- 2
