*Eighth Edition* Rollout - *Scourge*
====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 9, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












*[Enrage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enrage) bodes ill for the return of [Howl from Beyond](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howl+from+Beyond).*
