Quarterfinals: Antoine Menard vs. Farid Meraghni
================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 4, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_92.png)
By Mark Wraith











This match pitted two of the more experienced players in the top eight against each other. Antoine was playing blue-green, and Farid had an [Opposition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opposition) deck almost identical to the one played by Franck Canu to win the Chicago Masters. The matchup probably starts in Farid's favor, but it really depends on the sideboarding strategy. They saw each other's decklist, so Farid knows Antoine will have to bring in all of his enchantment-hate. If he wants he could take out his enchantments completely, leaving [Naturalize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize) dead and [Spellbane Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellbane+Centaur) weak. On the other hand, Meraghni will have real problems if Antoine can consistently get [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder) on the graveyard. After a brief consultation it was decided that the Top 8 was best of three.

### Game 1

![Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Phantom+Centaur)Farid got yet another great draw, busting out a first turn [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise), two [Llanowar Elves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) on his second turn, and a [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) on his third. Antoine just played [Aquamoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aquamoeba) and searched for answers with a couple of Careful Studies.Farid was definitely the beatdown deck this game. He attacked with the Centaur putting Antoine on 15, then played a [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) and a [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). Antoine said go holding [Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm), so Farid just attacked with his Centaur, and flashed back the [Call](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call). Menard [Unsummon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unsummon)ed [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) and played [Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm). He untapped and made [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) as well.

Farid couldn't sensibly attack any more given Antoine's enormous blockers. He attempted a [Squirrel Nest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squirrel+Nest) but it was [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic)ed. He resolved one on his next turn, though, and this put the pressure back on Antoine to find an immediate answer. The only thing that could help was probably [Wonder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wonder), although as Farid had two [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) in play it wouldn't have been enough either.

Farid made things a lot quicker by playing [Opposition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opposition) at which point Antoine swept up his cards.

Farid Meraghni 1 – Antoine Menard 0

**Sideboarding**

![Call of the Herd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Call+of+the+Herd)Farid took out all of his [Opposition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Opposition)s and [Squirrel Nest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squirrel+Nest)s for a fairly random collection of cards – 1 [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), 1 Centaur, 2 [Envelop](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Envelop), 2 [Roar of the Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Roar+of+the+Wurm) and [Quiet Speculation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quiet+Speculation), which was a very good plan as Antoine had brought in [Spellbane Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellbane+Centaur) and [Naturalize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize).### Game 2

This was rather an anticlimax as Antoine mulliganed twice to a hand with four land and a [Naturalize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize) with no enchantments in Farid's deck. Meraghni played and Elf and a [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel), with a [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) for Menard's [Spellbane Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spellbane+Centaur) and [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). This left Antoine with only a turn five [Arrogant Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrogant+Wurm). By that point Farid had a [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) token in play as well, and because of [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), Antoine's Wurm had to chump-block a lethal [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). Antoine drew his next card and conceded.

**Final Result:** Farid Meraghni defeats Antoine Menard 2 – 0
