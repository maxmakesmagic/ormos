TOP 8 PLAYER PROFILES
=====================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX SHIZUOKA 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpshi15)
 on January 11, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff










The field of 2205 players has been narrowed down to 8. You can learn more about Grand Prix Shizuoka 2015's Top 8 players below.



Yuki Matsumoto
==============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpshi15/TOP8_matsumoto-Yuki.jpg)


**Hometown:** Tokyo  



**Previous Accomplishments:**  



**Where do you normally play *Magic*?**|  

Hareruya. I started *Magic* Online this season.



**What was your record in Sealed Deck? What did you play, and did you have any key cards?**  

7-2 with 1 bye. I played Jeskai with [Arrow Storm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrow+Storm).



**What were your records in the drafts? What did you play, and did you have any key cards?**  

I played two Temur decks, going 3-0 both times.



**What is the most underrated card in this format?**  
[Blinding Spray](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blinding+Spray), [Monastery Flock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Monastery+Flock), [Bloodfire Mentor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodfire+Mentor), and Dragon’s Eye Savants.



**What is your favorite Draft strategy?**   

Walls, unblockables, and removals.



**What surprised you the most at Grand Prix Shizuoka?**   

In the 3rd pack of the 1st draft, I received a 12th-pick [Swiftwater Cliffs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swiftwater+Cliffs) and 13th-pick [Thornwood Falls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thornwood+Falls).



**Which *Fate Reforged* preview has excited you the most?**   

Temporal Trespass and Ugin, the Spirit Dragon.

Shinji Nakano
=============


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpshi15/TOP8_nakano-Shinji.jpg)


**Hometown:** Tokyo  
**Age:** 26  
**Occupation:** Student



**Previous Accomplishments:**  

Nothing.



**Where do you normally play *Magic*?**  

I mostly play *Magic* Online.



**What was your record in Sealed Deck? What did you play, and did you have any key cards?**  

8-1 with 0 byes. I played Abzan with [Wingmate Roc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingmate+Roc).



**What were your records in the drafts? What did you play, and did you have any key cards?**  

I played Jeskai twice. The first was 3-0 and second was 2-0-1. [Act of Treason](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Act+of+Treason) and [Jeskai Student](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jeskai+Student) were the best in each deck.



**What is the most underrated card in this format?**  
[Summit Prowler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Summit+Prowler).



**What is your favorite Draft strategy?**   

Pick [Flying Crane Technique](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flying+Crane+Technique) and Win.



**What surprised you the most at Grand Prix Nagoya?**  

My deck helped me when I needed anything.



**Which *Fate Reforged* preview has excited you the most?**  

Bloodfire Enforcers

Bo Sun
======


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpshi15/TOP8_bo-Sun.jpg)


**Hometown:** Beijing,China  
**Age:** 33  
**Occupation:** Owner of Angel Card Shop.



**Previous Accomplishments:**  

2 GP Top 8 (Manila 2006, Kobe 2014), and 2nd at the MTGO Player of the Year 2009.



**Where do you normally play *Magic*?**  

Angel Card Shop



**What was your record in Sealed Deck? What did you play, and did you have any key cards?**  

8-1 with 2 byes. I played Jeskai with [Mantis Rider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mantis+Rider).



**What were your records in the drafts? What did you play, and did you have any key cards?**  

I played Mardu with [Sorin, Solemn Visitor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sorin%2C+Solemn+Visitor), and Jeskai with [Jeskai Ascendancy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jeskai+Ascendancy). The scores were 2-1 and 3-0.



**What is the most underrated card in this format?**  
[Villainous Wealth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Villainous+Wealth)



**What is your favorite Draft strategy?**   

Jeskai is the most powerful in this draft format.



**What surprised you the most at Grand Prix Shizuoka?**   

I was very surprised at how orderly GP Shizuoka was. I’d like to make a trip to a Japanese GP again.



**Which *Fate Reforged* preview has excited you the most?**   

I didn’t check the list yet.
