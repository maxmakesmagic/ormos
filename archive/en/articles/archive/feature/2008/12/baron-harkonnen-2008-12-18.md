For our fourth installment of Rerun Week, Daily Decks brings you a rerun inside a rerun! Early in **Magic**'s history, famed deckbuilder Adrian Sullivan created a powerful control build that combined card draw, counterspells, and removal to answer every single threat his opponent could play. [Gaea's Blessing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gaea%27s+Blessing) then allowed him to reshuffle the most important cards back into his deck to be replayed later. The above list is a modern interpretation of the deck, a rerun given to Gaudenis Vidugiris for use at the 2007 Pro Tour-Yokohama. The new build works just like the classic, and is even rerunning the beloved [Gaea's Blessing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gaea%27s+Blessing).
