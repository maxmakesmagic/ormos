 Airport Shuttle Service: $10.00 per person for a one-way ticket from the airport to the central business district, and $10.00 for a return trip. Three bags per person. Call (504) 465-9780. Pickup and ticket purchases can be made on the lower level outside of baggage claim. The airport shuttle also provides a limousine service by reservation (limited availability). A stretch limo is $90.00 and can accommodate six people.  
 Jefferson Transit: $1.10 includes regular fare and one transfer good on any Jefferson Transit bus. Fares for the Airline Highway bus are $.80 from the airport to Tulane Avenue or $1.10 from the airport to Elk Place in the central business district. The Airline Highway bus stops outside of entrance #5 on the upper level and runs approximately every 15–20 minutes on weekdays, and every 30 minutes on weekends.

Player registration will occur at the tournament site from 4:00–8:00 P.M. on Thursday, October 30th, 2003.

Players must have a signed waiver and consent form to compete at Pro Tour–New Orleans. ### Format

* Extended Constructed format
* Check **[www.thedci.com](http://www.thedci.com)** for Extended format rules.

**Round Length**  


* 60 minute rounds, 16 Swiss Rounds total + Top 8 single-elimination finals.
* When time is called, the active player will finish the turn and five additional turns will be taken
* Stalling and slow play rules will be strictly enforced during the tournament.

**Advancement**  


* All players participate in Friday's 8-round competition.
* Players with 15 or more match points after round 8 advance to Saturday's competition.
* The top 8 players after round 16 advance to Sunday's top 8 single-elimination finals.

**Notes**  


* Players are responsible for knowing all DCI rules and high-level tournament rules.
* Players must bring pen, paper, card sleeves and appropriate counters.

**Friday Schedule**  
 8:45 AM: Seating for player meeting posted.   
 9:00 AM: Player meeting begins. Decklists are collected from player tables by judges.  
 9:15 AM: First round begins.

**Saturday Schedule**  
 8:45 AM: Pairings for Round 9 posted.  
 9:00 AM: Round 9 begins.

**Sunday Schedule**  
 8:00 AM: All Top 8 players check in at feature match area.  
 9:00 AM: Final matches begin. (best 3 out of 5 - untimed)
