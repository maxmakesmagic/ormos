Red decks of all kinds are mainstays in Standard, but DustyStorm has a different take for **Magic Online**. A return to the days of Goblins was responsible for Dusty's 3-1 performance in a recent daily event. Featuring five representatives from the most chaotic of tribes, DustyStorm takes advantage of [Warren Instigator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warren+Instigator)'s abilities. The deck also benefits from a healthy smattering of burn and heavy hitters like [Ball Lightning](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ball+Lightning) and [Hellspark Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hellspark+Elemental). A great choice for heading off to Friday Night **Magic** this week!
