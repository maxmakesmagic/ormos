The Extended season is winding down, but players are still innovating right to the very end. Kellen Abel recently finished in the Top 8 of a Pro Tour Qualifier in Boise, Idaho using this take on Cruel Control. With the powerful [Cruel Ultimatum](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Ultimatum) as a finishing touch, Kellen overwhelms his opponents. To get to that stage in the game he also relies on early spot removal like [Go for the Throat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Go+for+the+Throat) and his creature suite of [Inferno Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inferno+Titan) and [Sedraxis Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sedraxis+Specter).
