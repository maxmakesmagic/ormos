Mini-Movies from Latin America Championship 2000
================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












* [[view](http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/movies_legacy_migration/LAC00/images/movie8.mpg)] With complete board control, Raphael Le Saux shows his opponent many counterspells. His opponent, Carlos Romao, conceeds as the Chile crowd goes crazy for their hometown friend. (1.4M MPEG)
* [[view](http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/movies_legacy_migration/LAC00/images/movie7.mpg)] The spectators watch nervously as Carlos Romao (Brazil) and Raphael Le Saux (Chile) play the last few minutes of their semifinal match. (1.4M MPEG)
* [[view](http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/movies_legacy_migration/LAC00/images/movie6.mpg)] On Saturday evening, the Magic Quiz Show was a big hit among the fans. Special guest Mike Elliot from Magic R&D supplied the questions while Alvaro Lopez hosted the event in Spanish. (1.4M MPEG)
* [[view](http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/movies_legacy_migration/LAC00/images/movie1.mpg)] Round 5 action at Latin America Championship. (1.4M MPEG)
* [[view](http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/movies_legacy_migration/LAC00/images/movie2.mpg)] Gustavo Chapela Gaxiola in a round 6 match versus Rafael Alvarenga. (1.4M MPEG)
* [[view](http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/movies_legacy_migration/LAC00/images/movie3.mpg)] Round 9 match between Douglas Maioli and Joaquin Alvarez Rivera. (1.4M MPEG)
* [[view](http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/movies_legacy_migration/LAC00/images/movie4.mpg)] Feature Match between Gerardo Godinez-Estrada and Francisco Garcia-Barbosa. (1.4M MPEG)
* [[view](http://magic.wizards.com/sites/mtg/files/movies_legacy_migration/LAC00/images/movie5.mpg)] A Chilean TV station did live interviews during the weekend. (1.2M MPEG)
