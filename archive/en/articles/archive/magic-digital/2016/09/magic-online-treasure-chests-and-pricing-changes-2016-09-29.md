Magic Online Treasure Chests and Pricing Changes
================================================



 Posted in **Magic Digital**
 on September 29, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











There are some major changes coming to *Magic Online* with *Kaladesh*—lower event entry requirements, adjustments to redemption, and something called Treasure Chests that will change how you experience *Magic Online*. *Magic Online* Executive Producer Worth Wollpert and *Magic Online* Digital Product Manager Lee Sharpe detail the changes in the articles below.


* [Wollpert on changes to *Magic Online*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/magic-online-economy-changes-2016-09-29)
* [Sharpe on *Kaladesh* events changes](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/magic-online-kaladesh-event-changes-2016-09-29)
* [Announcing Treasure Chests](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/announcing-treasure-chests-2016-09-29)
