Menno Dolstra Top 8 Profile
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**by Sideboard Staff**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPAMS01/859.jpg)**Name:** Menno Dolstra   
**Age:** 19   
**Location:** Wageningen, The Netherlands   
**Occupation:** Student   
**Played Magic since:** 1995   
**Best accomplishments:** Top 8 GP Frankfurt 



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Amsterdam?**  

A: Because the GP was very close to my home it's extremely fun to play in a GP.



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: I hoped to make day 2 because I had 2 byes and I felt comfortable with the format.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: I got lucky at the right moments.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Because I drafted well and got lucky.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: Qualification for PT Barcelona, being able to play in the masters gateway.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: Brian Davis will win it.



**Q: Anything else you'd like to share with us?**  

A: It has been a very good weekend!
