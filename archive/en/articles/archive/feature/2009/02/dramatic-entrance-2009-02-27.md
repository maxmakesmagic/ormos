[Dramatic Entrance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dramatic+Entrance)? [Impromptu Raid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Impromptu+Raid)? Your eyes might not believe it, but those were the exact cards Ajnar25 used to put giant fatties into play for significantly less than cost. The payoff? Fifth place in a recent **Magic Online** Premier Event. What does the future hold for Ajnar's Mono-Green build? No one can say for certain, but a likely bet would be that it will rhyme with "[Progenitus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Progenitus)."
