=============================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 23, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)

New York City becomes the hub of the **Magic: The Gathering** universe in December as the city hosts the 2007 **Magic** World Championships. Home to the first Pro Tour back in 1996 and many other events, New York City is as rich in **Magic** history as any place on the globe.

From December 6-9, players from around the world will be flocking to the city that never sleeps with dreams of being crowned World Champion. Along with the main event, there are four packed days of public events and hall attractions open to all levels of players, including a chance [to win a 2007 Toyota FJ Cruiser](/en/node/732266)! Whatever your **Magic** interests, there's something for you at the 2007 **Magic: The Gathering**  World Championships.

### Calling all Magic Players: Be on National Television!

Wizards of the Coast and the CBS Early Show are looking for 200+ **Magic**  fans to appear on live national TV in NYC! We're taking players from the Javits Center over to the CBS Studios Thursday morning for a national TV appearance.

- 7 a.m. Load buses in front of the Javits Center (11th and West 35th). Free product and swag given to the first 150 people.
- 8 – 8:30 a.m. Filming LIVE on the Early Show from CBS Studios\*
- 8:45 a.m. Buses depart back to the Javits Center
\*Competing in Worlds? Don’t worry; you’ll make it back in time! A separate bus will depart the CBS Studios at 8:15 a.m. and have you back to the Javits Center by 8:45 a.m. 

If you’re interested in participating, please contact [pr.wizards@gmail.com](mailto:pr.wizards@gmail.com).

Players must have a signed waiver and consent form to compete at the 2007 World Championships. Forms can be downloaded at [www.wizards.com/protour](/en/articles/archive/pro-tour%E2%80%93amsterdam-artists-2010-08-26) and will be available onsite. At registration, all participants must present one of the following forms of identification upon request:- a driver’s license, or
- a passport, or
- a copy of their birth certificates and photo IDs (school IDs are acceptable).
We encourage players to attend mandatory registration on Wednesday. However we understand that delays might cause players to miss this and we have a registration on Thursday morning between 7:30 a.m. and 8:30 a.m. Players at late registration do not always receive the same gifts as those at mandatory registration.
 Players not registered by 8:30 a.m. on December 6 may be denied entry to the World Championships.

### Hall Attractions

Win a car! There are nine special qualifier events during Worlds, culminating in a Standard tournament where the winner drives home in a 2007 Toyota FJ Cruiser. Click [here](/en/node/732266) for complete contest details.

See all public events [here](/en/articles/archive/feature/2007-worlds-public-events-2007-10-08). Find a format you like and come on down...prizes include iPods, an Apple TV, a framed uncut sheet of **Magic** cards, and much more!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds06/mihara_trophy.jpg)*Who will hoist the 2007 World Champion trophy?*See **Magic** history unfold before your eyes as Wizards of the Coast inducts the third class of the [**Magic** Pro Tour Hall of Fame](/en/node/574031). What legends of the game will earn enshrinement?

Meet some of the game's top artists! Aleksi Briclot, Paolo Parente, Jim Murray, and Rebecca Guay will be in attendance, signing cards and talking with fans. Click [here](/en/articles/archive/feature/2007-magic-world-championships-artists-2007-11-20) for artist profiles, links to card lists, and signing schedules.

Want to see how you fare against the makers of **Magic**? Come to the **gunslinging area**  and test your skills against members of Wizards of the Coast's R&D department -- with prizes awarded when you win!

Do you think you're the master of **Magic** trivia? Test your knowledge at **Question Mark**, a Worlds tradition hosted by none other than **Magic** head designer Mark Rosewater. Team up with your friends and win great prizes.

Watch Sunday's Top 8 action on **plasma screens in the hall**, with Randy Buehler and Brian David-Marshall providing the play-by-play and color commentary. Sunday's action begins at 1 p.m. ET and is also available as a [webcast](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgevent/webcast/home) from the comfort of your own home.
