Last Day of *Innistrad* Flashback Draft
---------------------------------------


Tomorrow, November 7, is your last day to get in on the spooky action of triple *Innistrad* Flashback Draft. Enjoy frightful fights and the creepiest of creatures while you still can!




---

*Core Set* *Cube*
-----------------


The next event in the Cube Spotlight Series is almost upon us! A full week of *Core Set Cube* draft starts on November 7. This Cube features iconic cards from every core set on *Magic Online*, from *Seventh Edition* to *Core Set 2019*. Cube designer Kyle O'Neill has more on the cube in his article, available [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/cube-spotlight-series-core-set-cube-2018-10-30)!

PTQ Saturday and Sunday
-----------------------


There will be a Standard PTQ this Saturday at 9 a.m. PT. Then on Sunday, there will be a Modern PTQ at 9 a.m. PT. Each event costs 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).
