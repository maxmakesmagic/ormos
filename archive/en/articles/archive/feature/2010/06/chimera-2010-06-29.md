Nicholas Labarre is one of France's greatest deck building minds. At Worlds 2000 he took this deck, called "Chimera," to the Top 8. So how did it work? He used [Saproling Cluster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Saproling+Cluster), [Ashnod's Altar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashnod%27s+Altar), , and [Fecundity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fecundity), to generate creatures, mana, and to draw cards. Serra Avatar allowed him to ensure he never decked himself while Whetstone served as a win condition. It was a wacky combo deck even by turn of the century standards and remains one of Labarre's best known decks.
