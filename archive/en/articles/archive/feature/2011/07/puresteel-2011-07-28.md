With ***Magic** 2012* in the mix, the world of Standard is shaping up for an entirely new Standard season. On display this past weekend at the StarCityGames.com Open in Seattle, Washington were a host of archetypes, including the popular White Weenie strategy known as Puresteel. Chris Schafer played this version to a Top 16, relying on a plethora of Equipment to fuel ridiculous draws once [Puresteel Paladin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Puresteel+Paladin) is on the battlefield. Schafer even splashed enough blue to squeeze in [Trinket Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinket+Mage) and [Preordain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Preordain), both great options to hunt for more Equipment to fuel the Paladin! Visit StarCityGames.com for [full coverage](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/22342_The_StarCityGamescom_Open_Series_Is_Returning_To_Seattle_This_Weekend.html) of the event.
