Feature: The Top 8 Player Profiles
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 29, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/usnat06/top8_sittner.jpg)*Alex Sittner*


### Alex (John) Sittner


**Age:** 19


**Hometown:** Salt Lake City


**Occupation:** Student


**Number of Times Playing at Nationals:** 1


**How did you qualify for U.S. Nationals?**  

 Pro Points


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 0


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:**   

 Finished 7th at Pro Tour-Charleston


**What was your record by format?**  
**Standard:** 5-2 ***Ravnica* Draft:** 3-1  ***Coldsnap* Draft:** 3-0


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**  

 Playtested with team atogslc.com - my brother, Jack Stanton, Steve Jarvis


**What Standard deck are you playing? Why did you choose it?**  

 Black-white Hand Destruction - been playing and winning with it since Honolulu.


**What strategy did you use for the *Coldsnap* draft?**  

 Draft [Kjeldoran War Cry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kjeldoran+War+Cry)






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/usnat06/top8_cheon.jpg)*Paul Cheon*

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/usnat06/top8_kudlick.jpg)*Alex Kudlick*


### Alex Kudlick


**Age:** 19


**Hometown:** Sacramento, California


**Occupation:** Student


**Number of Times Playing at Nationals:** 1


**How did you qualify for U.S. Nationals?** Regionals


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 0


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** Qualified at Regionals


**What was your record by format?**  
**Standard:** 4-1-2  ***Ravnica* Draft:** 4-0  ***Coldsnap* Draft:** 2-1


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**


I playtested with my former dorm buddies. I also read Magic articles online extensively, and especially the Drafting with Rich Hoaen feature on Star City.


**What Standard deck are you playing? Why did you choose it?**


Black-white Husk. I was worried about the Tron matchup, but I figured that the metagame would be defined by decks that beat Tron, not Tron. Also, I was comfortable with Husk.


**What strategy did you use for the *Coldsnap* draft?**  

 I tried to read signals. I went green-red.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/usnat06/top8_derosa.jpg)*Antonino DeRosa*


### Antonino De Rosa


**Age:** 24


**Hometown:** Carlsbad, California


**Occupation:** Game Designer


**Number of Times Playing at Nationals:** 5 US Nats, 2 Italian Nats


**How did you qualify for U.S. Nationals?** PT Players Club


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 1 PT, 9 GPs, 3 GP Wins (Same as Finkel)


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** U.S. National Champ 2005


**What was your record by format?**  
**Standard:** 5-2 ***Ravnica* Draft:** 3-1 ***Coldsnap* Draft:** 3-0


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**  

 I drafted with the San Diego crew and played Standard with Hunter Cole and Gary Arant. Then I barned a deck from Osyp.


**What Standard deck are you playing? Why did you choose it?**  

 Ghazi-Glare, because it is good against creatures. Sadly it is bad in this Top 8.


**What strategy did you use for the *Coldsnap* draft?**  

 Don't touch a green card while forcing snow.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/usnat06/top8_aten.jpg)*Tim Aten*


### Tim Aten


**Age:** 400


**Hometown:** Vermilion, Ohio


**Occupation:** "Editor"


**Number of Times Playing at Nationals:** 5


**How did you qualify for U.S. Nationals?** Pro Tour Players Club


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 0


**Other Previous Magic accomplishments:** Won Grand Prix-Chicago


**What was your record by format?**  
**Standard:** 5-2 ***Ravnica* Draft:** 3-1 ***Coldsnap* Draft:** 2-1


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**  

 I ate at Stone Cold Creamery with Rich Hoaen


**What Standard deck are you playing? Why did you choose it?**  

 Tron. I was going to play black-white, but it's wicked lame, so I audibled to the deck I cashed with in Hawaii.


**What strategy did you use for the *Coldsnap* draft?**  

 I was going to force mono-blue, but I saw some black cards, and [Blizzard Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blizzard+Specter) tabled in pack 2, so I moved into black.
