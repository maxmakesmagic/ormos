The Basic Lands of Commander (2016 Edition)
===========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 25, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






Hey everyone, come see how good these lands look!


Seriously, the lands in *Commander (2016 Edition)* are home runs. With three versions of each basic land—one of each basic from [Rebecca Guay](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&output=spoiler&method=visual&artist=+%5b%22Rebecca%20Guay%22%5d), [Mark Poole](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&output=spoiler&method=visual&artist=+%5b%22Mark%20Poole%22%5d), and [Kev Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5b%22Kev+Walker%22%5d)—you're sure to find one you love.


Personally, I'm a fan of Rebecca Guay, but my individual favorite is the Kev Walker Plains, which manages to be haunting and serene at the same time.
