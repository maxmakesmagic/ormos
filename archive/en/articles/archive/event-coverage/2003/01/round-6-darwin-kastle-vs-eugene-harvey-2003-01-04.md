Round 6: Darwin Kastle vs. Eugene Harvey
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 4, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_benbleiweiss.jpg)
By Ben Bleiweiss











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a968.jpg)Your Move Games' Darwin Kastle, fresh off his top four finish at PT Houston, came to play in New Orleans with the deck that did him well in November: The Rock. GP Atlanta Champion Eugene Harvey also went with The Rock in Houston, but his top 64 finish wasn't enough to satisfy his competitive urges for here he decided to change sides to [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog). While both players seemed calm and collected as the match began, I've been told on good authority by several players that Mr. Harvey has been living quite the life the past few days in the streets of the French Quarter. Would this affect his game?

### Game 1

Eugene won the die roll and led with an [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island). He followed this with a second [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island), while Darwin cast [Vampiric Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor) to fetch [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress). On his second turn, the YMG player completely destroyed Harvey. First, he cast [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), which revealed two [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition)s, a [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), [Fact or Fiction](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction), [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval), and a [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp). Darwin knocked the FoF out of Harvey's hand. He then very stoically and properly cast [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy), waiting for Eugene to okay the resolve before naming [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition). This left Harvey with only the Tog, an [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval), and a [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) in hand, with two [Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)s on board.

Harvey attempted to get things going by playing the toothy monster on his following turn, but it died to [Diabolic Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Edict). Woe unto Eugene, as Darwin drew another [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress). Eugene burned the [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) in his hand to grab [Coffin Purge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coffin+Purge) from the Sideboard, but Darwin still stripped the [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval) from hand. [Yavimaya Elder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yavimaya+Elder) hit the board and began swinging, joined by a Bird of Paradise.

Both players drew their cards meticulously and showed no sign of emotion to betray their hands. Darwin sat with multiple lands he had drawn, while kept playing out the lands that were endlessly coming off the top of his deck. Finally, Darwin drew a third [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), took [Coffin Purge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coffin+Purge) out of Eugene's hand, and safely played [Ravenous Baloth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth). Eugene found a second [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) which ate the Elder. In return, the Elder found a [Diabolic Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Edict) to eat the tog. 

The Baloth kept on swinging, and since Eugene was forced to scoop.

Darwin Kastle 1 – Eugene Harvey 0.

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a967.jpg)Darwin (whose side of the table I was able to view more readily) kept a hand with double Deed, [Diabolic Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Edict), [Llanowar Wastes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Wastes), [Dust Bowl](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dust+Bowl), [Volrath's Stronghold](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volrath%27s+Stronghold), and [Yavimaya Elder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yavimaya+Elder). Before he could even take his first turn, Eugene gigged the Edict via [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress). Darwin drew...and found a [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) of his own, taking out one of a pair of [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) from the CMU player's hand. A turn later, he lacked a second [Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest), so Kastle attempted to [Vampiric Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor), but that was countered. Darwin drew his [Forest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) anyhow (Darwin: "That's the card I would have tutored for anyway"), and attempted to cast a third turn Elder, but Eugene had drawn a [Force Spike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+Spike) over the past turn, which caught Darwin unawares.

The game quickly went downhill for the big man. Eugene began using the [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)/[Polluted Delta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta) engine to set up a ridiculous hand, and faced no pressure from a slightly mana-stopped Darwin. Within turns, he had cast [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition), two [Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) (the first for one card, the second for four), a [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) (hard cast and flashed back), and [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress). Confident that Darwin had no tricks in hand after the [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), Eugene cast a 10th turn [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval), with enough mana floating to drop [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) on an empty board. Darwin managed to chump block a couple of turns with his [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise), but in the end the math was elementary and the blue/black monster came across for lethal damage.

Eugene Harvey 1 – Darwin Kastle 1.

### Game 3

By the start of Game 3, there were only fifteen minutes left on the clock. Darwin started out quickly with a pair of [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise), but had nothing to go with them except a sole [Yavimaya Elder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yavimaya+Elder). By contrast, Eugene cast back-to-back [Fact or Fiction](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction)s, first netting [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) + [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) (while sending [Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) and [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) to the grave), and then being given [Polluted Delta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta)/[Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)/[Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) (sending a second Knowledge to the graveyard along with a [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog)). Unfortunately, Eugene missed a couple of turns of land drops, and Darwin had found his sole [Volrath's Stronghold](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volrath%27s+Stronghold) to begin drawing three cards a turn with his Elder. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpno03/a966.jpg)The game kept getting uglier and uglier. Harvey burned through his deck four cards at a time between [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), [Polluted Delta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta), [Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) and [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis), but could not find a threat to kill Darwin. Darwin kept sacrificing his Elder, but amazingly drew land after land off his 'third' Elder card for many consecutive turns. On the tenth turn of the game, time ran out and the two men went into extra turns. On the first extra turn, Darwin's hand consisted of [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), [Haunting Echoes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haunting+Echoes), [Diabolic Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Edict), and a bajillion lands. He cast the [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), and Eugene took a good ten minutes to decide how to respond. Eventually, he cast [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) to get [Mana Short](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Short), cast [Mana Short](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Short) to tap Darwin out (except for two [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise)), sacrificed [Polluted Delta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta) to get a [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp), used the [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) to cast [Vampiric Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampiric+Tutor), and put [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval) on top of his deck. [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) finally resolved, showing [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) x2, [Thwart](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thwart) (which was taken), [Polluted Delta](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polluted+Delta) and [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog). 

On the following turn, Eugene went for the win: [Upheaval](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval) plus [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog). Darwin wisely floated two black mana with his [Birds](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds), and was able to Edict the atog once it reached the table. With the life totals reading 18-10 in Darwin's favor, nothing on the board, and only three turns left to play, the game pretty much ended as a draw (although Darwin insisted on playing it out to fruition).

**Final Result:** Tie: Darwin Kastle 1 – Eugene Harvey 1.
