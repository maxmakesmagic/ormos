The September 20, 2017 Update
=============================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on September 20, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_Nicholas_Wolfram.jpg)
By Nicholas Wolfram




 Nicholas Wolfram fell promptly in love with tabletop and video games when he was only two years old, and he has been writing about them since he figured out how words worked. Now he puts together words about Magic, which he started playing way back in 2000. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on September 20, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you amazing *Ixalan*-inspired artwork!


Today's Must
============


1. [Mana Bases in *Ixalan* Standard](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/mana-bases-in-ixalan-standard/) | ChannelFireball | Frank Karsten

If you've been waiting for some data-driven analysis of your upcoming Standard mana base options, Frank has you covered. This is a very deep dive into what you'll be looking at as you start your deck building in the coming week.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [Building with *Ixalan*'s Pirates](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?ID=14175&writer=Seth+Manfield&articledate=9-20-2017)| TCGplayer | Seth Manfield

They might not be Dinosaurs, but I *guess* Pirates are pretty cool. And are probably going to find a spot in the metagame pretty quickly.


1. [*Ixalan* Set Review: Commons and Uncommons](http://lrcast.com/limited-resources-407-ixalan-set-review-commons-and-uncommons/) | Limited Resources | Luis Scott-Vargas and Marshall Sutcliffe

Marshall and Luis make it through the entire suite of commons and uncommons in the set in just over three and a half hours.


1. [Ixalonging for New Rules!](http://www.magictheamateuring.com/mta-episode-243-ixalonging-for-new-rules/) | Magic the Amateuring | Meghan Wolff and Maria Bartholdi

Judge Rob McKenzie joins Meghan and Maria to discuss *Ixalan* mechanics and rules changes.


1. [*Ixalan* Limited Prerelease Primer](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/db/article.asp?ID=14174&writer=Steve+Rubin&articledate=9-20-2017) | TCGplayer | Steve Rubin

After you've finished with [Gavin Verhey's Prerelease Primer](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/ixalan-prerelease-primer-2017-09-18), check out Steve's take for a more strategic overview of the event.


1. [Game Knights with Meghan Wolff and Maria Bartholdi](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C3Kb-CBYTwE) | Game Knights | Jimmy Wong and Josh Lee Kwai

We hooked up the Game Knights guys with a few *Ixalan* Prerelease packs for this episode. Check it out to see Jimmy and Josh throw down with Meghan and Maria for some Ixa-llent Two-Headed Giant action!


Help Us Explore Ixalan
======================


1. [Submit your *Ixalan* decklists for *Magic: The Gathering Arena*](https://www.facebook.com/magicthegatheringarena/)

Starting later today, you can submit decklists made exclusively with cards from *Ixalan*. Then, in mid-October, we're going to be having a special stream where two outstanding streamers will play with the two coolest decks! So start brewing and show us what you got!
