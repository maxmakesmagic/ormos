Grand Prix - Rio de Janeiro
---------------------------


### Magic: The Gathering
 Pro Tour - Barcelona Qualifier
 March 9 - 11, 2001


The **Magic: The Gathering**® Grand Prix tournament series is an international circuit of large-scale Qualifier tournaments featuring cash prizes and multiple invitations to Pro TourTM events.


**TOURNAMENT FORMAT AND SCHEDULE**


* The format for Grand Prix - Rio de Janeiro is *InvasionTM* block Limited, with Day 2 Booster Draft. The Day 2 Booster Draft will use English cards. All DCITM Limited rules, **Magic**® Floor Rules announced on September 1, 2000, and all subsequent updates will be enforced.
* **Friday, March 9:** Registration from 6:00 - 9:00 P.M.
* **Saturday, March 10:** Registration from 7:30 - 8:30 A.M. Tournament begins at 9:00 A.M. The Top 64 players advance to competition on Sunday.
* **Sunday, March 11:** Top 64 competition begins at 9:00 A.M., followed immediately by the finals.

**TOURNAMENT**


* **Divisions**  

 The main tournament at Grand Prix - Rio de Janeiro is open to **Magic** players of all ages.

 *If the player who wins an invitation to Pro Tour - Barcelona at Grand Prix - Rio de Janeiro has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher. Previous qualification may occur through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid on the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*
* **Tournament Format**  

 The format for Grand Prix - Rio de Janeiro is *Invasion* block Limited, with Day 2 Booster Draft. The Day 2 Booster Draft will be in English. All DCI Limited rules, **Magic** Floor Rules announced on September 1, 2000, and all subsequent updates will be enforced.
* **Tournament Structure**  

 The structure for Grand Prix - Rio de Janeiro is modified Swiss (60-minute rounds, with the number of rounds determined by the number of participants), with the Top 64 players advancing to competition on Sunday for prizes and invitations.
