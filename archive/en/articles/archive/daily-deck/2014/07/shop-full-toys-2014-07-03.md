A Shop Full of Toys
===================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on July 3, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 






With the introduction of *Vintage Masters*, the fabled Power Nine is finally available on *Magic Online*! With [Black Lotus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Black+Lotus) and friends now available, Vintage Daily Events have started populating the *Magic Online* schedule.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Mishra's%20Workshop)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra's%20Workshop)
Vintage is a crazy, crazy format. Cards that are incredibly powerful are often combined with cards that rarely see Constructed play in other formats. This deck is based around the power of [Mishra's Workshop](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Workshop), and plays many expensive artifacts in order to take advantage of the incredible mana production that is possible. [Kuldotha Forgemaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kuldotha+Forgemaster), [Lodestone Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lodestone+Golem), and [Staff of Nin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Staff+of+Nin) are not cards that are commonly played in formats like Modern, but have a home in Vintage because of [Mishra's Workshop](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Workshop).


[Mishra's Workshop](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Workshop) decks are often mana-denial decks. [Sphere of Resistance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphere+of+Resistance), [Thorn of Amethyst](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thorn+of+Amethyst), and [Tangle Wire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tangle+Wire) are used to slow down the crazy starts from other Vintage decks. Meanwhile, this deck is much less affected by mana denial due to its many ways to generate multiple mana from one card. To take advantage of this disruption, large artifact threats are used to end the game. [Kuldotha Forgemaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kuldotha+Forgemaster) is the key threat, capable of getting [Wurmcoil Engine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wurmcoil+Engine), [Sundering Titan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sundering+Titan), [Steel Hellkite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steel+Hellkite), [Duplicant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duplicant), or a host of other artifacts that are powerful enough to search out with Forgemaster, but also very castable via the power of [Mishra's Workshop](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Workshop).
