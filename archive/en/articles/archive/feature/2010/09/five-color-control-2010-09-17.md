A former Pro Tour champion, Guillaume Wafo-Tapa of France found himself back in the Top 8 in Amsterdam. The event marked one of many successful finishes for the super pro, and he succeeded with his trademark card: [Mystical Teachings](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystical+Teachings). The linchpin in his successful Block deck that managed to earn him his title, Guillaume used the card to tutor up all manner of answers against his opponents throughout the weekend. Read full coverage of the event [here](/en/events/coverage/rietzl-sweeps-victory-amsterdam).
