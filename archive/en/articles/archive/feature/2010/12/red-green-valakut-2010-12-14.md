Simon Görtzen won the very first Pro Tour of 2010 in San Diego. At the 2010 World Championships in Chiba, Japan, he was still going strong in the Standard format! His Red-Green [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Valakut%252C%2Bthe%2BMolten%2BPinnacle) list served him well in that event, and even got him noticed for a deck tech. You can check out all the coverage of the World Championships in the archive [here](/en/events/coverage/theres-no-jace-guillaume).
