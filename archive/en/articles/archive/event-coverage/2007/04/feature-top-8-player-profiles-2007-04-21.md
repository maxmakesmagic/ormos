### Raphael Levy


  
**Age:** 25  



**Hometown:** Toulouse, France


**Occupation:** Teacher / Internet Columnist


**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** A couple...


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-Yokohama?** Top 50 in Geneva!


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 13 GP Top 8's / 3 PT Top 8's


**Other previous Magic accomplishments:** Top 50 in Geneva. Lucky. (Also member of the Magic Pro Tour Hall of Fame)


**What deck are you playing and why?**  

A mono-red burn deck called "Make It Rain." It seemed to be the most adapted deck to the format.


**How did you prepare for the event? Who did you prepare with?**  

I playtested on Magic Online a little to have an idea about the field. The REAL playtesting came on Thursday when I had a VERY close idea of what the field would be. Julien Nuijten showed me the deck I wanted to see... the best one!


**What was the key card in your deck and why?** 


[Keldon Marauders](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Keldon+Marauders), a two-casting cost creature / burn spell.


**What advice would you have for players using your deck archetype in a Pro Tour Qualifier?** 


Avoid [Wall of Roots](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Roots) if possible.
