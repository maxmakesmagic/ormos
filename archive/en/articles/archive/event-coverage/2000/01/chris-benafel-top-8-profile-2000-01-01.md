Chris Benafel Top 8 Profile
===========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**by Sideboard Staff**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPAMS01/862.jpg)**Name:** Chris Benafel  
**Age:** 18  
**Location:** Eugene, OR  
**Occupation:** Pro Magic player  
**Played Magic since:** 1994  
**Best accomplishments:** lots of seconds (not talking about food)



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Amsterdam?**  

A: Fun



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: Top 8 because anything else would be uncivilized.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: I made some very bad plays and some good ones. For the most part I played well.



**Q: What do you think about the format?**  

A: Dragon are overrated. Long live Nomadic Elf and any other 2-power, 2-mana creatures.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: I played a pretty good game and had luck on my side.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: It is very nice to make top eight and even nicer to win.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: I think everyone has a good shot .



**Q: Anything else you'd like to share with us?**  

A: Props to my sponsor Alpha Beta Unlimited.com, Ryan Fuller and Noah Boeken my team mates.
