iGoogle Themes
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 31, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Google just launched its gaming themes for iGoogle, and **Magic: The Gathering** is one of the 25 featured games! You can see the cool stuff [right here](http://www.google.com/help/ig/gaming/#mtg).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/157_ss.jpg)  
The themes change depending on the time of day, and there are twelve different themes. They feature artwork from **Magic**'s planeswalkers and giant monsters and there are even some preview images from *Alara Reborn*!

The announcement came at a big, fancy Google party. So if you've ever wondered what Nicol Bolas would look like projected on a wall, the answer is this:

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/157_dragon.jpg)
