Decks of the Week: 1/18/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 18, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations are in order to Ken Yukuhiro and Jon Stern, winners of Grands Prix in Singapore and Atlantic City, respectively. Ken and Jon are now qualified for Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze*, and you can join them by winning a PTQ! The format for the Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze* PTQ season is Modern. See the kinds of decks players are playing with these Top 8 lists, and find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#dgm)! Good luck; have fun!
