A control deck quietly sneaking under the Standard radar is this red-white control build played by Jody Dunnville to a recent PTQ Top 8. Powerful planeswalkers and creature token generators provide win conditions alongside an [Obelisk of Alara](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obelisk+of+Alara) and a single [Magma Phoenix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magma+Phoenix). Spot removal, board sweepers, and [Scepter of Dominance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scepter+of+Dominance) keep creatures under control. Quite an innovative look at the present world of Standard!
