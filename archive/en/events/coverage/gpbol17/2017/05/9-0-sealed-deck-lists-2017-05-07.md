9-0 Sealed Deck Lists
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 7, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











Six players escaped the first day's nine grueling rounds with a pristine record of 9-0: Grand Prix Kuala Lumpur 2014 champion Fabien Li, Grand Prix Madrid 2015 finalist Francesco Giorgio, Pro Tour *Aether Revolt* quarterfinalist (22) Martin Jůza, Michael Meier, Claudio Biscione, and Grand Prix Stuttgart quarterfinalist Patrizio Golia (pictured below, left to right).
