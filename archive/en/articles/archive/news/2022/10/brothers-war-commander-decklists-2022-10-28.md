The Brothers' War Commander Decklists
=====================================



 Posted in **News**
 on October 28, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











[*The Brothers' War*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/the-brothers-war) hits stores worldwide on November 18, and for Commander fans, we have two new Commander decks featuring epic legends, reprints, and all-new cards.



![Isperia](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/en_82nUWbewyd.png)Urza's Iron Alliance
Â 

![Starter](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/en_snwun3DrwE.png)Mishra's Burnished Banner
You can check out the cards in [*The Brothers' War* Commander Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/the-brothers-war-commander), as well as the regular cards in [*The Brothers' War* Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/the-brothers-war) and [Booster Fun variants of *The Brothers' War* cards](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/the-brothers-war-variants).


Plus, for a rundown of all the cards across the set and where you can find them, check out our [Collecting *The Brothers' War* article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/news/feature/whats-inside-the-brothers-war-boosters)!


Look for *The Brothers' War* Commander decks at your [local game store](https://locator.wizards.com), online through [Amazon](https://gtly.to/SrLywky5i), and everywhere you find *Magic: The Gathering* products sold.


***(Editor's Note: The decklists below pull the newest printing of each card automatically from*** [***Gatherer***](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Default.aspx)***, including printings which are not in this product. Decklists are not card-for-card product displays but rather interactive lists of the cards included in each deck.)***
