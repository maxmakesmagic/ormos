Checking in on Block Constructed
================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 6, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







I think it's safe to say that [Sphinx's Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%27s+Revelation) is still the defining card of *Return to Ravnica* Block Constructed. At a glance, it looked to me like straight white-blue was the most popular [Sphinx's Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%27s+Revelation) deck, but it's not hard to find someone playing white, blue, and any third color. 


![Gyre Sage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Gyre+Sage)

[Sphinx's Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%27s+Revelation) should win any game in which the opponent doesn't have it and gives the player who has it plenty of time to cast it, so Mono-Red aggro appears to be the dominant counter strategy, and that appears to be fairly successful. 


 There are also a handful of midrange decks that I assume exist to prey on the Red decks, but they generally also have some kind of plan for [Sphinx's Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%27s+Revelation). 


 Anyway, the coolest deck I saw, skimming through some **Magic Online** Daily results was this Bant deck that used [Gyre Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gyre+Sage) to power up huge [Sphinx's Revelation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphinx%27s+Revelation)s. 


 I think [Gyre Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gyre+Sage) is one of the more underappreciated cards in Standard right now, and this looks like a good way to use it. I like that this deck focuses on creatures over spells to have plenty of fuel to power [Gyre Sage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gyre+Sage), and I love [Trostani, Selesnya's Voice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trostani%2C+Selesnya%27s+Voice) in a format where people are playing a lot of Mono-Red. 


 Part of me wants to see [Stolen Identity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stolen+Identity) in this deck so Trostani can populate all the best creatures, but I suspect that's unrealistically greedy. There's a chance it's strong enough to bother with in block though, right? The deck has fliers and detain to force them through. Someone, make this happen!
