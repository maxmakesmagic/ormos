Darwin Kastle is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame and one of the cornerstones of the famous team Your Move Games. In the summer of 2001, he added to his resume by debuting this monster of a deck to the world. His name for it? Animal Farm, after the novel by George Orwell. The name was a nod to the fact the deck played exactly two types of cards: creatures, and lands. In the history of the game, it's one of the few successful decks that eschewed all manner of other cards. It just goes to show why Kastle is respected as one of the greatest to ever play the game.
