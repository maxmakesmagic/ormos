Decks of the Week: 3/18/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on March 25, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Extended continues to heat up the premier-event field. Former World Champion Katsuhiro Mori steamrolled the 1,122-player field at Grand Prix–Yokohama with his Thopter/Depths deck in a Top 8 that featured six different archetypes. There are only three weekends left in the [Pro Tour–San Juan Qualifier season](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/sanjuan10-qualifiers). There are [three more **Magic Online** qualifiers](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/events/ptqmtgo/sanjuan10), so even if there's not a PTQ in your local area, fire up that Extended deck and take aim at the Pro Tour.
