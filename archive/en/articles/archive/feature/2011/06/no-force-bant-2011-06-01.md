The latest Legacy Grand Prix is in the books! Grand Prix Providence was a smashing success with over 1,000 players in attendance. Its champion? Legacy stalwart James Rynkiewicz, who took the title without a single [Force of Will](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will) in his deck, even though he was playing blue! His amazing performance forced him to go through Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa to earn the dubya. Read more in the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/legacy-champion-descends-jupiter)!
