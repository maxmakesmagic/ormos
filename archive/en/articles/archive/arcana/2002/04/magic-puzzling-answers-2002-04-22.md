Magic: The Puzzling answers
===========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 22, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Here are the answers to "Build Me Up, Buttercup," Mark's **Magic**: The Puzzling from last Thursday. You can go back and look at the puzzle [here](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=mtgcom/feature/35).

Here are the answers written out:

* [Willow Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Willow+Elf) + [Lightning Reflexes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Reflexes) = [Elvish Archers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Archers)
* [Merfolk of the Pearl Trident](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merfolk+of+the+Pearl+Trident) + [Armor of Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Armor+of+Faith) + [Flight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flight) + [Unholy Strength](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unholy+Strength) + [Enfeeblement](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enfeeblement) = [Tower Drake](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tower+Drake)
* [Mons's Goblin Raiders](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mons%27s+Goblin+Raiders) + [Divine Transformation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Divine+Transformation) + [Giant Strength](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Giant+Strength) = [Trained Orgg](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trained+Orgg)
* [Muck Rats](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Muck+Rats) + [Maniacal Rage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Maniacal+Rage) + [Sicken](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sicken) = [Spineless Thug](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spineless+Thug)
* [Eager Cadet](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eager+Cadet) + [Firebreathing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebreathing) + [Holy Strength](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Holy+Strength) = [Angelfire Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelfire+Crusader)
