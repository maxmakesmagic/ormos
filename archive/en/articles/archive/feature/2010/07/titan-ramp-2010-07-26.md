We've already brought you a few examples of **Magic** R&D members' take on green and red ramp decks featuring the Titans from ***Magic** 2011*. Now Japanese player Tamada Ryouichi brings us another version, which he used to win a recent tournament held in Japan. Rather than be content with simply casting Titans, however, Ryouichi uses [Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan) as a combo finisher. By accelerating out land drops using [Harrow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harrow), [Cultivate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cultivate), and [Khalni Heart Expedition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Khalni+Heart+Expedition), Tamada set up [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle) for lethal damage. By the time [Primeval Titan](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primeval+Titan) is tutoring up extra copies of the *Zendikar* land as well as free [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain)s, the opponent is already crispy.
