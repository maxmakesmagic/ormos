Your Arts Conspire
==================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 20, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_mikemcartor.jpg)
By Mike McArtor




 Mike first played with Ice Age and became the copy editor for DailyMTG.com in December 2011. Before DailyMTG, he was an editor on D&D's Dragon magazine for four years. 






Y*ou might know* about the exciting new *Conspiracy* set releasing on June 6, 2014.


![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/arcana/mjhshb4kov_arc_logo.jpg)

*You might know* there are 210 cards in total, 65 new and 145 reprints.
