======

 on August 11, 2008

By Wizards of the Coast

### Collector's Highlights


The *Exodus* expert expansion for **Magic: The Gathering** offers 143 new cards and concluded the *Rath Cycle* block.




### New Features


Collect the [answers to your rules questions](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/expert/Exodus/Exodus_FAQ.asp).


See the [changes to **Magic** cards](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/expert/Exodus/Exodus_Changes.asp), starting with Exodus.




### Story Line


Join the crew of the Weatherlight as we explore the last journey of the *Rath Cycle* in [The Exodus Storybook](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/expert/Exodus/Exodus_Backstory.asp).
