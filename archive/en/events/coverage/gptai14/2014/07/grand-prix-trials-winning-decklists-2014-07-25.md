Grand Prix Trials Winning Decklists
===================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX TAIPEI 2014 - COVERAGE - EVENTS](/en/events/coverage/gptai14)
 on July 25, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman_icon_0.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











 A total of ten *Magic 2015 Core Set* were held yesterday, rewarding all winners two round byes for the main event today. From the looks of it, the most prevalent rare seems to be [Hornet Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Queen) so I'd keep the bug spray (or [Festergloom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Festergloom)) within arm's reach!
