Even though [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) is gone from Standard, there are plenty of planeswalkers still looking for the right deck. Could [Tezzeret, Agent of Bolas](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret%2C+Agent+of+Bolas) finally have found the 60 he was looking for? Xu Qian certainly thinks so, playing this combo/control Tezzeret deck into the China National Championships via a Last Chance Qualifier. Read more about China Nationals [here](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%86%A0%E5%86%9B%E8%B5%9B2011%E7%8E%B0%E5%9C%BA%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8%E6%8A%A5%E5%AF%BC-2011-07-03).
