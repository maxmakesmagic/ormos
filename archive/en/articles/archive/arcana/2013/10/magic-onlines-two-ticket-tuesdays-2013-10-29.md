Magic Online's Two Ticket Tuesdays
==================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 29, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1352_m14logo.jpg)  

[Download **Magic Online**](https://accounts.onlinegaming.wizards.com/)



Starting in a week, on November 5, and continuing for each Tuesday of November, you can take part in "Two Ticket Tuesdays" on **Magic Online**. It's a great way to get some play in and experiment with **Magic Online** if you haven't used it before!


These events will be scheduled every thirty minutes on each Tuesday in November! (Editor's Note: Initially we announced these events would run every two hours, the **Magic Online** team changed their schedule.)


Here are the event details:



**Dates:** November 5, 12, 19, and 26   
**Entry:** 2 Event Tickets or 4 Phantom Tickets   
**Format:** 
***Magic** 2014* Phantom Sealed   
**Size:** Min: 32 players, Max: 32 players   
**Rounds:** 5 Rounds, single elimination


So it's a 2-ticket event, but what do you get for playing? Here are the prizes.


**Prizes:** All prize boosters are ***Magic** 2014*. 

* 1st: 6 boosters
* 2nd: 4 boosters
* 3rd-4th: 2 boosters
* 5th-8th: 1 booster
