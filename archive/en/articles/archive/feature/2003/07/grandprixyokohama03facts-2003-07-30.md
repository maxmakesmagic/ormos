### TOURNAMENT DETAILS

**Divisions**  
 The main tournament at Grand Prix - Yokohama is open to **Magic** players of all ages.

*If the player who wins an invitation to Pro Tour – New Orleans at Grand Prix – Yokohama has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation shall be extended to the next-best finisher. Previous qualification may occur through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid on the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).*

**Tournament Format**  
 The format for Grand Prix – Yokohama is *Onslaught* Block Constructed. All DCI Constructed rules and **Magic** Floor Rules announced on October 1, 2002, along with all subsequent updates, will be enforced.

For the latest information on Constructed formats, refer to the [2002 Floor Rules](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=dci/utr/intro).

**Tournament Structure**  
 The structure for Grand Prix – Yokohama is modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds, with the number of rounds determined by the number of participants). The Top 256 placing players on Day 1 will advance to Day 2, where they will play an additional 8 rounds of Swiss using the same decks. The Top 8 players from Day 2 will compete in 3 rounds of single-elimination on Day 3 in order to decide the Grand Prix – Yokohama Champion.

**Tournament Byes**
 *(Byes are not cumulative through the season or year.)*  
Byes for Grand Prix – Yokohama are based on the Pro Tour—Yokohama 2003 professional standings and DCI ratings as of June 18, 2003. Please refer to the Grand Prix Yokohama [Bye List](/en/node/736446) for details.
