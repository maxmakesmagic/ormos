Experiment Jund
===============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 8, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samstoddard.jpg)
By Sam Stoddard




Sam Stoddard came to Wizards of the Coast as an intern in May 2012. He is currently a game designer working on final design and development for Magic: The Gathering.
 







Jund is a Standard deck that saw a big boon from *Gatecrash*, and not just because it received its third shockland in [Stomping Ground](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stomping+Ground). Tyler Lindsey also found room for both the [Ghor-Clan Rampager](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghor-Clan+Rampager) and [Experiment One](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Experiment+One). The experiment may look somewhat unassuming, but when followed up by a [Strangleroot Geist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Strangleroot+Geist), then a [Flinthoof Boar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flinthoof+Boar) or [Dreg Mangler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dreg+Mangler), he is suddenly a 3/3 with the ability to regenerate. This deck has its foot on the gas from turn one. 

![Experiment One](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Experiment+One)
[Ghor-Clan Rampager](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghor-Clan+Rampager) is another important addition. Its creature mode is able to raise the Experiment up to a 4/4, but it's biggest boon is when its bloodrush ability is used. An opponent flashing in a [Restoration Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Restoration+Angel) or leaving back a [Loxodon Smiter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Loxodon+Smiter) used to be the hardest things for decks similar to this one to beat, but not anymore. Ghor-Clan, like the honey badger before him, don't care. Up and over is the Rampager's motto. Step in the way, get the horns. Block and... I think you get the point.
