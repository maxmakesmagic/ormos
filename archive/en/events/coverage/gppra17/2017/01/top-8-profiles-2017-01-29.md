Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 29, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











After 15 rounds, with a total of 1,997 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!




---






### Roope Metsa



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppra17/t8_metsa.jpg)



**Age:** 21




**Occupation:** Student




**Hometown:** Vantaa, Finland




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



RPTQ Top 8.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Aethersphere Harvester](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aethersphere+Harvester), black-green, 8-1.



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Fabrication Module](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fabrication+Module), blue-red, 3-0.



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Lifecraft Cavalry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lifecraft+Cavalry), red-green, 2-1.




---






### Fabian Friedrich



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppra17/t8_friedrich.jpg)



**Age:** 31




**Occupation:** Mechanical engineer




**Hometown:** Berlin, Germany




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Top 8 Grand Prix Paris 2016, Team Germany at the 2016 WMC, one Pro Tour.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Quicksmith Spy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quicksmith+Spy). Blue-black. 8-1, lost to double [Herald of Anguish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Herald+of+Anguish).



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



3-0. Two [Sram, Senior Edificer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sram%2C+Senior+Edificer) and [Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter), followed by [Baral's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baral%27s+Expertise).



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Heart of Kiran](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heart+of+Kiran).




---






### Yusuf Kemal Vefa



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppra17/t8_vefa.jpg)



**Age:** 43




**Occupation:** Entrepreneur




**Hometown:** Istanbul, Turkey




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Five times Turkish National Champion, 42nd at Pro Tour *Theros*, and lots of Pro Tour attendances.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Red-green, [Walking Ballista](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Walking+Ballista).



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



White-blue, [Baral's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baral%27s+Expertise).



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Green-blue, [Baral's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baral%27s+Expertise) again.

### Ivan Floch



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppra17/t8_floch.jpg)



**Age:** 31




**Occupation:** Professional tourist




**Hometown:** Bratislava, Slovakia




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Member of Slovak national team that won in Chiba in 2010!



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



[Baral's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baral%27s+Expertise). I was playing blue-red. 8-1.



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Blue-black, 3-0, [Battle at the Bridge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battle+at+the+Bridge).



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Red-black, 2-0-1, [Destructive Tampering](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Destructive+Tampering).

![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppra17/t8_campanino.jpg)



**Name:** Fabrizio Campanino




**Age:** 27




**Occupation:** Sales manager for Dal Tenda




**Hometown:** Naples, but I live in Perugia, Italy




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Playing in five Pro Tours.

[Aetherwind Basker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherwind+Basker), Red-Green Aggro, 7-2.

[Winding Constrictor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Winding+Constrictor), Black-Green Counters, 3-0.

[Gifted Aetherborn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifted+Aetherborn), Black-Green Aggro with eight removal spells, 3-0.

### Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa



![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppra17/t8_damodarosa.jpg)



**Age:** 29




**Occupation:** *Magic* player and writer at ChannelFireball.com




**Hometown:** Porto Alegre, Brazil




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Ten Pro Tour Top 8s!



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Blue-red, but boarded into blue-black every round. 8-1, [Aethertide Whale](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aethertide+Whale).



**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Green-white, 2-1, [Rishkar, Peema Renegade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rishkar%2C+Peema+Renegade).



**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**



Green-white, 3-0, [Oviya Pashiri, Sage Lifecrafter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oviya+Pashiri%2C+Sage+Lifecrafter)

![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gppra17/t8_larsen.jpg)



**Age:** 28




**Occupation:** Navy




**Hometown:** Copenhagen, Denmark




**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**



Less than Paulo. Playing with all the Martins and friends.



**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**

[Aethertide Whale](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aethertide+Whale) and [Metallic Rebuke](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metallic+Rebuke). Maybe just the Whale. It's unbeatable!

[Marionette Master](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Marionette+Master)—both of them!—or [Solemn Recruit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solemn+Recruit), the revolt double hit you guy.
