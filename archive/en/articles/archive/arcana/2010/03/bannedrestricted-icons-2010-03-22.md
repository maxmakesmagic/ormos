Banned/Restricted Icons
=======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 22, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Once upon a time, there was a magazine called Sideboard. It used to cover **Magic** tournament play and rules changes and the like. Here's a scan of part of a page from 1997.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/411_BannedList.jpg)  
That's the banned and restricted list, and we'd like to draw your attention to the amusing clip-art icon. For eight issues in a row, there was a new illustration there for each issue's list of banned and restricted cards. And here they are!
