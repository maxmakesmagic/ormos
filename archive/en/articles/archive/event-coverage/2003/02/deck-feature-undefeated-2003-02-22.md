Deck Feature - The Undefeated
=============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 22, 2003 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff











The tall. The proud. Some say the ludicrously fortunate. These are the two people who have managed to escape the grinding nine rounds of Swiss without a single match loss. Divided between the two of them, all the colours are present with the sole exception of blue (which is to be expected in this format.) All the cards the players had available to them have been listed here. As one classic **Magic** website once said, “Study and grow strong.” (Of interest to note, both undefeated players have a Dream Chisel in their sideboard. One can only imagine the implications…)







#### Ben Rubin (9-0; 3 Byes)
