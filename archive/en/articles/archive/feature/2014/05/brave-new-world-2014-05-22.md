A Brave New World
=================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 22, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 







When *Journey into Nyx* was released, [Mana Confluence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Confluence) was hailed as the missing ingredient for multicolored aggressive decks. Now that we have a few weeks of tournaments, it would appear that [Mana Confluence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Confluence) has indeed given a boost to these multicolored aggressive decks! Jamie Arnold took his Naya Aggro deck to a finals appearance at [StarCityGames Knoxville](http://www.starcitygames.com/events/100514_knoxville.html). 


![Brave the Elements](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Brave+the+Elements)
 This is a base-white deck, partially to take advantage of white's strong creature suite, but mostly to take advantage of [Brave the Elements](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brave+the+Elements). Multicolored cards, such as [Fleecemane Lion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleecemane+Lion) and [Voice of Resurgence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence), are potent creatures, but even tougher to handle when they are backed up by a variety of support spells. [Ghor-Clan Rampager](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghor-Clan+Rampager) helps boost any attacking creature over a blocker, where [Boros Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Charm) is often used to save your creatures from a card like [Supreme Verdict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Supreme+Verdict). However, the most potent combo is when [Ghor-Clan Rampager](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghor-Clan+Rampager) and [Boros Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Charm) are combined for what is usually a lethal attack. 


 While maintaining an aggressive curve, this deck has a lot of tricks up its sleeve and lots of options. Both [Selesnya Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Selesnya+Charm) and [Boros Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Charm) provide many different options, and many of the deck's creatures are hard to remove.
