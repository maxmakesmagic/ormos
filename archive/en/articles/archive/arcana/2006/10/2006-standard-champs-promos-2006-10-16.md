2006 Standard Champs Promos
===========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 16, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/champs06.jpg)You haven’t been tuning in to the **magicthegathering.com** podcast, have you? Shame on you! If you had, you’d already know about the 2006 Standard Champs promo cards. You’d already know that the card you get for just participating in **2006 Standard Champs, October 21 or 28** (check the [schedule](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/magic/champs) for Champs in your area), is:


  
2006 Standard Champs Participation Promo Card



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2006/champs_factory300.jpg)
And you’d already know that the Top 8 prize card for **2006 Standard Champs, October 21 or 28**, is:


2006 Standard Champs Top 8 Promo Card



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2006/champs_serra300.jpg)
We’re bailing you out this time, but be sure to tune in to that podcast. And start testing that *Time Spiral* Standard deck now, because [Champs](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/magic/champs) is just around the corner!
