2009 Grand Prix Chicago: Top 8 Profiles
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 7, 2009 













![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//mtg/images/daily/events/gpchi09/Top8_Nassif.jpg)

**Name:** Gabriel Nassif  
**Age:** 25  
**Hometown:** Paris  
**Occupation:** Magic/Poker Player


**How many times have you played on the Pro Tour/Grand Prix Day 2?**   

 About 45 / about 20


**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 6.7 / 9


**Other previous Magic accomplishments:** a bunch


**What was your record?**  

 Day 1: 7-1-1  

 Day 2: 5-0-1


**How did you prepare for Grand Prix-Chicago?**  

 Played a bunch of games at R.I.W. Hobbies in Michigan with Chapin and Bucher.


**What deck are you playing and why?**  

 I’m playing Counterbalance/Top because I thought it was the best strategy. I got the decklist from Luis Scott-Vargas the night before (thanks Luis).

 Name The Vintage Event
