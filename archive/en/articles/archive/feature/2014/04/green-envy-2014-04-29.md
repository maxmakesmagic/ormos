Green with Envy
===============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 29, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 







Back at [Pro Tour *Theros*](/en/events/coverage/dezani-proclaims-allez-les-bleus-pro-tour), Makihito Mihara unleashed an explosive deck onto the rest of the Pro Tour, resulting in a semifinal appearance. Afterwards, Green Devotion has taken a backseat to both Blue Devotion and Black Devotion, but **Magic Online** user Gray Eyed Wanderer recently had success with Green Devotion with a 2nd-place finish in a **Magic Online** Premier Event. 

![Nylea, God of the Hunt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Nylea%2C+God+of+the+Hunt) One of the most powerful devotion cards is [Nykthos, Shrine to Nyx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nykthos%2C+Shrine+to+Nyx), which allows for truly explosive turns fueled by large amounts of mana. Green Devotion decks take advantage of this more than any other color. The primary reason for this involves [Voyaging Satyr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyaging+Satyr) allowing for multiple uses of the legendary land, but green decks feature enough mana acceleration to get permanents in play faster than other decks. 


*Born of the Gods* gave us [Courser of Kruphix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courser+of+Kruphix), which generates some extra card advantage while providing some additional synergy with [Domri Rade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domri+Rade). *Journey into Nyx* provides [Hydra Broodmaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hydra+Broodmaster), which is capable of generating an incredible amount of creatures. If you've ever activated [Polukranos, World Eater](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polukranos%2C+World+Eater)'s monstrous effect for X=17, then I think Green Devotion might be the deck for you.
