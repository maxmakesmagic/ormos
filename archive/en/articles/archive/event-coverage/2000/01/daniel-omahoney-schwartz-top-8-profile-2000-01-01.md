Daniel O'Mahoney-Schwartz Top 8 Profile
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











**by Sideboard Staff**


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPAMS01/906.jpg)**Name:** Daniel O'Mahoney-Schwartz   
**Age:** 21  
**Location:** Brooklyn, NY USA  
**Occupation:** University Student  
**Played Magic since:** 1994  
**Best accomplishments:**Team PT top 4, lots of GP top 4's.



**Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Amsterdam?**  

A: GP4L!



**Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?**  

A: An excellent shot at Top 8 if I received a decent sealed deck and made day 2.



**Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.**  

A: Tight as hell both day one and day two.



**Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?**  

A: Saying I am "tight" would be an understatement.



**Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?**  

A: I am indeed the better brother.



**Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

A: Jon Finkel will win-- oh wait, he didn't make day 2.



**Q: Anything else you'd like to share with us?**  

A: I game to live, I live to game.
