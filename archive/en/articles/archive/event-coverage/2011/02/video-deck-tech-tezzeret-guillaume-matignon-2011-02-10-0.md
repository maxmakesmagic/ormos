Video Deck Tech: Tezzeret with Guillaume Matignon
=================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 9, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 





2010 *Magic* World Champion Guillaume Matignon discusses his red-blue-black control deck featuring [Tezzeret, Agent of Bolas](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret%2C+Agent+of+Bolas). This is the deck Matignon will use to battle Brad Nelson in the 2010 Player of the Year Showdown on Saturday.
