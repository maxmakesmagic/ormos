### San Diego Convention Center
June 29-July 1, 2007



San Diego, California is the third stop in the 2007 Pro Tour season. But not only the Pros will be enjoying themselves: the Pro Tour is above all a great weekend of **Magic**  that brings you fantastic opportunities.


Pro Tour–San Diego is a Two-Headed Giant *Time Spiral*  Booster Draft event that runs June 29-July 1, 2007. The Last-Chance Qualifier will be run on Thursday, June 28 at the event site, starting at 5 p.m. Registration for the LCQ begins at 3 p.m.

| Pro Tour Registration and Party
**Date:** June 28, 2007**Time:** 5 p.m. - 8 p.m.**Location:**  San Diego Convention Center Hall E
 111 West Harbor Drive
 San Diego, CA 92101[www.sdccc.org](http://www.sdccc.org)
Players must have a signed waiver and consent form to compete at Pro Tour-San Diego. Forms can be downloaded at [www.wizards.com/protour](/en/articles/archive/pro-tour%E2%80%93amsterdam-artists-2010-08-26) and will be available onsite. Minors must have a parent or guardian’s signature on these forms. Minors will not be able to register or play unless we receive a waiver signed by a parent or guardian. No exceptions!
Bring these forms with you to registration. We will make forms available for competitors at registration, if necessary.
To register, all participants must present one of the following forms of identification:* a driver’s license, or
* a passport, or
* a copy of a birth certificate and photo IDs (school IDs are acceptable).

We encourage players to attend mandatory registration on Thursday. However we understand that delays might cause players to miss this and we have a registration on Friday morning between 7:30 a.m. and 8:30 a.m. Players at late registration do not always receive the same gifts as those at mandatory registration.
Players not registered by 8:30 a.m. on June 29 may be denied entry to the Pro Tour.
If you anticipate any difficulty registering during the official registration hours, please contact please contact Scott Larabee at (206) 793-3534 or [scott.larabee@wizards.com](mailto:scott.larabee@wizards.com). |

### Tournament Format and Schedule


Pro Tour–San Diego is a three-day Two-Headed Giant Booster Draft format event with a prize purse of $240,500. For more information about the format, schedule and prize payout, [click here](/en/articles/archive/pro-tour%E2%80%93san-diego-2007-format-and-schedule-2007-02-05).


### Site Attractions


**SIDE EVENTS**  

Play **Magic** all weekend long, even if you're not qualified for the Pro Tour! Information on all the events being held at Pro Tour-San Diego can be found in the [Side Event Schedule](/en/articles/archive/2007-pro-tour-san-diego-side-events-schedule-2007-05-11).


**ARTISTS ON SITE**  

Pro Tour–San Diego 2007 will feature appearances by Magic artists Lars Grant-West and Alan Pollack! [Click here](/en/articles/archive/pro-tour%E2%80%93san-diego-2007-artists-2007-05-18) to read more about these artists.


**WATCH THE TOP 8**  

All of Sunday's Top 8 action can be viewed on a projection screen in the hall, with Randy Buehler and Brian David-Marshall providing the play-by-play and color commentary. Sunday's action begins at 2 p.m. local time, which is 5 p.m. ET, and is available as a [webcast](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgevent/webcast/home) from the comfort of your own home.
