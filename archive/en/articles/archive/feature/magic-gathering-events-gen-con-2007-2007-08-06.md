Magic: The Gathering Events at Gen Con 2007
===========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 6, 2007 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/vintage_legacy.jpg)Going to [Gen Con](http://www.gencon.com/) with a **Magic** itch to scratch? Take a look through the full slate of **Magic**  events -- and prizes -- and find something that suits your interests. From the Vintage and Legacy Championships to Pro Tour Qualifiers to the Grand Melee to leagues and drafts, there's something for everyone.


### All Weekend


- **Magic: The Gathering Gen Con League** – Each participant receives a booster and league badge. Play against other league members all weekend – win promotional cards and boosters! Only $4.50

- **Booster Drafts** –*Time Spiral* Block or *Tenth Edition* Booster Draft sign-ups never end!!

### Thursday


**5 p.m.: Magic: The Gathering Legacy Championship Prelim**  
 Swiss rounds based on attendance plus single-elimination Top 8. Winner receives a 2-round bye at the 2007 Legacy Championship plus booster prizes.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/vintage_moxjet.jpg)*Win the Vintage Championship and you'll have the biggest Mox on the block.***6 p.m.: Magic: The Gathering Time Spiral Block Constructed Championship**  
 Players will play a number of Swiss rounds based on attendance, with a cut to a Top 8. First prize is a foil playset of the entire *Time Spiral* Block! (That's 4 *Time Spiral*, 4 *Planar Chaos* & 4 *Future Sight* complete foil sets, including Timeshifted cards!) Additional prizes to the Top 8 finishers!


**9 p.m.: Magic: The Gathering Two-Headed Giant Standard iPod Tournament**  
 Swiss rounds based on attendance. Each winning head takes home its own iPod!





### Friday


**10 a.m.: Magic: The Gathering Legacy Championship**  
 Swiss rounds based on attendance plus single-elimination Top 8. First and second place each choose two oversized cards from a pool of ten Legacy oversized cards. Third – Eighth places each chooses one oversized card from the remaining pool. Top finishers also win Vintage boosters (Italian *Legends*, Italian black-bordered **Revised**, Japanese *Portal Three Kingdoms*). For more information, check out the [Legacy Championship Fact Sheet](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/magic/legacychamps)
**4 p.m.: Magic: The Gathering iPod Special Limited Tournament**  
 Win an iPod! Players receive one booster of *Time Spiral*, *Planar Chaos*, *Future Sight*, *Ravnica*, *Guildpact*, *Dissension*, *Tenth Edition* and *Coldsnap*. Players compete in Swiss rounds based on attendance with a cut to Top 8. First place wins an iPod; additional prizes to Top 8!


**5 p.m.: Magic: The Gathering Vintage Championship Prelim**  
 Swiss rounds based on attendance plus single-elimination Top 8. Winner receives a 2-round bye at the 2007 Vintage Championship.


[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/ptqdecks_valencia.jpg)](/en/events/coverage/pro-tour%E2%80%93valencia-qualifying-season-top-8-decklists)**6 p.m.:**
**Magic: The Gathering Valencia Pro Tour Qualifier**  
 Players compete playing with *Time Spiral* Block Constructed decks. Winner receives an invitation to Pro Tour–Valencia and a travel reward. Swiss rounds based on attendance plus single-elimination Top 8.


**9 p.m.: Magic: The Gathering Limited $1K Tournament**  
 $1,000 in prizes, plus product prizes! Players receive one *Time Spiral* Tournament Pack plus one booster each of *Planar Chaos* and *Future Sight* to build a 40-card minimum deck. Players will play a number of Swiss rounds based on attendance with a cut to Top 8. First Prize $500, second $250, third and fourth $125, fifth-eighth receive product prizes!





### Saturday


**10 a.m.: Magic: The Gathering Valencia Pro Tour Qualifier**  
 Players compete playing with *Time Spiral* Block Constructed decks. Winner receives an invitation to Pro Tour–Valencia and a travel reward. Swiss rounds based on attendance plus single-elimination Top 8.
 **11 a.m.: Magic: The Gathering Vintage Championship**  
 Swiss rounds based on attendance plus single-elimination Top 8. The winner receives an alternative art framed Mox Jet trophy! Top finishers will receive boosters, including Vintage/Legacy boosters (Italian *Legends*, Italian black-bordered **Revised**, Japanese *Portal Three Kingdoms*). For more information, check out the [Vintage Championship Fact Sheet](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/magic/vintagechamps). 


**6 p.m.: Magic: The Gathering Grand Melee**  
 Standard format, Grand Melee style! *Attack to your left, defend on your right*. Last one standing wins huge! All players receive a special, high quality playmat for participating. Eliminate another player - win a booster! First Place receives a complete *Tenth Edition* set – all FOIL!! Second through eighth places receive a box of *Tenth Edition*. For more information on the Grand Melee format, check out [Section 608 in the Comprehensive Rules](http://www.wizards.com/magic/comprules/MagicCompRules070713.doc).


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2007/melee_playmat.jpg)*Every Melee battler gets his or her very own playmat.***10 p.m.: Magic: The Gathering Extended $1K Tournament**  
 $1,000 in prizes, plus product prizes! Players will play a number of Swiss rounds based on attendance with a cut to Top 8. First Prize $500, second $250, third and fourth $125, fifth-eighth receive product prizes!





### Sunday


**9 a.m.: Magic: The Gathering *Urza's Saga*  Limited**  
 Players receive one *Urza's Saga* Tournament Pack plus one booster each of *Urza's Legacy* and *Urza's Destiny* to build a 40-card minimum deck. Players participate in four Swiss rounds. Prizes based on record, no finals.
