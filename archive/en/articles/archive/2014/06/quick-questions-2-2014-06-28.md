What's the best card in your deck?






![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpmil14/QQMengucci.jpg)

***Andrea Mengucci:** – [Doomwake Giant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomwake+Giant). I'm playing a deck with 10 enchantments and [Kruphix's Insight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kruphix%27s+Insight) in it!* 




![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpmil14/QQLippi.jpg)

***Alessandro Lippi:** – [Fleecemane Lion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleecemane+Lion). Many decks lack fast removal and once it's monstrous it's unkillable.* 








![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpmil14/QQRutar.jpg)

***Andrej Rutar:** – [Hypnotic Siren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypnotic+Siren). Stealing your opponent's creatures is so good.*
