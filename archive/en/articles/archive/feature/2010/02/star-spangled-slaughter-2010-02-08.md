It's Presidents Day in the U.S., and we're celebrating with a red-white-and-blue special: Star Spangled Slaughter. Such decks have seen play off and on throughout the game's history (witness [All-American Control](/en/articles/archive/red-white-blue-control-2010-01-15) from Luis Scott-Vargas this Standard season). Splicing together the best blue, red, and white had to offer, Peter Jesuale used this deck in the 2002 U.S. Nationals Standard event. [Flametongue Kavu](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flametongue+Kavu), [Lightning Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Angel), and [Meddling Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) are a powerful triumvirate of creatures, with [Goblin Trenches](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Trenches) bringing up the rear just in case. Add a smatter of counters and burn, and voila! Instant classic.
