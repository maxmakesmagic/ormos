2014 Magic Online Championship in Seattle
=========================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on February 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_davidwhitfield.jpg)
By David Whitfield




 David works on the Magic community team as a content specialist. He spends his days writing about Magic Online and trying to play too many colors at once in Limited. 






**Seattle  

May 15–17, 2015**


The *Magic Online* Championship features sixteen players battling for a share of more than $100,000 in cash prizes. Viewers can tune into [Twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic) to watch a live broadcast of the event.


The sixteen 2014 *Magic Online* Championship finalists include:



* Twelve individual *Magic Online* Championship Series season winners
* One MOCS Player-of-the-Year Championship winner
* Two MOCS Last-Chance Qualifier winners
* The defending 2013 *Magic Online* champion, Lars Dam


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/mtgo/winner_photo.jpg)


Think you have the skills to make it to next year's 2015 *Magic Online* Championship? The 2015 *Magic Online* Championship Series is under way now. Visit [this page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/2015-magic-online-championship-series) for more information and a full schedule of season championships.

**Media Requests**


Please send an email to [press@wizards.com](mailto:press@wizards.com) if you wish to set up a media credential for the *Magic Online* Championship. The *Magic Online* Championship is not open to the public.

Player Information
==================


**Format and Tournament Length**


**Friday**



* Three rounds of Vintage
* Four rounds of Modern


**Saturday**



* Three rounds of DTK/DTK/FRF Draft
* Four rounds of Standard, cut to the Top 4


**Sunday**


Semifinals are Standard.


Finals are best of three matches.



* First Match: Vintage
* Second Match: Modern
* Third Match (if necessary): Standard

In addition to cash prizes, the winner of the *Magic Online* Championship is immediately advanced to Gold level for the remainder of the current season and the entirety of the following season (as if that player had reached the minimum number of Professional Points at that *Magic Online* Championship to be promoted to Gold level). The winner will also be invited back to next year's *Magic Online* Championship to defend the title!


All prizes in U.S. Dollars. Additional prizes include one complete Standard-legal foil premium digital set and one complete Standard-legal regular digital set for first and second place, and one complete Standard-legal regular digital set for all other participants. Each player may select the set(s) they receive. First place also earns an invitation to the 2015 *Magic* World Championship. Prizes subject to change.
