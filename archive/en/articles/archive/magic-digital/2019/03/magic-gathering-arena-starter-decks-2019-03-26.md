Magic: The Gathering Arena Starter Decks
========================================



 Posted in **Magic Digital**
 on March 26, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











In *MTG Arena*, our dear friend Sparky introduces you to the game and provides you with some sweet starter decks. We've had some players ask for official decklists, and with update 0.13 coming out next week containing a revamped onboarding experience, the timing couldn't be more perfect!


Monocolor Decks
===============


Note: The white deck, "Tactical Assault," contains cards unique to *MTG Arena*. These cards (shown below) are legal for Standard only within *MTG Arena*.
