From perches on high, the Angels of Bant rule over all beneath them. That's exactly what DCI judge Ingrid Lind-Jahn was trying to do with her Angel deck for multiplayer games. Originally presented on Starcitygames.com, Ingrid tried to pack every Angel she could find into the deck, ranging from the small ([Angelic Page](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Angelic+Page), technically an Angel Spirit) to the very top of the heap ([Seraph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seraph)). So, what does the newest set for **Magic: The Gathering** have in store for deck's like Ingrid's and the Angel tribe? Find out on the *Shards of Alara* [product page](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/shardsofalara).
