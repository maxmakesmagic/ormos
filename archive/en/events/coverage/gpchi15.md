GRAND PRIX CHIBA 2015
=====================




The 379 players that advanced into Day Two of Grand Prix Chiba's main event have arrived and begun their first of two booster drafts today. Tune in NOW for live coverage of Sunday here in Chiba, Japan!


[LIVE VIDEO BROADCAST SCHEDULE](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/organized-play/modern-masters-weekend-approaching-2015-05-20)

DAY 2 COVERAGE




 Grand Prix Chiba's Sunday action has begun, and while Makuhari Messa's event hall is filled with Magic players from all over the globe, 379 of those players are here for Day Two of the Grand Prix Chiba main event. The format for today's main event is *Modern Masters 2015 Edition* Booster Draft, where main event competitors will participate in two booster drafts before a cut to Top 8.



Who will be Chiba's modern master? Tune in for live coverage of the main event to find out!

 
  
  



DAY 1 COVERAGE




 The last of the players in Makuhari Messe are headed out for the evening, many intent on returning tomorrow to continue living *Magic* this weekend.



The final tally of main event participants today at Grand Prix Chiba? 3,551.



 The number of players returning for more *Modern Masters 2015 Edition* main event action tomorrow? 380.



Check back tomorrow at this very page to see more stories of greatness from Grand Prix Chiba. For now, however, you have Utrecht and Las Vegas to look forward to as Modern Masters Weekend continues into full gear!
