Love playing creatures? This is definitely the Standard deck for you! Wiks managed to make the Top 8 of a recent **Magic Online** Premier Event with this green-white list. Every card in it was either a land or a creature save for just eight cards. [Path to Exile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Path+to+Exile) keeps potential blockers out of the way, while [Shield of the Oversoul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shield+of+the+Oversoul) makes threats into impervious monsters! This take on Green-White is one that knows how to get into the red zone.
