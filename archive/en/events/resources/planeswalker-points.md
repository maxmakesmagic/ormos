PLANESWALKER POINTS
===================





















What are Planeswalker Points? They’re a system that rewards players like you for competing in sanctioned Magic events. You can rack up rankings, badges, and unique achievements, all for doing what you already love—playing Magic! Rally your friends, defeat your foes, and climb to the top—let Planeswalker Points help you get there!


[VISIT THE PLANESWALKER POINTS PAGE](http://www.wizards.com/magic/planeswalkerpoints) 
















### EARN ACHIEVEMENTS




With Planeswalker Points, you’ll amass badges and achievements for competing and contributing to the Magic community. Proudly display your badges and get the recognition you have earned!











### EARN BYES




Accumulate enough Planeswalker Points and you’ll earn Grand Prix byes, which are good for a first-, second-, or third-round bye at every Grand Prix throughout the season. They’re the perfect way to gain some ground at these incredibly competitive events!











### REPRESENT YOUR COUNTRY




Earn an opportunity to play in invitation-only World Magic Cup qualifiers— your chance to represent your country against the best the world has to offer. This is your chance to make Magic history for yourself and your country!
