The 8-0 Decklists of Grand Prix Prague 2018
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 26, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











Players were partying like it's 1999, and in total they were, that is, 1,999. This relatively high turnout, combined with the many players on three byes, gave us a whopping *thirteen* players who finished the first day of competition with perfect records of 8-0.


They didn't all fit into one photo, or rather one showed up late and another left early, so here are two:


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gppra18/8-0s1.jpg)  
*(9) Javier Dominguez, Bastian Lange, Troels Munk, Ondřej Stráský (front row, left to right), Louis Deltour, Alex Roebuck, Silvan Kruijer, Riccardo Picciafuochi, Florian Kirchbacher, Lauri Pispa, (10) Grzegorz Kowalski, and Michal Szulborski (back)*


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gppra18/8-0s2.jpg)  
*Troels Munk, Andreas Ehrhardt, Alex Roebuck (front row), Louis Deltour, (9) Javier Dominguez, Lauri Pispa, Riccardo Picciafuochi, Silvan Kruijer, (10) Grzegorz Kowalski, Bastian Lange, Ondřej Stráský, and Michal Szulborski (back)*


Now you've seen a lot of faces, but you've come for decklists, right? Well, here they are. Three Spirits, three white/blue-based control decks, two Burn, and one each of Moon Breach, Tron, Bridge Vine, Hardened Scales, and Ironworks. Enjoy!
