Grixis Control is a popular anti-Jund strategy in standard, but The\_Apologist is looking to make Aggro Grixis even more popular! The **Magic Online** user took this list to the Top 8 of a recent Premier Event. With creatures like [Sedraxis Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sedraxis+Specter) and [Goblin Ruinblaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ruinblaster) disrupting the opponent while [Bloodghast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodghast) proves impossible to kill, The\_Apologist stalls the game. From there, the Grixis hordes take over as powerful bombs like [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander) hit the battlefield. You don't want to be on the wrong end of that crew!
