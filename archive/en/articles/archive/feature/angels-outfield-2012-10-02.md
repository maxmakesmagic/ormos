Angels in the Outfield
======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on October 2, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_conleywoods.jpg)
By Conley Woods











So, with the release of *Avacyn Restored*, **Magic** gained access to miracles. The cards came across as unique and completely different, but also very powerful. Immediately following the release of *Avacyn Restored*, the [set's Pro Tour](/en/events/coverage/angels-carry-hayne-miraculous-victory) was won by a White-Blue Miracle deck. From there, the mechanic took a turn to Legacy, where [Brainstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm) allows it to be manipulated more. We have not seen all that much from miracles in Standard, though. Sundayss decided to change that on **Magic Online**.


![Entreat the Angels](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Entreat+the+Angels)Sundayss sleeved up a Bant Miracles deck. Similar to the UW Miracles decks we have seen before, the deck uses the likes of [Entreat the Angels](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Entreat+the+Angels) and [Temporal Mastery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temporal+Mastery) to gain a huge edge at various points in the game. [Terminus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terminus), and in this case [Day of Judgment](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Day+of+Judgment), buy the deck plenty of time while it looks to set up a big [Entreat the Angels](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Entreat+the+Angels). If you want more removal, [Feeling of Dread](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Feeling+of+Dread) and [Oblivion Ring](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Ring) make for nice complements.


Sundayss was not too keen on relying solely on miracles to win the game, though. In this list, [Tamiyo, the Moon Sage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tamiyo%2C+the+Moon+Sage) and [Gideon Jura](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gideon+Jura) act as a little bit of extra Planeswalker protection. [Thragtusk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thragtusk) fills out the win conditions with a solid finisher that also boosts your life total against the aggro decks, providing the deck with even more time. At any point in time, [Entreat the Angels](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Entreat+the+Angels) can spring off the top of your deck and just end a game, which makes for exciting game play if nothing else!
