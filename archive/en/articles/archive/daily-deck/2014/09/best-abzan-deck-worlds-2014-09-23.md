The Best Abzan Deck in the Worlds
=================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on September 23, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 






[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Doran,%20the%20Siege%20Tower)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran,%20the%20Siege%20Tower)
*Khans of Tarkir* is coming to Standard! Three-color wedges are about to make their way into Standard, but by no means will they be new to tournament *Magic*. This week, we will look at Pro Tour–winning decks that have been based in one of the wedges from *Khans of Tarkir*.



Today's deck belongs to none other than [2007 World Champion](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/worlds07/welcome), Uri Peleg. His Abzan deck took advantage of [Doran, the Siege Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower), which is one of the few tournament-caliber wedge cards that existed prior to *Khans of Tarkir*. To support Doran, Uri played accelerants in [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) and [Llanowar Elves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves). After that, Uri let his fake 5/5 do the majority of the work.


You might see some familiar faces in this deck, as many of its cards are currently Standard legal! [Llanowar Elves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) has gotten a facelift and become [Elvish Mystic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Mystic), while [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize); [Liliana Vess](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+Vess); and [Urborg, Tomb of Yawgmoth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urborg%2C+Tomb+of+Yawgmoth) are cards that are again Standard legal. When building your Abzan decks, perhaps you can learn a thing or two from a World Champion!
