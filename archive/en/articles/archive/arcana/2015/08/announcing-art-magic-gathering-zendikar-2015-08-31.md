Announcing The Art of Magic: The Gathering—Zendikar
===================================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 31, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*As long as we have walked this world, we have known it as a harsh and unforgiving place. We live in an angry world, and we are well acquainted with death.*


*—The Invokers' Tales*


Danger and adventure await in these pages, lavishly illustrated with the award-winning art of *Magic: The Gathering*! Explore a tumultuous world fraught with perils and join the fight for survival as the fragments of civilization confront the unnatural Eldrazi. Follow in the footsteps of heroic Planeswalkers as they overcome the greatest threats the Multiverse has ever known.


The secrets of Zendikar—its peoples, continents, and creatures—are ready for you to discover in this 244-page book featuring the art of *Magic: The Gathering.*


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/MTG-3DCover-Front.png)


**Title**: *The Art of Magic: The Gathering—Zendikar*


**Written by**: James Wyatt


**Cover Art**: Adam Paquette


**Release Date**: January 5, 2016


**Specifications**: Hardcover 10" x 11.25"; 244 pages; full color


**MSRP**: $39.99 ($45.00 CAN; £28.00 UK)


**Published by**:  Perfect Square, a division of Viz Media


**Officially licensed by**: Wizards of the Coast


**Availability**: Bookstores around the world


**Language**: English Only
