[Battlegrace Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlegrace+Angel) has turned many a head after being printed in *Shards of Alara*. John Wickham used the Angel to make good at the Maine State Championships, finishing in 6th place with this black-green-white Doran deck. Running only 17 lands, he was able to cheat by playing [Safewright Quest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Safewright+Quest) and mana Elves. That allowed him to still play many expensive cards right on time, cards like [Chameleon Colossus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chameleon+Colossus) or the aforementioned [Battlegrace Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlegrace+Angel). His end game was also shored up by [Elspeth, Knight-Errant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant) and a host of other fatties.
