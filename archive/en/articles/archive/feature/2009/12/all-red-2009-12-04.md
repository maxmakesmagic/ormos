A popular strategy during last Extended season, All-In Red fell out of favor during Pro Tour-Austin. It made a solid resurgence during the Extended day of competition at Worlds in Rome, however. Italy's own Mario Pascoli used the deck for a respectable finish, ritualing out powerful first-turn plays like [Magus of the Moon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magus+of+the+Moon) or even [Deus of Calamity](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deus+of+Calamity). For more information on the 2009 World Championships, [visit the official event coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12).
