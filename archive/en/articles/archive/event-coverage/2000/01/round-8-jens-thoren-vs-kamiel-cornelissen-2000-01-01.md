Round 8: Jens Thoren vs. Kamiel Cornelissen
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptsd02/a674.jpg)*Jens Thoren vs. Kamiel Cornelissen*### Game 1


Thoren two-dropped a [Frightcrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler) to start off the first game, and Kamiel went one better, as he summoned [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). Lithatog joined Cornelissen's side on the following turn. Thoren brought out [Dirty Wererat](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dirty+Wererat), and had only black sources of mana on the table, although two of them were [Bog Wreckages](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bog+Wreckages). The Mongrel came through for two more points, Chatter brought forth a squirrel, and Cornelissen used the rest of his mana to bring out [Krosan Avenger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Avenger). The [Frightcrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler) continued to beat for one a turn, and Thoren finally played a white source of mana. [Painbringer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Painbringer) hit the table, and one mana was left untapped to allow the Wererat to hold down the fort.


Cornelissen thought for a bit, then summoned a [Rabid Elephant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rabid+Elephant), and ended his turn. [Patriarch's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Patriarch%27s+Desire) killed off the [Lithatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lithatog), and the [Frightcrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler) came through for another point of damage. [Rabid Elephant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rabid+Elephant) then became [Disrupting Scepter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disrupting+Scepter), as it attacked and was blocked by the Wererat, which regenerated after Thoren discarded a land. On end step, [Painbringer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Painbringer) took down the Avenger. Thoren got a little more aggressive as he sent the Wererat and the [Frightcrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler) into combat, and played a second Wererat. 


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptsd02/a673.jpg)*Kamiel Cornelissen*[Rabid Elephant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rabid+Elephant) and the Mongrel attacked. The Wererat blocked the Mongrel, and Cornelissen discarded a card, forcing the Wererat to regenerate or die, and saving his Mongrel. Thoren discarded two cards, and removed the four cards in his library from the game to take down the Elephant. The [Frightcrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler) dealt another point of damage, and [Aven Cloudchaser](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Cloudchaser) hit the table. This left Thoren tapped out and unable to regenerate his Wererats, but this was not too risky since Cornelissen only had Werebear, Mongrel and a squirrel on his side.


 He discarded three lands, which allowed the Mongrel to take down a Wererat and the Cloudchaser. Chatter was flashed back, and Cornelissen only had one card in hand, and a board consisting of two squirrels and a 4/4 Werebear. Thoren brought out [Whispering Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whispering+Shade). The next attack saw it take down a Werebear, while the [Frightcrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler) continued on its merciless rampage of a point of damage each turn. The Glacier drew another Chatter, bringing his collection of Squirrels to four. One squirrel took down a [Crypt Creeper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Creeper) in combat, and Cornelissen brought out another Mongrel.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptsd02/a672.jpg)*Jens Thoren*With a regenerator and four cards in the graveyard to feed [Painbringer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Painbringer), Thoren was in a good position. He attacked with [Frightcrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler) and the Wererat. [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) turned black and blocked [Frightcrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frightcrawler), while Werebear blocked the Wererat. Thoren set up regeneration shields until he had threshold, allowing him to come out on top in combat. Looking at a poor board position, Cornelissen conceded.


Thoren- 1 Cornelissen- 0


Both players were deckchecked before Game 2, and it was discovered that Cornelissen had a marked [Barbarian Lunatic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barbarian+Lunatic) in his deck. The penalty was a match loss.


**Final Result:** Thoren- 2 Cornelissen-0
