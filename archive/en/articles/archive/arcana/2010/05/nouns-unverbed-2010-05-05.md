Nouns Unverbed
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 5, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











You're a hip, plugged-in member of the **Magic** community. You probably noticed the similarities between [Realms Uncharted](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Realms+Uncharted) and [Gifts Ungiven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gifts+Ungiven) right away.



|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| Gifts Ungiven | Realms Uncharted |

You probably also know about the 2007 holiday promo card Gifts Given.

![Gifts Given](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/443_giftsgiven.jpg)
