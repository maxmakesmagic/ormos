On the Mirrodin Site: October 30-November 5
===========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 5, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Welcome back to "On the Mirrodin Site," where we show off some of the recent highlights from the [Mirrodin Site](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/).


Now that that's out of the way, let's get down to it.


On the Phyrexia side, Unctus of the Synod [has a new Field Report](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/#!blog,626). There are also keep wallpapers for [Skithyryx, the Blight Dragon](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/#swamps,hotspots;B10;0;expanded) and [Pierce Strider](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/#plains,hotspots;W6;0;).


The Mirrans, meanwhile, have wallpapers for [Rusted Relic](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/#plains,hotspots;W11;0;) and [Peace Strider](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/#plains,hotspots;W6;0;). And there's also a wallpaper for something called [Goblin Wardriver](http://www.wizards.com/magic/mirrodin/#mountains,hotspots;R9;0;). Which is weird, because there doesn't appear to be a card by that name.


...yet.
