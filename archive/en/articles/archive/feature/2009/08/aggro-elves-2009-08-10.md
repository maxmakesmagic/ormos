Combo Elves have been all the rage of late, using [Elvish Archdruid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Archdruid) alongside [Heritage Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heritage+Druid) and [Nettle Sentinel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Sentinel) to repeatedly cast [Primal Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Command), all in one turn. Apparently no one told Steve Pulliam, who powered into the Top 8 of a Pro Tour Qualifier for Austin in Portland, Oregon with a completely different take on the Elf archetype. He paired the most powerful and cost efficient Elves with a modicum of burn to control the red zone. Particularly cool? His combo of [Manamorphose](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Manamorphose) and [Talara's Battalion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Talara%27s+Battalion)!
