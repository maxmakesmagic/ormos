Grand Prix-Chicago, featuring the Legacy format, is in the books, and here's a look at one of the whispered about decks heading into the event: Dredge. Yuu Fukudome took this build to the championship at the Legacy event held at Pro Tour-Kyoto. The deck seeks to abuse [Lion's Eye Diamond](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lion%27s+Eye+Diamond) to quickly fill the graveyard with dredge creatures which then allow the user to effectively draw numerous extra cards each turn. Of course, the deck struggles against opposing [Extirpate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Extirpate)s, [Tormod's Crypt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tormod%27s+Crypt)s, and other assorted graveyard hate, but that doesn't stop the brave from trying it!
