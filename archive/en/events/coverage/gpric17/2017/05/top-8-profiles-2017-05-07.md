Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 7, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamstyborski.jpg)
By Adam Styborski




 Stybs has played Magic the world over, writing and drafting as part of the event coverage team and slinging Commander everywhere his decks will fit. 






Learn more about Grand Prix Richmond's Top 8 competitors—including who they are, what they’ve done in the game, and more—by taking a look through their profiles!


  
**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpric17/t8_carpenter.jpg)Name:** Brad Carpenter


**Age:** 25


**Hometown:** St. Petersburg, Florida


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:** GP Washington DC, Top 8 2010; SCG Orlando Champion 2016


**What was your record on Day 1, and what did you play?**


8-1 Red-Green, had [Glorybringer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glorybringer) and [Rhonas the Indomitable](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhonas+the+Indomitable).


**What were your records on Day 2, and what did you play?**


3-0, 3-0 Abzan and Red-White


**Which cards performed well for you this weekend, and why?**


[Gust Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gust+Walker), it’s one of the best cards in the set.


**What were the most surprising cards you played against this weekend, and why?**


Anything blue, because they seem very bad.


**Which common or uncommon would you pick as Pack 1, Pick 1 in *Amonkhet*?**


[Gust Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gust+Walker)

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpric17/t8_barniecki.jpg)Name:** Michael Baraniecki


**Age:** 30


**Hometown:** Philly


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


1st Grand Prix Top 8; [played] at Pro Tour Gatecrash


**What was your record on Day 1, and what did you play?**


9-0 with [Glorybringer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glorybringer). I was just on auto-pilot until Day 2. The deck played itself.


**What were your records on Day 2, and what did you play?**


2-1 in both drafts. First was an aggressive Black-Red deck and the I played a BUG [Sultai] Ramp.


**Which cards performed well for you this weekend, and why?**


[Cartouche of Zeal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cartouche+of+Zeal). You’ll need to stop sleeping on this card.


**What were the most surprising cards you played against this weekend, and why?**


In Round 9, my 8-0 opponent played that desert that dealt one damage to me ... so yea, that. [[Sunscorched Desert](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sunscorched+Desert)]
