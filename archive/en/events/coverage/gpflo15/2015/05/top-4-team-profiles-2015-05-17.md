Grand Prix Florence 2015 Top 4 Team Profiles
============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 17, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











After fourteen rounds the original field of 1,296 players had been reduced to a mere twelve. They defeated all comers and sat down in the feature match area to finally do some drafting. Time to learn more about them!






![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpflo15/t4_anteri_royde_sajgalik.jpg)  
*Left to right: Fabrizio A*nteri, *Daniel Royde and Eduardo Sajgalik*




**Name:** Daniel Royde


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Software developer


**Hometown:** London, UK


**Your team's record on Day 1:** 7-1-1


**Your personal record on Day 1:** Don't know.


**Your team's record on Day 2:** 4-1


**Your personal record on Day 2:** Don't know.



**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


I won Grand Prix London in 2011 as well as the the UK Nationals in 2011; I came in 17th at Pro Tour Return to Ravnica in Seattle in 2012.



**Who are your teammates, and why did you play with them?**


Eduardo—we always team up together. Fabrizio—the best player we could convince to team with us.



**What color combination did you play in your first Sealed Deck, and what was your most important card?**


Green-white. Anafenza, Kin-Tree Spirit—a 2/2 for WW that I never played.



**What color combination did you play in your second Sealed Deck, and what was your most important card?**


Black-white. [Citadel Siege](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Citadel+Siege).

**Name:** Eduardo Sajgalik


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Seeking employment


**Hometown:** Reading, UK


**Your team's record on Day 1:** 7-1-1


**Your personal record on Day 1:** 6-3


**Your team's record on Day 2:** 4-1

**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


Four Top 8s at Nationals, played in three National teams at the World Magic Cup/Worlds (Canada in 2008, UK in 2011, and England in 2013), Top 8 at Pro Tour Return to Ravnica, two Grand Prix Top 8s.



**Who are your teammates, and why did you play with them?**


Daniel Royde—he's a good old friend and a long-time teammate. Fabrizio Anteri—he currently is the best UK player.



**What color combination did you play in your first Sealed Deck, and what was your most important card?**


Red-black. [Temur Battle Rage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temur+Battle+Rage) and [Butcher's Glee](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher%27s+Glee).



**What color combination did you play in your second Sealed Deck, and what was your most important card?**


Blue-red. [Thunderbreak Regent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thunderbreak+Regent) and [Icefall Regent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Icefall+Regent).

**Name:** Fabrizio Anteri


**Age:** 25


**Occupation:** Magic player (kind of)


**Hometown:** London, UK


**Your team's record on Day 1:** 7-1-1


**Your personal record on Day 1:** 9-0


**Your team's record on Day 2:** 4-1


**Your personal record on Day 2:** 2-3


**Previous Magic accomplishments:**


I won some Grand Prix (Grand Prix Manchester 2014 and Grand Prix Warsaw 2014) last May.



**Who are your teammates, and why did you play with them?**


Ed and Dan. They pointed out we would be three English National champions after we had already teamed up. I didn't have time to change my mind at that point.



**What color combination did you play in your first Sealed Deck, and what was your most important card?**


Blue-black. [Silumgar, the Drifting Death](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silumgar%2C+the+Drifting+Death), [Tasigur, the Golden Fang](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tasigur%2C+the+Golden+Fang), [Crux of Fate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crux+of+Fate), [Silumgar Assassin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silumgar+Assassin), and [Foul Renewal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Foul+Renewal). Rares are good!



**What color combination did you play in your second Sealed Deck, and what was your most important card?**


Green/Black Rock. [Den Protector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Den+Protector) and [Deathmist Raptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathmist+Raptor). I played the deck in Standard and liked it, so I decided to run it in Limited as well.
