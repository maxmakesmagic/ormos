Dauntlessly Escorting
=====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 13, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Let's talk about [Dauntless Escort](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dauntless+Escort) for a moment.

![Dauntless Escort](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Dauntless+Escort)  
First, we'll blow up the art a little.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/188_dauntlessescort.jpg)  
Have you noticed the shield?

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/188_shield.jpg)  
That looks sort of... *familiar*, doesn't it?

![Elspeth, Knight-Errant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant)  
I guess now we know who the Dauntless Escort has been dauntlessly escorting. Doubtlessly!
