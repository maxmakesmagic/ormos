Cutting the Cake
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 6, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











On August 2, two people named Morgan and Kristen were married in Fayetteville, Arkansas. Why do we mention that? Well, because of this:

![The Groom's Cake](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1649_Cake.jpg)That's the [Groom's Cake](http://www.google.com) right there. It was made, we're told, by Rick's Bakery in Fayetteville. And they did a pretty good job, too! Wanna see him cutting into it?

![Cutting the Cake](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1649_CakeCutting.jpg)If we had to guess, we'd theorize that the cake symbolizes a "new hand", and by cutting into it, Morgan is seeing what his new life will hold. Or it could be that he just really likes **Magic**. Let's take a look at the happy couple!

![Sharing the Cake](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1649_CakeSharing.jpg)Congratulations, Morgan and Kristen. The **magicthegathering.com** team wishes you all the best!
