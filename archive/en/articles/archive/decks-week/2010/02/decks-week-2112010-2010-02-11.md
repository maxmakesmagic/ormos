Decks of the Week: 2/11/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on February 11, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Now that *Worldwake* is out, it's time for new deck technology to flood the Internet! Unfortunately for you, humble reader, that flood is not coming today because all we have for you are decks from **Magic Online**, which won't be seeing *Worldwake* until February 22. To see how *Worldwake* impacts Extended, be sure to check out live coverage of Grand Prix–Oakland this weekend, and then come back next week for Pro Tour–San Diego, featuring both Standard deck lists and *Zendikar/Zendikar/Worldwake*  Booster Drafts!
