Mono-Black Beatdown
===================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on September 29, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/shuhei_authorpic.jpg)
By Shuhei Nakamura




Shuhei Nakamura is a member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, former Player of the Year, and is one of just six players with more than 400 lifetime pro points. 







[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Herald%20of%20Torment)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Herald%20of%20Torment)

Greetings! This is Shuhei Nakamura. Some of you may have seen me at a Grand Prix somewhere once upon a time. Starting this week, I'll be taking over the Daily Deck column for a spell. Here, each week I'll be highlighting Constructed decks that catch my eye, both on- and offline, and presenting them arranged into different themes.



 Let's get started. This week's theme will be *Theros* Block Constructed. Of course, by now everyone is getting excited over *Khans of Tarkir*, but one of the best ways to put together new decks is to look back to what worked in the environment before the newest expansion came out. For example, the Black Control deck that was all the rage over the last season grew out of the Black-Green Control deck that was prominent around the same time last year. Block Constructed is full of raw gems like this.




 Today's feature is Mono-Black Beatdown. This was considered the "deck to beat" going into Pro Tour [Pro Tour *Journey into Nyx*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptjou14), and picks up a powerful new component with [Bloodsoaked Champion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodsoaked+Champion). Will it be reborn as a new Standard contender? Time will tell...
