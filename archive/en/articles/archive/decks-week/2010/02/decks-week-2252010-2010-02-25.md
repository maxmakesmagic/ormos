Decks of the Week: 2/25/2010
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on February 25, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Congratulations to Simon Görtzen, your [Pro Tour–San Diego](/en/events/coverage/g%C3%B6rtzen-goes-all-way-san-diego) champion. Görtzen's Jund build conquered the event, but there was plenty of innovation in the Standard environment throughout the top decks played at the Pro Tour. The Boss Naya deck created by Tom Ross propelled him and Luis Scott-Vargas to a combined 19-1 record in Standard, Gaudenis Vidugiris played a Zvi Mowshowitz-designed deck dubbed "Mythic" also to a 9-1 record, and Patrick Chapin's new Blue-white Control got him to 8-1-1. But Standard wasn't the only format on display at the Pro Tour. Check out the Legacy public event Top 8 plus two Extended PTQs below, along with the regular assortment of Pro Tour Qualifiers and **Magic Online**  events brought to you each week by Decks of the Week.
