Pauper Delver
=============



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 7, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











[Delver of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets) has made a huge splash recently in Standard and Legacy, but those aren't the only formats the *Innistrad* common has been slowly taking over. It's also legal in Pauper, a format where only common cards are legal! In a recent **Magic Online** Premier Event, a deck featuring the Human Insect buzzed all the way to 2nd place. Take a look for yourself, and give this fun, unique **Magic Online** format a try!
