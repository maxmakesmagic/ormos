With the Eldrazi running rampant in Standard, [Polymorph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polymorph) is a reject rare that has been given a lot of second looks recently. But while most people have been following a blue-green path for their [Polymorph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polymorph) combo decks, Tim Sussino opted for a different method: blue and red. He uses [Dragon Fodder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon+Fodder), [Goblin Assault](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Assault), and the powerful [Stone Idol Trap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Idol+Trap) to provide himself the targets he needs for his [Polymorph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polymorph), and when it resolves? He picks up a free [Iona, Shield of Emeria](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iona%2C+Shield+of+Emeria). The list was strong enough to put him into the Top 8 of a PTQ in Hartford, Connecticut.
