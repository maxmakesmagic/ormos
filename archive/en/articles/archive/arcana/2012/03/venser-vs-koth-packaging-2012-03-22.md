Venser vs. Koth Packaging
=========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 22, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











You, of course, are looking forward to the release of [*Duel Decks: Venser vs. Koth*](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/productarticle.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/venservskoth/productinfo) on March 30. It will be available in English and Japanese and the MSRP is $19.99 . Well, the Japanese version will probably involve some amount of Yen, rather than US Dollars, but you get the general idea.


To help you in your quest to buy one of these, here's what it will look like. Enjoy!
