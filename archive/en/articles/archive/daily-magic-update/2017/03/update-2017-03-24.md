 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**

What to Watch This Weekend
==========================


1. [Grand Prix Orlando](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gporl17) | [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic) | Saturday and Sunday, 7 a.m. PT/10 a.m. ET/2 p.m. UTC

We're nearly done with *Aether Revolt*-*Kaladesh* Limited—but we're not done yet. Orlando plays host to the final individual Grand Prix in the format (there's a Team Limited Grand Prix in Mexico City coming up), and for at least one player, it's going to be the happiest place on earth.


1. [Commander the Gathering Live](https://twitter.com/CheshirePlays/status/844841243871870976) | [twitch.tv/DyoshiiTV](https://www.twitch.tv/dyoshiitv) | Friday at 7 p.m. PT/10 p.m. ET

I'm shining the spotlight on this event with the caveat that I have not seen this group stream before, but the idea seems cool and potentially fun if you love Commander—a live Commander stream in a local game store. The group is in Australia, so they basically live in the future. Don't be confused if they say it's Saturday or if someone gets eaten by a snake. That just happens down there.


After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
============================================


1. [Mastering Dynavolt Tower](http://www.toplevelpodcast.com/dynavolt-tower/) | Top Level Podcast | Mike Flores and Patrick Chapin

Chapin thinks the latest [Dynavolt Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dynavolt+Tower) list from Victor Fernando Silva that won the Grand Prix last weekend is a masterpiece—and a key player in Standard moving forward. Unfortunately, "Chapin enjoys tournament-winning blue deck" is the free space on Top Level Bingo, so you probably didn't get any closer there.


If you want more on [Dynavolt Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dynavolt+Tower), Chapman Sim covers [three important elements of the deck here](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/chapman-sim/three-important-elements-of-dynavolt-tower).


1. [Rating the Legendary Creatures in *Modern Masters 2017*](http://www.starcitygames.com/article/34794_Rating-The-Legends-In-Modern-Masters.html) | StarCityGames.com | Bennie Smith

I love semi-arbitrary ratings of things. The best burger. The top blood and sand. The best French fries. The best—well, you get the idea. I like food. But I also like *Magic* (clearly), and I enjoyed this totally subjective rating of the potential Commanders found in *Modern Masters 2017 Edition*. Even if I would never in a million years rate [Venser, Shaper Savant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Venser%2C+Shaper+Savant) a 2. In my view, it sends a message: "This could be [Azami, Lady of Scrolls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Azami%2C+Lady+of+Scrolls), but I don't even *need* her."


1. [Drafting with Numot—*Modern Masters 2017* #1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cIweq8VddLo) | Kenji Egashira

There's a lot of excitement in the streaming community over *Modern Masters* right now, so don't be shocked to see a lot of these types of videos coming from consistent streamers like Kenji Egashira.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/cIweq8VddLo>


1. [The Top 15 Modern Decks](https://soundcloud.com/user-121566285/gam-e27-the-top-15-modern-decks) | GAM Podcast | Gerry Thompson and Michael Majors

Even down one Andrew Brown (who soundly defeated me at the company *Modern Masters* release event—he's doing quite well for himself here), the GAM Podcast marches on as Thompson and Majors take a serious look at the Top 15—yes, 15—decks in Modern. In an hour-long podcast, that's 4 minutes per deck, which is a pretty good chuck. Yay math!


Want more Modern podcasts? Check out [Turn One Thoughtseize](http://mtgcast.com/mtgcast-podcast-shows/active-podcast-shows/turnonethoughtseize/turnonethoughtseize-ep-56-mo-betta-meta), which released an episode today on attacking the current metagame.

This week, the crew covers *Modern Masters 2017 Edition*, a judge conference, recent community talk on Planeswalkers, and the very definition of success.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Blue-Red Zombie Emerge](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-03-23)

As the format turns . . .


Tier 1 might be solidly locked, but tier 2 continues to churn interesting decks to the top, then recycle them and bring them back later. The flavor of this week is Blue-Red Zombie Emerge, which has seen an uptick in online play and in decks going 5-0 in Competitive Standard League. This one from *Magic Online* player fil2hot did just that, setting a fevered pace past its opponents.


The deck has a ton of play against the top decks. [Kozilek's Return](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kozilek%27s+Return) is very good against Mardu Vehicles, and this is one of the few decks that can take advantage of the return part at instant-speed and with some amount of consistency. [Lightning Axe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Axe) and [Fiery Temper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper) both can shut down the Copy Cat combo, and [Fevered Visions](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fevered+Visions) provides a ton of pressure against Four-Color and Temur Tower decks alike, as those decks often struggle to get cards out of their hands.


Throw in some generally resilient threats like a few Skaabs and a [Prized Amalgam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam), and you have a deadly recipe for the current Standard metagame.
