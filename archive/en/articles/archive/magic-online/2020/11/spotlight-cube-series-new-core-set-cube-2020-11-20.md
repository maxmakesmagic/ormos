Spotlight Cube Series – New Core Set Cube
=========================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on November 20, 2020 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Kyle O'Neill











Hello! I'm Kyle O'Neill ([@imkyleoneill](https://twitter.com/imkyleoneill) on Twitter), and I'm here to introduce you to the exciting refresh of the *Core Set Cube*.


As the senior retail development specialist for the Wizards Play Network, I put a premium on unique experiences that a wide audience of players can enjoy. It is a big part of the reason why I have such a deep respect for the role that core sets play. They're approachable, resonant, and encapsulate everything fun about *Magic* in a package easy for new players to digest.


Core sets are like coming home, or like checking in on friends you haven't seen in a while.


The original [*Core Set Cube*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/cube-spotlight-series-core-set-cube-2018-10-30) debuted on *Magic Online* just two years ago, and since then, we've had two new sets of cards with which to upgrade it. I have also added in some old core set powerhouses that were absent from the last version so you can try new strategies. Overall, about a third of the cube is different, so you are in for a fresh experience.


You can draft the new *Core Set Cube* from **November 25 to December 2**.

If this is your first time, here's the overview. The design rules for this cube are:


1. It includes only cards from core sets ranging from *Seventh Edition* to *Core Set 2021* (including *Magic Origins*).
2. It's 540 cards, Singleton.
3. It goes big on nostalgia and the most recognizable *Magic* archetypes.

This cube is a longtime favorite of our cubing group in the Wizards building. The core set restriction allows a great number of perfectly powerful cards to shine in a new environment, as you probably wouldn't see them as often in traditionally powered cubes.


We've learned a lot since the last time it's been on *Magic Online*. There are more flexible removal options and ways to deal with bombs, more of an ability to splash colors, and you can still draft Goblins or Elves.

**KEEPING CORE**
================


Control, Aggro, Mid-Range, Combo, Ramp, Spells, Tokens, Value, Reanimator, Life Gain, Mill, Lands, Goblins vs. Elves, Draw-Go, Burn . . . they're all here!


The *Core Set Cube* features the traditional Draft archetypes you know and love, except supercharged with a nostalgic core set flavoring. Below is where you can find these strategies the most, but these are not the only options available. Experiment with the card pool to discover exciting things to try!

**FIFTEEN THINGS TO TRY**
=========================


Looking for a challenge? Check out these strategies to add some spice to your *Core Set Cube* experience.


1. Try sacrificing [Mitotic Slime](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mitotic+Slime) to a [Bloodthrone Vampire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodthrone+Vampire). Then [Fling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fling) it.
2. Rebuy [Jester's Cap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jester%27s+Cap) over and over with [Buried Ruin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Buried+Ruin) and [Crucible of Worlds](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crucible+of+Worlds).
3. Cast [Scapeshift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scapeshift) into [Field of the Dead](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Field+of+the+Dead) with [Zendikar's Roil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zendikar%27s+Roil) in play.
4. Make an impossibly huge creature with [Kalonian Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kalonian+Hydra) and [Invigorating Surge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Invigorating+Surge).
5. Build a deck with [Polymorph](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polymorph) and no creature spells other than [Darksteel Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Colossus).
6. Cast a turn-two [Thundermaw Hellkite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thundermaw+Hellkite) off a [Seething Song](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seething+Song) and a [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves).
7. [Reanimate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reanimate) an [Omniscience](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) or [Form of the Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Form+of+the+Dragon) with [Starfield of Nyx](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Starfield+of+Nyx), then attack with it!
8. Go infinite with [Natural Affinity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Natural+Affinity), [Intruder Alarm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intruder+Alarm), and [Ant Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ant+Queen).
9. Get swampy with [Dread Presence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dread+Presence) and [Urborg, Tomb of Yawgmoth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urborg%2C+Tomb+of+Yawgmoth).
10. Take the [Door to Nothingness](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Door+to+Nothingness) challenge!
11. Kick [Hornet Nest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Nest) with a giant [Earthquake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earthquake).
12. [Pillage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pillage) and [Plow Under](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plow+Under) your opponent's lands and beat in with [Magnivore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magnivore).
13. Pick up your lands with [Trade Routes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trade+Routes) and throw them with [Molten Vortex](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Vortex).
14. Copy [Warp World](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Warp+World) with [Pyromancer's Goggles](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer%27s+Goggles).
15. Double a creature's power with [Unleash Fury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unleash+Fury), then do it again with [Overwhelming Stampede](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overwhelming+Stampede)!
