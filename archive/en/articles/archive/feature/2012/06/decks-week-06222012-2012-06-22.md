Decks of the Week: 06/22/2012
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 22, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations to Yuuya Watanabe for his amazing sixth Grand Prix victory this past weekend in Manila! Watanabe-*san* will be leading his country at the [World **Magic** Cup](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/worldmagiccup) in Indianapolis, IN, this August, during Gen Con! Not invited to the WMC? You can still get into high-level play by entering a [Pro Tour *Return to Ravnica*](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/seattle12-qualifiers) Qualifier [near you](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/pro-tour%E2%80%93amsterdam-standard-qualifier-season-top-8-decklists-2015-07). For ideas of the kinds of decks people are playing competitively, check out the Top 8 decklists from some of the recent PTQs (including those on **Magic Online**) and WMC Qualifiers!
