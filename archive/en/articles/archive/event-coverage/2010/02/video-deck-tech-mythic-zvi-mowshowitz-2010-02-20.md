Video Deck Tech: Mythic with Zvi Mowshowitz
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 20, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 






Pro Tour Hall of Famer and master deck designer Zvi Mowshowitz shows off his creation, a [Finest Hour](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Finest+Hour)/[Rafiq of the Many](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rafiq+of+the+Many)/[Knight of the Reliquary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary+)  build with tons of mana acceleration topping out with [Baneslayer Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baneslayer+Angel)s, [Rampaging Baloths](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rampaging+Baloths), and a [Thornling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thornling).
