*Modern Horizons 2* on MTGO
---------------------------


We're excited to be continuing the amazing play in *Modern Horizons 2*! Sealed, Draft, and Phantom Draft events we've been running will continue, as well as our special Over the Horizons Series (discussed below). Those Leagues will run until the release of *Adventures in the Forgotten Realms* in July.


Again, cards from the set are now legal in all formats and this set is non-redeemable.


***Modern Horizons 2*** **Draft League**


**Entry Options**:


* 25 Event Tickets
* 3 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 4 Event Tickets
* 250 Play Points

**Prizes**:


* 3 wins: 6 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 2 Qualifier Points
* 2 wins: 2 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters

***Modern Horizons 2*** **Sealed League**


**Entry Options**:


* 45 Event Tickets
* 6 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 3 Event Tickets
* 450 Play Points

**Prizes**:


* 5 wins: 6 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 675 Play Points + 5 Qualifier Points
* 4 wins: 3 *Modern Horizons 2* boosters + 450 Play Points + 2 Qualifier Points
* 3 wins: 1 *Modern Horizons 2* booster + 225 Play Points + 1 Qualifier Point

***Modern Horizons 2*** **Phantom Draft League**


**Entry Options**:


* 10 Event Tickets
* 100 Play Points

**Prizes**:


* 3 wins: 150 Play Points + 1 Treasure Chest + 2 Qualifier Points
* 2 wins: 100 Play Points
* 1 win: 50 Play Points

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. Learn more about [Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021).


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place. *NOTE: Pauper and Standard Challenges are never pre-empted**.*
