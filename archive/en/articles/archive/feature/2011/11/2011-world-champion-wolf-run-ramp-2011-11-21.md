2011 World Champion: Wolf Run Ramp
==================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 21, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Jun'ya Iyanaga is the 2011 **Magic**  World Champion! Iyanaga's Standard deck, Wolf Run Ramp, is a well-established archetype that served him well in the Top 8, dispatching Josh Utter-Leyton, Conley Woods, and Richard Bland on his way to the title. Check out the [full coverage](/en/events/coverage/japan-stands-tall-worlds-again) to see how!
