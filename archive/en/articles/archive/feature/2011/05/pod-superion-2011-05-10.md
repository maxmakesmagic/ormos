How was your *New Phyrexia* Prerelease? We talked with our own R&D members about some of the decks they brought to spellsling with this past weekend, and here's what Mons Johnson used. Mons, who is famous as the namesake of [Mons's Goblin Raiders](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mons%27s+Goblin+Raiders) and who has been helping to make **Magic** for many years, described his deck: "The key elements are a lot of support creatures for [Myr Superion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myr+Superion) and a reasonable chain for [Birthing Pod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod)." You'll be able to play with the set [this weekend](http://archive.wizards.com/%20http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/release-facts%20) when it goes on sale!
