Modern Banned Gauntlet
======================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on November 30, 2021 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The Modern Banned Gauntlet arrives on *Magic Online* tomorrow! From December 1–8, players can choose a preconstructed Modern deck with a card we've banned in it and see how these "too good for Modern" decks fare against each other.


In the Modern Banned Gauntlet, you'll get to select one of the following decks and battle other decks for up to three Best-of-Three rounds. Each deck features exactly one card that was banned at some point in the lifetime of the Modern format, which you'll find on the deck box when you choose it upon entering the event. The decks and their banned cards are:
