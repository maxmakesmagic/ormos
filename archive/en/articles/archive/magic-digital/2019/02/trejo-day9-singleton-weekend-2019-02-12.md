Trejo & Day[9] Singleton Weekend
================================



 Posted in **Magic Digital**
 on February 12, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











<https://www.youtube.com/embed/-G6NMEB5uQU>


Join Danny Trejo and Day[9] for an unforgettable weekend event—with no entry fee!


Trejo & Day[9] Singleton Weekend starts with the *Magic: The Gathering Arena* 0.12 Release on February 14 and continues until February 18 at 8 a.m. PT (4 p.m. UTC).


Have you ever thought to yourself . . .


*"I wish I could play Singleton this weekend."* 


*"I wish I could get alternate-art copies of [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) and [Ghalta, Primal Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghalta%2C+Primal+Hunger)."*


*"I wish I could get a picture of Danny Trejo holding Garruk's axe and Day[9] making a heart symbol, and maybe make it my desktop background."*


*MTG Arena* will make all those dreams into a reality this weekend. Below are the event details. We'll see you in game!


![VS_Valentines](https://media.wizards.com/2019/images/daily/PgnOAYETiD.jpg)


Trejo & Day[9] Singleton Weekend
================================


**Entry Fee:** None  
**Event Ends After:** 5 wins or 2 losses (whichever comes first)  
**Event Prizes:**


* **0 Wins:** None
* **1 Win:** 1 alternate-art copy of [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)
* **2 Wins:** 1 alternate-art copy of [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)
* **3 Wins:** 1 alternate-art copy of [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)
* **4 Wins:** 1 alternate-art copy of [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress)
* **5 Wins:** 1 alternate-art copy of [Duress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress), 1 alternate-art copy of [Ghalta, Primal Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghalta%2C+Primal+Hunger)
