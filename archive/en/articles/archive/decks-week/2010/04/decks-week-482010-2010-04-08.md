Decks of the Week: 4/8/2010
===========================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on April 8, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Congratulations to Adam Yurchick, the [2010 Grand Prix–Houston champion](/en/events/coverage/yurchick-first-last)! After a string of second-place finishes and near-misses at premier events, Yurchick broke into the victory circle with his Thopter-Depths deck, defeating Shaun Anderson and his Boom/Bust Zoo deck. We have the Top 32 Extended deck lists from the Grand Prix, featuring such top names as Shuhei Nakamura, Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa, Sam Black, Patrick Chapin, Martin Juza, and Conley Woods. With just one week left in the [Pro Tour–San Juan qualifier season](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/sanjuan10-qualifiers), if you're looking for last-minute deck ideas to earn that invite and plane ticket to paradise, that's a great place to start! For a different deckbuilding activity, take a look through numerous **Magic Online** Standard and Block Constructed decks alongside the [*Rise of the Eldrazi* visual spoiler](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/article.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/riseoftheeldrazi/spoiler) to start planning your decks once the new set is on sale!
