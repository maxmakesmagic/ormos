Video Feature: Preview
======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 28, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 





Rich Hagon and Marshall Sutcliffe set the stage for the titanic battle about to take place in Seattle between the 16 top Magic players in the world. The tournament features Jon Finkel, Luis Scott-Vargas, Brian Kibler, Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa, Jun'ya Iyanaga, Shouta Yasooka, Shuhei Nakamura, Yuuya Watanabe, Alexander Hayne, Samuele Estratti, David Ochoa, Josh Utter-Leyton, Owen Turtenwald, Reid Duke, Martin Juza, and Tzu-Ching Kuo. Formats are Cube Draft, Modern and *Magic 2013* Booster Draft.
