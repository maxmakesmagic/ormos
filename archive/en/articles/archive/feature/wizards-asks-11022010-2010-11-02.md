Wizards Asks: 11/02/2010
========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 2, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*What's your favorite [Timmy](http://www.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/mm/29) card, and why?*

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/authorpics/authorpic_MonsJohnson.jpg)**Mons Johnson:** My favorite Timmy card is probably the *USS Iowa*. At 68 points, this monster battleship can hit hard and move fast, and is very difficult to kill!

Oh, you meant **Magic** card. My bad ....

The Timmy card that I had the most fun with was definitely [Gurzigost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gurzigost). With a 6/8 body, 'Gost was the phattest guy in the time of madness. It came at a reasonable cost of a mere ![](https://web.archive.org/web/20161227130050im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/colorless3.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220123123600im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/green.gif)![](https://web.archive.org/web/20220123123600im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/modules/custom/wiz_autocard/wiz_manacost/manasymbols/green.gif) and could do a lot of damage directly to the opponent, particularly if you had a [Fiery Temper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fiery+Temper) in hand. Its upkeep was an actual advantage in some circumstances, most notably while you had a [Wild Research](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Research) in play.
