Katsuhiro Mori is the 2010 Japanese National Champion! He played a powerful red deck to take the title, becoming a repeat national champion. He'll lead a national team that includes fellow standout Tsuyoshi Ikeda at the [World Championships](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds) to be held in their native country in December. For full coverage of the event [click here](/en/events/coverage/mori-defeats-ikeda-japan-title-0).
