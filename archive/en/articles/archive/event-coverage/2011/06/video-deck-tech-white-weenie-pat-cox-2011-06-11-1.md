 on June 11, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author-photo_BDM.jpg)
By Brian David-Marshall

Fresh off his victory at the Star City Games Invitational, Pat Cox shares his deck choice for Pro Tour Nagoya: White Weenie featuring [Puresteel Paladin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Puresteel+Paladin). Designed by PT champion Mark Herberholz, Cox aims to equip a [Sword of War and Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+War+and+Peace+)  as soon as possible.
