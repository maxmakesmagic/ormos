### Local Information and Transportation


 The Hyatt Regency Chicago is located downtown on the Magnificent Mile, near the shores of Lake Michigan. The hotel is within easy walking distance to Chicago's premier shopping, theaters, and cultural attractions. 

The Hyatt Regency Chicago is 30 to 45 minutes from O'Hare International Airport and 20 minutes from Midway Airport. 


**O'Hare International Airport**  

Chicago O'Hare International Airport is 30 to 45 minutes from downtown. The airport features a new state-of-the-art international terminal along with the domestic terminals and long-term parking. 


**Midway Airport**  

Midway Airport is a one-building airport divided into three terminals, with the main lobby at the center entrance of the airport. 


 **Airport Express**  
[www.airportexpress.com](http://www.airportexpress.com)  

(800) 654-7871 


* Airport Express provides a shuttle service for guests of the Hyatt Regency Chicago hotel. The shuttle departs regularly from both the O'Hare and Midway airports, and guests may be picked up curbside at both.
* Guests on return shuttles to both airports may be picked up from the West Tower lobby.
* The shuttle to O'Hare departs at 5 minutes and 35 minutes after every hour, beginning at 5:05 A.M. and ending at 10:35 P.M.
* The shuttle to Midway departs at 15 minutes and 45 minutes after every hour, beginning at 5:15 A.M. and ending at 10:45 P.M.
* Please note that the shuttle will stop at other hotels en route to and from the airport.

**Downtown Service**  

(All departures from the airports include a $1 tax) 


* Serves all downtown hotels.
* Departs from O'Hare every 5 to 10 minutes; from Midway every 15 minutes.
* Scheduled service and reserved pickups from hotels.
* O'Hare to downtown service costs $16 one way; $29 roundtrip (the one-way price of a cab!).
* Midway to downtown service costs $11.50 one way; $21 roundtrip. Tickets
* O'Hare--look for the kiosk outside the international terminal. Ticket counters are also located across from the baggage claim area in the domestic terminals.
* Midway--ticket counter located opposite Southwest Airlines counter.

**Taxi Cabs**  

Cab lines are available outside the baggage claim areas of each airport. A "shared cab" service is provided to the major downtown hotels. 


**Amtrak**  

Amtrak connections are located at Union Station just minutes from the Hyatt Regency Chicago hotel. For AMtrak rates and schedule information, call (800) USA-RAIL. 


**Chicago Transit Authority:** Buses and Elevated Trains  

The bus system offers a quick and easy way to get around the city. There are no hassles with parking or traffic--you can just sit back, relax, and enjoy the sites of Chicago. 


Transfers are required when changing from bus to train services and vice versa. Transfers are not required from train to train. The exact fare is required on all buses. For information on bus and "L" train routes and schedules, contact the Chicago Transit Authority at (312) 836-7000.

### Pro Tour Chicago Masters Series


 The second Masters Series will be held at Pro Tour Chicago from November 30 to December 3. 

**Location**  

Hyatt Regency Chicago  

On the Riverwalk  

151 East Wacker Drive  

Chicago, IL 60601  

(312) 565-1234  

Columbus Rooms I & J for Thursday Masters and Gateway, Pro Tour Feature Match area all other days. 


**Invited Players** 


* Top 5 Limited players based on the DCI ratings published October 23, 2000
* Top 25 players in the PT New York 2000 Pro Standing (posted after Pro Tour New York, October 1)
* Top 2 players from the Masters Gateway Tournament, held November 30 in Chicago.

**Format**


* Single-Elimination Invasion Booster Draft
* All players will draft, play 2 rounds in their pod (cutting to 8 players) then draft again for the final 3 rounds of play.
