How has *Conflux* shaken up the Extended format? For Mike Magby, it means bringing the minions of Bant to the battlefield. Featuring the best blue, white, and green spells on the planet, Mike's deck benefits from the recently printed [Noble Hierarch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Noble+Hierarch). He took his build all the way to second place at a recent Pro Tour Qualifier for Honolulu in Pittsburgh.
