**Legacy Cube and Flashback *Odyssey* Queues**


Start the new year off right with some [Legacy Cube](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/resolve-legacy-cube-2015-01-05)!


Beginning Wednesday, January 14, following the downtime, the Legacy Cube will make its triumphant return to *Magic Online*. Accompanying the Legacy Cube will be triple *Odyssey* Flashback Drafts. Both *Odyssey* and Legacy Cube Events run in Swiss and Single Elimination Draft. Both sets of queues conclude with the Wednesday, January 28, downtime.


Check out the [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/resolve-legacy-cube-2015-01-05) for the full details.

***Magic Online* is Hiring!**


Do you know *Magic Online* inside-out? Do you have previous judge experience? If so, you should apply to be a [Digital Events Coordinator](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/digital-events-coordinator-temporary-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1) for *Magic Online*!


We're also on the search for a savvy [Assistant Digital Product Manager](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers/assistant-digital-product-manager-mtgo-cardsets-job-renton-wa-us?careers-view=1) to work on *Magic Online* Cardsets. Read about these jobs, as well as many others, on the Wizards of the Coast [careers page](http://company.wizards.com/about/careers).
