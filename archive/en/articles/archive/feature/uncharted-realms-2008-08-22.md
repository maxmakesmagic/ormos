Uncharted Realms
================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 22, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Uncharted Realms is a weekly column dedicated to the story and world of the **Magic** Multiverse. Every card carries a story, and in this column we dive in and explore them. Join Adam Lee, the **Magic** Creative Team, and various guest authors as they take you on these weekly journeys.
