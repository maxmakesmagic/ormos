My Colors, My Guild, My Hat
===========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 26, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











It's "Cyber Monday," which means that today is the day a lot of things go on sale. Like, say, hats!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1111_izzethat.jpg)  
Yes! Starting at 8 a.m. EST on November 26 (the day this Arcana appears), there are five guild hats for sale on [mtgmerch.com](http://www.mtgmerch.com/)!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1111_azoriushat.jpg)  
For the first day, they're $15. So if you're reading this *on November 26, 2012*, the hats are $15. But at midnight EST, they go up to $20.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1111_selesnyahat.jpg)  
And as long as we're talking about sales, all the **Magic** T-shirts are on sale for $18 on November 26, 2012. But just like the hats, prices go up to $20 at midnight.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1111_golgarihat.jpg)  
And there's free shipping with the purchase of $50 or more!


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1111_rakdoshat.jpg)  
To sum up: [mtgmerch.com](http://www.mtgmerch.com/). New hats! Both hats and shirts are on sale on November 26, 2012, but they both go up to $20 at midnight Eastern time.
