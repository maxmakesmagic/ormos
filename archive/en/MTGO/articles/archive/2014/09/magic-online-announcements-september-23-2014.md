What Is the *Magic Online* Weekly Announcements Blog?
![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150913023540im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/announcement_image.jpg)
Every Tuesday, we round up all of the biggest *Magic Online* news and post it right here in the Weekly Announcements Blog. Check in every Tuesday for all of the latest news!





Last Chance to Play the *Magic Online* Cube
The [refreshed *Magic Online* Cube](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-2015-cube-update-2014-09-01) continues until the downtime on Wednesday, October 1, so play it while you can!


As is the custom, paired flashback drafts are running alongside the *Magic Online* Cube! The flashback draft switches to *Innistrad* during the Wednesday, September 24 downtime.





Reminder: *From the Vault: Annihilation* Comes out Monday, October 6
Beyond Destruction Looms Annihilation. Harness the savage power of fifteen of the most brutal *Magic* cards ever unleashed.


*From the Vault: Annihilation* is coming out on Monday, October 6! In case you missed it when it was first announced, read up on these powerful board clearing cards [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/products/from-the-vault-annihilation).





Upcoming Extended Downtime on October 1
The downtime for Wednesday, October 1 is being extended. The *Magic Online* Twitter account will have updates as we make progress on introducing *Khans of Tarkir* to *Magic Online*.





* All tournaments will close October 1, at 5:00 a.m. Pacific (12:00 UTC)
* The system will be down from 8:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. Pacific (15:00 to 21:00 UTC)
