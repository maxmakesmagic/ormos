The 8-0 Decks
=============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_103.png)
By Rui Oliveira












This is what you aim for every time you sign up for a Grand Prix. The fable "all-win" first day. Today, after eight rounds, only two players managed to reach that honor: the guy who calls Grand Prix his home - Alex Shvartsman - and the guy who wins a Portuguese PTQ every single season - Tiago Chan.


Here are the deck lists, setting up the top table for the first round tomorrow. Two Grow decks with some differences but the same core design. As Zvi said, just blame Alan Comer. It's his fault.
