### The Searchable Decklist Archive

Welcome to MagicTheGathering.com's searchable archive of **Magic** decklists. You can enter a card name ("Call of the Herd"), a partial card name ("goblin"), or even a deck title or type ("casual"), and the archive will show you a list of decklists from past MagicTheGathering.com articles that contain that card or phrase. Depending on server traffic, the results of your search may take some time to return. **Enjoy!**
