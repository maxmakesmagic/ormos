In honor of the start of the Pro Tour Qualifier-Honolulu season, Daily Decks is bringing you some of the hottest Extended decks all week long! Fresh off his Pro Tour-Berlin championship, Luis Scott-Vargas continued his tear in the Extended format with a flawless record at the 2008 World Championships. While the super-pro stuck with a combo deck, he switched flavors from Elves to Swans. The Swans deck seeks to play [Swans of Bryn Argoll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swans+of+Bryn+Argoll), then play [Chain of Plasma](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+of+Plasma) targeting the 4/3. By (literally) chaining the Plasma over and over, LSV could draw his deck, remove two [Simian Spirit Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Simian%2BSpirit%2BGuide) (or play two [Chrome Mox](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Chrome%2BMox)), and flashback [Conflagrate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Conflagrate) for more than lethal against his opponent. To find an Extended PTQ for Honolulu near you, visit [this link](/en/node/640716).
