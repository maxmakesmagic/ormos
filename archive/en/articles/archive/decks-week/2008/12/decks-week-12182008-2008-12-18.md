=============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on December 18, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Welcome to Decks of the Week, 2008 World Championships edition! We've got all sorts of exciting decklists coming to you from the World Championships in Memphis, which you can read more about [here](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/japanese-coverage-2008-magic-world-championships-2008-12-11). In fact, Top 8 competitor Frank Karsten even built his deck using some complicated math and information he received from Decks of the Week! Read about [how he did it](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/2008-worlds-blog-day-2-2008-12-11).


Of course, it's not a Decks of the Week without the usual suspects. We've got more PTQ-Kyoto Top 8 lists (and of course, there are a few more PTQs left, which you can find out about [here](/en/node/640906). And as always, the Top 8s from this past weeks **Magic Online** premier events. So read up on the 2008 Worlds Coverage, then find out what all the latest tech is about!
