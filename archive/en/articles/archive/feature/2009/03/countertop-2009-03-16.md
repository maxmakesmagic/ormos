Just one week after winning Pro Tour-Kyoto, Gabriel Nassif is back in the champion's chair! He bested 1,230 players at Grand Prix-Chicago to take the Legacy title. His deck of choice? The infamous CounterTop, which uses a combination of [Sensei's Divining Top](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top) and [Counterbalance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterbalance) to lock an opponent out of the game. But Gabe didn't just run *any* version of the deck. Check out his Highlander sideboard; all one-ofs! To read more about the event, [visit the official web coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-grand-prix-chicago-2009-03-07).
