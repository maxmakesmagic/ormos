Announcing Premium Deck Series: Fire & Lightning
================================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 22, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/477_e8engo6sqw.jpg)  
Baked or fried? 


You choose how to burn down your opponents with this powerful, 60-card, all-foil deck. It contains famous flames, bolts, and beats from across **Magic** history, including several never before released in foil! Blaze your path to victory and serve your foes well done. 


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/477_514xdl0g0z.jpg)  
**Contents:**


* 60-card premium foil deck, including 6 rare cards
* Two cards featuring new art
* Exclusive Spindown life counter
* Foil deck box
* Strategy insert
* **Magic** "learn to play" guide

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/477_6fftbgftxz.jpg)Art by [Christopher Moeller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Christopher+Moeller%22%5D)


**Deck Design and Development**: Aaron Forsythe and Zac Hill   
**Release Date**: November 19th, 2010   
**MSRP**: $34.99
