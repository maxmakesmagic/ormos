Video Draft Tech: Mirrodin Besieged with Ben Stark
==================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 9, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_richhagon.jpg)
By Rich Hagon




Rich Hagon combines a deep knowledge of the players of the Pro Tour with a passionate love of the game. He's a regular commentator for Pro Tour and Grand Prix live video coverage, and is the official Pro Tour Statistician. He has been covering Magic events since 2006. 





Rich Hagon gets the lowdown on drafting Mirrodin Besieged from Ben Stark, who finished his first draft pod 3-0. That capped an 8-0 Day One for Stark, who sits atop the standings. You can see Ben's picks in the [Draft Viewer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/magic/draftools/draftviewer.asp?draftid=2_10_2011_1&player=3&pack=1&pick=1&showpick=false&alwaysshowpick=false).
