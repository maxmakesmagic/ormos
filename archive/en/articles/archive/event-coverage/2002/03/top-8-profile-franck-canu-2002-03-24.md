Top 8 Profile: Franck Canu
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 24, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpbar02/a910.jpg)**Name:** Canu Franck

**Age:** 22

**Occupation:** Pro Player

**Where do you live?** Paris

**Since when do you play Magic?** 6 years

**Best accomplishments:** Top 16 Pro Tour

**What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Barcelona; what did you expect to find?**  

Pro Tour Points

**What do you think is the main reason for you to make Top 8 in this tournament and why?**  

Good draft

**What are your predictions for the top 8?**  

Ruel or me will win the Grand Prix
