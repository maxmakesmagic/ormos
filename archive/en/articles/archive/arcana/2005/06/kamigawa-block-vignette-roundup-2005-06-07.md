Kamigawa Block Vignette Roundup
===============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 7, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











S*aviors of Kamigawa* is finally in stores -- so today we take a moment to look back. We [introduced](/en/node/606676) Kamigawa as "a world that waged a brutal war against its own gods." Three sets and over a year later, we've seen twenty short stories set in the context of that struggle between Kamigawa's mortals and kami. 

Did you enjoy the "Meet the *Champions/Betrayers/Saviors*" vignettes on **magicthegathering.com**? Ever wish you could see one big list of all those legend-riffic vignettes in one place?

Well, [now you can](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/kamigawa/vignettes).
