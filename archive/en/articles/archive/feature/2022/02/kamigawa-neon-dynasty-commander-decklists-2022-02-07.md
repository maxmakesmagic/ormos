Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty Commander Decklists
==========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 7, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











On February 18, [*Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/kamigawa-neon-dynasty) releases in stores worldwide, and with it, two new Commander decks that feature awesome reprints and powerful new cards.


![NEO Commander decks](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/en_n238sdnwDT.png)


Check out all the new and returning cards in the [*Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* Commander Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/kamigawa-neon-dynasty-commander), extended-art versions of *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* Commander cards available in Collector Boosters in the [*Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* Variant Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/kamigawa-neon-dynasty-variants)â€”including cards not found in these decks [exclusive to *Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty* Set and Collector Boosters](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/collecting-kamigawa-neon-dynasty-2022-01-27)!


***(Editor's Note: The decklists below pull the newest printing of each card automatically from*** [**Gatherer**](https://gatherer.wizards.com/)***, including printings which are not in this product. These decklists are not card-for-card product displays but rather interactive lists of the cards included in each deck. Additionally, card images for new Kamigawa: Neon Dynasty Commander cards will appear in decklists at a later time.)***
