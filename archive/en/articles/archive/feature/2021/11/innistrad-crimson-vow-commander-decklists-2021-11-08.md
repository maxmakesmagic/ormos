Innistrad: Crimson Vow Commander Decklists
==========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 8, 2021 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











On November 19, [*Innistrad: Crimson Vow*](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/innistrad-crimson-vow) releases in stores worldwide! Alongside Set Boosters, Collector Boosters, and more, two new Commander decks with exciting reprints and powerful new cards will arrive.


![VOW Commander decks](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/j5Y0LDbdPI-en.png)


Check out all the new and returning cards in the *Innistrad: Crimson Vow* Commander Card Image Gallery (available tomorrow), extended-art versions of *Innistrad: Crimson Vow* Commander cards available in Collector Boosters in the [*Innistrad: Crimson Vow* Variant Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/innistrad-crimson-vow-variants)â€”including cards not found in these decks [exclusive to *Innistrad: Crimson Vow* Set and Collector Boosters](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/collecting-innistrad-crimson-vow-2021-10-28)!


***(Editor's Note: The decklists below pull the newest printing of each card automatically from*** [**Gatherer**](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Default.aspx)***, including printings which are not in this product. These decklists are not card-for-card product displays but rather interactive lists of the cards included in each deck. Additionally, card images for new* Innistrad: Crimson Vow *Commander cards will appear in decklists at a later time.)***
