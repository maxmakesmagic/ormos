![Bazaar of Baghdad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Bazaar+of+Baghdad)Dredge is a powerful draw engine that allows a player to mill through their own library almost completely in a single turn. Why would one want to do such a thing, you ask? To take advantage of graveyard-matters cards like [Dread Return](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dread+Return) and [Bridge from Below](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bridge+from+Below). [Bazaar of Baghdad](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bazaar+of+Baghdad) turbo-charges the combo in **Magic Online** Classic, and the land is so important Dredge players in that format are willing to use [Serum Powder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serum+Powder) to make sure they start with it in their opening hand.
