**Sealed MOCS Open Event on December 18**


A *Kaladesh* Sealed *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) Open Event is coming up on Sunday, December 18! All players can join. No Qualifier Points are required to enter. The winner of the tournament will receive an invitation to Pro Tour *Amonkhet* in Nashville!


We look forward to seeing you on December 18 for the latest event! Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online* Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

**Upcoming *Aether Revolt* Regional Pro Tour Qualifier on Saturday, December 17**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Regional Pro Tour Qualifier (RPTQ) is coming to MTGO! The RPTQ will take place on this Saturday, December 17. The Top 8 players will receive invitations to compete in Pro Tour *Aether Revolt* in Dublin, Ireland. All participants will receive a promo copy of [Snapcaster Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snapcaster+Mage) for their MTGO account.


Learn more about Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers for *Aether Revolt* [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/instoreplay/rptqaer).

**The Year of Modern Flashbacks Continues with *Return to Ravnica* Block Starting Tomorrow**


It's 2016: The Year of Modern Flashbacks! If you want to build up a collection for Modern, these Flashback Drafts add everything you draft to your collection once the event is over. Even if Modern isn't your thing, you can enjoy a leisurely Draft through the planes of the past with rotating formats each week!


*Dragon's Maze*–*Gatecrash*–*Return to Ravnica* Flashback Drafts start tomorrow and run until Wednesday, December 21 and are followed up by *Magic 2014* Drafts. Get the full schedule [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/content/magic-online-limited-events#YearOfModernFlashbacks) and mark your calendar for the formats you're most excited about!


Please note that [Realmwright](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Realmwright), [Ooze Flux](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ooze+Flux), and [Structural Collapse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Structural+Collapse) will not appear in *Gatecrash* packs during drafts due to functionality issues.

**Final Downtime for 2016**


* All tournaments will close Wednesday, December 21, at 5:00 a.m. PT/1 p.m. UTC
* Store and trade activity will be suspended at approximately 7:45 a.m. PT/3:45 p.m. UTC
* The system will be down from 8:00 a.m./3 p.m. UTC until 12:00 p.m. PT/8 p.m. UTC
