Decks of the Week: 2/21/2014
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on February 21, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Pro Tour *Born of the Gods* happens this weekend! Catch all the highlights—including every game in the Top 8—with our [top-notch coverage](https://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/bornofthegods14#viewer). Last weekend, a country away, Javier Dominguez won [Grand Prix Paris](/en/events/coverage/dominguez-dominates-grand-prix-paris) while, across the Atlantic, Marc Lalague won his second Grand Prix, in [Mexico City](/en/events/coverage/lalague-monstrous-mexico-city). Congratulations to Javier and Marc! They will be heading to Atlanta in May for Pro Tour *Journey into Nyx* and if you'd like to join them, you still have time. Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#jou). Good luck; have fun!
