Standard: Through the Ages
--------------------------


Starting tomorrow, you can choose one of sixteen Standard decks from across the entire history of the format in *Magic Online*'s weeklong Standard: Through the Ages event, October 6–13! Check out the event details below and see [the associated article here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/standard-through-ages-2021-10-05) for available decklists.


**Location**: In the Limited Play Lobby, Gauntlet Section  
**Dates**: October 6, 10 a.m. PT (17:00 UTC) until October 13, 10 a.m. PT (17:00 UTC)  
**Format**: A League of up to three Best-of-Three matches  
**Entry**: 60 Play Points or 6 Event Tickets  
**Phantom**: Yes—players will not have cards in their decks added to their Collection  
**Prizes**:


* **3 Wins** – 90 Play Points and 1 Treasure Chest
* **2 Wins** – 60 Play Points
* **1 Win** – 30 Play Points

Pauper Qualifier Saturday
-------------------------


On Saturday, October 9, there will be a Pauper Qualifier with a special start time of 1 a.m. PT. The event costs 40 Season 3 QPs, and the winner will earn an invitation to the *Innistrad* Set Championship event on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021#ptq).




---

Standard Super Qualifier Sunday
-------------------------------


On Sunday, October 10, there will be a Standard Super Qualifier at 7 a.m. PT. The entry fee is 40 MOCS Qualifier Points (Season 3), 40 Event Tickets, or 400 Play Points. The top two finishers qualify for the *Innistrad* Championship on *MTG Arena*.


Learn more about Super Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021#sq).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats and provide excellent prizes for those looking to test their mettle in their favorite formats. [Learn more about Premier Play qualification](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2021) to get started today.


*Magic Online*'s Format Challenges run once on Saturdays and once on Sundays for each Constructed format! On weekends featuring Showcase Challenge events, the Format Challenge that starts at either 6 a.m., 8 a.m., or 10 a.m. Pacific time is pre-empted by the Showcase Challenge—Format Challenges scheduled at other times remain in place. *NOTE: Pauper, Vintage, and Standard Challenges are never pre-empted**.*


*New for MOCS Season 3: Pauper Showcase Challenges are held at 8 a.m. Saturday and do not pre-empt the Modern Challenge.*
