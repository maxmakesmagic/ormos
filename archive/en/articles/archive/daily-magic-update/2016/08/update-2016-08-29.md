The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on August 29, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by Announcement Day, coming soon!


Announcement Day is Coming!
===========================


1. August 31, 2016

Last May, we held [the first *Magic* Announcement Day](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/news/announcement-day-2016-05-16), wherein we announced a slate of upcoming new expansions and ways to play. During that announcement, we teased that the very next Announcement Day would be coming in September. Well, we were off by a day. Announcement Day—which will include announcements for the next block as well as supplementary sets and products into 2017—will be this Wednesday, August 31. Look for it right here on [DailyMTG](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/articles).

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Grand Prix Indianapolis Champion Brandon Burton's Naya Burn](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/gpind16/top-8-decklists-2016-08-28)

It's straightforward, it's brutal, and it's efficient. Brandon Burton took down Grand Prix Indianapolis with a turn-three kill that went [Goblin Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Guide), two [Lava Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lava+Spike)s, and three [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt)s. And just like that, he won on turn three.


The deck is nearly mono-red, but it dips into green and white for [Wild Nacatl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Nacatl), [Boros Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Boros+Charm), and [Atarka's Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Atarka%27s+Command), plus a number of sideboard cards. It's the most straightforward implication of the strategy—and, it seems, the best.
