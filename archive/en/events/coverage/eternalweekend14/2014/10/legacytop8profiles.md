### ETERNAL WEEKEND 2014


LEGACY TOP 8 PROFILES
=====================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)

Wizards of the Coast




October 25, 2014
 










The dust has settled and only eight remain.








  

  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/eternalweekend14/T8Grudzina.jpg)


### John Grudzina


**Age:** 28  
**Occupation:** Research/Trading  
  

**What did you play this weekend? Why?**
  
 Jeskai Delver
 



**What would you change about your deck if you played it next weekend?**
  
My sideboard
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend? Why?**
  
[Delver of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets) and [Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise)




  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/eternalweekend14/T8Markowicz.jpg)


### Eric Markowicz


**Age:** 24  
**Occupation:** Studying particle physics  
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 
  
Top 8 2010 Legacy Championship
 



**What did you play this weekend? Why?**
  
 Jeskai Delver. The deck seemed to have good matchups against other [Delver of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets) decks that I expected a lot of today.
 



**What would you change about your deck if you played it next weekend?**
  
Nothing
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend? Why?**
  
[Red Elemental Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Red+Elemental+Blast). The card has won many of my games with its versatility. It's a one mana removal spell or hard counter.




**What would winning the Legacy Championship mean to you?**
  
A sweet trophy! And sweet brags to Mark Tocco!!!
 



  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/eternalweekend14/T8Betesh.jpg)


### Ralph Betesh


**Age:** 28  
**Occupation:** Wifi Tech  
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 
  
Played in two Pro Tours
 



**What did you play this weekend? Why?**
  
 Temur Delver because Steven Daryl Ayers told me to.
 



**What would you change about your deck if you played it next weekend?**
  
I might play another [Forked Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forked+Bolt).
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend? Why?**
  
[Stifle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stifle) and [Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise)




**What would winning the Legacy Championship mean to you?**
  
Money for cookouts!
 



  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/eternalweekend14/T8Chiarlanza.jpg)


### Tony Chiarlanza


**Age:** 21  
**Occupation:** Student  
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 
  
Cashed Grand Prix Richmond and made the Top 8 of a few PTQs.
 



**What did you play this weekend? Why?**
  
 Blue/Red [Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise). My Friend Bobby Roche recommended it to me.
 



**What would you change about your deck if you played it next weekend?**
  
Miracles is tough. It's possible we should add [Sulfuric Vortex](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sulfuric+Vortex) or another [Pyroblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroblast) somewhere.
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend? Why?**
  
[Electrickery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrickery) in my sideboard for the mirror.




**What would winning the Legacy Championship mean to you?**
  
Approximately one Gaea's Cradle painting.
 



  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/eternalweekend14/T8DeLuca.jpg)


### Eric De Luca


**Age:** 31  
**Occupation:** CPA   
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 
  
Top 8ed well over 100 tournaments.
 



**What did you play this weekend? Why?**
  
 Team America, I feel it's the best positioned deck against other Delver decks and combo.
 



**What would you change about your deck if you played it next weekend?**
  
I would play more [Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise).
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend? Why?**
  
[Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman). It's always good, no matter what part of the game.




**What would winning the Legacy Championship mean to you?**
  
It would be pretty sweet. I would feel a great sense of accomplishment.
 



  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/eternalweekend14/T8Dobbin.jpg)


### Zach "Dobs" Dobbin


**Age:** 27  
**Occupation:** Conway Power Equipment Sales  
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 
  
Crushing FNMs at Gameskeep!




**What did you play this weekend? Why?**
  
 The Metroid Special/Helmerator. It's a great metagame call. [Helm of Obedience](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Helm+of+Obedience)/[Leyline of the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leyline+of+the+Void) is sweet and I love to troll.
 



**What would you change about your deck if you played it next weekend?**
  
Nothing. I loved my list.
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend? Why?**
  
[Helm of Obedience](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Helm+of+Obedience)/[Leyline of the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leyline+of+the+Void). Also, transmute wins out of nowhere.




**What would winning the Legacy Championship mean to you?**
  
It would mean a lot to me as a brewer. I feel like it would be a testament to my deckbuilding skills.
 



  
  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/eternalweekend14/T8Sturm.jpg)


### Nate Sturm


**Age:** 23  
**Occupation:** Math Teacher  
  

**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** 
  
PTQ Top 8s
 



**What did you play this weekend? Why?**
  
 Punishing Maverick because I love [Knight of the Reliquary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary).
 



**What would you change about your deck if you played it next weekend?**
  
I would add more artifact/enchantment removal.
 



**What has been the most important card in your deck this weekend? Why?**
  
[Maze of Ith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Maze+of+Ith). Untapping [Knight of the Reliquary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary) after combat damage is clutch.
 



**What would winning the Legacy Championship mean to you?**
  
[Treasure Cruise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treasure+Cruise) isn't [Ancestral Recall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancestral+Recall)
