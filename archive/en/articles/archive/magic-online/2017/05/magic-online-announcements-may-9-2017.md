**1v1 Commander starts tomorrow, May 10**


Last week Bryan Hawley introduced the new [1v1 Commander](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/1v1-commander-banned-list) tournament play structure, spurring conversations in the community we felt it best to [restructure](http://wizardsmtgo.tumblr.com/post/160343614814/update-mtgo-commander) the format starting from *Hour of Devastation* on out.


Play the upcoming 1v1 Commander tournaments on *Magic Online* starting tomorrow, May 10th!

**Upcoming Standard PTQ for Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation* on Saturday, May 13**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! The weekly suite of Preliminaries on Thursday through Tuesday, May 11 through May 16, will take place at their normal times. We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, May 13!


Remember, you are no longer required to enter into the PTQ Finals immediately following the Preliminary that you qualified through, thanks to the redesigned PTQ system that *Magic Online* Events Manager Lee Sharpe announced [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/pro-tour-qualifier-changes-2015-11-16).


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#HOU_schedule).

**Build Notes**


Modern | First and foremost, Anafenza, the Foremost’s replacement ability will no longer exile creature tokens.


General | Magic Online player rewards for April have been granted. Those cards are [listed in this article.](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-announcements-april-4-2017#item5)


Duel Scene | Players that drop from an event before it starts on longer appear in the event detail’s player list.


Limited | [Hazardous Conditions](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hazardous+Conditions) now correctly reduces the power and toughness of creatures without counters.


Limited | Split cards can now be cast using Oracle’s Vault second activated ability.


Limited | [Clone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clone)s put onto the battlefield with [See the Unwritten](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=See+the+Unwritten) will no longer prevent the game from progressing.


Limited | [Banefire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banefire) will not be countered or prevented if X is 5 or greater.


Limited | Graft will now trigger when creatures opponents control enter the battlefield.


Limited | If a spell or ability is cast in response to Fortune’s Favor, the piles will now be correctly indicated.


General | Zooming in on a card exiled by [Shelldock Isle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shelldock+Isle) and friends no longer shows a basic Swamp.


General | Glorious End’s delayed trigger now correctly ends if a player finds a way to survive losing the game the first time.


Limited | [Power Conduit](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Power+Conduit) no longer resets the game.


Commander | The Commander format has been updated as featured in [this article.](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/1v1-commander-banned-list)


Commander | Split cards are now the correct color identity in all cases.


General | Fixed several crashes in Collection and Limited Deck Building
