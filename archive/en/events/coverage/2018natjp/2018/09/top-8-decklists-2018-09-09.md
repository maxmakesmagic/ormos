Top 8 Decklists of the 2018 Japan Nationals
===========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 10, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/2018natjp/JPNATS2018_Top8_Group.jpg)


After seven rounds of Standard and six rounds of *Core Set 2019* Booster Draft, these eight players advanced to the playoffs. Will their Standard deck help them earn a spot at the upcoming World *Magic* Cup? Let’s take a quick look at the archetypes in the elimination rounds.


* Naoya Nanba (Black-Red Aggro)
* So Iwabuchi (Black-Red Aggro)
* Ken Yukuhiro (Mono-Blue Aggro)
* Takashi Boku (Black-Red Aggro)
* Kenta Harane (Blue-Black Midrange)
* Yuuya Watanabe (Black-Red Aggro)
* Masahide Moriyama (Black-Red Midrange)
* Kazuhiro Suzuki (Esper Control)
