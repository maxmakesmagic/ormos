Akroma-in-Art Quiz
==================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 13, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












When Phage killed Ixidor's love, Ixidor created the ultimate instrument of revenge: **[Akroma, Angel of Wrath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroma%2C+Angel+of+Wrath)**, a terrifying angel who bore the face of his slain lover.


Akroma was such an influential figure in the *Onslaught* Block storyline that she appeared in many different pieces of art in those sets. See if you can identify the card depicted by its art, below. Click once for a hint—click a second time for the answer.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1098_rex.jpg)  
*Art by Adam Rex*
This piece shows Akroma sheltering a pair of refugee clerics with her wings.


![Akroma's Blessing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Akroma%27s+Blessing)


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1098_cavotta.jpg)  
*Art by Matt Cavotta*
This piece shows Ixidor surrounded by magical images of himself doing things in his future, including pleading with his creation, Akroma.


![Future Sight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Future+Sight)


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1098_spears.jpg)  
*Art by Ron Spears*
This piece shows Akroma hanging on a happy dude as they emerge from Nevinyrral's Disco. (Note that Phage the Untouchable is also hanging on the dude—he may not be happy for long.)


![Ladies' Knight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Ladies%27+Knight)


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/arcana1000/1098_wilson.jpg)  
*Art by Matthew D. Wilson*
This piece depicts an awestruck barbarian falling to his knees upon seeing the terrible glory of Akroma.


![pacifism](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Pacifism)
