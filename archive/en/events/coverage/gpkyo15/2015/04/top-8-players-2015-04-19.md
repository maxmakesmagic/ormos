Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 19, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/benswa-author.jpg)
By Ben Swartz












### Kazuya Murakami


**Age, Hometown, Occupation:** 23, Aichi Prefecture, student


**Previous Accomplishments:** none


**Where do you normally play *Magic*?** Table Game Square “Draw”


**What deck did you play?** Counter Top Mentor


**Why did you choose it?** Because I wanted to win with an original deck.


**What was your record?**


Byes: 2


Day 1: 8-1


Day 2: 5-0-1


**What was your best sideboard card and what sideboard card did you wish you had?** [Flusterstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flusterstorm); a fourth [Meddling Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage)


**What's your favorite *Magic* card of all time?** [Cruel Ultimatum](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cruel+Ultimatum)




---




### Kei Umehara


**Age, Hometown, Occupation:** 25, Ōsaka, full-time company employee


**Previous Accomplishments:** Top 8, Grand Prix Yokohama 2012


**Where do you normally play *Magic*?** Project Core, KMC


**What deck did you play?** Temur Delver


**Why did you choose it?** Because someone told me it was like Faeries


**What was your record?**


Byes: 1


Day 1: 8-1


Day 2: 5-1


**What was your best sideboard card, and what sideboard card did you wish you had?** [Price of Progress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Price+of+Progress); [Dismember](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember)


**What's your favorite *Magic* card of all time?** [Cryptic Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command)—it pretty much solves any problem  

 




---


### Shōta Yasooka


**Age, Hometown, Occupation:** 30, Tōkyō, pro *Magic* player


**Previous Accomplishments:** Finalist, Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir*


**Where do you normally play *Magic*?** Online


**What deck did you play?** Omnitell


**Why did you chose it?** It seemed to be the strongest


**What was your record?**


Byes: 3


Day 1: 9-0


Day 2: 4-2


**What was your best sideboard card, and what sideboard card did you wish you had?** I wish I had included [Mindbreak Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindbreak+Trap)


**What's your favorite *Magic* card of all time?** Island. The best!  

 




---


### Kai Sawatari (Thiele)


**Age, Hometown, Occupation:** 23, Tōkyō, card shop employee (TōkyōMTG)


**Previous Accomplishments:** Eternal Pragoe


**Where do you normally play *Magic*?** TōkyōMTG


**What deck did you play?** Ad Nauseam Storm


**Why did you choose it?** It's the one I'm most familiar with, and it's strong against OmniTell and UR Delver!


**What was your record?**


Byes: 0


Day 1: 8-1


Day 2: 5-1


**What was your best sideboard card, and what sideboard card did you wish you had?** [Flusterstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flusterstorm); [Young Pyromancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Young+Pyromancer)


**What's your favorite *Magic* card of all time?** [Rain of Filth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rain+of+Filth). It's a macho card.  

 




---


### Yūta Takahashi


**Age, Hometown, Occupation:** 29, Tōkyō, card shop employee (Hareru-ya)


**Previous Accomplishments:** I've played a lot on Day 1 of Pro Tours.


**Where do you normally play *Magic*?** Hareru-ya


**What deck did you play?** UW Miracles


**Why did you choose it?** It has the best matchups—there are very few decks it has trouble with


**What was your record?**


Byes: 2


Day 1: 8-1


Day 2: 5-1


**What was your best sideboard card, and what sideboard card did you wish you had?** [Venser, Shaper Savant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Venser%2C+Shaper+Savant), because it's strong against Omnitell; [Dack Fayden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dack+Fayden), because I really should have at least one anti-artifact card


**What's your favorite *Magic* card of all time?** [Cryptic Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cryptic+Command)—most of my epic wins have come from it.

**What was your record?**

**What's your favorite *Magic* card of all time?** [Urza's Rage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Rage)—it was my favorite as a kid
