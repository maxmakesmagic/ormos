Top 8: Paco Llopis
==================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPPORTO00/Images/208.jpg)**Name:** Paco Llopis  
**Age:** 22  
**Location:**  Valencia, Spain  
**Occupation:** Student/Audiovisual  
**Played Magic since:** The Dark, 1994  
**Best accomplishments:** Top 32 PT Chicago 1999, Top 64 LA 2000, Spanish Champion 1998.



Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Porto?  

A: It's near Spain, and I like to compete in big championships.



Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?  

A: To make some money.



Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.  

A: I have played very quietly and I have constructed good decks.



Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?  

A: I have been playing quite good lately and I have trained for this environment with my team.



Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?  

A: Satisfaction.



Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?  

A: I'm trying to win.
