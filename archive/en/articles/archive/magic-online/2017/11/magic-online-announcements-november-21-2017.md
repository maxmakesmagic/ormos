***Iconic Masters*now available on *Magic Online*!**


*Iconic Masters*is now available on *Magic Online*! Available in the *Magic Online* store for $6.99 per booster pack, *Iconic Masters*released simultaneously with tabletop *Magic*on Friday, November 17, and will be available until December 6.


*Magic Online*will feature two Draft leagues for this release, regular and phantom.


More information about the *Magic Online*release of *Iconic Masters*can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/iconic-masters-comes-magic-online).

***Iconic Masters*Sealed PTQ Open this Friday, November 24**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in this PTQ Open on Friday, November 24 at 7 a.m. PT.


Join this event with 50 Tix or 500 Play Points.


Learn more about *Iconic Masters* on *Magic Online* [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/iconic-masters-comes-magic-online)*.*

***Magic Online*Modern RPTQ for Pro Tour *Rivals of Ixalan*this Saturday, November 25**


A *Magic Online*Regional Pro Tour Qualifier (RPTQ) for Pro Tour *Rivals ofIxalan*will take place on Saturday, November 25. These events are only open to players who qualified via in-store Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifier events—pre-registration is also required.


Additional details about Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers can be found [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/protour/regionalqualifiers).

***Ixalan*Sealed MOCS Monthly this Sunday, November 26**


A *Magic Online*Championship Series (MOCS) *Ixalan*Sealed Monthly Event is coming up on November 26! Players can enter the event with 35 Qualifier Points (QPs), and top finishers qualify for Playoff Events in the 2018 series.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points for the year. Top players on the [Leaderboard](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/leaderboard) at the end of the 2018 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.


Click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2018-magic-online-championship-series) for more information about the 2018 MOCS events.
