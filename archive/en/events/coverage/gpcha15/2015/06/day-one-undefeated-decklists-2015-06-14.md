Day 1 Undefeated Decklists
==========================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX CHARLOTTE 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpcha15)
 on June 14, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











Modern is a format where you can play nine different decks in Day 1 of a Grand Prix, and our selection of undefeated decks show that on any given day a skilled player wielding a deck they know well can beat anything they come across. With eight distinct archetypes in the nine decks that went undefeated in Day 1, there's no question that Modern is still a wide-open format in which anyone who arrives prepared can find success.
