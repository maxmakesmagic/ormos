Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013 Official Terms
==============================================


The Magic: The Gathering - Duels of the Planeswalkers Campaign 2013 (“Campaign”) is a Campaign offered by Wizards of the Coast LLC (“Wizards” or “Sponsor”). The Campaign starts on June 19, 2012 and ends December 13, 2012. The Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013 game (the “Game”) availability may vary by platform and region.

-------------------

-------------------------

All you have to do is use the URL link with the provided redemption code to register on Wizards’ web site located at [www.wizards.com/Magic2013Xbox](http://www.wizards.com/Magic2013Xbox) (for Xbox LIVE), [www.wizards.com/Magic2013Playstation](http://www.wizards.com/Magic2013Playstation) (for the Playstation Network), [www.wizards.com/Magic2013Steam](http://www.wizards.com/Magic2013Steam) (for Steam) or [www.wizards.com/Magic2013iPad](http://www.wizards.com/Magic2013iPad) (for iPad) on or after 12:01 a.m. PT on June 19, 2012 and before 11:59 p.m. PT on December 13, 2012. --------------


Every eligible customer will receive a voucher for a special Magic 2013 booster pack containing an exclusive alternate-art mythic rare for each platform. The alternate-art mythic rare in the Magic 2013 boosters will be distributed as follows: a voucher for a special Magic 2013 booster pack containing an alternate-art Primordial Hydra will be provided to eligible customers who purchased the Xbox LIVE® Arcade version of the Game, a voucher for a special Magic 2013 booster pack containing an alternate-art Serra Avatar will be provided to eligible customers who purchased the Steam Games or the iPad® version of the Game, and a voucher for a special Magic 2013 booster pack containing an alternate-art Vampire Nocturnus will be provided to eligible customers who purchased the PlayStation® Network version of the Game.
