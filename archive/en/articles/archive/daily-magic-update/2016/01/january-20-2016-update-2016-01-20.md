The January 20, 2016 Update
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on January 20, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know for today, January 20, 2016. Today's Update is brought to you by [Frank Karsten's super math](http://www.channelfireball.com/articles/magic-math-oath-of-the-gatewatch/) and [the year 2004](#2004).

Absolutely the Last Bannings-Related Item I'll Post. Maybe.
===========================================================


1. [SPLINTER TWIN BANNED?! (also Summer Bloom](http://www.rocketjump.com/listen/10897)) | Masters of Modern | Alex Kessler and Ben Bateman

The Masters of Modern, Alex Kessler and Ben Bateman, have been [very active on Twitter spurring discussion of the bannings in Modern](https://twitter.com/theMMcast), but now those discussions get verbal as the pair podcasts their thoughts away in the latest episode.


That said, if you're really salivating for more content on the Modern bannings, [StarCityGames.com](http://www.starcitygames.com/) has several premium entries today, including articles from both Shaun McLaren and Sam Black. The Sam Black one in particular is worth reading (again, if you have access to premium content), as he spoke with *Magic* Principle Game Designer Erik Lauer for some inside info.
