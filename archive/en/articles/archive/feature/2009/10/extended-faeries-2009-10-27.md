Faeries just won't die! After dominating for two years in Standard, the deck continues to see play in Extended. Jonas Wallendorf recently played this version to a respectable finish at Pro Tour–Austin. His build snuck a green splash in for the powerful [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), making it similar to many Next Level Blue builds that control the board without any Faeries at all. Could Jonas's deck prove to be the key to beating the powerful new Extended season? Find out at your upcoming Extended PTQs! Read more from Pro Tour–Austin in the [official event coverage](/en/events/coverage/kibler-completes-comeback-austin-victory).
