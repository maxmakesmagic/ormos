Round 9: Dion Stratford v Richard Grace
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 4, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_1.png)
By Dan Gow











Dion won the roll and elected to draw, being kindly reminded not to screw up by his New Zealand counterpart, "This match is covered" he remarked with a smile.

### Game 1

Ox opened with a [Leonin Den-Guard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Den-Guard) and a [Slith Ascendent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slith+Ascendent) which was matched by a Raise the Alarm and a Slith Ascendent of his own. Dion offers to the trade the fliers but a [Razor Barrier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Razor+Barrier) protect Graces interests.

Dion rallied with a [Goblin War Wagon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+War+Wagon), but Ox attacked to the unguarded skies with the slith, giving it a counter and the momentum it needs.

A [Vulshok Gauntlets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vulshok+Gauntlets) on the War Wagon caused some concern for Grace, chumping with the Den-Guard which he replaced with another. The second time the attack came thorough he was able to double block with the second Den-Guard and a [Soldier Replica](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soldier+Replica) and trade.

Grace's deck of white fliers with black support again took to the sky with a [Skyhunter Patrol](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyhunter+Patrol), bringing Stratford down to 13, but a [Loxodon Warhammer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Loxodon+Warhammer) scored him 4 life back when he attacked with one of his soldier tokens. 

The Skyhunter was equipped with a [Mask of Memory](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mask+of+Memory) enabling Grace to draw into an [Icy Manipulator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Icy+Manipulator) to deal with the Warhammer. A [Pewter Golem](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pewter+Golem) was also bought into to defend, but Stratford had been gaining 4 life a turn with Grace only dealing 2 damage on the transition.

Needing to clear the board, Grace drew into [Barter in Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barter+in+Blood) but still had the problem of trading on the life scores 17-4. Moving the mask onto a [Skyhunter Cub](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyhunter+Cub), gave him all the cards he needed.

An [Arrest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrest) meant that Stratford had no blockers and within 2 turns of the [Leonin Sun Standard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Sun+Standard), the game had swung in favour of Grace whop made no mistake in cleaning up the mess.

Grace 1 - Stratford 0

### Game 2

Stratford elected to play and both players re happy with their opening hands. Stratford jumped out from the blocks with a quick start with a [Viridian Longbow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Longbow), an [Iron Myr](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iron+Myr) and then a third turn [Skyhunter Patrol](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyhunter+Patrol), significantly better than Grace's [Leonin Den-Guard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Den-Guard). Grace follows with a [Moriok Scavenger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moriok+Scavenger), after attacking for 1 with the Den-Guard.

Trying to keep pace with Stratford, Grace attacked with an unequipped Den-Guard with a Skyhunter, but Dion was ready with a [Raise The Alarm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raise+The+Alarm), killing the Den Guard.

The fifth turn saw a bigger drop in the [Ogre Leadfoot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ogre+Leadfoot) of Stratford compared to the [Wizard Replica](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wizard+Replica) of Grace. It was all situational of course as the lead foot was promptly [Arrest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrest)ed and the Wizard equipped with the [Mask of Memory](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mask+of+Memory).

The clock is turned back in Dion's favour when the [Arrest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrest) was removed with an [Altar's Light](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Altar%27s+Light), and with no blockers he attacked all out, leaving Grace on 6 life. Bringing Dion to 13 Grace pushes forward with the wizard to gain card advantage, saving a [Terror](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terror) for the Skyhunter and another [Arrest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arrest) for the repeat offend Leadfoot.

Gaining more cards from the [Mask of Memory](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mask+of+Memory) sadly meant that Grace had no blockers left, and after Stratford had moved the longbow around the turn before, won with 3 more damage from a [Stalking Stone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stalking+Stone).

Startford 1 - Grace 1

### Game 3

"I'll start this one dude" was the call from Grace, as he failed to find a land in the first 7 cards and of course elected to Paris. His next 6 cards were seemingly playable with 2 land, all he needed was a slightly forgiving start from Stratford. That wasn't the case. A second turn Myr gave and a 3rd turn Warhammer. Adding that to the fact that Grace couldn't find a third land, this easily allowed Stratford to drag Grace down to 19, then 15, then to 11. Following with a [Leonin Den-Guard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Den-Guard) ([Terror](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terror)ed) and a [Soldier Replica](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soldier+Replica), Grace's belated fourth land cleard the board for Grace with a [Barter In Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barter+In+Blood), but the pressure didn't stop with Stratford pouring on the pressure with a [Skyhunter Patrol](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyhunter+Patrol), attacking the following turn to drop him to 6...

With his removal used and Dion's 2/3 flier being given a set of [Vulshok Gauntlet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vulshok+Gauntlet)s in addition to the "whammer" Grace could do little as the 10/5 flying, trampling menace came in for the win.

Stratford 2 defeats Grace 1
