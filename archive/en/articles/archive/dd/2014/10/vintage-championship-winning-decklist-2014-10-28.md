Vintage Championship Winning Decklist
=====================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on October 28, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










Congratulations to Mark Tocco on winning the 2014 Vintage Championship!



For his victory, Mark receives a unique, framed, oversized version of [Mox Pearl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Pearl) with a new piece of art by [Raoul Vitale](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5b%22Raoul%20Vitale%22%5d).
