2013 Magic Online Championship Event Information
================================================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles?source=MX_Nav2020)
 on February 3, 2014 











San Francisco
-------------


### March 28-30, 2014


### Quick Links


* [Viewer Information](#viewer)
* [Player Information](#player)

The **Magic Online**  Championship features sixteen players battling for a share of more than $100,000 in cash prizes in the Standard, Modern, Cube Draft, and *Theros/Born of the Gods* Booster Draft formats. Tune in for the live webcast starting Friday morning for complete access to the **Magic Online**  Championship: live match coverage, player interviews, and much more!


The sixteen 2013 **Magic Online** Championship finalists include:


* The defending 2012 **Magic Online** champion, Dmitriy Butakov
* Twelve individual **Magic Online** Championship Series season winners
* Two MOCS Player-of-the-Year Championship winners
* One MOCS Last-Chance Qualifier winner

Think you have the skills to make it to the 2014 **Magic Online**  Championship? The 2013 **Magic Online** Championship Series is under way now. Visit [this page](/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/110413/mocs2014) for more information and a full schedule of season championships.

**Media Requests**   

Please send an email to [press@wizards.com](mailto:press@wizards.com) if you wish to set up a media credential for the **Magic Online**  Championship. Wizards of the Coast asks that all media requests be submitted by March 14 (two weeks prior to the event). Requests made after that date, including requests made on-site, may be denied.


Media check-in will take place Thursday, March 27 from 4 p.m. - 7 p.m. If you cannot attend the check-in period, please make arrangements via the [press@wizards.com](mailto:press@wizards.com) email address prior to the event.


The **Magic Online**  Championship is not open to the public.

The 2013 **Magic Online**  Championship is a three-day invitation-only[Standard](/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/sfrstandard), [Modern](/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/sfrmodern), Cube Draft, and *Born of the Gods/Theros* Booster Draft format event with Standard Top 4 playoff.


**Format**


 

* [Standard](/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/sfrstandard), [Modern](/Magic/TCG/Resources.aspx?x=judge/resources/sfrmodern), Cube Draft, and *Theros/Born of the Gods* Booster Draft
* [Cube Draft card list](/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg_daily_other_03032014_MOCS2013ChampandCube#cardlist%20)

**Tournament Length**


 

* Day One will consist of three rounds of Cube Draft followed by four rounds of Modern
* Day Two will consist of three rounds of *Theros/Born of the Gods*  Booster Draft, followed by four rounds of Standard, followed by a cut to the Top 4 playoff
* The Top 4 playoff will be Standard format. Players are required to use the same Standard deck as on Day Two.
* All matches will be best two of three.

 


 


**Schedule**


**Friday**  

9 a.m. – 9:45 a.m.: Player registration at event site  

10 a.m.: Day One play begins


**Saturday**  

10 a.m.: Day Two play begins


**Sunday**  

Noon: Top 4 playoff play begins
