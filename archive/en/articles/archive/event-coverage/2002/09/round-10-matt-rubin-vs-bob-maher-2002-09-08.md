Round 10: Matt Rubin vs. Bob Maher
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 8, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_507.png)
By Brian Kowal











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcle02/a926.jpg)Matt Rubin is one of the rising players on the Pro Tour. Just a couple months ago he hit his first top 8 at US nationals. He was still undefeated in the GP at 6-0-3 with his mono black control deck. His deck was fairly standard. Bob was playing the same deck he played the week before at GP London. His own version of Zombie/[Upheaval](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval) that sent at least two people to Day 2.

Normally this would be a good matchup for Bob. Mono black has no real answer to [Upheaval](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval). Matt came out pretty quickly early with a pair of [Nantuko Shade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade)s though and Bob quickly found himself on the ropes with no black mana to remove them the first three turns. Bob found a [Darkwater Catacombs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darkwater+Catacombs) turn four and took a mana burn to [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood) away one of the Shades. Matt still was able to knock him to 1 with the remaining Shade. Bob found himself with an [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood) and only the filter land but he tapped out for [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) and found the [Swamp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) he needed to Blood the Shade pain free. Matt made a play on the next turn he later said he should not have made. He played [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth) on Bob's filter land when all he would need was Threshold to turn it into a win condition. Bob played a [Standstill](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill) the next turn which stood for a long time. Rubin waited ten turns before finally breaking the [Standstill](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Standstill) with a [Tainted Pact](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tainted+Pact). 

Rubin had 20 mana in play thanks to many swamps and a pair of [Cabal Coffers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Coffers). Bob was definitely in the danger zone this turn. Rubin played Tutor and then [Mirari](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari) which Bob let resolve. A choice the [Upheaval](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval) deck almost never makes. Next he attempted to play a Sludge and copy it, but Bob had a pair of Logics to answer the two Mind Twists. Bob untapped and played [Upheaval](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval). He still had no [Zombie Infestation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Infestation). He got out a turn two [Compulsion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compulsion), and now seemed to be on track to winning the game. He started setting up for a war with Rubin. First he played [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) for an [Envelop](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Envelop) and the next turn Wished again for [Flash of Insight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flash+of+Insight). 

Matt came in with the must-counter threats turn after turn. First a [Mirari](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari), then a [Mind Sludge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Sludge) the next turn followed by a [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth). Bob had to flashed back the Flash for 15 cards, found an [Envelop](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Envelop) and put the much needed [Zombie Infestation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Infestation) as close to the top as he could. He had five cards between him and the victory condition now. Bob still appeared to be ok. He had a Logic in hand and his last [Envelop](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Envelop) was one of the four cards he hadn't seen yet by the time Matt took his turn. Matt Tutored and played his last two [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth)s. Bob searched for the [Envelop](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Envelop), but it was the last card in his deck before the [Zombie Infestation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Infestation).

"All I needed was a turn" Bob told me.

Rubin 1, Maher 0

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcle02/a930.jpg)The next game Maher got his Infestation right away on turn 2. Rubin got his own Infestation, but Maher had more card drawing thanks to his [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Matt played a Braids, but Maher had the Logic. His tokens stalled Matt long enough for Bob to get to 8 mana. Bob unleashed the vicious [Upheaval](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Upheaval)/Infestation combo turn 8. Rubin was unable to stop the Zombie Horde.

Rubin 1, Maher 1

The clock had only 3 minutes left, but the shuffled up for game 3 anyway. Bob found a turn two Infestation again, but this time Rubin was able to resolve Braids. Bob had an Edict, but it was still a valuable 2 for 1 for Rubin with the Infestation in play. Rubin resolved [Mirari](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari) the next turn and then proceeded to [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth) away both of Bob's blue sources. Bobs only remaining option was to beat down with his zombies and the [Faceless Butcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) he played after the [Mirari](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari). Time was called, and it looked like Bob might be able to squeak out the win. Rubin copied a 2 point [Skeletal Scrying](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skeletal+Scrying) and played his own [Zombie Infestation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Infestation). Time was called and Rubin was able to hold off Bob for the remaining turns to earn his 4th draw of the tournament.

**Final Result:** Maher and Rubin draw 1-1
