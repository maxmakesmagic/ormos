It may be some time before the world of paper **Magic** adopts *Shards of Alara* Block Constructed, but players are already enjoying the exciting format on **Magic Online**. Bös managed to win a recent Premier Event with a very straightforward White Weenie list. [Akrasan Squire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akrasan+Squire)? [Knight-Captain of Eos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight-Captain+of+Eos)? Even Antoine Ruel's Invitational winning card [Ranger of Eos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ranger+of+Eos) manages to find its way into the list. For more exciting formats you can play around the clock, including *Shards of Alara* Block Constructed, check out [**Magic Online**](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/Digital/Default.aspx).
