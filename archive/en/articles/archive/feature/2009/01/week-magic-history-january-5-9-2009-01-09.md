This Week in Magic History: January 5-9
=======================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 9, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration//sideboard/images/gpno03/a921.jpg)On January 4, 2003, Zvi Mowshowitz wins Grand Prix New Orleans piloting his pet deck TurboLand. In short (though nothing about TurboLand is short) Zvi combined [Exploration](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exploration) and [Horn of Greed](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Horn+of+Greed) to generate massive amounts of mana and cards, capping things off with repeating [Time Warp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Time%2BWarp) thanks to drawing his whole deck and then putting the Warps back in his deck courtesy of [Battlefield Scrounger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlefield+Scrounger). Here's the famous deck in all its mad genius glory:

### Also This Week


* The first week of January, 2002, the **magicthegathering.com** web site officially launches. The initial site features five columnists, of which only [Mark Rosewater](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Archive.aspx?author=Mark%20Rosewater) still remains.
* **January 9, 2005:** Team P.S.2 (Masahiro Kuroda, Katzuhiro Mori, and Masahito Morita) [wins GP Osaka](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/gposaka05/welcome), managing an impressive repeat as Team Limited Grand Prix Champions.
