### 50 Reasons to Play in the [*Ravnica* Release Events](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/magiconline/article101705)

1. Gleemax commands you. Resistance is futile.
2. Popping packs just for singles isn't nearly as much fun.
3. Those gold-bordered *Ravnica* Release splash screens when MTGO fired up intrigued you. Admit it, baby.
4. RAV Starter + RAV booster + RAV booster = a *huuuuuge* stack of all brand new cards!
5. Play in a release league, get Loxodon Hierarch, the participation avatar. He's dressed really sharp too, unlike his exhibitionist cousin the token Elephant avatar.  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/MTGO_loxodon.jpg)
6. Go 5-0 in a release league, win Sisters of Stone Death, the prize avatar! (Just in time for Golgari Week.) She looks cool too, but... where are the other sisters? *Look out behind you!!*  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/MTGO_sisters.jpg)
7. Maybe you'll crack a foil [Sacred Foundry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sacred+Foundry) and be able to sell it and live off the interest for the rest of your natural born days. Or at least fill up your gas tank.
8. Play in a release flight, get Loxodon Hierarch, the participation avatar. Check out how high he levitates **Magic** cards!
9. Play in a 2x release flight, make top 8 and win RAV packs!

### Minty Fresh Vanguardness!

Okay, so now you've gotten your hands on the new avatars, what sort of decks are you going to build around them? Nice question! But first—speaking of avatars, has anyone seen the animated show [Avatar: The Last Airbender](http://www.nick.com/all_nick/tv_supersites/display_show.jhtml?show_id=ava)? I've run across it on one of the Nick channels my kids watch and it's actually a pretty cool show.

Anyway, back to the quest at hand: deckbuilding! Below, I'm going to present my first deck ideas for Ravnica Vanguard, but I'd love to hear what ideas you may have! Drop me an email and I'll post a couple of them next week.



|  |
| --- |
| **Participation Avatar: Loxodon Hierarch** Vanguard abilities: Hand +0, Life +1 Sacrifice a permanent: Regenerate target creature you control  |

So, what do we do with this ability? Cramming your deck full of “Destroy all creatures" effects pops to mind, but watch out for the “no regenerate” clause. [Akroma's Vengeance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroma%27s+Vengeance), [Void](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Void), [Engineered Explosives](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Explosives), [Oblivion Stone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Stone), [Pernicious Deed](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pernicious+Deed), [Myojin of Cleansing Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Myojin+of+Cleansing+Fire), [Plague Boiler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plague+Boiler). Sacrifice a few permanents to keep some of your key creatures in play and break the symmetry of these otherwise symmetrical creature-sweepers.

Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Festering+Goblin), [Ashen-Skin Zubera](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashen-Skin+Zubera) (and his
