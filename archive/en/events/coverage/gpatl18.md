Day 2 Coverage



Yesterday, after eight rounds of Swiss, the original field of 1,553 players had been whittled down to just 317. Overnight, 12 players sat on top of the standings with perfect 8-0 records: Marcelino Freeman, Martin Jůza, Goncalo Pinto, Michael Bonde, Yam Wing Chun, Caleb Scherer, James Baggett, Thomas Lee, Thiago Rodrigues, Jacob Ballington, Sol Malka, and Florian Trotte. Among the undefeated decks are 2 Humans, 2 W/U Control, and 2 Dredge.


Today, seven more rounds of Modern are left to play before the cut to the Top 8. We' ll have live coverage on [twitch.tv/magic](http://twitch.tv/magic) for you all day with commentary from Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa, Marshall Sutcliffe, Eduardo Sajgalik, and Brian David-Marshall.







Day 1 Coverage



Earlier this morning, 1,553 players arrived at the Cobb Galeria Centre in Atlanta to show off their Modern skills and decks. After eight rounds of Swiss, 317 of them have made the cut for Day 2, including twelve players at 8-0:Marcelino Freeman, Martin Jůza, Goncalo Pinto, Michael Bonde, Yam Wing Chun, Caleb Scherer, James Baggett, Thomas Lee, Thiago Rodrigues, Jacob Ballington, Sol Malka, and Florian Trotte.


The 8-0 deck archetypes are 2x Humans, 2x W/U Control, 2x Dredge, Bogles, Hollow One, Jund, Storm, Jeskai Control, and The Rock.


For tomorrow, all competitors effectively get an extra hour of sleep, as Daylight Savings time ends tonight in Atlanta. Tune back to the livestream tomorrow at 9 a.m. local time (ET)—thats 6 a.m. PT/2 p.m. UTC—for seven more rounds of Swiss and the Top 8 of Grand Prix Atlanta 2018!
