Draft 1: Tsuyoshi Fujita
========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 16, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett












Tsuyoshi Fujita is doing his darndest to quash his reputation for being a one-hit wonder. After cutting a path through Pro Tour - Tokyo only to fall to Champion Zvi Mowshowitz in the finals, he has found himself without a major accomplishment. This weekend he's worked to turn things around, ending up at the top table for the first draft thanks to three byes and four match wins. On his left was Japanese Sideboard Reporter Masashiro Kuroda, and he was being fed by Yusuke Sasaki of Sendai.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpfuku02/921.jpg)Fujita wasted no time taking [Concentrate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Concentrate) out of his first pack, leaving nothing of interest in blue. He followed up with [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) and then [Krosan Archer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Archer), though a [Hallowed Healer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hallowed+Healer) caught his eye. The problem was that his precious blue was being gobbled by Sasaki, who had take [Aven Windreader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Windreader), Looter and [Pulsating Illusion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulsating+Illusion). He let an [Aven Fisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Fisher) through, which was just enough to keep Fujita interested. He continued to show his disdain for white, passing up [Shelter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shelter) for [Krosan Archer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Archer), and [Embolden](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embolden) for [Think Tank](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Think+Tank).


Meanwhile, Sasaki refused to commit to a second color, what with all the blue cards falling into his pile. Of course, Kuroda was reaping the rewards of this confusion. Red and black cards flowed like wine, and he scrambled to scoop up as many of them as he could.


Pack two was better for Fujita, but not by much. He started out with [Seton's Desire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seton%27s+Desire) over [Nantuko Disciple](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Disciple), then [Puppeteer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Puppeteer) over [Rabid Elephant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rabid+Elephant) and [Aven Fisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Fisher). Then came a weak pack, and he chose [Blessed Orator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blessed+Orator) over [Leaf Dancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leaf+Dancer), realizing that his colors were being poached. He got a [Nantuko Disciple](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Disciple), but then chose [Aven Flock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Flock) over the hard-to-cast [Chlorophant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chlorophant) and [Krosan Archer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Archer). [Twigwalker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Twigwalker) and a pair of [Refresh](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Refresh)es rounded things out.


When he cracked his *Torment*, Fujita saw Chainer staring back at him. He shook his fist at the legend, then took [Nantuko Calmer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Calmer). [Gurzigost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gurzigost) made him feel a little better, but next door Sasaki was plucking a [Possessed Aven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Possessed+Aven).


After a third-pick [Deep Analysis](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis) things went downhill fast. He ended up with a stack of Liquifies. [Stern Judge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stern+Judge) was the only pick of note. He spent most of pack three just shaking his head in bemusement. He kept on shaking through construction, where he stitched together this monstroity. He was not confident in his chances.
