Donnie Noland is a big **Magic** fan. After all, in addition to being a player he's also a premier tournament organizer (he ran [Grand Prix–Nashville](/en/events/coverage/xmas-comes-early-gerry-t) this past weekend!). But when he isn't organizing events, Donnie is busy winning them. He used this Blue-Black-Green Control deck to win the Kentucky Open, a Standard tournament that draws hundreds of players to compete. Nice job Donnie!
