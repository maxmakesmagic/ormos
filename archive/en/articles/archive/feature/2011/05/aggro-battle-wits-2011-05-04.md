[Battle of Wits](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battle+of+Wits) has made sporadic appearances at competitive events over the year, but never before has the game seen a version quite like this one. Kenny Mayer played an Aggro Battle of Wits strategy to the Top 32 of the StarCityGames.com Legacy Open in Charlotte, North Carolina. What makes Kenny's deck unique is that he's trying to attack as his main method of winning, with the [Battle of Wits](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battle+of+Wits) "combo" being a solid Plan B. Read more about the event in the [coverage](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/21740_Live_Coverage_of_StarCityGamescom_Open_Series_Charlotte.html) at StarCityGames.com.
