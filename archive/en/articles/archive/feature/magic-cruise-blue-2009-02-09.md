In honor of the Magic Cruise, which set sail Saturday from Texas for southern Mexico, Daily Decks presents a Magic Cruise themed deck! Designed for the 60 card Highlander/Singleton format, every card in the deck is centered around an oceanic voyage. Be it the boats ([Ghost Ship](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghost+Ship), [Pirate Ship](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pirate+Ship), [Skeleton Ship](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skeleton+Ship)...), the scenery ([Underground Sea](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underground+Sea), [Volcanic Island](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Island), [Coral Atoll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coral+Atoll)...), or the supporting cast ([Spiny Starfish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spiny+Starfish), [Segovian Leviathan](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Segovian+Leviathan), [Electric Eel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electric+Eel)), every card contributes to some element of the theme. That's one of the great things about **Magic**! Not only can you have a great time building the most broken decks for the upcoming tournament, but you can relax with a more light hearted, less competitive exercise like building a theme deck to commemorate a cool **Magic** experience like the Game in the Gulf cruise!
