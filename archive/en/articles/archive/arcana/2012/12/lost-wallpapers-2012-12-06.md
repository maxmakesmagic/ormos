===================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 6, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley

And these are wallpapers featuring art that was specifically commissioned for Art Fight! So here are the ten guild wallpapers that were originally going to be distributed there, plus the *Dragon's Maze* wallpaper that actually was!
