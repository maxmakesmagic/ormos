Round 7: Osyp Lebedowicz vs. Joseph Crosby
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 17, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_626.png)
By Victor van den Broek











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a864.jpg)These two gentlemen were undefeated so far and had to battle it out to see who got to draft on table 1 tomorrow for sure. Both of them are of Togit/CMU affiliation – making that a total of 5 people from that team battling it out on the feature match tables this round. Talk about dominating the tournament!

### Game 1

Osyp won the flip but it was Joseph with the first play, a [Goblin Taskmaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Taskmaster). A [Wirewood Savage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Savage) and [Snarling Undorak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snarling+Undorak) from Osyp gave him a good position though. Joseph had cast a morph the previous turn, and attacked with just his Taskmaster on his turn. The morph turned out to be a [Disruptive Pitmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disruptive+Pitmage) – it countered Osyp's [Barkhide Mauler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Barkhide+Mauler). [Mistform Dreamer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Dreamer) was added to Joseph's side of the board. Osyp cast an [Embermage Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embermage+Goblin) to deal with the threats and he fetched his second copy of the Goblin Wizard. Joseph brought in the Taskmaster and Dreamer, beating Osyp down to 10. A [Pinpoint Avalanche](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pinpoint+Avalanche) destroyed the [Embermage Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embermage+Goblin).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a866.jpg)Osyp served with his Undorak and Savage, bringing Joseph down to 9. The second [Embermage Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embermage+Goblin) hit the board, but that got stolen due to a [Blatant Thievery](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blatant+Thievery). The Pitmage chumped the Undorak, while the Savage went unblocked. That brought both players down to 7 life. [Treespring Lorian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treespring+Lorian) was added to the mix and Joseph's Dreamer got [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)ed. Another [Blatant Thievery](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blatant+Thievery) from Joseph took Lorian. The Savage and Undorak came in once again and the two beasts trade. [Goblin Taskmaster](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Taskmaster) traded for a morphed [Skirk Commando](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Commando) and the battlefield was down to only the [Embermage Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embermage+Goblin) and [Wirewood Savage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Savage). Osyp's [Symbiotic Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Symbiotic+Elf) got [Discombobulate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Discombobulate)d and Joseph dug in his deck to find a way to stall this game out long enough to finish Osyp off with the [Embermage Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embermage+Goblin). The morph turned out to be a Charing Slateback. Joseph enchanted his [Embermage Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Embermage+Goblin) with a [Lavamancer's Skill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavamancer%27s+Skill) and finished off the Savage so the beast could finish off Osyp.

Osyp 0 – Joseph 1

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a867.jpg)[Goblin Sledder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sledder) hit the table along with a [Wirewood Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Elf) and a turn 3 morph. With Osyp having missed his third land drop, Joseph [Chain of Vapor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+of+Vapor)ed the Elf and made a [Mistform Dreamer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Dreamer). The Morph and Sledder went unblocked and Osyp replayed the [Wirewood Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Elf). All three came in next turn and [Snarling Undorak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snarling+Undorak) was unmorphed, bringing Joseph down to 10 life. Joseph had another eventless turn, and Osyp's next attack step brought Joseph down to 2 life already. In response to Joseph's [Blatant Thievery](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blatant+Thievery), Osyp [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)ed the young American and it was time for them start sideboarding again.

Osyp 1 – Joseph 1

### Game 3

Joseph's turn 2 [Imagecrafter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imagecrafter) got a Lavamancer's skill to kill off Osyp's [Wirewood Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Elf), only to be [Erratic Explosion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Erratic+Explosion)ed itself a little later. Up next was an [Ascending Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven) that got [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)ed, and a morph on Osyp's side. [Spitting Gourna](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spitting+Gourna) came down on Osyp's next turn, while Joseph cast a [Mistform Stalker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Stalker) and a morph of his own. A [Goblin Sledder](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sledder) allowed Osyp to attack with his creatures, and he unmorphed his [Snarling Undorak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snarling+Undorak). The Stalker got Skilled up, but it fell to a [Starstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Starstorm) for 2. Joseph scooped after that.

**Final Result:** Osyp 2 – Joseph 1
