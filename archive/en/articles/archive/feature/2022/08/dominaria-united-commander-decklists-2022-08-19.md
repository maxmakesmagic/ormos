Dominaria United Commander Decklists
====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 19, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*[Dominaria United](https://magic.wizards.com/products/dominaria-united)* hits stores worldwide on September 9, and for Commander fans, we have two new Commander decks featuring powerful legends, reprints, and all-new cards.


![Legends' Legacy](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/en_28NBweyydd.png)![Painbow](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/en_m2usdmweiy.png)


You can check out the cards in the [*Dominaria United* Commander Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/dominaria-united-commander), as well as the regular cards in the [*Dominaria United* Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/dominaria-united) and the [Booster Fun variants of *Dominaria United* cards](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/dominaria-united-variants).


Plus, for a rundown of all the cards across the set and where you can find them, check out the [Collecting *Dominaria United* article](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/collecting-dominaria-united-2022-08-18), which includes details on the [Lost *Legends* cards](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/lost-legends-2022-07-21)!


Look for the *Dominaria United* Commander decks at [your local game store](https://locator.wizards.com), online through [Amazon](https://gtly.to/HZV8BGWl_), and everywhere you find *Magic: The Gathering* products sold.
