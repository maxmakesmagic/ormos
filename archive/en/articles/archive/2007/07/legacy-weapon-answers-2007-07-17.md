 Posted in **Arcana**

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)

[Last week](/en/articles/archive/legacy-weapon-2007-07-10) we looked at the new Tenth Edition art of Legacy Weapon, in which Terese Nielsen embedded many, many of the Legacy artifacts into one piece of art. Today we reveal the answers.
