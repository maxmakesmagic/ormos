The debate over which colors to use in the Standard Caw-Blade deck had one more voice added to the mix as Gerry Thompson recently won the StarCityGames.com Standard Open in Dallas, Texas with this version. His solution? Adding black to the blue and white cards that are most popular. The strategy paid off and found him hoisting the champion's trophy. Read full coverage of the event [here](http://www.starcitygames.com/php/news/expandnews.php?Article=21392).
