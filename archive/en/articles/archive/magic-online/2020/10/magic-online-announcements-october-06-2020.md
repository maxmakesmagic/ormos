*Mystery Booster* on *Magic Online*
-----------------------------------


From tomorrow, October 7, until next Wednesday, October 14, players can enjoy the *Magic Online* version of one of last year's most exciting ancillary products: *Mystery Booster*! These Phantom drafts are a mostly faithful recreation of the Retail Edition of the product, working closely with game designer and original product architect Gavin Verhey to make some small tweaks for the *Magic Online* release. Curious what's in them? Well, we've got full lists for each of the slots here!
