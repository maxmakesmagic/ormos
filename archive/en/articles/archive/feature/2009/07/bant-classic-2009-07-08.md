Eternal formats, even in the digital world of **Magic Online**, isn't always about the most broken cards in the format. Tallandskinnyman proves exactly that using this blue-white-green concoction to Top 8 a recent Classic Premier Event online. The creatures are straight out of the plane of Alara: [Rhox War Monk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rhox+War+Monk), [Jenara, Asura of War](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Jenara%252C%2BAsura%2Bof%2BWar), even [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage). Proof positive that innovative deck design can be more important than powerful cards.
