Originally created by writer Richard Feldman, Red-Green Haterator features some of the strongest "hate" cards in the format to out-metagame an expected field. [Goblin Sharpshooter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sharpshooter) mows down Faeries and Elves, [Iwamori of the Open Fist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Iwamori+of+the+Open+Fist) is a cheap 5/5 in a format devoid of legends, and [Magus of the Moon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magus+of+the+Moon) punishes players relying too heavily on non-basic lands. Put it together and you have a successful strategy for **Magic Online** user newkidsontheblock who Top 8ed a recent Premier Event using the above list. But are there new hate options on the horizon? As *Conflux* joins the world of **Magic**, the world waits with bated breath.
