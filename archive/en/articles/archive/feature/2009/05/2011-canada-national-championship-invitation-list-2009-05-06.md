2011 Canada National Championship Invitation List
=================================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 6, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The Canada Nationals Invitation list comprises the following:

* The current Canadian National Champion and members of the current Canadian national team (including alternate).
* Players from Canada with Pro Tour Players Club level 2 or higher.
* Winners of invitations through National Qualifiers (including Last Chance National Qualifiers or “Grinders”).
* The Top 100 DCI-ranked players in the DCI Total format on June 1, 2011. (These invitations will be awarded on June 1, 2011. Do not book travel until your invitation appears on this list.)

All players on the list below are already qualified for Canada Nationals and are not permitted to play in Canada National Qualifiers and/or Canada Last Chance National Qualifiers (“Grinders”).
