GRAND PRIX TRIAL WINNING DECKLISTS
==================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX MILAN 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpmil15)
 on December 13, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/olle_rade_author.jpg)
By Olle Rade










The first thing to look at when trying to predict what you might face at a Grand Prix are the decks that won the trials the night before. Here in Milan 25 trials took place, and 25 lucky winners earned two byes to the main event today. 



 A surprisingly high number of [Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino)s earned byes for their owners, and also quite a few more Affinity decks than have been making top 8 at Grand Prix lately.



Here are some of the winners.
