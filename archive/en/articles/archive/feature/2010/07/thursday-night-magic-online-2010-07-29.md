Thursday Night **Magic Online**
===============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 29, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Now you can practice for [Friday Night **Magic**](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/fnm) from the comfort of your own home with Thursday Night **Magic Online**! Each Thursday beginning August 5, these casual 3-round Swiss pairing events provide a great way for inexperienced players to try out tournaments or for veteran players to try new strategies. As an added bonus, everyone who participates in a Thursday Night **Magic Online** event gets a digital copy of the monthly Friday Night **Magic** promo card!


Want to double the fun and test your skills at your friendly local game store? Find a  [Friday Night **Magic** location](http://ww2.wizards.com/StoreAndEventLocator/Default.aspx?link=true&ReturnParamMapZoom=10&ReturnParamMapSearch=Wizards%20of%20the%20Coast&ReturnParamMode=play&ReturnParamCheckedProducts=&ReturnParamCheckedEvents=1:9%3b&ReturnParamCheckedEventBrands=1:x&ReturnParamTablePage=1&ReturnParamLastDirectionsQuery=null) near you.


**THURSDAY NIGHT MAGIC ONLINE**


*START TIME:* Every Thursday at 4:00 AM PST (12:00 UTC), 9:00 AM PST (17:00 UTC), 3:00 PM PST (23:00 UTC), and 7:00 PM PST (Friday 3:00 UTC).  
*LOCATION:* Premier Events room.  
*ENTRY FEE:* Product or equivalent event tickets for draft and sealed deck events or 2 Event Tickets for constructed events.  
*PRODUCT:* Varies by event:


* 3 booster packs for draft
* 4 booster packs for 4-booster sealed deck
* 6 booster packs for 6-booster sealed deck
* A constructed deck for the appropriate format

*SIZE:* Minimum 16 players, maximum 256 players.  
*DURATION:* 3 rounds, Swiss pairings plus Drafting and deck building time where appropriate.  
*PRIZES:* Prizes for Swiss Pairing events are based on total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points.
