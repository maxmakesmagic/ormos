Ryan Reynolds took the top slot of the Santa Clara, California Pro Tour Qualifier by abusing [Howling Mine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine), [Temple Bell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temple+Bell), and [Jace Beleren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace+Beleren). How did he abuse them? With [Runeflare Trap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Runeflare+Trap) of course! With just a couple more months left until ***Magic** 2010* rotates out of the Standard format, Ryan made short work of his opponents with his blue-red combo deck.
