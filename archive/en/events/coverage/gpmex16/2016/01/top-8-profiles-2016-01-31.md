TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 31, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











Learn more about Grand Prix Mexico City's Top 8 competitors by taking a look through their profiles!  

 




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmex16/t8_santos.jpg)  

Name:** Rodrigo Goncalves Dos Santos


**Age:** 29


**Occupation:** Commercial Manager


**Hometown:** Brazil


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 2nd place Grand Prix Sao Paulo in 2012.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck, and what was your record?**  

Blue-Black Edlrazi. I went 9-0.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?**  
[Ruins of Oran-Rief](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ruins+of+Oran-Rief).


**What were your Draft decks, and what was your record with each?**  

White-Black Allies: 2-1; White-Black Allies: 1-0-2.


**What card do you most want to see in *Oath of the Gatewatch* Draft, and why?**  
[Linvala, the Preserver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Linvala%2C+the+Preserver) because I opened it in the first pack of the first draft!




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmex16/t8_saito.jpg)  

Name:** Tomoharu Saito


**Age:** 32


**Occupation:** Pro *Magic* player and CEO of Hareruya


**Hometown:** Narita, Chiba (love)


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** 22nd Grand Prix Top 8; five Pro Tour Top 8s.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck, and what was your record?**  

White-Red with some black – went 6-2-1.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?**  
[Press into Service](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Press+into+Service).


**What were your Draft decks, and what was your record with each?**  

Green-Blue: 3-0; Red-Green 3-0.


**What card do you most want to see in *Oath of the Gatewatch* Draft, and why?**  

Chandra! It’s the best card.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmex16/t8_dang.jpg)  

Name:** Martin Dang


**Age:** 34


**Occupation:** Platinum Pro Player


**Hometown:** Aarhus, Denmark.


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Pro Tour *Dragons of Tarkir* winner; Grand Prix Liverpool winner; World *Magic* Cup Top 8 appearance and member of Team EUreka.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck, and what was your record?**  

Black-Green and I went 9-0 obviously.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?**   
[Thought-Knot Seer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thought-Knot+Seer) and lands that make colorless mana.


**What were your Draft decks, and what was your record with each?**  

Blue-Black and went 2-1, and Black-Green in the second where I went 1-0-2.


**What card do you most want to see in *Oath of the Gatewatch* Draft, and why?**  

Chandra seems like the best card. Also Expeditions because they’re so shiny.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpmex16/t8_wallerstein.jpg)  

Name:** Robert Wallerstein


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Insurance


**Hometown:** Akron, Ohio


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** Accidentally sitting in Jon Finkel’s seat in Day 2 of a Grand Prix.


**What colors was your Sealed Deck, and what was your record?**  

Black-Red, went 8-1.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?**  
[Endbringer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Endbringer) – “DEL FIN.”


**What were your Draft decks, and what was your record with each?**  

I went 2-1 in the first draft with black-red and went Jund colors in the second to go 2-0-1.


**What card do you most want to see in *Oath of the Gatewatch* Draft, and why?**  

Anything red because that is the best card in the pack.

Name:** Cristian Velasco


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Software Project Manager


**Hometown:** Mexico City


**Previous Magic accomplishments:** None


**What colors was your Sealed Deck, and what was your record?** White-Blue, went 7-2.


**What was the best card in your Sealed deck?**
