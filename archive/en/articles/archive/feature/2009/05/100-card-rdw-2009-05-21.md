Where there's a will, there's a red deck. Having to build a list with at least 100 cards didn't stop PojotheChicken from playing everyone's favorite style of burn-and-small-creatures deck: Red Deck Wins! The deck served the player well, earning a Top 8 in a recent **Magic Online** 100 Card Singleton Premier Event, proving that while **Magic Online** may be home to many of the world's best sharpening their teeth for upcoming Pro Tours and Grand Prixs, it's also home to some of the most fun **Magic** formats available!
