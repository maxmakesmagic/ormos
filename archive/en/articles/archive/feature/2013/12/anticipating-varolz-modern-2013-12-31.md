Anticipating Varolz in Modern
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 31, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 






As we celebrate the holidays, we're bringing you some of our favorite decks from throughout the year.


Enjoy these fun flashbacks into last year’s favorites.


Wizards of the Coast Staff


  


---

  

Recently, osmanozguney finished 3–1 in a Modern Daily event with a black-green aggro deck with [Death's Shadow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) that used [Dark Confidant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant), [Overgrown Tomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Tomb), fetch lands, [Putrid Leech](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Putrid+Leech), [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), and [Dismember](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember) to pay life to make it big early in the game. 

![Varolz, the Scar-Striped](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Varolz%2C+the+Scar-Striped) While this is a relatively interesting strategy in Modern, in that it isn't all over, a 3–1 finish with a [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) and [Dark Confidant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) deck might not ordinarily warrant closer investigation. However, *Dragon's Maze* is about to come out, and [Varolz, the Scar-Striped](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Varolz%2C+the+Scar-Striped) has been hyped as part of a two-card combo with [Death's Shadow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) to turn [Death's Shadow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) into a one-mana "Aura" that gives +13/+13, which can be, as we say in the business, "big game." 

 The question has been finding a shell for this combo, and there are some who think it's all hype because the cards aren't independently good enough. I'm optimistic about this combo specifically because I think each card is good on its own, and they fit so well in the shell osmanozguney has built that I wonder if it was being tested specifically in anticipation of [Varolz, the Scar-Striped](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Varolz%2C+the+Scar-Striped). 


[Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) and [Inquisition of Kozilek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inquisition+of+Kozilek) are perfect for making sure your opponent can't kill one of your creatures before you invest your +13/+13 and, incidentally, [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) doesn't make a bad Aura after it's dead, either. 

 The question is, what can be cut to make room for [Varolz, the Scar-Striped](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Varolz%2C+the+Scar-Striped)? Unfortunately, I don't have an easy answer for that. All the cards in this deck look both good and important, and I expect it to be more a question of trimming than a direct replacement. Maybe I'd try three [Varolz, the Scar-Striped](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Varolz%2C+the+Scar-Striped)s over a [Liliana](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil), an [Inquisition of Kozilek](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inquisition+of+Kozilek), and a [Dismember](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember) to start.
