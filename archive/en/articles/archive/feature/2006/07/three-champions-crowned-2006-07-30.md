Three Champions Crowned
=======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 30, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














### [Cheon Conquers All at U.S. Nationals](/en/events/coverage/cheon-conquers-all-us-nationals)

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2006/usnat_fp.jpg)**Magic** Weekend didn't disappoint as Paul Cheon won the 2006 U.S. National Championship. Joining him on the national team will be close friend and playtest partner Luis Scott-Vargas and Ben Lundquist. Congratulations also to Arthur Stewart, the 2006 Junior Super Series champion! 

  
### [Stevenson Crowned English National Champion](/en/events/coverage/final-champion-crowned)

 Craig Stevenson, Calum Stevenson, and Andrew "Boomer" Clayton are your 2006 England National Team, coming fresh from a Top 8 heavy in board control and Loxodon Hierarchs. The Elephants will now march on Paris, where Team England will do it's best to take the world crown! 

### [Alvisi Wins Italian Championship](/en/node/575686)

Who will be crowned Italian National Champion? Manual Alvisi is your Italian National Champion! Filippini Gianluca and Luca Chiera round out the team ready to wave the Italian flag at 2006 Worlds.
