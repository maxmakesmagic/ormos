Round 10:The Hexproof Hustle
============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 30, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_SteveSadin.jpg)
By Steve Sadin












[Jon Finkel vs. Alexander Hayne (Draft)](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/tpc12/fm9)


David Ochoa (Zoo) vs. Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa (Zoo)
----------------------------------------------------


[Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa vs. Shouta Yasooka](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/eventcoverage/tpc12/fm11)


A few minutes before this round started, Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa beat David Ochoa in the final **Magic** 2013 draft match of the tournament. Ochoa didn't have to wait long to get his rematch as the two teammates (who are both 5-4, and need to 3-0 the remaining Modern rounds to have a chance to Top 4) now get to play against each other in a 73-card Domain Zoo mirror match.



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/tpc12/r10_pv_v_ochoa.JPG)  
*The two ChannelFireball teammates face off in a fight to remain in contention of the Top 4.*

### Game 1


Damo da Rosa had 3 [Steppe Lynx](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steppe+Lynx)es to start the match, but Ochoa was able to survive the early onslaught thanks to a [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), a [Lightning Helix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Helix), and a [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt). As soon as he stabilized, Ochoa went on the offensive with a [Qasali Pridemage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Qasali+Pridemage).


But before the mustachioed Ochoa could make any real headway, Damo da Rosa used a [Tribal Flames](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tribal+Flames) to take out a [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) and a [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) to kill off the [Qasali Pridemage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Qasali+Pridemage).



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/tpc12/r10_ochoa.JPG)  
*The mustachioed master could not keep a creature in play in the Domain Zoo mirror.*

After clawing Ochoa down to 7 life, Damo da Rosa used a pair of [Snapcaster Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snapcaster+Mage)s to finish off the job that his cats started, flashing back a [Lightning Bolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) and a lethal [Tribal Flames](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tribal+Flames).


Damo da Rosa 1, Ochoa 0


### Game 2


In the second game, David Ochoa took a mulligan into a removal heavy but creature light hand. Unfortunately for him, this hand was particularly ill equipped to deal with the [Geist of Saint Traft](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geist+of+Saint+Traft) that Damo da Rosa played on turn three.



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/tpc12/r10_pv.JPG)  
*Damo da Rosa capitalizes on his opponent's removal-packed hand with a hexproof heavy hitter.*

And by the time Ochoa started drawing potential blockers, it was already too late as Damo da Rosa had an ample supply of burn spells that he used to close out the match and keep himself in Top 4 contention.


Damo da Rosa 2, Ochoa 0
