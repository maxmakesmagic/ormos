Monstrous Naya
==============



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on July 16, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 






"Monsters" decks have been popular in Standard, focusing in red and green and capitalizing on using mana acceleration to cast [Polukranos, World Eater](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polukranos%2C+World+Eater) and [Stormbreath Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormbreath+Dragon) ahead of schedule. Later in the game, the mana creatures maintain their usefulness due to the monstrosity mechanic, allowing for powerful monsters to become even more powerful. [Courser of Kruphix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courser+of+Kruphix) and [Domri Rade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Domri+Rade) are also staples of the archetype and help bridge the gaps in the mana curve.



![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Ajani,%20Mentor%20of%20Heroes)

However, straight red-green often has some holes, namely its removal suite. [Mizzium Mortars](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mizzium+Mortars) is an excellent card, but it doesn't really address problematic creatures such as [Master of Waves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Waves) and [Desecration Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desecration+Demon). While adding black has been popular due to the addition of [Ultimate Price](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ultimate+Price), *Magic Online* user Goldfire07 has a different solution, one that is potentially more effectiveâ€”white!



[Chained to the Rocks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chained+to+the+Rocks) is one of the most efficient removal spells in Standard, taking out nearly any creature for a single mana. Adding white also allows this deck to diversify its threat base. [Fleecemane Lion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleecemane+Lion) is yet another efficient creature that can take advantage of monstrous while [Ajani, Mentor of Heroes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ajani%2C+Mentor+of+Heroes) provides another card-advantage Planeswalker to support the powerful creatures.
