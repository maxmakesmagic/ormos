One interesting strategy to come out of the recent Legacy Grand Prix held in Columbus, Ohio was Korey Age's Sneak and Show deck. The player's goal was to rush out a [Sneak Attack](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sneak+Attack) or [Show and Tell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell) using cards like [Lotus Petal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lotus+Petal) and [Ancient Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb). That would allow him to plop a fatty like [Emrakul, the Aeons Torn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Emrakul%2C+the+Aeons+Torn) or [Woodfall Primus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woodfall+Primus) onto the battlefield. From there it wasn't hard to mop up opponents! Read more at the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/saitos-legacy-continues).
