Kyle Boddy won the StarCityGames.com Legacy Open in Seattle playing his take on New Horizons. The deck, created by Legacy player David Price (no relation to the Pro Tour star from the 90s) features lots of utility lands that power creatures like [Terravore](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terravore), [Knight of the Reliquary](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+the+Reliquary), and even [Tarmogoyf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf). Top things off with some removal and a few counterspells and you have a powerful deck in the format!
