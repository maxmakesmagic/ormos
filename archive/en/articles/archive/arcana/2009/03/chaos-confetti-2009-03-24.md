Chaos Confetti!
===============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 24, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Back when Tom Jenkot did [his dossier](/en/articles/archive/magic-dossier-tom-jenkot-2009-03-03), he included a clever image of a sealed, PSA-graded copy of [Chaos Confetti](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chaos+Confetti):

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/137_chaosScan.jpg)  
What you probably didn't realize is that although some visual trickery was involved, that was a *real* PSA-graded [Chaos Confetti](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chaos+Confetti)! Tom's a big fan of the card, and also has a Mark-Tedin-customized "goth" variant:

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/152_confetti.jpg)
