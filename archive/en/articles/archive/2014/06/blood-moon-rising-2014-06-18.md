Blood Moon Rising
=================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on June 18, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 






[![](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Blood+Moon)](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon)
"Cycle [Deadshot Minotaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deadshot+Minotaur). Cycle [Street Wraith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Street+Wraith)."


You settle in for a game against [Living End](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End). You prepare to put your own creatures in the graveyard to make the [Living End](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End) not so one-sided. You try not to overcommit creatures to the battlefield.


"Cast [Blood Moon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon)."


Wait, what? You didn't expect this. [Living End](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End) doesn't PLAY [Blood Moon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon)! Oh well, this basic land will help you cast some spells, and hopefully they will be enough.


"[Avalanche Riders](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avalanche+Riders), target your basic land"


You've been had.


Modern is a format where the unexpected can be a potent advantage. Putting [Blood Moon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon) into a place where it is not expected is a perfect example of catching opponents by surprise. Land cyclers such as [Pale Recluse](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pale+Recluse) and [Twisted Abomination](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Twisted+Abomination) can help get the Forest and a Swamp so the pilot can operate under [Blood Moon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Moon).
