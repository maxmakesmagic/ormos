Decks of the Week: 4/20/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 26, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Prereleases for *Dragon's Maze* occur this weekend! If you didn't preregister for a nearby Prerelease, you might still be able to find one with availability by using the [event locator](http://locator.wizards.com/). This season's PTQs and WMCQs run the Standard format, and we will have Top 8 decks from them all season so you can see what's going on in the metagame, including how *Dragon's Maze* shakes it up. Find the nearest PTQ to you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros)! Good luck; have fun!
