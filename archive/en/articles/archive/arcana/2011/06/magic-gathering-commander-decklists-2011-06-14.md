Magic: The Gathering Commander Decklists
========================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 14, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











It's time to look at the full decklists for all five decks that make up the ***Magic: The Gathering** Commander* product! The new cards aren't in Gatherer yet, so not all of the cards will appear in the handy sidebar-window-thing. However, the [Card Image Gallery](/en/articles/archive/magic-gathering-commander-card-image-gallery-2011-05-25) has all of the card images, so that should help out until Gatherer gets populated.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/stf/stf146_decks.jpg)  
Each deck comes with one official commander, plus two other three-color legendary creatures that can be swapped in to create a different play experience. For today's purposes, we're just using the official commander. But if you want to play Heavenly Inferno with Tariel, Reckoner of Souls or [Oros, the Avenger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oros%2C+the+Avenger) as your commander, we're certainly not going to stop you.

### Heavenly Inferno

Kaalia of the Vast alone holds the forces of Angels, Dragons, and Demons within the palm of her hand. Watch her toss each bone-crushing creature onto the battlefield with merciless glee while your horrified enemies are sliced, diced, and then deep-fried.


**Commander:**
  
[Kaalia of the Vast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kaalia+of+the+Vast)![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/tcg/products/cmd/ahptptzayc_en.jpg)
