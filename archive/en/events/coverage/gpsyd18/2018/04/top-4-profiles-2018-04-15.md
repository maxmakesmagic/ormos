Top 4 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 15, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Walkinshaw-Profile.jpg)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw











350 teams descended upon Grand Prix Sydney this weekend, but we could only squeeze the four very best teams into the Top 4. After Day 1, we were down to just 45, but that wasn’t enough, we kept forcing them to face off against each other today until we were down to just four. I mean it says it right there on the label, it’s the *Top 4,* isn’t it?

================================

**Age:** 31.

**How long have you been playing *Magic*** ? 3 years – since M15.

**Deck for this weekend:** Titanshift.  
**Favourite Card in Modern:** [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle).

**Name:** Benny Yau.  
**Age:** 30 – Today is my birthday!  
**Hometown:** Hong Kong/Melbourne.  
**How long have you been playing *Magic*?** Back when fetchlands were still legal in Standard.  
**Best finish at a *Magic* event:** We will see later today!  
**Deck for this weekend:** Assault Loam/[Dredge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dredge).  
**Favourite Card in Modern:** [Eldrazi Displacer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eldrazi+Displacer).  
**Favourite flavour of Tim-Tams:** *(left blank, such self-control!)*


**Name:** Mathieu Beaujard.  
**Age:** 36.  
**Hometown:** Melbourne.  
**How long have you been playing *Magic*?** Played 4th Edition until 5th Edition ('95-'97) then started again with Dragons of Tarkir.  
**Best finish at a *Magic* event:** Top 4 GP, today!  
**Deck for this weekend:** Humans.  
**Favourite Card in Modern:** [Spell Queller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Queller).  
**Favourite flavour of Tim-Tams:** Dark chocolate.
