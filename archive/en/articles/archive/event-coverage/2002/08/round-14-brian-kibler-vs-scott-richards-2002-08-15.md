Round 14: Brian Kibler vs. Scott Richards
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 15, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











Many critics say this is a two deck format (blue/green and mono-black), but Scott Richards apparently thinks otherwise. He's running an aggressive white/green weenie deck that contains cards such as Spurmage Advocate, [Tireless Tribe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tireless+Tribe), [Benevolent Bodyguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Benevolent+Bodyguard), [Divine Sacrament](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Divine+Sacrament) and [Anurid Brushhopper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anurid+Brushhopper). 

### Game 1

![Spurnmage Advocate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Spurnmage+Advocate)Richards opened with [Spurnmage Advocate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spurnmage+Advocate), and had a second turn [Benevolent Bodyguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Benevolent+Bodyguard). [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth) slowed down Richards' mana development, and Kibler cast [Diabolic Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Tutor) on the following turn. [Divine Sacrament](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Divine+Sacrament) made Richards' team a lot more frightening, but Kibler used [Mind Sludge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Sludge) to strip away his hand. This was followed by [Mutilate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate) and [Nantuko Shade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade), leaving Richards with very few resources, and staring down a massive threat. After a few turns the Shade came through for the win without much trouble.

Kibler- 1 Richards- 0

### Game 2

Richards played a first turn [Tireless Tribe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tireless+Tribe), which got a Jarrod Bright joke out of Kibler. [Phantom Nomad](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nomad) continued the curve, and the next turn saw [Elephant Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide) boost the Tribe. [Faceless Butcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) took care of that threat, and Richards rebuilt with [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). This let him discard [Glory](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glory), which was joined by [Sylvan Safekeeper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Safekeeper). Kibler had [Mutilate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate), which cleared the board and gave Richards his Tribe back. It was joined by another Nomad, so Kibler [Mutilate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate)d once again. Richards summoned a [Glory](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glory), and Kibler killed it with [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood), and followed up with [Haunting Echoes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haunting+Echoes) to devastate Richards. [Mirari](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari) was played a turn later, while Richards cast [Battle Screech](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battle+Screech). Kibler cast [Chainer's Edict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainer%27s+Edict) and doubled it, and cast [Nantuko Shade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade). On the next turn he flashed back Edict and doubled it, leaving Richards with only two bird tokens in play, and a dead Bodyguard and Brushhopper. The resulting bird attack brought Kibler to two, but he drew a second [Cabal Coffers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Coffers) to pump his Shade to lethal damage.

**Final Result:** Kibler- 2 Richards- 0
