Core Set 2019 First Picks
=========================



 Posted in **How to Play Limited**
 on July 10, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_2016_LSV.jpg)
By Luis Scott-Vargas 




Luis Scott-Vargas plays, writes, and makes videos about Magic. He has played on the Pro Tour for almost a decade, and between that and producing content for ChannelFireball, often has his hands full (of cards). 






***Core Set 2019*** **Releases This Weekend!**


Celebrate the release of *Core Set 2019* at Draft Weekend July 14â€“15. Find a [store near you](http://locator.wizards.com/) to join in (or attempt to thwart) Nicol Bolas's dastardly scheming!


Soon, we will all be cracking open our first packs of *Core Set 2019*. What are we hoping to open? What are we willing to take early? What will we be excited about, or happy with, or begrudgingly take? I've made some lists that may help answer all of those questions, so let's dive in.


The Best of the Best
====================

[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+the+Ravager)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nicol+Bolas%2C+the+Ravager) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Palladia-Mors%2C+the+Ruiner)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Palladia-Mors%2C+the+Ruiner) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Vaevictis+Asmadi%2C+the+Dire)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vaevictis+Asmadi%2C+the+Dire)
These are the top cards of the set. If you open one of these, you're in luck! They are universally powerful, worth building around, and in some cases, worth constructing a three-color mana base. They mostly fly, though [Banefire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banefire) snuck in as a removal spell that can also finish off players.

If you open one of these cards, you should be thrilled, even if they aren't at the tippity-top of the power rankings.
