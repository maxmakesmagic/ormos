Players love innovating formats, and this year's Extended season was no different. The format du jour for the Nagoya Pro Tour Qualifier season was the first to use the four-block rotation in Extended. Bryce Kaff narrowly missed qualifying with this White Weenie build in Sacramento, California, but no worries. The [Philadelphia Pro Tour Qualifier season](/en/articles/archive/qualifiers-pro-tour-philadelphia-2011-2011-03-23) is just around the corner!
