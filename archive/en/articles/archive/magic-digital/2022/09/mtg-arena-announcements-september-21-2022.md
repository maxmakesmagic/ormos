MTG Arena Announcements, September 21, 2022
===========================================



 Posted in **Magic Digital**
 on September 21, 2022 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











This Weekend:
The Arena Championship 1!
=======================================


![Tune in to the Arena Championship 1 Saturday, September 24 starting at 9 a.m. on Twitch](https://media.wizards.com/2022/images/daily/blog_arena_championship_1.jpg)


The biggest *MTG Arena* Premier Play event is finally here! [Arena Championship 1](https://magic.gg/events/arena-championship-1) will host [top *MTG Arena* players](https://magic.gg/standings/Arena%20Championship%201) September 24–25 for an epic competition with a $200,000 total prize pool and that will determine who goes on to the *Magic* World Championship.


This is an event you don't want to miss—and you won't, because you can [watch it live at twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic)! Check out [the Arena Championship 1 Viewers Guide](https://magic.gg/news/arena-championship-1-viewers-guide) for all the event details and get ready to tune in starting Saturday, September 24, at 9 a.m. PT!


Bonus Play-In with Arena Championship 1 Decks!
==============================================


We've got a special bonus event happening alongside the [Arena Championship 1](http://magic.gg/events/arena-championship-1)—and it can even get you into next month's Qualifier Weekend. The Bonus Play-In: Arena Championship Showcase event features Best-of-One matches with the same decks used by the competitors in Arena Championship 1!


The Bonus Play-In: Championship Showcase event starts September 24 at 6 a.m. PT and runs until September 26 at 6 a.m. PT.


Arena Open Next Weekend!
========================


October starts off big with the Arena Open! Next weekend, October 1–2, compete in Best-of-One and Best-of-Three *Dominaria United* Limited matches for prizes up to $2,500! Plus, everyone who enters receives the Sheoldred, the Apocalypse Phyrexian card style:


Get all the details on the [Arena Open: *Dominaria United* Limited](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-digital/october-2022-arena-open-dominaria-united-limited-2022-09-21) page.

Premier Play Schedule
=====================


*All times listed are Pacific time (UTC -7).*


Qualifier Play-In Events
------------------------


[Qualifier Play-In events](https://magic.gg/events/qualifier-play-in) are single-day tournaments in which players compete to earn invitations to that month's Qualifier Weekend events.


### September


* **Bonus Play-In**  

 September 24, 6 a.m. PT–September 26, 6 a.m. PT
* **Best-of-One Qualifier Play-In**  

 October 7, 6 a.m. PT–October 8, 3 a.m. PT
	+ Format: Standard
* **Best-of-Three Qualifier Play-In**  

 October 14, 6 a.m. PT–October 15, 3 a.m. PT
	+ Format: Standard

Qualifier Weekend Events
------------------------


[Qualifier Weekend events](https://magic.gg/events/qualifier-weekend) are two-day events in which eligible players compete for invitations to the Arena Championship 1 held in the fourth quarter of 2022.


*Qualifier Weekend Day One and Day Two events open to entries at 6 a.m. PT (13:00 UTC) and close to new entries at 8 a.m. PT (UTC 15:00) each day. You must enter within this two-hour window —late entries cannot be accommodated.*


October
-------


* **Qualifier Weekend**  

 October 15, 6 a.m. PT–October 17, 4 p.m. PT
	+ Format: Standard (Best-of-Three)

Arena Championship Event
------------------------


The [Arena Championships](https://magic.gg/events/arena-championship-1) is an invitation-only, virtual two-day event for players who qualify during one of the four Qualifier Weekend events.


* [**Arena Championship 1 Weekend**](https://magic.gg/events/arena-championship-1-information-for-invitees)  

 September 24–25  

 Check out the [Arena Championship 1 Viewers Guide](https://magic.gg/news/arena-championship-1-viewers-guide)!

Arena Open
----------


*Arena Open Day 1 entry window begins at 6 a.m. PT (13:00 UTC) and closes to new entries the following day at 3 a.m. PT (10:00 UTC).* 


*Day 2 entry window is two hours only, from 6 a.m. PT (13:00 UTC) until 8 a.m. PT (15:00 UTC).*


* **Arena Open October 1–2**
	+ Day 1, October 1: *Dominaria United* Sealed  
	
	 Best-of-One and Best-of-Three  
	
	 All participants will receive the Phyrexian treatment Sheoldred, the Apocalypse card style.
	+ Day 2, October 2, Draft One and Draft Two: *Dominaria United* Player Draft  
	
	 Best-of-Three only  
	**Note**: There are *two* drafts for the Day 2 Arena Open, with three wins or one loss.
