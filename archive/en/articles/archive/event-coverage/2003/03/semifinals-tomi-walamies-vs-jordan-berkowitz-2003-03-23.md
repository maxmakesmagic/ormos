Semifinals: Tomi Walamies vs. Jordan Berkowitz
==============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on March 23, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_626.png)
By Victor van den Broek











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptven03/a780.jpg)And there he was, facing down Tomi Walamies in the Pro Tour Venice semifinals. Jordan Berkowitz has qualified for every Pro Tour this season the hard way, but he won't be playing in any PTQs next year. He also won the flip and would be playing R/G Beasts. Tomi on the other hand was playing Punisher Goblins.

### Game 1

Jordan chose to play first but it was Tomi with the first creatures of the game. A turn one [Skirk Prospector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Prospector) and turn two [Goblin Piledriver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Piledriver) put the pressure on Jordan. His answer was a turn three [Thoughtbound Primoc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtbound+Primoc). [Goblin Burrows](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Burrows) and [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) made sure that Tomi's creatures would be hitting it on next turn though. Jordan played a [Ravenous Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth) and attacked with his [Thoughtbound Primoc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtbound+Primoc). [Goblin Goon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Goon) joined the Finnish Army and [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) took down the Baloth. [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) and [Thoughtbound Primoc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtbound+Primoc) fought in Jordan's [Contested Cliffs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+Cliffs) but the Primoc died during Tomi's next turn due to a cycled [Gempalm Incinerator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gempalm+Incinerator). The subsequent attack of the Prospector, Piledriver and Goon along with the burrows took the young American down to 4 life. He played a [Tephraderm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tephraderm) next turn but died in the next attack phase. 

Tomi 1 – Jordan 0

Sideboard Jordan: -4 [Wirewood Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Elf), -4 [Avarax](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avarax), +4 [Broodhatch Nantuko](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodhatch+Nantuko), +4 [Gempalm Incinerator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gempalm+Incinerator)

Sideboard Tomi: -2 [Menacing Ogre](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Menacing+Ogre), -4 [Gempalm Incinerator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gempalm+Incinerator), -1 [Skirk Prospector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Prospector), +1 [Starstorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Starstorm), +2 [Rorix Bladewing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rorix+Bladewing), +4 [Thoughtbound Primoc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtbound+Primoc)

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptven03/a774.jpg)The pressure on Jordan was somewhat lower this time around as Tomi didn't play a Goblin until his second turn – a [Goblin Piledriver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Piledriver). It was [Shock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)ed by Jordan who played out a [Thoughtbound Primoc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtbound+Primoc). A new Piledriver hit the board next turn along with a [Goblin Sledder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sledder). Next turn, Jordan had to show the mediocrity of his hand. He had nothing to play as his land was only [Forgotten Cave](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forgotten+Cave), [Contested Cliffs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+Cliffs) and a [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain). Tomi played a [Clickslither](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clickslither) and ruthlessly attacked Jordan. The Primoc blocked the Piledriver but the [Goblin Sledder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sledder) saved it. Jordan only managed to play out a morph on his next turn and shuffled up for game 3. 

Tomi 2 – Jordan 0

### Game 3

Jordan slumped in his chair when he looked at his opening cards. Tomi kept his pokerface as he picked up his cards one at a time at a grueling pace. Neither player decided to trade in their hands though.

Jordan's hand didn't turn out to be all that bad, as he played out a second and third turn [Broodhatch Nantuko](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodhatch+Nantuko), to provide him with the necessary time to get out his big creatures. Tomi played out a [Skirk Prospector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Prospector) and [Goblin Piledriver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Piledriver), only to see his Prospector [Shock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)ed by Jordan. He then played a [Goblin Sledder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sledder) and attacked with his Piledriver, which took the American down to 18. Jordan only cycled a land and attacked with one of his [Broodhatch Nantuko](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodhatch+Nantuko)s. Tomi played out a [Goblin Burrows](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Burrows) along with a [Goblin Sledder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sledder) in order to force through 3 damage with his [Goblin Piledriver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Piledriver). 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptven03/a779.jpg)Jordan finally managed to get a reasonably big creature into play when he amplified his [Canopy Crawler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Canopy+Crawler) showing a [Krosan Tusker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Tusker). It died next turn in combat when it jumped in front of Tomi's Piledriver, along with the [Broodhatch Nantuko](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodhatch+Nantuko) that stayed home. Both [Broodhatch Nantuko](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodhatch+Nantuko)s came in on Jordan's next turn after he played a [Tephraderm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tephraderm). Tomi sent both his [Goblin Sledder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sledder)s, pumped the one that was blocked by the [Tephraderm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tephraderm) and [Shock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)ed the beast after damage resolved. The [Tephraderm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tephraderm) was replaced by a [Ravenous Baloth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ravenous+Baloth), which provided Jordan with some breathing space. He would need it as [Rorix Bladewing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rorix+Bladewing) came down, bringing Jordan down to 8 life. The American played out a [Contested Cliffs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+Cliffs) and the [Krosan Tusker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Tusker) he showed Tomi earlier. He then attacked with his Baloth and Nantukos to bring Tomi down to 8 life, giving him a window of opportunity to beat the dragon.

[Rorix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rorix) came in regardless and took Jordan down to 2 life. A [Clickslither](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clickslither) hit play to provide Tomi with some much-needed defense. Jordan thought very long and hard before sending one [Broodhatch Nantuko](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodhatch+Nantuko) and the [Krosan Tusker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Tusker) into the Red Zone. The [Clickslither](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clickslither) blocked the Tusker and the lone [Goblin Sledder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sledder) jumped in front of the [Broodhatch Nantuko](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broodhatch+Nantuko). The Tusker took down [Rorix Bladewing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rorix+Bladewing) in the [Contested Cliffs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Contested+Cliffs) only to be replaced by a new one next turn, giving Tomi the match.

**Final Result:** Tomi 3 – Jordan 0
