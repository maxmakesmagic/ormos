*From FFL playtester Dave Guskin:*


The Allies have gathered, and they're on a mission: crush your enemies! With the addition of the one-mana [Hada Freeblade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hada+Freeblade) and already solid two drops like [Kazandu Blademaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kazandu+Blademaster) and [Oran-Rief Survivalist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oran-Rief+Survivalist), you can begin the beatdown right out of the gates. The mana production of [Harabaz Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harabaz+Druid) quickly ramps you to an array of powerful possibilities: Wolves ([Turntimber Ranger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turntimber+Ranger)), cards ([Sea Gate Loremaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sea+Gate+Loremaster)) and even huge life swings (new recruit [Talus Paladin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Talus+Paladin)). Speaking of recruits, you can ambush your opponent with [Join the Ranks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Join+the+Ranks)—two Ally triggers at instant speed is no joke. Learning when the team you have deployed is enough, and saving the remaining Allies in your hand in case things go sour, is key with this deck ... but you'll find that once you get going, your quickly growing horde of allied adventurers will be nigh unstoppable!


I dropped a few matches at the Prerelease, mostly to huge creatures coming down after a board sweeper. The deck's late-game power comes from *Worldwake*'s new creature lands [Celestial Colonnade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Celestial+Colonnade) and [Stirring Wildwood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stirring+Wildwood), which are great in that situation, and of course, in most other situations the [Harabaz Druid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Harabaz+Druid) is powering out multiple [Turntimber Ranger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turntimber+Ranger)s!
