[Oath of Druids](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oath+of+Druids) is one of the most powerful cards in the history of **Magic**—so powerful it is presently banned in Legacy. But it took future Wizards of the Coast R&D member Erik Lauer to discover the card's power. In the summer of 1998 he built this deck and gave it to his friend, and fellow future R&D member, Randy Buehler. Randy ripped off a perfect record with the deck at U.S. Nationals using the enchantment to fetch up [Archangel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archangel), then using [Mulch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulch) to fuel [Scroll Rack](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scroll+Rack) draws and turning those extra cards into life with [Gerrard's Wisdom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gerrard%27s+Wisdom). [Gaea's Blessing](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gaea%27s+Blessing) made sure the Oath player couldn't be decked, and the rest is history!
