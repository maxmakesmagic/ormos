Whisperings of a Turbo Fog deck could be read in this very space just a few short weeks ago, but Gabe Carleton-Barnes piloted his own take on the deck to the Top 8 of Regionals in Edison, New Jersey. His plan was to draw tons of extra cards with [Howling Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) effects. Then, he'd lock out the combat step each turn with a Fog effect, before finally decking his opponent on the back of [Jace Beleren](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace+Beleren) or the regular draw step. To read about more Regionals Top 8 decklists, visit [the Regionals decklists page](/en/events/coverage/2009-regionals).
