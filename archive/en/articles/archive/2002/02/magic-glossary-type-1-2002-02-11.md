Magic Glossary: Type 1
======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 11, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












**Type 1**


Type 1 is the most comprehensive format. It allows cards from every **Magic** expansion, all printings and extensions of the base set, and all promotional cards.


Many cards have been restricted (only one copy allowed per deck, as opposed to four) or banned (not allowed at all) due to problematic power-levels.
