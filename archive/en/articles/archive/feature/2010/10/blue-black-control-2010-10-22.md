Nick Spagnolo managed to win both of the recent [TCGPlayer.com](http://magic.tcgplayer.com/index.asp?wwwlink=yes) Championship Series tournaments held in Boston. The deck he used for the Standard portion was Blue-Black Control. With the return of [Trinket Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trinket+Mage) in *Scars of Mirrodin*, Nick was able to include an artifact package from which he could tutor up the juiciest elements. Need to gain some life or reshuffle your graveyard? Simply call forth [Elixir of Immortality](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elixir+of+Immortality). Looking for some removal? How about [Brittle Effigy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brittle+Effigy)! Wanting a mana boost? [Everflowing Chalice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Everflowing+Chalice) to the rescue!
