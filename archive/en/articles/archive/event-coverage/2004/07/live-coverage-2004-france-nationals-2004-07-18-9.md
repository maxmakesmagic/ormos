Live Coverage of 2004 France Nationals
======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 18, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_92.png)
By Mark Wraith











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/frnat04/fm10_1.jpg)Coming into this round at 7-2, it was likely that the loser would be virtually eliminated from top eight contention. Gabriel is playing a mono-red land destruction deck, which should find a very favorable matchup against Antoine's [Tooth and Nail](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tooth+and+Nail) deck. (And by favourable, read, he'd have to try very very hard to ever lose). ### Game 1


Antoine mulliganned an opening hand containing [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) and [Urza's Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Tower) as it's only mana sources. The game when it came was a swift one. Antoine played [Tranquil Thicket](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tranquil+Thicket), Gab a [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain). Ruel dropped an [Urza's Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Tower), Nassif played a second [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) and a [Dwarven Blastminer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dwarven+Blastminer) earning an immediate concession as Antoine had the total of six basic lands in his entire deck and none in hand.


Gabriel Nassif 1 - Antoine Ruel 0


### Game 2


Nassif again had a turn 2 Blastminer but this time Antoine only had basic lands, two [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest)s and a sided in [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain). It was Ruel who cast the first land destruction, with a [Stone Rain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Rain) on Nassif's [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain), before [Pyroclasm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm)ing away the Miner. Unhindered by Gabriel, Antoine further developed his mana with a [Reap and Sow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reap+and+Sow) to fetch the full set of Urza's lands. He then hardcast a [Darksteel Colossus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darksteel+Colossus). Gabriel dropped an Arc Slogger which chump-blocked for a turn before they started Game 3


Gabriel Nassif 1 - Antoine Ruel 1


### Game 3


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/frnat04/fm10_2.jpg)Gab had to mulligan, but this time he had some early land destruction. [Molten Rain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Molten+Rain) and [Demolish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demolish) took out a pair of Urza's Lands. With Antoine stuck on a [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) and a Tower, Nassif played a Blastminer and started to go to work. Ruel had a [Wayfarer's Bauble](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wayfarer%27s+Bauble) to fetch a [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) but no [Pyroclasm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyroclasm) for the Miner, and the [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) fell to a [Lay Waste](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lay+Waste) in any case.


Nassif dropped an Arc Slogger with Antoine still stuck on two basic lands, and Ruel conceded.


Gabriel Nassif 2 - Antoine Ruel 1
