Quick Questions #2: What decks are great in Legacy now that Treasure Cruise is banned?
======================================================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 18, 2015 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Chapman Sim and Ben Swartz











![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpkyo15/QQ-Kenji-Tsumura.jpg)  
**Hall of Famer and Player of the Year, Kenji Tsumura:** Sultai and Jund!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpkyo15/q2Shouta-Yasooka.jpg)  
**Pro Tour Champion and Player of the Year, Shouta Yasooka:** Omni Tell, because the Delver decks have weakened.


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpkyo15/q2Ken-Yukuhiro.jpg)  
**Pro Tour Avacyn Restored Top 4 Competitor and Gold Pro, Ken Yukuhiro:** All the decks with [Dig Through Time](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dig+Through+Time).


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpkyo15/q2Tomoharu-Saito.jpg)  
**Five-Time Pro Tour Top 8 Competitor and Pro Tour Champion, Tomoharu Saito:** The Delver decks are still very good.
