Decks of the Week: 6/28/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 28, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Last weekend was a historic one for **Magic** pro play. By now, you almost certainly have heard about the record-setting ***event*** that was [Grand Prix Las Vegas](/en/events/coverage/oliver-modern-master-las-vegas). Meanwhile, across the Pacific, [Grand Prix Bangkok](/en/events/coverage/kurniadi-captures-bangkok) gave us **Magic**'s first Indonesian Grand Prix champion. Congratulations to Neal Oliver for his Vegas victory and to Patriawan Kurniadi for his win in Thailand! Neal and Patriawan will be in Dublin this October to compete at Pro Tour *Theros*. Would you like to join them there? One way to do it is to win a Pro Tour Qualifier! Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros). Good luck; have fun!
