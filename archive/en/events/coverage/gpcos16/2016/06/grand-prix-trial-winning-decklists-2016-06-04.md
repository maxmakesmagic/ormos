Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 4, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











No matter how lush the scenery, in the end we're here to play *Magic*. And no one remembers that more than the players. Every Grand Prix Friday, there's the last chance to earn a few byes and to sleep in later on Saturday morning. This weekend, despite the allure of the outside, is no different.


Last night six players got those coveted extra sleep hours. Doug Potter won a Sealed trial, and five players below won in the format of the Grand Prix—Standard. If they're good enough for a Grand Prix Trial, they are certainly good enough for your local Friday Night Magic. Even within the same deck archetypes, there is tons of different card choices and play.


Check ‘em out!
