Top 8 Player Profiles of Grand Prix Milwaukee 2018
==================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 18, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_meghanwolff_0.jpg)
By Meghan Wolff




 Meghan is one half of the Good Luck High Five podcast and an adjunct professor at Tolarian Community College. She loves Limited, likes Modern, and dips her toes into each Standard season. She's decidedly blue and is the #1 hater of Siege Rhino in the Multiverse. 






After fifteen rounds of Standard, only eight competitors could continue on. Meet your GP Milwaukee Top 8 players!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmil18/sullivan.jpg)


**Name:** Adrian Sullivan


**Hometown:** Madison, WI


**Occupation:** Communications Strategy Ad Consulting


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


2-time Wisconsin State Champ


Top 8 PT *Dragons of Tarkir*


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Jeskai Control


**What was your best card this weekend, and why?**


Niv-Mizzet


**What was the most exciting play you made this weekend?**


Explosion for 17 off of [Spell Swindle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spell+Swindle) on [Carnage Tyrant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carnage+Tyrant)


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


A long-awaited return to the PT.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmil18/hernandez.jpg)


**Name:** Adam Hernandez


**Hometown:** Canton, MI


**Occupation:** IT


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Top 32 SCG Open


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


GB Midrange, because mono-red had a bad Jeskai matchup, and I didn't like URor Jeskai in this field.


**What was your best card this weekend, and why?**


Either the 24th land or [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves). Having more keepable hands and consistent access to mana is excellent.


**What was the most exciting play you made this weekend?**


Casting down [Adanto Vanguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Adanto+Vanguard) in response to an activation in order to outrace WW in game three.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


FIRST PT INVITE

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmil18/tilk.jpg)


**Name:** Jake "FirstFNM" Tilk


**Hometown:** Grosse Ile, MI


**Occupation:** Twitch streamer (twitch.tv/firstfnm) and student


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


Way too many GP Top 16s, Pro Tour Day 2 competitor, back-to-back 5k champion.


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Mono-White Aggro. Ian Barber and Slater Claudel told me it was great.


**What was your best card this weekend, and why?**


[Adanto Vanguard](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Adanto+Vanguard), it's unstoppable


**What was the most exciting play you made this weekend?**


I got to draw the best I did all weekend in my feature match!


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


Everything. I've worked so hard for this. I want to thank everyone for supporting me, you're all wonderful. Bryan Carey, Nate Elkus, Anthony Sayre.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpmil18/krut.jpg)


**Name:** Isaak Krut


**Hometown:** Kazan, Russia/Toronto, ON


**Occupation:** student


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**


GP Montreal 2018 Top 8


**What deck did you play this weekend, and why?**


Jeskai Control. It seems to have good matchups against most other top archetypes.


**What was your best card this weekend, and why?**


[Deafening Clarion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deafening+Clarion). I drew it every time I needed one.


**What was the most exciting play you made this weekend?**


[Syncopate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Syncopate) opponent's Niv-Mizzet for zero to trigger my own Niv-Mizzet. Winning with Teferi -3 when I had no other win conditions left was nice too.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**


It takes me Bronze, that gives me two byes at future GPs.
