Round 12: Jon Finkel vs. Derek Bruneau
======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 18, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_626.png)
By Victor van den Broek











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a764.jpg)Ahhh ... it's back to the old days. Johnny Magic, back at the top tables to battle it out for top 8. One more win and a good pairing equal a chance to draw in the top 8. 

### Game 1

Bruneau started with an [Imagecrafter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imagecrafter) and a [Shock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock) for Finkel's second turn [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith). Finkel followed it up with an [Elvish Warrior](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Warrior) and a [Wall of Mulch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wall+of+Mulch). The Wall provided him with a good answer to Bruneau's morph – or so Finkel thought. On his turn, Bruneau flipped over the [Ascending Aven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven) and started serving it up in the sky. Finkel sacrificed his wall and played a morph. Bruneau got a [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) of his own though ... and cycled a Chocking Tethers to tap down Finkel's morph. It didn't stop the [Elvish Warrior](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Warrior) from attacking though, bringing him down to 14. Bruneau's [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) died to a cycled [Solar Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solar+Blast). [Riptide Shapeshifter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Riptide+Shapeshifter) was Bruneau's next answer to Finkel's creatures. It blocked the [Elvish Warrior](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Warrior), but died to a cycled [Primal Boost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Boost) on the Warrior. Finkel's morph turned out to be a [Skirk Commando](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirk+Commando), and it took down the [Ascending Aven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven). Bruneau just lost his flier and Shapeshifter and it didn't cost Finkel a single card. A [Pinpoint Avalanche](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pinpoint+Avalanche) targets Bruneau's next Shapeshifter. Finkel's creatures came in once again, bringing Bruneau down to 8. 

Bruneau cycled a [Choking Tethers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Choking+Tethers), in search for an answer to creatures but failed to find one. Finkel summoned a [Krosan Groundshaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Groundshaker) to finish off the situation and brings Bruneau down to 4. In a last-ditch effort Bruneau cast [Blatant Thievery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blatant+Thievery) on the Groundshaker. Finkel just made an [Elven Riders](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elven+Riders) and Elvish Courier to finish it off. 

Finkel 1 – Bruneau 0

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a805.jpg)Bruneau opened with a turn 2 [Sparksmith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) and a morph on turn 3. Because there was no second goblin just yet, Finkel too played a morph. Bruneau played his fourth [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain) and due to a lack of blue mana was unable to do anything at all. Finkel played [Solar Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solar+Blast) on Bruneau's Smith after Bruneau found his [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) next turn and played a [Mistform Dreamer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Dreamer). The Dreamer blocked Finkel's attacking morph and Finkel cycled his [Primal Boost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Boost) on the morph. A [Wellwisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher) joined Finkel's side of the board while Bruneau only has an extra [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain). He cycled his [Choking Tethers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Choking+Tethers) on Finkel's morph to dig for the second [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island) and found it. After that he played his [Riptide Shapeshifter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Riptide+Shapeshifter), but that too got [Solar Blast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Solar+Blast)ed. The two morphs clashed in combat next turn. After damage was on the stack, Finkel [Vitality Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vitality+Charm)ed his morph. Bruneau spent yet another turn without playing anything.

The [Krosan Groundshaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krosan+Groundshaker) hit the board again, without a big risk of being stolen as Bruneau was still stuck on 2 [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island)s. The creatures beat down, bringing Bruneau down to 8 life. Forced to do something, [Imagecrafter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imagecrafter) and [Goblin Sharpshooter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sharpshooter) came down on his turn. The wizard blocked the [Wellwisher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wellwisher) and the Sharpshooter chumped the morph – bringing Bruneau down to 2. His next draw step didn't provide an answer. Bruneau did have the [Blatant Thievery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blatant+Thievery) – had he drawn his 3rd [Island](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Island). 

**Final Result:** Finkel 2 – Bruneau 0
