Many players recognized [Liliana's Caress](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana%27s+Caress) as similar to [Megrim](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Megrim) when ***Magic** 2011* was released. At the 2010 Finland National Championship, Hannu Vallin realized everyone's dreams by making the Top 8 combining the Caress with the original. To get opponents discarding cards, Hannu used [Blightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blightning), [Burning Inquiry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Inquiry), [Inquisition of Kozilek](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inquisition+of+Kozilek), and even [Mind Rot](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Rot). Combined with additional burn like [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) and [Burst Lightning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burst+Lightning), Vallin made the Top 8 of the event!
