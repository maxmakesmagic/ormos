A Mushroom Man, a Menace, and a Lich Walk into a Deck
=====================================================



 Posted in [Serious Fun](/en/articles/columns/serious-fun-archive)
 on April 29, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_brucerichard.jpg)
By Bruce Richard




 Bruce's games invariably involve several friends, crazy plays, and many laughs. Bruce believes that if anyone at your table isn't having fun, then you are doing it wrong. 





These are the times I hate the most in the **Magic** calendar. You know, the weeks between the Prerelease and the actual release? That time when you know all the cards but can't get your hands on them? Sure, I love the Prerelease,[1](#0)and it was pretty sweet to have seen my spoiler cards before anyone else (neener neener neeeener!), but it just isn't the same! I want the cards—all the cards! 

![](https://media.wizards.com/images/magic/daily/sf/sf245_acidicslime.jpg)[Acidic Slime](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Acidic+Slime) | Art by [Karl Kopinski](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoileramp;method=visualamp;action=advancedamp;artist=[%22Karl Kopinski%22])

 In an effort to reduce the anxiety involved in waiting for the actual cards, I've taken a few of the new *Dragon's Maze* cards and added them to some older cards to get the creative juices flowing.
