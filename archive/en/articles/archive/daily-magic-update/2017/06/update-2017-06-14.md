Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Reiderrabbit's Legacy Elves](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-legacy-constructed-league-2017-06-13)

Fans will recognize the Reiderrabbit moniker, but it's the deck that's most striking. In the world where Miracles was the top deck, Elves struggled in a field of one-mana [Terminus](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terminus) and [Counterbalance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterbalance)/Top that could lock out virtually the entire deck. But with [Sensei's Divining Top](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top) gone, the Elves are free to roam Legacy again.


Could this be one of the big decks that comes out of Las Vegas? Tune in to [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic) starting tomorrow to find out!
