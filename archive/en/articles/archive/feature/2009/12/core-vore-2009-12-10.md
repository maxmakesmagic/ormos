Looking for a new format to try on **Magic Online**? Give Core Set Constructed a try! Using only cards printed in core sets like *Seventh Edition, Eighth Edition*, and ***Magic** 2010*, you build a 60-card deck to battle other players. Thabg3 recently got 5th place in a **Magic Online** event using a [Magnivore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magnivore) deck. Cards like [Wildfire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wildfire), [Stone Rain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stone+Rain), and [Sleight of Hand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sleight+of+Hand) ensured Thabg3 drew all gas while keeping the opponent's resources bogged down. Then, because all those spells were sorceries, a powerful [Magnivore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magnivore) could come out of nowhere to steal the win.
