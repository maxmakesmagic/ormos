Magic Online Born of the Gods Championships
===========================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on February 3, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_MikeTurian.jpg)
By Mike Turian




Before joining Magic Online as its digital product manager, Mike worked as a producer for Wizards's technology department, a product manager for organized play, and as an R&D lead developer. He has played Magic Online since it came out in 2002 and Magic since The Dark.

We're only two weeks away from the **Magic Online** release of *Born of the Gods* ([Prerelease Events](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/01132014) begin on Friday, February 14), and that means it's time to look ahead to the *Born of the Gods* Championship.


There will be two *Born of the Gods*  champions, one for Limited and one for Standard. Events kick off on Wednesday, April 9, with Standard Championship Qualifiers that run through Tuesday, April 15. The *Born of the Gods*  Standard Champion will be crowned after the *Born of the Gods*  Standard Championship on Sunday, April 20.




*Born of the Gods*  Limited Championship Qualifiers begin on Wednesday, April 16, and run through Tuesday, April 22. Then, on Sunday, April 27, the top players will compete for the title of *Born of the Gods*  Limited Champion.


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/avatar_battlethehorde.jpg)  

Battle the Horde Avatar
As we did with [the *Theros*  winners](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/01272014/theroschampionships), we will celebrate our *Born of the Gods*  champions with a winners' page featuring their winning decks and highlights from the matches following the last Championship event!
