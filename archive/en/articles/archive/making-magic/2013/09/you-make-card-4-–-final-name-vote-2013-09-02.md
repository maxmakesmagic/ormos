You Make the Card 4 – Final Name Vote
=====================================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on September 2, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/features/feat262a_ymtc_card.jpg)  
So, last week we had a revote on the Top 4 names for your new card due to an issue with the poll reporting. Except, the revote was also botched as well. For this, I can only apologize, as the blame falls squarely on me and what appears to be a bug in our software.


In light of this problem, which our web team is investigating, we are simplifying the process and trying to be as fair as possible.


The only right thing to do at this point is to run one unified poll with all eight options. While less exciting than a bracket, it gives all of the submitted names the respect they deserve with a poll unmarred by the previous chaos.


So, once again, here are your Top 8 names:



 **Final Vote** 

Vile PlunderDrudge RummageWaste NotSpoils of MiseryRiches From RagsNecroharvestSkullduggeryCarpe Noctem### Thank You


As way of thanking everyone for participating in You Make The Card 4, we have a special achievement you can earn on [PlaneswalkerPoints.com](http://www.planeswalkerpoints.com). Go to your achievement tab in PlaneswalkerPoints.com (you'll need to log in) and enter the code "MADETHECARD" and you'll earn the Card Crafter achievement!
