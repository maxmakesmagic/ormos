**Q:** When will *Return to Ravnica* previews begin?


-Steve, Wichita, KS


**A:** From **Trick Jarrett**, Editor-in-Chief DailyMTG.com


The *Return to Ravnica* previews will launch at our event at PAX Prime on September 1, 2012. The mini-site, preview gallery, and more will go live that evening!
