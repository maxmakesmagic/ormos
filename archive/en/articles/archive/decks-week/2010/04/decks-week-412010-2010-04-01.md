Decks of the Week: 4/1/2010
===========================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on April 1, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











As *Rise of the Eldrazi* approaches, deck builders are getting in their final chances to work on Constructed metagames before the new set arrives. [Grand Prix–Brussels](/en/events/coverage/thrinaxes-sprout-giusti-brussels) set the record for the largest Standard event ever last weekend, with Emanuele Giusti taking home the trophy with Jund, defeating Zoltan Szoke and Mythic in the final. Check out the Day One undefeated lists for Jeroen Aga's Allies deck that didn't lose a match until round 13! If Extended's more to your liking, check out the 11 fresh Top 8s from San Juan PTQs. The [season ends April 11](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/sanjuan10-qualifiers), so there's still a chance to earn that invite and plane ticket to Puerto Rico!
