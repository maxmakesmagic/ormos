***Kaladesh*-*Aether Revolt* Sealed MOCS Monthly on Saturday, February 25**


A *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) Standard Monthly Event is coming up on February 25! Players can spend 35 Qualifier Points (QPs) to enter the event. The series will contain 24 Monthly Events, each of which qualify top players for one of the four Playoff Events. These Playoff Events qualify top players for the *Magic Online* Championship, a Pro Tour, and the Pro Tour Challenge event at that Pro Tour.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points for the year as a whole. Top players on the Leaderboard at the end of the 2017 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.


We look forward to seeing you on February 25 for the latest Monthly Event! Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online* Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).

**The MOPR Promo Card period for March begins tomorrow, February 22**


With a new month comes new MOPR Promo Cards, and the period for March's Promo Cards begins tomorrow, February 22. This months cards are alternate-art versions of [Hero's Downfall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero%27s+Downfall) for Event Participation and [Incendiary Flow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Incendiary+Flow) for Store Activity, which can be seen below.


Players who participate in at least one Constructed, Sealed Deck, or Draft Tournament with a minimum of 8 players will receive a regular version of the alternate-art [Hero's Downfall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero%27s+Downfall). Players who participate in at least five such event with a minimum of eight players will receive a premium version of the card.


Players who spend a full 20 USD in the *Magic Online* Store (excluding coupons, shipping, and taxes) will receive a regular version of the alternate-art [Incendiary Flow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Incendiary+Flow). Players who spend a full 40 USD in the *Magic Online* Store (excluding coupons, shipping, and taxes) will receive a premium version of the card.

**Upcoming extended downtime tomorrow, February 22**


There will be an extended downtime on Wednesday, February 22, for *Magic Online*. Events close at 12:00 a.m. PT/8 a.m. UTC (that's midnight Pacific), and store activity and trades end at 2:45 a.m. leading up to the system coming down at 3:00 a.m. The system is estimated to return from downtime at 12:00 p.m.
