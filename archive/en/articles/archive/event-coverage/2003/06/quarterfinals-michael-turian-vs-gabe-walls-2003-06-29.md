Quarterfinals: Michael Turian vs. Gabe Walls
============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 29, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a731.jpg)Mike Turian was last in the Top 8 of Nationals three years ago, where he came just short of making the National team. Today he has a second chance, but unfortunately, he's against the perceived winner. Despite being the least accomplished player in the Top 8, Gabe Walls is favored to go home with the Championship trophy this weekend, because his [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) deck is the perfect foil for all the creature decks in the Top 8. Particularly, Walls' side of the bracket is loaded with red/green decks, which is a great matchup for Slide. Turian, of course, is running red/green himself.

### Game 1

Turian had no first or second turn play, getting a whistle of surprise out of Walls. [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) came down on turn three, and Turian played his first creature of the game in [Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). Walls played a face-down [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel), and cycled a land to put it under [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide). The Centaur attacked for five damage, and was joined by a second Centaur. The Angel came back into play and got to work in the Red Zone, and Walls played [Teroh's Faithful](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teroh%27s+Faithful). Both Centaurs attacked and were not blocked to deal ten damage, and [Volcanic Hammer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer) targeted [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel).

Turian summoned [Basking Rootwalla](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla), and on end step, [Violent Eruption](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption) looked to finish the job. It was the only play Turian could make since Walls only had one green mana untapped, but Walls had the [Tranquil Thicket](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tranquil+Thicket) to cycle and save his Angel with [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide). At this point, despite Turian's best efforts, the Slide, Angel and Faithful were too much to handle, and Walls easily took the first game.

Turian- 0 Walls- 1

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a732.jpg)This time, the early game was much better for Turian, as he played a first turn [Llanowar Elves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves), followed by [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). [Elephant Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide) went on the [Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) to increase the clock drastically, but [Moment's Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) slowed things down. Turian added more to his side with [Call of the Herd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd), and Walls cast [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God) to clear the board. [Elephant Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide) put a 3/3 into play after it resolved; Turian put [Elephant Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide) on the token, and attacked for six. Walls played [Teroh's Faithful](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teroh%27s+Faithful) and chump blocked with it, while Turian had a [Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) and elf.

A second [Wrath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath) once again cleared the board except for an elephant, which attacked and was joined by another when the [Call](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call) was flashed back. Walls played a face-down Angel, and cast a second [Moment's Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace) out of his hand. The Angel flipped over and attacked, giving Turian a moment of opportunity when Walls couldn't flash back a [Moment's Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace). The attack brought Walls to five, but Turian didn't have the burn he needed to finish the job. Walls played [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide), which Turian [Naturalize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize)d on end step, and Walls responded by cycling [Renewed Faith](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith) and killing a token. Turian cast another [Call](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call), and Walls tapped six to play a second Angel, taking the second game.

Turian- 0 Walls- 2

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a730.jpg)Turian mulliganed his opening hand, and had a first turn [Basking Rootwalla](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla). It attacked for three twice, and was joined by a second Rootwalla. [Slice and Dice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slice+and+Dice) was cycled to kill both of them, and Turian summoned [Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). Another cycled [Slice and Dice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slice+and+Dice) made it a little smaller, and Turian played [Call of the Herd](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd). [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God) killed the creatures on the board, and Turian flashed back the [Call](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call). Walls was now at six lands, and tapped out to play [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel). A pair of [Volcanic Hammer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer)s killed it, clearing the way for an elephant attack that brought Walls to seven.

Thanks to [Nantuko Monastery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Monastery), Turian's elephants were held back from attacking. Walls found another [Exalted Angel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Exalted+Angel), and Turian played [Elephant Guide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide) on one of his tokens, then attacked with it. Walls blocked, and cast [Disenchant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disenchant) on the Guide before damage. The Angel survived, but ended up dying to burn a few turns later. [Nantuko Monastery](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Monastery) took care of the rest, as [Teroh's Faithful](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teroh%27s+Faithful) and [Astral Slide](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Astral+Slide) made sure Turian couldn't mount a comeback.

Turian- 0 Walls- 3
