Guildmages
==========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 17, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







Today's deck, played by mtgo4macplz, is definitely my kind of deck. In addition to the newly printed [Voice of Resurgence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence) and [Ready & Willing](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ready+%26+Willing), this deck uses both [Korozda Guildmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Korozda+Guildmage) and [Vitu-Ghazi Guildmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vitu-Ghazi+Guildmage), which have not seen much play since their printing in *Return to Ravnica*. 

![Vitu-Ghazi Guildmage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Vitu-Ghazi+Guildmage)
[Vitu-Ghazi Guildmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vitu-Ghazi+Guildmage) is greatly improved by [Voice of Resurgence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voice+of+Resurgence), which offers an amazing token at an excellent rate. [Korozda Guildmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Korozda+Guildmage) is very clever with [Blood Artist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Artist) and [Lingering Souls](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lingering+Souls) as another way to make creatures die and create more tokens. Unfortunately, the Elemental token can't be sacrificed, but all the tokens [Korozda Guildmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Korozda+Guildmage) can make will make it huge, and giving it intimidate can make it lethal. 


[Trostani, Selesnya's Voice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trostani%2C+Selesnya%27s+Voice) offers another way to populate the Elemental token and gain huge amounts of life, giving the deck more time to take over with Guildmages. 

 The most surprising thing to me is the extremely low land count of twenty-two, with so many different ways to use mana every turn, between the Guildmages; [Gavony Township](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gavony+Township); and [Trostani, Selesnya's Voice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trostani%2C+Selesnya%27s+Voice). The spells are cheap, so the deck can operate off a low number of lands, but with so many ways to use mana, it looks like it's almost always happy to have a land to put into play. [Avacyn's Pilgrim](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avacyn%27s+Pilgrim) helps, but I'd still want some more. 

 On what might be a more casual note, I'd love to see [Death's Presence](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Presence) in this deck, with [Korozda Guildmage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Korozda+Guildmage), to really go crazy.
