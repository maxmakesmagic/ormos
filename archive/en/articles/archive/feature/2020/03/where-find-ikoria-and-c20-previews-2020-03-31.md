Where to Find Ikoria and C20 Previews
=====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 31, 2020 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*Ikoria: Lair of Behemoths* previews are almost upon us! And this time, they're monster size.


It can be a bit daunting to try to follow along with everything when everything is . . . So. Very. Big. But this page is going to be your guide.


This page will help you follow along with everything coming on every day by giving you the outlets we have planned on each day. The goal is to help you find what you're looking for every day and avoid engaging in leaks (which hopefully don't happen [knock on wood]) if you so choose.


Below, you'll find a list of dates as well as any outlets previewing a card on that date. Once the previews are live, we'll update this very article so that you can easily find previews once they're up. If the link simply goes to the front of a website or social media site, that means they probably haven't previewed the card yet.




---

Tune in April 2 at 7 a.m. PT/10 a.m. ET/2 p.m. UTC to [Twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic) as we kick things off  with a MASSIVE debut moment, includuing the worldwide debut of the *Ikoria: Lair of Behemoths* trailer!




---

But wait! There's more! Monster-size previews over just eight days means you're getting so very much more every day—including a Commander Weekend!


You see, this is really two preview seasons in one—*Ikoria* and *Commander 2020*. So you're going to start seeing Commander previews starting on April 3. Then April 4–5 are all Commander, all the time. On April 6, we'll start getting back to regular *Ikoria* previews, but throughout the day starting first thing in the morning (pacific time, at least), we'll have five of our biggest Commander channels showing off the full decklists of all five of the decks!


And if you just want to see all the cards that have been officially previewed in one place, you can always check out our [Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/ikoria-lair-behemoths) and our bonus [Variants Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/ikoria-lair-behemoths-variants) (which contains alternate versions of *Ikoria: Lair of Behemoths* cards). Note that the CIGs aren't published as of the posting of this article but will be updated every morning starting Friday, April 3, so they will contain all cards previewed the previous day (and earlier), but not the day of. For the most up-to-date listings, you can check this page!
