Attendance Statistics
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












There are 60 players in the tournament.


**Country Breakdown**


Brazil - 19  

Argentina - 15  

Mexico - 10  

Chile - 8  

Venezuela - 3  

Uruguay - 3  

Peru - 2


22 players that attended the tournament received invitations by finishing well in the national championships.


38 players that attended the tournament received invitations based on their DCI Composite rating.
