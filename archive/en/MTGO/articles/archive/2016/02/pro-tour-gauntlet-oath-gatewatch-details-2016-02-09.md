Pro Tour Gauntlet: Oath of the Gatewatch Details
================================================



 Posted in [Magic Online](/en/en/gameinfo/products/magiconline/resources)
 on February 9, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Pro Tour Gauntlet events for Pro Tour: *Oath of the Gatewatch* run from the February 10 downtime until February 17. Event details and available decklists can be found below!


* [Pro Tour Gauntlet Event Details](#PTGauntletOGWEvents)
* [Pro Tour Gauntlet Decklists](#Decks)



---


**Pro Tour Gauntlet**


**Start Times:** Wednesday, February 10, after downtime until Wednesday, February 17, downtime.  
**Location:** Play Lobby -> Constructed Tournaments -> Queues  
**Entry Option:**


* 6 Event Tickets
* 60 Play Points

**Product:** *Magic Online* one randomly-selected phantom deck from the Pro Tour Gauntlet decklists. Phantom product will not be added to a player's collection at the conclusion of the event.  
**Size:** 8 players  
**Play Style:** Swiss  
**Format:** Modern  
**Duration:** 3 minutes deck review. Three rounds, each round up to 50 minutes.  
**Prizes:**




| Place | Prizes | QPs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 3 Wins | 90 Play Points | 1 |
| 2 Wins | 60 Play Points | 0 |
| 1 Win | 20 Play Points | 0 |

**NOTE:** Wizards of the Coast employees and other persons affiliated with it are eligible to play in this event. For more information, please see this article and this FAQ about this policy.
