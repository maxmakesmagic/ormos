2003 World Championships Webcast Archive Index
==============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 13, 2003 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds03/a446.jpg)*Daniel Zink in the finals of the 2003 Magic: the Gathering World Championships*Welcome to the archive of the 2003 World Championship Top 8 Webcast!

On August 10, the Top 8 played off in a single-elimination playoff to determine the World Champion. In between the rounds, Teams USA and Finland played a round of Team Rochester Draft for the title of Team Champions. Wizards of the Coast had a live streaming webcast the whole day, so readers at home were able to watch the action as it unfolded.

And now we've got the files available for download, so you can relive the event as though you were there! The files are very large ZIP files, so we recommend that you have a Broadband connection.
