Jedit Ojanen Comic
==================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 29, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











You may know [Jedit Ojanen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jedit+Ojanen) as a 7-mana blue-and-white legend from *Legends*: 

![Jedit Ojanen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Jedit+Ojanen)But he also starred in a 1996 comic book from Armada / Acclaim Comics written to tell his story:

  


During issue 1 of 2 of Jedit's story, he encounters other creatures seen on **Magic** cards. He spots a [Fire Drake](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fire+Drake) and gets a [Healing Salve](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Healing+Salve) from passing [Desert Nomads](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desert+Nomads). At the cliffhanger climax on the last page of the issue, he encounters this recognizable fellow:
