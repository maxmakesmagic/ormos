A blast from the past, Tax Rack White Weenie was a deck bent on abusing the powerful draw engine [Land Tax](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Land+Tax). By playing an aggressive weenie deck, Pro Tour Champion Randy Buehler was able to keep a low in-play land count so that he could best abuse [Land Tax](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Land+Tax), drawing three cards a turn! He turned that advantage by abusing the additional cards with [Firestorm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firestorm), [Mox Diamond](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Diamond), and [Scroll Rack](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scroll+Rack). Being able to re-shuffle his deck each turn as well as drawing three additional cards was powerful enough to net him an Extended Championship title!
