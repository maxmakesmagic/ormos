Pauper is a fun format for **Magic Online** that prohibits the use of rares, mythic rares, and uncommon, requiring players to build entire decks using only commons. Baldr7 was up to the task, putting together this black-green madness / graveyard deck. Powerful spells like [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) allow madness spells like [Grave Scrabbler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grave+Scrabbler) to hit the battlefield on the cheap.
