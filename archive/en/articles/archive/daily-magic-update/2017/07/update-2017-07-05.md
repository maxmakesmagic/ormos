The July 5, 2017 Update
=======================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on July 5, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on July 5, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by the relief of every dog in America, now that their evening of terror and explosions is over.


Before we get started, there's one big thing that you may have missed from over the weekend: the [*Hour of Devastation*Pre-Prerelease](https://youtu.be/4Owug74znr0)! Be sure to catch up on all the action as you gear up for the Prerelease this weekend.


Today's Must
============


1. [*Hour of Devastation* Limited Set Review: Blue](https://www.channelfireball.com/articles/hour-of-devastation-limited-set-review-blue/) | Channel Fireball | Luis Scott-Vargas

It has begun. Or, more accurately, it began on Monday with his review of white, but I was on an island celebrating the day before our country's Independence Day, like a good American. If you were off holidaying around as well, make sure you catch up on the must-read Limited review of the season.


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [*Hour of Devastation* Commander Set Review](https://collected.company/2017/07/04/hour-devastation-commander-set-review-165/) | The Command Zone | Josh Lee Kwai and Jimmy Wong

LSV's article might be a must read, but it's far from the only one you should check out. There will be dozens of set reviews, and I'll try to pick out the best/most interesting/highest ratio of Josh Lee Kwais per episode. This one is at a 1:1 on the JLK scale, so certainly don't miss it.


1. [*Hour of Devastation* Spoilers: Best of the Rest Set Review—Part 2: Blue!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LYvlbYq0VVM) | The Mana Source | Wedge

More set reviews! What's amazing is that Wedge is putting out videos despite [his house literally being flooded over the weekend](https://twitter.com/TheManaSource/status/881891381987467265).


I don't know what a house being figuratively flooded looks like, but I would imagine the cleanup is much easier than what Wedge had to go through to get these videos into your eye holes.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/LYvlbYq0VVM>


1. [GP Vegas Art Show Review](http://www.gatheringmagic.com/mikelinnemann-07052017-gp-vegas-art-show-review/) | Gathering Magic | Mike Linnemann

This is a different sort of review, but I'll allow it. The Grand Prix Las Vegas Art Show was unique for the world of *Magic*, and we'd love to see it happen again in some form or fashion.


1. [Rhystic Study: Razaketh, the Foulblooded](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/prophet-of-kruphix/rhystic-study-razaketh-the-foulblooded) | MTG Mint Card | Prophet of Kruphix

Why bother with reviews when you can skip right to the brewing? Powerful card is powerful, so start building!


1. [Not. A. Gimmick.—GP Cleveland's All-Women Judge Team](https://blogs.magicjudges.org/seacat/not-a-gimmick-gp-clevelands-all-women-judge-team/) | Magic Judges Blog | Meghan Baum

Baum shares the story of the all-female judge team at Grand Prix Cleveland. But a team for, like, judging. Not playing. That's a touch confusing at a team event.


1. [Commander Anthology Gameplay w/ NFL Player Cassius Marsh—Game Knights #8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8NxfPRizX6I) | The Command Zone | Josh Lee Kwai and Jimmy Wong

Josh Kim and Cassius Marsh join Jimmy and Josh to try out *Commander Anthology* in another awesome episode. The JLK ratio is at 1:1 for this episode as we double-dip on The Command Zone today, so you know it's worth watching.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/8NxfPRizX6I>
