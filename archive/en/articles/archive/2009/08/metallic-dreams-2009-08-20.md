Metallic Dreams
===============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 20, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












P*lanechase* is coming on September 4! Refresh yourself on the rules [here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/feature/51), then prepare to purchase! Each deck will have an MSRP of $19.99. Today, we're going to tell you what's in the Metallic dreams deck. We'll show you the plane deck first, then move on to the sixty-card **Magic** deck. And watch for the *Zendikar* preview card!


### Metallic Dreams


The "Metallic Dreams" deck uses the power of artifacts to quickly produce the mana needed to cast powerful, mana-hungry creatures. Planeswalk to the Hippodrome to bog down combat while you amass your forces, then demolish the opposition.
