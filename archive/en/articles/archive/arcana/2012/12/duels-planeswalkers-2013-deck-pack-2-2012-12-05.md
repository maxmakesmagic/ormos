Duels of the Planeswalkers 2013 Deck Pack 2
===========================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on December 5, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











There's another Deck Pack for [**Duels of the Planeswalkers** 2013](http://www.wizards.com/magic/digital/duelsoftheplaneswalkers.aspx)! The first pack (which was released [last month](/en/articles/archive/duels-2013s-deck-pack-2012-11-07)) had a red-white deck and a green-blue deck. Or "Boros" and "Simic" if you prefer. And this month, we've got white-black (Orzhov) and red-green (Gruul). And both decks are usable in both the campaign and in multiplayer. Enjoy!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/1118_deckpack2.jpg)  
### Grim Procession

The end is nigh! Wield the spirits, shades, and life-sapping magic in the white and black “Grim Procession” deck to show opponents that their defeat is inevitable.
