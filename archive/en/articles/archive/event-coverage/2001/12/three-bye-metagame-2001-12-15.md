The Three-Bye Metagame
======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 15, 2001 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_746.png)
By Ron Foster and Josh Bennett











Looking over these decklists, the most striking thing about them is the number of pros banking on Donate. After being hated out of the Top 8 at Vegas, it seemed that the perennial combo menace had finally been forced off the scene. However, it's still here, and it's got some high-profile sponsors.


After that, of course the standouts are those who chose to follow in the footsteps of Mike Pustilnik, hoping they would lead to the championship trophy. Certainly, with a field this wide-open, midrange decks seem like the way to go. But everyone's got a theory, and no-one's sure what works. It's an exciting and frustrating season. See if you can make sense of it.








#### Kazuya Hirabayashi
