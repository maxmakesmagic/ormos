The Tokens of Commander (2019 Edition)
======================================



 Posted in **Card Preview**
 on August 8, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/author_Gleeson.jpg)
By Chris Gleeson




 Chris is the copy editor for DailyMTG. If the Ts are dotted and the eyes are crossed, he dun goofed. 






A whopping 29 tokens (including one emblem) are coming with *Commander (2019 Edition)*! They're all full-art, as you may have seen in the tokens from *Core Set 2020*.


If you haven't seen the cards that create these tokens (and one emblem), head over to the Card Image Gallery to check them out for yourself.


If you want to dive right in to these full-art beauties, keep scrolling!
