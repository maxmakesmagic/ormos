TOP 4 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 9, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim











148 teams battled at Grand Prix Mexico but only four teams remain after 14 rounds of intense competition. Let's get to know these 12 players a little!



### Kazuyuki Takimura / Shota Takao / Toru Inoue


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmex17/a_gpMexico2017_Top8_Team1_TakimuraTakaoOnoue.jpg)


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmex17/gpMexico2017_Top8_Team1A_Inoue.jpg)Name:** Toru Inoue


**Age:** 28


**Hometown:** Fukuoka, Japan


**Previous** **Magic Accomplishments:**


Top 8 at Grand Prix Beijing 2016


**Who did you team with this weekend and why?**


Shota Takao and Kazuyuki Takimura, because Takimura is the master of Team Limited.


**Say something nice (if possible) about your teammates.**


Takimura is a great leader. He kept our spirits up and helped us maintain the best mental condition to compete.


**What was the best card(s) in your decks this weekend?**


[Freejam Regent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Freejam+Regent) and [Herald of Anguish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Herald+of+Anguish). I had both cards in both of my Sealed Decks.


**What's your most memorable moment this weekend?**  

As above.


**What is your favorite previewed Amonkhet card so far?**


Cycling Dual Lands.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmex17/gpMexico2017_Top8_Team1B_Takao.jpg)Name:** Shota Takao


**Age:** 28


**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan


**Previous** **Magic Accomplishments:**


Three other Grand Prix Top 8s


**Who did you team with this weekend and why?**


Takimura is a master at Team Limited and Inoue is a “Limits” Champion. (“Limits” is a large scale Limited tournament held in Japan.)


**Say something nice (if possible) about your teammates.**


They're crazy.


**What was the best card(s) in your decks this weekend?**


[Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter).


**What's your most memorable moment this weekend?**  

Our Sealed pools on Saturday and Sunday were very similar, even the uncommons and rares!


**What is your favorite previewed Amonkhet card so far?**


Soul-[Scar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scar) Mage. It feels like Kazuyuki Takimura's Invitational card if he had one.




---

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmex17/gpMexico2017_Top8_Team1C_Takimura.jpg)Name:** Kazuyuki Takimura


**Age:** 32


**Hometown:** Japan


**Previous** **Magic Accomplishments:**


Pro Tour Battle for Zendikar Champion


**Who did you team with this weekend and why?**


Shota Takao and Toru Inoue, because both of them are great players!


**Say something nice (if possible) about your teammates.**


Toru Inoue won all his matches on Day 1. He is a really good Limited player.


**What was the best card(s) in your decks this weekend?**


[Baral's Expertise](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baral%27s+Expertise)


**What's your most memorable moment this weekend?**  

Saturday's card pool was very close to Sunday's card pool.


**What is your favorite previewed Amonkhet card so far?**


Soul-[Scar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scar) Mage (He looks like me.)

### Jon Stern / Greg Ogreenc / Stephen Neal


![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmex17/article_gpMexico2017_Top8_Team3_SternOgreencNeal.jpg)


**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmex17/gpMexico2017_Top8_Team3A_Stern.jpg)Name:** Jon Stern


**Age:** 39


**Hometown:** Victoria, Canada


**Previous** **Magic Accomplishments:**


7 other Grand Prix Top 8s including two wins.


**Who did you team with this weekend and why?**


Greg Ogreenc and Stephen Neal. I've teamed with Greg for a while and we needed a third person who could win every match.


**Say something nice (if possible) about your teammates.**


Both are awesome teammates who are process-oriented and understand how to work within the constraints of their teammates' strengths and weaknesses.


**What was the best card(s) in your decks this weekend?**


[Winding Constrictor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Winding+Constrictor) and [Ridgescale Tusker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ridgescale+Tusker)


**What's your most memorable moment this weekend?**  

Sweating tiebreaks and jumping two teams in the last round.


**What is your favorite previewed Amonkhet card so far?**


Cycling Dual Lands.

**![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gpmex17/gpMexico2017_Top8_Team3B_Ogreenc.jpg)Name:** Greg Ogreenc


**Age:** 30


**Hometown:** Milwaukee, Wisconsin, USA


**Previous** **Magic Accomplishments:**


Top 8 at Grand Prix Toronto 2013 and Grand Prix Kansas City 2013.


**Who did you team with this weekend and why?**


Jon Stern and Stephen Neal. Jon provides a calm, deliberate temperament while offers a dose of Madison Limited expertise.


**Say something nice (if possible) about your teammates.**


Stephen (he went 9-0) helped me carry Jon yesterday and he is a good friend of mine from Wisconsin. Jon has been my teammate for a few consecutive Grand Prix and he has great work ethic.


**What was the best card(s) in your decks this weekend?**


[Caught in the Brights](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Caught+in+the+Brights) and [Restoration Specialist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Restoration+Specialist) in Day 1 and double [Renegade Freighter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renegade+Freighter) in Day 2.


**What's your most memorable moment this weekend?**  

Stephen lost one match all weekend while Jon only won four matches. When Jon won Round 9, we joked that we couldn't lose.


**What is your favorite previewed Amonkhet card so far?**


Not sure, haven't looked at the cards yet.
