Nexus of Fate Update
--------------------


We realize that [Nexus of Fate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nexus+of+Fate) hasn't worked its way into as many decks as we like, so we are releasing more into the wild on *MTGO.* Check out this [Tumblr post](http://wizardsmtgo.tumblr.com/post/176534688819/update-nexus-of-fate) for more information.

MOCS Playoff
------------



MOCS Playoff event is this weekend, August 11 at 7 a.m. PT, and the format is Modern. To enter you must have finished with six or more wins in a MOCS Monthly event that has occurred since the previous MOCS Playoff event. The top 2 finishers will be invited to the 2018 Magic Online Championship, Pro Tour *Guilds of Ravnica* and the Pro Tour Challenge at that event.


We hope to see you there!





---

PTQ Sunday
----------



We're having a Pro Tour Qualifier event on August 12, at 7 a.m. PT. This event will feature *Core Set 2019* Sealed and entry will cost a Chandra or Jace PTQ Finals Entry Token. **NOTE**: This is the last chance to use Chandra PTQ Entry Tokens before they expire!


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

Downtimes August 29 and September 26
------------------------------------



The next two downtimes will be August 29, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC and August 26, 3 a.m.–noon PT/-7 from UTC. Please note that there are no more 'extended' or 'normal' downtimes; in the new world with fewer downtimes, they're all the same length of time.
