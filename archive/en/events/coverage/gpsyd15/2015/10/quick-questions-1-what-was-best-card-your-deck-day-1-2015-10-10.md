Quick Questions #1: What was the best card in your deck on Day 1?
=================================================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX SYDNEY 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpsyd15)
 on October 11, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Walkinshaw-Profile.jpg)
By Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw












 What was the best card in your deck on Day 1?
===============================================


After a day of *Battling for Zendikar* with sealed decks, I'm pretty sure most players will have an MVP in their deck, the card their feel won them the most games, or caused their opponents the most grief. I set out to ask them what it was.



![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsyd15/GPSydney_D2_QQ_Chung_2.jpg)  
**Jason Chung:** [Dispel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dispel). It actually won me some games.





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsyd15/GPSydney_D2_QQ_Bretherton_LG.jpg)  
**Dan Bretherton:** [Tightening Coils](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tightening+Coils).





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsyd15/GPSydney_D2_QQ_Loy_LG.jpg)  
**Sam Loy:** [Shambling Vent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shambling+Vent)s   
*(editor: it's actually "[Shambling Vent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shambling+Vent)")*  

 Sam Loy: Not the number of times I saw it in play, haha!





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsyd15/GPSydney_D2_QQ_Sugaya_LG.jpg)  
**Hironobu Sugaya:** [Woodland Wanderer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woodland+Wanderer).





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsyd15/GPSydney_D2_QQ_Chew_LG.jpg)  
**Kelvin Chew:** [Sheer Drop](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sheer+Drop).





---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpsyd15/GPSydney_D2_QQ_Nicastri_LG.jpg)  
**Aaron Nicastri:** [Serpentine Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serpentine+Spike).
