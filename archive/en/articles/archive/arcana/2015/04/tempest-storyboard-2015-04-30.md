Tempest Storyboard
==================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 30, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






*Tempest* used the card art from the set to explicitly show the story, virtually scene by scene. As part of *Tempest Remastered* Week, we’ve recreated the storyboard originally published years ago when *Tempest* was released. Enjoy!




---

*This feature originally appeared in* Duelist *#20. It is presented here in its entirety.*


T*empest* is the first set to illustrate its entire story from start to finish in its artwork. But why explain when we can just show you…


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_GerardsBattlecry.jpg)


[Gerrard's Battle Cry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gerrard%27s+Battle+Cry)—As the *Weatherlight* arrives in Rath to rescue Sisay, Gerrard and his crew are suddenly attacked by Volrath's own flying ship, the *Predator*. Through her link with Crovax, the dark angel Selenia has led the warship to the *Weatherlight*.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Invulnerability.jpg)


[Invulnerability](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Invulnerability)—The *Weatherlight* crew confronts the *Predator*'s forces, including the moggs that swarm the ship in search of the Legacy artifacts—one of which is Karn.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Pacifism.jpg)


[Pacifism](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pacifism)—Maintaining his vow to never harm another living being, Karn offers no resistance as the moggs surround him and haul him back to the *Predator*.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_LegacysAllure.jpg)


[Legacy's Allure](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Legacy%27s+Allure)—The moggs sweep the ship in search of the rest of the Legacy artifacts, sniffing about the holds for them. They are successful in finding and removing all but one of the artifacts.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Legerdemain.jpg)


[Legerdemain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Legerdemain)—Squee, the *Weatherlight*'s goblin cabin boy, sneaks below deck to save his favorite artifact, "Squee's toy," from the mogg searchers by hiding with it.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_CommanderGrevenilVec.jpg)


[Commander Greven il-Vec](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Commander+Greven+il-Vec)—As the *Weatherlight* crew struggles to hold the invaders at bay, the *Predator*'s commander, Greven *il-*Vec, boards the ship to personally take Gerrard prisoner.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_NoQuarter.jpg)


[No Quarter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=No+Quarter)—Moggs scatter when Greven finally finds Gerrard and closes in on the *Weatherlight*'s captain.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_SadisticGlee.jpg)


[Sadistic Glee](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sadistic+Glee)—During the battle that follows, Greven's polearm strikes home, cutting Gerrard's lip and driving him to the ship's railing. Greven moves in to finish the fight and take Gerrard back to Volrath.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_VhatiilDal.jpg)


[Vhati il-Dal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vhati+il-Dal)—Meanwhile, on board the *Predator*, Greven's first mate sees a prime opportunity to rid himself of Greven and gain command of the *Predator* for himself. He orders the ship's cannons to be fired on the *Weatherlight*.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Capsize.jpg)


[Capsize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Capsize)—The *Weatherlight* lists under the barrage of fire from the *Predator*, destabilizing the smaller ship.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_SuddenImpact.jpg)


[Sudden Impact](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=4852)—The blasts from the *Predator* scatter allies and enemies alike. Caught off guard by the blast, Gerrard plunges over the *Weatherlight*'s side, reaching for Hanna as she tries to save him.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_AbandonHope.jpg)


[Abandon Hope](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abandon+Hope)—Unfortunately. Hanna is too late to save Gerrard. He disappears down into the dark forest below as she watches helplessly.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_TahngarthsRage.jpg)


[Tahngarth's Rage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tahngarth%27s+Rage)—Its raid completed, the *Predator* retreats from the crippled *Weatherlight*, but the minotaur Tahngarth is not willing to let it go so easily. He leaps to the enemy ship as it pulls away.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Repentance.jpg)


[Repentance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Repentance)—Back aboard the *Predator*, a furious Greven *il-*Vec confronts Vhati *il-*Dal about the first mate's treachery in firing cannons at the *Weatherlight*.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_DiabolicEdict.jpg)


[Diabolic Edict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Edict)—Despite his pleas, Vhati *il-*Dal faces an ironic fate when Greven throws him overboard to his death.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_SkyshroudForest.jpg)


[Skyshroud Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyshroud+Forest)—Below the two flying ships lies the thick forest of Skyshroud. Gerrard plummets down into these dark woods, crashing through the trees and brush to the muck below.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_BrokenFall.jpg)


[Broken Fall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Broken+Fall)—Gerrard's fall into the dark forest is broken by the dense foliage but draws unwanted attention from a new enemy—merfolk.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Stun.jpg)


[Stun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stun)—The hideous merfolk are denizens of Rootwater, the marsh-like body of water Skyshroud Forest sits upon. The merfolk attack, but Gerrard breaks free with a well-placed blow to the first attacker.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_RootwaterShaman.jpg)


[Rootwater Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rootwater+Shaman)—As Gerrard flees, the merfolk leader orders his patrol to give chase.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_OracleenVec.jpg)


[Oracle en-Vec](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oracle+en-Vec)—After eluding the Rootwater merfolk, Gerrard finds himself amidst a gathering of the Vec on a pilgrimage through Skyshroud. Their leader, the Oracle, believes Gerrard to be a prophesized hero, the Korvecdal.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Anoint.jpg)


[Anoint](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Anoint)—The Vec tend Gerrard's wounds and invite him to join them as they make their way to the village of the Skyshroud elves. Gerrard accepts.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_MoggConscripts.jpg)


[Mogg Conscripts](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogg+Conscripts)—Meanwhile, on board the *Predator*, Tahngarth has managed to find Karn, and the two are spotted by moggs while attempting to escape.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_HandtoHand.jpg)


[Hand to Hand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hand+to+Hand)—Once cornered, Tahngarth fights ferociously against the moggs, but soon Greven *il-*Vec joins the battle and the tide turns against the minotaur.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_GiantStrength.jpg)


[Giant Strength](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Giant+Strength)—The crippled *Weatherlight* lands in Skyshroud to begin repairs. As Hanna and Mirri head off in search of Gerrard, who they believe may be dead, Orim notes Crovax's seemingly inhuman strength as they tend the wounded.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_ElvenWarhounds.jpg)


[Elven Warhounds](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elven+Warhounds)—Hanna and Mirri have not been long in Skyshroud when monstrous elven warhounds pick up their scent and begin to pursue the pair.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_MirrisGuile.jpg)


[Mirri's Guile](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirri%27s+Guile)—Using her cat warrior skills, Mirri leads Hanna deeper into the forest in an effort to lose the warhounds and their elven riders.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_UnstableShapeshifter.jpg)


[Unstable Shapeshifter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unstable+Shapeshifter)—Even as they elude the pursuing elves, Hanna and Mirri suddenly find themselves confronted by an odd creature that tries to imitate both of their features.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Gallantry.jpg)


[Gallantry](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gallantry)—The shapeshifter attacks, changing its appearance again, and sends Mirri sprawling. Hanna steps in to defend her friend from the monstrosity, killing it.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_NaturalSpring.jpg)


[Natural Spring](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=4773)—Once the shapeshifter is dead, Hanna helps Mirri to a nearby spring, but the running water drowns out the approach of yet another enemy…



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_ElvishFury.jpg)


[Elvish Fury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elvish+Fury)—…the Skyshroud elves. The elves attack, surprising Hanna and Mirri and taking them prisoner.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_EladamriLordofLeaves.jpg)


[Eladamri, Lord of Leaves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eladamri%2C+Lord+of+Leaves)—They are taken before the elven lord, Eladamri, who has just received into his court other visitors: the Vec… and Gerrard.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Insight.jpg)


[Insight](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Insight)—Gerrard explains his mission to Eladamri, convincing the Lord of Leaves to free Hanna and Mirri.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Respite.jpg)


[Respite](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Respite)—Eladamri's forces have discovered and surrounded the *Weatherlight*, but Eladamri—now allied with Gerrard and the Vec—is able to recall them before blood is shed.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Reap.jpg)


[Reap](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reap)—Once calm has been restored, Gerrard, Eladamri, Starke, the Oracle *en-* Vec, and the others sit down to discuss Gerrard's plans for reaching Volrath's stronghold.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Duplicity.jpg)


[Duplicity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duplicity)—Starke begins to suspect that the Oracle has recognized him as a servant of Volrath, and he plots to assassinate the old woman. Ultimately, he is forced to abandon his intentions when her bodyguards unexpectedly return.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_HelmofPossession.jpg)


[Helm of Possession](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Helm+of+Possession)—Meanwhile, the *Predator* has returned to Volrath's stronghold, where the evil evincar himself awaits his prizes.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Coercion.jpg)


[Coercion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Coercion)—Furious that Greven *il-*Vec failed to capture Gerrard, Volrath uses his dark magic to torture the *Predator*'s commander.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_TortureChamber.jpg)


[Torture Chamber](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torture+Chamber)—Certain that Gerrard is not dead and will come for his companions, Volrath imprisons the minotaur and the golem in his torture chamber.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_VolrathsCurse.jpg)


[Volrath's Curse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volrath%27s+Curse)—In time, the evincar turns his attention to his two prisoners. For Tahngarth, physical torture…



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Propaganda.jpg)


[Propaganda](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=4718)—...and for Karn, mental torture.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_SoltariPriest.jpg)


[Soltari Priest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soltari+Priest)—Following directions provided by Eladamri, the *Weatherlight* crew arrives at a mysterious portal, perhaps the only means for the ship to leave Rath. Their arrival is noted by strange shadow beings.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_small_Intuition.jpg)


[Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition)—Using ancient texts, Orim the Samite healer is able to translate the runes surrounding the portal. She tells the crew that only powerful magic can manipulate the device.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_SoltariEmissary.jpg)


[Soltari Emissary](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soltari+Emissary)—Ertai volunteers to stay behind and prepare the portal for the *Weatherlight*'s return from the stronghold. No sooner does the ship depart than Ertai is confronted by the mysterious Soltari.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_ShadowRift.jpg)


[Shadow Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadow+Rift)—The Soltari emissary, Lyna, draws Ertai into her world—the world of the shadows—populated by beings caught between Dominaria and Rath.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_RealityAnchor.jpg)


[Reality Anchor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Reality+Anchor)—Ertai then does the same to Lyna, pulling her into the "reality" of Rath. The two can then begin to discuss the plight of the Soltari and Lyna's interest in the portal.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_WhispersoftheMuse.jpg)


[Whispers of the Muse](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whispers+of+the+Muse)—As the *Weatherlight* crosses Rath after leaving the portal, Crovax feels the draw of Selenia, the dark angel he has followed to Rath.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_CinderMarsh.jpg)


[Cinder Marsh](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cinder+Marsh)—The *Weatherlight* reaches the Cinder Marsh, polluted by ventilation ducts that lead to the Furnace of Rath beneath Volrath's mountain. The ducts are a back-door route to the stronghold, recommended by both Eladamri and Starke.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_MetallicSliver.jpg)


[Metallic Sliver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metallic+Sliver)—Just beyond the Cinder Marsh, the *Weatherlight* flies into the massive lair of the slivers, vicious creatures that seem to share a hive mind.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Disenchant.jpg)


[Disenchant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disenchant)—Hanna destroys the artificial slivers, but others keep coming, undaunted.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_ClotSliver.jpg)


[Clot Sliver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clot+Sliver)—Soon enough, Hanna realizes that the slivers must be separated in order to be beaten.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Interdict.jpg)


[Interdict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Interdict)—Thanks to Hanna's quick thinking, the crew is able to defeat the slivers and travel deeper into Volrath's mountain, bound for the stronghold.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_FurnaceofRath.jpg)


[Furnace of Rath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Furnace+of+Rath)—The *Weatherlight* next reaches the scalding Furnace, where fire and electricity fill the air.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Kindle.jpg)


[Kindle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kindle)—Gerrard, Crovax, and the others scramble to put out fires that began all over the decks. Crew members cry out in agony as the battle the raging flames.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Havoc.jpg)


[Havoc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Havoc)—Orim focuses the wrath of the Furnace on herself when she uses her white magic to heal the injured crew members.


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_DeathPitsofRath.jpg)


[Death Pits of Rath](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Pits+of+Rath)—The *Weatherlight* leaves behind the Furnace and sails on into the Death Pits, where horrible monstrosities begin to awaken below.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_Carrionette.jpg)


[Carrionette](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carrionette)—The horrors from the Pits leap aboard, dragging crew members to their deaths. Terrified, Squee scrambles out of reach, bearing his toy as he goes.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_HerosResolve.jpg)


[Hero's Resolve](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hero%27s+Resolve)—Gerrard stands his ground against the carrionettes as they close in on him, giving the rest of the crew time to get below deck to safety.



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_OrimsPrayer.jpg)


[Orim's Prayer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Orim%27s+Prayer)—With the Samite healer's blessing to back him, Gerrard charges into the midst of the carrionettes, destroying them. As he and Orim turn to escape below, Gerrard realizes that Squee is still on deck!



> 
> 
> 
> ---
> 
> 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/cardart_SqueesToy.jpg)


[Squee's Toy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Squee%27s+Toy)—As Gerrard rescues Squee and fights his was back through the carrionette reinforcements, Squee accidentally activates his toy—just in time for it to save Gerrard from an attacking enemy. The two escape below deck.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/images/daily/ToBeContinued.jpg)


Beyond the Death Pits lies Gerrard's ultimate goal: Volrath's stronghold. As the ship approaches the vast fortress, Gerrard wonders if Sisay is still alive within, and if Volrath knows he's coming…


*—Storyboard arranged by Michael G. Ryan, Mark Rosewater, and Pete Venters*
