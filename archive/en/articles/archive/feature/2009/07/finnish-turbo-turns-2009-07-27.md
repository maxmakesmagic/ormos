It's Nationals week on Daily Decks, and we'll be highlighting some of the best decks the 2009 Nationals tournaments have to offer! Finland's Mikko Airaksinen, for example, took a strategy popularized by Jon Loucks to the top slot. How does it work? Play a ton of cantripping artifacts like [Kaleidostone](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kaleidostone) and [Howling Mine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Howling+Mine) early, then use [Time Sieve](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Time+Sieve) to sacrifice them and draw an extra card. From there, [Open the Vaults](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Open+the+Vaults) allows you to get them all back and rinse, wash, and repeat!


For recent Nationals coverage, check out the [Event Coverage Archive](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtg/eventcoverage/main).
