*Dark Ascension* Event Decks
============================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 1, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











The *Dark Ascension* Event Decks won't be on sale until February 24, but we thought you might like a look at them a little early. Here we go!

### Gleeful Flames

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/898_deck1_0ltpzgp77x.jpg)  
Cackle as you stoke the fires! A horde of sadistic little goblins and devils do your malicious bidding, delivering fiery pain to your opponents. Wicked combos force enemies to let you multiply your forces or to fan the flames of their own demise.

### Spiraling Doom

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/898_deck2_0ltpzgp77x.jpg)  
Mock life and cheat death! This green and black deck is packed with hard-to-kill creatures, including those with the new undying ability, and many more ways to turn your smaller threats into larger ones.
