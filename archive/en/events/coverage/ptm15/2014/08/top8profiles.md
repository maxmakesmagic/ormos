### PRO TOUR MAGIC 2015


TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)

Wizards of the Coast




August 2, 2014
 








  






  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/ptm15/t8_jensen.jpg)
### William Jensen


**Age:** 32  
**Hometown:** Millis, Massachusetts USA   
**Limited Record:** 6-0  
**Constructed Record:** 6-3-1


 

**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**   

Four Pro Tour Top 8s, Masters Champion, sixteen Grand Prix Top 8s, three wins, Hall of Fame class of 2013


**Did you know?**   

William Jensen's first Pro Tour Top 8 was over fifteen years ago!


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**   

Blue-Green - Very Good. Nearly mono White splash Blue - Below Average.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**   

White-Black Midrange


**What *Magic 2015* cards do you think had the greatest impact on the weekend?**   
[Caves of Koilos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Caves+of+Koilos), [Battlefield Forge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battlefield+Forge)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**   

Everything.


**Who did you prepare with?**   

ChannelFireball Pantheon








  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/ptm15/t8_ichikawa.jpg)
### Yuuki Ichikawa


**Age:** 26  
**Hometown:** Kanagawa, Japan  
**Limited Record:** 4-2  
**Constructed Record:** 9-1


 

**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**   

Pro Tour *Journey into Nyx* Top 4


**Did you know?**   

This is Yuuki Ichikawa's second Pro Tour Top 8 in just four total Pro Tours! It's like flipping a coin!


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**   

Green-White - Some rare cards, lack of concept (1-2). Green-White - Some rare cards, lack of concept (3-0).


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**   

Jund Planeswalkers, because it plays well against Black Devotion and White-Blue Control.


**What *Magic 2015* cards do you think had the greatest impact on the weekend?**   
[Nissa, Worldwaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa%2C+Worldwaker)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**   

Still an extension of my hobby.


**Who did you prepare with?**   

Kentaro Yamamoto








  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/ptm15/t8_cox.jpg)
### Patrick Cox


**Age:** 30  
**Hometown:** Oakron, Virginia USA  
**Limited Record:** 5-1  
**Constructed Record:** 8-1-1


 

**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**   

Top 8 Pro Tour Nagoya 2011; 5 Grand Prix Top 8s; SCG Invitational Champion


**Did you know?**   

Made his Pro Tour debut at Pro Tour Berlin in 2008


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**   

Both decks were green-white tokens, and quite good.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**   

Brave Naya because it is a powerful aggro deck that can randomly combo your opponent out.


**What *Magic 2015* cards do you think had the greatest impact on the weekend?**   
[Goblin Rabblemaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Rabblemaster)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**   

It has been a goal of mine for years, and coming into this tournament I was falling off the Pro Tour, so it would be incredible.


**Who did you prepare with?**   

Team ChannelFireball








  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/ptm15/t8_cunningham.jpg)
### Jackson Cunningham


**Age:** 27  
**Hometown:** Vancouver, British Columbia CA  
**Limited Record:** 4-2  
**Constructed Record:** 8-2


 

**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**   

Beat Kai Budde on *Magic Online*


**Did you know?**   

Made the Top 8 of the first Pro Tour he ever attended—this one.


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**   

Red-Black for 3-0: Started rough and ended well. Red-Green for 1-2: Not enough focus.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**   

Green-White Aggro. It plays well against Black Devotion, White-Blue Control, and have a really good board for Blue Devotion.


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**   

It would mean a lot.


**Who did you prepare with?**   

Jeff Cunningham trained me from scratch. This is my first Pro Tour and Constructed tournament.








  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/ptm15/t8_floch.jpg)
### Ivan Floch


**Age:** 28  
**Hometown:** Bratislava, Slovakia  
**Limited Record:** 5-1  
**Constructed Record:** 7-2-1


 

**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**   

Team World Champion, Chiba 2010


**Did you know?**   

Ivan won more than 70% of his Grand Prix matches this season.


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**   

Red splashing Blue; very aggressive with 7 one-drops, good deck. 2-1. Red-Green with very high curve and 2 rates; not that great, but it was good in the Red-Green mirror, which I played twice. 3-0.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**   

White-Blue Cleansing Control, because I like the style and because of Stanislav Cifka — he worked on the deck list.


**What *Magic 2015* cards do you think had the greatest impact on the weekend?**   

Maybe [Goblin Rabblemaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Rabblemaster).


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**   

A LOT!


**Who did you prepare with?**   

Stanislav Cifka, Martin Juza, Frank Karsten, Matej Zatlkaj, Robert Jurkovič, Robin Dollar, Adam Koska, Nikola Vavra, and Petr Sochurek.








  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/ptm15/t8_turtenwald.jpg)
### Owen Turtenwald


**Age:** 25  
**Hometown:** Milwaukee, Wisconsin USA  
**Limited Record:** 4-2  
**Constructed Record:** 8-2


 

**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**   

1 Pro Tour Top 8, 15 Grand Prix Top 8s


**Did you know?**   

Owen won back-to-back Grand Prix last year, taking home trophies in Washington, D.C. and Alberquerque, NM.


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**   

3-0 Blue-Green all commons, 1-2 Blue-Black control; both bad, but had a chance to win.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**   

White-Black midrange designed by the Pantheon. I'd be a fool not to play [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) and [Pack Rat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pack+Rat).


**What *Magic 2015* cards do you think had the greatest impact on the weekend?**   
[Caves of Koilos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Caves+of+Koilos)


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**   

Winning the Pro Tour has always been a dream of mine. I have a chance to make that real.


**Who did you prepare with?**   

ChannelFireball The Pantheon








  
![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/ptm15/t8_mondon.jpg)
### Pierre-Christophe Mondon


**Age:** 30  
**Hometown:** Columbus, Ohio (currently living in Kansas City, Kansas)  
**Limited Record:** 3-3  
**Constructed Record:** 9-0-1


 

**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**   

3 Limited Grand Prix Top 8s, 1 win.


**Did you know?**   

Pierre has a 67% win percentage this season.


**What colors were the decks you drafted? What did you think of each deck?**   

No idea, mono-bad.


**What Constructed deck are you playing, and why?**   

Jund Planeswalkers; tended to win more than I lost.


**What *Magic 2015* cards do you think had the greatest impact on the weekend?**   
[Nissa, Worldwaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nissa%2C+Worldwaker); pain-lands


**What would winning the Pro Tour mean to you?**   

$40k


**Who did you prepare with?**   

Bryan Upham (RIWBTU on MTGO): the creator and champion of the deck, who was gracious enough to give me the list and train me on how to play it. Derek McClasky (Dirknight) and his 1-year-old son Lil Papi, who played and speculated and supported me the last few weeks. My friends from Winmore Games in Kansas City, who lent me cards and who've always been kind.
