Community Spotlight
===================


1. [MTGEloProject.net](http://www.mtgeloproject.net/)

A new *Magic* stats player has entered the game with MTGEloProject.net, a site that uses [Elo ratings](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elo_rating_system) to rank pros since [Pro Tour *Magic* *2015*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptm15), looking only at Grand Prix, Pro Tours, and World Championships during that time span. The current top three in these rankings are Luis Scott-Vargas, Owen Turtenwald, and [Pro Tour *Eldritch Moon*](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/coverage/ptemn) Champion Lukas Blohon. Elo is an interesting deviation from Pro Points, because it doesn't reward volume the same way Pro Points do. So, for example, Gerry Thompson hasn't been on the official Top 25 for some time, but he's sixteenth on this site's Elo rating.


If you're a stat-head, or just love the Pro Tour, check it out.


Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Thing Ascension](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-modern-constructed-league-2016-08-30)

We saw a little bit of this mashup during the Modern Grand Prix weekend last week, but it looks like a few *Magic Online* users—including SKOUNIS—took notice and took it to the 5-0 room in a Modern Competitive League (note: the 5-0 room is not an actual room or place or anything that exists outside my head).


It's a mashup of the [Pyromancer Ascension](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer+Ascension) combo decks and, well, [Thing in the Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thing+in+the+Ice). Which makes sense. [Pyromancer Ascension](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer+Ascension) always relied a little too heavily on getting its namesake into play, and adding [Thing in the Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thing+in+the+Ice) gives it another payoff card for all of those spells being played. And with Affinity, [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) Aggro, Infect, and any number of creature strategies sitting at or near the top of the metagame, [Thing in the Ice](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thing+in+the+Ice) is a significant stumbling block for a lot of top decks.
