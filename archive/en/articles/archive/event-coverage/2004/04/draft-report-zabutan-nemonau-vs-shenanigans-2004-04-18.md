=============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 18, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TedKnutson.jpg)

According to the both teams, the drafted ended up a little strange, since each team was using the same draft strategy. [Betrayal of Flesh](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Betrayal+of+Flesh) was the pick for the first two players, but Turian's pack was ridiculous, as he grabbed yet another Betrayal for himself, leaving a [Mask of Memory](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mask+of+Memory) for Gary and an [Empyrial Plate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Empyrial+Plate) for Eugene, but there was still enough left to yield an incredible wheel of [Fangren Hunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fangren+Hunter) and [Blinding Beam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blinding+Beam) for Pat. Eugene continued to pick up solid cards throughout *Mirrodin*, ending up with three [Raise the Alarm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raise+the+Alarm), two [Skyhunter Patrol](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skyhunter+Patrol), and two [Tel-Jilad Archers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tel-Jilad+Archers) to go along with his [Viridian Longbow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Viridian+Longbow) and [Empyrial Plate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Empyrial+Plate).


His teammates decks didn't end up being quite as deep, with Turian drafting some strong, colored removal spells and [Skeleton Shard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skeleton+Shard), but was forced to play platinum hits like [Drill-Skimmer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drill-Skimmer) and the always-maligned [Titanium Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Titanium+Golem) to fill out his creature slots. Gary's deck was also a bit creature deficient, with two [Cobalt Golem](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cobalt+Golem)s, [Elf Replica](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elf+Replica), and [Wanderguard Sentry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wanderguard+Sentry) comprising a significant portion of his army. He had [Icy Manipulator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Icy+Manipulator), Spikeshot Golem, and [Leonin Bola](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Leonin+Bola) to help slow Osyp down, but the overall quality of his deck was probably not as high as he would have wished.


The Shenanigans faired a little better in terms of creature depth, with Sullivan ending up with two [Fangren Hunter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fangren+Hunter)s, a [Luminous Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Luminous+Angel), and numerous other solid men. Horvath's men were unspectacular, but they were generally better than Turian's scrubby artifact creatures, and he played a bunch of them, opting to try and overpower Mike before he could setup Shard recursion. Osyp's flier depth definitely looked like they could be a problem for Gary, provided he could avoid being shut down by Wise's tap effects.


When asked about the draft afterwards, here's what the teams had to say:


**Shenanigans**  
 Osyp - I'm kind of slow and I don't have enough artifacts, but my deck isn't bad. If I had another Myr, I'd actually be pretty happy. Thankfully, Gary's on their team, and I'm sure he'll make plenty of mistakes to help me out. Nah, don't tell him I said that. Ah hell, go aheadâ€¦ I love Gary.


Pat - We got out-opened pretty badly. The pick where I had to take [Pulse of the Forge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pulse+of+the+Forge) to keep it away from Mike was awful. If we had opened almost any other bomb rare at that point, I could have splashes it, but instead I got stuck with a hate draft.


**Nemonaut**  
 Gary - I have a pretty good matchup. Spikeshot is great against him, since it kills like half of his team. I only had two Equipment, and he ended up taking [Carry Away](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carry+Away) over [Mephitic Ooze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mephitic+Ooze) just to try and counteract my Bola and [Mask of Memory](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mask+of+Memory).


Mike - My draft went okay, but my creatures are pretty underpowered.
