While not his best performance, Craig Jones shot out to the lead in the Swiss on the first day of competition at Pro Tour-Los Angeles in 2005. The deck that got him there? A powerful [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) deck that utilized the newly printed dredge mechanic to fill up the graveyard even faster. Little did Jones know his deck would inspire the even more high-powered fully dredged [Ichorid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ichorid) decks that would pop up later in the Grand Prix/PTQ season. Those decks eschewed the tricks Jones's preferred and instead simply tried to fill their graveyard up as quickly as possible.
