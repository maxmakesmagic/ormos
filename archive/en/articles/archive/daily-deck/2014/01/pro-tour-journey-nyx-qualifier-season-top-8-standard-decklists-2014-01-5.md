Pro Tour Journey into Nyx Qualifier Season: Top 8 Standard Decklists
====================================================================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on January 6, 2014 










![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy//mtg/images/daily/events/ptq_2012.jpg)The Pro Tour Qualifier season for Pro Tour *Journey into Nyx* is under way. This is your chance to test your Standard Constructed skills, because invitations and tickets to Pro Tour *Journey into Nyx* go to PTQ winners! 

 Look through the events below for Top 8 Standard decklists. You can test each decklist thanks to our [Sample Hand Generator](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/feature/294). Just click on the card fan in the upper right of each decklist and you can see what your opening hand could be, along with mulligans and the first three cards you'd draw. 

 Decklists will be updated weekly during the PTQ season. The new season runs December 2, 2013 - March 9, 2014. For more information about the PTQ season, [click here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/protour/qualifiers/atlanta14).
