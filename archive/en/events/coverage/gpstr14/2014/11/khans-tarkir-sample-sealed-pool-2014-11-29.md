### GRAND PRIX STRASBOURG 2014


KHANS OF TARKIR – SAMPLE SEALED POOL
====================================



![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)

Olle Rade




November 29, 2014
 










2,000 players, 12,000 boosters, 12,000 rares and an infinite amount of mana fixing. If you had access to every sealed pool in the room, you could build a deck of nothing but the best Mythic rares and the perfect mana to cast them.



 However, all competitors are stuck with just one pool to build from. And to demonstrate the challenge, and to let you at home be part of the action we've scribbled down the content of six freshly opened boosters of *Khans of Tarkir*. So take a look, pour yourself a cup of coffee and see what you come up with.




 During the day, we'll let at least one of our competitors and at least one of the World *Magic* Cup competitors from our coverage team take a look at it and dissect the content.



Sealed deck is always a challenge, so don't miss out on the even greater challenge of building a deck from only a list of all the cards!



 (as a hint, the rares in the pool are [Duneblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast), [Ashcloud Phoenix](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ashcloud+Phoenix), [Dragon Throne of Tarkir](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragon+Throne+of+Tarkir), [Pearl Lake Ancient](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pearl+Lake+Ancient), Reflecting Palm, [Rakshasa Vizier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rakshasa+Vizier) and [Jeering Instigator](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jeering+Instigator))
