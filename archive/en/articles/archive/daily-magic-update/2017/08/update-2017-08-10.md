Today's Previews

1. Lightning Greaves | [Card of the Day](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/cards-day-archive)

I still think it's wild which older cards became Commander staples after the format took off. [Sol Ring](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sol+Ring) was a bit of a gimme, as it's super powerful, but [Lightning Greaves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Greaves) was just a mediocre (at the time) Equipment from the block that introduced Equipment. Now it's in most decks that care about casting and protecting their commander.

Deck of the Day
===============


1. My Azami, Lady of Scrolls Deck

I very often pick decks I personally enjoy for this spot, but almost never choose one of my own decks ([Panharmonicon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Panharmonicon) notwithstanding). But of my twelve Commander decks, I have exactly one tribal deck and it just so happens to be Wizards, and it happens to be my pride and joy. And on the Spikey end of things.


The deck is almost entirely foiled out (curse you *Seventh Edition* [Sky Diamond](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sky+Diamond)!), but that's not why I enjoy it. Okay, that's not entirely why I enjoy it. I enjoy it because it draws all of the cards.


[Azami, Lady of Scrolls](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Azami%2C+Lady+of+Scrolls) is obviously the culprit, and it's not difficult to redraw a hand of seven cards quickly and easily. From there, you can win immediately with [Mind Over Matter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Over+Matter), which combines with Azami to cycle through your entire deck, and [Laboratory Maniac](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Laboratory+Maniac), which combines with an empty library to just win.


But that's far from my favorite way to win. [Jushi Apprentice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jushi+Apprentice) is an underappreciated card in Commander, but it's often my win condition. Flipping it is trivial, and often times I'll activate the flipped Apprentice to draw ten cards at the end of someone's (last) turn, then draw another 25 or so, then use [Mind Over Matter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind+Over+Matter) to generate enough mana and untap the Apprentice and start making opponents draw 60 cards at a time. It happens almost as easily as [Laboratory Maniac](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Laboratory+Maniac), but the table tends to be less angry about it and more appreciative of the ingenuity.


Also, sometimes you just win with a horde of [Master of Waves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Waves) tokens, especially copied by [Phyrexian Metamorph](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Metamorph).


I'll admit, some of the cards are in this deck simply because I have them in foil. [Meloku the Clouded Mirror](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meloku+the+Clouded+Mirror) isn't really what this deck wants to do, for example, but she's friends with [Azami, Lady of Scrolls](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Azami%2C+Lady+of+Scrolls), so I let it pass.


Plus, who doesn't love drawing 60 cards at a time?
