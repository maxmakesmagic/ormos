New MTGO Avatars
================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 15, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






The *Return to Ravnica* events are going on right now on **Magic** Online! And that means that you can sign on and begin busting digital booster packs right now! To learn more about the events for *Return to Ravnica* on **Magic** Online, [read more here](http://staging.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/09252012c)!


One thing on that page is the announcement of five new *Return to Ravnica* avatars that players can earn by participating and winning in some of these events!


Remember also that we’re in the midst of our wide beta for the new **Magic** Online client, so if you want to help us determine the future of the client and help improve it, give it a try and then [send us feedback](http://www.surveygizmo.com/s3/1028055/magic-online-wide-beta-feedback-form)! We read it all and they are having direct impact on the application’s development and features!
