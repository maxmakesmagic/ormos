A blast from the past, Cephalid Breakfast, has returned to the world of Legacy. Using the powerful [Cephalid Illusionist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cephalid+Illusionist), the Breakfast deck the blue creature with *en*-Kor creatures, redirecting damage over and over again (whether there is any on the stack or not). When the graveyard is stocked, [Narcomoeba](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Narcomoeba) allow the deck to [Dread Return](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dread+Return) a juicy target back. In this case, [Kiki-Jiki, Mirror Breaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kiki-Jiki%2C+Mirror+Breaker) works nicely with [Sky Hussar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sky+Hussar) to generate infinite 4/3 flyers. Read more from the [event coverage](http://www.starcitygames.com/php/news/expandnews.php?Article=21462) of the StarCityGames.com Legacy Open in Los Angeles.
