Round 9: William Jensen vs. David Williams
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 8, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcle02/a937.jpg)There's a lot of people rooting for William Jensen this weekend, more than usual. Jensen is playing a heavily-metagamed black-red mana-denial deck, aiming to punish a field dense with mono-black control. 

He faces a pretty tough matchup in the resurgent Dave Williams and his version of the mono-black Pirates! deck that debuted at Grand Prix - London. Quick beatdown, Braids, [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth), [Ichorid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ichorid) and [Zombie Infestation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Zombie+Infestation) are all trouble for Jensen's sorcery-based removal.

Williams kept a mediocre hand for Game 1. Jensen's had a lot more action. He Edicted Williams's [Crypt Creeper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Creeper) then hammered down [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth) and [Earth Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earth+Rift) while Williams failed to develop his board. After missing a land drop, he drew, and played a third swamp.

"I don't know why I bother to mix it in. Obviously I'm drawing lands." - Dave Williams.

Jensen was ready with [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth) to bust him back to two lands. Then [Mirari](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirari) hit play. What followed was an exercise in unfairness. Copied Wishes, Rifts, and [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth)s quickly left Williams permanentless and staring down a gigantic [Magnivore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magnivore).

Jensen 1 - Williams 0

"What was I thinking? I kept about the worst hand ever. That was like mulliganing to three." - Dave Williams

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcle02/a933.jpg)Williams curved out with a [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) for [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood) (seeing a hand of five land, [Earth Rift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Earth+Rift) and [Diabolic Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Tutor)), making sure the way was clear for his turn-two [Millikin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Millikin), turn-three Braids. Jensen was dismayed.

Pinned to two lands, he kept playing them out while Williams brought out [Nantuko Shade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade) to aid the beats. [Burning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish) got Jensen [Volcanic Spray](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Spray), and he used it to axe the [Millikin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Millikin). Williams then tapped out for a second Shade. Jensen's eyebrows went up, but he just shrugged and wrecked Williams by flashing back the Spray.

"That thing Flashes back? Well, this is my inexperience." - Dave Williams

His egregious misplay went unpunished, however, as Jensen failed to find an answer for Braids, and she smashed him.

Jensen 1 - Williams 1

"I tried my best to give that one to you. I was doing stuff like that all day yesterday. I attacked into [Moment's Peace](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Moment%27s+Peace), it was right there in the graveyard." - Dave Williams

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpcle02/a935.jpg)Jensen was delighted to play first. Williams was first on the board with [Millikin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Millikin). Jensen Pacted into [Burning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish) and kept Williams in check with [Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth). Williams busted out another [Millikin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Millikin), and Jensen hammered with [Innocent Blood](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood) and another Earth.

Williams had plenty of action left. [Cabal Therapy](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) took care of the [Burning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish), and [Crypt Creeper](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Crypt+Creeper) reused it, taking [Skeletal Scrying](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skeletal+Scrying) and leaving Jensen with a [Magnivore](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Magnivore) he coudln't cast. He pulled up [Diabolic Tutor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Tutor) and cast it. Williams dropped Braids.

[Rancid Earth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rancid+Earth) took care of the [Millikin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Millikin) thanks to threshold, but again Jensen was unable to answer the Cabal Minion. Williams took it slow, developing his mana by recursing [Ichorid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ichorid) to pay the Braids upkeep. Soon the advantage was too much, and Jensen succumbed.

**Final Result:** Dave Williams defeats William Jensen 2-1
