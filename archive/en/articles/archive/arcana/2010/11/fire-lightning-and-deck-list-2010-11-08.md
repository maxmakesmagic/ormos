Fire, Lightning, and a Deck List
================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on November 8, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











As you may already be aware, the sixty-card, all-foil *Fire & Lightning* deck is coming out on November 19. That's only eleven days away! And you might be wondering what exactly is in it. Well, there's all-new art for [Jackal Pup](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jackal+Pup), for one thing.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/576_jackalpupnewart.jpg)[Jackal Pup](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jackal+Pup) | Art by [Kev Walker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=%5B%22Kev+Walker%22%5D)

Classy, right?

What? You want *more?* Well ... how about if we show you the entire deck list? Will that satisfy you? Remember, all of these cards will be in foil, using a process unique to this product. And it comes out November 19. Remember that part, too.
