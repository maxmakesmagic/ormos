Kaldheim Commander Decklists
============================



 Posted in **Card Preview**
 on January 20, 2021 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











*Kaldheim* releases with two Commander decks, each containing eight all-new *Magic* cards. These decks are designed to introduce and level up newer players exploring the Commander format.


***(Editor's Note: The decklists below pull the newest printing of each card automatically from [Gatherer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Default.aspx), including printings which are not in this product. These decklists are not card-for-card product displays but rather interactive lists of the cards included in each deck.)***
