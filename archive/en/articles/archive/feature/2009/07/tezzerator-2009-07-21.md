Looking for something outside the box to give a spin in the world of *Shards of Alara* Block Constructed? Goliat2 managed a Premier Event Top 8 with a take on Tezzerator. [Tezzeret the Seeker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tezzeret+the+Seeker) is the engine that turns solid cards like [Wildfield Borderpost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wildfield+Borderpost) and [Scepter of Dominance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scepter+of+Dominance) into an attacking force that can quickly put opponents under. Use [Vedalken Outlander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vedalken+Outlander) and [Bant Charm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bant+Charm) to hold the fort until it's time to go ultimate.
