Trial-Winning Decklists
=======================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 3, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











 


There are many traditions in the over-twenty-year history of the *Magic* Grand Prix. Many things change, but some are too fun and too time-honored to not partake in. Last Chance Trials (or “Grinders” as they are affectionately known) are just some of the tournaments held throughout Friday, to earn coveted byes for the Grand Prix main even the next day. They've been around since long ago, and they'll be here to stay until the sun burns out ... probably.


It's a fun way to spend your Friday, and can be quite rewarding.


Below are the decklists from the players who went 5-0 in the Standard Trials on Friday here at Grand Prix Denver. In addition to them, Kevin Deleve and Brian Lanning also earned some extra hours of sleep and two free wins in *Kaladesh* Sealed trials.


Enjoy!
