Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 19, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_meghanwolff_0.jpg)
By Meghan Wolff




 Meghan is one half of the podcast Magic the Amateuring and an adjunct professor at Tolarian Community College. She loves Limited, likes Modern, and dips her toes into each Standard season. She's decidedly blue and is the #1 hater of Siege Rhino in the Multiverse. 






Below are the profiles of the eight players who rose to the top in a field of 3200. Congratulations to the GP Vegas Modern Top 8 players!


 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gplv17/t8_mod_wu.jpg)Name:** Allen Wu


**Twitter Handle:** @lhefriel


**Hometown:** Mountain View, CA


**Occupation:** Student


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


GP Albuquerque winner


Some PT 10-6s


**What deck are you playing and why?**


Affinity, because [Mox Opal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Opal) is broken


**What was your best card this weekend and why?**


[Mox Opal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Opal). Of my personal choices, [Thoughtcast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtcast).


**What do you enjoy most about Modern?**


Having two mana on turn 1.


**What would winning the Grand Prix mean to you?**


Scoreboard rights over one Hunter Cochran.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gplv17/t8_mod_sorenson.jpg)Name:** Matt Sorensen


**Hometown:** San Jose, CA


**Occupation:** Teacher


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Day 2 GP LA


**What deck are you playing and why?**


Eldrazi Tron, I like colorless spells and big spaghetti monsters.


**What was your best card this weekend and why?**


[Chalice of the Void](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chalice+of+the+Void). It just stopped things.


**What do you enjoy most about Modern?**


The different decks and having to think about all the different lines of play and being able to turn 3 a Karn.


**What would winning the Grand Prix mean to you?**


It would mean that I could show my parents that this game isn't a waste of my money.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gplv17/t8_mod_mery.jpg)Name:** Theau Mery


**Hometown:** Paris


**Occupation:** Student


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Close to none. 11-4 at Copenhagen.


**What deck are you playing and why?**


Hatebears, the mono-white version. And I play it because the deck is sweet and because of Wescoe.


**What was your best card this weekend and why?**


[Thraben Inspector](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thraben+Inspector). It makes you draw cards, it’s obviously busted.


**What do you enjoy most about Modern?**


Hatebears.


**What would winning the Grand Prix mean to you?**


It would allow me to go to other GPs, which seems nice. And maybe find a good team for the Pro Tour.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gplv17/t8_mod_peterson.jpg)Name:** Paul Peterson


**Hometown:** Honolulu, HI


**Occupation:** US Army


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


I won a playmat.


**What deck are you playing and why?**


Burn. Ditched [Living End](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+End) at the last minute. Went with old faithful. I know it better than any deck.


**What was your best card this weekend and why?**


The one on top!


**What do you enjoy most about Modern?**


Diversity


**What would winning the Grand Prix mean to you?**


My wife could no longer tell me it’s a waste of time. Love you honey!




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gplv17/t8_mod_wescoe.jpg)Name:** Craig Wescoe


**Twitter Handle:** @Brimaz4Life


**Hometown:** Oreskos


**Occupation:** King


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Writer at TCGPlayer.com


Pro Tour Dragon’s Maze Champion


**What deck are you playing and why?**


Green-White Hatebears because I am Craig Wescoe


**What was your best card this weekend and why?**


[Mirran Crusader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mirran+Crusader) because everyone is on black this weekend.


**What do you enjoy most about Modern?**


Anyone can play their favorite strategy.


**What would winning the Grand Prix mean to you?**


That I was blessed.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gplv17/t8_mod_davoudi.jpg)Name:** Mani Davoudi


**Twitter Handle:** @zapgaze


**Hometown:** Vancouver, BC


**Occupation:** Bookkeeper


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Top 8 GP Houston 2013


**What deck are you playing and why?**


Affinity, it is my favorite Modern deck.


**What was your best card this weekend and why?**


[Mox Opal](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mox+Opal), it is an unfair card.


**What do you enjoy most about Modern?**


Every once in a while, people cut down on their Affinity hate.


**What would winning the Grand Prix mean to you?**


It would be nice to have a trophy.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gplv17/t8_mod_coursey.jpg)Name:** Benjamin Coursey


**Twitter Handle:** @ben\_cenobi


**Hometown:** Rumney, NH


**Occupation:** Manager/Level 2 Judge


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


3 PTQ Top 8s


**What deck are you playing and why?**


Robots. I felt the most comfortable with it.


**What was your best card this weekend and why?**


[Cranial Plating](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cranial+Plating). Applies the most pressure per square inch.


**What do you enjoy most about Modern?**


Everything


**What would winning the Grand Prix mean to you?**


Trying not to think about that right now.




---

 **![](https://media.wizards.com/2017/events/gplv17/t8_mod_wong.jpg)Name:** Daniel Wong


**Hometown:** Berkeley, CA


**Occupation:** Software Developer


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


Losing in RPTQs


**What deck are you playing and why?**


Blue-Black Turns. It’s an excellent deck and a blast to play. It also makes people salty, which is great.


**What was your best card this weekend and why?**


[Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push). Worth the splash, buys enough time to go off.


**What do you enjoy most about Modern?**


Even jank can Top 8!


**What would winning the Grand Prix mean to you?**


The start of an amazing Pro Magic career!
