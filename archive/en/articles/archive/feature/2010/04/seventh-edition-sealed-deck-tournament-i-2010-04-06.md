*Seventh Edition* Sealed Deck Tournament I
==========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 6, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











O*ld favorites like Serra Angel, Shivan Dragon, and Mahamoti Djinn returned - with a vengeance – in the 350-card* Seventh Edition *Core Set! Revisit some classic game mechanics drawn from the*  Alpha *through* Mercadian Masques *releases*.

### *SEVENTH EDITION* SEALED DECK TOURNAMENT I

*START TIME:* Saturday, April 24, 2010 11:00 AM PDT / 18:00 UTC.   
*LOCATION:*  Premier Events room.   
*ENTRY FEE:*  30 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 6 *Seventh Edition* booster packs for this event.  
*SIZE:*  33 players minimum, 512 players maximum.   
*DURATION:* 6 rounds, Swiss Pairings. Play in all six rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 18 |  Complete Premium Foil *Seventh Edition* Set | 3 |
| 15 | 15 *Seventh Edition* booster packs | 2 |
| 12 | 9 *Seventh Edition* booster packs  | 1 |
| 9 | 3 *Seventh Edition* booster packs  | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in the *Seventh Edition* Sealed Deck Tournament I will receive 1 copy of the promotional Arena version of [Pillage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pillage). Players with at least 15 match points at the end of the tournament will also receive a premium foil Pillage.
