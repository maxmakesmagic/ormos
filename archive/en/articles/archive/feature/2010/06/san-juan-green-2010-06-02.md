Young Noah Swartz was an unassuming member of the Pro Tour–San Juan Top 8. While his work as a coverage reporter for GGsLive.com made him known to some, this Top 8 was his first relevant Pro Tour finish. How'd he do it? By going undefeated during the Block Constructed portion of the tournament with the help of a deck design by Zvi Mowshowitz. Read the official coverage of Pro Tour–San Juan [here](/en/events/coverage/paulo-celebrates-six-beach-san-juan).
