Andrew Coimbra is the 2009 **Magic** World Champion! He bested David Reitbauer's Jund deck in the Finals with a construction he referred to as "Naya Lightsaber." Nearly every card is a powerful creature or removal spell, many of which provide card advantage! For more information on the 2009 World Championships, [visit the official event coverage](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-2009-world-championships-2009-11-12).
