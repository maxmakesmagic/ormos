TOP 8 PROFILES
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 23, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











After many rounds of Swiss and some great games of *Magic*, the Top 8 for Grand Prix Ottawa is set! Learn more about this event’s Top 8 competitors, their history at the top tables, and more by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles.




---


![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpott14/t8maynard.JPG)


### Pascal Maynard


**Age:** 21  
**Hometown:** Quebec City  
**Occupation:** Manager at Boutique Donjon and I'm back on the Pro Tour!


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

3 Grand Prix Top 8s


**What was your record on Day 1? What colors did you play? What were the MVP cards from your sealed deck?**  

8-1. Green/White Aggro. Sideboarded into Sultai on the draw. MVP = Two drops.


**What were your draft decks? How did they perform?**  

Four color, four [Wrath of God](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God), 3-0. Black/White Trainwreck 1-1-1.


**What was the hardest deckbuilding or drafting decision you've had to make so far this weekend?**  

My sealed pool. I thought I misbuilt, but I ended up hardly losing a game with it.


**What is your favorite deck to draft in *Khans of Tarkir*, and what is its most important common?**   

Blue/Green Aggro/Tempo. [Dragonscale Boon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dragonscale+Boon).


**Who is your pick to win the World Championship this year?**   

Shahar Shenhar

### Shahar Shenhar


**Age:** 21  
**Hometown:** Sacramento/Jerusalem  
**Occupation:** Professional *Magic* Player


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

3 Grand Prix wins, 1 finals. A lot of Pro Tour cashes, but no Top 8s. 2013 World Champion.


**What was your record on Day 1? What colors did you play? What were the MVP cards from your sealed deck?**  

8-1 Abzan deck with nine or ten removal spells. I lost to Matt Costa in two games and didn't lose otherwise.


**What were your draft decks? How did they perform?**  

Temur bombs 3-0. Mardu Tokens 2-0-1.


**What was the hardest deckbuilding or drafting decision you've had to make so far this weekend?**  

Spells over lands or lands over spells.


**What is your favorite deck to draft in *Khans of Tarkir*, and what is its most important common?**   

Unknown.


**Who is your pick to win the World Championship this year?**   

ME!

![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpott14/t8allegrucci.JPG)


### Xavier Allegucci


**Age:** 17  
**Hometown:** Montreal  
**Occupation:** Student


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

Grand Prix Washington D.C. Top 64


**What was your record on Day 1? What colors did you play? What were the MVP cards from your sealed deck?**  

My record was 7-2. I played an Abzan deck with [High Sentinels of Arashin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=High+Sentinels+of+Arashin), [End Hostilities](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=End+Hostilities), [Necropolis Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necropolis+Fiend), and [Hooded Hydra](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hooded+Hydra).


**What were your draft decks? How did they perform?**  

I 3-0ed my first draft with a Blue/Red tempo deck that had many fliers and decent removal. My second draft was a Black/White deck splashing red for powerful cards.


**What was the hardest deckbuilding or drafting decision you've had to make so far this weekend?**  

My hardest draft pick was [Ainok Bond-Kin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ainok+Bond-Kin) against [Ponyback Brigade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ponyback+Brigade). At that point, my deck had a high curve.


**What is your favorite deck to draft in *Khans of Tarkir*, and what is its most important common?**   

Blue/Green/Red Morphs. [Snowhorn Rider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snowhorn+Rider).


**Who is your pick to win the World Championship this year?**   

Shaun McLaren

![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpott14/t8oliver.JPG)


### Neal Oliver


**Age:** 25  
**Hometown:** Berkeley, CA  
**Occupation:** Magictraders.com content producer


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

Grand Prix Las Vegas Champion. Grand Prix Oakland finalist. WMCQ Winner 2014. Most importantly, beating Orie Guo in a fair game of *Magic*.


**What was your record on Day 1? What colors did you play? What were the MVP cards from your sealed deck?**  

7-2. Played Temur splashing Black. [Sultai Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sultai+Charm) and [Trap Essence](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trap+Essence) were great.


**What were your draft decks? How did they perform?**  

Sultai 3-0. Sultai splashing White 3-0.


**What was the hardest deckbuilding or drafting decision you've had to make so far this weekend?**  

I had to decide between [Savage Punch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savage+Punch) and a land pack 1 pick 2. I took the [Savage Punch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Savage+Punch) and regretted it.


**What is your favorite deck to draft in *Khans of Tarkir*, and what is its most important common?**   

I love Sultai. [Rotting Mastodon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rotting+Mastodon) is secretly great. [Mystic of the Hidden Way](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+of+the+Hidden+Way) is the best.


**Who is your pick to win the World Championship this year?**   

Owen Turtenwald

Green/Black. [Archers' Parapet](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archers%27+Parapet).

### Jessica Buchanan


**Age:** 24  
**Hometown:** Hamilton, ON, Canada  
**Occupation:** Factory Worker


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

Grand Prix Atlanta Top 100. Super Sunday Series Top 4.


**What was your record on Day 1? What colors did you play? What were the MVP cards from your sealed deck?**  

8-1. Played Mardu. MVP cards were Sorin and Crater's Claws.


**What were your draft decks? How did they perform?**  

Draft 1: Temur splash white 2-1. Draft 2: Jeskai Tempo 2-0-1.


**What was the hardest deckbuilding or drafting decision you've had to make so far this weekend?**  

Whether or not to jump completely into white during pack two of draft one or just splash.


**What is your favorite deck to draft in *Khans of Tarkir*, and what is its most important common?**   

I love five colour [Secret Plans](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Secret+Plans) decks. [Monastery Flock](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Monastery+Flock) is pretty good there.


**Who is your pick to win the World Championship this year?**   

Shaun McLaren

### Lucas Siow


**Age:** 28  
**Hometown:** Toronto, Canada  
**Occupation:** Financial Analyst


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:**   

Two Grand Prix Top 8s. I once beat Orie Guo in a fair game of *Magic*.


**What was your record on Day 1? What colors did you play? What were the MVP cards from your sealed deck?**  

8-1, five color, [Stubborn Denial](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stubborn+Denial), [Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino)


**What were your draft decks? How did they perform?**  

Jeskai 3-0. Temur 2-0-1. I had many copies of [Mystic of the Hidden Way](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+of+the+Hidden+Way). They were great!


**What was the hardest deckbuilding or drafting decision you've had to make so far this weekend?**  

Pick two [Mystic of the Hidden Way](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+of+the+Hidden+Way) or a second copy of [Flying Crane Technique](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flying+Crane+Technique).


**What is your favorite deck to draft in *Khans of Tarkir*, and what is its most important common?**   
[Mystic of the Hidden Way](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+of+the+Hidden+Way).


**Who is your pick to win the World Championship this year?**   

Shahar Shenhar
