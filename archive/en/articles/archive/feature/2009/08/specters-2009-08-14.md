Since the release of Alpha, when the first player cast [Dark Ritual](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Ritual) into [Hypnotic Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypnotic+Specter), the discard tribe has been a staple member of the Multiverse. Here at Daily Decks, we thought it'd be fun to put together a deck that answered the question "What happens if you put the best discard Specters in to one deck?" [Hypnotic Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Hypnotic%2BSpecter) is joined by [Hollow Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Hollow%2BSpecter), [Abyssal Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Abyssal%2BSpecter), [Shimian Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Shimian%2BSpecter), and [Needle Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Needle+Specter) amongst others with [Dark Ritual](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Ritual) to speed your flying, hand ravaging horde to the table and [Doom Blade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doom+Blade) to clean up anything that hits the battlefield first.
