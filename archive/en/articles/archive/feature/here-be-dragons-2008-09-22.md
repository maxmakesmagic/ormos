On the plane of Jund, there is one tribe that reigns supreme: Dragons! The largest of the shard's inhabitants, Dragons rule the roost (no pun intended!) just as Japanese pro Makihito Mihara managed to rule the 2006 World Championships with his Dragonstorm deck. Featuring the namesake sorcery to search up [Bogardan Hellkite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bogardan+Hellkite)s and [Hunted Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hunted+Dragon)s, Mihara bested all on the backs of the fiery, winged beasts. Some of the cards from his deck were even immortalized in [From the Vault: Dragons](/en/articles/archive/announcing-vault-dragons-2008-05-13). To find out more about the denizens of Jund, check out the *Shards of Alara* [product page](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/shardsofalara).
