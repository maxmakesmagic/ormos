### Eternal Siege

The best strategy against an overwhelming enemy is to stay on the offensive. You’ll forge a new alliance between Bant and Naya against the forces of Nicol Bolas, charging into the fray with an array of efficient, battle-ready knights and behemoths. As you cast trick after game-altering trick, victory will dawn over the trampled resolve of your foes.

### Legion Aloft

Your strategy revolves around ruling two fronts at once: the land and the sky. You’ll set down an impenetrable blockade of foe-frustrating creatures on the ground, and then launch a combined force of quick Bant and Esper fliers over enemy defenses. Keep your opponents off-balance with sneaky spells while you pummel their befuddled faces with your winged minions.
