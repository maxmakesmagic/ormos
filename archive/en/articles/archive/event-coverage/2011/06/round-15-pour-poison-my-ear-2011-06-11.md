Round 15: Pour the Poison in my Ear
===================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 11, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_rashadmiller.jpg)
By Rashad Miller











### Ben Stark (Tempered Steel)


### vs.


### Gaudenis Vidugiris (Mono-Black Infect)


We were at the home stretch in Nagoya, so, as you would expect, we had Magic superstars battling in the arena for a spot in the Top 8. On the left sat Grand Prix Atlanta and Pro Tour Paris Champion Ben Stark. On the right was Grand Prix Tampa and Grand Prix Denver Champion Gaudenis Vidugiris. Two would enter. One would leave victorious… unless the match was a draw.


 


### Game 1


 


Vidugiris won the die roll and chose to play first. Vidugiris took a mulligan to six cards while Stark kept his opening seven. The first plays were Stark's [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite) and [Vault Skirge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vault+Skirge) on turn one. Vidugiris had neglected to play a spell on his turn, which allowed him to [Virulent Wound](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Virulent+Wound) the Skirge at the end of Stark's. Stark now had one poison counter. Stark attacked with his [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite), which dropped Vidugiris to 19 life. Stark added [Glint Hawk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk) to the battlefield on the second turn.





|  |
| --- |
| 
*Gaudenis Vidugiris's Mono-Black Infect deck attacks the format from a uni—POW, TEN POISON, YOU'RE DEAD* |


Turn three showcased [Inkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkmoth+Nexus) and [Phyrexian Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Crusader) from Vidugiris and [Origin Spellbomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Origin+Spellbomb) from Stark. The Spellbomb triggered [Glint Hawk Idol](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk+Idol) and Vidugiris was attacked down to 17 life. [Phyrexian Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Crusader) and [Inkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkmoth+Nexus) brought Stark up to four poison counters. This was a losing race for Stark, and he decided to add a [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite) to his side of the board and leave his team back on defense. This proved not to matter, as Vidugiris had a second [Virulent Wound](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Virulent+Wound) for [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite) and [Piston Sledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Piston+Sledge) for his [Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=+213724). Stark activated [Glint Hawk Idol](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk+Idol) for the block, but [Dismember](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember) cleared the way. Six poison later, one from [Virulent Wound](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Virulent+Wound) and five from the crusader, and we were on to Game 2.


Vidugiris 1, Stark 0


### Game 2


Stark chose to play first and kept his hand. Vidugiris again started the game with six cards. We had another fast start from Stark as he lead with [Signal Pest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Signal+Pest) and [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite). [Inkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkmoth+Nexus) hit the board before Vidugiris passed the turn. Stark attacked for 2 with [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite) and [Signal Pest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Signal+Pest). Vidugiris went down to 18 life. Vidugiris decided to join the race and attacked with his [Inkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkmoth+Nexus). Stark attempted a [Marrow Shards](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Marrow+Shards), but Vidugiris paid 2 life for [Mutagenic Growth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutagenic+Growth). Vidugiris was at 16, while Stark gained three poison counters. Stark activated [Glint Hawk Idol](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glint+Hawk+Idol), and it attacked alongside [Signal Pest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Signal+Pest) and [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite). Vidugiris dropped to 14 life.


Still with no action, Vidugiris could only draw for his turn and pass back to Stark. Stark kept the beats coming and attacked with his team. Vidugiris attempted a [Dismember](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember) but was thwarted by Stark's double [Mutagenic Growth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutagenic+Growth). Stark stood at 16 life with three poison counters, while Vidugiris dropped to 6 life. Vidugiris added a second [Inkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkmoth+Nexus) to his board in hopes of defending himself from the onslaught of artifact creatures, but a [Dispatch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dispatch) from Stark sealed the deal these players were on to Game 3.


Vidugiris 1, Stark 1


### Game 3


Vidugiris chose to play first, and both players kept seven-card hands. Vidugiris laid a [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) while Stark opened with [Inkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkmoth+Nexus), [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite), and [Signal Pest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Signal+Pest). Vidugiris played his second [Swamp](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swamp) and a [Mortarpod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mortarpod). Vidugiris used the Germ attached to [Mortarpod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mortarpod) to deal 1 damage to Stark's Pest. Stark played a [Plains](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plains) and attacked with [Inkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkmoth+Nexus) and [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite). Vidugiris went down to 19 life and up to one poison counter.





|  |
| --- |
| 
*Ben Stark doesn't need this.* |


Vidugiris played an [Inkmoth Nexus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inkmoth+Nexus) of his own and [Phyrexian Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Crusader) on the third turn, and Stark added [Blade Splicer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blade+Splicer) to his board. Vidugiris used [Grasp of Darkness](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grasp+of+Darkness) to clear the way for his [Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=+213724). The Golem token went down and Vidugiris attacked Stark up to three poison counters. Stark swung back with his team of [Memnite](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Memnite) and [Blade Splicer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blade+Splicer), dropping Vidugiris down to 18 life. Perhaps Stark would have left one of his creatures back if he knew what Vidugiris had in store on his turn. [Piston Sledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Piston+Sledge) entered the battlefield and snap-attached to [Phyrexian Crusader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Crusader). That, alongside [Mutagenic Growth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutagenic+Growth), brought the [Crusader's](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?multiverseid=+213724) power to 7. That meant seven more poison counters for the defenseless Stark, which brought his total to ten.


**Vidugiris 2, Stark 1**
