Tournaments and Events Schedule
===============================



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on August 8, 2008 










| **Introduction to Magic Grand Prix** |
 Major **Magic: The Gathering** events are held around the world every year. Find a location near you and see how you stack up against the best players in the world! Pro Tour Qualifiers and Grand Prix are open to the public. Pro Tours, World **Magic** Cup Qualifiers, the World **Magic** Cup, and the **Magic** World Championship are invitation-only events. 

 The events listed below will feature live and/or post-event coverage in the [Daily MTG section](/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx) of the site. For a list of events featuring live streaming video, visit the [DailyMTG.com webcast page](/Magic/Magazine/Events.aspx?x=mtgevent/webcast/home).
