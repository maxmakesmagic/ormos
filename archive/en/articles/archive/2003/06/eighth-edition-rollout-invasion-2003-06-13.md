*Eighth Edition* Rollout - *Invasion*
=====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 13, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












[![Eighth Edition Rollout](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/mtgcom/articles/8EtopBar.gif)](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=mtgcom/8erollout/welcome)  

 
Invasion *dual lands will appear in the set as uncommons.*


### *[Invasion](http://archive.wizards.com/default.asp?x=magic/expansion/invasion)* Information:




|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Released:** October 2000**Design Team:** Bill Rose (lead), Mike Elliott, Mark Rosewater**Development Team:** Henry Stern(lead), Randy Buehler, Robert Gutschera, William Jockusch, Mark Rosewater with contributions from Brady Dommermuth and Beth Moursund**Art Director:** Dana Knutson & Ron Spears |  | **New Mechanics:** “comes into play tapped” dual lands, “divvy” cards, “domain” cards, dragon legends, kicker, split cards**Factoid:** *Invasion*'s multicolored theme brought many retired players back into the game. Multicolored cards have proven to be among players' favorites time and again. |
