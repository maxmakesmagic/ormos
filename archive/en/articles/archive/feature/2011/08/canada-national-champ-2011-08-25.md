Yesterday we brought you the winning decklist from the 2011 Great Britain national championships. Today we've got another winner, this time from Canada. The player? Marc Andresen, and his deck was a Twin Pod build that uses [Birthing Pod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod) to hunt up the [Deceiver Exarch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deceiver+Exarch)/[Splinter Twin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Splinter+Twin) combo. In a pinch, he also has a host of toolbox threats he can search up if the combo plan isn't prudent. Read [full coverage](/en/events/coverage/anderson-reigns-great-white-north) of Canada Nationals for more.
