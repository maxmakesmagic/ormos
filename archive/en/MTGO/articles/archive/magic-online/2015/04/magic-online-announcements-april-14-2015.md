***Dragons of Tarkir* Championship Events**


You might not be able to become a [Dragonlord](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?action=advanced&name=+%5BDragonlord%5D&type=+%5B%22Legendary%22%5D) and rule the skies of Tarkir, but you *can* become a champion who rules at *Dragons of Tarkir*. The *Dragons of Tarkir* Championships are coming up on *Magic Online*!


*Dragons of Tarkir* Championship Qualifiers begin on Wednesday, June 10. Refer to the full [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/dragons-tarkir-championship-events-2015-04-13) for all of the details.

**One More Week of *Dragons of Tarkir* Release Events**


This is your last week to compete in*Dragons of Tarkir* [Release Events](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/dragons-tarkir-prerelease-and-release-events-2015-03-09#release)—play now through downtime on Wednesday, April 22. For the duration of Release Events, there will be Queues of every size and shape, all full to the brim with Dragons. Be sure to check out the *Dragons of Tarkir/Fate Reforged* [Pack Per Win Event](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/dragons-tarkir-prerelease-and-release-events-2015-03-09#ppwdraft), a new addition to our Release Queue repertoire.


Release [64-Player Single Elimination Draft, with Top 8 Draft](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/dragons-tarkir-prerelease-and-release-events-2015-03-09#64playerdraft) and [Release Sealed, with Top 8 Draft](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/dragons-tarkir-prerelease-and-release-events-2015-03-09#swisssealed), are two scheduled Release events that will be running at regular intervals in the Scheduled Events room, so keep your eyes on those too!


Refer to the full *Dragons of Tarkir* Prerelease and Release Events [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/dragons-tarkir-prerelease-and-release-events-2015-03-09) for more details.

**Season 4 *Magic Online* Championship Series Preliminaries Begin Tomorrow**


The Season 4 *Magic Online* Championship Series (MOCS) Preliminaries begin tomorrow and run through April 17. Five or six wins in a MOCS Preliminary earns players 35 Qualifier Points (QPs). Not only is that enough QPs to get two copies of this season's promo card, but it will also earn you entry into the MOCS Season Finals!


Whether you've been saving QPs up all season long, or you earned them in one fell swoop via a MOCS Season Preliminary, you'll want to be sure to compete in the MOCS Season Finals. Big Prizes are up for grabs, including an invitation to the *Magic Online* Championship and Pro Tour *Magic Origins*!


Learn more about the *Magic Online* Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/2015-magic-online-championship-series).

**Stream the Modern Rotisserie Draft**


On Wednesday, April 15, at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7), Randy Buehler will be hosting a Modern Rotisserie Draft. In a Rotisserie Draft, players take turns drafting cards face-up from the available pool, in this case, all Modern-legal cards. Tune in tomorrow evening to watch Marshall Sutcliffe, Chris Pikula, Randy Buehler, Adam Prosak, Aaron Forsythe, Rashad Miller, Sam Black, and Brian David-Marshall play the decks they've been drafting for the past several weeks. The format will be three rounds of Swiss, cut to Top 4, with the 3-0 player choosing their semi-final opponent.


You can look at the order in which players drafted their decks [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1vce9vdhxqtu6zrptp_GmTyq3ik79wdqNxc4iXPF5DUk/edit#gid=0). Check out the table below to see where you can watch matches streamed live on Twitch!

**New Episode of Vintage Super League Tonight**


The [Vintage Super League](http://vintagesuperleague.com/) pits *Magic* celebrities against one another with *Magic*'s oldest and most powerful cards as their weapons of choice. Check it out tonight at 6:00 p.m. Pacific (UTC-7) on [twitch.tv/magic](http://www.twitch.tv/magic)!


For a review of the current state of the league, as well as a look at what's coming up in tonight's episode, head over to Randy Buehler's latest [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-episode-11-2015-04-14).
