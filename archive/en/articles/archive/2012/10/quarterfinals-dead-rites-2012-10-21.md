Willy Edel of Brazil is making his fourth Pro Tour Top 8 appearance today. His first appearance on the Sunday stage was at Pro Tour Charleston in 2006 with team mates Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa and Celso Zampere Jr. The format at that time was Ravnica Block Constructed, and now he's looking for his *Return to Ravnica* to lead him to victory this weekend.


However, his opponent, [Channel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Channel)[Fireball](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) team member David Ochoa, had no intentions of making Edel's path to victory easy. Known as a regular contributor to the community's online content and as one of the players who puts in a ton of work to help the team, today marks his first Pro Tour Top 8 appearance. Now that he's made it into Sunday's elimination rounds, he's just three match wins away from taking home the title.


Both players were piloting Jund, but both Edel and Ochoa have their different takes on the deck. Would more instant removal and [Thrun, the Last Troll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thrun%2C+the+Last+Troll) serve the Brazilian well, or would [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman) and [Liliana of the Veil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil) bring victory to the [Channel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Channel)[Fireball](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) team member?


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptrtr12/qf_edel_ochoa.jpg)
*Willy Edel makes his return to the Sunday stage, while David Ochoa is looking to go the distance in his first Pro Tour Top 8.* 

### Game 1


Ochoa started off slow, despite winning the die roll, while Edel led off with [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) on turn two. Ochoa was quick to dispose of the *Ravnica: City of Guilds* rare with [Liliana of the Veil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil), which used its -2 loyalty effect immediately.


Edel used [Treetop Village](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Village) to take out Liliana, while Ochoa untapped and unloaded with [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) and [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman), passing back to Edel, who had no fourth land and no action on his next turn.


Ochoa wasted no time in pushing with [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine) and [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) on Edel's next turn for 7, as Edel used [Jund Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jund+Charm) to take out [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman). Edel untapped and took the chance with [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf), finding [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks).


A [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) from Ochoa revealed the reason for Edel's slow start: [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), [Inquisition of Kozilek](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inquisition+of+Kozilek), [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf), [Jund Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jund+Charm), and [Thrun, the Last Troll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thrun%2C+the+Last+Troll). [Thrun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thrun) hit the graveyard, and [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) attacked in. Edel chump blocked with [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), as the creature persisted back into play and gave the Brazilian 2 more life, bringing him to 11. A post-combat [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf) found [Geralf's Messenger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geralf%27s+Messenger), dropping the Brazilian back to 9.


Edel used [Inquisition of Kozilek](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inquisition+of+Kozilek) to discard Ochoa's [Terminate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terminate), and followed up with [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) to rid him of his last card in hand: [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf). [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) gave Edel a much needed blocker, but he was going to need some help against Ochoa's creatures and [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine), which had one counter already.


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptrtr12/qf_ochoa1.jpg)
*Ochoa's in-hand spells did not remain there for long.* 

Ochoa woke up his land and sent in the team, losing his [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine) to [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), trading Bloodbraid Elves, and bringing Edel to 4. Edel had a [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks) to give him some additional life, but [Geralf's Messenger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geralf%27s+Messenger) would prove to be a problem. Edel blocked with his creatures, equalizing Geralf life-loss for [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks) life gain, but he lost his [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) to a post-combat [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt).


[Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf) gave him some more life in the form of [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), which teamed up with [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf) to take out [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), while the persisted Finks jumped in the way of [Geralf's Messenger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geralf%27s+Messenger). Edel went onto the offensive with [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), putting Ochoa to 16, and a follow-up [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) gave him a sizable blocker. Ochoa, unable to attack through, passed back with [Geralf's Messenger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geralf%27s+Messenger) untapped, but lost the 4/3 zombie to [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt). Attacks put Ochoa to 9, and another [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) put Edel way ahead.


Ochoa played [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) as a blocker, but ultimately succumbed to Edel's lhurgoyf army.


Willy Edel 1, David Ochoa 0


### Game 2


Both players sideboarded and shuffled up quietly as they prepared for a very tense Jund mirror match.


Ochoa led with a turn one [Inquisition of Kozilek](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inquisition+of+Kozilek), revealing a hand of [Treetop Village](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Village), [Wurmcoil Engine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wurmcoil+Engine), [Abrupt Decay](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay), [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt), and three other lands that could get Edel all the mana he would need. [Abrupt Decay](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay) hit the graveyard. Ochoa went to 19 and led into [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant), which ate a [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) from Edel on the second turn.


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptrtr12/qf_edel1.jpg)
*You have some monsters attacking me? I'm not impressed.* 

Ochoa was out of action at that point, while Edel played [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks) on turn three. "Bob," Ochoa announced on turn four, playing [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) with two other lands untapped. Edel sent the Finks in, and blew away [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) with a second [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt). Ochoa had a [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) to follow up, which left Edel unable to attack through the monster. [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) on the next turn got rid of Edel's [Wurmcoil Engine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wurmcoil+Engine), preventing the monstrous artifact creature from hitting play, but the [Channel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Channel)[Fireball](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fireball) team member was still unable to push through Edel's [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine), and [Treetop Village](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Village).


A second [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) threatened to change that, as Edel lacked any sort of action. One of the [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf)s attacked in, putting Edel to 16. Edel drew and passed, with plenty of mana untapped. Both [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf)s came in against the Brazilian player. Edel woke up [Treetop Village](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Village) and double blocked one with [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks) and the land. Ochoa used [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) on the [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), but [Dismember](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember) allowed the [Treetop Village](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Village) to take out the once-large creature.


Edel went on the offensive in the next turn with [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine) and [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), putting Ochoa to 6. A [Treetop Village](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Village) lying in wait with [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine) forced Ochoa to go with [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf), which found... a not-so-useful [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), which he chose not to cast. Ochoa went to 1 on the next attack, and a turn later had him picking up his cards for the third game.


Willy Edel 2, David Ochoa 0


### Game 3


Ochoa was on the play for the third straight game, with both players once again keeping their hands. Ochoa came out with a lightning fast [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman) into [Liliana of the Veil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil). She immediately went to 4 loyalty to force Edel into discarding, as Ochoa pitched [Ancient Grudge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Grudge) and Edel discarded [Thrun, the Last Troll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thrun%2C+the+Last+Troll).


Edel's first play was [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant), forcing Ochoa to use Liliana's -2, as [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman) attacked for 1. [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) followed, while Edel was content to gain some life with [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks). Liliana lost her last two loyalty counters to make Edel sacrifice the [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks),, while [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman) made sure the [Kitchen Finks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks) stayed dead before it persisted back onto the battlefield. [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) attacked as a 4/5, and Ochoa passed back after playing [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine).


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptrtr12/qf_edel2.jpg)
*Edel fights back from a brutal start.* 

Edel quickly untapped for [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf), which found the Brazilian pro a [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) for [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman). [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf) attacked, putting Ochoa to 17. Ochoa attacked back with [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), putting Edel to 10. Then, he played a fifth land and cast [Batterskull](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Batterskull).


Edel matched Ochoa's [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine) after playing out [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) and [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), but [Terminate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terminate) took down Edel's 'Goyf. Edel quickly traded his remaining creatures for [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), going to 6 from the [Batterskull](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Batterskull) attack. Ochoa sent the germ token in, Edel blocked, and [Olivia Voldaren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Olivia+Voldaren) followed for Ochoa to finish the lhurgoyf off. Edel had [Phyrexian Metamorph](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Metamorph) to take out the legendary creature, but an attack from [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine) wrapped things up.


Willy Edel 2, David Ochoa 1


### Game 4


Edel's untapped turn one [Blackcleave Cliffs](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blackcleave+Cliffs) let Ochoa [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) Ochoa's turn one [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman). Edel then played a [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant), as Ochoa was quick to [Terminate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Terminate) any notion that it would reach Edel's upkeep. Edel had no action on turn three, while Ochoa played a 3/4 [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf). Edel again had no spells, as Ochoa attacked in and followed up with [Fulminator Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage) on Edel's [Overgrown Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Tomb). Now, it looked as if Edel would spent even more turns passing with nothing to do.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Fulminator Mage)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator Mage)  
*Bah, you didn't need that [Overgrown Tomb](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overgrown+Tomb), right?* 

Without anymore lands, Edel could only look on as [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) kept attacking him, as a second one joined Ochoa's side. Edel found a fourth land, but still lacked green mana to cast the spells that could get him back into the game. After a few turns of being hit by the two [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), Edel scooped up his cards for the fifth and final game.


Willy Edel 2, David Ochoa 2


### Game 5


The fifth and final game started out in a similar fashion, with [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) from Ochoa once again becoming a human lightning rod for Edel's [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt). A [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) from Edel brought forth [Liliana of the Veil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil) from Ochoa, who promptly sent Edel's creature to his graveyard. [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) took out Liliana while Fuminator Mage left Ochoa without green mana.


Ochoa played a [Forest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forest) from hand and quickly cast [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), which was almost immediately [Dismember](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember)ed by Edel, who added [Treetop Village](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Village) to his board.


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptrtr12/qf_ochoa2.jpg)
*Ochoa bounces back from a rough start to the match.* 

Ochoa went to 17 to [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) Edel's remaining card, [Batterskull](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Batterskull), while a new 6/7 [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf) gave Ochoa a massive threat. Edel had a [Treetop Village](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Village) and [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine), but with plenty of spells and the giant [Tarmogoyf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tarmogoyf), Ochoa was in the driver's seat. [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) also threatened to give Ochoa a stream of cards.


Edel drew and played [Thrun, the Last Troll](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thrun%2C+the+Last+Troll), which would provide him with a solid blocker. However, with [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman) now in play, Ochoa could off-set any life loss from [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant)'s extra cards. As Edel's deck continued to cough up lands, Ochoa went aggressive with his creatures and [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine). [Fulminator Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fulminator+Mage) and [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) disposed of Edel's [Raging Ravine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Ravine) and [Treetop Village](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treetop+Village), as Edel dropped to 5.


Edel untapped, drew, and cast [Bloodbraid Elf](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf). It found an [Abrupt Decay](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrupt+Decay), but it was too little, too late. [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant)'s extra cards proved too back-breaking, [Deathrite Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deathrite+Shaman) gave Ochoa a form of direct damage, and [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) on the next turn sealed the deal.
