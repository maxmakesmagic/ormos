The Mimeoplasm Commander
========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on January 12, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Green-blue-black decks quickly increased in popularity after the release of ***Magic: The Gathering** Commander,* thanks to a brand new legend: The [Mimeoplasm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mimeoplasm)! This powerful commander can be different every time it's cast, doing anything from being a huge [Mulldrifter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mulldrifter) to killing in one hit with [Skithiryx, the Blight Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skithiryx%2C+the+Blight+Dragon)! **Magic** R&D member Max McCall is one such champion of this unique general. When not busy killing people with the legendary ooze, you can find him scheming up new creatures to copy. Check out his take on this very popular commander!
