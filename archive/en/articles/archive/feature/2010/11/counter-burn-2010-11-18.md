While it may seem counter-intuitive to die-hard burn fans, many a player has combined [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) with [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) to powerful effect. Anthony Justice used just such a strategy at Grand Prix–Milwaukee. His Counter Burn deck used [Prophetic Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prophetic+Bolt), [Urza's Rage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Urza%27s+Rage), and [Counterspell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) to stall the opponent, protect his own life total, and eventually take the opponent out. You can check out the brand new burn deck, *Premium Deck Series: Fire & Lightning*, [here](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/ProductArticle.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/pdsfirelightning/productinfo).
