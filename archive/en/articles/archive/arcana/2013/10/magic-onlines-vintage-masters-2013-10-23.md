Magic Online's *Vintage Masters*
================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on October 23, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/logo_vintagemasters.jpg)  


[Download **Magic Online**](https://accounts.onlinegaming.wizards.com/)


For those of you who didn't see it on the **Magic Online** website yesterday, we announced the arrival of Vintage as a format for **Magic Online** through the news of a new **Magic Online**-exclusive set called *Vintage Masters*.

You can read [the full announcement](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/10212013/vintagemasters) about *Vintage Masters* on the **Magic Online** website, where Hall of Famer and Wizards employee Mike Turian dives into it and explains what the set will be, what it will cost, and when to expect it!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/arcana/arc1348_art.jpg)

Join the discussion about the product with the Twitter hashtag: [#MTGOVM](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23MTGOVM).

**Set Name**: *Vintage Masters*
  
****Magic Online** Prerelease Events**: June 13-16, 2014   
****Magic Online** Release Date**: June 16, 2014
