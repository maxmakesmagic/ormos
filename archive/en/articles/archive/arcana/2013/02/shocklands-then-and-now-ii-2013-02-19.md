Shocklands: Then and Now II
===========================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on February 19, 2013 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











Last year, we took a look at [the way the shocklands changed](/en/articles/archive/shocklands-then-and-now-2012-09-24) between the original *Ravnica* block and *Return to Ravnica*. But we could only look at five of them, which seemed a little incomplete. So now that *Gatecrash* is out, let's do some side-by-side comparisons of [Godless Shrine](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Godless+Shrine), [Watery Grave](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Watery+Grave), [Stomping Ground](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stomping+Ground), [Sacred Foundry](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sacred+Foundry), and [Breeding Pool](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Breeding+Pool)!
