Quick Questions #4: What are you playing today and what would your second deck choice be?
=========================================================================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX KYŌTO 2015](/en/events/coverage/gpkyo15)
 on April 18, 2015 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Chapman Sim and Ben Swartz











What are you playing today and what would your second deck choice be?
=====================================================================


![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpkyo15/QQ-Chikara-Nakajima.jpg)  
**Two-Time Pro Tour Top 8 Competitor, Chikara Nakajima:** Elves! My second choice is Sultai Delver.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpkyo15/QQ-Yuuya-Watanabe.jpg)  
**Two-Time Player of the Year and Seven-Time Grand Prix Winner, Yuuya Watanabe:** I'm playing OmniTell, but my second choice wouuld be Temur Delver.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpkyo15/QQ-Kenji-Tsumura.jpg)  
**Hall of Famer and Player of the Year, Kenji Tsumura:** I'm on Sultai Delver today, otherwise I will play Infect or Grixis Delver.




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gpkyo15/QQ-Yuuta-Takahashi.jpg)  
**Pro Tour San Diego 2007 Finalist, Yuuta Takahashi:** I'm playing Miracles, if not I will choose OmniTell.
