GP Dallas-Fort Worth Top 8 Player Profiles
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on May 6, 2018 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Meghan Wolff and Marc Calderaro











After nine rounds of Sealed and two drafts, eight players topped the standings, ready to battle for the trophy in the GP Top 8. Congratulations to the GP DFW Top 8 competitors!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpdfw18/gpdfw18_t8_group.jpg)




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpdfw18/gpdfw18_t8_rubin.jpg)


**Name:** Aaron Rubin


**Hometown:** Pasadena, CA


**Occupation:** Accountant


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


4th place SCG Open


**What color combination did you play on Day 1, and what was your best card?**


WB – [Demonlord Belzenlok](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demonlord+Belzenlok)


**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best cards?**


Draft 1 - WB, [Lyra Dawnbringer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lyra+Dawnbringer)


Draft 2 - RW, [Goblin Barrage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Barrage)


**What's the most exciting play you've made in Dominaria Limited?**


[Fungal Infection](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fungal+Infection) into [Vicious Offering](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vicious+Offering) on camera in Round 12


**What would winning GP DFW mean to you?**


I really just wanted to qualify for the PT. This is gravy. But it would be a fantastic accomplishment.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpdfw18/gpdfw18_t8_brown.jpg)


**Name:** Robert Brown


**Hometown:** Broken Arrow, OK


**Occupation:** Assistant Professor of Engineering at Abilene Christian University


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


I 5-0-ed a couple of leagues on MTGO


**What color combination did you play on Day 1, and what was your best card?**


Blue-Black-Green, Tatyova or [In Bolas's Clutches](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=In+Bolas%27s+Clutches)


**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best cards?**


Draft 1 - Green-White, 3 [Jousting Lance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jousting+Lance)s with Saprolings


Draft 2 - Red-White, Keldon Marauder? The Pegasus? [Run Amok](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Run+Amok)?


**What's the most exciting play you've made in Dominaria Limited?**


[Grow from the Ashes](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grow+from+the+Ashes), kicked, with Tatyova on the battlefield


**What would winning GP DFW mean to you?**


Hard to parse at this point, but it would mean a lot more toward our first house.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpdfw18/gpdfw18_t8_elenbogen.jpg)


**Name:** Andrew Elenbogen


**Twitter Handle:** @aje8888


**Hometown:** Ann Arbor, MI


**Occupation:** Software Engineer


**Previous Magic Accomplishments:**


2.5 GP Top 8s


**What color combination did you play on Day 1, and what was your best card?**


UBr siding into UBw, [Demonlord Belzenlok](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Demonlord+Belzenlok)


**What color combinations did you draft on Day 2, and what were your best cards?**


Draft 1 - BR Tribal Mythic Control, Verix, Jaya, [Yawgmoth's Vile Offering](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yawgmoth%27s+Vile+Offering), [Phyrexian Scriptures](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phyrexian+Scriptures)


Draft 2 - UGr, MirariConjecture, [Fight with Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fight+with+Fire), Tatyova, Mending of Dominaria


**What's the most exciting play you've made in Dominaria Limited?**


Attack with 2x [Firefist Adept](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firefist+Adept)s, they both get eaten. Post-combat, +1 Jaya, cast [Yawgmoth's Vile Offering](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Yawgmoth%27s+Vile+Offering), recur Gharna, bring back both adepts.


**What would winning GP DFW mean to you?**


I'm back on the PT, I'm not washed out. I'm not the worst player on Delta Flyers.
