Scheduled Maintenance on Certain Wizards Services
=================================================



 Posted in **News**
 on October 27, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Later today, Monday, October 27, at 7 p.m. PST, several Wizards sites will be offline for a scheduled upgrade of the account system. This maintenance will affect Community, Planeswalker Points, legacy D&D, WER, the Store Locator, Customer Service, and the Judge Center. You can find further updates by following [@Wizards\_Magic](https://twitter.com/wizards_magic) on Twitter.


We expect all services to be restored in 24 hrs or less.
