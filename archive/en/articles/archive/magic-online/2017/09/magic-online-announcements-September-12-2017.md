**Vintage Super League premiers tonight!**


Vintage Super League (VSL) premiers tonight at 5 p.m. PT on [Twitch](https://www.twitch.tv/magic)! VSL is a series of competitive *Magic Online* matches between *Magic*'s most well known players. Matches will be available every Tuesday night at 5 p.m. PT, beginning tonight!

***Modern Cube* is now available to play**


*Modern Cube* events began last week on Wednesday, September 6. *Modern Cube* will continue to be available through Monday, September 25.


More information about *Modern Cube*, including the full cardlist can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-cube-cardlist).

**Commander 1v1 leagues changes**


During the September 20 downtime, we are moving the Commander 1v1 league from the friendly structure to the competitive structure. Please refer to Lee Sharpe's post [here](http://wizardsmtgo.tumblr.com/post/165228640819/commander-1v1-league-change) for further details.

***Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet* Sealed MOCS Monthly Friday, September 15**


A *Magic Online*Championship Series (MOCS) *Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet*Sealed Monthly Event is coming up on September 15! Players can enter the event with 35 Qualifier Points (QPs), and top finishers qualify for the last remaining Playoff Event on November 11.


Monthly and Playoff Events also award top players MOCS Leaderboard Points for the year. Top players on the [Leaderboard](http://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/leaderboard) at the end of the 2017 MOCS will qualify for the year-end Championship event.


We look forward to seeing you on September 15 for the latest Monthly Event! Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online*Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).


[Top](#top)

***Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet*Sealed MOCS Open Saturday, September 16**


A *Magic Online*Championship Series (MOCS) *Hour of Devastation*/*Amonkhet*Sealed Open Event is coming up on September 16! MOCS Open events do not require an invitation or Qualifier Points to join. The winner qualifies for the *Magic Online*Championship, a Pro Tour, and the Pro Tour Challenge event at that Pro Tour.


Learn more about the 2017 *Magic Online*Championship Series [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/2017-magic-online-championship-series).


[Top](#top)

***Magic Online* Format Challenges back this weekend**


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges introduced a new prize structure and recurring weekend events for Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper formats!


Events begin every Saturday at 8:00 a.m. PT, and run through Sunday. Check out [this page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-format-challenges) for further event details as well as the weekend schedule.
