Modern is beginning to flex its muscle as a new format, and recently had a new set of bannings announced. While that will change many Modern decklists, that hasn't stopped players from testing the waters with new creations. Take, for example, this [Goblin Charbelcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Charbelcher) list used on **Magic Online** by 1nf0rm4luko. It features just 3 [Mountain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain)s, but with a host of other mana sources it can cast its combo extremely quickly: [Goblin Charbelcher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Charbelcher), which is virtually guaranteed to be lethal with so few lands in the deck, or [Grapeshot](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grapeshot) combined with [Pyromancer's Swath](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pyromancer%27s+Swath). How will this deck change with [the bannings](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/feature/161a) in effect?
