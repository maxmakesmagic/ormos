Round 11: Justin Gary vs. Joshua Wagener
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 28, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a780.jpg)Justin Gary hasn't been in the Top 8 of Nationals since 1997, when he won the whole thing. He was quick to point out that he has come in ninth and tenth since then, both on tiebreakers. A win here would go a long way towards putting him in the Top 8 of Nationals for the first time in six years. His opponent, Josh Wagener, is no stranger to success either, with a Top 8 at Grand Prix Boston, and a third place finish at JSS Worlds a few years ago. A Top 8 finish in this tournament would be a huge step forward for his Magic career.

### Game 1

Wagener mulliganed down to six. He opened with [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla), which died to [Chainer's Edict](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainer%27s+Edict). [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) would have died to a second Edict, but Wagener discarded a Rootwalla into play, which took the bullet instead. A second [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel) made Wagener's side better, but he missed his third land drop. Gary cast [Diabolic Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Tutor), and took four more damage from the [Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mongrel)s, plus another two from [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a781.jpg)He untapped, and used [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress) to take away [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd), leaving Wagener with two [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption) in hand, but still only two lands. [Mutilate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate) followed, and Wagener drew two lands in a row to keep him in the game. [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) was flashed back, and the elephant died to [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt), bringing Gary up to a very comfortable eighteen life points. He played [Riptide Replicator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Riptide+Replicator) on the following turn, and set it to make white, 2/2 Chickens. He made a few 2/2 tokens, then played [Diabolic Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Tutor). Wagener conceded, but asked Gary to reveal the card he searched out, which was [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt).

Gary- 1 Wagener- 0

### Game 2

[Llanowar Elves](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Llanowar+Elves) traded with [Innocent Blood](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Innocent+Blood) to start, and Wagener had a turn two [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost), followed by [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel) and [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer) on turn three. [Volcanic Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer) went right to Gary to bring him to fourteen, and [Mutilate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate) killed both creatures. Wagener came right back with another Lavamancer, and [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd). Gary untapped, and with the help of [Cabal Coffers](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Coffers) played [Visara](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara) on turn five.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a782.jpg)The elephant attacked to bring Gary to eleven, and the [Call](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call) was flashed back to make another. [Visara](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara) attacked through the air, and [Mutilate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutilate) killed everything in play but the 5/5 Legend. [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) and [Volcanic Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer) took it down, and Wagener played a Rootwalla. Gary played yet another [Visara](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara), and it blocked the Rootwalla. Wagener tried to pump it, but Gary responded with [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother). Wagener played [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer), which could end the game with burn assistance since Gary was at eleven.

To make matters worse, Wagener played a second [Compost](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Compost). With Wagener at thirteen, Gary decided to use [Visara](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara) to kill the Lavamancer instead of attacking. [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) provided an even bigger threat on the board, and swung back when Gary attacked with [Visara](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Visara). Wagener cast [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd), and Gary attacked to bring Wagener down to three. [Diabolic Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diabolic+Tutor) searched up [Corrupt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corrupt), and that was the match.

**Final Result:** Gary- 2 Wagener- 0
