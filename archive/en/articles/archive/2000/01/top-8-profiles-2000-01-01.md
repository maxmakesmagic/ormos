### Roman Kubera


**Age:** 24


**Where are you from:** Szczecin, Poland


**Occupation:** /


**Played Magic Since:** 1998


**Best Accomplishments:** Top 8 at Grand Prix Warsaw


**What do you consider the best deck in *Invasion* Block Constructed?**  

 U/W/R - Lightning Angel and U/G/R


**What would you change your deck if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

 I would change my sideboard, my main deck is good but my sideboard is constructed awfully.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPWARSAW01/894.jpg)

### Michal Marcik


**Age:** 18


**Where are you from:** Brno, Czech Republik


**Occupation:** Student


**Played Magic Since:** 1995


**Best Accomplishments:**  

 - 9th at Czech Nationals 2001  

 - day two at Grand Prix Florence  

 - won some local tournaments


**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Warsaw?**  

 I was lucky. I have got three byes so I believed in my deck. After the first day I was 6-2, so I had to make a good result the second day.


**What do you consider the best deck in *Invasion* Block Constructed?**  

 My deck, R/B/G Tempo


**What would you change your deck if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

 I wouldn't play Fact or Fiction anymore.






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPWARSAW01/890.jpg)

### Felix Schneiders


**Age:** 20


**Where are you from:** Cologne, Germany


**Occupation:** Math student


**Played Magic Since:** 1997


**Best Accomplishments:**  

 - 9th at German Nationals 2001  

 - 15th At the European Championships 2001


**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Warsaw?**  

 During day one, I was very focused and concentrated and did not have to mulligan except once, which seems to be the most important thing is this format. On day two I got very lucky and had to 'screw' my last opponent to win.


**What do you consider the best deck in Invasion Block Constructed?**  

 R/G Beats: most consistent and (almost) never screwed


**What did you play and would you change your deck if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

 I played U/B/R and I would play the same deck, with maybe more land or cantrips (Opt, Repulse, Exclude)






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/GPWARSAW01/891.jpg)

### Marcin Sados


**Age:** 21


**Where are you from:** Warsaw, Poland


**Occupation:** Student


**Played Magic Since:** 1995


**Best Accomplishments:**  

 - 2000 Polish Nationals Champion  

 - Qualified for Pro Tour Los Angeles 1998


**How would you evaluate your play during Grand Prix Warsaw?**  

 The key thing was the red/green deck I have chosen and the good metagame decision - four Yavimaya Barbarian in the main deck.


**What do you consider the best deck in *Invasion* Block Constructed?**  

 Red/green beatdown


**What would you change your deck if you were to play this Grand Prix again?**  

 I would take out the Kavu Cameleons from the sideboard.
