**Vintage Super League tonight**


Vintage Super League (VSL) is live tonight at 5 p.m. PT on [Twitch](https://www.twitch.tv/magic)! VSL is a series of competitive *Magic Online* matches between *Magic*'s most well known players. 


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/images/hero/VSL%20W2%20promo.png)


Interested in the decklists being played on VSL? Decklists are updated weekly and can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-season-7-decklists).

***Modern Cube* is now available to play**


*Modern Cube* events began September 6 and will continue to be available through Monday, September 25.


More information about *Modern Cube*, including the full cardlist can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/modern-cube-cardlist).

**Commander 1v1 leagues changes**


After tomorrow's extended downtime, the Commander 1v1 leagues will change from the friendly structure to the competitive structure. Please refer to Lee Sharpe's post [here](http://wizardsmtgo.tumblr.com/post/165228640819/commander-1v1-league-change) for further details.


[Top](#top)

**Treasure Chest and MOCS updates**


Updates to Treasure Chests will be expanded upon in tomorrow's article by Lee Sharpe detailing the *Magic Online* release of *Ixalan*. Alongside this publication, *Magic Online* will also be releasing information to-do with the 2018 MOCS!



[Top](#top)

**No Premier Play this weekend due to *Ixalan* Pre-Release**


There will be no Premier Play on *Magic Online* this weekend. Venture out and enjoy the *Ixalan* [Pre-Release](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/ixalan-prerelease-primer-2017-09-18) at your [local game store](http://locator.wizards.com/)! Don't fret though, *Ixalan* events will begin on *Magic Online* Monday, September 25!


[Top](#top)

***Ixalan*events begin on *Magic Online*Monday, September 25!**


*[Ixalan](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/ixalan)*events will begin on *Magic Online*starting Monday, September 25! Keep an eye out tomorrow for Lee Sharpe's article detailing the changes that will come with the new release.


To view the *Ixalan*card gallery, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/ixalan/cards).


[Top](#top)

***Magic Online*Player Rewards**


The *Magic Online* August Player Rewards ([MOPR](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-player-rewards)) card of [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push) will been granted tomorrow! As a reminder, [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push) will be the last promo card to be recieved directly. MOPR promos will begin to be awarded through Player Reward Packs. More information about this change can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-player-rewards-changes-2017-07-31).


[Top](#top)





---

**Legality technicalities update​**


With the deployment of *Ixalan* during tomorrow's (September 20) extended downtime, the *Magic Online* team will also be updating the legality technicalities. This will include Standard being rotated, which will also bring new leagues, and the Legendary rule change will also take effect.


For more details about these updates please visit Lee Sharpe's Tumblr post [here](http://wizardsmtgo.tumblr.com/post/165513964539/legality-technicalities).


[Top](#top)





---

***Magic Online* Format Challenges back this weekend**


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges introduced a new prize structure and recurring weekend events for Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper formats!


Events begin every Saturday at 8:00 a.m. PT, and run through Sunday. Check out [this page](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-format-challenges) for further event details as well as the weekend schedule.


[Top](#top)





---

**Extended downtime tomorrow, September 20**


There will be an extended downtime (3 a.m. PT/10:00 p.m. UTC) tomorrow, September 20. The next regular downtime will be Wednesday, October 11.


Follow *Magic Online*'s [Twitter](https://twitter.com/MagicOnline) account for live updates during downtime.

**Build Notes**


Collection |The August MOPR card of [Fatal Push](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fatal+Push) has been granted! You can find out the details [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-announcements-august-8-2017#item3).

General | [Cavern of Souls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cavern+of+Souls)now correctly interacts with casting creatures of the chosen type.


Account | [Hazoret the Fervent](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hazoret+the+Fervent) now displays correctly as an Avatar.


General | Support for Ixalan cardset
