Yesterday we talked about the upcoming Pro Tour to be held in Amsterdam. The Constructed format for that event? Extended! It will be the debut of the newly revamped format, and the world of **Magic Online** has been gearing up to figure out what the best decks will be. Player \_Batutinha\_ recently went 4-0 in an Extended Daily Event playing a [Scapeshift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scapeshift) Control deck. [Grove of the Burnwillows](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grove+of+the+Burnwillows) and [Punishing Fire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Punishing+Fire) keep creatures under control while [Scapeshift](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scapeshift) can set up an instant win by fetching up a host of [Mountain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mountain)s and [Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut%2C+the+Molten+Pinnacle).
