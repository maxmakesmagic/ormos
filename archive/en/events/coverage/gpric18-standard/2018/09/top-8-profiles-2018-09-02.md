Top 8 Profiles of Grand Prix Richmond 2018 (Standard)
=====================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on September 2, 2018 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Alexander Ullman











After 8 rounds the 786 players in the Standard Grand Prix Richmond were cut down to 171. After another 7 rounds, we have a Top 8!




---

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpric18-standard/GP_Richmond18_JoeStempo.JPG)


**Name:** Joe Stempo


**Age:** 31


**Occupation:** Server


**Hometown:** Catasauqua, California


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** StarCityGames Invitational Top 8, SCG Open Top 8


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**  

Blue-Black Midrange. Played a bunch of games with Bant [Nexus of Fate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nexus+of+Fate) and it felt terrible. Went with an old standby instead.


**What was the best card in your deck and why?**  

One Witty Boi ([Champion of Wits](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Champion+of+Wits))! It's [Faithless Looting](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faithless+Looting) on a body.


**What, if anything, was the most surprising deck you played against this weekend?**  

Grixis Dragos. It wasn't Flame of Keld, Black-Red Aggro, or Esper Control.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**  

First Pro Tour!!!

**Name:** Oliver Tiu


**Age:** 20


**Occupation:** Student


**Hometown:** Cambridge, Massachusetts


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** This is my 6th Grand Prix Top 8 this year.


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**  

Blue-White Control. [Teferi, Hero of Dominaria](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Hero+of+Dominaria) is still good.


**What was the best card in your deck and why?**  

Teferi; it has cool abilities.


**What, if anything, was the most surprising deck you played against this weekend?**  

Black-Red Aggro - wasn't expecting that.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**  

Just another Sunday.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpric18-standard/GP_Richmond18_JoelSodowski.JPG)


**Name:** Joel Sadowsky


**Age:** 24


**Occupation:** Whole Foods


**Hometown:** Columbia, Maryland


**Previous *Magic* Accomplishments:** World *Magic* Cup competitor and one-time Pro Tour competitor.


**What deck did you play this weekend and why?**  

Esper Control because [Torrential Gearhulk](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Torrential+Gearhulk), [Teferi, Hero of Dominaria](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Teferi%2C+Hero+of+Dominaria), and [Vraska's Contempt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vraska%27s+Contempt) are great.


**What was the best card in your deck and why?**  
[Search for Azcanta](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Search+for+Azcanta) because it smoothed my draws.


**What, if anything, was the most surprising deck you played against this weekend?**  

None.


**What does this Top 8 mean to you?**  

I get Bronze, play on the Pro Tour, and finally reach one of my goals!
