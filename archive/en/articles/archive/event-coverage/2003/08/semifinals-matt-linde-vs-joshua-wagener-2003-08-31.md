Semifinals: Matt Linde vs. Joshua Wagener
=========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 31, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett











This semifinal saw two big names in U.S. Magic met this round to determine who would face the already-triumphant Marco Blume in the finals. Matt Linde, with a Worlds Top 4 and a pair of GP Top 4's, including the last Grand Prix – Atlanta, and Josh Wagener, the recent U.S. champ, returning from a successful re-acquisition of the Worlds Team title with team members Justin Gary and Gabe Walls. Looking at the decklists, Linde had a big advantage. His black-white control was designed to crush creature decks. Wagener was not optimistic.

### Game 1

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpatl03/a879.jpg)Linde opened with a [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) and thought back to his pre-match discussions with Gabriel Nassif and Brian Kibler. He chose [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel), and scored, leaving behind [Volcanic Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Volcanic+Hammer), [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption), [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur) and [Elephant Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide). Wagener's second turn came and went with a shrug and a [Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hammer) directed at Linde's dome. Linde played a facedown Angel and hoped, but Wagener had drawn a second [Hammer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hammer).

He untapped and cycled a [Decree of Justice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice) for a single soldier, fuelling the flashback on [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) and snagging the [Phantom Centaur](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur), leaving Wagener with no men in hand. [Skeletal Scrying](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skeletal+Scrying) got him some cards, and then he had an [Eternal Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon) on the board. Wagener pulled up [Birds of Paradise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds+of+Paradise) and put them into play, then dropped the Guide on them, giving it back.

Linde cycled an [Akroma's Vengeance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroma%27s+Vengeance) and beat in for five. He had the [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) to cut down the [Birds](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds) on Wagener's attack. He hit again. Wagener drew [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) and made two tokens, but Linde had [Decree of Justice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice) to provide ample blockers. He also had [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) to strip Wagener's hand, at which point he conceded.

Linde 1 – Wagener 0

### Game 2

Wagener had to ship his opening hand back because of its single forest. His next six had two, and he kept. Linde fired first with [Cabal Therapy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cabal+Therapy) for [Wild Mongrel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). It missed. Wagener held [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd), [Elephant Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide), [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt), [Naturalize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Naturalize) and a forest. His draws were good, though, and while Linde cycled his [Eternal Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon), Wagener was getting in with [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd) and [Sulfuric Vortex](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sulfuric+Vortex).

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpatl03/a881.jpg)Linde blunted the token's attack for a turn with a cycled [Decree of Justice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Decree+of+Justice), but Wagener had more pressure in the form of a [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla) that got the [Elephant Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide). Linde played his fifth land and put an [Undead Gladiator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Undead+Gladiator) in the way. [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) got rid of it, but Linde had [Smother](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smother) to spare himself a lot of hurt. The Vortex put him to eight. He hit six land, and [Vengeance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vengeance)d the board away.

Wagener flashed back his [Call](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call). Linde picked up his Gladiator and dropped [Sphere of Law](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphere+of+Law), dropping to five from Wagener's attack. Wagener showed a second [Call of the Herd](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call+of+the+Herd). Linde untapped and then tapped five, catching Wagener with a [Haunting Echoes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haunting+Echoes) that stripped his most of his Rootwallas, [Call](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Call)s, [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt)s, Vortices, and [Elephant Guide](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elephant+Guide)s. He had [Ghastly Demise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghastly+Demise) to survive the attack, but now he was at two.

He picked up his Gladiator and put it into play. Wagener showed him the Rootwalla he'd left in the deck. When an answer didn't present itself, Linde scooped.

Linde 1 – Wagener 1

### Game 3

While dealing out his opening hand for Game 3, Wagener accidentally pulled two cards with his last draw, seeing what would be the top card of his deck. He noted his error and brought it to the attention of the table judge, who gave him a warning and forced him to mulligan. He checked the top ten cards of his deck: lands and [Birds](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birds). That was something. His next six were far from enticing. Two mountains, [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt), [Stabilizer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stabilizer) and two Rootwallas. He kept.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpatl03/a880.jpg)Linde led off and watched as Wagener played [Grim Lavamancer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer) and [Basking Rootwalla](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basking+Rootwalla). He cycled an [Eternal Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eternal+Dragon) and passed it back. Wagener hit and dropped a [Stabilizer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stabilizer), forcing Linde to cycle his [Wipe Clean](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wipe+Clean) while he could. He played his fourth land and passed it back. Wagener hit, and deliberated pumping his Rootwalla. He did, and Linde showed him [Ghastly Demise](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ghastly+Demise). [Firebolt](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt) went to the dome, and he was done.

Linde hard cast [Renewed Faith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Renewed+Faith) before untapping, played his fifth land, and was done. With one Demise out of the way, Wagener went for the Guide on his Lavamancer. Linde showed him a second Demise. Wagener played a Rootwalla and was done. Linde calmly picked up his Dragon. Wagener played Vortex, and the crowd gasped when Linde wrecked his position with [Akroma's Vengeance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroma%27s+Vengeance). The Dragon followed and he started beating.

Wagener tried to draw some good ones. He got a Volcanic hammer and then a [Violent Eruption](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption), but Linde wasn't letting up. A cycled Decree brought some men to the table, as well as Wagener's outstretched hand.

**Final Result:** Matt Linde defeats Josh Wagener 2-1
