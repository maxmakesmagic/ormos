Finals Draft: Illuminati vs. The Jokas
======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 30, 2002 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpnj02/861.jpg)After two days of intense competition, it all came down to this: The Jokas vs. Illuminati. The winner would not only come home with a Grand Prix trophy, but an invitation to the Team Masters Series at Pro Tour Venice in March of 2003. Illuminati is looking to make their way to a victory after losing an upset to Les Plus Class in the Top 4 of Pro Tour New York back in September.

Rose opened the first pack, and grabbed [Syncopate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Syncopate), while Woods went right into his comfortable aggro niche with [Firebolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Firebolt). Gary drafted [Childhood Horror](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Childhood+Horror), Zvi picked up [Werebear](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Werebear) and a [Refresh](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Refresh), while Alex got a [Scrivener](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrivener). James got [Peek](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Peek), and [Sphere of Law](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphere+of+Law). The second pack let Woods ease into his familiar red/green, as he gladly took [Wild Mongrel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Mongrel). Gary drafted [Chainflinger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chainflinger), Zvi added [Springing Tiger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Springing+Tiger) to his green army, and Shvartsman got [Hallowed Healer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hallowed+Healer) to go into blue/white. Meanwhile, Rose drafted a second [Syncopate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Syncopate). The following pack gave Gary an [Afflict](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Afflict), and Zvi a second Tiger. In an interesting development, James got a second [Sphere of Law](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sphere+of+Law), which could prove to be very relevant against Gary's red/black deck. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpnj02/862.jpg)Zvi still had no second color, so he jumped at the opportunity to grab a [Puppeteer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Puppeteer) and go into blue. Shvartsman than drafted [Mystic Crusader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mystic+Crusader), which had potential to be great against James' green/red deck. The next pack hooked up The Jokas, as James got [Patriarch's Desire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Patriarch%27s+Desire), Rose drafted [Hallowed Healer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hallowed+Healer), and Woods picked [Sylvan Might](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Might). Rounding out the rest of *Odyssey*, James went into green to supplement his black with [Rabid Elephant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rabid+Elephant), Gary picked up [Mindslicer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslicer) and [Dirty Wererat](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dirty+Wererat), and Woods got [Overrun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overrun). 

To follow up in *Torment*, Gary drafted [Violent Eruption](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Violent+Eruption), while James got [Carrion Wurm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Carrion+Wurm). Shvartsman opened his *Torment* pack to reveal [Sengir Vampire](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sengir+Vampire), and Gary raised his hands in triumph, as he knew the 4/4 flier was his. As if that wasn't good enough, Gary's deck got even more nuts with a [Faceless Butcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Faceless+Butcher) in the next pack, and [Grotesque Hybrid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grotesque+Hybrid) followed in the very next one. Woods drafted [Centaur Chieftain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Centaur+Chieftain) to give him another breakthrough card in addition to [Overrun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overrun), while James got a second Butcher to make his matchup against Gary's powerful deck very interesting. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpnj02/863.jpg)In *Judgment*, Woods drafted *three*[Arcane Teachings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcane+Teachings), and Rose was shipped [Phantom Nishoba](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nishoba) in a pack opened by Alex. He was more than happy to splash green for it in his blue/white deck. Not all was lost for Illuminati, as Gary got [Masked Gorgon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Masked+Gorgon), and Mowshowitz drafted [Phantom Centaur](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Centaur). 

When all was said and done, there were plenty of impressive decks at the table. Woods' deck is a powerhouse, with three [Arcane Teachings](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Arcane+Teachings), [Overrun](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Overrun), [Centaur Chieftain](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Centaur+Chieftain), and everything in-between. Gary's deck is chock full 'o broken cards, and Rose's solid white/blue deck is splashing green for powerhouse finisher [Phantom Nishoba](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Phantom+Nishoba). If nothing else, the finals should be action packed, and entertaining.
