Grand Prix Trial Winning Decklists
==================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 25, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_FrankKarsten.jpg)
By Frank Karsten











Yesterday, Grand Prix Utrecht competitors had one last chance to earn two byes for the main event by going 5-0 in one of the Battle for Byes tournaments. These 5-0 decklists traditionally give a first impression of what to expect over the course of the weekend. You can find 14 of them below.


Among these 14, the "Big Three" dominate: there are three Four-Color Saheeli decks, six Black-Green Constrictor decks, and three Mardu Vehicles decks. But there are also two sweet Temur decks, both based around an energy theme. One uses [Dynavolt Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dynavolt+Tower); the other uses [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel), indicating that there are still avenues to explore beyond the "Big Three" decks in Standard.
