Top 8 Profiles

By Event Coverage Staff

### Guillaume Matignon


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptsj10/top%208_matignon.jpg)


**Age:**29  
**Hometown:**Bordeaux, France  
**Occupation:**Unemployed  
**Number of times playing on the Pro Tour:**Don’t know  
**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-San Juan?**Pro Level 4  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** None of that  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments:**Doing pretty good at Nationals every year.


**What was your record by format?   

Block Constructed:**7-0-3  
**Booster Draft:**5-1


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour-San Juan? Who did you prepare with?**  

Tested with Guillaume Wafo-Tapa as usual. Didn’t find anything good. I took the deck of Jeremie Dezani two days before the Pro Tour and worked on it with Oli Ruel.


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**  

Between 0 and 4.


**What Block Constructed deck did you play and why?**  

UGR Tokens because I didn’t find anything better than that.


**What is your preferred Rise of the Eldrazi draft strategy?**  

Never go white. Anything else is fine. I actually pretty much try to draft green decks.


**What’s your favorite part of the Pro Tour coming to San Juan?**  

Enjoying a trip with friends.

### Noah Swartz


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptsj10/top%208_swartz.jpg)


**Age:**21  
**Hometown:**Highland Park, IL  
**Occupation:**Student  
**Number of times playing on the Pro Tour:**2  
**How did you qualify for Pro Tour-San Juan?**PTQ (Dark Depths/Thopter Combo)  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:**0  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments:**54th at Pro Tour-Honolulu, Top 8 Legacy World Championships, *Magic Online* Live Series 1st


**What was your record by format?   

Block Constructed:**10-0   
**Booster Draft:**2-4


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour-San Juan? Who did you prepare with?**  

My constructed deck came from Zvi Mowshowitz and his testing group and I drafted onMagic Online and throughout the week while enjoying the Puerto Rican rain.


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**  

Very dependent on homework and my test schedule. Over the summer it will be much more.


**What Block Constructed deck did you play and why?**  

Monogreen BEATS because it beat Monored and Eldrazi and thus was well positioned in the format.


**What is your preferred Rise of the Eldrazi draft strategy?**  

I’m most comfortable with UW and RG but I’ll go for whatever is open.


**What’s your favorite part of the Pro Tour coming to San Juan?**  

The flurry of testing a brainstorming that pervades the pro community the week leading up to the Pro Tour, as well as seeing many of my friends who I only get to see a few times each year.
