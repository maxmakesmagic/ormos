Aaron Wayne managed to win a StarCityGames.com Open event in St. Louis in December. The large cash tournament series features back-to-back events, one in Standard and one in Legacy. Wayne returned to the Legacy event in Dallas and managed a second Top 8 there! His deck of choice? A white-blue tempo deck that plays four copies of [Fathom Seer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fathom+Seer)! It's truly a unique deck, especially in the world of Legacy.
