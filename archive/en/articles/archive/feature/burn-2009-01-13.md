In honor of the start of the Pro Tour Qualifier-Honolulu season, Daily Decks is bringing you some of the hottest Extended decks all week long! While former World Champion and 2008 Pro Tour-Kuala Lumpur victor Jon Finkel is famous for his prowess with blue cards, he took a different path at the 2008 Extended portion of the World Championships. Finkel served with none other than [Spark Elemental](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spark+Elemental) to get the job done against his opponents, burning to a crisp all who stood in his way.
