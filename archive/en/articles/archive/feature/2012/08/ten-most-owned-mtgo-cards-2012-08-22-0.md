Ten Most Owned MTGO Cards
=========================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 22, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TrickJarrett.jpg)
By Trick Jarrett




Patrick "Trick" Jarrett oversees web content and social media for Magic, and acts as the publisher of DailyMTG.com and Magic content on the web. He's an ardent Commander player and lover of the game he's played since Ice Age. 







Do you play **Magic Online**? Of course you do. It's one of the things I genuinely miss about not working for **Wizards of the Coast**, drafting on Magic Online that is.


Last week, Worth Wollpert, Magic's Senior Digital Producer, tweeted the ten most owned cards on Magic Online. That means, ignoring basic lands, they're the cards which people have the most copies of. He drew the guesses out over a week, but now that he's all done with his #mtgotop10 hashtag, we can share it with you all in a nice succinct list!
