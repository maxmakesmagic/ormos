Overview
--------


Three days, $750,000 in prize money, and 68 of the world’s very best competitors face off in *Magic: The Gathering Arena* at Mythic Championship V!


**Date:** October 18-20, 2019  
**Format:** Traditional Standard Best-of-Three  
**Official Hashtag:** [#MythicChampionshipV](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23MythicChampionshipV&src=typd)



Live Broadcast
--------------


Mythic Championship V will take place at Thunder Studios in Long Beach, California.


Follow live video coverage of Mythic Championship V on [twitch.tv/magic](https://www.twitch.tv/magic)! Coverage begins October 18!


Tournament Information
----------------------


The format for Mythic Championship V is Traditional Best-of-Three Standard Constructed across all three days of competition.


#### Day 1 – Modified Swiss


64 players battle it out in up to seven rounds on *Magic: The Gathering Arena* and 24 players will advance to Day 2. Players who earn five wins will automatically advance to Day 2. Players who lose four matches are eliminated from the tournament. Players who end Round 7 with four wins will move on to Day 2 based on their Day 1 tiebreakers until 24 players have advanced.


#### Day 2 – Modified Swiss


The Top 24 from Day 1 join the Magic Pro League divisional champions from the Core Split in a seven-round Swiss event. All players who reach five match wins move on to Day 3. Additional players will round out the Top 8 for Day 3 based on their tiebreakers in Day 2.


#### Day 3 – Top 8 Double-Elimination


8 players compete in a true double-elimination tournament. In the grand finals, the winners in the Upper Bracket and Lower Bracket will face off. The player in the Lower Bracket must win two matches to win the tournament!

How Can I Play in an MTG Arena Mythic Championship?
---------------------------------------------------


Players can qualify for Mythic Championships featuring *Magic: The Gathering Arena* by finishing in the Top 16 of the two-day Mythic Championship Qualifier Weekend.


Your first step towards the Mythic Championship is by being a top Mythic-ranked players on *Magic: The Gathering Arena*. Download now and start on your path to the Mythic Championship!
