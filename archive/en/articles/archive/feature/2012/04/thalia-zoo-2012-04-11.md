Thalia Zoo
==========



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 11, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorPic_GavinVerhey.jpg)
By Gavin Verhey




 When Gavin Verhey was eleven, he dreamt of a job making Magic cardsâ€”and now as a Magic designer, he's living his dream! Gavin has been writing about Magic since 2005. 






Modern PTQs might have come to a halt for this season, but that doesn't mean the format is showing any sign of slowing down! **Magic Online** is still pushing the robust new metagame ahead with a constant stream of tournaments. One older deck that has appeared out of the woodwork recently? Zoo!

Even with [Wild Nacatl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Nacatl) out of the picture, **Magic Online** player Rous Ita has done well with his version that uses [Thalia, Guardian of Thraben](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thalia%2C+Guardian+of+Thraben). Featuring a full four copies of the *Dark Ascension* legend, Rous Ita uses the Innistrad storyline's key character to slow down decks like Storm to a grinding halt. When all of your mana-producing spells and cantrips cost an extra mana to cast, generating a lethal storm count is nearly impossible.

In addition to Thalia, the Zoo deck still has all of the classic non-Nacatl pieces. Aggressively costed creatures fill up the one- and two-drop slots. This version also has a more burn-heavy bent, using eleven burn spells in addition to a full set of [Grim Lavamancer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Lavamancer) to knock down creatures and players alike.

If you like attacking and sending fire to the opponent's face in Modern, this is going to be a deck to keep an eye on. Don't miss the decklist below!
