### Battalion


When playing the "Battalion" deck, you'll marshal the military might of the Soldiers. It should be no surprise that you want to attack early and often. It might be a surprise that the finishing blow will often come from your air force!

### Going Rogue


With the "Going Rogue" deck, you won't smash your opponent into oblivion. Instead, you'll flit and stab and dodge and poke—all the way to your inevitable victory. By the time you've won, your opponent will just be figuring out that you might be trouble . . . .
