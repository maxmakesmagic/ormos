### GRAND PRIX TAIPEI 2014


Quick Questions #4
==================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/blisterguy_icon_1.jpg)

Ray “blisterguy” Walkinshaw




July 26, 2014
 










What was the best card in your sealed deck yesterday?





![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gptai14/02_QQ_Lee.jpg)
***Lee Shi Tian:** "[Unmake the Graves](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unmake+the+Graves). I had a bad deck with double [Necromancer's Assistant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Necromancer%27s+Assistant) and [Satyr Wayfinder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Satyr+Wayfinder)."* 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gptai14/02_QQ_Kuo.jpg)
***Tzu Ching Kuo:** "[Feral Incarnation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Feral+Incarnation)."* 







![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gptai14/02_QQ_Soh.jpg)
***Weng Heng Soh:** "[Polymorphist's Jest](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Polymorphist%27s+Jest). I managed to combo it with [Festergloom](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Festergloom)."* 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gptai14/02_QQ_Shimizu.jpg)
***Naoki Shimizu:** "[Hornet Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Queen) and [Chord of Calling](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chord+of+Calling). Combo!"* 







![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gptai14/02_QQ_Chew.jpg)
***Kelvin Chew:** "I can't decide which was best, but I had [Hornet Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hornet+Queen), [Soul of Shandalar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Soul+of+Shandalar) and [Siege Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Dragon)."* 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gptai14/02_QQ_Cheung.jpg)
***Justin Cheung:** "[Paragon of Eternal Wilds](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Paragon+of+Eternal+Wilds)."*
