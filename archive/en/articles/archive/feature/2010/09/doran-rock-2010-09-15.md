In the same weekend he was announced as a 2010 member of the Pro Tour Hall of Fame, Brian Kibler managed to rip off a powerful performance that saw him competing in the Top 8 of Pro Tour–Amsterdam! The deck that got him there? Doran Rock, which was also played by Top 8 member Brad Nelson. Like traditional Rock decks it controls the game with removal and hand-kill while beating down with powerful creatures like [Doran, the Siege Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower). As an added benefit, [Treefolk Harbinger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Treefolk+Harbinger) not only calls forth the 0/5, but even tutors for the only "dual" land in Extended: [Murmuring Bosk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Murmuring+Bosk)! Read full coverage at [here](/en/events/coverage/rietzl-sweeps-victory-amsterdam).
