Trials Breakdown
================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on July 23, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_164.png)
By Brian David Marshall











There were six Standard format Trials yesterday. They were all thirty-two person elimination events with the exception of the final Trial which was a sixteen person tournament. Each tournament awarded the winner with three byes to today's proceedings. Here are the archetype breakdowns for each tournament and the deck lists for the two finalists.
