What You Might Have Missed This Weekend
=======================================


1. [StarCityGames.com Indianapolis Open Weekend](http://www.starcitygames.com/events/011016_indianapolis.html)

We got our first look at new Standard with the traditional aggro-palooza as aggressive decks, particularly [Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter), lined the Top 8 nearly up and down. The one exception to the aggro rule is our [Deck of the Day](#item10). Check out the [Top 64 decklists](http://sales.starcitygames.com/deckdatabase/deckshow.php?&t%5BC1%5D=1&start_date=10/01/2016&end_date=10/02/2016&start=1&finish=64&event_ID=19&city=Indianapolis&limit=64) to see the first take on new Standard.


1. [Modern Studies 301: An Introduction to Naya Burn for *Magic: The Gathering*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wJDZXK6POzo) | Tolarian Community College | The Professor

Some people just want to watch the world burn—and now there's a video for those players!


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/wJDZXK6POzo>


1. [*Kaladesh* Prerelease Field Recordings](http://themanapool.com/podcast/episode-436/) | The Mana Pool | Chewie and the Dorks

This one is kind of interesting. The podcast is a broadcast from the field during the *Kaladesh* Prerelease, getting everyone's on-the-spot reactions to the set. If you want to hear squees of excitement and snap judgments about the relative strength of a pool, this might be an interesting podcast for you.

After Those, Read, Watch, or Listen to These

1. [Crewing and Cruising to Victory](http://www.mtgmintcard.com/articles/writers/martin-dang/crewing-and-cruising-to-victory) | MTGMintCard | Martin Dang

With two Limited Grand Prix coming this weekend ([Atlanta](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/premierplay/grandprix/fact-sheet-gpatl16) and [London](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/premierplay/grandprix/fact-sheet-gplon16)), Pro Tour Champion Martin Dang has some tips and advice for playing *Kaladesh* Sealed.

And, yes, [Cloudblazer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudblazer)'s presence did give him a leg up today. Let that be a lesson to you, kids, if you want your content to show up on DailyMTG.com, just toss in a good word or two for [Cloudblazer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudblazer).

Deck of the Day
===============


1. Grixis Emerge

Zach Voss took this hybrid emerge/Zombie strategy all the way to second place at the StarCityGames.com Open this past weekend. The deck heavily utilizes the graveyard with [Prized Amalgam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam), [Haunted Dead](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Haunted+Dead), [Scrapheap Scrounger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrapheap+Scrounger), and even [Perpetual Timepiece](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Perpetual+Timepiece), while also going big with [Kozilek's Return](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kozilek%27s+Return) and [Elder Deep-Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elder+Deep-Fiend). [Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter), of course, makes its presence felt as well.


Given the strong initial performance of this deck and the power of the [Elder Deep-Fiend](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elder+Deep-Fiend)/[Sanctum of Ugin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sanctum+of+Ugin)/[Kozilek's Return](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kozilek%27s+Return) engine and the [Prized Amalgam](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Prized+Amalgam) recursion package, I would expect this deck to stick around as a fixture of the metagame for some time.
