A Legacy is Established!
========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 12, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpphi05/fin.jpg)**Jon Sonne is the Grand Prix Philadelphia 2005 Champion!**



With almost a quarter of the field sporting goblins this weekend it was Jon Sonne's version that emerged on top. His deck incorporated the mana disruption elements of classic RDW. In his three game finals with the [Meddling Mage](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddling+Mage) himself, Chris Pikula, Jon's sideboarded [Patron of the Akki](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Patron+of+the+Akki) helped him over the hump of Chris' turn one [Engineered Plague](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Plague). 


It was an exciting weekend chock full of goblins, combos, and control. It will be exciting to see the effects of this event - the first major Legacy event of any kind - will have on the remaining event with the same format later in the year.

Chris tried Gerard's Verdict - "You are getting some good ones." - and nabbed Siege-Gang and another Piledriver. [Cursed Scroll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cursed+Scroll) punctuated his turn. Jon used [Wasteland](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland) on Chris' Scrubland and played another Piledriver. [Cursed Scroll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cursed+Scroll) one of the Piledrivers naming [Duress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duress). 

Chris looked up at the judge and smiled, "He forgot to attack one turn right?"

![Engineered Plague](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Engineered+Plague)Jon had been hoping that Chris wouldn't notice his slip up and grinned sheepishly, "I have been playing tooo good. I just had to make a mistake right?"

"It's cool now if I lose I can complain, 'I lost to some guy who just didn't attack with Piledriver.'"

Sonne Matroned for a Warchief. Chris killed the second Piledriver during his upkeep.

Ringleader changed the tempo of the game around as Sonne nabbed Siege-Gang and Lackey off the top. Chris tried to keep Jon off double red with Sinkhole but Jon ripped Mire off the top. Warchief was killed on upkeep. The goblins kept swinging though and Chris life was dribbling away. 

[Nantuko Shade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nantuko+Shade) traded with Ringleader but things were looking grim. 

"C'mon Sonne! SONNE! SONNE!" chanted Antonino DeRosa from somewhere behind the crowded rail. Chris made a pouty face and hollered back, "He already lost!" even though it was the opposite of true. 

### Game 2

Jon had a turn one Lackey but Chris came over the top on turn two with [Darkblast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darkblast) and [Cursed Scroll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cursed+Scroll). Sonne had to play fair with a two-drop on turn two and summoned Piledriver. Chris put out [Dark Confidant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) and when Sonne tried to cycle [Gempalm Incinerator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gempalm+Incinerator) to kill it, Pikula used Swords on the Piledriver. 

[Wasteland](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland) took out Jon's Port and Hymn to Tourach took his hand down a notch. 

[Withered Wretch](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Withered+Wretch) - Chris' sideboard against aggressive decks - lined up to enter the red zone in a turn. Sonne's Piledriver was killed EOT with [Dark Ritual](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Ritual) to activate [Cursed Scroll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cursed+Scroll). Chris bashed in for two and played [Hypnotic Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypnotic+Specter). 

Jon looked over the board and opted to save everyone some time and move onto Game 3. "That is the first game I have lost in five or six rounds."

"And no [Engineered Plague](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Plague)!" exclaimed Pikula. 

### Game 3

"I'm going to six," grumbled Chris. "That is my first no-land hand of the tournament."

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpphi05/sonne2.jpg)*Jonathan Sonne is the 2005 Grand Prix Philadelphia Champion!*Sonne had the first turn Lackey but Pikula had the first turn [Engineered Plague](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Plague). Sonne had no turn two play but Pikula was out of land. Sonne tried to get in for one with Warchief but Chris was ready with [Darkblast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darkblast). 

Sonne put down Piledriver and Chris - who still needed land - dredged up the [Darkblast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Darkblast). He did it again on the next turn to kill [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander). He was concerned about Jon being able to pay the offering cost on [Patron of the Akki](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Patron+of+the+Akki). He needn't have worried. Jon simply paid full price. Chris still didn't have a second land and took a whupping from the legend. 

Jon had [Goblin Ringleader](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Ringleader) into [Goblin Warchief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Warchief) on the next turn and attacked for a gaudy fifteen under the [Engineered Plague](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Plague). Chris shook his head, "I can't believe I lost this game with [Engineered Plague](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Engineered+Plague) in play and won the one without it."

Jon Sonne was the Grand Prix Philadelphia 2005 Champion!
