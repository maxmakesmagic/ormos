Magic at New York Comic Con
===========================



 Posted in **News**
 on January 15, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Stop by booth 1629 at New York Comic Con on February 6-8 to see the latest offerings from **Magic: The Gathering**.


Demos will be running daily for **Magic: The Gathering**, **Magic: The Gathering Online v3.0**, and the new offering for Xbox Live Arcade – [*Duels of the Planeswalkers*](/en/node/633021). [Steward of Valeron](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steward+of+Valeron+)  foil cards, tote bags and **Magic Online** promotional codes† will be given out to those who demo these products\*. 


Wizards of the Coast has a full schedule of gaming planned for New York Comic Con 2009. Stop by to enter **Magic: The Gathering**  convention league, the *Conflux*  release tournament, the Legacy tournament, or the other great events that will be run throughout the weekend. **Magic**  tote bags, deck boxes and life counters will be given out to all participants\*. A full schedule of events is available at [www.tjcollect.com](http://www.tjcollect.com). Additional fees may apply for some events.


For those players only interested in participating in **Magic**  events all weekend, New York Comic Con and Wizards have teamed up in 2009 to bring you a $15 3-day gaming badge that allows you entrance to the gaming area and the Javits Galleria(x). For more information, visit [www.nycomiccon.com](http://www.nycomiccon.com).


*\*Limit one per participant, while supplies last.* 


†***Magic: The Gathering Online** promotional codes available while supplies last, to participants 18 years and older only. Promotional codes are redeemable for $20 worth of merchandise in the MTGO store, and are valid for redemption from 2/6/09 through 2/19/09. Purchases made with promotional code are non-refundable. Must be eligible to register, and register for a Magic: The Gathering Online account and accept all terms of service to be valid.*


*(x)* *The gaming badge will grant you 3-day admission to the Javits Galleria, the Galleria gaming area, and all external areas outside of the main Exhibit Hall. Additional fees for individual events may apply.*
