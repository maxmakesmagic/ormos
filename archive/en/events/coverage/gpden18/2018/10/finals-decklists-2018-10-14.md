Finals Decklists of Grand Prix Denver 2018
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on October 15, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/hosler.jpg)
By Corbin Hosler











Team Draft is one of the most unique formats in *Magic.* Two teams of three players each, seated staggered so each player is flanked by two opponents as they draft before playing the match. It's one of the more skill-testing formats in the game, and one that is always hotly debated by players. Here are the decks each team put together for the finals.
