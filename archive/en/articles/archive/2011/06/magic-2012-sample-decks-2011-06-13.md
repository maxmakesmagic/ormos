Magic 2012 Sample Decks
=======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 13, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley












Just like with ***Magic** 2011*, the ***Magic** 2012* Core Set is going to have some thirty-card sample decks. These are the sort of thing that get handed out at conventions, distributed to stores where **Duels of the Planeswalkers** 2012 players might show up (for example, to get a promo card), that sort of thing. The idea is that they're a handy thing to give to someone who might be interested in learning **Magic**.


In case you're curious, here are the ten decklists. They mostly contain cards from ***Magic** 2012*, although there are a few cards from other blocks sprinkled in there. So don't go thinking that, say [Inquisitor Exarch](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inquisitor+Exarch) is in ***Magic** 2012*. Because it isn't.


One more thing: some of these cards haven't been officially previewed anywhere. So you can scroll down past the decklists to see those cards. Whee!
