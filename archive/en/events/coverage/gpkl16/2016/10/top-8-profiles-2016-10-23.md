Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX KUALA LUMPUR 2016](/en/events/coverage/gpkl16)
 on October 23, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/chapman.jpg)
By Chapman Sim












 After many rounds of Swiss and some great games of *Magic*, the Top 8 for Grand Prix Kuala Lumpur is set! Learn more about this event's Top 8 competitors, their history at the top tables, and more by taking a look through the Top 8 profiles.





---






### Yuuki Ichikawa



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpkl16/gpKL16_T8_1YuukiIchikawa.jpg)


**Age:** 28


**Occupation:** Team Cygames Pro Player


**Hometown:** Japan


**Previous Accomplishments:** 2 Pro Tour Top 8s and 7 Grand Prix Top 8s with 3 wins.


**Score on Day 1:** 8 - 1 (3 byes)


**Score on Day 2:** 5 - 0 - 1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  
White-Blue Flash. It was best for the new metagame.



**What is your favorite card from *Kaladesh*?**  
[Bomat Bazaar Barge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bomat+Bazaar+Barge)





---






### Anthony Lee



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpkl16/gpKL16_T8_2AnthonyLee.jpg)


**Age:** 24


**Occupation:** MTG "Bent" Card Captain (It's an inside joke within Team MTG Mint Card because I play with poor condition cards all the time!)


**Hometown:** Perth, Australia


**Previous Accomplishments:** Andrea Mengucci's favorite Australian.


**Score on Day 1:** 9 - 0 (2 byes)


**Score on Day 2:** 3 - 1- 2


**What deck are you playing and why?**  
White-Blue Flash splashing black for [Scrapheap Scrounger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scrapheap+Scrounger). It was built by Justin Cheung and Zen Takahashi so it must be good. White-Blue Flash is clearly the best deck at the moment.



**What is your favorite card from *Kaladesh*?**  
Lee Shi Tian's Pro Player Card





---






### Mark Lawrence Tubola



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpkl16/gpKL16_T8_3MarkLawrenceTubola.jpg)


**Age:** 31


**Occupation:** Bank Employee


**Hometown:** Iloilo City, Philippines



**Previous Accomplishments:** 15th place at Grand Prix Manila 2015



**Score on Day 1:** 9 - 0 (2 byes)


**Score on Day 2:** 3 - 2 -1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  
White-Blue Flash, because the internet said so.



**What is your favorite card from *Kaladesh*?**  
I love [Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter). Special shoutout to Team Iloilo!

### Yuta Takahashi



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpkl16/gpKL16_T8_4YutaTakahashi.jpg)


**Age:** 30


**Occupation:** Hareruya Employee


**Hometown:** Tokyo, Japan


**Previous Accomplishments:** 2 Pro Tour Top 8s and 12 Grand Prix Top 8s


**Score on Day 1:** 8 - 1 (3 byes)


**Score on Day 2:** 4 - 1 - 1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  
White-Blue Flash. There are no bad matchups and it feels like Blue-Black Faeries.



**What is your favorite card from *Kaladesh*?**  
[Smuggler's Copter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Smuggler%27s+Copter). It reminds me of "turn two Bitterblossom".

### Fumiya Matsumoto



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpkl16/gpKL16_T8_5FumiyaMatsumoto.jpg)


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Engineer


**Hometown:** Japan


**Previous Accomplishments:** 1 Grand Prix Top 8

### Marcus Oh



![](https://media.wizards.com/2016/events/gpkl16/gpKL16_T8_6MarcusOh.jpg)


**Age:** 27


**Occupation:** Tutor


**Hometown:** Singapore


**Score on Day 1:** 9 - 0 (2 byes)


**Score on Day 2:** 3 - 2 - 1


**What deck are you playing and why?**  
White-Blue Flash. I kept losing to it during playtesting so I decided to join them.

[Unlicensed Disintegration](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Unlicensed+Disintegration). Destroying creatures and dealing damage to my opponent are two of my favorite things in *Magic* .
