Feature: Fun Extended Decklists
===============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 2, 2005 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TedKnutson.jpg)
By Ted Knutson













![Armadillo Cloak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Armadillo+Cloak)

There's not enough time to give every interesting deck here at Worlds its due, but here are three interesting and at least somewhat competitive decks for players to consider. One of these is The Phantom Menace, a deck apparently designed by New York City native Tim McKenna which uses the power of [Tallowisp](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tallowisp) and Spirits to dig up a toolbox of creature enchantments, including the very saucy ability to cast [Armadillo Cloak](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Armadillo+Cloak) on Phantom creatures. The other two decks are the latest in Tsuyoshi Fujita tech, compositions always of interest to **Magic** players across the world.
