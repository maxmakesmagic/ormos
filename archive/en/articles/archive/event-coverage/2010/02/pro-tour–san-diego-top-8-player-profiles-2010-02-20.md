Pro Tour–San Diego Top 8 Player Profiles
========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 20, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast













![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/ptsd10/top8_scottvargas.jpg)

**Luis Scott-Vargas**


**Age:** 27  
**Hometown:**Oakland, California  
**Occupation:** Editor, channelfireball.com  
**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** 17?  
**How did you qualify for Pro Tour–San Diego?** Level 8  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 5 GP, 3 PT  
**Other previous Magic accomplishments:** U.S. National Champion 2007, Pro Tour–Berlin 2008 winner


**What was your record by format?**  
**Standard:** 10-0  
**Booster Draft:** 6-0


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour–San Diego? Who did you prepare with?**  

Tested live with PV, Gwalls, Brad Nelson, “The Boss” Tom Ross, Josh Utter-Leyton, and most of the qualified NorCal players


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**  

From 10-30 hours depending on what formats are current.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

Boss Naya. It’s either 50-50 or awesome versus everything but maybe u/w control


**Where do you play Friday Night Magic?**  

Superstars Game Center in San Jose, which is channelfireball.com’s headquarters


**Who taught you to play Magic and why?**  

I learned with a friend of mine back in ‘94


**What’s your favorite part of the Pro Tour coming to San Diego?**  

The location is just awesome, from the weather to the site to the restaurants.

**Niels Viaene**  
**Age:** 24  
**Hometown:**Gent, Belgium  
**Occupation:** Lab technician/research assistant  
**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:** 1  
**How did you qualify for Pro Tour–San Diego?**: Top 4, Grand Prix-Paris  
**Number of GP/PT Top 8s:** 1/1  

Other previous Magic accomplishments? Top 4 Grand Prix-Paris


**What was your record by format?**  
**Standard:** 7-2-1  
**Booster Draft:** 5-1


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour–San Diego? Who did you prepare with?**  

Testing and drafts with the other Belgians, siding with Mark Dictus and his goofy deck.


**How many hours do you spend on Magic Online in an average week?**  

I only just installed it but will be using it frequently in the days/weeks/months to come.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

Open Filigree: An Open the Vaults deck with Filigree Angel because it has solid to fair chances against everything but Bushwhacker/Mono-red.


**Where you do you play Friday Night Magic?**  

I play in Outpost Gent.


**Who taught you to play Magic and why?**  

Michiel T’jampens: he needed someone to play with after his brother wouldn’t anymore.


**What’s your favorite part of the Pro Tour coming to San Diego?**  

Love going to restaurants; the service and quality/quantity is surprising. Also the weather. Very nice to warm up a little in winter!
