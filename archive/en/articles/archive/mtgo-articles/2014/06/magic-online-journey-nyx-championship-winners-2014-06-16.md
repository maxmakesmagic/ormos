Magic Online Journey into Nyx Championship Winners
==================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on June 17, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_davidwhitfield.jpg)
By David Whitfield




 David works on the Magic community team as a content specialist. He spends his days writing about Magic Online and trying to play too many colors at once in Limited.

FilippoK



Thanks for the introductions, Iroas. Congratulations to the winners of the *Journey into Nyx* Championship! UglyCat was victorious in the Constructed Championship, while FilippoK claimed the title of Limited Champion!

Block Constructed Format Recap
[Top](#top)

**Block Constructed Game One**


The finals saw UglyCat's Red/Green/White ramp deck face off versus krom321's Black/Green/White [Eidolon of Blossoms](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eidolon+of+Blossoms) deck.


UglyCat started the first game with a [Sylvan Caryatid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Caryatid) into [Xenagos, the Reveler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos%2C+the+Reveler). The 2/2 Satyr was all that remained of [Xenagos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos) as krom321 cast a [Banishing Light](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banishing+Light).


![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150920165952im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/JOU_Const_Champ_Game_One_Turn_3-2.png)  

A well-timed [Banishing Light](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banishing+Light) saved krom321 from dealing with [Xenagos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos) early in the game. After dispatching the Planeswalker, krom321 was still overrun by a horde of non-Planeswalking Satyrs later in the game.
UglyCat built his mana with a second [Sylvan Caryatid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Caryatid) and a [Voyaging Satyr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyaging+Satyr) on the following turn, while krom321 brought out the powerful [Courser of Kruphix](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Courser+of+Kruphix). The mana ramp of UglyCat allowed him to cast a turn 5 [Revel of the Fallen God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Revel+of+the+Fallen+God), a turn 6 [Elspeth](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth) (killing krom321's just cast) [Doomwake Giant](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doomwake+Giant), and a second [Revel of the Fallen God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Revel+of+the+Fallen+God) on turn 7. The band of Satyrs frolicked all over krom321's life total to allow UglyCat to take game one.


**Block Constructed Game Two**


In the second game, Krom321 started off with a turn 3 [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) taking a [Banishing Light](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banishing+Light). UglyCat again ramped with a [Voyaging Satyr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyaging+Satyr) into a turn 3 [Xenagos, the Reveler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos%2C+the+Reveler). A [Brain Maggot](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brain+Maggot) took a second [Banishing Light](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Banishing+Light) from UglyCat. With only a second [Sylvan Caryatid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Caryatid), UglyCat attacked with two Satyrs and a [Voyaging Satyr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyaging+Satyr) taking krom321 down to 11.


With Krom321 missing a land play on turn 4, krom321 conceded when UglyCat cast a [Stormbreath Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormbreath+Dragon) and made a third Satyr.


![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150920214956im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/JOU_Const_Champ_Game_Two_Turn_5.png)  

UglyCat's mana ramp went off without a hitch, leaving him with [Xenagos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Xenagos), [Stormbreath Dragon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stormbreath+Dragon), [Voyaging Satyr](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voyaging+Satyr), [Sylvan Caryatid](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sylvan+Caryatid), three Satyrs, and the title of *Journey into Nyx* Block Constructed Champion by turn 5.
Congrats to UglyCat for winning the *Journey into Nyx* Block Constructed Championship!


You can view the brackets and the event details for the Constructed Championship [here](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/MagicOnlineTourn.aspx?x=mtg/digital/magiconline/tourn/7195038).

Limited Format Recap
[Top](#top)

The finals of the *Journey into Nyx* Limited Championship saw FilippoK's white-red aggro deck facing off versus BennyBoy2525's green-black Constellation deck.


**Limited Game One**


FilippoK came out fast in the first game with his first picks from *Journey into Nyx* and *Born of the Gods* in the first three turns of the game. The [Stonewise Fortifier](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stonewise+Fortifier) followed by [Brimaz, King of Oreskos](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brimaz%2C+King+of+Oreskos) were joined by an [Akroan Mastiff](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Mastiff) before BennyBoy2525 conceded game one of the finals without casting a single spell.


![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150920215044im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/JOU_Champ_Game_One_Screen_v2.png)  

FilippoK raised a sizeable army too quickly for BennyBoy2525 to have a chance at a comeback.
  
**Limited Game Two**


![](https://web.archive.org/web/20150923131351im_/http://www.wizards.com/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/JOU_Champ_Game_Two_Screen_3.png)  

With [Grim Guardian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Guardian+) [Chained to the Rocks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chained+to+the+Rocks),  

FilippoK had too strong of a lead, forcing BennyBoy2525 to concede.
While game two did not go much better for BennyBoy2525, he did manage to cast a few spells. A turn 2 [Golden Hind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Golden+Hind) traded with a [Oreskos Swiftclaw](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oreskos+Swiftclaw). FilippoK was missing land drops but did manage to cast a [Traveling Philosopher](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Traveling+Philosopher) and [Akroan Line Breaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Line+Breaker). BennyBoy2525 got to six mana and played a [Archetype of Finality](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archetype+of+Finality). The [Archetype](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Archetype) blocked and killed the [Akroan Line Breaker](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akroan+Line+Breaker) before dying to a final point of damage from [Forgeborn Oreads](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forgeborn+Oreads). The final blow of the match came when BennyBoy2525 cast a [Grim Guardian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Guardian) and enchanted it with [Nyx Infusion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nyx+Infusion). FilippoK saw fit to [Chained to the Rocks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chained+to+the+Rocks) the [Grim Guardian](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grim+Guardian) at which point BennyBoy2525 conceded the match.




Congratulations to FilippoK for winning the title of *Journey into Nyx* Champion!
