Top 8 Players of Grand Prix Lyon 2015
=====================================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX LYON 2015](/en/events/coverage/gplyo15)
 on November 1, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_FrankKarsten.jpg)
By Frank Karsten











After 15 rounds, with a total of 1,944 others eliminated, only eight players remained in the running for the fame and the fortune, the title and the trophy. Find out more about these people here!




---







![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplyo15/t8_neirynck.jpg)


### Branco Neirynck



**Age:** 25  
**Occupation:** *Magic Online* grinder  
**Hometown:** Bovekerke, Belgium  
**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**  Top 8 at Grand Prix Utrecht 2015 and Grand Prix Copenhagen 2015. 2009 national champion, 2015 World *Magic* Cup team captain.




**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Murk Strider](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Murk+Strider), blue-red, 9-0.




**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

 Black/Green Tokens with two [Swarm Surge](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Swarm+Surge)s and four [Eyeless Watcher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Eyeless+Watcher)s. 2-1.




**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Akoum Firebird](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Akoum+Firebird), blue-red, 2-0-1.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplyo15/t8_seibold.jpg)


### Christian Seibold



**Age:** 26  
**Occupation:** Student  
**Hometown:** Nuremberg, Germany  
**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** Pro Tour *Born of the Gods* Top 8, Grand Prix Barcelona winner, Grand Prix Utrecht semifinalist, Grand Prix Stockholm Top 8.




**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

 8-1, blue-black. [Clutch of Currents](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Clutch+of+Currents) was actually the best card in my deck.




**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Rolling Thunder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rolling+Thunder), red-black, 3-0.




**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Ob Nixilis Reignited](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ob+Nixilis+Reignited), red-black, 2-0-1.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplyo15/t8_saito.jpg)


### Tomoharu Saito



**Age:** 31  
**Occupation:** Hareruya CEO and pro player  
**Hometown:** Narita, Chiba, Japan  
**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**  Pro Tour winner, four-time Grand Prix winner, and more.




**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Ulamog, the Ceaseless Hunger](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ulamog%2C+the+Ceaseless+Hunger) and [Valakut Invoker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Valakut+Invoker). Blue/Black/Red Control and Red/White Beatdown, switch style! 8-1.




**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Grip of Desolation](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grip+of+Desolation), white-black, 3-0.




**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Ondu Rising](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ondu+Rising) and [Drowner of Hope](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Drowner+of+Hope), green-white-blue, 2-1.

![](https://media.wizards.com/2015/events/gplyo15/t8_reiter.jpg)


### Florian Reiter



**Age:** 28  
**Occupation:** Writer, editor, PR dude  
**Hometown:** Munich, Germany  
**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:** Member of Team #GPWhatever. Not getting a haircut before this.




**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

 I played blue-white to an 8-1 finish. Best cards were Quarantine field and [Oblivion Sower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Oblivion+Sower), shockingly.




**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

 I played Blue/Red Devoid, went 2-1. Best card was the splashed [Dominator Drone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dominator+Drone), though everyone disagrees with me on this.




**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  

 Black/Red Devoid Aggro. Best cards were my two copies of [Forerunner of Slaughter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Forerunner+of+Slaughter), although quadruple [Nettle Drone](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nettle+Drone) was helpful too.

### Vincent Lemoine



**Age:** 37  
**Occupation:** Travel agent  
**Hometown:** Brussels, Belgium  
**Previous *Magic* accomplishments:**  Pro Tour Paris Top 8, Grand Prix Athens finalist, four-time Belgian national champ, team finalist at Worlds in San Francisco, Top 8 at the *Magic* World Cup in Amsterdam.




**What was the best card in your sealed deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Resolute Blademaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Resolute+Blademaster), 9-0. White-red, but played white-blue after sideboarding a lot.




**What was the best card in your first draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Serpentine Spike](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Serpentine+Spike), blue-red, 2-1.




**What was the best card in your second draft deck, what color combination did you play, and what was your record?**  
[Tajuru Warcaller](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tajuru+Warcaller), green-white, 2-1.
