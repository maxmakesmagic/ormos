Round 15: Carlos Eduardo Romao vs. Peter Myrvig
===============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on August 8, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds03/a730.jpg)The cream is certainly rising to the top as we near the end of the individual swiss rounds. With just four rounds of play remaining, former World Champion Carlos Romao had to face Pro Tour – Houston Top 8er Peter Myrvig. Romao was with the much-hyped [Mind's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind%27s+Desire), and Myrvig was with its nemesis Sligh.

### Game 1

Myrvig won the die roll, something that always makes a Goblin player smile, and led off with [Raging Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin). Romao played a [Flooded Strand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flooded+Strand) and accepted more beats before getting an island. Myrvig added a [Goblin Piledriver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Piledriver). Romao played his second island and considered the [Snap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snap) in his hand, but then played [Sapphire Medallion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sapphire+Medallion). Myrvig made him pay with a second [Raging Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin), dropping Romao to just ten life. [Wasteland](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland) and [Seal of Fire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Fire) further complicated Romao's plans.

He drew his card and sighed. He looked at a hand of [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), [Snap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snap), [Mind's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind%27s+Desire), [Sapphire Medallion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sapphire+Medallion), [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), [Merchant Scroll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merchant+Scroll) and [Ancient Tomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb). He thought hard before playing his [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm). It turned up another [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm), [Flooded Strand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flooded+Strand), and [Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge). The Knowledge and Tomb headed back on top of his library. He played the Strand and fetched out an island. Myrvig looked thoroughly bored, but it was probably just the oppressive heat getting to him. The table was thronged with spectators.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds03/a739.jpg)Romao deliberately tapped an island and laid [Snap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snap) on the table, getting rid of one of the [Raging Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin)s. Then he [Merchant Scroll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merchant+Scroll)ed, and then [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed in response, getting another [Snap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snap) and looking at [Ancient Tomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb) and [Sapphire Medallion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sapphire+Medallion) before putting them back. His Scroll got him a [Frantic Search](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frantic+Search) and he passed it back to Myrvig with one island up.

Myrvig tapped three and played [Goblin Warchief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Warchief), then thought and added his [Raging Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin) and swung in. Romao [Snap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snap)ped the Warchief then Wished for a [Stifle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stifle) for the Piledriver's Ability. He fell to six. His back was against the wall. He untapped and drew. [Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) was waiting for him. He cast it for a blue and drew a Frantic search! With the [City of Traitors](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=City+of+Traitors) in his hand, he was able to Search away two lands and play a [Mind's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind%27s+Desire) for three.

The cards came down: [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition), [Ancient Tomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb), [Mind's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind%27s+Desire). It was a coup. Romao [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition)ed three islands out of his deck and Desired for five more. This time, the spells were [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition), [Cloud of Faeries](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloud+of+Faeries), [Frantic Search](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frantic+Search) and two lands. The second [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) got him [Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge)s, and he Searched up another Search while ditching two lands. The Fairies gave him some extra mana, and he drew four and then Searched again. Two more Fairies gave him still more mana, and then he [Merchant Scroll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merchant+Scroll)ed up a [Meditate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meditate). [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) got [Turnabout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turnabout), and he [Meditate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meditate)d, [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed, and finally had enough spells cast to fetch [Brain Freeze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brain+Freeze) and put all of Myrvig's library in the graveyard.

Myrvig untapped and looked at his hand: Lackey and [Lava Dart](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lava+Dart). With one more Dart milled away and the Seal on board he could deal five, one short of killing Romao. The kicker? His deck was supposed to have a third Dart in it, but he couldn't find it the night before.

Romao 1 – Myrvig 0

### Game 2

Myrvig had to send back his first two hands, staying on a grip of three mountains, [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander) and [Mogg Flunkies](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mogg+Flunkies). Far from excellent. Romao only had to go down to six. While Myrvig got his Flunkies into play, Romao [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed and fetched an island with [Flooded Strand](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Flooded+Strand), then [Merchant Scroll](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Merchant+Scroll)ed up a [Frantic Search](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frantic+Search). Myrvig's [Goblin Lackey](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Lackey) was late to the party. Romao [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm)ed again to get another fetchland.

Finally Myrvig was free to swing, and a [Raging Goblin](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raging+Goblin) made sure his Flunkies would get to attack. Romao did not use his [Chain of Vapor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+of+Vapor), and was punished when [Siege-Gang Commander](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege-Gang+Commander) entered play. Worse, Myrvig had a [Wasteland](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland) to frustrate any potential Cities of Traitors. Romao played an [Ancient Tomb](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ancient+Tomb) and used his [Chain of Vapor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+of+Vapor) to send the Commander home. Unfortunately, his life total was dwindling. Myrvig Wasted the Tomb and swung in, bringing Romao low. Unable to deal with the horde of monsters, Romao succumbed.

Romao 1 – Myrvig 1

### Game 3

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/worlds03/a738.jpg)Just nine minutes remained for the decisive third game, which was good news for Myrvig's goblins. Better still, Romao mulliganed and Myrvig had a Lackey on the first turn. On the fourth turn Romao was facing some serious pain from a horde across the table. Lucky for him [Mind's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind%27s+Desire) can do some ridiculous things.

He started out with a small burst of spells, netting mana from [Cloud of Faeries](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloud+of+Faeries) and [Frantic Search](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frantic+Search). A couple spells later he was busting out a [Mind's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind%27s+Desire) for five. He didn't get the degenerate second [Mind's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind%27s+Desire), but he did have a [Frantic Search](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Frantic+Search) and a [Snap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snap), as well as a [Brainstorm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brainstorm). He drew into a second [Mind's Desire](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mind%27s+Desire) and fired it off, this time for twelve. The first card was [Turnabout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Turnabout), signaling the end was very probably near, but then came the [Tendrils of Agony](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tendrils+of+Agony), and it was over.

**Final Result:** Carlos Romao defeats Peter Myrvig 2-1
