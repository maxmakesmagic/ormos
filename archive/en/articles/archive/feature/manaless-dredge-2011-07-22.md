Looking for a Legacy deck that doesn't require [Force of Will](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+of+Will) or [Wasteland](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wasteland)? Nicholas Rausch has you covered, and his deck is a proven winner! Manaless Dredge, a Dredge deck for Legacy that doesn't need mana to operate, allows Nicholas to fill up his graveyard without ever having to tap mana! From there he can beatdown with [Ichorid](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ichorid) or use [Bridge from Below](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bridge+from+Below) to swarm his opponents to submission. Read more about Nicholas's winning deck in the [official coverage](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/22243_The_StarCityGamescom_Open_Series_is_Coming_to_Cincinnati_on_July_1617.html) of the StarCityGames.com Open in Cincinnati.
