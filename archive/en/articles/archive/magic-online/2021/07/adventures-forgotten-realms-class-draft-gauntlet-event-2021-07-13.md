Adventures in the Forgotten Realms Class Draft Gauntlet Event
=============================================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on July 14, 2021 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_david_mcdarby.jpg)
By David McDarby











Welcome to the *Dungeons & Dragons: Adventures in the Forgotten Realms* Class Draft Gauntlet! From July 14 through July 21, this new type of *Magic Online* event offers you a choice from twelve different preconstructed decks built around each Class enchantment and compete against other players in a League!


![](https://media.wizards.com/2021/images/daily/tgxLN6WTIw.jpg)


Read on for event details, philosophy, and decklists.


Event Details
=============


*Adventures in the Forgotten Realms* Class Draft Gauntlet
---------------------------------------------------------


**Dates**: July 14–21


**Format**: A league of up to three Best-of-One, single game rounds.


**Phantom**: Yes


**Location**: In the Limited Play Lobby, Gauntlet Section


**Entry**: 600 Play Points or 6 Event Tickets


**Prizes:**





|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| 3 wins | 90 Play Points and 1 Treasure Chest |
| 2 wins | 60 Play Points |
| 1 win | 30 Play Points |


Choose Your Character
=====================


In previous Gauntlets on *Magic Online*, you played your matches against other players with randomly distributed decks. However now, thanks to the awesome team behind *Magic Online*, you can play with a deck of your choosing! This lets us feature all kinds of formats in the future using *Magic Online*’s large card pool, from Standard decks throughout the years, to Commander, and even to new formats such as this one.


We wanted to find a way to highlight the new cards in *Adventures in the Forgotten Realms*, while also leveraging *Magic Online*’s strengths—namely its massive card pool. Creating twelve unique decklists, each with their own playstyles and featuring the twelve Class enchantments from the set, highlights this newfound *Magic Online* agency by mirroring one of the very first choices you make when playing *Dungeons & Dragons*: choosing your class!


We’re always looking for ways to bring you different kinds of sweet experiences, and we hope this new way to play *Magic* brings you joy. You can always let us know your thoughts by emailing [magiconlinefeedback@wizards.com](mailto:magiconlinefeedback@wizards.com) or mentioning [@MagicOnline](https://twitter.com/MagicOnline) on Twitter.


Thank you for sticking with us, and may you find the class that resonates the most with you!
