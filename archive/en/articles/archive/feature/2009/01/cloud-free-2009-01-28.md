U.S National Champion Michael Jacob is fresh off a team championship at the 2008 World Championships. His follow-up act? Top 8ing Grand Prix-Los Angeles! He used an innovative Black-Green control deck similar to the Death Cloud decks that have become very popular on the Pro Tour Qualifier-Honolulu circuit. One key difference? He cut [Death Cloud](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death+Cloud)! For all the official coverage of Grand Prix-Los Angeles, [visit here](/en/events/coverage/luis-scott-vargas-conquers-la). To find out how to get to an Extended Pro Tour Qualifier for Honolulu near you, visit the [official PTQ page](/en/node/640716).
