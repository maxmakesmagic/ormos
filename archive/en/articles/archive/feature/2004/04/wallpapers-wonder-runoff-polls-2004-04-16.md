Wallpapers of Wonder Runoff Polls
=================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 16, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![Wallpapers of Wonder](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/console/icon_wallpapersofwonder.jpg)The nominations for [Wallpapers of Wonder](/en/articles/archive/wallpapers-wonder-2004-04-05) are in and counted, and the runoff polls are ready to go! Thanks to everyone who nominated cards to become desktop wallpapers, and a rousing "Bravo" to the diligent WizOs who waded through the nomination threads to count them up.


*You* now get to vote on which of the top ten most-nominated cards of each color will become desktop wallpapers.


So how do I vote in the runoff polls?
Go to the [magicthegathering.com message boards forum](http://boards1.wizards.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=481) and vote in the eight Wallpapers of Wonder runoff threads. If you don't already have a username on the message boards, you'll have to [register](http://boards1.wizards.com/register.php?s=&action=signup) there before you can vote.


### What cards will I be choosing from?


Here are the top ten nominated cards from each color. They are ordered alphabetically, not by rank. Check out all the pictures by clicking the links here, then head to the [boards](http://boards1.wizards.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=481) and vote for your favorite in each color!
