*Scars of Mirrodin* brought with it the first Affiliated tournaments. For the uninitiated, affiliated decks feature a minimum of ten cards watermarked as either Phyrexian or Mirran, and no cards from the other faction. In honor of Mirrodin Week we bring you one such deck list from the Mirrodin native's side of the faction chart. Jeremiah Edwards used it to win the 2010 Idaho State Championship.
