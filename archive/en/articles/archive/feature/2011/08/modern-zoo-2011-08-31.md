This weekend is Pro Tour Philadelphia, and the Constructed format on display will be the brand-new Modern. Players on **Magic Online** are already giving the format the college try, and Nemesisjoe racked up a 4-0 performance using an industry standard: Zoo. Zoo use the world's most flexible mana base to beat down with the most powerful creatures in red, white, and green backed by top notch burn. In this Modern take, the digital deckbuilder squeezed in just enough Swamps and Islands to play [Dark Confidant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dark+Confidant) and [Tribal Flames](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tribal+Flames). Find out more about the Modern format this weekend as we bring you live coverage of [Pro Tour Philadelphia](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/magicweekend/philadelphia11)!
