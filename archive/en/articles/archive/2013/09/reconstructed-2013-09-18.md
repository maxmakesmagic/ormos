RECONSTRUCTED
=============



 Posted in [NEWS](/en/articles)
 on September 18, 2013 










Give your deck some expert tuning. Each week, former Pro Tour competitor and R&D member Gavin Verhey takes an in-depth look at a reader’s deck, analyzing each card and offering ideas to tweak and rebuild it for maximum impact. Submit your deck for a chance to be featured and reviewed!
