In honor of the start of the Pro Tour Qualifier-Honolulu season, Daily Decks is bringing you some of the hottest Extended decks all week long! Former Grand Prix Top 8 competitor Ben Wienburg caught a "pre-season" PTQ for Honolulu at Worlds 2008, and managed to win with a deck many players had written off: Affinity. He made some dramatic innovations to the deck to get there, cutting longtime stalwart [Thoughtcast](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtcast) for [Ethersworn Canonist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ethersworn+Canonist) to keep up with Elves and Storm. The most interesting card from the list? [Krark-Clan Shaman](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Krark-Clan+Shaman) in the sideboard; it's a perfect means of turning Affinity's artifact lands into Wraths against Elves! To find an Extended PTQ for Honolulu near you, visit [this link](/en/node/640716).
