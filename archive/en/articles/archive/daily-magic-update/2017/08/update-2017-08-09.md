Deck of the Day
===============


1. Tribal Vampires Commander

Choices for quality Vampire commanders have been few and far between. [Vish Kal, Blood Arbiter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vish+Kal%2C+Blood+Arbiter) is pretty popular and quite Vampiric, but not tribal in any way. [Baron Sengir](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Baron+Sengir) scores you tons of cool points and does actually say Vampire in its text box, but it's a bit weak. Anowon, the Sage Ruin is cool, but pretty fragile. [Garza Zol, Plague Queen](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Garza+Zol%2C+Plague+Queen) also exists.


But [Olivia Voldaren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Olivia+Voldaren) is quite Vampiric in her ways, even if she isn't exactly tribal. Tappedout user [Ogrenoah](http://tappedout.net/users/ogrenoah/) created an [Olivia Voldaren](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Olivia+Voldaren) Commander deck that's all Vampires, all the time. But it could certainly benefit from the more Vampire-centric cards in *Commander (2017 Edition)*. Still, not a bad place to start.
