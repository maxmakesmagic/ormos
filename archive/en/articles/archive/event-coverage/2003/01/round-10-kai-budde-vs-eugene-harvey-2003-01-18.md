Round 10: Kai Budde vs. Eugene Harvey
=====================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 18, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/generic-avatar-150_626.png)
By Victor van den Broek











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a791.jpg)In the Phoenix Foundation vs. the World Feature Match round, this was the match between the best player in the world and the hottest player in the tournament. It would prove to be a good one. As both were 2-0 today, this match was also the one to decide who won the draft on table one. 

### Game 1

Kai won the toss and got a third turn [Mistform Wall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Wall). Both players got a morphed creature on their next turn.

"I wish I had a Skill..." – Kai

[Wirewood Savage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Savage) was up next for Eugene. Kai enchanted his Wall with a [Lavamancer's Skill](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavamancer%27s+Skill) and shot the Savage.

"I guess drawing the Skill works." – Kai

Eugene's morph came in but so did Kai's. Kai's morph was a [Quicksilver Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Quicksilver+Dragon) though. In the meanwhile Kai's skilled Wall was shooting down all of Eugene's morphs and the German added a [Sage Aven](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sage+Aven) to his side of the board. After that, it was time for Eugene to scoop up and start sideboarding. 

Kai 1 – Eugene 0

### Game 2

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a794.jpg)Eugene played a third turn [Wirewood Savage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wirewood+Savage) this time around, only to have it burned down by a [Chain of Plasma](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+of+Plasma). Kai only made a morph on his turn and Eugene answered it with a [Dive Bomber](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dive+Bomber). A [Choking Tethers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Choking+Tethers) tapped it down though and Kai turned his [Disruptive Pitmage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disruptive+Pitmage) face up so Eugene's morph cost 4 instead of 3. The Pitmage was enchanted by a [Lavamancer's Skill](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavamancer%27s+Skill) next. Eugene played [Improvised Armor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Improvised+Armor) on his [Dive Bomber](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dive+Bomber) with one green mana open. Kai shot it with the Pitmage but the [Dive Bomber](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dive+Bomber) recieved a [Vitality Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vitality+Charm).

The Pitmage got its revenge next turn on Eugene's other morph, a morphed [Silent Specter](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Silent+Specter) and Eugene made a [Mobilization](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mobilization) to keep the forces coming. A [Callous Oppressor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Callous+Oppressor) by Kai however made any non-soldiers by Eugene a lot less effective. The Bomber hit again, bringing the German down to 5 life. [Butcher Orgg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher+Orgg) was up next for the German, looking to finish off some soldiers on Eugene's side of the table. [Piety Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Piety+Charm) on the [Dive Bomber](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dive+Bomber) finished Kai off though.

Kai 1 – Eugene 1

### Game 3

Two [Birchlore Rangers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birchlore+Rangers) for Eugene started the beats early on in this game. Kai's [Mistform Wall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Wall) stopped that, but a third turn [Dive Bomber](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dive+Bomber) got Eugene some evasion. [Lavamancer's Skill](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavamancer%27s+Skill) ended those again. The Skill got [Piety Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Piety+Charm)ed and [Mobilization](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mobilization) gave Eugene a way to make many 1/1's. A [Battering Craghorn](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Battering+Craghorn) and [Callous Oppressor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Callous+Oppressor) were summoned by Kai to complicate the situation a bit. One of the soldiers received Improvsed armor and a [Primal Boost](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Primal+Boost) on top of that made sure Kai's [Mistform Wall](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Wall) didn't survive combat.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a792.jpg)He stole one of the [Birchlore Rangers](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birchlore+Rangers) during Eugene's upkeep. [Gustcloak Harrier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gustcloak+Harrier) came down to beat down through the sky. Kai's Craghorn came in once again and Kai's [Snapping Thragg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snapping+Thragg) got Skilled up. He also played [Chain of Plasma](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chain+of+Plasma) on the [Gustcloak Harrier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gustcloak+Harrier). With both players at 14 life, the game looked under control for Kai. He stole the Rangers and attacked with the Craghorn again. Eugene made some more tokens and the two tokens along with the Armored token killed it. Kai attempted an [Erratic Explosion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Erratic+Explosion) on it. Eugene pumped its toughness by one because of a [Vitality Charm](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vitality+Charm), but the Explosion hit for 4. [Butcher Orgg](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher+Orgg) hit play on Kai's next turn and that was game. Kai's long distance squad kept on slaughtering all of Eugene's soldiers and his creatures came through unhindered. 

**Final Result:** Kai 2 – Eugene 1
