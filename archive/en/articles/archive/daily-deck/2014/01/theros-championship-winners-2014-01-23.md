Theros Championship Winners
===========================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on January 27, 2014 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Clayton Kroh











![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/digital/magiconline/header_ths_champs.png)

|  |  |  |

Congratulations to the *Theros*  Championship Winners!Player **stainerson** claimed the winner's title in the Constructed competition. Game one saw stainerson's mono-black devotion deck face off against jp0822's black/white midrange deck. jp0822 took the early advantage as his [Sin Collector](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sin+Collector) and kill spells gave him a strong board position. stainerson fought back with multiple removal spells of his own, while attacking with a [Nightveil Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nightveil+Specter) followed by a [Desecration Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desecration+Demon). jp0822 eventually found a [Detention Sphere](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Detention+Sphere) to neutralize the Demon, but stainerson's [Underworld Connection](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underworld+Connection) generated additional threats which overwhelmed jp0822's removal attempts to win game one. 

Game two saw stainerson's [Thoughtseize](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) take jp0822's [Obzedat, Ghost Council](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Obzedat%2C+Ghost+Council), and then [Pharika's Cure](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pharika%27s+Cure) killed a [Lyev Skyknight](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lyev+Skyknight). stainerson played a turn-four [Underworld Connections](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Underworld+Connections), which fueled a barrage of discard and removal spells. stainerson's first [Desecration Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desecration+Demon) was killed by jp0822's [Ultimate Price](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ultimate+Price), but his second [Desecration Demon](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Desecration+Demon) earned stainerson the victory in the *Theros*  Constructed Championship! 

Check out [how the Constructed match-ups played out](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/MagicOnlineTourn.aspx?x=mtg/digital/magiconline/tourn/6622303) from quarterfinals to the finals, and [stainerson's winning decklist](#constructed). 

Player **bobbie1706** battled to the top position in the Limited format. In game one, bobbie1706's black/white deck created a 4/3 flier with a [Scourgemark](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Scourgemark) on a [Wingsteed Rider](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingsteed+Rider). RMVP tried a [Lightning Strike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Strike) to kill the Rider, but bobbie1706 cast a [Dauntless Onslaught](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dauntless+Onslaught) to grow the Rider. RMVP was out of answers and the [Wingsteed Rider](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingsteed+Rider) took game one. 

In game two, bobbie1706 started off strong with plays on turns two through five. RMVP cast a [Stymied Hopes](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stymied+Hopes) on bobbie1706's turn-two [Setessan Battle Priest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Setessan+Battle+Priest). Bobbie1706 followed up with a [Blood-Toll Harpy](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood-Toll+Harpy), a [Wingsteed Rider](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingsteed+Rider) and a [Gray Merchant of Asphodel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gray+Merchant+of+Asphodel). With RMVP stalled on three lands, bobbie1706 took the game and the match with a [Cutthroat Maneuver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cutthroat+Maneuver) to win the *Theros*  Limited Championship! 

Check out [how the Limited match-ups played out](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Digital/MagicOnlineTourn.aspx?x=mtg/digital/magiconline/tourn/6654004) from quarterfinals to the finals, and [bobbie1706's winning decklist](#limited). 

Who will claim the titles in the **Magic Online** *Born of the Gods*  Championships? One could be you! Hone your skills during the [**Magic Online** *Born of the Gods*  Prerelease and Release Events](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/01132014), which begin February 14. Then, challenge your fellow players in the *Born of the Gods*  Championship to take your place in the pantheon of champions!
