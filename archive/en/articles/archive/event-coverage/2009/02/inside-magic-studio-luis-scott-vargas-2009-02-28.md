Inside the Magic Studio: Luis Scott-Vargas
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 28, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











On the eve of Pro Tour-Kyoto, Richard Hagon sat down with Luis Scott-Vargas to discuss the American player's amazing run over the last few months that has included a Pro Tour win and two Grand Prix victories. Tune in to this 15-minute in-depth interview to get to know the hottest **Magic** player on the planet.
