Kaleidoscope is a fun Magic alternative available on Magic Online in which players must use decks where all nonland cards are multicolored! TheUsualSuspect took this list to first place during a recent Premier Event featuring the format. A key to the deckâ€™s success? Cascade! [Bloodbraid Elf](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bloodbraid+Elf) and [Enlisted Wurm](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enlisted+Wurm) make sure the best spells are always hitting the table. End result? A Kaleidoscope championship!
