8-0 Decks of Grand Prix Copenhagen 2018
=======================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 10, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)
By Tobi Henke











When the dust had settled at the end of the first day here at Grand Prix Copenhagen, only seven players had made it through with a perfect record of 8-0 still intact: Jakob Kirkebjerg, Christian Aahlstroem, Love Rask, (10) Javier Dominguez, Markku Anttila, Lukas Blohon (pictured below, left to right) as well as Tobias Maurer (not pictured).


![](https://media.wizards.com/2018/events/gpcop18/gpcop18_80players.jpg)


Although Chainwhirlers could be found in four, the decks these seven ran offered quite a bit of variety. Blue-Black Midrange, Esper Control, and White-Blue Approach all went undefeated, as did Mono-Red Aggro and three copies of Red-Black Chainwhirler.
