2006 Magic Invitational Auction of the Geniuses
===============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on April 27, 2006 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/magic/images/tournamentcenter/2006/invitational/banner2.jpg)For this year's **Magic** Invitational, Wizards of the Coast recruited 18 celebrity deck designers to participate in the "Auction of the Geniuses." They were given the following rules: 




1. Format is Online Classic with Vanguard.  
 2. Each designer was assigned an Avatar.  
 3. No sideboarding during play is permitted.  
 4. Deck may only have sideboard if 1 of the 5 wishes is present in the deck. In this case, the sideboard may ONLY be used with the wishes.   
 5. The players bid on decks based on their starting life totals and hand size. Each player starts with a base of 8/25, and the starting bid is modified by the stats of the Avatar associated with the deck up for bid. 
The 2006 **Magic** Invitational runs May 10-12. Tune in to **magicthegathering.com**  and ***Magic**  Online*  to follow all the matches.
