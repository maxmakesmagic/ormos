Round Eight Feature Match:
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 24, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpoka04/r8fm1en1.jpg)### Game 1

Okamoto mulliganed down to six. On turn two he played [Fertile Ground](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fertile+Ground), and he then used [Living Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+Wish) to get [Argothian Enchantress](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Argothian+Enchantress), but was none too eager to cast it into Nakamura’s untapped islands. On end step, Nakamura cast [Intuition](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) to get three [Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge), and sat back on his mana. Okamoto’s Enchantress was [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell)ed, and [Words of Wind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Words+of+Wind) was [Force Spike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+Spike)d. On Okamoto’s next turn, Nakamura responded to Enchantress’ Presence with [Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge), but didn’t draw a counterspell. [Words of Wind](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Words+of+Wind) followed, and this time Nakamura could stop it with [Mana Leak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak). He untapped and summoned [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), putting a bit of pressure on Okamoto. [Trade Rout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trade+Rout)es provided a good resource and drew Okamoto another card, and Nakamura responded to [Seal of Removal](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seal+of+Removal) with [Accumulated Knowledge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Accumulated+Knowledge) for four. The Seal resolved, giving Okamoto some [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog) protection. [Fertile Ground](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fertile+Ground) resolved as well, and [Living Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Living+Wish) searched out [Raven Familiar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raven+Familiar), which came right into play. The ‘Tog was chump blocked by the Familiar, which bounced back to Okamoto’s hand thanks to the Seal. Facing down a lot of trouble, Nakamura cast [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) to get [Mana Short](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Short), leaving one mana open. 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/gpoka04/r8fm1en2.jpg)[Trade Rout](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trade+Rout)es allowed Okamoto to cycle some lands, but [Mana Short](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Short) slowed down his production. Nakamura attacked with his [Psychatog](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Psychatog), and played a second. Despite all the cards he drew, Nakamura was oddly in no position to put up much resistance. Okamoto summoned an Enchantress and started playing enchantments, as Nakamura made a “Yeah yeah, okay, hurry up” motion with his hand. After Okamoto unloaded a ton of enchantments and drew plenty of cards, he ended his turn. Nakamura played [Fact or Fiction](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fact+or+Fiction) on end step, and Okamoto presented piles of [Force Spike](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Force+Spike), [Cunning Wish](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), [Counterspell](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterspell) and [Circular Logic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Circular+Logic), [Mana Leak](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mana+Leak)- Nakamura took the three card pile. This gave him some answers, but he was ill-equipped to deal with all the ammo Okamoto had accumulated. He unloaded all of it to draw plenty of cards and produce lots of mana, and [Ambassador Laquatus](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ambassador+Laquatus) convinced Nakamura to scoop.

Nakamura- 0 Okamoto- 1
