Decks of the Week: 5/16/2014
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on May 16, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Warsaw played host to the first [Block Limited Grand Prix](/en/events/coverage/anteri-completes-his-3-2-1-warsaw) featuring all three sets from the *Theros* block. After finishing 3rd and 2nd in two European Grand Prix, Venezuelan Fabrizio Anteri got his win in Poland. Meanwhile, here in North America, Jun Young Park worked his way through an impressive Top 8 to win Modern [Grand Prix Minneapolis](/en/events/coverage/parks-great-scape-minneapolis). Pro-level play hits its apex this weekend with Pro Tour *Journey into Nyx*! There will be [live coverage](http://www.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/journeyintonyx14#viewer) all weekend. And if you dream of Pro Tour glory yourself, head to a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) for Pro Tour ***Magic** 2015* near you. Find one with [this handy link](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#m15). Good luck; have fun!
