Pro Tour Dark Ascension Winner
==============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 13, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Congratulations to Pro Tour Hall of Famer Brian Kibler, your Pro Tour *Dark Ascension* champion. Kibler and his Wolf Run Ramp deck defeated Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa in a five-game final to earn him his second Pro Tour title. In the semifinals, Kibler played one of the most memorable matches in **Magic** history when he beat Pro Tour Hall of Famer Jon Finkel in five incredibly tense games. Complete coverage of Pro Tour *Dark Ascension* can be found [here](/en/events/coverage/kibler-triumphs-epic-pro-tour).
