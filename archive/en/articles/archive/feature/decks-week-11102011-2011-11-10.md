Decks of the Week: 11/10/2011
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 10, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Another group of players punched their tickets for Pro Tour *Dark Ascension* by winning Pro Tour Qualifiers in the *Innistrad* Limited format over the weekend. Check out the lists for emerging draft archetypes and see if the strategies hold up at this weekend's [Grand Prix San Diego](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/grandprix/sandiego11) and the [2011 **Magic** World Championships](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=events/magic/worlds) on November 17-20.
