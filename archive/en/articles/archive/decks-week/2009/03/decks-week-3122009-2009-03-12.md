Decks of the Week: 3/12/2009
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on March 12, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Just one week removed from winning Pro Tour–Kyoto, Gabriel Nassif has [won Grand Prix–Chicago](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/coverage-grand-prix-chicago-2009-03-07)! The format was Legacy, and what a doozy it was! Nassif won using an innovative [Counterbalance](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Counterbalance)/[Sensei's Divining Top](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sensei%27s+Divining+Top+)  control deck that had a 15 one-of sideboard strategy. His victory came in the largest Grand Prix in North American history, with more than 1,200 players showing up to compete. Make sure to check out the many deck lists from the event below.


PTQ results for Honolulu keep pouring right in, and we have another healthy serving of them this week. Check out what players from Nebraska, Indiana, Nevada, Texas, and more are using to earn their plane tickets to Honolulu. And, if you're interested in finding out where a PTQ for Honolulu near you can be found, check out [the official PTQ schedule](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/protour/honolulu09-qualifiers) .


And of course, it wouldn't be Decks of the Week without the latest **Magic Online**  decks. Whether the format is Standard, Extended, *Shards of Alara*  Block, or Classic, we've got the lists! So, what are you waiting for? Get reading!
