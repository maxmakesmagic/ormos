While most players were brewing up Big Red, giving Karn a whirl, or just settling on [Tempered Steel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Tempered+Steel) as the consensus best deck, Gaudenis Vidugiris packed something much more unusual for [Pro Tour Nagoya](/en/events/coverage/sharfman-masters-magic-weekend-nagoya): Mono-Black Infect. With a heavy removal package, scary infect creatures, and a few ways to pump them up, Gaudenis sported one of the eight highest Block Constructed records—a feat that helped propel him into the Top 8 of the tournament as well!
