Decks of the Week: 6/21/2013
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 21, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Shahar Shenhar showed he's one to watch with his third Grand Prix win at last weekend's [Grand Prix Houston](/en/events/coverage/shenhar-punishes-his-enemies-houston). With that win, Shahar will be in Dublin this October to compete at Pro Tour *Theros*. Would you like to join him there? One way to do it is to win a Pro Tour Qualifier! The current PTQ season uses the Standard format, so check out these Top 8 Standard decks from recent events and find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](http://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#theros). Good luck; have fun!
