White Weenie saw a resurgence in play during the *Lorwyn/Shadowmoor* Block PTQ season, but Argentinean Adrian Saredo felt the success train didn’t need to stop there. He managed to take his Standard list all the way to the title at the 2008 Argentina National Championships. No stranger to the tournament, this was Adrian’s tenth time competing, and his fourth time in the Top 8! He credited his maindeck [Kinsbaile Borderguard](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kinsbaile+Borderguard)s with helping him shore up matchups against decks with board sweepers like [Wrath of God](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wrath+of+God) and [Damnation](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Damnation). You can read an interview with Adrian in [this edition](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/twtw/3) of Brian David-Marshall’s column [The Week That Was](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Archive.aspx?tag=The%20Week%20That%20Was&description=The%20Week%20That%20Was).
