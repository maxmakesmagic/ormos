Wallpaper of the Week: Command Tower
====================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 10, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











This week's wallpaper features Ryan Yee's [Command Tower](/en/articles/archive/magic-gathering-commander-card-image-gallery-2011-05-25) from [***Magic: The Gathering** Commander*](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/productarticle.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/commander/productinfo). Command Tower is in all five of the decks being released on June 17, with [Launch Parties](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/events.aspx?x=mtg/event/commander-facts) going on all weekend. All the way to Monday in some locations! Enjoy.
