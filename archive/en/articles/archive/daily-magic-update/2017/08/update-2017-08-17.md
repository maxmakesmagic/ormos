Deck of the Day
===============


1. Temur Tower

Thanks to *Magic Online* player Danker, we have the triumphant return of Temur Tower! And it looks . . . pretty much the same.


[Abrade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrade) is an upgrade, but otherwise this deck is pretty much exactly the same—down to the single [Brutal Expulsion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Brutal+Expulsion)—as it was when it was very, very nearly a tier 1 deck during the Copy Cat era. [Shielded Aether Thief](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shielded+Aether+Thief) is particularly neat against Ramunap Red, as the flash ability means it can come down *after* the bevy of "can't block" abilities have been triggered—giving you more of that sweet, sweet energy.


Basing a deck around an artifact is risky with all the [Abrade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrade)s running around, but between the four Towers, the [Negate](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Negate)s, and the [Disallow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disallow)s, you certainly have more Towers and ways to protect them than they have [Abrade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Abrade)s.
