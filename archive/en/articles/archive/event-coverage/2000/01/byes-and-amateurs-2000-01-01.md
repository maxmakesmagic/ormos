Byes and Amateurs
=================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**







![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Sideboard Staff












394 players are eligible for the Amateur Awards, totalling $7,500. These are players that have zero Pro points. Pro points are won only at Pro Tour and Grand Prix events.


There are 168 players with byes, won either through having high numbers of Pro points, or a high DCI rating.


* 99 players with one bye
* 33 players with two byes
* 36 players with three byes
