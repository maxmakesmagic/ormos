### October 28-30, 2006: A Starring Role for Antoine Ruel


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptla05/ruel_wide.jpg)  
Packing an old-school Psychatog deck, Pro Tour veteran Antoine Ruel fought his way through the 340-player field to become champion of Pro Tour-Los Angeles 2005.


Ruel eschewed the just-rotated new Extended format's popular [Dredge](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dredge)/[Life from the Loam](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Life+from+the+Loam) addition to the classic 'Tog build and it proved to be a smart decision. He started his run through the Top 8 by defeating Tsuyoshi Fujita and his Boros Deck Wins 3-1 and never looked back.
