### GRAND PRIX UTRECHT 2014


THE PERFECT DECKS
=================



![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/henke_author.jpg)

Tobi Henke




August 10, 2014
 







Well, they might not be the perfect decks in the abstract, and even in this concrete setting they won't stay perfect for long, but the following five decks proved to be the perfect choice for yesterday's nine rounds, earning their pilots a perfect 9-0 start into the tournament.



 Interestingly enough, this best-of features five very distinct archetypes, a testament to the diversity of Standard at the moment. There's one Mono-Blue Devotion, one Black-White Midrange, one Red-White Burn, one Green-White Aggro, and one White-Blue Control. Notably absent are Jund Planeswalkers.
