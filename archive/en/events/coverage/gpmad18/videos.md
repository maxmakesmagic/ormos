Events: Grand Prix Madrid 2018
------------------------------


Green/Jones/Eliatamby Masters of Madrid
=======================================




In a star-studded Top 4, the relatively inexperienced British team of Christoph Green, Ben Jones, and Charles Eliatamby defeated all comers and became champions of Grand Prix Madrid 2018!






Mar 9-11, 2018


IFEMA - Feria de Madrid



Christoph Green, Ben Jones, and Charles Eliatamby
