The March 16, 2017 Update
=========================



 Posted in **Daily Magic Update**
 on March 16, 2017 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






The Daily *Magic* Update is a roundup of everything *Magic* you should know on March 16, 2017. Today's Update is brought to you by Draft tips for *Modern Masters 2017 Edition*. So many Draft tips.


Today's Must
============


1. [*Modern Masters 2017* Instant-Speed Tricks](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/modern-masters-2017-instant-speed-tricks) | MTGGoldfish | Sameer Merchant

With *Modern Masters 2017 Edition* releasing tomorrow, there will be drafts happening. Oh, so many drafts. If you're going to draft, be sure to make the most of it with this handy reference to all the instant-speed tricks in the format, complete with a handy PDF!


After That, Read, Watch, or Listen to These
===========================================


1. [Limited Review: *Modern Masters 2017*](http://puremtgo.com/articles/limited-review-modern-masters-2017) | PureMTGO | Vincent Borchardt

While you're perusing spreadsheets and crunching the numbers to mathematically prepare yourself for *Modern Masters 2017 Edition*, why not take a more holistic view? Borchard has an overview of the format that covers everything a little bit.

1. [*Modern Masters 2017* Design with Sam Stoddard](http://www.commanderinmtg.com/2017/03/16/e085-modern-masters-2017-design-with-sam-stoddard.html) | Commanderin' | Phil DeLuca, Sean Whatson, and Nate Burgess

Let's stick with *Modern Masters* a little bit longer and listen to Sam Stoddard talk about the set's design with the Commanderin' crew. Strong episode, but it needs more songs in my honor.


1. [STACK EM UP—MTG Gameshow Windmillionaire](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YBYDd4A0SWU) | Windmill Slam

It's been a bit since we've had the Windmillionaire game show in its non–special edition form, so revel in the absurdity and in Denis's flavor text prowess. Which is also absurd.

1. [Against the Odds: Zur-an (Modern)](https://www.mtggoldfish.com/articles/against-the-odds-zur-an-modern?src=wizards) | MTG Goldfish | SaffronOlive

Deck of the Day
===============


1. [Aetherworks Temur Tower](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-03-15)

Temur Tower—an energy-based [Dynavolt Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dynavolt+Tower) deck—has gained some popularity over the past few weeks as people uncovered its power. Likewise, people are rediscovering [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) as players look for ways to break through the Copy Cat/Mardu Vehicles paradigm.


Why not both?


Diego\_Mediano recently went 5-0 in a *Magic Online* Competitive Standard League with a deck that utilizes about 60% of a Temur Tower deck and combines it with part of an [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) deck, effectively dropping counterspells for the [Aetherworks Marvel](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherworks+Marvel) package.


This wasn't a fluke. How do I know? Because SEN\_ also went 5-0 with [the exact same decklist](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/mtgo-standings/competitive-standard-constructed-league-2017-03-15) at about the same time.


And this is what Standard is right now—evolution. People finding new ways to upgrade or shift existing strategies to find an edge. And, sometimes, smashing two sweet decks together.
