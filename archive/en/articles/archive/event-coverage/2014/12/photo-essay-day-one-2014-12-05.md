Photo Essay - Day One
=====================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 5, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_joshbennett.jpg)
By Josh Bennett











![](http://wotc-mx1.bkom.com/sites/wotc-mx1.bkom.com/files/images/misc/PE1.jpg)

Canadian Nationals is in full swing, and apparently one man has been marked for greatness.


![](http://wotc-mx1.bkom.com/sites/wotc-mx1.bkom.com/files/images/misc/PE2.jpg)

In addition to the expected WELCOME TO NATIONALS signage, competitors were greeted with this!


![](http://wotc-mx1.bkom.com/sites/wotc-mx1.bkom.com/files/images/misc/PE3.jpg)

Incredibly, it was NOT a practical joke. Here, the camera crew improvises a dolly shot.


![](http://wotc-mx1.bkom.com/sites/wotc-mx1.bkom.com/files/images/misc/PE4.jpg)

Real people attempting to keep Reality TV as real as possible, given the circumstances.


![](http://wotc-mx1.bkom.com/sites/wotc-mx1.bkom.com/files/images/misc/PE5.jpg)

That's just how Mike Gills rolls.


![](http://wotc-mx1.bkom.com/sites/wotc-mx1.bkom.com/files/images/misc/PE6.jpg)

Some sweet, sweet alters.


![](http://wotc-mx1.bkom.com/sites/wotc-mx1.bkom.com/files/images/misc/PE7.jpg)

Grave Titan with Swiftfoot Boots, yes, but sometimes you need even more zombies than that provides.


![](http://wotc-mx1.bkom.com/sites/wotc-mx1.bkom.com/files/images/misc/PE8.jpg)

Win or lose, Nats is a good time. Here, Nassim Ketita shows off the best sleeves in the tournament.
