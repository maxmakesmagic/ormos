Land Callbacks
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 10, 2011 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











As you might remember from last weekend's *New Phyrexia* Prerelease (or from [this Arcana](/en/articles/archive/new-phyrexia-basic-lands-2011-04-18)), *New Phyrexia* has basic lands in it. What you might *not* have noticed is that half of them depict the scene inside the Phyrexianized core of Mirrodin, while half of them contain references to lands from *Mirrodin*. Observe!
