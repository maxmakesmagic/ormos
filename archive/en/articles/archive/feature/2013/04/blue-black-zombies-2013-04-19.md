Blue-Black Zombies
==================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 19, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







This deck has the dubious honor of containing the worst card this week.


![Corpse Blockade](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Corpse+Blockade)
  
 Adam Breeden took second place at a 185-player PTQ in London with multiple cards that often don't make the cut in *Gatecrash* draft decks. 


 This is not to knock his choices. [Corpse Blockade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corpse+Blockade) is definitely fabulous against the wide variety of 3/3 creatures the aggressive decks are playing, and it offers a durable sacrifice outlet that's both on color and on tribe. 



[Shadow Alley Denizen](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shadow+Alley+Denizen) is another unconventional choice, but when [Corpse Blockade](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Corpse+Blockade) and [Gravecrawler](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gravecrawler) can let you cast as many black creatures as you want, giving all your creatures intimidate can be a great way to get through opposing [Thragtusk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thragtusk)s. 


 I may sound like I'm giving Adam a hard time, but I honestly like this deck. It looks excellent at killing the opponent with [Diregraf Captain](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Diregraf+Captain) and [Blood Artist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blood+Artist) by sacrificing Zombies, particularly undying ones like [Butcher Ghoul](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Butcher+Ghoul) and [Geralf's Messenger](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Geralf%27s+Messenger), and it also looks great at activating [Skirsdag High Priest](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Skirsdag+High+Priest), which is something I always love to do. 


The unconventional card choices only speak to Adam's creativity and willingness to capitalize on his synergies and push his game plan over playing more individually powerful cards.


Kudos to Adam on the unique build and excellent finish.
