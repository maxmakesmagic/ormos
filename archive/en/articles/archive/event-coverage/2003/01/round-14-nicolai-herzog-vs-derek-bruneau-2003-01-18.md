Round 14: Nicolai Herzog vs. Derek Bruneau
==========================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on January 18, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_TobyWachter.jpg)
By Toby Wachter











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a734.jpg)Nicolai Herzog hasn't been to the Top 8 since Worlds 1999, but he lost in the final round with a trip to Sunday on the line three times since then. For Derek Bruneau, this is an entirely new experience. In just his very first Pro Tour, Bruneau finds himself playing one match to earn the right to be in the Top 8- an achievement many players spend their whole careers aiming for, and never realize. 

Gary Wise mentioned from the crowd "This is the monster red-blue versus the monster red-blue. Both decks are insane."

### Game 1

Both players opened with [Imagecrafter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imagecrafter)s. Bruneau played a turn three morph creature, and Herzog did the same. Herzog's [Lavamancer's Skill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavamancer%27s+Skill) on [Imagecrafter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Imagecrafter) provided potential to dominate the board, and Bruneau's morph became an [Ascending Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven). Herzog's morph also became an Aven, and [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) joined the team to make Herzog's board very strong. [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock) took out the Skilled creature, but the damage had been done- Bruneau was left with no creatures on the board. He used [Blatant Thievery](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blatant+Thievery) to take Herzog's morph, which was taken care of right away by a second [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith), allowing the first to shoot it down ([Ascending Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven)). With two [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith)s out and an Aven swinging away, the game was soon over.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a735.jpg)Herzog- 1 Bruneau- 0 

### Game 2

Bruneau played a second turn [Aphetto Alchemist](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aphetto+Alchemist), followed by [Goblin Sharpshooter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Sharpshooter). [Erratic Explosion](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Erratic+Explosion) took down the Sharpshooter, as Herzog flipped over [Aven Fateshaper](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aven+Fateshaper). Seven damage makes for one dead Goblin. Bruneau's [Ascending Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven) died to another Explosion, but he kept the pressure on with [Mistform Dreamer](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Dreamer), while Herzog cycled [Slipstream Eel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slipstream+Eel) and [Mage's Guile](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mage%27s+Guile). Bruneau summoned a morph creature, and Herzog made an [Ascending Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven). Faceless One flipped over on Bruneau's side to provide a little more pressure, and Herzog cast [Slice and Dice](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Slice+and+Dice) to wipe out the board. On the following turn Herzog played two morph creatures, and Bruneau [Shock](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shock)ed each ([Ascending Aven](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ascending+Aven) and [Charging Slateback](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Charging+Slateback)). Herzog wasn't out of gas, and came right back with [Mistform Wall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Wall) and [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith). 

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/ptchi03/a736.jpg)Bruneau's own [Mistform Wall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mistform+Wall) was shot by [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith), and [Lavamancer's Skill](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lavamancer%27s+Skill) on the Wall looked to finish the job, but Bruneau had [Meddle](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Meddle) to switch the target to his Wall. The crowd gasped at this tremendous play, but Herzog seemed unconcerned and asked "You only played that Wall last turn right?" He calmly played a *second* Skill on his Wall, and took care of Bruneau's Wall that had just been enchanted with the stolen Skill. Bruneau summoned [Riptide Shapeshifter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Riptide+Shapeshifter), and Herzog shot it down. It was sacrificed in response naming Goblin, and pulled up a [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith). [Essence Fracture](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Essence+Fracture) bounced back Herzog's Skilled Wall and [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith), and suddenly Bruneau was back in the game. It's interesting to note that the [Essence Fracture](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Essence+Fracture) over [Pinpoint Avalanche](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pinpoint+Avalanche) pick Bruneau made during the draft was mocked, but the Avalanche wouldn't have gotten him out of the situation, while [Essence Fracture](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Essence+Fracture) did. Regardless, Herzog had [Pinpoint Avalanche](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pinpoint+Avalanche) to take out the [Sparksmith](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sparksmith) and easily won the game from that point.

**Final Result:** Herzog- 2 Bruneau- 0
