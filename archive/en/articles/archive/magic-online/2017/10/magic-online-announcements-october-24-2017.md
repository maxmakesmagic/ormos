**Streamer Showdown is back this Thursday, October 26**


There will be a new Streamer Showdown this Thursday. If you've never seen a Streamer Showdown before, they come in two eight-player events that feature some of the best and newcomer streamers in the community brawling it out on *Magic Online*. If you're trying to improve your game, *Magic Online*streamers are some of the best players out there—and some of the best teachers, too. Even if you're already a *Magic*pro, throw your support behind one of the competitors and cheer them on from their Twitch chat!


For this Streamer Showdown, the streamers will be taking on Flashback Favorites: 3x *Innistrad*! Here is the lineup for this Streamer Showdown:

**Standard PTQ this Saturday, October 28**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, October 28 at 9 a.m. PT. There is no need to qualify through a Preliminary– simply join this event with 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

***Ixalan*Sealed PTQ this Sunday, October 29**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, October 29 at 7 a.m. PT.


You can enter this event using a Limited PTQ Finals Entry Token (Blue or Red) earned in a PTQ Preliminary.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

**Extended downtime tomorrow, October 25**


The next two downtimes will be on October 25 (extended, 3 a.m. PT/10:00 p.m. UTC) and November 15 (regular, 8 a.m. PT/4:00 p.m. UTC).


In tomorrow's downtime any QPs earned from the 2017 MOCS will be cleared from your account, except those QPs earned on or after October 18.


Follow *Magic Online*'s [Twitter](https://twitter.com/MagicOnline) account for live updates during downtime.
