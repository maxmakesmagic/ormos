Exploring Standard Showdown (Videos)
====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on November 22, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











[Standard Showdown](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/standard-showdown) events kick off this weekend. Wondering what to play or what the tournament is going to be like? Not a Standard player but curious what the format is like? Check out the videos below to learn just what we have in store.


**Watch Players Showdown in Rotterdam**


We held a Showdown-style event for players at the World *Magic* Cup, and a good time was had by all. Don’t believe us? Check it out.


<https://www.youtube.com/embed/Y7XSL8y5eqY>


**What is the Standard Showdown**


That looks fun, but what is the Standard Showdown, you might ask? Good question, voice in my head. The coverage crew at the World *Magic* Cup introduced and discussed the Standard Showdown. If you know nothing about the Standard Showdown, this is a good place to start.


<https://www.youtube.com/v/zSowhyr5QHc>


**What Can You Do in Standard Right Now**


Standard *is* all fun and games, and this short video shows some of the wild things you can do in Standard right now.


<https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2FMagicTheGathering%2Fvideos%2F10157667665820307%2F&show_text=0&width=560>


**What People Talk About When They’re Talking About Standard Showdown (Recorded Live Stream)**


The Community team talks about Standard and even cracks a few of the prize packs to give you a glimpse of what’s to come.


<https://www.facebook.com/plugins/video.php?href=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2FMagicTheGathering%2Fvideos%2F10157655867855307%2F&show_text=0&width=560>
