Prison, a deck archetype that looks to lock down the things an opponent can do to control the flow of the game, has been around for ages. It has fallen out of fashion lately, but that didn't stop Ryland Foster, who brought this deck to a Top 16 in a large Legacy event held by StarCityGames.com in Orlando, Florida. [Back to Basics](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Back+to+Basics) locks down an opponent's mana, while [Propaganda](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Propaganda) forces them to pay to attack. Soon, they can't afford to attack *or* cast spells, and your fatty win conditions have all the time in the world to take things down. It's an exciting return of an old strategy for Legacy!
