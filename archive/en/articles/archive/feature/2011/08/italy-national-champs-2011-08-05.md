The 2011 Italy National Champion has been decided! Marcello Calvetta will lead his national team at the 2011 World Championships. The deck that got him there? An Italian take on the popular Caw-Blade deck, still chugging along even after the banning of two of its key cards. Check out the [official coverage](/en/events/coverage/i-falchi-di-calvetto-ghermiscono-il-titolo) of the 2011 Italian National Championships (in Italian) for more.
