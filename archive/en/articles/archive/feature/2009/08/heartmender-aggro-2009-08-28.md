[Heartmender](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heartmender) was custom-made for persist creatures, and Nathan Gelderloos combined his copies with [Kitchen Finks](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kitchen+Finks), a powerful pairing against any beatdown deck. Another combo in the deck? [Wilt-Leaf Liege](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wilt-Leaf+Liege) with almost all green-white creatures! Nathan played the deck to a local Top 8 at an event in Michigan.
