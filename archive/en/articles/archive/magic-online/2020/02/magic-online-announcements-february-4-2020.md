Limited PTQ Saturday
--------------------


On Saturday, February 8, there will be a Limited PTQ at 7 a.m. PT! The set will be *Theros Beyond Death*, and the event will cost 1 Red Limited PTQ Token. You can also join with 40 [MOCS Qualifier Points](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020). The winner qualifies for their choice of events in Series 2 of the [2020 Players Tour](https://magic.gg/players-tour) in May.


Learn more about Players Tour Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#ptq).




---

Standard PTQ Sunday
-------------------


On Sunday, February 9, there will be a Standard PTQ at 9 a.m. PT! The entry fee is 1 Red PTQ Finals Token or 40 [MOCS Qualifier Points](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020). The winner qualifies for their choice of events in Series 2 of the [2020 Players Tour](https://magic.gg/players-tour) in May.


Learn more about Players Tour Qualifier events [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020#ptq).

*Magic Online* Format Challenges Every Weekend
----------------------------------------------


Format Challenges are the first step toward qualifying for Premier Play through your favorite *Magic* formats. To learn more about Premier Play qualification, click [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/mtgo/premier-play-2020).


The *Magic Online* Format Challenges continue every weekend for Standard, Pioneer, Modern, Vintage, Legacy, and Pauper formats except when replaced by Showcase Challenges during scheduled Showcase Challenge dates. *NOTE: The Vintage Challenge happens every weekend regardless of scheduled Showcase Challenges.*


**Pacific Time (-8 from UTC)**





| Start Time | Event Type |
| --- | --- |
| Saturday, 6 a.m. | [Standard Challenge](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/standard-formats-magic-gathering) |
| Saturday, 8 a.m. | [Modern Challenge](http://magic.wizards.com/en/game-info/gameplay/formats/modern) |
| Saturday, 10 a.m. | [Vintage Challenge](http://magic.wizards.com/en/game-info/gameplay/formats/vintage) |
| Sunday, 6 a.m. | [Pioneer Challenge](https://magic.wizards.com/en/game-info/gameplay/formats/pioneer) |
| Sunday, 8 a.m. | [Legacy Challenge](http://magic.wizards.com/en/game-info/gameplay/formats/legacy) |

Build Notes
-----------


Collection | *Throne of Eldraine* prestige avatars are being distributed on February 5.


Duel Scene | [Fires of Invention](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fires+of+Invention) will no longer slow the Duel Scene down in most cases.


Duel Scene | Yielding to a copy of [Bronzehide Lion](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Bronzehide+Lion) will no longer cause a game server crash.


Duel Scene | Returning a split card to your hand with [Seasons Past](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Seasons+Past) will no longer cause a game server crash.


Account | Password requirements are now displayed during the password reset process.
