PRO TOUR SHADOWS OVER INNISTRAD
===============================




Live video coverage of Pro Tour *Shadows over Innistrad* begins April 22-24 at 9 a.m. local time (CET) / 12a.m. PT / 3 a.m. ET / 7 a.m. UTC.
















PRO TOUR SHADOWS OVER INNISTRAD
-------------------------------




* [PRE-SHOW COVERAGE](#tabs-0)


PRE-SHOW COVERAGE



Investigate the madness of *Shadows over Innistrad* and unlock the secrets of the brand new Standard and Booster Draft formats by tuning into LIVE coverage of Pro Tour *Shadows over Innistrad*! With a prize pool of $250,000, who will unravel the best decks and strategies and win the $40,000 first place prize and an invitation to the 2016 *Magic: The Gathering* World Championship? Tune in twitch.tv/magic starting Friday, April 22 at 12 a.m. PT/ 3 a.m.ET / 7a.m. UTC for live video coverage,and bookmark this page for the latest articles, decklists, and more!
