![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpla14/Profile%20-%20Goldsmith.jpg)


### Chris Goldsmith


**Age:** 33  
**Hometown:** San Diego, California   
**Occupation:** Systems Engineer  
  

**Favorite clan (Temur, Abzan, Jeskai, Sultai, Mardu)**
  
Abzan
 



**(Other) previous *Magic* Accomplishments** 
  
N/A
 



**What Standard deck are you playing, and why did you choose it?** 
  
Abzan, The deck had the most success in the recent Pro Tour and Abzan performed well at my local store since the release.
 



**What are your best and worst matchups?** 
  
Best: Temur Tempo, Worst: Big Burn/Hasty Flyers
 



**What card won you the most matches this weekend?** 
  

 Tied between [Siege Rhino](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Siege+Rhino), [Wingmate Roc](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wingmate+Roc), and Elspeth Sun's Champion, of course.
 



**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?** 
  
Sideboard, mostly. Felt the [Duneblast](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Duneblast) wasn't what I wanted, maybe too greedy. Games needing rests could be done at a lower cost. Few other changes.
 



**If you could meet any person from history, who would it be?** 
  
George Washington

![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpla14/Profile%20-%20Ulanov.jpg)


### Denis Ulanov


**Age:** 27  
**Hometown:** Samara, Russia   
**Occupation:** Software Engineer  
  

**Favorite clan (Temur, Abzan, Jeskai, Sultai, Mardu)**
  
Abzan
 



**(Other) previous *Magic* Accomplishments** 
  
a few StarCityGames Open Top 8s
 



**What Standard deck are you playing, and why did you choose it?** 
  
Mono-Red Sligh, a.k.a., "Red Army." The deck was outstanding during testing and this is the kind of deck I like to play.
 



**What are your best and worst matchups?** 
  
Most of the popular matches are favorable, including Abzan and Jeskai. The worst is probably Mardu Midrange.
 



**What card won you the most matches this weekend?** 
  
[Hammerhand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hammerhand), [Stoke the Flames](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoke+the+Flames), and [Goblin Rabblemaster](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Goblin+Rabblemaster)




**What changes would you make to your deck and why would you make them?** 
  
I would not play [Blinding Flare](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Blinding+Flare). The rest of the deck is pretty insane.
 



**If you could meet any person from history, who would it be?** 
  
My wife is the person I would like to meet, others are not even close. Even the ones from history.
