![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)

Here's something cool that went on sale on **Magic Online** yesterday.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/tcg/products/lds/lds_boxes.jpg)

Would you like to see what's in the decks? Well ... okay!
