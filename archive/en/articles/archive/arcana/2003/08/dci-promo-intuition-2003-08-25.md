DCI Promo Intuition
===================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on August 25, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The DCI has created a premium version of the *Tempest* powerhouse [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition).

The card will be used primarily as a reward for judges at major events.
