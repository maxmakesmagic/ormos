**Throwback Standard Gauntlet 4: *Mirrodin* Standard begins tomorrow, June 7**


Throwback Standard Gauntlet 4: *Mirrodin* Standard begins tomorrow! Randy Buehler outlined the event details in this [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/throwback-standard-gauntlet-4-mirrodin-standard-2017-05-31), which included an in-depth analysis of the decks that will be available to play. The event will begin tomorrow at 10 a.m. PT and will be available until June 14.  
  

More information about Throwback Standard Gauntlets can be found in Lee Sharpe’s article [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/flashback-favorites-and-throwback-standard-gauntlets-2016-12-15).

***Magic Online Amonkhet* Championships begin tomorrow, June 7**


The *Magic Online Amonkhet* Championships begin tomorrow, June 7, at 12:30 p.m. PT. Refer to last week’s [article](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-amonkhet-championship-event-details-2017-05-31) for *Amonkhet* Championship specifics as well as updated information regarding Constructed PTQs.

***Aether Revolt*/*Kaladesh* Redemption ends tomorrow, June 7**


Take advantage of *Magic Online*'s Redemption feature and trade in your *Aether Revolt*/*Kaladesh* card sets before 9:00 a.m. PT tomorrow, June 7.


Redemptions purchased after the downtime on May 24 will be processed on Wednesday, June 14.


Learn more about *Magic Online* Redemption [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/gameinfo/products/magiconline/redemption).

***Magic Online* Regional Pro Tour Qualifier for Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation* on Saturday, June 10** 


*Magic Online* Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers (RPTQ) for Pro Tour *Hour of Devastation* will take place on Saturday, June 10, and Saturday, June 17. These events are only open to players who qualified via in-store Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifier events – pre-registration is also required.


Additional information about RPTQs can be found [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/events/instoreplay/rptqhou).


[Top](#top)

***Magic Online*Format Challenges back this weekend**


The *Magic Online*Format Challenges introduced a new prize structure and recurring weekend events for formats Modern, Vintage, 1v1 Commander, Legacy, and Pauper! Check out Lee Sharpe’s article [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/magic-online-format-challenges-2017-05-04) for specific details about the Format Challenges.


Events begin Saturday, June 10 at 8:00 a.m. PT.

**The June MOPR Promo period is underway**


The June MOPR period runs from May 31 to June 28. Here are the promo cards:




|
|  |
| Event Promo

Shardless Agent | Store Promo

Aether Hub |

Play in one or more events during the May MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium Shardless Agent. Play in five or more and you'll get an additional premium Shardless Agent. Spend at least $20 total in the Magic Online Store during this MOPR period and you'll get a non-premium Aether Hub. By spending $40 or more, you'll get an additional premium Aether Hub.
