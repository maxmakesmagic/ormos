Decks of the Week: 6/25/2009
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on June 25, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











It's a Pro Tour–Honolulu redux this week! Decks of the Week is bringing you all of the Top 8 lists, the top finishing *Shards of Alara* Block Constructed lists, and after hours of typing, all 390+ Block Constructed lists from the event! On top of that we've got Regionals, PTQs, and **Magic Online** Premier Events. Make sure to check out special entries for Standard Vanguard, Pauper, Kaleidoscope, and more!

Enjoy!
