*Odyssey* Block Sealed Deck Tournament VI
=========================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 23, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











T *he meek shall inherit the Earth. But not Dominaria.* 

 *Odyssey* focuses on two new mechanics designed to harness the power of your graveyard: Flashback and Threshold. Play with this classic block and compete for great prizes.

### *ODYSSEY* BLOCK SEALED DECK TOURNAMENT VI

*START TIME:* Saturday, March 27, 2010 11:00 AM PST / 19:00 UTC.   
*LOCATION:*  Premier Events room.   
*ENTRY FEE:*  30 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide 2 *Odyssey* (ODY) booster packs, 2 *Torment* (TOR) booster packs, and 2 *Judgment* (JUD) booster packs for this event.  
*SIZE:*  33 players minimum, 512 players maximum.   
*DURATION:* 6 rounds, Swiss Pairings. Play in all six rounds, win or lose!   
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on the total match points at the end of the tournament. Each match win awards 3 points and each match loss awards 0 points. 



|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Match Points** | **Prizes** | **QPs** |
| 18 |  Complete premium-foil set of any 1 *Odyssey* block expansion, winner's choice | 3 |
| 15 | 15 booster packs (5 ODY, 5 TOR, 5 JUD) | 2 |
| 12 | 9 booster packs (3 ODY, 3 TOR, 3 JUD) | 1 |
| 9 | 3 booster packs (1 ODY, 1 TOR, 1 JUD) | 0 |

  
*ADDITIONAL PRIZES:* All players who participate in the *Odyssey* Block Sealed Deck Tournament VI will receive 1 copy of the Friday Night Magic version of [Deep Analysis](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Deep+Analysis). Players with at least 15 match points at the end of the tournament will also receive a premium foil Deep Analysis.
