Decks of the Week: 11/8/2013
============================



 Posted in **Decks of the Week**
 on November 8, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Several tournaments happened last weekend in North and South America! Let's start in Chile, where hometown player Luis Navas won [Grand Prix Santiago](/en/events/coverage/chilean-navas-shows-madcap-skills), thus keeping the trophy in-country. Waaaay up north, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, [Joel Lim won the Vintage half](/en/events/coverage/lims-fish-reel-deal-philly) of Eternal Weekend playing the deck Reel Fish, while [Ari Lax took down Legacy](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/legacy-coverage-eternal-championships-2013-2013-11-02) with Death and Taxes. Congratulations Luis, Joel, and Ari! For his win, Luis will be invited to Pro Tour *Born of the Gods* in Valencia, Spain, and if you'd like to join him there, one way is to win a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ)! This season's format is *Theros* Limited. Find a PTQ near you with [this handy link](https://www.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtg/event/protour/qualifierlist#bog)! Good luck; have fun!
