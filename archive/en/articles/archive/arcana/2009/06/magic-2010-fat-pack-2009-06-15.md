Magic 2010 Fat Pack
===================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on June 15, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











M**agic 2010** is coming. And it's bringing a Fat Pack with it!

  
![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/211_fatpack_tavbuggl0k.jpg)  
That is a handsome box!

...

Oh, you'd like to see inside? Well, okay!

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/211_fatpack_19p86q5l55.jpg)  
And here's what you get!

* Eight 15-card booster packs
* The ***Magic** 2010* Player's Guide, which contains play tips, story background, and a visual encyclopedia of every card in the set
* A learn-to-play insert
* Excerpts from the novel *The Purifying Fire* by Laura Resnick
* A pack of 40 ***Magic** 2010* basic land
* A ***Magic** 2010* Spindown Life Counter, available only in this Fat Pack

Well, that's it! Except for the handsome vertical mural, of course. *That* looks like *this*:

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/211_fatpack_n6aegk8x7v.jpg)
