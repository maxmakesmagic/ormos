Top 8 Players of Pro Tour Gatecrash
===================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on February 16, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_EventCoverageStaff.jpg)
By Event Coverage Staff











![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/events/ptgtc13/ben_stark.jpg)
### Ben Stark


**Age:** 29  
**Hometown:** Tamarac, FL  
**Occupation:** None


 

**Number of Times Playing on the Pro Tour:**  

36


**Lifetime Pro Points:**  

262


**Grand Prix Top 8s:**  

8


**Pro Tour Top 8s:**  

3


**Other Magic Accomplishments:**  

None.


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour Gatecrash?**  

Gold Pro


**What was your record by format?**


**Standard:**  

7-1-2


**Booster Draft:**  

5-1


**What guild are you? Why did you choose that guild?**  

Azorius, I’ll play whichever but I am a control player at heart.


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour Gatecrash? Who did you prepare with?**


**Team ChannelFireball:**  

LSV, Wrapter, Kibler, Efro, Gerry T, Ochoa, PV, Shuuhei, Martin Juza, Huey, Brock, Conley.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

Esper. LSV said it was good and I love me some Drownyards.


**How many Gatecrash drafts did you do on Magic Online this week?**  

20ish


**What is Magic to you? Why do you play?**  
  

The best game in the world. Games are sweet.

**Age:** 23  
**Hometown:** West Allis

174

0


**Other Magic Accomplishments:**  

Player of the Year 2011, 2nd GP Columbus 2007, 2nd GP Washington DC 2010.


**How did you qualify for Pro Tour Gatecrash?**  

Platinum


**What was your record by format?**


**Standard:**  

7-1-2


**Booster Draft:**  

5-1


**What guild are you? Why did you choose that guild?**  

Gruul. After my success at the prerelease and my choice of Jund I made the switch from Izzet to Gruul.


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour Gatecrash? Who did you prepare with?**  

Tested with StarCityGames.com. Played for hours upon hours with Reid Duke testing Jund against everything. Stayed at Jon Finkel’s Apartment.


**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**  

Jund. Played countless games with it and it has no bad matchups. With a deck like this you give yourself a good chance to win any matchup you play.


**How many Gatecrash drafts did you do on Magic Online this week?**  

One.


**What is Magic to you? Why do you play?**  
  

This.

Platinum Pro


**What was your record by format?**


**Standard:**  

6-3-1


**Booster Draft:**

**What guild are you? Why did you choose that guild?**  

Azorius – as a lawyer I feel I should uphold the law of Ravnica.


**How did you prepare for Pro Tour Gatecrash? Who did you prepare with?**

**What Standard deck are you playing and why?**

The ultimate intellectual competition; I love the challenge. I keep playing because of the great community & friends I’ve made through the game.
