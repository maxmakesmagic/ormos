Modern Death's Shadow
=====================



 Posted in **Daily Deck**
 on December 1, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/Melissa_DeTora.jpg)
By Melissa DeTora




 A Magic Pro player and strategy writer, Melissa's career Top 8s include PT Gatecrash and 2 GPs. She's currently a member of Magic R&D. 






Usually, when you're playing a game of *Magic*, you are trying to reduce your opponent's life total to 0. However, when playing today's deck, you're trying to reduce your own life total to 0. Well, not actually to 0, but you want to deal yourself as much damage as quickly as possible without actually dying.


So why would you want to inflict damage upon yourself? This deck is built around the card [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow). [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) gets stronger the less life you have. If you are at 10 life, [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) is a 3/3 creature for only one mana. That's a pretty good rate when you compare it to other aggressive one-mana creatures like [Wild Nacatl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Nacatl) or [Delver of Secrets](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets). Once you start getting down to a single-digit life total, [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) gets even bigger. If you're at 3 life, [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) is a 10/10 creature, and a single [Temur Battle Rage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Temur+Battle+Rage) can deal 20 points of trample damage.


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Death%27s+Shadow)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Thoughtseize)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize)
This deck plays lots of ways to deal yourself damage. Fetch lands and shock lands can cause you to lose 3 life per land drop, and Phyrexian mana spells like [Gitaxian Probe](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gitaxian+Probe), [Dismember](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dismember), and [Mutagenic Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutagenic+Growth) require a life payment to cast them. Even [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize), one of the strongest spells in Modern, fits the theme really well here. It's not uncommon to cast a [Thoughtseize](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Thoughtseize) on turn one and a [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) on turn two after cracking some fetches. An attack on turn three with your [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) followed by a couple of [Mutagenic Growth](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mutagenic+Growth)s and a [Become Immense](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Become+Immense) can kill your opponent out of nowhere.


The great thing about this deck is it's not reliant on [Death's Shadow](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Death%27s+Shadow) to win. This deck plays out like a typical Zoo deck, with one-drop creatures such as [Steppe Lynx](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Steppe+Lynx) and [Wild Nacatl](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Wild+Nacatl), and even spells such as [Lightning Bolt](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Lightning+Bolt) can end a game when your creatures can't get the job done.


Congratulations to A\_Reya for going 5-0 in the *Magic Online* Modern League with a very unique deck!
