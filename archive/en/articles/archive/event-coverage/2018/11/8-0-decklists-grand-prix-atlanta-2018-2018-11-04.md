The 8-0 Decklists of Grand Prix Atlanta 2018
============================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 4, 2018 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_FrankKarsten.jpg)
By Frank Karsten











Out of 1,322 players, twelve escaped the first day's eight rounds of Modern with their unblemished records still intact: Marcelino Freeman, Martin Jůza, Gonçalo Pinto, Michael Bonde, Yam Wing Chun, Caleb Scherer, James Baggett, Thomas Lee, Thiago Rodrigues, Jacob Ballington, Sol Malka, and Florian Trotte.


Their decks included 2x Humans, 2x W/U Control, 2x Dredge, Bogles, Hollow One, Jund, Storm, Jeskai Control, and The Rock.
