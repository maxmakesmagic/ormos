Dragons of Tarkir Intro Pack Decklists
======================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on March 19, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






Have no fear, *Dragons of Tarkir* is almost here! Let's all cheer! Something, something schmear!



Leaving aside the sick rhymes I just dropped on you there, *Dragons of Tarkir* is something to get crazy excited about. And today's bit of excitement is the decklists for All. Five. Intro. Packs.



Each one, as you might expect, or already know, is headlined by a Dragon from one of the dragon clans of new Tarkir.



*Dragons of Tarkir* releases everywhere March 27, but you can get a first look at the set this weekend at Prereleases in stores near you!
