After watching a similar deck Top 8 the recent Standard Pro Tour in [Kyoto](/en/events/coverage/nassif-greater-power), Wataru Hosaka has adapted the Dark Bant strategy for Extended. Instead of the usual Blue-White-Green configuration, Wataru has touched just enough black to play things like [Countersquall](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Countersquall) and [Doran, the Siege Tower](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Doran%2C+the+Siege+Tower). The powerful strategy was good enough for a PTQ Top 8 at a recent event held in Japan.
