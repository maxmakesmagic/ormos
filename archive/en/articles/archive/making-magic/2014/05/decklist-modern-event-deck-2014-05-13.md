Decklist for Modern Event Deck
==============================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on May 13, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_mikemcartor.jpg)
By Mike McArtor




 Mike first played with Ice Age and became the copy editor for DailyMTG.com in December 2011. Before DailyMTG, he was an editor on D&D's Dragon magazine for four years. 







In January, [we announced](/en/node/651256) the Modern Event Deck, which releases at the end of the month (May 30, to be exact)! We have more information about it, including the decklist.


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/images/magic/daily/features/2014/featmd1_wk19_299_product.jpg)Here's information from the back of the box!


*Your power is here.  

Your time is now.* 


Get started with the deep-strategy Modern format in style! Draw on rare, high-powered **Magic**™ cards from past sets with this tightly constructed 60-card deck. Also includes a 15-card sideboard, strategy guide, Spindown™ life counter, 80 exclusive card sleeves, 5 double sided tokens, and a deck box.


Pick up the Modern Event Deck and find a Modern event near you.


**March of the Multitudes**


*Leaders like [Elspeth, Knight-Errant](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Elspeth%2C+Knight-Errant) generate an army of token creatures, while spells like [Intangible Virtue](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intangible+Virtue) and [Honor of the Pure](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Honor+of+the+Pure) inspire them to victory. White removal cards along with black disruptions keep your opponent off balance until you can strike the killing blow.*
