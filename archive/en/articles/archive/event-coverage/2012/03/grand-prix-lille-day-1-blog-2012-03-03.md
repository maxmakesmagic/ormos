Saturday, 11:15 a.m. – A Sample of Trial Winning Deck Lists
-----------------------------------------------------------


*by Tobi Henke* 

The main event has only just begun, but there's been a lot of **Magic** going on yesterday already. A whopping number of 27 Grand Prix Trials were quickly filled up and provided 27 players with three byes for today's Grand Prix. Judging by their deck lists, the variety of viable strategies in the current Standard is staggering.


Raphaël Lévy's Reanimator deck ("Frites") won at least seven of the tournaments; blue-black control decks and blue-black Zombies have also racked up a fair share of victories. Blue-white Humans are still going strong, as is [Delver of Secrets](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Delver+of+Secrets). Then there's R/G Aggro, [Kessig Wolf Run](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Kessig+Wolf+Run) Ramp, G/W/r [Birthing Pod](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Birthing+Pod), B/W Tokens, and even Mono-Red! And these are just the winning deck lists ...
