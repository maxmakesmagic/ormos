Riding the Bee
==============



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on July 21, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












G[lamerdye](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=lamerdye)'s effect is straightforwardly mechanical: it changes the colors on a permanent.



![Glamerdye](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Glamerdye)

To visually portray that, Ralph Horsley depicts a faerie that has become twisted and savage, like a boggart. Let's blow the art up so we can get a good look at it:


[![Glamerdye](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/magic/images/cardart/eve/glamerdye_640.jpg)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Glamerdye)
*[Glamerdye](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glamerdye) art by Ralph Horsley*
But what would this faerie have looked like before it changed color? It would have been less savage, and its mount would have looked calmer. And of course, its colors would have been different. In short, it would have looked like this:


[![Glen Elendra Liege](https://web.archive.org/web/20090604073148im_/http://www.wizards.com/magic/images/cardart/SHM/Glen_Elendra_Liege_640.jpg)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?&name=Glen%2BElendra%2BLiege)
*[Glen Elendra Liege](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glen+Elendra+Liege) art by Kev Walker*
Clearly, someone cast the color-changing spell on [Glen Elendra Liege](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glen+Elendra+Liege)!
