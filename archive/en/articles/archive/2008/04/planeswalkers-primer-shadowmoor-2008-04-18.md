A Planeswalker's Primer for Shadowmoor
======================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on April 18, 2008 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20170709072651im_/http://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By magicthegathering.com Staff












*Shadowmoor*'s prerelease is imminent. But before you go into battle, you need to know what new tricks you'll have up your sleeve. We're sure you've been checking out the [Card Preview Archive](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=magic/shadowmoor/spoiler) every day and devouring every word in our preview articles. To aid in your preparation, we've recorded videos for all the new *Shadowmoor* mechanics so you can see **hybrid**, **wither**, **persist**, **conspire**, and **untap** in action.
