Looking for something new and exciting to take to your next Extended tournament? Nicholas Gulledge took this 75 all the way to the blue envelope at a Pro Tour Qualifier in Mobile, Alabama. With powerful spells like [Mindslaver](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mindslaver) accelerated onto the field of play by the Urzatron, Nicholas whomped through a field of Faeries, Zoo, Affinity, and more! You can find a PTQ near you [here](/en/node/640716). Of course, following *Conflux* Prerelease weekend, excitement is buzzing over what brand new cards might make every deck even better. To find out how to get a chance to play with the brand new set this weekend, the first it becomes available for sale, visit [this page](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/magazine/article.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/releases).
