Dual Lands in the Magic Online Cube
===================================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on May 9, 2012 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By Monty Ashley











If you're reading this early in the day on May 9, you could be playing [the **Magic Online** Cube](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtg/daily/other/04232012d) right now!
