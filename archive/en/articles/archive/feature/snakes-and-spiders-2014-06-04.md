Snakes and Spiders
==================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 4, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_adamprosak.jpg)
By Adam Prosak




From Friday Night Magic to the Pro Tour, Adam Prosak loves all types of tournament Magic. Currently, Adam is working in R&D as a developer. 







T*heros* Block is sprinkled with cards that interact with your graveyard. Cards like [Commune with the Gods](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Commune+with+the+Gods) and [Nyx Weaver](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nyx+Weaver) help fill your graveyard. [Nighthowler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nighthowler) and [Pharika, God of Affliction](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pharika%2C+God+of+Affliction) are more powerful as your graveyard grows in size. 


 Once you take advantage of the cross-block synergy provided by the Golgari guild in *Return to Ravnica*, a black-green self-mill deck becomes very appealing. [Grisly Salvage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Grisly+Salvage) and [Jarad, Golgari Lich Lord](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jarad%2C+Golgari+Lich+Lord) combine well with the strategies enabled by *Theros* Block. The end result is a synergy-driven Standard deck that is both fun and powerful.
