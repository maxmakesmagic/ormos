Top 8: Rui Mariani Rodrigues
============================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on December 16, 2015 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast














![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/GPPORTO00/Images/207.jpg)**Name:** Rui Mariani  
**Age:** 30  
**Location:** Lisbon, Portugal  
**Occupation:** Computer programmer  
**Played Magic since:**  Ice Age, 1996?  
**Best accomplishments:** 4th GP Lisbon 1999, 7th GP Madrid 2000, 27th PT Rome.



Q: What made you decide to come to Grand Prix Porto?  

A: It's near and I won a trial.



Q: What were your expectations going into this tournament, and why?  

A: Top 32, top 8 with some luck because I play really well. :)



Q: Briefly describe your tournament play-wise so far, both day one and day two.  

A: Day 1: 2-1-1 with an ok deck. Day 2: 5-1 with good drafts and some luck.



Q: What do you think is the main reason for you making top 8 in this tournament, and why?  

A: Constistency, luck and no stress.



Q: What does this top 8 performance mean to you?  

A: I really want to win this time! It's very satisfying. I'm happy.



Q: What are your predictions for the top 8?  

A: Can I win? It's a very competitive top 8.
