Magic Online 4th of July Celebration
====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on June 22, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Celebrate the 4th of July with **Magic Online** this year! Starting July 2, we will run a selection of 64-player premier drafts throughout the holiday weekend. Due to the popularity of these events, we will schedule 3 of each event to begin concurrently. To ensure that everyone who wants to participate can, please limit your participation to one event per flight. Have fun! 

**Independence Day Premier Draft Schedule:**

* *Ravnica* Block - Friday, July 2 at 6:00 AM PDT/13:00 UTC
* *Shadowmoor* Block - Friday, July 2 at 1:00 PM PDT/20:00 UTC
* *Kamigawa* Block - Friday, July 2 at 8:00 PM PDT PDT/03:00 UTC (Saturday)
* *Zendikar* Block (ZEN/WWK)- Saturday, July 3 at 4:00 AM PDT/11:00 UTC
* *Onslaught* Block - Saturday, July 3 at 11:00 AM PDT/18:00 UTC
* *Masters Edition I, II, III* - Saturday, July 3 at 6:00 PM PDT/1:00 UTC (Sunday)
* *Shards of Alara* Block - Sunday, July 4 at 1:00 AM PDT/8:00 UTC
* *Time Spiral* Block - Sunday, July 4 at 9:00 AM PDT/16:00 UTC
* *Lorwyn* Block - Sunday, July 4 at 3:00 PM PDT/22:00 UTC
* *Tempest* Block - Sunday, July 4 at 10:00 PM PDT/5:00 UTC (Monday)
* *Rise of the Eldrazi* - Monday, July 5 at 5:00 AM PDT/12:00 UTC
* *Odyssey* Block - Monday, July 5 at 12:00 PM PDT/19:00 UTC
* *Mirage* Block - Monday, July 5 at 7:00 PM PDT/2:00 UTC (Tuesday)

### Independence Day Premier Draft Weekend

*START TIME:* Scheduled start times beginning Friday, July 2 through July 5. See the schedule above.   
*LOCATION:*  Premier Events room.   
*ENTRY FEE:*  15 Event Tickets.   
*PRODUCT:* 
**Magic Online** will provide the product for these events.  
*SIZE:*  64 players.   
*DURATION:* Drafting and deck building time plus 3 Rounds, Single Elimination. The "Top 8" draft will consist of additional drafting and deck building time plus 3 Rounds, Single Elimination.  
*PRIZES:* Prizes are based on final standings. The product awarded will be based on the product used in the event.
