Intro Packs of Alara
====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on September 22, 2008 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast










As you remember, we're changing the way theme decks work for *Shards of Alara*. They're now "intro packs", and each one comes with a 41-card preconstructed deck (including a foil rare!) and include a strategy insert, a learn-to-play insert, and a 15-card random booster pack from *Shards of Alara*.


Ah, but what's *in* them? Let's look at three of them and find out!


### Bant Exalted


The plane of Bant is a regimented hierarchy of knights, aven, and tough-minded rhoxes who uphold the ideals of the angels. Summon Bant's most worthy heroes, then rally them around a mighty champion who will smite your planeswalker foes.


![](https://media.wizards.com/legacy/mtg/images/daily/arcana/1682_bantpack.jpg)
