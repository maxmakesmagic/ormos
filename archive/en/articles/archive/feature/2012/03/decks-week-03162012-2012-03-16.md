Decks of the Week: 03/16/2012
=============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 16, 2012 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Legacy got a moment in the spotlight last weekend at [Grand Prix Indianapolis](/en/events/coverage/martell%E2%80%99s-victory-lingers-indianapolis), where Tom Martell defeated Kenny Castor for the title. Check out the lists below to see what the top players in the world do when they have more than 10,000 cards at their disposal! For a slightly narrower field of cards, the Modern PTQ season is hurtling toward its conclusion. There are only three weekends left to [earn your invitation to Pro Tour *Avacyn Restored*](/en/node/640181) in Barcelona. The metagame remains varied, with six different archetypes emerging victorious among the eight events listed below.
