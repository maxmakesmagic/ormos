**Upcoming *Kaladesh* Sealed Pro Tour Qualifier Final on November 13**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: a Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! A *Kaladesh* Sealed Pro Tour Qualifier Final will take place on this Sunday, November 13.


Remember, you are no longer required to enter into the PTQ Finals immediately following the Preliminary that you qualified through, thanks to the redesigned PTQ system that *Magic Online* Events Manager Lee Sharpe announced [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/articles/archive/magic-online/pro-tour-qualifier-changes-2015-11-16).


Learn more about *Magic Online* Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers#AER_schedule).

**The Year of Modern Flashbacks Continues with *Magic 2012* Starting Tomorrow**


It's 2016: The Year of Modern Flashbacks! If you want to build up a collection for Modern, these Flashback Drafts add everything you draft to you collection once the event is over. Even if Modern isn't your thing, you can enjoy a leisurely Draft through the planes of the past with rotating formats each week!


Triple *Magic 2012* Flashback Drafts start tomorrow and run until Wednesday, November 16 and are followed up by *Avacyn Restored* Drafts. Get the full schedule [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/MTGO/content/magic-online-limited-events#YearOfModernFlashbacks) and mark your calendar for the formats you're most excited about!

**Upcoming Extended Downtime on Wednesday, November 16**


There will be an extended downtime on Wednesday, November 16, for *Magic Online*. Events close at 12:00 a.m. PT/8 a.m. UTC, and store activity and trades end at 2:45 a.m. leading up to the system coming down at 3:00 a.m. The system is estimated to return from downtime at 12:00 p.m.

**Original *Modern Masters* Phantom Draft League**


 


**Running Time:** Wednesday, November 9, 10:00 a.m. Pacific (UTC-8) until Wednesday, November 16, 3:00 a.m. Pacific (UTC-8).  
**Entry Options:**


* Option 1: 10 Event Tickets
* Option 2: 100 Play Points

**Product:** *Magic Online* will provide three Phantom booster packs from the original *Modern Masters*  

All boosters are Phantom product and will not be added to a player's collection at the conclusion of the event  
**Structure:** Draft, then play up to three matches at your convenience before the end of the event  
**Format:** Draft  
**Prizes:**


 




| Match Wins | Prizes |
| --- | --- |
| 3 Wins | 150 Play Points |
| 2 Wins | 100 Play Points |
| 1 Win | 40 Play Points |
| 0 Wins | 10 Play Points |
