Decks of the Week: 7/28/2011
============================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on July 28, 2011 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











The summer **Magic** scene is heating up with players taking aim at invitations to Pro Tour Philadelphia and spots at the 2011 World Championships. Two major National Championships were held last weekend featuring a variety of decks in their respective Top 8s, plus eight Pro Tour Qualifiers have Standard decks to put on display. The qualifier season ends this weekend, so find a PTQ near you and grab that invite to Philly!
