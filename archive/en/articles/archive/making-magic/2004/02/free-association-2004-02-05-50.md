Free Association
================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on February 5, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_markrosewater.jpg)
By Mark Rosewater




 Working in R&D since '95, Mark became Magic head designer in '03. His hobbies: spending time with family, writing about Magic in all mediums, and creating short bios. 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/PTNY0001/Images/780.jpg)Little piece o' trivia: The potato is the only vegetable to ever appear in the name of a winning [Pro Tour](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/daily/mr110-pt) team. The team in question was Potato Nation (Gary Wise, Mike Turian, and **MagictheGathering.com's** own Scott Johns) at [Pro Tour](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/Magazine/Article.aspx?x=mtgcom/daily/mr110-pt) New York 2000. “Potato” is a nickname for Mike Turian.
