 The Extended season is winding down, but it's still got a few surprises up its sleeves! Barrett Neigel used this innovative Zoo-like list to earn a spot in the Top 8 of a Pro Tour Qualifier in Calgary. One thing that sets it apart from other Extended decks? Barrett's utilization of [Stoneforge Mystic](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Stoneforge+Mystic). The tiny Equipment tutor was able to find [Basilisk Collar](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Basilisk+Collar), [Sword of Light and Shadow](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sword+of+Light+and+Shadow), and [Umezawa's Jitte](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Umezawa%27s+Jitte). All are powerful cards in the mirror match and against other specific decks in Extended. And check out a whopping EIGHT Leylines in Barrett's sideboard!
