Live Coverage of 2004 English National Championship
===================================================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 27, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











Well Day 1 of the 2004 English Nationals is finally over. The Players have all headed home to get some much needed sleep before day 2 begins. Much of the shocks of the tournament have been how badly many of the better English players have done today, though once you realise how little testing many of them have actually done, the results do not seem all that shocking. 


Join us tomorrow for the remaining matches in the 2004 English Nationals
