[Woolly Thoctar](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Woolly+Thoctar) was a card that drew many a pair of bugged out eyes when it was spoiled on the front page of **Magicthegathering.com**. Give Micah Rabin credit for being one of the first players to achieve mainstream success with the 5/4. It was the above list played by Rabin at Vermont States that took home the title. Other interesting *Shards of Alara* cards featured include [Rip-Clan Crasher](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rip-Clan+Crasher), [Hell's Thunder](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hell%27s+Thunder), and [Ranger of Eos](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Ranger+of+Eos). Find out about more neat States decks all week long on Daily Decks!
