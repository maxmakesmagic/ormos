Reminder: Throne of Eldraine Deluxe Collection on Sale Tomorrow
===============================================================



 Posted in **News**
 on October 1, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











This is your friendly reminder that *Throne of Eldraine* Deluxe Collection goes on sale tomorrow (October 2) at 8 a.m. PT/11 a.m. ET/3 p.m. UTC only at [magicstore.wizards.com](https://magicstore.wizards.com). There is a limit of 1 per customer.


![ELD Deluxe Collection](https://media.wizards.com/2019/images/daily/G6mnUHeKnD.png)


If you missed the announcement, here's what each $449.99 Deluxe Collection will contain, all packed in a premium, stylized box:


* 16 *Throne of Eldraine* Collector Boosters
* 1 Set-themed binder
* 1 Foil Garruk, Cursed Huntsman borderless planeswalker card
* 1 Art print of the borderless Garruk, Cursed Huntsman
* 1 Non-foil version of the Buy-a-Box Kenrith, the Returned King
* 1 3x3 card strip from a foil sheet of *Throne of Eldraine*
* 1 *MTG Arena* mega-code card, which grants:
	+ A digital Garruk exquisite sleeve
	+ One copy of each of the following cards, plus a card style for each:
		- Garruk, Cursed Huntsman
		- Charming Prince
		- The Magic Mirror
		- Witch's Vengeance
		- Bonecrusher Giant
		- Questing Beast

You can also see us unboxing a Deluxe Collection in this handy video.

Our new online store, [magicstore.wizards.com](https://magicstore.wizards.com) will assign you a place in line in our queue when you arrive. Once we are sure the storefront is ready for the traffic and there is inventory available to still purchase, you will be let into the shopping cart to buy. The shopping cart is designed to be simple and clear. Our goal is to provide a clean, simple buying experience for a premium product.
