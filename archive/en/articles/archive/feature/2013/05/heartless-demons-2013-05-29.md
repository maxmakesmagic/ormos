Heartless Demons
================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on May 29, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_samblack.jpg)
By Sam Black




Sam Black is a Platinum Pro Player and longtime writer for StarCityGames.com. He is a respected deck builder and took over Daily Decks for the first half of 2013. 







I'm on a bit of a reader submission kick this week because I just realized that my emails had been redirected to an account I hadn't been watching (which I'd requested, but it happened much later than I thought) and I hadn't noticed because I'd been so busy testing for [Pro Tour *Dragon's Maze*](/en/events/coverage/voice-raises-wescoe-above-all-pro-tour) (so if you wrote me and never got a reply, that might be why). 

![Master of Cruelties](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Master+of+Cruelties)This deck was submitted by Simon Betz, and when he wrote about a Demon Tribal deck, I wasn't optimistic—after all, what's the point of making a tribal deck if there aren't any cards that reward you for sticking to that tribe?

Well, it turned out his deck was doing some cool things.

 He'd been trying to take advantage of [Master of Cruelties](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Cruelties), a card that had captured the imaginations of more than a few readers, and realized that [Master of Cruelties](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Cruelties) doesn't actually need power to reduce an opponent to 1 life (although it's certainly worse against creatures if it doesn't have any), so he could use [Heartless Summoning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heartless+Summoning) to play [Master of Cruelties](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Master+of+Cruelties), since he was planning to make it unblockable with [Rogue's Passage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rogue%27s+Passage) anyway. 

 And once you're a black-red deck with [Heartless Summoning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heartless+Summoning), you really can't do anything better than cast more Demons anyway. 

 This is especially potent with [Sire of Insanity](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sire+of+Insanity), which can come out on turn four with a [Heartless Summoning](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Heartless+Summoning) to make your opponent discard his or her entire hand—but your opponent probably didn't need that, right? 

 Below is the list Simon sent. Personally, I'd cut [Rakdos Guildgate](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Rakdos+Guildgate) for a Swamp, probably cut a few spells for more Demons, and I might play one or two copies of [Disciple of Griselbrand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Disciple+of+Griselbrand). As for the sideboard, I'm not too impressed by [Illness in the Ranks](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Illness+in+the+Ranks) and might rather just pay full price and get [Curse of Death's Hold](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Curse+of+Death%27s+Hold).
