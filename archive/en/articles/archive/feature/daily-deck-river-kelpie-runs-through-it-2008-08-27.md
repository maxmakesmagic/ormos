Looking to get some extra mileage from the cards in your graveyard? Conley Woods' 34th place deck from Grand Prix–Denver lets you do just that! He used retrace from [Raven's Crime](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Raven%27s+Crime) and reanimation spells like [Profane Command](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Profane+Command), [Makeshift Mannequin](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Makeshift+Mannequin), and [Puppeteer Clique](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Puppeteer+Clique) to get multiple uses out of his spells. Plus, with one of his [River Kelpie](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=River+Kelpie)s in play he even gets to draw a card for doing so!
