Obviously the Best Deck
=======================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on February 6, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_gerrythompson.jpg)
By Gerry Thompson











 We've seen a few different flavors of [Show and Tell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell) strategies in Legacy. Some are mono-blue with [Omniscience](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience) (and win with [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish)), some are blue-red (and win with [Sneak Attack](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Sneak+Attack) and fatties), and there are some mish-mashes containing elements of both, with or without [Burning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Burning+Wish). 

![Show and Tell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Show+and+Tell)Today I have another version.


**Magic Online** player "obv" is well-known in the real world as well and is no stranger to combo decks. His update to [Show and Tell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell) includes six discard spells and [Omniscience](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Omniscience), with [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) instead of [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish), which seems like an upgrade for a couple reasons. 

 First of all, you get a full fifteen-card sideboard instead of having to play tutor targets. Secondly, [Intuition](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Intuition) functions as a tutor for your combo pieces, which [Cunning Wish](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cunning+Wish) never did. The downside is that you don't win immediately, but you don't have to play [Dream Halls](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dream+Halls) (which is slow and unwieldy) and you have more, better things to [Show and Tell](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Show+and+Tell), like [Griselbrand](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Griselbrand). 

There are pros and cons of each version, but obv's version is new, exciting, and potentially the best version.
