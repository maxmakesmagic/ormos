Super Series Championship 2000 Fact Sheet
=========================================



 Posted in **Feature**







![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast












Congratulations on qualifying for **Magic: The Gathering**® Junior Super Series Championship. This information will help you to prepare **now** to attend in **June**.


**What and Where:**


The 2000 **Magic: The Gathering** JSS Championship will be held at *Disney's Wide World of Sports*™™ Fieldhouse, June 9 - 11, 2000. **Total scholarship prize to be awarded is $50,000.** 


***Disney Sports Reservations,*** **the Official Travel Planner for the Junior Super Series Championship have arranged the following for our group:** 


Two-Night Hotel and Theme Park Packages Starting at $121\*!


Packages include:


Two-night accommodations at a WALT DISNEY WORLD® Good Neighbor Hotel


Admission to your choice of two of the following WALT DISNEY WORLD® Theme Parks (none can be chosen more than once): 


one day of admission to the *Magic Kingdom® Park*  

one day of admission to *Epcot®*  

one day of admission to *Disney-MGM Studios*  

one day of admission to *Disney's Animal Kingdom® Theme Park*


-or-


Customize Your Package


As the official travel planner for your event at the WALT DISNEY WORLD® Resort, Disney Sports Reservations can offer these exclusive benefits:


Hotel accommodations  

Two-night room packages starting at $45\*  

Airfare  

Car/van rental  

Specially priced theme park and water park tickets


Call *Disney Sports Reservations,* the Official Travel Planner for the Junior Super Series Championship and the U.S. Nationals at the WALT DISNEY WORLD® Resort: **(407) 939-7810**.


\*Above lead-in package rates listed are a PER PERSON, inclusive rate based on four people in a room. Above packages are available pre-arrival only, based on availability. Prices are subject to change. Specially priced theme park tickets are valid only on the specific dates of the event and for a limited period of time before and/or after the event.


**Travel Details:** 


For assistance with travel within the United States, please check [www.wizards.com/jss/Challenge\_info.asp](http://www.wizards.com/jss/Challenge_info.asp) for further updates.
