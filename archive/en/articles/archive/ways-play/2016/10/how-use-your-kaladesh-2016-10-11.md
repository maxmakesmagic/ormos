How to Use Your Kaladesh
========================



 Posted in **Ways to Play**
 on October 11, 2016 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_BlakeRasmussen.jpg)
By Blake Rasmussen




Blake is the content manager for DailyMTG.com, making him the one you should email if you have thoughts on the website, good or less good (or not good). He's a longtime coverage reporter and hasn't turned down a game of Magic in any format ever. 






So you've just bought some packs of *Kaladesh* or are thinking about it. Now what? Over the past few weeks we've been talking about playing with *Kaladesh*, but where do you start?


Right here.




---

Haven't picked up your *Kaladesh* yet? *Kaladesh* boxes, packs, bundles, and Planeswalker Decks are now available at Target, Walmart, and local stores near you for all of your creative needs.


[Find a Store Near You](http://locator.wizards.com/)

**Drafting in Style**


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Cloudblazer)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Cloudblazer) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Whirler+Virtuoso)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Whirler+Virtuoso) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Voltaic+Brawler)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Voltaic+Brawler)
Why just crack your packs when you can grab some friends and draft? Whether you like blinking creatures for value, creating crazy combos, or brashly beating down, *Kaladesh* drafting has just what you're looking for.


Kenji Egashira helps you navigate the exciting world of the *Kaladesh* Draft format.


[The Color Pairs of Kaladesh](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/how-play-limited/color-pairs-kaladesh-2016-10-04%20)

**How to Kaladesh—In Graphics**


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Experimental+Aviator)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Experimental+Aviator) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Era+of+Innovation)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Era+of+Innovation)
Sure, now you know how to pair white cards with blue cards and red cards with green cards, but what about some of the set's more fun interactions? Learn how to *Kaladesh* with this visual guide to the format's best synergies.


[How to Kaladesh in Graphics](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/how-play-limited/how-kaladesh-graphics-2016-09-23%20)

**How to Vroom**


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Speedway+Fanatic)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Speedway+Fanatic) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Fleetwheel+Cruiser)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fleetwheel+Cruiser) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Depala%2C+Pilot+Exemplar)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Depala%2C+Pilot+Exemplar)
After drafting, you've no doubt picked up more than a few Vehicles and their Pilots. Dwarves have the coolest rides and sure know how to make them vroom. Got a need for speed? Look no further.


Gaby Spartz shows you how to win the race in *Kaladesh*'s finest rides.


[How to Vroom](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/how-build/how-vroom-2016-09-27)

**Get Creative**


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Electrostatic+Pummeler)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Electrostatic+Pummeler) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Aetherflux+Reservoir)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Aetherflux+Reservoir) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Metalwork+Colossus)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Metalwork+Colossus)
The [Inventors' Fair](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inventors%27+Fair) isn't just for residents of Kaladesh—you too can create some inventive decks to do wild things with the odd inventions of *Kaladesh*. Attack for 120? Use your life total to shoot down opponents? Summon a giant Colossus for free—and then do it again? With *Kaladesh*, the possibilities are endless.


[Kaladesh Build-Arounds](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/top-decks/kaladesh-build-arounds-2016-09-30%20)

**Invent New Ways to Play**


[![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Inventors%27+Fair)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Inventors%27+Fair) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Dubious+Challenge)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Dubious+Challenge) [![](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Handlers/Image.ashx?type=card&name=Madcap+Experiment)](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Madcap+Experiment)
*Kaladesh* is all about possibilities—so why not create a new way to play with *Kaladesh*? Reinvent your game with a Tinkerer Speed Run, a game of Stop the Mad Scientist, or Race for the Prize.


Chas Andres has the details on how to spice up your play group.


[Ways to Play in Kaladesh's Bright World](http://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/ways-play/ways-play-kaladeshs-bright-world-2016-10-05)
