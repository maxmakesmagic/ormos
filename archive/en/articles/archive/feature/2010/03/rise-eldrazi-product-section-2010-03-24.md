*Rise of the Eldrazi* Product Section
=====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on March 24, 2010 






![](https://web.archive.org/web/20211024110319im_/https://magic.wizards.com/sites/all/themes/wiz_mtg/images/global/generic-avatar-150.png)
By magicthegathering.com Staff











The [*Rise of the Eldrazi* Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/riseoftheeldrazi) is here! And it's stuffed full of information you want to see! There's an article about the storyline, a puzzle, a wallpaper, and the product information page. And, of course, there's the Orb of Insight *and* an article introducing all the new mechanics in the new set. Look for it in the "Explore *Rise of the Eldrazi*" section.

But that's not all! There's also the Visual Spoiler, an archive of all preview cards that have been officially released. You might want to keep an eye on that one, because it's going to be updated frequently over the next two and a half weeks as we count down to the *Rise of the Eldrazi* Prerelease on April 17-18.

[![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/features/feature83a_u9nuofst1d.jpg)](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/riseoftheeldrazi)  
So what are you waiting for? Hit the [*Rise of the Eldrazi* Product Section](http://archive.wizards.com/magic/tcg/Products.aspx?x=mtg/tcg/products/riseoftheeldrazi) now!
