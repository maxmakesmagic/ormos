Top 8 Profiles
==============



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on June 29, 2003 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a715.jpg)*Richard Oleksak***What is your name?**  
 Richard Oleksak  
**Where do you regularly play Magic? (Store, City it's in, Shameless PLUG!)**  
 Wild Pitch Sports Cards in Milwaukee, OR.  
**What do you think is the best deck in Standard?**  
 It hurts me to say, probably Blue-Green Madness  
**Combo, Aggro, Control (Playing today? Favorite?)**  
 Control today, it's my favorite.  
**8th Edition?**  
 Rewind, I guess.  
**Are your parents here with you today?**  
 My dad is here today. Hi Mom! I told you.  
**What do you do besides playing Magic?**  
 I play soccer and I swim.  
**What's your favorite subject in school?**  
 Math, I'm good at it. It comes pretty easy.  
**Where do you want to go to college?**  
 Not sure yet, maybe a University in Oregon. I might go to a school out east. I'd like to study architecture.  
**How did you qualify?**  
 I qualified with Burning Tog at a qualifier in Portland.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a709.jpg)*Mitch Towner***What is your name?**  
 Mitch Towner  
**Where do you regularly play Magic? (Store, City it's in, Shameless PLUG!)**  
 No stores, my house. Occasionally online.  
**What do you think is the best deck in Standard?**  
 Slide, obviously. It's a good metagame choice, I don't know if it's the best deck though.  
**Combo, Aggro, Control (Playing today? Favorite?)**  
 It's like weird control. I would rather play combo.  
**8th Edition?**  
 What's coming back in 8th Edition? Merchant of Secrets.  
**Are your parents here with you today?**  
 No parents here today. Sure, I'll say hi mom! How's it going?  
**What do you do besides playing Magic?**  
 I play a lot of basketball. I like to do a lot of sports.  
**What's your favorite subject in school?**  
 PE obviously. After that I do the best in math.  
**Where do you want to go to college?**  
 Haven't really thought about it. I want to see how far athletics take me.  
**How did you qualify?**  
 Won a JSS Challenge in Idaho with Upheaval-Infestation.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a712.jpg)*Daniel Bryan***What is your name?**  
 Daniel Bryan  
**Where do you regularly play Magic? (Store, City it's in, Shameless PLUG!)**  
 Wherever I can. I play FNM at the Wizards store in Silverdale, WA.  
**What do you think is the best deck in Standard?**  
 Really I have no clue. It's a pretty balanced field. The Wake players were touting they had the best deck, but not a single one made the Top 8.  
**Combo, Aggro, Control (Playing today? Favorite?)**  
 I'm playing a sort of agro-control. I like playing combo for casual, but mostly I play whatever.  
**8th Edition?**  
 Royal Assassin I've always kind of liked. It is kind of sad to see Counterspell go. We do get Mana Leak back though.  
**Are your parents here with you today?**  
 Yes they are. I could gloat for my friends back home, but I don't think I will.  
**What do you do besides playing Magic?**  
 I play on the computer, and I play Magic. I do some other stuff, like hang out with friends, go outside every now and then.  
**What's your favorite subject in school?**  
 Not math. I like anything else, just not math.  
**Where do you want to go to college?**  
 Somewhere local, probably Evergreen College, or maybe U-dub.  
**How did you qualify for this?**  
 Placed 3rd in a JSS Challenge in Tacoma with Black-Green Cemetery.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a714.jpg)*Kyle Goodman***What is your name?**  
 Kyle Goodman  
**Where do you regularly play Magic? (Store, City it's in, Shameless PLUG!)**  
 Pandemonium, in Garden City, MI.  
**What do you think is the best deck in Standard?**  
 Probably tog, but it loses to red-green.  
**Combo, Aggro, Control (Playing today? Favorite?)**  
 Control, I like to play it the most.  
**8th Edition?**  
 Underworld Dreams  
**Are your parents here with you today?**  
 They're still at the hotel. Thank you for flying me down and supporting me.  
**What do you do besides playing Magic?**  
 Soccer, basketball, other sports.  
**What's your favorite subject in school?**  
 Math.  
**Where do you want to go to college?**  
 I'd like to go to Stanford. It's a long ways off though.  
**How did you qualify for this event?**  
 I won a qualifier in Grand Rapids, MI with mono-black control.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a708.jpg)*Josh Tabak***What is your name?**  
 Josh Tabak  
**Where do you regularly play Magic? (Store, City it's in, Shameless PLUG!)**  
 Misty Mountain Games in Madison, WI.  
**What do you think is the best deck in Standard?**  
 No idea, red-green?  
**Combo, Aggro, Control (Playing today? Favorite?)**  
 Playing Aggro today.  
**8th Edition?**  
 Hammer of Bogardan  
**Are your parents here with you today?**  
 They're around somewhere. Hi to Dan Bock for lending me the cards for the deck.  
**What do you do besides playing Magic?**  
 Tennis, and computer games.  
**What's your favorite subject in school?**  
 Science? I guess it's science.  
**Where do you want to go to college?**  
 Haven't thought about college yet. I'm only 15.  
**How did you qualify?**  
 Through a JSS Challenge in Madison.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a710.jpg)*Peter Matson***What is your name?**   
 Peter Matson  
**Where do you regularly play Magic? (Store, City it's in, Shameless PLUG!)**  
 Online  
**What do you think is the best deck in Standard?**  
 My deck, blue-green with Phantom Centaurs  
**Combo, Aggro, Control (Playing today? Favorite?)**  
 I guess it's agro, I like to play blue-green the most.  
**8th Edition?**  
 The tap lands  
**Are your parents here with you today?**  
 Yeah. Say hi to my brother and my mom  
**What do you do besides playing Magic?**  
 I snowboard during the winter, I play golf and I run cross country. I do all sorts of other stuff too.  
**What's your favorite subject in school?**  
 Math  
**Where do you want to go to college?**  
 Different places. I want to go somewhere in the northeast probably.  
**How did you qualify?**  
 I won a JSS challenge in Syracuse playing Monastery control.

![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/natus03/a713.jpg)*Sam Stein***What is your name?**  
 Sam Stein  
**Where do you regularly Magic?**  
 Costa Mesa Woman's Club  
**What do you think is the best deck in Standard?**  
 I didn't mind red-green. I'd say red-green.  
**Combo, Aggro or Control.**  
 Playing agro today, like to play it the most.  
**8th Edition?**  
 Probably Hammer of Bogardan  
**Are your parents here with you today?**  
 I took a train down here, I'm staying with a friend today. Hi mom and dad, thanks for supporting me.  
**What do you do besides Magic?**  
 A huge passion for basketball. My dad played and played well but he ended up only 5'10" and couldn't keep doing it after high school.  
**Favorite subject?**  
 Spanish, but I get my best grades in Math.  
**Where do you want to go to college?**  
 No clue, want to stay in California though.  
**How did you qualify?**  
 I didn't get past Top 4 in any challenges. I played a blue-green Opposition deck for a lot of those challenges and just qualified on rating.
