A Dragon Named Mitzy
====================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on April 14, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











We've mentioned the [Shivan Dragon](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Shivan+Dragon) statue we've got on the fourth floor, right? It looks like this:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/167_dragon1.jpg)  
Well, recently we on the web team learned two things about this dragon that we didn't know. Thing the first: its name is "Mitzy". Thing the second:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/167_mom.jpg)  
...it loves its mother!


There's a third thing, which is that it looks cool if you're lying on your back right below it:


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/167_dragon2.jpg)  
But we already knew that one.
