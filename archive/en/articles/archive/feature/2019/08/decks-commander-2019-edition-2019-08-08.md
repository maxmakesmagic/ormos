The Decks of Commander (2019 Edition)
=====================================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on August 8, 2019 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











It's *Commander (2019 Edition)* preview week! Woot!


We're trying something new this year, giving you the decklists earlier than the very end of the week. Here's how it's going to work:


* Each day we're previewing cards from just one deck (with one exception that will be made obvious).
* At 3 p.m. PT every day, you can check out [The Command Zone](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCLsiaNUb42gRAP7ewbJ0ecQ) for a breakdown of cards in the deck that haven't been previewed elsewhere, plus a look at how the deck itself plays out.
* At the same time, right here, we'll be posting full decklists. Note that our system won't be updated with the new cards, so all you'll have is a name to go on.
* But fret not! You can follow along with every preview in our "[Where to Find *Commander (2019 Edition)* Previews](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/feature/where-find-commander-2019-edition-previews-2019-07-31)" article or catch up with our [Card Image Gallery](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/card-image-gallery/commander-2019-edition), which is updated each morning.

We'll be adding a new article each day, so bookmark this page and check back then!
