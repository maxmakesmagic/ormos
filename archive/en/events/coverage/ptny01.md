2000-2001 PRO TOUR-NEW YORK COVERAGE
====================================




September 29-October 1, 2000












2000-2001 Pro Tour-New York Coverage
------------------------------------


![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/images/pt_lg.gif)![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/sideboard/PTNY0001/Images/780.jpg)Scott Johns, Gary Wise, and Michael Turian are the 2000-2001 Pro Tour-New York champions!


Team Potato Nation didn't lose a match all tournament, and defeated Team Car Acrobatic Team (which lost only two matches, both to Potato Nation) in the finals, 2-0. The next Pro Tour is in Chicago from December 1-3. The format will be Standard Constructed.
