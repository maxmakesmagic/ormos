![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/events/promo/grandprix_2011.jpg)*Get your free promo card by playing in the Grand Prix (while supplies last).*Star City Games and Wizards of the Coast proudly present Grand Prix Denver. 


*The **Magic: The Gathering**  Grand Prix tournament series is a great reason to get out of the house, catch up with old friends, meet some new ones, and play **Magic**. Grand Prix tournaments are world-wide open events that allow all **Magic**  players to participate and experience the thrill of a large-scale competition. In addition to $30,000 in cash prizes, Pro Tour invitations for the top 16 finishers, and exclusive foil promo cards for participants, Grand Prix feature a host of other activities, including public events and artist signings (prizes subject to change). Find out more about the Grand Prix [tournament schedule here](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=grandprix/welcome#1).*



**Format**: Sealed Deck (3 packs of *Scars of Mirrodin* and 3 packs of *Mirrodin Besieged*) / Day 2 Booster Draft   
**Participation Fee**: $40   
 Grand Prix Denver will feed Pro Tour Nagoya.


**Registration and Schedule** 


* Friday, February 18: Registration – Noon to 9 p.m.
* Saturday, February 19: Registration – 7:30 a.m. to 8:45 a.m.
* Saturday, February 19: Tournament begins at 9 a.m.
* Sunday, February 20: Player meeting begins at 8:45 a.m.
* Sunday, February 20: Day Two begins at 9 a.m.

The main tournament at Grand Prix Denver is open to **Magic**  players of all ages.


**Tournament Format**
  


* [Day 1: Sealed Deck](/en/node/704561)  (3 packs of *Scars of Mirrodin* and 3 packs of *Mirrodin Besieged*)
* [Day 2: Booster Draft](/en/node/704561) (*Mirrodin Besieged-Scars of Mirrodin-Scars of Mirrodin*)

**Tournament Structure** 


* Modified Swiss-style (50-minute rounds).
* Rules Enforcement Level (REL) is Competitive (Day 1) and Professional (Day 2)
* If 799 or fewer players register for the event, all players with an X-2-0 or better record OR the top 64 players—whichever number is greater—will advance to the second day of the event.
* If 800 or more players register for the event, all players with an X-2-0 or better record OR the top 128 players—whichever number is greater—will advance to the second day of the event.
* The Top 8 players after the final Swiss round on Day 2 will advance to the Top 8 single-elimination finals.
* The Top 8 players will be seated randomly at the draft table immediately prior to drafting. The Top 8 bracket will be determined using this random seating.
* Top 8 playoff matches will be best 2 of 3, and have no time limit. (Players are still expected to play at a normal pace and complete playoff matches in a reasonable amount of time.)
* Where necessary, the standings after the Swiss rounds will still be used to determine final order in the standings.

**Judges**


 The Primary Head Judge for Grand Prix Denver is Jeff Morrow, Level 4 from Oakland California, and the second Head Judge will be Chris Richter, Level 3 from Madison, Wisconsin. 


**Notes**
  


* 40K, Rules Enforcement Level (REL) is Competitive (Day 1) and Professional (Day 2)
* The **Magic: The Gathering**  Tournament Rules and the **Magic: The Gathering**  Infraction Procedure Guide are in effect for this event unless specifically overruled by information in this Format Document. Rules documents can be found in the [DCI Document Center](http://www.wizards.com/wpn/Events/Rules.aspx?category=magic:thegathering).
* Players must bring pen, paper, card sleeves and appropriate counters.
* In the event that a player who wins an invitation to a Pro Tour stop at a Grand Prix has already qualified for an invitation, the invitation does not pass down under any circumstances. Previous qualification may be through a Qualifier tournament, through performance at a professional tournament, or by being an invited DCI player (valid upon the date of the official rank announcement for the appropriate professional tournament).



---


Byes will be given to players in the Grand Prix based on the following criteria *(Byes are not cumulative throughout the season or year. Byes do not pass down under any circumstances. Players that accumulate multiple byes to a particular Grand Prix tournament receive only the bye for the greatest number of rounds)*:


**Third-Round Byes**
  


* Players with a [Players Club](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=protour/playersclub/guidelines) level of 4 or higher
* Players with a DCI Total rating of 2050 or higher. Byes are based on DCI Total ratings published on February 16, 2011.
* Winners of Grand Prix Denver Trial tournaments.

**Second-Round Byes** 
  


* Players with a [Players Club](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=protour/playersclub/guidelines) level of 2 or 3
* Players with a DCI Total rating of 1950 or higher. Byes are based on DCI Total ratings published on February 16, 2011.

**First-Round Byes** 
  


* Players with a [Players Club](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=protour/playersclub/guidelines) level of 1
* Players with a DCI Total rating of 1850 or higher. Byes are based on DCI Total ratings published on February 16, 2011.

**Trial winners** 
  
 People who win a Trial event are awarded 3 byes in the Grand Prix that it feeds. Trial byes are not cumulative with other byes and do not pass down.

### Artist Information


**rk post**
  
 Since his arrival in *Exodus*, rk post has been a favorite artist of **Magic** players. His macabre illustrations have brought some of **Magic**’s most iconic demons and horrors to life. If you have ever admired [Avatar of Woe](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Avatar+of+Woe) or [Knight of Dusk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Knight+of+Dusk), stop by to have some cards signed or buy a print! 


* [**Magic** card art by rk post](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5b%22rk+post%22%5d)

**Steve Argyle**
  
 Steve Argyle burst onto the **Magic**  cart art scene during *Alara*  Block and crafted some of the seminal images of *Zendikar*  with his art gracing [Chandra Ablaze](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra+Ablaze), [Vampire Lacerator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vampire+Lacerator+)  and [Admonition Angel](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Admonition+Angel). Steve shows no signs of slowing down and has already made his mark by re-imagining the classic [Nevinyrral’s Disk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nevinyrral%E2%80%99s+Disk+)  for *From the Vaults: Relics*.


* [**Magic** card art by Steve Argyle](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5b%22Steve+Argyle%22%5d)

**Douglas Shuler**  
 One of the classic **Magic**  artists, Doug has had art featured in sets since the days of Alpha. Iconic pieces such as [Benalish Hero](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Benalish+Hero), [Hypnotic Specter](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hypnotic+Specter), and [Icy Manipulator](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Icy+Manipulator) have made Doug a fan favorite! 


* [**Magic** card art by Douglas Shuler](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5b%22Douglas+Shuler%22%5d)


**Dan Frazier**  
 Dan’s work adorns some of the most powerful cards in **Magic**  history. [Berserk](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Berserk), [Enlightened Tutor](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Enlightened+Tutor+) , and [Jester’s Cap](https://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jester%E2%80%99s+Cap) are just the tip of the iceberg in his extensive collection that spans from Alpha to present day. 


* [**Magic** card art by Dan Frazier](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Search/Default.aspx?output=spoiler&method=visual&action=advanced&artist=+%5b%22Dan+Frazier%22%5d)
