QUICK QUESTIONS #1
==================



 Posted in [GRAND PRIX KOBE 2014 - COVERAGE - EVENTS](/en/events/coverage/gpkob14)
 on August 22, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/benswa-author.jpg)
By Ben Swartz










Which player do you want to round out the Japanese World *Magic* Cup Team?





![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpkob14/kao_mihara2.jpg)
***Makahito Mihara:** – Myself.* 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpkob14/kao_yasooka2.jpg)
***Shota Yasooka:** – (looks around) Anyone.* 







![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpkob14/kao_watanabe2.jpg)
***Yuuya Watanabe:** – Anyone but Shuhei.* 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpkob14/kao_nakamura2.jpg)
***Shuhei Nakamura:** – (points to Tsuyoshi Fujita) That guy.* 







![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpkob14/kao_tsumura2.jpg)
***Kenji Tsumura:** – Myself and Tomoharu Saito. If that happens Yuuya and us will team for GP Nashville.* 


![](https://media.wizards.com/2014/events/gpkob14/kao_ichikawa2.jpg)
***Yuuki Ichickawa:** – Any strong Japanese player.*
