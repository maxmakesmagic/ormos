*Worldwake* Promo Cards
=======================



 Posted in **Arcana**
 on January 11, 2010 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











W*orldwake* is just around the corner. The product section is coming *this Wednesday*! That's so soon, it's practically here. So why not show off a couple of promo cards?

Here, for example, is the Prerelease card, which will be available at the [Prereleases](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/prerelease-facts) on January 30-31.

![Comet Storm](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/361_3vov15ew8m.jpg)  
Say hello to "Multikicker"! Now, let's move on to the [Launch Parties](http://archive.wizards.com/Magic/TCG/Events.aspx?x=mtgcom/events/release-facts), which will be February 5-7.

![Joraga Warcaller](https://media.magic.wizards.com/image_legacy_migration/mtg/images/daily/arcana/361_bn4cd6ayoh.jpg)  
Say hello to "Multikicker" ... again! And stick around for Wednesday's *Worldwake* product section, which will reveal all sorts of exciting new things!
