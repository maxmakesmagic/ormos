Looking for something fun to try during this National Qualifier season? Why not give Laurence Swasey's Green-Blue Aggro deck a shot? Swasey managed to make the Top 8 of the recent StarCityGames.com Standard Open in Boston with the deck. [Fauna Shaman](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Fauna+Shaman), [Nest Invader](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Nest+Invader), and [Vengevine](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Vengevine) provide the beatdown while [Garruk Wildspeaker](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Garruk+Wildspeaker) and [Jace, the Mind Sculptor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Jace%2C+the+Mind+Sculptor) provide a heavy-hitting Plan B. Read more about the deck in the [official coverage](http://www.starcitygames.com/magic/standard/21683_Live_Coverage_of_StarCityGamescom_Open_Series_Boston.html) from StarCityGames.com.
