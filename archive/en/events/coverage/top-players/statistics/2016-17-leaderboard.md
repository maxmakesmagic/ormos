2016-17 Premier Play Leaderboard
--------------------------------


#### Updated September 13, 2016


The 2016-17 premier play leaderboard showcases the top-ranked players, with ranking determined by Professional Points, in the 2016-17 premier play season. Premier play events include Grand Prix, Pro Tours, the World *Magic* Cup, and the World Championship. For Grand Prix, the best six finishes are counted towards seasonal Pro Points. Those six best finishes are displayed on this leaderboard, and are labeled "GP1" to "GP6" in order of best finish at a Grand Prix.


The player with the most Pro Points over the course of a season is identified as the season’s Player of the Year. The players with the most Pro Points over the course of a season within their geo-region (North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America) will be identified as their geo-region’s champion for the season.


In addition, the points listed below are the Professional Points totals that will be used to determine 2017-18 Pro Players Club levels and invitations to top tournaments.


The explanation for what each column refers to is as follows:


* **Nationality:** The player's nationality. This is used to determine national champions for the 2017 World *Magic*Cup.
* **GeoRegion:** The player's geo-region. This is used to determine the geo-regional champions that are invited to the 2017 *Magic: The Gathering*World Championship.
* **Total Points**: The total number of Pro Points that a player has earned this season, factoring in Pro Points earned at Pro Tours, the World *Magic*Cup, the World Championship, and a player's best six Grand Prix finishes.
* **PT Top 8s:** The number of Pro Tour Top 8s that player has earned this season.
* **Most PT Match Pts:** The most number of match points that a player has earned in any one Pro Tour this season. Match points are awarded after each match a competitor plays. 3 match points are awarded for a win. 1 match point is awarded for a draw. 0 match points are awarded for a loss. Please note that match points are still awarded in playoff rounds.
* **GP Top 8s:** The number of Grand Prix Top 8s that player has earned this season.
* **Most GP Match Pts:** The most number of match points that a player has earned in any one Grand Prix this season. Match points are awarded after each match a competitor plays. 3 match points are awarded for a win. 1 match point is awarded for a draw. 0 match points are awarded for a loss. Please note that match points are still awarded in playoff rounds.
* **GP Cap Total Points:** The number of Pro Points earned in a player's best six Grand Prix finishes. This total is factored into a player's total points.
* **GP1 to GP6:** The Pro Points earned at a player's best six Grand Prix finishes.
