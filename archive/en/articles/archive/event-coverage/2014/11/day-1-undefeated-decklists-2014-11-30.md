Day 1 Undefeated Decklists
==========================



 Posted in **Event Coverage**
 on November 30, 2014 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/calderaro.jpg)
By Marc Calderaro











It's hard to wade through the deep Standard waters. Not only is picking the right deck difficult, playing it right can be even harder. David Ochoa's mantra is: “Know how to play a deck; make sure the deck is good.” Seems simple enough. It worked out well for him and three other players who were able to escape the first day without a loss in their column. Here are their good decks that they knew how to play.
