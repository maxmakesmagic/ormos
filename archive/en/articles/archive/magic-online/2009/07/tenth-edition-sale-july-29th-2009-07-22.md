Tenth Edition Off Sale July 29th!
=================================



 Posted in **Magic Online**
 on July 22, 2009 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_author.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











M***agic** 2010* arrives in the **Magic Online** Store August 3rd! Sadly this means *Tenth Edition* is leaving. After the July 29th downtime, *Tenth Edition* will no longer be available for sale. Nix Tix *Tenth Edition* queues are running until July 29th though, and are a fantastic way to say farewell to this great core set. 

*Tenth Edition* rotates out of the Standard format and ***Magic** 2010* becomes legal in ALL constructed formats following the July 29th downtime. 

All Standard Constructed format Daily Events, Premier Events and 8-player queues will have ***Magic** 2010* added to their prize payout mix starting on Monday, August 3rd at 9:00 am PDT. 

Pauper Weekend Challenges will have ***Magic** 2010* added to their prize payout mix starting with the Sunday, August 2nd Challenge. Pauper 8-player constructed queues and 2-player constructed queues will have ***Magic** 2010* added to their prize payout mix starting on Monday, August 3rd at 9:00 am PDT. 

Standard format 2-player queues will have ***Magic** 2010* added to their prize payout following the August 19th downtime.
