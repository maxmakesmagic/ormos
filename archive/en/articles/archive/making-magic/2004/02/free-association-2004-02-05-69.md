Free Association
================



 Posted in [Making Magic](/en/articles/columns/making-magic)
 on February 5, 2004 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/authorpic_markrosewater.jpg)
By Mark Rosewater




 Working in R&D since '95, Mark became Magic head designer in '03. His hobbies: spending time with family, writing about Magic in all mediums, and creating short bios. 






How many of you got that reference? I've always prided myself in sticking in allusions to as many pop culture references as I can. 

And with that, I bid you adieu.

Come on back now, you hear.

Join me next week when we try it your way.

Until then, all your base are belong to us.

Mark Rosewater
