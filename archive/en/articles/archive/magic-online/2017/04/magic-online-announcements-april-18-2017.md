**Build notes**


General | Rules change regarding split cards has been implemented. Now, split cards in a zone other than the stack have a converted mana cost equal to the sum of each halfâ€™s converted mana cost.


General | *Amonkhet* card set added to *Magic Online*. Treasure Chests and New Account Starter Kits will be updated on Monday, April 24, prior to *Amonkhet* events beginning.


General | *Magic Online Player* rewards for March have been granted. You can see which cards those are in this article.


General | *Aether Revolt* prestige avatars have been granted. You can find out more about those, in this article.


Collection | Art for the promo versions of [Glorybringer](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Glorybringer), [Trueheart Duelist](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Trueheart+Duelist), and [Capture of Jingzhou](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Capture+of+Jingzhou) now display correctly. Also corrected the art for some tokens.


Standard | Spells with X in their mana cost can now be cast when affected by the triggered ability of [Gonti, Lord of Luxury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gonti%2C+Lord+of+Luxury).


Modern | Putting a split card on the bottom of your library from a group of revealed cards no longer causes the split card to disappear and become inaccessible.


Modern | Players now correctly take damage from the emblem created by [Chandra, Torch of Defiance](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Chandra%2C+Torch+of+Defiance) when they are under the effects of [Runed Halo](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Runed+Halo) with Chandra as the named card.


Modern | Cards with delirium now correctly count split cards that are in the graveyard.


Modern | Cards with hybrid mana costs may now be correctly cast in situations where they could be cast for mana of any color, such as from the triggered ability of [Gonti, Lord of Luxury](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Gonti%2C+Lord+of+Luxury).


Modern | Using proliferate on more than 30 cards no longer causes the game to crash.


Modern | A rare circumstance in which [Mishra's Bauble](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Mishra%27s+Bauble) would cause the game to crash has been fixed.


Modern | [Pillar of Flame](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pillar+of+Flame) no longer exiles targets that leave the battlefield that turn, if the damage from [Pillar of Flame](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Pillar+of+Flame) is prevented.


Modern | [Plunge into Darkness](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Plunge+into+Darkness) now includes an OK button, and can be resolved correctly.


Modern | When cards are separated into ordered piles due to [Liliana of the Veil](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Liliana+of+the+Veil)'s third ability, associations between cards are now shown.


Modern | [Spawnsire of Ulamog](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Spawnsire+of+Ulamog)'s activated ability now costs the correct amount of mana.


Commander | Cards which add mana based on your commander's color identity, such as [Command Tower](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Command+Tower) and [Commander's Sphere](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Commander%27s+Sphere), now allow you add any color mana from your commander's color identity rather than just its color.


Duel Scene | Fixed several issues that could cause the game to restart


Standard | Modern | Fixed an issue where multiple copies of [Winding Constrictor](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Winding+Constrictor) and/or [Hardened Scales](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Hardened+Scales) added the wrong number of +1/+1 counters.
