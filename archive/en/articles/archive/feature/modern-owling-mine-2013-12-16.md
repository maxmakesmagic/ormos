Modern Owling Mine
==================



 Posted in **Feature**
 on December 16, 2013 






![](https://media.magic.wizards.com/styles/auth_small/public/images/person/wizards_authorpic_larger.jpg)
By Wizards of the Coast











 This is an old-school deck from Kamigawa times that has been rearing its head in Modern on **Magic Online**. What's interesting is just how similar it is to the old-school version from that timeframe. **Magic** Invitational winner and [Snapcaster Mage](http://gatherer.wizards.com/Pages/Card/Details.aspx?name=Snapcaster+Mage) himself, Tiago Chan, piloted [a version of this deck](/en/articles/archive/event-coverage/decklists-top-8-decklists-2006-03-04) to the Top 8 of Pro Tour Honolulu in 2006.
