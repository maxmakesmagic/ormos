**No Vintage Super League tonight**


There will be no Vintage Super League (VSL) tonight, the stream will resume next week on November 7.


Interested in the decklists being played on VSL? Decklists are updated weekly and can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/vintage-super-league-season-7-decklists).

**Flashback Favorites: 3x *Innistrad*ends tomorrow, November 1**


Entries for Flashback Favorites: 3x *Innistrad* will close at 10 a.m. PT tomorrow. Join this event in the Play Lobby -> Limited Tournaments -> Leagues. Entry options include 12 Event Tickets, 3 of the appropriate boosters + 2 Event Tickets, or 120 Play Points


More information about *Magic Online*'s Flashback Favorites can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/flashback-favorites-and-throwback-standard-gauntlets-2016-12-15).

**Throwback Standard Gauntlet 8: *Innistrad*era begins tomorrow, November 1**


Throwback Standard Gauntlet: *Innistrad*era begins tomorrow, November 1 and will run until November 8.


Decklists and additional information about Throwback Standard Gauntlet 8: *Innistrad*era can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/throwback-standard-gauntlet-8-innistrad-standard).


[Top](#top)

**Throwback Standard Gauntlet 9: Recent Past begins November 8**


Throwback Standard Gauntlet: Recent Past begins November 8 and will run until November 17. That's right! This Gauntlet will run two extra days! Expect Randy Buehler's article to be published tomorrow on [MTGO.com](https://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-products-game-info), including the Gauntlet's decklists.


More information about Throwback Standard Gauntlets can be found [here](https://magic.wizards.com/en/articles/archive/magic-online/flashback-favorites-and-throwback-standard-gauntlets-2016-12-15).


[Top](#top)

**Standard PTQ this Saturday, November 4**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Saturday, November 4 at 9 a.m. PT. There is no need to qualify through a Preliminary– simply join this event with 30 Event Tickets or 300 Play Points.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).


[Top](#top)





---

***Ixalan*Sealed PTQ this Sunday, November 5**


Attention all Pro Tour hopefuls: A Pro Tour Qualifier (PTQ) is approaching! We hope to see you in the PTQ Finals on Sunday, November 5 at 7 a.m. PT.


You can enter this event using a Limited PTQ Finals Entry Token (Blue or Red) earned in a PTQ Preliminary.


Learn more about *Magic Online*Pro Tour Qualifiers [here](http://magic.wizards.com/en/content/magic-online-pro-tour-qualifiers).

**No downtime tomorrow, November 1**


There will be no downtime tomorrow, November 1. The next two downtimes will be on November 15 (regular, 8 a.m. PT/4:00 p.m. UTC) and November 29 (extended, 3 a.m. PT/10:00 p.m. UTC).


Follow *Magic Online*'s [Twitter](https://twitter.com/MagicOnline) account for live updates during downtime.
